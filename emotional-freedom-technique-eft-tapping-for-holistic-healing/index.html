Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT): Tapping for Holistic Healing


Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT), also known as Tapping, is a powerful holistic healing technique that combines elements of psychology, acupuncture, and energy medicine. It has gained significant popularity in recent years due to its effectiveness in relieving emotional and physical pain, reducing stress, and promoting overall well-being. In this blog post, we will explore the basics of EFT, its benefits, and how you can incorporate it into your daily life for holistic healing.

What is EFT?

EFT is based on the belief that emotional stress and trauma can disrupt the body’s energy system, leading to physical and emotional imbalances. By tapping on specific acupressure points on the body while focusing on the issue at hand, EFT aims to restore the balance of energy and release negative emotions.

How does EFT work?

The theory behind EFT is rooted in the concept of meridians, which are energy channels in the body. According to Traditional Chinese Medicine, when these meridians are blocked or disrupted, it can result in physical and emotional ailments. By tapping on specific points on the body, EFT aims to unblock these energy channels and restore harmony.

The Tapping Sequence

To practice EFT, follow these simple steps:

  1. Identify the issue: Begin by identifying the specific issue or emotion you want to address. It could be anything from anxiety and stress to physical pain or a traumatic memory.

  2. The Setup: While focusing on the issue, create a setup statement that combines acknowledgment of the problem with self-acceptance. For example, “Even though I feel anxious about my upcoming presentation, I deeply and completely accept myself.”

  3. The Tapping: With the tips of your fingers, gently tap on the following acupressure points while repeating a reminder phrase that describes the issue:

    • Eyebrow point
    • Side of the eye
    • Under the eye
    • Under the nose
    • Chin
    • Collarbone
    • Under the arm
    • Top of the head

  4. The Gamut Point: After completing the tapping sequence, tap on the gamut point, located between the ring finger and the little finger, while performing a series of eye and body movements. This step helps to further stimulate the brain and integrate the tapping process.

  5. Reassess and Repeat: After completing one round of tapping, take a moment to reassess your emotional intensity or physical discomfort. If necessary, repeat the tapping sequence to further release any remaining tension or negative emotions.

Benefits of EFT

EFT has been shown to provide numerous benefits, including:

  • Stress reduction: By tapping on the acupressure points, EFT helps to calm the nervous system and reduce stress levels, allowing for a greater sense of peace and relaxation.

  • Emotional healing: EFT is particularly effective in releasing past traumas, negative emotions, and limiting beliefs. It allows individuals to process and release emotional pain, promoting emotional healing and personal growth.

  • Pain management: EFT has been found to be effective in reducing physical pain, including chronic pain conditions. By addressing the emotional component associated with pain, EFT can provide relief and improve overall well-being.

  • Improved energy flow: By tapping on the specific acupressure points, EFT helps to restore the flow of energy in the body, promoting balance and vitality.

Incorporating EFT into Your Daily Life

To experience the benefits of EFT, consider incorporating it into your daily routine. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Morning routine: Start your day by practicing EFT to set a positive and balanced tone for the day ahead. Focus on any emotions or issues that may be present and tap on them to release any negative energy.

  • Stressful situations: Whenever you find yourself in a stressful situation, take a moment to tap on the acupressure points to calm your nervous system and regain a sense of control.

  • Bedtime ritual: Use EFT as part of your bedtime ritual to release any accumulated stress or tension from the day. Focus on any worries or anxieties and tap on them to promote a restful night’s sleep.

Final Thoughts

EFT is a simple yet powerful technique that can be easily incorporated into your daily life. By tapping on specific acupressure points, you can release negative emotions, reduce stress, and promote overall well-being. Whether you’re dealing with emotional pain, physical discomfort, or simply looking to enhance your overall health, EFT offers a holistic approach to healing. Give it a try and experience the transformative power of tapping for yourself.
