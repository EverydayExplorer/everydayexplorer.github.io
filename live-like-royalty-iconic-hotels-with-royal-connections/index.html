Live Like Royalty: Iconic Hotels with Royal Connections


When it comes to experiencing luxury, there’s nothing quite like staying at a hotel with royal connections. These magnificent establishments have hosted kings, queens, and other members of royal families throughout history. In this blog post, we’ll take a closer look at some of the most iconic hotels with royal connections, allowing you to live like royalty during your next vacation.

1. The Ritz, London
Located in the heart of London, The Ritz is a hotel that exudes elegance and grandeur. This iconic establishment has been a favorite among royalty for over a century. It has accommodated numerous kings, queens, and even members of the British royal family. From its opulent rooms to its exceptional service, The Ritz offers a truly regal experience.

2. The Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur
Situated in the middle of Lake Pichola, the Taj Lake Palace in Udaipur, India, is a stunning hotel that has served as a royal residence in the past. This magnificent palace was built in the 18th century and boasts breathtaking views of the surrounding lake and city. With its luxurious accommodations and impeccable hospitality, the Taj Lake Palace offers guests a chance to experience the royal lifestyle.

3. The Plaza, New York City
Known as one of the most iconic hotels in New York City, The Plaza has a long history of hosting royalty. This grand hotel has welcomed members of various royal families from around the world, including the Duke and Duchess of Windsor and Princess Grace of Monaco. Its lavish rooms, exquisite dining options, and prime location make The Plaza a favorite among the elite.

4. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur
Another gem in Udaipur, India, is The Oberoi Udaivilas. This luxurious hotel is known for its stunning architecture inspired by the palaces of Rajasthan. It offers guests a chance to immerse themselves in the rich culture and royal heritage of India. With its sprawling gardens, private pools, and regal suites, The Oberoi Udaivilas is a true oasis fit for a king or queen.

5. The Gritti Palace, Venice
Located on the Grand Canal in Venice, Italy, The Gritti Palace is a historic hotel that has been frequented by royalty for centuries. With its elegant Venetian décor, luxurious accommodations, and breathtaking views of the canal, this palace-turned-hotel offers guests a taste of the opulent lifestyle enjoyed by European nobility.

6. The Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai
Situated in the heart of Mumbai, India, The Taj Mahal Palace is an architectural marvel that has hosted numerous members of royal families from around the world. This iconic hotel offers a perfect blend of traditional Indian hospitality and modern luxury. With its exquisite rooms, world-class dining, and impeccable service, The Taj Mahal Palace guarantees a royal experience.

7. The Savoy, London
Located on the banks of the River Thames, The Savoy is a legendary hotel that has attracted royalty since its opening in 1889. Over the years, it has welcomed distinguished guests, including kings, queens, and members of the British royal family. With its timeless elegance, luxurious amenities, and prime location, The Savoy continues to be a symbol of luxury and sophistication.

In conclusion, if you’re looking to live like royalty during your next vacation, consider staying at one of these iconic hotels with royal connections. From London to Udaipur to Venice, these establishments offer a glimpse into the grandeur and opulence enjoyed by kings and queens throughout history. With their regal accommodations, impeccable service, and rich history, these hotels are sure to make your stay truly unforgettable.
