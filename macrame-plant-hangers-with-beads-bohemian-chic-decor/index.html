Macrame Plant Hangers with Beads: Bohemian Chic Decor


When it comes to adding a touch of bohemian chic to your home decor, macrame plant hangers with beads are the perfect choice. These intricate and stylish hangers not only serve as a unique way to display your favorite plants, but they also add a touch of elegance and charm to any space. In this blog post, we will explore the art of macrame, the beauty of adding beads to your hangers, and how you can incorporate them into your home to create a bohemian oasis.

Macrame is an ancient crafting technique that involves knotting cords together to create beautiful and intricate patterns. This art form has been used for centuries to create stunning textile pieces, and it has recently made a comeback in the world of interior design. Macrame plant hangers are a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and interior decorators alike, as they provide a unique and eye-catching way to display your greenery.

Adding beads to your macrame plant hangers takes them to the next level of bohemian chic. Beads can be incorporated into the design in various ways, including as accents within the knots or as decorative elements hanging from the bottom of the hanger. The addition of beads adds texture, color, and a touch of whimsy to your hangers, making them truly one-of-a-kind.

To create your own macrame plant hangers with beads, you will need a few basic supplies. First, you will need a sturdy cord, such as cotton or jute, to create the knots. You will also need beads of your choice, which can be found in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors to suit your personal style. Additionally, you will need a pair of scissors, a ruler or measuring tape, and a plant pot that fits snugly into your hanger.

To get started, measure and cut your cord to the desired length for your hanger. Remember to leave extra length for the knots and the hanging loop. Next, create a series of knots using the macrame technique of your choice. There are various knotting patterns to choose from, such as the square knot, the spiral knot, or the double half hitch knot. Experiment with different patterns to create a unique design.

Once you have created the main body of your hanger, it’s time to add the beads. Thread your chosen beads onto the cord and incorporate them into your knots, or attach them to the ends of the hanger for a decorative touch. Be creative with your bead placement and consider mixing different bead sizes and colors to add visual interest to your hanger.

Once you have finished knotting and beading your hanger, it’s time to hang it up and display your favorite plant. Choose a spot in your home that receives plenty of natural light and watch as your macrame plant hanger with beads becomes a focal point of your bohemian chic decor.

In conclusion, macrame plant hangers with beads are a fantastic way to add a touch of bohemian chic to your home decor. The intricate knots and the addition of beads create a unique and eye-catching display for your favorite plants. Whether you are a plant enthusiast or simply looking to add a touch of elegance to your space, macrame plant hangers with beads are a must-have. So, grab your supplies and get crafty – your bohemian oasis awaits!
