How to Make Homemade Pizza from Scratch


Making homemade pizza from scratch is a delightful and rewarding experience. There’s something special about creating your pizza dough, selecting your favorite toppings, and baking it to perfection in the comfort of your own kitchen. In this blog post, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of making a delicious homemade pizza that will leave you wanting more. So, grab your apron and let’s get started!

1. Gather the ingredients

To make a homemade pizza from scratch, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Pizza dough: You can make your own pizza dough using flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil. Alternatively, you can buy pre-made pizza dough from your local grocery store.
  • Tomato sauce: Go for a good quality tomato sauce for the best flavor. You can also make your own by blending canned tomatoes with herbs and spices.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella cheese is the classic choice for pizza, but you can also experiment with other types like cheddar, provolone, or Parmesan.
  • Toppings: This is where you can get creative! Choose your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, or even pineapple.
  • Herbs and spices: Sprinkle some oregano, basil, red pepper flakes, or garlic powder to enhance the flavor of your pizza.

2. Prepare the pizza dough

If you’re making your own pizza dough, follow these steps:

  • In a large bowl, combine 2 ¼ teaspoons of active dry yeast with 1 ½ cups of warm water and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • Add 3 ½ cups of all-purpose flour, 2 teaspoons of salt, and 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  • Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

3. Shape and assemble the pizza

Once your pizza dough has risen, it’s time to shape and assemble your pizza:

  • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can reach (usually around 500°F or 260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  • Punch down the dough to remove any air bubbles. Divide it into two equal portions if you want to make two medium-sized pizzas.
  • On a floured surface, roll out the dough using a rolling pin. Start from the center and work your way out, rotating the dough as needed, until you achieve your desired thickness.
  • Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Be sure not to overload the pizza with sauce, as it can make the crust soggy.
  • Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings and sprinkle some herbs and spices for extra flavor.
  • Using the pizza peel or baking sheet, carefully transfer the assembled pizza into the preheated oven (or onto the hot pizza stone).
  • Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.

4. Enjoy your homemade pizza!

Once your pizza is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. This will ensure that the cheese sets and the flavors meld together. Then, slice it up and it’s time to indulge in your homemade pizza creation!

Making homemade pizza from scratch allows you to customize every aspect of your pie, from the thickness of the crust to the selection of toppings. It’s a fun and delicious way to gather friends and family together or simply enjoy a cozy night in.

Remember, practice makes perfect when it comes to pizza making. Don’t be discouraged if your first attempt doesn’t turn out exactly as you envisioned. With time and experience, you’ll become a pizza master in your own kitchen.

So, put on some good music, pour yourself a glass of wine or your favorite beverage, and embark on a pizza-making adventure that will satisfy your taste buds and impress your loved ones. Homemade pizza has never tasted so good!
