Adventure Awaits: Hotels for Daredevils and Adrenaline Junkies


Are you an adrenaline junkie? Do you love thrill-seeking adventures that get your heart racing and your adrenaline pumping? If so, then this blog post is for you! We’re going to take a look at some of the world’s most exciting hotels that are perfect for daredevils and adrenaline junkies. From skydiving to bungee jumping, these hotels offer a range of thrilling activities that will push your limits and leave you with unforgettable memories.

1. The Skylodge Adventure Suites, Peru:
Located in the Sacred Valley of Cuzco, the Skylodge Adventure Suites are not for the faint of heart. These unique transparent pods are suspended off the side of a cliff, 1,312 feet above the ground. In order to reach your pod, you have to climb 400 meters of via ferrata or tackle a zipline. Once inside, you’ll have breathtaking views of the valley and the stars above. As if that weren’t enough, guests can also enjoy gourmet meals and a zip line descent back to the valley floor.

2. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:
If you’ve ever dreamed of staying in an igloo, then the Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland is the place to make that dream a reality. Located in the Arctic Circle, this unique resort offers glass igloos that allow you to sleep under the Northern Lights. You can also enjoy a range of outdoor activities, such as husky sledding, snowmobiling, and ice fishing. For the ultimate adrenaline rush, you can even take a dip in the icy waters of the nearby Lake Inari.

3. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
For those seeking a different kind of adventure, Giraffe Manor in Kenya offers a truly unique experience. This boutique hotel is located on 12 acres of private land, where a herd of endangered Rothschild giraffes freely roam. You can have breakfast with the giraffes, feed them from your window, and even take a guided walk with them through the forest. With its stunning surroundings and gentle giants as your companions, Giraffe Manor is the perfect destination for animal lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

4. Hotel Costa Verde, Costa Rica:
If you’ve ever wanted to spend the night in a real airplane, then Hotel Costa Verde in Costa Rica is the place to do it. This fully-restored Boeing 727 was once used for transporting travelers around the world and now sits perched on a 50-foot pedestal in the middle of the jungle. The interior has been converted into a luxury suite with two bedrooms, a kitchenette, and a terrace overlooking the surrounding rainforest. When you’re not enjoying the unique accommodations, you can explore the nearby Manuel Antonio National Park, go zip-lining through the jungle, or try your hand at white-water rafting.

5. Null Stern Hotel, Switzerland:
For the ultimate adrenaline rush, the Null Stern Hotel in Switzerland offers a zero-star experience unlike any other. This open-air hotel has no walls or roof, providing guests with a unique opportunity to sleep under the stars. Located in the heart of the Swiss Alps, you’ll have panoramic views of the stunning mountain landscape. With no amenities or luxuries, this hotel is all about reconnecting with nature and embracing the thrill of the unknown.

These hotels are just a few examples of the countless options available for daredevils and adrenaline junkies. Whether you’re into extreme sports, wildlife encounters, or simply sleeping in unique accommodations, there’s a hotel out there for you. So, if you’re ready to take your next adventure to the next level, why not consider booking a stay at one of these thrilling destinations? Adventure awaits!
