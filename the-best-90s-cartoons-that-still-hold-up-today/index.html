The Best 90s Cartoons That Still Hold Up Today


The 90s was a golden age for cartoons. It was a time when Saturday mornings were eagerly anticipated, and kids would gather around the television to watch their favorite animated shows. Fast forward to today, and many of those beloved 90s cartoons still hold up remarkably well. In this blog post, we’ll take a trip down memory lane and explore some of the best 90s cartoons that are just as enjoyable today as they were back then.

1. ‘Batman: The Animated Series’:
First on our list is the critically acclaimed ‘Batman: The Animated Series.’ This dark and gritty show introduced a new generation to the caped crusader and quickly became a fan favorite. With its stunning animation, complex storytelling, and unforgettable characters like Batman, The Joker, and Harley Quinn, this cartoon set the bar high for future superhero adaptations.

2. ‘SpongeBob SquarePants’:
Who lives in a pineapple under the sea? SpongeBob SquarePants! This iconic cartoon has become a cultural phenomenon, winning the hearts of both children and adults alike. With its witty humor, lovable characters, and catchy theme song, ‘SpongeBob SquarePants’ continues to entertain audiences to this day.

3. ‘The Powerpuff Girls’:
Sugar, spice, and everything nice! Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup captured our imaginations in ‘The Powerpuff Girls.’ This action-packed show followed the adventures of three super-powered kindergarteners as they protected Townsville from various villains. With its unique art style, clever writing, and empowering message, ‘The Powerpuff Girls’ remains a beloved cartoon of the 90s.

4. ‘Animaniacs’:
‘Animaniacs’ was a zany and irreverent cartoon that pushed the boundaries of animation. Featuring a variety of characters, including the mischievous Warner siblings - Yakko, Wakko, and Dot, this show blended humor, music, and pop culture references into a delightful package. Its clever writing and memorable skits make it a timeless cartoon that still appeals to audiences today.

5. ‘Rugrats’:
What’s it like to see the world through the eyes of a baby? ‘Rugrats’ answered that question with its charming and imaginative storytelling. This show followed the adventures of Tommy Pickles and his gang of diaper-clad friends as they navigated the world around them. With its relatable characters and heartwarming themes, ‘Rugrats’ continues to resonate with both children and adults.

6. ‘Dexter’s Laboratory’:
‘Dexter’s Laboratory’ introduced us to the boy genius, Dexter, and his secret lab hidden behind his bedroom. This cartoon was a perfect mix of comedy and adventure, as Dexter’s inventions often led to unpredictable consequences. With its clever writing and memorable catchphrases, ‘Dexter’s Laboratory’ remains a favorite among 90s cartoon enthusiasts.

7. ‘Pokemon’:
Gotta catch ’em all! No list of 90s cartoons would be complete without mentioning ‘Pokemon.’ This global phenomenon took the world by storm, captivating audiences with its exciting battles, adorable creatures, and epic adventures. With numerous spin-offs, movies, and video games, ‘Pokemon’ has stood the test of time and continues to be a beloved franchise.

These are just a few examples of the best 90s cartoons that still hold up today. Whether you grew up in the 90s or are discovering these shows for the first time, there’s no denying the lasting impact they have had on popular culture. So, grab a bowl of cereal, sit back, and enjoy the nostalgia-filled journey through these timeless animated classics.
