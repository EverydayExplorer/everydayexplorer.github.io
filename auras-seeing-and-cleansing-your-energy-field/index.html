Auras: Seeing and Cleansing Your Energy Field


In the vast tapestry of the human experience, there exists a realm that is beyond the reach of our physical senses. It’s a world of energy, vibrations, and frequencies that influence our well-being in ways we are only beginning to understand. This mysterious realm is none other than the aura, the invisible but ever-present energy field that surrounds every living being.

What is an Aura?

The concept of the aura is not a new one. It has been explored and embraced by various cultures and belief systems for centuries. In essence, an aura is an electromagnetic field that surrounds every living organism, including humans, animals, and even plants. This field is composed of different layers, often referred to as the ‘subtle bodies’ or ’energy bodies.’

Each layer of the aura is associated with different aspects of our physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being. The aura is believed to contain information about a person’s state of health, emotions, thoughts, and even their spiritual development. It’s like a living, ever-changing fingerprint of one’s being.

Seeing Auras

While auras are typically invisible to the naked eye, some individuals possess the gift of perceiving these subtle energy fields. This ability is often referred to as ‘seeing auras’ or ‘aura reading.’ People who can see auras describe the experience as seeing a halo of colored light surrounding a person or object. Each color in the aura is associated with specific qualities and characteristics.

For those of us who do not naturally see auras, there are techniques and exercises that can be learned to develop this skill. Here are a few methods to get you started:

  1. Soft Gaze: When looking at a person or object, soften your focus and allow your peripheral vision to come into play. Over time, you may start to perceive auras as subtle colors or light.

  2. Aura Photography: Some specialized cameras claim to capture and display a person’s aura in a photograph. While the accuracy of these devices is debated, they can be a fun way to explore the concept of auras.

  3. Meditation and Visualization: Meditation and visualization techniques can help you attune your senses to the subtle energies around you. By regularly practicing these techniques, you may gradually begin to see auras.

  4. Aura Readers: Consider consulting with a professional aura reader or energy healer who can provide insights into your aura and its colors.

Understanding Aura Colors

Auras are often described in terms of colors, and each color is associated with specific qualities and characteristics. Here’s a brief overview of some common aura colors:

  1. Red: Associated with strength, passion, and vitality. A person with a strong red aura may be energetic and adventurous.

  2. Orange: Reflects creativity, enthusiasm, and optimism. Those with orange auras may have a zest for life and a creative spirit.

  3. Yellow: Represents intellect, clarity, and joy. People with yellow auras tend to be analytical and have a sunny disposition.

  4. Green: Signifies growth, healing, and balance. Green auras are often found in individuals who are nurturing and compassionate.

  5. Blue: Linked to communication, truth, and spirituality. A person with a blue aura may be a good communicator and deeply connected to their spiritual side.

  6. Purple: Indicates intuition, spirituality, and psychic abilities. Purple auras are often seen in individuals with strong intuitive gifts.

  7. White: Reflects purity, spiritual awakening, and high energy. A white aura is often associated with those who are spiritually evolved.

Cleansing Your Aura

Just as we cleanse our physical bodies, it’s essential to care for our energy bodies by regularly cleansing our auras.
