The Best Cars for Pet-Friendly Road Trips


When planning a road trip, it’s important to consider the needs of all your travel companions, including your furry friends. If you’re a pet owner, you know that bringing your pets along for the ride can make the journey even more enjoyable. However, not all cars are designed with pets in mind. That’s why we’ve compiled a list of the best cars for pet-friendly road trips to help you make the right choice for your next adventure.

1. Subaru Outback: The Subaru Outback is a popular choice among pet owners for several reasons. Its spacious interior provides ample room for your pets to stretch out and relax during the trip. The Outback also features a rear gate that opens wide, making it easy for your pets to hop in and out of the car. Additionally, Subaru offers several pet-friendly accessories, such as a cargo area cover and a pet harness, to keep your pets safe and secure.

2. Honda CR-V: The Honda CR-V is another great option for pet-friendly road trips. With its roomy cabin and large cargo area, your pets will have plenty of space to move around comfortably. The CR-V also offers features like rear seat covers and cargo organizers, making it easier to keep your car clean and organized while traveling with pets. Honda also offers a variety of pet-friendly accessories, including a pet ramp and a pet-friendly seat cover.

3. Volvo XC60: If luxury is what you’re after, the Volvo XC60 is a top choice for pet owners. This stylish SUV not only offers a spacious interior but also includes thoughtful features such as a built-in pet barrier and a pet safety harness attachment. The XC60’s high-quality upholstery is also resistant to pet hair and stains, making it a breeze to clean up after your furry friends.

4. Jeep Wrangler: For those who prefer an adventurous road trip with their pets, the Jeep Wrangler is an excellent choice. With its rugged design and off-road capabilities, the Wrangler allows you and your pets to explore even the most remote destinations. The removable doors and top also provide a unique open-air experience that your pets will surely enjoy.

5. Toyota RAV4: The Toyota RAV4 is known for its reliability and versatility, making it a great option for pet owners. Its spacious interior and wide rear gate allow for easy entry and exit for your pets. The RAV4 also offers a range of pet-friendly accessories, such as a pet ramp and a pet seat cover, to make traveling with pets more convenient.

Remember, when traveling with pets, safety should always be a top priority. Consider using a pet restraint or harness to keep your furry friends secure during the journey. Additionally, make sure to pack essentials such as food, water, and any necessary medications for your pets.

In conclusion, choosing the right car for your pet-friendly road trip can make all the difference in ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable journey for both you and your furry friends. From spacious interiors to pet-friendly accessories, these cars offer everything you need to make your road trip a memorable one. So, pack your bags, buckle up, and hit the road with your four-legged companions for an adventure you won’t forget. Happy travels!
