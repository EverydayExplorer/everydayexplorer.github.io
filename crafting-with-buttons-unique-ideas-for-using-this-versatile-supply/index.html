Crafting with Buttons: Unique Ideas for Using this Versatile Supply


Buttons are more than just functional fasteners; they are also fantastic crafting materials. With their wide range of colors, sizes, and shapes, buttons provide endless creative possibilities. Whether you’re an experienced crafter or just starting out, there are countless unique and exciting ways to use buttons in your projects. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most creative and innovative ideas for crafting with buttons.

Button Art

One of the most popular and visually striking ways to use buttons in crafting is by creating button art. Button art involves arranging buttons of different colors and sizes to form a design or picture. With a little bit of patience and creativity, you can transform a plain canvas or a piece of fabric into a stunning work of art. The possibilities are endless - you can create animals, flowers, landscapes, or even abstract designs. The best part about button art is that you can easily customize it to match your home decor or personal style.

Button Jewelry

Buttons can also be incorporated into unique and stylish jewelry pieces. From earrings to necklaces, bracelets to rings, buttons make for eye-catching accessories. You can either stitch a single button onto a chain or use multiple buttons of different sizes and colors to create intricate patterns. By combining buttons with other materials like beads, wire, or leather, you can elevate the appearance of your jewelry and make it truly one-of-a-kind.

Button Flowers

Flowers are a classic crafting motif, and buttons can be used to create beautiful, everlasting blooms. By layering buttons of different sizes and colors, you can easily mimic the shape and texture of various flowers. Attach these button flowers to hair clips, brooches, or even use them to embellish gift wrapping. The best part about button flowers is that they never wilt, making them ideal for those who love to enjoy the beauty of nature in their crafts.

Button Home Decor

Buttons can add a pop of color and whimsy to your home decor. Whether you have a plain pillow, a dull lampshade, or a boring picture frame, buttons can help transform these everyday items into unique and personalized pieces. Create a button monogram by arranging buttons of different sizes and colors in the shape of your initials. Glue buttons onto a plain frame to create a fun and vibrant border. The possibilities are endless and only limited by your imagination.

Button Embellishments

Buttons can also be used to add flair and personality to clothing and accessories. Sew buttons onto a plain sweater to create a unique pattern or design. Add buttons to a plain tote bag to make it stand out from the crowd. Attach buttons to a headband to add a touch of whimsy to your hairstyle. Buttons can be used to breathe new life into old items or enhance the appearance of new ones, giving them a personal touch.

Button Scrapbooking

For those who enjoy preserving memories through scrapbooking, buttons can be a valuable addition to your creative arsenal. Use buttons to embellish photo corners, borders, or even as accents on journaling cards. Buttons can add a three-dimensional element to your scrapbook pages and help tie the overall design together.

In conclusion, buttons are much more than just functional objects - they are a treasure trove of creative opportunities. Whether you’re an experienced crafter or just starting out, incorporating buttons into your projects can add a unique and personal touch. From button art and jewelry to home decor and scrapbooking, there are endless possibilities for using buttons in your crafting endeavors. So let your creativity soar and start exploring the wonderful world of crafting with buttons today!
