10 Creative Ways to Use Leftover Ham


Hello there! Are you looking for some creative ways to use leftover ham? Well, you’re in luck! In this blog post, we’ll explore ten delicious and inventive ways to make the most out of that leftover ham sitting in your refrigerator. From breakfast to dinner, we’ve got you covered. So, grab your apron and let’s get cooking!

1. Ham and Cheese Breakfast Casserole: Start your day with a hearty breakfast casserole packed with chunks of leftover ham, gooey melted cheese, and fluffy eggs. This delicious dish is perfect for feeding a crowd or meal prepping for the week ahead.

2. Ham and Pineapple Pizza: Take your homemade pizza game to the next level by adding some leftover ham and pineapple as toppings. The combination of sweet pineapple and savory ham creates a flavor explosion that will leave you craving for more.

3. Ham and Swiss Sliders: These bite-sized sliders are a crowd-pleaser at any party or gathering. Layer thinly sliced leftover ham, Swiss cheese, and a tangy mustard sauce between soft dinner rolls. Bake them until the cheese is melted and the rolls are golden brown.

4. Ham and Potato Soup: Warm up on a chilly day with a comforting bowl of ham and potato soup. Simmer diced leftover ham with potatoes, onions, carrots, and your favorite seasonings in a creamy broth. Top it off with a sprinkle of fresh herbs for an added burst of flavor.

5. Ham and Egg Breakfast Burritos: Transform your leftover ham into a grab-and-go breakfast option by making ham and egg breakfast burritos. Scramble some eggs, add in diced ham, cheese, and your favorite veggies. Wrap it all up in a tortilla for a satisfying morning meal.

6. Ham and Broccoli Quiche: Quiche is a versatile dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, brunch, or dinner. Combine leftover ham, steamed broccoli, and cheese in a flaky pie crust, then pour in a mixture of eggs and milk. Bake until the quiche is set and golden brown.

7. Ham and Cheese Stuffed Chicken Breasts: Elevate your weeknight dinners with this delicious and protein-packed dish. Stuff chicken breasts with leftover ham and your favorite cheese, then bake them until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted.

8. Ham and Cheddar Melt Sandwich: Take your regular grilled cheese sandwich up a notch by adding leftover ham and cheddar cheese. Grill it until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown. Serve it with a side of tomato soup for a classic comfort food combo.

9. Ham Fried Rice: Give your fried rice a twist by incorporating diced leftover ham. Sauté the ham with cooked rice, mixed vegetables, soy sauce, and scrambled eggs. This quick and easy dish is perfect for using up leftover rice and ham.

10. Ham and Cranberry Croissant Sandwich: For a sweet and savory combination, layer leftover ham, cranberry sauce, and creamy brie cheese on a buttery croissant. This sandwich is a delightful mix of flavors and textures that will satisfy your taste buds.

There you have it! Ten creative ways to use leftover ham that will keep your meals exciting and flavorful. Whether you’re looking for breakfast, lunch, or dinner ideas, these recipes will help you make the most out of your leftover ham. So, don’t let that delicious ham go to waste - get creative and enjoy these mouthwatering dishes!
