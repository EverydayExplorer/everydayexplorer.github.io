The Coolest Hotels with Rooftop Bars for a Night to Remember


Over the past few years, rooftop bars have become increasingly popular, and for good reason. There’s just something about sipping on a cocktail while enjoying breathtaking views of the cityscape that adds an extra touch of magic to the night. If you’re looking for a truly memorable experience, why not combine it with a stay at one of the coolest hotels with rooftop bars? In this blog post, we’ll take a look at some of the best options around the world.

1. The Standard, Downtown LA
Perched atop a historic building in downtown Los Angeles, The Standard offers stunning panoramic views of the city. Its rooftop bar is known for its iconic blue Astroturf, red waterbed lounges, and a grassy knoll perfect for catching the sunset. With a DJ spinning tunes and a trendy crowd, this hotel is the epitome of cool.

2. The Watergate Hotel, Washington D.C.
Located near the infamous Watergate complex, this recently renovated hotel boasts a rooftop bar with breathtaking views of the Potomac River. The stylish outdoor terrace offers comfortable seating, fire pits, and unique cocktails. It’s the perfect spot to unwind and take in the sights after a day of exploring the nation’s capital.

3. The Ned, London
Housed in a former bank building, The Ned is one of London’s most glamorous hotels. Its rooftop bar, known as The Rooftop, offers stunning views of the city’s iconic skyline. With a retractable roof, it’s a great spot to visit no matter the weather. Enjoy delicious cocktails and a lively atmosphere as you take in the breathtaking views.

4. The Peninsula, Bangkok
Located on the banks of the Chao Phraya River, The Peninsula Bangkok is renowned for its luxurious accommodations and breathtaking views. Its stylish rooftop bar, The Moon Bar, sits atop a 61-story tower, offering unmatched panoramic vistas of the city below. Enjoy a selection of expertly crafted cocktails while taking in the stunning sunset over the river.

5. The Standard, High Line, New York City
Situated in the trendy Meatpacking District, The Standard, High Line is a chic hotel with a rooftop bar worth visiting. Known as Le Bain, this rooftop oasis features stunning views of the Hudson River and the downtown Manhattan skyline. Relax in the plunge pool, sip on a delicious cocktail, and dance the night away to the beats of renowned DJs.

6. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore
The Marina Bay Sands boasts one of the most famous rooftop bars in the world, CÉ LA VI. Perched atop the iconic three-tower hotel, this rooftop oasis offers breathtaking 360-degree views of Singapore’s skyline. Enjoy a refreshing cocktail while taking in the panoramic vistas of the city-state’s futuristic architecture.

7. The Library, Koh Samui, Thailand
Located on the beautiful island of Koh Samui, The Library is a unique hotel that stands out from the crowd. Its beachfront rooftop bar, The Page, offers uninterrupted views of the turquoise waters and white sandy beach. Sip on a signature cocktail and embrace the tranquil beauty of this paradise.

8. The Lyall Hotel and Spa, Melbourne, Australia
In the heart of Melbourne’s fashionable South Yarra district, The Lyall Hotel and Spa offers a rooftop Champagne Bar experience like no other. Surrounded by lush greenery, this hidden gem provides a private and intimate setting for sipping on premium Champagne and enjoying stunning views of the city.

In conclusion, if you’re looking for a night to remember, consider staying at one of the coolest hotels with rooftop bars. From Los Angeles to Singapore, these hotels offer not only luxurious accommodations but also breathtaking views and unforgettable experiences. So, why not elevate your travel experience and take in the world from new heights?
