Exploring Astrocartography: Discovering Your Ideal Place on Earth


Have you ever wondered if there is a place in the world where you feel more aligned and in sync with the energies of the universe? A place where you can thrive and fulfill your highest potential? Astrocartography, also known as locational astrology, is a fascinating tool that can help you discover your ideal place on Earth based on the alignment of the stars and planets at your time of birth. In this blog post, we will explore the concept of astrocartography and how it can be utilized to find your perfect location.

What is Astrocartography?

Astrocartography is a concept that was developed by the renowned astrologer Jim Lewis in the 1970s. It combines astrology and cartography to create a map that shows the planetary influences on different locations around the world. By analyzing your birth chart and overlaying it onto a world map, astrocartography can reveal the areas where the planets were particularly strong at the time of your birth.

Each planet has its own unique energy and influence, and these energies can vary depending on the location. For example, if you have a strong Mars influence in your birth chart, it might indicate that you thrive in areas that are bold, assertive, and action-oriented. On the other hand, if your birth chart shows a strong Venus influence, you might feel more at home in places that are peaceful, harmonious, and aesthetically pleasing.

How Astrocartography Works

To determine your ideal place on Earth using astrocartography, the first step is to get a copy of your birth chart. This can be easily obtained from an astrologer, or you can use one of the many online birth chart calculators available. Your birth chart will provide valuable information about the planetary positions at the time of your birth, which will be used to create your astrocartography map.

Next, you will need to consult a professional astrocartographer who can overlay your birth chart onto a world map. This process involves determining the longitude and latitude of each planet in your birth chart and marking these points on the map. The resulting map will show you the areas where the planets were strong at the time of your birth.

Interpreting Your Astrocartography Map

Once you have your astrocartography map, the next step is to interpret the influences of the different planetary energies on the various locations. Each planet represents different aspects of life, such as love, career, health, and personal growth. By analyzing the planetary energies in specific areas, you can gain valuable insights into how these locations might impact different areas of your life.

For example, if your astrocartography map shows a strong Venus influence in a particular city, it might indicate that this location is ideal for love and relationships. On the other hand, if your map reveals a strong Saturn influence, it might suggest that this location is more suitable for career advancement and discipline.

Using Astrocartography in Decision-Making

Now that you have a better understanding of your astrocartography map, how can you use this information in practical terms? Astrocartography can be a valuable tool when it comes to making decisions about where to live, work, or travel. By aligning yourself with the planetary energies that are most supportive for your goals and aspirations, you can create a more harmonious and fulfilling life experience.

For example, if you are considering a career change or relocation, consulting your astrocartography map can provide guidance on which locations are most favorable for your professional growth. Similarly, if you are seeking a place to retire or settle down, astrocartography can help you identify areas that are conducive to your personal well-being and happiness.

Final Thoughts

Exploring astrocartography can be an exciting and eye-opening journey of self-discovery. By understanding the planetary influences on different locations, you can gain valuable insights into where you are likely to feel most aligned and in tune with the energies of the universe. Whether you are seeking a fresh start, a career change, or simply a deeper connection to the world around you, astrocartography can provide valuable guidance and support. So why not embark on your own astrocartography adventure and discover your ideal place on Earth? The answers may lie within the stars.
