The Best Infinity Gauntlet Storylines in the Marvel Universe


When it comes to epic and game-changing storylines in the Marvel Universe, few can compare to the power and intrigue of the Infinity Gauntlet. First introduced in 1991 in the iconic event series of the same name, the Infinity Gauntlet has become a symbol of ultimate power, capable of reshaping the fabric of reality itself. Over the years, Marvel has produced several incredible storylines centered around this powerful artifact, each with their own unique twists and turns. In this blog, we will explore some of the best Infinity Gauntlet storylines in the Marvel Universe.

1. ‘Infinity Gauntlet’ (1991): The storyline that started it all, ‘Infinity Gauntlet’ is a six-issue limited series written by Jim Starlin, with artwork by George Perez and Ron Lim. In this story, Thanos, the Mad Titan, acquires all six Infinity Gems and becomes virtually omnipotent. With the Gauntlet, Thanos challenges the cosmic beings of the Marvel Universe and wreaks havoc on Earth. The heroes of the Marvel Universe must unite to stop Thanos and save reality itself. This storyline is a classic and sets the stage for many future Infinity Gauntlet storylines.

2. ‘Infinity War’ (1992): Building upon the events of ‘Infinity Gauntlet,’ ‘Infinity War’ is a six-issue limited series written by Jim Starlin, with artwork by Ron Lim. In this story, Adam Warlock, a key character in the previous storyline, discovers that Thanos’ near-omnipotent power has caused a rift in reality. This rift allows evil doppelgangers of Marvel superheroes to emerge and wreak havoc. The heroes must once again band together to stop this new threat. ‘Infinity War’ delves deeper into the themes of power and identity and showcases the complexities of the Marvel Universe.

3. ‘Infinity Crusade’ (1993): The third installment in the Infinity Gauntlet trilogy, ‘Infinity Crusade’ is another six-issue limited series written by Jim Starlin, with artwork by Ron Lim. In this story, the cosmic entity known as the Goddess manipulates various superheroes and villains in an attempt to eradicate all evil in the universe. The heroes who resist her control must face off against their brainwashed allies and find a way to stop the Goddess before she remakes the universe in her image. ‘Infinity Crusade’ explores the concepts of free will, faith, and the balance between good and evil.

4. ‘Infinity’ (2013): Moving away from the traditional Infinity Gauntlet storyline, ‘Infinity’ is a six-issue crossover event written by Jonathan Hickman, with artwork by Jim Cheung and Jerome Opeña. In this story, Thanos sets his sights on Earth while the Avengers are preoccupied with a cosmic threat known as the Builders. The story explores the dynamic between Earth’s heroes and the cosmic entities of the Marvel Universe. ‘Infinity’ is a grand-scale event that showcases Marvel’s vast cosmic mythology and sets the stage for future stories.

5. ‘Thanos Wins’ (2018): In this storyline, written by Donny Cates, with artwork by Geoff Shaw, Marvel explores an alternate future where Thanos has finally achieved ultimate victory. With the Infinity Gauntlet in his possession, Thanos has remade the universe in his image, eliminating all threats and ruling uncontested. However, things are not as they seem, and a surprising twist takes the storyline in unexpected directions. ‘Thanos Wins’ is a dark and gripping tale that showcases the consequences of ultimate power in the hands of a madman.

These are just a few examples of the best Infinity Gauntlet storylines in the Marvel Universe. Each story offers its own unique take on the concept of ultimate power and the consequences that come with it. Whether it’s exploring the moral complexities of wielding the Infinity Gauntlet or delving into the cosmic lore of the Marvel Universe, these storylines are sure to captivate readers and leave them eagerly seeking more. The Infinity Gauntlet is an enduring symbol of power and its stories continue to resonate with fans of all ages.
