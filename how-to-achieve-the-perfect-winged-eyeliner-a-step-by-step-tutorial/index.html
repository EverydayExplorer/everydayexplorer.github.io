How to Achieve the Perfect Winged Eyeliner: A Step-by-Step Tutorial


Are you tired of struggling to achieve the perfect winged eyeliner? You’re not alone! Winged eyeliner is a classic makeup look that can add a touch of glamour and sophistication to any makeup look. However, mastering the technique can be a challenge, especially for beginners. But fear not! In this step-by-step tutorial, we’ll break down the process so you can achieve the perfect winged eyeliner every time.

Step 1: Prep your eyelids
Before applying any eyeliner, it’s important to prep your eyelids. Start by cleansing your face and applying a primer to help your eyeliner last longer. You can also apply a light layer of eyeshadow to create a smooth canvas.

Step 2: Choose the right eyeliner
There are various types of eyeliners available, including liquid, gel, and pencil. For beginners, a felt-tip liquid eyeliner is often the easiest to work with. It provides more control and precision compared to other types. Choose an eyeliner with a fine tip for a more precise application.

Step 3: Create a guideline
To ensure even wings on both eyes, start by creating a guideline. Use a small angled brush and a matte eyeshadow that matches your eyeliner color. Gently press the brush against the outer corner of your eye, angling it towards the end of your eyebrow. This will create a line that acts as a guide for your wing.

Step 4: Draw the wing
Now it’s time to draw the wing. Start from the outer corner of your eye and follow the guideline you created in the previous step. Use short, light strokes to gradually extend the line towards the outer corner of your eyebrow. The length of the wing is up to you, but make sure it’s symmetrical on both eyes.

Step 5: Connect the wing to the lash line
Next, connect the wing to your lash line. Starting from the outer corner, draw a thin line along your upper lash line, gradually getting thicker as you move towards the inner corner. Make sure to keep the line as close to the lash line as possible for a seamless look.

Step 6: Fill in the gap
If there’s a gap between the wing and your lash line, fill it in with short, light strokes. This will create a smooth and cohesive look. Take your time and build up the color gradually, ensuring a seamless blend between the wing and the rest of your eyeliner.

Step 7: Clean up any mistakes
Mistakes happen, especially when it comes to winged eyeliner. Don’t worry if your lines aren’t perfect! Use a cotton swab or a small angled brush dipped in makeup remover to clean up any smudges or uneven lines. Remember, practice makes perfect!

Step 8: Finish with mascara and other eye makeup
To complete your winged eyeliner look, apply mascara to your lashes. This will help define and enhance your eyes. You can also add other eye makeup, such as eyeshadow and false lashes, to further elevate your look.

And there you have it – a step-by-step tutorial on how to achieve the perfect winged eyeliner! Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t get discouraged if it takes a few tries to get it right. With time and patience, you’ll be rocking flawless wings in no time. Happy winging!
