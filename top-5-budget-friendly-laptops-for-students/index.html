Top 5 Budget-Friendly Laptops for Students


As a student, having a reliable laptop is a must. It provides access to resources for research, allows for easy communication with teachers and classmates, and is essential for submitting assignments. However, not everyone can afford the expensive high-end laptops that are marketed towards students. Luckily, there are many budget-friendly laptops that can get the job done without breaking the bank. In this article, we will discuss the top 5 best budget-friendly laptops for students.

1. Acer Chromebook R 11

The Acer Chromebook R 11 is a great option for students who are not power users. The Chromebook is powered by an Intel Celeron N3150 quad-core processor and comes with 4GB of RAM and 32GB of storage. It also has a 360-degree hinge, which allows you to use it as a tablet. The screen size is 11.6 inches, which makes it compact and easy to carry around. The battery life is around 10 hours, which is impressive for a budget laptop.

2. HP Stream 14

The HP Stream 14 is a reliable budget-friendly laptop that comes in a sleek design. It is powered by an Intel Celeron N3060 processor and comes with 4GB of RAM and 32GB of storage. It has a 14-inch screen, which is great for multitasking. The laptop also comes with Microsoft Office 365 Personal subscription for one year, which is a great bonus for students. The battery life is around 10 hours, which is great for a laptop in this price range.

3. ASUS VivoBook E203MA

The ASUS VivoBook E203MA is a budget-friendly laptop that packs a punch. It is powered by an Intel Celeron N4000 processor and comes with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of storage. The screen size is 11.6 inches, which makes it easy to carry around. It also has a long battery life of around 12 hours, which is great for students who are always on the go.

4. Lenovo IdeaPad 120S

The Lenovo IdeaPad 120S is a great budget-friendly laptop that is designed for students. It is powered by an Intel Celeron N3350 processor and comes with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of storage. The screen size is 14 inches, which is great for multitasking. The laptop also has a long battery life of around 8 hours, which is great for students who need to work on projects for extended periods.

5. Dell Inspiron 15 3000

The Dell Inspiron 15 3000 is another budget-friendly laptop that is great for students. It is powered by an Intel Celeron N3060 processor and comes with 4GB of RAM and 500GB of storage. The screen size is 15.6 inches, which makes it great for multitasking. The laptop also has a long battery life of around 8 hours, which is great for students who need to work on projects for extended periods.

In conclusion, having a reliable laptop that does not break the bank is important for students. The above laptops are great options for students who are on a tight budget. They offer essential features such as long battery life, multitasking capabilities, and ample storage space. By considering these budget-friendly laptops, students can get the job done without sacrificing performance.
