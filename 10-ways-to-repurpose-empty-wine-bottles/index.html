10 Ways to Repurpose Empty Wine Bottles


Do you have a collection of empty wine bottles lying around and are not sure what to do with them? Instead of throwing them away, why not get creative and repurpose them into something useful and beautiful? There are countless ways you can upcycle those empty bottles into something new and unique. Here are 10 creative ideas to inspire you:

  1. Wine Bottle Planters: Transform your empty wine bottles into charming planters for small succulents or herbs. Simply cut off the top of the bottle, fill it with soil, and plant your favorite greenery. These make great additions to your windowsill or kitchen countertop.

  2. Wine Bottle Lanterns: Create a cozy and romantic atmosphere in your outdoor space by turning your wine bottles into lanterns. Insert a candle or string lights into the bottle and hang them up to illuminate your backyard or patio during summer evenings.

  3. Wine Bottle Vases: Cut the top off your wine bottles and use them as stylish vases for fresh flowers. You can paint the bottles in different colors or leave them as they are for a chic and modern look.

  4. Wine Bottle Tiki Torches: Impress your guests at your next outdoor gathering by repurposing wine bottles into tiki torches. Fill the bottles with citronella oil and insert a wick to keep those pesky bugs at bay while adding a touch of ambiance to your backyard.

  5. Wine Bottle Candle Holders: Turn your empty wine bottles into elegant candle holders by cutting off the top and placing a tapered candle inside. These can be a beautiful centerpiece for your dining table or a lovely addition to your mantel.

  6. Wine Bottle Soap Dispensers: Add a touch of luxury to your bathroom or kitchen by repurposing a wine bottle into a soap dispenser. Simply clean out the bottle, add a pump dispenser, and fill it with your favorite liquid soap.

  7. Wine Bottle Wall Art: Cut your wine bottles into different shapes and sizes, then arrange them on a wooden board to create a unique piece of wall art. You can paint the bottles or leave them as they are for a rustic and eclectic look.

  8. Wine Bottle Bird Feeders: Help out your feathered friends by repurposing wine bottles into bird feeders. Cut a hole near the bottom of the bottle, fill it with birdseed, and hang it up outside for the birds to enjoy.

  9. Wine Bottle Incense Holder: Turn your empty wine bottles into stylish incense holders by cutting off the top and inserting incense sticks. The smoke will billow out of the bottle, creating a calming and aromatic atmosphere in your home.

  10. Wine Bottle Cheese Trays: Impress your guests at your next dinner party by repurposing wine bottles into cheese trays. Cut the bottles in half lengthwise, smooth out the edges, and voila - you have a chic serving platter for your favorite cheeses and crackers.

Repurposing your empty wine bottles is not only a fun and creative way to reduce waste, but it also allows you to add a personal touch to your home decor. So the next time you finish a bottle of wine, think twice before tossing it in the recycling bin and consider giving it a new lease on life with one of these creative ideas.
