Stay in Style: Hotels with Unique Room Designs


When it comes to travel, finding the perfect accommodation is a crucial part of the experience. While some may prefer the comfort of traditional hotel rooms, others seek a more unique and stylish stay. If you’re someone who loves to immerse yourself in one-of-a-kind designs, then this blog post is for you. We’ve curated a list of hotels around the world that offer rooms with extraordinary designs, making your stay truly unforgettable.

1. Icehotel, Sweden:
Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel is a stunning example of architectural brilliance. Built entirely out of ice and snow, this hotel offers guests the chance to sleep in sub-zero temperatures while surrounded by intricate ice sculptures. Each room is individually carved by artists from around the world, ensuring a unique experience for every guest.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
If you’ve ever dreamed of sharing your breakfast with a giraffe, then Giraffe Manor in Nairobi is the place to be. This boutique hotel allows guests to get up close and personal with these majestic creatures. With rooms that overlook the giraffe sanctuary, you can enjoy a truly unique experience as you wake up to giraffes peeking through your window in search of a treat.

3. Poseidon Undersea Resort, Fiji:
For those seeking an underwater adventure, the Poseidon Undersea Resort in Fiji is a must-visit. This luxurious hotel offers guests the opportunity to stay in underwater suites surrounded by vibrant marine life. Imagine waking up to the sight of colorful fish swimming past your bedroom window, creating a truly magical experience.

4. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:
Located in the heart of Finnish Lapland, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort is a winter wonderland like no other. Guests can choose from a range of accommodation options, including glass igloos that allow you to sleep under the Northern Lights. With panoramic views of the Arctic wilderness, this hotel offers a unique blend of luxury and natural beauty.

5. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada:
For those looking for a truly unconventional stay, Free Spirit Spheres in Vancouver Island, Canada, is the perfect choice. These suspended spherical treehouses offer guests a serene and immersive experience in the heart of nature. Nestled among the trees, you can enjoy a peaceful retreat while being gently rocked to sleep by the wind.

6. The Manta Resort, Tanzania:
If you’ve ever wanted to sleep underwater, The Manta Resort in Pemba Island, Tanzania, is the place to be. This unique hotel offers an underwater room that is fully submerged in the crystal clear waters of the Indian Ocean. With panoramic views of the marine life, you can fall asleep to the soothing sounds of the ocean surrounding you.

7. The Dog Bark Park Inn, USA:
For dog lovers, The Dog Bark Park Inn in Idaho, USA, is a quirky and charming hotel that resembles a giant beagle. Inside, you’ll find cozy rooms adorned with dog-themed decor. From the dog-shaped bed to the dog-themed artwork, this hotel offers a truly unique and dog-friendly experience.

In conclusion, if you’re looking to stay in style and want a hotel experience that goes beyond the ordinary, these unique room designs will surely leave a lasting impression. From sleeping in ice to waking up with giraffes, these hotels offer a range of extraordinary experiences that will make your stay truly unforgettable. So, why settle for a traditional hotel room when you can indulge in these unique and stylish accommodations around the world?
