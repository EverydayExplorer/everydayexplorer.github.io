10 Delicious and Healthy Slow Cooker Recipes


Slow cookers are a culinary marvel, allowing busy individuals to whip up hearty meals with minimal effort. Whether youâ€™re a working professional, a parent juggling multiple tasks, or simply someone who enjoys a good meal without the fuss, slow cooker recipes provide the perfect solution. These ten delicious and healthy slow cooker recipes are not only easy to prepare, but they also pack a nutritional punch, ensuring you and your loved ones enjoy wholesome, flavorful dishes.

1. Hearty Vegetable and Quinoa Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In your slow cooker, combine all the ingredients.
  2. Stir well to ensure the quinoa is evenly distributed.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  4. Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and adjust seasoning as needed.

This hearty stew is full of flavor and is a great way to incorporate a variety of vegetables into your diet. Quinoa adds a nutty flavor while providing protein and fiber.

2. Lemon Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken breasts in the slow cooker and pour the lemon-garlic mixture over them.
  3. Cook on low for 6-7 hours or high for 3-4 hours until the chicken is cooked through and tender.

This lemon garlic chicken is zesty and light, making it a perfect dish for any season. Serve with a side of steamed vegetables or a fresh salad for a complete meal.

3. Black Bean and Sweet Potato Chili

Ingredients:

  • 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in the slow cooker.
  2. Stir well and cover.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.

This chili is not only hearty and filling but also packed with nutrients. The sweet potatoes add a natural sweetness and creaminess to the dish, while black beans provide protein and fiber.

4. Thai Green Curry Lentils

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk (14 oz)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine lentils, coconut milk, vegetable broth, green curry paste, bell peppers, carrots, ginger, and salt.
  2. Stir to combine and cover.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.

This dish brings a taste of Thailand to your kitchen. The combination of coconut milk and green curry creates a rich and flavorful sauce that pairs perfectly with lentils.

5. Moroccan Chickpea Stew

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 oz)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in the slow cooker.
  2. Stir well to mix the spices evenly.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours.
  4. Serve garnished with fresh cilantro.

This Moroccan chickpea stew is aromatic and comforting, showcasing a beautiful blend of spices that transports you to the bustling markets of Morocco.

6. Balsamic Glazed Brussels Sprouts and Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1 lb mushrooms, quartered
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine Brussels sprouts, mushrooms, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss to coat the vegetables in the balsamic mixture.
  3. Cook on low for 4-6 hours or high for 2-3 hours until tender.

This side dish is a delightful way to enjoy vegetables, and the balsamic glaze adds a tangy sweetness that enhances the earthiness of the Brussels sprouts and mushrooms.

7. Spaghetti Squash with Marinara Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 spaghetti squash, halved and seeds removed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (28 oz)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. Place the spaghetti squash halves cut-side down in the slow cooker.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix crushed tomatoes, onion, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Pour over the squash.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  4. Once done, scrape the flesh of the squash with a fork to create spaghetti-like strands and serve with marinara sauce and Parmesan cheese.

This recipe is a fantastic low-carb alternative to traditional pasta, and itâ€™s loaded with vitamins and minerals.

8. Coconut Curry Chicken and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 can coconut milk (14 oz)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine chicken thighs, brown rice, coconut milk, chicken broth, curry powder, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stir to combine and cover.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours until the chicken is tender and the rice is fully cooked.

This dish is a delicious one-pot meal thatâ€™s both filling and comforting, perfect for cool evenings.

9. Lemon Herb Quinoa with Spinach

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine quinoa, vegetable broth, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  3. Stir in fresh spinach and lemon juice before serving.

This light and refreshing dish is perfect as a side or a base for a protein of your choice. The combination of quinoa and spinach makes it a powerhouse of nutrients.

10. Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 4 cups almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 2 apples, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup (optional)
  • Nuts and dried fruits for topping

Instructions:

  1. In the slow cooker, combine oats, almond milk, apples, cinnamon, and maple syrup.
  2. Stir well to combine and cover.
  3. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours until the oats are creamy and cooked through.
  4. Top with nuts and dried fruits before serving.

This comforting oatmeal is a great way to start your day, and itâ€™s packed with fiber and antioxidants from the apples and oats.
