The Best Fashion Blogs to Follow for Inspiration


When it comes to keeping up with fashion trends and seeking inspiration for your own personal style, fashion blogs are the go-to destination. These online platforms provide a wealth of information, outfit ideas, and insider tips from industry experts and fashion influencers. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best fashion blogs to follow for inspiration.

1. The Blonde Salad
Started by the internationally renowned fashion blogger Chiara Ferragni, The Blonde Salad is one of the most popular and influential fashion blogs on the internet. With a focus on personal style, beauty, and travel, Chiara shares her fashion adventures and collaborations with top fashion brands. Her impeccable taste in fashion makes The Blonde Salad a must-follow blog for any fashion enthusiast.

2. Man Repeller
If you’re tired of conforming to traditional fashion norms, Man Repeller is the blog for you. Founded by Leandra Medine, this blog takes a different approach to fashion by embracing and celebrating personal style that may not necessarily be appealing to the opposite sex. With their witty writing and unique content, Man Repeller challenges conventional fashion standards and encourages readers to express their individuality.

3. Into The Gloss
While not strictly a fashion blog, Into The Gloss focuses on all things beauty and skincare. Founded by Emily Weiss, this blog has become a go-to source for beauty enthusiasts worldwide. With interviews featuring top models, makeup artists, and beauty industry insiders, Into The Gloss offers a fresh and unique perspective on beauty and fashion.

4. The Sartorialist
If street style is your jam, then The Sartorialist is a blog you shouldn’t miss. Started by renowned photographer Scott Schuman, this blog captures the essence of street fashion in cities around the world. With his keen eye for style, Scott documents everyday individuals who exude effortless and unique fashion sense. The Sartorialist is a great source of inspiration for those looking to incorporate street style elements into their own wardrobe.

5. Fashion Toast
With a focus on minimalism and clean aesthetic, Fashion Toast is a blog founded by Rumi Neely. Known for her effortless style and neutral color palette, Rumi shares her fashion inspirations and personal style through her stunning photography. Whether you’re looking for outfit ideas or want to discover new brands, Fashion Toast offers a visually appealing and inspiring content that’ll keep you coming back for more.

6. Song of Style
Started by Aimee Song, Song of Style is a blog that covers all aspects of fashion, lifestyle, and interior design. With a strong emphasis on travel and creating a well-rounded life, Aimee showcases her eclectic personal style and shares practical tips for fashion lovers around the world. The blog also offers insights into Aimee’s interior design projects, giving readers a more holistic approach to style and inspiration.

7. The Fashion Guitar
Founded by Charlotte Groeneveld, The Fashion Guitar is a blog that perfectly blends high fashion with everyday wear. Charlotte’s blog offers a mix of luxurious designer pieces and accessible fashion that is suitable for any occasion. With her impeccable taste and ability to effortlessly style different looks, The Fashion Guitar is a great source of inspiration for fashion lovers looking to elevate their everyday wardrobe.

8. Advanced Style
If you think fashion is only for the young, think again. Advanced Style is a blog that celebrates style at any age. Founded by Ari Seth Cohen, this blog captures the unique and fearless fashion choices of older individuals. By showcasing their vibrant and eclectic styles, Advanced Style challenges the notion that fashion is limited to the youth. It is a true testament to the fact that style has no age limit.

In conclusion, fashion blogs play an important role in the fashion industry, providing readers with inspiration, ideas, and a fresh perspective. Whether you’re looking for street style inspiration, minimalistic aesthetics, or fashion for all ages, the blogs mentioned above are the perfect sources to keep you updated on the latest trends and help you define and refine your personal style. So, go ahead and follow these blogs for an endless array of fashion and style inspiration!
