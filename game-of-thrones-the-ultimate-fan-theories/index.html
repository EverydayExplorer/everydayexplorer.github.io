Game of Thrones: The Ultimate Fan Theories


The hit TV series Game of Thrones captivated audiences with its intricate plotlines, complex characters, and unpredictable twists. Throughout its eight-season run, fans were left on the edge of their seats, eagerly theorizing about what would happen next in the battle for the Iron Throne. In this blog post, we will dive into some of the ultimate fan theories that have greatly impacted the Game of Thrones community.

R+L=J

One of the most popular theories amongst Game of Thrones fans is the R+L=J theory. This theory suggests that Jon Snow, the bastard son of Ned Stark, is not actually his son, but instead the child of Rhaegar Targaryen and Lyanna Stark. This theory gained traction after various subtle hints were dropped throughout the series, including Ned’s mysterious promise to Lyanna to protect her son.

If this theory is true, it means that Jon Snow has a rightful claim to the Iron Throne, being a legitimate Targaryen heir. The revelation of Jon’s true parentage became a pivotal moment in the series, with vast implications for the final outcome.

The Night King is a Stark

The Night King, the leader of the White Walkers, remains one of the most mysterious and intriguing characters in Game of Thrones. Many fans believe that the Night King is actually a Stark, specifically Brandon Stark, also known as Bran. According to this theory, Bran became trapped in the body of the Night King after traveling back in time and attempting to prevent the creation of the White Walkers.

Support for this theory stems from several clues throughout the series. For instance, the Night King appears to have a personal vendetta against Bran, as seen in their confrontation at the Three-Eyed Raven’s cave. Additionally, both Bran and the Night King have similar mystical abilities, further fueling speculation about their connection.

Tyrion is a Targaryen

Another theory that gained traction amongst fans is the possibility that Tyrion Lannister is not actually a Lannister but a Targaryen. According to this theory, Tyrion’s mother Joanna Lannister had an affair with the Mad King, resulting in Tyrion’s birth. This revelation would explain Tywin Lannister’s disdain for his son and Tyrion’s unique bond with Daenerys Targaryen.

Hints supporting this theory can be found throughout the series. For instance, Tywin’s famous quote, ‘You are no son of mine,’ takes on a whole new meaning if Tyrion is indeed a Targaryen. Tyrion’s fascination with dragons further adds to the speculation, as dragons have historically been associated with House Targaryen.

Arya is Azor Ahai

Azor Ahai, often referred to as ‘The Prince That Was Promised,’ is a legendary figure in the Game of Thrones universe. According to prophecy, Azor Ahai is destined to be reborn and lead the fight against darkness. Many fans believe that Arya Stark, the skilled assassin, fits this description and is, in fact, Azor Ahai.

This theory gained ground after Arya’s impressive killing of the Night King during the Battle of Winterfell. The way Arya displayed her exceptional combat skills and her ability to take down such a formidable enemy led many to believe she was the prophesied savior.

Bran as the Master Manipulator

Throughout the series, Bran Stark’s role in the Game of Thrones has been enigmatic. Some fans have theorized that Bran, now known as the Three-Eyed Raven, has been subtly manipulating events to ensure his own ascension to power. This theory suggests that Bran orchestrated much of the chaos and conflict in Westeros, using his time-warging abilities to alter the course of history.

Supporting evidence for this theory includes Bran’s knowledge of past and future events, as well as his insistence that he doesn’t desire power. Additionally, Bran’s strange behavior and distance from his family have raised suspicions about his true motives.

The Importance of Fan Theories

What makes Game of Thrones fan theories so captivating is their ability to elicit discussion and engage viewers in the storytelling process. These theories not only keep the audience guessing but also add an extra layer of enjoyment to the series. The speculation and analysis surrounding Game of Thrones not only demonstrates the dedication of its fanbase but also showcases the skill and creativity of the show’s creators.

In conclusion, Game of Thrones has inspired a plethora of fan theories that have fascinated fans for years. From the true identity of Jon Snow to the secret motives of Bran Stark, these theories have sparked endless debate and speculation. Whether some of these theories turned out to be true or false, they have undoubtedly enriched the Game of Thrones experience for viewers worldwide. As we bid farewell to the series, it’s clear that the legacy of these ultimate fan theories will continue to live on in the hearts of fans long after the final episode has aired.
