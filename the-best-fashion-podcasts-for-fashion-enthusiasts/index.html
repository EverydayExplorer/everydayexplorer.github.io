The Best Fashion Podcasts for Fashion Enthusiasts


Podcasts have become a popular medium for consuming information and entertainment. Whether you’re commuting to work, hitting the gym, or simply looking for something new to listen to, podcasts offer a convenient and engaging way to stay connected and informed. If you’re a fashion enthusiast, podcasts can be an excellent source of inspiration and education. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best fashion podcasts that cater to fashion enthusiasts like you.

1. ‘The Business of Fashion Podcast’ - Hosted by Imran Amed, founder and CEO of Business of Fashion, this podcast features interviews with leading fashion industry insiders. From designers and CEOs to photographers and stylists, this podcast provides valuable insights into the inner workings of the fashion world. The Business of Fashion Podcast is a must-listen for anyone interested in getting an inside look at the business side of fashion.

2. ‘Fashion No Filter’ - Camille Charriere and Monica Ainley, two fashion insiders, host this podcast. They share their thoughts on current fashion trends, industry news, and interviews with influential fashion figures. With a conversational and accessible approach, Fashion No Filter offers a refreshing take on fashion that appeals to both fashion insiders and enthusiasts alike.

3. ‘The American Fashion Podcast’ - This podcast explores the American fashion industry from various angles. Hosted by Charles Beckwith, the podcast covers a wide range of topics, including sustainable fashion, emerging designers, and the history of American fashion. With insightful interviews and thought-provoking discussions, The American Fashion Podcast is a great resource for staying updated on the latest trends and developments in the American fashion scene.

4. ‘Fashion Is Your Business’ - Fashion industry veteran Rob Sanchez hosts this podcast, which delves into the intersection of fashion, technology, and business. Each episode features interviews with industry experts, entrepreneurs, and innovators who share their insights and experiences. Whether you’re interested in e-commerce, wearable technology, or the future of fashion, Fashion Is Your Business provides valuable perspectives on the ever-evolving fashion industry.

5. ‘The Glossy Podcast’ - The Glossy Podcast features interviews with executives and thought leaders from fashion, luxury, and beauty brands. Hosted by Jill Manoff, this podcast goes beyond just the clothing and explores the business side of the fashion industry. From retail strategies to marketing campaigns, The Glossy Podcast offers valuable insights for fashion enthusiasts looking to gain a deeper understanding of the industry.

6. ‘Fashion Revolution Podcast’ - Fashion Revolution is a global movement advocating for a more sustainable and transparent fashion industry. Their podcast features interviews with designers, activists, and industry insiders who are working towards positive change within the fashion industry. If you’re passionate about sustainability and ethics in fashion, Fashion Revolution Podcast is a must-listen.

7. ‘Dressed: The History of Fashion’ - Fashion is deeply intertwined with history, culture, and society. Dressed: The History of Fashion, hosted by April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, explores the stories behind the clothes we wear. From iconic fashion moments to lesser-known historical trivia, this podcast offers a fascinating look into the rich and diverse history of fashion.

8. ‘The Style Line’ - The Style Line podcast showcases the voices of creatives, entrepreneurs, and influencers who are shaping the fashion industry. Through candid conversations, host Emily Newman explores the personal journeys and insights of her guests. The Style Line is perfect for those looking for inspiration and a behind-the-scenes glimpse into the world of fashion.

These are just a few of the best fashion podcasts for fashion enthusiasts. By subscribing to these podcasts, you’ll gain access to valuable insights, industry updates, and inspirational stories from some of the most influential figures in fashion. Whether your interest lies in the business side of fashion, sustainable practices, or the history of fashion, there’s a podcast out there that caters to your interests. So plug in your headphones and start discovering the world of fashion through the power of podcasts.
