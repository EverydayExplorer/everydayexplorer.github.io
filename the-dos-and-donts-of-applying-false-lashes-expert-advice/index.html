The Dos and Don'ts of Applying False Lashes: Expert Advice


Applying false lashes can be a game-changer when it comes to enhancing your natural beauty and achieving a glamorous look. However, it’s essential to know the dos and don’ts to ensure a flawless application and prevent any mishaps. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll provide you with expert advice on how to apply false lashes like a pro. Let’s dive in!

DO: Choose the Right Lashes for Your Eye Shape
One size doesn’t fit all when it comes to false lashes. Consider the shape and size of your eyes before purchasing lashes. If you have round eyes, opt for lashes with elongated ends to create a cat-eye effect. If you have hooded eyes, choose lashes that are shorter in the center and longer towards the outer corners to lift and open up your eyes.

DON’T: Neglect Proper Preparation
Preparation is key to a successful lash application. Make sure your natural lashes are clean and free from any traces of mascara or oils. This will ensure that the false lashes adhere properly and last longer. Additionally, measure the length of the false lashes against your natural lash line and trim any excess if necessary.

DO: Use a High-Quality Lash Adhesive
Investing in a high-quality lash adhesive is crucial for a secure and long-lasting application. Look for a latex-free adhesive that dries clear and offers a strong hold. Apply a thin layer of adhesive along the lash band, allowing it to become tacky for a few seconds before placing the lashes on your lash line.

DON’T: Apply Lashes Straight Away
One common mistake is applying false lashes immediately after applying adhesive. Instead, wait for about 20-30 seconds until the adhesive becomes slightly tacky. This will ensure better adherence and prevent the lashes from sliding around or falling off.

DO: Apply Lashes Close to Your Natural Lash Line
To achieve a seamless and natural look, apply the lashes as close to your natural lash line as possible. Use tweezers or a lash applicator to carefully position the lashes and press them gently onto your lash line. Start from the center of your eyelid and work your way outwards, ensuring the inner and outer corners are well secured.

DON’T: Forget to Blend Your Natural Lashes
To create a seamless transition between your natural lashes and the false ones, gently pinch your natural lashes together with the false lashes using your fingers or a clean mascara wand. This will help blend the two together, giving a more natural and voluminous appearance.

DO: Practice, Practice, Practice!
Applying false lashes can be a challenging task, especially for beginners. Don’t get discouraged if you don’t get it right the first time. Practice makes perfect, so keep trying until you feel confident in your application skills. Experiment with different lash styles, adhesive techniques, and tools to find what works best for you.

DON’T: Sleep with Your False Lashes On
While false lashes can make you feel glamorous, it’s essential to remove them before going to bed. Sleeping with false lashes can damage both your natural lashes and the false ones. Gently remove the lashes using an oil-free makeup remover or warm water, and store them properly in their original packaging to maintain their shape and longevity.

In conclusion, applying false lashes can be a fun and transformative experience. By following these dos and don’ts, you’ll be well on your way to achieving flawless and stunning lash looks. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you. Now, go ahead and embrace your inner lash aficionado!
