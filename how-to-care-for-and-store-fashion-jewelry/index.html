How to Care for and Store Fashion Jewelry


When it comes to fashion jewelry, proper care and storage are essential to maintain the longevity and beauty of your pieces. Whether you have a collection of high-end jewelry or some fun costume pieces, following these tips for caring and storing your fashion jewelry will help keep them looking their best for years to come.

1. Keep it Clean: Regular cleaning is important to remove dirt, oils, and any residue that can accumulate on your fashion jewelry. To clean your pieces, use a mild soap and warm water. Gently scrub the jewelry with a soft brush or toothbrush, paying attention to hard-to-reach areas. Rinse thoroughly and dry with a soft cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials as they can damage the jewelry.

2. Protect from Chemicals: Fashion jewelry is typically made of non-precious metals, plated or with decorative coatings. These metals can easily react with certain chemicals, leading to tarnishing or discoloration. To avoid this, it is important to protect your jewelry from contact with chemicals such as perfume, hairspray, lotion, and household cleaners. Always put on your jewelry last after applying any products and remove it before engaging in activities that involve exposure to chemicals.

3. Avoid Water and Moisture: Water and moisture can cause fashion jewelry to tarnish and lose its shine. It is best to remove your jewelry before swimming, showering, or participating in any water-based activities. Moisture can also lead to rust or corrosion, especially for pieces with metal components. When not in use, store your jewelry in a dry and clean place.

4. Store Separately: To prevent tangling, scratching, or other damage, it is important to store your fashion jewelry separately. Consider using individual pouches or small bags to keep each piece separated. This will help to protect delicate chains, prevent gemstones from getting scratched, and avoid any metal-to-metal contact that can cause discoloration or damage.

5. Keep Away from Direct Sunlight: Fashion jewelry should be protected from direct sunlight as it can cause fading or discoloration. When not wearing your jewelry, store it in a dark place or use a jewelry box with a soft lining to shield it from sunlight. This will help to preserve the color and vibrancy of your pieces.

6. Be Gentle with Delicate Pieces: Some fashion jewelry may have delicate components such as feathers, beads, or fragile chains. Handle these pieces with extra care to avoid any breakage or damage. Avoid pulling or tugging on delicate parts and store them in a way that minimizes movement to prevent unnecessary stress.

7. Regular Check-ups: Take the time to inspect your fashion jewelry regularly for any signs of damage or wear. Check the clasps, prongs, and chains to ensure they are secure. If you notice any loose stones, missing parts, or other issues, have them repaired promptly to prevent further damage.

8. Professional Cleaning: Consider having your fashion jewelry professionally cleaned and serviced by a jeweler at least once a year. They have the expertise and proper tools to handle delicate materials and can help restore the shine and beauty of your pieces.

By following these tips for caring and storing your fashion jewelry, you can enjoy your pieces for a long time and maintain their original beauty. Remember, even though fashion jewelry may not be as valuable as fine jewelry, it still deserves proper care and attention to ensure it remains in top condition.
