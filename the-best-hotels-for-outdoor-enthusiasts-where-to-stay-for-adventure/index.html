The Best Hotels for Outdoor Enthusiasts: Where to Stay for Adventure


Are you an outdoor enthusiast looking for the perfect place to stay during your next adventure? Whether you’re a hiker, a surfer, a skier, or just someone who loves being surrounded by nature, the right hotel can elevate your experience to a whole new level. From breathtaking views to convenient access to outdoor activities, the best hotels for outdoor enthusiasts offer a combination of comfort and adventure that is hard to beat. In this blog post, we’ll take a look at some of the top hotels around the world that cater to outdoor lovers, providing you with the ultimate guide to where to stay for your next adventure.

Mountain Escapes

If you’re a fan of mountain adventures, there are few places more iconic than the Canadian Rockies. Nestled in the heart of Banff National Park, the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise offers unparalleled access to some of the most stunning hiking trails and skiing slopes in the world. The hotel itself is a masterpiece of luxury, with cozy rooms and world-class amenities, but it’s the surrounding natural beauty that truly steals the show. Waking up to the sight of the turquoise Lake Louise and the towering mountains beyond is an experience that every outdoor enthusiast should have at least once in their lifetime.

For those who prefer the rugged charm of the American West, the Amangiri in Utah is a desert oasis that seamlessly blends luxury with adventure. Set against the dramatic backdrop of red rock canyons and mesas, this hotel provides a base for exploring the otherworldly landscapes of the American Southwest. Hiking, rock climbing, and hot air ballooning are just a few of the activities that guests can enjoy, all while returning to the lap of luxury at the end of the day.

Coastal Retreats

For surfers and beach lovers, a hotel with easy access to the ocean is a must. The Wickaninnish Inn in Tofino, Canada, is a prime example of a hotel that caters to those who can’t get enough of the sea. Situated on the rugged west coast of Vancouver Island, this luxurious retreat offers direct access to Chesterman Beach, one of the best spots for surfing in Canada. When you’re not catching waves, you can indulge in the hotel’s spa or simply relax in your room with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean.

In Australia, the Southern Ocean Lodge on Kangaroo Island is a haven for nature enthusiasts. Perched on a secluded cliff, this eco-friendly hotel provides a front-row seat to the island’s stunning coastal scenery. From guided nature walks to wildlife encounters, guests can immerse themselves in the raw beauty of the Australian wilderness while enjoying the comforts of a world-class hotel.

Wilderness Getaways

If your idea of the perfect adventure involves untamed wilderness, there are hotels that cater to that as well. The Clayoquot Wilderness Resort in British Columbia, Canada, is a luxury tented camp that offers a taste of the great outdoors without sacrificing comfort. Set in a remote location amidst old-growth forest and rugged coastline, this all-inclusive resort provides an array of outdoor activities, including whale watching, bear watching, kayaking, and horseback riding. At the end of the day, guests can unwind in their luxurious tents and enjoy gourmet meals prepared with locally sourced ingredients.

For those who dream of exploring the African savannah, the Singita Sasakwa Lodge in Tanzania is a paradise for safari enthusiasts. Located in the Grumeti Game Reserve, this elegant lodge offers an exclusive safari experience, complete with game drives, bush walks, and the chance to witness the awe-inspiring wildlife of the Serengeti. After a day of adventure, guests can retreat to their opulent suites and savor the tranquility of the African bush.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel can make a world of difference for outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you’re seeking mountain thrills, coastal escapades, or wilderness wonders, there are hotels around the world that cater to your passion for adventure. From luxurious mountain lodges to eco-friendly beachfront retreats, the best hotels for outdoor enthusiasts offer the perfect blend of comfort and excitement, ensuring that your next adventure is nothing short of extraordinary. So, when planning your next outdoor escapade, consider these top hotels and get ready for the adventure of a lifetime.
