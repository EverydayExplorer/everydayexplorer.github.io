The Best Fashion Quotes from Fashion Icons


Fashion is not just about the clothes we wear, but also about the way we express ourselves and our personal style. Every fashion icon has their own unique perspective on fashion, and can inspire us with their wise and memorable words. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best fashion quotes from fashion icons that have stood the test of time.

1. ‘Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life.’ - Bill Cunningham

This quote by the late Bill Cunningham, a legendary fashion photographer, perfectly captures the transformative power of fashion. It reminds us that fashion has the ability to uplift our spirits and give us the confidence to face whatever challenges life throws at us.

2. ‘Fashion is not something that exists in dresses only. Fashion is in the sky, in the street, fashion has to do with ideas, the way we live, what is happening.’ - Coco Chanel

Coco Chanel, the iconic French designer, understood that fashion is not confined to clothing alone. It encompasses our entire lifestyle and reflects the times we live in. This quote reminds us that fashion is not just about what we wear, but also about how we choose to live our lives.

3. ‘Fashions fade, style is eternal.’ - Yves Saint Laurent

Yves Saint Laurent, one of the most influential fashion designers of all time, believed that while trends come and go, personal style is timeless. This quote serves as a reminder that true fashion is about expressing your own unique personality and not blindly following what is popular.

4. ‘In order to be irreplaceable one must always be different.’ - Coco Chanel

Coco Chanel was a trailblazer in the fashion industry, and this quote reflects her belief in the importance of standing out from the crowd. It encourages us to embrace our individuality and not be afraid to take risks with our fashion choices.

5. ‘Fashion is about dressing according to what’s fashionable. Style is more about being yourself.’ - Oscar de la Renta

Oscar de la Renta, a renowned fashion designer, emphasized the distinction between fashion and style. While fashion may change, style is a reflection of our true selves. This quote reminds us to stay true to our own unique style instead of blindly following fleeting trends.

6. ‘The joy of dressing is an art.’ - John Galliano

John Galliano, a British fashion designer, believed that getting dressed should be a joyful and creative process. This quote encourages us to have fun with fashion and view it as a form of self-expression and art.

7. ‘Fashion should be a form of escapism, and not a form of imprisonment.’ - Alexander McQueen

Alexander McQueen, an avant-garde fashion designer, saw fashion as a means of liberating oneself, rather than conforming to societal norms. This quote serves as a reminder that fashion should be freeing and allow us to express our true selves, rather than limiting us.

8. ‘To me, clothing is a form of self-expression - there are hints about who you are in what you wear.’ - Marc Jacobs

Marc Jacobs, an American fashion designer, understands the power of clothing as a means of self-expression. This quote reminds us that fashion is a way to communicate our personality and aspirations to the world.

9. ‘Don’t be into trends. Don’t make fashion own you, but you decide what you are, what you want to express by the way you dress and the way to live.’ - Gianni Versace

Gianni Versace, an Italian fashion designer, believed in the importance of personal style over following trends. This quote reminds us to be true to ourselves and use fashion as a tool for self-expression, rather than being slaves to fleeting fads.

10. ‘Fashion is about dreaming and making other people dream.’ - Donatella Versace

Donatella Versace, the sister of Gianni Versace, offers a poetic perspective on fashion with this quote. It reminds us that fashion has the power to inspire and transport us to a world of dreams and imagination.

In conclusion, fashion is not just about clothes, but a form of art that allows us to express our individuality. These fashion quotes from iconic fashion figures serve as a guide, reminding us to embrace our personal style, take risks, and use fashion as a means of self-expression. So, go ahead, embrace your unique style and let your fashion choices tell the world who you are.
