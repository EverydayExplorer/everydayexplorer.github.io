Fashion Forward: The Best Fashion Podcasts and Blogs to Follow


In the ever-evolving world of fashion, staying up-to-date with the latest trends and insights is crucial for fashion enthusiasts, bloggers, and professionals alike. With the rise of digital platforms, there are countless fashion podcasts and blogs available to cater to different tastes and preferences. In this article, we will explore some of the best fashion podcasts and blogs that you should follow to keep yourself fashion-forward.

Fashion Podcasts

1. The Fashion Law Podcast

Hosted by fashion lawyer and author, Orly Shani, The Fashion Law Podcast covers legal issues and business aspects of the fashion industry. With interviews featuring industry experts, designers, and lawyers, this podcast offers valuable insights into the business side of fashion.

2. The Business of Fashion

The Business of Fashion (BOF) is a leading media platform that delves into the business, social, and environmental aspects of the fashion industry. Hosted by Imran Amed, the podcast features interviews with industry leaders, designers, and influencers, discussing the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities in the fashion world.

3. Fashion Unraveled

Fashion Unraveled is a podcast that explores the history, craftsmanship, and cultural significance of fashion. Hosted by historian and author, Daniel James Cole, the podcast takes listeners on a journey through the evolution of fashion, from its origins to the present day.

4. The Fashion No Filter Podcast

Hosted by journalist and fashion commentator, Sarah Harris, The Fashion No Filter Podcast offers a candid and insightful look at the fashion industry. The podcast features interviews with industry insiders, designers, and celebrities, discussing everything from the latest trends to the challenges faced by the industry.

5. The Fashion Fix with Carly Heitlinger

The Fashion Fix with Carly Heitlinger is a weekly podcast that covers everything from fashion trends and beauty tips to career advice and personal stories. Hosted by fashion blogger and entrepreneur, Carly Heitlinger, the podcast offers a fresh and relatable perspective on the fashion industry.

Fashion Blogs

1. Vogue

Vogue, one of the most iconic fashion magazines in the world, has an extensive online presence through its blog. The Vogue blog covers everything from runway shows and designer profiles to beauty trends and celebrity style. With content curated by a team of fashion experts, Vogue’s blog is a must-follow for anyone interested in staying updated on the latest fashion trends.

2. The Sartorialist

The Sartorialist, founded by Scott Schuman, is a photography blog that showcases street style from around the world. The blog captures the essence of fashion by documenting the unique and creative outfits worn by people on the streets of cities like New York, Paris, and Milan. The Sartorialist offers a fresh perspective on fashion and serves as an inspiration for fashion enthusiasts and designers alike.

3. Garance Doré

Garance Doré is a French fashion blogger and illustrator known for her unique style and ability to capture the essence of fashion through her illustrations. Her blog features personal stories, interviews with industry insiders, and styling tips, offering a personal and intimate look at the fashion world.

4. The Coveteur

The Coveteur is a fashion and lifestyle blog that offers an inside look into the closets of celebrities, fashion influencers, and designers. The blog features stunning photography and detailed descriptions of the items in each subject’s wardrobe, providing readers with inspiration and insight into their personal style.

5. Man Repeller

Man Repeller is a fashion and lifestyle blog founded by Leandra Medine. The blog offers a unique and humorous perspective on fashion, with a focus on personal style and the challenges of dressing for different occasions. Man Repeller is a great resource for those who want to develop their own fashion sense and learn how to express their individuality through clothing.

Conclusion

In a world where fashion is constantly evolving, staying informed and inspired is essential for fashion enthusiasts and professionals. By following these fashion podcasts and blogs, you can gain valuable insights into the industry, learn about the latest trends, and develop your own unique sense of style. So, dive into the world of fashion and let these platforms guide you on your fashion journey.
