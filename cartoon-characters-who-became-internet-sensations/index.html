Cartoon Characters Who Became Internet Sensations


In the world of entertainment, cartoon characters have always held a special place in our hearts. They can make us laugh, cry, and even teach us valuable life lessons. But with the rise of the internet, some of these characters have transcended the small screen and become viral sensations in their own right. Here, we take a look at some of the cartoon characters who have captured the hearts of internet users around the world.

1. SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is arguably one of the most beloved cartoon characters of all time. Created by Stephen Hillenburg, this yellow sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea has become an internet sensation, inspiring countless memes, remixes, and viral videos. From the catchy theme song to the hilarious antics of SpongeBob and his friends, internet users have embraced this zany character and made him a staple in online culture.

2. Pepe the Frog

Initially created by artist Matt Furie as a laid-back, fun-loving frog in his comic series ‘Boy’s Club,’ Pepe the Frog took a dark turn when it was co-opted by some elements of the alt-right as a symbol of hate. Despite these negative connotations, Pepe became a viral sensation, with internet users remixing and reinterpreting the character in countless ways. This shows the power of the internet to take something innocent and transform it into something entirely different.

3. Pikachu

When it comes to cartoon characters, Pikachu is equivalent to a global phenomenon. This cute and cuddly electric mouse is best known for being the mascot of the Pokémon franchise. With the popularity of Pokémon games, TV shows, and movies, Pikachu quickly became an internet sensation. The character’s lovable and energetic personality has made it the subject of numerous memes, gifs, and viral videos. Pikachu’s iconic status in popular culture is a testament to its enduring appeal both on and offline.

4. Elsa and Olaf from Frozen

The success of Disney’s Frozen turned the characters Elsa and Olaf into internet sensations overnight. With the release of the hit movie, countless fan art, fanfiction, and parodies flooded the internet. Elsa’s powerful anthem ‘Let It Go’ became an online sensation with covers and remixes popping up all over social media platforms. Olaf, the adorable snowman, also captured the hearts of internet users with his humorous one-liners and infectious personality.

5. Rick Sanchez from Rick and Morty

Rick Sanchez, the eccentric and genius scientist from the animated series Rick and Morty, quickly became a cult favorite and internet sensation. With his witty remarks, nihilistic views, and unpredictable behavior, Rick has sparked countless discussions and debates among fans online. The character’s popularity has spawned memes, fan theories, and even a sauce craze when a limited-edition dipping sauce mentioned in the show was reintroduced by McDonald’s.

6. Homer Simpson

Homer Simpson, the bumbling and lovable patriarch of The Simpsons, has been a staple on TV screens for over three decades. However, it’s on the internet where this iconic character truly shines. Homer’s memorable catchphrases, such as ‘D’oh!’ and his unique way of looking at the world, have become a part of online culture. Fans have created countless memes and gifs featuring Homer’s hilarious moments, ensuring his place in the pantheon of internet sensations.

7. Spider-Man

Spider-Man, the friendly neighborhood superhero, has had a long and storied history in both comics and movies. However, it was his appearance in the 2002 film directed by Sam Raimi that catapulted him into internet stardom. The scene where Peter Parker, played by Tobey Maguire, strikes a pose in his Spider-Man suit quickly became a viral sensation, spawning numerous parodies and memes. Spider-Man’s popularity has only grown with the release of subsequent films and animated adaptations.

Conclusion

These are just a few examples of cartoon characters who have become internet sensations. Through the power of the internet, these characters have transcended their original mediums and become part of the collective online consciousness. They have inspired countless memes, remixes, and viral videos, and have connected people from all walks of life. Whether it’s through their catchy theme songs, humorous antics, or relatable personalities, these characters continue to bring joy, laughter, and entertainment to internet users around the world.
