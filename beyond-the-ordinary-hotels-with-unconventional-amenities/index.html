Beyond the Ordinary: Hotels with Unconventional Amenities


When it comes to travel, most people seek out hotels that offer comfort, convenience, and a touch of luxury. However, there are some hotels around the world that take it a step further, offering truly unconventional amenities that go beyond the ordinary. These unique accommodations provide guests with unforgettable experiences and create lasting memories. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most extraordinary hotels with unconventional amenities.

1. The Icehotel, Sweden:
Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, Sweden, the Icehotel is a one-of-a-kind hotel made entirely of ice and snow. Every year, the hotel is rebuilt with new designs and sculptures. Guests can experience sleeping in rooms made of ice and enjoy activities like ice sculpting and snowmobiling. It’s a truly magical and unforgettable experience.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
If you’ve ever dreamed of having breakfast with giraffes, then Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, is the place for you. This boutique hotel is set on 12 acres of private land and is home to a herd of resident Rothschild giraffes. Guests can interact with these gentle giants and even feed them from the breakfast table. It’s a truly unique experience that combines luxury with wildlife conservation.

3. Treehotel, Sweden:
For those who love nature, the Treehotel in Harads, Sweden, offers a chance to stay in architecturally stunning treehouses. Each treehouse has its own unique design and is suspended high up in the trees. From the Mirrorcube, which reflects its surroundings, to the UFO, which looks like a spaceship, these treehouses offer a truly unique and immersive experience in the heart of nature.

4. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:
Located in the Arctic Circle, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort offers guests the opportunity to stay in glass igloos and witness the mesmerizing Northern Lights from the comfort of their beds. The igloos are equipped with thermal glass, which keeps them warm and allows guests to marvel at the natural phenomenon. It’s an otherworldly experience that is sure to leave a lasting impression.

5. The Dog Bark Park Inn, USA:
If you’re a dog lover, then the Dog Bark Park Inn in Cottonwood, Idaho, is a must-visit. Shaped like a giant beagle, this unique hotel offers guests the chance to stay inside a work of art. The rooms are cozy and decorated with dog-themed furnishings. Guests can also take a stroll in the surrounding sculpture garden and visit the gift shop, which is housed in a smaller beagle-shaped building.

These hotels with unconventional amenities offer guests the chance to step out of their comfort zones and embrace new and exciting experiences. Whether it’s sleeping in an ice room, feeding giraffes, staying in a treehouse, witnessing the Northern Lights, or immersing yourself in dog-themed decor, these hotels offer something truly extraordinary. So, if you’re looking to add a touch of adventure and uniqueness to your next vacation, consider booking a stay at one of these extraordinary hotels.
