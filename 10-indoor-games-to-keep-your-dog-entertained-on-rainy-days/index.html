10 Indoor Games to Keep Your Dog Entertained on Rainy Days


Are rainy days putting a damper on your dog’s daily exercise routine? Don’t worry; we’ve got you covered! While going outside may not be an option when the weather takes a turn for the worse, there are still plenty of indoor games you can play with your furry friend to keep them entertained and burn off some energy.

1. Hide and Seek: Dogs love to use their sense of smell, so why not put it to good use? Grab your dog’s favorite treats or toys and hide them around the house. Encourage your dog to find them using their nose. Not only is this game mentally stimulating, but it also provides some physical exercise as they search for their treasure.

2. Tug of War: This classic game is perfect for even the smallest of spaces. Find a safe and sturdy toy, such as a rope or a rubber chew toy, and engage in a friendly game of tug of war. Not only will your dog get to burn off some energy, but it will also strengthen their jaw muscles.

3. Puzzle Toys: Invest in some puzzle toys that require your dog to figure out how to access treats or kibble hidden inside. This will keep them occupied for hours as they use their problem-solving skills to retrieve their reward.

4. Indoor Agility Course: Set up a mini agility course in your living room using household items. Encourage your dog to navigate through tunnels made from chairs or crawl under tables. You can also create hurdles with broomsticks or set up cones for them to weave in and out of. Not only will this challenge their physical abilities, but it will also provide mental stimulation.

5. Obedience Training: Rainy days are the perfect opportunity to brush up on your dog’s obedience training. Teach them new tricks or reinforce commands they already know. This not only keeps them engaged but also strengthens the bond between you and your pet.

6. Fetch: If you have enough space indoors, play a game of fetch using a soft or plush toy. Make sure to clear any fragile items out of the way to avoid accidents. Indoor fetch is a great way for dogs to burn off energy without the risk of slipping on wet surfaces.

7. Treat Hunt: Hide small treats around the house and encourage your dog to find them. Start with easy hiding spots and gradually increase the difficulty level as your dog becomes more skilled at the game. This game will keep them busy and entertained while satisfying their natural instinct to search for food.

8. Scent Work: Put your dog’s sense of smell to the test by hiding some of their favorite items throughout the house. This can be anything from their toys to pieces of clothing. Encourage your dog to find these items using only their nose. Scent work is mentally stimulating and a great way to keep your dog entertained indoors.

9. Staircase Workout: If you have a staircase in your home, use it to create a fun workout routine for your dog. Encourage them to go up and down the stairs a few times to get their heart rate up. This is a great way to burn off energy if going outside is not an option.

10. Interactive Toys: There are plenty of interactive toys available on the market that are designed to keep dogs entertained for hours. These toys dispense treats or make sounds when manipulated, providing mental stimulation and preventing boredom.

Remember, rainy days don’t have to put a halt to your dog’s exercise routine. With these indoor games, you can keep them entertained and mentally stimulated, providing a fun alternative to outdoor activities. So, the next time it’s pouring outside, try one of these games and watch your dog have a blast!
