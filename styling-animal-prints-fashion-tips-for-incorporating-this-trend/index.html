Styling Animal Prints: Fashion Tips for Incorporating This Trend


*Disclaimer: The following blog post is purely for informational purposes and does not promote the use of animal-derived materials in fashion.*

Animal prints have been a timeless fashion trend for years, and it seems like they are here to stay. From leopard spots to zebra stripes, these prints can add a touch of fierceness and sophistication to any outfit. However, styling animal prints can be a bit tricky. If not done right, you run the risk of looking tacky or overwhelming. But fear not! In this blog post, we will share some fashion tips for incorporating animal prints into your wardrobe with style and grace.

1. Start Small: If you’re new to animal prints, it’s best to begin with small and subtle touches. Incorporate a hint of these prints into your outfit with accessories like a leopard print scarf or a snake print belt. These small additions can instantly elevate your look without overpowering it.

2. Mix Prints with Neutrals: Animal prints act as neutrals in the fashion world. They can be paired effortlessly with other patterns or colors. To create a chic and balanced look, mix your animal print piece with solid colors or other classic patterns. For example, a leopard print blouse paired with black trousers or a zebra print skirt paired with a crisp white shirt can create a sophisticated ensemble.

3. Choose the Right Fit: When it comes to animal prints, the fit of your clothing is crucial. To avoid looking overly dramatic, opt for well-fitted pieces that flatter your body shape. Tailored dresses, fitted blazers, or skinny jeans with animal print accents can highlight your curves and create a polished look.

4. Accessories as Statement Pieces: Another great way to incorporate animal prints into your outfit is through statement accessories. A leopard print handbag, python print shoes, or a cheetah print hat can instantly transform a plain outfit into a bold and stylish statement. Remember, the key is to keep the rest of your outfit relatively simple, allowing the animal print accessory to take the spotlight.

5. Layer with Animal Prints: Layering is a great styling technique to add depth and visual interest to your outfits. Use animal print pieces as a layering element to create a trendy and modern look. A leopard print cardigan over a plain dress or a snakeskin jacket paired with a solid color blouse can add texture and dimension to your ensemble.

6. Mix Textures: Animal prints can be quite bold on their own, but when paired with different textures, they can create a fashionable visual contrast. For instance, pair a faux fur leopard print coat with a silk top or a leather skirt with a snakeskin blouse. Mixing textures adds intrigue and elevates the overall look.

7. Consider Color Combinations: Animal prints are not limited to the traditional shades of brown and black. Experiment with different color combinations of animal prints to make a unique style statement. For example, a blue zebra print or a red leopard print can add a fresh and unexpected twist to your outfit.

8. Keep it Balanced: The key to successfully styling animal prints lies in striking the right balance. Avoid going overboard by limiting your outfit to one or two animal print pieces at a time. Pairing a zebra print skirt with a leopard print top can create a striking ensemble, but adding more animal prints may make the outfit appear busy and overwhelming.

Remember, fashion is all about expressing your personal style and having fun. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different animal print pieces and accessories to find what works best for you. By following these fashion tips, you can confidently incorporate animal prints into your wardrobe and rock this timeless trend with grace and style.
