10 Ways to Add Volume and Texture to Your Hair


If you’re like many people, you may dream of having thick and voluminous hair. The good news is that there are numerous ways to add volume and texture to your locks, giving you bouncy and beautiful hair. In this article, we will discuss 10 effective ways to achieve that desired volume and texture. So, let’s get started!

1. Use volumizing products: One of the easiest ways to add volume to your hair is by using volumizing products such as mousse, volumizing sprays, or root-lifting serums. These products work by adding texture and lift to your hair, giving it that much-needed boost.

2. Blow-dry upside down: This may sound a bit unconventional, but flipping your hair upside down while blow-drying can actually create instant volume. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your damp hair, then flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair in that position. The gravity will help lift your hair at the roots, giving you instant volume.

3. Tease your hair: Teasing, also known as backcombing or backbrushing, is a technique that involves combing your hair in the opposite direction to add volume and texture. Start by taking small sections of hair near the roots and gently backcomb them using a fine-toothed comb. Be sure to smooth out the top layer of hair to hide any teasing, and finish off with hairspray to set the style.

4. Try a volumizing haircut: The right haircut can do wonders for adding volume to your hair. Opt for layering or a cut with shorter layers around the crown to create more movement and lift. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in volumizing techniques to find the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

5. Use dry shampoo: Dry shampoo is not just for oily hair days; it can also be a great tool for adding volume. Spray a small amount of dry shampoo at the roots of your hair and massage it into your scalp. The dry shampoo will absorb any excess oil, giving your hair more texture and volume.

6. Curl your hair: Adding curls or waves to your hair can instantly create the illusion of volume. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add body and bounce to your locks. For a more natural look, opt for larger barrel sizes and run your fingers through the curls to loosen them up.

7. Choose the right styling tools: The type of styling tools you use can make a big difference in how much volume you can achieve. Look for a round brush with boar bristles for blow-drying, as it will help lift the hair at the roots. Additionally, using a wide-toothed comb or a pick instead of a brush can prevent flattening the hair while detangling.

8. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner: Investing in a good-quality volumizing shampoo and conditioner can work wonders for adding volume to your hair over time. These products are specially formulated to make your hair appear thicker and fuller, giving you long-term results.

9. Opt for a volumizing hair spray: A volumizing hair spray is a must-have product in your hair care routine if you want to achieve long-lasting volume. After styling your hair, spray a small amount of volumizing hair spray all over your locks to lock in the volume and keep it in place throughout the day.

10. Embrace natural texture: Sometimes, the key to achieving voluminous hair lies in embracing your natural texture. Work with your natural waves, curls, or even straight hair by enhancing them with the right products and techniques. Remember, everyone’s hair is unique, and what works for someone else may not work for you. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for your hair type.

In conclusion, achieving voluminous and textured hair is possible with the right techniques and products. From using volumizing products to embracing your natural texture, there are numerous ways to add volume to your hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different methods until you find the perfect routine for your tresses. With a little practice and the right tools, you’ll be rocking voluminous and textured hair in no time!
