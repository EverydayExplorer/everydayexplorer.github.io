5 Creative Ways to Use Leftover Roast Chicken


Roast chicken is a dinner table staple, known for its simplicity and versatility. However, it’s the days following the initial feast—when the challenge of leftover chicken emerges—that the true culinary creativity can shine. Here, we explore five inventive ways to transform leftover roast chicken into new, exciting dishes that will revitalize your meal plan and reduce food waste. These recipes not only promise delicious flavors but also offer practical solutions to make the most of your leftovers.

1. Chicken Caesar Wrap

A Chicken Caesar Wrap is a refreshing way to repurpose your roast chicken. It’s easy to assemble and perfect for a quick lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients: - Leftover roast chicken, shredded - Romaine lettuce, chopped - Caesar dressing - Parmesan cheese, shaved or grated - Croutons, optional - Large tortillas or wraps

Instructions: 1. In a large bowl, toss the shredded chicken with enough Caesar dressing to coat. Add the chopped romaine lettuce and a generous handful of Parmesan cheese. Mix until everything is well combined. 2. Lay a tortilla flat and place a portion of the chicken mixture onto the center. If desired, add croutons for a crunchy texture. 3. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward and then roll it up tightly. Slice the wrap in half diagonally and serve with extra Caesar dressing on the side for dipping.

2. Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Stir-frying is a fantastic way to utilize a variety of leftovers, including roast chicken. This Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry is quick to prepare and packed with flavor.

Ingredients: - Leftover roast chicken, cut into strips - Assorted vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas) - 2 tablespoons soy sauce - 1 tablespoon oyster sauce - 1 teaspoon sesame oil - Garlic, minced - Fresh ginger, minced - Vegetable oil

Instructions: 1. Heat a small amount of vegetable oil in a wok or large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, and sauté until fragrant. 2. Add the vegetables to the wok and stir-fry until they are just tender. 3. Toss in the roast chicken strips and stir to combine. 4. In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Pour this mixture over the chicken and vegetables in the wok, stirring well to ensure everything is evenly coated. 5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes or until everything is heated through. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds or chopped green onions.

3. Chicken Tortilla Soup

Chicken Tortilla Soup is a comforting and hearty dish that’s perfect for using up leftover roast chicken. It’s full of flavor and can be customized with various toppings.

Ingredients: - Leftover roast chicken, shredded - 1 onion, chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 jalapeño, finely chopped (optional) - 1 can of diced tomatoes - 1 quart chicken broth - 1 teaspoon ground cumin - 1 teaspoon chili powder - 1 teaspoon paprika - Fresh cilantro, chopped - Lime wedges - Tortilla strips or chips - Avocado, sour cream, and shredded cheese for topping

Instructions: 1. In a large pot, sauté onion, garlic, and jalapeño (if using) in a bit of oil until the onion is translucent. 2. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, and paprika. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes. 3. Add the shredded chicken to the pot and continue to simmer until the chicken is heated through. 4. Serve the soup in bowls, topped with cilantro, a squeeze of lime juice, tortilla strips, and other toppings like avocado, sour cream, and shredded cheese.

4. Chicken Salad Sandwich

Transform your roast chicken into a delicious chicken salad sandwich. It’s perfect for a picnic or a satisfying lunch.

Ingredients: - Leftover roast chicken, finely chopped - Mayonnaise - Mustard (Dijon or whole grain) - Celery, finely chopped - Red onion, finely chopped - Salt and pepper to taste - Lettuce and tomatoes, sliced - Bread or croissants

Instructions: 1. In a mixing bowl, combine the finely chopped chicken with mayonnaise, a small amount of mustard, celery, and red onion. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 2. Stir the mixture until all ingredients are well combined. 3. Assemble the sandwich by spreading additional mayonnaise or mustard on bread or croissants, then layering with lettuce and sliced tomatoes. 4. Add a generous scoop of chicken salad to the sandwich, close it, and serve.

5. BBQ Chicken Pizza

Give your pizza night a twist by using leftover roast chicken as a topping on a homemade BBQ Chicken Pizza.

Ingredients: - Pizza dough, homemade or store-bought - BBQ sauce - Leftover roast chicken, shredded - Red onion, thinly sliced - Mozzarella cheese, shredded - Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions: 1. Preheat your oven as per the instructions for your pizza dough. 2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a thin layer of BBQ sauce over the surface of the dough. 3. Distribute the shredded chicken and sliced red onion evenly over the sauce. Cover with a generous amount of mozzarella cheese. 4. Bake according to your dough’s instructions, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. 5. After removing the pizza from the oven, sprinkle fresh cilantro over the top. Slice and serve hot.

With these five creative recipes, your leftover roast chicken will be transformed into exciting and delicious new meals. Not only do these dishes breathe new life into leftover ingredients, but they also expand your culinary repertoire and ensure that nothing goes to waste. Whether you’re whipping up a quick wrap or crafting a flavorful soup, these ideas promise satisfaction in every bite.
