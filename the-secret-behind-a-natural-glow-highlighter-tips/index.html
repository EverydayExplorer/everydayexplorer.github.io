The Secret Behind a Natural Glow: Highlighter Tips


As humans, we all desire to look our best. The beauty industry is a testament to how much we love taking care of ourselves and the lengths we go to look good. While makeup can undoubtedly up your beauty game, a natural glow always does the trick. This dewy, radiant, and luminous finish is achieved through the use of a highlighter. The right highlighter can give you that sought-after natural glow that leaves people guessing if you’ve even applied makeup.

Highlighters, also known as iluminators, are powders, creams, or liquids used to enhance the highpoints of the face. When applied correctly, they create a reflective glow on the skin that mimics the look of healthy, radiant skin. Highlighters come in varying shades and textures, making it easier to find one that suits your skin tone and type.

Now that we have an idea of what highlighters are let’s delve into how you can use them to get that natural glow you’re after.

Start with skincare

Before any makeup application, it’s important to begin with skincare. The better your skin looks, the better your makeup will look. A good skincare routine will help keep your skin hydrated, plump, and well-nourished. When the skin is healthy, makeup sits better on it, and the result is a more natural-looking canvas.

Choose the right formula

As earlier mentioned, highlighters come in different forms, including powders, creams, and liquids. Each formula has its own unique finish and texture. For example, powder highlighters provide a more intense, shimmery finish, while cream highlights give a subtle glow. Always consider your skin type and the look you want to achieve when picking a highlighter. If you have oily skin, a powder highlighter may work better for you, while drier skin types benefit from a cream or liquid formula.

Select the right shade

Highlighters come in various shades, so it’s essential to pick one that complements your skin tone. Generally, warm-toned highlighters with golden or bronze hues work best on olive or darker skin tones. Cooler and pearl-toned highlighters are perfect for those with fair skin. If you’re unsure which shade to pick, you can start with a universal shade that will work with most skin tones.

Apply strategically

To get the most natural-looking glow, apply highlighter only to the high points of your face. These include the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, the cupid’s bow, and the inner corners of the eyes. Use no more than a few dots or swipes of your chosen formula, then blend it well. A small detail brush is perfect for getting into those tight areas to ensure you don’t overapply.

The secret to getting a natural glow with highlighter lies in applying it sparingly and strategically. Remember that less is more - you can always add more, but it’s almost impossible to reverse an overdone highlighter.

In conclusion, if you’re looking for a skincare and makeup hack to achieve a natural-looking glow, highlighter should be your go-to. With the tips mentioned above, you can get that irresistible shine that makes you look like you’ve just returned from a well-deserved vacation. Remember to always start with good skincare, choose the right formula and shade that suits your skin type and tone, and apply strategically.
