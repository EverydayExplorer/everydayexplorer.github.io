Sizzling Summer BBQ Recipes for the Whole Family


As the sun warms our skin and the days stretch longer, it’s time to fire up the grill and indulge in the flavors of summer. Whether you’re hosting a backyard bash or just enjoying a family meal, these sizzling BBQ recipes will tantalize your taste buds and create lasting memories.

Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the butter, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the salmon fillets with the herb butter.
  4. Grill the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  5. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.

Grilled Chicken Skewers with Pineapple and Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup red bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, pineapple, and peppers.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, honey, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken mixture and toss to coat.
  4. Thread the chicken and vegetables onto skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.

Grilled Corn on the Cob with Chipotle Lime Butter

Ingredients:

  • 6 ears of corn
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon minced chipotle peppers in adobo
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Remove the husks and silk from the corn.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the butter, chipotle peppers, lime juice, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the corn with the chipotle lime butter.
  4. Grill the corn for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, or until tender and slightly charred.
  5. Serve immediately.

Grilled Vegetable Skewers with Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow squash, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  2. Thread the vegetables onto skewers.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the hummus, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the vegetable skewers with the hummus mixture.
  5. Grill the skewers for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  6. Serve with additional hummus for dipping.

Grilled Fruit Salad with Honey-Lime Vinaigrette

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 cup mango chunks
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the fruit.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the honey, lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the vinaigrette over the fruit and toss to coat.
  4. Grill the fruit salad for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, or until the fruit is slightly caramelized.
  5. Serve immediately.

Tips for Perfect Grilling:

  • Clean your grill before each use.
  • Preheat your grill to the desired temperature.
  • Use the right tools, such as a grill brush and tongs.
  • Season your food generously.
  • Don’t overcrowd the grill.
  • Let your food rest before serving.

With these sizzling BBQ recipes and grilling tips, you’ll create a memorable summer feast that will delight your family and friends. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and savor the flavors of the season.
