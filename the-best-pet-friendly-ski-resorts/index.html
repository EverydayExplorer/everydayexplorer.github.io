The Best Pet-Friendly Ski Resorts


Are you a pet owner who loves hitting the slopes? If you’re planning a ski vacation and don’t want to leave your furry friend behind, you’re in luck! There are plenty of pet-friendly ski resorts that offer a fantastic winter getaway for both you and your beloved pet. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best pet-friendly ski resorts around the world, so you can plan the perfect vacation for you and your four-legged companion.

1. Whistler Blackcomb, Canada

Whistler Blackcomb, located in beautiful British Columbia, is not only known for its world-class skiing but also for its pet-friendly policies. This resort offers a dedicated dog-friendly hotel, where your pet can relax and enjoy the comfort of their own space while you hit the slopes. Additionally, Whistler Blackcomb provides dog-friendly hiking trails and even a dog-friendly après-ski patio, so you and your furry friend can unwind together after a day on the mountain.

2. Aspen Snowmass, USA

Aspen Snowmass, located in the heart of the Rocky Mountains in Colorado, is another fantastic pet-friendly ski resort. This resort understands that your pet is part of your family and offers a range of pet-friendly accommodations, including hotels and vacation rentals. Aspen Snowmass also provides dog-friendly hiking trails and even a dog-friendly ski school, so your pet can join in on the fun and learn to navigate the slopes alongside you.

3. Les 3 Vallées, France

For those looking for an international ski adventure, Les 3 Vallées in France is a top choice. This vast ski area is not only pet-friendly but also offers a variety of accommodations that welcome pets. From cozy chalets to luxurious hotels, you’ll find the perfect place to stay with your furry friend. Les 3 Vallées also boasts pet-friendly restaurants and even dog-friendly activities such as dog sledding, ensuring that your pet has just as much fun as you do.

4. Niseko, Japan

If you’re looking for a unique ski experience, Niseko in Japan is a must-visit destination. This pet-friendly ski resort offers a range of pet-friendly accommodations, including traditional Japanese ryokans and modern hotels. Niseko also provides pet-friendly skiing and snowboarding lessons, so your pet can hit the slopes with confidence. After a day of skiing, relax in one of the resort’s pet-friendly hot springs, a perfect way to unwind and rejuvenate.

5. Queenstown, New Zealand

For those who prefer skiing during the summer months, Queenstown in New Zealand is the place to be. This pet-friendly ski resort offers a range of outdoor activities, including skiing, snowboarding, and even dog-friendly hiking trails. Queenstown also provides a variety of pet-friendly accommodations, ensuring that you and your pet have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Conclusion

Planning a ski vacation doesn’t mean you have to leave your furry friend behind. These pet-friendly ski resorts around the world offer a wonderful experience for both you and your pet. From dog-friendly accommodations to activities specifically designed for pets, these resorts go above and beyond to make sure your pet feels just as welcome as you do. So pack your bags, grab your skis, and get ready for an unforgettable adventure with your best friend by your side. Happy skiing!
