Exploring the World: Unique Hotels in Unusual Locations


Imagine waking up surrounded by rolling hills and mystical landscapes, away from the noise and chaos of the city, in a hotel that feels like it belongs in a fairytale. The world is full of unique hotels that are nestled in the most unusual locations, creating unforgettable experiences for travelers seeking something truly out of the ordinary. From underwater hideaways to treehouses in the sky, let’s embark on a journey to explore some of the most extraordinary hotels around the globe.

1. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
Tucked away in the heart of Nairobi, Giraffe Manor offers a one-of-a-kind experience. With its lush surroundings and resident Rothschild giraffes, guests can enjoy breakfast with these gentle giants, as they poke their heads through the windows in search of treats. The elegant manor, reminiscent of a bygone era, provides an intimate encounter with wildlife like no other.

2. Icehotel, Sweden:
Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel invites you to delve into a world of frozen enchantment. Each winter, artists from around the globe come together to sculpt this ethereal masterpiece entirely out of ice and snow. Spend the night in a surreal ice suite, adorned with ice sculptures and reindeer skins, and drift off into a magical slumber under the Northern Lights.

3. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada:
If you’ve ever dreamt of sleeping among the trees, this is the perfect destination for you. Nestled within the lush rainforests of Vancouver Island, Free Spirit Spheres are suspended between the towering trees. These spherical treehouses provide a cozy and whimsical escape, where you can reconnect with nature and revel in the serenity of your surroundings.

4. Manta Resort, Tanzania:
For those seeking an underwater adventure, the Manta Resort offers an unrivaled experience on the beautiful Pemba Island. The highlight of your stay here is the underwater room, which floats adrift in the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean. With panoramic views of the marine life passing by, this accommodation allows you to sleep surrounded by the vibrant colors and tranquility of the underwater world.

5. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:
Winter wonderland awaits at the Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, located in the Finnish Lapland. Known for its iconic glass igloos, this unique accommodation offers unobstructed views of the mesmerizing Northern Lights. Snuggle up in the warmth of your private igloo, marvel at the dancing skies above, and lose yourself in the enchantment of the Arctic wilderness.

6. Skylodge Adventure Suites, Peru:
Located in the Sacred Valley of Peru, the Skylodge Adventure Suites offer a thrilling stay for the adventurous souls. Perched atop a cliff, these transparent capsule-like pods grant you breathtaking views of the surrounding valley. To access your room, embark on an adrenaline-pumping climb or a zipline adventure. The reward is a cozy nest suspended in the sky, where you can sleep under a blanket of stars.

7. The Caves, Jamaica:
Immerse yourself in the laid-back island vibes at The Caves, a boutique hotel located atop cliffs in Negril, Jamaica. This Caribbean gem boasts a collection of stunningly designed cottages and suites nestled into the dramatic limestone cliffs. Dive into the turquoise waters, explore hidden sea caves, and indulge your senses in paradise.

8. Kokopelli’s Cave Bed and Breakfast, USA:
Deep within the sandstone cliffs of New Mexico, Kokopelli’s Cave Bed and Breakfast beckons the adventurous at heart. Accessible only by a rugged path, this unique hotel is carved directly into the rock, offering mesmerizing views of the La Plata River Valley. Experience the cozy ambiance of cave dwelling, complete with modern amenities and panoramic vistas.

These unique hotels in unusual locations offer more than just a place to rest your head; they create extraordinary memories that will stay with you long after your journey ends. Whether you seek the serenity of nature, the thrill of the unknown, or simply a break from the ordinary, these hidden gems invite you to explore the world in a way that is truly out of the ordinary. So pack your bags, embark on an adventure, and discover a new level of wanderlust in these exceptional hotels.
