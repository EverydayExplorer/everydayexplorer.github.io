The Top Educational YouTube Channels for Kids


In today’s digital era, YouTube has become a go-to platform for both entertainment and education for people of all ages. One particular audience that greatly benefits from educational YouTube channels is kids. With the right content, YouTube can be a powerful tool to engage children in learning and make education fun and interactive.

In this blog post, we will take a look at some of the top educational YouTube channels for kids. These channels offer a wide range of content, from science experiments and history lessons to language learning and math tutorials. Let’s dive in!

1. National Geographic Kids

National Geographic is a well-known brand that has been inspiring curiosity and exploration for many years. The National Geographic Kids YouTube channel brings the same enthusiasm for learning to young minds. With videos on animals, nature, and the world around us, National Geographic Kids takes kids on exciting adventures while teaching them about various topics in an engaging way.

2. TED-Ed

TED-Ed is an extension of the renowned TED Talks, but tailored specifically for young learners. The TED-Ed YouTube channel covers a vast range of subjects, from science and math to literature and history. With beautifully animated videos and thought-provoking content, TED-Ed aims to ignite curiosity and inspire a love for learning in kids.

3. SciShow Kids

SciShow Kids, created by the team behind the popular educational channel SciShow, is dedicated to making science fun and accessible to children. Through engaging experiments, explanations, and demonstrations, SciShow Kids covers a wide range of scientific concepts in an entertaining way. This channel encourages children to question, explore, and develop a strong interest in science.

4. Crash Course Kids

Crash Course Kids offers fast-paced, animated videos that cover a range of subjects, including science, engineering, and even social studies. With catchy tunes and colorful visuals, Crash Course Kids makes complex concepts easy to understand. The channel’s enthusiastic host guides kids through various topics, making learning enjoyable and engaging.

5. Khan Academy Kids

Khan Academy Kids is an offshoot of the popular online learning platform, Khan Academy. This YouTube channel offers a variety of educational videos that cover subjects such as math, reading, and social-emotional learning. Khan Academy Kids provides a holistic approach to education, helping children develop fundamental skills while fostering their love for learning.

6. Art for Kids Hub

Art for Kids Hub is a must-visit channel for budding young artists. Hosted by Rob and his family, this YouTube channel offers step-by-step drawing tutorials that are easy for kids to follow along. Whether it’s drawing animals, cartoon characters, or even famous landmarks, Art for Kids Hub encourages creativity and develops artistic skills in a fun and engaging way.

7. The Backyard Scientist

For kids who love science experiments and explosions, The Backyard Scientist is the channel to watch. This YouTube channel features captivating videos of various experiments and demonstrations that push the boundaries of science. While the content may be more suitable for older kids, younger ones can still be fascinated by the world of science through this exciting channel.

8. Simple History

Learning about history can be a daunting task for kids, but Simple History aims to change that. With fun and animated videos, this YouTube channel simplifies historical events and figures, making them easier to understand and remember. Simple History takes kids on a journey through time, allowing them to explore the past and gain a deeper appreciation for history.

9. PBS Kids

PBS Kids is a well-respected brand in children’s educational programming, and their YouTube channel is no exception. With a wide range of animated shows, such as ‘Daniel Tiger’s Neighborhood’ and ‘Wild Kratts,’ PBS Kids offers entertaining and educational content that covers various subjects, including science, math, and social-emotional development.

10. Numberblocks

Numberblocks is a fun and educational animated series that teaches children about numbers and basic math concepts. With vibrant characters representing different numbers, Numberblocks helps kids develop their numeracy skills in a playful and engaging way. The Numberblocks YouTube channel offers clips and full episodes from the series, making math learning enjoyable.

Conclusion

YouTube can be a valuable resource when it comes to educational content for kids. The channels mentioned in this blog post provide a wide range of engaging and informative videos that make learning fun and interactive. Whether it’s exploring nature with National Geographic Kids or learning about history with Simple History, these educational YouTube channels have something to offer for children of all ages and interests.

By incorporating these channels into your child’s screen time, you can enhance their learning experience and inspire a lifelong love for knowledge. So, why not harness the power of YouTube to make education an exciting adventure for your kids?
