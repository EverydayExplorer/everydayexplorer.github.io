The Coolest Hotel Pools in the World: Take a Dip in Style


Are you tired of the same old hotel pools? Looking for a unique and unforgettable swimming experience on your next vacation? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be exploring the coolest hotel pools in the world, where you can take a dip in style.

1. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

One of the most iconic hotels in Singapore, Marina Bay Sands boasts an incredible rooftop infinity pool that offers breathtaking views of the city skyline. Located on the 57th floor, this pool is the world’s largest rooftop infinity pool, spanning 150 meters! Soak yourself in luxury as you enjoy panoramic vistas and a unique swimming experience like no other.

2. The Joule, Dallas

Located in the heart of Dallas, The Joule is not just a hotel but also a hub for art and culture. Its pièce de résistance is the stunning rooftop pool that extends eight feet beyond the building’s edge. Known as the ‘cantilevered pool,’ it creates an illusion of floating above the city streets, making it a must-visit for thrill-seekers and pool enthusiasts alike.

3. The Cambrian Hotel, Switzerland

If you’re a fan of natural beauty, then the pool at The Cambrian Hotel in Switzerland is perfect for you. Surrounded by snow-covered mountains and breathtaking landscapes, this heated outdoor infinity pool offers serenity and tranquility. Immerse yourself in warm water while gazing at the stunning Swiss Alps. It’s the ideal spot for relaxation and rejuvenation.

4. Hotel Villa Mahal, Turkey

Nestled on a cliffside overlooking the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea, Hotel Villa Mahal in Turkey offers a pool experience that is simply out of this world. The infinity pool seamlessly blends with the sea, creating a mesmerizing sight. Take a swim and feel like you’re floating in the vast expanse of the Mediterranean.

5. The Library, Thailand

The Library in Koh Samui, Thailand, is known for its unique red pool. Yes, you read that right! The pool’s bright red color creates a striking contrast against the turquoise ocean, making it a feast for the eyes. Lounge on a poolside cabana or wade in the brilliant red waters – it’s an experience that you won’t forget.

6. The Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia

Located amidst the lush rainforests of Bali, The Hanging Gardens is famous for its gravity-defying, two-tiered infinity pool. The upper level overhangs the jungle valley below, offering a magnificent view of the surrounding greenery. It’s a true oasis of tranquility where you can unwind and enjoy the natural beauty of Bali.

7. The Oberoi Udaivilas, India

Situated on the banks of Lake Pichola in Udaipur, India, The Oberoi Udaivilas boasts an opulent pool that perfectly complements the hotel’s royal architecture. Surrounded by lush gardens and magnificent palaces, this pool offers a glimpse into the country’s rich cultural heritage. Take a dip and feel like a Maharaja or Maharani for a day.

Conclusion

If you’re looking to elevate your pool experience and create unforgettable memories, these hotels offer extraordinary swimming options. Whether you’re gazing at breathtaking vistas, floating above the city skyline, or simply immersing yourself in stunning architecture, these hotel pools redefine luxury and style.

So, pack your swimsuit and get ready for an extraordinary dip in some of the coolest hotel pools in the world. Dive in and create memories that will last a lifetime!
