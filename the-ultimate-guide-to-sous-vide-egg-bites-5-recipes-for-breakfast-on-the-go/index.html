The Ultimate Guide to Sous Vide Egg Bites: 5 Recipes for Breakfast on the Go


Sous vide egg bites have become a popular breakfast option for those on the go. These delicious and nutritious bites are not only easy to make, but they can also be customized to suit your taste preferences. In this ultimate guide, we will explore five mouth-watering recipes for sous vide egg bites that are perfect for a quick and satisfying breakfast.

1. Bacon and Cheddar Egg Bites:
Ingredients:
- 4 large eggs
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat your sous vide machine to 172°F (77.8°C).
2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the eggs and heavy cream until well combined.
3. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, salt, and pepper.
4. Divide the mixture evenly among six 4-ounce mason jars or silicone molds.
5. Place the filled jars or molds into the preheated water bath and cook for 60 minutes.
6. Once cooked, carefully remove the jars or molds from the water bath and allow them to cool for a few minutes.
7. Gently remove the egg bites from the jars or molds and serve warm.
