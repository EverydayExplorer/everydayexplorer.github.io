The best platformers for jumping enthusiasts


Platformers have always been a beloved genre for gamers, offering exciting challenges and adrenaline-pumping jumps. For jumping enthusiasts, a good platformer is like a dream come true. The feeling of perfectly timing a jump, soaring through the air, and landing gracefully is unmatched. Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or just starting out, there’s nothing quite like the thrill of a well-designed platformer.

In this article, we will be exploring some of the best platformers for jumping enthusiasts. These games have captivated players with their innovative level design, tight controls, and satisfying jumps. So, if you’re ready to embark on a jumping adventure, grab your controller and let’s dive into the world of platformers.

First on our list is the iconic ‘Super Mario Brothers’ series. Since its inception in the 1980s, this franchise has become synonymous with the platformer genre. With each new installment, Nintendo continues to refine the gameplay mechanics and introduce new challenges for players to conquer. The precision jumping required in these games is both challenging and incredibly satisfying. Whether you’re bouncing off enemies or leaping across treacherous gaps, the Super Mario series never fails to deliver a smooth and rewarding jumping experience.

Next up is ‘Celeste,’ an indie gem that took the gaming world by storm. Developed by Matt Makes Games, this platformer is a masterclass in level design and storytelling. You play as Madeline, a young girl who embarks on a journey to climb the treacherous Celeste Mountain. The game’s tight controls and precise jumping mechanics make each leap feel incredibly satisfying. Combine that with the heartfelt narrative and stunning visuals, and you have a platformer that is as emotionally captivating as it is thrilling.

Another standout platformer is ‘Hollow Knight,’ a beautifully crafted game from Team Cherry. Set in a dark and eerie underground world, players take on the role of a small bug armed with a nail, venturing through ancient ruins to battle grotesque creatures. The game’s platforming mechanics are top-notch, with precise controls that allow for precise jumps and precise landings. The interconnected world invites exploration and rewards players with hidden areas and secrets. With its dark and atmospheric setting, challenging gameplay, and satisfying platforming elements, ‘Hollow Knight’ is a must-play for jumping enthusiasts.

Moving on, we have ‘Rayman Legends,’ a vibrant and whimsical platformer developed by Ubisoft. The game’s colorful visuals and catchy soundtrack create an immersive and joyful experience. With its responsive controls and diverse levels, ‘Rayman Legends’ offers a wide range of jumping challenges. Whether you’re traversing through beautiful landscapes or navigating treacherous obstacles, each jump feels smooth and satisfying. The game also features a cooperative multiplayer mode, allowing you to team up with friends and tackle the platforming challenges together.

Last but not least, we have ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ by Moon Studios. This critically acclaimed platformer follows the story of Ori, a small forest spirit on a quest to restore the dying forest. The game’s breathtaking visuals and emotionally-driven story create a captivating atmosphere. The platforming mechanics in ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ are fluid and intuitive, with precise controls that make navigating the world a joy. The game seamlessly blends platforming with exploration and puzzle-solving, creating a truly immersive experience for jumping enthusiasts.

These are just a few examples of the best platformers for jumping enthusiasts. Each game offers its own unique charm and challenges, providing hours of entertainment for gamers of all skill levels. So, if you’re looking to hone your jumping skills or simply craving a thrilling platforming experience, be sure to check out these titles. Happy jumping!
