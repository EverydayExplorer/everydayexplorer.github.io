Stay and Play: Hotels with Exclusive Access to Golf Courses and Country Clubs


Welcome to a world where luxury and leisure intersect at the green landscapes of prestigious golf courses and exclusive country clubs. If you’re an avid golfer or simply appreciate the beauty of the game, we have something special in store for you. In this blog post, we will take you on a journey through some of the most luxurious hotels that offer exclusive access to golf courses and country clubs.

Imagine waking up in a lavish hotel room, stepping out onto your private balcony, and being greeted by stunning views of manicured fairways and rolling greens. With these hotels, you don’t just stay and play - you immerse yourself in an experience that combines the best of accommodation and golf.

First on our list is the breathtaking Pinehurst Resort in North Carolina. Known as the “Cradle of American Golf,” this historic resort boasts nine world-class golf courses, including the legendary Pinehurst No. 2, which has hosted numerous major championships. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or a beginner, Pinehurst offers something for everyone. The resort also features luxurious accommodations, spa facilities, and a wide range of dining options to ensure that your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.

Next up is The Lodge at Pebble Beach in California. Nestled along the rugged coastline of Monterey Peninsula, this iconic hotel offers unparalleled access to Pebble Beach Golf Links - one of the most famous courses in the world. With its stunning ocean views and challenging holes, this course has been the stage for some of golf’s most memorable moments. After a round of golf, you can relax at the lodge’s spa, indulge in gourmet dining, or simply take in the breathtaking beauty of the Pacific Ocean from your room.

For those seeking a European golf retreat, The Gleneagles Hotel in Scotland is a must-visit destination. Surrounded by the picturesque Scottish countryside, this five-star hotel is home to three championship golf courses. The King’s Course, Queen’s Course, and PGA Centenary Course, designed by the legendary Jack Nicklaus, offer golfers of all levels a chance to test their skills. The Gleneagles Hotel also offers a range of other activities, including clay pigeon shooting, falconry, and an award-winning spa, providing something for everyone to enjoy.

Heading to the tropics, we find the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel in Hawaii. Situated on the Kohala Coast, this luxurious hotel features an 18-hole championship golf course designed by Robert Trent Jones Sr. With its stunning ocean views and challenging fairways, this course is a dream destination for golf enthusiasts. After a round of golf, you can relax on the hotel’s pristine beach, indulge in Hawaiian cuisine, or explore the island’s natural wonders.

Last but certainly not least, we venture to the Caribbean and discover the Casa de Campo Resort & Villas in the Dominican Republic. This sprawling resort offers guests access to three championship golf courses, including Teeth of the Dog, which has been consistently ranked as one of the top courses in the world. With its breathtaking oceanfront holes and challenging design, playing at Teeth of the Dog is an experience like no other. The resort also offers an array of luxurious accommodations, gourmet dining options, and a private beach for the perfect tropical getaway.

These are just a few examples of the many hotels that offer exclusive access to golf courses and country clubs. Whether you’re a golf enthusiast or simply looking for a luxurious getaway, these destinations combine the best of both worlds. So, pack your golf clubs, book your stay, and get ready to tee off in style. Your dream golf vacation awaits!
