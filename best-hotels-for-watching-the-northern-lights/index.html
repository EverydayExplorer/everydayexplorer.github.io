Best hotels for watching the northern lights


Welcome to our blog post on the best hotels for watching the mesmerizing northern lights! If you’ve always dreamed of witnessing this breathtaking natural phenomenon, we’ve got you covered. In this blog, we will take you on a virtual journey to some of the most picturesque locations around the world, where you can enjoy the awe-inspiring display of the northern lights while ensconced in luxurious accommodations. So grab a cup of hot cocoa, sit back, and get ready to be inspired!

1. Hotel Kakslauttanen, Finland:
Let’s start our journey in the Arctic Circle, where Hotel Kakslauttanen in Finland offers an extraordinary experience. This iconic hotel is famous for its glass igloos, providing you with a unique opportunity to witness the northern lights from the comfort of your warm bed. Imagine gazing up at the starry sky as the vibrant colors dance above you – an experience you’ll cherish forever.

2. Ion Adventure Hotel, Iceland:
Next up, we head to Iceland, a popular destination for chasing the northern lights. The Ion Adventure Hotel is situated amidst breathtaking landscapes, providing a cozy and stylish retreat. Its floor-to-ceiling windows offer unobstructed views of the aurora borealis, allowing you to relax and enjoy the show without stepping outside.

3. Sorrisniva Igloo Hotel, Norway:
If you want an out-of-this-world experience, look no further than the Sorrisniva Igloo Hotel in Norway. This unique property is entirely made of ice and snow, providing an enchanting backdrop for witnessing the northern lights. Snuggle into your ice-carved bed, adorned with warm reindeer hides, and marvel at the celestial spectacle dancing above you.

4. Hotel Rangá, Iceland:
Iceland makes another appearance on our list with Hotel Rangá, known as Iceland’s premier northern lights hotel. Located in a secluded area near the Hekla volcano, this rustic yet luxurious hotel offers panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. With outdoor hot tubs and cozy log cabins, you can unwind after a day of northern lights hunting and let nature’s show take your breath away.

5. Abisko Mountain Lodge, Sweden:
Deep within the Arctic Circle, you’ll find the Abisko Mountain Lodge in Sweden. Renowned for its prime location, this lodge sits in the heart of the Aurora Sky Station zone, which boasts one of the highest chances of witnessing the northern lights. Enjoy the lodge’s authentic charm, comfortable rooms, and delicious Swedish cuisine while keeping an eye on the night sky for the magical display.

6. Chena Hot Springs Resort, Alaska:
Our journey wouldn’t be complete without a mention of the Chena Hot Springs Resort in Alaska. Nestled in a remote wilderness, this resort offers not only a cozy place to stay but also natural hot springs to relax in while marveling at the dancing lights above. With knowledgeable guides and excellent facilities, this is a prime spot to tick off your northern lights bucket list.

Remember, the best time to witness the northern lights is during the winter months in the Northern Hemisphere, when the nights are long and dark. However, it’s essential to plan your trip well in advance and keep an eye on the aurora forecasts for the best chances of catching this mesmerizing spectacle.

Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or a curious explorer, these hotels provide unforgettable experiences, combining luxury with nature’s most awe-inspiring light show. So, pack your warmest clothes, charge your camera batteries, and get ready to embark on a journey that will leave you in awe of the natural wonders that our world has to offer. Happy northern lights hunting!
