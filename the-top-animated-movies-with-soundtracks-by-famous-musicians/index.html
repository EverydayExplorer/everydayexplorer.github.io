The Top Animated Movies with Soundtracks by Famous Musicians


Welcome to our blog, where we dive into the enchanting world of animated movies with soundtracks by famous musicians! In this article, we’ll explore some of the top animated films that have been graced with exceptional musical talent. From heartfelt melodies to toe-tapping beats, these movies have not only captured our hearts but also our ears. So, grab some popcorn, sit back, and let’s embark on a melodious journey through the animated realm.

1. “The Lion King” (1994) - Music by Elton John and Hans Zimmer:
Starting off our list is the timeless classic, “The Lion King.” With an unforgettable soundtrack, this film transports us to the African savannah with a mesmerizing blend of melodies by the legendary Elton John and the enchanting score composed by Hans Zimmer. From the powerful “Circle of Life” to the emotional ballad “Can You Feel the Love Tonight,” the music beautifully complements the heartwarming story.

2. “Moana” (2016) - Music by Lin-Manuel Miranda, Opetaia Foa’i, and Mark Mancina:
Next up, we sail to the Pacific Islands with the musical adventure “Moana.” Lin-Manuel Miranda, known for his work on the Broadway hit “Hamilton,” brings his signature style to this animated gem. Combining catchy tunes with cultural influences, Miranda’s compositions, alongside Opetaia Foa’i and Mark Mancina, create a vibrant and immersive soundtrack. Hits like “How Far I’ll Go” and “You’re Welcome” will have you singing along in no time!

3. “Coco” (2017) - Music by Michael Giacchino and various artists:
Prepare to be transported to the colorful world of the Day of the Dead in Pixar’s “Coco.” With a celebration of Mexican culture and traditions, this film’s soundtrack is an absolute delight. Michael Giacchino’s enchanting score intertwines seamlessly with the film’s soul-stirring original songs, including the heartrending “Remember Me.” The soundtrack captures the essence of the story, making it a truly memorable experience.

4. “Tarzan” (1999) - Music by Phil Collins:
“Tarzan,” the Disney adaptation of Edgar Rice Burroughs’ classic tale, swings into our list with a groovy soundtrack by Phil Collins. The Grammy-winning artist lends his voice and musical talents to create a unique and catchy soundtrack. From the iconic “You’ll Be in My Heart” to the foot-tapping “Trashin’ the Camp,” Collins’ music brings an energetic and emotional dimension to the wild jungle setting.

5. “Anastasia” (1997) - Music by Lynn Ahrens and Stephen Flaherty:
Venturing into the world of non-Disney animation, we have the enchanting “Anastasia.” This musical fantasy, inspired by the Russian legend, boasts a beautiful score by Lynn Ahrens and Stephen Flaherty. The soundtrack, featuring powerful vocals and captivating melodies, transports us to a bygone era. Hits like “Journey to the Past” and “Once Upon a December” perfectly capture the film’s magical essence.

6. “The Prince of Egypt” (1998) - Music by Stephen Schwartz and Hans Zimmer:
Lastly, we journey to ancient Egypt with the biblical epic “The Prince of Egypt.” Stephen Schwartz’s awe-inspiring music and lyrics, combined with Hans Zimmer’s evocative score, create an epic soundscape. From the powerful “Deliver Us” to the show-stopping “When You Believe,” this soundtrack delivers an emotional and spiritual experience, leaving a lasting impact.

In conclusion, these animated movies with soundtracks by famous musicians have left an indelible mark on both the cinematic and musical worlds. Whether it’s the captivating melodies of “The Lion King” or the cultural richness of “Moana,” these films have elevated the art of storytelling through the power of music. So, next time you’re seeking an escape into an animated realm, don’t forget to tune into the harmonious world of these exceptional movies. Lights, camera, music!
