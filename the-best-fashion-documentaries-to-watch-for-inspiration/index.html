The Best Fashion Documentaries to Watch for Inspiration


Fashion documentaries are an excellent way to dive into the glamorous world of style, gain inspiration, and learn about the industry’s behind-the-scenes workings. These films offer a captivating blend of storytelling, aesthetics, and valuable insights from fashion icons, designers, models, and industry experts. If you are seeking some fashion-forward inspiration, look no further! Here are some of the best fashion documentaries that you should watch.

1. ‘The September Issue’ (2009): Directed by R.J. Cutler, this documentary takes you inside the prestigious world of Vogue magazine. Witness the creation of the famed September issue, famously known as the fashion bible, and observe the creative genius of Anna Wintour, the iconic editor-in-chief. This film provides a fascinating glimpse into the immense process behind curating fashion trends.

2. ‘Dior and I’ (2014): This documentary directed by Frédéric Tcheng showcases the journey of Raf Simons, a Belgian designer, during his debut season as the creative director of Christian Dior. Explore the challenges and triumphs of creating an entire collection within the span of just eight weeks. This film is a must-watch for those interested in the creative process and the pressure faced by renowned fashion houses.

3. ‘McQueen’ (2018): Directed by Ian Bonhôte and Peter Ettedgui, this documentary delves into the extraordinary life and career of British fashion designer Alexander McQueen. From his humble beginnings to becoming one of the most influential designers, this film explores McQueen’s unparalleled talent, dark inspirations, and unmatched creativity. Prepare to be captivated by his dramatic runway shows and innovative designs.

4. ‘The True Cost’ (2015): This insightful documentary directed by Andrew Morgan takes a closer look at the global fashion industry and its impact on the environment and human lives. Delve into the consequences of fast fashion, exploring issues like exploitation, sweatshops, and environmental degradation. This eye-opening film highlights the need for ethical and sustainable fashion practices.

5. ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ (2008): Directed by Matt Tyrnauer, this documentary follows the final two years of Valentino Garavani’s celebrated career as a fashion designer. Gain an intimate understanding of the legendary fashion house, its intricate designs, and the relationship between Valentino and his long-time business partner, Giancarlo Giammetti. Witness the grandeur of the fashion world through the lens of one of its icons.

6. ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ (2010): Directed by Richard Press, this documentary pays homage to the late Bill Cunningham, one of the most influential street-style photographers. Experience Cunningham’s passion for capturing everyday fashion on the streets of New York City and his genuine love for the art of self-expression through clothing. This film is a true celebration of individuality and the power of personal style.

Immerse yourself in the fascinating world of fashion by watching these thought-provoking and visually stunning documentaries. Each film offers a unique perspective on the industry, from the creative process to the societal and environmental impact of fashion. So snuggle up on the couch, grab some popcorn, and let these documentaries inspire you to push the boundaries of your own personal style. Happy watching!
