Forgotten Gems: Underrated Cartoons Worth Revisiting


In today’s fast-paced world, it’s easy for some amazing cartoons to slip through the cracks and be forgotten over time. While there are countless popular and well-known animated shows that have captured our hearts, there are also hidden gems that deserve a second look. These underrated cartoons may have been overshadowed by their more famous counterparts, but they are definitely worth revisiting. Join us as we dive into the world of forgotten gems – underrated cartoons that deserve more recognition!

1. ‘Gargoyles’:
‘Gargoyles’ is a dark and captivating animated series that originally aired in the 1990s. It follows a group of gargoyles who come to life at night to protect their city from various threats. With its complex characters, intricate storytelling, and mature themes, ‘Gargoyles’ was ahead of its time. Despite receiving critical acclaim, it never achieved the same level of popularity as other animated shows of its era. If you’re a fan of fantasy, action, and compelling storytelling, ‘Gargoyles’ is a must-watch.

2. ‘The Pirates of Dark Water’:
This swashbuckling adventure series aired in the early 1990s and showcased a unique blend of fantasy and science fiction. ‘The Pirates of Dark Water’ follows a young prince named Ren who embarks on a quest to save his world from a dangerous substance known as Dark Water. With its memorable characters, stunning animation, and epic storyline, this underrated gem deserves a special place in the hearts of animation enthusiasts.

3. ‘Samurai Jack’:
‘Samurai Jack’ is a critically acclaimed series that aired from 2001 to 2017. Created by Genndy Tartakovsky, the show tells the story of a samurai warrior who is thrown into the future by an evil sorcerer. With its distinctive art style, breathtaking action sequences, and deep philosophical undertones, ‘Samurai Jack’ offers a unique viewing experience. Despite its accolades, it often gets overlooked in discussions about classic animated shows. If you haven’t experienced the epic journey of Samurai Jack, now is the perfect time to dive in.

4. ‘The Legend of Tarzan’:
Based on the Disney movie, ‘The Legend of Tarzan’ is an animated series that aired from 2001 to 2003. It continues the story of Tarzan and his friends as they face new challenges in the African jungle. With its lush animation, strong character development, and engaging storylines, this underrated gem struck a chord with fans of the original film. If you’re a fan of adventure and exploration, ‘The Legend of Tarzan’ is a hidden treasure waiting to be discovered.

5. ‘Gravity Falls’:
‘Gravity Falls’ is a modern masterpiece that aired from 2012 to 2016. Created by Alex Hirsch, the show follows twins Dipper and Mabel as they spend their summer with their eccentric great-uncle in the mysterious town of Gravity Falls. With its clever writing, intricate mysteries, and lovable characters, ‘Gravity Falls’ has gained a dedicated fan base. However, it remains relatively unknown to the wider audience. If you’re in the mood for a thrilling and hilarious animated series, ‘Gravity Falls’ is an absolute must-watch.

These forgotten gems are just a taste of the countless underrated cartoons that deserve more recognition. Whether you’re nostalgic for the classics or looking for something new, these shows offer captivating storytelling, unforgettable characters, and unique animation styles. So, take a trip down memory lane or embark on a new adventure by revisiting these underrated cartoons. You may just discover a new favorite that has been waiting to be rediscovered!
