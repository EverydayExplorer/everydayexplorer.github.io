The Most Beautiful Hotel Pools around the World


When it comes to planning a vacation, there are few things as relaxing and beautiful as a hotel pool. Whether you choose to take a dip to cool off from the scorching heat or simply lounge poolside with a refreshing drink in hand, a beautiful hotel pool can truly elevate your travel experience. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most stunning hotel pools around the world that will leave you breathless.

1. The Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia:
Nestled in the heart of Bali’s lush rainforest, The Hanging Gardens is a truly magical oasis. Its iconic twin-tiered infinity pools seem to blend seamlessly with the surrounding jungle, offering breathtaking views of the Ayung River below. Surrounded by ancient trees and beautiful flowers, this pool is a paradise for those seeking tranquility and serenity.

2. The Amangiri, Utah, USA:
Located in the middle of the desert, the pool at Amangiri is a sight to behold. The sleek, modern design of the pool blends effortlessly with the rugged landscape, creating a unique and mesmerizing experience. Surrounded by towering canyon walls, guests can take a swim while admiring the stunning desert views and basking in the tranquility of this remote location.

3. Grand Hotel Tremezzo, Lake Como, Italy:
Situated on the shores of Lake Como, the Grand Hotel Tremezzo boasts a beautiful pool that seems to merge seamlessly with the glimmering lake waters. With stunning views of the surrounding mountains and charming Italian villas, this pool offers a perfect combination of natural beauty and elegance.

4. The Library, Koh Samui, Thailand:
The red pool at The Library is a striking and unique feature that sets it apart from other hotel pools. With its bright red tiles contrasting with the clear blue ocean beyond, this pool is a visual masterpiece. Situated right on Chaweng Beach, guests can enjoy stunning views of the Gulf of Thailand while taking a refreshing swim in this one-of-a-kind pool.

5. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:
The iconic rooftop infinity pool at Marina Bay Sands is a must-see for any pool enthusiast. Located 57 stories above ground, this pool offers an unprecedented view of Singapore’s stunning skyline. Floating in the crystal-clear water, guests can take in the breathtaking panorama of the city while feeling like they are on top of the world.

6. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, India:
The pool at The Oberoi Udaivilas is truly fit for royalty. Featuring elaborate fountains, intricate mosaics, and stunning views of Lake Pichola, this pool exudes opulence and grandeur. Surrounded by lush gardens and palatial architecture, guests can experience the luxury and serenity of Udaipur while lounging by this magnificent pool.

7. The Cambrian, Adelboden, Switzerland:
Nestled among the Swiss Alps, the pool at The Cambrian offers a picturesque and peaceful escape. With snow-capped peaks and verdant valleys in the background, guests can unwind in the warm waters while taking in the breathtaking natural beauty of the surrounding landscape. This pool truly showcases the enchanting charm of the Swiss countryside.

8. The Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti, Tanzania:
Imagine taking a dip in a pool overlooking the vast plains of the Serengeti, with wildlife roaming freely in the distance. The Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti allows guests to experience the thrill of an African safari while providing a luxurious and comfortable retreat. The infinity pool blends seamlessly with the African landscape, creating a sense of harmony and tranquility.

In conclusion, these are just a few examples of the most beautiful hotel pools around the world. From the tropical jungles of Bali to the rugged deserts of Utah, each pool offers its own unique charm and mesmerizing views. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, adventure, or simply a stunning backdrop for your vacation photos, these pools are sure to leave you in awe. So, next time you’re planning your trip, consider adding one of these breathtaking hotel pools to your itinerary for an unforgettable experience.
