Epic Road Trips: Unforgettable Journeys on the Open Road


Epic road trips are perfect for exploring the world with a sense of adventure. They allow you to see the natural beauty and hidden gems of various places while taking in everything at your own pace. There is no better feeling than hitting the open road and setting out for a memorable journey with your friends or family. When it comes to road trips, destinations only come second. It’s the journey that adds charm to every mile. Over the years, some of the most exhilarating and unforgettable journeys have been taken on the open road.

Let’s take a look at some of the most incredible epic road trips that you can take to explore the world.

1. Pacific Coast Highway- California, USA: This road trip in California is an epic journey in itself, with intricate and winding roads that edge along the coastal cliffs overlooking the Pacific Ocean. The drive covers the best of the California coastline, including the famous Big Sur and picturesque Central Coast towns. The magnificent view of the ocean, charming towns, and vibrant culture makes it one of the most popular road trips in the world.

2. Route 66: One of the most iconic road trips in the world, Route 66, is perfect for history buffs and those who want to explore the American Southwest. This epic journey starts from Chicago and ends in Santa Monica, taking you across heartland America. With many quirky roadside attractions such as the Cadillac Ranch, turkey legs, historic gas stations, and motels, a trip on Route 66 is one for the books!

3. The Ring Road- Iceland: The Ring Road of Iceland is a classic road trip that showcases some of the most beautiful landscapes on earth. This epic journey takes you through ice-capped mountains, geothermal pools, waterfalls, and black sand beaches. The journey starts near the capital city of Reykjavik and ends in the town of Hofn, which is famous for its glaciers.

4. The Great Ocean Road- Australia: The Great Ocean Road is an epic journey that takes you across 243 km of Australia’s southern coastline. This road trip offers a scenic drive along the cliffs which makes it one of the most picturesque drives in the world. You can enjoy the rugged coastline, emerald rainforests, and sandy beaches. The Great Ocean Road is also home to the world-famous 12 Apostles which are a must-see while driving along this scenic route.

5. The North Coast 500- Scotland: The North Coast 500 is the ultimate road trip for those who love history, culture, and stunning natural landscapes. This epic journey takes you through the Scottish Highlands with its rugged and wild beauty. The route starts and ends at Inverness and takes you across the Scottish coastline while exploring castles, forts, and ancient ruins, making it one of the most exciting drives in Scotland.

6. Garden Route- South Africa: The Garden Route in South Africa offers a unique and wide-ranging perspective on the country’s natural wonder. This epic journey covers a distance of around 300 kilometers, which starts in Cape Town and ends in Port Elizabeth. The route passes through some of the most picturesque coastal towns with long stretches of beach, stunning views of the mountains, rainforests, and wildlife.

Final thoughts

Epic road trips provide a journey that is enriching and unforgettable. The ever-changing landscapes, sights and sounds make a road trip like no other. Whether it’s taking an adventurous trip on the Great Ocean Road or exploring the Scottish Highlands on North Coast 500, epic road trips make for an experience of a lifetime. So, pack your bags, grab a map, and hit the road to create unforgettable memories. Wishing you an enjoyable and safe journey!
