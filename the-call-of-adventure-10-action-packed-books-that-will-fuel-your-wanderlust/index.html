The Call of Adventure: 10 Action-Packed Books That Will Fuel Your Wanderlust


The human spirit has always been one of curiosity and exploration, and nothing fuels that explorer spirit like a good adventure story. These stories transport us to different worlds and cultures while inspiring us to step outside our comfort zones and experience life to the fullest. Whether it’s through the eyes of an unlikely hero or a daring adventurer, these stories offer us a glimpse of what it means to be alive, to take risks, and to ultimately discover something about ourselves.

Here are ten action-packed books that will fuel your wanderlust and leave you ready to embrace your next great adventure:

1. The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho - This classic novel follows the journey of a shepherd boy named Santiago as he travels from Spain to Egypt in search of treasure. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about following his dreams and discovering his true destiny.

2. Into the Wild by Jon Krakauer - Based on a true story, this book chronicles the journey of a young man named Christopher McCandless as he leaves his comfortable life behind to explore the wilderness of Alaska. This gripping tale offers a cautionary tale about the risks and rewards of adventure.

3. Shantaram by Gregory David Roberts - Set in the bustling city of Mumbai, this epic novel follows the journey of an Australian fugitive who finds a new home in India. Filled with colorful characters and stunning descriptions of the city, this book offers a unique perspective on one of the world’s most vibrant cultures.

4. The Beach by Alex Garland - This fast-paced thriller follows the journey of a naïve backpacker who stumbles upon a secret island community in Thailand. As he becomes increasingly drawn into their world, he discovers that paradise comes at a high price.

5. The Lost City of Z by David Grann - This non-fiction book tells the true story of British explorer Percy Fawcett, who disappeared in the Amazon jungle while searching for a lost city. Filled with suspense and adventure, this book offers a glimpse into the dangers and wonders of discovery.

6. Wild by Cheryl Strayed - This memoir follows the author’s journey along the Pacific Crest Trail after a series of personal tragedies. With stunning descriptions of the wilderness and a powerful message about personal growth, this book is a must-read for anyone who is looking to overcome adversity and find their true calling.

7. The Count of Monte Cristo by Alexandre Dumas - This classic novel tells the story of a young man who escapes from prison after being falsely accused of a crime. As he seeks revenge against those who wronged him, he travels across Europe and North Africa, encountering adventure at every turn.

8. The Wind-Up Bird Chronicle by Haruki Murakami - This surreal novel follows the journey of a man who embarks on a quest to find his missing wife. As he explores the seedy underbelly of Tokyo and confronts his own personal demons, he discovers that reality is much stranger than he could have ever imagined.

9. State of Wonder by Ann Patchett - This novel tells the story of a medical researcher who travels to the Amazon jungle to investigate a new drug that could change the world. Filled with twists and turns, this thrilling book offers a vivid depiction of life in one of the world’s most exotic locales.

10. The Long Way Round by Ewan McGregor and Charley Boorman - This travelogue follows the journey of two actors as they ride motorcycles around the world. Filled with stunning photography and tales of adventure, this book is a must-read for anyone who dreams of seeing the world on two wheels.

In conclusion, these ten action-packed books offer a glimpse into the world of adventure, inspiring us to get out of our comfort zones and explore the world around us. Whether it’s through the eyes of a daring explorer or a determined hero, these stories remind us of the importance of taking risks, following our dreams, and discovering the unknown. So, set aside some time, grab a good book, and let your wanderlust take flight!
