How to Master the Winged Eyeliner Look


If you’re a makeup enthusiast, you’ve probably seen countless beauty tutorials showcasing the iconic winged eyeliner look. This stunning and versatile makeup technique has become a staple in the beauty industry, and for a good reason - it adds a touch of elegance and drama to any makeup look. However, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner can be quite challenging, especially for beginners. But fear not, because in this blog post, we will share some valuable tips and tricks to help you master the art of winged eyeliner application like a pro!

1. Gather Your Tools

Before diving into the world of winged eyeliner, it’s crucial to have the right tools on hand. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Eyeliner: Choose a formula that you’re comfortable working with. Gel, liquid, and pen eyeliners are popular options.
  • Brushes: Invest in a good eyeliner brush or use the one that comes with your gel eyeliner.
  • Q-tips or cotton buds: These will come in handy for correcting mistakes and achieving clean, sharp lines.

2. Prep and Prime

To ensure a flawless winged eyeliner look, always start with a clean and moisturized eyelid. Use an eyeshadow primer to create a smooth canvas for your eyeliner application. This step not only helps your eyeliner to glide on effortlessly but also enhances its longevity.

3. Find Your Shape

The key to mastering the winged eyeliner look lies in finding the perfect shape for your eyes. Various factors, such as eye shape, eye size, and personal preference, play a role in determining which winged eyeliner style suits you best. Experiment with different shapes and thicknesses to see what enhances your eye shape and flatters your features.

4. Map It Out

Before laying down your liner, it’s helpful to create a roadmap for your winged eyeliner shape. Start by drawing a thin line along your lash line using small, short strokes. Then, imagine a straight line extending from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. This line will guide you in creating the wing.

5. Wing It

Once you have your map, it’s time to create the winged effect. Start from the outer corner of your eye and draw a line towards the end of your imagined guideline. The length of the wing is up to you, but remember, a shorter wing tends to look more subtle and natural, while a longer wing adds drama and intensity.

6. Connect the Dots

After creating the wing, connect it to your upper lash line by drawing a thin line. Start from the inner corner and work your way towards the wing. Take your time and ensure that the line is smooth and continuous, without any gaps or jagged edges.

7. Clean Up Any Mistakes

Don’t worry if your wing is not perfect on the first try. It takes practice! Use a Q-tip or a small, angled brush dipped in makeup remover to clean up any errors or smudges. Take your time, and remember that precision is key when it comes to achieving a flawless winged eyeliner look.

8. Set with Eyeshadow

To ensure that your winged eyeliner stays in place all day, set it with a matching eyeshadow. Gently press a thin layer of matte black or a color that complements your eyeliner onto the line you created. This step not only helps to intensify the color but also prevents smudging and fading.

9. Experiment and Have Fun

Remember, makeup is all about creativity, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors. The winged eyeliner look is incredibly versatile and can be tailored to suit various makeup looks and occasions. Whether you prefer a classic cat-eye or a bold, graphic wing, let your imagination run wild and have fun with your makeup!

Conclusion

Mastering the winged eyeliner look may seem daunting at first, but with practice and the right techniques, you can achieve flawless results. Remember to be patient, experiment with different shapes, and most importantly, have fun along the way. So, grab your eyeliner and brushes, follow these tips, and get ready to rock that dramatic winged eyeliner look like a pro!
