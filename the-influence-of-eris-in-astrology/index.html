The Influence of Eris in Astrology


Eris, often referred to as the ‘Goddess of Discord,’ is a celestial body located in the outermost regions of our solar system. Discovered in 2005, Eris has since captured the attention and curiosity of astrologers and enthusiasts alike. While it is not technically considered a planet, its influence in astrology cannot be ignored. In this blog post, we will explore the significance of Eris in astrology and how it may affect our lives and the cosmic energies around us.

Astrology is the ancient practice of studying celestial bodies and their impact on human life and behavior. Traditionally, our understanding of astrology has been driven by the influence of the planets in our solar system. However, with the discovery of Eris, astrologers began to explore the potential impact of this relatively new celestial body.

Eris is often associated with chaos, strife, and discord. In mythology, Eris is known for triggering the Trojan War by causing a conflict among the gods and goddesses. This association with discord has led to the belief that Eris may influence turbulent and transformative events in our lives. It acts as a catalyst for personal growth, forcing us to confront our inner conflicts and make necessary changes in our lives.

One of the key aspects of Eris’ influence is its ability to bring hidden truths to the surface. Just as Eris revealed the true nature of the gods in mythology, it has the potential to expose the hidden aspects of ourselves and the world around us. This can be a challenging process, as it often requires confronting our fears and facing uncomfortable truths. However, it ultimately leads to personal growth and empowerment.

Eris is also associated with the concept of rebellion and standing up against injustices. It encourages us to challenge existing structures and question societal norms. This can be seen in the rise of social movements and activists fighting for equality and justice. Eris’ presence in our astrology charts may indicate a need to break free from conformity and embrace our individuality.

In addition to its influence on personal growth and societal change, Eris also plays a role in our relationships and interactions with others. It can symbolize the conflicts and power struggles that we encounter in our intimate relationships or partnerships. Eris challenges us to assert our boundaries and stand up for what we believe in, even if it means facing conflict and opposition.

While Eris does not have a direct impact on our daily lives like the planets in our solar system, its influence can be felt on a deeper, more transformative level. Its presence in our astrology charts can indicate periods of change, upheaval, and growth. By embracing the lessons and challenges that Eris presents, we can navigate these transformative energies with awareness and grace.

In conclusion, Eris is a celestial body that holds significant influence in astrology, despite not being classified as a planet. Its association with discord, rebellion, and personal growth makes it a powerful force to be reckoned with. By understanding and embracing the lessons and challenges that Eris brings, we can harness its transformative energies and embark on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Whether it’s facing our fears, questioning societal norms, or asserting our boundaries, Eris pushes us to grow, evolve, and make positive changes in our lives.
