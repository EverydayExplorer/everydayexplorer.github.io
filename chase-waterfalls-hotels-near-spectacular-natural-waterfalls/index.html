Chase Waterfalls: Hotels near Spectacular Natural Waterfalls


Welcome, adventure seekers! If you’re a fan of breathtaking natural wonders, nothing compares to the mesmerizing beauty of waterfalls. Whether you’re a nature lover, a photographer, or simply seeking a serene escape, exploring waterfalls is an experience that will leave you in awe. In this blog post, we will take you on a journey through some of the most stunning waterfalls around the world and recommend hotels that offer the perfect blend of comfort and proximity to these magnificent wonders.

1. Niagara Falls, USA/Canada:
Let’s start with the granddaddy of all waterfalls - Niagara Falls. Located on the border between the United States and Canada, this iconic waterfall is a must-visit for any traveler. To make the most of your visit, consider staying at the luxurious Niagara Falls Marriott Fallsview Hotel & Spa. This hotel not only offers stunning views of the falls but also provides easy access to popular attractions like the Journey Behind the Falls and Niagara Falls State Park.

2. Iguazu Falls, Argentina/Brazil:
Prepare to be spellbound by the awe-inspiring Iguazu Falls, located on the border between Argentina and Brazil. To fully immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this UNESCO World Heritage Site, we recommend the Belmond Hotel das Cataratas, the only hotel situated within the Iguazu National Park. Wake up to the sound of cascading waterfalls and enjoy exclusive access to the falls before the park opens to the public.

3. Plitvice Lakes National Park, Croatia:
For a unique waterfall experience, head to Plitvice Lakes National Park in Croatia. This lush paradise is home to a series of interconnected lakes and waterfalls, creating a picturesque setting like no other. Stay at the Hotel Jezero, located just minutes away from the park entrance. This cozy hotel offers comfortable accommodations and allows you to start your exploration of the park bright and early.

4. Angel Falls, Venezuela:
Get ready to witness the world’s highest uninterrupted waterfall - Angel Falls in Venezuela. To reach this remote natural wonder, you’ll need to embark on an adventurous journey. The closest accommodation option is the campsite offered by Canaima National Park, which provides basic yet comfortable tents and facilities. Rest assured, the surreal experience of gazing upon the majestic Angel Falls will make it all worthwhile.

5. Yosemite Falls, USA:
Nestled within the stunning Yosemite National Park in California, Yosemite Falls is a must-see for waterfall enthusiasts. For an unforgettable stay, book a room at the Majestic Yosemite Hotel. Located in the heart of the park, this elegant hotel offers stunning views of Yosemite Falls and provides easy access to hiking trails, scenic viewpoints, and other attractions.

6. Victoria Falls, Zambia/Zimbabwe:
Prepare to be awestruck by the sheer power and beauty of Victoria Falls, known as the ‘Smoke that Thunders.’ For a truly luxurious experience, stay at The Royal Livingstone Hotel, situated on the banks of the Zambezi River. This colonial-style hotel offers exquisite accommodations, impeccable service, and breathtaking views of the falls just a short walk away.

When planning your waterfall adventure, remember to check the weather conditions and pack accordingly. Additionally, always prioritize safety and follow any guidelines or rules implemented by the local authorities.

Embark on a journey to explore these spectacular natural wonders, and let the beauty of waterfalls ignite your sense of wonder and adventure. Remember to capture memories, but also take moments to simply be present and allow the mesmerizing power of these falls to captivate your soul.

So, what are you waiting for? Start planning your waterfall getaway today and let the magic of these natural wonders leave you spellbound!
