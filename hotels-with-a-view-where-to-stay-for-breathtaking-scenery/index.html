Hotels with a View: Where to Stay for Breathtaking Scenery


When it comes to planning a vacation, one of the most important factors to consider is where you’ll be staying. While amenities, service, and location all play a role, nothing can compare to waking up to a breathtaking view right outside your hotel room. Whether you prefer towering mountains, sparkling ocean waves, or sprawling city skylines, there are hotels all over the world that offer unforgettable scenery. In this blog post, we will explore some of these incredible hotels and why they should be on your bucket list.

1. Amangiri - Canyon Point, Utah, USA

Nestled in the heart of the vast Utah desert, Amangiri stands out as a true oasis. This luxury resort is surrounded by stunning red rock formations and offers unparalleled views of the desert landscape. Guests can enjoy breathtaking sunsets over the canyons, go hiking in the nearby national parks, or simply relax by the pool while taking in the mesmerizing scenery. For nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers, Amangiri is a must-visit destination.

2. Jade Mountain - St. Lucia, Caribbean

Imagine waking up to panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea and the iconic Pitons. At Jade Mountain in St. Lucia, this dream becomes a reality. This unique and luxurious resort is famous for its open-air suites, each with its own private infinity pool overlooking the sea. With no fourth wall, guests are completely immersed in the breathtaking surroundings. Whether enjoying a romantic dinner on your private terrace or taking a dip in your pool, the views at Jade Mountain are simply enchanting.

3. Shangri-La Hotel - Paris, France

For those seeking a cityscape view that will leave them in awe, the Shangri-La Hotel in Paris is the perfect choice. Located just across the Seine from the Eiffel Tower, this elegant hotel offers rooms and suites with magnificent views of the iconic landmark. Wake up to the sight of the Eiffel Tower sparkling in the morning light, or enjoy a romantic evening with the tower lit up in all its glory. With its prime location and breathtaking views, the Shangri-La Hotel is a true gem in the heart of Paris.

4. Hotel Escondido - Puerto Escondido, Mexico

If you’re a beach lover, look no further than Hotel Escondido in Puerto Escondido, Mexico. This intimate boutique hotel offers stunning ocean views and direct access to the pristine beaches of Oaxaca. Each of the hotel’s private bungalows comes with its own terrace and hammock, perfect for relaxing and enjoying the breathtaking scenery. Whether you’re catching a wave or simply unwinding with a margarita in hand, the views at Hotel Escondido will take your breath away.

5. Four Seasons Safari Lodge - Serengeti, Tanzania

For a truly unforgettable wildlife experience, the Four Seasons Safari Lodge in the Serengeti is the ultimate destination. With its prime location in the heart of the African plains, this luxury lodge offers guests the opportunity to witness the Great Migration and spot the ‘Big Five’ from their stylish suites. Imagine waking up to the sight of elephants roaming the savannah or enjoying a sunset drink on your private balcony overlooking the vast African landscape. The Four Seasons Safari Lodge is a paradise for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts alike.

6. Marina Bay Sands - Singapore

When it comes to city skylines, few can rival the jaw-dropping views of Singapore’s Marina Bay Sands. This iconic hotel features a sky park that spans across its three towers, offering guests an unparalleled view of the city and its stunning skyline. Whether you’re lounging by the rooftop infinity pool or sipping a cocktail at the rooftop bar, the views from Marina Bay Sands are nothing short of breathtaking. If you’re looking for a modern and cosmopolitan experience with a view to match, this hotel is a must-visit.

In conclusion, staying at a hotel with a view can elevate your vacation experience to a whole new level. Whether it’s mountains, oceans, cities, or wildlife, there are hotels all over the world that offer unforgettable views. From the Amangiri in Utah to Marina Bay Sands in Singapore, each of these hotels provides a unique and awe-inspiring setting. So, the next time you’re planning a trip, consider staying at one of these extraordinary hotels and prepare to be wowed by the breathtaking scenery.
