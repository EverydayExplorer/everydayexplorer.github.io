The Best Fashion Documentaries on Netflix


Fashion has always been a captivating industry, with its ever-changing trends and glamorous lifestyle. From the runways of Paris to the streets of New York, fashion has the power to inspire and transform. If you’re a fashion enthusiast looking for some insightful and entertaining content, you’re in luck! Netflix offers a wide range of fashion documentaries that take you behind the scenes of the fashion world, showcasing the creativity, hard work, and dedication that goes into creating iconic designs. In this blog post, we’ll be exploring the best fashion documentaries available on Netflix right now.

1. ‘The True Cost’ - This eye-opening documentary dives deep into the dark side of the fashion industry, shedding light on the environmental and social impacts of fast fashion. Directed by Andrew Morgan, the film takes you on a journey around the world, showcasing the exploitation of workers in developing countries and the detrimental effects of mass production. It’s a must-watch for anyone interested in ethical and sustainable fashion.

2. ‘Dior and I’ - Step into the world of haute couture with this captivating documentary that follows designer Raf Simons as he takes over the reins at the famous French fashion house, Christian Dior. Directed by Frederic Tcheng, the film provides an intimate look into the process of creating a fashion collection, from the initial sketches to the final runway show. It’s a mesmerizing portrayal of the artistic vision and craftsmanship that goes into high-end fashion.

3. ‘The First Monday in May’ - Directed by Andrew Rossi, this documentary takes you behind the scenes of the annual Met Gala, one of the most prestigious events in the fashion calendar. Explore the collaboration between fashion and art as you witness the planning and execution of the groundbreaking exhibition ‘China: Through the Looking Glass.’ This film offers a fascinating glimpse into the intersection of fashion, celebrity, and culture.

4. ‘Jeremy Scott: The People’s Designer’ - This documentary follows the rise of fashion designer Jeremy Scott, known for his bold and eccentric designs. Directed by Vlad Yudin, the film explores Scott’s journey from a small-town kid with big dreams to becoming the creative director of the iconic fashion brand Moschino. It’s an inspiring tale of perseverance and creativity that will leave you feeling motivated and inspired.

5. ‘McQueen’ - Dive into the world of the legendary British fashion designer, Alexander McQueen, with this mesmerizing documentary. Directed by Ian Bonhôte and Peter Ettedgui, the film offers a deeply personal and intimate look at McQueen’s life, career, and creative process. From his early days in London to his iconic runway shows, this documentary showcases the raw talent and innovative spirit of one of fashion’s greatest visionaries.

6. ‘The September Issue’ - Directed by R.J. Cutler, this documentary takes you behind the scenes of Vogue magazine’s most important issue of the year - the September issue. Follow the influential editor-in-chief, Anna Wintour, and her team as they navigate the challenges and pressures of producing a magazine that sets the tone for the entire fashion industry. It’s a fascinating portrayal of the power and influence of fashion media.

7. ‘Iris’ - Directed by Albert Maysles, this documentary celebrates the life and style of fashion icon Iris Apfel. Known for her bold and eclectic fashion choices, Apfel has become an inspiration to people of all ages. The film offers a glimpse into her vibrant world, showcasing her unique sense of style and her passion for fashion. It’s a feel-good documentary that reminds us to embrace our own individuality and express ourselves through fashion.

These are just a few of the incredible fashion documentaries available on Netflix. Whether you’re interested in the environmental impact of the fashion industry, the creative process of designers, or the glamorous world of high fashion events, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your popcorn, sit back, and prepare to be inspired by the colorful and captivating world of fashion.
