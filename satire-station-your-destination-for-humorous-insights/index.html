What is Satire?

Satire is a powerful form of humor that uses irony, sarcasm, and wit to critique and expose the flaws and absurdities of individuals, institutions, and society as a whole. It serves as a mirror that reflects the truth in a way that is both entertaining and thought-provoking. Satire has been used throughout history as a tool for social commentary, challenging the status quo and sparking conversations that can lead to positive change.
