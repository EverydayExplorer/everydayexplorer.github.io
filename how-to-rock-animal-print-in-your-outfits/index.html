How to Rock Animal Print in Your Outfits


Animal print has been a fashion staple for decades, often associated with boldness and confidence. From leopard to zebra, animal prints can add a touch of fierceness to any outfit. However, it can be tricky to style animal prints without going overboard or looking tacky. In this blog post, we will explore some tips and tricks on how to properly rock animal print in your outfits.

1. Start with a statement piece: Animal prints are quite bold on their own, so it’s best to start with one statement piece and build your outfit around it. For example, a leopard print skirt paired with a solid-colored top and neutral accessories can create a chic and balanced look.

2. Mix with solid colors: Animal prints are attention-grabbing, so it’s important to balance them out with solid colors. Pairing a leopard print blouse with black pants or a zebra print scarf with a white dress can create a visually appealing contrast.

3. Opt for neutral tones: Animal prints often come in neutral tones like brown, black, and white. These colors are versatile and can be easily paired with other pieces in your wardrobe. For instance, a black and white snakeskin print dress can be accessorized with colorful shoes or statement jewelry.

4. Don’t overdo it: Animal prints are best when used in moderation. Avoid wearing head-to-toe animal print or combining multiple animal prints in one outfit. Instead, choose one or two pieces and let them be the focal point of your look.

5. Consider scale and size: Different animal prints come in various sizes and scales. Larger prints tend to make a bolder statement, while smaller prints can be more subtle and easier to incorporate into your outfit. Choose prints that complement your body shape and personal style.

6. Experiment with textures: Animal prints can be found on various fabrics, such as silk, velvet, or faux fur. Experimenting with different textures can add depth and visual interest to your outfit. For example, a leopard print jacket made of faux fur can elevate a plain outfit instantly.

7. Use accessories: If you’re unsure about wearing animal print clothing, try incorporating it through accessories. A leopard print scarf, a zebra print belt, or snakeskin print shoes can instantly add a trendy element to your outfit without overpowering it.

8. Mix animal prints with other prints: If you’re feeling bold and adventurous, you can try mixing animal prints with other prints. However, this can be tricky, so make sure to choose prints that have a common color or a similar pattern size. For example, pairing a leopard print blouse with a polka dot skirt can create a unique and playful ensemble.

9. Consider the occasion: Animal prints can be appropriate for various occasions, but it’s essential to dress appropriately. For formal events, opt for more subtle animal print touches, like a snakeskin clutch or a leopard print shoe. For casual outings or parties, feel free to go bolder with your animal print choices.

10. Confidence is key: Regardless of what you choose to wear, the most important thing is to wear it with confidence. Animal prints are meant to make a statement, and if you feel great in them, it will show in your outfit.

In conclusion, animal print can be a fun and stylish addition to your wardrobe if worn with care and confidence. By following these tips and tricks, you can rock animal print in your outfits without looking overpowering or tacky. Remember to start with one statement piece, mix with solid colors, and choose prints that suit your style and body shape. Whether it’s a subtle touch or a bold ensemble, have fun experimenting with animal print and let your inner wild side shine.
