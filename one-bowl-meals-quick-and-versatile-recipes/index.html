One-Bowl Meals: Quick and Versatile Recipes


One-bowl meals have been gaining popularity in recent years due to their convenience and versatility. These meals are typically made up of a base, protein, and vegetables, all in one bowl, making meal prep and cleanup a breeze. In this blog, we will introduce you to some of our favorite one-bowl meals that are quick and easy to prepare.

1. Buddha Bowl

The Buddha Bowl is a vegan one-bowl meal that usually consists of a grain base, such as quinoa or brown rice, topped with a variety of vegetables. You can also add protein, such as tofu, edamame, or chickpeas, and a flavorful dressing.

One of our favorite Buddha Bowl recipes is a Mexican-inspired bowl using brown rice, black beans, avocado, lettuce, tomatoes, and cilantro. For the dressing, we recommend a mix of lime juice, avocado oil, and chili powder.

2. Poke Bowl

The poke bowl originated in Hawaii and consists of rice, raw fish, and vegetables. You can customize your poke bowl by choosing from a variety of fish options, such as tuna or salmon, and adding toppings like avocado, cucumber, and edamame.

A popular poke bowl recipe is a spicy tuna bowl using chopped tuna, sriracha mayo, green onions, and sesame seeds. For the rice base, we recommend using sushi rice and adding rice vinegar for extra flavor.

3. Grain Bowl

The grain bowl typically consists of a grain base, such as quinoa or farro, topped with roasted vegetables and protein. You can also add a flavorful sauce, such as a tahini or peanut sauce, to add richness to the dish.

One of our favorite grain bowl recipes is a Mediterranean bowl using farro, roasted sweet potato, feta cheese, and chickpeas. For the sauce, we recommend mixing tahini, lemon juice, and garlic.

4. Ramen Bowl

The ramen bowl is a comforting one-bowl meal that typically consists of broth, noodles, protein, and vegetables. You can customize your ramen bowl by choosing from a variety of broths, such as miso or chicken, and adding toppings like soft-boiled eggs and sliced pork.

One of our favorite ramen bowl recipes is a spicy miso ramen using chicken broth, miso paste, dried chili flakes, and ramen noodles. For the protein, we recommend using sliced chicken breast or tofu.

5. Burrito Bowl

The burrito bowl is a Mexican-inspired one-bowl meal that typically consists of rice, beans, protein, and vegetables. You can customize your burrito bowl by choosing from a variety of protein options, such as grilled chicken or steak, and adding toppings like salsa and guacamole.

One of our favorite burrito bowl recipes is a chipotle chicken bowl using brown rice, black beans, grilled chicken, and sautéed peppers and onions. For the seasoning, we recommend using a mix of chipotle powder, cumin, and garlic powder.

In conclusion, one-bowl meals are a convenient and healthy option for busy individuals. With a variety of base options, protein choices, and flavorful toppings, you can customize your one-bowl meal to your liking. Try one of our favorite recipes tonight and enjoy a delicious and easy meal.
