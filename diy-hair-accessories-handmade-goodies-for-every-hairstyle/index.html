DIY Hair Accessories: Handmade Goodies for Every Hairstyle


Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and looking for a way to add a touch of uniqueness to your look? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the wonderful world of DIY hair accessories that you can create at home. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned DIY enthusiast, there’s something for everyone in this collection of handmade goodies. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Floral Hairpins

Flowers are a timeless and beautiful addition to any hairstyle. To create your own floral hairpins, start by gathering some fresh or artificial flowers, preferably small ones. Carefully remove the stems, leaving just the flower heads. Next, attach a bobby pin or hairpin to the back of each flower using a hot glue gun. Once the glue has dried, you’re ready to adorn your hair with these lovely floral accessories.

2. Ribbon Headbands

Headbands are not only practical but also stylish. With just a few materials, you can make your own unique ribbon headband. Start by selecting a wide ribbon of your choice and measure it to fit comfortably around your head. Cut the ribbon to the desired length and sew the ends together. Finally, attach a small piece of elastic to the back of the headband to ensure a snug fit. Voila! You now have a trendy and personalized headband to complement any hairstyle.

3. Beaded Hair Combs

If you’re feeling a bit more adventurous, why not try making your own beaded hair combs? Start by selecting a plain hair comb and some beads in various colors and sizes. Thread the beads onto thin wire or nylon thread, and then wrap the wire/thread around the teeth of the comb. Secure the ends of the wire/thread, and you’re all set! These beaded hair combs are perfect for adding a touch of sparkle and glamour to your updo.

4. Scrunchies

Scrunchies are making a comeback, and what better way to stay on-trend than by making your own? To create a scrunchie, you’ll need some fabric, elastic, and a sewing machine. Cut a strip of fabric to your desired length and width, then fold it in half lengthwise with the right sides facing each other. Sew along the long edge to create a tube, leaving a small opening. Turn the tube inside out, insert the elastic through the tube, and sew the ends of the elastic together. Finally, sew the small opening closed, and you have your very own handmade scrunchie.

5. Feather Hair Clips

For a bohemian-inspired look, feather hair clips are the way to go. Start by selecting some feathers of different shapes and sizes. Trim the feathers to your desired length and attach them to a small hair clip using hot glue. You can also add beads or other embellishments to enhance the overall look. These feather hair clips are perfect for adding a touch of whimsy and elegance to your hairstyle.

Conclusion

DIY hair accessories are a fantastic way to express your creativity and add a personal touch to your hairstyles. The possibilities are endless, and with a little bit of time and effort, you can create unique and beautiful accessories that are sure to turn heads. So, why not give it a try? Let your imagination run wild and start crafting your own handmade goodies for every hairstyle. Happy DIY-ing!
