The Ultimate Guide to Crafting with Mason Jars: 20 DIY Ideas


Mason jars have become a trendy and versatile item for crafting enthusiasts. These glass jars, originally used for canning, have taken on a new life as a canvas for creative projects. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or just starting out, this ultimate guide will provide you with 20 DIY ideas to transform your mason jars into unique and beautiful pieces.

1. Mason Jar Lanterns

Create a cozy ambiance in your outdoor space by turning mason jars into lanterns. Fill the jars with small LED lights or candles, and hang them from tree branches or place them on tables. The soft glow will add a magical touch to your evenings.

2. Mason Jar Vases

Bring a touch of nature indoors by using mason jars as vases. Fill them with fresh flowers or plants to create stunning centerpieces for your dining table or add a pop of color to any room.

3. Mason Jar Herb Garden

If you have a green thumb, transform your mason jars into a mini herb garden. Plant different herbs in each jar, and place them on your kitchen windowsill for easy access while cooking. Not only will it add freshness to your meals, but it will also add a natural element to your kitchen décor.

4. Mason Jar Soap Dispenser

Give your bathroom a rustic touch by repurposing a mason jar into a soap dispenser. Simply attach a pump dispenser to the jar lid, fill it with your favorite liquid soap, and enjoy a unique and stylish addition to your sink area.

5. Mason Jar Candle Holders

Create a warm and inviting atmosphere by turning mason jars into candle holders. Decorate the jars with ribbons or twine, and place tealight candles inside. These homemade candle holders will add a cozy charm to any occasion.

6. Mason Jar Terrariums

Bring a piece of nature indoors by creating your own mini ecosystem in a mason jar terrarium. Layer the jar with rocks, soil, and small plants to create a low-maintenance and visually appealing piece of greenery for your home or office.

7. Mason Jar Snow Globes

Capture the magic of winter by making mason jar snow globes. Fill the jars with water, glitter, and small figurines like trees or animals, then seal the lid tightly. Shake the jar gently, and watch as the snowflakes dance around the scene.

8. Mason Jar Sewing Kit

Keep your sewing supplies organized and easily accessible by creating a mason jar sewing kit. Fill the jar with thread, needles, scissors, and other small sewing essentials. Decorate the lid with a pincushion for added convenience.

9. Mason Jar Photo Frames

Preserve memories in a unique way by turning mason jars into photo frames. Cut out small photos to fit the size of the jar, and place them inside. You can also add small decorative elements, like beads or ribbons, to enhance the visual appeal.

10. Mason Jar Desk Organizer

Keep your workspace neat and tidy by repurposing mason jars into a desk organizer. Use the jars to store pens, pencils, markers, and other office supplies. Arrange them in a row or stack them vertically for a stylish and functional addition to your desk.

11. Mason Jar Soap Dispenser

Give your bathroom a rustic touch by repurposing a mason jar into a soap dispenser. Simply attach a pump dispenser to the jar lid, fill it with your favorite liquid soap, and enjoy a unique and stylish addition to your sink area.

12. Mason Jar Bird Feeder

Invite feathered friends into your backyard by creating a mason jar bird feeder. Attach a small tray or plate to the jar lid, fill it with birdseed, and hang it from a tree branch. Enjoy the beauty of nature as you watch colorful birds visit your yard.

13. Mason Jar Oil Lamps

Add a touch of vintage charm to your outdoor space with mason jar oil lamps. Fill the jars with lamp oil, insert a wick, and light them for a soft and romantic glow. Perfect for summer evenings or special occasions.

14. Mason Jar Gift Ideas

Create personalized gifts by filling mason jars with various items. From homemade cookies to bath salts or small trinkets, the possibilities are endless. Decorate the jar with ribbons or labels to make it extra special.

15. Mason Jar Wall Organizer

Maximize your wall space by transforming mason jars into a practical wall organizer. Attach the jars to a wooden board or directly to the wall, and use them to store small items like keys, pens, or makeup brushes. This functional and stylish storage solution will declutter your space.

16. Mason Jar Chandelier

Make a statement in your home by creating a mason jar chandelier. Attach mason jars to a metal or wooden frame, and hang it from the ceiling. Fill the jars with LED lights for a stunning and unique lighting fixture.

17. Mason Jar Wedding Decor

Add a rustic touch to your wedding décor by incorporating mason jars. Use them as centerpieces, candle holders, or even as drinking glasses. They will add a charming and personal touch to your special day.

18. Mason Jar Wall Sconces

Enhance your interior décor with mason jar wall sconces. Attach the jars to wooden plaques or metal brackets, and hang them on your walls. Fill them with flowers, candles, or fairy lights to create a warm and inviting ambiance.

19. Mason Jar Snowman

Get into the holiday spirit by creating mason jar snowmen. Paint the jars white, add buttons for eyes, and use fabric or ribbon for scarves. Fill the jars with small ornaments or LED lights for a festive touch.

20. Mason Jar Beverage Dispenser

Impress your guests at parties by turning mason jars into beverage dispensers. Attach a spigot to the jar lid, fill it with your favorite drink, and let your guests serve themselves. A stylish and practical addition to any gathering.

These 20 DIY ideas are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to crafting with mason jars. Let your creativity run wild and explore the endless possibilities. Whether you’re looking to add a rustic touch to your home décor or create personalized gifts, mason jars are a versatile and affordable option. So grab your jars, gather your crafting supplies, and get ready to embark on a creative journey with these ultimate DIY ideas.
