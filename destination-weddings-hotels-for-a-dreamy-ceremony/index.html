Destination Weddings: Hotels for a Dreamy Ceremony


Are you dreaming of saying “I do” against a backdrop of stunning scenery, with the warm sun on your skin and the sound of the ocean as your soundtrack? If so, a destination wedding might be just what you’re looking for. Destination weddings have become increasingly popular in recent years, offering couples the opportunity to combine their wedding with a memorable vacation for themselves and their guests. One of the key elements to consider when planning a destination wedding is the choice of hotel for the ceremony and reception. The right hotel can make all the difference in creating a dreamy and unforgettable wedding experience. Here are some top hotels that are perfect for hosting a destination wedding.

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Bali

Nestled amidst the lush greenery and pristine beaches of Bali, The Ritz-Carlton offers a truly magical setting for a destination wedding. Imagine exchanging vows in a picturesque garden overlooking the Indian Ocean, followed by a reception on a private beach with the sunset as your backdrop. The hotel’s experienced wedding planners and luxurious amenities ensure that every aspect of your special day is taken care of with the utmost care and attention to detail.

2. Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora

For a wedding that feels like a fairy tale come true, the Four Seasons Resort in Bora Bora is an idyllic choice. Set against the backdrop of crystal-clear waters and majestic Mount Otemanu, this resort offers overwater wedding chapels, where you can tie the knot surrounded by the beauty of the South Pacific. The resort’s commitment to personalized service and breathtaking natural surroundings make it a top pick for couples seeking a truly dreamy ceremony.

3. Belmond Hotel Caruso, Amalfi Coast

If you’re envisioning a romantic Italian wedding, the Belmond Hotel Caruso on the stunning Amalfi Coast is an ideal choice. This historic hotel, perched on a cliff overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, provides the perfect blend of old-world charm and modern luxury. Whether you choose to exchange vows in the hotel’s lush gardens or in a centuries-old church nearby, the hotel’s stunning views and impeccable service will make your wedding day an unforgettable experience.

4. Aman Venice, Italy

For a wedding that exudes timeless elegance and sophistication, Aman Venice offers a truly enchanting setting. This historic palazzo, located on the Grand Canal, provides a sense of old-world romance and opulence that is perfect for a destination wedding. The hotel’s ornate interiors, private garden, and panoramic views of Venice create a magical atmosphere for couples looking to celebrate their love in a one-of-a-kind setting.

5. The St. Regis Princeville Resort, Kauai

For those dreaming of a Hawaiian paradise for their wedding, The St. Regis Princeville Resort on the island of Kauai is an exceptional choice. With its breathtaking ocean views, lush tropical gardens, and elegant event spaces, this resort captures the essence of romance and natural beauty. Whether you opt for a beachside ceremony or a garden reception, the resort’s dedicated wedding team will ensure that your special day is nothing short of extraordinary.

Planning a destination wedding can be an exciting and rewarding experience, especially when you have the perfect hotel setting to bring your dream ceremony to life. These top hotels offer unparalleled beauty, luxury, and service, making them ideal choices for couples seeking a truly magical destination wedding experience. With their stunning locations and commitment to creating unforgettable moments, these hotels are sure to make your wedding day a dream come true.
