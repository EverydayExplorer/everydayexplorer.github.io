The Best Scenic Train Rides Around the World


Train travel has always been a beloved mode of transportation, combining the efficiency of getting from one point to another with the added benefit of experiencing breathtaking scenery along the way. From towering mountain peaks to picturesque coastlines and everything in between, scenic train rides offer an unparalleled opportunity to soak in the beauty of the world. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best scenic train rides around the globe. So grab your ticket, sit back, and enjoy the view as we embark on a journey through some of the most stunning landscapes on earth.

1. The Rocky Mountaineer, Canada

Imagine yourself winding through the stunning Canadian Rockies, with snow-capped mountains, dense pine forests, and crystal-clear lakes surrounding you. This is exactly what the Rocky Mountaineer train ride offers. Starting from Vancouver, British Columbia, this scenic train journey takes you through Banff National Park, Jasper National Park, and the quaint mountain town of Whistler. With large viewing windows and open-air vestibules, passengers can take in panoramic views of the majestic landscapes, including the famous Spiral Tunnels and the stunning Athabasca Falls. The Rocky Mountaineer is a must-do for any train enthusiast or nature lover.

2. The Glacier Express, Switzerland

Switzerland is renowned for its picturesque alpine scenery, and the Glacier Express embodies the best of it. This train journey starts in Zermatt and takes you on a mesmerizing ride through the Swiss Alps to St. Moritz. The route crosses 291 bridges, including the famous Landwasser Viaduct, and goes through 91 tunnels, immersing passengers in a world of astounding beauty. From snow-covered peaks to charming alpine villages, the Glacier Express offers a delightful experience for travelers seeking natural wonders and scenic charm.

3. The Trans-Siberian Railway, Russia

The Trans-Siberian Railway is the epitome of epic train journeys. Stretching across Russia from Moscow to Vladivostok, this iconic railway covers a distance of approximately 9,289 kilometers (5,772 miles) and takes about a week to complete. Along the way, passengers are treated to an ever-changing tapestry of landscapes, from the rolling hills of the Russian countryside to the vast Siberian wilderness. The train passes over the Ural Mountains, through the stunning Lake Baikal region, and provides an authentic glimpse into the rich and diverse culture of Russia. The Trans-Siberian Railway is a true adventure that offers an unforgettable experience.

4. The Coastal Pacific, New Zealand

New Zealand is known for its stunning natural beauty, and the Coastal Pacific train ride is a fantastic way to explore its picturesque coastline. Running from Picton to Christchurch, this journey takes you through vineyards, rugged coastlines, and rolling hills. Passengers can enjoy panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, with the Kaikoura Ranges as a stunning backdrop. With its comfortable seating and large windows, the Coastal Pacific allows travelers to marvel at New Zealand’s unparalleled scenery while enjoying the tranquility of train travel.

5. The Ghan, Australia

For those looking to experience the vastness and diversity of the Australian Outback, the Ghan is the ultimate train ride. This iconic train traverses the heart of Australia, starting in Adelaide and ending in Darwin or vice versa. As the train cuts through the red desert landscapes, passengers will witness the changing colors of the Outback, from vibrant reds to golden hues. The Ghan is more than just a means of transportation; it is a unique way to experience the rich Indigenous culture and history of the region. With its luxurious accommodations and top-notch service, the Ghan provides an unforgettable journey through the Australian wilderness.

Conclusion

Scenic train rides offer an exceptional way to explore the beauty of the world, providing breathtaking views and a sense of adventure that cannot be replicated in any other form of transportation. Whether you are a train enthusiast, a nature lover, or simply seeking a unique travel experience, these five train rides should be on your bucket list. From the Canadian Rockies to the Swiss Alps, the Russian wilderness to the New Zealand coastline, and the Australian Outback, each journey offers its own unique charm and will leave you with memories to last a lifetime. So hop on board and indulge in the wonders of the world’s most scenic train rides.
