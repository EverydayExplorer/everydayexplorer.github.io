The Best Comedy Podcasts to Brighten Your Commute


Commuting – it’s a necessary evil for many of us. Whether you’re stuck in traffic or crammed onto a crowded train, the daily grind of getting to and from work can be a real drag. But fear not, dear commuter, for there is a bright side to your daily journey: comedy podcasts. These delightful audio gems have the power to turn even the most tedious commute into a laugh-filled adventure. From witty banter to hilarious sketches, here are some of the best comedy podcasts guaranteed to brighten your commute.

1. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’

Hosted by comedian Joe Rogan, this long-form podcast features candid conversations with a wide range of guests, from fellow comedians to scientists to celebrities. With episodes spanning a variety of topics – from politics to pop culture to psychedelics – ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ offers a perfect blend of humor, insight, and thought-provoking discussion to keep you entertained during your commute.

2. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’

If you’re in need of a good laugh, look no further than ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno.’ Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this hilarious podcast follows Jamie as he reads aloud chapters from the erotic novel written by his own father. With each episode, Jamie and his friends provide hilarious commentary and reactions to the absurdly bad writing, making for an uproarious listening experience that’s sure to make your commute fly by.

3. ‘The Dollop with Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds’

‘The Dollop’ is a comedic history podcast hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds. Each episode, Dave recounts a bizarre and little-known story from American history to Gareth, who hears it for the first time. With Gareth’s comedic reactions and Dave’s witty commentary, ‘The Dollop’ offers a unique blend of humor and education that’s perfect for long commutes.

4. ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’

Hosted by comedian Scott Aukerman, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ is a hilarious improv comedy podcast featuring a rotating cast of comedians, actors, and musicians. Each episode features a mix of interviews, sketches, and improv games, guaranteeing plenty of laughs to keep you entertained on your commute. With its irreverent humor and endless supply of absurd characters, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ is a must-listen for comedy fans.

5. ‘How Did This Get Made?’

Ever find yourself watching a hilariously bad movie and wondering, ‘How did this get made?’ Well, wonder no more, because ‘How Did This Get Made?’ has the answers. Hosted by comedians Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, this podcast delves into the world of cult and obscure films, providing hilarious commentary and analysis along the way. From cheesy ’80s action flicks to cringe-worthy rom-coms, ‘How Did This Get Made?’ is the perfect companion for your commute.

6. ‘The Ricky Gervais Show’

‘The Ricky Gervais Show’ is a comedy podcast hosted by British comedian Ricky Gervais, along with his friends and collaborators Stephen Merchant and Karl Pilkington. Originally a radio show, ‘The Ricky Gervais Show’ gained widespread popularity as a podcast thanks to its irreverent humor and off-the-wall conversations. With Ricky’s sharp wit, Stephen’s dry humor, and Karl’s unique perspective on the world, this podcast is guaranteed to have you laughing out loud on your way to work.

7. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend’

Hosted by late-night TV host Conan O’Brien, ‘Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend’ is a comedic interview podcast featuring conversations with some of the biggest names in entertainment. With Conan’s trademark self-deprecating humor and quick wit, along with his guests’ hilarious anecdotes and stories, ‘Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend’ offers a lighthearted and entertaining listening experience that’s perfect for your daily commute.

8. ‘The Adventure Zone’

‘The Adventure Zone’ is a comedy podcast that combines improv comedy with tabletop role-playing games. Hosted by the McElroy brothers – Justin, Travis, and Griffin – along with their father Clint, ‘The Adventure Zone’ follows the hilarious and often absurd adventures of a group of characters as they navigate various fantasy worlds and campaigns. With its mix of humor, storytelling, and role-playing antics, ‘The Adventure Zone’ is sure to keep you entertained during your commute.

9. ‘2 Dope Queens’

‘2 Dope Queens’ is a comedy podcast hosted by comedians Phoebe Robinson and Jessica Williams. Each episode features a mix of stand-up comedy performances, storytelling, and interviews with special guests, offering a fresh and diverse perspective on comedy. With their sharp wit and infectious chemistry, Phoebe and Jessica create a fun and engaging listening experience that’s perfect for brightening up your commute.

10. ‘The Office Ladies’

If you’re a fan of the hit TV show ‘The Office,’ then ‘The Office Ladies’ podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Jenna Fischer and Angela Kinsey, who played Pam and Angela on the show, respectively, ‘The Office Ladies’ offers behind-the-scenes insights, trivia, and anecdotes from the making of each episode. With their hilarious commentary and genuine affection for the show, Jenna and Angela provide a nostalgic and entertaining listening experience that’s perfect for reliving your favorite moments from ‘The Office’ during your commute.

Conclusion

So there you have it – ten of the best comedy podcasts to brighten your commute and turn your daily journey into a laugh-filled adventure. Whether you’re a fan of improv comedy, hilarious anecdotes, or absurd humor, there’s something for everyone on this list. So plug in your headphones, hit play, and get ready to laugh your way to work!
