Top 10 Fashion Influencers to Follow on Instagram


When it comes to fashion inspiration, Instagram has become the go-to platform for millions of people around the world. With its visually appealing content and exclusive behind-the-scenes glimpses into the fashion world, it’s no wonder that fashion influencers have taken the app by storm. These influencers are not only trendsetters but also individuals who have the ability to shape our fashion choices and style preferences. In this blog post, we will explore the top 10 fashion influencers to follow on Instagram, who have successfully carved a niche for themselves in the fashion industry.

1. Chiara Ferragni (@chiaraferragni): With over 23 million followers on Instagram, Chiara Ferragni is undoubtedly one of the biggest names in the fashion influencer game. The Italian blogger turned entrepreneur is known for her impeccable street style and has worked with several luxury brands.

2. Danielle Bernstein (@weworewhat): With an impressive following of 2.7 million, Danielle Bernstein is a force to be reckoned with in the fashion industry. Her feed is a showcase of her personal style, which seamlessly combines high-end designer pieces with affordable fashion finds.

3. Aimee Song (@aimeesong): Aimee Song, also known as Song of Style, has over 5 million followers on Instagram. Her posts are a mix of fashion, beauty, and travel, making her page a one-stop destination for all things stylish.

4. Bryan Yambao (@bryanboycom): Known for his flamboyant and extravagant style, Bryan Boy is an influential figure in the fashion world. With a following of 660k, his feed is a burst of colors and edgy fashion choices.

5. Negin Mirsalehi (@negin_mirsalehi): Negin Mirsalehi is a Dutch-Persian influencer with a following of 6 million. Her feed is a beautiful blend of fashion, beauty, and lifestyle content, showcasing her sophisticated and chic style.

6. Blair Eadie (@blaireadiebee): Blair Eadie, the face behind the blog Atlantic-Pacific, has a massive following of 1.3 million on Instagram. Her signature style is characterized by bold patterns, vibrant colors, and statement accessories.

7. Leonie Hanne (@leoniehanne): Leonie Hanne is a German fashion influencer with over 2 million followers. Her feed features a mix of high-end designer pieces and affordable fashion finds, all styled in a way that is effortlessly chic.

8. Julie Sariñana (@sincerelyjules): Julie Sariñana’s Instagram feed is a breath of fresh air, with her minimalist and laid-back style. With a following of 5.8 million, she has gained popularity for her relatable and achievable fashion content.

9. Camila Coelho (@camilacoelho): Camila Coelho is a Brazilian influencer known for her glamorous and polished style. With over 8 million followers, she has worked with several luxury fashion brands and has her own line of beauty products.

10. Olivia Palermo (@oliviapalermo): Olivia Palermo is an American socialite and fashion influencer with a following of 6.4 million. Her feed is a mix of high fashion, street style, and glimpses into her glamorous life, making her a must-follow for fashion enthusiasts.

These 10 fashion influencers are not only renowned for their impeccable style but also for their ability to connect with their audience and offer genuine fashion inspiration. From high-end luxury to affordable street style, they cater to a wide range of fashion preferences. So, whether you’re looking to stay updated on the latest trends or seeking personal style inspiration, following these influencers on Instagram is a surefire way to up your fashion game.
