The Best Running Routes for Jogging Enthusiasts


Are you a jogging enthusiast looking for the best running routes to switch up your routine and explore new paths? Look no further! In this blog, I will take you on a virtual tour of some of the most incredible running routes around the world. From scenic coastal trails to breathtaking mountain paths, these routes are sure to inspire and rejuvenate even the most seasoned joggers. Lace up your running shoes and let’s hit the road!

1. Central Park Loop, New York City, USA
Central Park is a runner’s paradise nestled in the heart of bustling New York City. With its lush greenery, tranquil ponds, and iconic landmarks, this 6-mile loop offers a refreshing escape from the concrete jungle. Whether you’re a novice runner or a seasoned marathoner, Central Park offers a variety of terrains and scenic views to keep you motivated along the way.

2. The Great Ocean Road, Victoria, Australia
For those seeking an awe-inspiring coastal route, look no further than the Great Ocean Road in Victoria, Australia. This iconic stretch of road hugs the cliffs and offers breathtaking views of the Southern Ocean and the famous Twelve Apostles. Lace up your shoes, feel the salty breeze on your face, and let the rhythmic sound of crashing waves accompany you on your run.

3. Stanley Park Seawall, Vancouver, Canada
Imagine running alongside the shimmering waters of the Pacific Ocean, with towering trees and stunning mountain views as your backdrop. If that sounds like a dream come true, then the Stanley Park Seawall in Vancouver, Canada, is the perfect running route for you. This 8.8 km paved path will take you on a picturesque journey around the park, offering a perfect blend of nature and urban beauty.

4. Cinque Terre, Italy
If you’re a fan of trail running and breathtaking coastal views, the Cinque Terre in Italy is an absolute must. This UNESCO World Heritage Site consists of five picturesque seaside villages connected by a coastal trail. As you make your way along the cliffside paths, you’ll be rewarded with stunning views of colorful houses, vineyards, and the sparkling waters of the Italian Riviera.

5. Table Mountain, Cape Town, South Africa
For adventure-seeking runners, the Table Mountain route in Cape Town, South Africa, is an absolute bucket list experience. Challenge yourself to climb to the top of the iconic Table Mountain, where you’ll be rewarded with panoramic views of the city and the vast Atlantic Ocean. This route offers a mix of challenging climbs, technical trails, and breathtaking scenery that will leave you feeling accomplished and inspired.

6. The Golden Gate Park, San Francisco, USA
San Francisco’s Golden Gate Park is not only a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts but also an ideal destination for a scenic run. With its winding trails, beautiful gardens, and serene lakes, this urban oasis provides a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Runners can soak in the beauty of the park while enjoying the diverse flora and fauna along their route.

7. The Lake District, England
For lovers of rolling hills, quaint villages, and picturesque lakes, the Lake District in England is an absolute paradise. With its extensive network of trails and breathtaking landscapes, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is a haven for runners seeking both challenge and beauty. Be prepared to encounter stunning vistas, peaceful forests, and charming stone cottages as you explore this runner’s heaven.

8. The Great Wall, China
Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime adventure by running along a marvel of human engineering: the Great Wall of China. This iconic landmark stretches over 21,196 km, and while it may not be possible to run the entire length, several sections are accessible for runners. Experience the grandeur and history of the Great Wall as you climb steep steps and admire the surrounding landscapes.

9. The Coastal Trail, Cape Town, South Africa
Cape Town’s Coastal Trail is a running route that combines the beauty of the Atlantic Ocean with breathtaking mountain views. Start at the colorful Bo-Kaap neighborhood and follow the scenic trail along the rugged coastline. You’ll pass iconic landmarks such as Lion’s Head and Camps Bay, with the sparkling ocean always by your side. This route offers a perfect balance between challenge and natural beauty.

10. Forest Park, Portland, Oregon, USA
Known as the largest urban forest in the United States, Forest Park in Portland, Oregon, offers runners a chance to reconnect with nature while enjoying a tranquil and peaceful run. With over 80 miles of trails, you can choose from a variety of routes that wind through lush greenery, tall trees, and sparkling streams. Whether you prefer easy trails or more challenging terrains, Forest Park has something for everyone.

Now that you have a list of some of the best running routes around the world, it’s time to pack your running gear, book your flights, and embark on your next adventure. Remember to stay hydrated, listen to your body, and most importantly, have fun exploring these incredible routes. Happy running!
