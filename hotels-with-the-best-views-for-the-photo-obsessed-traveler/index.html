Hotels with the Best Views: For the Photo-Obsessed Traveler


Please note that this blog post is optimized for SEO and targeted towards photo-obsessed travelers. The information provided is based on research and may not reflect personal experiences.

When it comes to travel, finding the perfect hotel is crucial. After all, where you stay can greatly impact your overall experience. But if you’re a photo-obsessed traveler like me, there’s something even more important than the amenities or the service – the view. There’s just something so captivating about waking up to breathtaking vistas and watching the sunsets from your hotel room window. So, if you’re looking for hotels with the best views to feed your photography addiction, you’re in the right place! In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the most scenic hotels around the world.

  1. Amangiri, Utah, USA – Tucked within the rugged desert landscape of Utah, Amangiri offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding canyons and rock formations. With its minimalist design and floor-to-ceiling windows, this luxury resort allows you to capture the beauty of nature from the comfort of your own room. Whether you’re photographing the sunrise over the desert or the night sky filled with stars, Amangiri is a dream come true for any aspiring or professional photographer.

  2. Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, Dubai, UAE – Standing tall like a sail on the Arabian Gulf, the Burj Al Arab Jumeirah is not only an iconic landmark in Dubai but also an architectural wonder. From its luxurious suites, you can enjoy breathtaking views of the city skyline and the azure waters below. Capture the mesmerizing sunset or the twinkling lights of the city at night for truly Instagram-worthy shots.

  3. Ladera Resort, St. Lucia – Located in the heart of the Caribbean, Ladera Resort offers unparalleled views of the famous Pitons and the sparkling Caribbean Sea. Perched atop a ridge overlooking a lush rainforest, each room boasts an open-air design that allows you to soak in the natural beauty surrounding you. Watch as the sky changes colors during sunrise and sunset or capture the vibrant flora and fauna during your stay.

  4. Hotel Arts Barcelona, Spain – With its prime location on the Mediterranean coast, Hotel Arts Barcelona provides breathtaking views of the sea and the cityscape. Its luxurious rooms and suites feature floor-to-ceiling windows, allowing guests to wake up to the sight of the glistening water or the bustling streets of Barcelona. Don’t miss the opportunity to photograph the iconic Frank Gehry’s ‘Fish’ sculpture just a stone’s throw away from the hotel.

  5. Singita Lebombo Lodge, Kruger National Park, South Africa – Nestled within the vast wilderness of Kruger National Park, Singita Lebombo Lodge offers an extraordinary safari experience with stunning views of the African savannah. Stay in one of its sleek glass-walled suites and observe wildlife in their natural habitat. Capture the Big Five during game drives or photograph the golden hues of the sunset as it envelops the horizon.

  6. Six Senses Yao Noi, Thailand – For a tropical paradise with breathtaking views, look no further than Six Senses Yao Noi in Thailand. Situated on a picturesque island in the Phang Nga Bay, this resort offers panoramic vistas of the limestone karsts and the azure waters. Imagine waking up to the sight of these dramatic rock formations or capturing the vibrant sunset over the bay – a true feast for the eyes.

  7. Jade Mountain, St. Lucia – Another gem in St. Lucia, Jade Mountain takes luxury and views to a whole new level. Each of its infinity pool suites boasts an open-wall concept, maximizing the breathtaking views of the Pitons and the Caribbean Sea. The seamless transition between the room and the natural surroundings creates a stunning backdrop for your photographs.

  8. Matakauri Lodge, Queenstown, New Zealand – Surrounded by the magnificent landscapes of Queenstown, Matakauri Lodge offers captivating views of Lake Wakatipu and the surrounding mountains. This picturesque setting provides ample opportunities for nature photography, including stunning reflections on the lake, snow-capped peaks, and vibrant autumn foliage. And let’s not forget about the star-filled nights, perfect for capturing astrophotography.

  9. Santani Wellness Resort & Spa, Sri Lanka – If you’re searching for a balance between luxury, wellness, and breathtaking views, Santani Wellness Resort & Spa should be on your list. Set amidst the lush hills of Sri Lanka, this retreat offers panoramic vistas of the Hunnasgiriya Mountain Range. Capture the misty mornings or the golden sunsets as you relax and rejuvenate in this tranquil oasis.

  10. JW Marriott Guanacaste Resort & Spa, Costa Rica – Situated along the pristine beaches of Costa Rica’s Pacific Coast, the JW Marriott Guanacaste Resort & Spa offers spectacular ocean views complemented by lush tropical greenery. Whether you’re photographing the crashing waves, the vibrant sunsets, or the abundant wildlife, this eco-friendly resort is a paradise for photography enthusiasts.

Remember, the views from these hotels are just the beginning. Venture outside and explore the surrounding areas for even more photographic opportunities. As a photo-obsessed traveler, make sure to capture the essence of the destination and share your breathtaking shots with fellow travel enthusiasts. Happy exploring and may your photos inspire wanderlust in others!
