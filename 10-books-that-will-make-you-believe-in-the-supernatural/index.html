10 Books That Will Make You Believe in the Supernatural


Have you ever found yourself captivated by the idea of the supernatural? The thought of ghosts, witches, or mysterious creatures lurking in the shadows can be both thrilling and terrifying. If you’re someone who believes in the existence of the supernatural or simply enjoys exploring the realm of the unknown, then this blog post is for you. We’ve curated a list of ten books that will make you believe in the supernatural, each one filled with spine-tingling stories and unexplained phenomena. So, grab a cup of tea, find a cozy spot, and get ready to embark on a journey into the supernatural.

1. ‘The Haunting of Hill House’ by Shirley Jackson: Considered a classic in the horror genre, this chilling tale follows a group of people who gather at Hill House, a mansion with a dark past. As they explore the house, they encounter strange occurrences that challenge their beliefs about the supernatural.

2. ‘The Witching Hour’ by Anne Rice: Dive into the world of witches, spirits, and voodoo in this enchanting novel. The story revolves around the Mayfair witches, a powerful and secretive family with a connection to the supernatural.

3. ‘The Mothman Prophecies’ by John A. Keel: Based on true events, this book explores the mysterious sightings of a winged creature known as the Mothman. Delve into the unexplained phenomena and eerie encounters that surround this supernatural being.

4. ‘Dracula’ by Bram Stoker: No list of supernatural books would be complete without the iconic vampire count. Immerse yourself in the world of Transylvania and witness the battle between good and evil in this gothic masterpiece.

5. ‘The Shining’ by Stephen King: This psychological horror novel takes readers to the secluded Overlook Hotel, where a young boy with psychic abilities and his family face supernatural forces that threaten their sanity.

6. ‘The Picture of Dorian Gray’ by Oscar Wilde: While not traditionally categorized as a supernatural book, this novel explores the concept of eternal youth and the consequences of making a Faustian bargain.

7. ‘The Woman in Black’ by Susan Hill: Prepare to be haunted by the chilling tale of a vengeful ghost who terrorizes the small town of Crythin Gifford. This atmospheric novel will send shivers down your spine.

8. ‘American Gods’ by Neil Gaiman: In a world where ancient gods exist alongside modern deities, this novel follows the journey of Shadow Moon as he becomes entangled in a supernatural battle for power and belief.

9. ‘Jonathan Strange & Mr Norrell’ by Susanna Clarke: Set in an alternate version of England during the Napoleonic Wars, this book weaves a tale of two magicians who bring back the practice of magic, uncovering a hidden world of mystery and enchantment.

10. ‘House of Leaves’ by Mark Z. Danielewski: This experimental novel is a mind-bending exploration of a haunted house. With its unconventional structure and eerie atmosphere, it will leave you questioning reality and the supernatural.

These ten books will transport you to worlds where the supernatural becomes a reality. Whether you’re a firm believer or a skeptic, these captivating stories will ignite your imagination and leave you pondering the existence of the supernatural. So, step into the unknown, embrace the thrill, and let these books take you on a spine-tingling journey you won’t soon forget. Happy reading!
