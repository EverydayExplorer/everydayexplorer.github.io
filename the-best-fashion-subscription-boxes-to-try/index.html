The Best Fashion Subscription Boxes to Try


The fashion subscription box industry has exploded in recent years, offering a convenient and exciting way to upgrade your wardrobe without the hassle of shopping. With so many different options available, it can be overwhelming to figure out which ones are worth trying. That’s where this blog comes in - we’ve done the research for you and compiled a list of the best fashion subscription boxes that are guaranteed to elevate your style game.

1. Stitch Fix
Stitch Fix is one of the most popular fashion subscription boxes on the market. Here’s how it works: you start by filling out a style profile, where you provide information about your personal preferences, sizes, and budget. Then, a team of expert stylists hand-picks five clothing items and accessories just for you. Once your Fix arrives, you have the freedom to try everything on in the comfort of your own home. You only pay for what you keep, and shipping is free both ways. Plus, Stitch Fix offers options for women, men, and children, so the whole family can get in on the fun.

2. Trunk Club
Trunk Club, owned by Nordstrom, is another top contender in the fashion subscription box market. With Trunk Club, you get access to a personal stylist who curates a trunk of clothing and accessories based on your preferences and needs. You have the option to review and approve the items before they are shipped to you. Once you receive your trunk, you have 5 days to try everything on. Keep what you love and send back the rest. Trunk Club offers a wide variety of high-quality brands and caters to both men and women.

3. Le Tote
Le Tote offers a different twist on the traditional fashion subscription box. Instead of purchasing and keeping the items you receive, Le Tote operates on a rental model. You pay a monthly fee and are sent a box of clothing and accessories to wear for as long as you like. Once you’re done, simply return the items in the prepaid shipping bag and a new box will be sent out to you. This is a great option for those who want to experiment with different styles and trends without committing to the purchase.

4. Rent the Runway
If you have a special occasion or event coming up, Rent the Runway is the perfect fashion subscription box for you. Rent the Runway allows you to rent designer clothing and accessories for a fraction of the retail price. With options for both one-time rentals and monthly subscriptions, you can wear runway-worthy outfits without breaking the bank. Rent the Runway offers a vast selection of styles and sizes, making it accessible to women of all shapes and preferences.

5. Wantable
Wantable is a fashion subscription box that prides itself on delivering personalized items directly to your doorstep. Their expert stylists hand-pick clothing, accessories, and beauty products based on your individual style preferences and body type. You can choose from different subscription plans, including options for fitness apparel, intimates, and women’s fashion. Wantable also offers the ability to skip a month or cancel anytime, giving you flexibility and control over your subscription.

In conclusion, fashion subscription boxes are a game-changer for those who want to stay on-trend without the hassle of traditional shopping. With options like Stitch Fix, Trunk Club, Le Tote, Rent the Runway, and Wantable, there’s something for everyone. So go ahead and give one (or more) of these fashion subscription boxes a try - you won’t be disappointed!
