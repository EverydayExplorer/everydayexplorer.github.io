10 Up-and-Coming Music Artists to Watch Out For


In the ever-evolving world of music, it’s not uncommon for new and exciting talent to emerge on the scene. The music industry is a dynamic ecosystem where fresh voices, innovative sounds, and creative artists continually push the boundaries of what’s possible. Whether you’re a dedicated music aficionado or simply looking for some new tunes to add to your playlist, keeping an eye on up-and-coming music artists can be a rewarding endeavor. In this blog post, we’re going to introduce you to 10 up-and-coming music artists from various genres who are making waves in the industry and deserve your attention.

1. Arlo Parks

Starting off our list is the incredibly talented British singer-songwriter Arlo Parks. Her music blends elements of indie pop, soul, and R&B, creating a sound that’s both soothing and introspective. Arlo Parks’ debut album ‘Collapsed in Sunbeams’ garnered critical acclaim and won her the 2021 Mercury Prize, cementing her as a rising star in the music world.

2. Remi Wolf

Remi Wolf is a genre-bending artist whose music effortlessly fuses pop, funk, and R&B. Her unique style and infectious energy have garnered her a dedicated fan base. Tracks like ‘Hello Hello Hello’ and ‘Liz’ showcase her playful yet soulful approach to music.

3. Claud

Claud is an artist who is challenging traditional genre boundaries with their indie-pop sound. They’ve gained recognition for their candid and relatable lyrics. Songs like ‘Gold’ and ‘Soft Spot’ capture the essence of their distinctive style.

4. Giveon

R&B is experiencing a revival, and Giveon is at the forefront of this movement. With a deep, velvety voice reminiscent of classic crooners, Giveon’s music is both nostalgic and fresh. Check out his debut album ‘Take Time’ for a taste of his smooth R&B vibes.

5. Olivia Rodrigo

If you haven’t already heard of Olivia Rodrigo, you’ve probably been living under a rock. This Disney star turned pop sensation took the world by storm with her debut single ‘drivers license.’ Her raw and emotional songwriting connects with audiences of all ages, making her a force to be reckoned with.

6. Tai Verdes

Tai Verdes brings a refreshing blend of pop and indie rock to the table. His music is characterized by catchy hooks, relatable lyrics, and a sense of authenticity that resonates with listeners. Tracks like ‘Stuck in the Middle’ and ‘A-O-K’ showcase his infectious sound.

7. Baby Keem

If you’re into hip-hop, Baby Keem is an artist you need to know. His unique approach to rap and production sets him apart from the crowd. With the release of his album ‘The Melodic Blue,’ Baby Keem is proving himself to be a rising star in the rap game.

8. Japanese Breakfast

Japanese Breakfast, the solo project of Michelle Zauner, offers a dreamy blend of indie pop and shoegaze. Her album ‘Jubilee’ has received widespread acclaim and showcases her talent for creating lush, atmospheric soundscapes.

9. Phoebe Bridgers

Phoebe Bridgers has been on the indie music scene for a few years now, but her star continues to rise. Her introspective and emotionally charged lyrics combined with her folk-rock sound make her a standout artist. Albums like ‘Stranger in the Alps’ and ‘Punisher’ are must-listens.
