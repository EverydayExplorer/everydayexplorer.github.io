10 Must-Have Fishing Apps for the Modern Angler


Welcome, fellow fishing enthusiasts! Are you ready to take your angling skills to the next level? In this blog post, we’ll be diving deep into the world of fishing apps. Yes, that’s right - the world of fishing has gone digital! With the advent of technology, modern anglers now have access to a plethora of handy apps that can enhance their fishing experience like never before. Whether you’re a seasoned angler or just starting out, these 10 must-have fishing apps are here to revolutionize the way you fish!

1. Fishbrain: Considered the social network for anglers, Fishbrain connects you with a community of fellow fishermen. Share your catches, discover new fishing spots, and get real-time fishing forecasts to maximize your chances of success.

2. Navionics: Navigation is crucial when it comes to fishing, and Navionics is the ultimate tool for that. This app provides detailed maps, charts, and weather information to ensure you never get lost on the water. Find the best fishing spots with ease!

3. FishAngler: From tracking your catches to recording your favorite fishing spots, FishAngler is a comprehensive app that covers all your angling needs. Join fishing tournaments, connect with other anglers, and gain insights into fishing trends.

4. Pro Angler: If you’re looking for an app that covers fishing across the globe, Pro Angler is your go-to companion. With detailed species information, fishing regulations, and tips from experts, you’ll be well-equipped for any fishing adventure.

5. MyRadar: Weather conditions play a significant role in fishing success. MyRadar provides up-to-date weather radar maps, storm tracking, and real-time weather alerts, ensuring you stay safe and make the most of your fishing trips.

6. Fishidy: Designed to help you catch more fish, Fishidy combines fishing maps, hotspots, and local knowledge from other anglers. Discover new fishing spots, access fishing reports, and plan your next fishing expedition with ease.

7. iSolunar Fishing & Hunting Times: Ever wondered when the best time to fish would be? Look no further! This app calculates the best fishing times based on the Solunar Theory, which predicts animal activity based on the moon’s position. Increase your chances of catching the big one!

8. Fishing Knots Lite: Knot tying is an essential skill for any angler. With Fishing Knots Lite, you have access to step-by-step instructions and animations for tying various fishing knots. Never struggle with a knot again!

9. GoFree Hooked: Keep a detailed log of your fishing trips with GoFree Hooked. Record your catches, take photos, and note down important details. The app also provides useful statistics and analytics to help you track your progress.

10. Fishing & Hunting Solunar Time: Similar to iSolunar, this app calculates the best fishing times based on the moon’s position, but it also factors in other variables such as sun and moon phases. Stay ahead of the game with this advanced solunar calendar.

There you have it, 10 must-have fishing apps that will revolutionize your angling experience. From social networking with fellow anglers to navigating the waters and predicting the best fishing times, these apps have got you covered. Embrace the power of technology and take your fishing game to new heights. Happy fishing!
