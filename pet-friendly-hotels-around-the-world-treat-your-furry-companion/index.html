Pet-Friendly Hotels Around the World: Treat Your Furry Companion


Traveling with pets has become increasingly popular in recent years as more and more people consider their furry companions as part of the family. With this growing trend, pet-friendly hotels are on the rise around the world, accommodating both humans and their four-legged friends. If you’re planning a trip and want to ensure a comfortable stay for yourself and your pet, here is a list of pet-friendly hotels across the globe.

1. United States:
In the US, many hotels offer pet-friendly accommodations. One notable chain is ‘Kimpton Hotels,’ which is renowned for its pet-friendly policies. They offer pet amenities such as beds, bowls, and treats, making your pet feel right at home. Another famous option is ‘La Quinta Inns & Suites,’ where pets stay for free, and there are even designated pet areas for walking and exercise.

2. Europe:
Europe is known for its love of animals, and you’ll find numerous pet-friendly hotels throughout the continent. In France, ‘Relais & Châteaux’ is a collection of luxurious hotels that welcome pets and often provide special services like dog-walking or pet-sitting. In the UK, the ‘Hotel du Vin’ chain warmly welcomes pets, providing pet beds and bowls upon request.

3. Australia:
Australia is a country that prides itself on being pet-friendly. Many hotels acknowledge this by offering comfortable stays for both you and your pet. ‘Ovolo Hotels’ has several locations across the country and provides pet amenities, including beds, toys, and even a pet mini-bar. Additionally, some ‘Meriton Suites’ locations allow pets and have pet-friendly apartments with designated play areas.

4. Asia:
Asia, too, has started embracing the concept of pet-friendly hotels. In Japan, where pets are considered important members of the family, ‘The Peninsula Tokyo’ offers luxurious pet-friendly rooms with stunning views and amenities such as pet beds, food bowls, and even doggy kimonos. In Singapore, the ‘Hotel Fort Canning’ provides pet-friendly rooms and is located near parks for walking your furry friend.

5. South America:
When traveling to South America, you don’t have to leave your pet behind. In Argentina, ‘Alvear Palace Hotel’ in Buenos Aires has dedicated pet-friendly floors where animals are pampered with their own beds, toys, and treats. Brazil offers ‘Fasano Hotels,’ which allow pets and provide pet beds, bowls, and grooming services upon request.

6. Africa:
Pet-friendly hotels can also be found in Africa, offering a unique experience for both humans and pets. For example, in Cape Town, South Africa, ‘The Twelve Apostles Hotel and Spa’ welcomes pets and offers special services such as dog walking and even pet-friendly massages. Many game reserves and safari lodges also accommodate pets, allowing you to explore the African wilderness together.

Traveling with pets can be a rewarding experience, and these pet-friendly hotels ensure that your furry companion receives the same level of comfort and care as you do. Remember to check each hotel’s specific pet policies and any additional fees they may charge. It’s also essential to be a responsible pet owner and adhere to local regulations regarding leashing and cleaning up after your pet.

So, the next time you plan a trip, consider staying at one of these pet-friendly hotels. Treat your furry friend to a vacation they’ll never forget, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Happy travels!
