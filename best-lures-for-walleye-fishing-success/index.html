Best Lures for Walleye Fishing Success


Walleye fishing is a beloved and challenging pursuit for many anglers. These elusive fish require strategic tactics and the right gear to maximize success. One essential component of a successful walleye fishing trip is selecting the right lures. In this blog post, we will explore the best lures for walleye fishing success, including their features, benefits, and when to use them. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced angler, these lures will help you increase your catch rate and make your walleye fishing experience truly memorable.

1. Jigging Rap

One of the most popular lures for walleye fishing is the Jigging Rap. This lure is incredibly versatile in attracting walleye in various fishing conditions. It features a minnow-like appearance and an enticing wobbling action that mimics a wounded baitfish. The Jigging Rap’s erratic movement is particularly effective in attracting the attention of walleye, especially when the fish are being selective or in a less active state.

This lure works best when jigged vertically, allowing it to imitate a dying fish. The Jigging Rap comes in multiple sizes and colors, allowing you to choose the perfect one for different water and light conditions.

2. Shad Raps

Another top-notch lure for walleye fishing is the Shad Rap. This crankbait lure is designed to imitate a small shad or baitfish, which are staple foods for walleye. The Shad Rap’s lifelike swimming action and realistic finishes make it irresistible to walleye.

One of the advantages of the Shad Rap is its ability to be fished effectively at various depths. Depending on the desired depth, you can select different sizes and adjust the speed of retrieval to target walleye at different depths. This flexibility makes the Shad Rap an excellent choice for walleye fishing in both shallow and deep waters.

3. Rapala Husky Jerk

The Rapala Husky Jerk is a classic walleye fishing lure that has proven its effectiveness over the years. This suspending lure features a neutral buoyancy, allowing it to hover in the strike zone and drive walleye crazy. The Husky Jerk mimics injured baitfish with its realistic swimming action, making it an irresistible meal for walleye.

This lure excels in clear water conditions, where walleye rely heavily on their sight to locate prey. The varied color patterns and sizes of the Husky Jerk give you the flexibility to match the prevailing conditions and trigger more strikes.

4. Berkley Flicker Shad

The Berkley Flicker Shad is a popular walleye fishing lure known for its incredible action and flash. This crankbait lure features a unique design that enhances its movement in the water, making it appear like an injured baitfish. The Flicker Shad’s tight wobbling action and highly reflective finishes draw aggressive strikes from hungry walleye.

This lure is particularly effective when trolling, as its action and vibration can cover a large area and attract walleye from a distance. The Flicker Shad also comes in a range of colors, allowing you to experiment and find the right color combination that triggers the most strikes.

5. Mepps Aglia Spinner

Although traditionally associated with trout and panfish, the Mepps Aglia Spinner is also an excellent choice for walleye fishing. This spinner lure imitates a small baitfish or a fleeing insect, which can trigger walleye’s predatory instincts.

The Aglia Spinner’s spinning blade creates flash and vibration in the water, grabbing the attention of nearby walleye. It is highly effective when cast and retrieved, and can also be trolled at varying speeds. The versatility of the Aglia Spinner makes it a valuable addition to any walleye angler’s tackle box.

Conclusion

When it comes to walleye fishing, selecting the right lures can make all the difference between a successful trip and a disappointing one. By considering the mentioned lures in your tackle box, you’ll have the necessary options to adapt to different fishing conditions and increase your chances of landing more walleye. Remember to experiment with different colors, sizes, and retrieve techniques to find what works best for you. The lures mentioned in this blog post have proven their effectiveness among anglers all around, so give them a try and make your walleye fishing trips a smashing success!
