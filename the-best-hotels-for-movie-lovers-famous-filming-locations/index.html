The Best Hotels for Movie Lovers: Famous Filming Locations


Lights, camera, action! Calling all movie lovers! If you’re a cinephile looking for a unique travel experience, then this blog post is for you. We’ve curated a list of the best hotels around the world that will transport you to your favorite movie sets. Get ready to embark on a cinematic journey and relive iconic moments from your beloved films. From classic Hollywood to international blockbusters, these hotels offer a one-of-a-kind experience for all movie enthusiasts. So, grab your popcorn and let’s dive into the world of famous filming locations!

1. The Plaza, New York City, USA
Located in the heart of Manhattan, The Plaza is a historic landmark that has been featured in numerous films, including ‘Home Alone 2: Lost in New York’ and ‘The Great Gatsby.’ This luxurious hotel offers opulent accommodations and breathtaking views of Central Park. Immerse yourself in the glamour of Old Hollywood and explore the nearby iconic filming locations.

2. The Marigold Hotel, Jaipur, India
Inspired by the film ‘The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel,’ this real-life hotel captures the vibrant spirit of India. Situated in the bustling city of Jaipur, the hotel offers a blend of modern amenities and traditional Indian hospitality. Indulge in a sensory journey through the bustling markets and architectural wonders that have graced the silver screen.

3. The Grand Budapest Hotel, Görlitz, Germany
Step into the whimsical world of Wes Anderson’s ‘The Grand Budapest Hotel’ with a stay at this charming hotel in Görlitz. The hotel’s stunning architecture and lavish interiors mirror the film’s visual style. Explore the picturesque town and discover the real-life shooting locations that served as the backdrop for this cinematic masterpiece.

4. The Shining Hotel, Estes Park, Colorado, USA
For those seeking a thrilling experience, check into The Stanley Hotel, which inspired Stephen King’s ‘The Shining.’ Nestled amidst the picturesque Rocky Mountains, this hotel exudes an eerie ambiance that will transport you into the world of Jack Torrance and the Overlook Hotel. Take a ghost tour or simply revel in the breathtaking mountain views.

5. The Millennium Biltmore Hotel, Los Angeles, USA
Known as the ‘host of the coast,’ The Millennium Biltmore Hotel has played a starring role in numerous films, including ‘Chinatown’ and ‘Ghostbusters.’ This iconic hotel showcases timeless elegance and Hollywood glamour. Immerse yourself in the rich history of Los Angeles and visit nearby filming locations that have made their mark on the silver screen.

6. The Park Hyatt Tokyo, Tokyo, Japan
Channel your inner Bill Murray and experience the vibrant cityscape of Tokyo at The Park Hyatt. Featured in Sofia Coppola’s ‘Lost in Translation,’ this hotel offers stunning panoramic views of the city. Dive into the bustling streets of Tokyo and explore the cultural landmarks that have captivated audiences worldwide.

7. The Royal Hawaiian, Honolulu, Hawaii, USA
Escape to paradise at The Royal Hawaiian, also known as the ‘Pink Palace of the Pacific.’ This iconic hotel has been featured in films like ‘Blue Hawaii’ and ‘From Here to Eternity.’ Immerse yourself in the tropical beauty of Hawaii and discover the breathtaking filming locations that have graced the silver screen.

8. The Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur, India
Experience the romance and grandeur of Bollywood at The Taj Lake Palace. Situated in the middle of Lake Pichola, this exquisite hotel has been featured in several Indian films, including ‘Octopussy.’ Indulge in the opulence and immerse yourself in the magic of Indian cinema.

9. The Langham Huntington, Pasadena, California, USA
Step into the world of classic Hollywood at The Langham Huntington. This historic hotel has been a favorite among filmmakers, featuring in movies like ‘Beverly Hills Cop’ and ‘Catch Me If You Can.’ Immerse yourself in the glitz and glamour of Los Angeles and discover the iconic filming locations that have shaped the history of cinema.

10. The Peninsula Hong Kong, Hong Kong
Experience the vibrant energy of Hong Kong at The Peninsula. This iconic hotel has been featured in films like ‘The Dark Knight’ and ‘Chungking Express.’ Immerse yourself in the bustling streets of Hong Kong and discover the hidden gems that have captivated audiences on the silver screen.

Whether you’re a fan of classic Hollywood or international cinema, these hotels offer a unique opportunity to step into the world of your favorite movies. Immerse yourself in the magic of cinema, explore iconic filming locations, and create unforgettable memories. Lights, camera, relaxation - it’s time to check in and let the movie magic begin!
