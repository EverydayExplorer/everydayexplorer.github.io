The Best Hotels for Outdoor Activities: Adventure at Your Doorstep


When it comes to travel, finding the perfect hotel can make all the difference in your experience. For those who love outdoor activities and adventure, choosing a hotel that offers easy access to thrilling experiences and natural wonders is essential. From hiking and rock climbing to water sports and wildlife encounters, the best hotels for outdoor activities provide a gateway to unforgettable experiences. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the top hotels around the world that cater to adventure seekers, offering a perfect blend of luxury and access to the great outdoors.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA
Nestled in the heart of the dramatic Utah desert, Amangiri offers a unique setting for outdoor enthusiasts. With easy access to iconic national parks such as Zion, Bryce Canyon, and the Grand Canyon, guests can embark on guided hikes, rock climbing expeditions, and even hot air balloon rides to witness the breathtaking landscape from above. The hotel itself is a masterpiece of modern design, seamlessly blending into the rugged surroundings.

2. Clayoquot Wilderness Resort, British Columbia, Canada
For a truly immersive outdoor experience, Clayoquot Wilderness Resort provides luxury tented accommodation in the heart of the Canadian wilderness. Surrounded by old-growth forest and the pristine waters of the Clayoquot Sound, guests can enjoy a range of activities including whale watching, bear spotting, kayaking, and horseback riding. The resort’s commitment to sustainability and conservation adds an extra layer of appeal for eco-conscious travelers.

3. Blancaneaux Lodge, Belize
Tucked away in the lush jungles of Belize, Blancaneaux Lodge offers a serene retreat for adventure seekers. Guests can explore the nearby Mountain Pine Ridge Forest Reserve, embark on cave expeditions, or take a refreshing dip in the natural rock pools and waterfalls. The lodge also provides easy access to ancient Mayan ruins, adding a cultural dimension to the outdoor experience.

4. Saffire Freycinet, Tasmania, Australia
Perched on the edge of Tasmania’s Freycinet National Park, Saffire Freycinet offers a luxurious base for exploring the diverse landscapes of the island. From guided walks along pristine beaches to encounters with unique wildlife such as wombats and Tasmanian devils, guests can immerse themselves in the natural beauty of this remote corner of the world. The hotel’s commitment to sustainable practices aligns with the conservation ethos of the surrounding area.

5. The Brando, Tetiaroa, French Polynesia
For those seeking a tropical paradise with ample opportunities for outdoor adventure, The Brando is a dream destination. Situated on a private atoll, guests can snorkel in crystal-clear waters, paddleboard through the lagoon, or join naturalist-led excursions to learn about the local flora and fauna. The hotel’s dedication to preserving the surrounding marine and birdlife adds to the appeal of this exclusive retreat.

In conclusion, the best hotels for outdoor activities offer much more than just a place to rest your head – they provide a gateway to unforgettable adventures. Whether it’s exploring the desert canyons of Utah, immersing yourself in the Canadian wilderness, or discovering the natural wonders of remote islands, these hotels cater to the thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. By choosing a hotel that prioritizes access to outdoor activities, you can elevate your travel experience and create lasting memories in some of the world’s most awe-inspiring landscapes.
