The Most Fuel-Efficient SUVs of 2021: Eco-Friendly and Powerful


When it comes to choosing an SUV, many drivers are in search of a vehicle that is not only powerful and spacious but also fuel-efficient. With the increasing concern for the environment and rising fuel costs, finding an eco-friendly SUV that doesn’t compromise on power and performance is becoming more important than ever. Thankfully, automakers have recognized this demand and are now offering a wide range of fuel-efficient SUVs in the market. In this blog post, we will explore the most fuel-efficient SUVs of 2021 that are both eco-friendly and powerful.

1. Toyota RAV4 Hybrid:
The Toyota RAV4 has long been a popular choice among SUV enthusiasts, and with the addition of a hybrid powertrain, it becomes even more appealing. The RAV4 Hybrid combines a 2.5-liter gasoline engine with electric motors to deliver a fuel economy of up to 41 mpg in the city and 38 mpg on the highway. With an impressive power output and Toyota’s reputation for reliability, the RAV4 Hybrid is a top pick for eco-conscious SUV buyers.

2. Honda CR-V Hybrid:
The Honda CR-V Hybrid blends style, comfort, and fuel efficiency into one package. Equipped with a 2.0-liter i-VTEC Atkinson-cycle gasoline engine and two electric motors, the CR-V Hybrid achieves an impressive fuel economy of up to 40 mpg in the city and 35 mpg on the highway. With its smooth ride and spacious cabin, the CR-V Hybrid is a great option for families looking for a fuel-efficient SUV.

3. Ford Escape Hybrid:
The Ford Escape has been a popular choice in the SUV segment, and with the introduction of a hybrid powertrain, it becomes an even more attractive option for eco-conscious buyers. The Escape Hybrid combines a 2.5-liter Atkinson-cycle gasoline engine with an electric motor to deliver a fuel economy of up to 44 mpg in the city and 37 mpg on the highway. With its stylish design and advanced safety features, the Escape Hybrid is a compelling choice for those seeking a fuel-efficient SUV.

4. Lexus UX 250h:
For SUV buyers looking for luxury and fuel efficiency, the Lexus UX 250h is a perfect choice. Powered by a 2.0-liter gasoline engine and two electric motors, the UX 250h delivers a fuel economy of up to 43 mpg in the city and 41 mpg on the highway. With its premium interior and smooth ride, the UX 250h offers a combination of style, comfort, and fuel efficiency that is hard to beat.

5. Hyundai Tucson Hybrid:
Hyundai has stepped up its game in the SUV market with the introduction of the Tucson Hybrid. Powered by a 1.6-liter turbocharged gasoline engine and an electric motor, the Tucson Hybrid achieves an impressive fuel economy of up to 38 mpg in the city and 38 mpg on the highway. With its spacious interior and user-friendly infotainment system, the Tucson Hybrid offers a compelling option for those in search of a fuel-efficient and practical SUV.

6. Subaru Crosstrek Hybrid:
Subaru is well-known for its all-wheel-drive capability, and now they have introduced a hybrid version of their popular Crosstrek model. The Crosstrek Hybrid combines a 2.0-liter BOXER gasoline engine with an electric motor to deliver a fuel economy of up to 35 mpg in the city and 90 MPGe when running on electricity only. With its rugged design and impressive off-road capabilities, the Crosstrek Hybrid is a great choice for those who love adventure but also prioritize fuel efficiency.

In conclusion, the demand for fuel-efficient SUVs is on the rise, and automakers are responding with a wide range of options that are both eco-friendly and powerful. Whether you are looking for a hybrid SUV from Toyota, Honda, Ford, Lexus, Hyundai, or Subaru, there is a model to suit your needs. These SUVs not only offer impressive fuel economy but also provide a comfortable and spacious driving experience. So if you are in the market for a new SUV and want to make an eco-friendly choice without compromising on power and performance, consider one of these fuel-efficient SUVs of 2021.
