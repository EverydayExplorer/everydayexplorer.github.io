Family Reunion Planning: Best Hotels for Large Groups


Planning a family reunion can be an exciting yet challenging endeavor. From coordinating schedules to finding a suitable venue, there are numerous details to consider. When it comes to accommodating a large group of relatives, finding the perfect hotel is essential. Thankfully, there are hotels that specialize in hosting large groups, ensuring a comfortable and memorable experience for everyone involved. In this blog post, we will explore the best hotels for large groups when planning a family reunion.

1. Marriott Hotels & Resorts

Marriott has long been known for its exceptional hospitality and willingness to accommodate large groups. With a vast network of hotels around the world, Marriott offers a wide range of options for family reunions. Many of their hotels have spacious suites and connecting rooms that can easily accommodate multiple guests. Additionally, Marriott offers personalized event planning services to assist with arranging family activities and meals during your stay.

2. Hilton Hotels & Resorts

Hilton is another well-established hotel brand that caters to large groups. With their extensive range of hotels, you are sure to find a property that suits your family’s needs. Hilton provides dedicated event planners to assist with organizing your family reunion, ensuring that every detail is taken care of. Their spacious guest rooms and suites are designed to accommodate families comfortably. If you are planning to stay for an extended period, consider the Hilton’s Home2 Suites, which offer apartment-style accommodations with fully equipped kitchens.

3. Hyatt Hotels Corporation

The Hyatt Hotels Corporation is renowned for its luxurious properties and exceptional service. When planning a family reunion, Hyatt offers a variety of options depending on your group’s size and preferences. From beachfront resorts to city center locations, there is a Hyatt hotel to suit every family’s needs. Hyatt’s event planning team can assist with organizing activities such as group dinners, family-friendly excursions, and even customized reunion souvenirs.

4. InterContinental Hotels Group (IHG)

The InterContinental Hotels Group, or IHG, includes popular hotel brands such as Holiday Inn, Crowne Plaza, and InterContinental. IHG properties are known for their spacious accommodations and extensive amenities, making them ideal for large group gatherings. With their global presence, you can find IHG hotels in various destinations around the world. In addition to comfortable accommodations, IHG offers group discounts and personalized event planning assistance to make your family reunion a success.

5. Wyndham Hotels & Resorts

Wyndham Hotels & Resorts is a popular choice for family reunions due to its diverse range of properties and affordable rates. With their portfolio of brands, including Wyndham, Ramada, and Days Inn, Wyndham offers a wide selection of accommodation options for large groups. Whether you prefer a beachfront resort, a mountain lodge, or a city hotel, Wyndham has something for everyone. Their spacious suites and flexible event spaces are perfect for hosting family activities and gatherings.

6. Choice Hotels International

Choice Hotels International encompasses well-known brands like Comfort Inn, Quality Inn, and Sleep Inn. Choice Hotels is ideal for budget-conscious families looking for comfortable accommodations and group-friendly amenities. Their properties offer a range of room types, including suites and adjoining rooms, making it easy to accommodate large groups. Choice Hotels also provides event planning assistance to help coordinate activities and meals during your family reunion.

When choosing the best hotel for your family reunion, it is important to consider factors such as location, amenities, and the size of the group. The hotels listed here are just a starting point, as there are many other options available. Remember to reach out to the hotel’s event planning team to discuss your specific needs and preferences. With careful planning and the right accommodations, your family reunion is sure to be a memorable experience for everyone involved.
