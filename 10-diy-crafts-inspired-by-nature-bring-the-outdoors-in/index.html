10 DIY Crafts Inspired by Nature: Bring the Outdoors In


Welcome to our blog, where we explore the beauty of nature and how we can bring its wonders into our homes through DIY crafts! In this article, we will be sharing 10 creative and easy-to-make craft ideas inspired by nature. So, let’s dive in and embrace the outdoors within the comfort of our own homes.

1. Leaf Pressed Wall Art:
Start by collecting various leaves from your garden or local park. Once you have a nice assortment, press them between two heavy books for a few weeks. Then, frame the pressed leaves and create a stunning wall art piece that captures the essence of nature.

2. Terrariums:
Terrariums are a fantastic way to bring the beauty of nature indoors. Find a clear glass container and create a mini ecosystem using a combination of small plants, pebbles, and soil. Not only will they add a touch of greenery to your living space, but they also require minimal maintenance.

3. Pinecone Bird Feeders:
Gather pinecones and attach a string to each one, creating a loop for hanging. Apply a generous layer of peanut butter to the pinecones and roll them in birdseed. Hang them outside near a window, and watch as birds flock to enjoy a tasty treat.

4. Seashell Candles:
If you’ve recently been to the beach and collected seashells, why not turn them into unique candles? Clean the shells thoroughly, melt candle wax, add a wick, and pour it into the shells. These seashell candles will infuse your home with a coastal ambiance.

5. Natural Dried Flower Wreath:
Gather an assortment of dried flowers, such as lavender, baby’s breath, and roses. Arrange them into a wreath shape, securing them with floral wire or hot glue. Hang this beautiful creation on your front door or as a wall decoration to add a touch of nature’s elegance.

6. Herb Garden in a Window Box:
Create a mini herb garden by planting your favorite herbs in a window box. Place it near a window that receives ample sunlight, and you’ll have fresh herbs at your fingertips for cooking and garnishing.

7. Natural Scented Potpourri:
Collect dried flowers, herbs, and citrus peels to create your own natural potpourri. Mix them together and place them in a decorative bowl or small fabric sachets. Not only will it add a pleasant aroma to your home, but it will also bring a touch of nature to any room.

8. Birch Bark Vases:
Find fallen birch bark and cut it into strips. Wrap the strips around glass vases and secure them with twine or glue. These rustic vases will add a natural and charming touch to your floral arrangements.

9. Nature-inspired Mobile:
Create a beautiful mobile using twigs, feathers, and string. Arrange them in a balanced and aesthetically pleasing way, and hang it near a window or in a corner of your room. As it gently sways with the breeze, it will remind you of the serenity of the outdoors.

10. Pressed Flower Coasters:
Collect and press a variety of flowers, then arrange them on ceramic tiles. Apply a coat of clear epoxy resin to seal and protect the flowers, creating unique and eye-catching coasters that will bring a burst of nature to your coffee table.

By incorporating these DIY crafts inspired by nature into your home, you can truly embrace the beauty of the outdoors and create a serene and calming atmosphere. So, gather your materials, get creative, and let nature be your guide. Happy crafting!
