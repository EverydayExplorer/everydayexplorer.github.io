10 Stand-Up Comedians You Need to Follow for Daily Doses of Humor


Is there anything better than a good laugh? Laughing is not only a great way to relieve stress, but it also brings people together, even in the darkest times. Stand-up comedy is an art form that has been entertaining audiences for decades, and there are so many incredibly talented comedians out there. So, if you’re looking to add some humor to your daily routine, here are ten stand-up comedians you need to follow for daily doses of laughter.

  1. Jerry Seinfeld – Known for his observational humor, Jerry Seinfeld has a unique ability to find comedy in the mundane aspects of life. His wit and cleverness shine through in his iconic TV show ‘Seinfeld’ and his stand-up specials.

  2. Ellen DeGeneres – Ellen is a household name and beloved by millions for her witty jokes and infectious energy. Whether it’s her TV show or her stand-up comedy, Ellen’s relatable humor and positive vibes are sure to brighten your day.

  3. Dave Chappelle – Known for his thought-provoking and boundary-pushing comedy, Dave Chappelle is a master of storytelling. His ability to tackle sensitive topics while making you laugh is a testament to his comedic genius.

  4. Amy Schumer – Amy Schumer’s bold and unapologetic comedy style resonates with many. She fearlessly addresses societal norms, relationships, and women’s issues, making her an empowering and hilarious comedian to follow.

  5. Kevin Hart – Kevin Hart’s high-energy performances and storytelling skills have made him a fan-favorite. His ability to turn personal anecdotes into laugh-out-loud moments is unparalleled, and his charisma is infectious.

  6. Trevor Noah – As the host of ‘The Daily Show,’ Trevor Noah has become renowned for his sharp wit and political humor. His unique perspective as a South African-born comedian adds an element of cultural diversity to his comedy.

  7. Ali Wong – Ali Wong’s no-holds-barred humor and willingness to discuss taboo subjects have made her a standout comedian. Her candidness about motherhood and relationships will leave you in stitches.

  8. John Mulaney – John Mulaney’s charm and sharp writing make him one of the funniest stand-up comedians today. His absurd and hilarious stories will transport you into his world of comedic brilliance.

  9. Mindy Kaling – While Mindy Kaling may be best known for her work in television and film, her stand-up comedy is not to be missed. Her quick-witted humor and relatable stories make her a refreshing and funny comedic voice.

  10. Hannah Gadsby – Hannah Gadsby’s groundbreaking comedy special, ‘Nanette,’ took the world by storm. Her ability to blend humor and profound storytelling showcases the power of stand-up comedy as a medium for social commentary.

These ten stand-up comedians encompass a wide range of styles and perspectives, ensuring that there’s something for everyone. Whether you’re a fan of observational humor, thought-provoking storytelling, or bold and fearless comedy, these comedians deliver daily doses of laughter that will brighten your day.

Following these comedians on social media, watching their stand-up specials, or even attending their live shows will inject a healthy dose of humor into your life. After all, who doesn’t need a good laugh every day?

Laughing is such an essential part of our lives, and these ten stand-up comedians are masters at their craft. So, take some time to explore their work, and get ready for a daily dose of humor that will lift your spirits and bring a smile to your face.
