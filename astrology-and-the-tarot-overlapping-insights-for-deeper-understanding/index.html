Astrology and the Tarot: Overlapping Insights for Deeper Understanding


For centuries, humans have sought guidance and meaning in their lives through various forms of divination. Two popular methods that have stood the test of time are astrology and the Tarot. Often seen as separate practices, astrology and the Tarot actually share significant similarities and can be used together to gain a deeper understanding of ourselves and the world around us.

Astrology is the study of how celestial bodies, such as the stars and planets, influence human behavior and personalities. By examining the positions of these celestial bodies at the time of one’s birth, an astrologer can make predictions and provide insights into an individual’s character, strengths, weaknesses, and even their future. The Tarot, on the other hand, is a deck of cards that are used for divination and self-reflection. Each card in the deck carries a specific meaning, and a reader can interpret the cards drawn to gain insight into a person’s current circumstances, emotional state, and potential future outcomes.

While these practices may appear different on the surface, they actually share a common foundation – the belief in interconnectedness and the power of symbolism. Both astrology and the Tarot place great importance on the language of symbols and archetypes. The celestial bodies in astrology and the images on the Tarot cards all carry symbolic meanings that can be interpreted to reveal deeper truths about ourselves and the world we inhabit.

Astrology and the Tarot also share the belief in the concept of synchronicity – the notion that events are not random but instead interconnected and influenced by unseen forces. In astrology, this is evident through the belief that the positions of the planets at the time of our birth can provide insight into our personalities and life paths. Similarly, when we draw a Tarot card, the card that appears is believed to have been drawn to us by the universe, offering guidance and insight into our current circumstances.

By combining astrology and the Tarot, we can tap into a powerful synergy of insights that can help us gain a deeper understanding of ourselves and the world around us. For example, if an astrologer has provided us with insights on our personality traits and potential life path, we can consult the Tarot cards to gain more specific guidance on how to navigate our current challenges or make important decisions.

Let’s say an astrologer has informed us that we are in a period of intense transformation and growth. By drawing a Tarot card, such as the Death card, which represents transformation and new beginnings, we can gain further understanding of the energies at play and the potential outcomes that await us. This combined approach can provide a more comprehensive and holistic understanding of our current circumstances, allowing us to make informed choices and take aligned action.

Additionally, the Tarot can also serve as a tool for self-reflection and personal growth when used in conjunction with astrology. By drawing a card that represents a particular aspect of our personality or current situation, we can gain insights into our subconscious patterns and beliefs. This awareness can help us identify areas of personal growth and self-improvement that are necessary for our overall well-being and development.

Moreover, astrology and the Tarot can be used to gain insights into our relationships. By examining the astrological compatibility between two individuals and then drawing Tarot cards for each person, we can gain a deeper understanding of the dynamics at play and the potential challenges and strengths within the relationship. This combined approach can help us navigate our relationships with more empathy and understanding, leading to healthier and more fulfilling connections.

In conclusion, astrology and the Tarot are two ancient practices that offer overlapping insights for deeper understanding. By recognizing the shared belief in symbolism, synchronicity, and interconnectedness, we can combine these practices to gain a more comprehensive understanding of ourselves, our circumstances, and our relationships. Whether used individually or in conjunction, astrology and the Tarot have the power to guide us on our journey towards self-discovery, personal growth, and a greater understanding of the world we inhabit.
