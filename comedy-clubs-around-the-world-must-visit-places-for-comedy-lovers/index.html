Comedy Clubs Around the World: Must-Visit Places for Comedy Lovers


Are you a fan of laughter, witty punchlines, and on-point comedic timing? If so, then you’re in for a treat! Comedy clubs offer a unique and unforgettable experience for comedy lovers. Whether you’re a casual fan or a die-hard comedy enthusiast, these comedy clubs around the world are definitely worth adding to your travel bucket list.

1. The Comedy Cellar, New York City, USA

Located in the heart of Greenwich Village, The Comedy Cellar is one of the most iconic comedy clubs in the world. It has become a breeding ground for up-and-coming comedians as well as a favorite spot for established stars to test new material. Many legendary comedians, including Dave Chappelle, Louis C.K., and Jerry Seinfeld, have graced its stage. With multiple shows every night, you’re guaranteed to have a hilarious and laughter-filled evening.

2. The Comedy Store, London, UK

Nestled in the trendy neighborhood of Soho, The Comedy Store is a mecca for comedy lovers. It has been the launching pad for many British comedic legends, such as Eddie Izzard and John Bishop. The venue offers a mix of stand-up shows, improv nights, and even themed comedy events. With its intimate atmosphere and top-notch line-up, The Comedy Store never fails to deliver a side-splitting night of laughter.

3. The Second City, Chicago, USA

Known as the birthplace of improvisational comedy, The Second City is a Chicago institution. It has served as a training ground for some of the biggest names in comedy, including Tina Fey, Bill Murray, and Amy Poehler. The club prides itself on its improv shows, where quick thinking, wit, and spontaneity take center stage. If you’re looking for a night of unscripted hilarity, The Second City should be at the top of your list.

4. Boom Chicago, Amsterdam, Netherlands

With its unique blend of sketch comedy and improvisation, Boom Chicago has been a comedy hotspot in Amsterdam for over 25 years. The club attracts both local and international talent, making it a melting pot of comedic styles and cultural references. Nestled in the historic Jordaan neighborhood, Boom Chicago offers a vibrant and energetic atmosphere that will leave you in stitches.

5. The Comedy Club, Melbourne, Australia

Melbourne has gained a reputation as a comedy haven, and for good reason. The city’s thriving comedy scene is home to The Comedy Club, a venue that has become synonymous with comedy excellence. From established international acts to emerging local talent, The Comedy Club showcases a diverse range of comedic styles and voices. With regular comedy nights and special events, Melbourne is a must-visit destination for comedy aficionados.

6. The Laugh Factory, Los Angeles, USA

Located on the iconic Sunset Strip, The Laugh Factory is a comedy institution in Los Angeles. This legendary club has been a launchpad for countless stars, including Richard Pryor, Jim Carrey, and Chris Rock. With its star-studded line-up and a constant stream of up-and-coming talent, The Laugh Factory offers an unforgettable night of laughter and entertainment. Plus, you never know who might drop in for a surprise set!

7. The Classic, Auckland, New Zealand

Situated in the heart of Auckland, The Classic is New Zealand’s premier comedy club. Renowned for its friendly and inclusive atmosphere, The Classic is a favorite among both comedians and audiences alike. The club offers a range of shows, from open mic nights to headline acts, ensuring there’s something for everyone. If you find yourself in Auckland, be sure to check out The Classic for a night of Kiwi comedy goodness.

8. The Comedy Mix, Vancouver, Canada

Vancouver’s premier comedy club, The Comedy Mix, has been serving up laughter since 2001. Known for its relaxed and intimate setting, this club provides a platform for local talent as well as big-name comedians touring through the city. The venue prides itself on its diverse line-up, featuring a wide range of comedic styles and perspectives. Whether you’re a seasoned comedy fan or new to the scene, The Comedy Mix promises an evening of laughter and good times.

So, there you have it - a list of comedy clubs around the world that are a must-visit for comedy lovers. Whether you’re in New York City, London, Chicago, Amsterdam, Melbourne, Los Angeles, Auckland, or Vancouver, you’re bound to find a comedy club that will leave you laughing till your sides hurt. Be sure to check out these venues and enjoy an evening of comedy magic at its finest.
