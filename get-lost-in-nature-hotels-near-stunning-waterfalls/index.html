Get Lost in Nature: Hotels Near Stunning Waterfalls


Welcome to our blog where we will be exploring the enchanting world of nature’s wonders and the hotels that allow you to get lost in their beauty. Today, we are taking you on a mesmerizing journey to discover some of the most stunning waterfalls around the world and the nearby hotels that provide an idyllic escape. So, pack your bags and let’s embark on this adventure together!

1. Iguazu Falls, Argentina and Brazil:
Prepare to be awestruck by the mighty Iguazu Falls, situated on the border of Argentina and Brazil. With over 275 individual cascades spread across nearly 2 miles, this natural wonder will leave you breathless. To fully immerse yourself in this mesmerizing experience, consider staying at a hotel like Belmond Hotel das Cataratas, which offers exclusive access to the falls and an opportunity to witness their beauty up close and personal.

2. Niagara Falls, Canada and United States:
No list of stunning waterfalls would be complete without mentioning the iconic Niagara Falls. Located on the border of Canada and the United States, this powerful waterfall attracts millions of visitors every year. To enhance your visit, you can choose to stay at one of the many hotels situated near the falls like The Red Coach Inn, which offers breathtaking views and a tranquil atmosphere.

3. Angel Falls, Venezuela:
Prepare to be transported to a place of pure serenity as we now journey to Angel Falls in Venezuela. As the world’s highest uninterrupted waterfall, it cascades from a staggering height of 3,212 feet, creating a truly awe-inspiring spectacle. For a unique experience, consider staying at the Canaima Camp, nestled amidst the lush tropics of Canaima National Park, offering stunning views of the falls.

4. Plitvice Falls, Croatia:
For those seeking the beauty of waterfalls in a European setting, Plitvice Falls in Croatia is an absolute must-visit. Situated within the stunning Plitvice Lakes National Park, this series of cascading lakes and waterfalls is a nature lover’s paradise. To fully immerse yourself in the surrounding natural beauty, stay at a hotel like Hotel Jezero, which offers easy access to the park and its breathtaking waterfalls.

5. Kaieteur Falls, Guyana:
Our final destination takes us to the remote and untouched beauty of Kaieteur Falls in Guyana. Situated in the heart of the Amazon rainforest, this majestic waterfall is five times taller than Niagara Falls, yet relatively unknown. To truly appreciate this hidden gem, consider staying at the Kaieteur Eco-Lodge, which offers a unique and immersive experience in the heart of the rainforest.

As you can see, the world is filled with captivating waterfalls, each offering a unique and awe-inspiring experience. Whether you choose to visit the iconic falls or venture into lesser-known territories, these hotels near stunning waterfalls will provide the perfect base for your unforgettable nature escapade.

So, go ahead and immerse yourself in the beauty of these mesmerizing waterfalls. Let nature’s wonders take your breath away as you relax in the comfort of these carefully selected hotels. Remember, when you get lost in nature, you often find yourself in the most extraordinary places.
