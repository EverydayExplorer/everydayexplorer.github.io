The Best Pet-Friendly Beaches for Summer Fun


Are you ready for some fun in the sun with your furry friend? With summer just around the corner, it’s time to start planning your pet-friendly beach getaway. Whether you have a dog who loves to splash in the waves or a cat who enjoys lounging in the sun, there are plenty of beautiful beaches around the world where you and your pet can create unforgettable summer memories. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best pet-friendly beaches for summer fun, so grab your sunscreen, pack some treats for your pet, and let’s dive in!

1. Cannon Beach, Oregon
Located along the breathtaking Oregon coast, Cannon Beach is a paradise for pets and their owners. This scenic beach is famous for its iconic Haystack Rock and miles of sandy shoreline where dogs can run off-leash and play to their heart’s content. With its picturesque views and pet-friendly accommodations, Cannon Beach is the perfect destination for a summer retreat with your four-legged companion.

2. Huntington Dog Beach, California
For a true doggy paradise, look no further than Huntington Dog Beach in sunny California. This one-mile stretch of coastline is dedicated to off-leash canine fun, making it a haven for dogs who love to socialize and frolic in the surf. The beach is equipped with amenities such as dog waste bags and water stations, ensuring a comfortable experience for both pets and their owners. Soak up the California sun and let your pup make a splash at this ultimate pet-friendly destination.

3. Jekyll Island, Georgia
If you’re seeking a more laid-back and serene beach experience, Jekyll Island in Georgia is the place to be. This secluded island boasts pet-friendly beaches where leashed dogs are welcome to explore the pristine sands and frolic in the gentle waves. Take a leisurely stroll with your pet along Driftwood Beach, known for its unique driftwood sculptures, and relish the tranquility of this hidden gem.

4. Fort De Soto Park, Florida
For pet owners in Florida, Fort De Soto Park near St. Petersburg is a must-visit destination. This award-winning park features not one, but two dog beaches where pets can romp off-leash in designated areas. With its calm, shallow waters and soft sand, Fort De Soto Park offers a safe and enjoyable environment for pets to cool off and play, while their owners can unwind and soak in the stunning coastal scenery.

5. Montrose Dog Beach, Illinois
In the heart of Chicago, pet owners can treat their furry companions to a day of sun, sand, and socializing at Montrose Dog Beach. This vibrant and lively beach provides a designated off-leash area where dogs can mingle and make new friends while enjoying the refreshing waters of Lake Michigan. After a day of play, explore the nearby walking trails and pet-friendly cafes for a well-rounded summer outing.

6. Cornwall, England
Venture across the pond to Cornwall, where you’ll find an array of pet-friendly beaches along the rugged and picturesque coastline. From the golden sands of Perranporth Beach to the hidden coves of Porthkidney Beach, Cornwall offers a diverse range of coastal landscapes for pets and their owners to explore. With pet-friendly accommodations and welcoming locals, Cornwall is an ideal destination for a pet-inclusive summer holiday.

7. Block Island, Rhode Island
Escape to the charming and laid-back atmosphere of Block Island, located just off the coast of Rhode Island. This idyllic island is home to pet-friendly beaches such as Ballard’s Beach and Crescent Beach, where leashed dogs can accompany their owners on leisurely strolls and enjoy the soothing sound of the ocean waves. With its unspoiled natural beauty and pet-welcoming community, Block Island is a delightful summer escape for both pets and their human companions.

When planning your pet-friendly beach adventure, remember to prioritize safety and comfort for your pet. Be mindful of local regulations and always clean up after your pet to ensure a positive experience for everyone. Additionally, consider packing essentials such as water, a collapsible bowl, and shade for your pet to stay cool and hydrated during your beach excursion.

With these top pet-friendly beaches in mind, you and your furry friend are all set for a summer of sun, sand, and sea. So, grab your beach gear, leash up your pet, and get ready to create cherished memories at these fabulous destinations. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply some quality bonding time with your pet, these beaches offer the perfect setting for an unforgettable summer getaway. Get ready to make a splash and bask in the joy of a pet-friendly beach vacation!
