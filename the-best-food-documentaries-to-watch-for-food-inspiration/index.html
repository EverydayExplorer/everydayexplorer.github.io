The Best Food Documentaries to Watch for Food Inspiration


Are you a food enthusiast looking for some inspiration in the kitchen? Look no further! We have rounded up a list of the best food documentaries that will not only tantalize your taste buds but also provide you with a fresh perspective on the culinary world. From mouth-watering dishes to thought-provoking stories, these documentaries are sure to ignite your passion for food. So grab some popcorn and get ready for a delectable movie night!

1. ‘Jiro Dreams of Sushi’ (2011)
This critically acclaimed documentary takes us on a mesmerizing journey into the world of Jiro Ono, an 85-year-old sushi master. Through stunning visuals and intimate interviews, we witness Jiro’s relentless pursuit of perfection as he prepares his sushi creations. This film not only showcases the artistry of sushi-making but also explores the dedication and passion required to excel in any culinary field.

2. ‘Chef’s Table’ (2015 - Present)
If you’re looking for a series that will introduce you to some of the world’s most innovative and influential chefs, ‘Chef’s Table’ is a must-watch. Each episode focuses on a different chef, providing an in-depth look into their background, philosophy, and signature dishes. With stunning cinematography and captivating storytelling, this series is a feast for the eyes and the soul.

3. ‘Food, Inc.’ (2008)
Prepare to have your eyes opened to the realities of the food industry with this eye-opening documentary. ‘Food, Inc.’ sheds light on the corporate control of our food system, highlighting the impact on our health, environment, and economy. This thought-provoking film will make you question where your food comes from and encourage you to make more informed choices about what you eat.

4. ‘Somm’ (2012)
For wine enthusiasts and aspiring sommeliers, ‘Somm’ is a fascinating exploration of the world of wine tasting and the rigorous process of becoming a master sommelier. The film follows four individuals on their journey to pass the prestigious Master Sommelier exam, showcasing the dedication, knowledge, and sensory skills required to excel in the world of wine.

5. ‘Salt Fat Acid Heat’ (2018)
Based on the best-selling book by Samin Nosrat, this four-part series explores the fundamental elements of good cooking: salt, fat, acid, and heat. Through travel and culinary adventures, Nosrat delves into different cultures and cuisines, showcasing how these four elements are the building blocks of delicious dishes. This visually stunning series is both educational and entertaining, leaving you inspired to experiment in your own kitchen.

6. ‘Barbecue’ (2017)
If you’re a fan of smoky flavors and mouth-watering barbecued meats, ‘Barbecue’ is a documentary that will transport you to different corners of the world to explore the art of cooking over an open flame. From traditional pitmasters in the United States to indigenous tribes in Namibia, this film celebrates the universal love for barbecue and the cultural significance behind this culinary tradition.

These food documentaries are not only entertaining but also serve as a valuable resource for food enthusiasts and aspiring chefs. So, whether you’re looking for inspiration, want to learn more about the food industry, or simply enjoy a visual feast, these films are a must-watch. Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure like no other and let these documentaries take your love for food to new heights!
