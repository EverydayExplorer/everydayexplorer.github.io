The Best Fashion Tips for Boyish Figures


Boyish figures, also known as rectangular or athletic body types, are characterized by minimal curves, with shoulders and hips being nearly the same width. While this body shape is often coveted by many, it can sometimes be a challenge to find clothing that enhances and flatters boyish figures. However, with a few key fashion tips, individuals with boyish figures can embrace their shape and create stylish and feminine looks. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best fashion tips for boyish figures, including outfit ideas and key styling techniques.

1. Create the Illusion of Curves:
One of the main goals for individuals with boyish figures is to create shape and add curves. This can be achieved by choosing clothing pieces that enhance the bust and hips. Opt for tops with ruffles, pleats, or gathering around the bust area to add volume and create the illusion of curves. Similarly, choose skirts and pants with details such as peplum, pockets, or embellishments around the hips to add dimension and create a more feminine silhouette.

2. Embrace Tailoring and Structure:
Well-tailored clothing is key when it comes to flattering a boyish figure. Invest in pieces that provide structure and definition, such as blazers, fitted dresses, and tailored pants. These items will help create a more defined waistline and add curves to your figure. Additionally, don’t shy away from belts or cinching your waist with a statement belt as it can accentuate your curves in all the right places.

3. Experiment with Patterns and Textures:
Patterns and textures can also play a significant role in enhancing a boyish figure. Opt for dresses, tops, or skirts with bold prints, such as floral, polka dots, or geometric designs. These patterns will add visual interest and draw attention away from a straighter figure. Mix and match different textures, such as lace, velvet, or sequins, to add depth and dimension to your outfits.

4. Play with Proportions:
Mixing different proportions can help create the illusion of curves and add interest to your overall look. For instance, pairing a structured blazer with a flowy skirt or opting for high-waisted pants with a cropped top can create a balance between masculine and feminine elements. Experimenting with different lengths and volumes will help you create more visual excitement in your outfits.

5. Add Layers and Accessories:
Layering can be a great technique to add depth and dimension to an outfit. Consider adding a structured jacket, cardigan, or vest over your tops to create more interest. Similarly, adding accessories such as scarves, statement jewelry, or belts can help draw attention to specific areas and add a touch of femininity to your look.

6. Choose the Right Fabrics:
When dressing a boyish figure, it’s essential to select fabrics that flatter your body type. Opt for softer, flowing fabrics, such as silk, chiffon, or satin, to add movement and create a more feminine look. These materials will drape nicely over your body, creating curves and adding a touch of elegance to your outfits. Avoid stiff fabrics that may accentuate your straighter figure.

7. Opt for A-Line Skirts and Dresses:
A-line skirts and dresses are a great option for boyish figures. These styles cinch at the waist and flare out, creating the illusion of curves and adding feminine flair to your look. Pair them with a fitted top or blouse to highlight your waist and complete the hourglass effect.

In conclusion, boyish figures can be stylishly enhanced and flattered with the right clothing choices and styling techniques. Embrace your shape by creating the illusion of curves, experimenting with patterns and textures, playing with proportions, adding layers and accessories, choosing the right fabrics, and opting for A-line skirts and dresses. With these fashion tips, you can confidently dress in a way that celebrates your unique body shape while looking chic and feminine.
