The Best Animated Netflix Shows to Watch


Welcome to our blog! Today, we’re diving into the captivating world of animated shows on Netflix. Whether you’re a fan of heartwarming stories, thrilling adventures, or thought-provoking narratives, Netflix has an impressive repertoire of animated shows that cater to every taste. So, grab your popcorn and get ready to explore the best animated Netflix shows to watch!

1. “BoJack Horseman”:
Kicking off our list is the critically acclaimed “BoJack Horseman.” This bittersweet adult animated series follows the life of a washed-up sitcom star, BoJack Horseman, as he navigates his way through the highs and lows of fame and personal struggles. With its clever humor, complex characters, and poignant exploration of mental health, “BoJack Horseman” is a must-watch.

2. “Avatar: The Last Airbender”:
Prepare to be transported to the fantastical world of the Four Nations with “Avatar: The Last Airbender.” This beloved animated series follows the journey of Aang, the last surviving Airbender, as he embarks on a quest to master all four elements and bring balance to the world. With its rich storytelling, diverse characters, and stunning animation, “Avatar: The Last Airbender” is a timeless masterpiece.

3. “The Dragon Prince”:
From the creators of “Avatar: The Last Airbender” comes “The Dragon Prince,” an epic tale set in a magical land of humans, elves, and dragons. Filled with captivating lore, complex relationships, and jaw-dropping animation, this show follows two human princes and an elf as they band together to end an ancient conflict and restore peace. “The Dragon Prince” is a visual treat that will leave you eagerly awaiting each new season.

4. “Castlevania”:
Prepare for a dark and thrilling adventure with “Castlevania,” a Netflix original animated series based on the popular video game franchise. Set in a world filled with supernatural beings, this show follows the last surviving member of the Belmont clan as he battles against Dracula and his army of demons. With its stunning animation, intense action sequences, and compelling storyline, “Castlevania” is a must-watch for fans of gothic horror.

5. “She-Ra and the Princesses of Power”:
Get ready to embark on an empowering journey with “She-Ra and the Princesses of Power.” This reimagining of the classic 1980s series follows Adora, a young soldier, as she discovers a magical sword that transforms her into She-Ra, the Princess of Power. Together with her newfound friends, she fights against the evil Horde to save the world. With its diverse cast, themes of friendship and self-discovery, and captivating animation, “She-Ra and the Princesses of Power” is perfect for both kids and adults.

6. “Big Mouth”:
If you’re in the mood for a hilarious and unabashedly honest coming-of-age series, look no further than “Big Mouth.” This adult animated show tackles the awkwardness and challenges of puberty through a group of middle school friends. With its witty writing, talented voice cast, and clever exploration of sensitive topics, “Big Mouth” strikes the perfect balance between comedy and relatable storytelling.

7. “Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts”:
Prepare for an enchanting post-apocalyptic adventure with “Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts.” Set in a future where mutated animals roam the Earth’s surface, this show follows a young girl named Kipo as she embarks on a quest to find her missing father. With its vibrant animation, diverse characters, and memorable soundtrack, “Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts” is a hidden gem that deserves more recognition.

In conclusion, Netflix offers a wide array of animated shows that cater to diverse audiences. Whether you’re in the mood for laughter, thrilling escapades, or thought-provoking stories, these shows have got you covered. So, sit back, relax, and allow these captivating animated series to transport you to new and exciting worlds. Happy binge-watching!
