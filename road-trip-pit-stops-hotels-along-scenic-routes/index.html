Road Trip Pit Stops: Hotels Along Scenic Routes


Are you ready to hit the open road and explore some of the most picturesque landscapes our country has to offer? A road trip along scenic routes is an adventure like no other, and one of the key aspects of a successful road trip is finding the perfect pit stops along the way. When it comes to road trip pit stops, choosing the right hotels can make all the difference in turning your journey into a memorable experience. In this blog post, we’ll take a look at some of the best hotels along scenic routes, offering comfort, convenience, and breathtaking views that will elevate your road trip to a whole new level.

1. Pacific Coast Highway, California:
If you’re planning a road trip along the iconic Pacific Coast Highway, you’re in for a treat. This breathtaking route offers stunning views of the Pacific Ocean, rugged cliffs, and beautiful coastal towns. As you make your way through this scenic drive, consider making a pit stop at the luxurious Post Ranch Inn. Perched on the cliffs of Big Sur, this award-winning hotel offers unparalleled views of the coastline, along with world-class amenities and exceptional service. Whether you choose a cozy room or a spacious suite, you’ll be treated to an unforgettable experience that perfectly complements your coastal road trip.

2. Blue Ridge Parkway, North Carolina and Virginia:
The Blue Ridge Parkway is renowned for its natural beauty, with winding roads that lead through the majestic Blue Ridge Mountains. As you navigate this picturesque route, take a break at The Omni Grove Park Inn in Asheville, North Carolina. This historic resort provides a perfect blend of modern luxury and timeless charm, making it an ideal pit stop for road trippers seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. After a day of driving along the parkway, unwind in the hotel’s renowned spa, indulge in exceptional dining options, and revel in the panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

3. Going-to-the-Sun Road, Montana:
Montana’s Going-to-the-Sun Road traverses Glacier National Park, offering breathtaking vistas of snow-capped peaks, pristine lakes, and lush forests. For a memorable stay along this scenic route, consider booking a room at the Many Glacier Hotel. Situated on the shores of Swiftcurrent Lake, this historic hotel exudes old-world charm and provides a perfect base for exploring the natural wonders of Glacier National Park. Whether you’re embarking on a day hike or simply soaking in the awe-inspiring scenery, the Many Glacier Hotel offers a serene and enchanting retreat amidst nature’s grandeur.

4. Overseas Highway, Florida Keys:
Driving along the Overseas Highway in the Florida Keys is a journey marked by turquoise waters, swaying palms, and an inviting island atmosphere. To enhance your road trip experience, consider a stop at The Moorings Village and Spa in Islamorada. This tropical oasis offers a tranquil escape with its private beach, lush gardens, and stylish accommodations. Whether you’re seeking a romantic getaway or a family-friendly retreat, The Moorings Village and Spa provides an idyllic setting to unwind and savor the laid-back charm of the Florida Keys.

5. Historic Columbia River Highway, Oregon:
The Historic Columbia River Highway winds through the stunning Columbia River Gorge, showcasing dramatic waterfalls, lush forests, and awe-inspiring vistas. As you explore this scenic route, make a pit stop at the Columbia Gorge Hotel & Spa in Hood River. This historic hotel, perched on a scenic bluff overlooking the Columbia River, offers a perfect blend of elegance and natural beauty. From the hotel’s gorge-facing rooms to its renowned gardens, you’ll find yourself immersed in a captivating environment that perfectly complements the splendor of the surrounding landscape.

In conclusion, a road trip along scenic routes presents a wonderful opportunity to discover the beauty of our natural surroundings and create lasting memories. By choosing the right hotels as pit stops along your journey, you can elevate your road trip experience to new heights. From coastal getaways to mountain retreats, these hotels offer the perfect combination of comfort, convenience, and spectacular views, ensuring that your road trip is not just a journey, but an unforgettable adventure. So, pack your bags, hit the road, and get ready to embark on an extraordinary road trip filled with scenic wonders and enchanting accommodations.
