The Top Gaming Influencers to Follow for Reviews and Recommendations


If you’re a gaming enthusiast, you know how important it is to stay up-to-date with the latest releases, trends, and recommendations in the gaming industry. With countless games being released every year, it can be overwhelming to choose which ones are worth your time and money. That’s where gaming influencers come in. These individuals have established themselves as authorities in the gaming community, providing honest and insightful reviews, recommendations, and gameplay content. In this blog post, we’ll introduce you to some of the top gaming influencers you should follow for the best gaming advice.

1. The Completionist

The Completionist, also known as Jirard Khalil, is a gaming influencer who focuses on completing every aspect of a game before giving a detailed review. With his meticulous approach, he provides comprehensive insights into a game’s mechanics, story, and overall experience. Whether you’re interested in retro classics or the latest releases, The Completionist’s reviews will help you make informed decisions.

2. AngryJoeShow

If you’re looking for honest and passionate game reviews, look no further than AngryJoeShow. Joe Vargas, the man behind the channel, has built a reputation for his no-nonsense approach to gaming critique. From AAA titles to indie gems, AngryJoeShow covers a wide range of games, ensuring you get unbiased recommendations and reviews.

3. Outside Xbox

Outside Xbox is a channel that offers a unique blend of gaming news, let’s plays, and top 10 lists. With a team of charismatic hosts, they provide entertaining and informative content that caters to both casual and hardcore gamers. Their reviews are insightful and their recommendations cover a variety of genres, making it a must-follow channel for any gaming enthusiast.

4. Game Informer

Game Informer is more than just a magazine; it’s a reliable source of gaming news and reviews. With a team of experienced journalists, Game Informer delivers in-depth analysis, interviews, and feature articles that cover all aspects of the gaming industry. Whether you’re interested in console gaming, PC gaming, or mobile gaming, Game Informer has got you covered.

5. TotalBiscuit (RIP)

Although TotalBiscuit, also known as John Bain, passed away in 2018, his influence on the gaming community is undeniable. Known for his detailed reviews and “WTF is…?” series, TotalBiscuit was admired for his honesty and integrity. While he may no longer be with us, his legacy lives on, and his content continues to be a valuable resource for gamers.

6. Kinda Funny Games

Kinda Funny Games is a YouTube channel and podcast network that offers a mix of gaming news, reviews, and discussions. With a team of experienced hosts, Kinda Funny Games covers a wide range of gaming topics, providing valuable insights and recommendations. Whether you’re interested in industry news or game reviews, Kinda Funny Games is a channel that should be on your radar.

7. Eurogamer

Eurogamer is a reputable gaming website that covers all things gaming-related. With a team of talented writers, Eurogamer offers in-depth reviews, previews, and features that delve into the heart of the gaming industry. Whether you’re a fan of AAA blockbusters or indie darlings, Eurogamer’s diverse content will keep you informed and entertained.

Conclusion

In the vast world of gaming, it’s essential to have reliable sources for reviews and recommendations. The gaming influencers mentioned in this blog post have proven themselves to be authorities in the industry, providing valuable insights and honest opinions. By following these influencers, you’ll stay informed about the latest releases, trends, and gaming news, ensuring that you make the most out of your gaming experience. So, go ahead and give them a follow, and get ready to level up your gaming knowledge!
