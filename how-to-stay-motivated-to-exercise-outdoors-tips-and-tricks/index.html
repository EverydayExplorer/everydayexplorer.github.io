How to Stay Motivated to Exercise Outdoors: Tips and Tricks


When it comes to exercise, it can be easy to fall into a routine and lose motivation. This is especially true when it comes to exercising outdoors. With so many distractions and factors that can deter us from getting outside and breaking a sweat, it’s important to have a few tricks up our sleeves to stay motivated and committed to our outdoor fitness routine.

Here are some tips and tricks to help you stay motivated to exercise outdoors:

1. Set clear goals: One of the first steps to staying motivated for outdoor exercise is to set clear and specific goals. Whether it’s running a certain distance, improving your cardiovascular endurance, or training for a specific event, having a clear goal in mind can help keep you focused and motivated.

2. Find a workout buddy: Exercising with a friend or joining a group can make your outdoor workouts more enjoyable and motivating. Not only will you have someone to hold you accountable, but you’ll also have the opportunity to socialize and make exercise a fun and social activity.

3. Mix up your routine: Doing the same workout every day can get monotonous and lead to a loss of motivation. To keep things interesting, mix up your outdoor exercise routine. Try different activities such as hiking, cycling, swimming, or even outdoor yoga. This will not only keep you excited about your workouts but also challenge different muscle groups and prevent burnout.

4. Utilize technology: Nowadays, there are plenty of fitness apps and devices available that can help you track your progress, set goals, and even connect with other outdoor fitness enthusiasts. Use technology to your advantage and make it a part of your outdoor exercise routine. Tracking your workouts and seeing your progress can be a great motivator in itself.

5. Dress for success: Investing in comfortable and weather-appropriate workout gear is essential for staying motivated to exercise outdoors. Make sure you have the right clothing and footwear for different weather conditions, as this will not only keep you comfortable but also make you more likely to get out and exercise.

6. Set a schedule and stick to it: Treat your outdoor exercise routine as you would any other appointment or commitment. Set a specific time each day or week for your outdoor workouts and stick to it as much as possible. By incorporating exercise into your daily routine and making it non-negotiable, you’ll be more likely to stay motivated and consistent.

7. Take advantage of nature: One of the biggest perks of exercising outdoors is the beautiful scenery and fresh air. Take the time to connect with nature during your workouts by exploring different parks, trails, or beaches in your area. Challenge yourself to find new routes and embrace the natural beauty around you. This can not only make your workouts more enjoyable but also serve as a great stress reliever.

8. Reward yourself: Celebrate your accomplishments and milestones along the way. Whether it’s treating yourself to a new workout outfit or indulging in a healthy post-workout snack, having rewards to look forward to can provide that extra push of motivation.

9. Keep a fitness journal: Keep track of your workouts, progress, and how you feel after each outdoor exercise session. Not only will this help you stay accountable, but it will also show you how far you’ve come and serve as a reminder of the positive benefits of exercising outdoors.

10. Focus on the benefits: Lastly, always keep in mind the numerous benefits of exercising outdoors. From improved mood and mental health to increased vitamin D absorption, outdoor exercise offers a multitude of advantages for your overall well-being. Remind yourself of these benefits on days when motivation runs low.

In conclusion, staying motivated to exercise outdoors requires a combination of setting goals, finding community support, mixing up your routine, utilizing technology, dressing appropriately, sticking to a schedule, connecting with nature, rewarding yourself, keeping track of your progress, and focusing on the benefits. By incorporating these tips and tricks into your outdoor exercise routine, you’ll be well on your way to staying motivated and reaping the rewards of exercising in the great outdoors. So, lace up your sneakers, grab your sunscreen, and embrace the many opportunities for outdoor fitness that await you!
