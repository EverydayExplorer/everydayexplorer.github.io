Just for Laughs: The Best Comedy Festivals Around the World


Comedy has a way of bringing people together through humor and laughter. One of the best ways to experience the power of comedy on a global scale is by attending a comedy festival. Just for Laughs is one such renowned comedy festival that has been entertaining audiences for over three decades in Montreal, Canada. However, Just for Laughs is just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to comedy festivals around the world. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best comedy festivals that are guaranteed to tickle your funny bone and leave you in stitches.

  1. Edinburgh Festival Fringe (Edinburgh, Scotland)

The Edinburgh Festival Fringe is the world’s largest arts festival, featuring a wide array of comedy shows along with theater, dance, and music performances. Comedians from all over the globe flock to Edinburgh every August to showcase their talents and entertain audiences with their wit and humor. With hundreds of shows to choose from, there’s something for everyone at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe.

  1. Melbourne International Comedy Festival (Melbourne, Australia)

The Melbourne International Comedy Festival is one of the biggest and most prestigious comedy festivals in the world. Held annually in March and April, the festival features a lineup of top-notch comedians from Australia and beyond. With over 600 shows spread across more than 100 venues, the Melbourne International Comedy Festival offers a diverse range of comedic styles and performances.

  1. Montreux Comedy Festival (Montreux, Switzerland)

The Montreux Comedy Festival takes place in the picturesque town of Montreux on the shores of Lake Geneva. The festival features a mix of stand-up comedy, sketch shows, and improv performances by some of the best comedians in the business. With its stunning backdrop and world-class lineup, the Montreux Comedy Festival is a must-visit for comedy fans looking for a unique and unforgettable experience.

  1. Bridgetown Comedy Festival (Portland, Oregon, USA)

The Bridgetown Comedy Festival is a four-day event that takes place in the vibrant city of Portland, Oregon. The festival showcases a mix of up-and-coming talent and established comedians, making it a hotbed for discovering new comedic voices. With a laid-back atmosphere and a diverse lineup of shows, the Bridgetown Comedy Festival has earned a reputation as one of the best comedy festivals in the United States.

  1. Just for Laughs (Montreal, Canada)

No list of the best comedy festivals would be complete without mentioning Just for Laughs. Founded in 1983, Just for Laughs has grown to become one of the most prestigious and well-respected comedy festivals in the world. The festival features a mix of stand-up shows, galas, and comedy competitions, attracting top comedians and comedy fans from around the globe. With its infectious energy and non-stop laughter, Just for Laughs is a celebration of all things comedy.

Whether you’re a die-hard comedy fan or just looking for a good laugh, attending a comedy festival is a surefire way to have a great time. From the bustling streets of Edinburgh to the serene shores of Montreux, these comedy festivals offer a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the world of comedy and discover new comedic talents. So pack your bags, grab your tickets, and get ready to laugh until your sides hurt at one of the best comedy festivals around the world.
