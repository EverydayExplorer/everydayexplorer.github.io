The Best Hunting Shows to Watch for Tips and Inspiration


If you’re an avid hunter or simply someone fascinated by the great outdoors, there’s no better way to gain insights, tips, and inspiration than by tuning into hunting shows. These programs not only showcase thrilling hunts but also delve into techniques, conservation efforts, and the rich traditions of the hunting community. Here, we explore some of the best hunting shows that will not only entertain you but also enhance your skills and knowledge as a hunter.

1. MeatEater

Hosted by the charismatic and knowledgeable Steven Rinella, MeatEater is a must-watch for anyone interested in hunting and cooking. The show takes viewers on a journey through diverse landscapes, showcasing various hunting techniques for different game species. What sets MeatEater apart is its focus on ethical hunting and conservation. Rinella emphasizes the importance of respecting wildlife and the environment, making it an educational experience for both novice and seasoned hunters alike.

The show also features segments on field dressing, processing game, and preparing delicious meals with harvested meat, providing practical tips that you can apply in your own hunts. Whether you’re interested in big game, small game, or fishing, MeatEater covers it all with a unique blend of humor and respect for nature.

2. Duck Dynasty

While Duck Dynasty is primarily a reality television series, it has become a cultural phenomenon that celebrates hunting and the outdoors. The show follows the Robertson family, who run a successful duck call business while maintaining their deep-rooted hunting traditions. The humorous interactions between family members, combined with their hunting escapades, make for entertaining viewing.

Although Duck Dynasty primarily focuses on duck hunting, the show also touches on themes of family, faith, and the importance of camaraderie in the outdoors. Viewers can glean tips on duck hunting strategies, gear, and the significance of habitat conservation, all while enjoying the lighthearted antics of the Robertson clan.

3. Alone

For those who crave a challenge, Alone takes survival to the next level. This reality competition series drops contestants in remote locations where they must survive with limited supplies. While not exclusively a hunting show, participants often rely on their hunting and foraging skills to sustain themselves.

Watching Alone provides invaluable insights into wilderness survival techniques, including tracking, trapping, and fishing. The show emphasizes the importance of adaptability and resilience, qualities that are essential for any hunter. It’s an inspiring reminder of the skills that can be honed through practice and perseverance in the great outdoors.

4. The Hunting Public

If you’re looking for raw and authentic hunting experiences, The Hunting Public is worth tuning into. This YouTube series features a group of friends who document their hunting adventures across the United States, focusing on public land hunting. Their down-to-earth approach and relatable storytelling resonate with viewers, making hunting accessible to everyone.

The Hunting Public emphasizes the importance of scouting, understanding animal behavior, and using the right equipment for various hunting scenarios. The tips shared in each episode are practical and applicable, providing valuable lessons for both beginners and experienced hunters. With a focus on transparency and authenticity, this show fosters a sense of community among hunters.

5. The Sportsman Channel’s “Into the Wild”

Into the Wild, hosted by renowned outdoorsman and conservationist, is a captivating show that explores the relationship between humans and nature. Each episode takes viewers on a journey to some of the most breathtaking locations around the world, showcasing both the beauty of the wilderness and the thrill of the hunt.

This show goes beyond just hunting; it delves into the cultural and ecological aspects of outdoor life. The host often interviews local hunters and conservationists, providing a broader perspective on the challenges and rewards of hunting. The emphasis on ethical practices and sustainability makes Into the Wild a valuable resource for anyone looking to deepen their understanding of hunting and conservation.

6. Wild & Whole

For those who appreciate the culinary side of hunting, Wild & Whole is a delightful show that focuses on preparing and cooking wild game. Hosted by Chef Hank Shaw, the series celebrates the farm-to-table movement by showcasing how to transform harvested game into delicious meals.

Shaw shares recipes, cooking techniques, and tips for sourcing local ingredients, making this show perfect for hunters who want to elevate their cooking skills. Each episode highlights the importance of respecting the animal and the environment, reinforcing the connection between hunting and sustainable living. Wild & Whole is not just a hunting show; it’s a culinary adventure that inspires viewers to embrace the flavors of the wild.

7. Jim Shockey’s Hunting Adventures

Jim Shockey’s Hunting Adventures is a long-running series that takes viewers all over the globe in pursuit of various game species. Jim Shockey, a well-respected figure in the hunting community, brings his wealth of knowledge and experience to the show. Each episode features stunning landscapes, thrilling hunts, and insights into different hunting cultures.

Shockey’s focus on ethical hunting practices and conservation resonates throughout the series. He often discusses the importance of sustainable hunting and wildlife management, making this show both entertaining and educational. Viewers can learn valuable tips on gear selection, tracking techniques, and the significance of respecting the environment.

8. Outdoor Channel’s “Pursuit Channel”

The Pursuit Channel offers a variety of hunting shows that cater to different interests and hunting styles. From big game hunting to bowfishing, this channel has something for everyone. The diverse programming allows viewers to explore different techniques and gear while connecting with fellow outdoor enthusiasts.

One standout show on the Pursuit Channel is Hunting with the Stars, where celebrity hunters share their adventures and insights. This show not only entertains but also provides practical tips that can inspire viewers to embark on their own hunting journeys. The Pursuit Channel is a treasure trove of hunting content that fosters a sense of community among outdoor lovers.

9. Field & Stream’s “Adventure Bound”

Adventure Bound, produced by Field & Stream, takes viewers on thrilling hunting expeditions across the United States. Each episode showcases a different hunting adventure, from deer to turkey to waterfowl. The show emphasizes the importance of preparation, skill, and respect for the environment.

Adventure Bound often features expert guests who share tips and tricks for successful hunting. The blend of education and entertainment makes this show perfect for both beginners and seasoned hunters. By highlighting the beauty of outdoor adventures, Adventure Bound inspires viewers to explore the great outdoors and pursue their hunting passions.

10. The Bowhunter

For archery enthusiasts, The Bowhunter is a must-watch. This show focuses on the art and science of bowhunting, offering tips on equipment, techniques, and strategies for success. Each episode features experts who share their knowledge and experiences, making it an invaluable resource for anyone interested in bowhunting.

The Bowhunter also emphasizes the importance of ethical hunting practices and conservation efforts. Viewers will learn about the challenges and rewards of bowhunting, as well as the camaraderie that comes with being part of a dedicated community. This show is a celebration of the sport and an inspiration for those looking to hone their skills.

Conclusion

Hunting shows offer a unique blend of entertainment, education, and inspiration for anyone interested in the outdoors. From ethical hunting practices to culinary adventures, these programs provide valuable insights that can enhance your hunting experiences. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting, tuning into these shows can help you develop your skills, connect with the hunting community, and deepen your appreciation for nature.

So grab your gear, sit back, and let these hunting shows inspire your next adventure in the wild. Happy hunting!
