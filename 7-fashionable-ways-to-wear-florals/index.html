7 Fashionable Ways to Wear Florals


When it comes to fashion, there’s one print that never goes out of style - florals. Whether it’s a delicate daisy or a bold tropical print, florals can instantly add a touch of femininity and a breath of fresh air to any outfit. If you’re looking to incorporate florals into your wardrobe but don’t want to look too girly or outdated, fear not! There are plenty of fashionable ways to wear florals without sacrificing style or staying stuck in the past. In this article, we’ll explore 7 trendy ways to rock florals and stay fashion-forward.

1. Mix and Match: One of the key tricks to wearing florals is to mix and match different prints. Don’t be afraid to experiment with contrasting patterns and colors. For instance, pair a bold floral top with a striped skirt or mix different floral prints by wearing a floral blouse with a floral skirt. This unexpected combination adds an element of interest and adds depth to your outfit.

2. Floral Accessories: If you’re not quite ready to dive headfirst into the world of floral prints, start small by incorporating floral accessories into your look. A floral scarf, handbag, or even a pair of floral earrings can instantly elevate any outfit. This is a subtle yet stylish way to add florals to your wardrobe without overpowering your overall look.

3. Bold Florals: For those who are not afraid to make a statement, opt for bold and oversized floral prints. This trend has been spotted on runways and street style fashionistas alike. A maxi dress with large, vibrant flowers can make a stunning and eye-catching statement. Keep the rest of your outfit simple to let the florals take center stage.

4. Floral Bottoms: Floral pants, skirts, or shorts are a fun and trendy way to incorporate florals into your outfit. Pair a floral midi skirt with a plain white tee for a chic and effortless look, or go bold with floral-patterned culottes. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect floral bottoms for your personal style.

5. Edgy Florals: Who says florals can’t be edgy? Pair a floral print dress with a leather jacket and combat boots for a cool and unexpected look. Adding a touch of edge to your floral outfit balances the femininity of the print, creating a unique and fashion-forward ensemble.

6. Floral Outerwear: When the weather gets cooler, why not keep the floral trend going with a floral print outerwear piece? A floral bomber jacket or a trench coat with floral accents can instantly transform a basic outfit into a stylish one. Keep the rest of your outfit simple to allow the floral outerwear to be the focal point.

7. Monochrome Florals: If you’re not a fan of mixing prints, consider going with a monochrome floral outfit. Opt for a top and bottom with the same floral pattern for a cohesive and polished look. This style is timeless and elegant, perfect for any occasion.

In conclusion, florals are a versatile and timeless print that can be styled in countless fashionable ways. Whether you choose to mix and match, add floral accessories, go bold with oversized prints, or opt for more edgy ensembles, there’s a floral look for everyone. Experiment, have fun, and embrace the beauty of florals in your wardrobe. With these 7 fashionable ways to wear florals, you’re sure to turn heads and stay on-trend.
