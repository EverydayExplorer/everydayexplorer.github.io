The Most Anticipated Games of 2022: Get Ready for an Epic Year


Are you ready for an epic year of gaming? Well, get excited because 2022 is packed with highly anticipated games that are sure to blow your mind! From thrilling sequels to groundbreaking new titles, this is the year that gamers have been waiting for. In this blog, we’ll take a closer look at some of the most anticipated games of 2022, and why you should definitely keep an eye out for them.

1. ‘Elden Ring’: Developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, ‘Elden Ring’ is an action role-playing game that has already garnered immense attention. What makes this game so highly anticipated is the collaboration between renowned game director Hidetaka Miyazaki (known for the ‘Dark Souls’ series) and acclaimed author George R.R. Martin. Set in a vast open world, players will embark on a thrilling adventure filled with intricate lore, challenging combat, and stunning visuals.

2. ‘God of War: Ragnarok’: The sequel to the critically acclaimed ‘God of War’ (2018), ‘Ragnarok’ continues the story of Kratos and his son, Atreus. This action-adventure game, developed by Santa Monica Studio, transports players to the mystical world of Norse mythology, where they will face off against iconic gods and monsters. With its impressive gameplay mechanics, immersive storytelling, and breathtaking graphics, ‘God of War: Ragnarok’ is set to be an absolute game-changer.

3. ‘Horizon Forbidden West’: Guerrilla Games wowed the gaming community with the release of ‘Horizon Zero Dawn’ in 2017, and now they’re back with its highly anticipated sequel. ‘Horizon Forbidden West’ takes players on an epic journey with protagonist Aloy as she explores a post-apocalyptic world filled with gigantic robotic creatures and stunning landscapes. With upgraded visuals, a captivating storyline, and improved gameplay mechanics, this game promises to be an unforgettable experience.

4. ‘Starfield’: Bethesda Game Studios, the creators of the beloved ‘Elder Scrolls’ and ‘Fallout’ series, are venturing into space with their upcoming game, ‘Starfield’. As an open-world role-playing game set in a futuristic universe, ‘Starfield’ will offer players limitless exploration opportunities as they navigate the vastness of outer space. With the promise of rich storytelling, immersive gameplay, and Bethesda’s proven track record, ‘Starfield’ is a must-watch title for 2022.

5. ‘Metroid Dread’: Nintendo fans finally have something to look forward to with the release of ‘Metroid Dread’. This long-awaited continuation of the iconic Metroid series follows Samus Aran as she battles deadly aliens and explores mysterious landscapes. With its classic platforming gameplay and modern upgrades, ‘Metroid Dread’ will surely delight both longtime fans and newcomers to the franchise.

6. ‘Breath of the Wild 2’: The sequel to the critically acclaimed ‘Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild’ is on its way, and fans couldn’t be more excited. Building upon the successful formula of the original game, ‘Breath of the Wild 2’ promises to deliver even more innovative gameplay mechanics, a vast open world, and an engrossing story that will keep players hooked for hours on end. This is definitely a game that no Nintendo Switch owner will want to miss.

7. ‘Far Cry 6’: Ubisoft’s ‘Far Cry’ series has always been known for its stunning visuals, immersive gameplay, and gripping storytelling, and ‘Far Cry 6’ looks to continue that tradition. Set in the tropical paradise of Yara, players will step into the shoes of Dani Rojas, a guerrilla fighter, as they join the revolution against the oppressive rule of Anton Castillo. With an expansive open world, intense combat scenarios, and a formidable villain portrayed by Giancarlo Esposito, ‘Far Cry 6’ is shaping up to be an unforgettable gaming experience.

8. ‘Final Fantasy XVI’: Fans of the long-standing ‘Final Fantasy’ series have a lot to look forward to in 2022 with the release of ‘Final Fantasy XVI’. This highly anticipated role-playing game takes players on a journey through the land of Valisthea, where they will witness the clash between different realms and powerful mystical beings. With stunning graphics, a compelling storyline, and the promise of a return to the series’ roots, ‘Final Fantasy XVI’ is sure to capture the hearts of both longtime fans and newcomers.

These are just a few of the many highly anticipated games set to release in 2022. From action-packed adventures to immersive open worlds, this year’s lineup is going to be epic. So mark your calendars, get your gaming setup ready, and prepare for an unforgettable year of gaming! Whether you’re a fan of RPGs, action-adventure, or first-person shooters, there’s something for everyone in the exciting world of gaming in 2022. Get ready for an epic year!
