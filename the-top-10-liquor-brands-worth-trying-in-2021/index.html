The Top 10 Liquor Brands Worth Trying in 2021


Liquor aficionados are constantly on the lookout for new brands to try, and with so many options on the market, it can be challenging to narrow down the choices. To help you with your quest to find the best liquor options, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 liquor brands worth trying in 2021. From whiskey to tequila and everything in between, these brands offer exceptional quality and flavor profiles that are sure to impress even the most discerning palates.

1. The Macallan
The Macallan is a revered name in the whiskey world, known for its impeccable craftsmanship and outstanding Scotch whiskies. With a rich history dating back to 1824, the brand is famous for its exceptional single malt whiskies aged in sherry oak casks, producing flavors that are both complex and delightful.

2. Patron
If tequila is more to your liking, look no further than Patron. This premium tequila brand is widely recognized for its dedication to quality and craftsmanship. From their classic Silver tequila to the ultra-premium Gran Patron Burdeos, each expression is carefully crafted with the utmost attention to detail, resulting in a smooth and refined drinking experience.

3. Grey Goose
Vodka enthusiasts will be hard-pressed to find a brand more well-regarded than Grey Goose. With its origins in France, Grey Goose is made using the finest French ingredients and distilled in a unique copper pot still process. The result is a vodka known for its smoothness and versatility, making it a perfect choice for any cocktail.

4. Hendrick’s
For gin lovers, Hendrick’s is a brand that should not be missed. Distilled in Scotland, Hendrick’s gin stands out for its unique infusion of cucumber and rose petals, giving it a refreshing and floral taste that sets it apart from other gins on the market.

5. Jameson
No list of liquor brands would be complete without mentioning Jameson, one of the most iconic Irish whiskey brands. Triple distilled and aged in oak casks, Jameson prides itself on its smoothness and approachability. Whether you prefer it neat, on the rocks, or in a cocktail, Jameson is a reliable and classic choice.

6. Don Julio
Don Julio is another tequila brand that deserves attention. With its range of tequilas aged to perfection, Don Julio offers a wide array of flavors that cater to different preferences. From the Blanco, perfect for cocktails, to the luxury offering of their 1942 Añejo, Don Julio delivers a tequila experience that is both authentic and exceptional.

7. Bacardi
Known for its rum, Bacardi has been a staple in many bars across the globe. With its extensive range of rum offerings, Bacardi appeals to rum enthusiasts of all levels. From their award-winning Bacardi Superior to their aged rums like Bacardi 8 and Bacardi Reserva Limitada, the brand continues to impress with its diverse and quality products.

8. Glenfiddich
Glenfiddich is a renowned name in the single malt whiskey category. With a legacy spanning over a century, the brand is known for its innovative approach to whiskey making. From their iconic 12-year-old expression to their experimental range, Glenfiddich continually pushes the boundaries of what can be achieved in whiskey.

9. Ciroc
If you’re looking for a premium vodka with a twist, Ciroc is an excellent choice. Made from grapes instead of grains, Ciroc offers a smooth and distinctive flavor profile that sets it apart from other vodkas. With a variety of flavored options, including Apple, Peach, and Watermelon, Ciroc provides a range of options for those seeking a unique vodka experience.

10. Maker’s Mark
Last but certainly not least, Maker’s Mark is a bourbon brand that has gained a loyal following over the years. With its iconic red wax seal, Maker’s Mark is known for its consistent quality and rich flavors. Whether enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in a classic bourbon cocktail, Maker’s Mark offers a quintessential bourbon experience.

In conclusion, these top 10 liquor brands are worth trying in 2021 for their exceptional quality, craftsmanship, and diverse flavor profiles. Whether you’re a whiskey connoisseur, a tequila enthusiast, or an avid vodka drinker, there’s something on this list for everyone. So, go ahead and indulge in these renowned liquor brands and elevate your drinking experience. Cheers!
