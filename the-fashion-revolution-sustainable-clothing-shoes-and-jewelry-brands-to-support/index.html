The Fashion Revolution: Sustainable Clothing, Shoes, and Jewelry Brands to Support


With increasing awareness about the environmental and social impact of the fashion industry, many consumers are now looking for sustainable and ethical alternatives to mainstream fashion brands. The Fashion Revolution movement, which emerged in response to the Rana Plaza factory collapse in 2013, has been instrumental in raising awareness about the need for transparency and improved conditions in the fashion supply chain. In support of this movement, we have compiled a list of sustainable clothing, shoes, and jewelry brands that are leading the way in creating a more ethical and environmentally-friendly fashion industry.

1. Patagonia: Patagonia is a well-known outdoor clothing brand that has been committed to sustainability for decades. They are known for their high-quality clothing made from organic and recycled materials, as well as their transparent and ethical supply chain. Additionally, they donate a percentage of their profits to environmental organizations.

2. Eileen Fisher: Eileen Fisher is a women’s clothing brand that focuses on sustainability and timeless style. They prioritize using organic and sustainable materials such as organic cotton and Tencel, and also have recycling and take-back programs for their products. Their commitment to fair trade, worker welfare, and circularity makes them a leader in ethical fashion.

3. Veja: Veja is a French footwear brand that has gained popularity for its sustainable and ethically sourced sneakers. They use organic cotton, wild rubber, and other eco-friendly materials in their shoe production. Veja also prioritizes transparency and fair trade practices by working closely with their suppliers and paying fair wages.

4. Stella McCartney: Stella McCartney is a high-end fashion brand that is leading the way in sustainable luxury fashion. Their commitment to animal welfare and sustainability is evident in their use of innovative and eco-friendly materials like vegan leather. They are also known for their circular approach by creating products that can be easily repaired and recycled.

5. People Tree: People Tree is a pioneer in fair trade and sustainable fashion. They work directly with producers in developing countries to create ethical and stylish clothing collections made from organic and sustainable materials. People Tree’s commitment to fair trade and empowering women in the fashion industry sets them apart.

6. Pura Vida Bracelets: Pura Vida Bracelets is a jewelry brand that not only creates beautiful, handcrafted accessories but also supports artisans in Costa Rica and around the world. They prioritize ethical and sustainable production practices and also contribute to charitable causes through the sale of their bracelets.

7. Reformation: Reformation is a popular clothing brand that is known for its stylish and sustainable designs. They focus on using eco-friendly fabrics and have transparently shared their environmental impact through their ‘RefScale’ tool. Reformation’s commitment to sustainability extends to their supply chain and packaging practices.

8. Matt & Nat: Matt & Nat is a vegan and sustainable accessories brand that produces stylish handbags and wallets. They use sustainable materials like recycled nylon and vegan leather in their products. Matt & Nat’s commitment to animal welfare, sustainability, and style makes them a popular choice for conscious consumers.

9. Able: Able is a women’s clothing and accessories brand that is committed to empowering women through ethical and fair trade practices. They prioritize transparency by publishing the wages of their employees, ensuring fair working conditions, and providing job opportunities for vulnerable women.

10. Amour Vert: Amour Vert is a sustainable fashion brand that specializes in women’s clothing, emphasizing eco-friendly materials and ethical production practices. They are known for their use of organic cotton, low-impact dyes, and their ‘Buy A Tee, Plant a Tree’ initiative where they plant a tree for every t-shirt sold.

Supporting these sustainable clothing, shoes, and jewelry brands is a great way to contribute to the Fashion Revolution and promote sustainability in the fashion industry. By making conscious choices about the brands we support, we can help drive positive change towards a more sustainable future. Let’s join the Fashion Revolution and support ethical and eco-friendly fashion!

-–

In this blog post, we have highlighted ten sustainable clothing, shoes, and jewelry brands that are making a positive impact in the fashion industry. These brands have set the bar high when it comes to transparency, ethical production practices, and environmentally-friendly materials. By supporting these brands, we can all be a part of the Fashion Revolution and work towards a more sustainable and ethical future.
