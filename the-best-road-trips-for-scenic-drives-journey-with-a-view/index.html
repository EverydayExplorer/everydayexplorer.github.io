The Best Road Trips for Scenic Drives: Journey with a View


Are you tired of the same old mundane road trips? Are you craving a sense of adventure and a breathtaking view along the way? Look no further! In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to discover the best road trips for scenic drives. Buckle up and get ready to embark on an unforgettable adventure!

1. Pacific Coast Highway, California, USA:
Starting in sunny California, the Pacific Coast Highway is a must for any road trip enthusiast. This iconic route stretches over 600 miles along the stunning coastline, offering panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, rugged cliffs, and picturesque beaches. As you wind your way through charming towns like Santa Barbara, Big Sur, and Carmel, be prepared to be awestruck by the majestic beauty that surrounds you.

2. Ring Road, Iceland:
For a road trip like no other, head to Iceland’s Ring Road. This 1,332-kilometer loop will take you on a journey through a land of otherworldly landscapes. From cascading waterfalls and bubbling geysers to vast glaciers and volcanic black sand beaches, every turn will leave you breathless. Don’t forget to keep an eye out for the mystical Northern Lights dancing across the sky during the winter months.

3. The Great Ocean Road, Australia:
Prepare to be mesmerized by the dramatic coastal scenery of Australia’s Great Ocean Road. Stretching for 243 kilometers along the southeastern coast, this road trip showcases the best of Australia’s natural beauty. Marvel at the iconic Twelve Apostles, limestone stacks rising majestically from the turquoise sea. Take your time to explore the lush rainforests, charming seaside towns, and pristine beaches along the way.

4. Amalfi Coast, Italy:
Indulge in the beauty of the Amalfi Coast, a UNESCO World Heritage site in Italy. This scenic drive hugs the cliffs of the Sorrentine Peninsula, offering breathtaking views of the sparkling Mediterranean Sea. Wind your way through charming villages like Positano, Ravello, and Amalfi, where colorful houses cling to the cliffsides. Immerse yourself in the rich history, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality of this enchanting region.

5. Garden Route, South Africa:
Venture off the beaten path and explore the Garden Route in South Africa. This diverse road trip takes you through lush forests, pristine beaches, and rugged mountains. Along the way, encounter stunning wildlife, including elephants, whales, and penguins. Don’t miss the chance to explore the Tsitsikamma National Park, go wine tasting in the picturesque town of Stellenbosch, and experience the vibrant culture of Cape Town.

6. Trollstigen, Norway:
For an adrenaline-pumping road trip, conquer the legendary Trollstigen in Norway. Known as the ‘Troll’s Path,’ this mountain road will test your driving skills as you navigate hairpin bends and steep inclines. But the effort is well worth it, as you’ll be rewarded with awe-inspiring views of cascading waterfalls, snow-capped peaks, and deep fjords. This is a road trip that will leave you feeling like you’ve stepped into a fairytale.

7. The Garden Route, New Zealand:
Last but not least, explore the natural wonders of New Zealand’s South Island along the Garden Route. This road trip takes you through towering mountains, pristine lakes, and lush rainforests. Marvel at the breathtaking beauty of Milford Sound, hike in the majestic Fiordland National Park, and soak in the healing waters of the Hanmer Springs. New Zealand’s Garden Route is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

So, if you’re looking for a road trip that combines adventure, natural beauty, and unforgettable experiences, these are the best scenic drives to embark on. Take your time, soak in the views, and create memories that will last a lifetime. Happy road tripping!
