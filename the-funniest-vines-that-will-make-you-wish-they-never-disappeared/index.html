The Funniest Vines That Will Make You Wish They Never Disappeared


There’s no denying that online videos have taken the world by storm. From short snippets on YouTube to TikTok dances, people all around the globe have been captivated by the power of visual content. One platform that left a significant impact on the world of online videos was the now extinct Vine.

For those who aren’t familiar with Vine, it was a short-form video hosting service that allowed users to share looping six-second video clips. In its prime, Vine was home to a plethora of hilarious, creative, and downright bizarre content that left viewers coming back for more.

Although Vine may no longer exist, its impact on popular culture cannot be ignored. Many of the platform’s most popular videos have become legendary in the world of internet humor. Let’s take a trip down memory lane and revisit some of the funniest Vines that will make you wish they never disappeared.

1. ‘Why you always lyin’?’ - We can’t discuss Vine without mentioning this iconic phrase. The Vine begins innocently enough, with a man innocently asking his friend why he is always lying. However, the tone quickly changes as the man hilariously sings the same question over and over again. This Vine’s catchiness and humor made it an instant viral sensation.

2. ‘It’s an avocado!’ - This Vine features a young boy holding up an avocado and enthusiastically exclaiming, ‘It’s an avocado!’ The simplicity and innocence of this Vine made it incredibly endearing and relatable. Plus, who doesn’t love avocados?

3. ‘What are those?!’ - In this meme-worthy Vine, a man points his camera at a police officer’s shoes and yells, ‘What are those?!’ This particular Vine sparked a worldwide trend of people playfully mocking each other’s footwear choices.

4. ‘Hi, welcome to Chili’s’ - This Vine features a youngster hilariously imitating a Chili’s restaurant greeter. The child’s deadpan delivery and miniature size combined to create an unexpectedly funny moment.

5. ‘Back at it again at Krispy Kreme’ - This Vine showcases a man who can’t resist doing tricks outside a Krispy Kreme doughnut shop. He excitedly exclaims, ‘Back at it again!’ as he performs a series of acrobatic moves. The Vine’s energy and humor made it an instant hit.

6. ‘Is it real?’ - A Vine that left many viewers in stitches features a boy opening a refrigerator door and yelling, ‘Is it real?’ The comedic timing and unexpected twist of the Vine made it an unforgettable comedy gem.

7. ‘Freestyle singing in public’ - This Vine features a man walking around a park and randomly bursting into freestyle singing. His on-the-spot lyrics and reactions from passersby created a hilarious and memorable Vine.

8. ‘On all levels except physical, I am a wolf’ - In this absurd Vine, a person is asked about their goals, to which they reply, ‘On all levels except physical, I am a wolf.’ The sheer randomness and deadpan delivery of such an outrageous statement made this Vine both bizarre and side-splittingly funny.

9. ‘We could’ve had it all’ - This Vine is a perfect example of comedic genius in just six seconds. A girl dramatically lip-syncs to Adele’s famous song ‘Someone Like You’ while her friend throws a slice of bread at her face. The unexpected element and perfect timing of the Vine made it pure gold.

10. ‘Iridocyclitis’ - Last but definitely not least, this Vine features a young boy hilariously mispronouncing the term ‘iridocyclitis.’ The boy’s enthusiasm, mispronunciation, and contagious laughter made this Vine an instant classic.

These are just a few examples of the countless hilarious Vines that populated the platform during its peak. Although Vine may be gone, its impact on internet culture and the comedy landscape continues to be felt. It’s a testament to the power of short-form video content and the ability to create genuine connections through humor.

While Vine may have disappeared, its spirit lives on through platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels. The internet continues to produce an endless stream of funny video content, but Vine will always hold a special place in the hearts of those who experienced its golden age.

So, next time you find yourself scrolling through funny videos online, take a moment to appreciate the legacy of Vine. It may be gone, but its laughter-inducing clips will forever be etched in internet history.
