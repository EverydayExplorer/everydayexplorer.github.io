Top 10 Sports Documentaries to Watch Right Now


Are you a sports fan looking for something exciting to watch during your free time? Look no further than these top 10 sports documentaries that will keep you on the edge of your seat. From heartwarming tales of triumph to jaw-dropping tales of determination, these documentaries showcase the unparalleled power of sports in both inspiring and entertaining us. So, grab some popcorn, sit back, and enjoy these remarkable stories.

1. Hoop Dreams (1994)
Considered one of the greatest sports documentaries of all time, Hoop Dreams follows the lives of two young African-American high school students in Chicago who dream of becoming professional basketball players. This documentary delves deep into their struggles, aspirations, and the harsh realities they face on their journey.

2. Senna (2010)
Senna is a riveting documentary that tells the story of Ayrton Senna, the legendary Brazilian Formula One racing driver. Through incredible footage and interviews, this film offers a poignant look into the life and tragic death of a true sporting icon.

3. The Last Dance (2020)
If you’re a basketball fan, you cannot miss The Last Dance, which showcases the career of Michael Jordan and the Chicago Bulls during their extraordinary dynasty in the 1990s. This series offers a captivating behind-the-scenes look at one of the greatest sports teams in history.

4. O.J.: Made in America (2016)
Although not solely focused on sports, O.J.: Made in America explores the rise and fall of O.J. Simpson, the football star turned infamous criminal. This in-depth documentary examines the role of race and celebrity in America’s cultural landscape.

5. When We Were Kings (1996)
When We Were Kings provides a thrilling account of the historic boxing match known as the ‘Rumble in the Jungle’ between Muhammad Ali and George Foreman. This documentary captures the energy and excitement surrounding the fight, along with Ali’s larger-than-life personality.

6. Icarus (2017)
Icarus takes an unexpected twist when a filmmaker’s investigation into doping in sports leads him to a major international scandal involving Russia’s state-sponsored doping program. This eye-opening documentary highlights the extent of corruption within the world of professional sports.

7. The Two Escobars (2010)
Fusing together the worlds of soccer and criminality, The Two Escobars tells the parallel stories of Colombian soccer player Andres Escobar and drug lord Pablo Escobar. This captivating documentary explores the complex relationship between sports and society.

8. Free Solo (2018)
Free Solo follows professional rock climber Alex Honnold as he attempts to conquer the first ropeless climb of the notorious El Capitan in Yosemite National Park. This breathtaking documentary will leave you in awe of Honnold’s incredible athleticism and courage.

9. 30 for 30: O.J.: Made in America (2016)
Part of ESPN’s acclaimed ‘30 for 30’ series, this five-part documentary dives deep into the life and trial of O.J. Simpson. Through interviews and archival footage, it offers a comprehensive look at the cultural impact of the ‘Trial of the Century.’

10. The Battered Bastards of Baseball (2014)
For baseball enthusiasts, The Battered Bastards of Baseball tells the story of the Portland Mavericks, an independent minor league baseball team. This heartwarming documentary celebrates the underdog spirit and the importance of staying true to one’s passion.

These top 10 sports documentaries provide an immersive and captivating viewing experience for sports fans and documentary enthusiasts alike. Whether you’re a die-hard fan of a specific sport or simply interested in human stories of triumph and perseverance, these films are sure to leave a lasting impression. So, grab your remote, turn on your favorite streaming platform, and enjoy these incredible tales from the world of sports.
