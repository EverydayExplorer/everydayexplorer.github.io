Creating a Family Vision Board: Setting Goals and Dreams Together


Every family has dreams and aspirations, and creating a family vision board is a powerful way to bring those dreams to life. A family vision board is a visual representation of the goals and dreams that a family wants to achieve together. It’s a fun and creative activity that can bring everyone closer and help align the family towards a common purpose. In this blog post, we will explore the benefits of creating a family vision board, provide step-by-step guidance on how to make one, and offer tips on setting and achieving goals as a family.

Why Create a Family Vision Board?

Creating a family vision board is more than just a craft project – it’s a way to manifest your family’s hopes and dreams. By putting your goals and aspirations on display, you are reminding yourselves of what you are working towards. It serves as a constant visual reminder of the things you want to achieve as a family, whether it’s traveling the world, buying a house, or simply spending more quality time together. Moreover, creating a family vision board can foster communication, collaboration, and a sense of togetherness among family members.

How to Create a Family Vision Board

Now that we understand the significance of a family vision board, let’s delve into the steps to create one:

1. Set aside a dedicated time: Choose a day when all family members can participate in creating the vision board. This could be a weekend or any other day when everyone can come together without distractions.

2. Gather supplies: You’ll need a large poster board or corkboard, magazines, newspapers, scissors, glue, markers, and any other decorative items you’d like to include.

3. Reflect and brainstorm: Encourage each family member to think about their personal goals as well as the family’s collective ambitions. Reflect on what you want to achieve in the short-term and long-term.

4. Cut and paste: Go through the magazines and cut out images and words that represent your goals and dreams. Paste them on the board in a collage style.

5. Personalize and share: Encourage everyone to add personal touches to the vision board, such as drawings, photos, or written affirmations. After completing the board, take turns sharing the significance of the elements you included.

Setting and Achieving Goals as a Family

Once the family vision board is created, the next step is to use it as a tool for setting and achieving goals. Here are some tips for making the most of your family vision board:

1. Regularly revisit the board: Place the vision board in a prominent location at home, such as the living room or kitchen, where everyone can see it daily. This will serve as a constant reminder of your family’s goals and dreams.

2. Discuss progress and updates: Schedule periodic family meetings to discuss the progress towards your goals. Use these meetings to update the vision board with new goals or to celebrate achievements.

3. Take action as a family: Work together to turn your vision into reality. Whether it’s saving money for a family trip, volunteering together, or supporting each other in personal endeavors, taking action as a family reinforces your commitment to the shared vision.

4. Celebrate achievements: When a family goal is achieved, take the time to celebrate together. Acknowledge the hard work and dedication that went into reaching the goal and use it as motivation to pursue the next one.

In conclusion, creating a family vision board is a wonderful way to unite your family around common goals and dreams. It’s a visual representation of your shared vision for the future and can serve as a source of motivation and inspiration. By setting and working towards goals as a family, you are not only strengthening your bond but also creating lasting memories and experiences together. So gather your family, get creative, and start building your family vision board today!
