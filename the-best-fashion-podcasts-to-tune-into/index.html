The Best Fashion Podcasts to Tune Into


Are you a fashion enthusiast looking for a new way to stay up-to-date with the latest trends, industry news, and insider tips? Look no further than fashion podcasts! These audio gems have taken the fashion world by storm, offering a unique and convenient way to consume fashion content on the go. Whether you’re a seasoned fashionista or just starting to dip your toes into the world of style, here are some of the best fashion podcasts that you should definitely tune into.

1. “Dressed: The History of Fashion”

If you’re a history buff with a love for fashion, then “Dressed: The History of Fashion” is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this podcast takes you on a journey through the fascinating and often overlooked stories behind our clothes. From the evolution of the little black dress to the influence of wartime fashion, each episode explores a different aspect of fashion history, making it both educational and entertaining.

2. “Talking Fashion with Tim Gunn”

You may know Tim Gunn from his role as a mentor on “Project Runway,” but did you know that he also has a fashion podcast? In “Talking Fashion with Tim Gunn,” Tim sits down with industry insiders, designers, and fashion icons to discuss all things fashion. From behind-the-scenes stories to industry trends and personal anecdotes, Tim’s witty and insightful conversations make this podcast a must-listen for any fashion enthusiast.

3. “Fashion No Filter”

For those who crave an inside look at the fashion industry, “Fashion No Filter” is the podcast for you. Hosted by fashion journalists Camille Charriere and Monica Ainley, this podcast explores the unfiltered side of fashion. With interviews with industry insiders, discussions on sustainability, and in-depth analyses of fashion trends, “Fashion No Filter” offers a fresh and thought-provoking perspective on the world of fashion.

4. “The Cut on Tuesdays”

“The Cut on Tuesdays” is a podcast by New York Magazine’s The Cut, where fashion meets culture. Each episode delves into a different topic, from body positivity and inclusivity in fashion to the impact of social media on the industry. With a mix of interviews, personal stories, and cultural analysis, this podcast offers a well-rounded and dynamic view of the fashion world.

5. “American Fashion Podcast”

If you’re interested in the American fashion scene, “American Fashion Podcast” is a must-listen. Hosted by Charles Beckwith and Cathy Schepis, this podcast features interviews with designers, industry veterans, and influencers who share their insights and experiences. From the business side of fashion to emerging trends and the future of the industry, “American Fashion Podcast” covers it all.

6. “The Business of Fashion Podcast”

“The Business of Fashion Podcast” is an extension of the highly respected fashion publication, The Business of Fashion. Hosted by founder and editor-in-chief Imran Amed, this podcast explores the business side of the fashion industry. From interviews with top executives to discussions on the future of retail, this podcast offers valuable insights into the ever-changing world of fashion.

Conclusion

Fashion podcasts have become an invaluable resource for fashion enthusiasts, providing a wealth of knowledge, inspiration, and entertainment. Whether you’re interested in fashion history, industry insights, or simply staying up-to-date with the latest trends, these podcasts offer something for everyone. So grab your headphones, tune in, and let these fashion podcasts take your style game to the next level!

Please note that the mentioned podcasts are subject to change or discontinuation.
