Hotels with Private Helicopter Tours: Seeing the World from Above


In today’s fast-paced world, where travel is becoming increasingly accessible, people are constantly seeking unique and unforgettable experiences. One such experience that has gained immense popularity in recent years is taking private helicopter tours to explore the stunning landscapes and breathtaking views from above. And what better way to enhance this extraordinary adventure than by staying at hotels that offer private helicopter tours? In this blog post, we will dive into the world of hotels that provide this extraordinary service, allowing guests to see the world from above like never before.

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Bali: Nestled amidst the beauty of the Indonesian paradise, The Ritz-Carlton Bali offers an unparalleled experience to its guests. With its private helicopter tours, guests can soar above the pristine beaches, lush green rice terraces, and majestic volcanic peaks of Bali. The tour takes you on a journey to witness iconic landmarks such as Tanah Lot Temple and Uluwatu Temple, offering a bird’s-eye view of these cultural treasures.

2. The Plaza, New York: As an iconic symbol of luxury and sophistication, The Plaza in New York City takes its guests to new heights with its private helicopter tours. Take off from the helipad atop the hotel and embark on a mesmerizing journey over the city that never sleeps. Marvel at the breathtaking skyline, fly over famous landmarks like Central Park, and witness the magical lights of Times Square from above.

3. Emirates Palace, Abu Dhabi: Situated on the shores of the Arabian Gulf, the Emirates Palace in Abu Dhabi offers a luxurious escape like no other. With its private helicopter tours, guests can explore the vibrant city and its surrounding desert landscapes from a unique vantage point. Soar above the stunning sand dunes, witness the architectural marvels of the city, and catch a glimpse of the majestic Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque from above.

4. Singita Lebombo Lodge, South Africa: Located in the heart of the Kruger National Park, Singita Lebombo Lodge provides an unmatched safari experience. Enhance your stay at this luxurious lodge with a private helicopter tour, where you can witness Africa’s incredible wildlife from a whole new perspective. Soar above the vast plains, spot herds of elephants, lions, and giraffes, and marvel at the beauty of this untamed wilderness.

5. Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, Dubai: Known as the epitome of luxury, the Burj Al Arab Jumeirah in Dubai takes its guests on an extraordinary adventure with its private helicopter tours. Take off from the hotel’s helipad and fly over Dubai’s iconic landmarks, including the Palm Jumeirah, the Burj Khalifa, and the World Islands. Experience the glitz and glamour of this futuristic city from the sky.

These hotels not only provide luxurious accommodation but also offer an exclusive opportunity to explore the world from an entirely different perspective. With private helicopter tours, guests can witness stunning landscapes, iconic landmarks, and breathtaking views that are otherwise inaccessible. Whether it’s soaring above the tropical paradise of Bali or experiencing the vibrant cityscape of New York, these hotels ensure an unforgettable journey that will leave a lasting impression.

So, if you’re seeking an extraordinary adventure that combines luxury, thrill, and unparalleled views, consider staying at one of these hotels with private helicopter tours. Embark on a journey that will take you to new heights and allow you to see the world from above, creating memories that will last a lifetime.
