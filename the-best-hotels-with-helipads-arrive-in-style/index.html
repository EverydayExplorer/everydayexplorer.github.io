The Best Hotels with Helipads: Arrive in Style


When it comes to luxury travel, there’s nothing quite like arriving at your destination in style. And what better way to make a grand entrance than by helicopter? In this blog post, we’ll be exploring some of the best hotels around the world that offer helipads, allowing you to touch down in ultimate luxury and convenience.

1. Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, Dubai, UAE
Dubai is known for its opulence, and the Burj Al Arab Jumeirah is the epitome of luxury. This iconic hotel boasts a stunning helipad that extends out into the Arabian Gulf, offering breathtaking views of the city’s skyline. Arriving at this hotel by helicopter is a truly unforgettable experience.

2. Atlantis Paradise Island, Bahamas
Located on the picturesque Paradise Island in the Bahamas, Atlantis is a world-class resort that caters to both thrill-seekers and relaxation enthusiasts. With its own helipad, you can fly directly to the resort and start your vacation in style. Soar above the crystal-clear waters and lush landscapes as you make your way to this tropical paradise.

3. The Peninsula, Hong Kong
Situated in the heart of Hong Kong, The Peninsula is renowned for its timeless elegance and impeccable service. The hotel’s rooftop helipad offers panoramic views of the city’s skyline, Victoria Harbour, and the surrounding mountains. Arriving by helicopter allows you to skip the bustling streets and arrive at the hotel with ease.

4. The Ritz-Carlton, New York City, USA
For those seeking a luxurious stay in the Big Apple, The Ritz-Carlton in New York City is a perfect choice. This iconic hotel features a helipad on its rooftop, providing a bird’s-eye view of the city that never sleeps. Touch down in Manhattan and experience the vibrant energy of the city right from the moment you arrive.

5. The St. Regis Vommuli Resort, Maldives
Escape to paradise at The St. Regis Vommuli Resort in the Maldives. This secluded retreat offers a helipad that allows you to arrive directly at the resort’s private island. Immerse yourself in the turquoise waters and pristine beaches as you enjoy a luxurious stay in one of the world’s most breathtaking destinations.

6. The Chedi Andermatt, Switzerland
Nestled in the Swiss Alps, The Chedi Andermatt is a haven for winter sports enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. The hotel’s helipad offers a convenient way to reach this alpine paradise, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and picturesque landscapes. Whether you’re hitting the slopes or simply indulging in the hotel’s world-class amenities, arriving by helicopter adds an extra touch of luxury to your stay.

7. The Ritz-Carlton, Tokyo, Japan
Experience the vibrant city of Tokyo from above at The Ritz-Carlton. This luxurious hotel features a helipad on its rooftop, providing stunning views of the city’s skyline, including the iconic Tokyo Tower and Mount Fuji on clear days. Arriving by helicopter allows you to appreciate the city’s bustling energy from a unique perspective.

In conclusion, if you’re looking for a truly unforgettable travel experience, consider staying at one of these hotels with helipads. Arriving in style by helicopter not only adds a touch of luxury to your journey but also allows you to take in breathtaking views of your destination. So go ahead, indulge in the ultimate travel experience and make a grand entrance at these remarkable hotels.
