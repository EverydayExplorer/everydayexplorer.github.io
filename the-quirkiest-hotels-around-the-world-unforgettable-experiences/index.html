The Quirkiest Hotels Around the World: Unforgettable Experiences


Are you tired of staying in the same old boring hotels when you go on vacation? Do you crave a unique and unforgettable experience during your travels? If so, then you need to check out these quirkiest hotels around the world. From sleeping in a converted airplane to staying in a treehouse, these hotels are sure to leave a lasting impression.

  1. The Manta Resort, Tanzania

Located on a remote island in Tanzania, The Manta Resort offers guests a truly unique experience. One of its most popular attractions is its underwater hotel room, where guests can sleep while surrounded by the vibrant marine life of the Indian Ocean. This is truly a once-in-a-lifetime experience that shouldn’t be missed.

  1. Dog Bark Park Inn, Idaho, USA

If you’re a dog lover, then you’ll love the Dog Bark Park Inn in Idaho, USA. Shaped like a giant beagle, this hotel offers a cozy and comfortable stay. Inside, you’ll find dog-themed decor and amenities. It’s the perfect place to relax and unwind with your furry friend.

  1. Icehotel, Sweden

For those who love winter and all things icy, the Icehotel in Sweden is a must visit. This hotel is entirely made of ice and snow, and it’s rebuilt every year with a new design. Guests can sleep on a bed made of ice and enjoy a range of winter activities such as ice sculpting and ice fishing.

  1. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada

Nestled in the lush forests of Vancouver Island, Canada, the Free Spirit Spheres offer a truly magical experience. These spherical treehouses are suspended from the trees and provide a cozy and unique stay. It’s the perfect place to disconnect from the world and reconnect with nature.

  1. Null Stern Hotel, Switzerland

If you’re looking for a minimalist experience, then the Null Stern Hotel is for you. This ‘zero-star’ hotel has no walls, no roof, and no privacy. Guests sleep under the open sky, surrounded by the beautiful Swiss Alps. It’s a truly immersive experience in nature.

  1. Costa Verde, Costa Rica

For the budding adventurer, the Costa Verde in Costa Rica offers a one-of-a-kind experience. This hotel features a fully restored vintage Boeing 727 airplane that has been converted into a luxury suite. Guests can sleep in the cockpit and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding rainforest.

  1. Giraffe Manor, Kenya

If you’ve ever dreamed of having breakfast with a giraffe, then Giraffe Manor in Kenya is the place to go. This boutique hotel is home to a herd of resident giraffes who love to join guests for a meal. It’s a surreal experience to have these majestic creatures poke their heads through the windows while you enjoy your morning coffee.

  1. Magic Mountain Hotel, Chile

Nestled in the heart of the Patagonian rainforest, the Magic Mountain Hotel is truly a fairytale come to life. This hotel is shaped like a volcano and has a waterfall cascading down its sides. The rooms are cozy and rustic, and the surroundings are perfect for exploring the stunning natural beauty of Chile.

  1. Karostas Cietums, Latvia

For the brave and adventurous, Karostas Cietums in Latvia offers a stay in a former military prison. Guests can experience the life of a prisoner, complete with cold cells and strict rules. It’s definitely not your typical hotel experience, but it’s certainly one that you won’t forget easily.

  1. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland

Last but not least, the Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland is a dream come true for anyone who has ever wanted to sleep under the Northern Lights. This resort offers glass igloos where guests can watch the breathtaking Aurora Borealis from the comfort of their beds. It’s a truly magical experience that will leave you in awe.

In conclusion, if you’re tired of the same old hotel experience and want to try something truly unique and unforgettable, then these quirkiest hotels around the world are a must visit. From sleeping underwater to staying in treehouses, each of these hotels offers something special. So why settle for ordinary when you can have an extraordinary experience? Book your stay at one of these quirky hotels and create memories that will last a lifetime.
