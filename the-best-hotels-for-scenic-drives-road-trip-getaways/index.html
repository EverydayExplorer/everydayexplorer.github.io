The Best Hotels for Scenic Drives: Road Trip Getaways


Are you tired of the same old road trip destinations? Looking for a unique and picturesque experience? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the best hotels for scenic drives, offering you the ultimate road trip getaways. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast or simply crave the open road, these hotels will take your trip to the next level.

1. The Grand Canyon Lodge, Arizona
Located on the South Rim of the Grand Canyon, this historic lodge offers breathtaking views of one of the most iconic natural wonders in the world. Start your day with a sunrise drive along Desert View Drive, where you can witness the ever-changing hues of the canyon. The lodge itself provides cozy accommodations and a variety of dining options, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay.

2. The Ahwahnee Hotel, California
Nestled in the heart of Yosemite National Park, The Ahwahnee Hotel is the perfect base for exploring the park’s awe-inspiring landscapes. Take a drive on Tioga Road, winding through alpine meadows and granite peaks. Don’t forget to stop at Tuolumne Meadows for a picnic surrounded by pristine wilderness. After a day of exploration, unwind in the hotel’s luxurious rooms and indulge in gourmet cuisine.

3. The Fairmont Banff Springs, Alberta, Canada
Situated in the heart of the Canadian Rockies, The Fairmont Banff Springs offers unparalleled views and access to some of the most scenic drives in North America. Take the Icefields Parkway, a 144-mile stretch of road connecting Banff and Jasper National Parks. Marvel at glaciers, turquoise lakes, and towering mountains along the way. The hotel itself is a castle-like masterpiece, providing an enchanting retreat after a day of adventure.

4. The Amangiri, Utah
For those seeking a unique desert experience, The Amangiri is a hidden gem. Located in Canyon Point, Utah, this luxurious resort is surrounded by stunning desert landscapes. Take a drive along the iconic Highway 12, known as the All-American Road, and witness the beauty of red rock canyons and towering cliffs. Back at the resort, indulge in spa treatments and enjoy the tranquility of the desert surroundings.

5. The Wickaninnish Inn, British Columbia, Canada
Perched on the rugged west coast of Vancouver Island, The Wickaninnish Inn offers breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean. Take a drive along the Pacific Rim Highway, exploring the stunning beaches and rainforests of the region. Don’t miss the opportunity to visit Long Beach and catch a glimpse of migrating gray whales. After a day of coastal exploration, relax in the hotel’s cozy rooms and savor locally sourced cuisine.

These are just a few of the many incredible hotels that offer unforgettable scenic drives. So pack your bags, hit the open road, and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature. These road trip getaways will leave you with memories to last a lifetime. Happy travels!

Note: The information provided in this blog post is accurate at the time of writing. Please check with the respective hotels for the latest updates and availability.
