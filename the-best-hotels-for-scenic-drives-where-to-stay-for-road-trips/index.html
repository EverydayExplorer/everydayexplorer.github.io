The Best Hotels for Scenic Drives: Where to Stay for Road Trips


Embarking on a road trip is one of the most exhilarating ways to explore the great outdoors, offering a sense of freedom that is hard to replicate. Whether you’re meandering through the vibrant colors of a fall forest or cruising along a sun-soaked coastline, the journey is often just as memorable as the destination. To enhance your road trip experience, staying at the right hotel can elevate your adventure, providing comfort and convenience that make all the difference. Here’s a guide to some of the best hotels for scenic drives across the United States, perfect for every kind of traveler.

1. Pacific Coast Highway: The Post Ranch Inn, Big Sur, California

The Pacific Coast Highway is renowned for its breathtaking coastal views, winding cliffs, and lush landscapes. Nestled along this iconic route, the Post Ranch Inn offers a luxurious retreat that perfectly complements the dramatic scenery.

Why Stay Here:

  • Stunning Views: Each room features floor-to-ceiling windows that showcase the mesmerizing views of the Pacific Ocean and the rugged Big Sur coastline.
  • Eco-Conscious Luxury: The hotel emphasizes sustainability, using local materials and maintaining a minimal environmental footprint.
  • Amenities: Enjoy infinity pools, a world-class spa, and gourmet dining experiences that highlight organic, locally-sourced ingredients.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Bixby Creek Bridge: An architectural marvel, this bridge offers one of the most photographed views along the coast.
  • Pfeiffer Big Sur State Park: Perfect for hiking, this park boasts beautiful trails and stunning waterfalls.

2. Route 66: The Wigwam Motel, Holbrook, Arizona

No road trip would be complete without a nod to the historic Route 66. The Wigwam Motel in Holbrook is a nostalgic stop that captures the essence of Americana.

Why Stay Here:

  • Unique Accommodations: Stay in one of the iconic wigwam-shaped rooms, each offering a glimpse into the past.
  • Affordable Rates: Enjoy a budget-friendly experience without sacrificing comfort.
  • Cultural Experience: The motel is steeped in history, making it an ideal spot for those looking to immerse themselves in the nostalgia of the open road.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Petrified Forest National Park: Explore ancient trees turned to stone, along with vibrant badlands and rich fossils.
  • Historic Route 66: Take a stroll down the original highway and explore quirky roadside attractions.

3. Blue Ridge Parkway: The Omni Grove Park Inn, Asheville, North Carolina

The Blue Ridge Parkway is known for its stunning vistas, vibrant fall foliage, and rich wildlife. The Omni Grove Park Inn provides an idyllic base for exploring this natural wonder.

Why Stay Here:

  • Historic Charm: This historic hotel features Arts and Crafts architecture and beautifully maintained gardens.
  • Wellness Facilities: Rejuvenate at the spa or enjoy the scenic golf course.
  • Dining with a View: Enjoy exceptional dining options with panoramic views of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Great Smoky Mountains National Park: A short drive away, this park offers endless opportunities for hiking, wildlife viewing, and breathtaking scenery.
  • Asheville’s Arts District: Explore local galleries, craft breweries, and vibrant street art.

4. The Great Ocean Road: The Twelve Apostles Hotel & Retreat, Victoria, Australia

For those venturing beyond the United States, the Great Ocean Road is a must-see, and The Twelve Apostles Hotel & Retreat offers an unforgettable stay.

Why Stay Here:

  • Spectacular Location: Positioned near the iconic Twelve Apostles rock formations, guests are treated to picturesque views.
  • Luxurious Comfort: Enjoy spacious accommodations equipped with modern amenities and stunning ocean views.
  • Personalized Service: The hotel prides itself on providing bespoke experiences tailored to each guest.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Twelve Apostles: Witness the breathtaking limestone stacks that rise majestically from the Southern Ocean.
  • Loch Ard Gorge: Explore the scenic beauty and rich maritime history of this stunning coastal area.

5. Highway 1: The Fairmont Miramar Hotel & Bungalows, Santa Monica, California

Highway 1, or the Pacific Coast Highway, offers some of the most scenic drives in California. The Fairmont Miramar Hotel & Bungalows is an ideal place to unwind after a day of exploration.

Why Stay Here:

  • Beachfront Access: Located just steps from the beach, enjoy sun-soaked days and vibrant sunsets.
  • Luxurious Amenities: With a full-service spa, pool, and fine dining options, relaxation is just a few steps away.
  • Eco-Friendly Initiatives: The hotel is committed to sustainability, offering a range of green practices.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Santa Monica Pier: A classic Californian experience, complete with amusement rides and dining options.
  • Venice Beach: Just a short drive away, this eclectic beach offers unique shops, street performers, and a vibrant atmosphere.

6. The Florida Keys: Cheeca Lodge & Spa, Islamorada, Florida

The Florida Keys offer a unique road trip experience with stunning views of the ocean. Cheeca Lodge & Spa is the perfect place to unwind after a day of exploration.

Why Stay Here:

  • Tropical Paradise: Enjoy a beachfront location surrounded by lush gardens and palm trees.
  • Water Activities: Take advantage of the hotel’s private beach, water sports, and world-class fishing.
  • Relaxation and Wellness: Indulge in spa treatments or relax by the pool with a tropical drink in hand.

Nearby Attractions:

  • John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park: Explore the underwater beauty of the Keys through snorkeling or diving excursions.
  • Theater of the Sea: An entertaining marine park featuring shows and opportunities to swim with dolphins.

7. The Cabot Trail: Keltic Lodge Resort & Spa, Nova Scotia, Canada

The Cabot Trail is one of the most scenic drives in Canada, offering beautiful coastal views and rich cultural experiences. Keltic Lodge Resort & Spa is an excellent choice for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure.

Why Stay Here:

  • Stunning Views: Located within Cape Breton Highlands National Park, enjoy breathtaking vistas right from your room.
  • Culinary Delights: Savor locally sourced seafood and traditional dishes at the on-site restaurant.
  • Outdoor Activities: From hiking to golfing, the hotel offers easy access to various outdoor activities.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Cape Breton Highlands National Park: Explore hiking trails that showcase the park’s stunning landscapes and diverse wildlife.
  • Celtic Colours International Festival: If you’re visiting in October, experience the vibrant music and culture of Cape Breton.

8. The Ring Road: Hotel Rangá, Iceland

For a unique road trip experience, consider Iceland’s Ring Road, which showcases the country’s otherworldly landscapes. Hotel Rangá is a perfect base for your adventures.

Why Stay Here:

  • Stargazing: The hotel is known for its excellent views of the Northern Lights during the winter months.
  • Luxurious Comfort: Enjoy well-appointed rooms with modern amenities and beautiful views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Gourmet Dining: The on-site restaurant features a menu focused on local ingredients and traditional Icelandic dishes.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Golden Circle: Explore the famous Geysir geothermal area, Gullfoss waterfall, and Þingvellir National Park.
  • South Coast Adventures: Visit stunning waterfalls, black sand beaches, and dramatic cliffs.

9. The Alaskan Highway: The Northern Lights Resort & Spa, Whitehorse, Yukon

Traveling along the Alaskan Highway is an adventure filled with stunning landscapes and wildlife. The Northern Lights Resort & Spa offers a cozy retreat amidst the wilderness.

Why Stay Here:

  • Unique Accommodations: Choose from luxurious cabins or spa suites, each designed for comfort and relaxation.
  • Wellness Retreat: Unwind with spa treatments or relax in the outdoor hot tub while enjoying the surrounding nature.
  • Local Experiences: Participate in guided tours that showcase the beauty of the Yukon.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Kluane National Park: Explore rugged mountains, glaciers, and diverse wildlife in this UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Whitehorse Trolley: Discover the charming town of Whitehorse on this guided tour.

10. The Scenic Byways: The Lodge at Mount Rainier, Washington

For those exploring the scenic byways of Washington State, The Lodge at Mount Rainier offers a perfect blend of comfort and adventure.

Why Stay Here:

  • Proximity to Nature: Located just minutes from Mount Rainier National Park, it’s an ideal base for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Rustic Charm: The lodge features a cozy atmosphere with a rustic design that complements its natural surroundings.
  • Dining with a View: Enjoy delicious meals while taking in the breathtaking views of the mountains.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Mount Rainier National Park: Explore hiking trails, wildflower meadows, and stunning vistas of the iconic peak.
  • Sunrise Visitor Center: The highest point in the park accessible by car, offering panoramic views and hiking opportunities.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel can significantly enhance your road trip experience, providing comfort and convenience while allowing you to immerse yourself in the surrounding beauty. Whether you’re driving along the Pacific Coast Highway, exploring the historic Route 66, or traversing the stunning landscapes of Iceland, these hotels offer the perfect blend of luxury and accessibility. So pack your bags, hit the road, and create unforgettable memories at these scenic drives and their accompanying accommodations. Happy travels!
