1. ‘Uptown Funk’ by Mark Ronson ft. Bruno Mars:
Kicking off our list is this infectious hit that took the world by storm. The energetic beats and catchy chorus will have you dancing in no time. It’s impossible not to feel good when this song starts playing!

2. ‘Someone Like You’ by Adele:
Prepare to be moved by the soulful voice of Adele as she pours her heart out in this emotional ballad. This timeless song resonates with anyone who has experienced heartbreak and showcases Adele’s incredible vocal range.

3. ‘Shape of You’ by Ed Sheeran:
Ed Sheeran’s chart-topping hit needs no introduction. Its groovy rhythm and clever lyrics make it a crowd favorite. Whether you’re at a party or just jamming alone, this song will make you feel like dancing.

4. ‘Royals’ by Lorde:
Lorde’s breakout hit showcased her unique voice and perspective. With its minimalist production and thought-provoking lyrics, ‘Royals’ became an anthem for a generation that questioned societal norms.

5. ‘Happy’ by Pharrell Williams:
Feeling down? Pharrell Williams has the perfect remedy with this upbeat and uplifting track. ‘Happy’ will instantly put a smile on your face and have you singing along in no time.

6. ‘Take Me to Church’ by Hozier:
Hozier’s hauntingly beautiful voice combined with powerful lyrics make this song an absolute masterpiece. Its raw emotion and thought-provoking themes leave a lasting impact on listeners.

7. ‘Rolling in the Deep’ by Adele:
Another Adele classic makes our list, and for good reason. ‘Rolling in the Deep’ is a powerful anthem that showcases Adele’s vocal prowess and emotional depth. It’s a song that demands your attention from the very first note.

8. ‘Radioactive’ by Imagine Dragons:
Prepare to be captivated by the explosive energy of ‘Radioactive.’ This rock anthem combines powerful vocals with a punchy beat, making it impossible not to feel exhilarated while listening.

9. ‘Shallow’ by Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper:
A star-studded collaboration that took the world by storm, ‘Shallow’ is a breathtaking ballad that showcases the raw talent of Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper. Its emotional depth and soaring vocals will leave you spellbound.

10. ‘Old Town Road’ by Lil Nas X ft. Billy Ray Cyrus:
Rounding off our list is the catchy and genre-bending hit ‘Old Town Road.’ This chart-topper broke barriers and became a viral sensation, blurring the lines between country and hip-hop. It’s a true testament to the power of music to bring people together.

And there you have it, folks! Ten must-listen songs of the decade that have left an indelible mark on the music industry. Whether you’re into pop, rock, or soulful ballads, this list has something for everyone.
