The Iconic Jewelry Pieces of Hollywood Celebrities


When it comes to red carpet events and glamorous galas, Hollywood celebrities spare no expense in adorning themselves with the most exquisite and iconic jewelry pieces. These dazzling accessories often become as legendary as the stars who wear them, capturing the imagination of fashion enthusiasts around the world. In this blog, we’ll take a journey through time to explore some of the most iconic jewelry moments in Hollywood history.

Elizabeth Taylor’s La Peregrina Pearl Necklace

Few names evoke the same level of Hollywood glamour as Elizabeth Taylor. Known for her unparalleled beauty and extraordinary taste, Taylor’s jewelry collection was nothing short of legendary. Among her most cherished pieces was the La Peregrina Pearl Necklace, a gift from her then-husband Richard Burton.

The La Peregrina pearl, originally discovered in the Gulf of Panama in the 16th century, had a storied history before it came into Taylor’s possession. The pearl was set into a stunning necklace featuring rubies, diamonds, and pearls. Taylor’s love affair with this piece was so intense that she even wore it while swimming.

Audrey Hepburn’s Breakfast at Tiffany’s Necklace

In the 1961 film ‘Breakfast at Tiffany’s,’ Audrey Hepburn solidified her status as a fashion icon, and one piece of jewelry forever associated with her is the multi-strand pearl necklace she wore in the opening scene. Designed by the renowned luxury jeweler, Tiffany & Co., this iconic necklace perfectly complemented Hepburn’s timeless elegance.

The piece, known as the ‘Tiffany Diamond,’ was a creation of immense craftsmanship, featuring an intricate arrangement of pearls and a central diamond clasp. It remains an enduring symbol of sophistication and style.

Marilyn Monroe’s Diamond Rivière Necklace

Marilyn Monroe, the epitome of Hollywood glamour, had an affinity for all things opulent, and her jewelry collection was no exception. One of her most notable pieces was a diamond rivière necklace, gifted to her by her then-husband, baseball legend Joe DiMaggio, during their honeymoon.

This necklace featured 44 dazzling round-cut diamonds and was a testament to the enduring love between the two icons. Monroe wore it on numerous occasions, including during her famous performance of ‘Diamonds Are a Girl’s Best Friend’ in the film ‘Gentlemen Prefer Blondes.’

Princess Diana’s Sapphire Engagement Ring

While not a Hollywood actress in the traditional sense, Princess Diana’s impact on fashion and style was immeasurable. Her engagement ring, now worn by the Duchess of Cambridge, Kate Middleton, is one of the most iconic pieces of jewelry in history.

Featuring a 12-carat sapphire surrounded by 14 solitaire diamonds, this ring was a break from the traditional diamond solitaire and sparked a trend for colored gemstone engagement rings. Its timeless elegance and emotional significance have solidified its place in the annals of iconic jewelry.

Nicole Kidman’s Harry Winston Diamond Necklace

Nicole Kidman’s appearance at the 2008 Academy Awards was a masterclass in red carpet glamour. She graced the event in a stunning red Balenciaga gown, but it was her necklace that stole the show. The 1,399-carat diamond necklace by Harry Winston was a dazzling display of luxury and craftsmanship.

This piece, known as ‘The House Burning Down Necklace,’ featured a jaw-dropping arrangement of diamonds, culminating in a central 7-carat diamond pendant. Kidman’s choice to wear this masterpiece was a testament to her appreciation for the artistry of fine jewelry.

In conclusion, these iconic jewelry pieces serve as timeless reminders of the elegance and opulence that define Hollywood’s golden era. Each piece carries with it a story of love, luxury, and impeccable craftsmanship, leaving an indelible mark on the world of fashion and beyond.

As admirers of these iconic pieces, we’re reminded that the allure of fine jewelry transcends time and trends, continuing to inspire and captivate generations to come.
