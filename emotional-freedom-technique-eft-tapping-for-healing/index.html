Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT): Tapping for Healing


Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT) is a powerful self-help tool that has gained popularity in recent years. Also known as tapping, EFT is a simple yet effective technique used to release emotional blockages and promote overall well-being. In this post, we will delve into the world of EFT, exploring its benefits, how it works, and how you can incorporate it into your daily life.

What is EFT?

EFT is a form of energy psychology that uses a specific sequence of tapping on the body’s meridian points to release emotional blockages. Developed by Gary Craig in the 1990s, EFT is based on the concept that our thoughts, emotions, and physical sensations are all interconnected. By tapping on specific points on the body, we can disrupt the flow of energy and release emotional blockages, promoting emotional freedom and well-being.

How Does EFT Work?

EFT works on the principle that our thoughts, emotions, and physical sensations are all linked to our body’s energy system. The energy system is believed to be composed of pathways, or meridians, that flow through the body, influencing our emotions, thoughts, and physical sensations. When we experience emotional pain or trauma, it is believed that our energy pathways become blocked, leading to physical and emotional dis-ease.

The EFT Tapping Sequence

The EFT tapping sequence involves tapping on specific points on the body, typically using the fingertips. The sequence is as follows:

  1. Karate Chop Point: Located on the side of the hand, the karate chop point is the starting point for the EFT sequence.
  2. Top of the Head: Located at the crown of the head, the top of the head point is the second point in the sequence.
  3. Eyebrow Point: Located between the eyebrows, the eyebrow point is the third point in the sequence.
  4. Side of the Eye: Located at the outer corner of the eye, the side of the eye point is the fourth point in the sequence.
  5. Under the Eye: Located at the bone beneath the eye, the under the eye point is the fifth point in the sequence.
  6. Under the Nose: Located at the point where the nose meets the upper lip, the under the nose point is the sixth point in the sequence.
  7. Chin Point: Located at the center of the chin, the chin point is the seventh point in the sequence.
  8. Collarbone Point: Located at the center of the collarbone, the collarbone point is the eighth point in the sequence.
  9. Under the Arm: Located at the crease of the elbow, the under the arm point is the ninth point in the sequence.

How to Use EFT

Using EFT is simple and straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Identify Your Issue: Identify the issue or problem you’d like to work on. This could be a specific emotion, a traumatic event, or a physical symptom.
  2. Set Your Intention: Set your intention to release the issue or problem. You can say something like, “I intend to release this emotional blockage and promote emotional freedom.”
  3. Tap the Points: Tap each of the EFT points in sequence, using your fingertips. Tap each point 5-7 times, focusing on the sensation of the tap.
  4. Repeat the Intention: Repeat your intention, saying something like, “I release this emotional blockage and promote emotional freedom.”
  5. Take a Deep Breath: Take a deep breath in and out, allowing yourself to fully release the issue or problem.

Benefits of EFT

The benefits of EFT are numerous and varied. Some of the most common benefits of EFT include:

  • Reduced Stress and Anxiety: EFT has been shown to reduce stress and anxiety by releasing emotional blockages and promoting relaxation.
  • Improved Emotional Well-being: EFT can help improve emotional well-being by releasing emotional blockages and promoting emotional freedom.
  • Pain Relief: EFT has been shown to reduce physical pain by releasing emotional blockages and promoting relaxation.
  • Improved Sleep: EFT can help improve sleep quality by releasing emotional blockages and promoting relaxation.

Conclusion

Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT) is a simple yet powerful tool that can be used to release emotional blockages and promote overall well-being. By incorporating EFT into your daily life, you can reduce stress and anxiety, improve emotional well-being, and promote overall health and wellness. Whether you’re looking to address a specific issue or simply promote overall well-being, EFT is a valuable tool that can be used by anyone.
