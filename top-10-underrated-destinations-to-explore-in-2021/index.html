Top 10 Underrated Destinations to Explore in 2021


Welcome, travel enthusiasts! Are you tired of visiting the same popular destinations that everyone else is flocking to? Well, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we will take you on a virtual journey to the top 10 underrated destinations that deserve your attention in 2021. From hidden gems to off-the-beaten-path wonders, these stunning locations are waiting to be explored. So, let’s dive right in and discover some truly unique and awe-inspiring places!

1. Bagan, Myanmar: Nestled in the heart of Myanmar, Bagan is a true archaeological wonder. With over 2,000 temples scattered across its vast plains, this ancient city will transport you back in time. Imagine watching the sunrise over a landscape dotted with golden pagodas – it’s a sight you won’t soon forget.

2. Albarracín, Spain: Stepping into Albarracín is like entering a fairytale. This medieval town, with its charming cobblestone streets and red-hued buildings, is a photographer’s dream. Explore the narrow alleyways, visit the impressive Cathedral, and soak up the enchanting atmosphere.

3. Kotor, Montenegro: Tucked away in the Adriatic Sea, Kotor is a hidden gem that will leave you speechless. This fortified town is surrounded by breathtaking mountains, and its winding streets are lined with historic buildings and cozy cafes. Don’t miss the opportunity to hike up to the ancient fortress for a panoramic view that will take your breath away.

4. Jiufen, Taiwan: If you’re a fan of Studio Ghibli’s ‘Spirited Away,’ then Jiufen is a must-visit destination. This mountain town is known for its narrow alleys, traditional teahouses, and stunning views of the ocean below. Indulge in delicious street food, explore the vibrant markets, and immerse yourself in the magical atmosphere.

5. Chefchaouen, Morocco: Often referred to as the ‘Blue Pearl,’ Chefchaouen is a picturesque town painted entirely in shades of blue. Wander through the winding streets, admire the quaint blue houses, and soak in the tranquil ambiance. This hidden gem is perfect for those seeking a unique and colorful experience.

6. Hvar, Croatia: While Croatia is no stranger to tourism, the island of Hvar remains relatively undiscovered. With its crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and charming old town, Hvar is a true Mediterranean paradise. Explore the lavender fields, indulge in delicious seafood, and soak up the vibrant nightlife.

7. Luang Prabang, Laos: Nestled amidst lush green mountains, Luang Prabang is a UNESCO World Heritage site that will captivate your senses. This charming town is known for its ancient temples, traditional architecture, and vibrant night markets. Don’t miss the opportunity to witness the daily ritual of monks collecting alms at dawn – a truly magical experience.

8. El Nido, Philippines: If you’re a lover of sun, sand, and sea, then El Nido is the ideal destination for you. Located in the province of Palawan, this tropical paradise boasts stunning limestone cliffs, turquoise waters, and secluded beaches. Take a boat tour, go snorkeling, or simply relax and enjoy the breathtaking scenery.

9. Český Krumlov, Czech Republic: Often overshadowed by Prague, Český Krumlov is a fairytale town that feels like stepping back in time. Its enchanting castle, cobblestone streets, and colorful buildings make it a photographer’s dream. Explore the medieval old town, visit the castle gardens, and don’t forget to try the local Czech cuisine.

10. Huacachina, Peru: Tucked away in the Peruvian desert, Huacachina is a true oasis. This tiny village is built around a natural lagoon, surrounded by towering sand dunes. Go sandboarding, take a thrilling dune buggy ride, or simply relax and enjoy the stunning sunset over the desert landscape.

There you have it, fellow travelers – the top 10 underrated destinations to explore in 2021. These hidden gems offer unique experiences, cultural richness, and natural beauty that will leave you in awe. So, why not step off the beaten path this year and embark on an unforgettable adventure? Happy travels!
