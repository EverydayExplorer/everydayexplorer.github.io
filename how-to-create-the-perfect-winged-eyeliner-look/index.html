How to Create the Perfect Winged Eyeliner Look


Winged eyeliner is a classic makeup technique that can instantly elevate your look and add a touch of glamour to any occasion. Whether you’re a makeup novice or a seasoned pro, mastering the art of the perfect winged eyeliner can be a game-changer. In this post, we’ll guide you through the steps to create the perfect winged eyeliner look that will make your eyes pop.

Step 1: Prep and Prime

Before you begin applying your winged eyeliner, it’s important to prep your eyelids to ensure a smooth and long-lasting application. Start by gently cleansing your eyelids with a mild cleanser to remove any excess oils or debris. Follow up with an eyelid primer to create a smooth canvas for your eyeliner.

Step 2: Choose the Right Eyeliner

There are various types of eyeliners available on the market, including liquid, gel, and pencil. For creating the perfect winged eyeliner look, it’s recommended to use a liquid or gel eyeliner as they offer precision and control. Choose a formula that is highly pigmented and has a fine-tipped applicator for easy application.

Step 3: Map Out Your Wing

Creating a symmetrical winged eyeliner look requires careful planning. Start by envisioning the desired shape and angle of your wing. To achieve a natural-looking wing, imagine a straight line extending from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. Mark this point lightly with a small dot using your eyeliner.

Step 4: Draw the Outline

Using the fine-tipped applicator of your chosen eyeliner, start by drawing a thin line along your upper lash line from the inner corner towards the outer corner of your eye. Once you reach the dot you marked in step 3, begin angling the line upwards to create the wing. The length and angle of the wing can be adjusted based on your preference and eye shape.

Step 5: Fill in the Wing

Once you have created the outline of your wing, fill it in by connecting the outer end of the wing to the rest of your eyeliner. This will create a smooth and seamless winged eyeliner look. Take your time and work in small strokes to ensure precision and accuracy.

Step 6: Clean Up and Perfect

To achieve a flawless finish, clean up any mistakes or uneven lines with a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover. Use the swab to gently erase any smudges or imperfections, ensuring that your winged eyeliner looks sharp and defined. If desired, you can also use a small angled brush to sharpen the edges of your wing.

Step 7: Complete the Look

To complete your winged eyeliner look, apply mascara to your lashes to add volume and length. You can also opt to curl your lashes beforehand for an extra dramatic effect. Additionally, consider pairing your winged eyeliner with a neutral eyeshadow or a bold lip color to further enhance your overall makeup look.

Creating the perfect winged eyeliner look may take some practice, but with patience and these step-by-step instructions, you’ll be able to achieve a flawless result. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be discouraged if your first attempt isn’t exactly what you envisioned. Keep refining your technique, and soon you’ll be rocking the perfect winged eyeliner like a pro.

Now that you know the secrets to create the perfect winged eyeliner look, go ahead and experiment with different variations to find the style that suits you best. Whether you prefer a subtle wing for everyday wear or a bold wing for a special occasion, mastering this timeless makeup technique will undoubtedly elevate your beauty game. So, grab your eyeliner and start creating those mesmerizing wings!
