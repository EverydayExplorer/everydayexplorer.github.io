Jewelry Trends to Watch Out for in 2022


As we bid adieu to 2021, it’s time to welcome the new year with open arms and a fresh set of jewelry trends. If you’re a jewelry enthusiast or simply looking to update your collection, get ready to embrace some exciting styles in 2022. From bold statement pieces to delicate designs, there’s something for everyone. In this blog post, we will explore the top jewelry trends to watch out for in 2022.

1. Chunky Chains

Chunky chain necklaces, bracelets, and even anklets are here to stay in 2022. These bold and edgy pieces add a touch of attitude to any outfit. Whether you opt for silver, gold, or even mixed metals, these chunky chains make a statement and instantly elevate your look. Layer them together for a trendy, stacked effect or wear them individually for a standout piece.

2. Colored Gemstones

Move over diamonds, colored gemstones are taking center stage in 2022. From vibrant rubies to deep sapphires and mesmerizing emeralds, these gemstones add a pop of color and personality to any jewelry piece. From statement rings to elegant earrings, incorporating colored gemstones into your collection is a surefire way to stay on-trend in the coming year.

3. Hoop Earrings

Hoop earrings have made a comeback in recent years, and they’re not going anywhere in 2022. These versatile accessories can be found in various sizes and thicknesses, allowing you to tailor your look to suit your style. From classic gold hoops to modern oversized designs, hoop earrings add a touch of sophistication and playfulness to any outfit.

4. Layered Necklaces

Layered necklaces continue to be a hot trend in 2022. This style allows you to mix and match different necklace lengths, styles, and textures to create a unique and personalized look. Whether you prefer dainty chains or bold pendants, layering them together adds depth and dimension to your neckline. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different combinations to find the perfect layered necklace look for you.

5. Nature-inspired Designs

Nature-inspired jewelry is set to be a major trend in 2022, with botanical motifs and organic shapes taking the spotlight. From leaf-shaped pendants to floral-inspired earrings, these nature-inspired pieces bring a sense of whimsy and connection to the natural world. Look out for designs featuring intricate details and delicate craftsmanship to make a chic and eco-friendly statement.

6. Vintage-inspired Pieces

Vintage-inspired jewelry has long been a favorite among fashion enthusiasts, and it’s making a strong comeback in 2022. From Art Deco-inspired designs to Victorian-era pieces, vintage jewelry captures the elegance and charm of bygone eras. Whether you choose to invest in authentic vintage pieces or opt for modern reproductions, incorporating vintage-inspired jewelry into your collection adds a touch of timeless glamour.

7. Statement Rings

Make a bold statement with oversized and eye-catching rings in 2022. From chunky cocktail rings to sculptural designs, these statement pieces instantly draw attention to your hands. Whether you prefer gemstone-adorned rings or intricate metalwork, don’t be afraid to experiment with different shapes and styles to find a ring that truly represents your personality and style.

8. Minimalist Designs

While statement jewelry has its place, minimalist designs are still going strong in 2022. Clean lines, simple geometric shapes, and delicate details are the hallmarks of minimalist jewelry. Whether you choose a dainty pendant necklace, a slim bangle bracelet, or a sleek pair of earrings, minimalist jewelry effortlessly complements any outfit and adds a touch of understated elegance.

In conclusion, 2022 is set to be an exciting year for jewelry trends. From chunky chains to colored gemstones and nature-inspired designs, there’s a diverse range of styles to suit every taste. Whether you prefer bold statement pieces or delicate minimalist designs, investing in these jewelry trends will ensure you stay on-trend and make a fashionable statement in the coming year.
