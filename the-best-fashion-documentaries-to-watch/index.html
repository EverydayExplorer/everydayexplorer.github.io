The Best Fashion Documentaries to Watch


Fashion documentaries provide a captivating glimpse into the world of haute couture, inspiring fashionistas and enthusiasts alike. With their ability to showcase the creative process, the glamour, and the behind-the-scenes drama, fashion documentaries have become a popular choice for those seeking inspiration or simply looking to indulge in the world of high fashion. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best fashion documentaries that are a must-watch for any fashion lover.

1. ‘The September Issue’ (2009): Directed by R.J. Cutler, this documentary takes us inside the world of Vogue magazine, following the creation of their iconic September issue. With exclusive access to editor-in-chief Anna Wintour and her team, viewers get an intimate look at the meticulous planning and execution that goes into producing one of the most influential fashion publications in the world.

2. ‘Dior and I’ (2014): Directed by Frédéric Tcheng, this documentary provides an intimate portrayal of the creative process behind Raf Simons’ first haute couture collection for Dior. From the initial sketches to the final runway show, viewers witness the pressure, challenges, and triumphs that come with reinventing an iconic fashion house.

3. ‘The First Monday in May’ (2016): Directed by Andrew Rossi, this documentary explores the intersection of fashion and art through the preparation of the annual Met Gala. With exclusive access to the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Costume Institute, viewers get a behind-the-scenes look at the meticulous planning and curation of one of the most prestigious fashion events.

4. ‘Iris’ (2014): Directed by Albert Maysles, this documentary celebrates the life and style of fashion icon Iris Apfel. Known for her eclectic fashion choices and bold accessories, Iris’ unique sense of style and infectious personality make for an entertaining and inspiring watch.

5. ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ (2010): Directed by Richard Press, this documentary pays homage to the late street fashion photographer, Bill Cunningham. Known for his keen eye and genuine love for fashion, Cunningham’s work is showcased, offering viewers a glimpse into the fashion trends and personalities that populated the streets of New York City.

6. ‘McQueen’ (2018): Directed by Ian Bonhôte and Peter Ettedgui, this documentary delves into the life and career of the legendary fashion designer Alexander McQueen. From his humble beginnings to his rise to fame and tragic end, this film explores the mind and artistry of a true fashion visionary.

7. ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ (2008): Directed by Matt Tyrnauer, this documentary follows the final two years of Valentino Garavani’s career as a fashion designer. Offering an intimate look at his life, work, and relationship with his longtime partner, Giancarlo Giammetti, this film showcases the dedication and passion that made Valentino a true icon in the industry.

These are just a few of the best fashion documentaries that offer a captivating glimpse into the world of fashion. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or simply looking for some inspiration, these films are sure to delight and educate. So grab some popcorn, sit back, and immerse yourself in the captivating world of fashion through these must-watch documentaries.
