Top 10 Comedic Podcasts to Brighten Your Day


Are you in need of a good laugh? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing our top 10 comedic podcasts that are guaranteed to brighten your day. Whether you’re stuck in traffic, going for a run, or simply need a pick-me-up, these podcasts will have you laughing out loud in no time. So, without further ado, let’s dive right into the list!

1. “The Joe Rogan Experience”

Hosted by the hilarious and quick-witted Joe Rogan, this podcast features interviews with a wide range of guests, from comedians to scientists and everything in between. With over 1,000 episodes to choose from, there’s never a shortage of laughter on “The Joe Rogan Experience.”

2. “Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend”

Join the one and only Conan O’Brien as he sits down with celebrities and friends for candid and hilarious conversations. With Conan’s trademark wit and charm, this podcast is sure to leave you in stitches.

3. “The Dollop”

If you’re a fan of history and comedy, “The Dollop” is the podcast for you. Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, each episode explores a bizarre and often forgotten event from history, infused with their unique brand of humor.

4. “How Did This Get Made?”

Ever wondered how some of the worst movies ever made actually got made? Join hosts Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas as they dissect and make fun of the most absurd films in existence. Prepare to laugh until your stomach hurts!

5. “Comedy Bang! Bang!”

Hosted by Scott Aukerman, “Comedy Bang! Bang!” is a hilarious blend of improv, sketches, and interviews with some of the funniest comedians in the world. With its unpredictable and off-the-wall humor, this podcast is sure to keep you entertained.

6. “My Dad Wrote A Porno”

In this outrageously funny podcast, Jamie Morton reads chapters from his dad’s erotic novel while his friends, James Cooper and Alice Levine, provide hilarious commentary. Brace yourself for awkward and side-splitting moments as you listen to this unique and hilarious podcast.

7. “The Misfits Podcast”

Join a group of YouTubers and content creators as they share their wildest stories, discuss current events, and engage in hilarious banter. With their infectious energy and relatable humor, “The Misfits Podcast” is a must-listen for anyone looking for a good laugh.

8. “The Hilarious World of Depression”

Despite its title, “The Hilarious World of Depression” is a podcast that sheds light on mental health through the lens of comedy. Hosted by John Moe, this podcast features interviews with comedians who have dealt with depression, offering a unique perspective on the topic while still providing plenty of laughs.

9. “No Such Thing As A Fish”

If you’re a fan of trivia and humor, “No Such Thing As A Fish” is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by the researchers behind the hit show “QI,” this podcast explores the most bizarre, interesting, and hilarious facts they’ve come across each week.

10. “The Ricky Gervais Show”

Last but certainly not least, we have “The Ricky Gervais Show.” Join Ricky Gervais, Stephen Merchant, and Karl Pilkington as they engage in hilarious conversations and share their unique perspectives on various topics. With their witty banter and comedic chemistry, this podcast is a true gem.

And there you have it, our top 10 comedic podcasts to brighten your day. Whether you’re a fan of interviews, improv, or bizarre historical events, there’s something on this list for everyone. So, grab your headphones and get ready to laugh your way through the day with these fantastic podcasts. Happy listening!
