Mixing it Up: The Best Cocktail Shakers and Tools


Whether you consider yourself a novice mixologist or a seasoned bartender, having the right cocktail shakers and tools is essential for creating the perfect drink. A great cocktail shaker not only adds a touch of style to your home bar, but it also enhances the flavor and presentation of your cocktails. In this blog post, we will explore the best cocktail shakers and tools available in the market, highlighting their features, benefits, and how they can take your mixology skills to the next level.

1. Boston Shaker

The Boston Shaker is a classic cocktail shaker that consists of two parts: a metal tin and a mixing glass. This shaker is favored by professionals for its versatility and ability to accommodate large batch cocktails. It allows for a better aeration and integration of ingredients, resulting in a well-balanced and flavorful drink. The Boston Shaker is also fairly easy to use, making it suitable for both beginners and experts alike.

2. Cobbler Shaker

If you’re looking for a cocktail shaker that is more user-friendly and has all the necessary components in one piece, then the Cobbler Shaker is the perfect choice for you. This shaker features a built-in strainer and a cap that doubles as a measuring cup, making it convenient and easy to use. It’s a great option for home bartenders who want a stylish and functional shaker without the need for additional tools.

3. Parisian Shaker

For the sophisticated mixologist who appreciates a touch of elegance, the Parisian Shaker is a must-have. This shaker consists of two tapered metal tins that fit snugly together, providing a leak-proof seal. Its sleek design and polished finish make it a stylish addition to any bar cart. The Parisian Shaker is not only aesthetically pleasing, but it also allows for a controlled and precise pour, ensuring consistent cocktail quality.

4. Japanese Cocktail Jigger

A jigger is an essential tool for measuring ingredients accurately and maintaining the correct balance of flavors in your cocktail. The Japanese Cocktail Jigger is a popular choice among bartenders for its ergonomic design and precise measurements. It typically features multiple measurement lines, allowing you to measure different volumes in one tool. Whether you prefer ounces or milliliters, the Japanese Cocktail Jigger has got you covered.

5. Hawthorne Strainer

After shaking your cocktail, you’ll need a reliable strainer to separate the liquid from the ice. The Hawthorne Strainer is widely used in both professional and home settings due to its versatility and ease of use. It features a spring coil that fits snugly over the shaker tin, preventing ice and any unwanted ingredients from entering your glass. The Hawthorne Strainer also adds a professional touch to your cocktail presentation with its stylish design.

6. Mixing Glass

A mixing glass is an essential tool for stirring cocktails that require a gentler mixing technique. It allows for a more delicate blending of ingredients, perfect for drinks like the classic Martini or Old Fashioned. When choosing a mixing glass, opt for one made of durable and high-quality glass that provides good temperature control. A well-made mixing glass not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of your home bar but also ensures a smoother and more enjoyable mixing process.

7. Muddler

For cocktails that require muddling, such as the Mojito or the Caipirinha, a muddler is a must-have tool. This simple yet effective tool allows you to extract the flavors from fruits, herbs, and spices by gently crushing them. Look for a muddler made of hardwood or stainless steel, as they are more durable and effective in breaking down ingredients.

In conclusion, investing in the right cocktail shakers and tools is crucial for crafting delicious and visually appealing cocktails. Whether you prefer the classic Boston Shaker, the user-friendly Cobbler Shaker, or the elegant Parisian Shaker, there is a wide range of options available to suit your style and skill level. Don’t forget to complement your shaker with essential tools like a jigger, strainer, mixing glass, and muddler, which will enhance your cocktail-making experience and allow you to unleash your creativity behind the bar. Cheers to mixing it up with the best cocktail shakers and tools!
