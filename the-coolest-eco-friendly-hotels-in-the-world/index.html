The Coolest Eco-Friendly Hotels in the World


When it comes to travel, many people are becoming more conscious of the impact their choices have on the environment. As a result, eco-friendly hotels have been gaining popularity around the world. These unique establishments combine luxury and sustainability, offering travelers a chance to experience both comfort and a greener way of living. In this blog post, we will explore some of the coolest eco-friendly hotels in the world, showcasing their innovative practices and commitment to preserving our planet.

1. The 1 Hotel Brooklyn Bridge, New York City, USA:
Located in the heart of Brooklyn, this hotel is an architectural marvel that seamlessly blends nature with urban living. With breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline and the iconic Brooklyn Bridge, the 1 Hotel is a haven for eco-conscious travelers. The building features a living green wall, rooftop garden, and sustainable materials throughout. Guests can enjoy organic farm-to-table dining experiences and relax in rooms adorned with reclaimed wood and hemp mattresses.

2. Whitepod, Switzerland:
Nestled in the Swiss Alps, Whitepod offers a truly unique experience for nature lovers. The hotel consists of individual geodesic domes, or ‘pods,’ which are designed to have minimal impact on the environment. Each pod is equipped with a wood-burning stove, organic bedding, and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Guests can partake in various outdoor activities, such as snowshoeing and dog sledding, while also learning about sustainable living practices.

3. Finolhu, Maldives:
Known for its pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters, the Maldives is a dream destination for many. Finolhu takes luxury to a whole new level while maintaining a strong commitment to sustainability. The resort is powered by solar energy and uses a desalination plant for water supply. Guests can indulge in organic spa treatments, dine at the world’s first 1.5km-long sandbank dining destination, and snorkel in the stunning coral reefs, which the hotel actively works to protect.

4. The Brando, French Polynesia:
Located on the private island of Tetiaroa, The Brando is a tropical paradise that goes above and beyond to preserve the natural beauty of its surroundings. The resort operates entirely on renewable energy, utilizing solar power and coconut oil biofuel. Guests can explore the island’s diverse ecosystem, engage in cultural activities, and relax in luxurious villas built with sustainable materials. The Brando also supports local conservation efforts, ensuring the protection of endangered species and ecosystems.

5. The Scarlet, Cornwall, England:
Nestled on the rugged coast of Cornwall, The Scarlet is a boutique hotel that offers breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean. This adults-only retreat seamlessly combines luxury with sustainability. The hotel features a natural reed bed system for wastewater treatment, a geothermal energy source for heating, and organic and locally sourced food options. Guests can unwind in the cliff-top hot tubs, enjoy holistic spa treatments, and explore the stunning coastal landscape.

These are just a few examples of the coolest eco-friendly hotels in the world. By choosing to stay at these establishments, travelers not only enjoy a luxurious experience but also contribute to the preservation of our planet. Whether it’s innovative architecture, renewable energy sources, or a commitment to conservation, these hotels are leading the way in sustainable tourism. So, the next time you plan a vacation, consider staying at one of these eco-friendly havens and make a positive impact while enjoying the beauty of our world.
