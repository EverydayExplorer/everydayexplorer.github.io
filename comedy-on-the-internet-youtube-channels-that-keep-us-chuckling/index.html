Comedy on the Internet: YouTube Channels That Keep Us Chuckling


In the age of digital media, comedy has found a new platform to thrive - the internet. More specifically, YouTube has become a breeding ground for hilarious content creators who know just how to make us laugh. With their witty skits, funny commentary, and relatable humor, they have managed to carve out a niche in the online entertainment world.

The rise of YouTube as a comedy hub can be attributed to several factors. First and foremost, it allows for greater creative freedom. Unlike mainstream television, YouTube channels do not have to abide by strict regulations or censor their content. This has given comedians the freedom to experiment with unconventional ideas and push boundaries, resulting in fresh and innovative comedy.

Moreover, YouTube provides a level playing field for aspiring comedians. Anyone with a camera and an internet connection can start their own channel and upload their content. This has allowed for a diverse range of voices to emerge, and we are now able to enjoy comedy from perspectives that might not have been represented in traditional media.

So, without further ado, let’s dive into some of the top comedy YouTube channels that have been keeping us chuckling:

1. ‘The Try Guys’

Formerly part of BuzzFeed, ‘The Try Guys’ are now a force to be reckoned with on YouTube. With their hilarious and often daring challenges, Eugene, Ned, Keith, and Zach have won the hearts of millions. From experimenting with weird food combinations to trying out extreme sports, The Try Guys never fail to entertain with their infectious energy and witty banter.

2. ‘Good Mythical Morning’

Rhett and Link have been making us laugh for years with their comedic chemistry and offbeat sense of humor. ‘Good Mythical Morning’ is a daily talk show where they engage in bizarre and hilarious challenges, taste-testing unusual food combinations, and interviewing fascinating guests. Their unique brand of comedy and their relatable personalities have made them one of the most popular comedy duos on YouTube.

3. ‘Nigahiga’

Ryan Higa, better known as Nigahiga, is a veteran of the YouTube comedy scene. With his skits, parodies, and comedic commentary, he has amassed a huge following over the years. What sets Nigahiga apart is his ability to combine humor with thought-provoking messages, creating content that is not only funny but also carries a deeper meaning.

4. ‘Smosh’

Smosh, created by Ian Hecox and Anthony Padilla, has been a comedy powerhouse on YouTube since its inception in 2005. Their blend of sketches, music videos, and funny commentary resonates with a wide audience, and they have become a household name in the online comedy world. Despite changes in the team over the years, Smosh continues to deliver laughs with their unique brand of humor.

5. ‘TheOdd1sOut’

James Rallison, known as TheOdd1sOut, has gained popularity through his animated stories and witty observations about life. With his relatable content and charming animation style, he has become a favorite among viewers of all ages. TheOdd1sOut often tackles funny anecdotes from his own life, capturing the essence of everyday situations and turning them into hilarious tales.

These are just a few examples of the many talented comedians who have made their mark on YouTube. From sketch comedy to vlogs, from animation to storytelling, the internet provides a plethora of comedic content that can keep us laughing for hours. Whether you’re in the mood for quick comic relief or a deep belly laugh, there is a YouTube channel out there that has got you covered.

As comedy on the internet continues to evolve, it’s exciting to see how these YouTube channels will push the boundaries and keep us entertained. With the rise of social media and the increasing accessibility of video content, the future of comedy looks bright. So, sit back, relax, and enjoy the laughter that these talented individuals bring into our lives.
