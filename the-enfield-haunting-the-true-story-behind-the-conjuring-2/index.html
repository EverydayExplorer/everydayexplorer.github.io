The Enfield Haunting: The True Story Behind 'The Conjuring 2'


When it comes to horror movies, few can rival the spine-chilling thrills of ‘The Conjuring 2’. Released in 2016, this supernatural horror film left audiences on the edge of their seats, but did you know that it was based on a true story? The Enfield Haunting, upon which ‘The Conjuring 2’ is loosely based, is a bone-chilling tale of paranormal activity that gripped a small community in Enfield, London, during the late 1970s.

The Enfield Haunting began in August 1977 when single mother Peggy Hodgson and her four children claimed that their house had become plagued by supernatural forces. From furniture moving on its own to unexplained levitation, the Hodgson family found themselves at the center of a terrifying ordeal.

News of the Enfield Haunting quickly spread, attracting the attention of paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren, who were already renowned for their work in the field. The Warrens arrived in Enfield to investigate the strange occurrences and document the supernatural phenomena.

What they discovered was a haunting unlike any they had encountered before. The spirit responsible for the Enfield Haunting was supposedly that of an elderly man named Bill Wilkins, who had passed away in the house years earlier. Wilkins’ spirit was said to possess the young Janet Hodgson, one of Peggy’s daughters, often speaking through her in a deep, gruff voice.

The Warrens’ investigation of the Enfield Haunting was filled with eerie encounters, including levitations, poltergeist activity, and even an incident where Ed Warren was allegedly knocked down the stairs by an unseen force. The Hodgson family endured months of terror before the activity eventually subsided, leaving behind a legacy of fear and disbelief.

‘The Conjuring 2’ brought the story of the Enfield Haunting to the big screen, captivating audiences with its terrifying depiction of the events that unfolded. While the movie takes artistic liberties, the core elements of the Enfield Haunting remain intact, serving as a chilling reminder that sometimes the truth can be more terrifying than fiction.

The Enfield Haunting has since become one of the most famous and well-documented paranormal cases in history. It continues to intrigue and haunt those who delve into its dark mysteries. Whether you believe in the supernatural or not, the Enfield Haunting and its connection to ‘The Conjuring 2’ will undoubtedly send shivers down your spine.

In conclusion, the Enfield Haunting is a fascinating true story that inspired the horror film ‘The Conjuring 2’. The paranormal activity experienced by the Hodgson family in their Enfield home continues to captivate audiences and serves as a testament to the unexplained mysteries that exist in our world. So, the next time you watch ‘The Conjuring 2’, remember that the horrors depicted on screen were rooted in a chilling reality that still sends shivers down the spines of those who dare to explore the Enfield Haunting.
