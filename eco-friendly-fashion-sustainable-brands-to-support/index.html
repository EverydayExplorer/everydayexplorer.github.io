Eco-Friendly Fashion: Sustainable Brands to Support


In recent years, there has been a growing awareness of the environmental impact of the fashion industry. From excessive water usage to pollution and garment waste, the traditional fashion industry has taken a toll on the planet. Fortunately, a shift towards eco-friendly fashion has emerged, with more brands focusing on sustainability and ethical practices. If you’re looking to update your wardrobe while minimizing your carbon footprint, here are some sustainable brands to support.

  1. Patagonia: Patagonia has long been a leader in sustainable outdoor clothing. They are committed to using recycled materials, reducing their carbon footprint, and promoting fair labor practices. From their iconic fleeces to durable outerwear and activewear, Patagonia offers a wide range of eco-friendly options for outdoor enthusiasts.

  2. Eileen Fisher: Eileen Fisher is known for their timeless and minimalist designs made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton, linen, and Tencel. They prioritize ethical manufacturing practices and offer a recycling program for old garments through their ‘Renew’ initiative. From versatile basics to elegant pieces for every occasion, Eileen Fisher embodies sustainable luxury.

  3. Reformation: Reformation is a popular choice for trendy and sustainable fashion. They use eco-friendly materials like Tencel, organic cotton, and recycled fabrics, and prioritize transparency in their supply chain. With their stylish dresses, tops, and denim, Reformation proves that fashion can be both chic and sustainable.

  4. Veja: Veja is a footwear brand known for their stylish and eco-friendly sneakers. They use sustainable materials such as organic cotton, wild rubber from the Amazon rainforest, and vegetable-tanned leather. Veja prioritizes fair trade practices and transparency, making them a favorite among conscious consumers looking for fashionable and ethical footwear.

  5. People Tree: People Tree is a pioneer in sustainable and fair trade fashion. They work with artisans and farmers in developing countries to create beautiful clothing using organic cotton, Tencel, and traditional craftsmanship. People Tree offers a range of clothing for women, including dresses, tops, and loungewear, all with a focus on sustainability and social responsibility.

  6. Thought: Thought is a British brand that specializes in sustainable and ethical clothing for men and women. They use natural and eco-friendly materials like organic cotton, bamboo, and hemp, and prioritize fair labor practices throughout their supply chain. Thought offers a wide range of clothing, from casual basics to stylish dresses and accessories, all with a commitment to sustainability.

  7. Amour Vert: Amour Vert is a sustainable fashion brand known for their timeless and feminine designs. They use eco-friendly materials like organic cotton, Tencel, and recycled polyester, and prioritize ethical manufacturing practices. Amour Vert offers a range of clothing, including tops, dresses, and denim, all designed with sustainability in mind.

  8. ABLE: ABLE is a fashion brand dedicated to empowering women through ethical and sustainable manufacturing practices. They prioritize transparency and pay fair wages to their workers, predominantly women in developing countries. ABLE offers a range of clothing, accessories, and jewelry, all with a focus on social impact and sustainability.

By supporting these sustainable brands, you can make a positive impact on the planet while still enjoying stylish and high-quality clothing. Whether you’re in need of outdoor gear, everyday basics, or trendy accessories, there are plenty of eco-friendly options available to suit your style and values. So, next time you’re looking to update your wardrobe, consider supporting these brands that are leading the way towards a more sustainable fashion industry.
