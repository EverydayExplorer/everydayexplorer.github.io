The Best RPG Games of the Decade: A Must-Play List


Greetings, fellow gaming enthusiasts! As we approach the end of the decade, it’s time to reflect on the incredible journey we’ve had in the realm of RPG games. The past ten years have provided us with a plethora of unforgettable adventures, captivating storylines, and immersive gameplay mechanics. Today, we’re excited to present to you a must-play list of the best RPG games of the decade, showcasing the cream of the crop that will surely satisfy your gaming cravings.

1. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - CD Projekt Red delivered an absolute masterpiece with this epic fantasy RPG. Immerse yourself in the shoes of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter with a heart of gold, as you navigate a sprawling open world filled with intriguing quests, memorable characters, and breathtaking landscapes. The Witcher 3 is a testament to the power of storytelling in gaming.

2. Skyrim - Bethesda Game Studios struck gold with this immersive, open-world masterpiece. Embark on an adventure in the land of Tamriel, where dragons roam the skies and your choices shape the world around you. The sheer depth of customization, vastness of the world, and endless possibilities for exploration make Skyrim a timeless RPG that will keep you hooked for hours on end.

3. Persona 5 - Atlus crafted a stunning, stylish RPG experience that defies conventions. Step into the shoes of a high school student by day and a phantom thief by night, as you navigate the intricate web of societal issues and personal struggles. With its unique blend of turn-based combat, captivating characters, and a groovy soundtrack, Persona 5 stands as a shining gem in the RPG genre.

4. Divinity: Original Sin 2 - Larian Studios pushed the boundaries of the RPG genre with this deep and complex adventure. Offering an immersive world, engaging combat mechanics, and an unprecedented level of freedom, Divinity: Original Sin 2 is a true masterpiece. Whether you prefer to tackle quests alone or with friends in cooperative multiplayer, the game offers an experience that caters to all playstyles.

5. Dark Souls - FromSoftware revolutionized the action RPG genre with their punishing yet rewarding masterpiece. Prepare to die as you navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable enemies. Dark Souls challenges your skills and resilience like no other game, making each victory feel incredibly gratifying. This series has become a staple for RPG enthusiasts seeking a true test of their gaming prowess.

6. Fallout: New Vegas - Obsidian Entertainment breathed new life into the post-apocalyptic franchise with this exceptional installment. Set in a morally ambiguous world, Fallout: New Vegas offers players a rich and branching narrative filled with compelling choices. The game’s intricate RPG mechanics and memorable cast of characters make it a must-play for fans of the genre.

7. Dragon Age: Inquisition - BioWare’s epic fantasy RPG takes players on a grand adventure filled with political intrigue, mythical creatures, and a battle to save the world. With its expansive world, deep character development, and strategic combat, Dragon Age: Inquisition offers an immersive experience that will keep you invested from start to finish.

These are just a few of the stellar RPG games that have graced our screens in the past decade. Each title on this list offers a unique and unforgettable experience, showcasing the evolution of the genre and the boundless creativity of game developers.

So, gather your party, sharpen your blades, and embark on these incredible journeys that will transport you to fantastical worlds. The best RPG games of the decade await your exploration, ready to captivate your imaginations and provide countless hours of unforgettable gameplay. Happy adventuring, fellow gamers!
