Stay and Golf: Hotels with Access to World-Class Golf Courses


Planning a vacation that combines luxury accommodation with access to world-class golf courses? Look no further! In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore some of the top hotels around the globe that not only offer exquisite stays but also grant you access to some of the most renowned golf courses, ensuring an unforgettable experience for both golf enthusiasts and leisure travelers alike.

  1. Pebble Beach Resorts, California, USA: Nestled along the picturesque Monterey Peninsula, Pebble Beach Resorts boasts a legendary reputation among golf aficionados. Home to the iconic Pebble Beach Golf Links, consistently ranked as one of the top courses worldwide, this resort offers unparalleled views of the rugged coastline and challenging play for golfers of all skill levels. After a day on the greens, unwind in luxurious accommodations overlooking the Pacific Ocean, complete with world-class amenities and exceptional service.

  2. St. Andrews, Scotland, UK: Known as the ‘Home of Golf,’ St. Andrews in Scotland is a pilgrimage site for golfers from across the globe. Stay at the historic Old Course Hotel, ideally situated just steps away from the renowned Old Course, where golfing history has been made for centuries. Tee off on this hallowed ground, surrounded by the stunning Scottish landscape, and then return to the hotel’s elegant rooms and indulgent spa for a truly rejuvenating experience.

  3. Barnbougle, Tasmania, Australia: For those seeking a golfing adventure off the beaten path, Barnbougle in Tasmania offers a unique blend of natural beauty and world-class golf. The Barnbougle Dunes and Lost Farm courses present challenging layouts set amidst rolling sand dunes and breathtaking coastal vistas. Stay at the Barnbougle Resort, where rustic luxury meets unparalleled hospitality, and savor gourmet dining and outdoor activities in addition to your golfing escapades.

  4. Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica at Peninsula Papagayo: Immerse yourself in tropical paradise at the Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica, located on the stunning Peninsula Papagayo. Here, you’ll discover the award-winning Arnold Palmer-designed golf course, featuring lush fairways and challenging holes overlooking the Pacific Ocean. After a round of golf, unwind in luxurious accommodations surrounded by pristine rainforest, and indulge in rejuvenating spa treatments and exhilarating outdoor adventures.

  5. The Lodge at Torrey Pines, California, USA: Perched atop the cliffs of La Jolla, overlooking the Pacific Ocean, The Lodge at Torrey Pines offers a quintessential California experience for golf and relaxation. Play a round at the iconic Torrey Pines Golf Course, host of the PGA Tour’s Farmers Insurance Open, and enjoy breathtaking ocean views and championship-caliber play. Afterward, retreat to the lodge’s craftsman-style accommodations and savor farm-to-table cuisine at its award-winning restaurants.

  6. Ballybunion Golf Hotel, County Kerry, Ireland: Experience the magic of Irish links golf at the Ballybunion Golf Hotel, situated on the rugged shores of County Kerry. Tee off on the renowned Old Course, ranked among the finest links courses in the world, and take in stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and ancient ruins. After a day on the links, relax in luxurious accommodations overlooking the picturesque countryside, and savor traditional Irish hospitality at its finest.

Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or just starting out, these hotels offer the perfect blend of luxury accommodation and access to world-class golf courses, ensuring an unforgettable stay for golf enthusiasts and travelers alike. So pack your clubs and prepare for an extraordinary golfing getaway unlike any other!
