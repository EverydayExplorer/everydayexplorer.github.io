The Most Unique Hotels Around the World


Are you tired of the same old hotel experience? Want to spice up your travel adventures with something truly extraordinary? Look no further than the world’s most unique hotels. From sleeping in a converted airplane to bedding down in an ice hotel, these accommodations are anything but ordinary. If you’re ready to take your travel experiences to the next level, here are some of the most unique hotels around the world that are sure to leave a lasting impression.

Jumbo Stay, Stockholm, Sweden
Ever dreamed of spending the night in a jumbo jet? At Jumbo Stay in Stockholm, Sweden, you can do just that. This one-of-a-kind hotel is located inside a decommissioned Boeing 747 and offers guests the chance to sleep in converted cockpit rooms or relax in the luxury suite located in the converted cockpit. With stunning views of the airport and a unique aviation-themed experience, Jumbo Stay is perfect for aviation enthusiasts and travelers looking for an unforgettable stay.

Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland
For a truly magical experience, head to Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland. This unique hotel offers guests the opportunity to stay in glass igloos, allowing for uninterrupted views of the Northern Lights during the winter months. In addition to the glass igloos, the resort also offers traditional snow igloos and cozy log cabins, providing a range of options for guests seeking an unforgettable Arctic adventure.

Giraffe Manor, Nairobi, Kenya
Animal lovers, rejoice! Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, offers a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to interact with these majestic creatures. This boutique hotel is home to a herd of resident Rothschild giraffes that freely roam the grounds and often poke their heads through the windows during meal times. With its beautiful colonial architecture and unique wildlife experience, Giraffe Manor is the perfect choice for a memorable and unique getaway.

Dog Bark Park Inn, Idaho, USA
If you’re looking for a truly quirky place to stay, look no further than the Dog Bark Park Inn in Idaho, USA. Shaped like a giant beagle, this bed and breakfast offers a one-of-a-kind experience for dog lovers and anyone with a sense of fun. Guests can sleep inside the beagle’s body and enjoy the surrounding dog-themed decor, making for a delightfully unusual stay.

Treehotel, Harads, Sweden
For those seeking a nature-inspired escape, Treehotel in Harads, Sweden, offers an array of uniquely designed treehouses nestled in the pristine forests of Swedish Lapland. From the UFO and Mirrorcube to the Bird’s Nest and Dragonfly, each treehouse at Treehotel is a work of art, offering guests a chance to reconnect with nature in a truly exceptional way.

Staying at one of these unique hotels promises an experience like no other. Whether you’re seeking adventure, romance, or just a break from the ordinary, these extraordinary accommodations are sure to make your travels unforgettable. So, why settle for the usual when you can indulge in the extraordinary? Start planning your next getaway and prepare to be amazed by the world’s most unique hotels.
