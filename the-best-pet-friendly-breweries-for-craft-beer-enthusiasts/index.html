The Best Pet-Friendly Breweries for Craft Beer Enthusiasts


Are you a craft beer enthusiast who loves spending time with your furry friend? Look no further! We’ve compiled a list of the best pet-friendly breweries that will allow you to enjoy delicious craft beer while bonding with your beloved pet. So grab your four-legged companion, hop in the car, and embark on a beer-filled adventure!

1. Tail Wagging Brews
Located in the heart of a quaint town, Tail Wagging Brews is a pet-friendly brewery that welcomes dogs of all sizes. With a spacious outdoor patio, your furry friend can roam freely while you indulge in a variety of handcrafted beers. From hoppy IPAs to smooth stouts, there’s something for every beer lover’s palate. Don’t forget to try their signature ‘Pawsome Pale Ale,’ specially brewed for four-legged patrons!

2. Bark and Brew
Nestled amidst lush greenery, Bark and Brew is a hidden gem for pet-loving beer enthusiasts. This charming brewery boasts a dog-friendly beer garden where your pup can socialize with other furry visitors. As you sip on their refreshing seasonal brews, your furry friend can enjoy a special doggie treat from their pet-friendly menu. The friendly staff even organizes regular dog-centric events, including pup parades and adoption drives.

3. Paws and Pints
Paws and Pints is a pet-friendly brewery that takes ‘dog-friendly’ to a whole new level. Not only are dogs allowed on their spacious patio, but they also have dedicated play areas and agility courses for your four-legged companion. While you enjoy their extensive selection of craft beers, your furry friend can make new friends and burn off some energy. This unique brewery even offers obedience classes and dog training workshops, ensuring a memorable experience for both you and your pet.

4. Brews and Barks
Situated in a refurbished industrial space, Brews and Barks is a trendy pet-friendly brewery that guarantees a good time for all. Their outdoor seating area is equipped with water bowls, dog-friendly snacks, and even doggie waste stations. As you sample their diverse range of beers, your furry friend can relax under the shade of umbrellas or explore the designated play area. Don’t miss their ‘Yappy Hour’ specials, where you can enjoy discounted drinks while your pet enjoys complimentary treats.

5. Hops and Hounds
Hops and Hounds is a dog lover’s paradise, offering a unique brewery experience that combines craft beer and dog-centric activities. Apart from their pet-friendly patio, they have a doggie obstacle course and a doggie splash pool for those warm summer days. While you savor their award-winning brews, your furry friend can participate in doggie agility competitions or simply lounge around with fellow canine companions. This pet-friendly brewery truly goes above and beyond to create a memorable experience for both you and your pet.

Remember, when visiting these pet-friendly breweries, it’s important to follow the guidelines and rules set by each establishment. Always keep your pet on a leash, clean up after them, and ensure they are well-behaved around other patrons. With these precautions in mind, you’re all set to enjoy the perfect outing with your furry friend and indulge in the finest craft beers at these pet-friendly breweries. Cheers to a wonderful time filled with great beer and even greater memories!
