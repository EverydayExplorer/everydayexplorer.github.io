The Best Online Courses for Learning Survival Skills


In an increasingly unpredictable world, the ability to survive in various environments is becoming more essential than ever. Whether you are an outdoor enthusiast, a prepper, or simply someone looking to gain confidence in your self-sufficiency, learning survival skills can be incredibly beneficial. Fortunately, there are numerous online courses available that cater to all levels of expertise, from beginners to advanced survivalists. In this post, we will explore some of the best online courses for learning survival skills, covering a variety of topics from basic first aid to advanced wilderness survival techniques.

Understanding Survival Skills

Before diving into specific courses, it’s important to understand what survival skills encompass. Survival skills can be broadly categorized into several areas:

  1. Shelter Building: Knowing how to construct a shelter to protect yourself from the elements.
  2. Firecraft: Techniques for starting and maintaining a fire, which is essential for warmth, cooking, and signaling for help.
  3. Water Procurement: Methods for finding, purifying, and conserving water.
  4. Food Foraging: Identifying edible plants and hunting or trapping for food.
  5. Navigation: Skills for orienting yourself in the wilderness using maps, compasses, and natural landmarks.
  6. First Aid: Knowing how to treat injuries and illnesses in remote settings.
  7. Emergency Preparedness: Planning for emergencies, including creating survival kits and developing evacuation plans.

With these fundamental skills in mind, let’s explore some of the best online courses that can help you master each area of survival.

1. Survival Mastery: The Ultimate Survival Course

Platform: Udemy
Duration: 10.5 hours
Level: Beginner to Intermediate

This comprehensive course covers a wide range of survival skills in a structured manner. With over 10 hours of video content, it provides a solid foundation for anyone looking to learn essential survival techniques. Topics include shelter building, fire-making, foraging, and navigation. The course also emphasizes the importance of mental preparedness and situational awareness.

Key Features:

  • Lifetime access to course materials.
  • Downloadable resources and quizzes.
  • A community of fellow students for discussion and support.

2. Wilderness Survival Bootcamp

Platform: Skillshare
Duration: 1.5 hours
Level: Beginner

For those who are short on time but eager to learn, this bite-sized bootcamp is perfect. It provides an introduction to essential wilderness survival skills, including fire starting, shelter construction, and water purification methods. The course is engaging and concise, making it ideal for beginners who want to dip their toes into survival training.

Key Features:

  • Short, digestible video lessons.
  • Access to a community of learners for sharing experiences.
  • Practical exercises to reinforce learning.

3. Survival Skills: The Complete Guide to Surviving in the Wild

Platform: Coursera
Duration: 6 weeks (3-4 hours/week)
Level: Intermediate

Offered by a leading university, this course dives deep into the scientific principles behind survival techniques. Students will learn about the psychology of survival, how to build effective shelters, and advanced fire-making techniques. It also includes assessments to ensure comprehension of the material.

Key Features:

  • University-level instruction with a focus on scientific principles.
  • Peer-reviewed assignments to enhance learning.
  • Certificate of completion to showcase your skills.

4. Advanced Wilderness Survival Training

Platform: The Survival University
Duration: 4 weeks
Level: Advanced

This course is designed for those who already have basic survival skills and want to take their knowledge to the next level. Topics include advanced navigation techniques, wilderness first aid, and survival psychology. The course incorporates real-life scenarios and challenges to prepare students for actual survival situations.

Key Features:

  • Emphasis on hands-on learning and practical application.
  • Access to expert instructors with real-world survival experience.
  • Comprehensive resources for further study.

5. Emergency Preparedness and Response

Platform: FEMA
Duration: Self-paced
Level: All Levels

FEMA offers a variety of free courses focused on emergency preparedness. While not strictly survival skills in the wilderness, understanding how to prepare for natural disasters and emergencies is crucial for survival in urban environments. This course covers creating emergency plans, building disaster kits, and understanding the response process.

Key Features:

  • Free access to valuable resources.
  • Focus on preparedness for various emergencies.
  • Certification upon completion.

6. Bushcraft Essentials: A Practical Guide to Wilderness Survival

Platform: MasterClass
Duration: 2 hours
Level: Intermediate

Taught by a renowned survival expert, this MasterClass offers a unique perspective on bushcraft, which emphasizes using natural resources to survive. Students will learn about traditional skills such as tracking, foraging, and crafting tools from natural materials. This course is perfect for those who want a holistic approach to survival.

Key Features:

  • Expert-led instruction from a seasoned survivalist.
  • Engaging video format with practical demonstrations.
  • Access to a community of like-minded learners.

7. First Aid for Survival Situations

Platform: Red Cross
Duration: 5 hours
Level: All Levels

This course focuses on first aid skills necessary for survival situations. It covers essential topics such as wound care, CPR, and how to handle medical emergencies in remote locations. Given that injuries can happen anywhere, having a solid understanding of first aid can be lifesaving.

Key Features:

  • Certification in first aid upon completion.
  • Comprehensive materials covering various injury scenarios.
  • Access to ongoing resources and updates.

8. Foraging Fundamentals: Find Food in the Wild

Platform: Udemy
Duration: 3 hours
Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Foraging is an essential skill for any survivalist. This course teaches students how to identify edible plants, mushrooms, and other food sources in the wild. It also covers safety tips and ethical foraging practices. By learning how to find food in nature, you can significantly enhance your survival capabilities.

Key Features:

  • In-depth lessons on identification and preparation of wild foods.
  • Downloadable guides and foraging lists.
  • Engaging quizzes to reinforce learning.

9. Wilderness Navigation: Mastering the Basics

Platform: Outdoor Survival Skills
Duration: 4 hours
Level: All Levels

Navigating through the wilderness can be daunting, especially without modern technology. This course offers a detailed exploration of traditional navigation techniques using maps, compasses, and natural landmarks. Students will learn how to confidently find their way in the great outdoors.

Key Features:

  • Practical exercises for hands-on learning.
  • Access to a knowledgeable instructor for Q&A.
  • Resources for further navigation study.

10. Survival Cooking: Techniques and Recipes for the Outdoors

Platform: Skillshare
Duration: 2 hours
Level: All Levels

Cooking in the wild is an essential skill that can enhance your survival experience. This course teaches students how to prepare meals using limited resources and fire. It covers techniques for cooking over an open flame, as well as recipes that utilize foraged ingredients.

Key Features:

  • Engaging cooking demonstrations.
  • Tips for meal planning and food preservation.
  • Access to a community of outdoor chefs for sharing ideas.

Conclusion

Learning survival skills is not just about preparing for emergencies; it’s also about gaining confidence and independence in your abilities to navigate the wild. With the variety of online courses available, anyone can find a program that suits their interests and skill level. Whether you’re interested in basic first aid, advanced wilderness survival, or foraging, there is a course out there for you.

As you embark on your journey to become more self-sufficient, remember that practice is key. Engage in hands-on learning, participate in outdoor activities, and connect with other survival enthusiasts. The skills you acquire will not only prepare you for unexpected challenges but also enrich your outdoor experiences, allowing you to fully embrace the beauty and adventure that nature has to offer.
