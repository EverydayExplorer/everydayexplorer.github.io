Hunting from Above: Tips for Tree Stand and Elevated Platform Hunting


Hunting has been a fundamental part of human history, providing sustenance, clothing, and tools for survival. Today, hunting continues to be a popular outdoor activity, allowing enthusiasts to connect with nature and experience the thrill of the chase. For many hunters, the use of tree stands and elevated platforms has become a preferred method for pursuing game. In this blog post, we will explore the art of hunting from above and share valuable tips for maximizing success while perched in the treetops.

Tree stand hunting offers several advantages, including elevated vantage points, improved visibility, and enhanced concealment. By positioning themselves above the ground, hunters can gain a strategic advantage over their prey, increasing their chances of a successful hunt. However, hunting from a tree stand or elevated platform requires careful planning, attention to safety, and a solid understanding of the surrounding environment.

Selecting the Right Location

Choosing the right location for your tree stand or elevated platform is crucial for a successful hunt. Before installing your stand, scout the area to identify game trails, feeding areas, and bedding sites. Look for signs of animal activity such as tracks, droppings, and rubs to determine the best location for your stand. Additionally, consider wind direction and prevailing weather patterns to ensure that your scent will not be easily detected by game animals.

Safety First

Safety should always be the top priority when hunting from an elevated position. Before using a tree stand or platform, familiarize yourself with the equipment and follow all manufacturer’s guidelines for installation and use. Inspect the stand for any signs of wear or damage, and always wear a safety harness while hunting from an elevated position. Additionally, consider using a lifeline or safety strap to secure yourself to the tree, providing an extra layer of protection in case of a fall.

Concealment and Camouflage

Proper concealment and camouflage are essential for hunting from above. Choose a tree stand or platform that blends in with the surrounding vegetation, minimizing the chance of detection by game animals. Additionally, use natural foliage and camouflage netting to further conceal your position and break up your silhouette. Pay attention to your movements and avoid sudden or unnecessary motions that could alert nearby wildlife.

Shot Placement and Shooting Angles

When hunting from a tree stand or elevated platform, it’s crucial to understand shot placement and shooting angles. Practice shooting from an elevated position to familiarize yourself with the trajectory of your arrows or bullets. Consider the angle of your shot and the potential for deflection when hunting from above, and aim for vital areas that offer a clean and ethical kill. Understanding the anatomy of your target species is essential for making effective and humane shots while hunting from an elevated position.

Patience and Observation

Hunting from a tree stand or elevated platform requires patience and keen observation. Take the time to immerse yourself in the natural surroundings and observe the behavior of game animals. Use binoculars to scan the area for movement and listen for sounds that may indicate the presence of nearby wildlife. Remember that hunting from above offers a unique perspective and the opportunity to witness natural behaviors that may go unnoticed from ground level.

Environmental Considerations

Be mindful of environmental considerations when hunting from above. Be aware of changes in weather conditions, such as wind speed and precipitation, that may impact your hunting experience. Dress appropriately for the elements and consider using a portable blind or rain cover to provide protection from the elements while hunting from an elevated position. Additionally, be respectful of the natural environment and leave minimal impact on the surrounding ecosystem.

In conclusion, hunting from above using tree stands and elevated platforms offers a unique and rewarding experience for outdoor enthusiasts. By following these tips and guidelines, hunters can enhance their safety, increase their chances of a successful hunt, and deepen their connection to the natural world. Whether you’re pursuing deer, turkey, or other game species, hunting from above provides a thrilling and immersive experience that allows hunters to test their skills and adapt to the challenges of the great outdoors. So, the next time you head out into the woods, consider taking your hunting experience to new heights by embracing the art of hunting from above.
