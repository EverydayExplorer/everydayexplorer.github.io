The Best Hotels for Scenic Drives: Explore Breathtaking Routes


Are you ready for an incredible adventure? Buckle up and get ready to explore some of the most breathtaking scenic drives in the world. From winding coastal roads to mountain passes with jaw-dropping views, these routes will leave you in awe of the beauty of nature. And what better way to complete your journey than by staying at the best hotels along the way, where you can relax and rejuvenate after a long day of driving?

Let’s start our journey in the United States, where the Pacific Coast Highway awaits. This iconic route stretches along the California coastline, offering mesmerizing views of the Pacific Ocean on one side and stunning cliffs on the other. As you wind your way through Big Sur, make sure to stop at the Post Ranch Inn, perched on the cliffs and offering unparalleled ocean views. With its luxurious accommodations and world-class amenities, this hotel will make your trip truly unforgettable.

Next, let’s head across the pond to Europe, where the Amalfi Coast in Italy beckons. This picturesque drive hugs the cliffs along the Mediterranean Sea, offering breathtaking views of colorful towns and azure waters. Along the way, make a pit stop at the Hotel Santa Caterina, a charming boutique hotel with panoramic views of the coast. Indulge in gourmet cuisine, relax by the pool, and soak in the beauty of the Amalfi Coast.

Now, let’s venture to the Land Down Under and explore the Great Ocean Road in Australia. This scenic drive winds its way along the rugged coastline of Victoria, showcasing stunning rock formations and pristine beaches. Stop at the iconic Twelve Apostles and be sure to book a stay at the Great Ocean Road Resort, where you can enjoy comfortable accommodations and stunning views of the ocean.

Heading north to Canada, the Icefields Parkway awaits. This 144-mile stretch of road in the Canadian Rockies offers awe-inspiring views of glaciers, turquoise lakes, and towering mountains. Make a stop at the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, a luxurious hotel nestled in the heart of Banff National Park. With its breathtaking views of Lake Louise and the surrounding mountains, this hotel is the perfect base for exploring the beauty of the Rockies.

Last but not least, let’s make our way to New Zealand and experience the Milford Road. This scenic drive takes you through Fiordland National Park, where you’ll be mesmerized by dramatic mountains, cascading waterfalls, and mirror-like lakes. Stay at the Milford Sound Lodge, located at the end of the road and offering stunning views of the famous Milford Sound. Immerse yourself in nature and discover why New Zealand is known as the adventure capital of the world.

So there you have it, a guide to some of the best hotels for scenic drives around the world. Whether you’re planning a road trip in the United States, Europe, Australia, Canada, or New Zealand, these hotels will provide the perfect blend of comfort and natural beauty. Get ready to hit the open road and create memories that will last a lifetime. Happy driving!
