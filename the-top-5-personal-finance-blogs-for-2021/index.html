The Top 5 Personal Finance Blogs for 2021


In today’s fast-paced world, managing personal finances has become increasingly important. With countless blogs available online, it can be challenging to sift through the noise and find the best resources to help you achieve financial success. In this post, we will explore the top five personal finance blogs for 2021, each offering valuable insights and practical advice to improve your financial well-being.

1. Mr. Money Mustache

Mr. Money Mustache (https://www.mrmoneymustache.com/) is a popular personal finance blog that has been around since 2005. The blog’s creator, Pete Adeney, is a retired software engineer who achieved financial independence at the age of 30. His mission is to inspire others to do the same by living a frugal lifestyle and investing wisely.

Mr. Money Mustache offers a unique perspective on personal finance, focusing on financial independence and early retirement. The blog covers topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and lifestyle design. Some of the most popular posts include ‘The Shockingly Simple Math Behind Early Retirement’ and ‘The 10 Best Books to Read on Your Path to Financial Independence.’

2. Budgets Are Sexy

Founded by J. Money, Budgets Are Sexy (https://www.budgetsaresexy.com/) is a personal finance blog that aims to make managing money fun and engaging. The blog features a mix of personal stories, financial advice, and money-saving tips, all presented in an entertaining and relatable manner.

Budgets Are Sexy covers a wide range of topics, from budgeting and saving to investing and side hustles. Some of the most popular posts include ‘How to Start a Side Hustle in 2021’ and ‘The Ultimate Guide to Personal Finance in Your 20s.’ The blog also has a strong community aspect, with readers sharing their own experiences and offering support to one another.

3. The College Investor

As the name suggests, The College Investor (https://www.thecollegeinvestor.com/) focuses on personal finance for college students and recent graduates. Founded by Robert Farrington, the blog provides practical advice on managing student loans, investing, and building wealth while still in school or in the early stages of one’s career.

The College Investor covers topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and paying off student loans. Some of the most popular posts include ‘How to Pay Off $100,000 in Student Loans in 3 Years’ and ‘The Ultimate Guide to Investing in Your 20s.’ The blog also offers resources for entrepreneurs, such as ‘How to Start a Business While in College.’

4. Afford Anything

Paula Pant is the founder of Afford Anything (https://www.affordanything.com/), a personal finance blog that emphasizes the importance of living a life of financial freedom. The blog covers topics such as real estate investing, passive income, and lifestyle design, all with the goal of helping readers create the financial freedom they desire.

Afford Anything offers a variety of content, including podcasts, articles, and videos. Some of the most popular posts include ‘How to Travel the World for Cheap (or Free)’ and ‘The Simple Math Behind Early Retirement.’ The blog also features interviews with successful entrepreneurs and investors, providing valuable insights into their financial journeys.

5. Wealthy Gary

Wealthy Gary (https://wealthygary.com/) is a personal finance blog that focuses on building wealth through real estate investing. Founded by Gary Collins, the blog offers practical advice on buying, renovating, and renting properties to create passive income streams.

Wealthy Gary covers topics such as real estate investing, budgeting, saving, and building wealth through rental properties. Some of the most popular posts include ‘The Ultimate Guide to Real Estate Investing for Beginners’ and ‘How to Find the Best Rental Property Deals.’ The blog also offers resources for those interested in starting their own real estate investment business, such as ‘How to Start a Real Estate Wholesaling Business.’

In conclusion, these top five personal finance blogs for 2021 offer valuable insights and practical advice to help you achieve financial success. Whether you’re looking to achieve financial independence, pay off student loans, invest in real estate, or simply improve your money management skills, these blogs have something to offer. By incorporating the tips and strategies shared by these experts, you can take control of your finances and work towards a more secure financial future.
