The Best Spirits for Brunch: Bloody Marys and Mimosas


When it comes to brunch, the perfect cocktail can elevate the entire experience. Whether you’re a fan of the savory kick of a Bloody Mary or the light, bubbly sweetness of a Mimosa, there’s a spirit out there that can take your brunch to the next level. In this blog post, we’ll explore the best spirits for crafting these beloved brunch classics, and offer tips for creating the ultimate Bloody Mary or Mimosa that will have your guests coming back for more.

Bloody Marys: The key to a stellar Bloody Mary is the vodka. While any decent vodka will do, premium vodkas like Tito’s Handmade Vodka or Belvedere Vodka can add an extra smoothness and depth of flavor to your cocktail. If you’re feeling adventurous, consider infusing your vodka with flavors like horseradish, bacon, or even pickle for a unique twist on this classic drink. Don’t forget the importance of the mix – a high-quality Bloody Mary mix is essential for achieving that perfect balance of spice and tang. Look for artisanal mixes that boast natural ingredients and complex flavor profiles to take your Bloody Mary game to the next level.

Mimosas: A great Mimosa starts with a sparkling wine, traditionally champagne, but prosecco or cava are also excellent choices. For those who prefer a sweeter Mimosa, a demi-sec sparkling wine or even a sparkling rosé can provide a delightful twist. When it comes to the juice, fresh-squeezed orange juice is the gold standard, but feel free to experiment with other citrus juices like grapefruit or blood orange for a unique flavor profile. To add an extra layer of sophistication to your Mimosa, consider incorporating a splash of a fruit liqueur such as elderflower, peach, or raspberry – the possibilities are endless!

Now that we’ve covered the best spirits for Bloody Marys and Mimosas, let’s talk about some additional tips for taking your brunch cocktails to the next level. First, don’t skimp on the garnishes – a well-crafted Bloody Mary or Mimosa is as much about the visuals as it is about the taste. Think beyond the standard celery stick or orange slice and consider adding pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, or even a crispy bacon garnish to wow your guests.

Furthermore, presentation is key. Invest in elegant glassware to showcase your cocktails, and consider experimenting with different serving vessels like mason jars or vintage coupes for a unique touch. Lastly, don’t be afraid to get creative and put your own spin on these classic brunch cocktails – after all, the best cocktails are often the result of a little experimentation and a lot of passion.

In conclusion, the best spirits for brunch cocktails like Bloody Marys and Mimosas are the ones that reflect your personal taste and style. Whether you prefer a bold and savory Bloody Mary or a light and effervescent Mimosa, there’s a spirit out there that can help you craft the perfect brunch cocktail. By selecting high-quality spirits, experimenting with unique flavors, and paying attention to the details, you can elevate your brunch game and become the ultimate host or hostess. Cheers to delicious brunch cocktails and unforgettable gatherings!
