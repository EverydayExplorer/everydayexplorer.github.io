Crafts for Baby Photography: DIY Props and Setups


Capturing the precious moments of a baby’s early days is a joyous and cherished experience for parents and photographers alike. Baby photography allows us to freeze those fleeting moments in time and create lasting memories. One way to enhance these photos is by using DIY props and setups that add a touch of creativity and personalization. In this blog post, we will explore some unique and easy-to-make crafts for baby photography.

1. Dreamy Cloud Backdrop

Create a dreamy and ethereal atmosphere for your baby’s photoshoot by making a cloud backdrop. Start by cutting out cloud-shaped templates from white cardstock or foam board. Then, attach these clouds to a sturdy backdrop using transparent fishing line. Add some soft, pastel-colored fabric to complete the dreamy look. This simple DIY prop will lend a whimsical touch to your baby’s photos.

2. Floral Wreath Halo

For a natural and angelic look, make a floral wreath halo for your baby. Select an assortment of small flowers, such as baby’s breath or mini roses. Carefully arrange and secure them onto a flexible wire or floral crown base. This DIY prop will add a delicate and enchanting touch to your baby’s photos, giving them an ethereal and angelic appearance.

3. Vintage Suitcase Prop

Give your baby’s photos a vintage and nostalgic vibe by incorporating a vintage suitcase prop. Look for an old suitcase in thrift stores or online marketplaces. Clean it up and add a fresh coat of paint if necessary. Line the inside with soft fabric or a fluffy blanket. You can place your baby inside or on top of the suitcase for a charming and timeless look that will surely make your photos stand out.

4. DIY Newborn Nest

Create a cozy and adorable nest for your newborn using simple materials. Start with a basket or wooden crate as the base. Line it with a soft blanket or faux fur fabric. Add some fluffy pillows or stuffed animals to complete the nest. This DIY prop will not only provide a comfortable space for your baby but also add a touch of natural beauty to your photos.

5. Baby Headbands and Bow Ties

Accessorize your baby’s photoshoot with handmade headbands and bow ties. Use soft, stretchy fabric or ribbons to create adorable headbands adorned with flowers, bows, or other cute embellishments. For the bow ties, use a small piece of fabric and attach it to a clip or elastic band. These DIY accessories will add a touch of charm and personality to your baby’s photos.

Remember, safety should always be a top priority when using props and setups for baby photography. Make sure that all props are securely attached and never leave your baby unattended. Additionally, always consider your baby’s comfort and well-being throughout the photoshoot.

Incorporating DIY props and setups into your baby photography sessions can add a personal and creative touch to your photos. These crafts allow you to create unique and memorable moments that will be cherished for years to come. So, grab your crafting supplies and get ready to capture some beautiful and adorable memories with your little bundle of joy. Happy crafting and happy photographing!
