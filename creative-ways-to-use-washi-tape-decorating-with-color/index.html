Creative Ways to Use Washi Tape: Decorating with Color


Washi tape is a versatile and colorful tool that has taken the world of crafting and decorating by storm. With its wide range of colors, patterns, and textures, wash tape can add a touch of whimsy and creativity to any project. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative ways to use washi tape specifically for decorating with color. Get ready to unleash your imagination and transform your space with these unique ideas.

1. Colorful Frames: Give your plain old picture frames a vibrant makeover with washi tape. Simply wrap the tape around the edges of the frame, overlapping different colors and patterns to create a colorful mosaic effect. This simple touch can instantly revitalize your space and make your favorite memories stand out.

2. Rainbow Bookmarks: Add a pop of color to your reading routine with DIY washi tape bookmarks. Cut a strip of cardstock or thick paper to your desired bookmark size and decorate it with different colored washi tape. Mix and match patterns, or create a rainbow effect by layering different shades of tape. Not only will these bookmarks keep your place in your favorite books, but they’ll also bring a smile to your face every time you open them.

3. Colorful Wall Art: Who needs expensive artwork when you can create your own colorful masterpieces using washi tape? Start by sketching out a design or pattern on your wall, such as geometric shapes or a simple line drawing. Then, use different colors of washi tape to fill in the shapes and create a stunning visual display. The best part? If you change your mind or want to try a new design, the tape can be easily removed without leaving a trace.

4. Colorful Furniture Accents: Give your old furniture a fresh and colorful makeover with washi tape. Whether it’s a plain dresser, a tired side table, or a lackluster bookshelf, washi tape can instantly breathe new life into it. Choose a color palette that complements your existing decor and use the tape to create patterns or stripes on the surface of the furniture. This simple DIY project can transform your space and make a big impact on a small budget.

5. Colorful Plant Pots: Add a touch of color to your indoor or outdoor plants by decorating their pots with washi tape. Start by cleaning the surface of the pot and then stick the tape around the rim or create patterns along the sides. You can even mix and match different tape patterns to create a unique look for each plant. Not only will this brighten up your space, but it will also give your plants a stylish home.

6. Colorful Gift Wrapping: Make your gifts stand out with colorful and unique gift wrapping using washi tape. Instead of traditional wrapping paper, use plain white or brown paper and decorate it with strips of washi tape in different colors and patterns. You can create stripes, chevron patterns, or even spell out the recipient’s name using the tape. This personalized touch will make your gifts extra special.

7. Colorful Wall Calendar: Stay organized and add a splash of color to your workspace with a DIY washi tape wall calendar. Start by creating a grid using thin strips of washi tape on a blank wall. Then, use different colors of tape to mark the days, weeks, and months. You can even use different patterns to distinguish between different types of events or appointments. This fun and functional project will keep you on track while adding a touch of creativity to your space.

These are just a few creative ways to use washi tape for decorating with color. Let your imagination run wild and explore the endless possibilities that this versatile tool offers. Whether you’re looking to revamp your living space, add a personal touch to your gifts, or simply infuse more color into your life, washi tape is the perfect solution. So grab your favorite rolls of tape and get ready to unleash your inner artist!
