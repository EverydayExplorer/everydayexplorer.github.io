The Coolest Bookstores around the World


Hello bookworms and avid readers! Today, we embark on a literary journey around the world to explore the coolest and most enchanting bookstores. These literary havens are not just places to find your favorite novels; they are architectural wonders, cultural landmarks, and oases for all those who cherish the written word. Let us dive into this adventure and discover the hidden gems that await us!

1. El Ateneo Grand Splendid, Buenos Aires, Argentina
Our first stop takes us to the captivating El Ateneo Grand Splendid in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Housed in a former theater, this bookstore preserves its ornate frescoes, elegant balconies, and a majestic stage that now showcases rows upon rows of books. Immerse yourself in the grandeur of this cultural hotspot as you explore its extensive collection and find a cozy nook to indulge in your next read.

2. Shakespeare and Company, Paris, France
Next up is the legendary Shakespeare and Company in the heart of Paris. This iconic bookstore has been a haven for writers, artists, and intellectuals since it first opened its doors in 1919. Its bohemian atmosphere and abundant bookshelves make it a beloved spot for bibliophiles worldwide. Don’t forget to grab a vintage typewriter and let your inner Hemingway soar as you soak in the literary history that permeates every corner.

3. Livraria Lello, Porto, Portugal
In the picturesque city of Porto, Portugal, lies Livraria Lello, a bookstore that seems straight out of a fairy tale. Its neo-Gothic architecture, intricate wooden staircases, and stained glass ceilings transport visitors to a bygone era. With its spellbinding charm, it’s no wonder that Livraria Lello is said to have inspired J.K. Rowling during her time in Porto, shaping the magical world of Harry Potter.

4. Bart’s Books, Ojai, California, USA
Nestled in the quaint town of Ojai, California, Bart’s Books boasts the unique distinction of being the world’s largest outdoor bookstore. Here, book lovers can browse through a vast collection of used and rare books, surrounded by towering oak trees and peaceful gardens. On a sunny day, grab a book from the honor system shelves, find a cozy spot, and lose yourself in the tranquility of this literary oasis.

5. Atlantis Books, Santorini, Greece
Picture this: whitewashed buildings perched on the edge of a cliff overlooking the stunning Aegean Sea. Welcome to Atlantis Books in Santorini, Greece. This dreamlike bookstore hosts a curated selection of books, creating an ambiance that perfectly complements the breathtaking views. Spend an afternoon browsing literary treasures, then find a spot on the terrace to savor the sunset while diving into your new literary find.

6. Libreria Acqua Alta, Venice, Italy
Venice, renowned for its canals and gondolas, is also home to the whimsical Libreria Acqua Alta. Here, books are piled high in bathtubs, gondolas, and even a full-size boat, creating a surreal and magical environment for book enthusiasts. Amidst the charming chaos, you can uncover rare finds, enjoy a cup of espresso, and even venture into the store’s backyard, which leads to a stunning view of the canals.

7. The Last Bookstore, Los Angeles, California, USA
Our final stop takes us to the vibrant city of Los Angeles, where The Last Bookstore serves as a symbol of the resilience of independent bookshops. This sprawling bookstore occupies a former bank building, complete with towering bookshelves, cozy reading corners, and a magnificent book tunnel that invites visitors to wander through a labyrinth of literary wonders. It’s a paradise for book lovers and a testament to the enduring power of the written word.

As our literary adventure comes to an end, we have only scratched the surface of the world’s coolest bookstores. From enchanting architectural marvels to cozy sanctuaries nestled in bustling cities, each bookstore has its own unique story to tell. So, whether you’re a traveler seeking literary inspiration or a local looking for a new reading nook, remember to venture off the beaten path and discover these magical and unforgettable destinations.

Happy reading, and may your bookish adventures be filled with wonder and inspiration!
