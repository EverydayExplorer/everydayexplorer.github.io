The best cars for long road trips and cross-country journeys


As restrictions begin to lift and more people start to travel, the appeal of long road trips and cross-country journeys is becoming even more pronounced. From exploring the Great American West to traversing Route 66, hitting the open road can be one of the most rewarding and thrilling ways to see the country. However, when it comes to embarking on a journey of this scale, your choice of vehicle is paramount. Here, we take a look at some of the best cars for long road trips and cross-country journeys.

1. Honda Accord

The Honda Accord is a well-rounded vehicle that delivers good fuel economy, ample legroom, and a comfortable ride, making it one of the best cars for long road trips. The Accord’s spacious interior and trunk offer plenty of room for luggage, while its available Honda Sensing safety suite adds peace of mind on long journeys. Its quiet engine and well-cushioned seats help to minimize driver fatigue, making it an ideal option for those who plan to spend long hours behind the wheel.

2. Subaru Outback

The Subaru Outback is a tough and reliable vehicle that’s great for off-road adventures, long hauls, and cross-country trips. With its standard all-wheel drive, versatile cargo area, and roof rails, the Outback can handle anything the road throws its way. Additionally, it has a comfortable ride, a spacious and well-appointed cabin, and an infotainment system that keeps you connected while on the road.

3. Toyota Prius

The Toyota Prius is perhaps the most fuel-efficient car on this list, making it a great choice for those who want to save money on gas during long distance drives. The Prius is also spacious enough to accommodate luggage or camping gear, and its advanced safety features ensure peace of mind while on the road. When it comes to navigating through congested urban areas, its compact size and agile handling make it a breeze to maneuver in traffic.

4. Ford Expedition

If space is a priority, the Ford Expedition is one of the best cars for long road trips. This full-size SUV offers plenty of seating and cargo capacity, making it an ideal choice for large families or groups. The Expedition also delivers a comfortable ride, a quiet cabin, and a range of features that keep you entertained and safe while on the road.

5. Mazda CX-5

The Mazda CX-5 is a stylish and sporty compact SUV that’s great for road trippers who want a high-end driving experience. The CX-5 offers a spacious and comfortable cabin, precise handling, and excellent fuel economy, which should have you spending more time enjoying the journey than refueling. Its advanced safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and forward collision warning, help to mitigate the effects of driver distraction and keep everyone onboard safe.

No matter which vehicle you choose, remember to prioritize comfort, space, and safety when selecting the best car for your long road trip or cross-country journey. Keep in mind that a well-maintained vehicle is essential, regardless of make or model, so ensure that your car is properly serviced before embarking on any long journey. Finally, remember to plan your route, carry a map or GPS device, and enjoy the ride. With the right car, a cross-country journey can be one of the most unforgettable experiences of your life.
