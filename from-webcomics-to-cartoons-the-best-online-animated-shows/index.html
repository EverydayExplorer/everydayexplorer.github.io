From Webcomics to Cartoons: The Best Online Animated Shows


Webcomics have long been a beloved medium of storytelling, captivating readers with their unique blend of art and narrative. In recent years, however, some webcomics have made the leap from the digital realm to the world of animation, bringing their characters to life in a whole new way. These online animated shows have gained a loyal following, attracting viewers from all walks of life. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best webcomics that have successfully transitioned into animated shows, and delve into why they have become such a hit among audiences.

One such webcomic-turned-animated-show is ‘Lore Olympus,’ a modern retelling of Greek mythology. Created by Rachel Smythe, this visually stunning webcomic quickly gained popularity for its vibrant art style and engaging storyline. The success of the webcomic led to a partnership with a production studio, resulting in an animated adaptation that has captivated fans worldwide. The show beautifully brings to life the love story between Hades and Persephone, while also exploring the complexities of the gods’ relationships and their struggles in the modern world.

Another notable example is ‘The Adventure Zone,’ a podcast turned webcomic turned animated series. Created by the McElroy brothers, this comedic and heartfelt show follows a group of siblings and their father as they embark on epic tabletop role-playing adventures. The webcomic adaptation, illustrated by Carey Pietsch, perfectly captures the humor and heart of the podcast, and the animated series takes it to a whole new level. With its unique blend of humor, storytelling, and memorable characters, ‘The Adventure Zone’ has become a fan favorite among both webcomic enthusiasts and animation lovers.

One webcomic that has gained a massive following and recently made the jump to animation is ‘Homestuck.’ Created by Andrew Hussie, this sprawling epic follows a group of teenagers as they navigate a complex, virtual world. Known for its intricate plot, unique art style, and interactive storytelling, ‘Homestuck’ has amassed a dedicated fanbase over the years. The animated adaptation, which was crowdfunded by fans, has brought this complex webcomic to life, allowing viewers to fully immerse themselves in its rich and imaginative universe.

These examples are just a glimpse into the world of webcomics turned animated shows. The success of these adaptations highlights the growing popularity and influence of online animated content. With their ability to captivate audiences and bring beloved characters to life, these shows have proven that the transition from webcomic to animated series can be a successful one.

In conclusion, webcomics turned animated shows have become a significant part of the entertainment landscape. They offer a unique and engaging storytelling experience, combining the visual allure of animation with the narrative depth of webcomics. From ‘Lore Olympus’ to ‘The Adventure Zone’ to ‘Homestuck,’ these shows have captured the hearts of viewers around the world, showcasing the power and potential of online animated content. As the popularity of webcomics continues to soar, we can only anticipate more exciting adaptations in the future. So, grab your popcorn and get ready to dive into the world of webcomic-inspired animated shows!
