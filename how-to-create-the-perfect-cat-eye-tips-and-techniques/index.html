How to Create the Perfect Cat Eye: Tips and Techniques


The cat eye, also known as the winged eyeliner, is a timeless and classic makeup look that adds instant drama and allure to any eye shape. While mastering the perfect cat eye can seem daunting, with the right tips and techniques, anyone can achieve this iconic look. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore step-by-step instructions, expert tips, and insider tricks for creating the perfect cat eye that will leave you feeling fierce and fabulous.

  1. Prep and Prime: Before diving into eyeliner application, it’s essential to prep and prime the eyelids to ensure a smooth and long-lasting finish. Start by applying an eyeshadow primer to create a flawless base and prevent creasing. This will help your eyeliner glide on effortlessly and stay put throughout the day or night.

  2. Choose the Right Eyeliner: When it comes to creating the perfect cat eye, the type of eyeliner you use can make all the difference. While liquid eyeliner provides precise and intense color payoff, gel and pencil eyeliners offer more control and flexibility. Choose the formula that you feel most comfortable working with, whether it’s a liquid pen, gel pot, or pencil liner.

  3. Map Out Your Wing: The key to a flawless cat eye is symmetry, so it’s essential to map out your wing before applying eyeliner. Start by using a small piece of tape or a straight edge, such as a makeup brush or business card, to create a guide for your wing. Angle the tape or brush from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow, ensuring that both wings are even and symmetrical.

  4. Start Small: When it comes to drawing your winged eyeliner, it’s best to start small and gradually build up the intensity. Begin by drawing a thin line along the upper lash line, starting from the inner corner and extending towards the outer corner. Use short, light strokes to create a smooth and even line, gradually thickening the line as you reach the outer corner.

  5. Create the Wing: Once you’ve mapped out your wing, it’s time to create the flick. Using the guide you created earlier, draw a diagonal line extending from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. This will serve as the base of your wing. Then, connect the end of the diagonal line to the outer corner of your upper lash line, creating a triangular shape. Fill in the triangle, making sure to taper the line towards the inner corner of your eye for a seamless finish.

  6. Perfect the Shape: After creating the wing, it’s time to perfect the shape and ensure that both eyes are symmetrical. Use a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover to clean up any mistakes or smudges and sharpen the edges of your wing. You can also use a small, angled brush dipped in concealer to clean up and define the edges for a crisp and polished look.

  7. Finish with Mascara: To complete the cat eye look, apply several coats of mascara to define and volumize your lashes. Wiggle the mascara wand from the root to the tip of your lashes, ensuring full coverage and separation. For added drama, consider using false eyelashes to enhance the winged effect and add extra length and volume.

  8. Practice Makes Perfect: Creating the perfect cat eye takes practice, so don’t be discouraged if it doesn’t turn out perfectly the first time. Experiment with different techniques, angles, and products to find what works best for you. Remember, practice makes perfect, so keep practicing and refining your skills until you achieve the flawless cat eye of your dreams.

In conclusion, mastering the art of the cat eye is a rite of passage for any makeup enthusiast. By following these tips and techniques, you can create the perfect cat eye that enhances your natural beauty and adds instant glamour to any look. Whether you’re rocking a bold wing for a night out on the town or opting for a subtle flick for everyday wear, the cat eye is sure to turn heads and make a statement wherever you go. So grab your eyeliner and get ready to unleash your inner feline fierceness with the perfect cat eye!
