Cocktail Trends to Watch in 2022


As the calendar flips to a new year, cocktail enthusiasts and mixologists alike eagerly anticipate the latest trends set to shake up the scene. With each passing year, the world of cocktails evolves, influenced by a myriad of factors including cultural shifts, emerging ingredients, and innovative techniques. So, what libation trends are set to take center stage in 2022? Let’s pour ourselves a glass and explore.

  1. Low-ABV Libations: With a growing emphasis on health and wellness, consumers are seeking lighter options that allow them to indulge without overindulging. Enter low-ABV cocktails, which offer all the flavor and complexity of their higher-proof counterparts but with a more moderate alcohol content. Think spritzes, shandies, and vermouth-based cocktails that are perfect for sipping all day long.

  2. Sustainable Spirits: As sustainability continues to be at the forefront of consumer consciousness, expect to see a rise in eco-friendly spirits and practices behind the bar. From distilleries employing renewable energy sources to bartenders utilizing surplus ingredients to create unique concoctions, sustainability is set to be a defining trend in 2022.

  3. Global Influences: In today’s interconnected world, culinary influences know no borders, and the same can be said for cocktails. From Japanese whisky highballs to Latin American-inspired agave concoctions, expect to see a melting pot of global flavors infusing cocktail menus around the world.

  4. Creative Garnishes: The days of a simple lemon twist are long gone as bartenders push the boundaries of creativity with their cocktail garnishes. From edible flowers and dehydrated fruits to elaborate ice sculptures, expect to see cocktails adorned with eye-catching accouterments that elevate the drinking experience to new heights.

  5. CBD Cocktails: With the increasing popularity of CBD products, it was only a matter of time before they found their way into the world of cocktails. CBD-infused libations offer a relaxing alternative to traditional drinks, providing a subtle sense of calm without the psychoactive effects of THC. Look out for CBD tinctures and oils making their way onto cocktail menus in 2022.

  6. DIY Mixology: The pandemic saw a surge in home bartending as consumers sought to recreate the bar experience in the comfort of their own homes. This DIY mentality is set to continue into 2022, with enthusiasts experimenting with homemade syrups, bitters, and infusions to craft their perfect cocktail creations.

  7. Spirit-Free Cocktails: Whether it’s for health reasons, designated drivers, or simply a desire to cut back on alcohol consumption, the demand for spirit-free cocktails is on the rise. Bartenders are rising to the challenge, crafting inventive mocktails that deliver all the flavor and flair of traditional cocktails without the booze.

  8. Cocktail Pairings: Move over wine, there’s a new pairing in town. With the rise of craft cocktails, expect to see more restaurants offering cocktail pairings alongside their tasting menus. From savory cocktails paired with appetizers to dessert cocktails perfectly matched with sweet treats, the possibilities are endless.

  9. Interactive Experiences: In the age of social media, consumers crave experiences that are not only delicious but also visually stunning. Bartenders are tapping into this desire by incorporating interactive elements into their cocktails, from tableside mixology to smoke-infused drinks that dazzle the senses.

  10. Cocktail Collaborations: In a nod to the collaborative spirit of the industry, expect to see more partnerships between distilleries, bars, and mixologists in 2022. These collaborations will result in limited-edition spirits, bespoke cocktail menus, and one-of-a-kind drinking experiences that celebrate the creativity and camaraderie of the cocktail community.

In conclusion, 2022 promises to be an exciting year for cocktail enthusiasts, with a diverse array of trends shaping the drinking landscape. Whether you’re sipping on a low-ABV spritz, indulging in a sustainable spirit, or experimenting with DIY mixology at home, there’s never been a better time to raise a glass and toast to the ever-evolving world of cocktails. Cheers to a spirited year ahead!
