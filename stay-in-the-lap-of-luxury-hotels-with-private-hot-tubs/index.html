Stay in the Lap of Luxury: Hotels with Private Hot Tubs


Are you ready to elevate your travel experience to a whole new level of luxury? Imagine soaking in a private hot tub, sipping champagne, and enjoying the breathtaking views from the comfort of your own hotel suite. If this sounds like the ultimate indulgence, you’re in for a treat. In this blog post, we’ll take you on a journey through some of the world’s most exquisite hotels that offer the opulence of private hot tubs. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway or simply seeking a lavish escape, these hotels are sure to exceed your expectations.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA
Nestled within the serene desert landscape of Utah, Amangiri is a luxurious retreat that seamlessly blends with its natural surroundings. The hotel’s suites boast private terraces with stunning views, and some even feature secluded hot tubs where guests can unwind under the expansive desert sky. The juxtaposition of rugged wilderness and refined luxury makes Amangiri a haven for discerning travelers seeking a one-of-a-kind experience.

2. The Royal Crescent Hotel & Spa, Bath, England
Step into the regal elegance of The Royal Crescent Hotel & Spa, a historic gem located in the UNESCO World Heritage city of Bath. This opulent hotel offers a selection of suites with private hot tubs, allowing guests to revel in the serenity of their own secluded oasis. After a day of exploring the city’s rich cultural heritage, immerse yourself in the warm waters of your private tub and let your worries fade away.

3. Jade Mountain, St. Lucia
Prepare to be mesmerized by the unparalleled luxury of Jade Mountain in St. Lucia. This exclusive resort is renowned for its breathtaking open-air sanctuaries, known as ‘sanctuaries’ rather than rooms, and each one features an infinity pool with mesmerizing views of the Caribbean Sea. Some of these sanctuaries also include extravagant hot tubs, where guests can bask in the beauty of the tropical surroundings in complete privacy.

4. The Peninsula Paris, France
Indulge in the epitome of Parisian elegance at The Peninsula Paris, where luxury knows no bounds. The hotel’s rooftop garden suites offer an extraordinary experience, complete with private terraces and hot tubs that overlook the iconic city skyline. As you soak in the warm waters, you’ll be treated to panoramic views of the Eiffel Tower and the enchanting Parisian rooftops, creating an unforgettable moment of pure bliss.

5. The Chedi Andermatt, Switzerland
Escape to the Swiss Alps and discover the unparalleled charm of The Chedi Andermatt. This alpine retreat sets the stage for an extraordinary getaway, with its sumptuous suites featuring stylish hot tubs on private balconies. After a day of skiing or exploring the picturesque surroundings, sink into the rejuvenating waters of your hot tub and relish the tranquil ambiance of the Swiss mountains.

In conclusion, the allure of hotels with private hot tubs lies in the promise of unparalleled luxury and exclusivity. From desert sanctuaries to urban havens, these exceptional hotels offer a symphony of opulence, privacy, and breathtaking views. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply seeking a lavish escape, these hotels are certain to elevate your travel experience to new heights of indulgence. So, why not treat yourself to the ultimate luxury and immerse yourself in the splendor of a hotel with a private hot tub? After all, you deserve nothing but the best.
