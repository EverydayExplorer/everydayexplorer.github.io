Crafting with Seashells: Bring the Beach into Your Home


Are you longing to keep the beach vibes alive even after you’ve returned from your vacation? Look no further than crafting with seashells to bring the beach into your home! Seashells are not only beautiful natural treasures but also versatile materials that can be transformed into stunning and unique decorative pieces. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of creative crafting ideas using seashells, ensuring that your home becomes a coastal paradise.

Seashell Wall Art

One of the easiest ways to incorporate seashells into your home decor is by creating stunning wall art pieces. Whether you choose to frame a single large seashell or create an intricate collage using various sizes and shapes, seashell wall art adds a touch of elegance and natural beauty to any room.

To get started, gather a collection of seashells in different shapes and sizes. These can be easily found on the beach or purchased from local craft stores. Clean the seashells thoroughly and allow them to dry completely before you begin your project.

Next, decide on a layout for your wall art. You can arrange the seashells in a geometric pattern, a wave-like design or simply create a random arrangement that looks visually appealing. Once you are happy with the layout, use a strong adhesive or a hot glue gun to attach the seashells to a canvas, wooden board, or a picture frame. Finally, let it dry before hanging it up on your wall.

Seashell Candles

Another creative way to incorporate seashells into your home is by making seashell candles. These charming candles not only provide a soft and warm glow but also fill your home with a soothing aroma.

To make seashell candles, you will need a collection of seashells, candle wicks, candle wax, and essential oils of your choice for fragrance. Begin by cleaning and drying the seashells as mentioned before. Next, select a seashell with a shallow cavity to hold the melted wax.

Melt the candle wax according to the manufacturer’s instructions and add a few drops of your chosen essential oil for fragrance. Place the candle wick at the bottom center of the seashell, ensuring that it is held in place. Carefully pour the melted wax into the shell, avoiding overfilling it. Allow the wax to cool and solidify before lighting the candle.

Seashell Jewelry

If you are feeling particularly crafty, seashell jewelry can be a fun and fashionable way to bring the beach into your everyday style. Create stunning necklaces, bracelets, and earrings using seashells as the central focus. Not only will wearing seashell jewelry remind you of your beach getaway, but it will also be a unique statement piece that sparks conversations.

To make seashell jewelry, start by selecting seashells that have natural holes or can be easily drilled to add jump rings. Clean and dry the seashells thoroughly before painting or decorating them with your favorite colors or designs. Once the shells are ready, attach jump rings through the holes and add chains or earring hooks as necessary. Be creative with your designs and mix and match different seashell sizes and shapes for a personalized touch.

Seashell Planters

Transforming seashells into miniature planters is a delightful way to incorporate greenery into your beach-inspired home decor. Choose small succulents or air plants that thrive well in a shallow environment.

First, clean and dry a collection of seashells. If the seashells have a natural opening or a cavity, this will make the planting process easier. Otherwise, you may need to carefully drill a small hole in the shell. Once your seashells are prepared, fill them with soil suitable for your chosen plants.

Carefully plant your succulents or air plants into the seashells, ensuring that their roots are firmly secured. Place your seashell planters in a sunny spot in your home and water the plants according to their specific care instructions. Not only will these seashell planters bring a touch of nature into your home, but they will also serve as conversation starters for guests.

Seashell Mobiles

Creating a seashell mobile is an excellent way to add a whimsical touch to your home decor. Mobiles can be hung in windows, porches, or any other area that catches the breeze, allowing the shells to sway gently in the air.

To make a seashell mobile, gather an assortment of seashells in different sizes and shapes. Clean and dry them thoroughly and attach a length of fishing line or clear thread to each shell. Once the shells are attached, string them onto a wooden hoop or any other lightweight circular frame. Be creative with your design by incorporating other materials like driftwood, feathers or beads.

Hang your seashell mobile in a location where it catches the breeze, allowing the shells to twirl and create a soothing sound. This simple yet elegant craft will instantly transport you back to the beach.

Crafting with seashells is not only a fun and creative activity, but it also brings the refreshing and tranquil ambiance of the beach straight into your home. Whether it’s a seashell wall art, homemade candles, jewelry, planters, or mobiles, the possibilities are endless. So, gather those seashells and let your creativity take flight as you craft beautiful pieces that capture the essence of the beach.

Remember, the beauty of crafting with seashells lies in the process itself. Take your time, experiment, and enjoy the therapeutic nature of working with these natural treasures. By incorporating seashell crafts into your home decor, you’ll be able to create a peaceful and coastal atmosphere that will remind you of the beach all year round.
