The Negroni Sbagliato: A Bubbly and Aperitif Favorite


In the world of classic cocktails, there are few drinks as iconic and beloved as the Negroni. With its perfect balance of bitterness, sweetness, and herbal complexity, the Negroni has become a staple in bars and homes around the globe. But have you heard of its sparkling cousin, the Negroni Sbagliato?

Originating in Italy, the Negroni Sbagliato offers a delightful twist on the traditional Negroni recipe. The word ‘sbagliato’ translates to ‘mistaken’ in Italian, and this cocktail was born out of a happy accident. Legend has it that a bartender mistakenly grabbed a bottle of sparkling wine instead of gin while preparing a Negroni, and the Negroni Sbagliato was born.

The Negroni Sbagliato swaps out the gin for sparkling wine, adding a touch of effervescence and lightness to the drink. This simple substitution transforms the cocktail into a refreshing and bubbly aperitif that is perfect for any occasion.

To make a Negroni Sbagliato, you will need the following ingredients:

- 1 ounce Campari
- 1 ounce sweet vermouth
- 1 ounce sparkling wine
- Orange twist or slice, for garnish

To start, fill a rocks glass with ice and add the Campari and sweet vermouth. Stir gently to combine the ingredients and chill the mixture. Next, top the glass with sparkling wine, allowing the bubbles to mingle with the other flavors. Give it a gentle stir to marry all the elements together.

Garnish your Negroni Sbagliato with an orange twist or slice, which adds a citrusy aroma and complements the drink’s bitterness. The vibrant red color of the cocktail, coupled with the orange garnish, creates an inviting presentation that is sure to impress your guests.

The Negroni Sbagliato is perfect for those who appreciate the sophisticated flavors of a Negroni but prefer a lighter and more effervescent drink. The sparkling wine adds a touch of elegance and celebration to the traditional recipe, making it an ideal choice for festive gatherings and special occasions.

As an aperitif, the Negroni Sbagliato also excels in setting the stage for a memorable dining experience. Its bitter notes stimulate the appetite, preparing your taste buds for the delicious meal that lies ahead. Sip on this bubbly concoction while enjoying some light snacks or hors d’oeuvres, and you’ll find yourself in the perfect mood to savor the flavors of your main course.

The Negroni Sbagliato has gained a loyal following among cocktail enthusiasts and has become a favorite on drink menus worldwide. Its simplicity, elegance, and unique twist on a classic have made it a go-to choice for those seeking something different yet familiar.

So, the next time you’re in the mood for a refreshing aperitif with a touch of sparkle, give the Negroni Sbagliato a try. Let its effervescence tickle your senses, and allow its vibrant flavors to transport you to the streets of Italy. Cheers to the Negroni Sbagliato, a bubbly and aperitif favorite that is sure to leave a lasting impression.
