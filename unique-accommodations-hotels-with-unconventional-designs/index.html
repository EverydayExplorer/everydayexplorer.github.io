Unique Accommodations: Hotels with Unconventional Designs


When it comes to travel, finding unique accommodations can truly enhance the overall experience. While traditional hotels offer comfort and convenience, there is something incredibly exciting about staying in a hotel with an unconventional design. These hotels not only provide a place to rest your head but also serve as a memorable and immersive part of your journey. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the most fascinating hotels around the world that boast unconventional designs, leaving a lasting impression on their guests.

1. Treehotel, Sweden: Nestled in the picturesque forests of Harads, Treehotel offers a truly unique experience. Each room is designed by a different architect, resulting in treehouses that are both luxurious and eco-friendly. From the Mirrorcube, which reflects its surroundings, to the UFO, a futuristic pod suspended in the treetops, Treehotel provides an extraordinary escape for nature lovers.

2. The Dog Bark Park Inn, USA: Located in Idaho, this hotel takes the form of a giant beagle. Guests enter through the dog’s belly and are welcomed into cozy rooms with dog-themed decor. It’s an ideal spot for dog lovers who want to experience a quirky and fun stay.

3. Icehotel, Sweden: Built entirely from ice and snow, the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi is a true marvel. Each winter, artists from around the world come together to create unique ice sculptures and ice rooms that make for a truly magical experience. From sleeping in a frozen art installation to enjoying a drink at the Icebar, this hotel offers an unforgettable Arctic adventure.

4. Giraffe Manor, Kenya: Animal lovers will adore Giraffe Manor, a boutique hotel in Nairobi. This elegant manor is known for its resident herd of Rothschild’s giraffes, who often poke their heads through the windows during breakfast. With only a handful of rooms, guests have the opportunity to intimately interact with these magnificent creatures.

5. Hotel Marques de Riscal, Spain: Designed by renowned architect Frank Gehry, this hotel in the heart of the Rioja wine region is a true architectural masterpiece. The building’s stunning titanium ribbons and undulating shapes create a visually striking contrast against the surrounding vineyards. Inside, guests can indulge in luxurious amenities and world-class wines.

6. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada: Tucked away in the forests of Vancouver Island, Free Spirit Spheres offers a truly unique accommodation experience. These suspended spherical treehouses provide a tranquil retreat, allowing guests to reconnect with nature while enjoying the comforts of a cozy interior. It’s an ideal choice for those seeking solitude and serenity.

7. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland: For those who have always dreamt of sleeping under the Northern Lights, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort is the perfect destination. This Arctic resort offers glass igloos that provide unobstructed views of the mesmerizing night sky. With the added bonus of a private sauna, guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of the Arctic wilderness.

Whether it’s sleeping in a treehouse, a dog-shaped inn, or an ice palace, these unconventional hotels offer a chance to step out of the ordinary and into a world of wonder. Each one provides a unique and unforgettable experience, ensuring that your stay will be as memorable as the destination itself. So, the next time you plan your travels, consider adding one of these extraordinary hotels to your itinerary for a truly extraordinary adventure.
