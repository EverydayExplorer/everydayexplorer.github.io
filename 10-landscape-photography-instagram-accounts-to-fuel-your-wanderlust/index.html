10 Landscape Photography Instagram Accounts to Fuel Your Wanderlust


As the world becomes more digitally connected, social media platforms have become a source of inspiration and wanderlust for many. One platform, in particular, has captured the hearts of photographers and adventure enthusiasts alike - Instagram. With its visually captivating photos and vibrant community, Instagram has become a hub for showcasing the beauty of our planet and fueling our wanderlust. In this blog post, we will explore 10 landscape photography Instagram accounts that will take you on a visual journey and leave you yearning to explore the great outdoors.

1. @wonderlustcollective: This account is a collective of talented photographers who capture breathtaking landscapes from all around the world. From towering mountains to serene lakes, their images will make you feel like you’re right there, experiencing the beauty firsthand.

2. @earthfocus: If you’re looking for a diverse range of landscapes, @earthfocus is the account to follow. They curate stunning images from photographers all over the globe, showcasing everything from vast deserts to lush rainforests.

3. @dreaming_outloud: This account focuses on moody, atmospheric landscape photography that will transport you to otherworldly places. Each image tells a story and evokes a sense of wanderlust like no other.

4. @mountain.stories: As the name suggests, @mountain.stories is dedicated to capturing the majesty and grandeur of mountains. From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the rugged beauty of the Rockies, this account is a must-follow for all mountain enthusiasts.

5. @explorewithme: If you’re looking for a dose of adventure, @explorewithme is the account for you. Their photos will inspire you to pack your bags and embark on a journey to remote and untouched landscapes.

6. @seascapesgram: For those who find solace and inspiration by the sea, @seascapesgram is the perfect account to follow. From tranquil sunsets to crashing waves, their images capture the raw power and beauty of the ocean.

7. @forestlovers: If you’re in need of a nature escape, @forestlovers will transport you to enchanting forests around the world. With their dreamy and ethereal images, you’ll feel a sense of serenity and connection with nature.

8. @sunsetchasers: There’s something magical about a beautiful sunset, and @sunsetchasers captures this magic perfectly. Their vibrant and awe-inspiring images will have you planning your next sunset-viewing adventure.

9. @wilderness_captures: Prepare to be captivated by the raw and untamed beauty of the wilderness through the lens of @wilderness_captures. Their photos showcase the untouched landscapes and remind us of the importance of preserving our natural world.

10. @adventure_culture: Last but not least, @adventure_culture is a treasure trove of stunning landscape photos that will ignite your sense of wanderlust and inspire you to step outside of your comfort zone.

These 10 landscape photography Instagram accounts are just a glimpse into the vast world of visual inspiration that Instagram has to offer. Whether you’re seeking solace in nature, craving adventure, or simply looking to fuel your wanderlust, these accounts will surely leave you inspired and eager to explore the world around you.

So, grab your camera, follow these accounts, and let the beauty of our planet guide you on your next adventure. Remember, the world is vast, and there’s always something new to discover - both on Instagram and beyond!
