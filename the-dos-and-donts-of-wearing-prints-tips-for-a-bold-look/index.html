The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Prints: Tips for a Bold Look


When it comes to fashion, nothing makes a statement quite like wearing prints. From vibrant florals to bold animal prints, incorporating patterns into your wardrobe can instantly elevate your look and showcase your unique sense of style. However, mastering the art of wearing prints requires some finesse. To help you navigate this fashion frontier, we’ve compiled a comprehensive guide to the dos and don’ts of wearing prints.

Do: Start Small

If you’re new to wearing prints or feeling hesitant about diving into bold patterns, start small. Opt for pieces with subtle prints, such as pinstripes or delicate polka dots. This allows you to ease into wearing prints without feeling overwhelmed. Once you feel more comfortable, you can gradually incorporate bolder prints into your outfits.

Don’t: Overdo It

While wearing prints can add visual interest to your ensemble, it’s essential to avoid overdoing it. Mixing too many prints in one outfit can create a chaotic and overwhelming look. Instead, opt for one statement print and keep the rest of your outfit relatively simple. This allows the print to take center stage without competing with other patterns.

Do: Mix and Match

One of the fun aspects of wearing prints is experimenting with mixing and matching different patterns. However, mastering the art of print mixing requires a bit of finesse. When combining prints, look for complementary colors or themes to tie the look together. For example, pair a floral top with a striped skirt for a playful yet cohesive ensemble.

Don’t: Clash Prints

While print mixing can be a stylish choice, it’s essential to avoid clashing prints. Combining patterns that clash in color or scale can create a disjointed and unflattering look. Instead, opt for prints that complement each other and create a harmonious visual effect.

Do: Embrace Statement Pieces

Printed statement pieces, such as a bold floral blazer or a vibrant animal print skirt, can instantly elevate your look and add a touch of personality to your outfit. When wearing a statement print, keep the rest of your outfit simple to let the print shine.

Don’t: Shy Away from Accessories

Accessories are a great way to incorporate prints into your outfit without committing to a full-on printed ensemble. Try adding a printed scarf, handbag, or pair of shoes to add a pop of pattern to your look. Just be sure to keep the rest of your outfit relatively neutral to avoid overwhelming the eye.

Do: Consider Proportions

When wearing prints, it’s essential to consider proportions to ensure a balanced and flattering look. If you’re petite, opt for smaller, more delicate prints that won’t overwhelm your frame. Conversely, if you’re tall or curvy, you can pull off larger prints with ease.

Don’t: Forget About Fabric

The type of fabric can significantly impact how a print looks and feels. Opt for high-quality fabrics that drape well and hold their shape to ensure that the print looks polished and sophisticated. Avoid cheap, stiff fabrics that can make the print appear cheap and tacky.

In conclusion, wearing prints is a fun and stylish way to express your personality through fashion. By following these dos and don’ts, you can confidently incorporate prints into your wardrobe and create bold, eye-catching looks that turn heads wherever you go. So go ahead, embrace your inner fashionista, and let your prints do the talking!
