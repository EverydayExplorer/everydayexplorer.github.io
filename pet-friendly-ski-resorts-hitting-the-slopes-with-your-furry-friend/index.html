Pet-Friendly Ski Resorts: Hitting the Slopes with Your Furry Friend


As the winter season approaches, many pet owners find themselves faced with the dilemma of what to do with their furry friends while they hit the slopes. Luckily, there are a number of pet-friendly ski resorts that cater to both humans and their beloved pets. In this blog post, we will explore the top pet-friendly ski resorts around the world, and provide you with all the information you need to plan the perfect ski vacation with your four-legged companion.

1. Aspen Snowmass, Colorado: Known for its world-class skiing, Aspen Snowmass is also a haven for pet owners. With numerous pet-friendly hotels and accommodations, as well as designated pet-friendly trails, you and your furry friend can enjoy the slopes together. Some hotels even offer pet-sitting services, so you can hit the black diamonds while your pet is well taken care of.

2. Whistler Blackcomb, British Columbia: Located in Canada, Whistler Blackcomb is renowned for its breathtaking views and extensive ski terrain. This resort also welcomes pets with open arms. Many hotels in the area offer pet-friendly rooms, and there are plenty of pet-friendly activities to keep your furry friend entertained while you’re on the slopes. From dog sledding to snowshoeing, there’s something for every pet at Whistler Blackcomb.

3. Stowe Mountain Resort, Vermont: If you’re looking for a pet-friendly ski resort on the East Coast, Stowe Mountain Resort is a top choice. With its charming New England atmosphere and pet-friendly accommodations, Stowe offers a cozy and welcoming environment for both you and your pet. Take a break from the slopes and explore the nearby hiking trails or enjoy a leisurely walk through the picturesque town of Stowe.

4. Lake Louise Ski Resort, Alberta: Nestled in the heart of the Canadian Rockies, Lake Louise Ski Resort is a pet-friendly paradise. With its stunning mountain vistas and pet-friendly hotels, this resort offers a truly unforgettable experience for both you and your furry friend. Take a break from skiing and explore the snow-covered trails together, or simply relax by a cozy fireplace in one of the pet-friendly lodges.

5. Park City Mountain Resort, Utah: Known for its world-class skiing and vibrant après-ski scene, Park City Mountain Resort is also a great choice for pet owners. With its pet-friendly accommodations and designated pet-friendly areas, you can enjoy the best of both worlds. After a day on the slopes, take a stroll through the charming town of Park City, which offers a variety of pet-friendly shops and restaurants.

In conclusion, hitting the slopes with your furry friend is not only possible, but also highly enjoyable thanks to the numerous pet-friendly ski resorts around the world. From the majestic mountains of Colorado to the picturesque landscapes of Canada, there are plenty of options for pet owners seeking a winter adventure with their beloved pets. So pack your bags, grab your skis, and embark on a memorable ski vacation with your furry friend by your side.
