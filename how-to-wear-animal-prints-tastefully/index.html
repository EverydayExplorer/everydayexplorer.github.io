How to Wear Animal Prints Tastefully


Animal prints have been a fashion staple for decades, but they can be intimidating to incorporate into your wardrobe. When done right, animal prints can add a touch of wild sophistication to any outfit. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or a newbie to the trend, mastering the art of wearing animal prints tastefully is a valuable skill. From leopard spots to zebra stripes, here are some tips to help you rock animal prints with confidence.

Embrace Subtlety

The key to wearing animal prints tastefully is to embrace subtlety. Instead of going all out with a head-to-toe animal print ensemble, start small. Opt for a chic leopard print scarf, a pair of zebra print shoes, or a snake print belt. These subtle touches can instantly elevate your look without overwhelming it.

Mix and Match

Don’t be afraid to mix and match different animal prints. The key is to keep the rest of your outfit simple and let the prints take center stage. For example, pair a leopard print blouse with a snake print skirt for a stylish and daring combination. Just remember to stick to the same color palette to ensure a cohesive look.

Neutral Accessories

When wearing animal prints, it’s important to balance the boldness with neutral accessories. Choose solid-colored handbags, shoes, and jewelry to complement the prints without competing with them. This will help create a polished and put-together ensemble.

Use Animal Prints as Accents

If you’re still hesitant about diving into full-on animal prints, consider using them as accents. A leopard print belt, a zebra print scarf, or even animal print earrings can add a touch of edginess to an otherwise simple outfit. These small details can make a big impact without feeling overwhelming.

Play with Texture

Experiment with different textures of animal prints. From silk to faux fur, there’s a wide range of textures available in animal prints. Mixing textures can add depth and visual interest to your outfit while keeping the print the focal point.

Keep it Simple

When wearing animal prints, less is often more. Avoid over-accessorizing or pairing multiple animal print pieces together. Instead, focus on one statement piece and build your outfit around it. This will ensure that the print remains the highlight of your look.

Confidence is Key

Ultimately, the most important tip for wearing animal prints tastefully is to wear them with confidence. Whether you’re rocking a leopard print coat or snake print boots, owning your look is essential. When you feel good in what you’re wearing, it shows, and that’s the true mark of tasteful fashion.

In conclusion, animal prints can be a fun and daring addition to your wardrobe when worn tastefully. By embracing subtlety, mixing and matching, incorporating neutral accessories, using prints as accents, playing with texture, keeping it simple, and exuding confidence, you can master the art of wearing animal prints with style and sophistication. So, go ahead and unleash your wild side with these tips in mind!
