The Best Fashion Podcasts for Behind-the-Scenes Insights


Are you a fashion enthusiast on the lookout for some valuable behind-the-scenes insights? Look no further! Fashion podcasts have become a popular medium for fashion mavens to gain insider knowledge, industry trends, and exclusive conversations with influential figures in the fashion world. We have compiled a list of the best fashion podcasts that offer a deep dive into the glamorous and sometimes enigmatic world of fashion.

1. ‘The Business of Fashion Podcast’:
Considered the holy grail of fashion podcasts, ‘The Business of Fashion’ podcast is hosted by Imran Amed, the founder and CEO of The Business of Fashion. This podcast features candid conversations with fashion insiders, industry experts, and influential designers. From exploring the business side of fashion to dissecting the latest trends, this podcast delivers insightful content that is both engaging and educational.

2. ‘Vogue Podcast’:
Hosted by the editors of Vogue magazine, this podcast brings you conversations with fashion industry heavyweights, profiles of up-and-coming designers, discussions on industry news, and behind-the-scenes coverage of fashion events. With its unparalleled access to the world of fashion, ‘Vogue Podcast’ offers listeners an all-encompassing look at the ever-evolving fashion landscape.

3. ‘Fashion No Filter’:
Curated by fashion journalists Monica Ainley and Camille Charriere, ‘Fashion No Filter’ takes an intimate and entertaining approach to uncovering the real stories behind the glamour. With interviews with industry insiders, editors, and photographers, this podcast delves into the realities of the industry, breaking down the barriers between fashion and its audience.

4. ‘Dressed: The History of Fashion’:
If you’re interested in the historical aspects of fashion, look no further than ‘Dressed: The History of Fashion.’ Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this podcast explores the intersection of fashion, culture, and identity throughout the ages. From ancient civilizations to contemporary trends, this show will broaden your understanding of fashion’s rich history.

5. ‘The Memory of…With John Galliano’:
For those seeking a more avant-garde take on fashion, John Galliano’s podcast, ‘The Memory Of…With John Galliano,’ is an absolute must-listen. In each episode, Galliano takes listeners on a journey through his creative process, inspirations, and experiences. This podcast offers a unique glimpse into the mind of one of fashion’s most visionary designers.

6. ‘Pop Fashion’:
‘Pop Fashion’ is a podcast that combines fashion, culture, and business. Hosted by Lisa Rowan and Kaarin Vembar, this show provides insights on everything from fashion industry news to ethical and sustainable practices. With a focus on the intersection of fashion and everyday life, ‘Pop Fashion’ is both informative and entertaining.

7. ‘Fashion Revolution Podcast’:
If you’re passionate about sustainability in fashion, the ‘Fashion Revolution Podcast’ is a must-listen. Hosted by Tamsin Blanchard, this podcast features interviews with industry leaders, activists, and influencers who are driving change in the fashion landscape. From exploring the impacts of fast fashion to highlighting innovative sustainable practices, this podcast offers a thought-provoking and informative perspective on sustainable fashion.

8. ‘The American Fashion Podcast’:
‘The American Fashion Podcast’ takes a deep dive into the American fashion industry, covering a wide range of topics such as design, manufacturing, retail, and entrepreneurship. Hosted by Charles Beckwith and Cathy Schepis, this podcast features insightful interviews with fashion insiders, designers, and entrepreneurs. Whether you’re looking for tips on launching your own fashion brand or want to learn more about the business side of fashion, this podcast has got you covered.

9. ‘Fashion Culture Design’:
Hosted by Simon Collins, former dean of the School of Fashion at Parsons School of Design, ‘Fashion Culture Design’ offers a fresh perspective on the fashion industry. With interviews featuring designers, industry experts, and cultural influencers, this podcast explores the intersection of fashion, art, and culture, providing thought-provoking insights into the creative process and the evolving nature of fashion.

10. ‘Fashion Hags’:
For a lighthearted take on all things fashion, ‘Fashion Hags’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by Abby Shumka, Evan Ducharme, and Katie Garnham, this show combines humor and critical analysis in discussing industry news, trends, and pop culture moments. It’s a fun and refreshing take on fashion that doesn’t take itself too seriously.

Now that you’re armed with this list of the best fashion podcasts, you can stay up to date on the latest industry trends, gain insider insights, and explore the fascinating world behind the glamorous facade. So sit back, relax, and tune in to these podcasts for an enriching and entertaining fashion journey!
