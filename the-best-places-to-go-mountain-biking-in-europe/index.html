The Best Places to Go Mountain Biking in Europe


Welcome to an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Europe, where adventure-seeking mountain bikers can find their ultimate playground. With its diverse terrain, stunning vistas, and well-maintained trails, Europe boasts some of the best places in the world to indulge in the thrill of mountain biking. Whether you are an adrenaline junkie craving steep descents or a nature lover seeking tranquility, Europe has you covered. Let’s saddle up and explore the top destinations for mountain biking on this incredible continent.

1. Finale Ligure, Italy:
Nestled on the Italian Riviera, Finale Ligure offers a unique mountain biking experience. Its Mediterranean climate, combined with a variety of trails, makes it a year-round mountain biking destination. From the adrenaline-pumping downhills of Nato Base 23 to the technical climbs of Caprazoppa, Finale Ligure caters to riders of all skill levels. The breathtaking sea views and charming Italian villages add an extra touch of magic to your biking adventures.

2. Morzine, France:
As a mountain biker’s paradise, Morzine stands tall in the French Alps. Known for its extensive network of trails, it offers endless opportunities for adrenaline-fueled descents and technical challenges. The Portes du Soleil bike park, with its fantastic lift system, provides access to over 600 kilometers of trails across France and Switzerland. Morzine also hosts a vibrant mountain biking community, making it an ideal place to connect with fellow riders and share epic tales over a post-ride croissant.

3. Bike Park Wales, United Kingdom:
Nestled in the picturesque Welsh valleys, Bike Park Wales is an absolute gem for mountain biking enthusiasts. Boasting over 40 trails catering to various skill levels, this park truly has something for everyone. From flowy jump lines to technical singletracks, the diverse terrain ensures an exciting ride every time. The park’s commitment to sustainability and well-maintained trails makes it a top choice for those seeking an eco-friendly biking experience.

4. Zermatt, Switzerland:
Welcome to the land of breathtaking landscapes and iconic mountain peaks. Zermatt, with its postcard-worthy scenery, is a paradise for mountain bikers. Offering an extensive network of trails, riders can explore the stunning Swiss Alps while enjoying adrenaline-pumping descents. From the challenging singletracks of Schwarzsee to the picturesque Gornergrat trail, Zermatt’s trails offer a mix of technical challenges and awe-inspiring beauty.

5. Rotorua, New Zealand (Yes, it’s in Europe!):
Okay, just kidding! We couldn’t resist sneaking in this incredible destination, although it’s technically not in Europe. Rotorua, located in New Zealand, deserves a special mention for its world-class mountain biking offerings. The Whakarewarewa Forest, known as the “Redwoods,” is a mountain biking hub with an extensive trail network. With trails for all abilities, Rotorua is renowned for its flowy berms, well-built jump lines, and stunning geothermal landscapes. It’s definitely worth the extra air miles!

So, there you have it: a tantalizing taste of the best places to go mountain biking in Europe (and a bonus destination!). These destinations offer not only thrilling trails but also unforgettable experiences amidst stunning natural beauty. Remember to always ride responsibly, respect the environment, and check local regulations before setting off on your adventure. Whether you’re a seasoned rider or a beginner, Europe’s mountain biking scene is ready to welcome you with open arms. So grab your helmet, hop on your bike, and get ready for an unforgettable two-wheeled adventure!
