Artisanal Agave Spirits: Beyond Tequila and Mezcal


When people think of agave spirits, most tend to immediately associate them with Tequila and Mezcal. While these two iconic Mexican spirits have gained immense popularity worldwide, it’s worth exploring the world beyond them to discover the hidden gems of artisanal agave spirits.

Artisanal agave spirits are crafted with care and precision, showcasing the rich cultural heritage and traditional production methods of different regions in Mexico. These spirits are made from the agave plant, a succulent known for its unique flavors and versatility. Just as wine varies with grape varietals and terroir, artisanal agave spirits offer a wide range of flavors, aromas, and profiles depending on the type of agave used, the region it’s grown in, and the production techniques employed.

So, let’s embark on a journey to explore the magical world of artisanal agave spirits that go beyond Tequila and Mezcal:

1. Bacanora: Originating from the state of Sonora, Bacanora is an agave spirit made from the Angustifolia Haw variety of agave. With its smooth and smoky flavor profile, Bacanora is often compared to Mezcal. It has gained recognition globally for its unique characteristics and is now protected by a Denomination of Origin (DO).

2. Raicilla: Hailing from the western state of Jalisco, Raicilla is often referred to as the ‘Mexican moonshine.’ Made from wild agave varieties, this spirit offers a diverse range of flavors, from fruity and floral to earthy and herbaceous. It’s a true representation of the biodiversity found in Jalisco.

3. Sotol: Although not technically an agave spirit, Sotol deserves a mention due to its similarities. Produced from the Desert Spoon (Dasylirion wheeleri) plant, Sotol is native to the Chihuahuan Desert in northern Mexico. Its flavor profile is clean, with herbal and vegetal notes. Sotol distilleries are often family-owned, preserving traditional production methods.

4. Bacanora: Similar to the aforementioned Bacanora, Lechuguilla is another fascinating spirit produced in Sonora. Made from Agave parryi, this rare spirit offers a complex flavor profile with hints of citrus, spice, and earthiness. It’s often compared to a smoky Tequila.

5. Comiteco: A lesser-known agave spirit from Chiapas, Comiteco is produced from Agave comiteco, which thrives in the highlands. This spirit has an alluring sweetness with notes of tropical fruit and honey, making it a unique addition to any agave lover’s collection.

6. Choricero: Hailing from Oaxaca, Choricero is a spirit crafted from roasted agave hearts, much like Mezcal. However, it differs in its aging process, which involves being aged in new oak barrels, giving it a rich and woody flavor profile.

7. Pulque: With a history dating back thousands of years, Pulque is an ancient fermented agave beverage. Made from the sap of the Agave americana, it offers a slightly sweet and tangy taste. While not technically a distilled spirit, it’s worth mentioning for its cultural significance and contribution to the world of agave.

These are just a few examples of the vast range of artisanal agave spirits waiting to be discovered. Each of these spirits represents a unique expression of the land, tradition, and craftsmanship. Exploring them allows us to delve deeper into the rich Mexican culture and heritage associated with agave spirits.

In recent years, there has been increased interest in these lesser-known agave spirits among spirits connoisseurs and cocktail enthusiasts. Bartenders and mixologists have begun incorporating them into innovative cocktails, showcasing their versatility and depth of flavors.

As the global interest in agave spirits grows, it’s important to recognize and support the artisanal producers who uphold tradition, sustainable practices, and promote biodiversity. Choosing artisanal agave spirits means not only enjoying exceptional flavor profiles but also supporting local communities and preserving cultural heritage.

So, the next time you find yourself in search of a unique and unforgettable libation, look beyond Tequila and Mezcal. Explore the fascinating world of artisanal agave spirits and let your taste buds embark on a sensory adventure that captures the essence of Mexico’s centuries-old agave traditions. Cheers to the wonders that lie beyond the well-trodden path!
