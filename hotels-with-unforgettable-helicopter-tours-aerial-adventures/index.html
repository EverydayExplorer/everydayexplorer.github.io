Hotels with Unforgettable Helicopter Tours: Aerial Adventures


When it comes to luxurious and unforgettable travel experiences, nothing quite compares to a helicopter tour. Imagine soaring through the sky, taking in breathtaking aerial views of stunning landscapes, iconic landmarks, and vibrant cities. Now, combine that exhilarating experience with the comfort and elegance of a luxury hotel, and you have the recipe for an extraordinary vacation. In this blog post, we will explore hotels around the world that offer unforgettable helicopter tours, allowing you to take your vacation to new heights - quite literally!

1. Amangiri - Utah, USA:
Nestled in the heart of the dramatic Utah desert, Amangiri is a haven of tranquility and beauty. This luxury resort offers guests the opportunity to embark on helicopter tours that showcase the mesmerizing landscapes of the Grand Canyon, Monument Valley, and Lake Powell. From the comfort of your helicopter, you’ll witness the vibrant colors and vastness of these natural wonders, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

2. Emirates One&Only Wolgan Valley - New South Wales, Australia:
Located amidst the picturesque Blue Mountains in Australia, Emirates One&Only Wolgan Valley offers a unique blend of luxury and adventure. Take to the skies in a helicopter and explore the stunning wilderness from above. Soar over the iconic Three Sisters rock formation, marvel at the cascading waterfalls, and enjoy panoramic views of the breathtaking Wolgan Valley. This aerial adventure promises an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

3. Singita Boulders Lodge - Sabi Sand Game Reserve, South Africa:
For those seeking a wildlife-filled adventure, Singita Boulders Lodge in South Africa is the perfect destination. Embark on a helicopter safari and witness the incredible Big Five - lions, elephants, leopards, rhinos, and buffalos - in their natural habitat. Fly over vast savannahs, winding rivers, and lush forests, and get up close and personal with some of the most majestic creatures on Earth. This once-in-a-lifetime experience will leave you in awe of the wonders of the African wilderness.

4. The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong - Hong Kong:
If you prefer a cityscape over natural landscapes, The Ritz-Carlton in Hong Kong offers a unique helicopter tour that showcases the vibrant city from above. Fly over the iconic Victoria Harbour, admire the stunning skyline dotted with towering skyscrapers, and get a bird’s-eye view of famous landmarks such as the Peak and the Symphony of Lights. This aerial adventure provides a fresh perspective on one of the world’s most dynamic cities.

5. Awasi Patagonia - Torres del Paine, Chile:
Immerse yourself in the untamed beauty of Patagonia with a helicopter tour from Awasi Patagonia. Soar above the majestic Torres del Paine National Park, a UNESCO World Biosphere Reserve, and marvel at its awe-inspiring glaciers, snow-capped mountains, and crystal-clear lakes. This aerial adventure will leave you breathless as you witness the raw power and untouched wilderness of this remote corner of the Earth.

These are just a few examples of hotels around the world that offer unforgettable helicopter tours. Whether you’re seeking natural wonders, wildlife encounters, or cityscapes, these aerial adventures will take your vacation to new heights. So, why settle for a conventional travel experience when you can elevate your journey with a helicopter tour? Embark on an adventure that will leave you with memories that will last a lifetime.
