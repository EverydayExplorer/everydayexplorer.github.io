10 Easy-to-Make Pasta Recipes for Home Cooks


Pasta is undoubtedly one of the most beloved and versatile foods in the world. It’s quick to cook, budget-friendly, and can be prepared in countless ways to suit any palate. Whether you’re a novice in the kitchen or a seasoned home cook looking for some fresh inspiration, these 10 easy-to-make pasta recipes are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your family and friends. Plus, they’re perfect for busy weeknight dinners or special occasions when you want to whip up something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen.

1. Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti carbonara is a Roman classic known for its creamy sauce and savory pancetta. To make this dish, all you need are eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, spaghetti, pancetta, and black pepper. The combination of these simple ingredients creates a luscious and comforting meal that’s ready in just 20 minutes.

Recipe: - Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. - While pasta cooks, sauté diced pancetta until crispy. - In a bowl, whisk eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper. - Drain pasta and immediately toss it with the egg mixture. - Add crispy pancetta and serve hot.

2. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

If you’re a fan of creamy pasta sauces, this recipe is a winner. It’s a delightful blend of garlic, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese that coats your favorite pasta beautifully. Pair it with linguine or fettuccine for an indulgent experience.

Recipe: - Sauté minced garlic in butter until fragrant. - Pour in heavy cream and simmer until it thickens. - Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until smooth. - Toss with cooked pasta, and garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan.

3. Tomato Basil Penne

For a lighter pasta option with a burst of flavor, try tomato basil penne. It’s a classic Italian dish that combines the sweetness of ripe tomatoes with fresh basil and garlic. This recipe is perfect for those looking for a healthier pasta option.

Recipe: - Sauté minced garlic in olive oil until aromatic. - Add diced tomatoes and simmer until they break down. - Toss in fresh basil leaves and cooked penne. - Finish with a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.

4. One-Pot Chicken Alfredo

Who doesn’t love a creamy Alfredo sauce? This one-pot chicken Alfredo recipe simplifies the process by cooking everything together, making cleanup a breeze. Creamy, cheesy, and comforting, it’s a crowd-pleaser for sure.

Recipe: - Sauté diced chicken until cooked through. - Add garlic, heavy cream, chicken broth, and pasta. - Simmer until pasta is tender and the sauce is creamy. - Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.

5. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Linguine

If you’re a seafood lover, this lemon garlic shrimp linguine is a must-try. It combines the freshness of shrimp with a zesty lemon garlic sauce. This recipe feels gourmet but is surprisingly simple to prepare.

Recipe: - Sauté shrimp in olive oil until pink and cooked through. - Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant. - Deglaze the pan with white wine and lemon juice. - Toss in cooked linguine and fresh parsley.

6. Pesto Pasta with Cherry Tomatoes

Pesto pasta is a classic Italian favorite. This version adds sweet cherry tomatoes for a burst of freshness. It’s a colorful and flavorful dish that’s perfect for a summer evening.

Recipe: - Blend basil, pine nuts, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil to make pesto. - Toss cooked pasta with the pesto sauce. - Add halved cherry tomatoes and fresh mozzarella balls. - Garnish with basil leaves and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

7. Mushroom and Spinach Alfredo

For a vegetarian pasta option that’s both creamy and hearty, try mushroom and spinach Alfredo. The earthy flavors of mushrooms paired with the freshness of spinach create a well-balanced dish.

Recipe: - Sauté sliced mushrooms until browned. - Add minced garlic and spinach and cook until wilted. - Pour in heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese. - Toss with cooked fettuccine and season to taste.

8. Cacio e Pepe

Cacio e Pepe is a Roman classic that showcases the simplicity of Italian cuisine. With just three ingredients—Pecorino Romano cheese, black pepper, and spaghetti—you can create a dish that’s both elegant and delicious.

Recipe: - Cook spaghetti until al dente. - Toss with grated Pecorino Romano cheese and freshly ground black pepper. - Add pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.

9. Veggie Primavera

For a pasta dish packed with vibrant vegetables, try veggie primavera. This recipe is highly customizable, allowing you to use your favorite seasonal veggies. It’s a healthy and colorful option that’s as delicious as it is nutritious.

Recipe: - Sauté a variety of chopped vegetables in olive oil until tender. - Toss with cooked pasta and a splash of vegetable broth. - Finish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

10. Sausage and Broccoli Rabe Orecchiette

Sausage and broccoli rabe orecchiette is a hearty and satisfying pasta dish that combines savory sausage with the bitterness of broccoli rabe. It’s a taste of Southern Italy that’s easy to replicate at home.

Recipe: - Sauté Italian sausage until browned and cooked through. - Add blanched broccoli rabe and garlic. - Toss with cooked orecchiette and red pepper flakes.
