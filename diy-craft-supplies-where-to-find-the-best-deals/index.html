DIY Craft Supplies: Where to Find the Best Deals


Are you a DIY enthusiast constantly on the hunt for affordable and high-quality craft supplies? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll explore various sources and websites where you can find the best deals on DIY craft supplies. Whether you’re into sewing, painting, scrapbooking, or any other craft, we’ve got you covered.

Online Marketplaces

  1. Amazon - This e-commerce giant offers a wide range of craft supplies at competitive prices. With its vast inventory and user reviews, you can easily find the perfect materials for your next DIY project. Amazon also frequently offers discounts, coupons, and deals on craft supplies.

  2. eBay - Another popular online marketplace where you can find an immense variety of craft supplies. eBay allows you to bid on items or buy them outright, giving you the freedom to choose the best deal that suits your budget.

  3. Etsy - If you’re looking for unique and handmade craft supplies, Etsy is the go-to platform. Many independent artists and sellers offer beautifully crafted items at competitive prices. You can also find vintage supplies that add a touch of nostalgia to your DIY projects.

Craft Store Websites

  1. Michaels - Known for its wide selection of art and craft supplies, Michaels also offers discounted items through their website. They often have seasonal sales, coupon codes, and weekly promotions, making it an ideal destination for frugal crafters.

  2. Joann - Joann is a leading craft and fabric store that features regular sales and promotions on their website. They offer an extensive selection of materials for all DIY enthusiasts, from beginners to experienced crafters. Keep an eye out for their weekly deals and online-exclusive discounts.

  3. Hobby Lobby - Hobby Lobby caters to a variety of craft categories, including sewing, painting, scrapbooking, and more. They frequently offer online special offers, clearance sales, and discounts on specific departments. Keep an eye on their website for the latest deals.

Discount and Wholesale Stores

  1. Dollar Tree - Dollar Tree is a treasure trove for budget-conscious crafters. While their selection may be limited, you can find a variety of craft supplies at unbeatable prices. From crafting basics like glue and tape to seasonal decorations, Dollar Tree is worth checking out.

  2. Oriental Trading - If you’re looking to buy craft supplies in bulk at wholesale prices, Oriental Trading is a great option. They specialize in party supplies but have an extensive craft section featuring discounted items. Their website allows you to browse by theme, making it easy to find supplies for specific projects.

Membership Programs and Rewards

  1. AC Moore Rewards Program - AC Moore offers a rewards program where members can earn points for every purchase. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases, allowing you to get even better deals on your craft supplies.

  2. Michaels Rewards Program - Michaels also has a rewards program that offers exclusive discounts, early access to sales, and personalized coupons. By joining, you can maximize your savings on craft supplies.

Conclusion

Finding the best deals on DIY craft supplies doesn’t have to be a daunting task. By exploring the various sources and websites mentioned in this blog post, you’ll be able to save money while pursuing your creative endeavors. Whether you prefer online marketplaces, craft store websites, or discount stores, there are plenty of options available for every budget. Happy crafting!
