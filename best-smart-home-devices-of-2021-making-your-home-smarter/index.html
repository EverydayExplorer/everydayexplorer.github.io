Best Smart Home Devices of 2021: Making Your Home Smarter


In recent years, the concept of the smart home has gone from being a futuristic idea to a reality that many homeowners are enjoying today. With technological advancements, we now have a wide array of smart home devices that can make our lives easier, more convenient, and efficient. From controlling our lights and appliances with a simple voice command to monitoring our homes remotely, these devices have revolutionized the way we interact with our living spaces.

In this blog, we will explore the best smart home devices of 2021 that can take your home to the next level in terms of automation and connectivity. Whether you are a tech enthusiast or simply looking for ways to make your life more comfortable, these devices are worth considering.

1. Amazon Echo (4th Generation)

The Amazon Echo has been a game-changer in the smart home industry since its introduction. The 4th generation Echo takes the experience to the next level with its improved sound quality, sleek design, and integrated smart home hub. With Alexa as your virtual assistant, you can control your smart devices through voice commands, play music, set reminders, and even make calls.

2. Philips Hue Smart Bulbs

Transforming your home’s lighting is made easy with Philips Hue Smart Bulbs. These energy-efficient LED bulbs can be controlled remotely via a smartphone app or linked to your virtual assistant. With endless color options and dimming capabilities, you can create the perfect ambiance for any occasion.

3. Nest Learning Thermostat

The Nest Learning Thermostat is a smart device that improves your home’s energy efficiency while keeping you comfortable. It learns your preferred temperature settings and adjusts itself accordingly. With its built-in sensors and connectivity to your smartphone, you can control the thermostat remotely, ensuring a cozy environment whenever you arrive home.

4. Ring Video Doorbell

Enhance your home’s security with the Ring Video Doorbell. This device allows you to see and communicate with visitors at your door, even when you’re not at home. With its motion sensor and night vision capabilities, you’ll receive instant alerts on your smartphone and have a clear view of who’s at your doorstep.

5. Google Nest Cam Outdoor Security Camera

The Google Nest Cam Outdoor Security Camera offers 24/7 monitoring of your home’s exterior. With its weatherproof design and high-definition video quality, you can keep an eye on your property from anywhere. The camera also features two-way audio, allowing you to speak to anyone on your property through the app.

6. Samsung SmartThings Hub

If you have multiple smart devices from different brands, the Samsung SmartThings Hub can bring them all together into one centralized system. With this hub, you can control and automate various devices, create routines, and monitor your home’s security - all from a single app.

7. August Smart Lock Pro

Tired of fumbling with keys? The August Smart Lock Pro allows you to control your door locks using your smartphone. You can grant temporary access to guests, monitor who enters and leaves your home, and even connect it to your virtual assistant for voice control capabilities.

8. iRobot Roomba s9+ Robot Vacuum

Make cleaning a breeze with the iRobot Roomba s9+ Robot Vacuum. With its advanced mapping technology and self-emptying feature, this robot vacuum can efficiently navigate through your home and keep your floors spotless. You can schedule cleaning sessions, control it remotely, and even integrate it with your smart home routines.

9. Ecobee SmartThermostat with Voice Control

The Ecobee SmartThermostat offers voice control, allowing you to adjust the temperature and control other smart devices effortlessly. With its built-in Amazon Alexa, you can ask for the weather, play music, or even control your entire smart home system.

10. TP-Link Kasa Smart Plug

Transform any ordinary device into a smart one with the TP-Link Kasa Smart Plug. This plug allows you to control your devices remotely, set schedules, and monitor energy consumption. With its easy setup and compatibility with virtual assistants, you can control your devices with a simple voice command.

In conclusion, smart home devices have become increasingly popular, offering homeowners a new level of convenience, comfort, and security. With the variety of options available in 2021, you can find the perfect smart home devices that suit your needs and budget. From voice-controlled virtual assistants to automated security systems and energy-efficient appliances, these devices can transform your home into a futuristic oasis. So why not embrace the latest technological advancements and make your home smarter today?
