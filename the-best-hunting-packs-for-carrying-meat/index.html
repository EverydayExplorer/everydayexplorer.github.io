The Best Hunting Packs for Carrying Meat


One of the most critical aspects of any successful hunting trip is the ability to transport your hard-earned game back to your vehicle or campsite. This is where a reliable hunting pack comes into play. The right hunting pack can make all the difference in terms of comfort, functionality, and durability.

When it comes to carrying meat, you’ll want a pack that can handle the weight and securely hold your harvest. But with so many options on the market, how do you choose the best hunting pack for carrying meat? In this blog post, we will discuss some of the top-rated hunting packs specifically designed for carrying meat, taking into consideration their capacity, features, and overall performance.

1. Badlands 2200 Hunting Pack:
The Badlands 2200 Hunting Pack is a popular choice among hunters thanks to its spacious design and rugged construction. With a total capacity of 2,255 cubic inches, this pack can easily handle large quantities of meat. The pack features a meat shelf that allows you to carry your harvest close to your body, minimizing the strain on your back and shoulders. Additionally, it comes with a built-in hydration reservoir and multiple pockets for organizing your gear.

2. Eberlestock X2 Hunting Pack:
The Eberlestock X2 Hunting Pack is another excellent option for carrying meat. Its unique design allows you to carry heavy loads without sacrificing comfort. The pack is equipped with a sturdy aluminum frame and adjustable shoulder straps, providing optimal weight distribution. The X2 pack also offers a spacious main compartment, additional pockets, and a built-in meat carrier that keeps your game clean and well-ventilated.

3. Tenzing TC 1500 Hunting Pack:
The Tenzing TC 1500 Hunting Pack is a lightweight and versatile option for meat transportation. With a capacity of 1,500 cubic inches, it strikes the perfect balance between size and functionality. The pack features a large main compartment, side compression straps, and a padded waist belt for added stability. The TC 1500 also includes a removable meat bag that can be easily cleaned after use.

4. Alps OutdoorZ Pathfinder Hunting Pack:
For the budget-conscious hunter, the Alps OutdoorZ Pathfinder Hunting Pack is an excellent choice. Despite its affordable price tag, this pack offers exceptional durability and functionality. It has a total capacity of 2,700 cubic inches and features a fully adjustable torso range, allowing for a customized fit. The Pathfinder pack also includes a removable fanny pack and a meat-hauling system for convenient meat transportation.

5. Mystery Ranch Metcalf Hunting Pack:
If you’re looking for a premium hunting pack that can handle heavy loads of meat, the Mystery Ranch Metcalf Hunting Pack is worth considering. With a total capacity of 4,500 cubic inches, this pack can carry sizeable amounts of game. It features a unique three-zip design for easy access to the main compartment, as well as a built-in meat shelf for secure transportation.

In conclusion, selecting the best hunting pack for carrying meat is essential for any serious hunter. The right pack should offer sufficient capacity, durability, and comfort. The Badlands 2200 Hunting Pack, Eberlestock X2 Hunting Pack, Tenzing TC 1500 Hunting Pack, Alps OutdoorZ Pathfinder Hunting Pack, and the Mystery Ranch Metcalf Hunting Pack all meet these criteria and are highly rated among hunters. Consider your specific needs and preferences when choosing the perfect hunting pack, and remember that investing in a high-quality pack will ensure years of successful and enjoyable hunting trips.
