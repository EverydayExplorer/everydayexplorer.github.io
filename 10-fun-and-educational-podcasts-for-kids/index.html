10 Fun and Educational Podcasts for Kids


Welcome to our blog where we are excited to introduce you to the world of fun and educational podcasts for kids! In this digital age, podcasts have become a popular and effective tool for learning on the go. Whether you’re on a long car ride, at home, or simply looking for some engaging content, podcasts offer a world of knowledge and entertainment for your little ones. So without further ado, here are 10 fun and educational podcasts that your kids will love!

1. ‘Wow in the World’ - Hosted by Guy Raz and Mindy Thomas, this podcast takes children on exciting adventures exploring various scientific wonders and discoveries. From outer space to the depths of the ocean, each episode is packed with fascinating facts and humor that keeps kids hooked.

2. ‘Brains On!’ - This award-winning science podcast is both educational and entertaining. Co-hosted by Molly Bloom and a different child every week, it tackles curious questions about the world, featuring interviews with experts and experiments that your kids can try at home.

3. ‘But Why: A Podcast for Curious Kids’ - If you have a curious little one who loves asking ‘why?’, this is the perfect podcast for them. Each episode delves into topics like why we dream, how airplanes fly, or why we have seasons, providing in-depth answers to these intriguing questions.

4. ‘The Past and The Curious’ - This delightful podcast brings history to life through storytelling. Hosted by Mick Sullivan, it covers various fascinating topics from different time periods, making history engaging and relatable for young listeners.

5. ‘Smash Boom Best’ - Are your kids into friendly debates? This podcast presents epic showdowns between two popular things, like unicorns vs. dragons or pizza vs. tacos. It encourages critical thinking and supports children in formulating persuasive arguments.

6. ‘Story Pirates’ - Combining storytelling and comedy, ‘Story Pirates’ transforms children’s creative writing into hilarious skits and songs. This podcast sparks imagination and encourages young listeners to explore their own storytelling abilities.

7. ‘Tumble’ - Dive into the wonders of science with this podcast that explores big ideas through the lens of kid-friendly experiments and interviews with scientists. ‘Tumble’ inspires young minds to ask questions and discover the world around them.

8. ‘Circle Round’ - Perfect for bedtime or quiet time, ‘Circle Round’ presents folktales from around the world. Each story is beautifully narrated and accompanied by enchanting music, fostering imagination and cultural appreciation.

9. ‘Brains On! Spanish’ - Designed for children learning Spanish or bilingual kids, this spin-off of the original ‘Brains On!’ podcast explores scientific concepts in both English and Spanish. It’s a fantastic resource for building language skills while expanding scientific knowledge.

10. ‘Ear Snacks’ - Hosted by Andrew and Polly, this quirky podcast combines music, humor, and educational content. With each episode centered around a particular theme, ‘Ear Snacks’ invites young listeners to sing along, dance, and learn in a fun and interactive way.

These 10 podcasts offer a plethora of educational benefits, from fostering critical thinking and creativity to expanding knowledge across various subjects. Whether your child is a science enthusiast, a history lover, or simply enjoys a good story, there’s something for everyone in the world of kids’ podcasts.

So, the next time you’re looking for educational entertainment for your little ones, tune in to one of these podcasts and let the learning begin! With their captivating content and interactive nature, these podcasts are sure to keep your kids engaged and eager to explore the world around them. Happy listening!
