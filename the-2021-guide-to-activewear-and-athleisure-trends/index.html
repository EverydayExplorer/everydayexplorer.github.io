The 2021 Guide to Activewear and Athleisure Trends


In recent years, activewear and athleisure have taken the fashion world by storm, becoming a wardrobe staple for both fitness enthusiasts and fashionistas alike. The versatility and comfort of these clothing styles have made them a go-to choice for individuals looking to embrace a healthy and active lifestyle without compromising on style. As we transition into 2021, it’s time to take a closer look at the latest trends in activewear and athleisure that will undoubtedly make waves in the coming months.

1. Sustainability

As the importance of sustainable fashion continues to rise, more and more activewear brands are incorporating eco-friendly materials into their collections. From utilizing recycled fabrics, such as polyester made from recycled plastic bottles, to adopting more sustainable manufacturing processes, the focus on sustainability is driving a change in the activewear industry. Consumers now have the option to choose stylish and high-performance activewear that is also kind to the environment.

2. Bold Prints and Colors

Gone are the days of monotonous gym wear. This year, activewear is all about making a statement with bold prints and vibrant colors. Whether it’s a pair of leggings adorned with animal prints or a sports bra in a neon shade, expect to see more eye-catching designs that allow individuals to express their personality and stand out from the crowd. Pair these bold pieces with neutral basics to create a balanced and fashionable look.

3. Seamless Technology

Innovation in fabric technology has led to the rise of seamless activewear. This trend offers both style and functionality, as seamless garments provide a smoother and more comfortable fit. Seamless leggings, tops, and sports bras are designed to move with your body, offering optimal flexibility and support during workouts. This trend caters to the increasing demand for comfortable clothing that enhances performance without sacrificing style.

4. Hybrid Pieces

Athleisure has become a lifestyle, and as such, it has expanded beyond the gym or yoga studio. This year, expect to see more hybrid pieces that seamlessly transition from workout to everyday wear. Think leggings with stylish cutouts or sports bras with unique designs that can double as a trendy crop top. These versatile pieces allow individuals to effortlessly go from a workout session to running errands or meeting friends for brunch, all while looking fashionable and put together.

5. Elevated Basics

While bold prints and vibrant colors are making a splash, the importance of classic basics cannot be overlooked. Elevated basics, such as black leggings, white tees, and simple hoodies, serve as the foundation of any activewear wardrobe. These timeless pieces offer great flexibility and can be easily mixed and matched with trendier items. Investing in high-quality basics ensures that you always have a reliable and stylish ensemble to rely on, whether you’re hitting the gym or going for a casual outing.

6. Athleisure Accessories

Accessories play a crucial role in completing any outfit, and activewear is no exception. This year, athleisure accessories are set to take center stage. From sleek and functional gym bags to stylish sneakers and hats that effortlessly blend fashion and utility, these accessories provide the finishing touches to any activewear ensemble. Keep an eye out for unique designs and innovative features that cater to the needs of active individuals on the go.

Conclusion

The activewear and athleisure industry is constantly evolving, and the trends for 2021 focus on sustainability, bold prints and colors, seamless technology, hybrid pieces, elevated basics, and athleisure accessories. Whether you’re a fitness enthusiast looking to upgrade your workout gear or a fashion-forward individual embracing the athleisure trend, these trends offer an array of options to express your personal style while embracing an active and healthy lifestyle. So go ahead, explore the latest activewear trends and let your fashion choices motivate and inspire you to reach your fitness goals in style!
