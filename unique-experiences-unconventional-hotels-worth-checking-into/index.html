Unique Experiences: Unconventional Hotels Worth Checking Into


Are you tired of staying at traditional hotels that all look the same? Are you looking for a truly unforgettable and out-of-the-ordinary experience? Look no further! In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to explore some of the most unique and unconventional hotels around the world that are worth checking into.

1. Icehotel, Sweden
Imagine sleeping in a hotel made entirely of ice and snow! Located in the picturesque town of Jukkasjärvi, Sweden, the Icehotel offers a one-of-a-kind experience. Each winter, a team of artists designs and builds the hotel using tons of ice from the nearby Torne River. While staying at the Icehotel, you can sleep in an intricately carved ice suite under warm reindeer hides and sleeping bags.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya
Ever wanted to have breakfast with giraffes? At Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, you can! This charming boutique hotel allows you to share your morning meal with friendly giraffes who roam freely on the property. The Manor itself is a beautiful colonial-style building surrounded by lush vegetation, making it a truly unique encounter with nature.

3. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland
Have you dreamt of seeing the Northern Lights up close? Look no further than Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland. This magical resort offers glass igloos that allow you to sleep under the stars and watch the dazzling light show of the Aurora Borealis. It’s an experience like no other, combining luxury and nature in the remote Finnish Lapland.

4. The Manta Resort, Tanzania
If you’ve always wanted to sleep underwater, The Manta Resort in Zanzibar, Tanzania, should be on your bucket list. The resort offers a unique underwater room where you can spend the night surrounded by the colorful marine life of the Indian Ocean. The floating suite features windows on all sides, allowing you to have an unobstructed view of the stunning underwater world.

5. Treehotel, Sweden
Nestled in the forests of Harads, Sweden, the Treehotel offers guests a chance to stay in beautifully designed treehouses. From a UFO-shaped cabin to a mirrored cube, each treehouse offers a different architectural experience. You can reconnect with nature while enjoying modern amenities and breathtaking views of the surrounding forest.

6. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada
Ever wondered what it would be like to sleep in a suspended tree sphere? At Free Spirit Spheres in British Columbia, Canada, you can find out. These unique spherical treehouses are suspended from tree branches and offer a serene and peaceful experience. Each sphere is designed to provide maximum comfort and is a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

7. Dog Bark Park Inn, USA
For dog lovers, the Dog Bark Park Inn in Cottonwood, Idaho, is a must-visit. This quirky bed and breakfast is shaped like a giant beagle, complete with dog-themed decor. Inside, you’ll find cozy accommodations and a doggy-themed gift shop. It’s a truly fun and memorable experience for dog enthusiasts.

8. Skylodge Adventure Suites, Peru
If you have a sense of adventure and a love for stunning views, the Skylodge Adventure Suites in Sacred Valley, Peru, is the perfect place for you. These transparent capsules are suspended on a cliffside, offering panoramic views of the surrounding valley. To get to your suite, you have to climb up the cliff or take an exhilarating zipline. It’s an experience that will leave you breathless.

In conclusion, if you’re in search of a unique and unforgettable hotel experience, these unconventional hotels are worth checking into. Whether it’s sleeping in an ice suite, sharing a meal with giraffes, or staying in a suspended tree sphere, these hotels offer experiences that are far from ordinary. So, go ahead and add some excitement and adventure to your next vacation by choosing one of these extraordinary hotels.
