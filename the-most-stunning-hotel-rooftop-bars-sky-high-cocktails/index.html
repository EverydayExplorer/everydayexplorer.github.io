The Most Stunning Hotel Rooftop Bars: Sky-High Cocktails


In the world of hospitality, hotel rooftop bars have become the epitome of luxury and elegance. These elevated sanctuaries offer guests breathtaking panoramic views while sipping on exquisite cocktails. Today, we delve into the realm of the most stunning hotel rooftop bars, where sky-high cocktails are the ultimate indulgence.

1. Ozone, The Ritz-Carlton Hong Kong: Perched on the 118th floor, Ozone is the highest rooftop bar in the world. With its floor-to-ceiling windows and outdoor terrace, this bar offers a mesmerizing view of Hong Kong’s iconic skyline. Sip on their signature cocktails like the O3 Negroni, while experiencing the electrifying atmosphere that only Ozone can provide.

2. Rock Bar, Ayana Resort and Spa, Bali: Carved into the cliffside, the Rock Bar offers an awe-inspiring view of the Indian Ocean. Nestled amidst the crashing waves, this bar is a true testament to architectural genius. Indulge in their mixology creations like the Rock My World Martini, while being serenaded by the sound of the ocean.

3. The Rooftop at The Standard, Downtown LA: Located on the 13th floor of The Standard hotel, this rooftop bar boasts a stunning panoramic view of downtown Los Angeles. The trendy and vibrant atmosphere makes it a popular spot for locals and tourists alike. Savor their carefully crafted cocktails, such as the Standard Mule, while immersing yourself in the buzzing energy of the city.

4. Sky Bar, Lebua at State Tower, Bangkok: Famous for its appearance in the movie ‘The Hangover Part II,’ Sky Bar offers a breathtaking view of Bangkok’s skyline. Situated on the 63rd floor, this open-air bar allows guests to enjoy a 360-degree perspective of the city. Sip on their signature Hangovertini, while taking in the mesmerizing sights of the bustling metropolis below.

5. Radio Rooftop Bar, ME London: Situated on the top floor of the iconic ME London hotel, Radio Rooftop Bar offers a stunning view of the River Thames and the city’s skyline. This glamorous bar exudes an air of sophistication, making it a favorite among London’s elite. Delight in their creative cocktails, like the London Calling, while being surrounded by the vibrant energy of the city.

6. Sky Bar, Sirocco, Bangkok: Another Bangkok gem, Sirocco’s Sky Bar is perched on the 63rd floor of the State Tower. Offering a breathtaking view of the Chao Phraya River and the cityscape, this bar is a must-visit for cocktail enthusiasts. Savor their signature Hangovertini, a blend of herbal flavors and fresh ingredients, while enjoying the gentle breeze and the enchanting atmosphere.

7. The Press Lounge, Ink48 Hotel, New York City: Located in the Hell’s Kitchen neighborhood, The Press Lounge offers a stunning view of the Manhattan skyline. Sit back and relax on their spacious outdoor terrace, while enjoying a handcrafted cocktail like the West Side Story. As the sun sets over the city, watch as the lights illuminate the streets, creating an unforgettable experience.

In conclusion, hotel rooftop bars are the epitome of luxury and sophistication. These elevated sanctuaries offer guests an unparalleled experience, with breathtaking views and exquisite cocktails. Whether you find yourself in Hong Kong, Bali, Los Angeles, Bangkok, London, or New York City, make sure to indulge in the sky-high cocktails of these stunning hotel rooftop bars. Cheers to unforgettable moments and extraordinary experiences!
