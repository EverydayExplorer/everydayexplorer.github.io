10 Mouthwatering Middle Eastern Recipes to Try


Middle Eastern cuisine is renowned for its unique flavors, aromatic spices, and mouthwatering dishes that are loved by food enthusiasts worldwide. From hearty kebabs to flavorful mezze platters, Middle Eastern recipes are a gastronomic delight that should not be missed. If you are looking to explore this rich culinary tradition, here are 10 mouthwatering Middle Eastern recipes to try.

1. Hummus

No Middle Eastern meal is complete without a bowl of creamy hummus. Made from chickpeas, tahini, garlic, and lemon juice, hummus is a versatile dip that can be enjoyed with pita bread, vegetables, or as a spread on sandwiches. It is not only delicious but also packed with protein and healthy fats.

2. Falafel

Falafel is a popular Middle Eastern street food that has gained global popularity. These deep-fried, crispy patties are made from ground chickpeas, onions, herbs, and spices. Serve them in a pita pocket with tahini sauce, fresh salad, and pickles for a delightful and satisfying meal.

3. Shawarma

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern-style wrap that bursts with flavors. Traditionally made from marinated meat (usually chicken, beef, or lamb), it is slowly roasted on a vertical spit, resulting in tender and juicy slices of meat. Wrap it in a pita bread with garlic sauce, pickles, and salad for a truly authentic experience.

4. Tabbouleh

Tabbouleh is a refreshing and vibrant salad made from bulgur wheat, fresh parsley, mint, tomatoes, cucumbers, and lemon juice. It is light, tangy, and packed with nutrients. This salad is a perfect side dish to accompany grilled meats or can be enjoyed as a light meal on its own.

5. Kofta

Kofta refers to Middle Eastern-style meatballs or skewers made from ground meat (beef, lamb, or chicken) mixed with spices, herbs, and onions. These juicy morsels can be grilled, baked, or fried and are often served with rice, salad, or wrapped in a flatbread.

6. Muhammara

Muhammara is a flavorful and spicy spread made from roasted red peppers, walnuts, breadcrumbs, olive oil, and spices. It has a smoky and tangy taste with a hint of sweetness. Muhammara is perfect as a dip, spread, or sauce, adding a delightful twist to any dish.

7. Fattoush

Fattoush is a refreshing salad that is bursting with Mediterranean flavors. It is made with mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, radishes, toasted pita bread, and a tangy dressing of lemon juice and sumac. This crispy and flavorful salad is a great addition to any summer meal.

8. Baba Ganoush

Baba Ganoush is a creamy and smoky dip made from roasted eggplants, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil. It is a great alternative to hummus and is commonly enjoyed as a dip with pita bread or vegetables. The rich and earthy flavors of baba ganoush are simply irresistible.

9. Baklava

Baklava is a decadent and sweet pastry made from layers of filo pastry filled with a mixture of chopped nuts, spices, and sweet syrup. This indulgent dessert is a staple in Middle Eastern cuisine and is often served with a cup of aromatic tea or coffee.

10. Kunafa

Kunafa is a popular Middle Eastern dessert made from a shredded phyllo dough pastry filled with gooey cheese and drenched in sweet syrup. It is often garnished with pistachios or other nuts. The combination of sweet and savory flavors in kunafa is a true delight for the taste buds.

Middle Eastern cuisine offers a wide range of delicious dishes that cater to all taste preferences. Whether you are a meat lover, vegetarian, or have a sweet tooth, there is something for everyone in this rich culinary tradition. So, next time you want to experiment with new flavors and indulge in a gastronomic adventure, try one of these 10 mouthwatering Middle Eastern recipes - your taste buds will thank you!
