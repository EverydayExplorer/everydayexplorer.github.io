The Best Hotels for Scenic Drives: Road Trip Retreats


Let’s start our road trip retreat with a visit to the stunning Amangiri in Utah. Nestled amidst the dramatic canyons and mesas of the American Southwest, this architectural marvel seamlessly blends with its natural surroundings. The drive to Amangiri is an experience in itself, with winding roads that unveil the grandeur of the desert landscape. Once you arrive, you’ll be treated to unparalleled luxury, including private terraces with expansive views, a world-class spa, and exquisite dining options.

For those seeking a European road trip adventure, look no further than the Amalfi Coast in Italy. Along this iconic stretch of coastline, you’ll discover boutique hotels such as the Monastero Santa Rosa Hotel & Spa. The drive along the winding roads of the Amalfi Coast offers panoramic vistas of the Mediterranean Sea, while the hotel perched on a cliff provides an exclusive retreat with its terraced gardens and infinity pool overlooking the azure waters.

Heading Down Under, the Great Ocean Road in Australia beckons with its awe-inspiring coastal scenery. At the end of this iconic drive lies the incomparable Southern Ocean Lodge on Kangaroo Island. As you navigate the coastal route, be prepared to be mesmerized by the rugged cliffs and pristine beaches. The lodge itself offers eco-friendly luxury, with its suites featuring floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the wild beauty of the Southern Ocean.

Venturing into the heart of Africa, the Cape Winelands in South Africa offers a road trip filled with vineyards, mountains, and charming towns. Delaire Graff Estate, nestled in the Stellenbosch Mountains, is a haven for wine enthusiasts and art lovers alike. The scenic drive through the Winelands is a sensory delight, with rolling vineyards and majestic mountain backdrops. Upon arrival at Delaire Graff Estate, guests are treated to opulent accommodations, award-winning wines, and an art collection that rivals any museum.

In the Land of the Rising Sun, the Nakasendo Trail in Japan presents a unique road trip experience through ancient villages and tranquil countryside. Tucked away in the Kiso Valley, the luxury ryokan Nukumorinosato is a serene retreat that captures the essence of Japanese hospitality. The drive along the Nakasendo Trail allows you to immerse yourself in Japan’s rich history and natural beauty, while the ryokan offers traditional tatami rooms, hot spring baths, and kaiseki dining.

These road trip retreats are not just about the destination, but the journey itself. From the red rocks of the American Southwest to the vine-covered hills of South Africa, each drive is a visual masterpiece waiting to be explored. So, pack your bags, hit the road, and indulge in the ultimate fusion of luxury and natural splendor at The Best Hotels for Scenic Drives: Road Trip Retreats. Your adventure of a lifetime awaits.
