Comedy Podcasts That Will Make Your Daily Commute a Lot Funnier


Are you tired of the same old routine on your daily commute? Do you find yourself longing for some laughter to brighten up the mundane journey? Well, look no further! Comedy podcasts are the perfect solution to make your daily commute a lot funnier. With a wide range of options available, there is something for everyone’s comedic taste. In this blog post, we will explore a few comedy podcasts that are guaranteed to leave you in stitches during your journey.

1. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’

Known for its long-form conversations, ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ is a podcast hosted by stand-up comedian Joe Rogan. With a roster of guests ranging from fellow comedians to politicians and even scientists, this podcast offers a unique blend of comedy, intellectual discussions, and pure entertainment. Joe’s witty humor combined with interesting guests creates a show that is both informative and hilarious.

2. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend’

If you’re a fan of Conan O’Brien’s late-night shows, you’re in for a treat with ‘Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend.’ In this podcast, Conan brings his hilarious personality and sharp wit to engage in candid conversations with guests from various walks of life. From fellow comedians to actors and musicians, the show promises laughter throughout as Conan navigates the highs and lows of friendships.

3. ‘How Did This Get Made?’

Have you ever watched a terrible movie and wondered how it even got made? Well, wonder no more! ‘How Did This Get Made?’ is a podcast where hosts Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas analyze and discuss some of the worst films ever made. Their humorous take on these cinematic disasters, along with occasional guest appearances, guarantees you a side-splitting experience that will make your commute feel like a joyride.

4. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’

Prepare yourself for an incredibly unique podcast experience with ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno.’ Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this hilarious podcast involves Jamie reading chapters from his own father’s erotic novel, aptly titled ‘Belinda Blinked.’ The hosts offer their live commentary and reactions to the absurdly funny and often cringe-worthy content, resulting in an exceptionally entertaining and laughter-inducing show.

5. ‘The Dollop’

History enthusiasts with a sense of humor will thoroughly enjoy ‘The Dollop.’ Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, this podcast delves into bizarre and obscure stories from history. Dave presents the historical narrative, while Gareth provides humorous commentary and reactions to these unusual events. From forgotten facts to bizarre characters, ‘The Dollop’ offers a comedic twist on history that will keep you entertained during your commute.

6. ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’

If you’re a fan of improv comedy, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by Scott Aukerman, this long-running podcast features a mix of celebrity interviews, character sketches, and improvised games. With a rotating roster of comedians, actors, and musicians, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ offers a variety of hilarious moments that will have you laughing out loud on your daily commute.

In conclusion, incorporating comedy podcasts into your daily commute can elevate your experience from mundane to hilarious. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up comedy, intriguing conversations, or humorous takes on history and pop culture, there is a comedy podcast out there that will suit your taste. So, the next time you find yourself stuck in traffic or on a monotonous journey, tune in to one of these comedy podcasts, and let the laughter brighten up your daily commute!
