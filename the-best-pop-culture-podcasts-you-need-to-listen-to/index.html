The Best Pop Culture Podcasts You Need to Listen to


Pop culture is constantly evolving and staying up-to-date with the latest trends can be overwhelming. Fortunately, podcasts have become an increasingly popular medium for discussing all things pop culture. Whether you’re a film fanatic, a music lover, or a TV junkie, there’s a podcast out there for you. To help you navigate the sea of options, we’ve compiled a list of the best pop culture podcasts that you need to listen to. So grab your headphones and get ready to immerse yourself in the world of pop culture!

1. ‘Pop Culture Happy Hour’

Hosted by Linda Holmes, Stephen Thompson, Glen Weldon, and Aisha Harris, ‘Pop Culture Happy Hour’ is a lively and engaging podcast that covers a wide range of pop culture topics. From movies and TV shows to books and music, the hosts share their insightful opinions and recommendations. This podcast is perfect for those looking for intelligent and thought-provoking discussions about the latest pop culture happenings.

2. ‘The Watch’

Hosted by Chris Ryan and Andy Greenwald, ‘The Watch’ explores the world of television and streaming. The hosts provide in-depth analysis and commentary on the latest TV shows, including both popular hits and hidden gems. With their humorous banter and deep knowledge of the industry, Chris and Andy make ‘The Watch’ a must-listen for any TV enthusiast.

3. ‘Binge Mode’

If you’re a fan of TV series and movies like Harry Potter, Game of Thrones, or Marvel, then ‘Binge Mode’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by Mallory Rubin and Jason Concepcion, this podcast dives deep into the most popular franchises and breaks down every aspect of their storytelling. From character analysis to fan theories, ‘Binge Mode’ is a treasure trove of pop culture knowledge.

4. ‘The Rewatchables’

Ever find yourself rewatching your favorite movies repeatedly? If so, ‘The Rewatchables’ will be right up your alley. Hosted by Bill Simmons and a rotating panel of guests, this podcast dissects beloved films and discusses why they’re so rewatchable. With episodes dedicated to everything from action flicks to romantic comedies, ‘The Rewatchables’ is sure to rekindle your love for your favorite films.

5. ‘Song Exploder’

For music enthusiasts, ‘Song Exploder’ is a must-listen podcast. Hosted by Hrishikesh Hirway, this show invites musicians to take apart their songs and reveal the intricate details of their creation. From the lyrics to the melodies, ‘Song Exploder’ offers a unique behind-the-scenes look at some of the most captivating songs of our time.

6. ‘How Did This Get Made?’

If you enjoy laughing at bad movies, then ‘How Did This Get Made?’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, this show delves into the world of cult classics and critically panned films. The hosts, along with their hilarious guests, dissect the absurdity and hilarity of these movies, making for an entertaining and laughter-filled listening experience.

7. ‘You Must Remember This’

Hosted by Karina Longworth, ‘You Must Remember This’ explores the hidden stories and untold history of Hollywood’s golden age. From scandals and romances to behind-the-scenes drama, this podcast provides a captivating glimpse into the lives of Hollywood’s biggest stars. If you’re a fan of classic movies and love untold stories, ‘You Must Remember This’ is a podcast you won’t want to miss.

These are just a few of the many great pop culture podcasts out there. Whether you’re looking for insightful discussions, behind-the-scenes insights, or simply some entertaining banter, these podcasts have got you covered. So grab your phone, subscribe to a few of these shows, and get ready to immerse yourself in the world of pop culture. It’s time to discover your new favorite podcast!
