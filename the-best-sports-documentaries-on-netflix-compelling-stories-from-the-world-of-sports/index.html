The Best Sports Documentaries on Netflix: Compelling Stories from the World of Sports


Sports documentaries have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. These films offer a unique and captivating glimpse into the world of sports, providing viewers with thrilling storytelling and inspiring tales of triumph and perseverance. And when it comes to finding the best sports documentaries, look no further than Netflix. With its expansive library of films, the streaming giant has a wide variety of choices that are sure to satisfy any sports enthusiast.

One of the standout documentaries on Netflix is ‘The Last Dance.’ This 10-part series chronicles the career of NBA legend Michael Jordan and the Chicago Bulls’ quest for a sixth championship. With never-before-seen footage and exclusive interviews, ‘The Last Dance’ offers an intimate look into the life of one of the greatest athletes of all time. Whether you’re a basketball fan or not, this documentary is a must-watch.

Another must-see documentary on Netflix is ‘Icarus.’ Directed by Bryan Fogel, this film earned an Oscar for Best Documentary Feature in 2018. ‘Icarus’ is a thrilling and shocking exposé of the Russian state-sponsored doping program during the 2014 Sochi Olympics. The documentary starts as a personal experiment by Fogel to see if he can evade detection while doping for a cycling race but quickly escalates into a full-blown international scandal. With its gripping storytelling and fascinating revelations, ‘Icarus’ is a testament to the power of investigative journalism.

If you’re a football fan, ‘Diego Maradona’ is a documentary you should not miss. Directed by Asif Kapadia, the film tells the story of the Argentine football legend and his rise to stardom. Through archival footage and insightful interviews, ‘Diego Maradona’ explores the highs and lows of Maradona’s career, including his controversial ‘Hand of God’ goal and his struggles with addiction. This documentary offers a compelling portrait of one of the most iconic figures in the history of football.

For those who appreciate the beauty of the great outdoors and human endurance, ‘Free Solo’ is a documentary that will leave you speechless. Directed by Elizabeth Chai Vasarhelyi and Jimmy Chin, the film follows professional rock climber Alex Honnold as he attempts to become the first person to free solo climb El Capitan in Yosemite National Park. The documentary expertly captures the physical and mental challenges faced by Honnold, as well as the breathtaking shots of the stunning landscape. ‘Free Solo’ is a testament to the indomitable human spirit and the pursuit of greatness.

Last but not least, ‘Cheer’ is a documentary series that offers a behind-the-scenes look at the competitive world of collegiate cheerleading. Directed by Greg Whiteley, this heartwarming and suspenseful series follows the Navarro College cheer team as they prepare for the National Cheerleading Championship. With its compelling characters and gripping narrative, ‘Cheer’ sheds light on the dedication, discipline, and determination required to excel in this demanding sport.

These are just a few examples of the best sports documentaries available on Netflix. Whether you’re a fan of basketball, cycling, football, rock climbing, or cheerleading, there’s something for everyone. These films go beyond the realm of sports, delving into the personal stories of the athletes and the challenges they face on and off the field. So grab your popcorn, settle into your couch, and prepare to be entertained and inspired by these compelling stories from the world of sports.
