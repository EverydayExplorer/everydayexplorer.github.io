Best hotels with breathtaking views


Welcome, wanderlust seekers, to our blog post dedicated to the best hotels with breathtaking views! Prepare to be transported to a world of stunning vistas, luxurious accommodations, and unforgettable experiences. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or simply in need of some wanderlust-inducing travel inspiration, these hotels promise to leave you awe-struck.

1. Hotel Aria - Santorini, Greece:
Nestled on the cliffside of this picturesque Greek island, Hotel Aria offers unparalleled panoramic views of the azure Aegean Sea. Immerse yourself in the beauty of Santorini’s sunsets from your private terrace while enjoying world-class amenities and impeccable service. This idyllic retreat is a true sensory delight.

2. Jade Mountain Resort - St. Lucia:
Prepare to have your breath taken away by the dramatic beauty of the iconic Pitons at Jade Mountain Resort. Each of its luxury suites is designed with an open wall concept, allowing uninterrupted views of the Caribbean Sea and lush tropical surroundings. Indulge in the ultimate relaxation while basking in the island’s natural wonders.

3. Amangiri - Utah, USA:
For those seeking a unique blend of luxury and natural beauty, Amangiri in Utah is a true desert oasis. Surrounded by the mesmerizing desert landscape, this architectural marvel offers breathtaking views of the majestic canyons and towering mesas. Experience tranquility and serenity in this secluded haven.

4. Singita Lebombo Lodge - Kruger National Park, South Africa:
Imagine waking up to the sight of wildlife roaming freely across the African savannah. Singita Lebombo Lodge, situated within Kruger National Park, offers an unrivaled safari experience with its exclusive accommodations overlooking the expansive wilderness. Witness stunning sunsets, encounter the Big Five, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

5. Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora - French Polynesia:
Step into paradise at the Four Seasons Resort in Bora Bora, where luxury and natural beauty harmoniously collide. Set amidst crystal-clear turquoise waters and vibrant coral reefs, this resort features overwater bungalows with private decks, allowing you to marvel at the breathtaking views of Mount Otemanu and the South Pacific.

6. The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong:
Elevate your experience at The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong, perched high above the iconic cityscape. Gaze out from the floor-to-ceiling windows of your luxurious room and marvel at the stunning panoramic views of the Victoria Harbour, glittering city lights, and surrounding mountains. This urban retreat is a haven of sophistication and elegance.

7. Anantara Al Jabal Al Akhdar Resort - Oman:
Escape to the heights of Oman’s majestic Al Hajar Mountains at Anantara Al Jabal Al Akhdar Resort. This cliffside oasis offers breathtaking views of the rugged canyons and terraced gardens below. Indulge in luxurious accommodations, rejuvenate at the renowned spa, and savor the enchanting beauty of this Middle Eastern gem.

8. Fogo Island Inn - Newfoundland, Canada:
For a unique adventure, head to Fogo Island Inn, perched on the dramatic shores of Newfoundland. Surrounded by the North Atlantic Ocean, this contemporary masterpiece offers unparalleled views of the wild coastline and icebergs. Immerse yourself in the local culture, enjoy exceptional hospitality, and embark on breathtaking hiking trails.

9. Marina Bay Sands - Singapore:
Experience the epitome of luxury at Marina Bay Sands, an architectural marvel that redefines the Singapore skyline. Indulge in opulent accommodations and be captivated by the breathtaking views of the cityscape, Gardens by the Bay, and the iconic infinity pool atop the towering hotel. This urban oasis offers a truly unforgettable experience.

10. Belmond Hotel Caruso - Amalfi Coast, Italy:
Perched on a cliff high above the Amalfi Coast, Belmond Hotel Caruso offers a Mediterranean paradise like no other. Delight in panoramic views of the sparkling Tyrrhenian Sea, colorful hillside villages, and lush gardens. Immerse yourself in Italian charm, savor exquisite cuisine, and unwind in the infinity pool that seemingly merges with the horizon.

We hope this list of the best hotels with breathtaking views has ignited your wanderlust and inspired your next adventure. Whether you’re seeking natural wonders, urban skylines, or remote landscapes, these exceptional hotels promise to provide you with unparalleled experiences and memories that will last a lifetime. Happy travels!
