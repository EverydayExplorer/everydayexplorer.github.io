Around the World in 80 Dishes: Exotic Recipes for the Adventurous Foodie


If you consider yourself a foodie with a daring palate, this cookbook is a must-have for your kitchen collection. Written by renowned chef and world traveler, this book takes you on a gastronomic adventure like no other. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the vibrant markets of Marrakech, every page of this cookbook is a passport to a new and exciting flavor experience.

In ‘Around the World in 80 Dishes,’ you will find a treasure trove of exotic recipes from every corner of the globe. Each dish is carefully crafted to capture the essence of its country of origin, bringing the flavors and aromas of distant lands right into your home. Whether you’re craving the fiery spices of Indian cuisine, the delicate flavors of Japanese sushi, or the hearty comfort foods of Italy, this cookbook has it all.

But what sets this cookbook apart from the rest? It’s not just about the recipes; it’s about the journey. As you flip through the pages, you’ll be transported to far-off lands, immersing yourself in the culture and traditions of each country. Each recipe is accompanied by captivating stories and anecdotes, giving you a glimpse into the lives of the people who created these culinary masterpieces.

The recipes themselves are carefully curated to suit the adventurous foodie in you. From the unusual ingredients to the intricate cooking techniques, each dish presents a challenge that will push your culinary skills to new heights. But fear not, the step-by-step instructions and helpful tips ensure that even the most novice of cooks can embark on this culinary adventure with confidence.

What makes this cookbook truly unique is its focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing. The author’s commitment to using local, seasonal ingredients shines through in every recipe. By choosing to cook these dishes, you’re not only expanding your culinary horizons, but you’re also supporting small-scale farmers and sustainable food practices around the world.

But it doesn’t stop there. ‘Around the World in 80 Dishes’ goes beyond the kitchen. It’s a celebration of diversity, a reminder that food has the power to unite us all. It’s an invitation to step out of your comfort zone and embrace the unknown. So, grab your apron, sharpen your knives, and get ready to embark on a culinary journey like no other.

In conclusion, ‘Around the World in 80 Dishes: Exotic Recipes for the Adventurous Foodie’ is more than just a cookbook. It’s a passport to a world of flavors, a gateway to new experiences, and a celebration of the rich tapestry of cultures that make up our global community. So, if you’re ready to tantalize your taste buds and broaden your culinary horizons, this cookbook is a must-have for your kitchen shelf. Happy cooking and bon appétit!
