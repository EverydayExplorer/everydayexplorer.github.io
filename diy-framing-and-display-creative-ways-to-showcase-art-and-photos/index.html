DIY Framing and Display: Creative Ways to Showcase Art and Photos


Are you tired of the same old picture frames and basic wall displays? Do you want to showcase your art and photographs in a unique and creative way? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore DIY framing and display ideas that will add a touch of personality and style to your home.

1. Floating Shelves: Instead of hanging your framed art on the wall, why not create a gallery-style display using floating shelves? Simply install a few shelves at different heights and arrange your framed photos and artwork on them. This not only adds visual interest but also allows for easy rearrangement whenever you want to change things up.

2. Clipboards: Who says you need frames to display your art and photos? Get creative with clipboards! Attach clipboards to your wall and use them to hold your favorite prints or photographs. This is a great option if you want to frequently rotate and update your display.

3. Window Frames: Old window frames can be repurposed into stunning photo displays. Remove the glass panes and attach chicken wire or twine across the frame. Use clothespins or mini bulldog clips to hang your photos. This rustic and charming display adds a vintage touch to any space.

4. Hanging Mobiles: Create a whimsical and artistic display by crafting a hanging mobile. Use fishing wire or string to suspend your art and photos from a wooden or metal frame. Hang the mobile in a corner or near a window to catch the light and create a captivating visual display.

5. Shadow Boxes: Shadow boxes are a great way to showcase three-dimensional art pieces or collections. Choose a deep frame and arrange your objects inside, securing them with pins or glue. This allows you to display unique items like seashells, dried flowers, or small sculptures.

6. Clothesline Display: For a fun and interactive display, string a clothesline across a wall and use mini clothespins to hang your art and photos. This works especially well for displaying children’s artwork or Polaroid pictures. You can easily add or remove items as you please.

7. Frame within a Frame: Add depth and visual interest to your display by framing your art or photo within a larger frame. Choose contrasting colors or textures to create a striking effect. This technique is perfect for emphasizing a particular piece or creating a focal point.

8. Repurposed Materials: Get creative with materials you already have at home. Use old doors, window shutters, or even vintage suitcases as unique frames for your art and photos. Not only will this save you money, but it will also give your display a one-of-a-kind look.

9. Rope and Clips: If you’re looking for a bohemian-inspired display, try using rope and clips. String a length of rope across your wall and attach your art and photos with decorative clips. This relaxed and casual display adds a touch of charm to any space.

10. Wall Collage: Create a visually stunning wall collage by combining different sizes and styles of frames. Mix and match colors, textures, and finishes for an eclectic yet cohesive look. This versatile display allows you to showcase multiple pieces and tells a unique story.

In conclusion, DIY framing and display offer endless possibilities for showcasing your art and photos in a creative and personalized way. Whether you prefer a minimalist approach or a more eclectic style, these ideas will help you transform your space into a gallery of memories. So, roll up your sleeves, grab your tools, and let your creativity shine!
