Best Game Calls for Different Hunting Situations


When it comes to hunting, having the right tools and equipment is crucial for a successful outing. One important tool that every serious hunter should have in their arsenal is a game call. A game call is a device that imitates the sounds and calls made by different animals, attracting them towards the hunter. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or a beginner, investing in the best game calls for different hunting situations can greatly increase your chances of a successful hunt. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best game calls available for various hunting situations.

1. Turkey Hunting:
Turkey hunting requires a specific set of skills and the right equipment. When it comes to game calls for turkey hunting, two popular options are diaphragm calls and box calls. Diaphragm calls are versatile and allow for hands-free operation, making them easy to use while aiming your weapon. Box calls, on the other hand, produce loud and realistic turkey sounds. The Primos Hook-Up Magnetic box call and the WoodHaven Cherry Classic slate call are two excellent options for turkey hunting.

2. Deer Hunting:
For deer hunting, grunt calls are extremely effective. Grunt calls imitate the sounds made by deer and are used to attract them, especially during the rutting season. The Primos ‘The Original Can’ Deer Call and the Flextone Buck Collector Plus are popular choices among deer hunters. Both of these calls produce realistic sounds that can bring bucks in close.

3. Waterfowl Hunting:
Waterfowl hunting requires a different set of game calls. Duck calls and goose calls are the most common types used for attracting ducks and geese, respectively. Duck calls come in various types, such as single reed, double reed, and short reed calls. The Duck Commander Triple Threat and the Echo Timber Poly Double Reed are two highly recommended duck calls. For goose calls, the Zink Calls Power Speck and the Buck Gardner Canada Hammer II are popular choices.

4. Predators Hunting:
When it comes to hunting predators like coyotes or foxes, mouth calls are the go-to game calls. These calls are compact, easy to use, and produce a wide range of sounds to attract predators. The Primos Randy Anderson Signature Series Lil’ Dog and the Foxpro Shockwave Electronic Game Call are both excellent options for predator hunting.

5. Elk Hunting:
Elk hunting requires a different approach, and elk bugle calls are essential for attracting these majestic animals. Elk bugle calls imitate the sounds made by bull elks during the rutting season. The Primos Hyper Lip Single and the Rocky Mountain Hunting Calls Bully Bull Extreme are both highly recommended elk bugle calls.

In conclusion, having the best game calls for different hunting situations can greatly improve your chances of success in the field. Turkey calls, deer calls, waterfowl calls, predator calls, and elk bugle calls are all essential tools for attracting game and making the most of your hunting experience. Investing in high-quality, realistic game calls will help you bring the animals closer, increasing your chances of a successful hunt. So, gear up with the right game calls and get ready for a rewarding hunting adventure!
