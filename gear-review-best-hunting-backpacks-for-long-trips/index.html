Gear Review: Best Hunting Backpacks for Long Trips


Hunting is a thrilling and challenging adventure that often requires a lot of physical exertion and endurance. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or embarking on your first long hunting trip, having the right gear can make a significant difference in your overall experience and success. One essential piece of equipment that every hunter needs is a reliable and durable hunting backpack that can carry all the necessary supplies, provide comfort during long treks, and withstand the harsh outdoor conditions. In this gear review, we will discuss some of the best hunting backpacks available in the market for long trips, helping you choose the right one to enhance your hunting experience.

Mystery Ranch Metcalf Hunting Backpack  
The Mystery Ranch Metcalf is a popular choice among avid hunters because of its exceptional durability and ample storage capacity. With a total volume of 4,600 cubic inches and a weight of 6.1 pounds, this backpack allows you to carry all the essentials needed for a long hunting trip. It features an innovative load carriage system that distributes weight evenly, reducing fatigue and enabling you to trek through rough terrains comfortably. The Metcalf also includes various compartments and organizational pockets, allowing easy access to your gear while keeping everything organized.

Eberlestock F1 Mainframe Backpack  
For hunters who prefer versatility and customization, the Eberlestock F1 Mainframe Backpack is an excellent choice. This modular backpack system can be customized by adding different accessory bags to fit your specific needs. With a total capacity of 4,500 cubic inches, this backpack provides ample space for your hunting gear and supplies, while its lightweight design (5 pounds) ensures ease of mobility. The F1 Mainframe also features a unique pack compression system that keeps your load secure and stable, even during strenuous hikes.

Badlands 2200 Hunting Backpack  
The Badlands 2200 Hunting Backpack is a favorite among hunters who prioritize comfort and functionality. With a total volume of 2,400 cubic inches and a carrying capacity of up to 40 pounds, this backpack is perfect for shorter trips or for hunters who prefer a more minimalist approach. The ergonomic design and padded shoulder straps provide maximum comfort during long treks, while its KXO-32 fabric ensures durability and resistance to harsh weather conditions. Additionally, the 2200 features a built-in rifle or bow boot, allowing you to securely carry your weapon of choice hands-free.

Tenzing TZ 6000 Hunting Backpack  
If you're planning an extended hunting trip and require substantial storage space, the Tenzing TZ 6000 Hunting Backpack is an exceptional option. With a whopping capacity of 6,013 cubic inches, this backpack offers ample room for all your gear, allowing you to be fully prepared for any scenario. The TZ 6000 incorporates multiple specialized pockets, including a large main compartment, a sleeping bag compartment, and a detachable fanny pack. Its lightweight design and breathable back panel provide comfort and support during long treks, while its robust construction ensures durability in rugged outdoor conditions.

ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Freighter Frame Pack  
For hunters who need to carry heavy loads or prefer a pack that doubles as a meat hauler, the ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Freighter Frame Pack is a top choice. This backpack combines a durable frame pack with a detachable day pack, allowing versatility in carrying capacities. The aluminum frame provides excellent support for heavy loads, while the Lycra shoulder straps and waist belt distribute weight evenly, reducing strain on your back. With a total capacity of 5,250 cubic inches and a weight of 7 pounds, this backpack is perfect for extended hunting trips or for packing out game on the way back.

In conclusion, a reliable hunting backpack is an essential piece of gear for long hunting trips. The Mystery Ranch Metcalf, Eberlestock F1 Mainframe, Badlands 2200, Tenzing TZ 6000, and ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Freighter Frame Pack are among the best options available, offering durability, ample storage space, comfort, and customization features. When choosing a hunting backpack, consider your specific needs, the duration of your trip, the terrain you’ll be navigating, and the weight you’ll be carrying. With the right backpack in tow, you’ll be well-equipped for an unforgettable and successful hunting adventure.
