Street Style Inspiration: Fashionistas to Follow on Instagram


Street style has become a major source of inspiration for fashion lovers all over the world. And when it comes to finding the latest trends and style ideas, Instagram is the place to be. With its vast pool of fashionistas, influencers, and style icons, Instagram offers a never-ending stream of street style inspiration. In this blog post, we have curated a list of some of the most fashionable Instagram accounts that you should be following for your daily dose of street style inspiration.

1. @weworewhat: Danielle Bernstein, the face behind the popular blog ‘We Wore What,’ is a must-follow for anyone looking for effortlessly chic street style looks. Her feed is filled with trendy outfits, unique accessories, and a mix of high-end and affordable fashion.

2. @thefashionguitar: Charlotte Groeneveld, known as The Fashion Guitar, is a Dutch fashion blogger and stylist. Her Instagram feed is a visual delight, featuring her impeccable sense of style and a mix of high fashion and everyday looks. She is known for her ability to put together unexpected combinations that always look effortlessly chic.

3. @chrissyford: Chrissy Rutherford, the Senior Fashion Editor at Harper’s Bazaar, is a fashionista to watch on Instagram. Her style is a perfect blend of classic and trendy, and she has a knack for incorporating statement pieces into her everyday outfits.

4. @songofstyle: Aimee Song is a fashion and interior design blogger with an eye for style. Her Instagram feed is a mix of fashion, beauty, and travel, and her street style looks are always on point. Aimee’s style is eclectic and fun, and she’s not afraid to take risks when it comes to fashion.

5. @xeniaadonts: Xenia Adonts is a German fashion influencer known for her edgy and bold style. Her Instagram feed is a mix of high fashion editorials and everyday street style looks. Xenia’s outfits are always eye-catching and unique, making her a great source of inspiration for those who want to push the boundaries of their own style.

6. @blaireadiebee: Blair Eadie is a fashion blogger and the face behind the popular blog ‘Atlantic-Pacific.’ Her Instagram feed is a colorful explosion of fashion, featuring her signature mix of prints, bold colors, and statement accessories. Blair’s style is playful and feminine, and she never shies away from experimenting with different trends.

7. @rocky_barnes: Rachel Barnes, also known as Rocky Barnes, is a model and fashion influencer with a killer sense of style. Her Instagram feed is filled with beachy vibes, bohemian prints, and effortlessly cool outfits. Rocky’s style is laid-back and relaxed, making her a great source of inspiration for those who love a more casual approach to fashion.

8. @negin_mirsalehi: Negin Mirsalehi is a Dutch fashion and beauty influencer with a love for luxurious fashion. Her Instagram feed is a mix of glamorous outfits, beauty tips, and travel adventures. Negin’s style is elegant and sophisticated, and she always manages to look polished and put together.

9. @sincerelyjules: Julie Sariñana, the face behind the blog ‘Sincerely Jules,’ is a fashion influencer and designer known for her effortless California style. Her Instagram feed is filled with laid-back looks, casual chic outfits, and a mix of high and low fashion. Julie’s style is accessible and relatable, making her a great source of inspiration for everyday fashionistas.

10. @tamumcpherson: Tamu McPherson is a renowned street style photographer and style influencer. Her Instagram feed is a visual feast, showcasing the best street style looks from fashion weeks around the world. Tamu’s style is eclectic and bold, and she has an eye for capturing the most stylish individuals on the streets.

Following these fashionistas on Instagram will not only give you a daily dose of style inspiration but also introduce you to new trends, brands, and fashion events. So, go ahead and hit that follow button to upgrade your street style game. Happy scrolling!
