DIY Jewelry Organizers: Crafty Storage Solutions


Are you tired of tangled necklaces, missing earrings, and a messy collection of jewelry? Don’t worry, we have the perfect solution for you—DIY jewelry organizers! With a little creativity and some simple materials, you can create beautiful and functional storage solutions for all your precious accessories. Say goodbye to the chaos and hello to a well-organized jewelry collection!

DIY jewelry organizers are a great way to showcase your creativity and personalize your storage space. Whether you prefer a rustic, bohemian look or a sleek and modern design, there is a DIY jewelry organizer out there for everyone. Not only are these organizers practical for keeping your jewelry untangled and easily accessible, but they also serve as beautiful home decor pieces.

So, let’s dive into some crafty storage solutions that will inspire you to create your own DIY jewelry organizer!

1. Picture Frame Jewelry Holder:
One of the easiest and most versatile DIY jewelry organizers is a picture frame holder. All you need is an old picture frame, some chicken wire or mesh, and a staple gun. Cut the wire or mesh to fit the frame and staple it securely to create a grid. Hang your earrings on the mesh, and you can even use S-hooks to hang necklaces and bracelets on the frame. This organizer not only keeps your jewelry neat but also adds a decorative touch to your wall.

2. Branch Jewelry Display:
If you’re a nature lover, a branch jewelry display is a perfect choice. Find a sturdy branch from your backyard or a park, and give it a good clean. Once dry, you can paint it, wrap it with twine, or leave it in its natural state. Place the branch in a vase or attach it to a wooden base for stability. Hang your necklaces, bracelets, and earrings on the branches, creating a unique and whimsical jewelry organizer.

3. Driftwood Jewelry Hanger:
For a beachy vibe, repurpose a piece of driftwood into a stylish jewelry hanger. Simply attach hooks, knobs, or even small curtain hooks along the length of the driftwood. You can use different sizes and styles of hooks for added visual interest. Hang the driftwood on your wall, and voila! You have a rustic and charming jewelry organizer that will transport you to the seaside every time you see it.

4. Recycled Drawer Jewelry Storage:
Don’t throw away that old dresser; transform it into a fabulous jewelry storage solution! Remove a drawer from the dresser and clean it thoroughly. Sand and paint the drawer in your desired color, and then line the inside with velvet or felt fabric. Install small hooks or dowels within the drawer to hang necklaces and attach small compartments to store earrings, rings, and bracelets. Place the drawer on a shelf or mount it on the wall, creating a functional and eye-catching storage piece.

5. Pegboard Jewelry Organizer:
If you’re looking for a more customizable option, a pegboard jewelry organizer is perfect for you. Purchase a pegboard from a local hardware store and paint it in a color that complements your room. Attach hooks, dowels, and small baskets to the board, allowing you to hang necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, as well as store additional accessories. Hang the pegboard on your wall or prop it up on a dresser for easy access to your jewelry collection.

These are just a few of the many DIY jewelry organizer ideas out there. Remember, the key is to get creative and tailor the organizer to your style and needs. Not only will you enjoy a clutter-free and organized jewelry collection, but you’ll also have stunning and unique pieces of decor.

In addition to being practical and aesthetically pleasing, DIY jewelry organizers are also budget-friendly. You can find most of the materials around your home or at a low cost in your local craft store. Plus, crafting your own jewelry organizer allows you to customize it to fit your space perfectly.

So, why wait? Get started on your DIY jewelry organizer today and say goodbye to the days of untangling necklaces and searching for misplaced earrings. With a little time and creativity, you’ll have a stunning and functional jewelry storage solution that reflects your personal style. Happy crafting!
