10 Hilarious Ways to Survive a Boring Meeting


Whether you’re stuck in a never-ending meeting or you find yourself in a boring lecture, we all know the feeling of wanting to escape the dullness. Instead of daydreaming or doodling on your notebook, why not make the most of the situation? In this article, we will explore 10 hilarious ways to survive a boring meeting and inject some laughter into an otherwise tedious event.

1. Create a Bingo Game: Before the meeting starts, create a bingo card filled with common phrases or actions that often occur during meetings. Cross them out whenever they happen, and see who can get a bingo first. This game will not only keep you entertained but also help you stay engaged in the discussion.

2. Start a Whisper Chain: Team up with a colleague who shares your boredom and initiate a secret whisper chain. Pass on funny statements or observations about the meeting agenda discreetly. Just make sure you don’t laugh out loud and give yourselves away!

3. Non-Verbal Communication: Develop a secret code for communication using non-verbal cues with a friend sitting across the room. You can create signals for agreement, disagreement, or even a ‘save me’ sign. Having a silent conversation can turn the boring meeting into an amusing game.

4. Take Silly Notes: Instead of jotting down serious meeting minutes, take silly notes that capture the essence of the boredom. Sketch funny cartoons, write sarcastic remarks, or create your own fictional story based on the meeting topics. Sharing these notes with your friends afterward will provide endless laughter.

5. Play Hangman: Create a game of hangman with a colleague by guessing words related to the meeting or company jargon. Take turns drawing and guessing letters during the meeting. Not only will it keep you entertained, but it will also boost your focus on finding the right answer.

6. Paper And Pen Games: Remember those games from your school days? Play classics like tic-tac-toe, dots and boxes, or even battleship on a piece of paper. Compete against a fellow attendee or play solo to pass the time and make the meeting more enjoyable.

7. Apply the ‘Yes, and…’ Rule: If you find yourself engaged in a discussion, challenge yourself to use the ‘Yes, and…’ rule from improvisational theater. Seamlessly add absurd and hilarious suggestions to the ongoing conversation. Just be careful not to derail the meeting completely!

8. Create Impromptu Skits: Pick a topic from the meeting, assign roles to willing participants, and create a 2-minute impromptu skit during a break. Get creative with overacting and parodying the meeting’s monotony. Perform it for your colleagues and see who can bring the most laughter to the room.

9. Hold an Unspoken ‘Best Poker Face’ Contest: Challenge your co-workers to a game of maintaining a serious face throughout the meeting, no matter how ridiculous the statements or situations get. Whoever cracks first, loses. This game adds a hilarious twist to the usual poker face challenge.

10. Play Buzzword Bingo: Create a bingo card with commonly used buzzwords from your workplace. Cross them out as they are being used and try to get a bingo. This game will not only provide amusement but also highlight the excessive use of buzzwords in meetings.

-–

With these ten hilarious ways to survive a boring meeting, you can turn even the most tedious events into memorable experiences filled with laughter. Remember to use these strategies with caution, as maintaining professionalism is still important. So, next time you find yourself in a dreary meeting, embrace the opportunity to inject some humor and make the most out of the situation. Your colleagues will thank you for bringing some much-needed entertainment into their day!
