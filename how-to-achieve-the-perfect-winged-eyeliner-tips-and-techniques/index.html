How to Achieve the Perfect Winged Eyeliner: Tips and Techniques


Achieving the perfect winged eyeliner can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and techniques, you can master this classic makeup look. Whether you’re a makeup newbie or a beauty enthusiast looking to up your winged eyeliner game, this blog post will guide you through the process step by step.

1. Start with a clean canvas
Before you begin applying your winged eyeliner, it’s important to start with a clean and dry eyelid. Remove any excess oil or makeup residue by gently cleansing your eyelids. This will help your eyeliner glide on smoothly and stay in place throughout the day.

2. Choose the right eyeliner
There are various types of eyeliners available, including liquid, gel, and pencil formulas. Each has its own pros and cons, so it’s important to choose the one that you feel most comfortable using. Liquid eyeliner provides a precise and sharp line, while gel eyeliner offers more control and can be easily smudged for a softer look. Pencil eyeliner is great for beginners as it allows for easier application.

3. Use a guide
If you struggle with getting the perfect wing shape, using a guide can be immensely helpful. One popular technique is to use a small piece of tape or a business card as a stencil. Place it at an angle from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow, and use it as a guide to create a straight line. Once you remove the guide, you’ll be left with a clean and sharp wing.

4. Start with the outer corner
When applying your winged eyeliner, start from the outer corner of your eye and work inwards. This will help you create a gradual and seamless line. Begin by drawing a thin line as close to your lash line as possible, and then extend it outwards to create the wing shape. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be discouraged if it takes a few tries to get it right.

5. Fill in the gaps
After you’ve created the wing shape, fill in any gaps or spaces along your lash line. This will ensure that your eyeliner looks smooth and even. If you prefer a bolder look, you can thicken the line by going over it once or twice.

6. Clean up any mistakes
Don’t worry if you make any mistakes while applying your winged eyeliner. Simply dip a clean angled brush in some makeup remover or concealer and use it to clean up any smudges or uneven lines. This will give you a clean and polished finish.

7. Set it in place
To ensure that your winged eyeliner lasts all day, it’s important to set it in place. You can do this by gently dusting a translucent powder over your eyelids. Alternatively, you can use a setting spray to lock in your makeup.

8. Experiment and have fun
Remember, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner is all about practice and experimentation. Play around with different eyeliner styles, thicknesses, and wing shapes to find what suits you best. Don’t be afraid to step out of your comfort zone and try new techniques.

In conclusion, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner requires patience, practice, and the right tools. By following these tips and techniques, you’ll be well on your way to mastering this timeless makeup look. So go ahead, grab your eyeliner and get ready to rock those wings!
