Top 10 Altcoins to Watch in 2021


Cryptocurrencies have gained significant traction in recent years, and 2021 is gearing up to be another thrilling year for the crypto market. While Bitcoin remains the king of cryptocurrencies, there are several altcoins that have the potential to make a big splash in 2021. In this blog post, we will discuss the top 10 altcoins to watch in 2021.

1. Ethereum (ETH) - Ethereum is the leading smart contract platform and the second-largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization. Its innovative technology allows developers to build decentralized applications (DApps) on top of its blockchain. Ethereum’s upcoming upgrade to Ethereum 2.0, which aims to improve scalability and security, makes it an exciting altcoin to watch in 2021.

2. Cardano (ADA) - Cardano is a blockchain platform that aims to provide a more secure and scalable infrastructure for the development of DApps and smart contracts. With its emphasis on peer-reviewed academic research and formal verification, Cardano is anticipated to make significant strides in 2021.

3. Polkadot (DOT) - Created by Ethereum co-founder Gavin Wood, Polkadot is a multi-chain platform that enables different blockchains to interact and share information. With its interoperability and scalability features, Polkadot has quickly gained attention in the crypto community and is expected to continue its rise in 2021.

4. Chainlink (LINK) - Chainlink is a decentralized oracle network that connects smart contracts with real-world data and off-chain events. Its secure and reliable infrastructure has garnered significant attention from both developers and investors. As more applications rely on external data, Chainlink has the potential to thrive in 2021.

5. Binance Coin (BNB) - Binance Coin is the native cryptocurrency of the Binance exchange, one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges in the world. BNB has multiple use cases within the Binance ecosystem, including discounted trading fees, participating in token sales, and more. With Binance’s continued growth and expansion, BNB is an altcoin worth keeping an eye on in 2021.

6. Litecoin (LTC) - Often referred to as the silver to Bitcoin’s gold, Litecoin is a popular altcoin that offers faster transaction confirmations and a different mining algorithm. With its strong community and established position in the crypto market, Litecoin has the potential to see substantial growth in 2021.

7. ChainX (PCX) - ChainX is a Polkadot-based blockchain project that aims to bridge Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies to the Polkadot ecosystem. By allowing Bitcoin users to interact with decentralized applications on Polkadot, ChainX provides an innovative solution for Bitcoin’s limited smart contract capabilities.

8. VeChain (VET) - VeChain is a blockchain platform that focuses on supply chain management and product authentication. With partnerships with notable companies like Walmart China and DNV GL, VeChain has established itself as a leader in the enterprise blockchain sector. As global supply chains become increasingly digitized, VeChain has the potential to experience significant growth in 2021.

9. Aave (AAVE) - Aave is a decentralized lending and borrowing protocol built on Ethereum. By enabling users to lend and borrow cryptocurrency assets, Aave provides a crucial infrastructure for decentralized finance (DeFi) applications. As DeFi continues to gain prominence, Aave is well-positioned to be a key player in the space in 2021.

10. Yearn.finance (YFI) - Yearn.finance is a DeFi platform that aims to optimize yield farming strategies by automatically reallocating funds across various protocols. With its innovative approach and strong community, Yearn.finance has seen significant growth in a short period. As the DeFi market expands, Yearn.finance has the potential to generate substantial returns for investors in 2021.

It’s important to note that investing in cryptocurrencies can be highly volatile and speculative. It’s crucial to do thorough research and consult with a financial advisor before making any investment decisions.

-–

With their innovative technologies and strong communities, these top 10 altcoins have the potential to make waves in the crypto market in 2021. However, as always, it’s important to conduct your own research and stay up to date with the latest developments in the cryptocurrency space. Happy investing!
