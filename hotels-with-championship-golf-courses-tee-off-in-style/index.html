Hotels with Championship Golf Courses: Tee Off in Style


When it comes to planning the perfect getaway, there are few things more luxurious than combining a relaxing hotel stay with a round of golf on a championship course. The thrill of teeing off against a backdrop of stunning scenery, the challenge of navigating each hole, and the satisfaction of sinking that perfect putt - it’s an experience that golf enthusiasts dream about. In this blog post, we will explore some of the world’s most prestigious hotels that offer championship golf courses, allowing you to tee off in style.

1. Pebble Beach Resorts, California, USA:
Nestled along the picturesque coastline of California’s Monterey Peninsula, Pebble Beach Resorts is a golfer’s paradise. The resort boasts not one, but four championship golf courses, including the world-renowned Pebble Beach Golf Links. Designed by legendary golf architects, these courses offer breathtaking ocean views, challenging fairways, and impeccably manicured greens. After a day on the links, unwind in one of the luxurious oceanfront accommodations and indulge in gourmet dining options.

2. St. Andrews Links, Scotland:
For golf enthusiasts, St. Andrews Links in Scotland needs no introduction. This historic destination is often referred to as the ‘Home of Golf’ and is a pilgrimage site for golfers from around the world. The Old Course, one of the many championship courses in St. Andrews, is a true icon of the sport. With its rolling fairways, famous bunkers, and challenging greens, playing a round here is a once-in-a-lifetime experience. Stay in one of the charming hotels nearby and immerse yourself in the rich golfing heritage of this iconic destination.

3. Sun City Resort, South Africa:
Located in the heart of the African bushveld, Sun City Resort offers a unique blend of luxury, entertainment, and championship golf. The resort is home to two world-class golf courses, the Gary Player Country Club and the Lost City Golf Course. The Gary Player Country Club has hosted the prestigious Nedbank Golf Challenge, attracting some of the best golfers in the world. After a round of golf, relax in the lavish surroundings of the resort, take a safari tour, or enjoy the vibrant nightlife.

4. Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic:
Situated on the southeastern coast of the Dominican Republic, Casa de Campo is a tropical paradise that combines luxury accommodations with world-class golf. The resort’s Teeth of the Dog golf course is consistently ranked among the top courses in the world. Designed by Pete Dye, this challenging course features seven holes along the Caribbean Sea, offering breathtaking ocean views and a unique golfing experience. After a day on the links, unwind in one of the stunning villas or indulge in the resort’s spa and dining facilities.

5. Emirates Golf Club, Dubai, UAE:
Dubai is known for its opulence, and the Emirates Golf Club is a testament to this. Featuring two championship courses, the Majlis and the Faldo, this golfing destination offers a luxurious experience in the heart of the desert. The Majlis course has hosted the prestigious Omega Dubai Desert Classic, attracting top golfers from around the world. After a round of golf, enjoy the modern amenities and world-class service of the club’s hotel, and explore the vibrant city of Dubai.

These are just a few examples of the many hotels around the world that offer championship golf courses. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or just starting out, these destinations provide the perfect blend of luxury and golfing excellence. So, pack your clubs, book your stay, and get ready to tee off in style at these remarkable hotels. Happy golfing!
