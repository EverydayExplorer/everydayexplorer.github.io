The Best Hotels for Outdoor Weddings: Say I\ Do in Nature


When it comes to planning a wedding, choosing the perfect venue is essential. For couples who love the great outdoors and dream of a picturesque wedding surrounded by nature, outdoor venues offer a unique and stunning backdrop. From intimate garden settings to breathtaking beachfront locations, there are countless hotels around the world that specialize in hosting outdoor weddings. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotels for outdoor weddings, where you can say ‘I Do’ in nature and create memories that will last a lifetime.

1. Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora, French Polynesia:
Imagine exchanging vows on a pristine white sandy beach, with the crystal-clear turquoise waters of the South Pacific as your backdrop. The Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora offers couples the opportunity to have a truly magical outdoor wedding experience. With lush tropical gardens, stunning overwater chapels, and luxurious beachfront villas, this idyllic resort provides the perfect setting for a romantic and unforgettable wedding celebration.

2. Amankila, Bali, Indonesia:
Nestled among the lush hills and overlooking the Indian Ocean, Amankila offers couples a serene and tranquil setting for their outdoor wedding. With its intimate garden terraces, infinity pools, and secluded beach cove, this luxury resort in Bali sets the stage for a romantic and intimate celebration. The stunning views of Mount Agung and the surrounding rice terraces create a breathtaking backdrop for your special day.

3. The Resort at Pedregal, Cabo San Lucas, Mexico:
If you are looking for a destination wedding with a touch of luxury and sophistication, The Resort at Pedregal in Cabo San Lucas is the perfect choice. With its picturesque clifftop location and panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, this resort offers a stunning setting for an outdoor wedding. Whether you choose to have your ceremony on the beach, in their beautiful cliffside gazebo, or in their lush tropical gardens, you are guaranteed a wedding experience like no other.

4. Big Sur, California, USA:
For nature-loving couples seeking a more rustic and natural wedding experience, Big Sur in California is a dream destination. With its breathtaking coastline, towering redwoods, and sweeping mountain views, Big Sur offers a multitude of outdoor wedding venues. From beachside ceremonies to intimate forest clearings, you can find the perfect spot to say your vows and immerse yourselves in the beauty of nature.

5. Masai Mara National Reserve, Kenya:
For adventurous couples who want a truly unique and unforgettable wedding experience, the Masai Mara National Reserve in Kenya is an excellent choice. Imagine exchanging vows amidst the stunning landscapes of the African savannah, with the sounds of wildlife in the background. Many luxury lodges in the area offer outdoor wedding packages that include game drives, bush dinners, and even hot air balloon rides, allowing you to create a once-in-a-lifetime wedding celebration.

6. Santorini, Greece:
Famous for its breathtaking sunsets and stunning Caldera views, Santorini provides a picturesque backdrop for outdoor weddings. With its cliffside caldera dwellings, blue-domed churches, and whitewashed buildings, this Greek island offers a romantic and scenic setting for your special day. Whether you choose to have your ceremony on a cliffside terrace, overlooking the Aegean Sea, or in a charming vineyard, Santorini promises an unforgettable outdoor wedding experience.

In conclusion, if you are a nature lover and dream of an outdoor wedding, there are plenty of incredible hotels around the world that can turn your dream into reality. From secluded beachfront resorts to picturesque mountain escapes, the options are endless. Whether you opt for a tropical destination in French Polynesia or a rustic wedding in Big Sur, the key to a successful outdoor wedding is finding a venue that captures your vision and enhances the natural beauty of the surroundings. So, say ‘I Do’ in nature and create memories that will last a lifetime at one of these breathtaking hotels.
