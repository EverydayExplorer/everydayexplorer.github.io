How to Get Upgrades and Perks at Hotels


When it comes to staying at hotels, who wouldn’t like to be treated like a VIP and enjoy special perks and upgrades? Whether you’re a frequent traveler or planning a special getaway, getting upgrades and perks at hotels can not only enhance your stay but also make you feel like a valued guest. In this article, we will explore some tips and techniques to help you score those coveted upgrades and perks at hotels around the world.

*1. Loyalty Programs:*
Joining a hotel loyalty program is a great way to start enjoying exclusive perks and upgrades. Most major hotel chains have their own loyalty programs that allow members to accumulate points with each stay. These points can be redeemed for complimentary room upgrades, free nights, spa treatments, and other exciting perks. By being a loyal patron of a hotel chain, you are more likely to be recognized and rewarded for your loyalty.

*2. Be Polite and Friendly:*
Hotel staff members are more inclined to provide upgrades and perks to guests who are polite, friendly, and respectful. Remember, positive energy goes a long way! Greet the staff with a smile, engage in friendly conversations, and express gratitude for their assistance. Building a rapport with the hotel staff can increase your chances of being noticed and considered for an upgrade.

*3. Timing is Key:*
Timing plays a crucial role in getting upgrades and perks at hotels. Consider booking your stay during the hotel’s off-peak season or weekdays when occupancy rates are typically lower. Hotels are more likely to have vacant rooms or suites during these times, making it more convenient for them to offer upgrades. Additionally, if you arrive at the hotel late in the day when they might have a better sense of occupancy, there’s a higher chance of being offered an upgrade.

*4. Special Occasions and Celebrations:*
Planning a special occasion or celebrating a milestone? Be sure to inform the hotel in advance. Hotels often offer special perks and upgrades for birthdays, anniversaries, and honeymoons. They might surprise you with complimentary champagne, chocolate-covered strawberries, or even room upgrades to make your celebration truly memorable. Don’t forget to mention the reason for your stay while making the reservation or during the check-in process.

*5. Utilize Social Media:*
In this digital age, social media can be a powerful tool for getting upgrades and perks at hotels. Follow your preferred hotel chains on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. Many hotels use social media platforms to engage with their guests and occasionally offer exclusive perks or discounts. Additionally, if you have a substantial following on social media or are a travel influencer, hotels may be more inclined to offer upgrades for the sake of exposure.

*6. Offer Feedback:*
Hotels value their guest’s feedback and reviews. If you have had a pleasant experience during your stay, don’t hesitate to express your appreciation to the hotel staff or management. Write a positive review on websites like TripAdvisor or Google, highlighting the excellent service or amenities you enjoyed. By providing positive feedback, you are increasing the chances of being remembered and valued, which might lead to future upgrades or perks during your next stay.

*Conclusion:*
Getting upgrades and perks at hotels is not an impossible task. By utilizing these effective strategies, you can enhance your hotel experience and make the most out of your stay. Join loyalty programs, be polite and friendly, consider booking during off-peak times, notify the hotel of special occasions, leverage social media, and offer feedback to the hotel. Remember, a little effort can go a long way in ensuring that you have a truly exceptional and memorable stay at your favorite hotels.
