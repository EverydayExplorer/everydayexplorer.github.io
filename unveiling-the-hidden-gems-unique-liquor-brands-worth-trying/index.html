Unveiling the Hidden Gems: Unique Liquor Brands Worth Trying


If you’re a liquor enthusiast, you probably enjoy exploring unique and interesting liquor brands. While there are many well-known brands on the market, there are also hidden gems waiting to be discovered. In this blog post, we’ll be unveiling some of these hidden gems – unique liquor brands that are worth trying.

1. Amrut Indian Single Malt Whisky: India might not be the first country that comes to mind when you think of whisky. However, Amrut Indian Single Malt Whisky is changing that perception. This award-winning whisky is made from Indian barley and aged in oak barrels. It offers a complex flavor profile with hints of spice, fruit, and oak. Give it a try, and you might be pleasantly surprised!

2. St. George Spirits: Based in California, St. George Spirits is known for their innovative and unconventional approach to distilling. Their lineup includes a variety of spirits such as gin, vodka, absinthe, and even an agricole-style rum. Each bottle is meticulously crafted using unique ingredients and techniques, resulting in a range of flavors that is truly exceptional.

3. Kavalan Whisky: Hailing from Taiwan, Kavalan is known for its distinct and flavorsome whiskies. Their commitment to craftsmanship and attention to detail has earned them numerous awards and accolades. Each expression is matured in different cask types, such as sherry, bourbon, and wine casks, resulting in a diverse range of flavors to explore.

4. Greenbar Distillery: Located in Los Angeles, Greenbar Distillery is a pioneering force in the craft spirits movement. They produce a wide array of spirits including vodka, gin, rum, tequila, and liqueurs. What sets them apart is their focus on sustainability and using organic ingredients. Their spirits are not only delicious but also made with a commitment to making a positive impact on the environment.

5. Ryan & Wood Distilleries: Operating out of Gloucester, Massachusetts, Ryan & Wood Distilleries is a family-owned and operated business with a passion for creating high-quality spirits. They are best known for their handcrafted Folly Cove Rum, made using molasses and aged in charred oak barrels. If you’re a rum enthusiast, this is one brand you don’t want to miss!

6. Cotswolds Distillery: For lovers of gin, the Cotswolds Distillery offers a unique experience. Nestled in the picturesque Cotswolds region of England, this distillery produces a range of gin expressions characterized by their complex botanical profiles and high-quality ingredients. From classic London Dry to more adventurous seasonal gins, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

7. Amaro Montenegro: Amaro, an Italian herbal liqueur, has been steadily gaining popularity in recent years. Amaro Montenegro is a true gem in the category, offering a perfect balance of bitterness, sweetness, and herbal complexity. It’s versatile enough to be enjoyed on its own, on the rocks, or as an ingredient in cocktails.

8. Westland Distillery: For those who appreciate American single malt whiskey, Westland Distillery in Seattle, Washington, is a must-try. They focus on using locally sourced ingredients and traditional techniques to produce exceptional single malts. With a commitment to cask experimentation and a range of unique expressions, Westland is pushing the boundaries of American whiskey.

9. Chateau de Laubade Armagnac: Armagnac, a traditional French brandy, often gets overshadowed by its more famous cousin, Cognac. Chateau de Laubade is an exceptional Armagnac producer that deserves your attention. Their brandy is made using grapes grown in the Armagnac region and is aged in oak barrels, resulting in rich, complex flavors.

10. Compass Box Whisky: Blended whisky doesn’t always receive the recognition it deserves, but Compass Box is changing the game. This independent Scotch whisky company is known for its innovative blends and dedication to transparency. Each blend is carefully crafted to showcase a unique flavor profile, making them a great choice for whisky enthusiasts.

In conclusion, there are numerous hidden gems in the liquor world that are worth exploring. From innovative craft spirits to unconventional whiskies, these unique liquor brands offer a chance to expand your palate and discover new flavors. So, the next time you’re looking to try something different, consider giving one of these hidden gems a try. Cheers!
