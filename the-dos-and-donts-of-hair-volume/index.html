The Dos and Don'ts of Hair Volume


Luscious, voluminous hair is a coveted trait that many people desire. Whether you have thin, limp hair or just want to add a little extra oomph to your locks, achieving the perfect hair volume can be a game-changer. However, it’s important to know the dos and don’ts to avoid any hair disasters. In this blog post, we’ll explore some expert tips and tricks to help you achieve the hair volume of your dreams.

DO: Use the Right Products
Choosing the right hair products is crucial for achieving and maintaining hair volume. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that add body and bounce to your hair. These products are specially formulated to give your hair that extra lift. Additionally, using a lightweight mousse or a volumizing spray before styling can provide added support and hold.

DO NOT: Overload on Heavy Products
While it’s important to use the right products, it’s equally important not to go overboard. Avoid using heavy styling products such as gels or heavy serums, as they can weigh your hair down and make it appear flat. Opt for lightweight products that won’t compromise the volume you’re trying to achieve.

DO: Blow Dry Upside Down
When blow-drying your hair, flip your head upside down. This technique helps to create natural lift at the roots, resulting in enhanced volume. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots while blow-drying, focusing on the areas where you want the most volume.

DO NOT: Overuse Heat Styling Tools
Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it appear flat and lifeless. Limit the use of heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling wands. Over time, heat can weaken the hair shaft, leading to breakage and loss of volume. Embrace your natural hair texture or experiment with heatless styling techniques to minimize damage and maintain hair volume.

DO: Tease with Caution
Teasing, or backcombing, can be an effective way to add instant volume to your hair. However, it’s important to tease with caution. Use a fine-tooth comb and gently backcomb small sections of hair at the roots. Avoid aggressive teasing, as it can cause damage and breakage. Remember to always brush out the teased sections gently to avoid tangling and further damage.

DO NOT: Neglect Regular Trims
Regular trims are essential for maintaining healthy hair and preventing split ends. Split ends can make your hair appear thinner and less voluminous. Schedule regular trims every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking fresh, healthy, and full of volume.

DO: Experiment with Hairstyles
There are numerous hairstyles that can help create the illusion of volume. Consider opting for layered haircuts, as they add movement and body to your hair. Play around with different updos and braids to add texture and volume. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

DO NOT: Neglect Your Diet and Lifestyle
Healthy hair starts from within. Make sure you’re nourishing your body with a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals. Incorporate foods such as salmon, avocados, and spinach, which are known to promote healthy hair growth. Stay hydrated, exercise regularly, and manage stress levels, as these factors can also impact the overall health of your hair.

In conclusion, achieving hair volume is all about finding the right balance and using the right techniques. By following these dos and don’ts, you’ll be well on your way to achieving the voluminous hair you’ve always desired. Remember, it’s not just about the products you use, but also about how you care for your hair and embrace your natural beauty. So go ahead, embrace the volume and let your hair do the talking!
