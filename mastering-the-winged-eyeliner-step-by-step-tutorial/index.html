Mastering the Winged Eyeliner: Step-by-Step Tutorial


Are you tired of struggling with your winged eyeliner? Have you ever seen someone with perfectly winged eyeliner and wondered how they achieve that flawless look? Well, wonder no more! In this step-by-step tutorial, we will guide you through the process of mastering the winged eyeliner technique.

The winged eyeliner look is a classic and timeless makeup trend that adds a touch of elegance and sophistication to any makeup look. Whether you want to create a subtle wing or a dramatic cat-eye, this tutorial has got you covered. So grab your favorite eyeliner and let’s get started!

## Step 1: Prep your eyelids

Before you start applying your eyeliner, it’s important to prep your eyelids. Begin by thoroughly cleansing your face to ensure that your eyelids are clean and free of any oils or dirt. Next, apply a primer specifically designed for the eyelids. This will help create a smooth canvas for your eyeliner and prolong its wear.

## Step 2: Choose your eyeliner

The next step is to choose the right eyeliner for you. There are various types of eyeliners available, such as liquid, gel, pencil, and pen. Each type has its own pros and cons, so choose the one that you feel most comfortable using and that matches your desired winged eyeliner look.

## Step 3: Begin at the inner corner

Start by lining your upper lash line, beginning at the inner corner of your eye. Use short, light strokes to create a thin line. This will act as the base for your winged eyeliner.

## Step 4: Create the wing

Now, it’s time to create the wing. Imagine an invisible line extending from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. This will be the guide for your wing. Begin at the outer corner of your eye and draw a small line upwards, following the angle of the imaginary line. The length and angle of the wing are up to you, depending on the desired look.

## Step 5: Connect the wing to the lash line

Next, connect the wing to your upper lash line. Start at the tip of the wing and draw a line towards the center of your eyelid, gradually tapering the line as you reach the inner corner of your eye. Ensure that the line is smooth and even.

## Step 6: Fill in the gap

After connecting the wing to the lash line, go back and fill in any gaps or uneven areas. Use short, gentle strokes to create a smooth and consistent line from the inner to the outer corner of your eye.

## Step 7: Optional: Tightline and apply mascara

If you want to enhance the winged eyeliner look further, you can tightline your upper waterline with a black eyeliner pencil. This will create the illusion of thicker lashes and make your winged eyeliner appear more seamless. Finish off the look by applying mascara to your upper lashes to add volume and length.

## Step 8: Clean up any mistakes

If you make any mistakes or if your eyeliner isn’t as sharp as you’d like, don’t worry! Take a clean angled brush or a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover and gently clean up any smudges or uneven lines. This will help you achieve that perfect winged eyeliner look.

## Step 9: Practice makes perfect

Remember, mastering the winged eyeliner technique takes time and practice. Don’t be discouraged if your first attempt doesn’t turn out exactly as you envisioned. Keep practicing and experimenting with different techniques until you find what works best for you. With time, you’ll become a winged eyeliner pro!

## Final Thoughts

Mastering the winged eyeliner technique is all about practice, patience, and finding the right products for you. By following this step-by-step tutorial and putting in the time to perfect your technique, you’ll be able to achieve flawless winged eyeliner looks every time.

So, grab your favorite eyeliner, let your creativity soar, and get ready to rock that winged eyeliner with confidence! You’re just a few strokes away from achieving a stunning, eye-catching look that will turn heads wherever you go.
