The Best Jewelry Subscription Boxes to Try Right Now


Jewelry subscription boxes are a fantastic way to treat yourself to a rotating selection of stunning accessories, or to give the gift of wearable art to someone you love. With a wide variety of options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. To help you navigate the world of jewelry subscription boxes, we’ve compiled a list of some of the best ones to try right now.

  1. RocksBox RocksBox is a popular jewelry subscription service that offers a personalized selection of designer jewelry based on your style preferences. You can try out high-end pieces from brands like Kendra Scott, Kate Spade, and Gorjana for a monthly fee. With the option to purchase any pieces you love at a discounted price, RocksBox makes it easy to refresh your jewelry collection.

  2. Emma & Chloe If you’re a fan of unique, French-inspired designs, Emma & Chloe is the perfect subscription box for you. Each month, you’ll receive a piece of handmade jewelry from a talented independent designer in Paris. From delicate earrings to statement necklaces, Emma & Chloe’s pieces are sure to add a touch of chic sophistication to any outfit.

  3. Goddess Provisions For those who prefer their jewelry with a side of spirituality, Goddess Provisions offers a monthly subscription box that combines ethically sourced crystals and gemstones with wellness and self-care products. Each box is curated around a different theme, such as moon magic or chakra healing, and includes a piece of crystal jewelry to help you tap into your inner goddess.

  4. myJemma myJemma stands out for its focus on sustainable and ethical jewelry. Each month, subscribers receive a handcrafted piece of jewelry made from recycled materials, such as reclaimed metals or vintage beads. With a commitment to eco-friendly practices and a dedication to supporting independent artisans, myJemma is a great choice for conscious consumers who want to look good while doing good.

  5. EarFleek If you’re a fan of statement earrings, EarFleek is the perfect subscription box for you. Each month, you’ll receive a fun and trendy pair of earrings delivered right to your door. With options for both pierced and clip-on styles, EarFleek makes it easy to experiment with new looks and add a pop of personality to your outfit.

  6. mintMONGOOSE mintMONGOOSE offers a customizable jewelry subscription box that allows you to choose your preferences for metal color, jewelry type, and style. Each month, you’ll receive a curated selection of high-quality pieces, such as necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, that are tailored to your taste. With the option to skip a month or cancel anytime, mintMONGOOSE gives you the flexibility to build your dream jewelry collection at your own pace.

  7. JustFab JustFab is a fashion subscription service that offers a wide range of products, including jewelry. By taking a style quiz, you’ll receive personalized recommendations for jewelry pieces that match your taste and preferences. With affordable pricing, high-quality materials, and regular new arrivals, JustFab makes it easy to stay on-trend and accessorize your wardrobe with ease.

In conclusion, jewelry subscription boxes are a fun and convenient way to discover new accessories and elevate your personal style. Whether you prefer classic elegance, bohemian flair, or modern minimalism, there’s a subscription box out there for everyone. By trying out different services and exploring a variety of designs, you can curate a jewelry collection that reflects your unique personality and keeps your look fresh and exciting.
