Skateboarding Tricks: Taking Your Skills to the Next Level


Hey there! Are you ready to take your skateboarding skills to the next level? Well, look no further because in this blog post, we’re diving headfirst into the exciting world of skateboarding tricks. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced rider looking to up your game, we’ve got you covered with some awesome tips and tricks that will have you shredding like a pro in no time!

Before we dive into the tricks themselves, let’s talk about the importance of mastering the basics. Just like anything in life, a strong foundation is key. So, make sure you’ve got the basics down pat – from balancing and pushing off to turning and stopping. Once you’ve got those skills honed, you’ll be ready to tackle some awesome tricks.

Now, let’s get into the nitty-gritty of skateboarding tricks. We’ll break them down into three categories: beginner, intermediate, and advanced. This way, you can progress at your own pace and feel a sense of achievement as you move up the ladder.

Beginner Tricks:
1. Ollie: The bread and butter of skateboarding tricks, the ollie is your gateway to the world of tricks. This maneuver involves popping the tail of your skateboard, sliding your front foot forward, and leveling the board in mid-air.
2. Kickturn: Mastering the kickturn will give you the ability to make quick direction changes while riding. It involves lifting either the front or back wheels off the ground and pivoting on the other set of wheels.
3. Manual: This trick involves balancing on either the front or back wheels of your skateboard while moving. It’s a great way to showcase control and balance.

Intermediate Tricks:
1. Shuvit: Take your ollie to the next level by adding a 180-degree spin to it. This trick is all about timing and coordination, as you need to scoop the tail and kick your back foot out to make the board spin.
2. Boardslide: Ready for some rail action? The boardslide involves sliding your skateboard along a rail or ledge with the underside of the deck. Start with a low rail and work your way up to more challenging obstacles.
3. Heelflip: Similar to the ollie, the heelflip adds a kickflip-like spin to it. Instead of using your front foot, you’ll use your back foot to flick the board and make it rotate.

Advanced Tricks:
1. Hardflip: A combination of a kickflip and a frontside 360 pop shove-it, the hardflip is a challenging trick that requires precise timing and footwork.
2. Nollie Heelflip: Step up your game with this trick that combines a nollie (a trick where you pop the nose of the board) and a heelflip. It’s a trick that will definitely turn heads at the skatepark.
3. 900: The holy grail of skateboarding tricks, the 900 is a two-and-a-half spin in mid-air. Only a handful of skaters have ever landed it, but with dedication and practice, who knows? You might be the next one to pull it off!

Remember, practice makes perfect. Don’t get discouraged if you don’t nail a trick right away – it takes time, patience, and determination. Always wear protective gear and skate within your limits. And most importantly, have fun with it!

So, there you have it – a comprehensive guide to taking your skateboarding skills to the next level. From beginner tricks to mind-blowing advanced maneuvers, there’s something for everyone. Get out there, push yourself, and embrace the thrill of learning new tricks. Happy shredding!
