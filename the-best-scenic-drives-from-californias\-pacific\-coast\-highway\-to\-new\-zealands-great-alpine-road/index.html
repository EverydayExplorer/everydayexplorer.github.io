The Best Scenic Drives: From Californias\ Pacific\ Coast\ Highway\ to\ New\ Zealands Great Alpine Road


California’s Pacific Coast Highway and New Zealand’s Great Alpine Road are two of the most breathtaking scenic drives in the world. Both routes offer stunning landscapes, jaw-dropping vistas, and unforgettable experiences for travelers. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, an adventure seeker, or simply someone who appreciates the beauty of the open road, these drives are a must-do on any bucket list.

Starting with the Pacific Coast Highway, this iconic route stretches along California’s coastline for more than 600 miles. The drive begins in southern California near Dana Point and ends in the northern city of Leggett. Along the way, drivers are treated to panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, stunning cliffs, and charming beach towns.

One of the highlights of this drive is the famous Big Sur coastline. This rugged stretch of coastline is often referred to as one of the most beautiful drives in the world. The road twists and turns along the cliffs, offering breathtaking views at every turn. Keep an eye out for the iconic Bixby Creek Bridge, which has been featured in countless movies and commercials.

As you continue north along the Pacific Coast Highway, you’ll pass through picturesque towns like Santa Barbara, Morro Bay, and Santa Cruz. Each of these stops offers its own unique charm and attractions, making them perfect places to take a break and explore.

Another must-see along the Pacific Coast Highway is the iconic Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco. This world-famous landmark is not only a marvel of engineering but also offers stunning views of the city and the bay. Be sure to stop and take a walk along the bridge or explore the nearby Presidio and Golden Gate Park.

After San Francisco, the drive takes you through the beautiful wine country of Sonoma and Napa Valley. This is the perfect opportunity to indulge in some wine tasting and enjoy the picturesque vineyards that dot the region. From there, the drive continues north towards the rugged and remote beauty of the Mendocino Coast. Here, you’ll find pristine beaches, towering cliffs, and charming seaside villages.

Now, let’s shift gears and travel halfway across the world to New Zealand’s Great Alpine Road. This scenic drive spans 460 kilometers and takes you through the heart of the South Island’s rugged and breathtaking landscapes.

Starting in the east coast city of Christchurch, the Great Alpine Road winds its way through the Southern Alps, offering stunning views of snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, and lush valleys. This drive is truly a nature lover’s paradise, with ample opportunities for hiking, skiing, and exploring the great outdoors.

One of the highlights along the route is Arthur’s Pass National Park. This alpine wonderland is home to some of the country’s most stunning landscapes, including snow-capped peaks, glaciers, and beautiful waterfalls. There are numerous hiking trails and viewpoints throughout the park, allowing visitors to fully immerse themselves in the beauty of the area.

As you continue west along the Great Alpine Road, you’ll pass through the picturesque town of Hokitika, famous for its jade carving and stunning sunsets. Further along the route, you’ll come across the Franz Josef and Fox glaciers, where you can take a guided tour or even go heli-hiking to explore these icy wonders up close.

The drive concludes in the charming town of Wanaka, which sits on the shores of Lake Wanaka. This is the perfect spot to relax and take in the stunning surroundings or partake in a variety of outdoor activities, such as hiking, mountain biking, or even skydiving.

Both the Pacific Coast Highway and the Great Alpine Road offer unforgettable experiences for travelers. Whether you choose to explore the rugged coastline of California or the majestic landscapes of New Zealand, you’re bound to be amazed by the natural beauty and awe-inspiring scenery along these routes.

In conclusion, if you’re a fan of scenic drives and breathtaking landscapes, the Pacific Coast Highway and the Great Alpine Road should be at the top of your travel bucket list. From the iconic cliffs of Big Sur to the snow-capped peaks of the Southern Alps, these routes offer a truly unforgettable experience for anyone who loves the open road. So grab your sunglasses, hit the road, and get ready to be amazed by the best scenic drives in the world.
