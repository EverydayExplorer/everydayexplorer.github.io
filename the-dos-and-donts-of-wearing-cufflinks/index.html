The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Cufflinks


Cufflinks are a timeless accessory that can add a touch of sophistication and elegance to any outfit. Whether you’re dressing up for a formal event or adding a stylish flair to your everyday attire, cufflinks are a versatile accessory that can elevate your look. However, like any accessory, there are certain dos and don’ts to keep in mind when it comes to wearing cufflinks. In this blog post, we’ll explore the best practices for wearing cufflinks and avoid any fashion faux pas.

DO: Choose the Right Shirt
When it comes to wearing cufflinks, the first step is to choose the right shirt. Opt for a shirt with French cuffs, as these are specifically designed to accommodate cufflinks. French cuffs have longer sleeves that fold back and are fastened with cufflinks, creating a clean and polished look. Make sure the shirt fits well and isn’t too loose or too tight, as this can affect the way your cufflinks sit on your wrists.

DON’T: Overdo It
While cufflinks can be a stylish accessory, it’s important not to overdo it. Remember that less is often more when it comes to accessorizing, so choose cufflinks that complement your outfit without overpowering it. If you’re wearing a bold patterned shirt, opt for simple and understated cufflinks. On the other hand, if you’re wearing a plain shirt, you can experiment with more intricate and eye-catching designs.

DO: Match Metals
When selecting cufflinks, try to match the metals with your other accessories, such as your watch or belt buckle. This creates a cohesive and put-together look. If you’re wearing a gold watch, for example, choose cufflinks in a similar gold tone. Mixing metals can sometimes work, but it’s best to stick to one metal tone for a more polished appearance.

DON’T: Forget the Occasion
Consider the occasion when choosing cufflinks. For formal events such as weddings or black-tie affairs, opt for classic and elegant cufflinks in silver or gold tones. On the other hand, for more casual or creative settings, you can explore cufflinks with unique designs or pops of color. Just remember to keep it appropriate and avoid anything too flashy or novelty-like.

DO: Pay Attention to Details
When wearing cufflinks, it’s important to pay attention to the small details. Ensure that your shirt cuffs are neatly ironed and properly aligned. Make sure the cufflink fastening mechanism is secure and that the cufflinks sit evenly on your wrists. Attention to these details will enhance your overall look and demonstrate your style and attention to detail.

DON’T: Neglect Maintenance
Lastly, remember to take care of your cufflinks. After wearing them, wipe them clean with a soft cloth to remove any dirt or fingerprints. Store them in a jewelry box or pouch to prevent scratching or damage. Regular maintenance will ensure that your cufflinks stay in great condition and continue to enhance your outfits for years to come.

In conclusion, wearing cufflinks can elevate your style and add a touch of sophistication to any outfit. By following these dos and don’ts, you’ll be able to confidently rock cufflinks and make a fashion statement. Remember to choose the right shirt, match metals, consider the occasion, pay attention to details, and maintain your cufflinks properly. With these tips in mind, you’ll be a cufflink connoisseur in no time.
