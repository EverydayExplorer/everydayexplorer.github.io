Top 10 Fishing Blogs to Follow for Expert Advice and Inspiration


Are you an avid angler looking for expert advice and inspiration? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing the top 10 fishing blogs that you should follow. These blogs are packed with valuable tips, techniques, and stories from experienced anglers who are passionate about their craft. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced angler, these blogs will provide you with the guidance and motivation you need to enhance your fishing skills and have an unforgettable fishing experience. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of fishing blogs that you should add to your reading list!

  1. In-Fisherman - If you are seeking well-researched articles, comprehensive fishing reports, and pro-level advice, then In-Fisherman is the blog for you. With contributions from seasoned anglers and industry experts, this blog covers a wide range of topics including freshwater and saltwater fishing techniques, tackle reviews, and conservation tips.

  2. Bassmaster - Known as the authority on bass fishing, Bassmaster offers informative articles, videos, and podcasts for anglers seeking to improve their bass fishing skills. Whether you are interested in tournament fishing, bass biology, or tackle selection, this blog has it all.

  3. Salt Strong - Salt Strong is a must-follow blog for saltwater anglers. With an emphasis on education and conservation, this blog provides in-depth tutorials, product reviews, and inspirational stories for anglers of all skill levels. Salt Strong also offers a supportive online community where anglers can connect and share their fishing experiences.

  4. Wired2Fish - If you’re looking to stay up to date with the latest fishing trends and techniques, Wired2Fish should be on your radar. This blog covers a wide range of fishing topics including tips for specific species, instructional videos, and gear reviews. With contributions from a team of experienced anglers, you’ll find a wealth of information to help you become a better angler.

  5. Orvis Fly Fishing - Fly fishing enthusiasts will love the Orvis Fly Fishing blog. With articles on fly tying, casting techniques, and destination spotlights, this blog is an excellent resource for anyone interested in the art of fly fishing. The blog also features beautiful photography and captivating stories that will inspire you to explore new waters.

  6. In-Fisherman Ice Fishing - Ice fishing is a unique and thrilling experience, and In-Fisherman Ice Fishing blog is your go-to resource for all things ice fishing. From tips on finding hot spots to advice on drilling the perfect hole, this blog covers everything you need to know to make the most of your time on the ice.

  7. The Fisherman Magazine - With a focus on the East Coast fishing scene, The Fisherman Magazine blog provides valuable insights into regional fishing tactics, regulations, and reports. Whether you are interested in surf fishing, offshore trolling, or inshore angling, this blog has you covered.

  8. MidCurrent - Are you passionate about fly fishing for trout? Look no further than MidCurrent. This blog offers a wealth of information on fly fishing techniques, gear recommendations, and conservation efforts specific to trout and their habitats. Whether you’re a beginner or an expert, MidCurrent has something for everyone.

  9. BamaBass - BamaBass is a fantastic resource for bass fishing enthusiasts, especially those fishing in the Southeastern United States. The blog features informative articles, videos, and tournament coverage, providing valuable insights into the techniques and strategies used by successful bass anglers in the region.

  10. Inshore Fishing - If you enjoy fishing in shallow coastal waters, Inshore Fishing is the blog for you. With a focus on redfish, snook, and speckled trout, this blog offers expert advice on techniques, gear, and location recommendations specific to inshore fishing. The blog’s contributors are passionate about their craft and provide valuable insights for all inshore anglers.

By following these top 10 fishing blogs, you will stay informed about the latest trends, discover new fishing spots, and learn valuable techniques from experienced anglers. Whether you’re passionate about freshwater or saltwater fishing, bass or trout, there is a blog on this list that will suit your interests. So start following these blogs today and take your fishing skills to the next level! Happy fishing!
