10 Inspiring Music Biopics You Need to Watch


The world of music is filled with countless stories of passion, perseverance, and triumph. These tales are not only inspiring but also serve as a testament to the power of music in shaping lives and culture. Music biopics are a great way to explore these stories, and in this article, we will discuss ten inspiring music biopics that you absolutely need to watch.

1. ‘Straight Outta Compton’ (2015)

This biopic tells the story of the groundbreaking rap group N.W.A., which played a significant role in shaping the sound and culture of hip-hop in the late 1980s and early 1990s. The film focuses on the group’s formation, the challenges they faced, and their impact on the music industry.

2. ‘What’s Love Got to Do with It’ (1993)

Based on the life of legendary singer Tina Turner, this biopic delves into her tumultuous personal life and her rise to fame. The film covers her early years, her abusive marriage to Ike Turner, and her eventual comeback, which earned her the title ‘Queen of Rock ’n’ Roll.’

3. ‘La La Land’ (2016)

Although not a traditional music biopic, this romantic musical drama tells the story of two aspiring artists in Los Angeles. The film explores themes of ambition, love, and the sacrifices one must make to achieve their dreams.

4. ‘Ray’ (2004)

This biopic chronicles the life of Ray Charles, a blind musician who became one of the most influential artists in the history of American music. The film covers his early years, his struggle with addiction, and his immense contribution to the world of music.

5. ‘A Star is Born’ (2018)

This latest iteration of the classic story tells the tale of a seasoned musician who discovers and falls in love with a young, talented singer. As the younger artist’s career takes off, the two must navigate the challenges of fame, addiction, and love.

6. ‘Amadeus’ (1984)

Set in Vienna during the 18th century, this film tells the story of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, one of the greatest composers in the history of Western music. The film explores Mozart’s life, his relationship with his rival, Antonio Salieri, and the impact of his music on the world.

7. ‘Walk the Line’ (2005)

Based on the life of Johnny Cash, this biopic delves into the country music legend’s early years, his struggle with drug addiction, and his tumultuous relationship with his wife, June Carter Cash. The film explores the themes of love, loss, and redemption.

8. ‘Coal Miner’s Daughter’ (1980)

This biopic tells the inspiring story of Loretta Lynn, a country music icon who overcame poverty, gender stereotypes, and personal tragedies to become one of the most successful female singers in history.

9. ‘Notorious’ (2009)

This film focuses on the life of Christopher Wallace, also known as Notorious B.I.G., one of the most influential rappers in the history of hip-hop. The biopic covers his rise to fame, his rivalry with fellow rapper Tupac Shakur, and his untimely death.

10. ‘Control’ (2007)

Based on the life of Ian Curtis, the lead singer of the influential post-punk band Joy Division, this biopic explores his struggles with depression, his turbulent relationship with his wife, and the band’s meteoric rise to fame before his untimely death.

These inspiring music biopics offer a glimpse into the lives of some of the most talented and influential musicians in history. By watching these films, you can gain a deeper appreciation for the passion, dedication, and resilience it takes to achieve greatness in the world of music.
