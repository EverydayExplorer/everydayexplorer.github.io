Haircare Tips for Volume and Body: Adding Oomph to Your Hair


Do you dream of having voluminous and bouncy hair? If so, you’re not alone. Many people crave that extra oomph and body in their hair, and achieving it is easier than you may think. In this blog post, we’ll discuss some top haircare tips for adding volume and body to your hair, so you can flaunt gorgeous locks every day.

1. Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The foundation of any good haircare routine starts with selecting the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically formulated to add volume and body to your hair. These products often contain ingredients like keratin and biotin, which can strengthen and plump up your hair strands. Additionally, opt for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, as sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it appear flat and lifeless.

2. Blow-Dry Upside Down

One quick trick to instantly add volume to your hair is to blow-dry it upside down. This technique helps to lift your roots and create volume at the crown of your head. After washing your hair, flip your head over and blow-dry the roots while gently lifting them with your fingers. Once your hair is mostly dry, flip your head back and use a round brush to finish drying and styling your hair. This simple method can make a world of difference in adding body and fullness to your locks.

3. Use a Volumizing Mousse or Spray

If you’re looking for an extra boost of volume, consider incorporating a volumizing mousse or spray into your haircare routine. Apply the product to the roots of your towel-dried hair, then use a round brush to blow-dry and style as desired. The mousse or spray will add texture and lift, giving your hair that coveted oomph and body.

4. Experiment with Hair Extensions

If you’re looking for a more dramatic transformation, hair extensions can be a game-changer. There are various types of hair extensions available, from clip-ins to tape-ins, that can instantly add volume and body to your natural hair. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a seamless blend. Professional assistance may be required for the application process, so consider visiting a salon for the best results.

5. Tease and Backcomb for Added Height

Teasing your hair may seem old-fashioned, but it’s a tried and true method for creating volume and body. Take small sections of your hair near the roots and gently backcomb them using a fine-toothed comb. This will create some temporary volume and lift. Remember to be gentle to avoid damaging your hair. Once you have achieved the desired volume, smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased sections for a polished look.

6. Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a significant difference in adding volume and body to your hair. Opt for layers or a cut with textured ends to create movement and lift. Long layers can add dimension and fullness, while a blunt cut can make your hair appear thicker. Consult with a trusted hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your hair type and desired look.

7. Avoid Excessive Heat Styling

While heat-styling tools like straighteners and curling irons are great for achieving different hairstyles, excessive use can lead to damage and flat hair. The high heat can weaken your hair strands, making them more prone to breakage and making your hair look limp. Whenever possible, give your hair a break from heat styling and let it air dry. If you do use heat styling tools, always apply a heat protectant spray beforehand to minimize damage.

8. Consider a Volumizing Haircut

For those who struggle with thin or fine hair, a volumizing haircut can work wonders. A skilled stylist can create layers and additional texture to make your hair appear fuller and voluminous. The right cut can instantly transform your hair, giving it the body and oomph you’ve always desired.

9. Enhance Volume with Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is not just for absorbing excess oil between washes - it can also amplify volume and body. Spray your roots with dry shampoo and gently massaging it into your scalp. The dry shampoo will absorb oil, adding texture and lift to your hair. This is an excellent option for quick touch-ups and adding freshness to your hairstyle.

10. Eat a Healthy Diet

Lastly, healthy hair starts from within. A balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can promote hair growth and overall hair health. Incorporate foods high in vitamins A, B, C, and E, as well as biotin and folic acid, into your diet. Foods such as leafy greens, nuts, eggs, and fatty fish are all great choices for supporting hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, achieving voluminous and bouncy hair is within reach. By following these haircare tips, you can add oomph and body to your hair, transforming it from flat to fabulous. Remember to choose the right products, incorporate simple styling techniques, and nourish your hair from within. With a little effort and the right techniques, you can enjoy voluminous locks that turn heads wherever you go!
