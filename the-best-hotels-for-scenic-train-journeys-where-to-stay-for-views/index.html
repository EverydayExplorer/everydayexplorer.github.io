The Best Hotels for Scenic Train Journeys: Where to Stay for Views


When it comes to travel, few experiences rival the magic of a scenic train journey. The rhythmic clatter of wheels on tracks, combined with breathtaking landscapes that unfold outside your window, creates an unforgettable adventure. However, the experience can be elevated even further by choosing accommodations that complement your travels. Imagine waking up with a stunning view of mountains, valleys, or rivers, all while being just a stone’s throw away from the train station. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best hotels for scenic train journeys around the world, ensuring that your stay enhances the beauty of your travels.

1. Glacier Park Lodge, Montana, USA

Nestled at the entrance of Glacier National Park, Glacier Park Lodge is an iconic choice for those looking to explore the majestic landscapes of Montana. The lodge combines rustic charm with modern amenities, offering stunning views of the surrounding Rocky Mountains. After a day of exploring the park by train on the famous Amtrak Empire Builder route, you can unwind on the hotel’s expansive decks that overlook the breathtaking scenery. The lodge’s historical significance, combined with its picturesque location, makes it a prime choice for travelers seeking a serene escape.

Amenities:

  • On-site dining featuring local cuisine
  • Outdoor activities such as hiking and fishing
  • Historical tours of the surrounding area

2. The Gresham Hotel, Dublin, Ireland

If you’re planning to take a scenic train journey through Ireland, the Gresham Hotel in Dublin is an excellent choice. Located near the city’s main train station, this historic hotel offers luxurious accommodations with stunning views of the bustling streets of Dublin. After a day spent exploring the charming landscapes of the Irish countryside aboard the DART or intercity trains, return to enjoy the elegant atmosphere of the Gresham. The hotel’s proximity to the train station allows you to easily embark on your next adventure.

Amenities:

  • Elegant dining options with a focus on Irish cuisine
  • Stylish rooms with modern amenities
  • Proximity to cultural attractions and shopping districts

3. The Fairmont Banff Springs, Alberta, Canada

Known as the “Castle in the Rockies,” the Fairmont Banff Springs is a must-visit hotel for those embarking on scenic train journeys through the Canadian Rockies. Accessible via the stunning Rocky Mountaineer train, the Fairmont offers guests unparalleled views of the surrounding mountains and lush forests. The hotel itself is a marvel of architecture, blending seamlessly into the breathtaking landscape. After a day of exploring, guests can relax at the world-class spa or enjoy a meal at one of the hotel’s exquisite restaurants while indulging in the views.

Amenities:

  • Award-winning dining with local ingredients
  • Outdoor and indoor pools with mountain views
  • Access to outdoor adventures including skiing, hiking, and golfing

4. The Royal Scotsman, Scotland

For those who want to experience the beauty of Scotland in style, the Royal Scotsman is the ultimate train journey experience. This luxury train not only takes you through stunning landscapes but also offers comfortable accommodations on board. However, for those who wish to explore a specific area, the Gleneagles Hotel serves as an excellent base. Situated near the train station, Gleneagles boasts lush grounds and breathtaking views of the Scottish countryside. After a scenic train journey, unwind in the hotel’s luxurious spa or enjoy a round of golf on one of its championship courses.

Amenities:

  • Fine dining with a focus on local Scottish fare
  • Extensive spa services and wellness programs
  • Championship golf courses and outdoor activities

5. The Belmond Hiram Bingham, Peru

Traveling through the majestic landscapes of Peru is an unforgettable experience, especially when aboard the Belmond Hiram Bingham train, which takes you to the enchanting ruins of Machu Picchu. For the ultimate stay, consider the Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel. Nestled in the lush cloud forest, this hotel offers a serene escape after a day filled with breathtaking views from the train. With stunning gardens and a focus on sustainability, you can relax in your luxurious accommodation while soaking in the beauty of your surroundings.

Amenities:

  • Eco-friendly accommodations with a focus on local culture
  • Guided tours of Machu Picchu and the surrounding area
  • Relaxing spa treatments inspired by ancient traditions

6. The Shangri-La Hotel, Sydney, Australia

For those embarking on train journeys along the scenic coast of Australia, the Shangri-La Hotel in Sydney provides a stunning backdrop. Located near the Sydney Central Station, the hotel offers panoramic views of the Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge. After enjoying the sights along the Pacific Coast via train, relax in the hotel’s luxurious rooms, where you can soak in the views of the harbor. With exceptional dining options and a world-class spa, the Shangri-La allows you to unwind in style after a day of exploration.

Amenities:

  • Award-winning dining with breathtaking views
  • A luxurious spa offering a range of treatments
  • Proximity to Sydney’s cultural attractions and beaches

7. The Alphen Boutique Hotel, Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town is a fantastic starting point for scenic train journeys through the breathtaking landscapes of South Africa. The Alphen Boutique Hotel offers a luxurious stay in the heart of Constantia, surrounded by vineyards and mountains. After a day exploring the scenic routes of the Garden Route by train, return to the hotel to enjoy its exquisite dining options and beautiful gardens. With a focus on personalized service and comfort, the Alphen is an excellent choice for travelers looking to experience the beauty of South Africa.

Amenities:

  • Fine dining with a focus on local produce
  • Beautiful gardens and outdoor spaces
  • Proximity to famous vineyards and hiking trails

8. The Pullman Hotel, Bangkok, Thailand

Embarking on train journeys through Thailand offers stunning views of lush landscapes and vibrant cities. The Pullman Hotel in Bangkok is a modern choice that provides quick access to the city’s train stations, including the Skytrain. After exploring the scenic routes of the countryside or taking a leisurely train ride along the river, guests can retreat to the hotel’s stylish accommodations. The Pullman offers panoramic views of the city skyline, making it an ideal place to unwind after a day of adventure.

Amenities:

  • Rooftop bar with stunning city views
  • Modern fitness center and pool
  • Easy access to public transport and attractions

9. The Hotel Danieli, Venice, Italy

For those who wish to experience the scenic train journey through Italy, the Hotel Danieli in Venice is a breathtaking option. Situated near the Santa Lucia train station, this luxurious hotel offers stunning views of the Grand Canal. After a day exploring the picturesque landscapes of Italy by train, return to the opulence of the Hotel Danieli. With its stunning architecture, exquisite dining options, and proximity to iconic attractions, this hotel provides a perfect blend of comfort and culture.

Amenities:

  • Rooftop dining with stunning canal views
  • Luxurious rooms with classic Venetian decor
  • Proximity to historic sites and cultural attractions

10. The Nines Hotel, Portland, Oregon, USA

For those journeying through the Pacific Northwest, The Nines Hotel in Portland is a perfect base for exploring scenic train routes. Located downtown, this luxury hotel offers stunning views of the city skyline and Mt. Hood. After a day spent traveling through the stunning landscapes of Oregon aboard the Amtrak Cascades, you can return to relax in the hotel’s chic accommodations. The Nines also boasts an array of dining options that celebrate local flavors, ensuring your culinary experience is as memorable as the views.

Amenities:

  • Roof-top bar with panoramic views
  • Local dining experiences that highlight Pacific Northwest cuisine
  • Proximity to Portland’s cultural attractions and parks

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel can significantly enhance your scenic train journey experience. From the luxurious accommodations of the Fairmont Banff Springs to the historic charm of the Gresham Hotel in Dublin, each of these hotels offers unique views and amenities that perfectly complement your travels. Whether you’re exploring the breathtaking landscapes of the Rockies, the picturesque countryside of Ireland, or the vibrant cities of Australia, these hotels provide the perfect base for your scenic adventures. So pack your bags, hop on a train, and get ready for a journey filled with stunning views and unforgettable experiences!
