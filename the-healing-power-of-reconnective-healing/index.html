The Healing Power of Reconnective Healing

What is Reconnective Healing?

Reconnective Healing is a form of energy healing that was developed by Dr. Eric Pearl. It is based on the idea that we are all connected to a vast field of energy and information that permeates the universe. This field, known as the Reconnective Healing spectrum, contains an infinite potential for healing and transformation.

Unlike other healing modalities, Reconnective Healing does not focus on specific symptoms or conditions. Instead, it aims to bring balance and harmony to the entire being - physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual. By accessing the Reconnective Healing spectrum, practitioners are able to facilitate a healing process that goes beyond the limitations of traditional methods.
