The Best Trails for Trail Running: Explore Nature on Foot


When it comes to exploring nature and staying active, trail running is the perfect way to combine these two passions. Trail running allows you to immerse yourself in natural surroundings while enjoying the physical and mental benefits of running. In this blog post, we will be diving into some of the best trails for trail running, where you can truly connect with nature on foot.

1. The Pacific Crest Trail:

Stretching over 2,650 miles from Mexico to Canada, the Pacific Crest Trail (PCT) offers trail runners an epic adventure through some of the most diverse and stunning landscapes in the United States. Whether you choose to tackle a small portion or complete the entire trail, you will be rewarded with breathtaking views of mountains, forests, and deserts. The PCT is a challenging trail that demands physical endurance and mental resilience, but it is an experience like no other.

2. The Appalachian Trail:

Another iconic trail in the United States, the Appalachian Trail spans over 2,180 miles across 14 states. This trail offers a wide range of experiences, from steep ascents through rocky terrain to gentle meanders through lush forests. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced trail runner, there are sections of the Appalachian Trail that will suit your skill level. Along the way, you will encounter stunning viewpoints, tranquil streams, and charming towns that add a touch of local culture to your journey.

3. The Great Ocean Walk:

For those looking for a trail running experience with a coastal twist, the Great Ocean Walk in Australia is a must-visit. Stretching over 63 miles along the stunning coastline of Victoria, this trail offers a mix of challenging terrain and breathtaking views of the Southern Ocean. As you run along the clifftops, you’ll be greeted with panoramic views of rugged cliffs, pristine beaches, and vibrant native wildlife. Don’t forget to take a break from running to explore the iconic Twelve Apostles, a collection of limestone stacks rising from the ocean.

4. The Inca Trail:

Elevate your trail running adventures with a historic and cultural experience on the Inca Trail in Peru. This 26-mile trail takes you through the heart of the Andes Mountains, leading to the magnificent ruins of Machu Picchu. The trail is not for the faint of heart, with steep ascents, high altitudes, and challenging terrain. However, the reward at the end is worth it all. As you run through the clouds, you’ll witness ancient ruins, lush cloud forests, and breathtaking views of the Andes.

5. The Zion National Park:

Located in southwestern Utah, Zion National Park offers trail runners a unique and awe-inspiring experience. With a variety of trails to choose from, you can explore the park’s stunning canyons, towering sandstone cliffs, and cascading waterfalls. One highly recommended trail is the Angel’s Landing, known for its challenging and exposed sections that reward you with panoramic views of the park. Running through Zion National Park will leave you feeling small in the presence of its majestic natural beauty.

6. The Mont Blanc Circuit:

For trail runners seeking a European adventure, the Mont Blanc Circuit is a dream come true. This 110-mile trail circumnavigates the iconic Mont Blanc massif, taking you through France, Italy, and Switzerland. You’ll be treated to breathtaking alpine scenery, picturesque villages, and challenging mountain passes. Running the Mont Blanc Circuit requires proper preparation and physical fitness, but the unique experience of trail running in the heart of the European Alps is truly unforgettable.

7. The Wild Pacific Trail:

Located on Vancouver Island, Canada, the Wild Pacific Trail is a hidden gem that combines coastal beauty with lush rainforests. This 7.5-mile trail hugs the rugged coastline, offering stunning views of the Pacific Ocean, rocky headlands, and ancient Cedar trees. As you run along the trail, keep your eyes peeled for whales, sea lions, and other marine wildlife. The combination of the wild ocean scenery and calming rainforest ambiance makes the Wild Pacific Trail a truly magical trail running experience.

Trail running allows you to escape the confines of the city and venture into nature’s playground. As you hit the trails, remember to stay safe, respect the environment, and enjoy the journey. With an array of stunning trails to choose from, you can explore the world on foot and experience nature in a way that few others have. So lace up your running shoes, hit the trails, and let nature guide your footsteps.
