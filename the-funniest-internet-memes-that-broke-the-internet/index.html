The Funniest Internet Memes That Broke the Internet


When it comes to internet culture, memes have become an integral part of our online experience. These humorous and often relatable images or videos have the power to bring people together, create viral sensations, and make us laugh uncontrollably. Over the years, the internet has witnessed numerous memes that broke the internet, capturing the attention of millions of people worldwide. In this blog, we’re going to delve into some of the funniest internet memes that have left a lasting impact on our online community.

1. ‘Rickrolling’:
One cannot talk about internet memes without mentioning the famous ‘Rickrolling.’ The prank involves redirecting someone to the music video of Rick Astley’s 1987 hit song, ‘Never Gonna Give You Up.’ This meme became so popular that it even reached the point of being one of the most-watched videos on YouTube. Its unpredictability and the catchy chorus have made it a classic in the meme world.

2. ‘Doge’:
The ‘Doge’ meme originated from a 2010 photograph of a Shiba Inu dog. The hilarious captions in Comic Sans font, often using broken English and phrases like ‘such wow’ and ‘very doge,’ turned this meme into a meme sensation. The simplicity and absurdity of the Doge meme made it widely shared and loved by people of all ages.

3. ‘Harambe’:
In 2016, Harambe, a gorilla living in the Cincinnati Zoo, tragically lost its life. This event sparked an unexpected internet phenomenon, with memes flooding social media platforms. While some criticized the trend for making light of a tragic incident, others saw it as a way to cope with the grief. Regardless, the Harambe meme became a symbol of pop culture and the power of internet humor.

4. ‘The Dress’:
‘The Dress’ meme took the internet by storm in 2015. A photograph of a dress that some people saw as gold and white while others saw as blue and black sparked a viral debate. This meme divided social media users, who passionately argued about the true colors of the dress. The viral nature of the debate made ‘The Dress’ one of the most talked-about memes of that year.

5. ‘Distracted Boyfriend’:
‘The Distracted Boyfriend’ meme is a stock photograph featuring a man checking out another woman while his girlfriend looks on disapprovingly. This meme quickly became a relatable representation of infidelity or disloyalty, with people creatively using it to depict various scenarios in an amusing way. The simplicity and versatility of this meme have made it go viral time and time again.

6. ‘Arthur Fist’:
The animated television show ‘Arthur’ introduced the world to the ‘Arthur Fist’ meme. The image of Arthur clenching his fist became a popular reaction image, symbolizing frustration or anger. This meme resonated with people from all walks of life, creating a viral sensation that still gets shared extensively today.

7. ‘I Can Has Cheezburger?’:
Perhaps one of the most iconic internet memes, ‘I Can Has Cheezburger?’ started with a picture of a cat and a caption written intentionally in broken English. This meme birthed countless variations, with users adding their humorous captions to images of cats or other animals. The simplicity and humor of this meme played a significant role in its widespread popularity.

8. ‘This Is Fine’:
Originally a webcomic by KC Green, the ‘This Is Fine’ meme depicts a dog sitting in a burning room, pretending that everything is okay. This meme resonated with people’s struggles to stay positive in difficult situations, making it relatable and hilarious. The ‘This Is Fine’ meme caught on like wildfire, quickly becoming a recognizable symbol of internet humor.

9. ‘Grumpy Cat’:
No list of internet memes would be complete without the inclusion of the beloved ‘Grumpy Cat.’ The sour-faced feline became an internet sensation with its relatable aura of disdain. ‘Grumpy Cat’ quickly became a cultural icon and a favorite among internet users looking to express their own cynical views.

10. ‘Expanding Brain’:
The ‘Expanding Brain’ meme gained popularity for its clever representation of a series of thoughts progressing into more profound and often absurd territory. This meme resonated with people who enjoy witty jokes and clever visual representations. The simplicity and versatility of the ‘Expanding Brain’ meme made it widely shared across various platforms.

In conclusion, internet memes have the power to bring laughter and joy to our lives. These humorous creations continue to break the internet and create viral sensations time and time again. From ‘Rickrolling’ to ‘Grumpy Cat,’ these memes have left an indelible mark on our online culture. Whether we need a good laugh or a pick-me-up, internet memes never fail to deliver. So, the next time you come across a meme that cracks you up, remember that you are not alone, as millions of others are laughing along with you.
