How to Achieve the Perfect Beach Waves Hairstyle


Are you dreaming of effortlessly chic and tousled waves that perfectly capture the essence of a day at the beach? Beach waves have become a timeless hairstyle that is beloved by many for its ability to add texture, volume, and an overall laid-back vibe to any look. Whether you have short hair or long locks, achieving the perfect beach waves is easier than you may think. In this article, we’ll guide you through the steps to achieve this coveted hairstyle and provide you with some helpful tips along the way.

Step 1: Prep and Protect

Before starting the styling process, it’s essential to prepare your hair for heat styling to avoid damage. Begin by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body. After towel drying, apply a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from the heat of styling tools. This step is crucial as it helps prevent breakage and keeps your hair healthy.

Step 2: Create the Foundation

To achieve the perfect beach waves, it’s vital to start with a solid foundation. If your hair is naturally straight or lacks texture, consider applying a lightweight mousse or texturizing spray to add grip and hold. For those with naturally wavy or curly hair, you can proceed to the next step after applying a small amount of styling product to tame frizz.

Step 3: Choose Your Styling Tool

When it comes to creating beach waves, you have a few different options for styling tools. The most popular choices are either a curling wand or a flat iron. A curling wand will give you loose, natural-looking waves, while a flat iron can create more defined and structured waves. Choose the tool that best suits your desired look.

Step 4: Section and Curl

Divide your hair into manageable sections, starting from the bottom layers and gradually working your way up. Take a small-to-medium-sized section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the curling wand or clamp your flat iron around the section of hair and twist as you slide down. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Repeat this process until all sections of hair have been curled.

Step 5: Texturize and Define

Once you have finished curling your hair, let it cool completely before touching it. Once cooled, using your fingers, gently run them through your hair to break up the curls and create that carefree beachy texture. If desired, you can then apply a small amount of texturizing spray or sea salt spray to enhance the waves and add additional hold.

Step 6: Set the Waves

To ensure that your beach waves last all day, it’s crucial to set them in place. Take a lightweight hairspray and evenly mist it over your hair, holding the can about 12 inches away. This will help to maintain the shape and volume of your waves while keeping them from falling flat.

Step 7: Add Finishing Touches

To complete your beach wave hairstyle, consider adding some optional finishing touches. You can use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently tease the roots of your hair for added lift and volume. You can also use a shine-enhancing serum for a glossy and polished look. Don’t forget to set your style with a final spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Bonus Tips:

  • For a more lived-in, bohemian look, consider braiding your hair loosely before going to bed and unraveling it in the morning. This will create natural-looking waves without the need for heat styling.
  • If your hair tends to hold curl well, you can skip the hairspray at the end to maintain a more effortless appearance.
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes or flat iron techniques to achieve varying wave patterns and textures.
  • Remember to adjust the heat setting on your styling tool according to your hair type to prevent unnecessary damage.

In conclusion, achieving the perfect beach waves hairstyle doesn’t have to be a daunting task. By following these simple steps and incorporating the bonus tips, you can effortlessly create this timeless and chic look that is sure to turn heads. Whether you’re headed to the beach or simply want to add some texture to your everyday style, beach waves are a versatile and highly sought-after hairstyle that anyone can achieve. So go ahead, embrace your inner beach babe, and rock those stunning waves with confidence!
