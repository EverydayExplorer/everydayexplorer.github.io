The Best Comedy Shows on TV Right Now for a Good Laugh


Are you in need of a good laugh? Look no further than these top comedy shows on TV right now that are guaranteed to have you rolling on the floor with laughter. From clever humor to hilarious characters, these shows are sure to brighten your day and keep you entertained for hours on end.

1. ‘Brooklyn Nine-Nine’
One of the most beloved comedy shows on TV right now, ‘Brooklyn Nine-Nine’ follows the misadventures of the detectives at the 99th precinct of the NYPD. The witty banter between characters, zany plotlines, and heartwarming moments make this show a must-watch for anyone in need of a good laugh.

2. ‘The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel’
Set in 1950s New York City, ‘The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel’ follows the journey of Miriam ‘Midge’ Maisel as she discovers her talent for stand-up comedy. The show is not only hilarious but also offers a glimpse into the world of comedy during a time of significant social change.

3. ‘Schitt’s Creek’
‘Schitt’s Creek’ has quickly become a fan favorite for its quirky humor and lovable characters. The show follows the wealthy Rose family as they adjust to life in a small town after losing their fortune. With a stellar cast and sharp writing, ‘Schitt’s Creek’ is a comedy masterpiece that will leave you in stitches.

4. ‘The Good Place’
‘The Good Place’ is a unique comedy that follows Eleanor Shellstrop as she navigates the afterlife. With its clever jokes, philosophical themes, and surprising plot twists, this show is unlike anything else on TV right now. The talented cast, led by Kristen Bell and Ted Danson, brings the witty script to life and keeps viewers laughing until the very end.

5. ‘Ted Lasso’
‘Ted Lasso’ follows the titular character, a small-time college football coach from Kansas who is hired to coach a professional soccer team in England. The show blends humor, heart, and sports in a way that is both refreshing and uplifting. Jason Sudeikis shines as the endlessly optimistic Ted Lasso, bringing a warmth and charm to the role that is simply irresistible.

6. ‘What We Do in the Shadows’
Based on the mockumentary film of the same name, ‘What We Do in the Shadows’ follows a group of vampires living together in Staten Island. The show is a hilarious blend of supernatural comedy and dry wit, with each character bringing their own unique brand of humor to the table. If you’re a fan of offbeat comedy, this show is a must-watch.

7. ‘The Great’
‘The Great’ is a historical comedy-drama that follows the rise of Catherine the Great, Empress of Russia. The show expertly blends humor with drama, creating a compelling and entertaining story that keeps viewers coming back for more. Elle Fanning and Nicholas Hoult deliver standout performances, bringing depth and humor to their respective roles.

8. ‘It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia’
‘It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia’ has been a staple of TV comedy for over a decade, and for good reason. The show follows the antics of the self-absorbed owners of Paddy’s Pub as they get into all sorts of ridiculous schemes and misadventures. With its dark humor and outrageous plotlines, ‘It’s Always Sunny’ is a comedy show that is not afraid to push boundaries and take risks.

In conclusion, these top comedy shows on TV right now are guaranteed to bring a smile to your face and a laugh to your lips. Whether you’re in the mood for witty banter, zany humor, or heartwarming moments, there is something for everyone on this list. So grab some popcorn, settle in on the couch, and get ready to laugh until your sides hurt.
