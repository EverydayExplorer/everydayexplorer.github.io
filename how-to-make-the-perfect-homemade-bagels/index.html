How to Make the Perfect Homemade Bagels


Do you love the chewy, flavorful goodness of a freshly baked bagel? Are you tired of spending a fortune at your local bakery for that perfect bite? Look no further, because today we are going to dive into the art of making the perfect homemade bagels. Get ready to impress your friends and family with your baking skills!

Making bagels from scratch may seem intimidating, but with a little bit of practice and patience, you can achieve bakery-quality results right in your own kitchen. So, let’s roll up our sleeves, gather our ingredients, and get started!

The first step is to gather the necessary ingredients. You will need:

- 4 cups of bread flour
- 2 teaspoons of instant yeast
- 2 teaspoons of salt
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- 1 and ½ cups of warm water
- Optional toppings such as sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or everything bagel seasoning

Once you have your ingredients ready, it’s time to mix and knead the dough. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, salt, and sugar. Gradually add the warm water while stirring the mixture. Once the dough starts to come together, transfer it to a clean, floured surface.

Knead the dough for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough feels too sticky, you can add a little bit of flour, but be careful not to add too much. The dough should be slightly tacky but not overly sticky.

Next, shape the dough into a ball and place it in a greased bowl. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let the dough rise for about 1 to 1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size. This step is crucial for achieving that light and airy texture in your bagels.

After the dough has risen, it’s time to shape the bagels. Punch down the dough to release any air bubbles, then divide it into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a ball, then use your thumb to poke a hole through the center. Gently stretch the hole to about 1.5 times the size of the bagel. Repeat with the remaining dough.

Once you have shaped all the bagels, place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover the sheet with a damp cloth and let the bagels rise for another 15-20 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

While the oven is preheating, bring a large pot of water to a boil. Once the water is boiling, carefully drop the bagels, a few at a time, into the pot. Boil them for about 1-2 minutes on each side, then remove them using a slotted spoon and place them back on the baking sheet.

Now comes the fun part - adding your favorite toppings! Sprinkle sesame seeds, poppy seeds, or everything bagel seasoning on top of the bagels. Be generous with the toppings as they add extra flavor and texture.

Finally, it’s time to bake the bagels. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until the bagels turn golden brown. Keep a close eye on them to prevent over-baking.

Once the bagels are done, remove them from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack. It might be tempting to dive right in, but allowing them to cool will ensure that they retain their perfect texture.

Congratulations! You have just made your own batch of delicious homemade bagels. Now, all that’s left to do is to spread your favorite cream cheese or toppings and enjoy your creation. Whether you prefer classic flavors or want to experiment with unique combinations, the possibilities are endless.

Making your own bagels allows you to customize flavors, control the ingredients, and most importantly, experience the joy of baking. So, why not give it a try? With a little practice, you’ll be a bagel-baking pro in no time. Happy baking!
