DIY Wedding Favors Your Guests Will Love


Are you planning a wedding and looking for unique ways to show your appreciation to your guests? DIY wedding favors are a great way to add a personal touch to your special day while also creating memorable keepsakes for your loved ones. Not only are DIY wedding favors cost-effective, but they also give you the opportunity to get creative and showcase your personality as a couple. In this blog post, we will explore some DIY wedding favor ideas that your guests will surely love.

  1. Personalized Mini Succulents: Succulents are not only trendy but also low-maintenance plants. Consider creating personalized mini succulents as wedding favors. You can purchase small succulents, wrap them in burlap, and tie them with a ribbon. Attach a custom tag with your names and wedding date for an added personal touch. Succulents make for great wedding favors as they can be easily taken home and planted, serving as a beautiful reminder of your special day.

  2. Homemade Soap: Treat your guests to a luxurious and personalized gift with homemade soap. Making soap at home is easier than you think. You can choose from a variety of scents and colors to match your wedding theme. Package the soaps in individual bags or boxes, and don’t forget to include a heartfelt thank you note. Homemade soap is not only a practical wedding favor but also a thoughtful one.

  3. Customized Seed Packets: If you and your partner love gardening, customized seed packets are a great wedding favor option. Choose a variety of flower or herb seeds that represent your love story and package them in personalized packets. Include instructions on how to plant the seeds and watch your love bloom, just like the seeds will. Your guests will appreciate this eco-friendly and meaningful gift.

  4. Delicious Homemade Jam: Share the sweetness of your love with your guests by gifting them homemade jam. Whether it’s strawberry, blueberry, or even a unique flavor combination, homemade jam is always a crowd-pleaser. Package the jam in small jars, and consider adding a custom label with your names and wedding date. Your guests will thank you every time they enjoy a delicious spread of jam on their toast.

  5. Personalized Coasters: Coasters are practical wedding favors that your guests can use daily. Get creative by designing custom coasters that reflect your wedding theme or incorporate your names and wedding date. You can choose to use materials like ceramic, wood, or cork, depending on your preference. Coasters are not only functional but also make for great keepsakes.

  6. Gourmet Infused Olive Oil: Impress your guests with a gourmet DIY wedding favor like infused olive oil. Purchase high-quality olive oil and infuse it with flavors like garlic, chili pepper, or rosemary. Decorate the bottles with personalized labels and ribbons. Your guests will appreciate the effort and enjoy the delicious flavors.

When it comes to DIY wedding favors, the options are endless. Whether you decide to create succulent planters, homemade soap, customized seed packets, homemade jam, personalized coasters, or gourmet infused olive oil, your guests will love the thought and effort that goes into these personalized gifts. Remember to showcase your creativity and personality, and your wedding favors will definitely be a hit. Make sure to prepare these favors well in advance to avoid any last-minute rush. Happy crafting and congratulations on your upcoming wedding!
