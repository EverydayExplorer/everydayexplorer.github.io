Comedy Podcasts: Hilarious Shows Worth Binge-Listening


Welcome, comedy enthusiasts! Prepare yourselves for a laughter-filled adventure as we dive into the world of comedy podcasts. In a fast-paced world where stress and anxiety seem to be ever-present, podcasts provide the perfect escape, injecting doses of laughter and humor into our lives. Today, we bring you a handpicked selection of comedy podcasts that are worth binge-listening to. Get ready to laugh until your sides hurt!

1. ‘The Comedy Bang! Bang! Podcast’: Hosted by the hilarious Scott Aukerman, this show features a mix of interviews, improvised character sketches, and wacky conversations. With a long list of celebrity guests, including comedians, actors, and musicians, ‘The Comedy Bang! Bang! Podcast’ is a guaranteed laugh riot that will leave you in stitches.

2. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend’: We all know Conan O’Brien as the master of late-night comedy, but did you know he also has a side gig as a podcast host? In this show, Conan invites his celebrity friends for candid, entertaining conversations. From heartfelt insights to hilarious anecdotes, this podcast is a delightful glimpse into the world of comedy.

3. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’: Brace yourselves for some uncontrollable laughter with this one! Jamie Morton reads out chapters from his dad’s self-published erotic novel, while his friends James Cooper and Alice Levine provide hilarious commentary. The result? A wonderfully bizarre and side-splittingly funny podcast that will keep you entertained for hours on end.

4. ‘The Dollop’: If you’re a fan of history and comedy, then ‘The Dollop’ is the perfect podcast for you. Hosts Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds take a deep dive into bizarre and often forgotten stories from the annals of history. With a healthy dose of humor, they bring these incredible tales to life, making history incredibly entertaining.

5. ‘How Did This Get Made?’: Ever found yourself watching a terrible movie and wondering, ‘How on earth did this get made?’ Well, wonder no more! Comedians Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas dissect the most absurd and baffling movies in their quest to understand the ‘how’ and ‘why’ behind them. Get ready for a hilarious journey through the worst of cinema!

6. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’: Joe Rogan’s podcast is a cultural phenomenon, covering a wide range of topics from comedy and science to politics and philosophy. While not solely a comedy podcast, Joe’s humorous insights and conversations with his celebrity guests, including comedians like Bill Burr and Tom Segura, will have you laughing out loud.

7. ‘The Misfits Podcast’: For those who crave raw and unfiltered humor, ‘The Misfits Podcast’ is a must-listen. Hosted by a group of YouTubers and former members of the gaming community, this podcast is a wild ride full of outrageous stories, irreverent banter, and dark humor that pushes the boundaries.

So there you have it, folks! These comedy podcasts are guaranteed to brighten your day and leave you in fits of laughter. Whether you’re a fan of celebrity interviews, hilarious storytelling, or simply want to explore the lighter side of life, these shows have got you covered. So put on your headphones, sit back, and prepare to laugh your heart out with these hilarious comedy podcasts. Happy listening!
