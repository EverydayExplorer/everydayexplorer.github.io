The best hotel bars to check out


Welcome to our blog post all about the best hotel bars to check out! If you’re a fan of mixing travel and the art of mixology, you’ve come to the right place. Hotel bars are often hidden gems that offer unique experiences, delicious drinks, and stunning views. In this article, we will take you on a virtual journey to some of the most extraordinary hotel bars around the world. So, hop on board and let’s explore!

1. Sky Bar at Lebua State Tower, Bangkok, Thailand:
Perched on the 63rd floor of the Lebua State Tower, the Sky Bar offers breathtaking panoramic views of the vibrant city of Bangkok. Sip on their signature cocktail, the “Hangovertini,” and enjoy the luxurious atmosphere. This bar gained international fame after it was featured in the movie “The Hangover Part II.”

2. The Bar Hemingway at Ritz Paris, France:
Step into the timeless elegance of The Bar Hemingway at Ritz Paris, where Ernest Hemingway himself used to frequent. This legendary bar boasts a rich history and a classic ambiance. Indulge in their famous “Serendipity” cocktail, crafted with a blend of apple liqueur, calvados, and champagne.

3. The Broken Shaker at Freehand, Miami, USA:
For a more laid-back and hip vibe, head to The Broken Shaker in Miami. This bar offers a fantastic selection of handcrafted cocktails using fresh and exotic ingredients. The tropical garden setting and eclectic decor create a relaxed and vibrant atmosphere that perfectly captures the essence of Miami.

4. The Long Bar at Raffles Hotel, Singapore:
A trip to Singapore wouldn’t be complete without a visit to The Long Bar at Raffles Hotel. This iconic bar is famous for inventing the classic cocktail, the “Singapore Sling.” Immerse yourself in the colonial-era ambiance while sipping on this delightful concoction.

5. The Rock Bar at Ayana Resort and Spa, Bali, Indonesia:
Located atop natural rock formations and surrounded by the Indian Ocean, The Rock Bar is a truly mesmerizing spot. This open-air bar allows you to witness breathtaking sunsets while enjoying creative cocktails. Feel the ocean breeze as you sip on their famous “Rock My World” cocktail, a refreshing blend of vodka, tropical fruits, and spices.

6. The American Bar at The Savoy, London, UK:
Steeped in history and elegance, The American Bar at The Savoy is a must-visit for cocktail enthusiasts. This bar has been a favorite among many notable figures, including Frank Sinatra and Marilyn Monroe. Indulge in their award-winning classic cocktails and relish the stylish surroundings.

7. The Bar at Baccarat Hotel, New York City, USA:
The Bar at Baccarat Hotel in New York City exudes opulence and sophistication. Adorned with stunning crystal chandeliers and a red-lacquered bar, this establishment offers a luxurious experience. Savor their meticulously crafted cocktails while surrounded by a world of crystal and elegance.

8. The Bar at Hotel Costes, Paris, France:
For a taste of Parisian chic, head to The Bar at Hotel Costes. This trendy and glamorous venue is known for its fashionable crowd and stylish decor. Sip on their signature “Costes Sour” cocktail and enjoy the lively atmosphere that embodies the essence of the French capital.

9. The Bar at Burj Al Arab, Dubai, UAE:
Situated on the 27th floor of the iconic Burj Al Arab, the world’s only seven-star hotel, The Bar offers stunning views of Dubai’s skyline. This luxurious venue serves an array of signature cocktails in an elegant and glamorous setting. Enjoy the breathtaking views while sipping on a perfectly crafted drink.

10. The Carousel Bar at Hotel Monteleone, New Orleans, USA:
Last but not least, we can’t forget to mention The Carousel Bar in the heart of New Orleans. This unique bar features an actual carousel that slowly rotates as you enjoy your drink. Immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere of this famous jazz city while indulging in classic cocktails with a twist.

We hope this virtual journey to the best hotel bars around the world has ignited your wanderlust and appetite for exceptional experiences. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or an armchair explorer, these bars offer an extraordinary blend of luxury, ambiance, and mixology. Cheers to unforgettable moments and unforgettable drinks!

Disclaimer: Remember to check the availability and safety regulations of these establishments before planning your visit. Enjoy responsibly!
