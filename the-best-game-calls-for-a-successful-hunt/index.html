The Best Game Calls for A Successful Hunt


When it comes to hunting, having the right game call can make all the difference. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, using a game call can greatly increase your chances of a successful hunt. But with so many options on the market, how do you know which one is the best for you? In this blog post, we will explore the top game calls for a successful hunt, taking into consideration factors such as ease of use, versatility, and overall effectiveness.

1. Primos Hunting 302 Turbo Dogg Electronic Predator Call
The Primos Hunting 302 Turbo Dogg Electronic Predator Call is a top pick amongst hunters for its innovative features and realistic sounds. This electronic predator call offers 36 different sounds, including coyote vocals, prey distress calls, and even different species of birds. With a remote control that has a range of up to 150 yards, hunters can easily manipulate the sounds without alerting their target. The high-quality speakers ensure that the sounds carry far, increasing the chances of luring in the desired game. The Turbo Dogg also features a superior battery life, lasting up to six hours on just four AA batteries.

2. Flextone Buck Collector Plus Deer Call
If you’re looking for a versatile deer call, the Flextone Buck Collector Plus Deer Call is a great option. This call is designed to mimic realistic deer vocalizations, such as grunts, bleats, and snort-wheezes, allowing hunters to attract deer from all stages of the rut. The Flextone Buck Collector Plus is made with a blend of hard and soft materials, which creates the perfect balance of sound and realism. The call is also easy to use, with a compact design that fits comfortably in the hand. This call is a must-have for any deer hunter looking to improve their chances of a successful hunt.

3. Duck Commander Triple Threat Duck Call
When it comes to waterfowl hunting, the Duck Commander Triple Threat Duck Call is a go-to choice for many hunters. This call produces a wide range of sounds, including loud hail calls, soft quacks, and realistic feeding chatter. The Triple Threat is made of high-impact plastic, which ensures durability and longevity. With its easy-to-use design, hunters can quickly switch between different sounds and tones to replicate authentic duck vocalizations. This call is specifically designed to attract a variety of duck species, making it a valuable tool for any waterfowl hunting expedition.

4. FOXPRO Shockwave Electronic Game Call
The FOXPRO Shockwave Electronic Game Call is a top-of-the-line predator call that offers unmatched versatility and performance. This call features a massive library of over 500 high-quality sounds, covering a wide range of predator species and scenarios. The included remote control has a full-color display, making it easy to navigate through the extensive sound library. With its powerful speakers and ability to play multiple sounds simultaneously, the Shockwave can create a realistic predator scenario that will lure in even the most wary of predators. The call also comes with rechargeable batteries, ensuring that you never have to worry about running out of power during a hunt.

5. Quaker Boy Hurricane Box Turkey Call
For turkey hunters, the Quaker Boy Hurricane Box Turkey Call is a top choice. This call is designed to mimic the authentic sounds of a wild turkey, including gobbles, purrs, and yelps. The Hurricane Box features a waterproof design, making it the perfect choice for hunting in wet conditions. The call is easy to use, producing crisp and clear tones that will attract even the most cautious of toms. With its durable construction, this call is built to last and will provide many successful hunts for years to come.

In conclusion, having a high-quality game call can greatly improve your chances of a successful hunt. The Primos Hunting 302 Turbo Dogg, Flextone Buck Collector Plus, Duck Commander Triple Threat, FOXPRO Shockwave, and Quaker Boy Hurricane Box are all excellent options for hunters looking to up their game. Consider the type of hunting you plan to do and choose a call that offers the right sounds and features for your needs. Happy hunting!
