DIY Summer Crafts: Fun Activities for Sunny Days


Summer is the perfect time to unleash your creativity and engage in fun and exciting DIY crafts. Whether you’re looking to entertain your kids, add a personal touch to your home, or simply enjoy some relaxing and fulfilling activities, these DIY summer crafts are sure to brighten up your sunny days. So grab your supplies and let’s get started!

1. Beach-themed Mason Jar Lanterns

Bring the beach vibes into your home with these adorable beach-themed mason jar lanterns. Start by painting the outside of the mason jar with a light blue or turquoise color to resemble the ocean. Then, glue some seashells, starfish, and small pieces of driftwood onto the jar. Once the glue dries, place a tealight candle inside the jar and watch as the soft glow illuminates your space.

2. Watermelon Slice Doormat

Give your front porch a refreshing summer makeover with a watermelon slice doormat. Start by cutting a semi-circle shape out of a plain outdoor doormat. Then, paint the remaining area with bright green paint to resemble the rind of a watermelon. Once the paint is dry, use red fabric paint to create the juicy watermelon flesh. Add black seeds using a permanent marker or black paint. Your guests will be delighted by this vibrant and playful entrance!

3. Ice Cream Cone Garland

Add a touch of sweetness to any party or gathering with an ice cream cone garland. Cut out ice cream cone shapes from colored cardstock or construction paper. Decorate each cone with different flavors, toppings, and sprinkles using markers or colored pencils. Punch holes in the top of each cone and string them together with twine or ribbon. Hang the garland across your patio or indoor space for a delightful and whimsical decoration.

4. Sunflower Wreath

Brighten up your front door with a beautiful sunflower wreath that captures the essence of summer. Start by wrapping a foam wreath form with burlap or ribbon. Then, use hot glue to attach artificial sunflowers, leaves, and other complementary flowers. Add a bow or ribbon for an extra touch of charm. Hang your sunflower wreath with pride and watch as it welcomes guests with its cheerful and sunny disposition.

5. Citrus Fruit Coasters

Protect your tables and add a pop of color with these citrus fruit coasters. Cut out round shapes from cork or felt to serve as the base of your coasters. Then, paint them with vibrant colors to resemble slices of citrus fruits like oranges, lemons, and limes. Add details such as seeds or zest using a fine-tip permanent marker or paintbrush. Not only will these coasters keep your surfaces safe, but they will also add a fresh and summery vibe to your home.

Conclusion

Summer is the season of endless possibilities, and these DIY summer crafts are just the tip of the iceberg. Let your imagination run wild and explore various crafts that bring joy and creativity into your life. Whether you’re crafting alone or with loved ones, these activities are sure to make your summer days even more enjoyable. So grab your supplies, soak up the sun, and let your inner artist shine!
