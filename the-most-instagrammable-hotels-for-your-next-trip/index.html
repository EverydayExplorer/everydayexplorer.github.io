The Most Instagrammable Hotels for Your Next Trip


Are you tired of scrolling through your Instagram feed, filled with envy-inducing photos of stunning hotels from around the world? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we will take you on a virtual tour of the most Instagrammable hotels that are guaranteed to make your followers green with envy. From breathtaking views to unique and stylish interiors, these hotels are the perfect backdrop for your next vacation photoshoot.

1. The Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia
Nestled in the heart of the lush rainforest, The Hanging Gardens of Bali offers a truly magical experience. With its iconic twin-tiered infinity pools that seem to float above the treetops, this hotel is a photographer’s dream. Capture the perfect shot as you relax in the pool, surrounded by the natural beauty of Bali.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya
At Giraffe Manor, you can sip your morning coffee while being greeted by friendly giraffes poking their heads through the windows. This unique hotel allows you to get up close and personal with these beautiful creatures. Imagine the Instagram-worthy moments as you pose with a giraffe as your backdrop!

3. The Icehotel, Sweden
If you’re looking for an extraordinary experience, head to The Icehotel in Sweden. This hotel is entirely made of ice and snow, offering a one-of-a-kind stay. Imagine the jaw-dropping photos you can take as you explore the ice sculptures and sleep in a bed made of ice. Just make sure to bundle up!

4. The One & Only Reethi Rah, Maldives
The Maldives is known for its picture-perfect beaches, and The One & Only Reethi Rah takes it to the next level. With its stunning overwater villas and crystal-clear turquoise waters, this hotel offers a slice of paradise. Capture the beauty of the Maldives as you lounge in your private infinity pool or walk along the pristine white sand beaches.

5. The Silo Hotel, South Africa
Located in Cape Town, The Silo Hotel is a masterpiece of design and luxury. Situated in a converted grain silo, this hotel boasts incredible panoramic views of Table Mountain and the city skyline. The bold and vibrant interiors provide the perfect backdrop for your Instagram photos, making every corner a potential photoshoot spot.

6. The Caves, Jamaica
For a truly unique experience, head to The Caves in Jamaica. This boutique hotel is built into the cliffs of Negril and offers breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea. Each room is individually designed, creating a vibrant and eclectic atmosphere. Capture the magic of the Caribbean as you relax in a private cave or take a dip in the turquoise waters.

7. The Manta Resort, Zanzibar
If you’ve ever dreamed of sleeping underwater, The Manta Resort in Zanzibar can make that dream come true. Their underwater room offers a once-in-a-lifetime experience, surrounded by the vibrant marine life of the Indian Ocean. Imagine the envy-inducing photos you can take as you wake up to the sights of colorful fish swimming by your window.

These Instagrammable hotels offer more than just a place to stay - they provide unforgettable experiences and stunning backdrops for your photos. Whether you’re seeking adventure, luxury, or a unique stay, these hotels will ensure that your Instagram feed is filled with envy-inducing content. So pack your bags, charge your camera, and get ready for a trip of a lifetime! Happy Instagramming!
