7 Must-Try Red Wines Under 0


When it comes to enjoying a good bottle of wine, you don’t always have to break the bank. There are plenty of delicious red wines out there that are budget-friendly and can be enjoyed without burning a hole in your wallet. In fact, you can find some great red wines for under $20 that rival more expensive options. Whether you are a seasoned wine connoisseur or just starting to explore the world of red wines, here are seven must-try red wines that are not only delicious but also budget-friendly.

  1. Columbia Crest H3 Merlot - Columbia Crest is known for producing high-quality wines at affordable prices, and their H3 Merlot is no exception. This Washington State Merlot is rich and velvety with flavors of black cherry, plum, and a hint of spice. It pairs well with a variety of dishes and is perfect for sipping on its own.

  2. Bogle Vineyards Old Vine Zinfandel - Bogle Vineyards is a family-owned winery in California that has been producing exceptional wines for generations. Their Old Vine Zinfandel is a crowd-pleaser with its bold flavors of blackberry, vanilla, and a touch of pepper. This wine is perfect for BBQs and hearty meals.

  3. Montepulciano d’Abruzzo - Montepulciano d’Abruzzo is a versatile Italian red wine that is both affordable and delicious. This wine is known for its bold fruit flavors, soft tannins, and earthy undertones. It pairs well with a variety of Italian dishes, making it a great choice for a weeknight dinner.

  4. Santa Julia Malbec - Malbec is a popular red wine varietal known for its dark fruit flavors and smooth tannins. Santa Julia Malbec from Argentina is no exception, with its notes of plum, blackberry, and a hint of mocha. This wine is perfect for pairing with grilled meats or enjoying on its own.

  5. Ravenswood Vintner’s Blend Cabernet Sauvignon - Ravenswood is a well-known California winery that specializes in Zinfandel, but their Vintner’s Blend Cabernet Sauvignon is also worth trying. This wine is full-bodied with flavors of black cherry, cassis, and a touch of oak. It pairs well with grilled steaks and hearty stews.

  6. Cono Sur Bicicleta Pinot Noir - Pinot Noir is a beloved red wine varietal known for its delicate flavors and silky texture. Cono Sur Bicicleta Pinot Noir from Chile is an excellent value wine with notes of cherry, raspberry, and a hint of earthiness. This wine is perfect for pairing with roasted chicken or salmon.

  7. Fleurie Beaujolais - Beaujolais is a region in France known for producing light and fruity red wines from the Gamay grape. Fleurie Beaujolais is a great example of this style, with its flavors of red berries, violet, and a hint of spice. This wine is perfect for serving slightly chilled and pairs well with charcuterie or cheese.

In conclusion, you don’t have to spend a fortune to enjoy a good bottle of red wine. These seven must-try red wines under $20 offer exceptional quality and value, making them perfect for any occasion. Whether you prefer bold and robust wines or light and fruity options, there is something for everyone on this list. So the next time you’re in the mood for a glass of red wine, don’t hesitate to try one of these budget-friendly options. Cheers!
