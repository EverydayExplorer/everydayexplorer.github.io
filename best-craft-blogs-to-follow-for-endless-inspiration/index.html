Best Craft Blogs to Follow for Endless Inspiration


Crafting is a beloved hobby that allows people to unleash their creativity and create beautiful handmade items. Whether you’re into knitting, sewing, woodworking, or any other craft, it’s always great to have some inspiration to fuel your creative fire. That’s where craft blogs come in!

Craft blogs are filled with endless inspiration, tips, tutorials, and ideas that can help take your crafting skills to the next level. From DIY projects to expert advice, these blogs are a treasure trove for craft enthusiasts. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best craft blogs to follow for endless inspiration.

1. ‘A Beautiful Mess’ - This popular blog covers a wide range of crafts, from home decor to fashion and everything in between. With beautiful photography and easy-to-follow tutorials, this blog is a must-follow for any craft lover.

2. ‘Crafts by Amanda’ - Amanda’s blog is filled with fun and creative craft ideas for kids and adults alike. Whether you’re looking for seasonal crafts or simple DIY projects, this blog has got you covered.

3. ‘Craft Gossip’ - If you’re looking for a blog that covers a wide range of crafts, then Craft Gossip is the one for you. From knitting and sewing to paper crafts and jewelry making, this blog features a plethora of craft ideas from various sources around the web.

4. ‘Crafty Gemini’ - This blog, run by Vanessa Wilson, is dedicated to sewing and quilting. With step-by-step video tutorials, patterns, and tips, Crafty Gemini is a go-to resource for anyone interested in these crafts.

5. ‘The Craftaholic Witch’ - This blog is a treasure trove of Halloween and spooky-themed crafts. From home decor to costume ideas, The Craftaholic Witch has a wide range of unique and creative projects to inspire you.

6. ‘Damask Love’ - Run by Amber Kemp-Gerstel, this blog is all about ‘making things that matter.’ Whether it’s quick DIY projects or intricate paper crafts, Damask Love has something for everyone.

7. ‘Lia Griffith’ - If you’re into paper crafting, then Lia Griffith’s blog is a must-follow. With stunning paper flower tutorials, party decor ideas, and more, this blog will leave you amazed at what you can create with paper.

8. ‘Jen Woodhouse’ - If you’re a fan of woodworking, then Jen Woodhouse’s blog is a must-follow. With detailed tutorials, plans, and tips, this blog will help you get started on your woodworking journey.

9. ‘Crafts Unleashed’ - This blog by ConsumerCrafts is a one-stop-shop for all your crafting needs. From seasonal crafts to home decor and party ideas, Crafts Unleashed has it all.

10. ‘Craftberry Bush’ - This blog is a beautiful mix of crafts, home decor, and gardening. With stunning photography and creative ideas, Craftberry Bush will leave you inspired to create beautiful things.

In conclusion, these craft blogs are the cream of the crop, offering endless inspiration and creative ideas. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced crafter, following these blogs will surely ignite your creative passion. So, grab your crafting supplies and start exploring the wonderful world of crafts with these amazing blogs. Happy crafting!
