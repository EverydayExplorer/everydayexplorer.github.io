Hotel Room with a View: Where to Find the Best Scenic Stays


Picture this: you wake up to the soft rays of sunlight streaming through your window, casting a warm glow on your face. As you slowly open your eyes, you’re greeted by a breathtaking panoramic view that takes your breath away. The world outside seems to come alive as you soak in the beauty of nature, cityscapes, or stunning landscapes. A hotel room with a view is a dream come true for any traveler seeking an unforgettable experience.

In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best scenic stays around the world, where you can indulge in the luxury of waking up to a stunning vista. From remote hideaways nestled in the heart of nature to urban retreats with city skylines at your fingertips, these hotels offer a front-row seat to some of the most awe-inspiring sights on the planet.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA
Nestled in the heart of the desert, Amangiri boasts uninterrupted views of the dramatic landscape of Utah. From your room, you can marvel at the rugged beauty of the canyons, mesas, and plateaus that stretch as far as the eye can see. As night falls, the starry sky above will leave you mesmerized.

2. Fogo Island Inn, Newfoundland, Canada
Perched on the rocky shores of Fogo Island, this contemporary inn offers panoramic views of the North Atlantic Ocean. From your room, you can watch icebergs float by or catch a glimpse of whales breaching in the distance. The ever-changing seascape is a sight that will leave you in awe.

3. Anantara Al Jabal Al Akhdar Resort, Oman
Located on the edge of a dramatic canyon in Oman, this resort offers breathtaking views of the rugged mountains and deep gorges below. Wake up to the sight of the sunrise painting the sky with hues of orange and pink, and spend your days exploring the natural wonders that surround you.

4. Hotel Ranga, Iceland
Imagine gazing out of your window at the dancing Northern Lights illuminating the night sky. At Hotel Ranga, you can experience this magical spectacle from the comfort of your room. With panoramic views of the Icelandic countryside, including volcanoes, glaciers, and rivers, this hotel is a nature lover’s paradise.

5. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore
For those who prefer an urban landscape, Marina Bay Sands offers unparalleled views of the Singapore skyline. Perched atop three soaring towers, the hotel’s infinity pool is an architectural marvel that provides a breathtaking backdrop for your stay. Take in the glittering lights of the city as you relax and unwind.

These are just a few examples of the countless hotels around the world that offer rooms with a view. Whether you’re seeking tranquility in nature or the vibrant energy of a bustling city, there is a scenic stay out there that will fulfill your wildest dreams.

So, the next time you plan your getaway, consider booking a hotel room with a view. Immerse yourself in the beauty of your surroundings and create memories that will last a lifetime. A scenic stay is not just a place to rest your head; it’s an experience that will leave you feeling inspired and connected to the world around you.
