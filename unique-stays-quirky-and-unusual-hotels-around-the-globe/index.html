Unique Stays: Quirky and Unusual Hotels Around the Globe


In the realm of travel, accommodations have evolved far beyond the conventional hotel room. Today, travelers seek experiences that are as memorable as the destinations they visit, and unique stays have emerged as the answer. These quirky and unusual hotels offer guests the chance to immerse themselves in local culture, embrace the unexpected, and create lasting memories.

1. The Dog Bark Park Inn, Idaho, USA

Nestled in the charming town of Cottonwood, Idaho, The Dog Bark Park Inn is a canine-themed paradise. The hotel’s two suites are housed within a pair of giant beagle sculptures, complete with wagging tails and barking sounds. Guests can enjoy a cozy stay surrounded by dog-themed artwork and amenities, including a ‘Hydrant Bar’ and a ‘Doggone Good Time’ room.

2. The Icehotel, Sweden

For a truly Arctic experience, head to the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden. This ephemeral hotel is rebuilt every winter from blocks of ice harvested from the frozen Torne River. Guests can choose from a range of ice rooms, each adorned with intricate ice sculptures. The hotel also features an ice bar, a chapel, and a sauna, offering a unique blend of adventure and relaxation.

3. The Giraffe Manor, Kenya

Escape to the heart of the African savannah at Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya. This elegant boutique hotel allows guests to interact with friendly giraffes that roam the lush grounds. Enjoy breakfast on the veranda as giraffes peek their heads through the windows, and observe these majestic creatures up close during guided tours.

4. The Hotel Marques de Riscal, Spain

Designed by renowned architect Frank Gehry, the Hotel Marques de Riscal in Elciego, Spain, is a masterpiece of modern architecture. The hotel’s undulating titanium façade resembles a flowing ribbon, creating a striking contrast against the surrounding vineyards. Inside, guests can indulge in luxurious accommodations, a world-class spa, and a Michelin-starred restaurant.

5. The Treehotel, Sweden

Perched high among the pines in the remote forests of Harads, Sweden, the Treehotel offers a unique perspective on nature. Guests can choose from a variety of treehouses, each with its own distinct design. From the Mirrorcube, which reflects the surrounding forest, to the Bird’s Nest, suspended among the branches, these treehouses provide an unforgettable escape amidst the natural beauty of Sweden.

6. The Hobbiton Movie Set, New Zealand

Step into the magical world of J.R.R. Tolkien at the Hobbiton Movie Set in Matamata, New Zealand. This film location from the ‘Lord of the Rings’ trilogy features authentic hobbit holes, a mill, and the Green Dragon Inn. Guests can explore the set, learn about the filmmaking process, and immerse themselves in the enchanting atmosphere of Middle-earth.

7. The Underwater Hotel, Maldives

For an unforgettable underwater experience, book a stay at the Underwater Hotel in the Maldives. Located 16 feet below the surface of the Indian Ocean, this hotel offers guests a panoramic view of the vibrant marine life that surrounds it. Each suite features a private deck and large windows that provide breathtaking views of the underwater world.

8. The Capsule Hotel, Japan

Experience the efficiency of Japanese living at a capsule hotel in Tokyo or Osaka. These hotels offer tiny, pod-like sleeping quarters that are just large enough for a bed and a few personal belongings. While not the most spacious, capsule hotels provide a clean and affordable alternative to traditional accommodations and a glimpse into Japan’s unique culture.

9. The Dog House Inn, Idaho, USA

For dog lovers, there’s no better place to stay than the Dog House Inn in Cottonwood, Idaho. This dog-friendly hotel features themed rooms decorated with canine memorabilia, including the ‘Pawsitive Suite’ and the ‘Doggone It Suite.’ Guests can bring their furry companions along and enjoy a relaxing stay surrounded by fellow dog enthusiasts.

10. The Jail Hotel, Sweden

Spend a night behind bars at the Jail Hotel in Stockholm, Sweden. This former prison has been transformed into a stylish hotel, offering guests a unique opportunity to experience life behind the walls. The hotel’s rooms are former prison cells, complete with original iron bars and graffiti.

Conclusion

Unique stays offer travelers the chance to break free from the mundane and embrace the extraordinary. From dog-themed hotels to underwater retreats, these quirky and unusual accommodations provide unforgettable experiences that will create lasting memories. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a taste of the unexpected, these unique stays around the globe are sure to satisfy your wanderlust and leave you with a story to tell.
