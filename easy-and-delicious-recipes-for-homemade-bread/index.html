Easy and Delicious Recipes for Homemade Bread


There’s something truly special about the aroma of freshly baked bread filling the kitchen. It’s a comforting and inviting scent that can make any house feel like a home. If you’ve ever wanted to try your hand at baking bread but felt intimidated by the process, fear not! With the right recipe and a little bit of patience, you can create beautiful, delicious loaves of bread right in your own kitchen.

Classic White Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add warm water and oil; mix well.
  2. Stir in the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough comes together.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top. Cover and let it rise in a warm place until doubled, about 1 hour.
  5. Punch the dough down and shape it into a loaf. Place in a greased 9x5 inch loaf pan. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake the bread for 30 to 35 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the pan and cool on a wire rack.

Whole Wheat Honey Oat Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve honey in warm water. Sprinkle yeast over the mixture and let stand for about 5 minutes, until foamy.
  2. Add the whole wheat flour, oats, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top. Cover and let it rise in a warm place until doubled, about 1 hour.
  5. Punch the dough down, shape into loaves, and place in greased 9x5 inch loaf pans. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the bread for 25 to 30 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from the pans and cool on wire racks.

Rosemary Garlic No-Knead Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, salt, and yeast. Stir in the warm water until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it sit at room temperature for 12 to 18 hours, until the surface is dotted with bubbles.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a well-floured surface. Sprinkle with rosemary and garlic, then fold the dough over on itself once or twice.
  4. Cover loosely with plastic wrap and let it rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Using just enough flour to keep the dough from sticking to your fingers, shape the dough into a ball. Place it in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 to 2 hours.
  6. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a covered Dutch oven into the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  7. Carefully place the dough into the hot pot. Cover and bake for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown.

These easy and delicious bread recipes are a great way to bring the warmth and aroma of freshly baked bread into your home. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a beginner, there’s a recipe here for everyone to enjoy. So, roll up your sleeves, dust off your apron, and let’s get baking!
