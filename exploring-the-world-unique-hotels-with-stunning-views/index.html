Exploring the World: Unique Hotels with Stunning Views


Are you tired of staying at ordinary hotels with mundane views? Are you craving a unique and unforgettable experience during your travels? If so, this blog post is perfect for you! Today, we are going to explore some of the world’s most unique hotels that offer stunning views you won’t find anywhere else. From breathtaking mountain ranges to glittering city skylines, these hotels will surely leave you in awe and create memories that will last a lifetime. So pack your bags and let’s dive into a world of extraordinary accommodations!

1. ICEHOTEL, Sweden:

Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi in Sweden, ICEHOTEL is not your ordinary hotel. As the name suggests, the entire hotel is made of ice and snow! Every year, a team of artists and architects create a new masterpiece, designing and sculpting the hotel using ice from the nearby Torne River. The hotel features beautifully crafted ice rooms and suites, each with its unique design. Imagine sleeping in a bed made of ice while surrounded by stunning ice sculptures and illuminated by the magical glow of the Northern Lights - a truly remarkable experience!

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:

If you have ever dreamt of having breakfast with giraffes, Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, is the place for you. This boutique hotel is located in a 140-acre forested sanctuary, where a herd of friendly giraffes freely roams. The manor allows guests to interact with these gentle creatures, who often poke their long necks through the windows in search of treats. Imagine waking up to the sound of giraffes grazing outside your window, sipping your morning coffee while giraffes peer in, and indulging in a truly unique experience found nowhere else in the world.

3. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:

For those seeking an extraordinary winter retreat, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland is the perfect destination. This remote resort offers glass igloos, providing a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness the Northern Lights from the comfort of your own bed. Imagine lying under the stars, cocooned in a cozy and warm igloo, with the mesmerizing aurora borealis dancing across the Arctic sky. It’s a surreal experience that will leave you breathless.

4. Hotel La Montaña Mágica, Chile:

Tucked away within the Huilo Huilo Biological Reserve in Chile, Hotel La Montaña Mágica, or ‘The Magic Mountain Hotel,’ is a fairytale-like lodge hidden in the heart of a forest. The hotel is shaped like a volcano and adorned with lush trees and greenery. Water cascades down the sides, making it seem like the mountain is constantly erupting. With its whimsical design and surroundings, this hotel offers a unique and enchanting experience in the midst of nature.

5. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:

If you prefer the hustle and bustle of the city, Marina Bay Sands in Singapore is a must-visit. This iconic hotel boasts an awe-inspiring rooftop infinity pool situated on the 57th floor, offering a breathtaking panoramic view of the city skyline. Swim in the pool or relax on the poolside deck and take in the stunning sights of the towering skyscrapers and overlooking the famous Marina Bay. It’s an experience that perfectly combines luxury, modernity, and an unparalleled view of the vibrant city below.

6. Treehotel, Sweden:

Immerse yourself in nature’s embrace at Treehotel in Harads, Sweden. This unique hotel features a collection of unconventional treehouses suspended high above the forest floor. From a UFO-inspired cabin to a giant bird’s nest, each treehouse is architecturally stunning and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding forest. Disconnect from the bustling world and reconnect with nature as you enjoy a truly extraordinary stay in the treetops.

7. Amangiri, United States:

Nestled among the rugged canyons of Utah, Amangiri is a luxury resort that seamlessly blends into its surroundings. With its minimalist design and earthy color palette, this hotel allows the natural beauty of the landscape to take center stage. Enjoy the mesmerizing view of the dramatic rock formations from the comfort of your suite or indulge in a spa treatment with a backdrop of red rock cliffs. Amangiri offers a serene and soul-soothing experience in the heart of the desert.

These seven unique hotels offer experiences that go beyond the ordinary. Whether you’re looking to sleep in an ice palace, commune with giraffes, witness the wonders of the Northern Lights, or immerse yourself in nature, these hotels will provide you with memories that will last a lifetime. So, the next time you’re planning a trip and searching for accommodations, ditch the conventional options, and opt for a hotel that offers stunning views and an extraordinary experience. Your wanderlust and love for adventure deserve nothing less!
