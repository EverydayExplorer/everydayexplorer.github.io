10 Fashion Designers to Watch for Future Trends


Fashion is an ever-evolving industry, constantly influenced by emerging designers who bring fresh ideas and innovative approaches to the table. These designers are often the ones to watch, as they have the potential to set new trends and shape the fashion landscape in the years to come. Today, we will take a closer look at 10 fashion designers who are quickly making a name for themselves and are worth paying attention to for future trends.

1. Jonathan Anderson - Known for his eponymous label J.W. Anderson, Jonathan has been making waves in the industry with his gender-fluid designs and unique garment construction. His ability to marry traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics has garnered him a loyal following and has positioned him as a designer to watch for future trends.

2. Marine Serre - This French designer gained recognition for her crescent moon motif, which has become synonymous with her brand. Her emphasis on sustainability and upcycling has resonated with consumers who are seeking more eco-conscious fashion choices. With her ability to combine streetwear and luxury in a thoughtful way, Marine Serre is definitely a designer to keep an eye on.

3. Rokh Hwang - Rokh’s designs often draw inspiration from his multicultural background, resulting in collections that seamlessly blend Western and Eastern influences. His meticulous attention to detail and ability to create contemporary silhouettes have allowed him to stand out in an oversaturated market. As he continues to push boundaries, Rokh is definitely a designer to watch for future trends.

4. Molly Goddard - Known for her whimsical and voluminous designs, Molly Goddard has made a name for herself in the fashion world. Her mastery of tulle and unique approach to femininity have caught the attention of industry insiders and celebrities alike. With her ability to create garments that are both elegant and unconventional, Molly Goddard is a designer to watch for future trends.

5. Matthew Williams - As the founder of the luxury streetwear brand 1017 ALYX 9SM, Matthew Williams has established himself as a designer to watch. Known for his edgy aesthetic and focus on technical details, Williams has collaborated with major brands like Nike, Dior, and Moncler. With his ability to bridge the gap between streetwear and high fashion, Matthew Williams is definitely a designer to keep an eye on.

6. Grace Wales Bonner - This British-Jamaican designer has been celebrated for her exploration of black male identity through her designs. Her collections often feature tailored pieces with rich cultural references and impeccable craftsmanship. With her unique perspective and ability to push boundaries, Grace Wales Bonner is undoubtedly a designer to watch for future trends.

7. Richard Quinn - Known for his vibrant and bold prints, Richard Quinn has quickly gained recognition in the fashion industry. His ability to create show-stopping pieces that embrace maximalism has caught the attention of influential figures like the Queen of England. With his fearless approach to design and dedication to craftsmanship, Richard Quinn is certainly a designer to keep an eye on.

8. Harris Reed - This gender-fluid designer has been making waves with their romantic and flamboyant designs. Known for their affinity for Victorian-era aesthetics and intricate tailoring, Reed’s unique approach to fashion has resonated with many. With their ability to challenge norms and create genderless garments, Harris Reed is definitely a designer to watch for future trends.

9. Peter Do - With experience working for labels like Celine and Derek Lam, Peter Do has launched his own eponymous brand that focuses on modern tailoring and architectural silhouettes. His minimalist yet striking designs have garnered attention from fashion insiders, and his dedication to sustainability and ethical production practices is also noteworthy. As he continues to refine his aesthetic and innovate, Peter Do is a designer to keep an eye on.

10. Anrealage - This Japanese brand, headed by designer Kunihiko Morinaga, is known for its experimental approach to fashion. With a strong emphasis on technology, Anrealage explores unique fabric manipulation techniques and incorporates elements like LED lights into their designs. This brand’s boundary-pushing aesthetic and dedication to innovation make them a key player in future trend forecasting.

As the fashion industry continues to evolve, these 10 designers stand out for their creativity, craftsmanship, and unique perspectives. Whether it’s through their innovative use of materials, exploration of gender boundaries, or dedication to sustainability, each of these designers has the potential to shape future trends and influence the fashion landscape. By keeping an eye on these rising stars, you can stay ahead of the curve and be at the forefront of the latest fashion trends.
