Hotels with Unique and Quirky Amenities: Expect the Unexpected


In the ever-evolving world of hospitality, hotels are constantly vying for the attention of travelers seeking unique experiences. Gone are the days of cookie-cutter accommodations with standard amenities. Today, hotels are pushing the boundaries of creativity and offering quirky amenities that leave guests with unforgettable memories. From underwater hotels to treehouse retreats, here are some hotels around the world that will make you say, ‘I never expected that!’

1. The Manta Resort, Pemba Island, Tanzania:
Imagine sleeping underwater, surrounded by the vibrant marine life of the Indian Ocean. The Manta Resort offers an extraordinary experience with its underwater room. This floating island paradise provides guests with a 360-degree view of the ocean, allowing them to sleep peacefully while being mesmerized by the colorful coral reefs and passing fish.

2. The Dog Bark Park Inn, Idaho, USA:
For dog lovers, this hotel is a dream come true. Shaped like a giant beagle, the Dog Bark Park Inn offers a truly one-of-a-kind experience. Step inside and you’ll find dog-themed decor, cozy furnishings, and a warm welcome from the owners, who are passionate about their four-legged friends. Enjoy a peaceful night’s sleep in the belly of the beagle and wake up to the breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

3. Free Spirit Spheres, Vancouver Island, Canada:
Nestled in the lush forests of Vancouver Island, the Free Spirit Spheres are a haven for those seeking a unique accommodation experience. These spherical treehouses are suspended from the trees, allowing guests to sleep among the branches and birds. Each sphere is beautifully crafted and offers a cozy interior, complete with comfortable beds and stunning panoramic views of the forest.

4. Icehotel, Jukkasjärvi, Sweden:
Step into a winter wonderland at the Icehotel, where everything, from the walls to the furniture, is made entirely of ice. Each year, artists from around the world gather to create this ephemeral masterpiece, offering guests the chance to sleep in rooms sculpted from ice and snow. Don’t worry about freezing, though - the hotel provides warm sleeping bags and reindeer hides to keep you snug throughout the night.

5. The Library Hotel, New York City, USA:
Book lovers will adore the Library Hotel, a literary-themed boutique hotel in the heart of Manhattan. Each floor of the hotel is dedicated to a different category of the Dewey Decimal System, and each room is adorned with books related to its theme. Guests can curl up with a good book from the hotel’s extensive library or enjoy a literary-inspired cocktail at the rooftop bar.

6. Giraffe Manor, Nairobi, Kenya:
Prepare to share your breakfast with a gentle giant at Giraffe Manor. This boutique hotel is home to a herd of resident giraffes, who often visit during mealtimes, poking their long necks through the windows in search of a tasty treat. Interact with these majestic creatures as you enjoy your morning coffee, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Hotels with unique and quirky amenities are redefining the concept of luxury and providing guests with experiences they never thought possible. Whether it’s sleeping underwater, in a giant beagle, or among the treetops, these hotels are pushing the boundaries of what a hotel stay can be. So, the next time you’re planning a trip, expect the unexpected and choose a hotel that offers more than just a comfortable bed.
