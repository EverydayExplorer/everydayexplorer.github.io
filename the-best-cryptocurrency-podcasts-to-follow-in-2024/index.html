The Best Cryptocurrency Podcasts to Follow in 2024


In the rapidly evolving world of cryptocurrency, staying informed is crucial. With new developments, trends, and technologies emerging almost daily, engaging with knowledgeable sources is essential for both beginners and seasoned investors. Podcasts have become a popular medium for delivering insights, expert opinions, and diverse perspectives on the crypto landscape. In 2024, numerous podcasts have risen above the rest, offering valuable content that can help you navigate the complexities of cryptocurrency. Below, we explore some of the best cryptocurrency podcasts to follow this year.

1. Unchained

Hosted by the insightful Laura Shin, Unchained has established itself as a leading podcast in the crypto space. Shin, a former Forbes journalist, interviews key figures in the industry, including developers, investors, and thought leaders. The discussions often delve deep into the technical aspects of blockchain technology, market dynamics, and regulatory issues, making it an excellent resource for those looking to deepen their understanding.

Each episode is well-researched and presents a balanced view, ensuring listeners gain a comprehensive understanding of the topics covered. With its engaging format and quality content, Unchained is a must-listen for anyone serious about cryptocurrency.

2. The Pomp Podcast

Anthony Pompliano, affectionately known as “Pomp,” hosts this highly regarded podcast that covers a wide range of topics within the cryptocurrency ecosystem. From interviews with industry leaders to discussions on macroeconomic trends affecting the crypto market, Pomp’s podcast offers a unique blend of insights and entertainment.

What sets The Pomp Podcast apart is its ability to break down complex topics into digestible segments. Whether you’re interested in Bitcoin, DeFi, or NFTs, Pomp’s engaging style and expert guests make learning about cryptocurrency enjoyable. In 2024, this podcast continues to be a go-to source for both new and experienced crypto enthusiasts.

3. What Bitcoin Did

Hosted by Peter McCormack, What Bitcoin Did focuses primarily on Bitcoin and its implications for the future of money. McCormack’s approach is conversational, often featuring in-depth discussions with experts from various fields, including economics, finance, and technology.

The podcast covers a wide array of topics, from the philosophical implications of Bitcoin to technical analysis and market trends. McCormack’s ability to ask thought-provoking questions fosters engaging discussions that challenge conventional thinking. For those looking to explore Bitcoin in-depth, this podcast is a fantastic resource.

4. Crypto 101

For those just starting their cryptocurrency journey, Crypto 101 is an excellent introduction to the world of digital currencies. This podcast is structured to educate listeners on the fundamentals of cryptocurrency, blockchain technology, and investing strategies.

Each episode features expert guests who share their knowledge and experiences, making complex topics accessible to newcomers. The friendly and informative tone of the show makes it easy to follow along, ensuring listeners can grasp even the most intricate concepts. As the crypto landscape continues to evolve, Crypto 101 remains a valuable resource for beginners in 2024.

5. The Bad Crypto Podcast

Hosted by Joel Comm and Travis Wright, The Bad Crypto Podcast offers a lighthearted take on cryptocurrency news and trends. This podcast stands out for its entertaining format and engaging discussions that make learning about crypto fun.

The hosts cover a variety of topics, including market analysis, interviews with industry experts, and discussions about the latest happenings in the crypto world. Their humorous approach and casual style make it easy for listeners to stay engaged and informed. If you’re looking for a podcast that combines entertainment with education, The Bad Crypto Podcast is a great choice.

6. The Decrypt Daily

As the name suggests, The Decrypt Daily provides daily news updates on the cryptocurrency market. Hosted by Matthew Aaron, this podcast covers the latest headlines, trends, and developments in the crypto space, making it perfect for listeners who want to stay up-to-date with minimal effort.

Each episode is concise and packed with information, allowing listeners to quickly grasp the day’s most important news. The Decrypt Daily also features interviews with industry experts, providing additional insights into the trends shaping the crypto landscape. In 2024, this podcast remains a go-to source for daily crypto news.

7. CoinDesk Podcast Network

CoinDesk, a leading news and analysis platform in the cryptocurrency industry, offers a diverse range of podcasts under its network. From in-depth interviews to market discussions, the CoinDesk Podcast Network caters to various interests within the crypto community.

Listeners can choose from different shows, each focusing on different aspects of the cryptocurrency ecosystem. The quality of production and the expertise of the hosts make this network a valuable resource for anyone looking to stay informed about the latest trends and developments in the crypto world.

8. The Tim Ferriss Show

While not exclusively a cryptocurrency podcast, The Tim Ferriss Show often features discussions about blockchain technology and digital currencies. Tim Ferriss, an author and entrepreneur, interviews top performers across various fields, including finance and technology.

Many guests share their insights on cryptocurrency, investment strategies, and the future of money. Ferriss’s inquisitive nature and ability to extract valuable information make this podcast a treasure trove of knowledge for anyone interested in the intersection of technology and finance.

9. Crypto Frontiers

Crypto Frontiers takes a unique approach by focusing on the stories of innovators and entrepreneurs in the cryptocurrency space. Hosted by the passionate and knowledgeable host, the podcast features interviews with individuals who are pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with blockchain technology.

Listeners will gain insights into the challenges and successes faced by these innovators, along with a deeper understanding of the potential applications of cryptocurrency. For those looking to explore the human side of the crypto industry, Crypto Frontiers offers compelling narratives and valuable lessons.

10. The Token Metrics Podcast

Token Metrics is a platform that provides cryptocurrency investment research and analysis. Their podcast extends this mission by offering insights into market trends, investment strategies, and the latest developments in the crypto space.

Hosted by a team of experts, the Token Metrics Podcast covers a range of topics, from technical analysis to interviews with industry leaders. Their data-driven approach and focus on research make this podcast a valuable resource for investors looking to make informed decisions in the ever-changing cryptocurrency market.

Conclusion

As the cryptocurrency landscape continues to evolve, staying informed is more important than ever. The podcasts mentioned above offer a wealth of knowledge, insights, and perspectives that can help you navigate the complexities of the crypto world. Whether you’re a beginner seeking to understand the basics or an experienced investor looking for expert analysis, these podcasts provide valuable content for everyone.

In 2024, engaging with these podcasts can enhance your understanding of cryptocurrency and keep you updated on the latest trends and developments. So, grab your headphones and start exploring the fascinating world of cryptocurrency through these exceptional podcasts!
