The Spooky History of Ouija Boards


Deep in the realm of the supernatural, lies a mysterious tool that has both fascinated and terrified people for decades - the Ouija board. With its origins shrouded in mystery, this eerie device has captivated the imaginations of many, serving as a gateway to the unknown. In this blog post, we will embark on a journey through the spooky history of Ouija boards, unearthing the secrets and stories that surround this enigmatic tool.

The Ouija board, often referred to as a ’talking board,’ is believed to have its roots in the spiritualist movement of the late 19th century. Spiritualism, a religious and philosophical movement, gained popularity during a time when people sought to communicate with the spirits of the deceased. It was in this context that the Ouija board made its first appearance.

The exact origins of the Ouija board are muddled in a haze of conflicting claims and mystical lore. However, one story that has persisted over the years revolves around two enterprising individuals named Elijah Bond and Charles Kennard. In 1890, they patented the first commercially produced Ouija board, claiming to have received the name ‘Ouija’ from a mysterious encounter with the board itself. According to their account, the board spelled out the word ‘Ouija’ when asked what it should be called, and when they inquired about the meaning, it responded with ‘Good luck.’

With its introduction into the public domain, the Ouija board quickly gained popularity, attracting both believers and skeptics alike. Many saw it as a harmless parlor game, while others believed it to be a powerful tool for contacting the spirit world. However, as its use spread, so did the tales of strange and unsettling experiences.

One of the most infamous stories associated with the Ouija board involves the Zeigler family. In the early 20th century, the Zeiglers claimed to have made contact with a spirit named Patience Worth through their Ouija board. Patience, according to their accounts, was a 17th-century Englishwoman who dictated poems, novels, and plays through the board. The Zeiglers’ claims of Patience’s existence and literary prowess stirred both intrigue and controversy, leaving many to wonder if the Ouija board truly had a connection to the spirit world.

As the Ouija board continued to capture the public’s imagination, it also found itself embroiled in tales of horror and supernatural encounters. Countless reports emerged of people experiencing strange occurrences during Ouija board sessions - from objects moving on their own, to unexplained noises and eerie voices. Some even claimed to have encountered malevolent spirits or found themselves haunted long after their encounters with the board.

Despite the skeptics who dismiss the Ouija board as nothing more than a product of the subconscious mind, its allure persists. It has become a staple in pop culture, appearing in movies, books, and television shows as a conduit for supernatural experiences. The Ouija board continues to both fascinate and terrify, inviting individuals to explore the boundaries between the earthly realm and the spirit world.

In conclusion, the history of the Ouija board is as mysterious as the tool itself. Born out of a desire to communicate with the dead, it has left an indelible mark on the world of the supernatural. Whether you believe in its powers or see it as nothing more than a product of the human imagination, one thing remains certain - the Ouija board will forever hold a place in the annals of the paranormal, beckoning the curious to explore the realms beyond our own.
