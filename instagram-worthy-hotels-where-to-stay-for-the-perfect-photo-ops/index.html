Instagram-Worthy Hotels: Where to Stay for the Perfect Photo Ops


Are you ready to take your Instagram game to the next level? If you’re seeking the perfect backdrop for your travel photos, look no further than these Instagram-worthy hotels. With stunning architecture, breathtaking views, and picturesque settings, these hotels are sure to be a hit on your social media feed. Get ready to wow your followers with these top hotel recommendations for the perfect photo ops.

1. The Marina Bay Sands - Singapore
Located in the heart of Singapore, the Marina Bay Sands is an architectural marvel and a haven for Instagrammers. Its iconic rooftop infinity pool offers stunning views of the city skyline, making it the perfect spot for that envy-inducing poolside snap. With its sleek design and impressive cityscape vistas, this hotel is sure to garner likes and comments galore.

2. Santorini Secret Suites & Spa - Santorini, Greece
Santorini is well-known for its stunning sunsets and breathtaking views, and staying at Santorini Secret Suites & Spa will give you front-row access to all the beauty this island has to offer. Nestled into the picturesque cliffs of Oia, this hotel offers luxurious accommodations with a private terrace overlooking the Aegean Sea. Whether you’re capturing the famous blue-domed churches or simply soaking up the sun by the infinity pool, every shot here is Instagram gold.

3. The Ace Hotel - Palm Springs, California
Escape to the desert oasis of Palm Springs and indulge in retro vibes at The Ace Hotel. Known for its mid-century modern architecture and vibrant pool scene, this hotel is a favorite among Instagrammers. Snap a photo in one of the stylish rooms or strike a pose by the vintage-inspired poolside lounge. The colorful geometric accents and the majestic mountains in the background make for a picture-perfect Instagram moment.

4. Giraffe Manor - Nairobi, Kenya
For a truly unique experience, head to Giraffe Manor in Nairobi. This boutique hotel is famous for its resident herd of Rothschild’s giraffes, who often poke their heads through the windows during breakfast. Capture the awe-inspiring sight of these gentle giants up close and personal for an unforgettable Instagram post. The manor itself is a charming colonial-era property, providing a stunning backdrop for your wildlife encounters.

5. The Standard - Miami Beach, Florida
Nestled on the shores of Miami Beach, The Standard stands out with its vibrant and whimsical architecture. The hotel’s colorful pool, complete with bright pink deck chairs and floating hammocks, will transport you to a tropical paradise. The hotel’s stylish rooms and stunning sunset views add the perfect finishing touches. Get your camera ready for some Insta-worthy shots that will make your followers instantly jealous.

6. 1 Hotel Brooklyn Bridge - New York City, New York
If you’re in search of the perfect cityscape backdrop, 1 Hotel Brooklyn Bridge is the place to be. Boasting breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline, the Brooklyn Bridge, and the East River, this hotel offers a prime location for those looking to capture the essence of the Big Apple. Whether you’re snapping a shot from the rooftop bar or strolling along the nearby waterfront promenade, the opportunities for stunning photos are endless.

7. The Royal Mansour - Marrakech, Morocco
Prepare to be transported to a fairytale world at The Royal Mansour in Marrakech. This opulent hotel is a masterpiece of Moroccan architecture, boasting intricate tilework, lush gardens, and luxurious suites. The hotel’s grand entrance and stunning courtyard provide the perfect setting for an Instagram-worthy photo. Don’t forget to explore the winding alleys of the nearby medina and capture the vibrant colors and bustling atmosphere of Marrakech.

8. The Ned - London, United Kingdom
Located in the heart of London, The Ned is a stylish hotel housed in a former bank. Its stunning architecture, featuring grand chandeliers and marble floors, provides a glamorous setting for your Instagram feed. From the rooftop pool to the elegant dining halls, every corner of this hotel offers a unique photo opportunity. Capture the elegance and sophistication of London’s financial district and let your followers immerse themselves in the city’s history.

9. Amangiri - Canyon Point, Utah
For those seeking a desert retreat, Amangiri in Canyon Point, Utah, is the ultimate destination. This secluded hotel blends seamlessly into its natural surroundings, with its clean lines and earthy palette. The stunning desert landscapes will serve as the backdrop for your Instagram photos, whether you’re lounging by the pool or exploring the nearby canyons. Don’t miss the chance to capture the breathtaking vistas and serene atmosphere that make Amangiri a true hidden gem.

10. The Icehotel - Jukkasjärvi, Sweden
If you’re seeking a unique and unforgettable experience, the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden, is the perfect choice. Each year, this hotel is rebuilt entirely out of ice and snow, making it a jaw-dropping wonderland for photography enthusiasts. From the ethereal ice sculptures to the stunning ice suites, every angle offers a chance to create an Instagram-worthy masterpiece. Embrace the cold and capture the magic of this ephemeral hotel before it melts away.

With these Instagram-worthy hotels, your travel photos are guaranteed to impress. From iconic landmarks to breathtaking natural landscapes, each destination offers a unique backdrop that will elevate your Instagram game. So grab your camera, pack your bags, and get ready to discover these picture-perfect hotels for your next adventure. Happy snapping!
