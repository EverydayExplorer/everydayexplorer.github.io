Hotels with Helicopter Skiing: Conquering Majestic Slopes


For those who crave the ultimate skiing adventure, there’s nothing quite like the exhilaration of heli-skiing. Imagine being whisked away in a helicopter to untouched powder slopes, far away from the crowded ski resorts. With the rise in popularity of this extreme sport, luxury hotels around the world have begun offering guests the opportunity to experience the thrill of helicopter skiing right from their doorstep. In this blog post, we will explore some of the top hotels that provide helicopter skiing adventures, allowing skiers and snowboarders to conquer majestic slopes and create unforgettable memories.

  1. The Little Nell, Aspen, Colorado Nestled in the heart of the stunning Rocky Mountains, The Little Nell in Aspen, Colorado offers guests an exclusive heli-skiing experience. The hotel has partnered with renowned heli-skiing operators to provide access to the pristine backcountry terrain of the surrounding mountains. Guests can embark on thrilling helicopter rides to remote peaks and enjoy endless runs through deep powder snow. With professional guides and expert instructors, even intermediate skiers can partake in this adrenaline-pumping adventure while soaking in the breathtaking views of the Colorado Rockies.

  2. CMH Heli-Skiing & Summer Adventures, British Columbia, Canada CMH Heli-Skiing & Summer Adventures is not just a hotel but an entire resort dedicated to heli-skiing and mountain adventures. Located in the breathtaking Canadian Rockies of British Columbia, this resort offers unmatched access to some of the world’s best heli-skiing terrain. Guests can choose from a range of packages, from multi-day adventures to single-day excursions. With a fleet of helicopters and a team of experienced guides, CMH ensures guests experience the thrill of untouched powder, steep chutes, and incredible glacial landscapes.

  3. The Chedi Andermatt, Andermatt, Switzerland Situated in the heart of the Swiss Alps, The Chedi Andermatt offers a luxurious base for those seeking a heli-skiing experience in Europe. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a private helicopter taking them to nearby peaks where they can carve their way down pristine slopes. With access to vast off-piste areas and breathtaking alpine scenery, this hotel provides an unforgettable heli-skiing adventure combined with the renowned Swiss hospitality and sophistication.

  4. Blanket Bay, Queenstown, New Zealand Blanket Bay, located near Queenstown, New Zealand, offers a unique heli-skiing experience amidst the stunning landscapes of the Southern Alps. Guests can take off from the hotel’s helipad and explore untouched snowfields and dramatic mountain ranges. With a range of terrain suitable for all skill levels, from gentle slopes to challenging couloirs, heli-skiing at Blanket Bay is a dream come true for winter sports enthusiasts. After an exhilarating day on the slopes, guests can unwind in the hotel’s luxurious spa facilities or indulge in gourmet cuisine paired with local wines.

  5. The St. Regis Deer Valley, Park City, Utah The St. Regis Deer Valley in Park City, Utah offers guests the opportunity to combine the luxury of a five-star hotel with the thrill of heli-skiing in the Wasatch Mountains. Skiers and snowboarders can enjoy access to thousands of acres of untouched powder snow and challenging slopes. The hotel’s concierge can arrange helicopter tours to nearby peaks, providing a seamless and unforgettable heli-skiing experience. After a day of conquering majestic slopes, guests can relax in the hotel’s elegant spa or savor gourmet cuisine in one of its renowned restaurants.

In conclusion, hotels that offer helicopter skiing experiences provide adventurous skiers and snowboarders with the opportunity to conquer majestic slopes and experience the thrill of untouched powder snow. Whether you choose to explore the Rocky Mountains in Colorado, the Canadian Rockies, the Swiss Alps, the Southern Alps in New Zealand, or the Wasatch Mountains in Utah, these hotels offer a seamless blend of luxury accommodations and adrenaline-pumping heli-skiing adventures. So, gear up, take to the skies, and immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring beauty of these extraordinary destinations for an unforgettable skiing experience.
