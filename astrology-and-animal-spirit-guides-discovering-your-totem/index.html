Astrology and Animal Spirit Guides: Discovering Your Totem


Have you ever wondered why certain animals seem to have a special significance in your life? Do you find yourself drawn to a particular animal, seeing it everywhere you go? If so, you may have an animal spirit guide or totem guiding and protecting you. In this blog post, we will explore the fascinating world of astrology and animal spirit guides, and how you can discover and connect with your own animal totem.

Astrology and animal spirit guides are two ancient practices that have been used for centuries to gain insight into our lives and the world around us. Astrology is the study of celestial bodies and their impact on human behavior and events, while animal spirit guides are believed to be spiritual entities that take the form of animals to provide guidance and protection.

In astrology, each zodiac sign is associated with specific traits, characteristics, and elements. Similarly, animal spirit guides are associated with different qualities, strengths, and energies. By understanding the relationship between astrology and animal spirit guides, we can gain a deeper understanding of ourselves and our life’s journey.

To discover your animal spirit guide or totem, it is important to first understand your astrological sign. Your zodiac sign can provide clues about the type of animal that may be guiding you. For example, Aries, a fire sign, is often associated with the spirit of the ram. The ram represents strength, courage, and determination – qualities that are often attributed to those born under the sign of Aries.

In addition to your zodiac sign, it can also be helpful to explore your birth chart. Your birth chart is a map of the positions of the planets at the exact time and location of your birth. It provides a unique snapshot of your personality and potential. By analyzing your birth chart, an astrologer can help identify the animal spirit guides that may be influencing your life.

One way to connect with your animal spirit guide is through meditation or visualization. Find a quiet space and take a few moments to clear your mind. Visualize yourself in a peaceful natural setting, surrounded by the sounds and sights of the animal kingdom. Allow yourself to be open to any animals that appear in your mind’s eye. Pay attention to their size, features, and behavior. This can give you insights into the qualities and messages they may have for you.

Another way to connect with your animal spirit guide is through dreamwork. Before going to bed, set the intention to receive guidance from your animal totem in your dreams. Keep a dream journal next to your bed to record any animal encounters or messages you receive during the night. Over time, you may start to see patterns and recurring themes that can help you identify your animal spirit guide.

Once you have discovered your animal spirit guide, it is important to nurture and honor your connection with them. Spend time researching the symbolism and cultural significance of your animal totem. Learn about their natural habitat, behaviors, and characteristics. This knowledge can deepen your understanding of the messages your animal spirit guide is sending you.

You can also strengthen your bond with your animal spirit guide by incorporating rituals and practices into your daily life. This can include creating a sacred space in your home dedicated to your animal totem, wearing jewelry or clothing that represents your animal guide, or even incorporating totem animal imagery into your artwork or decor.

Remember, your animal spirit guide is there to provide guidance and support on your spiritual journey. They may offer wisdom, protection, and inspiration when you need it most. By nurturing and honoring your connection with your animal totem, you can tap into their unique energy and experience a deeper sense of connection with the natural world.

In conclusion, astrology and animal spirit guides offer valuable insights into our lives and the world around us. By exploring the relationship between astrology and animal spirit guides, we can discover and connect with our own animal totem. Whether through meditation, dreamwork, or research, the process of discovering your animal spirit guide can be a powerful and transformative experience. Embrace the wisdom and guidance of your animal totem, and let their energy lead you on your spiritual journey.
