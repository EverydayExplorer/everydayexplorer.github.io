Get Close to Wildlife: Hotels Near National Parks and Reserves


In the vast wilderness of national parks and reserves, there is a unique opportunity to immerse oneself in the wonders of nature. For those seeking a close encounter with wildlife, staying at hotels near these protected areas can provide an unforgettable experience. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotels that offer proximity to national parks and reserves, allowing guests to get up close and personal with the incredible flora and fauna that call these places home.

1. Rocky Mountain Retreat: Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, this hotel offers breathtaking views of the surrounding national park. Guests can wake up to the sight of elk grazing just outside their windows or catch a glimpse of a majestic bald eagle soaring overhead. With guided wildlife tours and expert naturalists on hand, visitors have the opportunity to learn about the diverse ecosystem and witness animals such as bears, moose, and mountain goats in their natural habitats.

2. Serengeti Safari Lodge: Located on the outskirts of the renowned Serengeti National Park in Tanzania, this lodge provides an authentic African safari experience. Guests can witness the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and other wildlife traverse the plains in search of greener pastures. From the comfort of their rooms or private balconies, visitors can observe lions, elephants, and giraffes roaming freely in the distance. The lodge also organizes game drives and walking safaris, allowing guests to get even closer to the action.

3. Amazon Rainforest Resort: For those craving an adventure in the world’s largest tropical rainforest, this resort offers an unparalleled experience. Set deep within the Amazon Rainforest, guests can wake up to the sounds of howler monkeys and rare bird species. Guided tours through the dense jungle provide the chance to spot elusive creatures like jaguars, pink river dolphins, and giant river otters. The resort’s knowledgeable guides offer insights into the rainforest’s delicate ecosystem and the importance of conservation.

4. Glacier Bay Lodge: Situated in Alaska’s Glacier Bay National Park, this lodge provides a front-row seat to the mesmerizing beauty of glaciers. Guests can witness massive chunks of ice calving off glaciers and crashing into the sea, creating a spectacle unlike any other. Whales, seals, and sea lions are a common sight in the nearby waters, and guided kayak tours allow visitors to paddle alongside these marine giants. The lodge also offers educational programs and presentations, shedding light on the park’s rich history and fragile ecosystem.

5. Kruger Safari Camp: Located on the outskirts of South Africa’s iconic Kruger National Park, this camp offers an authentic bushveld experience. Guests can stay in luxury tents and wake up to the sounds of lions roaring in the distance. Expert guides take visitors on thrilling game drives, where encounters with the Big Five (elephant, lion, leopard, rhinoceros, and buffalo) are almost guaranteed. The camp’s elevated viewing decks provide panoramic views of the savannah, making it the perfect spot for sundowners and stargazing.

Staying at these remarkable hotels near national parks and reserves allows travelers to forge a deep connection with the natural world. From the rugged mountains to the vast plains and dense rainforests, these accommodations offer an immersive experience that is hard to replicate elsewhere. Whether it’s witnessing the Great Migration in Africa or marveling at the icy wonders of Alaska, these hotels provide a gateway to some of the most awe-inspiring wildlife encounters on the planet. So, if you’re ready to embark on an adventure like no other, pack your bags and get ready to get close to wildlife in some of the world’s most breathtaking national parks and reserves.
