Books That Make You Cry: 10 Emotional Reads That Leave a Lasting Impact


Reading has the incredible power to transport us to different worlds, evoke a range of emotions, and leave a lasting impact on our lives. There’s something truly cathartic about a book that can make us cry, as it taps into the deepest corners of our hearts and souls. In this blog post, we will explore ten emotional reads that have the ability to tug at your heartstrings, leaving you with an indelible impression.

1. ‘The Kite Runner’ by Khaled Hosseini:
This powerful tale of friendship, betrayal, and redemption will undoubtedly leave you in tears. Set against the backdrop of war-torn Afghanistan, the story follows the lives of Amir and Hassan, two young boys from different social backgrounds. Through their journey, Hosseini explores themes of guilt, forgiveness, and the enduring power of love.

2. ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ by John Green:
Prepare to have your heart shattered by this heart-wrenching love story between two teenagers fighting cancer. Green’s beautiful prose and authentic portrayal of the characters’ emotions will leave you reaching for the tissues. It is a poignant reminder of the fragility of life and the power of true love.

3. ‘A Little Life’ by Hanya Yanagihara:
This emotionally devastating novel delves into the lives of four college friends as they navigate the complexities of adulthood. Yanagihara skillfully explores themes of trauma, friendship, and the enduring resilience of the human spirit. Be warned, this book is a true emotional rollercoaster that will leave you emotionally drained yet profoundly moved.

4. ‘The Book Thief’ by Markus Zusak:
Narrated by Death, this hauntingly beautiful story follows Liesel Meminger, a young girl living in Nazi Germany. As she discovers the power of words and the horrors of war, Zusak’s novel will bring tears to your eyes and remind you of the enduring strength of the human spirit even in the darkest of times.

5. ‘Me Before You’ by Jojo Moyes:
Prepare to have your heartstrings tugged by this bittersweet love story between Louisa Clark and Will Traynor. Moyes explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the right to choose, leaving readers with a profound sense of empathy and a newfound appreciation for the beauty of life.

6. ‘The Lovely Bones’ by Alice Sebold:
Sebold’s haunting novel takes readers on a journey through grief, loss, and healing. The story revolves around Susie Salmon, a young girl who watches over her family and friends from heaven after being brutally murdered. This emotionally charged narrative will leave you contemplating the fragility of life and the power of love.

7. ‘The Color Purple’ by Alice Walker:
Walker’s Pulitzer Prize-winning novel tells the story of Celie, a young African-American woman living in the early 20th century. Through Celie’s powerful voice, the novel explores themes of racism, sexism, and the transformative power of self-love. Prepare to shed tears of both sorrow and joy as you follow Celie’s journey towards empowerment and liberation.

8. ‘The Time Traveler’s Wife’ by Audrey Niffenegger:
This unique love story transcends time and space, as it follows the lives of Henry, a time traveler, and Clare, his wife. Niffenegger’s poignant exploration of love, loss, and the complexities of relationships will leave you emotionally invested in their story, reaching for the tissues with each turn of the page.

9. ‘The Giving Tree’ by Shel Silverstein:
Although technically a children’s book, this timeless classic by Shel Silverstein is a tear-jerker for readers of all ages. Through simple yet profound storytelling, Silverstein explores themes of selflessness, love, and the passage of time. Prepare to shed tears as you witness the unwavering love of a tree for a boy, leaving you with a lasting impact on the importance of giving and gratitude.

10. ‘The Diary of a Young Girl’ by Anne Frank:
This poignant and heartbreaking memoir, written by Anne Frank during her time in hiding from the Nazis, offers a glimpse into the life of a young girl amidst the horrors of the Holocaust. Anne’s courage, resilience, and hope in the face of adversity will leave you both inspired and deeply moved.

These ten emotional reads have the power to elicit tears, provoke introspection, and leave a lasting impact on readers. Whether you’re in search of a cathartic cry or a thought-provoking exploration of the human experience, these books are sure to deliver. So grab a box of tissues and immerse yourself in the emotional journey that awaits within the pages of these remarkable stories.
