Creative and Delicious Ways to Use Leftover Turkey


Thanksgiving is over, and you’re left with a mountain of leftover turkey. While you could just reheat it and serve it as is, why not get a little creative with your leftovers? There are so many delicious ways to transform that leftover turkey into new and exciting dishes. Not only will you be able to enjoy a variety of flavors, but you’ll also reduce food waste and save money in the process. Here are some creative and delicious ways to use leftover turkey:

1. Turkey Pot Pie:
One classic dish that comes to mind when thinking of leftover turkey is turkey pot pie. This comforting dish is made with a flaky crust and filled with a creamy mixture of turkey, vegetables, and gravy. It’s a great way to use up leftover turkey and turn it into a hearty meal that the whole family will love.

2. Turkey Enchiladas:
Give your leftover turkey a Mexican twist by making turkey enchiladas. Shred the leftover turkey and roll it up in corn tortillas, along with some cheese and your favorite enchilada sauce. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and serve with some sour cream and guacamole. These enchiladas are flavorful and will be a hit at any dinner table.

3. Turkey and Cranberry Grilled Cheese:
Take your grilled cheese sandwich to the next level by adding leftover turkey and cranberry sauce. Layer sliced turkey, cranberry sauce, and cheese between two slices of bread, and grill until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. The combination of flavors in this sandwich is simply divine.

4. Turkey Fried Rice:
Transform your leftover turkey into a delicious fried rice dish. Sautee some garlic, onions, and vegetables in a pan, then add chopped turkey and cooked rice. Season with soy sauce and other Asian spices to create a flavorful and satisfying meal. This dish is quick, easy, and perfect for using up leftover turkey.

5. Turkey Salad:
For a lighter option, consider making a turkey salad. Shred the leftover turkey and mix it with some mayonnaise, celery, onions, and your favorite seasonings. Serve it on a bed of greens or between two slices of bread for a satisfying and refreshing meal.

6. Turkey Curry:
Looking to spice things up? Try making a turkey curry with your leftover turkey. Sautee onions, garlic, and ginger in a pan, then add spices like curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Add in the shredded turkey and coconut milk, and let it simmer until the flavors meld together. Serve with rice or naan bread for a comforting and aromatic meal.

7. Turkey Tacos:
Who doesn’t love tacos? Use your leftover turkey to make delicious turkey tacos. Warm up some tortillas, fill them with shredded turkey, and add your favorite toppings like salsa, avocado, and cheese. This is a great way to enjoy a lighter and more casual meal after the heavy Thanksgiving feast.

These are just a few creative and delicious ways to use leftover turkey. Don’t let those leftovers go to waste – get creative and transform them into new and exciting dishes. Your taste buds will thank you, and you’ll make the most out of your Thanksgiving turkey.
