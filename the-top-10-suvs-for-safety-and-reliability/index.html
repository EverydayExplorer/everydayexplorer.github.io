The Top 10 SUVs for Safety and Reliability


When it comes to choosing a sport utility vehicle (SUV), safety and reliability are often at the top of the list of priorities for many drivers and families. In the ever-growing market of SUVs, it can be challenging to narrow down the options and find the models that excel in both safety and reliability. To make this process easier for you, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 SUVs that are known for their exceptional safety features and reliable performance. Whether you’re looking for a compact SUV, a midsize model, or a full-size option, this comprehensive guide will help you make an informed decision when it comes to selecting a safe and dependable SUV.

1. Volvo XC90
The Volvo XC90 is a standout in the SUV market, known for its top-notch safety features and reliability. With advanced driver assistance systems, including automatic emergency braking, lane departure warning, and blind-spot monitoring, the XC90 provides peace of mind for drivers and passengers. Its solid build quality and long-term reliability make it a top contender in the luxury SUV segment.

2. Subaru Forester
Subaru has a long-standing reputation for producing safe and reliable vehicles, and the Forester is no exception. Equipped with Subaru’s renowned symmetrical all-wheel drive and EyeSight driver assist technology, the Forester offers exceptional safety and stability in various driving conditions. Its impressive reliability ratings make it a popular choice for those seeking a dependable SUV.

3. Toyota RAV4
The Toyota RAV4 has consistently ranked high in safety and reliability assessments, making it a perennial favorite among SUV enthusiasts. With standard safety features such as adaptive cruise control, lane tracing assist, and road sign assist, the RAV4 provides a secure and comfortable driving experience. Its reputation for longevity and low maintenance costs adds to its appeal as a reliable SUV option.

4. Honda CR-V
The Honda CR-V has earned a reputation for its stellar safety ratings and long-term dependability. Equipped with Honda Sensing safety suite and available all-wheel drive, the CR-V offers a comprehensive safety package. Its solid build quality and low cost of ownership make it a practical choice for those prioritizing safety and reliability.

5. Mazda CX-5
Combining sporty handling with advanced safety features, the Mazda CX-5 stands out as a stylish and secure SUV option. With features like smart brake support, lane-keep assist, and adaptive front-lighting system, the CX-5 emphasizes safety without compromising performance. Its strong reliability ratings make it a compelling choice for those seeking a well-rounded SUV.

6. Audi Q5
Luxurious and safe, the Audi Q5 boasts a plethora of safety features and a solid track record for reliability. From Audi pre sense basic to Audi side assist, the Q5 offers advanced safety technologies to enhance driver and passenger protection. Its upscale interior and strong build quality contribute to its reputation as a safe and reliable luxury SUV.

7. Ford Escape
The Ford Escape has made a name for itself as a practical and safe SUV, featuring Ford Co-Pilot360 suite of driver-assist technologies and available all-wheel drive. With a solid reputation for reliability and a range of safety features such as automatic emergency braking and blind-spot information system, the Escape is a strong contender in the compact SUV category.

8. Kia Telluride
As a newcomer to the SUV market, the Kia Telluride has quickly gained recognition for its exceptional safety ratings and reliability. Packed with advanced safety features like blind-spot view monitor and safe exit assist, the Telluride offers a secure and comfortable ride for families. Its impressive build quality and long warranty coverage make it a compelling choice for those seeking a safe and reliable midsize SUV.

9. Mercedes-Benz GLE
The Mercedes-Benz GLE combines luxury with safety, offering an array of advanced safety features and a reputation for long-term reliability. From active brake assist to attention assist, the GLE prioritizes safety without compromising on performance and comfort. Its upscale interior and strong safety ratings make it a top choice for those seeking a safe and reliable luxury SUV.

10. Hyundai Palisade
Rounding out our list is the Hyundai Palisade, known for its exceptional safety features and solid reliability. Equipped with Hyundai SmartSense safety suite and available all-wheel drive, the Palisade prioritizes driver and passenger protection. Its spacious interior, strong warranty coverage, and impressive safety ratings make it a compelling option for those prioritizing safety and reliability in a midsize SUV.

In conclusion, when it comes to selecting an SUV that prioritizes safety and reliability, these top 10 models stand out for their exceptional features and long-term dependability. Whether you prioritize advanced safety technologies, solid build quality, or low cost of ownership, there’s an SUV on this list to meet your specific needs. By considering these top contenders, you can make an informed decision when selecting a safe and reliable SUV that suits your lifestyle and driving preferences.
