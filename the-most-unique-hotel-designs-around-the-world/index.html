The Most Unique Hotel Designs around the World


When it comes to traveling, one of the most exciting aspects is staying in a unique and beautifully designed hotel. While traditional hotel designs can be comfortable and luxurious, there are some hotels around the world that take design to a whole new level. These hotels not only provide top-notch accommodation but also offer an unforgettable experience with their one-of-a-kind architecture and interior design. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most unique hotel designs around the world that are sure to leave you in awe.

1. Icehotel, Sweden:

Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi in Sweden, Icehotel is truly a one-of-a-kind destination. Every winter, the hotel is rebuilt entirely from ice and snow, making it the world’s first and largest ice hotel. The architectural design is meticulously crafted, with ice sculptures and intricate details adorning each room. From sleeping on ice beds to exploring the ice bar, guests are transported to a magical winter wonderland during their stay.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:

If you’ve ever dreamed of waking up to the sight of giraffes wandering outside your window, Giraffe Manor in Kenya is the perfect hotel for you. This boutique hotel, located on 12 acres of private land, allows guests to get up close and personal with endangered Rothschild giraffes. The manor’s design seamlessly blends with the natural environment, and guests can interact with these majestic animals during breakfast and tea time.

3. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:

For those seeking a truly unique experience, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland offers the chance to stay in glass igloos and witness the beauty of the Northern Lights. These specially designed igloos provide a cozy and warm atmosphere while offering an unobstructed view of the night sky. Imagine falling asleep under a canopy of stars and waking up to the dancing lights of the Aurora Borealis. It’s a once-in-a-lifetime experience!

4. Treehotel, Sweden:

Nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers will fall in love with Treehotel, located in the beautiful forests of Sweden. This hotel features multiple treehouses, each with its own distinct design. From mirrored cabins reflecting the surrounding landscape to UFO-shaped structures hovering amongst the trees, Treehotel offers a range of options to suit different tastes and preferences. Staying in a suspended treehouse provides a tranquil and immersive experience in nature.

5. The Manta Resort, Tanzania:

Imagine staying in a floating hotel room surrounded by crystal-clear turquoise waters. The Manta Resort, located off the coast of Pemba Island in Tanzania, offers just that. The highlight of this unique hotel is the Underwater Room, a three-level floating structure that gives guests a 360-degree view of the underwater world. With a bedroom submerged below the surface, guests can experience marine life up close and personal while enjoying the comfort and luxury of their own private paradise.

6. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada:

For those who prefer a more whimsical and unconventional accommodation, Free Spirit Spheres in British Columbia, Canada, provides a truly unique experience. These suspended spherical treehouses are accessed via suspension bridges and feature comfortable interiors with panoramic views of the surrounding forest. Sleeping among the treetops and feeling one with nature is an experience that cannot be replicated anywhere else.

7. Das Park Hotel, Austria:

Ever wondered what it would be like to stay in a concrete sewage pipe? Das Park Hotel in Austria offers a minimalist yet unique experience with its repurposed concrete pipes turned into cozy rooms. Each pipe is equipped with a comfortable bed, storage space, and large windows that let in natural light. This unconventional hotel proves that luxury can be found in the most unexpected places.

These are just a few examples of the many unique hotel designs around the world that push the boundaries of traditional accommodation. Whether it’s spending the night in an ice hotel, observing giraffes outside your window, or sleeping among the treetops, these hotels provide unforgettable experiences that will leave a lasting impression. So, if you’re looking for an extraordinary adventure, consider adding one of these sites to your travel bucket list - after all, life is too short to stay in ordinary hotels!
