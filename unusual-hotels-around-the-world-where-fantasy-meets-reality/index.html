Unusual Hotels Around the World: Where Fantasy Meets Reality


Have you ever dreamt of staying in a hotel that takes you to a different realm altogether? A place where reality merges with fantasy, and every corner is a work of art? If you’re an adventurous traveler seeking unique experiences, then look no further. In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to some of the most extraordinary and unusual hotels around the world that will leave you in awe.

Icehotel, Sweden

Imagine sleeping in a room made entirely of ice and snow, where the temperature hovers around -5 degrees Celsius. Welcome to the Icehotel, located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, Sweden. Every year, this hotel is rebuilt using ice from the nearby Torne River. Each room is a masterpiece, crafted by artists from around the world, with intricate ice sculptures and dazzling lighting. Snuggle up tight in warm reindeer hides and witness the Northern Lights dancing above you – a truly magical experience.

Giraffe Manor, Kenya

Are you a wildlife enthusiast? Then Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, is the place for you. This charming boutique hotel is home to a herd of Rothschild giraffes who casually roam the premises, often sticking their long necks through open windows during breakfast. Interact with these gentle giants and capture Instagram-worthy moments while enjoying the luxurious surroundings. Giraffe Manor is not just a hotel; it’s an encounter with nature that you’ll cherish forever.

Free Spirit Spheres, Canada

Escape the ordinary and find refuge in the enchanting Free Spirit Spheres located on Vancouver Island, Canada. These suspended treehouses offer a unique way to reconnect with nature. Nestled amidst a lush forest, each sphere is a cozy, self-contained accommodation that sways gently with the wind. Immerse yourself in the tranquility of the surrounding woodland, listen to the birdsong, and let your imagination take flight.

Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland

Ever wanted to sleep under the breathtaking beauty of the Northern Lights? At Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland, your dreams can become a reality. Stay in a glass igloo and marvel at the celestial display above, as the vibrant colors dance across the night sky. The resort also offers a range of other unique accommodations, including snow igloos and log cabins, providing a true Arctic experience.

Dog Bark Park Inn, USA

If you’re a dog lover, the Dog Bark Park Inn in Cottonwood, Idaho, is a must-visit. This quirky hotel is shaped like a giant beagle, and yes, you can actually stay inside it! The cozy interiors are adorned with dog-themed decor, and the owners, who are chainsaw artists, have filled every nook and cranny with their canine-inspired creations. It’s a haven for dog enthusiasts and a one-of-a-kind stay that will surely make tails wag.

Conclusion

Unusual hotels offer a glimpse into a world where fantasy meets reality. From ice and snow in Sweden to giraffes in Kenya, suspended treehouses in Canada to glass igloos in Finland, and a beagle-shaped hotel in the United States, these exceptional accommodations provide unforgettable experiences that will leave you in awe. So, why settle for the ordinary when you can embark on a journey to the extraordinary? Pack your bags and let your imagination run wild as you explore these unique and remarkable hotels around the world.
