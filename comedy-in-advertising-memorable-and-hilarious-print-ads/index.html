Comedy in Advertising: Memorable and Hilarious Print Ads


In the world of advertising, capturing the attention of consumers is crucial. And what better way to do that than through humor? Memorable and hilarious print ads have the power to leave a lasting impression, making us laugh and ensuring the brand stays top of mind. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most amusing and unforgettable print ads that have tickled our funny bones.

1. Volkswagen’s ‘Think Small’ Campaign:
Back in the 1960s, Volkswagen introduced a series of print ads that revolutionized the industry. Instead of focusing on the typical sleek and glamorous car shots, they took a comedic approach. One of their most iconic ads featured a tiny Volkswagen Beetle at the bottom of an otherwise empty page, with the headline ‘Think Small.’ This unexpected and humorous take on advertising instantly caught people’s attention and helped Volkswagen become a household name.

2. Old Spice’s ‘The Man Your Man Could Smell Like’:
Old Spice is known for its clever and humorous advertisements, and ‘The Man Your Man Could Smell Like’ campaign is no exception. This series of print ads showcased a shirtless, confident, and charismatic man delivering funny one-liners. These ads not only made us laugh but also created a memorable image of the brand and increased sales significantly.

3. Snickers’ ‘You’re Not You When You’re Hungry’:
Snickers took a hilarious approach to highlight the consequences of being hungry with their ‘You’re Not You When You’re Hungry’ campaign. Their print ads featured famous celebrities like Betty White and Mr. T in unexpected situations, accompanied by the tagline ‘You’re Not You When You’re Hungry.’ These ads successfully used humor to communicate their message and reinforce the idea that a Snickers bar can satisfy your cravings and restore your true self.

4. Skittles’ Quirky Print Ads:
Skittles is known for its eccentric and out-of-the-box advertising, and their print ads are no exception. They often feature bizarre scenarios and witty captions that make you do a double-take. By embracing the absurd and unexpected, Skittles manages to create a unique brand identity that stands out in the crowded candy market.

5. Doritos’ ‘Crash the Super Bowl’ Contest:
Doritos’ ‘Crash the Super Bowl’ contest allowed fans to submit their own hilarious commercials for a chance to have it aired during the Super Bowl. This user-generated content not only engaged consumers but also resulted in some of the most memorable and funny print ads. Through humor, Doritos successfully tapped into their target audience’s sense of humor and created a buzz around their brand.

These examples demonstrate the power of comedy in advertising and how it can create a lasting impact on consumers. By making us laugh, these print ads manage to break through the noise and capture our attention. They not only entertain us but also leave a positive brand association in our minds.

So, the next time you come across a funny print ad, take a moment to appreciate the creativity and wit behind it. Remember, laughter is a powerful tool in advertising, and these memorable and hilarious print ads are a testament to that.
