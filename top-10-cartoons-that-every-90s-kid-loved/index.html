Top 10 Cartoons That Every 90s Kid Loved


The 90s were a golden era for cartoons, and every kid who grew up during that time has fond memories of coming home from school and watching their favorite shows. From the unforgettable characters to the catchy theme songs, these cartoons played a significant role in shaping our childhoods. In this blog post, we will be taking a trip down memory lane as we countdown the top 10 cartoons that every 90s kid loved.

  1. Pokemon: No 90s cartoon list is complete without mentioning Pokemon. This franchise became a global phenomenon, captivating kids with its adventures of Ash Ketchum and his quest to become a Pokemon Master. With adorable creatures like Pikachu, Charmander, and Bulbasaur, Pokemon introduced us to the world of fantasy and imagination.

  2. Dragon Ball Z: This iconic anime series followed the adventures of Goku and his friends as they battled against powerful villains. Dragon Ball Z had a perfect blend of action, humor, and character development, making it a fan favorite among 90s kids. Who can forget the intense battles and the famous ‘Kamehameha’ move?

  3. Scooby-Doo: Mystery-solving adventures were at the heart of this beloved cartoon. Scooby-Doo and his gang, Velma, Daphne, Fred, and Shaggy, entertained us with their hilarious antics as they unmasked paranormal villains. This cartoon not only provided fun and laughter but also taught us the importance of teamwork and courage.

  4. Rugrats: Rugrats followed the imaginative adventures of a group of babies, including Tommy, Chuckie, and the fearless Angelica. With relatable storylines and loveable characters, this cartoon captured the essence of childhood. It gave us a glimpse into the world of imagination, friendship, and growing up.

  5. Powerpuff Girls: Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup were the three crime-fighting superheroes who protected the town of Townsville from various villains. This cartoon was unique, as it combined action-packed fights with themes like friendship and girl power. The Powerpuff Girls became role models for many girls, proving that they could be strong and independent.

  6. Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Leonardo, Donatello, Michelangelo, and Raphael were the four pizza-loving turtles who fought crime in New York City. This action-packed cartoon was a favorite among 90s kids, who loved the thrilling adventures and the witty banter between the turtles. Cowabunga!

  7. The Simpsons: The Simpsons is a timeless cartoon that continues to entertain audiences to this day. This satirical comedy follows the lives of the dysfunctional Simpson family, including the lovable troublemaker Bart. With its clever humor and relatable characters, The Simpsons became a cultural phenomenon and a staple of 90s television.

  8. Dexter’s Laboratory: Dexter, a boy genius, had a secret laboratory where he conducted his wild experiments. This cartoon brought science and humor together, as we followed Dexter’s misadventures and his interactions with his annoying sister, Dee Dee. Dexter’s Laboratory showcased the importance of curiosity and the value of family.

  9. Captain Planet and the Planeteers: This environmentally conscious cartoon introduced kids to the concept of saving the planet. With the help of their rings, the Planeteers fought against pollution and other environmental issues. Captain Planet promoted eco-awareness and inspired a generation to take care of the Earth.

  10. X-Men: Based on the popular Marvel comics, X-Men told the story of mutants with superhuman abilities fighting against evil forces. This action-packed cartoon showcased important themes like acceptance, diversity, and standing up for what is right. X-Men resonated with kids who felt different and taught us to embrace our unique traits.

These cartoons not only entertained us but also imparted valuable life lessons. They transported us to fantastical worlds, stirred our imaginations, and taught us the importance of friendship, teamwork, and kindness.

In conclusion, the 90s produced some of the most memorable cartoons of all time. From the adventures of Pokemon to the hilarious antics of Scooby-Doo, these shows are forever etched in our hearts. Whether you’re a 90s kid reminiscing about your childhood or a new generation discovering these classics, these top 10 cartoons are sure to bring joy and nostalgia. So grab a bowl of cereal, sit back, and relive the magic of the 90s through these timeless cartoons.
