The Most Shocking Spider-Man and Mary Jane Moments in Comics


Spider-Man and Mary Jane Watson’s relationship has been at the heart of the Spider-Man comics for decades. From their first meeting to their marriage and subsequent separations, their love story has captured the hearts of fans worldwide. Over the years, there have been many shocking moments involving these two iconic characters that have left readers shocked and amazed. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most shocking Spider-Man and Mary Jane moments in comics.

1. The Death of Gwen Stacy - Perhaps one of the most iconic and shocking moments in Spider-Man’s history is the death of Gwen Stacy. In The Amazing Spider-Man #121, Spider-Man’s love interest, Gwen Stacy, meets a tragic end at the hands of the Green Goblin. This moment shocked readers as it marked the first major character death in Spider-Man’s world and forever changed the trajectory of the series.

2. The Clone Saga - The Clone Saga is one of the most controversial storylines in Spider-Man’s history. It centers around the revelation that Peter Parker was actually a clone, and the real Peter had been absent from the series for years. During this time, Mary Jane played a pivotal role in supporting Ben Reilly, who believed himself to be the real Peter Parker. This revelation shocked readers and led to a major upheaval in the Spider-Man mythos.

3. One More Day - In the controversial storyline ‘One More Day,’ Spider-Man made a deal with Mephisto to save the life of Aunt May. As a result of this deal, Spider-Man’s marriage to Mary Jane was erased from existence and their relationship was reset to its early stages. This shocking turn of events left readers stunned and divided, as it marked a significant departure from the established Spider-Man lore.

4. Renew Your Vows - Following the events of ‘One More Day,’ many fans were left disappointed with the dissolution of Peter and Mary Jane’s marriage. In response to fan outcry, Marvel launched a new series titled ‘Amazing Spider-Man: Renew Your Vows,’ in which Peter and Mary Jane were married with a young daughter named Annie. This surprising move delighted fans who had long wished to see the couple together again.

5. Mary Jane’s Iron Spider Suit - In the 2006 storyline ‘The Other,’ Mary Jane received her own suit modeled after Spider-Man’s iconic costume. This shocking development not only showcased Mary Jane’s bravery and willingness to step into the role of a superhero, but also added a dynamic twist to her relationship with Peter. Seeing Mary Jane donning her own superhero costume was a thrilling and unexpected moment for readers.

6. Carnage’s Attack on Mary Jane - In the storyline ‘The Worst Foe,’ writer Kevin Shinick introduced a shocking twist involving Carnage and Mary Jane. Carnage targets Mary Jane in an attempt to get revenge on Spider-Man, leading to a brutal and intense battle between the two. This shocking turn of events revealed the lengths that villains were willing to go to hurt Spider-Man, adding a new level of danger to Mary Jane’s character.

7. The Return of Mary Jane - In the 2019 storyline ‘The Amazing Spider-Man: Red Goblin,’ Mary Jane returns to the series after a long absence. Her unexpected return shocked readers and reignited the romance between Peter and Mary Jane. This surprising development thrilled fans who had long missed their beloved couple and added a new dimension to the ongoing Spider-Man saga.

In conclusion, Spider-Man and Mary Jane’s relationship has had its fair share of shocking moments throughout the years. From character deaths to controversial storylines and surprising returns, these moments have kept readers on the edge of their seats. The enduring love story between Peter Parker and Mary Jane Watson continues to captivate readers and reminds us why Spider-Man is one of the most beloved superheroes of all time.
