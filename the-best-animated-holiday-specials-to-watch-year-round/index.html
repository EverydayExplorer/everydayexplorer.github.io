The Best Animated Holiday Specials to Watch Year-Round


The holiday season brings with it a sense of warmth, joy, and the perfect opportunity to indulge in the best animated holiday specials. While these shows are typically associated with a specific time of year, their timeless themes and heartwarming stories make them perfect for year-round enjoyment. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best animated holiday specials that you can watch anytime, regardless of the season.

1. ‘How the Grinch Stole Christmas!’ (1966):
Based on Dr. Seuss’ beloved book, this classic special tells the story of the Grinch, a grumpy creature who plans to steal Christmas from the Whos of Whoville. With its catchy songs, memorable characters, and a heartwarming message about the true meaning of Christmas, this special has earned its place as a perennial favorite.

2. ‘A Charlie Brown Christmas’ (1965):
No list of animated holiday specials would be complete without mentioning this iconic Peanuts adventure. In this special, Charlie Brown embarks on a quest to find the true spirit of Christmas amidst commercialism and materialism. It teaches important lessons about friendship, love, and the value of simplicity.

3. ‘Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer’ (1964):
This stop-motion animation, based on the song of the same name, tells the tale of Rudolph, a reindeer with a unique ability: a glowing red nose. In his journey to find acceptance and belonging, Rudolph discovers that what makes him different is also what makes him special. This heartwarming story reminds us to embrace our uniqueness.

4. ‘Frosty the Snowman’ (1969):
Narrated by Jimmy Durante, this animated special follows the magical adventures of Frosty, a snowman brought to life by a magical hat. As Frosty tries to escape the clutches of a villainous magician, he teaches us valuable lessons about friendship, sacrifice, and the importance of cherishing every moment.

5. ‘The Polar Express’ (2004):
Adapted from the children’s book by Chris Van Allsburg, this computer-animated film takes viewers on a magical train ride to the North Pole. The story follows a young boy who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and learns to believe in the spirit of Christmas. With its stunning visuals and enchanting storyline, ‘The Polar Express’ is a holiday classic for all ages.

6. ‘Mickey’s Once Upon a Christmas’ (1999):
In this anthology of three short stories, Mickey Mouse and his friends explore the true meaning of Christmas through laughter, love, and the spirit of giving. The film captures the essence of Disney magic while delivering heartwarming messages that resonate with audiences throughout the year.

7. ‘Arthur Christmas’ (2011):
This modern animated film offers a fresh take on the Santa Claus mythos. It revolves around Arthur, Santa’s clumsy youngest son, as he embarks on an adventure to ensure a little girl’s Christmas wish comes true. With its clever humor, endearing characters, and heartfelt message about the power of belief, ‘Arthur Christmas’ is a delightful watch anytime.

These are just a few examples of the best animated holiday specials that you can enjoy year-round. Each one shares themes of love, friendship, and the importance of embracing the spirit of the season. So, whether it’s a chilly winter evening or a warm summer day, these timeless classics are sure to bring joy and warmth to your heart. Sit back, relax, and let the magic of animation transport you to a world filled with holiday cheer!
