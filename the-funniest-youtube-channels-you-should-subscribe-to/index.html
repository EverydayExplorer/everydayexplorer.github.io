The Funniest YouTube Channels You Should Subscribe To


We all love a good laugh, and YouTube has become the go-to platform for discovering hilarious content. From sketch comedy to prank videos, there is an abundance of funny YouTube channels that are guaranteed to make you laugh out loud. In this article, we will introduce you to some of the funniest YouTube channels you should subscribe to for your daily dose of humor and entertainment.

  1. Smosh: Smosh is one of the oldest and most popular comedy channels on YouTube. Their sketches and parodies cover a wide range of topics, including video games, pop culture, and everyday life. With over 25 million subscribers, Smosh has established themselves as a leading comedy brand on the platform.

  2. CollegeHumor: CollegeHumor is known for their hilarious, relatable, and often outrageous comedy videos. From skits, parodies, and original series, their content is sure to leave you in stitches. They have a knack for poking fun at common situations we all encounter, making them a favorite among young adults.

  3. Funny or Die: As the name suggests, Funny or Die is all about delivering side-splitting content. Created by comedian Will Ferrell, this channel features a mix of original sketches, funny interviews, and celebrity cameos. The channel showcases a variety of comedic styles, making it a stand-out option for laughter-inducing entertainment.

  4. The Key of Awesome: If you’re a fan of music parodies, The Key of Awesome is a must-watch channel. They transform popular songs into hilarious spoofs that often outshine the originals. With their talented cast of actors and musicians, The Key of Awesome offers a fresh, funny take on some of the biggest hits in the music industry.

  5. Good Mythical Morning: Good Mythical Morning is hosted by Rhett McLaughlin and Link Neal, two charismatic friends who engage in humorous discussions, experiments, and challenges. Their chemistry and witty banter make for an entertaining show that has garnered a massive following. From taste tests to bizarre internet trends, this channel will keep you entertained for hours.

  6. The Try Guys: The Try Guys are a group of four friends who continuously put themselves in hilarious and often ridiculous situations. From trying extreme sports to taking on bizarre challenges, their bravery and comedic timing make for entertaining content. Their camaraderie and willingness to push boundaries make The Try Guys a laugh-out-loud experience.

  7. Jenna Marbles: Jenna Marbles is a personality-driven channel that showcases the comedic talents of Jenna Mourey. With her unique humor and relatability, Jenna has gained a loyal following on YouTube. Her videos range from hilarious vlogs to life advice, and everything in between. If you’re in need of a good laugh, Jenna Marbles has got you covered.

  8. Conan O’Brien: While Conan O’Brien is a household name in late-night television, his YouTube channel has also become a hub for comedic content. From behind-the-scenes footage to hilarious sketches, Conan’s channel provides a fresh take on his unique brand of comedy. With his witty writing and clever delivery, he continues to entertain audiences of all ages.

  9. NigaHiga: Created by Ryan Higa, NigaHiga is known for its witty and clever skits. Ryan’s comedic timing and ability to parody popular culture make his videos a hit with viewers. Whether he’s reenacting famous movie scenes or giving his humorous take on trending topics, NigaHiga always delivers laughs.

  10. EpicMealTime: For those with a taste for culinary comedy, EpicMealTime is the channel to follow. They create outrageously large, unhealthy meals while injecting humor and absurdity into every video. From bacon-wrapped everything to over-the-top fast-food creations, EpicMealTime will undoubtedly leave you hungry for more laughs.

These are just a few of the many hilarious YouTube channels that are worth subscribing to. Whether you’re in the mood for sketch comedy, pranks, or musical parodies, there is something for everyone in the vast world of YouTube. So sit back, relax, and prepare to laugh until your stomach hurts, as these channels provide some of the funniest content on the internet.
