How to Achieve the Perfect Winged Eyeliner Look


Winged eyeliner has been a timeless makeup trend that adds a touch of elegance and allure to any look. However, mastering the art of creating the perfect wing can be a daunting task. Fear not! In this comprehensive guide, we will walk you through the step-by-step process of achieving that flawless winged eyeliner look that will make heads turn.

1. Prep your canvas:
Before diving into the world of winged eyeliner, it is essential to prepare your eyes for a flawless application. Start by gently cleansing your eyelids to remove any excess oil or makeup residue. This will ensure a smooth and long-lasting finish.

2. Choose the right tools:
Investing in good quality eyeliner is crucial for achieving the perfect wing. You can opt for a liquid eyeliner, gel liner, or a felt-tip pen, depending on your preference and skill level. Remember to choose a shade that complements your eye color and desired look.

3. Map out your wing:
Creating a winged eyeliner look is all about precision and symmetry. Begin by using a light-colored eyeliner pencil or eyeshadow to draw a guide line from the outer corner of your eye, following the natural curve of your lower lash line. This guide will help you achieve a symmetrical wing on both eyes.

4. Start at the inner corner:
To ensure a smooth and controlled application, start by drawing a thin line along your upper lash line, starting from the inner corner of your eye. This will serve as the base for your wing.

5. Create the wing:
Using the guide line you drew earlier, extend the line outward from the outer corner of your eye, gradually increasing the thickness as you reach the desired wing length. The angle and thickness of the wing can be adjusted according to your personal preference and eye shape.

6. Connect the wing:
To complete the winged eyeliner look, draw a thin line from the outer corner of your wing towards the center of your upper lash line. Carefully connect this line with the initial line you drew at the inner corner, ensuring a seamless and cohesive look.

7. Fill in the gaps:
To intensify the winged effect, fill in any gaps between the lines you created. This will result in a bold and defined wing that enhances your eyes.

8. Clean up and perfect:
Use a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover or concealer to clean up any mistakes or smudges. This step will give your winged eyeliner a polished and professional finish.

9. Set it in place:
To ensure your winged eyeliner lasts all day, gently dust a small amount of translucent powder over your liner. This will help prevent smudging and keep your wing looking sharp.

10. Experiment and practice:
Remember, practice makes perfect! Don’t be discouraged if your first attempts don’t turn out as expected. Winged eyeliner is a skill that takes time to master. Experiment with different techniques, liner types, and wing shapes to find what works best for you.

With these step-by-step instructions, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner look is within your reach. Remember to be patient, take your time, and most importantly, have fun with it! So go ahead and unleash your inner winged goddess. You’ve got this!
