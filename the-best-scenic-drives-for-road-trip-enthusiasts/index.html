The Best Scenic Drives for Road Trip Enthusiasts


As the open road stretches out before you, the thrill of adventure beckons. There’s something special about hitting the highway, feeling the wind in your hair, and taking in the breathtaking views that unfold before your eyes. For road trip enthusiasts, the journey is just as important as the destination. And when it comes to scenic drives, there are few experiences more exhilarating.

From the rugged coastlines of the Pacific Northwest to the sun-kissed highways of the American Southwest, the United States is home to some of the most spectacular scenic drives on the planet. In this article, we’ll take you on a journey to explore the best scenic drives for road trip enthusiasts.

Pacific Coast Highway (California, USA)

One of the most iconic scenic drives in the world, the Pacific Coast Highway (PCH) stretches 655 miles from San Francisco to San Diego. This winding road takes you along the rugged California coastline, offering breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean, rocky cliffs, and sandy beaches. Be sure to stop at Bixby Bridge, Big Sur, and Malibu to take in the stunning vistas.

Going-to-the-Sun Road (Montana, USA)

Located in Glacier National Park, Going-to-the-Sun Road is a 50-mile scenic drive that spans the Continental Divide. This alpine route takes you through towering mountain peaks, alpine lakes, and lush forests. Be prepared for breathtaking views of glaciers, waterfalls, and wildflowers.

Blue Ridge Parkway (North Carolina and Virginia, USA)

Stretching 469 miles through the Blue Ridge Mountains, this scenic drive offers stunning views of rolling hills, waterfalls, and historic sites. Take in the fall foliage, visit the Cradle of Forestry, and stop at the Linn Cove Viaduct for a breathtaking view of Grandfather Mountain.

Great River Road (Minnesota to Louisiana, USA)

Following the Mississippi River, the Great River Road stretches 3,000 miles from Minnesota to Louisiana. This scenic drive takes you through rolling hills, river valleys, and historic towns. Be sure to stop at the Gateway Arch in St. Louis, the Iowa Great River Road, and the Mark Twain National Forest.

Overseas Highway (Florida, USA)

Connecting the Florida Keys, the Overseas Highway is a 113-mile scenic drive that takes you over 42 bridges and islands. This tropical route offers stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, mangrove forests, and coral reefs. Be sure to stop at Key West, Islamorada, and the Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum.

Icefields Parkway (Alberta, Canada)

Located in Banff and Jasper National Parks, the Icefields Parkway is a 144-mile scenic drive that takes you through the Canadian Rockies. This alpine route offers breathtaking views of glaciers, lakes, and towering mountain peaks. Be prepared for stunning vistas of Athabasca Glacier, Lake Louise, and Moraine Lake.

Atlantic Coast Highway (Maine, USA)

Stretching 385 miles from Kittery to Calais, the Atlantic Coast Highway takes you along the rugged Maine coastline. This scenic drive offers stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, rocky cliffs, and sandy beaches. Be sure to stop at Acadia National Park, Pemaquid Point Lighthouse, and the Maine Maritime Museum.

Beartooth Highway (Montana and Wyoming, USA)

Connecting the Beartooth Mountains, this 68-mile scenic drive takes you through alpine lakes, glaciers, and towering mountain peaks. Be prepared for breathtaking views of the Beartooth Plateau, the Absaroka-Beartooth Wilderness, and the Shoshone River.

Overseas Highway (New Zealand)

Spanning the North Island, the Overseas Highway takes you through rolling hills, coastal towns, and historic sites. Be sure to stop at the Bay of Islands, the Coromandel Peninsula, and the Tongariro National Park.

Transfagarasan Highway (Romania)

Located in the Carpathian Mountains, the Transfagarasan Highway is a 56-mile scenic drive that takes you through alpine lakes, waterfalls, and historic sites. Be prepared for breathtaking views of the Fagaras Mountains, the Olt Valley, and the Bistrița River.

Milford Road (New Zealand)

Located in Fiordland National Park, the Milford Road is a 60-mile scenic drive that takes you through stunning fjords, waterfalls, and glaciers. Be prepared for breathtaking views of Milford Sound, Lake Te Anau, and the Fiordland Mountains.

Blue Danube Scenic Road (Austria)

Located in the Wachau Valley, the Blue Danube Scenic Road takes you along the Danube River, offering stunning views of vineyards, castles, and historic sites. Be sure to stop at the Melk Abbey, the Krems Cathedral, and the Wachau Valley.

The Best Scenic Drives for Road Trip Enthusiasts

Whether you’re a seasoned road tripper or just starting out, these scenic drives offer some of the most breathtaking views on the planet. From the rugged coastlines of the Pacific Northwest to the sun-kissed highways of the American Southwest, there’s something for every road trip enthusiast. So pack up your car, hit the open road, and get ready for the adventure of a lifetime.
