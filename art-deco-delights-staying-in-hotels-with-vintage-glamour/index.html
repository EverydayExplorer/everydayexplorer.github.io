Art Deco Delights: Staying in Hotels with Vintage Glamour


Welcome to a world where vintage glamour meets modern luxury - Art Deco Delights! In this blog post, we’ll take you on a journey through time to discover the allure of staying in hotels that embrace the iconic Art Deco style. From the striking architecture to the lavish interiors, these hotels exude an elegance that transports guests back to the roaring 1920s and 1930s. So, buckle up and get ready for a glamorous adventure!

Art Deco, a style that flourished during the interwar period, is characterized by its geometric shapes, bold colors, and lavish ornamentation. It emerged as a symbol of opulence and sophistication, reflecting the spirit of the Jazz Age. Today, many hotels around the world have embraced this timeless aesthetic, offering guests a chance to experience a slice of history while enjoying modern comforts.

One of the most iconic Art Deco hotels is the legendary Waldorf Astoria in New York City. This architectural gem has been synonymous with luxury since its opening in 1931. From the moment you step into its grand lobby adorned with intricate chandeliers and elaborate moldings, you’ll feel like a true Hollywood star. The meticulously restored guest rooms and suites feature plush furnishings, marble bathrooms, and panoramic views of the city skyline. Indulge in the hotel’s fine dining options, relax in the spa, or simply soak in the atmosphere of a bygone era.

If you’re looking to immerse yourself in Art Deco splendor on the other side of the Atlantic, look no further than the Claridge’s in London. This iconic hotel has been a favorite of royalty, celebrities, and discerning travelers since the 1920s. The lavish Art Deco interiors, designed by renowned architects and artists of the era, will transport you back to an age of elegance and sophistication. From the stunning chandeliers to the exquisite marble bathrooms, every detail has been carefully curated to create a truly immersive experience. Treat yourself to afternoon tea in the elegant Foyer or enjoy a cocktail at the stylish Bar at Claridge’s.

For those seeking a taste of Art Deco in the heart of Paris, the Hôtel Lutetia is an absolute must-visit. Built in 1910 and recently restored to its former glory, this landmark hotel showcases a perfect blend of Art Nouveau and Art Deco influences. The elegantly appointed rooms and suites offer a luxurious retreat, while the hotel’s restaurants and bars provide a delightful culinary journey. Take a leisurely stroll through the charming streets of Saint-Germain-des-Prés or relax in the hotel’s exclusive spa, which features an impressive mosaic-lined swimming pool.

These are just a few examples of the many remarkable Art Deco hotels around the world that transport guests to a bygone era of glamour and elegance. Whether you’re a history buff, an architecture enthusiast, or simply someone who appreciates the finer things in life, staying in one of these hotels is sure to be an unforgettable experience.

So, why not treat yourself to a trip back in time and indulge in the vintage charm of Art Deco? Whether you choose to explore the vibrant streets of New York City, the timeless elegance of London, or the romantic allure of Paris, you’re guaranteed to be captivated by the magic of these Art Deco Delights. Book your stay and get ready to be transported to a world of vintage glamour and modern luxury!
