How to Keep Your Makeup from Melting in the Summer


Summer is a time of heat, humidity, and all kinds of excellent outdoor activities. However, for makeup enthusiasts, summer can also mean the struggle of keeping their carefully applied makeup intact. There’s nothing more frustrating than spending time perfecting your makeup look only to have it melt away under the scorching sun. But fear not! With a few simple tips and tricks, you can keep your makeup from melting in the summer and ensure that it stays flawless all day long.

1. Prep Your Skin:
Before applying any makeup, it’s important to properly prepare your skin. Start by cleansing your face with a gentle cleanser to remove excess oil and sweat. Follow it up with a lightweight, oil-free moisturizer that is suitable for your skin type. This step is crucial as it helps create a smooth canvas for your makeup and prevents it from sliding off.

2. Use a Primer:
A primer is a must-have product in your summer makeup routine. Not only does it create a barrier between your skin and makeup, but it also helps to control oil and keep your makeup in place. Look for a mattifying primer if you have oily skin, or a hydrating primer if your skin tends to get dry in the summer heat. Apply a pea-sized amount all over your face, focusing on your T-zone, where makeup tends to break down the fastest.

3. Opt for Lightweight Formulas:
During the summer months, it’s best to go for lightweight formulations when it comes to foundation, concealer, and other makeup products. Heavy, full-coverage products are more likely to melt and feel uncomfortable on your skin. Instead, choose a lightweight, oil-free foundation or a tinted moisturizer that provides coverage without suffocating your skin.

4. Set Your Makeup:
Setting your makeup is a crucial step in ensuring its longevity. After applying your foundation and concealer, set it in place with a translucent powder. This helps to absorb excess oil and prevents your makeup from slipping off. Use a fluffy brush to lightly dust the powder all over your face, paying special attention to your T-zone.

5. Waterproof Your Eyes:
One of the most common complaints during summer is mascara smudging and eyeliner smearing. To avoid raccoon eyes, opt for waterproof mascara and eyeliner. These formulas are specially designed to withstand sweat and humidity, keeping your eye makeup intact all day long. Additionally, using an eyeshadow primer can also help prevent creasing and fading of your eyeshadow.

6. Avoid Heavy Cream Products:
Cream-based makeup products may feel heavy on the skin and have a higher chance of melting in the heat. During the summer, swap out your cream blushes and eyeshadows for powder alternatives. Powder formulas are lightweight, blend easily, and are less likely to turn cakey or smudge in the heat.

7. Carry Blotting Papers:
Even with all the preventative measures, excess oil and sweat can still accumulate on your skin throughout the day. Instead of piling on more powder, which can look cakey, reach for blotting papers to absorb the excess oil and refresh your makeup. Gently press the blotting paper onto your skin, particularly in the T-zone, to remove any shine without disturbing your makeup.

8. Set Your Makeup with a Setting Spray:
To amp up the longevity of your makeup, finish off your routine with a setting spray. Setting sprays are designed to create a protective layer on top of your makeup, helping it stay in place even in hot and humid conditions. Mist the product evenly over your face, keeping your eyes closed, and let it dry naturally. Not only will it help your makeup last longer, but it will also give your skin a fresh, dewy finish.

9. Keep Your Makeup Away from Direct Sunlight:
Exposing your makeup products to direct sunlight can cause them to melt or spoil. To prevent this, store your makeup in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. This will help preserve the integrity of the products and prevent them from melting or becoming less effective.

10. Take Care of Your Skin:
Lastly, taking care of your skin’s health is crucial in ensuring that your makeup stays intact. Proper hydration, regular exfoliation, and sunscreen application are all essential steps in maintaining a healthy complexion. When your skin is healthy, your makeup will adhere better and last longer.

In conclusion, keeping your makeup from melting in the summer doesn’t have to be a daunting task. By following these tips and tricks, you can enjoy the sunny season while looking flawless. Prepping your skin, using lightweight products, setting your makeup, and protecting it from sunlight are all essential steps. Remember to take care of your skin’s health as well to ensure a long-lasting and beautiful makeup look throughout the summer months. With these strategies in mind, you’ll be able to strut your stuff confidently, knowing your makeup will stay put even on the hottest of days.
