How to Incorporate Animal Prints into Your Outfits


Are you someone who loves to add a touch of wildness and excitement to your wardrobe? Are you tired of sticking to the same old neutral colors and patterns? If the answer is yes, then it’s time to embrace the power of animal prints! Animal prints have been a fashion trend for decades and continue to be popular in the fashion world. From leopard to zebra to snakeskin, animal prints can give your outfits a bold and fierce look. In this blog post, we will explore some tips on how to incorporate animal prints into your outfits to add that extra oomph factor.

  1. Start Small - If you’re new to wearing animal prints, it’s best to start small and work your way up. Begin by incorporating accessories such as a belt, scarf, or clutch in an animal print. These pieces can instantly elevate a basic outfit and add a touch of glamour without being overpowering. Plus, they are easy to mix and match with your existing wardrobe.

  2. Mix and Match - Don’t be afraid to mix different animal prints together. Playing with different patterns, such as leopard with zebra or snake with cheetah, can create a unique and eye-catching look. To keep the outfit balanced, make sure to choose one dominant print and use the other as an accent.

  3. Pair with Neutrals - Animal prints go hand in hand with neutral colors. Pairing an animal print item with black, white, beige, or gray will create a chic and sophisticated look. For example, a leopard print blouse paired with black trousers and heels can instantly transform a simple outfit into a stylish ensemble.

  4. Choose the Right Fit - When incorporating animal prints into your outfits, it’s important to choose the right fit. Animal prints can bring attention to certain areas, so make sure the item flatters your body shape. A tailored animal print dress or a fitted animal print blazer can accentuate your curves in all the right ways.

  5. Accessorize Wisely - If you’re not ready to go all-out with an animal print garment, accessories are the perfect way to dip your toes into this trend. Opt for animal print shoes, a handbag, or even a statement belt. These small touches can instantly update your look and show off your fashion-forwardness.

  6. Layer with Solid Colors - Layering is a great way to incorporate animal prints into your outfits, especially during transitional seasons. For a stylish and trendy look, layer an animal print blouse under a solid-colored blazer or cardigan. This adds depth and interest to your outfit while keeping it balanced and versatile.

  7. Experiment with Texture - Animal prints come in a variety of textures, such as silk, velvet, or faux fur. Experimenting with different textures can add dimension and richness to your outfit. For example, a snakeskin print skirt paired with a chunky knit sweater creates a perfect blend of feminine and cozy.

  8. Try a Statement Piece - If you’re feeling bold and adventurous, try incorporating a statement piece in an animal print. A leopard print coat, a snakeskin jumpsuit, or even a cheetah print maxi dress are guaranteed to turn heads and make a fashion statement. Remember to keep the rest of your outfit simple to let the statement piece shine.

  9. Don’t Forget about Animal Print Makeup - Animal prints can also be incorporated into your makeup routine. Experiment with leopard print eye shadow, zebra print nails, or even a cheetah-inspired makeup look. This allows you to showcase your wild side in a fun and creative way.

In conclusion, animal prints are a versatile and timeless trend that can add excitement and personality to your outfits. Whether you prefer a subtle touch through accessories or want to make a bold statement with a statement piece, incorporating animal prints into your wardrobe is a surefire way to stand out from the crowd. Remember to start small, mix and match patterns, and pair it with neutrals to create a chic and fashionable look. So go ahead, embrace your wild side, and let animal prints take center stage in your wardrobe!
