Hotel Hacks: Secrets to Getting Upgraded Rooms


Are you tired of staying in the same old standard hotel room? Do you dream of being upgraded to a luxurious suite without breaking the bank? Well, you’re in luck! In this blog post, we will reveal some insider secrets and hotel hacks that can increase your chances of getting upgraded rooms. So, let’s dive right in!

1. Timing is Key

One of the most important factors in securing an upgraded room is timing. Avoid peak travel seasons when hotels are usually fully booked. Instead, opt for off-peak periods when occupancy rates are lower. By choosing the right time to travel, you increase the chances of getting an upgraded room as hotels may have more availability.

2. Loyalty Pays Off

Being a loyal customer can work wonders when it comes to getting an upgrade. Join hotel loyalty programs and make sure to book directly through the hotel’s website. By doing so, you not only earn points but also increase your chances of being recognized as a valued guest. Hotels often prioritize loyal customers when it comes to room upgrades.

3. Be Polite and Friendly

When checking in, always be polite and friendly to the hotel staff. A smile and a kind word can go a long way in creating a positive impression. Remember, hotel employees deal with numerous guests every day, and being pleasant and respectful can set you apart. Building a good rapport with the staff might just make them more inclined to grant you an upgrade.

4. Dress to Impress

Believe it or not, your attire can influence the way hotel staff perceive you. Dressing smartly and looking presentable can make a difference. It shows that you take pride in your appearance and are likely to appreciate an upgraded room. So, leave your sweatpants and flip-flops at home and opt for a more sophisticated look when checking in.

5. Celebrate Special Occasions

If you’re celebrating a special occasion, whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or honeymoon, make sure to let the hotel staff know. Hotels love to help their guests celebrate and may be more inclined to provide an upgraded room to make your stay even more memorable. Don’t be shy about mentioning your special occasion during the booking process or at check-in.

6. Engage with Social Media

Hotels are increasingly using social media to engage with their guests. Follow the hotel’s social media accounts, interact with their posts, and leave positive comments. You never know, the hotel might just take notice and reward your online engagement with an upgraded room.

7. Tip Generously

While tipping shouldn’t be seen as a bribe, it can be a gesture of appreciation for exceptional service. A generous tip at check-in can create a positive impression and may increase your chances of getting an upgraded room. However, it’s important to remember that tipping alone doesn’t guarantee an upgrade. It should be done genuinely and without any expectations.

8. Be Flexible

Flexibility is key when it comes to getting upgraded rooms. If the hotel staff offers you a different room than the one you booked, be open to the suggestion. Sometimes, hotels may have limited availability in certain room categories but can offer you an upgrade to a different room type. Embrace the opportunity and enjoy the unexpected upgrade.

9. Leave Positive Reviews

After your stay, take the time to leave positive reviews on popular review websites. Hotels value positive feedback, and by sharing your positive experiences, you contribute to their reputation. Hotels may take note of your kind words and reward you with an upgraded room during your next visit.

10. Manage Your Expectations

Last but not least, it’s important to manage your expectations. While these hotel hacks can increase your chances of getting upgraded rooms, they are not foolproof. Upgrades are subject to availability and hotel policies. So, even if you don’t get an upgrade, remember to enjoy your stay and make the most of the room you booked.

In conclusion, getting upgraded rooms is not as elusive as it may seem. By following these hotel hacks and implementing them during your next stay, you increase your chances of experiencing a more luxurious accommodation. Remember, timing, loyalty, politeness, and flexibility can make all the difference. Happy travels and enjoy your upgraded room!

Note: This blog post is intended for informational purposes only and does not guarantee the availability of upgraded rooms in every hotel.
