The Best Hunting Backpacks for Carrying Gear and Harvests


Hunting backpacks are an essential piece of equipment for any hunting enthusiast. They not only keep your gear organized and easily accessible, but they also provide a means of carrying out any harvests you may make throughout your hunting trip. However, with so many hunting backpacks on the market, it can be hard to know which one to choose. That’s why we’ve put together this guide on the best hunting backpacks for carrying gear and harvests.

1. Eberlestock X2 Pack

The Eberlestock X2 Pack is an excellent choice for hunters who want a versatile backpack that can hold a range of gear and is comfortable to wear for extended periods. The pack features an expandable main compartment that can hold up to 2,000 cubic inches of gear, and it also has nine other pockets and compartments for smaller items. The X2 Pack also has a rifle scabbard that can hold most rifles, making it a great option for hunters who need to keep their hands free.

2. Tenzing TZ 1250 Pack

The Tenzing TZ 1250 Pack is perfect for hunters who need to carry a lot of gear and want a backpack that is lightweight and comfortable to wear. The pack has a total capacity of 1,250 cubic inches and features a range of pockets and compartments, including a large main compartment, a front shove-it pocket, and a hydration pocket. The TZ 1250 Pack also has a range of compression straps and load lifters that help distribute weight evenly across your body.

3. Badlands Superday Pack

The Badlands Superday Pack is a top-of-the-line hunting backpack that features a combination of durability, comfort, and organization. The pack has a total capacity of 1,950 cubic inches and features a T-6 aircraft-grade aluminum frame that helps distribute weight evenly across your body. The Superday Pack also has a range of pockets and compartments, including a main compartment, a front pocket with a built-in pistol holster, and side pockets for water bottles or spotting scopes.

4. Mystery Ranch Metcalf Pack

The Mystery Ranch Metcalf Pack is an excellent choice for hunters who need to carry a lot of gear and want a backpack that is both comfortable and durable. The pack has a total capacity of 4,350 cubic inches and features a range of pockets and compartments, including a main compartment that can expand to hold larger items. The Metcalf Pack also has a built-in rifle scabbard, making it easy to carry your rifle on your back while keeping your hands free.

5. ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Pack

The ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Pack is a great option for hunters who want a backpack that can carry large items, such as a meat hauler, but is still comfortable to wear for extended periods. The pack has a capacity of 5,250 cubic inches and features a range of pockets and compartments, including a meat shelf with a capacity of up to 200 pounds. The Commander Pack also has a hydration pocket and a range of compression straps to help distribute weight evenly across your body.

Conclusion

When it comes to hunting backpacks, there are many factors to consider, such as capacity, durability, and comfort. The five backpacks we’ve highlighted above are all excellent options for hunters who need to carry gear and harvests. Whether you’re looking for a versatile backpack like the Eberlestock X2 Pack or a large-capacity backpack like the ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Pack, there’s a hunting backpack out there for everyone.
