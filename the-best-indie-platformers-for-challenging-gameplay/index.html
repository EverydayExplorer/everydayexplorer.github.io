The Best Indie Platformers for Challenging Gameplay


Are you tired of the mainstream video games that offer the same old formulaic gameplay? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the world of indie platformers, where creativity and challenging gameplay take center stage. From pixelated adventures to hand-drawn masterpieces, these games will test your skills and keep you entertained for hours on end. So grab your controller or keyboard and get ready to dive into the best indie platformers for challenging gameplay!

1. ‘Celeste’ - Developed by Matt Makes Games, ‘Celeste’ is a critically acclaimed platformer that combines tight controls with a heartfelt story. You play as Madeline, a young girl who embarks on a journey to climb the treacherous Celeste Mountain. With its elegant level design and punishing difficulty, ‘Celeste’ is a must-play for platforming enthusiasts.

2. ‘Hollow Knight’ - Dive into the hauntingly beautiful world of ‘Hollow Knight’ by Team Cherry. This metroidvania-style platformer takes you on an epic adventure through the decaying kingdom of Hallownest. With its atmospheric soundtrack, stunning hand-drawn visuals, and challenging boss battles, ‘Hollow Knight’ offers an immersive experience that will keep you hooked for hours.

3. ‘Super Meat Boy’ - Prepare for a fast-paced and unforgiving experience with ‘Super Meat Boy’ by Team Meat. This retro-style platformer follows the adventures of Meat Boy, a small, animated meat cube on a mission to rescue his girlfriend, Bandage Girl, from the clutches of the evil Dr. Fetus. With its precise controls and relentless difficulty, ‘Super Meat Boy’ will put your platforming skills to the test.

4. ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ - Embark on a breathtaking journey through a visually stunning world in ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ by Moon Studios. As Ori, a guardian spirit, you must restore the forest’s balance by navigating through challenging platforming sequences and solving puzzles. With its emotional storytelling, beautiful art style, and fluid gameplay, ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ is an indie gem that shouldn’t be missed.

5. ‘Cuphead’ - Inspired by 1930s cartoons, ‘Cuphead’ by StudioMDHR offers a unique blend of run-and-gun action and challenging boss battles. Play as Cuphead or Mugman and traverse a hand-drawn world filled with colorful characters and jazz-infused music. With its old-school difficulty and stunning visuals, ‘Cuphead’ is a love letter to classic platformers that will test your reflexes and perseverance.

6. ‘Shovel Knight’ - Take a trip down memory lane with ‘Shovel Knight’ by Yacht Club Games. This retro-style platformer pays homage to the 8-bit era with its charming pixel art and catchy soundtrack. Play as the titular Shovel Knight and wield your trusty shovel as you battle against the Order of No Quarter to save your beloved Shield Knight. With its challenging levels and nostalgic gameplay, ‘Shovel Knight’ is a must-play for fans of classic platformers.

7. ‘Inside’ - From the creators of ‘Limbo’ comes ‘Inside’ by Playdead. Step into a dark and atmospheric world as a young boy who must navigate through a series of puzzles and platforming challenges. With its minimalist storytelling, eerie ambiance, and thought-provoking gameplay, ‘Inside’ offers a unique and immersive experience that will leave you questioning its mysterious narrative.

These are just a few examples of the best indie platformers for challenging gameplay. Each game offers a unique experience that will test your skills, push your limits, and keep you engaged from start to finish. So, if you’re craving a break from the mainstream, give these indie gems a try and discover the joy of challenging platformers. Happy gaming!
