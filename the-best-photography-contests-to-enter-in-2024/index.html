The Best Photography Contests to Enter in 2024


Photography is not just a hobby; it’s an art form that allows individuals to express their creativity, capture moments, and share their unique perspectives. As we step into 2024, photographers around the world are presented with numerous opportunities to showcase their work and gain recognition through various contests. This blog post will explore some of the best photography contests to enter in 2024, highlighting their unique themes, entry requirements, and the potential rewards for participants.

1. The Sony World Photography Awards

Every year, the Sony World Photography Awards attracts thousands of photographers globally, making it one of the most prestigious photography competitions. In 2024, the contest features several categories, including Professional, Open, Youth, and Student competitions.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: January 14, 2024, for Professional and January 7, 2024, for Open categories.
  • Entry Fee: Free for Open; fees apply for Professional submissions.

Why Enter?

Winners receive cash prizes, Sony camera equipment, and international exposure through exhibitions. This contest is ideal for photographers looking to establish their reputation on a global scale.

2. National Geographic Photo Contest

The National Geographic Photo Contest is a revered competition that invites photographers to submit their best work that embodies the spirit of exploration and storytelling.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: March 31, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: Free to enter.

Why Enter?

Winners may see their work featured in National Geographic magazine and online platforms, gaining immense visibility. This contest is perfect for those who capture the beauty of nature, wildlife, and cultural moments.

3. The International Photography Awards (IPA)

The IPA celebrates photographers from all over the world, offering an extensive range of categories from Fine Art to Advertising.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: July 31, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: Varies by category; typically around $20 to $30.

Why Enter?

Winners receive cash prizes, trophies, and the opportunity to exhibit their work in New York City. This competition is ideal for those looking to network within the photography industry.

4. The LensCulture Exposure Awards

The LensCulture Exposure Awards focus on innovative and contemporary photography, encouraging artists to push boundaries and explore new ideas.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: April 30, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: $15 for early entries, increasing closer to the deadline.

Why Enter?

Winners receive cash prizes, worldwide exposure, and opportunities for portfolio reviews by industry professionals. This contest is particularly beneficial for emerging photographers seeking to make their mark.

5. The Wildlife Photographer of the Year

Run by the Natural History Museum in London, this contest is dedicated to celebrating the world’s best wildlife photography. It is an opportunity to showcase the beauty and fragility of the natural world.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: February 14, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: £30 for adult entries; free for under-18s.

Why Enter?

Winning this contest not only brings prestige but also the chance to have work exhibited in a high-profile setting. This competition is perfect for those passionate about conservation and wildlife.

6. The Black and White Spider Awards

This competition is dedicated to black and white photography, offering a unique platform for artists to explore this timeless medium.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: January 31, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: $25 for the first entry; $15 for additional entries.

Why Enter?

Winners receive accolades and recognition in the form of trophies and certificates. This contest is ideal for photographers who specialize in monochrome images and want to showcase their skills.

7. The Epson International Pano Awards

This contest focuses on panoramic photography, celebrating the best in landscape and portrait panoramas.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: July 31, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: $25 for the first image; discounts for multiple submissions.

Why Enter?

The winners receive cash prizes and recognition in the photography community. This competition is perfect for those who enjoy capturing expansive landscapes and immersive scenes.

8. The Photography Show’s Photo 24

Photo 24 is a unique 24-hour photography challenge that takes place in various cities, allowing photographers to capture the essence of their surroundings in a limited timeframe.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: Event dates vary; check the official website for specifics.
  • Entry Fee: Free to participate.

Why Enter?

This contest encourages creativity under pressure and offers the chance to win awards and recognition. It’s a great opportunity for street photographers and urban explorers.

9. The Travel Photographer of the Year

This competition is dedicated to travel photography, inviting photographers to share their adventures and the cultures they encounter.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: May 31, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: £10 for each entry.

Why Enter?

Winners receive cash prizes and their work is showcased online and in exhibitions. This contest is ideal for those who love to explore the world and document their experiences.

10. The Portrait of Humanity Awards

These awards celebrate the art of portraiture and invite photographers to showcase their interpretation of what it means to be human.

Entry Details:

  • Deadline: April 15, 2024.
  • Entry Fee: £12 per entry.

Why Enter?

Winners receive cash prizes and the chance to have their work exhibited globally. This competition is perfect for portrait photographers looking to make a statement.

Conclusion

Entering photography contests can be a fulfilling way to enhance your skills, gain exposure, and connect with fellow photographers. As 2024 unfolds, these contests provide a valuable platform for both amateur and professional photographers to showcase their talents. Whether you’re interested in wildlife, travel, portraits, or experimental photography, there’s a contest for you.

Take advantage of these opportunities, refine your craft, and share your unique vision with the world. Remember, every photograph tells a story, and contests are a great way to share yours. Happy shooting!
