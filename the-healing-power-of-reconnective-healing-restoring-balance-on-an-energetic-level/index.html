This suggests that Reconnective Healing has the ability to restore our energetic blueprint and promote overall well-being.

How Reconnective Healing Works

Reconnective Healing works by accessing a spectrum of healing frequencies that exist in the universe. These frequencies contain information and energy that can facilitate healing on multiple levels – physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual. During a healing session, a practitioner acts as a conduit, allowing these frequencies to flow through them and into the client’s energy field.

The healing frequencies of Reconnective Healing are intelligent and know exactly what is needed for healing. They work on a deep level, targeting the root cause of imbalances and facilitating the body’s innate ability to heal itself. Reconnective Healing offers a wide range of benefits for those who seek its healing power. Some of the benefits reported by people who have experienced Reconnective Healing include:
