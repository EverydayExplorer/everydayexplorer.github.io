Decorating with Vintage Maps: Adding Wanderlust to your Home


In recent years, there has been a resurgence in the popularity of vintage maps as a home decor element. These unique pieces not only add a touch of nostalgia and history to your space but also create a sense of wanderlust and adventure. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or simply someone with a love for all things vintage, incorporating vintage maps into your home decor can be a fun and creative way to showcase your personality and interests.

There are countless ways to decorate with vintage maps, from creating a focal point on your walls to incorporating them into furniture and accessories. Here are some ideas to inspire you to add a touch of wanderlust to your home.

1. Framed Artwork: One of the simplest and most effective ways to showcase vintage maps is by framing them as artwork. Choose maps that feature areas you’ve traveled to, dream destinations, or simply maps that catch your eye. Frame them in sleek and modern frames for a contemporary look or opt for ornate vintage frames to enhance the old-world charm of the maps.

2. Wall Murals: If you’re looking to make a bold statement, consider using vintage maps as wall murals. This is a fantastic option for those who want to transform an entire wall into a captivating focal point. Choose a map that covers a significant area and have it custom printed as a wall mural. This will instantly transport you to different parts of the world and create a sense of adventure and exploration in your space.

3. Map Decoupage: Another creative way to incorporate vintage maps into your decor is through decoupage. Apply pieces of vintage maps onto various surfaces such as tabletops, trays, or even furniture pieces like dressers or cabinets. This technique adds a unique and personalized touch to your space while also preserving the beauty and history of the vintage maps.

4. Gallery Wall: Create a gallery wall using a combination of vintage maps, travel photographs, and other travel-related artwork. This is a great opportunity to showcase your wanderlust and display your personal travel experiences. Mix and match different-sized frames, and consider adding other elements like postcards, travel quotes, or souvenirs to make the gallery wall truly unique.

5. Vintage Map Pillows and Upholstery: Add a cozy and whimsical touch to your living space by incorporating vintage map pillows or using vintage map upholstery in your furniture pieces. This is a great way to infuse your love for travel into your everyday life, making your home a reflection of your adventurous spirit.

6. Unique Accessories: Look for vintage map accessories to add the finishing touches to your decor. Consider vintage map coasters, luggage tags, or even a globe adorned with vintage maps. These small details can tie your entire theme together and make your space feel truly curated and cohesive.

When it comes to sourcing vintage maps, there are several options available. Hunt for them at local flea markets, antique stores, or online marketplaces. Keep in mind that vintage maps can come in various sizes, colors, and styles, so be sure to select ones that complement your overall aesthetic.

In conclusion, decorating with vintage maps is an excellent way to add a sense of wanderlust and adventure to your home decor. Whether you choose to frame them as artwork, use them in decoupage projects, or incorporate them into unique accessories, vintage maps are sure to spark conversation and inspire your inner traveler. Transform your space into a personal sanctuary that reflects your love for exploration and history, and let the vintage maps transport you to far-flung destinations without ever leaving home.
