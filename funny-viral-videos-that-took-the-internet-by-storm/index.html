Funny Viral Videos That Took the Internet by Storm


In the vast realm of the internet, funny viral videos have become a significant part of our daily lives. These short but hilarious clips have the power to captivate millions of viewers, causing them to be shared across various social media platforms. From cats doing peculiar things to people’s epic fails, these videos never fail to bring a smile to our faces and brighten up our day. In this blog, we will explore some of the funniest viral videos that have taken the internet by storm.

1. ‘Charlie Bit My Finger’ - This iconic video features two adorable British brothers, Harry and Charlie. In the clip, Harry playfully puts his finger near Charlie’s mouth, and the mischievous little boy playfully bites him. The combination of Harry’s cute British accent and Charlie’s toothy grin turned this innocent sibling interaction into an internet sensation.

2. ‘Gangnam Style’ - Who could forget the legendary music video by South Korean singer Psy? Released in 2012, ‘Gangnam Style’ quickly became the first YouTube video to reach one billion views. With its catchy tune and eccentric dance moves, it took the world by storm, inspiring countless parodies and dance covers.

3. ‘David After Dentist’ - Filmed by a concerned father, this video captures the priceless aftermath of young David’s trip to the dentist. Still under the influence of anesthesia, David hilariously contemplates his existence and questions if he really woke up from his dental procedure. His slurred speech and bewildered expressions made this video an instant hit.

4. ‘Baby Yoda - The Mandalorian’ - When the Disney+ series ‘The Mandalorian’ introduced Baby Yoda, also known as The Child, the internet went wild. Memes featuring the adorable and enigmatic creature flooded social media feeds. The sheer cuteness of Baby Yoda captured the hearts of Star Wars fans and non-fans alike, resulting in a seemingly never-ending stream of viral videos.

5. ‘Dramatic Chipmunk’ - Short but undeniably funny, this video features a chipmunk turning its head dramatically with suspenseful music playing in the background. People quickly began using it as a reaction gif to express shock and surprise, making it one of the most beloved and widely shared internet memes.

6. ‘Epic Sax Guy’ - Originally from the Eurovision Song Contest, this video clip features a saxophone player performing an infectious solo. With his intense facial expressions, the musician instantly became an internet sensation, inspiring remixes and mashups that spread like wildfire.

7. ‘The Evolution of Dance’ - Choreographed by comedian Judson Laipply, this video showcases a hilarious journey through popular dance moves from the 1950s to the present day. Laipply’s energetic performance and impressive dance skills make for an entertaining and nostalgic trip down memory lane.

8. ‘Keyboard Cat’ - This video stars a cat named Fatso, wearing a blue shirt, sitting at a keyboard, and ‘playing’ a catchy tune. The clip gained popularity as an amusing way to end various fail videos, with the cat ‘playing off’ people’s embarrassing moments.

9. ‘Double Rainbow’ - In this video, a man films a vivid double rainbow and has an emotional, awe-struck reaction. His over-the-top enthusiasm and pure joy resonated with viewers and quickly turned the video into a viral sensation.

10. ‘Rickrolling’ - A phenomenon where internet users are tricked into clicking on a link that leads them to singer Rick Astley’s music video for the song ‘Never Gonna Give You Up.’ This prank became so prevalent that it became a global meme, even infiltrating official channels and high-profile events.

These are just a few examples of the countless funny viral videos that have taken the internet by storm. Their ability to bring joy, laughter, and a sense of shared amusement is what makes them so popular. In a world where we often face challenges and stress, these videos offer a delightful escape, reminding us of the simple pleasures that can be found online. So, the next time you stumble upon a funny viral video, don’t hesitate to share it with friends and spread the laughter far and wide.
