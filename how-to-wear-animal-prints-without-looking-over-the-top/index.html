How to Wear Animal Prints Without Looking Over the Top


Animal prints have made a roaring comeback in the world of fashion, but many people are hesitant to embrace this bold trend for fear of looking over the top. However, with the right styling tips and tricks, you can effortlessly incorporate animal prints into your wardrobe without going overboard. In this blog post, we will explore various ways to wear animal prints while maintaining a chic and sophisticated look.

1. Start with accessories: If you’re new to animal prints, start by incorporating them into your accessories. A leopard print clutch or a snakeskin belt can instantly elevate any outfit without overpowering it. These small touches of animal print can add a touch of glamour and make a subtle statement.

2. Mix and match: Don’t be afraid to mix different animal prints together. Pair a leopard print blouse with a zebra print skirt or snake print heels with a cheetah print dress. The key is to choose prints that share a similar color palette and scale. By mixing and matching different animal prints, you create a unique and fashionable look that is sure to turn heads.

3. Opt for neutral tones: Animal prints can be quite bold on their own, so it’s best to stick to neutral tones when incorporating them into your outfit. Shades of brown, black, and beige are classic choices that will keep your look sophisticated and timeless. Remember, less is more when it comes to animal prints, so choose one statement piece and keep the rest of your outfit neutral.

4. Balance with solid colors: To avoid looking overwhelmed by animal prints, balance them out with solid colors. For example, pair a leopard print skirt with a simple black or white blouse. This creates a harmonious balance between the bold print and a more subdued color, allowing the animal print to take center stage without overpowering your overall look.

5. Choose the right fit: When wearing animal prints, it’s important to choose the right fit for your body shape. Opt for pieces that flatter your figure and highlight your best features. If you’re unsure, try different styles and silhouettes until you find the one that suits you best. A well-fitted animal print dress or tailored animal print blazer can instantly elevate your style and make you feel confident and stylish.

6. Use animal prints as a statement piece: Instead of wearing animal prints from head to toe, use them as a statement piece in your outfit. For example, pair a leopard print coat with a simple black dress or wear snakeskin boots with jeans and a plain white t-shirt. By keeping the rest of your outfit minimal, you allow the animal print to shine and become the focal point of your look.

7. Experiment with textures: Animal prints come in a variety of textures, from faux fur to silk to leather. Experiment with different textures to add depth and interest to your outfit. For example, pair a leopard print faux fur coat with a sleek leather skirt or wear a snakeskin silk blouse with velvet trousers. Mixing different textures adds a luxurious touch to your ensemble and takes your animal print look to the next level.

In conclusion, wearing animal prints doesn’t have to be intimidating. By following these styling tips, you can confidently embrace this bold trend without looking over the top. Remember to start small with accessories, mix and match prints, stick to neutral tones, balance with solid colors, choose the right fit, use animal prints as a statement piece, and experiment with textures. With a little creativity and confidence, you can rock animal prints in a chic and sophisticated way that is uniquely you.
