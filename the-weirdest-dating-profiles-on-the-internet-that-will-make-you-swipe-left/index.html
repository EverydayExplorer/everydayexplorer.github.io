The Weirdest Dating Profiles on the Internet That Will Make You Swipe Left


Dating in the digital age has made finding love—or at least a date—easier than ever. With the plethora of dating apps available, you can browse through potential matches with just a swipe of your finger. However, along with the ease of online dating comes the inevitable weirdness that some profiles exhibit.

We’ve all come across dating profiles that make us stop and wonder, ‘What were they thinking?’ From bizarre photos to cringeworthy bios, the internet is full of dating profiles that will make you want to swipe left faster than you can say ‘unmatch.’

So, grab your popcorn and get ready to cringe as we take a look at some of the weirdest dating profiles on the internet:

1. The Cat Lover: We all love our furry friends, but there’s a fine line between being a cat lover and being a cat fanatic. This profile features not one, not two, but ten photos of the user’s cats in various poses and outfits. The bio reads, ‘Looking for someone who loves cats as much as I do. Must be willing to wear cat ears on date nights.’

2. The Foodie: This profile is a virtual buffet of food photos. From sushi to burgers to gourmet desserts, this user doesn’t just love food – they worship it. The bio states, ‘Swipe right if you can handle my love for food. Bonus points if you can cook me a five-course meal.’

3. The Conspiracy Theorist: ‘The truth is out there,’ reads the bio of this profile. The user’s photos feature them in a tin foil hat, holding a sign that reads, ‘Aliens are real.’ If you’re not a believer in government conspiracies and UFO sightings, this profile is definitely not for you.

4. The Emoji King/Queen: Who needs words when you have emojis? This profile is a sea of smiley faces, hearts, and thumbs-up symbols. The bio is a series of emoji sentences that only the most fluent emoji speaker can decipher. If you’re up for a challenge, swipe right and see if you can crack the code.

5. The Ghost Hunter: ‘Looking for a partner to explore haunted houses with,’ reads the bio of this profile. The user’s photos feature them posing in front of spooky locations and holding a Ouija board. If you’re not afraid of things that go bump in the night, this profile might just be your perfect match.

6. The Fitness Fanatic: This profile is a shrine to all things fitness. The user’s photos feature them flexing at the gym, running marathons, and doing yoga poses on mountain tops. The bio states, ‘If you can’t keep up with my workout routine, don’t bother swiping right.’

7. The LARP Enthusiast: Live Action Role Playing, or LARPing, is a hobby for some, but a way of life for others. This profile features the user dressed in full medieval garb, brandishing a foam sword, and casting spells in the park. If you’re not into cosplay and fantasy realms, you may want to swipe left on this one.

8. The Mime Artist: ‘Actions speak louder than words,’ reads the bio of this profile. The user’s photos feature them in full mime makeup, trapped in invisible boxes, and pretending to walk against the wind. If you’re looking for a partner who can communicate without using words, this profile might just be your mime-mate.

These are just a few examples of the weird and wonderful dating profiles that can be found on the internet. While some may find these profiles entertaining, others may find them downright bizarre. Whatever your reaction, one thing is for sure: the world of online dating is full of surprises, and you never know what—or who—you might come across. So, the next time you’re swiping through potential matches, keep an eye out for the weirdest profiles out there. Who knows, you might just find your perfect match in the strangest of places.
