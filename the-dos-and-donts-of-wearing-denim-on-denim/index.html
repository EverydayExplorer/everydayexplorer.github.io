The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Denim on Denim


Denim on denim, also known as the ‘Canadian tuxedo,’ has been a fashion trend that has stood the test of time. From cowboys to celebrities, this iconic style has made its mark in the fashion industry. However, mastering the art of wearing denim on denim can be a tricky endeavor. Fear not! In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the dos and don’ts of rocking this trend, ensuring that you turn heads for all the right reasons.

DO: Play with Different Shades

When it comes to denim on denim, one of the golden rules is to play with different shades. Opt for contrasting tones that create depth and dimension in your outfit. Pair a light wash denim shirt with dark wash jeans or vice versa. This contrast will add visual interest and prevent your look from appearing too monotonous.

DON’T: Combine Similar Washes

While mixing different shades is encouraged, combining similar washes is a definite no-no. Wearing the exact same shade of denim from head to toe can make you look like you’re wearing a denim jumpsuit rather than a stylish ensemble. Embrace variety to keep your denim on denim look fresh and fashionable.

DO: Experiment with Textures

Denim comes in various textures, and incorporating different textures into your denim on denim look can take it to the next level. Pair a distressed denim jacket with a sleek pair of denim jeans or mix a denim shirt with a denim skirt that features a subtle pattern. This play with textures adds visual intrigue to your outfit and elevates your style.

DON’T: Overdo It with Accessories

When it comes to denim on denim, less is more. Avoid going overboard with accessories, as they can distract from the main focus of your outfit. Opt for minimalistic jewelry like a dainty necklace or a simple bracelet to complement your denim ensemble. Remember, the star of the show should always be the denim itself.

DO: Accessorize with Statement Pieces

While keeping accessories minimalistic, don’t be afraid to make a statement with a standout accessory. A bold belt, a vibrant scarf, or a stylish hat can add a touch of personality to your denim on denim look. Choose one statement piece that enhances your outfit without overpowering it.

DON’T: Forget About Proportions

Proportions are key when it comes to denim on denim. Balance out your outfit by mixing fitted and loose-fitting pieces. Pair a fitted denim shirt with wide-leg jeans or a loose denim jacket with skinny jeans. This balance creates harmony and ensures that your denim on denim look is flattering and well put together.

DO: Complete the Look with the Right Footwear

The right footwear can make or break your denim on denim outfit. Opt for shoes that add a touch of sophistication, such as ankle boots, loafers, or heels. Avoid sneakers or flip-flops, as they can make your outfit look too casual or mismatched. Choose footwear that complements your denim ensemble and adds a polished touch.

In conclusion, denim on denim is a fashion trend that allows you to express your personal style while paying homage to a timeless classic. By following these dos and don’ts, you can confidently rock this trend with finesse and turn heads wherever you go. Remember, fashion is about self-expression, so don’t be afraid to experiment and make the trend your own. Happy styling!
