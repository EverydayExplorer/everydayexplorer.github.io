Crafting with Washi Tape: Simple and Stylish Projects


Washi tape has taken the crafting world by storm, and for good reason. This versatile decorative tape comes in a wide variety of colors, patterns, and designs, making it perfect for adding a touch of personality and style to just about anything. From scrapbooking to home decor, the possibilities are endless when it comes to crafting with washi tape.

What is Washi Tape?

Washi tape is a type of decorative adhesive tape that is made from natural fibers like bamboo or hemp and is often translucent. It originated in Japan and was traditionally used for paper crafts, but it has quickly gained popularity all over the world. Washi tape is known for its unique texture and easy-to-tear properties, which make it ideal for crafting projects.

Why Craft with Washi Tape?

Crafting with washi tape is not only fun and enjoyable but also an opportunity to express your creativity. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or a beginner, working with washi tape is simple and requires minimal supplies. Its low cost and wide availability make it a favorite among DIY enthusiasts.

In addition to being easy to use, washi tape offers endless possibilities when it comes to creative projects. Its colorful patterns and designs can add a pop of color to any plain surface. Whether you want to personalize a notebook, decorate a picture frame, or jazz up your walls, washi tape is the perfect tool for the job.

Simple Washi Tape Projects

There are countless ways to incorporate washi tape into your crafting projects. Here are a few simple and stylish ideas to help you get started:

  1. Customized Gift Wrapping: Use washi tape to add a personal touch to your gifts. Create unique designs on plain wrapping paper or use it to seal envelopes and packages. The recipient will be delighted by the extra effort and thought put into the presentation.

  2. DIY Wall Art: Want to spruce up your walls without the commitment of paint or wallpaper? Use washi tape to create geometric shapes, chevron patterns, or even a faux headboard. The best part is, it’s easily removable, so you can change your design whenever you want.

  3. Personalized Journals: Turn a plain notebook into a personalized work of art with washi tape. Create patterns, borders, or cover the entire notebook with your favorite tape. The result is a unique and stylish journal that reflects your personality.

  4. Decorative Plant Pots: Add a touch of whimsy to your plant collection by decorating plain ceramic pots with washi tape. Choose patterns and colors that complement your home decor and wrap the tape around the pot to create a stylish look. Double-sided tape can be used to help secure the ends and prevent peeling.

  5. Upcycled Furniture: Give old furniture a new lease on life by incorporating washi tape into your upcycling projects. Use it to create a decorative border on a mirror frame or add a pop of color to the edges of a bookshelf. Let your imagination run wild and transform your furniture into unique and eye-catching pieces.

Tips for Crafting with Washi Tape

Now that you’re excited to start crafting with washi tape, here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Prepare your surface: Make sure the surface you’re applying the tape to is clean and dry for maximum adhesive strength.
  • Experiment with different widths and textures: Washi tape comes in various widths and textures. Play around with different combinations to create unique effects in your projects.
  • Mix and match patterns: Don’t limit yourself to using just one pattern or design. Mixing and matching different washi tapes can create a visually striking and diverse look.
  • Use scissors or craft punches: To create clean edges and intricate shapes, consider using scissors or craft punches to cut your washi tape.
  • Remove and reposition with care: While washi tape is generally easy to remove, it’s best to do so carefully to avoid damaging the surface underneath. Test a small area first to see how easily it peels off.

Final Thoughts

Crafting with washi tape is a delightful way to express your creativity and add flair to any project. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced crafter, this versatile tape is sure to inspire you with its endless possibilities. From simple DIY projects to more intricate creations, the beauty of washi tape lies in its simplicity and style. So, grab your tape, unleash your creativity, and get crafting with washi tape today!
