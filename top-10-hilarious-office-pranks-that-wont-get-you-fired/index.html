Top 10 Hilarious Office Pranks That Won't Get You Fired


Working in an office can sometimes be monotonous, and a little laughter can go a long way in boosting morale and creating a positive work environment. While office pranks may seem risky, there are plenty of harmless and hilarious pranks that won’t get you fired. In this article, we’ll share the top 10 office pranks that are sure to bring a smile to everyone’s face.

1. The Sticky Note Surprise

For this prank, all you need are sticky notes and a willing participant. While your colleague is away from their desk, cover their entire workspace with colorful sticky notes. From their computer monitor to their chair, leave no surface untouched. When they return, they’ll be greeted by a vibrant and amusing surprise.

2. The Upside-Down Desktop

This prank requires a bit of technical know-how. With a simple keyboard shortcut, you can flip the display of your colleague’s computer screen upside down. Sit back and watch as they struggle to navigate their suddenly topsy-turvy desktop. To fix it, they’ll have to discover the shortcut or seek help from their tech-savvy coworkers.

3. The “Unicorn” Coffee Mug

If you have a coworker who loves their coffee, this prank is perfect. Purchase a unicorn-themed coffee mug and secretly replace their regular mug with it. Watch as they bemusedly sip their morning coffee from a whimsical cup. They’ll surely be left wondering how it happened and who the mysterious unicorn enthusiast in the office is.

4. The Sneaky Mouse

This prank is a classic. Attach a small piece of tape or a sticky note to the bottom of your coworker’s mouse. As they try to move the cursor, it won’t budge, leading to confusion and frustration. It’s a harmless trick that always brings a few laughs.

5. The Fake Spider

For those who are not arachnophobes, this prank is a great way to elicit a scare or two. Place a realistic-looking fake spider on your colleague’s desk or inside a drawer. Their reaction when they stumble upon the eight-legged intruder will undoubtedly be priceless.

6. The Office Supply Swap

This prank requires a bit of preparation but is well worth it. With the help of your coworkers, swap all the items on your colleague’s desk with identical, but slightly different, versions. Replace their pens with ones that have disappearing ink, swap their stapler with a malfunctioning one, or even switch their chair with a slightly squeaky alternative. The confusion that ensues will provide endless entertainment.

7. The “Invisible” Cup

For this prank, all you need is a transparent cup and some water. Fill the cup to the brim and place it upside down on your colleague’s desk. When they return, they’ll be puzzled by the illusion of a cup floating in mid-air. They’ll try to pick it up, only to realize it’s a clever trick.

8. The Post-it Keyboard

This prank is simple but effective. Cover your coworker’s keyboard with colorful post-it notes, ensuring that every key is hidden. When they sit down and try to type, they’ll be met with a sea of sticky squares. It may take them a while to uncover the keys, leading to some humorous typos along the way.

9. The Mysterious Phone Call

Enlist the help of a friend or colleague to play a prank phone call on your coworker. Have the caller pretend to be a potential client or someone with an absurd request. Sit back and enjoy their confused and comical conversation as they try to navigate the unexpected call.

10. The Office “Pet”

For this prank, bring a goldfish bowl to the office and place it on your colleague’s desk when they’re away. Fill the bowl with water and add a few decorative rocks, but leave out the fish. When they return, they’ll be surprised to find an office pet waiting for them, or at least the illusion of one. It’s a lighthearted prank that brings a touch of whimsy to the workplace.

Remember, while these pranks are meant to bring laughter and joy to the office, it’s important to consider your coworker’s personalities and preferences. Always ensure that the pranks are harmless, respectful, and won’t disrupt productivity. With that in mind, let the laughter and good-natured fun fill your office!
