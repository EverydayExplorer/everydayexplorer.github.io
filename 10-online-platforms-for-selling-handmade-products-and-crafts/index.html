10 Online Platforms for Selling Handmade Products and Crafts


Hello there, creative minds and craft enthusiasts! Today, we’re diving into the world of selling handmade products and crafts online. With the rise of e-commerce and the ever-growing demand for unique, personalized items, it’s no wonder that many creators are turning to online platforms to showcase and sell their beautiful creations. Whether you’re a seasoned artisan or just starting out on your crafting journey, we’ve got you covered with a list of 10 fantastic online platforms where you can sell your handmade products and crafts. So, let’s jump right in!

1. Etsy: Let’s begin with the big one. Etsy is an established marketplace that caters specifically to creators of handmade goods. With a wide range of categories and a supportive community, Etsy provides a user-friendly platform for artists to showcase their talents and connect with buyers from all over the world.

2. Shopify: If you’re looking to create your own online store, Shopify is a great option. With its intuitive interface and extensive customization options, you can build a professional-looking e-commerce website that truly reflects your brand. Plus, Shopify offers seamless integration with various payment gateways, making it easier for customers to purchase your handmade products.

3. Handmade at Amazon: As the name suggests, Handmade at Amazon is a platform dedicated to handmade products. With its vast reach and customer base, selling on Amazon can open up new opportunities for your crafts to be discovered by millions of potential buyers.

4. Folksy: For UK-based artisans, Folksy is a wonderful platform to showcase and sell your handmade creations. With a focus on supporting local talent and a visually appealing interface, Folksy provides a great opportunity to connect with customers who appreciate the beauty of handmade products.

5. ArtFire: ArtFire is a versatile platform that allows artists to sell a wide array of handmade goods, including crafts, art, and vintage items. With its straightforward listing process and built-in promotional tools, ArtFire makes it easy for creators to showcase their products to a global audience.

6. Zibbet: Zibbet is an online marketplace that caters to artists and crafters. With its emphasis on community and handmade products, Zibbet provides a supportive environment for creators to connect with like-minded individuals and potential customers.

7. Big Cartel: Big Cartel is a platform that caters to artists, makers, and small businesses. With its user-friendly interface and affordable pricing plans, Big Cartel offers a budget-friendly option for creators to set up their own online store and sell their handmade products.

8. Not on the High Street: If you’re based in the UK and looking for a platform that emphasizes unique and one-of-a-kind products, Not on the High Street is a fantastic option. With its curated selection of handmade goods and personalized gifts, this platform attracts discerning customers who appreciate the artistry behind each creation.

9. Handmadeology: Handmadeology is not a traditional marketplace but instead a blog and resource hub for artisans looking to grow their businesses. With insightful articles and tips on marketing, branding, and selling handmade products, Handmadeology is a valuable tool for any crafty entrepreneur.

10. Instagram: While not a dedicated e-commerce platform, Instagram has become a powerful tool for creators to showcase their handmade products and engage with a large and active community of users. By utilizing hashtags, engaging captions, and visually appealing photos, you can turn your Instagram account into a virtual storefront and attract potential buyers.

There you have it, folks! Ten fantastic online platforms where you can sell your handmade products and crafts. Whether you’re a painter, jewelry maker, candle artisan, or any other type of creator, these platforms offer you a chance to share your passion with the world and connect with customers who truly appreciate your handmade creations. So go ahead, unleash your creativity, and embark on an exciting journey of selling your crafts online!
