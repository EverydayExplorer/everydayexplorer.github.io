The Best Cartoon Podcasts to Tune Into


Are you a cartoon enthusiast looking for a way to dive deeper into your favorite animated shows and characters? Podcasts have become a popular medium for exploring and discussing all things related to cartoons, from nostalgic classics to the latest animated series. Whether you’re interested in behind-the-scenes insights, fan theories, or simply want to relive the magic of your favorite cartoons, there’s a podcast out there for you. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore some of the best cartoon podcasts that are sure to delight fans of all ages.

1. Animation Addicts Podcast

Hosted by Morgan Stradling and Chelsea Robson, the Animation Addicts Podcast is a delightful journey into the world of animated films. From Disney classics to contemporary animated features, this podcast provides insightful commentary, interviews with industry professionals, and engaging discussions about the art of animation. Whether you’re a casual fan or a dedicated animation buff, this podcast offers a treasure trove of content for you to explore.

2. The Retroist Podcast

For those nostalgic for the golden age of cartoons, The Retroist Podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by the affable Retroist himself, this podcast delves into the world of retro pop culture, including classic cartoons from the 70s, 80s, and 90s. With episodes dedicated to iconic shows like He-Man, Thundercats, and The Smurfs, this podcast is a trip down memory lane for fans who grew up during the heyday of Saturday morning cartoons.

3. Saturday Mourning Cartoons

Looking for a podcast that celebrates the art of Saturday morning cartoons? Look no further than Saturday Mourning Cartoons. Hosted by Sean Paul Ellis and Dave Trumbore, this podcast explores the history and impact of animated shows that have graced the Saturday morning airwaves. From beloved classics to obscure gems, each episode is a delightful blend of nostalgia, humor, and insightful analysis.

4. Talking Simpsons

If you’re a fan of the iconic animated series, The Simpsons, then Talking Simpsons is the podcast for you. Hosted by Bob Mackey and Henry Gilbert, this podcast offers a deep dive into every episode of The Simpsons, providing fans with trivia, behind-the-scenes anecdotes, and a fresh perspective on the long-running series. Whether you’re a die-hard Simpsons fan or simply appreciate the genius of Matt Groening’s creation, this podcast is a must-listen.

5. Toon in Pod

Hosted by Alex and Retrokaiser, Toon in Pod is a lively and entertaining podcast dedicated to all things animation. From classic Looney Tunes to modern-day animated series, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including character analysis, animation techniques, and interviews with industry insiders. With a healthy dose of humor and genuine passion for the medium, Toon in Pod is a delightful listen for anyone with a love for cartoons.

6. The Ink and Paint Club Podcast

For fans of animated films and television, The Ink and Paint Club Podcast is a treasure trove of in-depth analysis and thoughtful commentary. Hosted by JD Duran and a team of passionate contributors, this podcast covers a wide range of animated content, including Disney classics, anime, and independent animation. With a focus on storytelling, artistry, and the impact of animation on popular culture, this podcast offers a rich and enlightening exploration of the medium.

7. The Morning Stream

While not exclusively dedicated to cartoons, The Morning Stream is a lively and engaging podcast that often delves into animated series and pop culture. Hosted by Scott Johnson and Brian Ibbott, this daily show features humorous discussions, interviews, and news about a wide range of topics, including cartoons and animation. With its casual and entertaining format, The Morning Stream is a great choice for listeners interested in a variety of pop culture subjects.

Conclusion

With the rise of podcasts, cartoon enthusiasts have a wealth of options for immersing themselves in the world of animation. Whether you’re seeking insightful analysis, nostalgic discussions, or behind-the-scenes insights, the best cartoon podcasts offer something for every fan. By tuning into these engaging and informative shows, you can deepen your appreciation for the art of animation and connect with a vibrant community of fellow enthusiasts. So, grab your headphones and get ready to embark on a delightful journey through the colorful and captivating world of cartoon podcasts. Happy listening!
