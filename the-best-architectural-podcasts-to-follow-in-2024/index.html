The Best Architectural Podcasts to Follow in 2024


As the world of architecture continues to evolve, it is crucial for professionals and enthusiasts alike to stay updated on the latest trends, insights, and discussions. With the rise of podcasts, architecture enthusiasts now have a convenient and engaging way to delve into the world of design, construction, and urban planning. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best architectural podcasts to follow in 2024. These podcasts cover a wide range of topics and feature interviews with renowned architects, designers, and industry experts. So, grab your headphones and get ready to immerse yourself in the fascinating world of architecture.

1. “99% Invisible”

Hosted by Roman Mars, “99% Invisible” is a podcast that explores the hidden design and architecture that shapes our world. Each episode delves into the unnoticed aspects of our built environment, uncovering the stories behind buildings, infrastructure, and everyday objects. With over 400 episodes, this podcast offers a treasure trove of knowledge for anyone interested in the intricacies of architectural design.

2. “Archinect Sessions”

“Archinect Sessions” is a podcast that features in-depth conversations with leading architects, designers, and thinkers in the industry. Hosted by Paul Petrunia, this podcast provides a platform for discussing the latest trends, challenges, and innovations in architecture. From sustainable design to urban planning, “Archinect Sessions” offers a comprehensive exploration of the field.

3. “The Architecture Happy Hour”

If you’re looking for a podcast that combines architecture with a touch of humor, “The Architecture Happy Hour” is the perfect choice. Hosted by Laura Davis and Holly Hall, this podcast explores various architectural topics while maintaining a relaxed and conversational tone. With episodes covering everything from design principles to project management, “The Architecture Happy Hour” is both informative and entertaining.

4. “Design Matters”

“Design Matters” is a podcast hosted by Debbie Millman, a renowned designer and author. While not solely focused on architecture, this podcast offers a unique perspective on design and its impact on our lives. Debbie Millman engages in insightful conversations with designers, artists, and creatives, exploring their journeys, inspirations, and philosophies. With over 400 episodes, “Design Matters” is a must-listen for anyone interested in the creative process.

5. “The Urbanist”

For those intrigued by urban planning and the future of cities, “The Urbanist” is an excellent podcast to follow. Hosted by Andrew Tuck, this podcast delves into the challenges and opportunities faced by urban environments. From sustainable transportation to affordable housing, “The Urbanist” examines the social, economic, and environmental aspects of city life, providing valuable insights for architects and urban planners alike.

6. “The Business of Architecture”

“The Business of Architecture” offers a unique perspective on the architectural industry. Hosted by Enoch Sears, this podcast focuses on the business side of architecture, providing practical tips, strategies, and advice for architects looking to grow their firms and navigate the challenges of the profession. From marketing to project management, “The Business of Architecture” equips architects with the knowledge they need to succeed in today’s competitive market.

7. “TED Talks Architecture”

TED Talks have long been a source of inspiration and knowledge, and “TED Talks Architecture” is no exception. This podcast features curated talks by architects, designers, and thought leaders, offering a diverse range of perspectives on architecture and its impact on society. From innovative building materials to the power of design in creating inclusive spaces, “TED Talks Architecture” will broaden your horizons and ignite your creativity.

Whether you’re a seasoned architect or simply curious about the world of design, these podcasts provide a wealth of information, inspiration, and thought-provoking discussions. By following these podcasts in 2024, you’ll stay ahead of the curve and gain valuable insights into the ever-evolving field of architecture. So, tune in, listen, and let your imagination soar as you explore the best architectural podcasts of 2024.

Note: The recommendations provided in this blog post are based on personal preferences and popular opinion. The order of the podcasts does not indicate any ranking or hierarchy.

Remember to check out these amazing architectural podcasts and let your passion for design thrive. Stay tuned for more exciting content on the world of architecture!
