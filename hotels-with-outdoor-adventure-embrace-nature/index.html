Hotels with Outdoor Adventure: Embrace Nature


Are you tired of the same old vacation routine? Do you crave adventure and a deeper connection with nature? Look no further than hotels with outdoor adventure! These hidden gems offer the perfect blend of luxury and the great outdoors, allowing you to embrace nature like never before. In this blog post, we’ll explore the top hotels around the world that cater to outdoor enthusiasts, providing exhilarating activities and breathtaking landscapes that will leave you in awe.

  1. The Lodge at Blue Sky, Utah, USA Nestled in the heart of the Wasatch Mountains, The Lodge at Blue Sky offers a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. From exhilarating horseback riding to fly fishing in the Provo River, this luxurious retreat allows guests to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of Utah. The hotel also offers guided hiking and mountain biking excursions, providing the perfect opportunity to explore the stunning landscapes surrounding the property.

  2. Four Seasons Tented Camp Golden Triangle, Thailand For those seeking a truly unique outdoor adventure, the Four Seasons Tented Camp Golden Triangle in Thailand is a must-visit destination. Set in the heart of the Golden Triangle, where Thailand, Laos, and Myanmar converge, this hotel offers guests the opportunity to interact with rescued elephants in their natural habitat. Guests can also embark on guided treks through the lush jungle, offering a rare glimpse into the region’s diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes.

  3. Clayoquot Wilderness Resort, British Columbia, Canada Tucked away in the pristine wilderness of Vancouver Island, Clayoquot Wilderness Resort provides an unparalleled outdoor experience. Guests can partake in a wide range of activities, including kayaking through the tranquil waters of Bedwell Sound, bear watching in the surrounding forests, and even heli-hiking to remote alpine meadows. The resort’s commitment to sustainability and conservation ensures that guests can embrace nature while minimizing their impact on the environment.

  4. Amangiri, Utah, USA Situated in the dramatic landscape of southern Utah, Amangiri offers a luxurious retreat for those seeking outdoor adventure. Guests can embark on guided via ferrata climbing expeditions, explore the otherworldly beauty of Antelope Canyon, or take a scenic hot air balloon ride over the mesmerizing desert terrain. The hotel’s stunning architecture seamlessly blends with the natural surroundings, providing a serene sanctuary for travelers seeking an authentic connection with nature.

  5. Tierra Patagonia Hotel & Spa, Chile Set against the backdrop of the iconic Torres del Paine National Park, Tierra Patagonia Hotel & Spa invites guests to immerse themselves in the untamed beauty of Patagonia. With a wide range of outdoor activities, including guided treks to the base of the park’s famous granite peaks, horseback riding across the vast pampas, and wildlife safaris to spot guanacos and condors, this hotel offers a true escape into the wild.

Hotels with outdoor adventure are the perfect choice for travelers seeking a unique and immersive experience in nature. Whether you’re an avid hiker, a wildlife enthusiast, or simply someone who appreciates the beauty of the great outdoors, these hotels offer an unparalleled opportunity to embrace nature in all its glory. So, pack your bags, leave the city behind, and get ready for an unforgettable adventure in the great outdoors!
