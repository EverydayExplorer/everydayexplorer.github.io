How to Mix and Match Prints Like a Pro


Are you tired of wearing the same old outfits and want to add some excitement to your wardrobe? Mixing and matching prints is a great way to express your unique style and create eye-catching looks. However, it can be a bit tricky to get it right. Don’t worry, though! In this post, we’ll share some expert tips on how to mix and match prints like a pro. Let’s dive in!

1. Start with Similar Colors

One of the easiest ways to ensure a cohesive look when mixing prints is to start with similar colors. Choose prints that share at least one common color, as this will help tie the different patterns together. For example, you could pair a floral top with a striped skirt if they both have a touch of pink. This subtle connection between the prints will create a harmonious and balanced outfit.

2. Vary the Scale

When mixing prints, it’s important to vary the scale of the patterns to avoid overwhelming the outfit. If you have a bold, large-scale print, pair it with a smaller, more intricate pattern. This creates visual interest and prevents the prints from competing with each other. For instance, you could pair a large polka dot blouse with a small houndstooth skirt for a playful and stylish look.

3. Stick to One Dominant Print

To avoid a chaotic and cluttered appearance, it’s best to choose one dominant print and let the others play supporting roles. The dominant print will be the focal point of your outfit, while the secondary print(s) will add depth and interest. For example, you could wear a striped blazer as the dominant print and pair it with a floral blouse and polka dot shoes as the supporting prints. This way, the outfit remains cohesive and visually appealing.

4. Experiment with Textures

Mixing prints doesn’t have to be limited to just fabrics with different patterns. You can also experiment with textures to add another layer of visual interest to your outfit. For instance, you could pair a silky leopard print dress with a chunky knit cardigan for a unique and stylish combination. The contrast in textures will create a dynamic and visually appealing look.

5. Accessorize Wisely

When it comes to accessories, keep them simple and understated when mixing prints. Opt for solid-colored accessories that complement the colors in your prints rather than competing with them. A neutral handbag or a pair of classic black pumps can help anchor your outfit and let the prints take center stage. Remember, less is more when it comes to accessories in a print-heavy ensemble.

6. Confidence is Key

Last but not least, the most important tip for mixing prints like a pro is to have confidence in your choices. Fashion is subjective, and what matters most is how you feel in your outfit. Embrace your unique style and wear your prints with pride. Confidence is the best accessory you can wear, and it will make any print combination look effortlessly chic.

So there you have it – six expert tips on how to mix and match prints like a pro. Remember to start with similar colors, vary the scale of the prints, stick to one dominant print, experiment with textures, accessorize wisely, and most importantly, have confidence in your choices. With a little practice and a touch of creativity, you’ll be able to create stunning and stylish outfits that showcase your individuality. Happy mixing and matching!
