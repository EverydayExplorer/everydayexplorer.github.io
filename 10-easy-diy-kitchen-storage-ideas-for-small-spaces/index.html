10 Easy DIY Kitchen Storage Ideas for Small Spaces


Are you tired of constantly searching for your kitchen utensils or struggling to find space for your pots and pans in your small kitchen? If so, you’re in luck! Today, we’re sharing 10 easy DIY kitchen storage ideas that will help you maximize your space and keep your kitchen organized. These ideas are perfect for small spaces and can be easily customized to fit your needs. Let’s dive in!

1. Utilize the Inside of Cabinet Doors: The inside of your cabinet doors is prime real estate for storage. Install hooks or small shelves to hold measuring cups, pot lids, or even spices. This will free up valuable drawer and countertop space.

2. Hang a Pegboard: A pegboard is a versatile and cost-effective storage solution. Mount a pegboard on an empty wall in your kitchen and hang your most frequently used items, such as pots, pans, and cooking utensils. You can also add hooks or small baskets to hold smaller items like measuring spoons and spatulas.

3. Invest in Magnetic Knife Strips: Say goodbye to bulky knife blocks taking up your precious countertop space. Install a magnetic knife strip on your kitchen wall to keep your knives within reach and free up drawer space.

4. Use Tension Rods: Tension rods are not just for curtains! Install tension rods vertically between shelves or inside cabinets to create additional storage for cutting boards, baking sheets, and trays. This simple hack will make it easier to grab what you need without creating a messy pile.

5. Install a Rolling Kitchen Cart: If you have a small kitchen with limited counter space, a rolling kitchen cart can be a game-changer. Use it as an extra work surface, a storage space for appliances, or even as a mobile bar cart during parties.

6. DIY Hanging Fruit Basket: Don’t let your fruits take up valuable counter space. Create a stylish and functional hanging fruit basket using a tiered wire basket and some rope. Hang it from the ceiling or a sturdy hook, and you’ll have easy access to your favorite fruits without cluttering your countertops.

7. Repurpose Mason Jars: Mason jars are a versatile storage solution. Use them to store dry ingredients like pasta, rice, or cereal. You can also attach the lids to the underside of shelves or cabinets and screw the jars in place, creating handy storage for spices or small items like rubber bands and paper clips.

8. Add Shelves Above Cabinets: If you have space between the top of your kitchen cabinets and the ceiling, utilize it! Install floating shelves or baskets to store items you rarely use, such as seasonal bakeware or extra serving dishes.

9. DIY Drawer Dividers: Keep your utensils organized and easily accessible by creating custom drawer dividers. Use thin pieces of wood or even repurposed cardboard to create compartments that fit your specific utensils.

10. Hang a Pot Rack: If you have limited cabinet space, hanging your pots and pans can be a stylish and practical solution. Install a pot rack above your kitchen island or near your stove for easy access and extra storage.

By implementing these 10 easy DIY kitchen storage ideas, you can transform your small kitchen into a functional and organized space. Remember, creativity is key when it comes to maximizing storage in small areas. So roll up your sleeves and start tackling those kitchen clutter issues today!
