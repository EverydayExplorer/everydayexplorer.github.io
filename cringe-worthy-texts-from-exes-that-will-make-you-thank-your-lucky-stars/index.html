Cringe-worthy Texts from Exes That Will Make You Thank Your Lucky Stars


Breakups can be tough, and sometimes the aftermath can be even tougher. One common post-breakup pain point is having to deal with cringe-worthy texts from exes. Whether they’re trying to win you back, seeking closure, or just being plain awkward, these texts can range from mildly uncomfortable to downright embarrassing. If you’ve ever received a cringe-worthy text from an ex, you’re definitely not alone. Here are some examples that will make you thank your lucky stars that you’re no longer in that relationship.

1. The ‘I Miss You’ Text:
This is a classic text that many of us have probably received at some point. Your ex decides to hit you up out of the blue with a heartfelt message about how much they miss you and wish things were different. While it may initially pull at your heartstrings, it’s important to remember why you broke up in the first place. This text is often more about their own feelings of loneliness or regret than a genuine desire to rekindle the relationship.

2. The ‘Can We Talk?’ Text:
If your ex sends you a text asking to talk, it can be nerve-wracking. Are they going to apologize for their past behavior? Do they want to get back together? Or are they just looking for closure? Whatever the reason, this text can stir up a mix of emotions and uncertainty. It’s important to approach these conversations with caution and set boundaries to protect your own well-being.

3. The Drunk Text:
Ah, the classic drunk text from an ex. We’ve all been there – receiving a late-night message filled with misspelled words and rambling declarations of love. While it may be tempting to respond in the moment, it’s best to save yourself the embarrassment and ignore these texts. It’s never a good idea to engage with someone who is under the influence, as it often leads to more confusion and hurt feelings.

4. The Jealous Text:
When your ex starts asking about your love life or making passive-aggressive comments about your new relationships, it can be cringe-worthy and uncomfortable. This type of text shows that they may still have feelings for you or are struggling to move on. While it’s natural to feel a sense of satisfaction knowing that you’re on their mind, it’s important to set boundaries and prioritize your own well-being.

5. The Apology Text:
Apologies can be tricky, especially when they come from an ex. While it may be nice to hear that they regret their actions and want to make amends, it’s essential to take their words with a grain of salt. Make sure they’re taking accountability for their behavior and not just trying to manipulate you into taking them back. Remember, actions speak louder than words, so pay attention to how they behave moving forward.

Dealing with cringe-worthy texts from exes can be a challenging part of the healing process after a breakup. While it’s natural to feel a range of emotions when receiving these messages, it’s essential to prioritize your own well-being and set boundaries to protect yourself. Remember that you deserve to be treated with respect and kindness, both during and after a relationship. So the next time you receive a cringe-worthy text from an ex, take a deep breath, remind yourself of your worth, and thank your lucky stars that you’re no longer together.
