10 Quick and Easy Hairstyles for Updos


Are you tired of the same old ponytail or messy bun? Looking for some quick and easy hairstyles to elevate your look? Well, you’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 fabulous updo hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re heading to a formal event, a casual brunch, or just want to add a touch of elegance to your everyday look, these hairstyles are sure to impress. Plus, I’ll share some tips on how to achieve these looks with minimal effort. Let’s dive in!

1. The Classic Chignon
The chignon is a timeless updo that exudes sophistication and elegance. It’s perfect for formal events or a day at the office. Simply gather your hair into a low ponytail, twist it into a bun, and secure it with bobby pins. For a softer look, pull out a few face-framing pieces.

2. Messy French Twist
The messy French twist is a chic and effortless updo that adds a touch of romance to any look. To achieve this style, twist your hair upwards from the nape of your neck, tuck the ends, and secure with bobby pins. Gently pull on the twist to create a soft, undone finish.

3. Braided Updo
Braids are a versatile and stylish way to elevate your updo. Create a simple braid or fishtail braid, then wrap it around the base of a ponytail or bun for an intricate and eye-catching updo.

4. Sleek Low Bun
For a polished and sophisticated look, opt for a sleek low bun. Smooth your hair into a low ponytail, twist it into a neat bun, and secure with a hair tie and bobby pins. Finish with a spritz of hairspray for a sleek, polished finish.

5. Twisted Updo
This effortless updo is perfect for those days when you’re short on time. Simply twist sections of your hair and pin them at the back of your head for a relaxed and stylish updo.

6. Half-Up Twisted Bun
The half-up twisted bun is a modern and playful take on the classic bun. Simply gather the top half of your hair, twist it, and secure it into a bun at the crown of your head. This style is perfect for a casual day out or a fun night with friends.

7. Tousled Updo
Embrace your natural texture with a tousled updo. Simply gather your hair into a loose bun at the back of your head, allowing a few pieces to fall naturally for a relaxed and effortless look.

8. Elegant Updo with Hair Accessories
Elevate any updo with the addition of stylish hair accessories. Whether it’s a sparkly barrette, a delicate headband, or a statement hairpin, accessories can take your updo to the next level.

9. Curly Updo
If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture with a curly updo. Gather your curls into a loose bun or ponytail, allowing your curls to cascade down for a romantic and whimsical look.

10. Voluminous High Bun
For a statement-making updo, opt for a voluminous high bun. Tease the crown of your head for added volume, then gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bold, voluminous bun.

Achieving these updos is easier than you think! With the right tools and a bit of practice, you can master these hairstyles in no time. Remember to use a good quality hair tie, bobby pins, and hairspray to ensure your updo stays put all day or night. Whether you’re attending a special event or simply want to elevate your everyday look, these quick and easy updos are sure to turn heads.

So, there you have it – 10 quick and easy hairstyles for updos that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or something more relaxed and effortless, there’s an updo for everyone. Experiment with these hairstyles and have fun expressing your personal style. Don’t be afraid to get creative and make these looks your own. With a little practice, you’ll be rocking these updos with confidence in no time!
