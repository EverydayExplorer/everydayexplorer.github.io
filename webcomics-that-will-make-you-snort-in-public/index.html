Webcomics that will Make You Snort in Public


Are you a fan of comics? Do you enjoy a good laugh? Then get ready to dive into the world of webcomics that will have you snorting in public. With the rise of the internet, webcomics have become a popular medium for artists to showcase their talent and entertain readers around the globe. From witty one-liners to relatable characters, these webcomics are sure to bring a smile to your face and maybe even cause a few uncontrollable laughs.

But what exactly is a webcomic? In simple terms, it is a comic strip or series published exclusively on the internet. Unlike traditional print comics, webcomics have the advantage of reaching a much larger audience and being easily shared and discovered through social media platforms. This has given rise to an explosion of creativity and diversity in the webcomics scene, allowing artists to explore various genres, styles, and themes.

One of the reasons why webcomics have become so popular is their ability to capture everyday situations in a humorous and relatable manner. Whether it’s poking fun at social quirks, exploring the absurdity of life, or playing with unexpected twists, webcomics have a way of resonating with readers on a personal level. They act as a mirror that reflects our quirks and idiosyncrasies, reminding us that we’re not alone in navigating the chaos of everyday life.

One such webcomic that never fails to make readers snort with laughter is ‘The Oatmeal’ by Matthew Inman. Known for its hilarious and often satirical take on various topics, ‘The Oatmeal’ has gained a massive following due to its clever writing and distinctive artwork. Whether it’s addressing the struggles of grammar Nazis or chronicling the misadventures of the Internet’s favorite creatures, cats, ‘The Oatmeal’ never fails to elicit laughter and snorts from its readers.

Another webcomic that has gained immense popularity is ‘xkcd’ by Randall Munroe. Known for its witty and geeky humor, ‘xkcd’ explores a wide range of topics, including science, mathematics, relationships, and everyday situations. With its stick-figure drawings and sarcastic captions, ‘xkcd’ has managed to develop a cult following among readers who appreciate its intelligence and humorous take on life’s absurdities.

If you’re a fan of dark and twisted humor, then ‘Cyanide and Happiness’ is the webcomic for you. Created by the Explosm Entertainment team, this webcomic delves into the realms of dark comedy, tackling taboo subjects with a unique and often offensive approach. With its distinctive stick-figure style and dark humor, ‘Cyanide and Happiness’ has garnered a loyal fanbase that enjoys its irreverent take on life’s darkest moments.

Moving away from the dark and into the realm of whimsical humor, ‘Sarah’s Scribbles’ by Sarah Andersen depicts the everyday struggles of a young woman navigating adulthood. With her relatable characters and doodle-like drawings, Sarah Andersen captures the awkwardness, anxiety, and insecurities of growing up in a humorous and lighthearted way. ‘Sarah’s Scribbles’ has gained a large following, especially among millennials who find comfort and solace in its understanding and hilarious take on life’s challenges.

These are just a few examples of webcomics that have gained popularity for their ability to make readers snort in public. The beauty of webcomics lies in their versatility and accessibility. With thousands of webcomics available online, there is something for everyone, regardless of their sense of humor or interests.

So, the next time you find yourself in need of a good laugh, don’t hesitate to explore the vast world of webcomics. Whether you’re a fan of clever wordplay, geeky humor, or dark comedy, there’s a webcomic out there that will have you snorting in public. Just remember to be cautious when reading them in public spaces, as spontaneous laughter may attract some curious glances.

In conclusion, webcomics have revolutionized the way we consume and enjoy comics. With their ability to entertain and make us snort with laughter, they have carved out a special place in the hearts of readers worldwide. So, sit back, relax, and get ready to snort in public as you dive into the never-ending world of webcomics. Happy reading!
