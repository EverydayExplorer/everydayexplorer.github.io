Savory and Delicious One-Pot Pasta Recipes


One-pot pasta recipes have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. They are easy to make, require minimal cleanup, and can be customized to suit any taste or dietary preference. In this article, we will explore some savory and delicious one-pot pasta recipes that are sure to become a staple in your household.

Creamy Tomato Pasta

This classic one-pot pasta recipe is a staple for a reason. It’s quick, easy, and packed with flavor. To make it, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Cook the pasta, onion, and garlic in the olive oil until the pasta is al dente. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, heavy cream, and dried basil. Stir until the sauce is smooth and the pasta is well coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Spaghetti Bolognese

This one-pot pasta recipe is a twist on the classic spaghetti Bolognese. Instead of cooking the ground beef and tomato sauce separately, you’ll cook everything in one pot. To make it, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Cook the spaghetti, ground beef, onion, garlic, and carrot in the olive oil until the pasta is al dente and the ground beef is fully cooked. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, tomato paste, and dried oregano. Stir until the sauce is smooth and the pasta is well coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Lemon Chicken Pasta

This one-pot pasta recipe is a refreshing twist on traditional pasta dishes. To make it, you’ll need the following ingredients:

  • 1 pound pasta of your choice
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Cook the pasta, chicken, onion, and garlic in the olive oil until the pasta is al dente and the chicken is fully cooked. Add the chicken broth, heavy cream, lemon juice, and dried thyme. Stir until the sauce is smooth and the pasta is well coated. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Conclusion

One-pot pasta recipes are a great way to make a delicious and satisfying meal with minimal effort. Whether you’re in the mood for something classic and comforting or something fresh and flavorful, there’s a one-pot pasta recipe out there for you. With these recipes, you’ll be well on your way to becoming a one-pot pasta master. So go ahead, get creative, and start cooking!
