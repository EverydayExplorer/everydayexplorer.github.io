Trendy Ways to Wear Animal Prints This Season


Animal prints have been a fashion staple for decades, and this season, they are making a roaring comeback in the style scene. From classic leopard to bold zebra, animal prints are versatile and can add a wild touch to any outfit. If you’re looking to unleash your inner fashionista, here are some trendy ways to wear animal prints this season that will have you looking fierce and fabulous.

  1. Statement Coat: A timeless way to incorporate animal prints into your wardrobe is through a statement coat. Whether it’s a chic leopard print trench coat or a bold snakeskin jacket, a statement coat can elevate any outfit. Pair it with a simple black dress or jeans and a t-shirt for a look that’s effortlessly stylish.

  2. Accessorize: If you’re not ready to fully commit to the animal print trend, start small by incorporating accessories. A leopard print scarf, zebra print belt, or snakeskin handbag can add a pop of wild flair to your ensemble without being overwhelming. Accessories are a great way to dip your toes into the trend without going overboard.

  3. Mix and Match: Don’t be afraid to mix and match different animal prints. For the fashion-forward individual, combining different prints can create a bold and eye-catching look. Pair a leopard print skirt with a zebra print top, or mix snakeskin boots with a tiger print bag. Just remember to keep the rest of your outfit simple to let the prints take center stage.

  4. Subtle Touches: If you prefer a more understated approach, opt for subtle touches of animal print. A pair of leopard print heels, snake print flats, or even a hint of animal print in your jewelry can add a touch of glamour to your outfit without being too overwhelming.

  5. Printed Pants: Animal print pants are a bold choice that can make a striking fashion statement. Whether it’s a pair of leopard print trousers or zebra print leggings, printed pants can be dressed up with a blazer for a sophisticated look, or dressed down with a casual tee for a more relaxed vibe.

  6. Layering: Layering different animal prints can create a visually interesting and stylish outfit. Try layering a leopard print blouse under a zebra print sweater or pair a snakeskin jacket with a tiger print scarf. When done tastefully, layering animal prints can result in a fashion-forward and unique ensemble.

  7. Color Combinations: Experiment with different color combinations when wearing animal prints. While traditional animal prints are often in neutral tones, don’t be afraid to try prints in unexpected colors. A vibrant blue leopard print or a red zebra print can add a modern twist to the classic trend.

  8. Mix with Neutrals: For a more subdued look, pair animal prints with neutral colors such as black, white, or beige. This allows the print to stand out while maintaining a sophisticated and polished appearance.

  9. Dresses and Skirts: Embrace the wild side by incorporating animal print dresses or skirts into your wardrobe. Whether it’s a flowing leopard print maxi dress or a sleek snakeskin pencil skirt, these pieces can be styled for both casual and formal occasions.

  10. Printed Outerwear: Step up your outerwear game with animal print jackets, blazers, or vests. These statement pieces can instantly elevate any outfit, whether it’s a casual daytime look or a night out on the town.

In conclusion, animal prints are a timeless trend that can add a touch of boldness and sophistication to any outfit. Whether you choose to make a statement with a full-on animal print ensemble or add subtle touches with accessories, there are endless ways to incorporate this trend into your wardrobe. Embrace your wild side this season and let your style roar with these trendy ways to wear animal prints.
