10 Fashionable Ways to Wear the Color of the Year


Every year, a color is chosen as the ‘Color of the Year’ by Pantone, the authority on color. This color influences various industries, including fashion, interior design, and graphic design. The Color of the Year sets the tone for the seasons ahead and inspires designers and fashionistas to incorporate it into their collections and outfits. In 2022, the color chosen as the Color of the Year is Aurora Yellow, a vibrant and energetic shade that is sure to elevate your fashion game. If you’re wondering how to incorporate this captivating color into your wardrobe, here are 10 fashionable ways to wear the Color of the Year.

1. Statement Dress Make a bold fashion statement with a striking Aurora Yellow dress. Opt for a flowy maxi dress for a bohemian look or choose a fitted cocktail dress for a more polished and glamorous outfit. Pair your dress with neutral accessories to let the vibrant color take center stage.

2. Monochrome Look Create a chic and sophisticated monochrome outfit with different shades of yellow. Pair an Aurora Yellow top with mustard pants or a lemon yellow skirt for a cohesive and on-trend look. Don’t be afraid to mix different textures and fabrics to add depth to your ensemble.

3. Pop of Color If you’re not ready to fully commit to an Aurora Yellow outfit, incorporate this trendy color as a pop of color in your ensemble. Add an Aurora Yellow handbag, shoes, or belt to instantly elevate your outfit and make a fashionable statement.

4. Color-Blocked Outfit For a bold and modern look, try color-blocking Aurora Yellow with complementary colors. Pair it with a vibrant shade like electric blue or fuchsia for a striking contrast that will turn heads wherever you go. Remember to balance the look by keeping accessories and makeup minimal.

5. Printed Patterns Experiment with Aurora Yellow in printed patterns like floral, leopard, or abstract. Choose a statement piece like a blouse or skirt and pair it with neutral or monochrome pieces to let the print shine. This mix of color and pattern will add interest and depth to your outfit.

6. Layered Look Create a layered look with Aurora Yellow as the focal point. Start with a basic white tee or a neutral-toned blouse and layer it with a cardigan or jacket in Aurora Yellow. This adds dimension to your outfit and allows you to play with different textures and lengths.

7. Accessorize with Yellow Incorporate the Color of the Year into your outfits through accessories. Opt for yellow statement earrings, a chunky yellow necklace, or a trendy yellow hat. These accessories will instantly update your look and show off your fashion-forward style.

8. Casual Chic For a laid-back yet fashionable look, style Aurora Yellow with a casual denim piece. Pair an Aurora Yellow sweater with high-waisted jeans or layer a yellow camisole under a denim jacket. This combination strikes the perfect balance between casual and chic.

9. Office Attire Make a strong statement at work by adding Aurora Yellow to your office attire. Pair an Aurora Yellow blazer with a white blouse and tailored black pants for a professional yet stylish ensemble. This unexpected burst of color will set you apart from the crowd.

10. Shoes and Nail Polish If you’re not ready to fully commit to Aurora Yellow clothing, add this vibrant color to your outfit through your shoes or nail polish. Opt for a pair of stylish yellow heels or flats to instantly brighten up any outfit. Alternatively, paint your nails with a vibrant yellow polish for a trendy and eye-catching manicure.

In conclusion, incorporating the Color of the Year, Aurora Yellow, into your wardrobe can be a fun and fashionable way to stay on top of the latest trends. From bold statement dresses to subtle pops of color in accessories, there are endless possibilities for styling this vibrant hue. Experiment with different looks and find the ones that best express your personal style. So go ahead, embrace the Color of the Year and let your fashion sense shine!
