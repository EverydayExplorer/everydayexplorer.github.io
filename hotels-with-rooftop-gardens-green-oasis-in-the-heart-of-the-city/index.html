Hotels with Rooftop Gardens: Green Oasis in the Heart of the City


In the hustle and bustle of urban life, finding a tranquil escape can sometimes feel like an impossible feat. However, many hotels around the world have found a solution by creating rooftop gardens – green oases in the heart of the city where guests can unwind, relax, and reconnect with nature. In this blog post, we’ll take a closer look at some of the best hotels with rooftop gardens, offering guests a serene retreat amidst the urban jungle.

1. The Peninsula Shanghai, China
Perched atop the historic Bund district, The Peninsula Shanghai boasts one of the most stunning rooftop gardens in the city. Spanning over 8,000 square meters, this lush oasis offers panoramic views of the Huangpu River and the iconic Shanghai skyline. Guests can stroll through beautifully landscaped gardens, adorned with colorful flowers, tranquil ponds, and winding pathways. The rooftop garden also features cozy seating areas where guests can enjoy a quiet moment with a book or simply soak in the breathtaking vistas. Whether it’s a morning yoga session or an evening cocktail hour, The Peninsula Shanghai’s rooftop garden provides a serene sanctuary amidst the vibrant energy of the city.

2. The Ritz-Carlton, Central Park, New York City, USA
Nestled in the heart of Manhattan, The Ritz-Carlton, Central Park offers guests a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the Big Apple. The hotel’s rooftop garden, aptly named ‘La Prairie Spa Terrace,’ provides a tranquil escape with sweeping views of Central Park and the iconic city skyline. Designed with relaxation in mind, the garden features lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and comfortable lounge chairs where guests can unwind and rejuvenate. The La Prairie Spa Terrace also offers a range of rejuvenating spa treatments inspired by nature, allowing guests to indulge in a holistic wellness experience amidst the urban landscape.

3. The Fullerton Bay Hotel, Singapore
Situated in the vibrant Marina Bay district, The Fullerton Bay Hotel boasts a stunning rooftop garden that offers panoramic views of the city skyline and the iconic Marina Bay Sands. Known as the ‘Lantern Rooftop Bar,’ this stylish oasis features lush tropical foliage, elegant furnishings, and a sparkling infinity pool overlooking the glittering city below. Guests can sip handcrafted cocktails and savor gourmet bites while enjoying live music performances against the backdrop of the Singapore skyline. Whether it’s a romantic evening under the stars or a lively gathering with friends, the Lantern Rooftop Bar at The Fullerton Bay Hotel offers an unforgettable experience that celebrates the beauty of Singapore’s urban landscape.

4. Hotel Unique, São Paulo, Brazil
Perched atop the iconic Hotel Unique in São Paulo, the Skye Bar & Restaurant offers guests a chic and sophisticated rooftop garden experience. With panoramic views of the city skyline and Ibirapuera Park, this vibrant oasis is the perfect setting for a memorable dining experience. The rooftop garden features lush greenery, contemporary art installations, and comfortable seating areas where guests can enjoy innovative cocktails and delectable cuisine inspired by Brazilian flavors. Whether it’s a sunset cocktail hour or a gourmet dinner under the stars, the Skye Bar & Restaurant at Hotel Unique provides an unparalleled rooftop experience that captures the essence of São Paulo’s cosmopolitan charm.

5. The Westin Palace, Madrid, Spain
Located in the heart of Madrid, The Westin Palace boasts a spectacular rooftop garden that offers sweeping views of the city’s historic landmarks, including the Royal Palace and the Almudena Cathedral. The hotel’s rooftop terrace, known as ‘La Rotonda Terrace,’ features lush Mediterranean gardens, elegant sculptures, and comfortable lounge areas where guests can unwind and soak in the vibrant atmosphere. La Rotonda Terrace also hosts regular events, including live music performances, wine tastings, and culinary festivals, providing guests with a dynamic and engaging rooftop experience that celebrates the rich cultural heritage of Madrid.

In conclusion, hotels with rooftop gardens offer guests a serene sanctuary amidst the hustle and bustle of the city. From Shanghai to New York City, these green oases provide the perfect setting for relaxation, rejuvenation, and connection with nature. Whether it’s savoring panoramic views, enjoying gourmet cuisine, or simply soaking in the atmosphere, rooftop gardens offer a unique and unforgettable experience that celebrates the beauty of urban landscapes around the world.
