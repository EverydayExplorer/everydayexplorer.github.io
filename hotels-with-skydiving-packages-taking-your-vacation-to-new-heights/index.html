Hotels with Skydiving Packages: Taking Your Vacation to New Heights


Looking to add an adrenaline rush to your next vacation? Imagine soaring through the sky with breathtaking views of stunning landscapes below. Hotels with skydiving packages offer thrill-seekers the opportunity to take their vacation to new heights, quite literally. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best hotels around the world that offer skydiving packages, allowing guests to experience the ultimate adventure during their stay.

1. InterContinental Fiji Golf Resort & Spa
Nestled along the picturesque Natadola Bay in Fiji, the InterContinental Fiji Golf Resort & Spa offers guests a luxurious tropical getaway with the added thrill of skydiving. The resort partners with local skydiving operators to offer guests tandem skydiving experiences over the stunning Fijian coastline. After an exhilarating freefall and peaceful parachute descent, guests can return to the resort to relax on the white sandy beaches or indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment.

2. Hilton Queenstown Resort & Spa, New Zealand
Situated on the shores of Lake Wakatipu in Queenstown, New Zealand, the Hilton Queenstown Resort & Spa offers guests the opportunity to experience skydiving amidst the breathtaking scenery of the South Island. The hotel partners with local skydiving companies to offer guests tandem skydiving experiences over Lake Wakatipu and the surrounding mountains. After landing safely back on solid ground, guests can unwind in the hotel’s luxurious spa or enjoy a gourmet meal at one of the on-site restaurants.

3. Amara Resort and Spa, Sedona, Arizona
For those seeking adventure in the desert, the Amara Resort and Spa in Sedona, Arizona, offers guests the chance to experience skydiving over the stunning red rock landscape of the American Southwest. The hotel partners with local skydiving operators to offer guests tandem skydiving experiences with unparalleled views of Sedona’s iconic rock formations and canyons. After an adrenaline-pumping skydive, guests can relax and take in the breathtaking scenery from the comfort of the hotel’s outdoor pool or hot tub.

4. Hotel del Coronado, San Diego, California
Perched on the sandy shores of Coronado Island near San Diego, California, the historic Hotel del Coronado offers guests the opportunity to experience skydiving over the sparkling waters of the Pacific Ocean. The hotel partners with local skydiving companies to offer guests tandem skydiving experiences with panoramic views of Coronado Beach and the surrounding coastline. After a thrilling skydive, guests can relax on the hotel’s sun-drenched beach or enjoy a refreshing drink at one of the oceanfront bars.

5. Hilton Dubai The Walk, Dubai, United Arab Emirates
Located in the vibrant Jumeirah Beach Residence neighborhood of Dubai, the Hilton Dubai The Walk offers guests the chance to experience skydiving over the iconic Dubai Marina and Palm Jumeirah. The hotel partners with local skydiving operators to offer guests tandem skydiving experiences with stunning views of the city’s skyline and coastline. After a heart-pounding skydive, guests can unwind in the hotel’s rooftop pool or enjoy a meal at one of the nearby waterfront restaurants.

In conclusion, hotels with skydiving packages offer guests the opportunity to experience the ultimate thrill of freefalling through the sky while enjoying breathtaking views of stunning landscapes below. Whether you’re soaring over tropical islands, desert canyons, or urban skylines, these hotels provide the perfect base for an unforgettable skydiving adventure during your next vacation.
