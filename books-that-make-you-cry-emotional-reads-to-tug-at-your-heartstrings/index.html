Books That Make You Cry: Emotional Reads to Tug at Your Heartstrings


Have you ever picked up a book that made you cry? The kind of book that tugs at your heartstrings and leaves you feeling emotionally raw? These ’emotional reads’ have a unique power to touch our souls and make us reflect on the depth of the human experience. In this blog post, we will explore some of these books that have the ability to evoke tears and leave a lasting impact on our lives.

1. ‘The Kite Runner’ by Khaled Hosseini: Set against the backdrop of Afghanistan’s turbulent history, this novel tells the story of Amir, a privileged boy, and his loyal friend Hassan. As their lives intertwine, the consequences of betrayal and redemption unfold, making it impossible to read this book without shedding a tear.

2. ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ by John Green: This heart-wrenching YA novel follows the love story of Hazel and Gus, two teenagers who meet at a cancer support group. As they navigate their illness and the challenges it brings, their love for each other grows, bringing both joy and sorrow. Be prepared to have tissues handy for this one.

3. ‘A Little Life’ by Hanya Yanagihara: This epic tale delves into the lives of four college friends as they navigate the complexities of adulthood. At its core is Jude, a man haunted by a traumatic past, and his enduring friendships. This emotionally charged novel will leave you emotionally drained yet grateful for the power of human connection.

4. ‘Me Before You’ by Jojo Moyes: Prepare to have your heart shattered by this bittersweet love story between Louisa, a quirky young woman, and Will, a once adventurous man now paralyzed from the neck down. As they form an unlikely bond, they both learn valuable lessons about life, love, and the power of choice.

5. ‘The Book Thief’ by Markus Zusak: Set in Nazi Germany, this powerful novel follows the life of Liesel Meminger, a young girl who finds solace in books during the darkest of times. Through her eyes, we witness the resilience of the human spirit and the profound impact of storytelling. This book is guaranteed to leave you in tears.

6. ‘The Lovely Bones’ by Alice Sebold: A haunting and poignant tale, this novel explores the aftermath of a young girl’s murder and its impact on her family and community. Through the eyes of Susie Salmon, who watches from the afterlife, we are taken on a journey of grief, healing, and the power of love.

7. ‘The Nightingale’ by Kristin Hannah: Set in World War II France, this story follows the lives of two sisters, Vianne and Isabelle, who find themselves on opposite sides of the war. As they navigate the horrors of the Nazi occupation, their bond and resilience are tested. This emotionally charged historical fiction will leave you breathless.

These books are just a glimpse into the world of emotional reads that will tug at your heartstrings. Whether it’s through heartbreak, loss, or the resilience of the human spirit, these stories have the power to move us and remind us of our shared humanity. So grab a box of tissues, find a cozy spot, and immerse yourself in the emotional rollercoaster that these books have to offer. Happy reading!
