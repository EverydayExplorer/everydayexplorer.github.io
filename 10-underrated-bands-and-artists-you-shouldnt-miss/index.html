10 Underrated Bands and Artists You Shouldn't Miss


In the vast ocean of music, there are often hidden gems waiting to be discovered. While popular bands and artists dominate the airwaves, there is a wealth of underrated talent that often slips under the radar. In this blog, we will shed light on ten such bands and artists that you shouldn’t miss. These acts might not be receiving mainstream attention, but their unique sound and unrivaled talent deserve recognition. So, let’s dive in and explore the world of underrated music!

1. Moonchild:
Moonchild’s soulful blend of jazz and R&B is a soothing experience for the ears. Their dreamy melodies and mesmerizing vocals transport listeners to a state of pure bliss. With albums like ‘Voyager’ and ‘Little Ghost,’ this LA-based trio creates music that is both nostalgic and fresh.

2. Khruangbin:
Hailing from Texas, Khruangbin blends elements of funk, soul, and psychedelic rock to create a sound that is truly mesmerizing. The band’s groovy instrumentals take listeners on an unforgettable musical journey. Albums like ‘The Universe Smiles Upon You’ and ‘Con Todo El Mundo’ will transport you to another universe.

3. Ripe:
For those craving high-energy and feel-good tunes, Ripe has got you covered. This seven-piece band from Boston seamlessly blends funk, rock, and pop to create infectious and dance-worthy tracks. Explore their albums like ‘Joy in the Wild Unknown’ and ‘Hey Hello’ for an instant mood boost.

4. Sampha:
Sampha’s hauntingly beautiful voice and heartfelt lyrics have made him an underrated gem in the industry. His soulful R&B melodies combined with introspective songwriting make for a captivating listening experience. Dive into his critically acclaimed album ‘Process’ for a glimpse into his immense talent.

5. The Teskey Brothers:
Bringing back the golden era of soul, The Teskey Brothers will teleport you to a time when music was raw and soul-stirring. Hailing from Australia, this quartet perfectly captures the essence of classic blues and vintage R&B. Their album ‘Run Home Slow’ will make you feel like you’re sitting in a smoky blues club.

6. Lianne La Havas:
Lianne La Havas effortlessly blends folk, soul, and pop, creating a sound that is both soothing and empowering. Her delicate yet powerful vocals and emotionally charged songwriting are a treat for the soul. Albums like ‘Blood’ and ‘Lianne La Havas’ showcase her incredible range and versatility.

7. Jacob Banks:
With a voice that commands attention and lyrics that resonate deeply, Jacob Banks is an artist you won’t want to miss. His fusion of soul, R&B, and hip-hop creates a powerful and emotionally charged musical experience. Dive into his album ‘Village’ for a taste of his raw talent.

8. Little Simz:
Little Simz is a force to be reckoned with in the hip-hop world. Her introspective lyrics, razor-sharp delivery, and genre-bending sound make her an artist worth paying attention to. Albums like ‘GREY Area’ and ‘Stillness in Wonderland’ showcase her ability to push the boundaries of hip-hop.

9. Big Thief:
Big Thief’s indie rock sound, led by Adrianne Lenker’s mesmerizing vocals, creates a deeply intimate and emotive experience. Their music, with poetic lyrics and delicate instrumentals, strikes a chord with its vulnerability. Albums like ‘Capacity’ and ‘Two Hands’ will leave you moved and captivated.

10. Tank and The Bangas:
Blending elements of funk, soul, and spoken word, Tank and The Bangas bring a refreshing and unique sound to the table. The band’s infectious energy and frontwoman Tarriona ‘Tank’ Ball’s dynamic vocals make for an unforgettable live experience. Explore their album ‘Green Balloon’ for a taste of their energetic artistry.

Conclusion:

While the music industry may often shine the spotlight on the popular acts, it’s important not to overlook the wealth of talent that often goes unnoticed. The ten bands and artists mentioned above offer a fresh and diverse array of sounds that deserve recognition. So, if you’re in search of musical gems, don’t miss out on the tunes of Moonchild, Khruangbin, Ripe, Sampha, The Teskey Brothers, Lianne La Havas, Jacob Banks, Little Simz, Big Thief, and Tank and The Bangas. Give them a listen, and you’ll be rewarded with exceptional music experiences that will leave a lasting impression.
