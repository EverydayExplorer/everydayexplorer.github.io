Top 10 Online Learning Platforms of 2021


Are you interested in expanding your knowledge and learning new skills but find it challenging to fit traditional classroom-based courses into your busy schedule? You’re not alone! Luckily, the rise of online learning platforms has made it easier than ever to learn at your own pace and in your own time. With a plethora of options available, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 online learning platforms of 2021 that offer high-quality courses across various subjects. Whether you’re looking to enhance your professional skills, learn a new hobby, or indulge your intellectual curiosity, these platforms have got you covered.

1. Udemy: With over 150,000 courses taught by industry experts, Udemy is one of the most popular online learning platforms. From programming and web development to photography and music, Udemy offers a wide array of courses to suit every interest and skill level.

2. Coursera: Collaborating with leading universities and companies, Coursera provides specialized courses and online degrees from top institutions. Whether you want to gain expertise in data science, business, or psychology, Coursera has a comprehensive range of courses to choose from.

3. LinkedIn Learning: Formerly known as Lynda.com, LinkedIn Learning offers a vast library of video courses taught by industry professionals. With a focus on professional skills and career development, LinkedIn Learning is an excellent choice for individuals looking to enhance their employability.

4. Skillshare: Skillshare stands out by offering a wide range of creative courses, including graphic design, illustration, writing, and more. With both free and premium membership options, it’s an accessible platform for aspiring artists and creative professionals.

5. Khan Academy: Khan Academy is renowned for its free, high-quality educational videos across various subjects, primarily aimed at K-12 students. However, it also offers college-level courses in math, science, and arts, making it a valuable resource for learners of all ages.

6. Udacity: Focusing on technical and professional skills, Udacity offers nanodegree programs developed in collaboration with tech industry leaders. If you’re looking to strengthen your expertise in fields like programming, artificial intelligence, or data science, Udacity is an excellent choice.

7. Codecademy: As the name suggests, Codecademy is a platform dedicated to teaching coding and programming skills. Offering interactive coding exercises and projects, it’s an ideal platform for beginners to get started on their programming journey.

8. MasterClass: If you’ve ever dreamt of learning from world-renowned experts and celebrities, MasterClass is the platform for you. With courses taught by the likes of Serena Williams, Neil Gaiman, and Gordon Ramsay, MasterClass brings expertise from various domains right into your home.

9. FutureLearn: FutureLearn partners with top universities and institutions to offer a vast range of courses across multiple disciplines. The platform emphasizes social learning, enabling participants to engage in discussions and collaborate with fellow learners.

10. edX: Developed by Harvard and MIT, edX is a nonprofit learning platform that hosts online courses from prestigious institutions worldwide. With a strong focus on university-level courses, edX provides opportunities to earn verified certificates and even full degrees.

These are just a few examples of the wide range of online learning platforms available in 2021. With their flexibility, affordability, and diverse course offerings, they offer a valuable alternative to traditional education. Whether you want to acquire new skills for personal growth or enhance your professional prospects, online learning platforms provide a wealth of opportunities for learners of all backgrounds. So, take advantage of the digital revolution and embark on your learning journey today!
