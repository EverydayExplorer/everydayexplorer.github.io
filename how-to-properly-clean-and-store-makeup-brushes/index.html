How to Properly Clean and Store Makeup Brushes


As beauty enthusiasts, we all know that using makeup brushes is a necessary step to achieving a flawless, polished look. And just like how we take care of our skin and maintain our skincare routine, we should also prioritize cleaning and storing our makeup brushes correctly. Not only does this practice extend the brushes’ longevity, but it also ensures that they remain hygienic and free from bacteria buildup that can harm our skin.

In this blog post, we’ll dive into the best practices for cleaning and storing makeup brushes to keep them in top condition.

Why is it important to clean your makeup brushes regularly?

Think of your makeup brushes as a breeding ground for bacteria. Every time you use them, they come in contact with your skin, oils, dirt, and products, making them prone to buildup and contamination. Using unclean brushes can lead to breakouts, clogged pores, and even skin infections. Thus, it’s crucial to wash your makeup brushes regularly, especially if you’re using them often.

How often should you clean your makeup brushes?

The frequency of cleaning your makeup brushes depends on how often you use them. Experts suggest cleaning them every week or two if you use them daily. Meanwhile, if you’re using the brushes for cream-based products such as foundation or concealer, it’s best to wash them after each use to keep them from drying up and causing buildup.

The following are the steps to properly clean your makeup brushes:

Step 1: Rinse the brushes under running water.

Start by wetting the brush heads under warm running water. Avoid submerging the entire brush into water as it can cause damage to the handle or weaken the glue holding the bristles together.

Step 2: Use a gentle brush cleanser.

Apply a small amount of gentle brush cleanser to your palms or a cleaning mat. Massage the brush bristles in circular motions to work the product into the brush’s fibers and remove any makeup residue, oils, or dirt.

Step 3: Rinse the brushes again.

Rinse the brushes under running water until the water runs clear, and the suds are gone. Make sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid leaving any soap residue on the brush.

Step 4: Squeeze out excess water and reshape the brush.

Using a clean towel, gently squeeze out any excess water from the brush bristles. Then, reshape the brush head by running your fingers through the bristles and molding them back into their original shape.

Step 5: Dry the brushes.

Lay the brushes flat on a clean towel or a brush drying stand to air dry. Avoid drying them upright as water can seep down and loosen the glue that’s holding the bristles together.

How to properly store your makeup brushes?

After cleaning your makeup brushes, it’s essential to store them correctly to maintain their shape and keep them clean till the next use. Here are the best ways to store your brushes:

Option 1: Brush Roll

A brush roll is a compact and convenient way to store your brushes, especially if you’re always on the go. It protects your brushes from getting damaged or dirty during travel while keeping them organized and easily accessible. A brush roll usually has slots of different sizes that can accommodate various brush sizes and shapes.

Option 2: Brush Cup

A brush cup is another option for storing your makeup brushes at home. It’s simple and straightforward as it only requires an empty cup or jar and a clean towel. Simply place the brushes in the cup with the bristle heads facing upward, and wrap a towel around the base to keep them in place. This will help maintain the brushes’ shape and keep them clean and dry in between uses.

Final Thoughts

Cleaning and storing your makeup brushes may seem like a tedious task, but it’s essential for maintaining the brush’s quality and keeping your skin healthy. By following the proper steps for cleaning and storing, you can keep your brushes in excellent condition for longer and avoid any skin-related issues that may arise from using dirty or contaminated brushes.

Remember to clean your brushes frequently and store them properly to keep them in top condition, which is essential for your overall health and well-being. By prioritizing your tools’ maintenance, you can achieve fantastic makeup looks and keep your skin healthy and beautiful.
