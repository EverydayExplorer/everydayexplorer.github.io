The Most Romantic Hotels for a Dream Wedding Destination


Are you looking to tie the knot in a breathtaking and romantic setting? Imagine exchanging vows with your loved one in a destination that feels like a dream come true. We have curated a list of the most romantic hotels around the world that serve as perfect wedding destinations. Whether you are planning an intimate ceremony or a grand celebration, these hotels will make your wedding day truly memorable.

1. Santorini Grace Hotel - Santorini, Greece:
Situated on the stunning island of Santorini, the Santorini Grace Hotel offers a picture-perfect backdrop for your dream wedding. With its breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea and the iconic Santorini sunset, this hotel is a favorite among those seeking a romantic destination wedding. You can choose to have your ceremony on the hotel’s terrace or by the poolside, ensuring a truly magical experience.

2. Villa d’Este - Lake Como, Italy:
Nestled on the shores of Lake Como, Villa d’Este is the epitome of elegance and luxury. This historic hotel is renowned for its beautiful gardens, opulent interiors, and stunning views of the lake. Imagine exchanging vows in the hotel’s charming garden or hosting a grand reception in one of the hotel’s magnificent dining halls. Your wedding at Villa d’Este is guaranteed to be a fairytale come to life.

3. Bovey Castle - Dartmoor, England:
If you’re dreaming of a fairytale wedding in the English countryside, Bovey Castle is the perfect venue. This grand hotel is set amidst the rolling hills of Dartmoor National Park, providing a picturesque setting for your special day. The hotel offers a variety of wedding packages, including outdoor ceremonies in their beautiful gardens or indoor events in their luxurious ballrooms. Bovey Castle is the ultimate destination for couples seeking a romantic and elegant wedding in England.

4. Amanjena - Marrakech, Morocco:
For a unique and exotic wedding experience, Amanjena in Marrakech stands out as a top choice. This stunning hotel reflects the traditional architecture of Morocco and offers a tranquil and romantic atmosphere. The hotel’s expansive gardens and peaceful courtyards provide idyllic settings for your ceremony and reception. With its luxuriously appointed suites and world-class service, Amanjena guarantees an unforgettable wedding celebration.

5. The Ritz-Carlton, Kapalua - Maui, Hawaii:
Hawaii has long been known as a dream wedding destination, and The Ritz-Carlton in Kapalua is the epitome of luxury and romance. With its breathtaking ocean views, lush gardens, and stunning sunsets, this hotel offers a truly magical setting for your special day. The hotel’s experienced wedding planners will ensure that every detail is taken care of, allowing you to relax and enjoy your celebration in paradise.

6. Viceroy Bali - Ubud, Bali:
For couples seeking a wedding surrounded by nature’s beauty, Viceroy Bali in Ubud is the perfect choice. This luxurious boutique hotel is nestled in the heart of Bali’s lush rainforest, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valley. The hotel’s infinity pool and private villas create a serene and romantic ambiance for your wedding. With its personalized service and attention to detail, Viceroy Bali ensures a truly unforgettable wedding experience.

7. Amangiri - Canyon Point, Utah, USA:
If you prefer a more unconventional and awe-inspiring wedding destination, Amangiri in Utah is an incredible choice. Situated in a remote desert landscape, this lavish resort blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings. Exchange your vows against the backdrop of towering cliffs and red rock formations for a truly extraordinary experience. Amangiri offers a range of wedding packages tailored to your specific needs, ensuring a flawless and unforgettable celebration.

8. Singita Sasakwa Lodge - Grumeti Reserve, Tanzania:
For an unforgettable wedding in the heart of the African wilderness, Singita Sasakwa Lodge in Tanzania is the ultimate destination. This exclusive lodge offers a unique blend of luxury and adventure, with its breathtaking views of the Serengeti plains and an abundance of wildlife. Say ‘I do’ in the lodge’s beautiful gardens or opt for a wilderness ceremony overlooking the vast savanna. Singita Sasakwa Lodge will create a wedding experience that combines the beauty of nature with the utmost sophistication.

These are just a few of the most romantic hotels around the world that offer dream wedding destinations. Each of these hotels provides a unique and unforgettable experience for couples looking to celebrate their love in a breathtaking setting. Whether you envision an intimate ceremony or a grand affair, these hotels will ensure that your wedding day is everything you’ve ever dreamed of and more. Say ‘I do’ in one of these spectacular venues and create memories that will last a lifetime.
