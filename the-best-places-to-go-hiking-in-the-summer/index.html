The Best Places to Go Hiking in the Summer


Hello there, adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers! As the summer season approaches, it’s the perfect time to lace up your hiking boots, put on your sun hat, and embark on an exciting journey through some of the most breathtaking hiking trails around the world. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or just getting started, we’ve got you covered with a list of the best places to go hiking this summer. So, let’s dive right in and explore these stunning destinations!

1. The Inca Trail, Peru: Prepare to be awe-struck as you trek through the majestic ruins of Machu Picchu along the iconic Inca Trail. This ancient pathway will lead you through lush forests, cloud-covered mountains, and awe-inspiring landscapes. It’s a journey that combines history, culture, and natural beauty in a way that is truly unforgettable.

2. The Appalachian Trail, USA: Stretching over 2,000 miles through 14 states, the Appalachian Trail offers hikers a diverse range of terrains and stunning vistas. From the lush forests of Georgia to the rugged peaks of New Hampshire, this trail is a hiker’s paradise. Make sure to plan your hike in sections or challenge yourself to complete the entire trail if you’re up for an epic adventure!

3. The Dolomites, Italy: Prepare to be enchanted by the dramatic peaks and picturesque valleys of the Dolomites. This UNESCO World Heritage site offers hikers a plethora of trails to choose from, surrounded by breathtaking alpine scenery. Immerse yourself in the beauty of these Italian mountains and be rewarded with panoramic views that will leave you in awe.

4. Torres del Paine National Park, Chile: Known for its mesmerizing landscapes, Torres del Paine National Park in Chile is a true gem for hikers. Marvel at the towering granite peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and sprawling glaciers as you explore the various trails this park has to offer. The famous “W Trek” is a must-do, taking you through some of the park’s most iconic sights.

5. Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, China: Step into a real-life fairytale as you hike through the otherworldly landscapes of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park. This UNESCO World Heritage site served as inspiration for the floating mountains in the movie Avatar, and it’s not hard to see why. Walking amidst towering sandstone pillars and lush greenery will make you feel like you’ve entered a magical realm.

6. The Pacific Crest Trail, USA: If you’re up for an epic adventure, the Pacific Crest Trail (PCT) is the ultimate challenge. Spanning over 2,600 miles from Mexico to Canada, this long-distance trail traverses diverse terrains and ecosystems. Journey through deserts, alpine meadows, and dense forests, encountering wildlife and fellow hikers along the way. Be sure to plan ahead and pack well, as this trail is not for the faint of heart!

7. The Haute Route, Switzerland/France: Strap on your hiking boots and prepare for an alpine adventure on the Haute Route. This iconic trail takes you through the heart of the Swiss and French Alps, showcasing breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, pristine glaciers, and charming mountain villages. It’s a challenging yet rewarding experience that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.

Remember, before setting off on any hiking adventure, ensure you are well-prepared with proper gear, maps, and knowledge of the trail. Always prioritize safety and respect nature by leaving no trace behind.

So, fellow adventurers, start planning your summer hiking escapades and let the wonders of nature be your guide. Whether you seek tranquility, stunning vistas, or a physical challenge, these hiking destinations will surely satisfy your wanderlust. Happy hiking!
