Collecting Vintage Buttons: Small Treasures with a Rich History


Have you ever stumbled upon a small, seemingly insignificant button in your grandmother’s sewing kit or a dusty box at a flea market? Did you ever stop to think about the story behind that tiny object? Buttons may be small, but they hold a rich history and have become highly sought-after collectibles for enthusiasts around the world. In this blog post, we will delve into the fascinating world of collecting vintage buttons and explore why they are considered small treasures with a rich history.

A Brief History of Buttons

Buttons have been used by humans for thousands of years, with evidence of their existence dating back to ancient civilizations. The earliest buttons were typically made from natural materials, such as shells, bones, or wood. It wasn’t until the 13th century that buttons made from metals, such as brass or bronze, started to appear.

During the 18th and 19th centuries, buttons became more intricate and decorative. They were made from materials like ivory, mother of pearl, and glass, and adorned with beautiful designs, engravings, and even gemstones. This period, known as the ‘Golden Age of Buttons,’ saw buttons being used not only as functional fasteners but also as symbols of wealth and fashion.

Why Collect Vintage Buttons?

Collecting vintage buttons offers a unique opportunity to delve into a wide range of historical and cultural aspects. Each button tells a story of its own, representing the fashion trends, materials, and craftsmanship of its time. Whether you are fascinated by the intricate designs of Victorian buttons or the streamlined Art Deco styles of the 1920s, collecting vintage buttons allows you to connect with the past and appreciate the artistry of yesteryear.

Furthermore, vintage buttons are not only beautiful but also highly versatile. They can be used in various crafting projects, such as jewelry making, clothing embellishment, or even as decorative pieces in art projects. Collecting vintage buttons not only satisfies the desire for historical preservation but also provides a practical and creative outlet.

The Thrill of the Hunt

One of the most exciting aspects of collecting vintage buttons is the thrill of the hunt. Unlike contemporary buttons that can be easily purchased online or at a store, vintage buttons require a keen eye and an adventurous spirit. Collectors often need to visit flea markets, antique stores, and estate sales, spending hours combing through piles of buttons in search of that hidden gem.

The joy of finding a rare or highly sought-after button can be exhilarating. It’s like unearthing a tiny piece of history, a treasure that has been waiting patiently to be discovered. The hunt for vintage buttons is not only rewarding but also a great way to connect with fellow collectors and exchange stories, tips, and knowledge.

Tips for Starting Your Vintage Button Collection

If you’re ready to embark on your own vintage button collecting journey, here are a few tips to get you started:

  1. Educate yourself: Familiarize yourself with different styles, materials, and time periods of vintage buttons to build your knowledge base. Books and online resources offer valuable information to help you make informed choices.

  2. Start small: Begin your collection by focusing on a specific era, style, or material that interests you the most. This will help narrow down your search and make it more manageable.

  3. Connect with other collectors: Join online forums, social media groups, or local meet-ups to connect with fellow collectors. They can provide valuable insights, guidance, and even lead you to hidden sources or sellers.

  4. Take care of your collection: Proper storage is essential to preserve the condition of your vintage buttons. Avoid exposure to sunlight, moisture, or extreme temperatures, as these can damage the materials over time.

  5. Explore different sources: Don’t limit yourself to traditional antique stores or flea markets. Check out online auctions, estate sales, secondhand shops, and even thrift stores. You never know where you might find your next prized button.

The Value of Vintage Buttons

While the value of vintage buttons can vary depending on factors such as rarity, condition, and desirability, they can be a valuable investment. Particularly rare or sought-after buttons can fetch high prices in the collector’s market. However, for many collectors, the true value lies in the joy and fulfillment that comes from preserving a piece of history and appreciating the craftsmanship of the past.

Final Thoughts

Collecting vintage buttons is a fascinating hobby that allows you to uncover the hidden stories behind these small treasures of the past. From the materials used to the intricate designs, each button holds a piece of history, waiting to be explored and appreciated. Whether you’re a seasoned collector or just starting out, the world of vintage buttons offers countless opportunities for discovery and creativity. So next time you come across a vintage button, remember that it’s not just a simple fastening device; it’s a small treasure with a rich history. Happy collecting!
