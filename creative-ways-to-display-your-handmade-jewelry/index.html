Creative Ways to Display Your Handmade Jewelry


Are you tired of your beautiful handmade jewelry getting lost in the mix? Do you want to find creative ways to display your unique pieces and make them stand out? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore a variety of innovative and eye-catching ways to showcase your handmade jewelry.

1. Shadow Box Frames: Shadow box frames are a great way to display your jewelry while adding an artistic touch to your space. Simply mount your favorite pieces on a piece of velvet or satin and arrange them in the frame. This not only keeps your jewelry organized but also creates a stunning visual display.

2. Vintage Drawer Displays: Give your jewelry a vintage vibe by repurposing old drawers as display cases. Line the drawers with soft fabric or velvet and arrange your jewelry inside. This unique display will add character to your space and make your jewelry collection a focal point.

3. Branch Jewelry Trees: Bring a touch of nature indoors by creating a jewelry tree using branches. Find a sturdy branch and paint it in a color that complements your decor. Attach small hooks or wire to the branches to hang your jewelry. This rustic and whimsical display is sure to catch the eye of anyone who enters your space.

4. Picture Frame Earring Holders: Don’t let your earrings get tangled! Convert a picture frame into an earring holder by removing the glass and attaching a mesh or wire to the frame. Hang your earrings from the mesh or wire, and you’ll have a stylish and organized way to display your earring collection.

5. Vintage Suitcase Displays: If you’re a fan of vintage charm, repurpose an old suitcase as a jewelry display. Line the inside with fabric and add small compartments or dividers to keep your jewelry organized. This portable display is not only functional but also adds a touch of nostalgia to your space.

6. Jewelry Display Boards: Create a personalized and customizable display board for your jewelry. Cover a corkboard or foam board with fabric or decorative paper, then attach hooks or pins to hang your jewelry. You can arrange the hooks or pins in any pattern you like, making it easy to change the display as you add new pieces to your collection.

7. Vintage Tea Cup Jewelry Dishes: Give your jewelry a whimsical home by repurposing vintage tea cups as jewelry dishes. Place a small amount of cotton or velvet inside the cup to create a soft surface for your jewelry. Not only will this display option keep your jewelry organized, but it will also add a touch of vintage elegance to your space.

8. Jewelry Display Mannequins: If you want to showcase your necklaces and bracelets, consider using jewelry display mannequins. These small mannequins are specifically designed to hold and display your jewelry. Choose a mannequin that fits your style and add your favorite pieces to create an eye-catching display.

9. Floating Shelves: Install floating shelves on your wall to create a minimalist and modern display for your jewelry. Place small decorative dishes or trays on the shelves and arrange your jewelry inside. This sleek and stylish display option allows your jewelry to take center stage.

10. Pegboard Displays: Pegboards are not just for tools! Use a pegboard to create a versatile and customizable jewelry display. Attach hooks or pegboard accessories to hang your jewelry and arrange them in any pattern you like. This display option is perfect for those who like to change their displays frequently.

With these creative and unique ways to display your handmade jewelry, you’ll be able to showcase your pieces in a way that reflects your personal style. Not only will these displays make your jewelry stand out, but they will also add a touch of personality to your space. So go ahead, get creative, and let your handmade jewelry shine!
