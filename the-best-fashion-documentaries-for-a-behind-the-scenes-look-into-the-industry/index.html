The Best Fashion Documentaries for a Behind-the-Scenes Look into the Industry


Introduction Fashion documentaries have become increasingly popular in recent years, offering viewers a behind-the-scenes look into the glamorous and often mysterious world of the fashion industry. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or simply curious about the inner workings of this fascinating world, these documentaries provide an in-depth exploration of the creative process, the business side of fashion, and the renowned figures that have shaped the industry. In this blog post, we will be diving deep into the best fashion documentaries that offer unique insights into the fashion industry, its history, and its impact on society. So grab some popcorn, sit back, and get ready to be inspired.

The September Issue Directed by R.J. Cutler, ‘The September Issue’ is often regarded as one of the best fashion documentaries ever made. It provides an unprecedented look into the creation of Vogue’s iconic September issue, known for being the largest and most revered issue of the year. The documentary follows the legendary Editor-in-Chief, Anna Wintour, and her team as they work tirelessly to produce a visually stunning masterpiece. From the editorial meetings to the photo shoots, viewers gain an understanding of the immense effort that goes into creating a fashion magazine.

Dior and I ‘Dior and I’ allows viewers to step into the world of luxury fashion through the eyes of Raf Simons, the newly appointed Creative Director of Christian Dior. Directed by Frederic Tcheng, this documentary showcases the intricate process of creating a haute couture collection within a demanding timeframe. Viewers witness the collaboration between Simons and the skilled atelier of Dior, capturing the artistic vision, craftsmanship, and emotions involved in bringing a collection to life. This film is a must-watch for those interested in the behind-the-scenes of a renowned fashion house.

The First Monday in May Directed by Andrew Rossi, ‘The First Monday in May’ offers a compelling look into the intersection of fashion and art by highlighting the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s annual Met Gala. The film explores the months of preparation that go into curating the museum’s fashion exhibition and organizing the star-studded gala. With appearances from influential figures such as Anna Wintour and Rihanna, this documentary showcases the fusion of fashion, celebrity culture, and high art, offering an intriguing glimpse into the immense effort behind one of fashion’s most anticipated events.

McQueen As the name suggests, ‘McQueen’ is a documentary about the late Alexander McQueen, one of the most celebrated and innovative designers of our time. Directed by Ian Bonh√¥te and Peter Ettedgui, this film delves deep into McQueen’s creativity, inspirations, and troubled personal life. Through interviews with his friends, family, and collaborators, viewers gain a profound understanding of McQueen’s genius and the challenges he faced as he pushed the boundaries of fashion. This documentary is a touching tribute to McQueen’s unrivaled artistic vision and his enduring impact on the industry.

Bill Cunningham New York ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ pays homage to the iconic street fashion photographer, Bill Cunningham. Directed by Richard Press, the film celebrates Cunningham’s unique style and unwavering commitment to capturing the ever-changing trends of New York City. Through intimate interviews and glimpses into Cunningham’s daily routine, viewers witness his unmatched passion for fashion, despite his reluctance to be part of the elite fashion scene. This captivating documentary is a reminder of the transformative power of personal style and the influence of street fashion on the industry.

Advanced Style Directed by Lina Plioplyte, ‘Advanced Style’ celebrates the elegance and individuality of older women who have defied societal expectations and embraced bold, unconventional fashion choices. This uplifting documentary profiles seven stylish women ranging in age from 62 to 95, offering an inspiring message about self-expression and ageless style. ‘Advanced Style’ is a refreshing departure from traditional fashion narratives, highlighting the beauty and confidence that comes with embracing personal style at any age.

Conclusion From exploring the inner workings of fashion publications to celebrating the extraordinary talents of designers and photographers, these fashion documentaries provide an inside look into the fashion world. Through compelling storytelling and captivating visuals, viewers gain a deeper understanding of the industry’s complexities, its historical significance, and the individuals who have shaped its evolution. Whether you’re a fashion aficionado or simply interested in the creative process, these documentaries offer a unique and enlightening experience. So, grab your remote and embark on a fashion-filled journey with these captivating films that will leave you inspired and in awe of the magic that happens behind the scenes of the fashion industry.
