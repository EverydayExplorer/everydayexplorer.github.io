Woodworking Projects for Intermediate Crafters


Woodworking is a fulfilling and rewarding hobby that allows you to create functional and beautiful pieces of furniture and decor. If you’ve already mastered the basics and are looking to challenge yourself with more complex projects, then this blog is for you. In this article, we will explore a variety of woodworking projects specifically designed for intermediate crafters.

1. Floating Shelf with Hidden Drawer
Add both style and functionality to any room in your house with a floating shelf that features a hidden drawer. This project requires intermediate woodworking skills but is well worth the effort. The secret drawer adds a touch of mystery to your shelf while providing extra storage space. You can customize the size, shape, and finish to match your existing decor.

2. Wine Rack
If you’re a wine enthusiast, why not display your collection proudly with a handmade wine rack? There are countless designs and styles to choose from, depending on your skill level and preference. Building a wine rack can be a great opportunity to experiment with different joinery techniques, such as mortise and tenon or dovetail joints.

3. Desk Organizer
Keep your workspace clutter-free and organized with a custom-made desk organizer. This project can be as simple or as complex as you desire, depending on the number of compartments and dividers you include. Intermediate woodworkers can challenge themselves by incorporating interesting joinery methods and experimenting with different types of wood.

4. Jewelry Box
Create a stunning jewelry box to store your precious trinkets and accessories. This intermediate-level project allows you to practice fine woodworking skills such as hinge installation, drawer construction, and decorative inlay work. You can personalize the design by choosing unique hardware and finishes to add an extra touch of elegance.

5. Picture Frame
Display your cherished memories with a handcrafted picture frame. Intermediate crafters can experiment with different molding profiles and joinery techniques to create a unique and personalized frame. Whether you choose a simple design or a more intricate one, a handmade picture frame adds a touch of sophistication to any photograph.

6. Outdoor Bench
Enhance your outdoor living space with a sturdy and beautiful wooden bench. Intermediate woodworkers can challenge themselves by incorporating joinery techniques like mortise and tenon or lap joints. You can also experiment with different finishes to protect the wood from the elements and ensure its longevity.

7. Bookshelf with Built-in Storage
Upgrade your home library or living room with a custom bookshelf that includes built-in storage. This intermediate-level project allows you to practice your cabinet-making skills while providing ample space for your favorite books, decor, or even a hidden compartment. Choose a design that complements your existing furniture and showcases your woodworking skills.

8. Toy Chest
For intermediate crafters with little ones in their lives, building a toy chest can be a rewarding project. Focus on creating a functional and safe storage solution that is durable and sturdy. You can personalize it by adding hand-painted designs or decorative elements to make it extra special.

9. Coffee Table with Storage
Combine style and functionality by building a coffee table with built-in storage. Intermediate woodworkers can challenge themselves by incorporating interesting joinery methods and experimenting with different types of wood. This versatile piece of furniture will not only serve as a focal point in your living room but also provide extra storage space for books, magazines, or blankets.

10. Cutting Board
Challenge yourself with a woodworking project that is both practical and artistic – a wooden cutting board. This project allows you to experiment with different wood species and create unique patterns using different types of wood and grains. Ensure you use food-safe finishes to protect the board and maintain its longevity.

In conclusion, woodworking projects for intermediate crafters are an excellent way to expand your skills and create beautiful, functional pieces. Whether you choose to build a floating shelf with a hidden drawer or a wooden cutting board, these projects offer a balance between complexity and creativity. Remember to practice proper safety measures and enjoy the process of bringing your woodworking ideas to life. Happy woodworking!
