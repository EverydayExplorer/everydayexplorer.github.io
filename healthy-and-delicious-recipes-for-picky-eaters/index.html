Healthy and Delicious Recipes for Picky Eaters


Are you tired of struggling to find healthy and delicious recipes that your picky eaters will actually enjoy? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore a variety of mouthwatering recipes that are not only packed with nutrients but also cater to the taste buds of even the most finicky eaters. Let’s dive in!

Recipe 1: Sneaky Veggie Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:
- 2 cups whole wheat macaroni
- 1 cup butternut squash puree
- 1 cup cauliflower puree
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook the macaroni according to the package instructions and set aside.
2. In a saucepan, combine the butternut squash puree, cauliflower puree, cheddar cheese, milk, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Heat over medium heat until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
3. Pour the sauce over the cooked macaroni and stir until well coated.
4. Serve hot and watch as your picky eaters devour this nutritious and delicious mac and cheese without even realizing they’re eating veggies!

Recipe 2: Turkey Meatball Subs with Hidden Veggies

Ingredients:
- 1 pound ground turkey
- 1/2 cup grated zucchini
- 1/2 cup grated carrot
- 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 egg
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 4 whole wheat sub rolls
- Marinara sauce
- Shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, grated zucchini, grated carrot, onion, garlic, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
3. Shape the mixture into meatballs and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
4. Bake the meatballs for about 20 minutes or until cooked through.
5. Slice the sub rolls in half and spread marinara sauce on one side.
6. Place a few meatballs on top of the sauce and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
7. Bake the subs in the oven for a few minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Serve these tasty meatball subs to your picky eaters and witness them gobble up the hidden veggies with delight!

Recipe 3: Veggie-packed Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients:
- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 cup grated zucchini
- 1 cup grated carrot
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Cooking spray

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized chunks.
3. In a food processor, combine the grated zucchini, grated carrot, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Pulse until well combined.
4. Dip each chicken chunk into the veggie mixture, making sure to coat it evenly. Place the coated chicken pieces on the prepared baking sheet.
5. Spray the nuggets lightly with cooking spray to promote browning.
6. Bake the nuggets for about 15-18 minutes or until cooked through and golden brown.
7. Serve these veggie-packed chicken nuggets with a side of dipping sauce for a nutritious and kid-friendly meal.

Conclusion:

With these healthy and delicious recipes for picky eaters, mealtime battles will become a thing of the past. By incorporating hidden veggies and using wholesome ingredients, you can create meals that are both nutritious and appealing to even the most selective eaters. So, get into the kitchen and start whipping up these delightful dishes that will leave your picky eaters asking for seconds!
