DIY Macrame Plant Hangers: Adding Greenery to Your Space


In today’s fast-paced world, creating a tranquil and harmonious living space has become more important than ever. One way to achieve this is by incorporating greenery into your home. Plants not only add a touch of nature but also provide numerous health benefits, such as improving air quality and reducing stress. If you’re looking for a creative and stylish way to display your plants, DIY macrame plant hangers are the perfect solution.

Macrame plant hangers have gained popularity in recent years due to their versatility and aesthetic appeal. They are made using a technique called macrame, which involves knotting cords together to create beautiful patterns. With just a few supplies and some patience, you can create stunning macrame plant hangers that will elevate the look of any room.

To get started, gather the following materials:

1. Macrame cord: Choose a cord that is strong and durable. Natural cotton cord is a popular choice, but you can also experiment with different materials like jute or hemp.

2. Wooden or metal ring: This will serve as the anchor for your plant hanger. You can find these at craft stores or repurpose an old keyring or curtain ring.

3. Scissors: Make sure you have a sharp pair of scissors for cutting the cord.

4. Plant pot: Select a pot that complements the style of your macrame hanger. Consider the size and weight of your plant when choosing a pot.

Now that you have your materials ready, let’s dive into the steps of creating your macrame plant hanger:

Step 1: Cut your cord into several lengths, depending on the desired length of your plant hanger. A good starting point is around four times the length of your finished hanger.

Step 2: Fold each cord in half and attach them to the ring using a basic lark’s head knot. This will create a loop for hanging your plant hanger.

Step 3: Divide the cords into groups of two, and start creating knots using a square knot technique. Repeat this process until you reach the desired length for your plant hanger.

Step 4: Once you’ve reached your desired length, gather all the cords together and tie them off with a simple knot. Leave a small tail for a decorative touch.

Step 5: Trim the ends of the cords to your desired length, keeping in mind that longer tassels can add a bohemian flair to your design.

Step 6: Place your plant pot into the macrame hanger and adjust the cords as needed to ensure a secure fit.

Congratulations! You have just created your own macrame plant hanger. Now it’s time to find the perfect spot to display your greenery. Whether it’s in your living room, bedroom, or even your office, the addition of a macrame plant hanger will instantly bring life and style to any space.

To maintain the beauty of your macrame plant hanger, be sure to care for your plants properly. Water them regularly, provide adequate sunlight, and dust the leaves to keep them looking their best.

In conclusion, DIY macrame plant hangers are a fantastic way to add a touch of nature and style to your living space. By following these simple steps, you can create your own unique plant hangers that will impress your friends and family. So why not unleash your creativity and embark on a macrame journey? Get ready to transform your space into a green oasis!
