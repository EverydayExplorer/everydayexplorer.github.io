Bourbon Cocktails for Summer Sipping: Cool Down with a Drink


When the temperature starts to rise and the sun is shining brightly, nothing beats a refreshing bourbon cocktail to help you cool down and relax. Bourbon is a versatile spirit that can be enjoyed in a variety of cocktails, from classic Old Fashioneds to innovative creations. Whether you’re lounging by the pool, having a backyard barbecue, or simply looking for a way to unwind after a long day, here are some delicious bourbon cocktails to try this summer.

1. Mint Julep
The Mint Julep is a classic cocktail that’s perfect for sipping on a hot summer day. This refreshing drink is made with bourbon, fresh mint, sugar, and crushed ice. It’s simple to make but packs a flavorful punch, making it a favorite among bourbon enthusiasts. Serve it in a chilled silver cup for an added touch of elegance.

2. Bourbon Arnold Palmer
For a twist on the classic Arnold Palmer, try adding bourbon to the mix. This cocktail combines bourbon, iced tea, and lemonade for a refreshing and slightly boozy beverage that’s perfect for summer sipping. Garnish with a lemon wedge and a sprig of mint for a pop of color and flavor.

3. Peach Bourbon Smash
Take advantage of summer’s ripe peaches by making a Peach Bourbon Smash. This fruity cocktail combines bourbon, fresh peaches, lemon juice, and simple syrup for a sweet and tangy flavor profile. Muddle the peaches to extract their juices and flavors before mixing them with the bourbon and other ingredients. Top with a splash of soda water for some effervescence.

4. Bourbon Blackberry Lemonade
Blackberries are a quintessential summer fruit, and they pair beautifully with bourbon in this Bourbon Blackberry Lemonade cocktail. Muddle fresh blackberries with bourbon, lemon juice, and simple syrup for a vibrant and flavorful drink that’s sure to impress. Strain out the blackberry seeds before serving over ice for a smooth and refreshing sip.

5. Bourbon Peach Sweet Tea
Sweet tea is a staple in the South, and adding bourbon and fresh peaches takes it to a whole new level. This Bourbon Peach Sweet Tea is a delightful blend of sweet tea, bourbon, peach puree, and lemon juice. Served over ice with a peach slice garnish, it’s the perfect way to unwind and cool down on a hot summer day.

6. Bourbon Ginger Beer Cooler
For a spicy and refreshing bourbon cocktail, try a Bourbon Ginger Beer Cooler. This zesty drink combines bourbon, ginger beer, lime juice, and a splash of bitters for a bold and invigorating flavor profile. Garnish with a lime wedge and a slice of fresh ginger for a visually appealing touch.

7. Bourbon Watermelon Mojito
Watermelon is a summertime favorite, and it shines in this Bourbon Watermelon Mojito. Muddle fresh watermelon with bourbon, mint leaves, lime juice, and simple syrup for a sweet and herbaceous cocktail that’s perfect for sipping by the pool. Top with soda water for some fizz and garnish with a watermelon wedge for a tropical flair.

These are just a few bourbon cocktails to try this summer. Whether you prefer classic concoctions or more inventive creations, there’s a bourbon cocktail out there to suit your taste. So grab your favorite bottle of bourbon, some fresh ingredients, and get shaking and stirring. Cheers to summer sipping and cool down with a delightful bourbon cocktail in hand.
