10 Must-Visit Photography Exhibitions Around the World


Welcome to our blog, where we take you on a visual journey exploring the most captivating photography exhibitions around the world. We have curated a list of ten must-visit exhibitions that will appeal to both photography enthusiasts and art lovers alike. Get ready to be inspired and amazed by the power of visual storytelling!

1. ‘The Family of Man’ - Luxembourg

We kick off our list with an iconic exhibition that showcases the beauty and diversity of human life. ‘The Family of Man’ in Luxembourg is a timeless collection of photographs that transcends boundaries and speaks to the universal human experience. This exhibition will leave you with a profound sense of connection to the world and its people.

2. ‘Ansel Adams: Masterworks’ - San Francisco, USA

Ansel Adams is a household name in the world of photography, and this exhibition in San Francisco is a tribute to his incredible mastery of landscape photography. Immerse yourself in the grandeur of nature and witness Adams’ unparalleled ability to capture the sublime beauty of the American wilderness.

3. ‘Helmut Newton: Polaroids’ - Berlin, Germany

Prepare to be seduced by the provocative and glamorous world of fashion photography at the ‘Helmut Newton: Polaroids’ exhibition in Berlin. Newton’s instantly recognizable style comes to life through his candid Polaroid shots, giving you a glimpse into his creative process and revealing the intimate moments behind the glossy images.

4. ‘Daido Moriyama: Stray Dog’ - Tokyo, Japan

Step into the gritty streets of Tokyo with Daido Moriyama’s ‘Stray Dog’ exhibition. Known for his raw and spontaneous black-and-white photography, Moriyama captures the essence of urban life like no other. This exhibition will transport you into the heart of Japan’s vibrant cityscape, filled with energy and hidden stories.

5. ‘Cindy Sherman: Untitled Horrors’ - New York City, USA

Dive into the captivating world of self-portraiture with Cindy Sherman’s ‘Untitled Horrors’ exhibition in New York City. Sherman’s thought-provoking photographs challenge notions of identity, gender, and societal expectations. Prepare to question your own assumptions and explore the complexities of the human psyche.

6. ‘Sebastião Salgado: Genesis’ - Paris, France

Embark on a visual journey through some of the most remote and untouched corners of the world with Sebastião Salgado’s ‘Genesis’ exhibition in Paris. Salgado’s breathtaking black-and-white photographs capture the beauty of nature and the resilience of indigenous cultures. Prepare to be awed by the sheer grandeur of our planet.

7. ‘Steve McCurry: The Iconic Photographs’ - London, UK

Steve McCurry’s name is synonymous with powerful storytelling through his lens. ‘The Iconic Photographs’ exhibition in London is a celebration of McCurry’s most recognizable and influential works. From the haunting image of the Afghan girl to the vibrant streets of India, McCurry’s photographs will transport you to different corners of the world.

8. ‘Edward Burtynsky: Water’ - Toronto, Canada

Edward Burtynsky’s ‘Water’ exhibition in Toronto shines a light on one of the most vital resources on our planet. Through his large-scale photographs, Burtynsky explores humanity’s relationship with water, from its scarcity to its exploitation. Prepare to be mesmerized by the beauty and fragility of our planet’s water systems.

9. ‘Annie Leibovitz: Portraits’ - Sydney, Australia

Annie Leibovitz’s ‘Portraits’ exhibition in Sydney is a masterclass in the art of capturing the essence of her subjects. From iconic celebrities to influential figures, Leibovitz’s photographs showcase her ability to capture the depth and personality of her subjects. Prepare to be captivated by her ability to reveal the vulnerability and strength of those in front of her lens.

10. ‘Raghu Rai: India’ - Delhi, India

Our journey concludes with Raghu Rai’s ‘India’ exhibition in Delhi, a celebration of the vibrant and diverse country he calls home. Rai’s photographs capture the essence of India’s rich culture, spirituality, and everyday life. Immerse yourself in the colors, traditions, and stories that make India truly unique.

These ten photography exhibitions offer a glimpse into the power of visual storytelling and the diverse perspectives of talented photographers from around the world. Whether you’re a photography enthusiast or simply appreciate art’s ability to evoke emotions, these exhibitions are not to be missed. So grab your camera, immerse yourself in these captivating exhibitions, and let the world of photography inspire and move you!
