Unforgettable Views: Hotels with Spectacular Scenery


When it comes to travel, there are few things that can beat the feeling of waking up to a breathtaking view right outside your hotel room window. From snow-capped mountains to crystal-clear turquoise waters, the world is full of stunning landscapes that can make your stay truly unforgettable. In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to some of the most awe-inspiring hotels with spectacular scenery, where you can indulge in the beauty of nature while enjoying the comforts of luxury accommodation.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA:
Tucked away in the heart of Utah’s desert landscape, Amangiri is a hidden gem that offers unparalleled views of the surrounding canyons and mesas. This minimalist luxury resort blends seamlessly with its natural surroundings, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the rugged beauty of the American Southwest. Wake up to the sight of the sun rising over the red rock formations, and spend your days exploring the nearby national parks or simply lounging by the stunning infinity pool.

2. Fogo Island Inn, Newfoundland, Canada:
Perched on the edge of the wild Atlantic Ocean, Fogo Island Inn is a contemporary masterpiece that offers panoramic views of the dramatic coastline. Each of its 29 guest rooms features floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the rugged beauty of the surrounding landscape. Whether you’re watching icebergs drift by in the spring or catching a glimpse of the Northern Lights in the winter, the ever-changing scenery at Fogo Island Inn is sure to leave you in awe.

3. Singita Sasakwa Lodge, Serengeti, Tanzania:
Located in the heart of the Serengeti, Singita Sasakwa Lodge offers guests a front-row seat to the annual Great Migration. From the comfort of your suite or private infinity pool, you can witness millions of wildebeest and zebras crossing the vast plains in search of greener pastures. The lodge’s elevated position provides uninterrupted views of the Serengeti, allowing you to witness the circle of life in one of Africa’s most iconic destinations.

4. Jade Mountain, St. Lucia:
Nestled high on a hillside overlooking the Caribbean Sea, Jade Mountain is a true tropical paradise. Each of its open-air suites, known as ‘sanctuaries,’ boasts a private infinity pool and breathtaking views of the iconic Pitons, two volcanic peaks that rise majestically from the turquoise waters below. Immerse yourself in the lush tropical surroundings, and let the sounds of nature lull you to sleep each night.

5. Six Senses Yao Noi, Phang Nga Bay, Thailand:
Perched on a cliffside overlooking the pristine waters of Phang Nga Bay, Six Senses Yao Noi offers guests a slice of paradise. Wake up to the sight of limestone karsts jutting out of the emerald-green sea, and spend your days exploring the nearby islands and hidden lagoons. The resort’s private villas are designed to blend seamlessly with the natural landscape, allowing you to soak in the beauty of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

In conclusion, if you’re looking for a truly unforgettable travel experience, consider staying at one of these hotels with spectacular scenery. From the desert canyons of Utah to the tropical paradise of St. Lucia, each of these properties offers a unique opportunity to connect with nature and create memories that will last a lifetime. So, pack your bags and get ready to be mesmerized by the beauty of the world. Your next adventure awaits!
