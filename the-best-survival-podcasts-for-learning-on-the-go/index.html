The Best Survival Podcasts for Learning on the Go


In today’s fast-paced world, it’s easy to get caught up in the hustle and bustle of daily life. But what if you could learn essential survival skills and stay informed on the go? Podcasts are an excellent way to do just that. With a vast array of topics and styles to choose from, you’re sure to find a podcast that suits your interests and learning style. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best survival podcasts out there, covering topics from wilderness survival to self-defense and more.

Wilderness Survival Podcasts

When it comes to wilderness survival, knowledge is power. These podcasts will teach you how to stay safe and thrive in the great outdoors.

  • The Survival Podcast: Hosted by Jack Spirko, this podcast is a comprehensive guide to survival, covering topics from wilderness survival to self-sufficiency and preparedness.
  • The Wilderness Survival Podcast: With a focus on wilderness survival, this podcast covers topics such as building shelters, finding food, and navigating through the wilderness.
  • The Bushcraft Podcast: Hosted by Dave Canterbury, this podcast explores the art of bushcraft, covering topics such as knife skills, shelter building, and fire starting.

Self-Defense Podcasts

When it comes to self-defense, knowledge is key. These podcasts will teach you how to protect yourself and your loved ones in any situation.

  • The Self-Defense Podcast: Hosted by Michael Janich, this podcast covers topics such as self-defense techniques, personal protection, and martial arts.
  • The Krav Maga Podcast: With a focus on Krav Maga, this podcast explores the self-defense system developed for the Israeli military, covering topics such as hand-to-hand combat and self-defense techniques.
  • The Women’s Self-Defense Podcast: Hosted by a woman, for women, this podcast covers topics such as self-defense techniques, personal safety, and confidence building.

Prepper Podcasts

When it comes to prepping, knowledge is power. These podcasts will teach you how to prepare for any situation, from natural disasters to economic collapse.

  • The Prepper Podcast: Hosted by James Rawles, this podcast covers topics such as preparedness, self-sufficiency, and survival skills.
  • The Survival Mom Podcast: With a focus on prepping and self-sufficiency, this podcast explores topics such as food storage, water purification, and emergency preparedness.
  • The Prepper Broadcasting Network: This podcast network features a variety of prepper shows, covering topics such as survival skills, self-defense, and preparedness.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many excellent survival podcasts out there, covering a wide range of topics and styles. Whether you’re interested in wilderness survival, self-defense, or prepping, there’s a podcast out there for you. By listening to these podcasts on the go, you’ll be able to learn essential survival skills and stay informed, no matter where life takes you. So why not give one a try? You never know when you might need the skills you learn.
