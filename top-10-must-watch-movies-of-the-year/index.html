Top 10 Must-Watch Movies of the Year

4 min · 705 words

As the curtains close on another year in the world of cinema, film enthusiasts are left with a plethora of choices to pick from. With the numerous releases throughout the year, it’s easy to get lost in the sea of movies. But fear not, dear cinephiles, for we’ve got you covered. After scouring the globe for the most critically acclaimed and audience-pleasing films, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 must-watch movies of the year.

10. The Lighthouse

Robert Eggers’ psychological horror film is a masterclass in atmospheric tension. Set in the 1890s, the story follows two lighthouse keepers who descend into madness while stationed on a remote island. With its stunning black-and-white cinematography and outstanding performances, The Lighthouse is a cinematic experience unlike any other.

9. Parasite

Bong Joon-ho’s South Korean dark comedy is a scathing commentary on class struggle. The film tells the story of the Kims, a poor family who scheme their way into the lives of a wealthy family. With its clever writing and outstanding performances, Parasite is a must-see for anyone looking for a thought-provoking cinematic experience.

8. 1917

Sam Mendes’ war drama is a visually stunning film that puts the viewer in the midst of the action. The story follows two soldiers on a mission to deliver a message during World War I. With its innovative cinematography and outstanding performances, 1917 is a cinematic experience that will leave you breathless.

7. Knives Out

Rian Johnson’s whodunit murder mystery is a clever and entertaining film that pays homage to the genre. The story follows a detective as he investigates the murder of a wealthy author. With its outstanding performances and clever plot twists, Knives Out is a must-see for anyone who loves a good mystery.

6. Marriage Story

Noah Baumbach’s drama is a heart-wrenching portrayal of a couple going through a divorce. The film follows Charlie and Nicole as they navigate the complexities of co-parenting and the emotional toll of their breakup. With its outstanding performances and poignant storytelling, Marriage Story is a cinematic experience that will leave you feeling emotional.

5. Joker

Todd Phillips’ origin story of the Joker is a thought-provoking film that explores the themes of mental health and societal isolation. The story follows Arthur Fleck, a struggling comedian who descends into madness as he becomes the infamous Joker. With its outstanding performance from Joaquin Phoenix and its thought-provoking themes, Joker is a must-see for anyone looking for a cinematic experience that will leave them thinking long after the credits roll.

4. Once Upon a Time in Hollywood

Quentin Tarantino’s love letter to 1960s Hollywood is a nostalgic and entertaining film that explores the intersection of fact and fiction. The story follows a faded TV actor and his stunt double as they navigate the changing landscape of Hollywood. With its outstanding performances and clever writing, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood is a must-see for anyone who loves Tarantino’s work.

3. Little Women

Greta Gerwig’s adaptation of Louisa May Alcott’s classic novel is a beautiful and poignant portrayal of the March sisters as they navigate love, loss, and identity. The film features outstanding performances from Saoirse Ronan and Emma Watson, and its poignant storytelling will leave you feeling emotional.

2. Ford v Ferrari

James Mangold’s biographical sports drama is a thrilling film that explores the rivalry between Ford and Ferrari in the world of racing. The story follows Carroll Shelby and Ken Miles as they work to build a car that can take down Ferrari. With its outstanding performances and pulse-pounding action sequences, Ford v Ferrari is a must-see for anyone who loves cars or racing.

1. Avengers: Endgame

Anthony and Joe Russo’s epic conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe is a thrilling film that brings together some of the most iconic superheroes of all time. The story follows the surviving members of the Avengers as they attempt to undo the damage caused by Thanos. With its outstanding performances, pulse-pounding action sequences, and emotional payoff, Avengers: Endgame is a must-see for anyone who loves superhero movies.

As the curtains close on another year in the world of cinema, we’re left with a plethora of choices to pick from. But these top 10 must-watch movies of the year are a great place to start. With their outstanding performances, clever writing, and thought-provoking themes, these films are sure to leave you feeling entertained, emotional, and inspired. So grab some popcorn, get comfortable, and get ready to experience the best of the best in the world of cinema.
