Discovering New Authors: A Guide to Finding Hidden Literary Gems


In a world where bestsellers and blockbuster releases often dominate the literary scene, it can be easy to overlook the vast pool of talented writers who are waiting to be discovered. Yet, discovering new authors can be a gratifying and enriching experience for both readers and writers alike. When you delve into the works of an unknown writer, you become part of a community that appreciates and promotes new talent, fueling their growth and helping them to achieve greater recognition. If you’re curious to learn how to find hidden literary gems, read on to discover valuable tips and tricks.

1. Start with literary magazines and anthologies

The first and perhaps most obvious place to look for new authors is in literary magazines. Literary magazines publish works by up-and-coming writers, giving them a chance to break into the industry. Take some time to peruse the pages of literary magazines like Granta, The Paris Review, and The New Yorker. By doing so, you may discover a previously unknown author who speaks to you and whose style you admire.

Anthologies can also be a great place to find new authors. Anthologies are collections of short stories, poems, or essays by a diverse group of writers. These collections often feature lesser-known writers alongside established writers. You can find anthologies in many bookstores and libraries, as well as online.

2. Join online writing communities

The internet has made it easier than ever to connect with like-minded people and engage with literary communities. Online writing communities like Wattpad, Medium, and Scribophile are excellent places to discover new writers. These platforms allow writers to share their work online, and they also offer opportunities for feedback, critiques, and discussions.

Wattpad, for example, is a free platform where writers can share their stories with readers all over the world. Readers can comment on the stories they read and provide feedback to the writers. Medium, on the other hand, is a platform that offers high-quality, in-depth articles and essays on a diverse range of topics. Finally, Scribophile is a writing community where writers can join groups, give and receive feedback, and share their work with a supportive community.

3. Check out independent bookstores

Independent bookstores can be great places to discover lesser-known authors. Independent booksellers often take pride in curating unique and diverse collections of books that you may not find at larger chain bookstores. These booksellers are passionate about literature and can be an excellent resource for discovering new authors.

Independent bookstores also often host book clubs, author events, and discussions that can help you discover new writers. These events are an excellent opportunity to meet other book enthusiasts, interact with authors, and learn about new and exciting writers.

4. Follow literary award winners and nominees

Literary awards offer a great way to discover new authors. When an author wins a literary award or is nominated for one, it often means that their work is compelling and worth reading. Some of the most famous literary awards include the Pulitzer Prize, the National Book Award, and the Man Booker Prize.

Keep an eye on the nominees and winners of these awards, as well as smaller and more niche awards like the Lambda Literary Awards (awarded to LGBTQ writers) or the Kirkus Prize (awarded to novels across a variety of genres). You can often find these lists online, and they can be a great resource for discovering new and exciting authors.

5. Embrace book recommendations from friends and family

Finally, don’t underestimate the value of personal recommendations. Books are a very personal experience, and what one person loves, another may not. However, asking friends and family for recommendations is a great way to discover new authors and work that you may not have known existed. In addition, it’s an excellent way to spark conversations about literature and deepen your appreciation for the written word.

In conclusion, discovering new authors is an exciting and rewarding experience that can lead readers to exciting and unique literary perspectives. Whether you start with literary magazines, online writing communities, independent bookstores, literary awards, or personal recommendations, remember to keep an open mind and embrace new experiences. By doing so, you’ll be sure to discover many hidden literary gems.
