Experience the Northern Lights: Hotels in Aurora Borealis Viewing Spots


There are few natural phenomena that can rival the awe-inspiring beauty of the Northern Lights, also known as Aurora Borealis. This breathtaking celestial display of dancing lights in the night sky is a sight to behold, and for many, it is a once-in-a-lifetime experience. If you’re planning to witness this enchanting spectacle, choosing the right accommodation is crucial for optimizing your viewing experience. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotels in Aurora Borealis viewing spots, ensuring that your trip is truly unforgettable.

1. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:
Located in the Finnish Lapland, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort is renowned for its unique glass igloos that provide uninterrupted views of the night sky. Imagine lying in bed and gazing at the dancing lights above you – truly a magical experience! The resort also offers a range of activities such as reindeer safaris and snowmobiling, making it an ideal destination for adventure seekers.

2. Hotel Rangá, Iceland:
Nestled in the picturesque Icelandic countryside, Hotel Rangá offers a luxurious and cozy retreat for aurora enthusiasts. With its remote location away from light pollution, this hotel provides excellent opportunities for viewing the Northern Lights. The hotel even offers wake-up calls when the lights appear, so you won’t miss a single moment of this ethereal phenomenon.

3. Tromsø Ice Domes, Norway:
For the ultimate Arctic experience, consider staying at Tromsø Ice Domes in Norway. This unique accommodation allows guests to sleep in intricately carved ice rooms, surrounded by a captivating icy landscape. In addition to witnessing the Northern Lights, you can engage in activities like dog sledding and snowshoeing, immersing yourself in the beauty of the wintry Norwegian countryside.

4. Hotel Arctic, Greenland:
If you’re seeking an off-the-beaten-path adventure, Hotel Arctic in Ilulissat, Greenland, is the perfect choice. Located just above the Arctic Circle, this hotel offers magnificent panoramic views of the Northern Lights. Its remote location also provides an opportunity to explore the stunning ice fjords and witness massive icebergs calving into the sea. It’s a true Arctic paradise.

5. Abisko Mountain Lodge, Sweden:
Situated in the pristine wilderness of Swedish Lapland, Abisko Mountain Lodge is renowned for its clear skies and optimal aurora visibility. The lodge offers comfortable accommodations and knowledgeable guides who can help you make the most of your Northern Lights experience. Additionally, the nearby Abisko National Park provides a beautiful setting for winter activities such as cross-country skiing.

6. Chena Hot Springs Resort, Alaska:
If you’re looking for a unique combination of natural beauty and relaxation, Chena Hot Springs Resort in Fairbanks, Alaska, is an excellent choice. This resort boasts natural hot springs that provide a warm haven amidst the cold Alaskan winter. The remote location ensures minimal light pollution, making it an ideal spot for viewing the Northern Lights while soaking in the therapeutic hot springs.

7. Hotel Kirkjufell, Iceland:
Located near the iconic Kirkjufell mountain on the Snæfellsnes Peninsula, Hotel Kirkjufell is a charming boutique hotel that offers spectacular views of the Northern Lights. The surrounding area provides plenty of opportunities for hiking, exploring lava fields, and discovering picturesque waterfalls. After a day of outdoor adventures, you can unwind in the hotel’s cozy lounge and keep an eye out for the magical Aurora Borealis.

When planning your trip to witness the Northern Lights, remember that visibility is dependent on several factors, including weather conditions and solar activity. While no one can guarantee a sighting, staying at one of these hotels will significantly enhance your chances of witnessing this celestial phenomenon. Whether you choose Finland, Iceland, Norway, Sweden, Greenland, or Alaska, each destination offers a unique experience that combines natural beauty, luxury, and the chance to witness one of nature’s most awe-inspiring spectacles.

Embark on a journey to experience the Northern Lights and create memories that will last a lifetime. Book your accommodation wisely, and get ready to be mesmerized by the ethereal dance of the Aurora Borealis in the night sky.
