Comedy Podcasts That Will Make Your Commute Way More Enjoyable


Commuting can often be a mundane and monotonous part of our daily routine. Spending hours stuck in traffic or cramped on public transportation can feel like a waste of time. However, there is a simple solution to make your commute way more enjoyable - comedy podcasts. These hilarious audio shows are the perfect way to lighten your mood, entertain yourself, and make the time fly by. In this blog post, we will introduce you to some of the best comedy podcasts that will transform your commute into a laughter-filled adventure.

1. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ - Hosted by comedian Joe Rogan, this podcast features candid conversations with a wide range of guests, including fellow comedians, actors, musicians, and scientists. With its long-form format and diverse topics, this podcast will keep you engaged and entertained throughout your commute.

2. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend’ - Late-night talk show host Conan O’Brien brings his trademark humor and wit to this podcast. Each episode features Conan’s conversations with celebrities and friends, where they discuss everything from comedy to life’s absurdities. This podcast is guaranteed to make you laugh out loud and forget about the traffic.

3. ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ - Hosted by comedian Scott Aukerman, this podcast combines hilarious interviews with improvised sketches and music. With a rotating cast of comedians and celebrities, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ offers a unique and unpredictable listening experience that will keep you entertained from start to finish.

4. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’ - If you’re looking for something a bit more risqué, this podcast might be just what you need. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this show follows Jamie as he reads chapters from his dad’s erotic novel. The hosts’ hilarious commentary and reactions to the absurdity of the story will have you in stitches, making your commute a lot more enjoyable.

5. ‘How Did This Get Made?’ - If you’re a fan of movies, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by comedians Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, this show delves into the world of bad movies. The hosts and their guests dissect and make fun of the most bizarre and nonsensical films ever made, providing you with hours of entertainment during your commute.

6. ‘The Dollop’ - This podcast takes a humorous approach to history, with hosts Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds exploring bizarre and lesser-known stories from the past. Their comedic chemistry and witty banter make ‘The Dollop’ an educational and hilarious listen that will make your commute way more enjoyable.

7. ‘The Misfits Podcast’ - Hosted by a group of YouTubers and streamers, this podcast offers a mix of humor, gaming, and storytelling. With their outrageous stories and irreverent humor, the hosts will have you laughing uncontrollably and forgetting about the traffic around you.

8. ‘No Such Thing As A Fish’ - If you love trivia and random facts, this podcast is perfect for you. The hosts, who are researchers for the popular TV show ‘QI,’ share their favorite bizarre and fascinating facts each week. With their witty commentary and infectious enthusiasm, ‘No Such Thing As A Fish’ is a delightful and educational choice for your commute.

These are just a few examples of the many comedy podcasts available. Whether you enjoy stand-up comedy, improvisation, or humorous conversations, there is a podcast out there that will cater to your taste. So, the next time you find yourself dreading your daily commute, plug in your headphones and let the laughter begin. These comedy podcasts will not only make your journey more enjoyable but also leave you in a better mood for the day ahead. Happy listening!
