The Best Music Podcasts to Tune into for Music News and Reviews


In the vast world of music, staying updated with the latest news and reviews can be a daunting task. With so many platforms and sources to choose from, it’s easy to get overwhelmed. That’s where music podcasts come to the rescue. These audio shows provide a convenient and entertaining way to stay connected with the music industry, discover new artists, and get insightful reviews. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best music podcasts that are sure to keep you tuned in and up to date.

1. ‘Song Exploder’ - Hosted by Hrishikesh Hirway, this podcast takes you on a journey through the creative process behind some of the most popular songs. Artists from various genres break down their tracks, revealing the inspiration, production techniques, and hidden meanings. It’s a fascinating look into the minds of musicians and a must-listen for any music enthusiast.

2. ‘Switched on Pop’ - If you’re interested in the intricacies of pop music, this podcast is for you. Hosts Nate Sloan and Charlie Harding dive deep into the world of popular songs, analyzing their composition, lyrics, and cultural impact. With a mix of thoughtful analysis and fun banter, they uncover the artistry behind the biggest hits.

3. ‘Dissect’ - This podcast takes a scholarly approach to music analysis. Each season focuses on dissecting a specific album, exploring its themes, influences, and production techniques. Host Cole Cuchna brings a wealth of knowledge and a passion for storytelling, making each episode a captivating experience.

4. ‘The Trap Set’ - For those who love drumming and rhythm, this podcast is a drumming paradise. Host Joe Wong interviews some of the world’s best drummers, discussing their musical journeys, techniques, and insights. It’s an intimate and inspiring glimpse into the minds of these rhythmic maestros.

5. ‘All Songs Considered’ - Produced by NPR, this long-running podcast covers a wide range of musical genres and styles. Hosts Bob Boilen and Robin Hilton curate a selection of new releases, interviews, and live performances, providing a comprehensive overview of the music landscape. It’s a great way to discover both established and emerging artists.

6. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ - While not solely focused on music, Joe Rogan’s podcast often features in-depth conversations with musicians. From rock legends to hip-hop artists, Joe Rogan’s casual and insightful interviews offer a unique glimpse into the lives and creative processes of these talented individuals.

7. ‘Songland’ - Based on the hit TV show, this podcast delves into the world of songwriting. Hosted by Ben Platt, each episode features discussions with songwriters and producers, exploring the stories behind their creations. It’s an inspiring resource for aspiring songwriters and music enthusiasts alike.

These are just a few examples of the many incredible music podcasts available. Whether you’re looking for music news, reviews, or a deeper understanding of the creative process, these podcasts will keep you entertained and informed. So, grab your headphones, tune in, and let the music podcasts take you on a sonic adventure like no other. Happy listening!

Note: The inclusion of these podcasts does not indicate endorsement or affiliation with the mentioned shows. It’s always a good idea to explore different podcasts and find the ones that resonate with your personal preferences and interests.
