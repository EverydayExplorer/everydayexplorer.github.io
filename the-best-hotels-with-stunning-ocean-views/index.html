The Best Hotels with Stunning Ocean Views


When it comes to planning a vacation, finding the perfect hotel with stunning ocean views can truly elevate your experience. There’s something magical about waking up to the sound of crashing waves and stepping out onto your balcony to witness the vastness of the ocean. If you’re a lover of the sea, then this blog post is tailored just for you. We’ve curated a list of the best hotels around the world that offer breathtaking ocean views, ensuring that your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.

1. Amankila, Bali, Indonesia:
Nestled on a hillside overlooking the Lombok Strait, Amankila offers unparalleled views of the turquoise ocean and the dramatic coastline of East Bali. With its traditional Balinese architecture and luxurious amenities, this resort is a haven for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

2. Jade Mountain, St. Lucia:
Perched on a hillside overlooking the Caribbean Sea, Jade Mountain is a marvel of architectural design. Each of its suites, known as ‘sanctuaries,’ features an open-wall concept, allowing guests to enjoy uninterrupted views of the ocean and the iconic Pitons. This eco-friendly resort offers an unforgettable experience amidst lush tropical surroundings.

3. Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc, French Riviera, France:
Located on the glamorous French Riviera, Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc is a legendary retreat that has attracted celebrities and royalty for over a century. Set amidst manicured gardens overlooking the sparkling Mediterranean Sea, this elegant hotel offers a sense of timeless luxury and unparalleled views that have captivated visitors for generations.

4. The Ritz-Carlton, Laguna Niguel, California, USA:
Perched atop a bluff overlooking the Pacific Ocean, The Ritz-Carlton, Laguna Niguel offers breathtaking coastal views that stretch as far as the eye can see. This luxurious resort combines the natural beauty of the California coastline with impeccable service and world-class amenities, providing an unforgettable stay for its guests.

5. Six Senses Yao Noi, Phang Nga Bay, Thailand:
Situated on a picturesque island in Phang Nga Bay, Six Senses Yao Noi offers stunning panoramic views of the limestone karsts and crystal-clear waters. The resort’s villas are designed to blend seamlessly with the surrounding nature, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the awe-inspiring beauty of their surroundings.

6. Hotel Costa dei Fiori, Sardinia, Italy:
Nestled amidst lush Mediterranean gardens overlooking the sparkling waters of the Tyrrhenian Sea, Hotel Costa dei Fiori is a hidden gem on the island of Sardinia. With its charming architecture, serene atmosphere, and breathtaking coastal views, this boutique hotel offers a truly enchanting experience.

7. InterContinental Bora Bora Resort & Thalasso Spa, French Polynesia:
Set on a pristine lagoon in Bora Bora, this luxurious resort boasts overwater villas with private decks and direct access to the turquoise waters. Wake up to the sight of colorful coral reefs and lush tropical landscapes, and soak in the beauty of the South Pacific from the comfort of your own villa.

These hotels are just a glimpse into the world of luxury accommodations that offer stunning ocean views. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, adventure, or simply a chance to reconnect with nature, these properties will undoubtedly leave you in awe of the majestic beauty of the ocean. So, go ahead and plan your next getaway to one of these extraordinary destinations, and let the ocean be your constant companion throughout your stay.
