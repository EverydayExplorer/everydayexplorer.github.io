The Top 10 Cartoon Shows for Adults


As a writer, I’m always excited to explore new and exciting topics, and today’s subject is no exception. We’re diving into the world of animated television and exploring the top 10 cartoon shows that are specifically tailored for adult audiences. While cartoons have traditionally been associated with children’s entertainment, there’s a whole world of animated shows that cater to grown-up sensibilities. So, if you’re a fan of animation and are looking for some fresh recommendations, buckle up and get ready to explore some of the best cartoon shows for adults!

1. ‘BoJack Horseman’
Kicking off our list is the critically acclaimed ‘BoJack Horseman.’ This Netflix original series follows the life of BoJack, a washed-up 90s sitcom star who happens to be a horse. The show delves into themes of depression, addiction, and the complexities of adult life, all while delivering clever humor and poignant storytelling.

2. ‘Rick and Morty’
‘Rick and Morty’ has taken the adult animation world by storm with its blend of science fiction, dark humor, and existential themes. The show follows the misadventures of an eccentric, alcoholic scientist and his good-hearted but easily influenced grandson, as they navigate through different dimensions and realities.

3. ‘Archer’
For fans of spy spoofs and workplace comedy, ‘Archer’ is a must-watch. This animated series follows the exploits of Sterling Archer, a suave but incredibly dysfunctional secret agent, and his colleagues at the International Secret Intelligence Service. The show’s sharp wit and unique animation style make it a standout in the adult cartoon genre.

4. ‘Big Mouth’
Navigating the awkwardness of puberty is the central theme of ‘Big Mouth,’ a raunchy and irreverent animated comedy that has gained a dedicated fan base. The show fearlessly tackles the trials and tribulations of adolescence, while also providing a healthy dose of humor and heart.

5. ‘Boondocks’
‘Boondocks’ is a satirical take on African American culture and politics, wrapped in a package of sharp humor and social commentary. The show follows the Freeman family as they navigate life in the predominantly white suburb of Woodcrest, often clashing with their eccentric neighbors and confronting issues of race and identity.

6. ‘F is for Family’
Set in the 1970s, ‘F is for Family’ is a darkly comedic exploration of the Murphy family’s struggles with work, love, and raising children. The show’s nostalgic backdrop, combined with its raw and unapologetic humor, makes it a standout in the world of adult animation.

7. ‘South Park’
No list of adult cartoon shows would be complete without mentioning the iconic ‘South Park.’ Known for its fearless satire and no-holds-barred approach to social and political commentary, the show has become a cultural juggernaut since its debut in 1997.

8. ‘Venture Bros’
‘Venture Bros’ is a loving parody of the adventure genre, following the misadventures of the Venture family as they navigate through a world filled with super science, supervillains, and 1960s pop culture references. The show’s blend of action, humor, and intricate storytelling has garnered a dedicated following over the years.

9. ‘The Simpsons’
While ‘The Simpsons’ may have started as a family-friendly show, it has evolved over the years to include more mature themes and humor. The satirical take on American society and pop culture has made it a beloved classic for adult audiences.

10. ‘Metalocalypse’
Rounding out our list is ‘Metalocalypse,’ a darkly comedic portrayal of the fictional death metal band Dethklok. The show’s blend of over-the-top violence, absurd humor, and brutal metal music has earned it a loyal fan base among adult animation enthusiasts.

In conclusion, the world of adult animation is a diverse and exciting landscape, filled with shows that push the boundaries of traditional storytelling and humor. Whether you’re a fan of dark comedy, social satire, or off-the-wall adventures, there’s something for everyone in the realm of cartoon shows for adults. So, grab your popcorn, settle in, and start exploring these animated gems that are sure to entertain and captivate adult audiences.
