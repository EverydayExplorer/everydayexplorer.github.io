How to Style Espadrilles for a Summer Look


Summer is here, and it’s time to update your wardrobe with some stylish and comfortable footwear. If you’re looking for a versatile and chic option, espadrilles are the way to go. These classic shoes have been a summer staple for decades, and with good reason. They’re not only fashionable but also incredibly comfortable. In this blog post, we’ll explore how to style espadrilles to create the perfect summer look that’s both trendy and effortless.

1. Casual Chic:
For a laid-back yet stylish look, pair your espadrilles with a breezy sundress or a flowy maxi skirt. Opt for vibrant colors or floral prints to embrace the summer vibes. Complete the outfit with a floppy hat and a straw tote bag for that perfect touch of bohemian chic. This ensemble is perfect for a day out at the beach or a casual brunch with friends.

2. City Slicker:
Espadrilles aren’t just for the beach; they can also be dressed up for a more polished look. Pair them with high-waisted wide-leg trousers or culottes for a sophisticated city look. Add a fitted blouse or a tailored blazer to elevate the outfit. Don’t forget to accessorize with statement jewelry and a structured handbag. This ensemble is perfect for a lunch meeting or a day of exploring the city.

3. Effortlessly Cool:
For a cool and casual summer outfit, pair your espadrilles with distressed denim shorts and a graphic t-shirt. Layer with a lightweight cardigan or a denim jacket for those cooler summer evenings. Complete the look with a crossbody bag and some oversized sunglasses. This ensemble is perfect for running errands or grabbing coffee with friends.

4. Boho Babe:
If you have a bohemian style, espadrilles are a must-have in your summer wardrobe. Pair them with a flowy boho dress or a peasant top and a maxi skirt. Layer with a fringed kimono or a crochet vest for that ultimate boho look. Accessorize with a floppy hat, some wooden bangles, and a fringe bag. This ensemble is perfect for music festivals or outdoor summer parties.

5. Nautical Vibes:
Nothing screams summer more than nautical-inspired outfits. Pair your espadrilles with high-waisted sailor shorts or a striped midi skirt. Add a crisp white button-down shirt or a striped top for that classic nautical look. Accessorize with a straw hat, a wicker bag, and some anchor-inspired jewelry. This ensemble is perfect for a day out on a yacht or a seaside vacation.

Remember, when styling espadrilles, it’s important to choose the right pair that complements your outfit. Opt for neutral colors like beige, black, or navy for versatility, or go for bold colors or patterns to make a statement. Whether you’re heading to the beach, exploring the city, or attending a summer party, espadrilles are the perfect footwear choice for a stylish and comfortable look.

So, embrace the summer vibes, and let your espadrilles take center stage in your summer wardrobe. With these styling tips, you’ll be rocking the perfect summer look in no time. Get ready to turn heads and make a fashion statement while staying comfortable all day long. Happy styling!
