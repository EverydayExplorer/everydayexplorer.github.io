The Best Craft Beer Bars Around the World


Craft beer has become a global phenomenon, with beer lovers everywhere seeking out unique and flavorful brews. If you’re a beer enthusiast looking to explore the best craft beer bars around the world, you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we’ll take you on a virtual tour of some of the top craft beer bars that are making waves in the industry.

1. The Mikkeller Bar - Copenhagen, Denmark:
Located in the heart of Copenhagen, The Mikkeller Bar is a must-visit for any craft beer lover. With an impressive selection of over 20 rotating taps, this bar offers a wide range of styles and flavors. Whether you’re a fan of hoppy IPAs or rich stouts, you’re sure to find something to satisfy your taste buds here.

2. Brouwerij ‘t IJ - Amsterdam, Netherlands:
Situated next to a picturesque windmill, Brouwerij ’t IJ is a hidden gem in Amsterdam. This small brewery and bar offers a cozy atmosphere and a selection of unique, Belgian-style beers. From fruity saisons to complex tripels, the beers here are brewed with passion and precision.

3. Torst - Brooklyn, New York:
If you find yourself in the hip neighborhood of Brooklyn, make sure to stop by Torst. Known for its minimalist design and expertly curated beer list, this bar has garnered a reputation for showcasing the best of the craft beer scene. The knowledgeable staff will guide you through their extensive menu, helping you discover new and exciting brews.

4. The Porterhouse - Dublin, Ireland:
Dublin is famous for its traditional pubs, but The Porterhouse stands out as a craft beer haven. Located in the heart of the city, this bar offers an impressive selection of their own brews, alongside a range of international craft beers. From their signature Oyster Stout to their award-winning Hophead IPA, there’s something for everyone at The Porterhouse.

5. BierCaB - Barcelona, Spain:
Barcelona may be known for its sangria and cava, but BierCaB is putting the city on the craft beer map. With 30 rotating taps and an extensive bottle list, this bar showcases the best of both local and international craft beers. The cozy atmosphere and knowledgeable staff make it the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a pint.

6. The Local Taphouse - Melbourne, Australia:
Down under, The Local Taphouse is a go-to destination for beer enthusiasts. This bar boasts an impressive lineup of both local and international craft beers, with 20 taps and over 200 bottles to choose from. With regular events and tastings, The Local Taphouse is a hub for the Melbourne craft beer scene.

7. BrewDog - London, United Kingdom:
Known for their boundary-pushing brews, BrewDog has become a global craft beer phenomenon. Their bar in London is a must-visit for any beer lover. With a rotating selection of over 30 taps, including their own innovative creations, BrewDog offers a unique and exciting beer-drinking experience.

These are just a few of the many craft beer bars around the world that are worth exploring. Each one offers its own unique atmosphere and selection of brews, making them a must-visit for any beer enthusiast. So, the next time you find yourself in a new city, be sure to seek out the local craft beer scene and discover your new favorite brew. Cheers!
