Crafting for Outdoor Entertaining: BBQ and Picnic Ideas


Welcome to our blog dedicated to crafting for outdoor entertaining! Today, we are going to dive into the world of BBQ and picnic ideas that will make your outdoor gatherings unforgettable. Whether you’re hosting a backyard BBQ with friends or planning a delightful picnic in the park, we have some creative and fun ideas to help you elevate your outdoor entertaining game.

1. DIY Picnic Blankets:
Let’s start with the basics – a stylish and comfortable picnic blanket. Instead of buying one, why not customize your own? Use fabric markers or fabric paint to create unique designs and patterns on a plain blanket. You can even add some fringe or tassels for an extra touch of charm. Not only will your homemade picnic blanket be practical, but it will also be a conversation starter.

2. Mason Jar Lanterns:
Create a warm and inviting ambiance with homemade mason jar lanterns. Clean out some empty jars and decorate them with colorful tissue paper or paint. Then, place a small tea light candle inside each jar and watch as they illuminate your outdoor space with a soft glow. Hang them from tree branches or place them on tables for a magical atmosphere.

3. Personalized BBQ Aprons:
If you’re the grill master of the group, why not showcase your skills with a personalized BBQ apron? Get crafty with fabric markers or iron-on letters to create a unique design or add a humorous phrase. Not only will it protect your clothing from BBQ sauce splatters, but it will also make you stand out as the host with the most style.

4. Ice Cream Cone Cupcakes:
A BBQ or picnic isn’t complete without a sweet treat. Instead of traditional cupcakes, surprise your guests with ice cream cone cupcakes. Prepare your favorite cupcake batter, pour it into ice cream cones, and bake them. Once they’re cooled, decorate them with frosting and toppings of your choice. These delightful treats will surely impress your guests and add a touch of whimsy to your outdoor entertaining.

5. DIY Garden Games:
Keep your guests entertained with homemade garden games. Create a DIY ring toss game using empty glass bottles and colorful rings. You can also make a giant Jenga set by cutting and sanding wooden blocks. These games will add a fun and competitive element to your outdoor gathering and keep guests of all ages entertained.

6. Fresh Fruit Infused Water:
Stay refreshed and hydrated with fresh fruit-infused water. Fill a large beverage dispenser with cold water and add slices of your favorite fruits, such as lemon, strawberry, or cucumber. Let it sit for a few hours to allow the flavors to infuse. Your guests will appreciate this healthy and flavorful alternative to sugary sodas.

7. DIY Outdoor Centerpieces:
Add a touch of nature to your outdoor tables with DIY centerpieces. Collect small branches, pinecones, and colorful leaves from your surroundings. Arrange them in glass vases or mason jars, and tie a ribbon or twine around them for a rustic look. These simple yet beautiful centerpieces will bring the beauty of the outdoors to your picnic or BBQ setup.

Remember, the key to successful outdoor entertaining is to let your creativity shine. These crafting ideas will not only add a personal touch to your gathering, but they will also create a warm and inviting atmosphere. So, gather your supplies, put on your creative hat, and get ready to host the ultimate BBQ or picnic that your friends and family will rave about for years to come. Happy crafting!
