The Best Trail Running Destinations: Where to Hit the Ground Running


Are you a running enthusiast looking for the perfect trail running destination that offers stunning views, challenging terrains, and a chance to connect with nature? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore some of the best trail running destinations around the world that are sure to get your heart pumping and your feet moving. Whether you’re a seasoned trail runner or just starting out, these destinations have something for everyone.

1. Chamonix, France:
Nestled in the breathtaking French Alps, Chamonix is a haven for trail runners. With over 350 kilometers of trails to explore, this picturesque destination offers a variety of terrains, from gentle slopes to steep climbs. The trails here wind through lush forests, stunning glaciers, and panoramic views of the Mont Blanc massif. Chamonix also hosts the famous Ultra-Trail du Mont-Blanc, attracting trail runners from all over the world.

2. Moab, Utah, USA:
Known for its otherworldly landscapes, Moab offers an unforgettable trail running experience. The diverse terrain, including red sandstone cliffs, slickrock, and canyons, provides a unique challenge for runners of all levels. The iconic trails such as the Slickrock Bike Trail and the famous Arches National Park are a must-visit for any trail running enthusiast.

3. Zermatt, Switzerland:
Nestled at the foot of the iconic Matterhorn, Zermatt is a trail running paradise. The Swiss Alps provide a stunning backdrop as you navigate the well-maintained trails that wind through beautiful alpine meadows and breathtaking mountain vistas. With varying distances and difficulties, there is a trail for every runner to enjoy.

4. Queenstown, New Zealand:
Queenstown, located on the South Island of New Zealand, offers an incredible trail running experience. The region is famous for its stunning mountain ranges, crystal-clear lakes, and lush forests. The trails around Queenstown offer a combination of challenging ascents and descents, along with breathtaking views that will leave you in awe.

5. Cape Town, South Africa:
Cape Town, with its beautiful landscapes and diverse trails, is a trail runner’s dream. The iconic Table Mountain and the surrounding national parks provide a variety of trails with breathtaking views of the city and the Atlantic Ocean. The infamous Table Mountain Challenge attracts elite trail runners from around the world and offers a true test of one’s endurance.

6. Sedona, Arizona, USA:
Sedona, nestled in the heart of Arizona’s red rock country, offers some of the most spectacular trail running routes in the United States. The trails wind through stunning rock formations, towering cliffs, and lush canyons. With mild temperatures during the fall and spring, Sedona is a popular destination for trail runners looking to escape the winter cold.

7. Glen Coe, Scotland:
For those seeking a trail running adventure in a rugged and breathtakingly beautiful setting, Glen Coe in Scotland is the place to be. The trails here take you through the dramatic Scottish Highlands, offering stunning mountain vistas and challenging terrains. With rich wildlife and a sense of remoteness, Glen Coe is a haven for adventure-seeking trail runners.

8. El Chaltén, Argentina:
Surrounded by the towering peaks of the Patagonian Andes, El Chaltén is a hidden gem for trail runners. The trails here lead you through ancient forests, turquoise lakes, and granite spires, providing a truly immersive experience. The Fitz Roy Challenge, a trail running event held in El Chaltén, is one of the most awe-inspiring races in the world.

9. Madeira, Portugal:
Madeira, an archipelago off the coast of Portugal, is a trail running paradise. Known as the ‘Pearl of the Atlantic,’ the islands offer a diverse range of trails that take you through lush green forests, rugged mountains, and breathtaking coastal cliffs. The Madeira Island Ultra-Trail, a challenging and scenic race, attracts trail runners from around the globe.

10. Wanaka, New Zealand:
Located on New Zealand’s South Island, Wanaka is a beautiful destination that boasts incredible trail running routes. With its stunning alpine scenery, crystal-clear lakes, and lush forests, Wanaka offers an unforgettable trail running experience. The Motatapu Icebreaker Off-Road Marathon, held annually in Wanaka, is one of the most iconic trail running races in New Zealand.

In conclusion, these ten trail running destinations around the world offer unparalleled experiences for runners seeking adventure, stunning scenery, and a chance to push their limits. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned trail runner, these destinations will leave you inspired and in awe of the natural beauty that surrounds them. So, lace up your trail running shoes, pack your sense of adventure, and get ready to hit the ground running!
