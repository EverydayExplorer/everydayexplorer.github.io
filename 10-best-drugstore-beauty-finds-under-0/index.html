10 Best Drugstore Beauty Finds Under 0


Looking fabulous doesn’t have to come with a hefty price tag. In fact, some of the best beauty products can be found right at your local drugstore, and they won’t break the bank. Whether you’re on a budget or just love a good bargain, these 10 drugstore beauty finds under $10 will have you looking and feeling your best without emptying your wallet.

  1. Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation ($7.99)
    Achieve a flawless complexion without spending a fortune with Maybelline’s Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation. This lightweight formula provides buildable coverage and helps minimize the appearance of pores for a smooth, matte finish that lasts all day.

  2. E.L.F. Cosmetics Baked Highlighter ($4.00)
    Get your glow on with E.L.F. Cosmetics Baked Highlighter. This silky-smooth powder delivers a luminous finish that enhances your natural radiance. Available in a variety of shades to suit every skin tone, it’s a must-have for any makeup lover.

  3. NYX Professional Makeup Butter Gloss ($5.00)
    Indulge your lips with NYX Professional Makeup Butter Gloss. Infused with shea butter and available in an array of gorgeous shades, this lip gloss delivers sheer to medium coverage with a non-sticky, buttery-smooth feel.

  4. Wet n Wild Color Icon Eyeshadow Palette ($4.99)
    Create endless eye looks with Wet n Wild’s Color Icon Eyeshadow Palette. Featuring a mix of matte and shimmer shades, this highly pigmented palette allows you to unleash your creativity without breaking the bank.

  5. Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser ($9.99)
    Keep your skin clean and refreshed with Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser. This soap-free formula is gentle enough for even the most sensitive skin types and effectively removes dirt, oil, and makeup without stripping the skin’s natural moisture barrier.

  6. Rimmel London Stay Matte Pressed Powder ($4.99)
    Say goodbye to shine with Rimmel London Stay Matte Pressed Powder. This lightweight powder helps control oil and minimize the appearance of pores for a smooth, matte finish that lasts up to 5 hours.

  7. Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel ($9.99)
    Hydrate your skin from within with Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel. Formulated with hyaluronic acid, this oil-free moisturizer quenches thirsty skin and locks in hydration for smooth, supple results.

  8. L’Oreal Paris Voluminous Lash Paradise Mascara ($9.99)
    Achieve voluminous lashes with L’Oreal Paris Voluminous Lash Paradise Mascara. Its soft wavy bristle brush coats each lash for intense volume and dramatic length without clumping or flaking.

  9. Physicians Formula Butter Bronzer ($9.99)
    Get a sun-kissed glow year-round with Physicians Formula Butter Bronzer. Infused with Murumuru Butter, this ultra-creamy bronzer blends seamlessly onto the skin for a natural-looking finish that lasts.

  10. Essence Make Me Brow Eyebrow Gel Mascara ($2.99)
    Define and set your brows with Essence Make Me Brow Eyebrow Gel Mascara. Its tinted gel formula contains tiny fibers that adhere to your brows, filling in sparse areas and adding volume for a fuller-looking arch.

In conclusion, you don’t have to spend a fortune to look and feel beautiful. With these 10 drugstore beauty finds under $10, you can achieve stunning results without breaking the bank. So go ahead, treat yourself to some affordable beauty essentials and unleash your inner glam goddess!
