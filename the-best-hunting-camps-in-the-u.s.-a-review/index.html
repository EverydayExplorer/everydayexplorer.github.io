The Best Hunting Camps in the U.S.: A Review


As the leaves start to change colors and the crisp autumn air sets in, many hunters begin to plan their next adventure. Among the many options available, hunting camps offer a unique and immersive experience that allows you to connect with nature and test your skills. But with so many camps to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is right for you. In this review, we’ll take a closer look at some of the best hunting camps in the U.S., highlighting their unique features, amenities, and hunting opportunities.

1. Wilderness Outfitters - Alaska

Located in the heart of Alaska, Wilderness Outfitters offers a truly remote and rustic hunting experience. With over 30 years of experience, the guides at Wilderness Outfitters know the Alaskan wilderness like the back of their hand, and will take you on a journey to hunt some of the most elusive game in the country. From moose and bear to caribou and sheep, the opportunities are endless. The camp itself is simple but comfortable, with canvas tents and a warm campfire to gather around at night.

2. Montana Hunting Camps - Montana

For those looking for a more traditional hunting experience, Montana Hunting Camps is the perfect choice. Located in the scenic Big Sky State, this camp offers a range of hunts for deer, elk, and antelope, with expert guides to help you track down your prey. The camp itself is cozy and rustic, with comfortable cabins and a warm lodge to relax in after a long day in the field. With over 20 years of experience, the guides at Montana Hunting Camps know the area like the back of their hand, and will work tirelessly to ensure that you have a successful and enjoyable hunt.

3. Tall Timber Hunting Camp - Oregon

For those looking for a more laid-back hunting experience, Tall Timber Hunting Camp in Oregon is the perfect choice. Located in the scenic Willamette National Forest, this camp offers a range of hunts for deer, elk, and turkey, with expert guides to help you track down your prey. The camp itself is simple but comfortable, with cozy cabins and a warm campfire to gather around at night. With over 20 years of experience, the guides at Tall Timber Hunting Camp know the area like the back of their hand, and will work tirelessly to ensure that you have a successful and enjoyable hunt.

4. Rocky Mountain Elk Foundation - Colorado

For those looking for a more challenging and physically demanding hunt, the Rocky Mountain Elk Foundation in Colorado is the perfect choice. Located in the scenic Rocky Mountains, this camp offers a range of hunts for elk, deer, and antelope, with expert guides to help you track down your prey. The camp itself is simple but comfortable, with cozy cabins and a warm lodge to relax in after a long day in the field. With over 20 years of experience, the guides at the Rocky Mountain Elk Foundation know the area like the back of their hand, and will work tirelessly to ensure that you have a successful and enjoyable hunt.

5. Texas Hill Country Hunting Camp - Texas

For those looking for a more exotic hunting experience, the Texas Hill Country Hunting Camp is the perfect choice. Located in the scenic Hill Country of Texas, this camp offers a range of hunts for exotic game such as axis deer, feral hogs, and javelina, with expert guides to help you track down your prey. The camp itself is simple but comfortable, with cozy cabins and a warm campfire to gather around at night. With over 20 years of experience, the guides at the Texas Hill Country Hunting Camp know the area like the back of their hand, and will work tirelessly to ensure that you have a successful and enjoyable hunt.

In conclusion, these five hunting camps offer a range of unique and immersive experiences that are sure to satisfy even the most seasoned hunter. From the remote and rustic wilderness of Alaska to the rolling hills of Texas, each camp offers a unique opportunity to connect with nature and test your skills. Whether you’re looking for a challenging and physically demanding hunt or a more laid-back and relaxed experience, these camps have something for everyone. So why wait? Book your hunt today and experience the thrill of the hunt like never before.
