The Hidden Gems: Underrated Games You Need to Play


In the vast and ever-evolving landscape of video games, countless titles vie for our attention, promising epic adventures, breathtaking graphics, and exhilarating multiplayer experiences. However, amidst the barrage of blockbusters and AAA releases, there lies a treasure trove of hidden gems that deserve equal recognition for their exceptional gameplay, compelling narratives, and innovative designs.

Beneath the Surface: Exploring Underrated Masterpieces

These underappreciated gems often lack the marketing budgets and widespread hype of their more popular counterparts, but they more than compensate with their unique charm, immersive worlds, and captivating experiences. They are the unsung heroes of the gaming industry, waiting to be discovered and cherished by discerning players.

1. The Banner Saga: A Triumph of Tactical Storytelling

This critically acclaimed tactical RPG seamlessly blends stunning hand-drawn visuals with a gripping narrative that unfolds through a series of player-driven choices. The Banner Saga transports you to a war-torn world where every decision carries weight and shapes the fate of your companions and the conflict itself.

2. Inside: A Haunting and Thought-Provoking Puzzle-Platformer

From the creators of Limbo comes Inside, a visually arresting and thought-provoking puzzle-platformer that explores dark and unsettling themes. With its minimalist art style, atmospheric sound design, and enigmatic narrative, Inside will linger in your mind long after you finish playing.

3. Transistor: A Symphony of Action and Storytelling

Supergiant Games, known for their exceptional storytelling, delivers another masterpiece with Transistor. This action RPG features a unique combat system that allows you to record and play back your attacks, creating a symphony of destruction and strategy. Its captivating story and stunning soundtrack will leave an unforgettable mark.

4. Oxenfree: A Supernatural Mystery with a Twist

This atmospheric adventure game combines paranormal investigations with a compelling coming-of-age story. Oxenfree follows a group of teenagers who stumble upon a mysterious island and become entangled in a supernatural mystery that challenges their beliefs and relationships.

5. Celeste: A Triumphant Journey of Self-Discovery

Celeste is a challenging and rewarding platformer that follows the story of Madeline as she climbs Celeste Mountain. Along the way, she confronts her inner demons and discovers the importance of perseverance, self-acceptance, and the power of community.

6. Titanfall 2: A Masterclass in Fluid Movement and Epic Battles

Respawn Entertainment’s Titanfall 2 is a fast-paced first-person shooter that combines exhilarating mech combat with fluid movement and a gripping campaign. Its innovative gameplay mechanics, stunning visuals, and memorable characters make it a must-play for fans of the genre.

7. Ori and the Blind Forest: A Breathtaking Metroidvania

Moon Studios’ Ori and the Blind Forest is a visually stunning Metroidvania that follows the journey of a spirit creature through a decaying forest. With its intricate level design, beautiful art style, and emotional soundtrack, Ori and the Blind Forest is an unforgettable adventure.

8. Undertale: A Subversive and Heartfelt RPG

Undertale is an unconventional RPG that challenges traditional game mechanics and explores the themes of compassion, morality, and the power of choice. With its quirky characters, engaging combat system, and thought-provoking story, Undertale is a unique and unforgettable experience.

9. Shovel Knight: A Nostalgic Throwback with Modern Charm

Yacht Club Games’ Shovel Knight is a retro-inspired platformer that pays homage to classic NES games while introducing its own innovative mechanics and charming characters. With its tight controls, challenging levels, and delightful soundtrack, Shovel Knight is a love letter to the golden age of platforming.

10. Hades: A Roguelike Masterpiece

Supergiant Games once again delivers a masterpiece with Hades, a roguelike dungeon crawler that combines fast-paced action with a compelling story and memorable characters. Its unique gameplay mechanics, stunning visuals, and witty dialogue make Hades an instant classic.

Conclusion: Embracing the Hidden Gems

These hidden gems are not just games; they are works of art that transport us to new worlds, challenge our perceptions, and evoke powerful emotions. They remind us that true gaming excellence can come from unexpected places. By embracing these underrated masterpieces, we not only expand our gaming horizons but also support the passionate developers who create them. So, venture beyond the mainstream and seek out these hidden gems. They will reward you with unforgettable experiences that will stay with you long after the credits roll.
