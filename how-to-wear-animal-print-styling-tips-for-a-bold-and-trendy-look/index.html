How to Wear Animal Print: Styling Tips for a Bold and Trendy Look


Animal prints have stood the test of time and continue to be a popular trend in the fashion world. From leopard and zebra to snake and cheetah, animal prints bring a bold and daring element to any outfit. However, styling animal prints can be intimidating for many fashion enthusiasts. If you’re looking to incorporate animal print into your wardrobe but are unsure how to go about it, fear not! In this blog post, we will provide you with some styling tips to help you confidently rock animal print and create a trendy and eye-catching look.

Start Small with Accessories

If you’re new to animal print or hesitant to fully embrace the trend, a great way to dip your toes into the style is by starting small with accessories. Animal print belts, scarves, handbags, or shoes can instantly elevate a simple outfit and add a touch of sophistication. Pair a leopard print belt with a solid-colored dress or jeans and a blouse for a chic and effortless look. Animal print accessories act as statement pieces and can be versatile additions to your wardrobe.

Mix and Match with Neutrals

One foolproof way to style animal print is by pairing it with neutrals. Neutrals such as black, white, beige, or camel provide a balanced foundation for your bold animal print pieces. For example, a leopard print blouse looks great with black trousers or a camel-colored skirt. Opt for neutral-colored bottoms and let your animal print top take center stage. This combination allows the print to shine without overwhelming the overall outfit.

Go Monochromatic

If you’re feeling extra daring and want to make a strong fashion statement, try wearing animal print head-to-toe. Choose a single animal print and wear it in different pieces, such as a zebra print blouse and matching pants. It might sound bold, but when done right, it can create a sophisticated and trendy look. Remember to keep the accessories and shoes simple to avoid overwhelming the outfit. Going monochromatic with animal print is sure to turn heads and display your fashion-forward sense of style.

Layer with Solid Colors

Another way to wear animal print is by layering it with solid-colored pieces. For example, you can pair a snake print blazer with a white shirt and black pants. The solid colors serve as a backdrop to highlight the animal print and create a visually interesting outfit. Don’t be afraid to play with different textures and fabrics. For a touch of glam, try layering a fur vest over an animal print dress. The combination of textures adds depth to your look and makes it more visually appealing.

Experiment with Colors

Animal print doesn’t always have to be in its traditional colors. Designers have taken creative liberties with prints, offering pieces in unexpected hues. Experiment with colorful animal prints, such as a purple zebra print top or a blue snake print skirt. These unconventional colors can inject a fun and fresh twist to your outfit, making you stand out from the crowd. Just remember to pair them with complementary solid colors that won’t clash with the print.

Combine with Statement Pieces

To add an extra element of style to your animal print outfit, consider incorporating statement pieces. Whether it’s a bold necklace, a chunky bracelet, or a wide-brimmed hat, statement pieces enhance your look and make it more memorable. Opt for accessories in contrasting colors or textures to create a visually interesting ensemble. For instance, pair a leopard print top with a statement red handbag or bold gold earrings. The key is to strike a balance between the animal print and the statement piece, making sure they complement each other rather than compete for attention.

Conclusion

Animal print is a timeless trend that allows you to make a bold fashion statement. By following these styling tips, you can confidently incorporate animal print into your wardrobe and create trendy and eye-catching looks. Whether you start small with accessories or go all-out with monochromatic animal print outfits, the key is to have fun and experiment with different combinations. With the right styling, you’ll undoubtedly turn heads and showcase your fashion-forward sense of style. So unleash your inner fashionista and embrace the wild side with animal print!
