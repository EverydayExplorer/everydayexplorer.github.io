The Best Hunting Apps for Tracking, Mapping, and Weather


In today’s digital age, technology has made its way into every aspect of our lives, and hunting is no exception. Gone are the days of relying solely on instinct and experience. Now, with the help of hunting apps, hunters can enhance their skills, track their progress, and stay updated on weather conditions. In this article, we will explore the best hunting apps available for tracking, mapping, and weather forecasting.

1. HuntStand:
HuntStand is a comprehensive hunting app that offers a range of features for both beginner and experienced hunters. With HuntStand, you can view property boundaries, track your location, and mark waypoints. The app also provides detailed weather forecasts, including temperature, wind speed, and precipitation, allowing you to plan your hunting trips with precision.

2. OnX Hunt:
OnX Hunt is a popular app among hunters, known for its accurate mapping and land ownership data. The app provides detailed topographic maps, property boundaries, and public land information. OnX Hunt also offers offline maps, so you can navigate even in areas with no cell service. Additionally, the app allows you to track your hunting activities, including distance traveled and elevation gain.

3. ScoutLook Weather:
Weather plays a crucial role in hunting success, and ScoutLook Weather is designed to provide hunters with accurate and detailed weather forecasts. The app offers hourly, daily, and 10-day weather forecasts, including temperature, wind direction, and barometric pressure. With ScoutLook Weather, you can plan your hunting trips based on optimal weather conditions, increasing your chances of success.

4. HuntWise:
HuntWise is a versatile hunting app that combines mapping, weather forecasting, and tracking features. The app provides detailed topographic maps, property boundaries, and hunting unit boundaries. HuntWise also offers real-time weather updates, including radar and satellite imagery. Additionally, the app allows you to record your hunting activities, log your sightings, and share your experiences with the hunting community.

5. HuntStand HuntZone:
HuntStand HuntZone is a unique hunting app that uses advanced algorithms to predict animal movement and behavior. The app combines data such as weather conditions, moon phase, and barometric pressure to determine the best hunting times and locations. HuntStand HuntZone also offers mapping and tracking features, allowing you to plan your hunts and monitor your progress.

6. Weather Underground:
Weather Underground is a popular weather app that provides hyperlocal weather forecasts. The app uses data from thousands of personal weather stations to deliver accurate and up-to-date weather information. With Weather Underground, you can track weather conditions in your hunting area, including temperature, wind speed, and precipitation. The app also offers radar and satellite imagery for a comprehensive weather overview.

7. BaseMap:
BaseMap is a mapping app that offers a range of features for hunters. The app provides detailed topographic maps, property boundaries, and hunting unit boundaries. BaseMap also offers offline maps, so you can navigate even in remote areas. Additionally, the app allows you to mark waypoints, track your hunting activities, and share your location with other hunters in real-time.

In conclusion, hunting apps have revolutionized the way hunters track, map, and monitor weather conditions. Whether you are a seasoned hunter or just starting out, these apps can enhance your hunting experience and increase your chances of success. From accurate mapping and property boundaries to detailed weather forecasts, these apps provide all the tools you need for a successful hunting trip. So, download these apps, gear up, and get ready for your best hunting season yet!
