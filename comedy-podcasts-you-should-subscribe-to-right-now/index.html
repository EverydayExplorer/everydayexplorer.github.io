Comedy Podcasts You Should Subscribe to Right Now


Podcasts have become a popular form of entertainment in recent years, and there is no shortage of options to choose from. Whether you’re a fan of true crime, self-help, or politics, there’s a podcast out there for everyone. But if you’re looking to add some laughter to your day, then comedy podcasts are the way to go. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best comedy podcasts that you should subscribe to right now.

1. The Joe Rogan Experience

Known as one of the most popular podcasts in the world, The Joe Rogan Experience features comedian and UFC commentator Joe Rogan engaging in long-form conversations with a variety of guests. From fellow comedians to scientists and celebrities, Joe Rogan covers a wide range of topics in a casual and humorous way.

2. My Dad Wrote A Porno

If you’re in the mood for something light-hearted and hilarious, My Dad Wrote A Porno is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this podcast features Jamie reading chapters from his dad’s erotic novel while James and Alice provide hilarious commentary. It’s a unique and side-splitting concept that will have you laughing out loud.

3. The Dollop

If you’re a history buff with a sense of humor, The Dollop is the podcast for you. Hosted by Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, this show delves into bizarre and lesser-known historical events, with Dave presenting the stories and Gareth reacting to them in real-time. The result is a humorous and educational podcast that will transport you to the weirdest moments in history.

4. How Did This Get Made?

If you’re a fan of bad movies, then How Did This Get Made? is the podcast you need in your life. Hosted by Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, this show tackles some of the most baffling and hilarious films ever made. Each episode features the hosts discussing a particular movie, dissecting its flaws, and providing plenty of laughs along the way.

5. Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend

Late-night talk show host Conan O’Brien takes his wit and humor to the podcasting world with Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend. In this podcast, Conan sits down with celebrities and fellow comedians for candid and hilarious conversations. With his quick wit and hilarious banter, Conan O’Brien creates an entertaining and laughter-filled podcast experience.

6. Comedy Bang! Bang!

Hosted by comedian Scott Aukerman, Comedy Bang! Bang! is a beloved podcast that features interviews with celebrities, improvisational games, and hilarious sketches. With a rotating cast of guest comedians, every episode is filled with laughter-inducing banter and absurd humor.

7. The Adventure Zone

If you’re a fan of tabletop role-playing games and comedy, then The Adventure Zone is the podcast for you. Hosted by the McElroy brothers (Justin, Travis, and Griffin), this show follows their adventures in various role-playing games, with plenty of improvisation, humor, and heartwarming moments along the way. It’s a unique blend of comedy and storytelling that will keep you entertained for hours.

8. No Such Thing As A Fish

No Such Thing As A Fish is a podcast presented by the QI offices, where the researchers share their favorite bizarre facts of the week. With a humorous and lighthearted approach, the hosts take you on a journey through fascinating and often hilarious trivia from a range of topics, making it the perfect podcast for trivia lovers and comedy enthusiasts alike.

9. Call Chelsea Peretti

Comedian Chelsea Peretti takes calls from her listeners and answers their questions in her own unique and comedic style in Call Chelsea Peretti. With her dry wit and hilarious perspective, Chelsea offers entertaining and often absurd advice on topics ranging from relationships to pop culture.

10. The Weekly Planet

For fans of comic books, movies, and pop culture, The Weekly Planet is a must-listen. Hosted by James Clement and Nick Mason, this podcast covers the latest news and trailers, while also providing humorous commentary and discussions. With their comedic chemistry and extensive knowledge, James and Nick make each episode an enjoyable and laughter-filled experience.

In conclusion, comedy podcasts provide a much-needed dose of laughter and entertainment in our busy lives. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up comedy, improv, or storytelling, there is a comedy podcast out there for you. From well-known comedians to hilarious concepts, the podcasts mentioned above offer a wide range of styles and content. So, go ahead and subscribe to these comedy podcasts right now, and get ready to laugh your way through your daily commute or chores. Your funny bone will thank you!
