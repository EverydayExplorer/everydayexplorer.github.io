Mastering the Perfect Winged Eyeliner with Tape


Winged eyeliner has been a beauty staple for decades, adding a touch of glamour and sophistication to any makeup look. Many of us have tried countless techniques and spent hours in front of the mirror just to get those perfect, symmetrical wings. If you’ve ever struggled with creating the perfect winged eyeliner, fear not! We have a game-changing tip for you: tape.

Yes, you read it right. Tape can be the secret weapon in your battle for flawless wings. This simple yet effective trick will transform your eyeliner game and give you the confidence to rock those wings like a pro. Not only is it an easy technique to learn, but it also guarantees sharp, clean lines every single time.

So, how does it work? Let’s dive into the step-by-step process of mastering the perfect winged eyeliner with tape.

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies
To get started, you’ll need a few basic supplies: eyeliner (preferably a liquid or gel formula), a small angled brush, a pair of tweezers, and some adhesive tape. Opt for surgical tape or any tape that is gentle on the skin to avoid any irritation.

Step 2: Prep Your Eyes
Before you apply the tape, make sure your eyelids are clean and dry. Remove any oils or residue from your lashes using a gentle cleanser. This will ensure that your eyeliner adheres smoothly and lasts longer.

Step 3: Measure and Position the Tape
Take the tape and measure the angle and length of your desired wing. Position the tape from the outer corner of your eye upwards towards the end of your brow. The tape will act as your stencil, guiding you to create a sharp and precise wing.

Step 4: Create the Wing
Using the angled brush, dip it into your preferred eyeliner formula. Starting from the inner corner of your eye, trace along your lash line towards the tape. Once you reach the tape, smoothly continue the line, following its angle. This will define the wing shape. For a thinner wing, use a light hand, and for a bolder wing, add more pressure.

Step 5: Fill in the Gap
Once you’ve outlined the wing, you’ll notice a gap between the lash line and the wing shape. This can be easily filled in by connecting the wing to the inner corner of your eye. Use short, precise strokes to gradually fill in the entire line.

Step 6: Remove the Tape
After you’ve completed the eyeliner application, gently remove the tape by pulling it away from your skin at a downward angle. Take your time to ensure you’re not pulling or tugging on delicate eye skin. As the tape comes off, you’ll reveal a perfect, clean winged eyeliner look.

Step 7: Finishing Touches
To complete the look, mascara is a must. Apply a few coats of your favorite mascara to both your upper and lower lashes. This will add volume, length, and intensity, perfectly complementing your winged eyeliner.

Mastering the perfect winged eyeliner with tape might take a few tries to get the hang of it, but practice makes perfect! Experiment with different angles, thicknesses, and eyeliner formulas until you find your preferred style. Remember, everyone’s eye shape is unique, so feel free to adjust the tape placement and wing angle to suit your features.

Implementing this genius technique not only saves time and frustration but also ensures a flawless result every single time. No more wonky wings or uneven eyeliner flicks – just beautifully precise wings that will make heads turn.

In conclusion, whether you’re a makeup beginner or a seasoned pro, mastering the perfect winged eyeliner with tape is a game-changer. It’s a simple technique that guarantees stunning results. So, grab your tape, your eyeliner, and get ready to elevate your eye makeup game to new heights!
