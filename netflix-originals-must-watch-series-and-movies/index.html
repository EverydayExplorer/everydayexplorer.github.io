Netflix Originals: Must-Watch Series and Movies


Netflix has revolutionized the way we consume entertainment. With a plethora of original content, the streaming giant has carved a niche for itself in the industry. One of the factors that sets Netflix apart from its competitors is its focus on producing high-quality series and movies under the label ‘Netflix Originals.’ These Originals have become cult favorites and have attracted a massive fan base. In this blog post, we will dive into some of the must-watch Netflix Originals series and movies that have left a lasting impact on viewers around the world.

Series

Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a supernatural sci-fi series that has taken the world by storm. Set in the 1980s, this show follows a group of friends as they uncover dark secrets in their small town. With its nostalgic references and compelling storyline, Stranger Things has become an instant classic. The show’s ability to combine horror, suspense, and heartfelt moments makes it a must-watch for any television enthusiast.

Money Heist

Money Heist, also known as La Casa de Papel, is a critically acclaimed Spanish series that follows a group of criminals as they plan and execute the biggest heist in history. This show is known for its intricate plot, compelling characters, and unexpected twists. Money Heist has captivated audiences worldwide and has become one of the most-watched non-English language series on Netflix.

The Crown

If you are a fan of historical dramas, then The Crown is the perfect series for you. This show tells the story of the reign of Queen Elizabeth II, starting from her ascension to the throne in 1947. With its stunning cinematography, detailed costumes, and impeccable acting, The Crown has received widespread acclaim. The series takes viewers behind the scenes of the British royal family, exploring the challenges and triumphs that come with being a monarch.

Black Mirror

Black Mirror is a unique anthology series that offers a dark and dystopian take on modern technology and its impact on society. Each episode presents a standalone story, delving into the unintended consequences of technological advancements. From exploring the possibilities of virtual reality to examining the dangers of social media, Black Mirror holds a mirror up to our society and asks us to reflect on the implications of our reliance on technology.

Movies

Roma

Roma, directed by Alfonso Cuarón, is a masterpiece in filmmaking. This black and white drama tells the story of a domestic worker in Mexico City during the 1970s. The film beautifully captures the nuances of everyday life while exploring themes of class, race, and family. Roma received critical acclaim, winning numerous awards, including three Academy Awards. It is a visually stunning and emotionally powerful film that showcases the artistry of cinema.

The Irishman

Directed by Martin Scorsese, The Irishman is a mob drama that reunites legendary actors Robert De Niro, Al Pacino, and Joe Pesci. This epic film spans several decades, following the life of Frank Sheeran, a hitman for the mob. The Irishman is a testament to Scorsese’s storytelling prowess, combining gripping performances with a thought-provoking narrative. The film received widespread acclaim and was nominated for multiple accolades, solidifying its status as a must-watch for any film lover.

Bird Box

Bird Box, starring Sandra Bullock, is a psychological thriller that kept audiences on the edge of their seats. The film is set in a world where supernatural entities cause anyone who sees them to commit suicide. The tension-filled plot follows a mother’s journey to protect her children and find sanctuary in a post-apocalyptic world. Bird Box became a sensation on social media and quickly gained a massive following. Its gripping storyline and excellent performances make it a thrilling watch.

Marriage Story

Marriage Story is a heartfelt drama that explores the complexities of divorce. Directed by Noah Baumbach and starring Adam Driver and Scarlett Johansson, the film delves into the dissolution of a marriage and the emotional toll it takes on both partners. Marriage Story received critical acclaim for its realistic portrayal of relationships and its powerful performances. This thought-provoking film is a must-watch for anyone interested in exploring the intricacies of human connections.

In conclusion, Netflix Originals have become synonymous with quality and innovation. The streaming giant has continuously delivered compelling series and movies that cater to a wide range of tastes. From Stranger Things to Roma and everything in between, there is something for everyone on Netflix. Whether you are a fan of supernatural thrillers, historical dramas, or thought-provoking films, these must-watch Netflix Originals are sure to keep you entertained for hours on end. So grab your popcorn and settle in for a binge-watching session of these captivating shows and movies!
