Finding Strength: 12 Empowering Books for Women


As a woman, life can be incredibly challenging. From societal expectations to personal struggles, women often face unique obstacles that can feel overwhelming to overcome. However, there are countless stories of strong, empowering women who have defied the odds and succeeded against all the odds, and we can learn from their experiences.

If you’re looking for a little inspiration, these 12 empowering books for women are sure to give you the strength you need to tackle whatever comes your way.

1. Big Magic by Elizabeth Gilbert

Elizabeth Gilbert’s Big Magic is a must-read for anyone pursuing their creative passions. In this book, Gilbert explains how creativity can help you overcome fear and self-doubt and gives you tools to help you tap into your own unique brand of magic.

2. Hunger by Roxane Gay

In this powerful memoir, Roxane Gay explores the relationship between her body, her trauma, and her sense of self-worth. It’s a raw, honest look at the ways in which society teaches women to hate their own bodies, and how we can begin to heal from that trauma.

3. The Four Agreements by Don Miguel Ruiz

The Four Agreements is a guide to living a more fulfilled life. In this book, Don Miguel Ruiz outlines four simple principles that can help us let go of the things that are holding us back and find the strength to pursue our dreams.

4. The Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle

The Power of Now is a guide to living in the present moment and letting go of anxiety about the past and future. For women who struggle with stress and anxiety, this book can be a powerful tool to help you find peace and presence in your daily life.

5. The Year of Yes by Shonda Rhimes

In The Year of Yes, Shonda Rhimes, the creator of hit TV shows like Grey’s Anatomy and Scandal, shares how saying “yes” to new experiences helped her overcome her fear and find happiness and success. This book is a testament to the power of stepping out of your comfort zone and embracing new challenges.

6. Untamed by Glennon Doyle

Untamed is a powerful memoir about Glennon Doyle’s journey to finding her own strength and embracing her true self. It’s a raw, unfiltered look at the challenges women face as they navigate societal expectations and find their own sense of purpose.

7. Rising Strong by Brené Brown

In Rising Strong, Brené Brown shares the secrets to overcoming failure and finding the strength to persevere. This book is an invaluable resource for women who are struggling to bounce back from setbacks and move forward with resilience.

8. Daring Greatly by Brené Brown

Daring Greatly is a guide to embracing vulnerability and finding the courage to live authentically. This book is an excellent resource for women who struggle with self-doubt and fear of judgment, helping them learn to let go of shame and embrace their own unique gifts.

9. The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

The Alchemist is a timeless classic that tells the story of a young shepherd boy who embarks on a journey to discover his own destiny. This book is an inspiring reminder that we all have a purpose in life, and that finding the strength to pursue our passions is the key to true fulfillment.

10. Year of Yes: How to Dance It Out, Stand In the Sun and Be Your Own Person by Shonda Rhimes

In Year of Yes, Shonda Rhimes takes us on a journey of personal growth and self-discovery. This book is a powerful reminder of the importance of self-care and taking the time to invest in our own well-being.

11. The Power by Rhonda Byrne

The Power is a book about the power of positive thinking and the law of attraction. This book is an excellent resource for women who struggle with negative self-talk and want to learn how to harness the power of positivity to achieve their goals.

12. Lean In by Sheryl Sandberg

In Lean In, Sheryl Sandberg, the COO of Facebook, offers advice to women who want to succeed in business and leadership. This book is a must-read for any woman who wants to take charge of her career and find the strength to climb the corporate ladder.

In conclusion, these 12 empowering books for women offer invaluable wisdom and inspiration for women on their journey to finding strength. Whether you’re struggling with self-doubt, anxiety, or simply feeling overwhelmed by life’s challenges, these books can help you tap into your own inner power and find the courage to achieve your dreams.
