The Best Hunting Apps for Tracking and Scouting


In the world of hunting, technology has become an invaluable tool for both experienced hunters and beginners alike. With the rise of smartphones, there are now countless hunting apps available to help enhance your hunting experience. These apps can assist with tracking game, scouting new hunting spots, and even provide helpful tips and tricks. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hunting apps that hunters can use to improve their skills and increase their chances of a successful hunt.

1. HuntStand: HuntStand is a comprehensive hunting app that offers a wide range of features to help hunters plan their hunts. With HuntStand, you can easily view property boundaries, track your movements in real-time, and even mark the locations of game sightings. The app also provides detailed weather forecasts, wind direction, and moon phase information, all of which are crucial factors for a successful hunt.

2. OnX Hunt: OnX Hunt is another popular hunting app that offers detailed maps and land ownership information. The app provides users with access to public and private land boundaries, allowing hunters to determine where they can legally hunt. OnX Hunt also offers features such as tracking, waypoint marking, and offline map access, making it a reliable companion for any hunting trip.

3. ScoutLook: ScoutLook is a hunting app that focuses primarily on weather conditions and their impact on hunting. The app provides detailed weather forecasts, including temperature, wind speed, and barometric pressure. It also offers features such as scent cone visualization, which helps hunters determine the direction in which their scent will carry. ScoutLook is a valuable tool for hunters who want to make informed decisions based on weather conditions.

4. HuntWise: HuntWise is an all-in-one hunting app that offers a range of features to help hunters plan their hunts. The app provides detailed weather forecasts, hunting forecasts, and even solunar data, which helps hunters determine the best times to be in the field. HuntWise also offers mapping features, including property boundaries and public land overlays, making it an essential tool for hunters looking for new hunting spots.

5. iHunt Journal: iHunt Journal is a hunting app that focuses on tracking and logging your hunting activities. With iHunt Journal, you can easily record your sightings, shot placement, and other important details about your hunts. The app also provides analytics and trends based on your hunting data, allowing you to track your progress and make improvements over time.

In conclusion, hunting apps have revolutionized the way hunters approach their craft. Whether you’re looking to track game, scout new hunting spots, or simply stay informed about weather conditions, these apps can be a valuable asset in your hunting arsenal. So, why not take advantage of technology and give these hunting apps a try? They might just help you bag that trophy buck or have a more successful hunting season. Happy hunting!
