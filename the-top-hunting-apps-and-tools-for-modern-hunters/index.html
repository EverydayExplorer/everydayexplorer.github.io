The Top Hunting Apps and Tools for Modern Hunters


As modern hunters continue to rely on technology for their hunting expeditions, there are several hunting apps and tools that can make the hunt much easier and more productive. These apps and tools allow hunters to track game, find new hunting spots, collect accurate weather data, and even communicate with other hunters to share tips and information.

Here are the top hunting apps and tools of 2021:

1. HuntWise - This app has a variety of features that truly make it an all-in-one hunting app. With real-time weather conditions, solunar tables, and wind patterns, hunters can stay up-to-date on the optimal hunting times and locations. Additionally, HuntWise also has a mapping function that lets you save and mark your favorite hunting spots, as well as viewing public and private property boundaries.

2. onX Hunt - This GPS hunting app is one of the most popular for serious hunters. Its maps also show ownership boundaries for both private and public areas, so you can avoid trespassing. Its tracking feature allows you to follow your hunting party, so you can avoid getting lost in the woods.

3. iHunt - This app is excellent for new hunters who are still refining their skills. The app includes a database of over 600 animal calls and sounds, which can be played over Bluetooth speakers to attract game. You can also use it to learn more about each animal’s habits and favorite terrain.

4. ScoutLook - This app has a weather forecast feature that provides localized data such as wind speed and barometric pressure. The app can also help detect peak activity times for different game, based on sunrise and sunset, moon phases, and other environmental factors. You can also track your own hunting stats to improve your techniques.

5. Tactacam - Tactacam is a great tool for both hunting and filming. Tactacam’s cameras are simple to use and can capture high-quality footage that can be shared with other hunters. The cameras are built and designed specifically for hunting and the outdoors.

6. HuntStand - HuntStand is a unique mapping app that allows users to pinpoint exactly where game is located on a map. The app also features a variety of useful tools, such as property lines, topographic maps, and weather forecasts. While the app is free to download, there are paid subscription options available that offer additional resources.

There are countless other hunting apps and tools available, such as trail cameras, scent eliminators, and even virtual reality devices designed to improve aim and accuracy. Regardless of your hunting style, there are endless possibilities when it comes to enhancing your hunting experience through technology.

Keep in mind that while these apps and tools can be great, they will never replace the benefits of experience and knowledge. Take time to learn from seasoned hunters, familiarize yourself with your hunting areas, and maintain respect for the game and nature itself.

In conclusion, hunting apps and tools can truly be a game-changer for modern hunters. They offer numerous advantages, such as improved navigation, predicting peak hunting periods, and locating game. Investing in high-quality apps and tools can help you become a more efficient hunter while still respecting the environment.
