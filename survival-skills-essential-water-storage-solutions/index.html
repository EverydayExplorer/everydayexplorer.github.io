Survival Skills: Essential Water Storage Solutions


In today’s uncertain world, having essential survival skills is more important than ever. One crucial skill that often gets overlooked is water storage. In emergency situations, access to clean and safe drinking water can be limited, making it essential to have effective water storage solutions. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most important water storage techniques and solutions that can help you survive in any situation.

1. Rainwater Harvesting:
Rainwater harvesting is a simple yet effective way to store water for future use. By collecting rainwater from rooftops or other surfaces, you can create a sustainable source of water. Install a rain barrel or a larger water storage tank to collect and store rainwater. Make sure to filter and treat the water before consumption.

2. Underground Cisterns:
Underground cisterns are a long-term water storage solution that can provide a significant amount of water. These large tanks are buried underground and can hold thousands of gallons of water. They are an ideal option for those living in areas with limited water resources or facing extended drought conditions.

3. Water Storage Containers:
Investing in high-quality water storage containers is essential for any survivalist. Look for food-grade containers that are designed specifically for water storage. These containers are durable, BPA-free, and can store water for an extended period without contamination. Make sure to rotate and treat the water regularly to ensure its freshness.

4. Water Wells:
If you have access to a well on your property, it can serve as an excellent source of water during emergencies. Ensure the well is properly maintained and regularly tested for water quality. In case of power outages, consider having a manual pump or an alternative power source to access the water.

5. Portable Water Filters:
Having a portable water filter is a wise investment for any survivalist. These compact devices are easy to carry and can turn contaminated water into safe drinking water by removing bacteria, viruses, and other harmful contaminants. Look for filters that meet industry standards and have a high filtration rate.

6. Water Purification Tablets:
Water purification tablets are another lightweight and portable solution for water storage. These tablets are easy to use and can kill most microorganisms present in water. They are an excellent backup option when other water sources are limited or compromised.

7. Solar Still:
In situations where water sources are scarce, a solar still can help you extract water from various sources such as vegetation or even urine. Solar stills use the power of the sun to evaporate water and collect the condensed vapor. While this method may not yield large quantities of water, it can be a lifesaver in desperate situations.

Remember, it’s crucial to regularly inspect and maintain your water storage solutions to ensure their effectiveness. Keep track of expiration dates, rotate stored water, and conduct routine water quality tests.

By implementing these essential water storage solutions, you can be better prepared for emergencies and ensure the availability of clean and safe drinking water for you and your family. Stay prepared, stay hydrated, and stay safe!
