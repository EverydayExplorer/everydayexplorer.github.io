Cooking for Beginners: Simple and Delicious Recipes to Get Started


Are you new to the world of cooking? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we’ll share some simple and delicious recipes that are perfect for beginners. Whether you’re looking to impress your friends or simply want to enjoy a homemade meal, these recipes will help you get started on your culinary journey.

1. Easy Pasta Carbonara
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g bacon, diced
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 large eggs
- 50g grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large pan, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove from the pan and set aside.
3. In the same pan, sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
4. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the garlic and toss to coat.
6. Remove the pan from the heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture. The residual heat will cook the eggs without scrambling them.
7. Add the crispy bacon back to the pan and give everything a good mix.
8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

2. Baked Lemon Herb Chicken
Ingredients:
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 2 lemons, juiced and zested
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish and pour the lemon herb mixture over them, making sure they are evenly coated.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
5. Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.

3. Homemade Margherita Pizza
Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- Fresh basil leaves
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can reach (usually around 500°F or 260°C).
2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
6. Tear the fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, and then slice and serve.
