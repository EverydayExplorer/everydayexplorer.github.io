The Best Adult Animated TV Shows


Welcome to our blog, where we’re about to embark on an animated adventure into the world of adult animated TV shows! From the laugh-out-loud comedies to the gripping dramas, adult animated TV shows have found a special place in our hearts. So, grab some popcorn and get ready to explore the best adult animated TV shows that will keep you entertained for hours on end.

1. “BoJack Horseman”:
Kicking off our list is a show that delves deep into the complexities of life and mental health. “BoJack Horseman” has captivated audiences with its brilliant storytelling, razor-sharp wit, and emotional depth. This animated gem follows the life of a washed-up sitcom star, BoJack Horseman, as he navigates his way through Hollywood’s highs and lows. With its thought-provoking themes and relatable characters, this show has won critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base.

2. “Rick and Morty”:
Prepare yourself for mind-bending adventures across different dimensions with “Rick and Morty.” This sci-fi comedy series is a timeless classic that beautifully blends humor, science fiction, and social commentary. Follow the eccentric genius scientist Rick Sanchez and his timid grandson Morty as they embark on intergalactic escapades that will leave you laughing and pondering the meaning of existence.

3. “Archer”:
If you’re a fan of spy comedies, then “Archer” is the show for you. This animated series introduces us to the suave yet narcissistic secret agent Sterling Archer and his dysfunctional team at the International Secret Intelligence Service (ISIS). With its witty dialogue, pop culture references, and impeccable voice acting, “Archer” offers a unique and entertaining take on the spy genre.

4. “Big Mouth”:
Puberty can be a challenging time, but the creators of “Big Mouth” have found a way to make it hilarious. This coming-of-age comedy series follows a group of middle school friends as they navigate the awkward and often embarrassing moments of adolescence. With its witty writing and relatable characters, “Big Mouth” tackles the uncomfortable topic of puberty with humor and heart.

5. “The Simpsons”:
No list of adult animated TV shows would be complete without mentioning the iconic “The Simpsons.” This legendary show has been entertaining viewers for over three decades, with its clever satire, memorable characters, and timeless humor. Join the Simpson family in the fictional town of Springfield as they take on everyday life, societal issues, and everything in between.

6. “South Park”:
Pushing the boundaries of what’s acceptable, “South Park” has become a cultural phenomenon since its debut in 1997. This irreverent animated series fearlessly tackles controversial topics and social issues with its biting satire and crude humor. Follow the lives of four foul-mouthed boys—Stan, Kyle, Cartman, and Kenny—as they navigate their way through the absurdity of life in the small town of South Park.

7. “Boondocks”:
“The Boondocks” is a thought-provoking and socially conscious animated series that explores racial and cultural issues in America. Based on the comic strip of the same name, the show follows the lives of the Freeman family as they navigate through the predominantly white suburb of Woodcrest. With its sharp writing and powerful social commentary, “Boondocks” challenges the status quo and sparks conversations.

So there you have it, a roundup of some of the best adult animated TV shows that you should definitely add to your watchlist. Whether you’re in the mood for comedy, drama, or social commentary, these shows offer a little something for everyone. Prepare to be entertained, enlightened, and hooked from the very first episode. Happy binge-watching!
