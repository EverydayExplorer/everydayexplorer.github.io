How to Rock Animal Print Without Going Overboard


Animal print has been making a roaring comeback in the fashion world, and it’s easy to see why. From leopard spots to zebra stripes, animal prints add a fierce and bold touch to any outfit. However, there’s a fine line between rocking animal print with confidence and going overboard. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips on how to incorporate animal print into your wardrobe without overwhelming your style.

1. Start with Small Accessories: If you’re new to animal print or unsure about going all out, start with small accessories. A leopard print clutch, a zebra print belt, or even a pair of snake print shoes can instantly elevate your outfit without taking it over the top. These accessories act as statement pieces, adding a touch of wildness without overpowering your entire look.

2. Mix and Match: Animal print doesn’t have to be worn head-to-toe. In fact, mixing different animal prints can create a visually interesting and stylish ensemble. Try pairing a leopard print top with a snake print skirt or a zebra print blouse with cheetah print pants. Just remember to keep the rest of your outfit simple and neutral to let the prints take center stage.

3. Balance with Solids: To avoid going overboard with animal print, balance it out with solid colors. Opt for solid black, white, or earthy tones like beige or olive green to create a harmonious contrast. This allows the animal print to stand out without overwhelming your entire look. A black blazer over a leopard print dress or a white blouse with cheetah print trousers are great examples of achieving this balance.

4. Choose Classic Silhouettes: Animal print can be bold on its own, so it’s best to stick to classic silhouettes when incorporating it into your outfit. A simple sheath dress, a tailored blazer, or a pencil skirt are timeless pieces that provide a sleek and polished look, allowing the animal print to shine without being too overpowering.

5. Opt for One Statement Piece: If you prefer a more subtle approach, choose one statement piece in animal print and keep the rest of your outfit minimal. For example, a leopard print coat paired with a black dress and boots will make a stylish and chic statement without going overboard. Remember, less is more when it comes to animal print.

6. Tone it Down with Neutrals: Animal print can be bold and eye-catching, but if you’re concerned about going overboard, tone it down with neutrals. Incorporate animal print into your outfit by layering it with neutral pieces like a camel coat, a white button-down shirt, or beige pants. This creates a sophisticated and balanced look, ensuring that the animal print doesn’t overpower your entire ensemble.

7. Confidence is Key: Ultimately, the key to rocking animal print without going overboard is confidence. Wear it with pride and embrace the wild side of fashion. Remember, fashion is about expressing your unique personality and style, so own it with confidence.

In conclusion, animal print can be a fun and stylish addition to your wardrobe when worn with care. By starting small, mixing and matching, balancing with solids, choosing classic silhouettes, opting for one statement piece, toning it down with neutrals, and most importantly, wearing it with confidence, you can rock animal print without going overboard. So, unleash your inner fashionista and let your wild side shine!
