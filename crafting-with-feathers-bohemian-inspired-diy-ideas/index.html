Crafting with Feathers: Bohemian-Inspired DIY Ideas


Are you a craft enthusiast looking for some unique and bohemian-inspired DIY ideas? Look no further than feathers! Feathers are a versatile and beautiful crafting material that can add a touch of whimsy and elegance to any project. From dream catchers to jewelry, there are endless possibilities when it comes to crafting with feathers.

In this blog post, we will explore various bohemian-inspired DIY ideas using feathers. Get ready for some creative and inspiring projects that will have you reaching for your feathers and crafting supplies!

1. Feather Dream Catchers:
Dream catchers have long been associated with the bohemian lifestyle and are believed to ward off bad dreams. Add a boho twist to your dream catcher by incorporating feathers. Gather a hoop, some string, beads, and feathers of your choice. Wrap the hoop with the string, leaving a loop at the top for hanging. Attach strings of varying lengths and add feathers and beads to create a beautiful boho dream catcher.

2. Feathered Wall Hangings:
Create a stunning feathered wall hanging using different sizes and colors of feathers. Start by attaching a piece of driftwood or a wooden dowel to a string for hanging. Then, tie individual feathers to the string at varying lengths, creating a cascading effect. You can also add additional boho-inspired elements like beads, seashells, or macrame knots to enhance the overall design.

3. Feather Earrings:
Make a statement with bohemian feather earrings. Start by choosing a pair of plain earring hooks or studs. Then, select feathers of different sizes, colors, and textures. Attach the feathers to the earring hooks using small jump rings or wire. You can also add beads or other embellishments to make your earrings even more unique.

4. Feather Hair Accessories:
Add a touch of bohemian flair to your hairstyle with feather hair accessories. Create a simple yet stylish feather hair clip or headband using an assortment of feathers. Attach the feathers to a clip or headband using hot glue or wire. You can also add ribbons, beads, or other decorative elements to enhance the overall look.

5. Feathered Boho Wreath:
Welcome guests to your home with a feathered boho wreath. Start with a plain wreath ring, either made from natural materials like grapevine or a foam base. Attach feathers of different lengths and colors to the wreath using hot glue or wire. Add other boho-inspired elements like dried flowers, ribbons, or shells for a unique and eye-catching wreath.

6. Feathered Clothing and Accessories:
Take your bohemian style to the next level by incorporating feathers into your clothing and accessories. Add feathers to the hem of a dress or skirt for a whimsical touch. Create a feathered headpiece or collar using feathers of different lengths and colors. You can also embellish bags, shoes, or hats with feathers to add a boho-chic vibe to your overall look.

Remember, when crafting with feathers, always choose ethically sourced feathers to ensure the welfare of birds and wildlife. Feathers can be found in various stores or ordered online from reputable suppliers.

Crafting with feathers allows you to tap into your creativity and embrace the bohemian spirit. These DIY ideas are just the beginning – let your imagination run wild and create unique, bohemian-inspired crafts that reflect your personal style. So gather your feathers, crafting supplies, and get ready to embark on a creative journey filled with whimsy and beauty!
