15 Quick and Easy Meal Prep Recipes for Busy Weekdays


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen every day, trying to whip up nutritious and delicious meals? Are you always on the go and struggling to find time to cook? Fear not! We’ve got you covered with 15 quick and easy meal prep recipes that will save you time and energy during your busy weekdays. These recipes are not only simple to prepare but also packed with flavor and nutrition, ensuring you stay fueled and satisfied throughout the week.

1. Chicken and Quinoa Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients:
- 4 bell peppers, halved and seeds removed
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 cup cooked, shredded chicken
- 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1/2 cup corn kernels
- 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, chicken, black beans, corn, tomatoes, cilantro, cumin, salt, and pepper.
3. Stuff the bell pepper halves with the quinoa mixture and top with cheddar cheese.
4. Place stuffed peppers on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and peppers are tender.

2. Turkey and Avocado Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients:
- 12 large lettuce leaves
- 1 pound ground turkey
- 1/2 cup diced onion
- 1/2 cup diced red bell pepper
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/2 cup salsa

Instructions:
1. In a large skillet, cook ground turkey, onion, bell pepper, and garlic over medium heat until turkey is browned and vegetables are tender.
2. Stir in chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve turkey mixture in lettuce leaves, topped with avocado slices and salsa.

3. Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:
- 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
- 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
- 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, feta cheese, parsley, and mint.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Pour dressing over salad and toss to combine.

4. One-Pan Garlic Herb Chicken and Vegetables

Ingredients:
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 2 cups baby potatoes, halved
- 1 pound asparagus, trimmed
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
- 1 tablespoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. In a large bowl, toss chicken, potatoes, and asparagus with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread the mixture in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
