The Coolest Hotels with Unusual Room Features


When it comes to choosing a hotel, most people consider factors such as location, amenities, and price. But for those seeking a truly unique and unforgettable experience, there are a handful of hotels around the world that offer unusual room features that go above and beyond the ordinary. From underwater suites to treehouse hideaways, these hotels push the boundaries of design and innovation to provide guests with an experience they will never forget. In this article, we will explore some of the coolest hotels with unusual room features that are sure to leave you in awe.

1. The Manta Resort, Pemba Island, Tanzania

Located off the coast of Tanzania, The Manta Resort offers a one-of-a-kind experience with its underwater room. This floating oasis sits in the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean, allowing guests to sleep surrounded by the vibrant marine life that inhabits the area. The room features a glass-walled underwater bedroom, giving you a 360-degree view of the ocean floor. With the soothing sounds of the waves and the enchanting sights of the underwater world, this is a truly mesmerizing experience.

2. V8 Hotel, Stuttgart, Germany

Car enthusiasts will be thrilled to stay at the V8 Hotel in Stuttgart, Germany. This unique hotel pays homage to the automotive industry by offering rooms themed around classic cars. From replicas of famous models to upcycled car parts incorporated into the décor, each room at this hotel is a work of art. Whether you choose the Route 66 room with its vintage charm or the Cadillac room with its sleek design, you are sure to feel like a true car aficionado.

3. Free Spirit Spheres, British Columbia, Canada

Tucked away in the lush forests of British Columbia, the Free Spirit Spheres offer a truly magical experience. These spherical treehouses are suspended from the trees, providing a tranquil and secluded retreat. The rooms are cozy and beautifully designed, offering panoramic views of the surrounding nature. Sleeping in a floating orb amidst the trees is a surreal experience that will make you feel closer to nature than ever before.

4. Icehotel, Jukkasjärvi, Sweden

Located in the small Swedish village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel is a true wonderland. Each winter, artists from around the world come together to create this hotel entirely out of ice and snow. From the magnificent ice sculptures to the frozen beds, every detail is meticulously crafted. Although it might seem chilly, the hotel provides warm sleeping bags and thermal clothing to keep you cozy throughout the night. Staying in an ice room is an unforgettable experience that will transport you to a winter fairy tale.

5. Skylodge Adventure Suites, Sacred Valley, Peru

For the adventure seekers, the Skylodge Adventure Suites in Peru offers a thrilling experience. These transparent capsules are suspended from the side of a cliff, providing panoramic views of the Sacred Valley. To reach your room, you have to climb up a 400-foot via ferrata or hike along a challenging trail. Once inside, you will find a comfortable and well-appointed space, complete with a private bathroom and dining area. Falling asleep under the stars with the Andes Mountains as your backdrop is an experience like no other.

6. Earthship Biotecture, Taos, New Mexico, USA

The Earthship Biotecture in Taos, New Mexico, is more than just a hotel; it is a sustainable living experience. These eco-friendly structures are made from recycled materials, such as tires and bottles, and are completely off-grid. The interior of each Earthship is designed to provide sustainable comfort, with features like solar panels, rainwater collection systems, and natural ventilation. Staying at an Earthship allows you to experience sustainable living firsthand while enjoying the unique and artistic ambiance of these remarkable structures.

These are just a few examples of the incredible hotels around the world that offer unusual room features. Whether you are seeking an underwater adventure, a car-themed escape, or a treetop sanctuary, there is a hotel out there that can fulfill your wildest dreams. These unique accommodations go beyond providing a place to stay; they offer an experience that will leave a lasting impression. So the next time you’re planning a trip, consider adding one of these hotels to your bucket list for a truly unforgettable experience.
