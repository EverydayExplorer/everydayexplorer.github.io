The Best Fishing Tournaments to Join This Year


Are you an avid angler looking for some exciting fishing tournaments to participate in this year? Well, you’re in luck! We’ve compiled a list of the best fishing tournaments happening in various locations around the world. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or just starting out, these tournaments offer a fantastic opportunity to showcase your fishing skills and compete with fellow fishing enthusiasts. So, grab your fishing gear and get ready for some fierce competition!

1. The Bassmaster Classic:
Considered the ‘Super Bowl’ of fishing tournaments, the Bassmaster Classic is the most prestigious event in the bass fishing world. This annual tournament brings together the best anglers from across the globe to compete for a chance to be crowned the champion. With a massive cash prize and a shot at becoming a fishing legend, this tournament is a must-attend for any serious angler.

2. The Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament:
If you’re a fan of big game fishing, then the Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament is perfect for you. Held in North Carolina’s Crystal Coast, this legendary tournament attracts anglers from all over who are eager to hook the biggest blue marlin. With prizes worth millions of dollars, this tournament promises excitement and adrenaline-pumping action.

3. The FLW Tour:
The FLW Tour is a series of fishing tournaments held across the United States. Known for its high competition level, this tour is a great platform for aspiring professional anglers. With multiple events throughout the year, anglers have plenty of opportunities to showcase their skills and earn valuable points towards the prestigious Angler of the Year title.

4. The Tuna Jackpot Tournament:
For those who love saltwater fishing, the Tuna Jackpot Tournament in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, is a dream come true. With its crystal clear waters and abundance of tuna, this tournament attracts anglers from all over the world. The tournament offers various categories, including biggest tuna, heaviest dorado, and most billfish released, ensuring there’s something for every angler.

5. The Bass Pro Shops Big Cat Quest:
If you’re into catfishing, then the Bass Pro Shops Big Cat Quest is the tournament for you. Held in multiple locations across the United States, this tournament brings together catfish anglers of all skill levels. From small local events to larger regional competitions, this series offers a chance to test your catfishing prowess and win some fantastic prizes.

6. The Kingfish Cup Series:
For those who love the thrill of saltwater fishing, the Kingfish Cup Series is a must-join tournament. Held in various coastal locations across the southeastern United States, this series focuses on king mackerel fishing. With its fast-paced action and exhilarating battles, this tournament is a favorite among saltwater enthusiasts.

7. The Sailfish Cup:
If you’re passionate about sailfishing, then the Sailfish Cup in Miami, Florida, is the tournament you’ve been waiting for. This highly competitive event attracts some of the best sailfish anglers from around the world. With its stunning blue waters and impressive sailfish population, Miami provides the perfect backdrop for this thrilling tournament.

So, there you have it - our top picks for the best fishing tournaments to join this year. Whether you’re into bass fishing, big game fishing, or saltwater fishing, these tournaments offer a chance to compete, showcase your skills, and make lifelong memories. Remember to check the tournament rules and regulations, and make sure to pack your fishing gear and a healthy dose of competitive spirit. Good luck and tight lines!
