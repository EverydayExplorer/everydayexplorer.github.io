10 Must-Visit Hotels with Stunning Ocean Views


Are you dreaming of a getaway to paradise? Picture yourself waking up to the gentle sound of waves crashing on the shore, the salty ocean air filling your lungs, and breathtaking views extending as far as the eye can see. If this sounds like the perfect vacation for you, then you’ll want to check out our list of 10 must-visit hotels with stunning ocean views. Each of these incredible properties promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you feeling rejuvenated and inspired. So pack your bags, grab your sunscreen, and get ready to embark on a journey to these coastal havens.

  1. Hotel del Coronado - San Diego, California Located on the picturesque island of Coronado in San Diego, Hotel del Coronado is a true gem. With its legendary Victorian architecture and a prime beachfront location, this historic hotel offers guests unparalleled views of the sparkling Pacific Ocean. Spend your days lounging by the pool, relaxing on the pristine sandy beach, or exploring the nearby vibrant downtown area.

  2. Atlantis The Palm - Dubai, UAE Situated on the stunning Palm Jumeirah Island, Atlantis The Palm is a luxurious resort that will transport you to another world. Boasting extravagant design and world-class amenities, guests can enjoy breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea from their spacious rooms and suites. Take a dip in the famous Aquaventure Waterpark, indulge in fine dining, or unwind in the luxurious spa for an unforgettable experience.

  3. InterContinental Bora Bora Resort & Thalasso Spa - Bora Bora, French Polynesia Escape to paradise at the InterContinental Bora Bora Resort & Thalasso Spa. Located on a private motu (island), this resort offers unparalleled views of the crystal-clear turquoise lagoon and majestic Mount Otemanu. Guests can stay in spacious overwater villas, relax on private decks, and enjoy world-class dining options. Immerse yourself in the beauty of Bora Bora and create memories that will last a lifetime.

  4. Jade Mountain Resort - St. Lucia Nestled amidst the lush greenery of St. Lucia, Jade Mountain Resort is a true architectural marvel. With its open-air design and private infinity pools in each suite, guests can enjoy uninterrupted views of the Caribbean Sea and the iconic Pitons. Indulge in gourmet cuisine, pamper yourself at the spa, or embark on thrilling adventures like snorkeling and hiking.

  5. Mandarin Oriental Tokyo - Tokyo, Japan Perched high above the cityscape, Mandarin Oriental Tokyo is an oasis of serenity in the bustling capital of Japan. With its elegant design and panoramic windows, this hotel offers mesmerizing views of Tokyo Bay and the iconic Rainbow Bridge. Guests can unwind in the luxurious spa, savor culinary delights at various Michelin-starred restaurants, and explore the vibrant neighborhoods surrounding the hotel.

  6. One&Only Ocean Club - Paradise Island, Bahamas As the name suggests, One&Only Ocean Club in the Bahamas is a true paradise for ocean lovers. This exclusive resort boasts pristine white sandy beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters of the Caribbean Sea. Enjoy stunning ocean views from your luxurious suite, tee off at the beautiful golf course, or surrender to the ultimate relaxation at the award-winning spa.

  7. Amankila - Bali, Indonesia Nestled on a hillside overlooking the Lombok Strait, Amankila is a world-class resort that showcases the natural beauty of Bali. With its cascading infinity pools and open-air suites, guests can admire breathtaking views of the turquoise ocean and the surrounding verdant landscapes. Explore local temples, indulge in traditional Balinese spas, and immerse yourself in the rich culture of Bali.

  8. Cape View Clifton - Cape Town, South Africa Located in one of the most beautiful cities in the world, Cape View Clifton offers magnificent vistas of the Atlantic Ocean and the stunning coastline. This boutique hotel provides guests with luxurious accommodations, personalized service, and easy access to Cape Town’s most famous attractions. Embark on a scenic drive along Chapman’s Peak, explore Table Mountain, or simply relax on the pristine beaches nearby.

  9. Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica at Peninsula Papagayo - Guanacaste, Costa Rica Experience the wonders of the Costa Rican jungle while enjoying breathtaking ocean views at the Four Seasons Resort Costa Rica. Nestled on a hillside overlooking the Pacific Ocean, this eco-friendly resort offers guests the perfect blend of luxury and adventure. Discover incredible wildlife, tee off at the championship golf course, or relax in the lavish accommodations surrounded by lush tropical greenery.

  10. Hotel Esencia - Riviera Maya, Mexico Escape to the idyllic shores of the Riviera Maya and immerse yourself in luxury at Hotel Esencia. This exclusive boutique hotel offers breathtaking ocean views from its private beach and stylishly appointed suites. Guests can indulge in farm-to-table dining, rejuvenate at the in-house spa, or unwind by the pool overlooking the turquoise Caribbean Sea.

Whether you’re drawn to the tropical beauty of Bora Bora, the cultural richness of Bali, or the glamour of Dubai, these 10 must-visit hotels with stunning ocean views will undoubtedly fulfill your wanderlust. Treat yourself to an unforgettable experience, embrace the soothing rhythm of the ocean, and make memories that will last a lifetime. Bon voyage!
