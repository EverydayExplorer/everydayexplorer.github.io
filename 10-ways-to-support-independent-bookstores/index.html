10 Ways to Support Independent Bookstores


In the age of online retail giants like Amazon, independent bookstores have faced numerous challenges to stay afloat. These neighborhood gems offer a unique and personal touch to the book-buying experience that cannot be replicated by their online counterparts. But how can we ensure the survival and success of these independent bookstores? In this blog post, we will delve deep into 10 effective ways to support independent bookstores and contribute to the literary community.

  1. Shop Local: The most obvious way to support independent bookstores is by actually purchasing books from them. By diverting our book purchases away from giant online retailers and towards local bookshops, we can help to keep these establishments alive and thriving. So next time you’re looking for a new read, consider visiting your nearest independent bookstore instead of clicking that ‘Add to Cart’ button.

  2. Spread the Word: One of the biggest challenges independent bookstores face is visibility. Help them gain more traction by spreading the word through word-of-mouth recommendations, social media posts, blogs, or even sharing their events and promotions. By amplifying their reach, you can attract more customers and help these bookstores stay on their feet.

  3. Attend Author Events: Independent bookstores often host author readings, book signings, and other literary events. By attending these events, you not only have the opportunity to meet and engage with talented authors, but also show your support for the bookstore. Many authors have gained popularity and recognition through independent bookstores, so attending these events fosters a vibrant literary community.

  4. Join Book Clubs: Book clubs provide an excellent platform for readers to come together to discuss and share their love for books. By joining a local book club that supports independent bookstores, you not only get to enjoy insightful discussions but also contribute to the financial success of the bookstore that hosts the club. It’s a win-win situation for all involved!

  5. Become a Member: Some independent bookstores offer membership programs that provide exclusive perks and benefits to their members. These can include discounts, access to pre-release copies, or invitations to members-only events. By becoming a member, you not only enrich your own reading experience but also contribute directly to the financial stability of the bookstore.

  6. Buy Merchandise: Many independent bookstores offer a range of merchandise beyond books, such as mugs, tote bags, bookmarks, and t-shirts. By purchasing these items, you can show off your love for literature while also financially supporting the bookstore. It’s a great way to showcase your support even when you’re not buying a book.

  7. Gift Certificates: If you’re looking for the perfect present for a book lover in your life, consider purchasing a gift certificate from an independent bookstore. It not only allows the recipient to choose a book of their liking but also introduces them to the wonders of supporting local businesses. It’s a gift that keeps on giving!

  8. Engage with Social Media: Follow your favorite independent bookstores on social media platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. Like and share their posts, leave comments, and engage in conversations. You can help increase their online presence and attract more customers, all with the click of a button.

  9. Donate or Volunteer: Many independent bookstores rely on community support to keep their doors open. Consider making a financial donation or offering your time as a volunteer. Your contribution can go a long way in ensuring the success and sustainability of these beloved literary havens.

  10. Educate Others: Lastly, but certainly not least, spread awareness about the importance of independent bookstores. Educate your friends, family, and colleagues about the unique benefits these bookstores offer and the impact of supporting local businesses. By disseminating this knowledge, you can inspire others to make a conscious effort to choose independent bookstores over online giants.

Supporting independent bookstores is not only about buying books—it’s about nurturing a sense of community and preserving the beauty of the literary world. By employing these ten strategies, we can collectively ensure that independent bookstores continue to flourish, providing us with endless literary treasures for generations to come.
