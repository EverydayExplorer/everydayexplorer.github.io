10 Underrated Music Artists You Should Be Listening To


Music is a universal language that transcends borders, cultures, and generations. While we often find ourselves listening to chart-topping hits and mainstream artists, there’s a world of incredible talent beyond the limelight waiting to be discovered. In this blog, we’ll introduce you to 10 underrated music artists from various genres who deserve your attention. These artists may not be topping the charts, but their creativity and passion for music shine brightly.

1. Tash Sultana**

Tash Sultana is a multi-instrumentalist and singer-songwriter hailing from Australia. Their genre-defying music blends elements of rock, reggae, and electronic, creating a sound that’s both captivating and unique. Tash’s extraordinary talent is showcased through their mastery of instruments like the guitar, bass, and various synths. Tracks like ‘Jungle’ and ‘Notion’ demonstrate their musical prowess and soulful vocals.

2. Rival Sons**

If you’re a fan of classic rock with a modern twist, Rival Sons should be on your radar. This American band draws inspiration from rock legends like Led Zeppelin and The Rolling Stones, crafting a sound that’s both nostalgic and fresh. Songs like ‘Pressure and Time’ and ‘Do Your Worst’ showcase their ability to revitalize the spirit of rock ’n’ roll.

3. Maggie Rogers**

Maggie Rogers gained widespread recognition after her song ‘Alaska’ went viral, but she remains relatively underrated in the grand scheme of things. Her folk-pop style, characterized by heartfelt lyrics and ethereal melodies, has the power to move audiences. Tracks like ‘Light On’ and ‘Fallingwater’ exemplify her talent and emotional depth.

4. Khruangbin**

Khruangbin is a Texan band that fuses elements of psychedelic rock, funk, and world music. Their instrumental compositions are a sonic journey that transcends cultural boundaries. With tracks like ‘Maria También’ and ‘Pelota,’ Khruangbin transports listeners to a realm of musical exploration that’s truly enchanting.

5. Jacob Collier**

Jacob Collier is a British singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist known for his intricate arrangements and extraordinary vocal talent. He has won multiple Grammy awards and continues to push the boundaries of music. His songs, such as ‘In My Room’ and ‘Time Alone With You,’ showcase his virtuosity and innovation in music production.

6. AURORA**

AURORA, a Norwegian singer-songwriter, possesses an otherworldly voice that can send shivers down your spine. Her music combines elements of electro-pop and indie-folk, creating a unique and mesmerizing sound. Songs like ‘Runaway’ and ‘Queendom’ reveal her ability to craft emotionally charged, empowering anthems.

7. Shakey Graves**

Shakey Graves, the stage name of Alejandro Rose-Garcia, is an Americana artist whose music weaves together elements of folk, blues, and rock. His raw, soulful voice and storytelling prowess shine in tracks like ‘Dearly Departed’ and ‘Late July.’ Shakey Graves offers a fresh take on traditional genres, making him a standout artist.

8. Tove Styrke**

Hailing from Sweden, Tove Styrke is an electro-pop artist whose infectious melodies and clever lyricism set her apart. Her songs, such as ‘Say My Name’ and ‘Mistakes,’ are undeniably catchy and showcase her ability to craft pop music with depth and substance.

9. Big Thief**

Big Thief is an indie folk-rock band that delivers emotionally charged performances and introspective songwriting. Led by the hauntingly beautiful vocals of Adrianne Lenker, their music is both intimate and powerful. Tracks like ‘Not’ and ‘Mary’ capture the essence of their emotionally charged sound.

10. Tank and The Bangas**

Tank and The Bangas, a New Orleans-based band, defy categorization with their genre-blending music. They infuse elements of funk, soul, and spoken word into their energetic performances. Songs like ‘Spaceships’ and ‘Quick’ exemplify their ability to get you grooving while delivering poignant messages.

Conclusion

The world of music is a vast and diverse landscape, and these 10 underrated artists are proof that there’s always something new and exciting to discover. Whether you’re into rock, pop, folk, or experimental genres, these musicians offer a fresh perspective and a sonic journey worth embarking on. So, expand your musical horizons and give these artists a listen – you might just find your new favorite song or artist among them.
