10 Thrilling Suspense Novels That Will Keep You Guessing


Are you a fan of mysteries and suspenseful stories that keep you on the edge of your seat? If so, you’re in for a treat! In this blog post, we will explore ten thrilling suspense novels that are guaranteed to keep you guessing until the very end.

1. ‘Gone Girl’ by Gillian Flynn
‘Gone Girl’ is a psychological thriller that tells the story of Nick and Amy Dunne, a married couple whose relationship takes a turn for the worse on their fifth wedding anniversary. As the plot unfolds, readers are left wondering what really happened to Amy and who is responsible for her disappearance.

2. ‘The Girl on the Train’ by Paula Hawkins
‘The Girl on the Train’ follows the life of Rachel Watson, a recently divorced woman who becomes obsessed with a couple she sees every day during her train commute. When the woman from the couple goes missing, Rachel becomes entangled in a web of lies and deceit, desperately trying to uncover the truth.

3. ‘The Da Vinci Code’ by Dan Brown
‘The Da Vinci Code’ is a captivating thriller that combines art, history, and religion. Robert Langdon, a Harvard professor, finds himself on a quest to solve a murder and uncover a secret society’s ancient mystery. This fast-paced novel will keep you guessing at every turn.

4. ‘The Silent Patient’ by Alex Michaelides
‘The Silent Patient’ introduces us to Alicia Berenson, a seemingly perfect artist who is convicted of killing her husband and then never speaks again. Theo Faber, a psychotherapist, becomes obsessed with understanding Alicia’s motive and unraveling the truth behind her silence. Prepare to be shocked when all is revealed!

5. ‘Big Little Lies’ by Liane Moriarty
‘Big Little Lies’ takes readers into the lives of three women whose seemingly perfect lives are disrupted by a murder at the local school trivia night. As the investigation unfolds, dark secrets are revealed, and the reader is left guessing who the killer is until the very end.

6. ‘The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo’ by Stieg Larsson
‘The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo’ focuses on Mikael Blomkvist, a journalist hired to solve the mysterious disappearance of a young woman 40 years ago. He teams up with a young computer hacker, Lisbeth Salander, and together they uncover a dark and twisted family secret. This novel is brimming with suspense and unexpected twists.

7. ‘Sharp Objects’ by Gillian Flynn
In ‘Sharp Objects,’ we follow the story of reporter Camille Preaker, who is sent back to her hometown to cover a series of murders. As Camille dives deeper into the investigation, she unravels her own troubled past and finds herself in grave danger. Gillian Flynn’s writing will keep you guessing until the final page.

8. ‘The Girl Before’ by JP Delaney
‘The Girl Before’ tells the story of Emma and Jane, two women who separately rent the same mysterious house. As they discover the house’s dark secrets, they become entwined in a complex and dangerous web that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

9. ‘Before I Go to Sleep’ by S.J. Watson
‘Before I Go to Sleep’ introduces us to Christine Lucas, a woman who wakes up every morning with no memory of her past. As she tries to piece together her life, she begins to uncover shocking truths that make her question everything she thought she knew. This novel is a gripping psychological thriller that will leave you guessing until the final page.

10. ‘The Silent Wife’ by A.S.A. Harrison
‘The Silent Wife’ explores the deteriorating marriage of Jodi and Todd Gilbert. Told from alternating perspectives, we delve into the intricacies of their relationship and witness a slow-burning suspense that builds to an unexpected climax. This book is a masterclass in psychological tension.

In conclusion, these ten thrilling suspense novels are sure to keep you guessing from start to finish. Whether you’re a fan of psychological thrillers, mysteries, or complex twists, there’s something on this list for everyone. So grab a cup of coffee, find a cozy spot, and prepare to embark on a suspense-filled journey that will leave you breathless.
