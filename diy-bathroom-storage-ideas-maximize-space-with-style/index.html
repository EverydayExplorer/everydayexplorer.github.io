DIY Bathroom Storage Ideas: Maximize Space with Style


When it comes to bathroom storage, not all bathrooms are created equal. Some bathrooms have ample cabinet and shelving space, while others are lacking in storage options. If you find yourself in the latter category, don’t worry - there are plenty of creative DIY bathroom storage ideas that can help you maximize space without sacrificing style.

In this blog post, we will explore various DIY bathroom storage ideas that will not only help you organize your bathroom essentials but also add a touch of style to your space. Get ready to transform your cluttered bathroom into an organized oasis!

1. Floating Shelves

One popular and practical solution for bathroom storage is floating shelves. These shelves are installed directly onto the wall, offering a sleek and minimalist look while maximizing storage potential. Floating shelves can be customized to fit any bathroom size or style, making them a versatile option.

To create your own floating shelves, all you need is a few wooden boards, brackets, screws, and a level. Measure the space on your wall where you want to install the shelves, cut the boards to the desired length, attach the brackets to the wall, and then secure the boards onto the brackets. Voila! You now have stylish and functional storage space for your bathroom essentials.

2. Over-The-Toilet Storage

If you have limited floor space in your bathroom, utilizing the area above your toilet is a great way to add extra storage. You can find a variety of over-the-toilet storage options, such as shelves, cabinets, or ladder-style holders. These storage solutions are not only practical but can also enhance the overall look of your bathroom.

One popular DIY option is to repurpose an old ladder into a stylish over-the-toilet storage unit. Simply sand and paint the ladder to match your bathroom decor, and then place baskets or wooden crates on the rungs to store your toiletries and towels. This unique storage solution adds a rustic and charming touch to any bathroom.

3. Mason Jar Organizers

For those who prefer a more eclectic and DIY look, mason jar organizers are a great option. Mason jars can be repurposed into stylish storage containers that add a touch of charm to any bathroom. Simply attach the lids of the mason jars to a wooden plank or board, and then screw the jars onto the lids. You can paint the lids or add labels to personalize the look.

Mason jar organizers are perfect for storing small bathroom essentials like cotton swabs, cotton balls, or makeup brushes. Not only do they help declutter your bathroom countertops, but they also add a colorful and unique element to your space.

4. Hanging Baskets

If you’re tight on wall space but still need extra storage, hanging baskets are a fantastic solution. You can easily find a variety of decorative hanging baskets in stores, or you can get creative and make your own.

To create your own hanging baskets, all you need is some rope or twine, a wire basket, and your desired items for storing. Simply attach the rope or twine to the sides of the wire basket, tie knots at the top for hanging, and then fill the basket with your bathroom essentials. Hang the basket from a hook or nail, and you’ve got yourself a trendy storage solution that takes up minimal space.

5. Repurposed Furniture

One of the most budget-friendly and original DIY options for bathroom storage is repurposing old furniture. Think outside the box and transform a vintage dresser or a bookshelf into a stunning bathroom storage unit.

For example, you can remove the drawers from an old dresser and use the remaining structure to showcase towels, toiletries, and other bathroom essentials. Alternatively, you can repurpose a bookshelf by adding baskets or fabric bins to store your items. These repurposed furniture pieces not only provide ample storage but also add character and a unique touch to your bathroom decor.

Conclusion

With these DIY bathroom storage ideas, you can transform your bathroom into a functional and stylish space. Whether you opt for floating shelves, over-the-toilet storage, mason jar organizers, hanging baskets, or repurposed furniture, there are endless possibilities to maximize storage while adding a touch of personal style.

Remember, organization is key when it comes to a clutter-free bathroom, so take the time to declutter and sort your items before implementing these storage solutions. Get creative, think outside the box, and have fun designing a bathroom that maximizes space without compromising on style. Your bathroom will thank you!
