The Best Fishing Apps for Tracking Your Catches


When it comes to fishing, technology has made it easier than ever to track your catches and improve your angling skills. Gone are the days of relying solely on memory or pen and paper to keep a record of your prized catches. With the advent of fishing apps, anglers can now conveniently log and track their catches right from their smartphones. In this blog post, we will explore the best fishing apps available that can help you keep a detailed record of your fishing adventures.

1. Fishbrain: Fishbrain is one of the most popular fishing apps out there, and for good reason. It allows you to log your catches, track your fishing spots, and even connect with a community of fellow anglers. With Fishbrain, you can see what fish species are being caught in your area, discover new fishing spots, and get real-time fishing reports. The app also provides valuable information about weather conditions, tide charts, and lunar phases, helping you plan your fishing trips more effectively.

2. Anglr: If you’re a serious angler looking for a comprehensive fishing app, Anglr is the one for you. This app goes beyond just logging catches; it offers advanced features such as GPS tracking, mapping, and even a virtual tackle box. Anglr allows you to record details about each catch, including location, weather conditions, bait used, and even the specific technique employed. The app then analyzes this data and provides insights and trends to help you improve your fishing strategy over time.

3. Pro Angler: Pro Angler is a must-have app for both freshwater and saltwater anglers. It provides access to detailed fishing regulations and allows you to log your catches with ease. One of the standout features of Pro Angler is its fish identification tool, which helps you quickly identify the species you’ve caught. The app also offers fishing forecasts, tide charts, and moon phase information, ensuring that you’re always well-prepared when you hit the water.

4. Fishing Points: If you’re looking for a fishing app that combines functionality with a user-friendly interface, Fishing Points is worth considering. This app allows you to log catches, track your fishing spots, and even create custom fishing maps. Fishing Points also provides detailed weather forecasts, including wind speed and direction, barometric pressure, and air and water temperature. With its intuitive design and comprehensive features, Fishing Points is a great choice for anglers of all levels.

5. iAngler: Developed by the Angler Action Foundation, iAngler is not only a powerful fishing app but also a platform for anglers to contribute to fisheries research and conservation efforts. By logging your catches with iAngler, you not only keep track of your personal fishing records but also contribute to a larger database that helps scientists and fisheries managers better understand fish populations and ecosystems. iAngler also provides real-time fishing reports and allows you to connect with other anglers who share your passion.

In conclusion, fishing apps have revolutionized the way anglers track and document their catches. Whether you’re a casual angler looking to keep a log of your fishing adventures or a dedicated angler seeking to improve your skills, these fishing apps can provide valuable insights and enhance your overall fishing experience. So, download one or more of these apps, grab your fishing gear, and get ready to catch some memories on your next fishing trip!
