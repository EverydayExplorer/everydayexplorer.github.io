The Best Liquor Stores Around the World: Must-Visit Destinations


When it comes to finding the best liquor stores around the world, the options are truly endless. From small, hidden gems to iconic establishments, there are destinations that every liquor connoisseur should visit at least once in their lifetime. Whether you’re a whiskey enthusiast, a wine lover, or a lover of all things spirits, these must-visit destinations will surely impress.

1. La Maison du Whisky - Paris, France:
This legendary liquor store, situated in the heart of Paris, is a true mecca for whiskey lovers. Boasting an impressive collection of over 1,000 different whiskies from all over the world, La Maison du Whisky offers a unique shopping experience. Their knowledgeable staff can guide you through their extensive selection, helping you find the perfect bottle to suit your taste.

2. Binny’s Beverage Depot - Chicago, USA:
Binny’s Beverage Depot is a true paradise for alcohol enthusiasts in the bustling city of Chicago. With several locations across the city, this liquor store offers an extensive range of wines, spirits, and beers. The store’s knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist customers in finding the best bottles for their taste and budget. Binny’s Beverage Depot is a must-visit for anyone looking to explore the world of alcohol in the Windy City.

3. Lavinia - Barcelona, Spain:
Located in the vibrant city of Barcelona, Lavinia is a wine lover’s paradise. This massive store features an extraordinary selection of more than 4,500 wines from all over the world. Lavinia’s knowledgeable team of sommeliers and wine experts can guide visitors through their extensive collection, providing recommendations based on personal preferences and budgets. From rare vintages to up-and-coming producers, Lavinia is a must-visit for any wine enthusiast.

4. Milroys - London, UK:
Nestled in the heart of London’s vibrant Soho district, Milroys is a historic whiskey store that has been serving customers since 1964. Known for its extensive collection of over 1,000 different whiskies, Milroys is a haven for whiskey aficionados. The store also offers regular tasting events and masterclasses, allowing customers to expand their knowledge and appreciation of this fine spirit. A visit to Milroys is a must for anyone visiting the UK’s capital city.

5. Dom Perignon Flagship Store - Épernay, France:
Épernay, located in the Champagne region of France, is home to the prestigious Dom Perignon Flagship Store. This elegant boutique offers a wide variety of Dom Perignon champagnes, including limited editions and special releases. Visitors can immerse themselves in the world of champagne, learn about the production process, and even indulge in a tasting experience. The Dom Perignon Flagship Store is a luxurious destination for champagne lovers.

6. BevMo! - San Francisco, USA:
BevMo! is a household name in California and a go-to destination for liquor enthusiasts. With several locations across the state, this large-scale retailer offers an extensive selection of wines, spirits, and beers from around the world. BevMo! is known for its frequent promotions and discounts, making it a favorite among budget-conscious shoppers. Whether you’re searching for a rare bottle or stocking up on your favorites, BevMo! is a must-visit in San Francisco.

7. La Grande Epicerie de Paris - Paris, France:
Situated in the heart of Paris, La Grande Epicerie de Paris is not only a food lover’s paradise but also a haven for spirits enthusiasts. This upscale grocery store boasts an impressive selection of wines, spirits, and liqueurs from around the world. Visitors can browse the shelves and discover rare spirits, unique liqueurs, and even select their own bottle of exclusive, private-label products. La Grande Epicerie de Paris offers a truly exquisite shopping experience.

8. Park Avenue Liquor Shop - New York City, USA:
Located in the prestigious Upper East Side of Manhattan, Park Avenue Liquor Shop is a hidden gem for those seeking a curated collection of fine spirits. This boutique liquor store offers an extensive selection of rare and hard-to-find spirits, including whiskey, rum, gin, and tequila. With knowledgeable staff and regular tastings, Park Avenue Liquor Shop is a must-visit destination for spirits enthusiasts visiting the Big Apple.

9. The Green Welly Shop - Scotland, UK:
For those exploring the world of Scotch whisky, The Green Welly Shop in Scotland is a must-visit destination. Situated in the heart of the Scottish Highlands, this iconic shop offers an extensive selection of whiskies, including rare and limited editions. Visitors can explore the shelves, discover new distilleries, and even sample different expressions in the on-site tasting room. The Green Welly Shop is a whisky lover’s dream come true.

10. The Old Liquor Store - Singapore:
The Old Liquor Store in Singapore is a destination that stands out from the rest. This unique store specializes in vintage and rare spirits, offering an exceptional selection of aged whiskey, rum, cognac, and more. Each bottle tells a story, and visitors can immerse themselves in the history and craftsmanship behind these aged treasures. The Old Liquor Store is a must-visit for those seeking an extraordinary and rare drinking experience.

No matter where your travels take you, exploring the best liquor stores around the world can be a fascinating adventure. From high-end boutiques to hidden gems, these destinations offer something for every alcohol enthusiast. So whether you’re a whiskey lover, a wine enthusiast, or a lover of all things spirits, make sure to add these must-visit destinations to your travel itinerary. Cheers to discovering new flavors and experiencing the world of liquor in a unique and exciting way!
