10 Easy Crafts for Kids That Will Keep Them Entertained for Hours


Are you looking for fun and creative ways to keep your kids entertained for hours? Look no further! In this blog post, we will share with you 10 easy crafts for kids that are not only enjoyable but will also stimulate their imagination and encourage their creativity. So, roll up your sleeves and get ready for some crafting fun!

Crafting with kids is a wonderful way to spend quality time together, while also providing them with a hands-on learning experience. It allows children to explore their artistic side and express themselves in a unique and personal way. So, let’s dive into our list of crafts that will keep your kids entertained for hours.

1. Paper Plate Animals
Paper plates are a versatile craft material that can be transformed into various animals. Grab some paints, markers, and construction paper, and let your kids create their own zoo! They can make a lion, a giraffe, or even a butterfly. The possibilities are endless and the best part is that they can also use their creations for imaginative play later on.

2. Pipe Cleaner Puppets
Pipe cleaners are another great craft material that can be used to make adorable puppets. Your kids can let their imagination run wild and create different characters. With some googly eyes, fabric scraps, and glue, they can bring their puppets to life and put on their own puppet show.

3. Nature Collage
Take your kids on a nature walk and collect various items such as leaves, flowers, and twigs. Then, provide them with a large piece of paper and glue, and let them create a beautiful nature collage. This activity not only allows children to be creative but also helps them connect with the natural world around them.

4. DIY Paper Airplanes
Every child loves making paper airplanes! Show your kids different folding techniques to create various types of airplanes. Let them experiment with different paper sizes and weights to see how it affects the flight. They can even have a contest to see whose airplane flies the farthest!

5. Tissue Paper Suncatchers
Brighten up your windows with colorful tissue paper suncatchers! Cut out various shapes from different colored tissue paper and have your kids glue them onto contact paper or a clear plastic folder. Hang them up and watch as the sunlight filters through, creating a beautiful display of colors.

6. DIY Yarn Pom Poms
Yarn pom poms are a simple but fun craft that kids will love. All you need is some yarn and scissors. Show your kids how to wrap the yarn around their fingers or a piece of cardboard and then carefully cut the loops. They can make pom poms of different sizes and colors and use them for various crafts or even as decorations around the house.

7. DIY Story Stones
Story stones are a fantastic way to encourage storytelling and imaginative play. Collect smooth stones from your backyard or a local beach and paint different pictures on them. Each stone can represent a character, an object, or a scene. Then, use the stones as prompts for your kids to create their own unique stories.

8. Salt Dough Creations
Salt dough is a simple and affordable craft material that you can make at home. Mix equal parts of flour, salt, and water to create a soft dough. Your kids can then use cookie cutters or their own hands to shape the dough into various objects. Once they are done, let the creations air dry or bake them in the oven for a more durable finish.

9. Cardboard Box City
Don’t throw away those cardboard boxes just yet! They can be transformed into a mini city for your kids to play with. Cut out windows and doors, and let your kids decorate the buildings with markers or paint. They can even create small cars and people out of paper or cardboard to populate their city.

10. DIY Friendship Bracelets
Teach your kids the art of making friendship bracelets. With some colorful embroidery floss and basic braiding techniques, they can create beautiful bracelets for themselves and their friends. Not only is this activity fun, but it also helps promote fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination.

There you have it - 10 easy crafts for kids that will keep them entertained for hours. These crafts are not only enjoyable, but they also provide numerous opportunities for learning and creativity. So, gather your craft supplies and get ready to embark on a fun-filled crafting adventure with your children. Happy crafting!
