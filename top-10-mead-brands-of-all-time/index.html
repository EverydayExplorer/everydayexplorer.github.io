Top 10 Mead Brands of All Time


Welcome to the world of mead, the timeless elixir that has captivated mankind for centuries! In this blog post, we will embark on a journey to explore the top 10 mead brands of all time. From ancient recipes passed down through generations to innovative modern flavors, these meaderies have truly mastered the art of crafting this delightful beverage. So sit back, relax, and let’s dive into the world of mead!

1. **Honeywood Winery**: Kicking off our list is the renowned Honeywood Winery, located in the heart of Willamette Valley, Oregon. With a history spanning over 80 years, Honeywood Winery is celebrated for its exquisite meads, showcasing flavors like raspberry, marionberry, and huckleberry. Their commitment to using local ingredients shines through in every sip.

2. **Redstone Meadery**: Nestled in the picturesque state of Colorado, Redstone Meadery takes mead-making to new heights. Known for their award-winning Nectar of the Hops, a unique blend of honey and hops, Redstone Meadery has gained a loyal following among craft beer enthusiasts seeking a different kind of brew.

3. **Chaucer’s Mead**: Transporting us to medieval times, Chaucer’s Mead has been producing exceptional meads since 1964. Located in California, this meadery specializes in traditional honey wines, crafted with pure, natural honey. Chaucer’s Mead offers a delightful taste of history with each bottle.

4. **Moonlight Meadery**: Prepare to be enchanted by Moonlight Meadery’s offerings. Situated in New Hampshire, their meads boast imaginative names like “Desire,” “Fling,” and “Sensuous.” Moonlight Meadery pushes the boundaries of traditional mead-making, infusing flavors like apple, cranberry, and chocolate to create a truly memorable experience.

5. **Bunratty Mead**: Traveling across the Atlantic to Ireland, we find Bunratty Mead, a true gem in the world of mead. Produced at the historic Bunratty Castle, this mead captures the essence of Irish folklore and tradition. Made from pure honey, Bunratty Mead offers a taste of old-world charm that cannot be replicated.

6. **Schramm’s Mead**: Michigan is home to Schramm’s Mead, a meadery that has gained international recognition for its exceptional craftsmanship. With an emphasis on using premium ingredients and a meticulous approach to fermentation, Schramm’s Mead creates meads that are complex, balanced, and utterly delicious.

7. **Mystic Mead**: Journeying to the land down under, we discover Mystic Mead in Australia. This family-owned meadery showcases the unique flora and fauna of Australia in its meads, incorporating indigenous ingredients like eucalyptus and lemon myrtle. Mystic Mead offers a taste of Australian terroir, bringing a touch of the exotic to the world of mead.

8. **B. Nektar Meadery**: Hailing from Ferndale, Michigan, B. Nektar Meadery is a true innovator in the field. Combining traditional mead-making techniques with a modern twist, B. Nektar Meadery produces meads infused with unexpected flavors like coffee, cherry, and even jalapeno. Their bold and adventurous approach has earned them a dedicated following.

9. **Superstition Meadery**: Nestled in the scenic town of Prescott, Arizona, Superstition Meadery offers a tantalizing array of meads that are sure to impress even the most discerning palates. From their flagship meads like “Blueberry Spaceship Box” to their limited-edition releases, Superstition Meadery is a haven for mead enthusiasts seeking complexity and quality.

10. **Hidden Legend Winery**: Rounding out our list is Hidden Legend Winery, situated in Montana. Known for their dedication to the craft and commitment to authenticity, Hidden Legend Winery produces meads made from Montana wildflower honey. With a focus on sustainability and traditional techniques, their meads embody the spirit of the untouched wilderness.

In conclusion, the world of mead is a rich tapestry of flavors and traditions, and these top 10 mead brands have elevated the craft to new heights. Whether you’re a seasoned mead connoisseur or just starting your journey, these meaderies offer a tantalizing range of options that are sure to delight your taste buds. So go ahead, raise a glass, and savor the centuries-old magic of mead!
