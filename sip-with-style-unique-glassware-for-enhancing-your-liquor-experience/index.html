**The Science of Sipping:**

It’s not just about looks—science also plays a role in how glassware can enhance your liquor experience. Different types of spirits have unique characteristics that can be accentuated or muted based on the shape of the glass. For example, a tulip-shaped glass can concentrate the aroma of a fine whisky, allowing you to truly appreciate its complex bouquet. On the other hand, a wide-mouthed tumbler might be the perfect vessel for your favorite cocktail, letting you relish the mingling of flavors with each sip.

‘Sip with Style’ understands the importance of this science and offers glassware designed with these principles in mind. Each glass is carefully chosen to enhance the specific qualities of the liquor it’s intended for, ensuring that you get the most out of every sip.
