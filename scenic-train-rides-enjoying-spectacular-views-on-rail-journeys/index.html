Scenic Train Rides: Enjoying Spectacular Views on Rail Journeys


There’s something magical about embarking on a train journey. The rhythmic chugging of the locomotive, the gentle swaying of the carriages, and the anticipation of the unknown ahead - all contribute to the charm and allure of train travel. But what truly sets train journeys apart from other modes of transportation is the opportunity to witness breathtaking scenery unfold right before your eyes. In this blog post, we’ll take you on a virtual tour of some of the world’s most scenic train rides, where you can immerse yourself in the beauty of nature and marvel at spectacular vistas along the way.

1. The Glacier Express, Switzerland
Known as the ‘slowest express train in the world,’ the Glacier Express winds its way through the heart of the Swiss Alps, connecting the mountain resorts of Zermatt and St. Moritz. As you glide through the snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and charming villages, you’ll be treated to panoramic views that seem straight out of a postcard. Make sure to keep your camera ready to capture the awe-inspiring Matterhorn, the iconic symbol of Switzerland.

2. The Rocky Mountaineer, Canada
Prepare to be captivated by the untamed beauty of the Canadian Rockies as you embark on a journey with the Rocky Mountaineer. This luxurious train takes you through breathtaking landscapes, from towering mountain ranges to deep canyons and crystal-clear lakes. As you savor gourmet meals and relax in plush seats, keep an eye out for wildlife sightings, including bears, elk, and eagles. This is a train ride that truly embodies the spirit of adventure.

3. The Flam Railway, Norway
Norway is renowned for its fjords, and the Flam Railway offers a front-row seat to some of the country’s most spectacular natural wonders. Starting in the picturesque village of Flam, this train ride takes you on a thrilling ascent through steep mountainsides, cascading waterfalls, and narrow valleys. The highlight of the journey is reaching the stunning Kjosfossen waterfall, where the train makes a special stop, allowing you to soak in the awe-inspiring beauty of nature.

4. The Ghan, Australia
For a truly unique train experience, hop on board The Ghan in Australia. This legendary train journey spans the continent from Darwin to Adelaide, traversing the vast and diverse landscapes of the Outback. From the rugged red deserts to ancient rock formations and lush green oases, The Ghan showcases the rich tapestry of Australia’s natural wonders. As you indulge in gourmet cuisine and unwind in luxurious cabins, you’ll witness the ever-changing scenery that makes this journey unforgettable.

5. The Blue Train, South Africa
Step back in time and relish the elegance and luxury of The Blue Train in South Africa. This iconic train ride takes you on a journey through the country’s diverse landscapes, from the bustling city of Pretoria to the breathtaking beauty of Cape Town. Marvel at the expansive savannahs, vineyards, and mountains as you enjoy impeccable service, gourmet dining, and opulent accommodations. This is a train ride that combines old-world charm with the splendor of Africa.

Whether you’re an avid train enthusiast or simply seeking a unique way to explore the world, scenic train rides offer an unparalleled opportunity to immerse yourself in nature’s wonders. From the majestic Swiss Alps to the rugged Australian Outback, these journeys will leave you in awe of the world’s natural beauty. So, sit back, relax, and let the train carry you to destinations where breathtaking views await around every bend.
