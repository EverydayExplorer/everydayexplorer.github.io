The Influence of Planetary Retrogrades


Astrology has fascinated humanity for centuries. The alignment of celestial bodies, their positions in the night sky, and their movements have long been believed to have a profound influence on our lives. Among the many astrological phenomena that capture our attention, planetary retrogrades stand out as particularly intriguing and impactful. In this blog, we’ll dive into the mystical world of planetary retrogrades, exploring what they are, how they affect us, and how you can navigate their influence in your life.

What Is Planetary Retrograde?

To understand planetary retrogrades, we first need to grasp the basics of planetary motion. In our solar system, planets revolve around the sun in elliptical orbits. However, from our perspective here on Earth, it often appears as though these planets occasionally reverse their direction in the sky, moving backward instead of forward. This apparent backward motion is what we refer to as planetary retrograde.

Planetary retrogrades are not exclusive to any one planet but can occur with all of them. However, the most famous retrograde is undoubtedly the Mercury retrograde. It’s worth noting that the term ‘retrograde’ comes from the Latin word ‘retrogradus,’ which means ‘backward step.’
