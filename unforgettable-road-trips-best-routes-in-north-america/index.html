Unforgettable Road Trips: Best Routes in North America


Are you itching to hit the open road and embark on an unforgettable adventure? Look no further than North America, a treasure trove of scenic drives that will leave you breathless and yearning for more. From rugged coastlines to majestic mountains, this fascinating continent offers a multitude of road trip options that cater to all preferences and interests. Whether you’re a nature lover, history buff, or simply seeking a thrilling escape, these best routes in North America have got you covered.

1. Pacific Coast Highway, California

One of the most iconic road trips in North America is along the Pacific Coast Highway in California. Starting in the charming city of San Francisco and stretching all the way down to San Diego, this breathtaking drive showcases the state’s diverse landscapes and stunning coastal vistas. Traveling along Highway 1, you’ll pass by picturesque seaside towns, dramatic cliffs, and golden sandy beaches. Make sure to stop by Big Sur, a rugged stretch of coastline renowned for its dramatic vistas and pristine natural beauty.

2. Icefields Parkway, Alberta and British Columbia

For those seeking a truly awe-inspiring road trip, Canada’s Icefields Parkway is an absolute must. Connecting Jasper National Park and Banff National Park in the Canadian Rockies, this scenic drive is often hailed as one of the most beautiful in the world. As you navigate through this 144-mile stretch of highway, you’ll witness towering snow-capped mountains, glistening glaciers, and turquoise-blue lakes. Keep an eye out for wildlife such as elk, bighorn sheep, and black bears that call this area home.

3. Blue Ridge Parkway, Virginia and North Carolina

Immerse yourself in the charm of the American South with a drive along the Blue Ridge Parkway. Winding its way through the Appalachian Mountains, this 469-mile route offers breathtaking views of rolling hills, dense forests, and cascading waterfalls. Take your time exploring charming small towns like Asheville, North Carolina, and enjoy the region’s rich music, arts, and culinary scene. The Blue Ridge Parkway is especially enchanting during the autumn months when the foliage turns into a vibrant display of colors.

4. Going-to-the-Sun Road, Montana

Located in Glacier National Park, Going-to-the-Sun Road is a masterpiece of engineering that takes you through the heart of this pristine wilderness. Spanning just over 50 miles, this epic road trip showcases the park’s stunning alpine scenery, with towering mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and abundant wildlife. Be prepared for hairpin bends and exhilarating heights as you climb to Logan Pass, the highest point on the road. Make sure to stop at the various overlooks and hike the picturesque trails for a truly immersive experience.

5. Route 66, Chicago to Santa Monica

No list of unforgettable road trips in North America would be complete without a mention of the legendary Route 66. Dubbed the ‘Main Street of America,’ this historic highway stretches over 2,400 miles, connecting the heartland of America from Chicago, Illinois, to Santa Monica, California. Journeying along Route 66 is like stepping back in time, with iconic roadside attractions, retro diners, and quirky motels. Embrace the nostalgia and explore small towns along the way, soaking up the rich history and vibrant culture.

6. Cabot Trail, Nova Scotia

Escape to the picturesque landscapes of Atlantic Canada with a drive along the Cabot Trail in Nova Scotia. This 185-mile route winds its way around the stunning Cape Breton Island, offering breathtaking views of rugged coastline, charming fishing villages, and the striking Cape Breton Highlands. Immerse yourself in Acadian and Celtic culture, sample fresh seafood, and hike the scenic trails that lead to hidden gem beaches and waterfalls. Don’t forget your camera – you’re sure to capture countless Instagram-worthy shots along the way.

7. Monument Valley Scenic Drive, Arizona and Utah

Journey through the heart of the American Southwest with a drive through Monument Valley. This iconic landscape, known for its towering sandstone buttes and dramatic rock formations, has been featured in countless movies and Westerns. The 17-mile scenic drive takes you through the heart of the valley, offering breathtaking views at every turn. Witness the changing colors of the desert as the sun sets, casting a warm glow over the surreal landscape. For an even more immersive experience, consider taking a guided tour with a Navajo guide to learn about the area’s rich cultural heritage.

Now that you have a glimpse of some of the best road trips in North America, it’s time to start planning your next adventure. Whether you prefer coastal drives, mountainous landscapes, or iconic historic routes, these unforgettable road trips are sure to leave a lasting impression. Hit the open road, embrace the freedom and embark on a journey that will create memories to last a lifetime.
