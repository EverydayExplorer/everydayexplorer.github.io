The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Choker Necklaces


Choker necklaces have made a major comeback in the fashion world, and it’s easy to see why. They add a touch of edginess and sophistication to any outfit, but there are definitely some dos and don’ts to keep in mind when rocking this trendy accessory. Whether you’re a choker necklace aficionado or a newbie looking to spice up your jewelry game, here are some essential tips to help you slay the choker necklace look with confidence.

Do: Consider Your Neckline
When choosing a choker necklace, take into account the neckline of the outfit you’ll be wearing. Chokers pair beautifully with off-the-shoulder, V-neck, and scoop neck tops and dresses. They draw attention to the neckline and collarbone, creating a stunning, elongated look. However, be cautious when wearing chokers with high-neck or turtleneck tops, as they can create a visually cluttered effect.

Don’t: Overdo It
While layering necklaces can be a fun way to express your style, be mindful not to overdo it with chokers. Wearing multiple chokers at once can look cluttered and detract from the statement piece you’re trying to make. Keep it simple and let your chosen choker shine on its own.

Do: Match Your Style
Choker necklaces come in a variety of styles, from delicate chains to bold statement pieces. When selecting a choker, consider your personal style and the occasion. A dainty choker can add a touch of elegance to a casual daytime look, while a chunky, embellished choker can elevate a nighttime ensemble.

Don’t: Forget Comfort
It’s essential to prioritize comfort when wearing choker necklaces. Ensure that the choker isn’t too tight or too loose around your neck, as this can cause discomfort and detract from your overall look. Opt for adjustable chokers or ones with a bit of stretch for a comfortable fit.

Do: Experiment with Materials
Chokers come in a wide range of materials, including velvet, leather, metal, and beading. Embrace the opportunity to experiment with different textures and materials to add depth and interest to your outfit. Consider the overall aesthetic you want to achieve and select a choker that complements it.

Don’t: Clash with Hairstyles
When styling choker necklaces, consider how they interact with your hairstyle. Avoid wearing chokers that will clash with your hair, especially if you have long locks or a bold hairstyle. Opt for chokers that will complement your hair and frame your face in a flattering way.

Do: Pay Attention to Proportions
The length and width of a choker can significantly impact how it complements your neckline and overall look. Pay attention to the proportions of the choker in relation to your neck and outfit. A choker that sits too high or too low can disrupt the visual balance, so take the time to find the perfect fit.

Don’t: Neglect the Rest of Your Look
While choker necklaces can undoubtedly steal the show, don’t forget to pay attention to the rest of your outfit and accessories. Coordinate your choker with complementary earrings, bracelets, or rings to create a cohesive and polished look.

By keeping these dos and don’ts in mind, you’ll be well-equipped to rock choker necklaces with confidence and style. Embrace the versatility of this trendy accessory and let your personality shine through in your choker necklace choices. With a little experimentation and attention to detail, you’ll be a choker connoisseur in no time.
