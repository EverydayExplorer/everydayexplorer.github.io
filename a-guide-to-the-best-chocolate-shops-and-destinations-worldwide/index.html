A Guide to the Best Chocolate Shops and Destinations Worldwide


There’s no denying the fact that chocolate is loved around the world. No matter where you go, you’ll find different variations and recipes of chocolate. Many cities have their own famous chocolate shops and destinations that chocolate lovers should explore. In this guide, we’ll take a look at some of the best chocolate shops and destinations worldwide that every chocolate lover should visit at least once.

1. Belgium: Brussels

It goes without saying that Belgium is the birthplace of some of the best chocolate in the world. In the capital city of Brussels, you’ll find a plethora of chocolate shops and chocolatiers, all with their unique styles and recipes. The most famous Belgian chocolate is the praline, which originated in Brussels. Patrick Roger, Neuhaus, and Mary Chocolatier are some of the most famous chocolatiers in Brussels.

2. Switzerland: Zurich

Swiss chocolate is famous all around the world, and Zurich is one of the best places to sample it. The city boasts of some of the best chocolate shops and destinations that you can explore. Chocolat Frey is a popular chocolate shop in Zurich, and they are the largest Swiss exporter of chocolate. Sprüngli is also another famous chocolatier in Zurich and is famous for their pralines and chocolate truffles.

3. France: Paris

Paris is the city of love and romance, and it’s no surprise that it’s also a haven for chocolate lovers. The city is home to numerous chocolate shops that offer an array of chocolate delicacies. Ladurée, Angelina, and La Maison du Chocolat are some of the most famous chocolate shops in Paris that you cannot miss. These shops offer everything from chocolate truffles to chocolate bars and exquisite macarons.

4. Italy: Turin

Turin is a city famous for its chocolate and is known as the chocolate capital of Italy. The city is home to the famous chocolate brand, Ferrero Rocher, the makers of Nutella and Kinder. You can visit the Ferrero Rocher factory and enjoy a chocolate tour. Venchi, Guido Gobino, and Peyrano are some of the other famous chocolate shops in the city that you should explore.

5. USA: New York City

New York City is famous for its food scene, and chocolate is no different. The city boasts of some of the best chocolate shops and destinations that you can explore. Jacques Torres, Max Brenner, and Mast Brothers are some of the most famous chocolatiers in the city. They offer everything from chocolate bars to truffles and even hot chocolate.

6. Canada: Montreal

Montreal is a haven for chocolate lovers, and the city boasts of numerous chocolate shops and destinations. Some of the famous chocolatiers in Montreal are Juliette et Chocolat, Chocolats Geneviève Grandbois, and Suite 88. These shops offer everything from chocolate truffles to chocolate-covered nuts and exquisite pastries.

7. Spain: Barcelona

Barcelona is famous for its chocolate, and the city boasts of some of the best chocolate shops and destinations. Chök, Xocoa, and Cacao Sampaka are some of the famous chocolate shops in the city that you should explore. These shops offer everything from chocolate bars to chocolate truffles and even hot chocolate.

Conclusion

Chocolate is loved around the world, and for good reason. It’s a delicious and versatile ingredient that can be used in various recipes and dishes. The above-listed chocolate shops and destinations are just a few of the best places worldwide that every chocolate lover should visit at least once. Explore these destinations, sample the local chocolate recipes, and indulge in the sweetness of chocolate.
