The Secrets to a Voluminous Hairstyle


When it comes to hair, we all want that voluminous, full-bodied look that turns heads and leaves a lasting impression. But achieving that salon-worthy hairstyle can sometimes feel like an elusive secret. Well, fret no more! In this blog post, we’re going to spill the beans on the secrets to a voluminous hairstyle that will have you feeling like a superstar. So grab your hair tools and let’s dive in!

1. Choose the right haircut: One of the first steps to achieving volume is selecting the right haircut. Layers are your best friend when it comes to adding dimension and movement to your hair. Opt for long layers or a shaggy bob to create the illusion of fullness. Remember, the right cut can make all the difference!

2. Blow-dry like a pro: The key to voluminous hair starts with your blow-drying technique. Begin by applying a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair. Then, flip your head upside down and rough-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help lift the roots and create instant volume. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip it back up and use a round brush to smooth and add more volume as you finish drying.

3. Tease it up: Teasing, also known as backcombing, is an age-old trick for creating volume. Take small sections of hair near the crown area and gently backcomb them using a fine-toothed comb. Be careful not to go too rough, as this can damage your hair. Once teased, lightly brush over the top layer to smooth it out, leaving you with a voluminous base.

4. Use the right products: Investing in the right hair products can make a world of difference when it comes to achieving volume. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that add body without weighing your hair down. Additionally, spritzing a texturizing spray or dry shampoo on your roots can give your hair an extra lift and grit for that perfect voluminous look.

5. Embrace the power of hot tools: Hot tools like curling irons and wands are not just for creating curls. They can also be used to add volume to your hair. Wrap small sections of hair around a curling wand, starting from the root and leaving the ends out. Hold for a few seconds, then release. This will create loose waves and add instant volume. Don’t forget to finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

6. Consider extensions: If you’re looking for an instant boost of volume, hair extensions are a game-changer. Clip-in extensions can be easily added to your hair, giving you the appearance of fuller, thicker locks. Remember to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a seamless blend.

7. Sleep on it: Yes, you read that right! Sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid can create natural, voluminous waves. Simply twist your hair into a loose bun or braid before bed, secure it with a hair tie, and let it loose in the morning. The result? Effortless, voluminous locks without any heat styling.

Now that you’re armed with these secrets to a voluminous hairstyle, it’s time to put them into action. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type. Remember, confidence is key, so rock that voluminous hairstyle and let your hair do the talking!
