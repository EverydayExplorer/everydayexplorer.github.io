10 Must-Try Instant Pot Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Life can get hectic, and finding time to cook healthy and delicious meals during the week can be a challenge. Enter the Instant Pot – a game-changer for busy weeknights! This versatile kitchen appliance allows you to whip up a variety of meals in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods. If you’re looking to streamline your weeknight dinner routine, here are 10 must-try Instant Pot recipes that are quick, easy, and sure to please the whole family.

  1. Instant Pot Chicken Burrito Bowls: This one-pot meal is a crowd-pleaser! Simply add chicken, rice, beans, corn, salsa, and spices to your Instant Pot, and let it cook to perfection. Serve with your favorite toppings like avocado, cheese, and sour cream for a delicious and satisfying meal.

  2. Instant Pot Spaghetti: Say goodbye to standing over a pot of boiling water! With the Instant Pot, you can cook spaghetti noodles and sauce together for a flavorful and fuss-free dinner. Just add spaghetti noodles, marinara sauce, water, and your choice of meat or veggies to the pot, and let it cook under pressure for a quick and easy meal.

  3. Instant Pot Beef Stew: Craving comfort food? Look no further than this hearty beef stew. With tender beef, potatoes, carrots, and peas cooked in a savory broth, this Instant Pot recipe is sure to warm you up on a chilly evening.

  4. Instant Pot Chili: Perfect for game day or a cozy night in, Instant Pot chili is quick, easy, and packed with flavor. Simply brown some ground beef, add beans, tomatoes, spices, and broth to the pot, and let it simmer under pressure for a delicious and satisfying meal.

  5. Instant Pot Macaroni and Cheese: Forget about the boxed stuff – homemade macaroni and cheese is easy to make in the Instant Pot! Just add pasta, water, butter, and cheese to the pot, and let it cook under pressure for a creamy and decadent dish that the whole family will love.

  6. Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala: Take your taste buds on a trip to India with this flavorful chicken tikka masala. Tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato sauce with aromatic spices – this Instant Pot recipe is sure to become a family favorite.

  7. Instant Pot BBQ Pulled Pork: Craving barbecue but short on time? Instant Pot BBQ pulled pork to the rescue! Simply season a pork shoulder with your favorite BBQ rub, add some BBQ sauce and broth to the pot, and let it cook under pressure for tender and flavorful pulled pork.

  8. Instant Pot Risotto: Yes, you read that right – you can make risotto in the Instant Pot! Skip the constant stirring and let the Instant Pot do the work for you. With just a few simple ingredients like arborio rice, broth, butter, and Parmesan cheese, you can have creamy and delicious risotto in no time.

  9. Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken: Light, fresh, and bursting with flavor, this Instant Pot lemon garlic chicken is perfect for a quick and healthy weeknight meal. Just marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of lemon juice, garlic, and herbs, then cook in the Instant Pot for a tender and flavorful dish.

  10. Instant Pot Vegetable Soup: Looking for a healthy and satisfying meal? Instant Pot vegetable soup is the answer! Packed with veggies, beans, and hearty broth, this soup is not only nutritious but also quick and easy to make in your Instant Pot.

With these 10 must-try Instant Pot recipes, you’ll have plenty of delicious and hassle-free options for busy weeknights. Whether you’re craving comfort food, international flavors, or simple and nutritious meals, the Instant Pot has you covered. So dust off your Instant Pot, stock up on ingredients, and get cooking – your taste buds will thank you!
