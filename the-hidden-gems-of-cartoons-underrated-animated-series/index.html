The Hidden Gems of Cartoons: Underrated Animated Series


In the vast landscape of animated entertainment, certain gems often go unnoticed amidst the clamor for mainstream hits. While popular series like ‘The Simpsons,’ ‘SpongeBob SquarePants,’ and ‘Adventure Time’ rightly earn their acclaim, there exists a trove of lesser-known animated shows waiting to be discovered. These are the hidden gems of cartoons—underrated treasures that deserve recognition for their creativity, storytelling, and cultural impact.

One such hidden gem is ‘Gravity Falls,’ a supernatural mystery-comedy created by Alex Hirsch. Set in the fictional town of Gravity Falls, Oregon, the series follows twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend the summer with their great-uncle Stan, who runs a tourist trap called the Mystery Shack. What sets ‘Gravity Falls’ apart is its intricate plot, filled with cryptic codes and hidden messages that invite viewers to unravel the mysteries alongside the characters. With its blend of humor, adventure, and heartfelt moments, ‘Gravity Falls’ has garnered a dedicated fanbase and critical acclaim, yet it remains underrated compared to other animated series.

Another hidden gem worth exploring is ‘Over the Garden Wall,’ a mini-series created by Patrick McHale. This enchanting tale follows two half-brothers, Wirt and Greg, as they journey through a mysterious forest known as the Unknown. With its hauntingly beautiful animation, rich folklore-inspired storytelling, and memorable characters, ‘Over the Garden Wall’ offers a surreal yet poignant exploration of themes such as loss, identity, and the passage of time. Despite winning critical praise and an Emmy Award, ‘Over the Garden Wall’ remains relatively unknown to mainstream audiences.

For fans of offbeat humor and irreverent storytelling, ‘The Oblongs’ is a hidden gem worth seeking out. Created by Angus Oblong, the series takes place in the fictional town of Hill Valley, where the affluent residents live atop a polluted hill, while the deformed and eccentric Oblong family resides in the valley below. With its dark humor, satirical take on societal norms, and distinctive visual style, ‘The Oblongs’ offers a refreshingly unconventional viewing experience. Though it developed a cult following during its brief run, the series never achieved widespread recognition.

In addition to these examples, there are countless other underrated animated series waiting to be discovered, each offering its own unique blend of creativity and entertainment. From the surreal adventures of ‘The Mighty Boosh’ to the quirky charm of ‘Harvey Beaks,’ these hidden gems enrich the world of animation with their originality and imagination.

In conclusion, while mainstream animated series rightfully receive acclaim for their popularity and impact, it’s important not to overlook the hidden gems that exist beneath the surface. Whether it’s unraveling mysteries in ‘Gravity Falls,’ embarking on a surreal journey in ‘Over the Garden Wall,’ or indulging in dark humor with ‘The Oblongs,’ these underrated animated series offer a wealth of entertainment waiting to be explored. So, the next time you’re in search of something new to watch, consider delving into the world of hidden gems and discovering the magic that awaits.
