The Best Hunting GPS Devices: Navigation Tools for the Wilderness


When it comes to hunting in the wilderness, having a reliable navigation tool is crucial. The right GPS device can not only help you navigate through unfamiliar terrain but also keep you safe and on track during your hunting adventures. In this blog post, we will be exploring the best hunting GPS devices that are available on the market today. These devices offer advanced features, durability, and accuracy, making them essential tools for any serious hunter.

1. Garmin GPSMAP 64st:
The Garmin GPSMAP 64st is a top-of-the-line hunting GPS device that is known for its accuracy and reliability. It comes with a high-sensitivity GPS and GLONASS receiver, which ensures precise positioning even in challenging environments. The device also features a 2.6-inch color display and a rugged design that can withstand harsh conditions. With a preloaded worldwide basemap and a one-year BirdsEye Satellite Imagery subscription, the Garmin GPSMAP 64st provides detailed maps and imagery for your hunting expeditions.

2. Magellan eXplorist 610:
The Magellan eXplorist 610 is another excellent hunting GPS device that offers a range of features to enhance your hunting experience. It comes with a 3-inch color touchscreen display and a 3.2-megapixel camera, allowing you to capture photos and record important waypoints along your journey. The device also includes a barometric altimeter and a three-axis electronic compass, providing accurate elevation and directional information. With its rugged construction and waterproof design, the Magellan eXplorist 610 is built to withstand the toughest hunting conditions.

3. Bushnell BackTrack D-Tour:
If you’re looking for a compact and easy-to-use hunting GPS device, the Bushnell BackTrack D-Tour is a great choice. This pocket-sized device allows you to mark and store up to five locations, making it ideal for marking hunting spots or tracking your way back to camp. It also features a digital compass and a self-calibrating accelerometer, ensuring accurate navigation wherever you go. With its simple interface and long battery life, the Bushnell BackTrack D-Tour is a reliable companion for any hunting adventure.

4. Garmin Oregon 750t:
The Garmin Oregon 750t is a high-end hunting GPS device that offers a wide range of advanced features. It comes with a 3-inch sunlight-readable touchscreen display and a built-in 8-megapixel camera, allowing you to capture high-quality photos and geotag them with your location. The device also includes a three-axis compass, an accelerometer, and a barometric altimeter, providing precise navigation and environmental data. With its preloaded topographic maps and BirdsEye Satellite Imagery subscription, the Garmin Oregon 750t offers detailed and up-to-date mapping information for your hunting expeditions.

In conclusion, having a reliable hunting GPS device is essential for any serious hunter. The devices mentioned in this blog post, including the Garmin GPSMAP 64st, Magellan eXplorist 610, Bushnell BackTrack D-Tour, and Garmin Oregon 750t, offer advanced features, durability, and accuracy to ensure a successful and safe hunting experience. So, whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, consider investing in one of these top-notch hunting GPS devices to enhance your navigation skills and make the most out of your wilderness adventures.
