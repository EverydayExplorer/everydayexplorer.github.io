Top 10 Fitness Apps to Enhance Your Outdoor Workouts


The world of technology has seamlessly integrated into our lives, even into our fitness routines. Fitness apps have become popular companions for those looking to enhance their outdoor workouts. With a wide range of options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right app. To make your decision easier, we have compiled a list of the top 10 fitness apps that are guaranteed to take your outdoor workouts to the next level.

1. Nike Training Club: This app is perfect for outdoor workouts, offering a wide variety of exercises and training plans designed by world-class trainers. With customizable workouts, detailed instructions, and progress tracking, Nike Training Club caters to all fitness levels.

2. Strava: If you’re a runner, cyclist, or simply enjoy tracking your outdoor activities, Strava is a must-have app. It uses GPS to record your workouts, analyze performance metrics, and connect with other athletes for healthy competition.

3. MyFitnessPal: Outdoor workouts go hand in hand with proper nutrition. MyFitnessPal offers an extensive food database, allowing you to track your calorie intake and nutrient consumption. It also syncs with other fitness apps, creating a holistic approach to your health and fitness goals.

4. MapMyRun: Similar to Strava, MapMyRun tracks your outdoor runs and provides detailed statistics. What sets it apart is its vast library of pre-designed routes, allowing you to explore new places while keeping track of your progress.

5. Runtastic: This user-friendly app is packed with features to help you optimize your outdoor workouts. Whether you’re running, cycling, or hiking, Runtastic tracks your routes, distance, pace, and calories burned—all while providing real-time audio coaching.

6. Zombies, Run!: For those seeking an adventurous twist to their outdoor workouts, this app turns exercise into a thrilling game. Zombies, Run! is an immersive app that combines audio storytelling with interval training, transporting you into a post-apocalyptic world where you must outrun zombies to survive.

7. JEFIT: Strength training enthusiasts will appreciate JEFIT’s comprehensive workout tracking and customization capabilities. With a wide range of exercises and training plans, this app ensures you get the most out of your outdoor strength workouts.

8. Seven: Short on time? Seven is the perfect app for anyone looking to squeeze in a quick outdoor workout. Offering 7-minute HIIT (high-intensity interval training) sessions, this app provides effective and efficient workouts that target multiple muscle groups.

9. YogaGlo: Outdoor workouts don’t always have to be high intensity. YogaGlo offers a vast library of yoga classes that can be done anywhere, providing a great way to improve flexibility, strength, and mental well-being amidst nature.

10. Fitbod: Designed for gym-goers who want to take their workouts outdoors, Fitbod creates personalized strength training plans suited to your fitness level, available equipment, and preferred outdoor locations. It adjusts workouts based on how you’re progressing, making it an ideal app for those dedicated to strength and muscle gain.

In conclusion, these top 10 fitness apps offer a wide range of features, ensuring everyone can find the perfect companion for their outdoor workouts. So, whether you’re a runner, cyclist, hiker, yogi, or strength training enthusiast, there’s an app out there waiting to elevate your fitness journey. Embrace the power of technology and let these fitness apps guide you towards a fitter, healthier, and more enjoyable outdoor workout experience.
