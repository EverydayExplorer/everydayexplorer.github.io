DIY Advent Calendars: Crafts for Counting Down to the Holidays


December is here, and the holiday season is in full swing. It’s a time of joy, celebration, and most importantly, anticipation. If you’re like me, you love counting down to the big day with an advent calendar. But why settle for store-bought when you can create your own DIY advent calendar? Not only will it add a personal touch to your holiday decor, but it’s also a fun and creative activity that the whole family can enjoy. In this blog post, we’ll explore some DIY advent calendar crafts that will help you count down to the holidays in style.

1. Paper Chain Advent Calendar:
One of the simplest and most classic DIY advent calendars is the paper chain. All you need is some colorful paper, scissors, and glue. Cut the paper into strips and create loops by gluing the ends together. Connect the loops to form a chain, with each loop representing a day leading up to Christmas. On each loop, write a special activity or treat to enjoy that day. As you remove a loop each day, you’ll be one step closer to Christmas!

2. Clothespin Advent Calendar:
For a more rustic and versatile option, try a clothespin advent calendar. Gather 24 wooden clothespins, paint them in festive colors, and attach them to a string or ribbon. On each clothespin, write a number representing the days leading up to Christmas. You can then attach small gifts, notes, or treats to each clothespin. Hang it on a wall or mantel, and each day, remove a clothespin to reveal the surprise waiting for you.

3. Mini Stocking Advent Calendar:
If you have basic sewing skills, a mini stocking advent calendar might be the perfect DIY project for you. Cut out 24 mini stocking shapes from colorful felt or fabric. Sew them together, leaving the top open. Decorate each stocking with numbers or other festive embellishments. Fill each stocking with a small treat or note, and hang them on a string or clothesline. Every day, open a new stocking to discover what’s inside.

4. Matchbox Advent Calendar:
For a unique and compact DIY advent calendar, repurpose matchboxes. Gather 24 empty matchboxes and decorate them with wrapping paper, paint, or stickers. Number each box and fill it with a surprise. Arrange the matchboxes in a grid or pyramid shape to create a visually appealing display. Each day, open a matchbox to unveil the surprise hidden inside.

5. Book Advent Calendar:
If you’re a book lover, why not combine your love for reading with the advent calendar tradition? Collect 24 Christmas-themed books or wrap up books you already own. Number each book and stack them in a festive display. Each day, unwrap and read a new book together as a family, immersing yourselves in the magic of the holiday season.

These are just a few of the countless DIY advent calendar ideas out there. Get creative and personalize your advent calendar to suit your style and preferences. Whether you choose to make a paper chain, clothespin, mini stocking, matchbox, or book advent calendar, the joy of counting down to Christmas will be even more special with a handmade touch. So gather your crafting supplies, unleash your creativity, and start crafting your own DIY advent calendar today. Happy holidays and happy crafting!
