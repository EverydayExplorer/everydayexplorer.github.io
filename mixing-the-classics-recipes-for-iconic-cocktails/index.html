Mixing the Classics: Recipes for Iconic Cocktails


Cocktails have been a beloved part of our social culture for centuries. They’re more than just drinks; they’re art forms, crafted with precision, creativity, and a dash of flair. While the world of mixology has seen a surge in innovative concoctions and new ingredients, there’s something timeless and comforting about the classics. These iconic cocktails have stood the test of time, and for good reason. In this blog post, we’ll delve into the world of classic cocktails and provide you with some recipes to recreate these timeless libations at home.

  1. Martini: The Epitome of Elegance Ingredients:
  2. 2 1/2 oz Gin or Vodka
  3. 1/2 oz Dry Vermouth
  4. Lemon twist or Olives for garnish

Instructions: 1. Fill a mixing glass with ice cubes. 2. Pour in the gin or vodka and dry vermouth. 3. Stir well until chilled, then strain into a chilled martini glass. 4. Garnish with a lemon twist or olives.

The martini is the quintessential classic cocktail. Its simplicity and elegance have made it a staple at bars and parties worldwide.
