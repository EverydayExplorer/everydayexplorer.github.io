Stay Fit and Active: Hotels with Outdoor Sports Facilities


When it comes to maintaining an active lifestyle, finding the right accommodations can make all the difference. Whether you’re a fitness enthusiast or simply looking to stay fit while on vacation, hotels with outdoor sports facilities are the perfect choice. These establishments go above and beyond to ensure that their guests have access to a wide range of activities that promote health and wellness. In this blog post, we will explore some of the top hotels around the world that offer exceptional outdoor sports facilities, allowing you to stay fit and active during your stay.

1. The Resort at Clearwater Beach, Florida, USA
Situated along the stunning coastline of Clearwater Beach, this resort boasts an array of outdoor sports facilities that cater to all fitness levels. From beach volleyball courts to tennis courts, guests can enjoy friendly matches while soaking up the sun and breathing in the fresh ocean air. For those who prefer water sports, the resort offers a variety of activities such as paddleboarding, kayaking, and even surfing lessons. With an emphasis on outdoor fitness, The Resort at Clearwater Beach is a haven for active travelers.

2. The Chedi Andermatt, Andermatt, Switzerland
Nestled in the heart of the Swiss Alps, The Chedi Andermatt offers a unique combination of luxury and outdoor sports facilities. Surrounded by breathtaking mountain vistas, guests can explore the great outdoors through activities such as hiking, mountain biking, and even rock climbing. The hotel also features a state-of-the-art golf course, allowing golf enthusiasts to practice their swing amidst the stunning alpine landscape. After an exhilarating day of outdoor activities, guests can unwind in the hotel’s luxurious spa, which offers a range of wellness treatments and facilities.

3. Anantara Layan Phuket Resort, Phuket, Thailand
For those who seek an active getaway in a tropical paradise, Anantara Layan Phuket Resort is the perfect choice. Located on the pristine shores of Phuket, this resort offers an extensive list of outdoor sports facilities that cater to all interests. Guests can take part in beachside yoga sessions, join a Muay Thai boxing class, or explore the crystal-clear waters through snorkeling or paddleboarding. The resort’s lush gardens also provide the perfect backdrop for a leisurely bike ride or a game of outdoor chess. With a wide range of activities available, Anantara Layan Phuket Resort ensures that guests stay fit and active while enjoying the beauty of Thailand.

4. The Ritz-Carlton, Bali, Indonesia
Situated on the serene shores of Nusa Dua, The Ritz-Carlton, Bali offers an idyllic setting for those seeking outdoor sports facilities. The resort features a well-equipped fitness center overlooking the ocean, allowing guests to maintain their workout routines with state-of-the-art equipment. Additionally, guests can enjoy a game of tennis on the hotel’s courts or take part in beachside yoga and meditation sessions. With its stunning beachfront location, The Ritz-Carlton, Bali provides a perfect blend of relaxation and outdoor activities.

In conclusion, hotels with outdoor sports facilities offer a unique opportunity to stay fit and active while enjoying your vacation. From beachside volleyball courts to mountain biking trails, these establishments go above and beyond to cater to the needs of active travelers. So, the next time you plan your vacation, consider staying at a hotel that offers exceptional outdoor sports facilities. You’ll not only get to experience the beauty of your destination but also maintain your fitness routine in a fun and engaging way.
