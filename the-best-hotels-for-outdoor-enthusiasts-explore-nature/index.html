The Best Hotels for Outdoor Enthusiasts: Explore Nature


When it comes to planning a vacation for outdoor enthusiasts, there are few things more important than choosing the right hotel. A great hotel can enhance your experience in nature, offering comfortable accommodations, convenient amenities, and easy access to breathtaking outdoor attractions. If you’re an adventure seeker or nature lover looking to explore the great outdoors, here is a list of the best hotels that cater to outdoor enthusiasts.

1. The Lodge at Glacier National Park, Montana:
Nestled amidst the stunning scenery of Montana’s Glacier National Park, The Lodge is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. With its rustic charm and proximity to hiking trails, wildlife spotting opportunities, and fishing spots, this hotel is perfect for those seeking an authentic outdoor experience. After a day of exploring, guests can relax by the fireplace or enjoy a delicious meal in the hotel’s cozy restaurant.

2. The Chateau Lake Louise, Alberta, Canada:
Located in the heart of Banff National Park, The Chateau Lake Louise offers unparalleled access to a variety of outdoor activities. From hiking and mountain biking to kayaking and skiing, this hotel caters to all types of outdoor enthusiasts. As if the natural beauty of the surroundings wasn’t enough, the hotel itself boasts luxurious amenities, including a spa and multiple dining options.

3. The Under Canvas, various locations across the USA:
For the adventurous souls who prefer camping but still want some comfort, The Under Canvas is an excellent choice. With locations near national parks such as Yellowstone, Zion, and Great Smoky Mountains, this hotel offers deluxe tents equipped with proper beds and private bathrooms. It allows guests to immerse themselves in nature without compromising on comfort.

4. The Brando, French Polynesia:
If your idea of outdoor exploration involves beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters, then The Brando is the perfect hotel for you. Situated on a private island in French Polynesia, this eco-friendly luxury resort offers a range of activities like snorkeling, paddleboarding, and guided nature walks. You can also learn about the region’s rich marine life and conservation efforts at the on-site research center.

5. The Oberoi Vanyavilas, Ranthambore, India:
For wildlife enthusiasts, The Oberoi Vanyavilas combines luxury with a unique safari experience. Located near Ranthambore National Park, known for its tiger population, this hotel offers guests the opportunity to embark on thrilling tiger safaris. After a day in the wilderness, guests can unwind in luxurious tents, dine under the stars, or indulge in treatments at the spa.

6. The Basecamp Hotel, South Lake Tahoe, California:
Situated near the stunning Lake Tahoe, The Basecamp Hotel is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts throughout the year. Whether it’s hiking in the summer, skiing in the winter, or simply enjoying the picturesque beauty of the lake, this hotel provides easy access to it all. With cozy rooms inspired by camping, communal fire pits, and outdoor adventure packages, guests will feel right at home.

7. The Explora Patagonia, Torres del Paine, Chile:
For those seeking a remote and awe-inspiring outdoor experience, The Explora Patagonia is an excellent choice. Located in the heart of Torres del Paine National Park, this hotel offers unparalleled access to glaciers, mountains, and pristine lakes. With a range of guided hikes and horseback riding adventures, guests can explore the region’s breathtaking landscapes while enjoying luxurious accommodations and amenities.

When planning your next adventure-filled vacation, consider these hotels that cater specifically to outdoor enthusiasts. Each of these establishments offers unique experiences, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the wonders of nature while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed hotel. Whether it’s hiking through national parks, spotting wildlife, or simply enjoying the serenity of a beautiful setting, these hotels are sure to make your outdoor adventure truly unforgettable.
