The Best Platformers to Test Your Jumping Skills


Platform games, also known as platformers, have been a staple in the gaming world for decades. These games challenge players’ jumping skills, requiring precise timing, coordination, and quick reflexes. Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or new to the world of platformers, this blog post will introduce you to some of the best platformers out there that will put your jumping skills to the test.

1. Super Mario Bros. (1985)
No list of platformers would be complete without mentioning the game that revolutionized the genre. Super Mario Bros. is a true classic that introduced players to the iconic plumber, Mario. With its tight controls and well-designed levels, this game remains a must-play for anyone wanting to experience the roots of platforming.

2. Donkey Kong Country (1994)
Donkey Kong Country took the gaming world by storm with its stunning graphics and innovative gameplay mechanics. This game follows the adventures of Donkey Kong and his sidekick, Diddy Kong, as they navigate through challenging levels filled with enemies and obstacles. The game’s unique blend of platforming and barrel-blasting action makes it a standout in the genre.

3. Rayman Legends (2013)
Rayman Legends is a visually stunning platformer that offers a whimsical and vibrant world to explore. With its beautiful hand-drawn art style and fluid gameplay, this game provides a satisfying challenge for players of all skill levels. The game also features multiplayer modes, allowing you to team up with friends and tackle the levels together.

4. Celeste (2018)
If you’re looking for a platformer that will truly test your jumping skills, Celeste is the game for you. This indie gem follows the journey of Madeline as she climbs a treacherous mountain filled with challenging obstacles. With its tight controls and pixel-perfect precision, Celeste offers a rewarding and addictive experience for platforming enthusiasts.

5. Ori and the Blind Forest (2015)
Ori and the Blind Forest is a visually stunning platformer that combines breathtaking visuals with a heartfelt story. As the guardian spirit Ori, players must navigate through a beautifully crafted world, solving puzzles and facing off against formidable enemies. The game’s fluid movement mechanics and immersive atmosphere make it a standout choice for any platforming enthusiast.

6. Shovel Knight (2014)
Drawing inspiration from classic platformers of the past, Shovel Knight offers a delightful blend of retro charm and modern gameplay mechanics. Players take on the role of the titular Shovel Knight as he embarks on a quest to defeat the evil Enchantress. With its tight controls, challenging levels, and memorable boss fights, Shovel Knight is a must-play for fans of old-school platforming.

7. Hollow Knight (2017)
Hollow Knight is a dark and atmospheric platformer that takes players on a haunting journey through a forgotten kingdom. With its sprawling interconnected world and challenging combat, this game offers a deep and immersive experience. The game’s precise platforming mechanics and intricate level design will put your jumping skills to the ultimate test.

These are just a few of the best platformers that will challenge your jumping skills and provide hours of entertainment. Whether you’re a fan of classic platformers or prefer more modern offerings, there’s something on this list for everyone. So grab your controller, get ready to jump, and embark on these thrilling platforming adventures.
