Destination Weddings: Hotels that Make Your Big Day Truly Special


Destination weddings have become increasingly popular in recent years, and it’s no wonder why. The idea of saying ‘I do’ in a stunning location, surrounded by loved ones, is a dream for many couples. When it comes to planning a destination wedding, one of the most important aspects to consider is the venue. Luckily, there are plenty of hotels around the world that specialize in hosting weddings and can make your big day truly special.

When choosing a hotel for your destination wedding, there are a few key factors to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to find a venue that offers breathtaking views and a beautiful backdrop for your ceremony. Whether it’s a white sandy beach, a lush garden, or a historic castle, the setting should be something out of a fairytale. After all, your wedding photos will be treasured for a lifetime, so you want them to be truly remarkable.

Another factor to consider is the size and capacity of the hotel. Depending on the size of your guest list, you may need a venue that can accommodate a large number of people. Some hotels specialize in hosting grand, extravagant weddings, while others excel in creating intimate and romantic settings for smaller celebrations. It’s important to choose a hotel that aligns with your vision for your big day and can comfortably accommodate your guests.

In addition to the aesthetics and capacity, it’s important to consider the services and amenities that the hotel offers. Many hotels that cater to weddings have in-house wedding planners who can help you with every detail, from the decor and flowers to the menu and entertainment. Having a dedicated wedding planner can alleviate a lot of stress and ensure that everything goes smoothly on your special day.

Furthermore, some hotels offer all-inclusive wedding packages that can be a great option for couples who want to simplify the planning process. These packages typically include accommodations for the couple and their guests, as well as meals, drinks, and even activities. All-inclusive packages not only make the planning process easier but also ensure that everyone has a fantastic time during their stay.

Now let’s dive into some of the top hotels around the world that are renowned for their destination weddings:

1. Banyan Tree Mayakoba, Riviera Maya, Mexico:
Located on the stunning beaches of Mexico’s Riviera Maya, Banyan Tree Mayakoba offers a picture-perfect setting for a destination wedding. The resort features luxurious villas with private pools, beautiful gardens, and a dedicated wedding team to help plan your special day. With its white sand beaches and turquoise waters, this hotel is perfect for couples who want a romantic beachside wedding.

2. Ashford Castle, County Mayo, Ireland:
For couples looking for a fairytale wedding, Ashford Castle in Ireland is the perfect choice. This 800-year-old castle is situated on a 350-acre estate and offers a breathtaking backdrop for your wedding. The castle’s grandeur and elegance, combined with its stunning gardens and lake views, create a truly magical setting for your special day.

3. The Plaza Hotel, New York City, USA:
If you’ve ever dreamed of a glamorous city wedding, The Plaza Hotel in New York City is the ideal choice. This iconic hotel oozes luxury and sophistication and is known for hosting extravagant weddings. From the stunning ballroom to the glamorous rooftop terrace, The Plaza offers a variety of beautiful spaces for your wedding celebrations. Plus, being located in the heart of Manhattan means you and your guests will have plenty of attractions to explore before and after the big day.

4. Castello di Montignano, Umbria, Italy:
For a romantic and intimate wedding, Castello di Montignano in Italy is a hidden gem. This 12th-century castle has been beautifully restored and offers breathtaking views of the rolling hills of Umbria. With its medieval charm and picturesque surroundings, it’s the perfect place for a fairytale wedding in the Italian countryside.

5. Amanera, Cabrera, Dominican Republic:
Nestled between the turquoise waters of the Caribbean Sea and the lush greenery of the Dominican Republic, Amanera is a luxurious resort known for its exceptional service and stunning views. The resort offers a variety of venues for your wedding, from beautiful beachfront settings to romantic gardens. With its pristine beaches and lush tropical landscapes, Amanera provides an idyllic backdrop for your destination wedding.

These are just a few examples of the many hotels around the world that offer exceptional wedding experiences. When choosing a hotel for your destination wedding, it’s important to consider your personal style, the size of your guest list, and the services and amenities that are important to you. Whether you dream of a beachside ceremony, a fairytale castle wedding, or a glamorous city celebration, there’s a hotel out there that can make your big day truly special.
