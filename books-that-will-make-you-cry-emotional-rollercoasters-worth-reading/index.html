Books That Will Make You Cry: Emotional Rollercoasters Worth Reading


When it comes to literature, there is a wide range of books that can evoke a multitude of emotions within us. From joy to anger, from fear to excitement, books have the power to make us feel a whole spectrum of emotions. However, there is something truly special about those books that have the ability to bring tears to our eyes, to touch us at our core, and to leave a lasting impact on our souls. In this blog post, we will explore a collection of books that will take you on an emotional rollercoaster, leaving you feeling vulnerable, moved, and ultimately transformed.

1. ‘The Kite Runner’ by Khaled Hosseini:
This powerful and heart-wrenching novel tells the story of Amir, a young boy from Kabul, and his journey of redemption. Through the tumultuous backdrop of war-ravaged Afghanistan, Hosseini weaves a tale of friendship, betrayal, and sacrifice that will undoubtedly bring tears to your eyes.

2. ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ by John Green:
Prepare to have your heartstrings tugged as you follow the love story of Hazel and Gus, two teenagers battling cancer. John Green’s poignant writing explores the fragility of life, the beauty of love, and the inevitability of loss. This book will leave you with a renewed appreciation for the preciousness of every moment.

3. ‘A Little Life’ by Hanya Yanagihara:
This emotionally intense and deeply affecting novel follows the lives of four college friends as they navigate love, friendship, and the lasting impact of childhood trauma. Yanagihara’s raw and unflinching portrayal of human suffering and resilience is bound to leave you emotionally drained yet profoundly moved.

4. ‘The Book Thief’ by Markus Zusak:
Set during World War II, this extraordinary novel tells the story of Liesel Meminger, a young girl who finds solace in the power of words. Through the eyes of Death itself, Zusak explores themes of love, loss, and the indomitable spirit of the human soul. Get ready for a rollercoaster of emotions that will leave you breathless.

5. ‘Me Before You’ by Jojo Moyes:
This heart-wrenching love story follows the journey of Louisa Clark, a quirky and compassionate young woman, as she becomes a companion to Will Traynor, a man paralyzed from the neck down. Moyes delicately explores themes of love, choices, and the value of life, taking readers on an emotional journey that will leave them in tears.

6. ‘The Lovely Bones’ by Alice Sebold:
Told through the eyes of a murdered teenage girl, this haunting and beautiful novel explores themes of grief, loss, and the power of love. Sebold’s lyrical prose will transport you to a world where the boundaries between life and death blur, leaving you with a profound sense of both sorrow and hope.

7. ‘The Color Purple’ by Alice Walker:
Walker’s Pulitzer Prize-winning novel delves into the life of Celie, an African-American woman living in rural Georgia in the early 20th century. Through Celie’s letters, we witness her journey from oppression to empowerment, from pain to healing. This emotionally charged novel will leave you with a renewed sense of resilience and the power of the human spirit.

These books are not just stories, but profound explorations of the human experience. They delve deep into our emotions, forcing us to confront our vulnerabilities, our fears, and our capacity for love and compassion. So, if you are ready to embark on an emotional rollercoaster, grab a box of tissues and dive into these truly remarkable reads that will make you cry, reflect, and ultimately, feel more alive than ever before.
