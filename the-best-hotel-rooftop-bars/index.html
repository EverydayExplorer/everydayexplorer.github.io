The best hotel rooftop bars


Welcome to our blog post all about the best hotel rooftop bars! Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or just looking for a unique way to enjoy a drink with a view, rooftop bars offer an amazing experience that combines stunning vistas, delicious cocktails, and a vibrant atmosphere. In this article, we’ll take you on a journey to discover some of the most outstanding hotel rooftop bars around the world. So, let’s raise our glasses and get started!

1. Sky Bar at Lebua, Bangkok, Thailand:
Let’s begin our adventure in the vibrant city of Bangkok, where the Sky Bar at Lebua reigns supreme. This iconic rooftop bar gained global fame after it was featured in the movie “The Hangover Part II.” Located on the 63rd floor, Sky Bar provides a breathtaking 360-degree view of the cityscape, including the Chao Phraya River. Sip on a signature Hangovertini and soak in the atmosphere that makes this bar an absolute must-visit.

2. Radio Rooftop Bar, London, United Kingdom:
Next stop, London! Perched on the 10th floor of the ME London hotel, Radio Rooftop Bar offers panoramic views of the city’s iconic skyline, including landmarks like the Shard, St. Paul’s Cathedral, and the River Thames. Indulge in their extensive cocktail menu, which features both classic favorites and innovative creations. Whether you visit during the day or at night when the city lights up, this rooftop bar will leave you mesmerized.

3. AER, Mumbai, India:
Prepare to be enchanted by the magic of AER, located atop the Four Seasons Hotel in Mumbai. With stunning views of the Arabian Sea, this glamorous open-air lounge is the perfect place to enjoy the city’s skyline. Sip on handcrafted cocktails, sample delectable bites, and revel in the ambient music that sets the mood for a memorable evening. AER is proof that Mumbai’s nightlife scene is as vibrant as its daytime energy.

4. The Press Lounge, New York City, USA:
Nestled in the heart of Manhattan, The Press Lounge offers a chic and sophisticated escape from the bustling city below. Located on the 16th floor of the Ink48 Hotel, this rooftop bar provides breathtaking views of the Hudson River and the Midtown skyline. The signature cocktails are crafted with precision, and the bar’s extensive wine list ensures that even the most discerning wine lover will find something to please their palate.

5. Ozone, Hong Kong, China:
Our final destination takes us to the highest bar in the world – Ozone. Located on the 118th floor of the Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong, this sky-high establishment offers unparalleled views of the city’s skyline and Victoria Harbour. The sleek and contemporary design complements the innovative cocktail menu, making for a truly memorable experience. As you sip on your drink, take a moment to appreciate the breathtaking view, which extends all the way to the distant mountains.

As we conclude our journey through some of the best hotel rooftop bars worldwide, we hope you’ve discovered new places to add to your travel bucket list. These elevated havens not only provide breathtaking views but also offer a unique ambiance that makes every visit a memorable one. Whether you’re a fan of vibrant cities, serene coastlines, or breathtaking skylines, there’s a rooftop bar out there waiting to be discovered. So, cheers to the countless enchanting sunsets, twinkling city lights, and unforgettable moments that await you on these magnificent rooftops!
