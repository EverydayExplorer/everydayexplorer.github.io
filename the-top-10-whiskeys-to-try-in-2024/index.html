The Top 10 Whiskeys to Try in 2024


Whiskey, a timeless spirit that has captivated the palates of connoisseurs for centuries, continues to evolve and surprise us with its diverse offerings. As we step into the year 2024, it’s the perfect time to explore the top 10 whiskeys that are making waves in the world of spirits. From traditional favorites to innovative newcomers, this list encompasses a wide range of flavors, aromas, and distillation techniques. So, grab your tasting glass and get ready to embark on a whiskey journey like no other.

1. The Macallan Edition No. 7: Known for their exceptional single malt Scotch whiskies, The Macallan has once again pushed the boundaries with Edition No. 7. This limited edition release showcases a blend of different cask types, resulting in a vibrant and complex flavor profile that will leave you craving for more.

2. Pappy Van Winkle’s Family Reserve 15-Year-Old: Often referred to as the holy grail of bourbon, Pappy Van Winkle’s Family Reserve is a legend in its own right. Aged for 15 years, this bourbon boasts rich and velvety notes of caramel, vanilla, and oak, making it a must-try for any whiskey enthusiast.

3. Yamazaki Mizunara Cask 2019 Edition: From the land of the rising sun comes a whiskey that pays homage to Japan’s unique Mizunara oak. The Yamazaki Mizunara Cask 2019 Edition offers a delicate balance of fruity and spicy flavors, with hints of sandalwood and incense, creating an unforgettable sensory experience.

4. Redbreast 21-Year-Old: If you’re a fan of Irish whiskey, then Redbreast 21-Year-Old is a true gem. This single pot still whiskey is matured for over two decades, resulting in a luscious and complex flavor profile. Notes of dried fruits, spices, and a lingering sweetness make it a whiskey worth savoring.

5. Ardbeg Corryvreckan: For those who appreciate the peaty side of Scotch whisky, Ardbeg Corryvreckan is an absolute must-try. With its intense smoky character, combined with flavors of dark chocolate, black pepper, and brine, this Islay single malt will transport you to the rugged shores of Scotland.

6. WhistlePig The Boss Hog VII: Magellan’s Atlantic: This limited edition rye whiskey from WhistlePig showcases the brand’s commitment to quality and innovation. Aged for 17 years and finished in Spanish oak barrels that once held Madeira wine, The Boss Hog VII: Magellan’s Atlantic offers a unique and luxurious drinking experience.

7. Booker’s 2024-01 ‘Tagalong Batch’: As part of their ongoing small-batch releases, Booker’s Bourbon presents the 2024-01 ‘Tagalong Batch.’ This high-proof whiskey bursts with bold flavors of toasted nuts, caramel, and a touch of cinnamon, delivering a memorable and robust tasting experience.

8. Nikka Taketsuru 25-Year-Old: Created to honor the founder of Nikka Whisky, Masataka Taketsuru, this Japanese blended malt is a true masterpiece. Aged for a quarter of a century, it showcases a harmonious balance of fruits, spices, and elegant oak, resulting in a whiskey that exudes sophistication and refinement.

9. Buffalo Trace Antique Collection: This highly sought-after collection includes five limited edition whiskeys that demonstrate the artistry and craftsmanship of Buffalo Trace Distillery. From the rich and spicy George T. Stagg to the complex and velvety William Larue Weller, each expression offers a unique taste of American whiskey history.

10. GlenDronach 18-Year-Old Allardice: Rounding off our list is the GlenDronach 18-Year-Old Allardice, a sherry cask matured Scotch whisky that showcases the distillery’s commitment to traditional production methods. With its deep and indulgent flavors of dark chocolate, dried fruits, and warm spices, this expression is a true delight for the senses.

These top 10 whiskeys to try in 2024 represent the epitome of craftsmanship, innovation, and tradition in the world of spirits. Whether you’re a seasoned whiskey aficionado or just beginning your journey into the world of whiskey, these selections are sure to leave a lasting impression. So, raise your glass and toast to the incredible whiskies that await you in this new year. Cheers!
