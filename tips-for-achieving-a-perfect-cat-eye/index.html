Tips for Achieving a Perfect Cat Eye


Are you tired of struggling to achieve the perfect cat eye? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks to help you master this classic and timeless makeup look. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned pro, these tips will elevate your cat eye game to the next level. So, grab your eyeliner and let’s dive in!

1. Start with a clean canvas: Before you begin, make sure your eyelids are clean and free from any oils or residues. This will ensure that your eyeliner glides on smoothly and stays put throughout the day.

2. Choose the right tool: The key to a flawless cat eye is using the right eyeliner. While there are many options available, we recommend using a liquid eyeliner with a fine tip for precise application. This will give you more control and help you create sharp, clean lines.

3. Map out your shape: To achieve a symmetrical cat eye, it’s important to map out the shape beforehand. Start by drawing a thin line from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. This will serve as a guide for the wing of your cat eye.

4. Connect the dots: Once you have your guide, connect it to the center of your upper lash line. Start from the inner corner of your eye and gradually thicken the line as you move towards the outer corner. Take your time and keep the line as close to the lash line as possible for a seamless look.

5. Create the wing: Now it’s time to create the iconic wing. Extend the line from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. The angle and length of the wing are up to you, but we recommend starting with a subtle flick and gradually building it up if desired.

6. Fill in the gaps: After you’ve created your wing, go back and fill in any gaps or uneven areas along the lash line. This will ensure a smooth and consistent look. If you prefer a more dramatic cat eye, you can also thicken the line towards the outer corner for added intensity.

7. Clean up any mistakes: Don’t worry if you make any mistakes along the way. Simply dip a small angled brush into some makeup remover and gently clean up any smudges or uneven lines. You can also use a concealer to sharpen the edges and make your cat eye pop.

8. Set it in place: To ensure that your cat eye stays put all day long, set it with a translucent powder or a matching eyeshadow. This will prevent smudging and keep your eyeliner looking fresh and vibrant.

9. Practice makes perfect: Achieving the perfect cat eye takes practice, so don’t get discouraged if it doesn’t turn out perfectly the first time. Keep experimenting, trying different techniques, and soon you’ll become a cat eye pro!

10. Have fun and be creative: The cat eye is a versatile look that can be customized to suit your personal style. Play around with different colors, textures, and finishes to create unique and eye-catching looks. Don’t be afraid to think outside the box and let your creativity shine!

There you have it – our top tips for achieving a perfect cat eye. Remember, practice makes perfect, so keep experimenting and don’t be afraid to try new things. With time and patience, you’ll be able to create flawless cat eyes that will turn heads wherever you go. So, grab your eyeliner and get ready to rock that fierce feline look!
