The Best Pop Culture Podcasts to Listen To


Podcasts have become a staple in the world of pop culture, offering listeners a unique blend of entertainment, insights, and discussions about all things trending in music, movies, TV shows, and more. If you’re looking to stay up-to-date with the latest pop culture buzz or dive deep into nostalgic favorites, here’s a curated list of some of the best pop culture podcasts that deserve a spot in your listening lineup.

1. Pop Culture Happy Hour Hosted by NPR, Pop Culture Happy Hour is a lively roundtable discussion featuring a panel of pop culture experts. From dissecting the latest blockbuster movies to reviewing hit TV series and analyzing viral internet trends, this podcast covers a wide range of topics with wit and humor. Each episode is like joining a fun conversation with friends who share a passion for all things pop culture.

2. The Watch The Watch, part of The Ringer Podcast Network, is hosted by Chris Ryan and Andy Greenwald. This dynamic duo offers insightful commentary on the hottest happenings in television, film, and streaming platforms. Their in-depth reviews and interviews with industry insiders make The Watch a must-listen for pop culture enthusiasts seeking thoughtful analysis and recommendations.

3. You Must Remember This For those intrigued by Hollywood’s golden age and iconic stars, You Must Remember This is a captivating podcast hosted by Karina Longworth. Delving into the hidden and often scandalous stories of Hollywood’s past, this podcast explores the lives and legacies of legendary figures like Marilyn Monroe, Elizabeth Taylor, and more. Longworth’s deep research and storytelling prowess make each episode a fascinating journey through Tinseltown’s history.

4. The Rewatchables If you’re a movie buff who loves revisiting classic films, The Rewatchables is the podcast for you. Hosted by Bill Simmons and a rotating cast of cinephiles, each episode focuses on a beloved movie deemed endlessly rewatchable. The discussions cover everything from standout performances to memorable quotes, making The Rewatchables a delightful homage to cinema’s greatest hits.

5. Keep It Hosted by Ira Madison III, Louis Virtel, and Aida Osman, Keep It is a pop culture podcast that blends humor, wit, and sharp cultural critique. The hosts unpack the latest headlines, from celebrity gossip to political controversies, offering unfiltered opinions on the intersection of entertainment and society. With its irreverent style and thought-provoking commentary, Keep It is a refreshing addition to any podcast playlist.

6. The Big Picture Another gem from The Ringer Podcast Network, The Big Picture is hosted by film aficionado Sean Fennessey. This podcast dives deep into the world of movies, exploring industry trends, award season predictions, and interviews with directors and actors. Fennessey’s passion for cinema shines through in each episode, making The Big Picture a must-listen for cinephiles seeking intelligent film discourse.

7. Buffering the Vampire Slayer For fans of cult TV shows, Buffering the Vampire Slayer is a delightful podcast hosted by Jenny Owen Youngs and Kristin Russo. Each episode dissects an episode of Buffy the Vampire Slayer, offering analysis, musical recaps, and behind-the-scenes anecdotes. Whether you’re a die-hard Buffy fan or discovering the series for the first time, this podcast provides a fun and immersive journey into the Buffyverse.

8. Song Exploder While not strictly pop culture, Song Exploder delves into the creative process behind music. Hosted by Hrishikesh Hirway, each episode features an artist breaking down one of their songs, revealing the inspirations, instruments, and production techniques. From indie bands to chart-topping musicians, Song Exploder offers a fascinating glimpse into the artistry behind the music that shapes pop culture.

In conclusion, these pop culture podcasts cater to a diverse range of interests, from movies and television to music and celebrity gossip. Whether you’re seeking insightful commentary, nostalgic discussions, or just a good laugh, these podcasts are sure to keep you entertained and informed about the ever-evolving world of pop culture. So, grab your headphones and start listening to these top-notch shows that celebrate the art and impact of entertainment. Happy listening!
