The Dos and Don'ts of False Eyelash Application


The art of false eyelash application can be a game-changer for anyone looking to enhance their natural beauty. Whether you’re preparing for a special occasion or simply want to add some extra oomph to your everyday look, false lashes can make all the difference. However, applying false eyelashes can be a daunting task if you’re not familiar with the dos and don’ts. In this blog post, we’ll explore some essential tips and tricks to help you achieve flawless false lash application.

Dos:

1. Choose the Right False Lashes: Before diving into the application process, it’s crucial to select the right pair of false lashes. Consider the length, volume, and style that best complements your eye shape and desired look. There are various types of false lashes available, including natural, dramatic, and wispy styles. Experiment with different options to find the perfect fit for you.

2. Prep Your Natural Lashes: Prepping your natural lashes is a crucial step that often gets overlooked. Start by curling your lashes with an eyelash curler to create a seamless blend between your natural lashes and the falsies. Additionally, applying a coat of mascara can help create a sturdy base for the false lashes to adhere to.

3. Trim and Customize: False lashes are designed to be longer than your natural lash line, allowing you to customize their length to fit your eyes perfectly. Before applying the lashes, hold them up to your eye and trim any excess from the outer corners. This will ensure a comfortable fit and prevent poking or lifting.

4. Apply Adhesive Correctly: When it comes to applying lash adhesive, less is more. Use a high-quality adhesive and apply a thin layer along the lash band. Wait a few seconds for the adhesive to become tacky before applying the lashes. This will help them adhere better and prevent any sliding or lifting throughout the day.

5. Placement is Key: Proper placement is crucial for a natural-looking result. Instead of applying the lashes directly on top of your natural lash line, start by placing them just above it. This will create the illusion of longer, fuller lashes while maintaining a seamless blend.

Don’ts:

1. Rush the Application: False eyelash application requires patience and precision. Avoid rushing through the process as it can lead to uneven placement and a less-than-desirable result. Take your time, follow the steps carefully, and enjoy the process.

2. Neglect Proper Removal: Removing false lashes is just as important as applying them correctly. Never pull or tug on the lashes as it can damage your natural ones. Instead, gently soak a cotton pad with an oil-based makeup remover and hold it against your lashes for a few seconds to loosen the adhesive. Slowly and carefully peel off the false lashes from the outer corner, working your way towards the inner corner.

3. Forget to Clean and Store: After removing your false lashes, it’s crucial to clean them properly to maintain their shape and integrity. Use a gentle eye makeup remover to remove any leftover adhesive or mascara. Once cleaned, store the lashes in their original packaging or a clean, dry container to keep them protected and ready for future use.

4. Overdo the Mascara: While a coat of mascara can help blend your natural lashes with the falsies, it’s essential not to overdo it. Applying too much mascara can clump the lashes together and make them appear unnatural. Stick to a light coat for a seamless finish.

5. Be Afraid to Experiment: False eyelashes are a fun and versatile beauty accessory, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles, lengths, and densities. Play around with different looks to find what suits you best and embrace your unique style.

By following these dos and don’ts of false eyelash application, you’ll be well on your way to achieving flawless, natural-looking lashes every time. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t get discouraged if it takes a few tries to master the technique. With time and patience, you’ll become a pro at applying false lashes and elevate your beauty game to new heights.
