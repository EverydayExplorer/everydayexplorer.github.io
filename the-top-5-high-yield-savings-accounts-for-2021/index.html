The Top 5 High-Yield Savings Accounts for 2021


In today’s fast-paced world, it’s crucial to make the most of your hard-earned money. One of the best ways to do this is by choosing a high-yield savings account that offers competitive interest rates and minimal fees. With so many options available, it can be challenging to find the best account for your needs. In this blog post, we’ll explore the top 5 high-yield savings accounts for 2021, helping you make an informed decision and maximize your savings potential.

1. Marcus by Goldman Sachs High-Yield Online Savings Account

Marcus by Goldman Sachs is a well-known name in the financial industry, and their High-Yield Online Savings Account is no exception. This account offers a competitive annual percentage yield (APY) of 0.50%, which is significantly higher than the national average.

Pros:

* No monthly maintenance fees
* No minimum balance requirements
* FDIC insured up to the maximum allowed amount

Cons:

* No branch locations or ATM access
* Limited customer service options (phone and online chat)

2. Discover Bank Online Savings Account

Discover Bank is another popular choice for high-yield savings accounts, with an APY of 0.50%. They offer a variety of features that make this account an attractive option for many consumers.

Pros:

* No monthly maintenance fees
* No minimum balance requirements
* FDIC insured up to the maximum allowed amount
* Free ATM withdrawals at any ATM in the United States

Cons:

* No branch locations
* Limited customer service options (phone, online chat, and email)

3. American Express National Bank High Yield Savings Account

American Express National Bank offers a competitive APY of 0.50% on their High Yield Savings Account. This account is an excellent choice for those who want a simple, no-frills savings option.

Pros:

* No monthly maintenance fees
* No minimum balance requirements
* FDIC insured up to the maximum allowed amount
* Easy online account management

Cons:

* No branch locations
* Limited customer service options (phone and online chat)

4. Synchrony Bank High-Yield Savings Account

Synchrony Bank’s High-Yield Savings Account offers an APY of 0.50%, making it a strong contender in the high-yield savings market. This account is perfect for those who prefer a bank with a long history of financial stability.

Pros:

* No monthly maintenance fees
* No minimum balance requirements
* FDIC insured up to the maximum allowed amount
* Competitive interest rates

Cons:

* No branch locations
* Limited customer service options (phone and online chat)

5. Ally Bank Online Savings Account

Ally Bank is well-known for its online banking services, and their Online Savings Account is no exception. With an APY of 0.50%, this account offers competitive interest rates and a range of features that make it an excellent choice for many consumers.

Pros:

* No monthly maintenance fees
* No minimum balance requirements
* FDIC insured up to the maximum allowed amount
* Free ATM withdrawals at any Allpoint ATM

Cons:

* No branch locations
* Limited customer service options (phone, online chat, and email)

Conclusion

Choosing the right high-yield savings account is essential for maximizing your savings potential. Each of the accounts listed above offers competitive interest rates and minimal fees, making them excellent options for 2021. When selecting the best account for your needs, consider factors such as account features, customer service options, and accessibility. By doing so, you’ll be well on your way to maximizing your savings and securing a brighter financial future.
