The Dos and Don'ts of Properly Applying Lipstick


When it comes to achieving the perfect pout, applying lipstick can truly transform your look. However, there are a few dos and don’ts to keep in mind to ensure that your lipstick application is flawless and long-lasting. In this blog post, we will delve into the world of lipstick and share some valuable tips on how to properly apply it without any mishaps. So, let’s get started!

DO: Exfoliate your lips
Before applying any lipstick, it’s important to prep your lips properly. Exfoliating your lips helps remove any dry or flaky skin, leaving them smooth and ready for a flawless application. You can use a lip scrub or even a soft toothbrush to gently exfoliate your lips. Remember to moisturize them afterward to keep them hydrated.

DO: Use a lip primer
Just like you prime your face before applying makeup, using a lip primer can make a significant difference in the longevity of your lipstick. A lip primer creates a smooth base for the lipstick to adhere to and prevents it from feathering or smudging throughout the day.

DO: Line your lips
To achieve a defined and polished look, always use a lip liner that matches your lipstick shade. Start by outlining your lip shape and then fill in the entire lip area with the liner. This not only helps prevent the lipstick from bleeding but also provides a base for the color to adhere to, making it last longer.

DO: Apply lipstick with a brush
While you can directly apply lipstick from the tube, using a lip brush allows for more precision and control. A brush helps you evenly distribute the color and reach the corners of your lips effortlessly. Plus, it helps to layer the lipstick for a more intense or subtle look, depending on your preference.

DO: Blot and reapply
After applying the first layer of lipstick, blot your lips with a tissue to remove any excess product. This step helps set the color and prevents it from transferring onto your teeth or fading quickly. Once blotted, reapply another layer of lipstick for a more vibrant and long-lasting result.

DON’T: Rush the application
Properly applying lipstick requires patience and attention to detail. Avoid rushing through the process as it can lead to a messy application. Take your time to ensure that your lips are properly lined and filled with color. Remember, a little extra time spent will result in a more polished and professional finish.

DON’T: Neglect lip care
Apart from exfoliating and moisturizing your lips before applying lipstick, it’s essential to take care of them regularly. Hydrate your lips by drinking plenty of water and applying a lip balm throughout the day. This will prevent your lips from becoming dry and chapped, ensuring a smooth canvas for lipstick application.

DON’T: Forget to check your teeth
One common lipstick mishap is the color transferring onto your teeth. To avoid this embarrassing situation, after applying your lipstick, insert your finger into your mouth, purse your lips around it, and then pull it out. Any excess lipstick on the inner part of your lips will be removed, preventing it from getting onto your teeth.

DON’T: Overdo it with bold colors
While bold and vibrant lipstick shades can make a statement, it’s important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and outfit. Avoid overwhelming your look by balancing a bold lip with more neutral eye makeup. Remember, the key is to enhance your natural beauty, not overpower it.

In conclusion, applying lipstick is an art that requires practice and attention to detail. By following these dos and don’ts, you can achieve a flawless and long-lasting lip look that will turn heads wherever you go. So, go ahead, experiment with different shades and textures, and embrace the power of a well-applied lipstick.
