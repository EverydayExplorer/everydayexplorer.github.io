The best 2D platformers


Welcome to our blog, where we delve into the fascinating world of 2D platformer games! If you’re a fan of exciting gameplay, stunning visuals, and immersive worlds, then this article is perfect for you. We’ve carefully curated a list of some of the best 2D platformers out there, so grab your controller, and let’s dive in!

1. “Super Mario World” (1990) – A timeless classic, this game introduced us to the iconic character, Mario, and his adventures in the colorful land of Dinosaur Land. With its tight controls, clever level design, and memorable power-ups, “Super Mario World” stands as one of the greatest 2D platformers of all time.

2. “Celeste” (2018) – Prepare to embark on a challenging, yet incredibly rewarding journey in this indie gem. Guide the protagonist, Madeline, through treacherous mountain terrain, and overcome inner demons in this heartfelt narrative. With precise controls and a beautifully crafted soundtrack, “Celeste” is a must-play for platforming enthusiasts.

3. “Rayman Legends” (2013) – Join Rayman and his wacky friends in this vibrant and visually stunning platformer. Boasting creative level design, rhythmic gameplay, and a plethora of unlockable characters, “Rayman Legends” guarantees endless hours of fun and laughter for players of all ages.

4. “Hollow Knight” (2017) – Descend into the hauntingly beautiful underground kingdom of Hallownest in this captivating Metroidvania adventure. Explore vast interconnected areas, engage in intense battles with unique enemies, and uncover the secrets that lie within. With its atmospheric world, challenging gameplay, and rich lore, “Hollow Knight” is an absolute masterpiece.

5. “Shovel Knight” (2014) – Paying homage to the classic 8-bit era, this game delivers a delightful blend of nostalgia and modern gameplay. Take control of the valiant Shovel Knight as he fights against the evil Enchantress and her Order of No Quarter. With its charming pixel art, tight controls, and exhilarating boss battles, “Shovel Knight” is a true love letter to retro gaming.

6. “Ori and the Blind Forest” (2015) – Prepare to be enchanted by this visually stunning and emotionally resonant platformer. Set in a lush, hand-painted world, guide Ori through breathtaking environments while unraveling a touching story of love, sacrifice, and hope. With its precise controls, enchanting soundtrack, and tear-jerking narrative, “Ori and the Blind Forest” is an unforgettable experience.

7. “Cuphead” (2017) – Step into the shoes of Cuphead and Mugman in this visually striking and challenging run-and-gun platformer. Inspired by 1930s cartoons, “Cuphead” combines tight platforming with intense boss battles, all accompanied by a jazzy soundtrack. Prepare to test your skills and perseverance in this unique and captivating game.

These are just a few of the best 2D platformers that have left a significant impact on the gaming community. Each game offers its own unique gameplay mechanics, art styles, and memorable moments that have captured the hearts of players worldwide.

Whether you’re a long-time platforming enthusiast or just starting your gaming journey, these titles are guaranteed to provide hours of joy, excitement, and even a few moments of frustration. So, grab your favorite platforming companion, and get ready for an unforgettable adventure!

Remember, the world of 2D platformers is constantly evolving, with new and exciting titles being released regularly. So, keep exploring, discovering, and experiencing the wonders of this beloved genre. Happy gaming!
