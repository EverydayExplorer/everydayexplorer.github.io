Sleep in Style: Hotels with Unique and Artistic Bed Designs


In a world where hotels are constantly striving to offer unique and unforgettable experiences to their guests, one aspect that often gets overlooked is the design of the beds. After all, a good night’s sleep is crucial for any traveler, and what better way to enhance that experience than with a bed that is not only comfortable but also visually stunning? In this blog post, we will explore some of the most extraordinary hotels around the world that have taken the concept of ‘sleeping in style’ to a whole new level with their artistic bed designs.

1. Icehotel, Sweden:
Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel is a true masterpiece of frozen art. Each year, the hotel is rebuilt using ice from the nearby Torne River, and the beds are no exception. Imagine sleeping on a bed made entirely of ice, covered with reindeer hides and warm sleeping bags. It’s a surreal experience that will make you feel like royalty in the midst of a winter wonderland.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
If you’ve ever dreamt of waking up to a giraffe peering through your bedroom window, then Giraffe Manor is the place for you. This luxury hotel in Nairobi is famous for its resident herd of Rothschild giraffes, who love to poke their heads into the windows and share breakfast with the guests. The beds in each room are elegantly designed, with a touch of African-inspired decor, allowing you to sleep amidst the wildlife.

3. Null Stern Hotel, Switzerland:
For those seeking a truly minimalist experience, the Null Stern Hotel in Switzerland offers a unique concept. With no walls, no roof, and no doors, the hotel provides a bed set in the middle of nature, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the surrounding beauty. Fall asleep under a blanket of stars, and wake up to breathtaking views of the Swiss Alps.

4. The Manta Resort, Tanzania:
If sleeping underwater is on your bucket list, then The Manta Resort in Pemba Island, Tanzania, is the ultimate destination. The resort’s signature Underwater Room is located four meters below the surface of the Indian Ocean, with a glass-walled bedroom that offers a 360-degree view of the vibrant marine life. Drift off to sleep as colorful fish swim by, creating a truly unforgettable experience.

5. Propeller Island City Lodge, Germany:
For those with a taste for the eccentric, the Propeller Island City Lodge in Berlin is a dream come true. Each room in this art hotel is designed with a unique theme, including beds that resemble cages, coffins, or floating islands. It’s a whimsical and surreal environment that will transport you to a different world as soon as you lay your head down.

These hotels are just a glimpse into the world of artistic bed designs that can truly elevate your sleeping experience. Whether you prefer a frozen palace, an African safari, a minimalist retreat, an underwater sanctuary, or an eccentric art installation, there’s a hotel out there that can make your dreams come true. So, next time you plan a trip, consider sleeping in style and adding a touch of artistry to your journey. Sweet dreams!
