DIY Jewelry Storage: Organize Your Accessories


Jewelry has a special way of adding that perfect finishing touch to any outfit. Whether it’s a statement necklace, delicate earrings, or a stack of bracelets, our accessories hold sentimental value and can elevate our style to new heights. However, if you’re like me, you might find yourself struggling with the challenge of keeping your jewelry organized and tangle-free. Fear not, because today we’re going to explore the world of DIY jewelry storage solutions that will not only keep your accessories organized but also add a touch of personalization to your space.

1. Repurposed Picture Frame Jewelry Organizer

One of the easiest and most creative ways to organize your jewelry is by repurposing an old picture frame. Start by removing the glass from the frame and attaching a mesh or wire grid to the back using a staple gun. This will create a space for hanging your earrings, necklaces, and bracelets. You can also use small hooks or push pins to hang additional items such as rings or hair accessories. To add a decorative touch, paint the frame in a color that matches your room’s aesthetic or cover it with patterned fabric for a more bohemian look.

2. Vintage Tea Cup Ring Holders

If you’re a fan of vintage charm, why not repurpose some beautiful tea cups to hold your rings? Simply gather a few mismatched tea cups from thrift stores or your own collection, and arrange them on a vanity or dresser. Not only will this keep your rings organized and easily accessible, but it will also add a touch of elegance to your space. You can even place a small tray underneath to catch any loose rings or earrings.

3. Wall-Mounted Branch Jewelry Display

For those who love nature-inspired decor, a wall-mounted branch jewelry display can be the perfect solution. Find a sturdy branch with interesting twists and turns, and mount it on the wall using hooks or brackets. Hang your necklaces, bracelets, and even scarves from the branches, allowing them to dangle freely and create an eye-catching display. This DIY project not only keeps your jewelry organized but also brings a touch of the outdoors into your space.

4. DIY Drawer Dividers

If you prefer a more discreet storage solution, consider creating your own drawer dividers. Start by measuring the dimensions of your drawers and cutting pieces of foam board or sturdy cardboard to fit. Then, use a hot glue gun to attach the dividers to the inside of the drawer, creating separate compartments for different types of jewelry. This method allows you to customize the size and layout of your storage space based on your specific needs.

5. Hanging Jewelry Organizer

If you’re short on space or prefer a portable option, a hanging jewelry organizer might be the perfect solution. You can purchase a pre-made hanging organizer with pockets and compartments, or you can create your own using fabric, clear vinyl, and a sewing machine. Hang the organizer on the back of your closet door or on a hook in your bedroom, and fill it with your favorite pieces. This method allows you to easily see all of your jewelry at a glance and saves precious counter or drawer space.

By utilizing these DIY jewelry storage solutions, you can keep your accessories organized and easily accessible while adding a touch of personal style to your space. Whether you opt for a repurposed picture frame, vintage tea cup holders, a wall-mounted branch display, DIY drawer dividers, or a hanging organizer, you’ll find that these creative solutions not only make your life easier but also add a unique flair to your home. So, get creative, unleash your inner DIY guru, and let your jewelry shine!
