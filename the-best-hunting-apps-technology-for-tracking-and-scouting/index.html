The Best Hunting Apps: Technology for Tracking and Scouting


In today’s digital age, technology has seeped into every aspect of our lives, including the ancient art of hunting. Gone are the days of relying solely on instinct and intuition; now, hunters have a wide array of hunting apps at their disposal, designed to enhance their tracking and scouting abilities. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hunting apps available, revolutionizing the way we approach the hunt.

1. HuntStand: Considered one of the most comprehensive hunting apps on the market, HuntStand offers a range of features that assist hunters in planning and executing their hunts. With detailed topographic maps, property boundaries, and weather information, this app allows hunters to scout new locations, mark trails, and even track their own movements during the hunt. Additionally, HuntStand provides access to a vast database of hunting-related data, including wind direction, moon phases, and animal activity patterns, helping hunters make informed decisions in the field.

2. ScoutLook: For hunters who prioritize accurate weather forecasting, ScoutLook is a game-changer. This app provides hyper-local weather information, including temperature, wind speed, and direction, moon phases, and barometric pressure, all specific to the hunter’s location. By utilizing this data, hunters can strategically plan their hunts to maximize their chances of success. Furthermore, ScoutLook offers a scent cone feature, which simulates the direction in which a hunter’s scent will disperse, allowing for better scent control and stealth during the hunt.

3. onX Hunt: Designed with the avid hunter in mind, onX Hunt offers a plethora of features to assist in scouting and tracking. This app provides access to detailed maps, including public and private land boundaries, landowner information, and specific hunting unit boundaries. Additionally, onX Hunt allows users to save waypoints, track their movements, and even download offline maps, ensuring that hunters have access to crucial information even in areas with limited or no cellular service. With its comprehensive mapping features, onX Hunt is an invaluable tool for hunters looking to explore new hunting grounds.

4. HuntWise: As the name suggests, HuntWise is all about providing hunters with the knowledge and insight they need to make successful hunting decisions. This app offers features such as real-time weather updates, moon phases, solunar data, and even a deer movement prediction algorithm. With HuntWise, hunters can plan their hunts based on the most favorable conditions and increase their chances of encountering game. Additionally, the app allows users to log their hunts, record vital statistics, and share their experiences with fellow hunters, creating a strong sense of community among hunting enthusiasts.

5. HuntStand Lite: For hunters looking for a free app that still offers essential features, HuntStand Lite is the perfect choice. This lightweight version of HuntStand provides access to topographic maps, property boundaries, and the ability to mark trails and waypoints. While it may not have the extensive features of its premium counterpart, HuntStand Lite still offers a powerful tool for hunters looking to enhance their scouting efforts without breaking the bank.

In conclusion, hunting apps have revolutionized the way we approach tracking and scouting. From comprehensive mapping features to real-time weather updates and animal activity predictions, these apps provide hunters with a wealth of information at their fingertips. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, incorporating these hunting apps into your arsenal will undoubtedly enhance your hunting experience and increase your chances of a successful hunt. So, embrace technology, download these apps, and let the hunt begin!
