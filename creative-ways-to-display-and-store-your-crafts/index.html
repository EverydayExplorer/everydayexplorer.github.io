Creative Ways to Display and Store Your Crafts


Crafting is a beloved hobby that allows individuals to tap into their creativity, express themselves, and produce unique and beautiful pieces of art. However, when it comes to storing and displaying our crafts, it can sometimes be challenging to find the perfect solution that showcases our hard work while keeping everything organized and easily accessible. In this article, we will explore creative ways to display and store your crafts that not only ensure they are well-preserved but also add a touch of charm to your living space.

1. Wall-Mounted Pegboards: Pegboards are a versatile and practical way to display your crafts. With their grid-like pattern and a variety of hooks and shelves, pegboards can be customized to fit your specific needs. Attach hooks to hang small tools or thread spools, and use shelves to display finished projects or small jars of craft supplies. Not only do pegboards keep your crafts organized, but they also act as a visually appealing focal point in any room.

2. Mason Jars: Mason jars have become a popular trend in home decor, and they are also a great storage option for crafts. They are ideal for storing small items like buttons, beads, or colored pencils. To add a touch of creativity, consider painting the lids with chalkboard paint and labeling the jars with the contents inside. Arrange them on a shelf or install a wooden plank on the wall with metal rings to hold the jars for an eclectic and functional display.

3. Vintage Suitcases: If you love the charm of vintage items, consider repurposing old suitcases as craft storage solutions. These suitcases can be stacked, creating additional storage space, and they add a touch of nostalgia to your craft room. Use dividers or small containers inside the suitcases to keep your crafts organized. To add a personal touch, you could also paint or stencil the suitcases to match your room’s decor.

4. Floating Shelves: Floating shelves are an excellent option for displaying completed crafts in a stylish and sophisticated way. They provide a clean, minimalist look while allowing your crafts to take center stage. Arrange your crafts in a visually appealing way, using different heights and groupings. This will not only showcase your projects but also allow for easy access and swapping out of items as you create new ones.

5. Hanging Baskets: Hanging baskets are a fantastic storage solution for crafts that come in various shapes and sizes. They can be hung on the wall or from the ceiling, making use of vertical space and keeping your crafts within arm’s reach. Choose baskets with different textures and colors to add an interesting visual element to your craft room. Fill them with fabric scraps, yarn, or any other craft supplies you need to have readily available.

6. Repurposed Furniture: Get creative with repurposed furniture pieces to store and display your crafts. Old bookshelves, drawers, or cabinets can be transformed into unique storage solutions. Paint them in vibrant colors or distress them for a shabby-chic look. Use the shelves to store bins or baskets filled with craft supplies, and drawers can hold smaller items. The added bonus is that repurposed furniture adds character and a sense of history to your craft room.

7. Decorative Hooks: Don’t underestimate the power of decorative hooks when it comes to displaying your crafts. Install hooks on walls, behind doors, or on the sides of furniture to hang finished projects, tools, or even spools of thread. Not only are decorative hooks functional, but they also enhance the overall aesthetic of your craft space.

8. Multi-Purpose Storage Carts: Another innovative way to store and display your crafts is by using multi-purpose storage carts. These carts often come with wheels, making them easily movable and allowing you to work on your crafts in different areas of your home. Load them up with your supplies and tools, and arrange your completed crafts on top. These carts are not only practical but also a stylish addition to any craft room.

9. Vintage Ladders: For a rustic and unconventional approach, consider using vintage ladders to display your crafts. Lean a ladder against the wall and hang finished projects or store supplies on the rungs. This unique display method adds personality and charm to your craft space and can also be easily rearranged or repurposed for other uses.

10. DIY Fabric-Wrapped Corkboards: Create your own fabric-wrapped corkboards to display your crafts and inspiration. Choose a fabric that complements your room’s decor or matches the theme of your crafts. Simply wrap the fabric tightly around a corkboard, secure it with thumbtacks or hot glue, and hang it on the wall. Use clothespins or mini clips to attach your crafts, notes, or inspirational images. This not only keeps your crafts organized but also acts as a decorative wall feature.

When it comes to displaying and storing your crafts, the possibilities are endless. Whether you prefer a tidy and minimalist look or a more eclectic and whimsical vibe, there are plenty of creative solutions to choose from. Experiment with different ideas, mix and match different storage options, and don’t be afraid to let your personal style shine through. By combining functionality and aesthetics, you can create a craft space that is both inspiring and efficient. So, get started on transforming your craft area into a haven that showcases your creativity and allows you to easily access all your cherished crafts.
