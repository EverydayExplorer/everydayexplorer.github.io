The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Animal Print: Tips for a Fierce Look


Animal print has long been a fashion statement, evoking both wild and sophisticated vibes. From leopard spots to zebra stripes, incorporating animal prints into your wardrobe can add an element of fierceness and flair to any outfit. However, mastering the art of wearing animal print requires a delicate balance to avoid veering into tacky territory. To help you navigate the dos and don’ts of donning these bold patterns, we’ve compiled a comprehensive guide filled with expert tips and tricks.

Do: Start Small

If you’re new to wearing animal print, dipping your toes into this trend with small accessories is a great way to ease into it. Consider adding a leopard print belt, scarf, or even a pair of shoes to your ensemble for a subtle yet stylish touch. These understated accents can effortlessly elevate even the simplest of outfits, making them perfect for beginners.

Don’t: Overdo It

While a pop of animal print can undoubtedly make a statement, it’s essential to exercise restraint to avoid overwhelming your look. Opt for one statement piece at a time, whether it’s a bold leopard print coat or a pair of eye-catching zebra print trousers. Remember, less is often more when it comes to incorporating animal print into your outfit.

Do: Mix and Match

Don’t be afraid to experiment with mixing different animal prints for a fashion-forward look. Pairing leopard print with snake print or zebra stripes with tiger stripes can create a visually striking ensemble that exudes confidence and style. Just be sure to balance out the prints with neutral colors and simple silhouettes to prevent your outfit from looking too busy.

Don’t: Forget About Proportions

When wearing animal print, it’s crucial to consider the scale of the print in relation to your body proportions. If you’re petite, opt for smaller, more delicate prints to avoid overwhelming your frame. Conversely, if you’re tall or curvy, larger prints can help balance out your silhouette and make a bolder statement.

Do: Embrace Neutral Pairings

Animal print is incredibly versatile and can be paired with a wide range of colors and textures. For a classic and sophisticated look, pair your animal print piece with neutral hues such as black, white, beige, or camel. These timeless color combinations allow the print to take center stage while ensuring a chic and polished aesthetic.

Don’t: Shy Away From Texture

Incorporating different textures into your animal print ensemble can add depth and visual interest to your look. Experiment with mixing fabrics like silk, suede, leather, or faux fur to create a luxurious and multi-dimensional outfit. Just be sure to balance out the textures for a cohesive and harmonious ensemble.

Do: Confidence is Key

Perhaps the most important rule when it comes to wearing animal print is to wear it with confidence. Whether you’re rocking a daring leopard print dress or a subtle snakeskin clutch, owning your look and carrying yourself with poise will ensure that you exude style and sophistication wherever you go. After all, confidence is the ultimate accessory.

In conclusion, mastering the art of wearing animal print is all about finding the right balance and embracing your unique sense of style. By following these dos and don’ts, you can confidently incorporate this bold and timeless trend into your wardrobe for a fierce and fashionable look that’s sure to turn heads. So go ahead, unleash your wild side and let your style roar!
