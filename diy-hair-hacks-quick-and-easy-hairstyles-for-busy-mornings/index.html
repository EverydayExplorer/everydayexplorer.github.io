DIY Hair Hacks: Quick and Easy Hairstyles for Busy Mornings


When it comes to getting ready in the morning, we can all use a few time-saving tricks, especially when it comes to styling our hair. Waking up early enough to create an intricate and flawless hairstyle is often a distant dream for most busy individuals. However, with a little creativity and some DIY hair hacks, you can achieve quick and easy hairstyles that will have you looking fabulous in no time. In this blog post, we will explore some handy hair hacks for those rushed mornings, so you can step out the door with confidence.

1. The Classic Ponytail with a Twist
The ponytail is a timeless hairstyle that is not only quick and easy to create, but also versatile. To add a twist to this classic look, separate a small section of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it. Secure the wrapped section with a bobby pin, and voila! You have a chic and put-together ponytail within minutes.

2. Messy Bun Magic
If you’re running late and don’t have time to wash your hair, a messy bun is a go-to style. Start by flipping your hair over and gathering it into a high ponytail. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail, creating a bun, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few face-framing strands for a relaxed and effortless look. If you want to add some volume, tease your hair before creating the bun.

3. Sleek Low Bun
For a more polished look, opt for a sleek low bun. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. To smooth down any flyaways or frizz, use a small amount of hairspray or a toothbrush sprayed with hairspray to tame those unruly strands. This hairstyle is perfect for those formal office settings or special events when you need a quick and elegant updo.

4. Braided Crown
If you have a bit more time and want to try something different, the braided crown is an excellent choice. This hairstyle gives the illusion of a delicate crown made out of braids. Part your hair down the middle and braid each section. Secure the braids with small elastic bands, then cross them over your head like a crown, securing them in place with bobby pins. This hairstyle is not only beautiful but also keeps your hair off your face, making it perfect for busy days or outdoor activities.

5. Quick Curls
For those who love curly hair but don’t have the time for a full style, quick curls are the way to go. Start with dry or slightly damp hair and divide it into two sections. Twist each section tightly and wrap it around itself, creating two buns. Use a hairdryer to blow hot air on the buns for a minute or two, then switch to cool air to set the curls. Undo the buns and gently finger-comb the curls for a natural and effortless look.

6. Half-Up Twist
The half-up twist is a trendy and easy hairstyle that adds a touch of elegance to your morning routine. Simply take a small section of hair from each side of your head, twist each section away from your face, and bring them together at the back of your head. Secure the twists with bobby pins and leave the rest of your hair down or add curls for extra glamour.

These DIY hair hacks are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to quick and easy hairstyles for busy mornings. With a little practice, you’ll be able to master these styles and adapt them to suit your own hair type and desired look. Remember, the key is to keep it simple, embrace imperfections, and most importantly, feel confident in your own skin. So, say goodbye to those rushed mornings and hello to fabulous hair all day long!
