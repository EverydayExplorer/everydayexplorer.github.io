The Quirkiest Hotels Around the World: Unforgettable Stays


When it comes to travel, the accommodation you choose can be just as exciting as the destination itself. If you’re tired of the standard hotel experience and are looking for something truly unique, then these quirky hotels around the world are sure to pique your interest. From treehouses to underwater rooms, these unconventional accommodations offer unforgettable stays that will leave a lasting impression on any traveler.

Icehotel - Jukkasjärvi, Sweden

Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, Sweden, the Icehotel is a truly one-of-a-kind experience. As the name suggests, this hotel is constructed entirely from ice and snow, offering guests the chance to sleep in sub-zero temperatures while surrounded by stunning ice sculptures. Each year, the hotel is rebuilt with a new design, ensuring that no two visits are ever the same. With activities such as ice sculpting and Northern Lights tours, the Icehotel provides an otherworldly experience for adventurous travelers.

Giraffe Manor - Nairobi, Kenya

For animal lovers, Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, offers a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to interact with these graceful creatures. Set within 12 acres of private land, the hotel is home to a herd of endangered Rothschild’s giraffes, who are known to poke their heads through the windows during meal times, creating a truly unique dining experience. The colonial-style manor and its resident giraffes provide a charming and whimsical atmosphere that is sure to delight guests of all ages.

Dog Bark Park Inn - Cottonwood, Idaho, USA

If you’ve ever wanted to spend the night inside a giant beagle, then the Dog Bark Park Inn is the place for you. Shaped like a massive beagle, this bed and breakfast offers cozy accommodations inside the world’s largest beagle-shaped building. Guests can relax in the dog-themed rooms and enjoy the surrounding prairie landscape, making it a must-visit destination for dog enthusiasts and those seeking a lighthearted and memorable stay.

Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort - Saariselkä, Finland

For a truly magical experience, the Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland offers the chance to stay in glass igloos under the Northern Lights. These unique igloos provide unobstructed views of the starry sky, allowing guests to witness the breathtaking natural light show from the comfort of their own private accommodation. In addition to the glass igloos, the resort also offers traditional log cabins and snow igloos, providing a range of options for an unforgettable Arctic adventure.

Costa Verde - Quepos, Costa Rica

For those with a sense of adventure, Costa Verde in Costa Rica offers the chance to stay in a refurbished vintage airplane. Perched on a 50-foot pedestal, the Boeing 727 fuselage provides a truly unique hotel room with stunning ocean and jungle views. The interior has been transformed into a spacious and luxurious suite, offering a blend of modern comforts and a sense of adventure, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a one-of-a-kind experience.

Magic Mountain Hotel - Huilo Huilo, Chile

Tucked away in the Chilean Patagonian rainforest, the Magic Mountain Hotel is a fairytale come to life. Shaped like a volcano and covered in lush greenery, this hotel exudes an enchanting and mystical atmosphere. Guests can stay in rooms adorned with natural wood and stone, surrounded by the sights and sounds of the pristine forest. The hotel’s cascading water feature adds to its ethereal charm, making it a truly magical retreat for nature lovers and those seeking a unique and immersive experience.

Jules’ Undersea Lodge - Key Largo, Florida, USA

For those who have ever dreamt of sleeping with the fishes, Jules’ Undersea Lodge in Key Largo, Florida, offers the opportunity to do just that. This underwater hotel, located 21 feet beneath the surface of the ocean, provides a truly immersive experience for adventurous travelers. Accessible only by scuba diving, guests can spend the night in a cozy and intimate setting, surrounded by the vibrant marine life of the Florida Keys. With the chance to dine underwater and explore the surrounding coral reef, Jules’ Undersea Lodge offers a one-of-a-kind stay that is sure to leave a lasting impression.

Unforgettable Experiences Await

These quirky hotels around the world offer much more than just a place to rest your head; they provide unforgettable experiences that will stay with you long after you’ve returned home. Whether you’re seeking adventure, romance, or a touch of whimsy, these unconventional accommodations are sure to add a unique and memorable twist to your next travel adventure. So, why not step outside the traditional hotel experience and embark on a journey to discover the world’s quirkiest and most unforgettable stays? After all, life’s too short for ordinary accommodations.
