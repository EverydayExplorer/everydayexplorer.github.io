The World's Most Instagrammable Hotels: Picture-Perfect Stays


Are you tired of scrolling through your Instagram feed, envying all those stunning hotel photos? Well, look no further because we have curated a list of the world’s most Instagrammable hotels that will make all your followers green with envy. These picture-perfect stays are not only aesthetically pleasing but also offer top-notch amenities and unforgettable experiences. Get ready to up your Instagram game with these stunning hotels!

1. The Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:
Situated in the heart of Singapore, The Marina Bay Sands is an architectural marvel that dominates the city’s skyline. Its iconic infinity pool, perched on the 57th floor, offers breathtaking views of the city and makes for the perfect Instagram shot. The hotel’s sleek design and luxurious interiors provide numerous photo opportunities, making it a must-visit destination for any Instagram enthusiast.

2. Amangiri, Utah, USA:
Nestled in the heart of the Utah desert, Amangiri is a haven for nature lovers and luxury seekers alike. This minimalist desert retreat boasts sleek lines and earthy tones that blend effortlessly with the stunning desert landscape. The hotel’s infinity pool, which seemingly disappears into the surrounding rocks, is an Instagrammer’s dream come true. Capture the magic of the desert and make your followers swoon with envy.

3. Giraffe Manor, Nairobi, Kenya:
If you’re a fan of wildlife and unique experiences, Giraffe Manor is the place for you. This boutique hotel allows guests to dine with friendly giraffes who often poke their heads through the windows for a quick snack. The manor’s ivy-covered façade and vintage interiors create a fairytale-like ambiance that is bound to impress your Instagram followers. Get ready to capture the unforgettable moment of giraffes photobombing your breakfast!

4. The Icehotel, Jukkasjärvi, Sweden:
For those who crave an out-of-this-world experience, The Icehotel in Sweden is the ultimate destination. This unique hotel is built entirely out of ice and snow, providing guests with a truly magical experience. From ice sculptures to ice beds, every corner of this hotel is a work of art. Imagine capturing the ethereal beauty of the Northern Lights dancing above your ice bed - a photo that will surely make your Instagram feed stand out.

5. The One&Only Reethi Rah, Maldives:
No list of Instagrammable hotels would be complete without mentioning the Maldives. The One&Only Reethi Rah is a paradise on earth, with its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious villas. The hotel’s over-water hammocks and infinity pools offer endless photo opportunities against the backdrop of the stunning Maldivian scenery. Prepare to transport your followers to a tropical paradise with every picture you post.

6. The Cavo Tagoo, Mykonos, Greece:
If you’re searching for a destination that combines luxury with Mediterranean charm, look no further than The Cavo Tagoo in Mykonos. This boutique hotel boasts sleek white architecture, minimalist design, and breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. The infinity pool, seemingly extending into the horizon, is the perfect spot to capture the essence of the Greek Islands. Get ready to make your followers dream of their next island getaway.

7. The Taj Lake Palace, Udaipur, India:
Located in the middle of Lake Pichola, the Taj Lake Palace is a floating palace that exudes elegance and grandeur. This architectural masterpiece is made entirely of white marble, creating a stunning contrast against the blue waters of the lake. Capture the romance of this fairytale-like palace and transport your followers to a world of royalty and opulence.

Each of these hotels offers a unique experience and a plethora of Instagrammable moments. Whether you’re a fan of breathtaking views, unique architecture, or luxurious interiors, these picture-perfect stays will help you curate an envy-inducing Instagram feed. So pack your bags, charge your camera, and get ready to capture the world’s most Instagrammable hotels. Happy travels and happy Instagramming!
