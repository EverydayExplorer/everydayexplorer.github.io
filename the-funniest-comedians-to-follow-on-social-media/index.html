The Funniest Comedians to Follow on Social Media


Are you tired of scrolling through the same old posts on social media? Looking to inject a bit of laughter into your daily feed? Look no further! In this blog post, we will introduce you to some of the funniest comedians to follow on social media. From witty one-liners to hilarious skits, these comedians are sure to brighten your day.

1. Amy Schumer - @amyschumer
Amy Schumer is a force to be reckoned with in the world of comedy. Known for her unapologetic and candid style, she brings her unique brand of humor to social media. She posts relatable and funny content that will have you laughing out loud. From sharing embarrassing anecdotes to satirical takes on pop culture, Amy always delivers the laughs.

2. Conan O’Brien - @conanobrien
Conan O’Brien, the late-night talk show host, is not just funny on television – he also brings his humor to social media. His Twitter feed is filled with hilarious quips and observations. Whether he’s making fun of current events or engaging in witty banter with his followers, Conan never fails to hit the mark with his comedy.

3. Kevin Hart - @kevinhart4real
When it comes to comedic timing, Kevin Hart knows how to deliver. The stand-up comedian and actor has a massive following on social media, and for good reason. His posts are a mix of funny videos, motivational quotes, and behind-the-scenes glimpses into his life. Kevin’s energetic and vibrant personality shines through in his content, making him a must-follow for comedy enthusiasts.

4. Sarah Cooper - @sarahcpr
Sarah Cooper rose to fame with her hilarious lip-syncing videos of Donald Trump’s speeches. Her ability to mimic Trump’s mannerisms and deliver them in a comedic way is nothing short of impressive. Sarah’s social media platforms are filled with more than just these videos – she also shares funny sketches, witty commentary, and insights into her creative process.

5. Trevor Noah - @trevornoah
As the host of ‘The Daily Show,’ Trevor Noah has established himself as a sharp and insightful comedian. On social media, Trevor showcases his wit and humor through a mix of politically charged jokes, funny anecdotes, and thought-provoking commentary. Following him on social media will not only provide you with a good laugh but also keep you informed about current events.

6. Mindy Kaling - @mindykaling
Known for her roles in ‘The Office’ and ‘The Mindy Project,’ Mindy Kaling is a powerhouse comedian, actress, and writer. On social media, Mindy shares funny and relatable anecdotes from her life, along with behind-the-scenes glimpses of her projects. Her witty and self-deprecating humor will have you laughing out loud and feeling like you’re chatting with a friend.

7. Ellen DeGeneres - @theellenshow
Ellen DeGeneres needs no introduction. As a talk show host and stand-up comedian, Ellen has an incredible talent for making people laugh. On her social media platforms, she shares hilarious videos, heartwarming stories, and uplifting messages. Following Ellen will not only brighten your day but also remind you of the power of laughter.

8. Patton Oswalt - @pattonoswalt
Patton Oswalt is a comedian known for his intelligent and insightful humor. On social media, he shares funny observations, witty commentary, and even engages in thought-provoking discussions with his followers. Patton’s unique perspective and clever writing make him a must-follow for anyone looking for a good laugh that goes beyond surface-level comedy.

9. Hannah Gadsby - @hannah_gadsby
Hannah Gadsby is an Australian comedian who gained international recognition with her groundbreaking Netflix special, ‘Nanette.’ On social media, she shares funny sketches, musings on life, and thought-provoking insights. Hannah’s humor is both clever and introspective, examining themes of identity, gender, and society. Following her on social media will not only make you laugh but also challenge your perspective.

10. Ricky Gervais - @rickygervais
Known for his no-holds-barred comedy and biting sarcasm, Ricky Gervais is a comedian who always delivers a good laugh. On social media, Ricky shares funny memes, witty tweets, and behind-the-scenes glimpses of his projects. His acerbic wit and unfiltered humor make him a must-follow for fans of comedy.

So there you have it – a list of the funniest comedians to follow on social media. From Amy Schumer’s unapologetic humor to Ricky Gervais’ biting sarcasm, these comedians will bring laughter to your daily scroll. So go ahead and hit that follow button – your feed will thank you.
