The Funniest Cat Memes That Will Make You LOL


Who doesn’t love a good cat meme? These furry little creatures have taken over the internet with their hilarious antics and adorable expressions. If you’re in need of a good laugh, look no further. We’ve rounded up some of the funniest cat memes that are guaranteed to make you LOL.

1. The “I Can Has Cheezburger?” Cat

Remember the iconic “I Can Has Cheezburger?” cat meme? This classic meme features a cute kitty with a funny caption, often misspelling words for comedic effect. Whether it’s asking for a cheeseburger or expressing confusion, this meme never fails to bring a smile to your face.

2. The Cat That Hates Mondays

We all know the feeling of dreading Monday mornings, and apparently, cats do too! This meme showcases a grumpy cat with a disdainful expression, accompanied by captions like “I hate Mondays” or “When you realize it’s not the weekend anymore.” It’s the perfect meme to commiserate with on those dreaded workdays.

3. The “Nope” Cat

Have you ever seen a cat react so dramatically to something they don’t like? The “Nope” cat meme captures that moment perfectly. This meme usually features a startled or unimpressed feline with a caption that simply says “Nope.” It’s the perfect expression for those times when you just want to say “no” to everything.

4. The Cat That Can’t Fit in a Box

Cats and boxes have a special relationship, and this meme takes it to a whole new level. It showcases a cat attempting to fit into a box that is clearly too small for them. The contorted expressions and awkward poses make for a hilarious visual. It’s a reminder that cats can be both graceful and clumsy at the same time.

5. The Cat That Photobombs

If you’ve ever tried to take a picture of your cat, you know how challenging it can be to get the perfect shot. That’s where the photobombing cat meme comes in. This meme features a cat suddenly appearing in the background of a photo, often with a mischievous expression. It’s a reminder that cats always find a way to steal the spotlight.

6. The Cat That Makes Silly Faces

Cats are masters of making funny faces, and this meme captures their silly expressions perfectly. From tongue sticking out to crossed eyes, these cats know how to make us laugh with their adorable antics. It’s a reminder that even the most serious-looking cats have a playful side.

7. The Cat That Acts Like a Dog

Who says cats can’t act like dogs? This meme showcases cats doing dog-like things, like fetching, begging for treats, or playing fetch. It’s a hilarious contradiction that reminds us that cats are full of surprises.

8. The Cat That Sits Like a Human

Cats often surprise us with their unique sitting positions, and this meme celebrates their human-like behavior. Whether they’re perched on a chair or lounging on the couch with their legs crossed, these cats prove that they have their own sense of style and comfort.

9. The Cat That Gives the Death Stare

We’ve all experienced the infamous cat death stare, and this meme captures it perfectly. Whether they’re staring at a closed door, a bird outside the window, or just giving you a judgmental look, these cats have mastered the art of intimidation with their intense gazes.

10. The Cat That Loves Boxes More Than Anything

Last but not least, we have the cat that loves boxes more than anything else in the world. This meme showcases cats squeezing themselves into boxes of all shapes and sizes, defying the laws of physics. It’s a reminder that cats will always find a way to make themselves comfortable, no matter how small the space.

So there you have it, the funniest cat memes that are sure to make you LOL. These furry friends never fail to bring a smile to our faces with their hilarious expressions and silly antics. Next time you’re in need of a good laugh, just remember to turn to these feline comedians. Happy laughing!
