Crafting with Concrete: DIY Coasters and Trivets


Concrete is a versatile material that is often associated with construction and infrastructure projects. However, did you know that you can also use concrete for crafting beautiful and functional items for your home? In this blog post, we will explore the world of crafting with concrete and guide you through the process of making DIY coasters and trivets that will add a unique touch to your living space.

Crafting with concrete has gained popularity in recent years, as people have discovered the beauty and durability of this material. The best part is that it’s relatively easy to work with and allows for endless creativity. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or a beginner looking for a new project, making DIY coasters and trivets with concrete is a great way to unleash your artistic side.

To get started, you’ll need a few basic materials:

1. Concrete mix: You can find concrete mix at your local hardware store or online. Opt for a high-quality mix that will ensure a smooth finish.

2. Molds: You can use a variety of molds to create different shapes and designs. Silicone molds are a popular choice as they are flexible and easy to remove once the concrete has cured. You can also experiment with using everyday objects like cookie cutters or cardboard boxes as molds.

3. Reinforcement: Adding reinforcement to your coasters and trivets will make them more durable. You can use materials like wire mesh or fiberglass mesh to strengthen the concrete.

4. Mixing tools: A bucket, a mixing spoon, and a measuring cup will come in handy for preparing the concrete mixture.

Now that you have your materials ready, let’s dive into the process of making DIY concrete coasters and trivets:

1. Prepare your mold: Grease the inside of your mold with cooking spray or a release agent to ensure easy removal of the concrete once it’s cured.

2. Mix the concrete: Follow the instructions on the packaging to mix the concrete. It’s important to achieve a thick but pourable consistency. Use your measuring cup to ensure accurate proportions of water and concrete mix.

3. Pour the concrete: Carefully pour the concrete mixture into your mold, filling it to the desired thickness. You can experiment with different colors by adding pigment to the mix or even create marbled effects by swirling different colored concrete together.

4. Add reinforcement: If you want your coasters and trivets to be extra sturdy, now is the time to add reinforcement. Place the wire mesh or fiberglass mesh on top of the poured concrete, ensuring that it is fully submerged.

5. Remove air bubbles: To ensure a smooth surface, gently tap the mold on a hard surface to remove any air bubbles trapped in the concrete.

6. Let it cure: Allow the concrete to cure for at least 24 hours, or as recommended by the manufacturer. Keep the mold in a cool and dry place during this time.

7. Demold your creations: Once the concrete has fully cured, carefully remove the mold. If you’re using a silicone mold, gently flex it to release the concrete. For other molds, you may need to cut or peel away the mold material.

8. Finishing touches: Sand the edges of your coasters and trivets to achieve a smooth finish. You can also seal the concrete with a concrete sealer to protect it from stains and moisture.

Now that you have a set of beautiful DIY concrete coasters and trivets, it’s time to showcase your craftsmanship. These unique pieces can be used to protect your furniture from hot or cold beverages, as well as serving as stylish decorative items. You can even personalize them by adding decorative elements like pressed flowers, beads, or even small trinkets embedded in the concrete.

Crafting with concrete opens up a whole new world of possibilities for DIY enthusiasts. Whether you’re a fan of minimalist designs or prefer bold and colorful creations, there’s no limit to what you can achieve with this versatile material. So grab your materials, let your imagination run wild, and start crafting with concrete today!
