Tips on how to mix and match patterns with your clothing and jewelry


Welcome to our fashion-forward blog, where we will be delving into the art of mixing and matching patterns with your clothing and jewelry. Whether you’re a seasoned fashionista or someone looking to add a little flair to their wardrobe, this blog post is the perfect resource for you. So, let’s dive in!

1. Start with a Solid Foundation:
When it comes to mixing patterns, it’s important to have a solid foundation. Begin by selecting a neutral base for your outfit to create balance. Think of neutral shades like black, white, or beige. Pairing a patterned top with solid bottoms, or vice versa, allows the patterns to take center stage without overwhelming the eye.

2. Understand the Power of Scale:
To create a harmonious pattern mix, pay attention to the scale of the patterns. Combine a large-scale pattern with a smaller one to avoid overwhelming your look. For example, if you’re wearing a bold, oversized floral print top, pair it with dainty polka dot earrings or a subtle striped accessory.

3. Complement Colors:
When mixing patterns, consider the colors in each pattern. Look for common hues and select accessories or clothing items that feature these shared colors. This will help tie the patterns together and create a cohesive look. For instance, if you’re wearing a blouse with a vibrant paisley pattern that includes shades of blue and green, pick jewelry with similar tones to complement your outfit.

4. Experiment with Similar Motifs:
Another way to achieve pattern mixing success is by combining similar motifs. If you’re sporting a striped blouse, consider adding accessories with a different type of stripe, such as vertical stripes on your pants or a diagonal striped bracelet. This method maintains a cohesive theme while adding interest to your overall look.

5. Incorporate Texture:
Texture is a fantastic tool when it comes to mixing patterns. Combining different textures can add depth and dimension to your outfit. For example, pair a lace top with a floral skirt or a chunky cable-knit sweater with a plaid scarf. The interplay of textures will create an intriguing visual effect that is sure to turn heads.

6. Embrace the Rule of Three:
When it comes to pattern mixing, the rule of three is a tried and true principle to guide your styling choices. Aim for a maximum of three patterns in your outfit to avoid overwhelming the eye. By sticking to this guideline, you’ll maintain a balanced and visually appealing look.

7. Confidence is Key:
Lastly, remember that confidence is the ultimate accessory. Experimenting with pattern mixing requires stepping out of your comfort zone and embracing your unique style. Don’t be afraid to take risks and trust your instincts. Fashion is all about self-expression, so wear your patterns with pride!

Now that you’re armed with these fabulous tips for mixing and matching patterns with your clothing and jewelry, it’s time to unleash your creativity. Remember to have fun, explore different combinations, and let your personal style shine through. Happy pattern mixing!
