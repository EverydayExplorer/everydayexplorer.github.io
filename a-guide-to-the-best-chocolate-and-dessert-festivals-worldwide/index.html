A Guide to the Best Chocolate and Dessert Festivals Worldwide


Have you ever been to a chocolate or dessert festival? If you haven’t, then you’re missing out on an amazing experience! Chocolate and dessert festivals are a celebration of all things sweet and delicious, bringing together some of the best chocolatiers and pastry chefs from around the world.

In this guide, we’ll take a look at some of the best chocolate and dessert festivals worldwide, where you can indulge in a variety of desserts, educational seminars and workshops, tasting sessions, and so much more.

1. Salon du Chocolat - Paris, France

The Salon du Chocolat has become one of the most internationally renowned chocolate trade shows, attracting more than 120,000 visitors every year. This French festival showcases some of the best chocolate creations from around the world, with exhibitors from over 60 countries. Here, you’ll find an endless variety of chocolate bars, truffles, cocoa-bean-based drinks, and other chocolate-based delicacies.

2. Chocolate Festival - London, United Kingdom

The Chocolate Festival, held annually in London, is a must-visit festival for chocolate lovers. This festival features a combination of familiar and new chocolate creations, with exhibitors ranging from artisan chocolatiers to multinational brands. Attendees can expect to participate in chocolate-making workshops, chocolate-themed events, and indulge in rare chocolate creations.

3. Salon du Chocolat - New York, United States

The Salon du Chocolat also has a footprint in the United States, with a festival held in New York City every year. This American edition of the festival features a wide variety of chocolate drinks and dishes, along with fashion shows and workshops.

4. Cioccolato in Piazza - Perugia, Italy

Cioccolato in Piazza is an annual chocolate festival held in the beautiful Italian city of Perugia. This festival features a variety of chocolate-based events, including chocolate-making workshops, bakery competitions, and more. Attendees can also indulge in an array of chocolate-inspired dishes and drinks from the local chefs and chocolatiers.

5. Chocolate Fest - Saint Lucia

Chocolate Fest is a week-long chocolate festival held in Saint Lucia. This Caribbean festival features an abundance of different chocolate events, including cocoa plantation tours, chocolate-centric cooking classes, and an all-out chocolate-inspired street party. Attendees can also sample some of the best chocolate and dessert creations from the island’s top chocolate chefs.

6. Salon de Chocolate - Lima, Peru

The Salon de Chocolate in Lima, Peru, is one of the most popular chocolate and dessert festivals in South America. This festival features a wide variety of chocolate creations, along with live music, performances, and entertainment. Expect a Mediterranean climate and a cool atmosphere that celebrates the art of chocolate-making.

7. Melbourne Chocolate Festival - Melbourne, Australia

The Melbourne Chocolate Festival, Australia’s largest chocolate festival, is held annually in July. This festival features a wide variety of chocolate-inspired activities, including chocolate-making workshops, tastings, demonstrations, and a range of competitions. Attendees can expect to indulge in some of the best chocolate creations from Australia’s top chocolatiers.

In conclusion, attending a chocolate or dessert festival can be a great way to experience all things sweet and satisfying while immersing yourself in an exciting environment. With events taking place all over the world, you’re sure to find a festival that caters to your taste and satisfies your sweet tooth. So why not plan your next vacation around attending one of these festivals and indulge in some of the best chocolate and dessert creations from around the world?
