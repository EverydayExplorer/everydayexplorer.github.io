The Best Drugstore Makeup Finds Under 0


In the realm of beauty, finding high-quality makeup that won’t break the bank can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. But fear not, savvy shoppers, for I’m here to unveil the best drugstore makeup finds under $20 that will have you looking fabulous without burning a hole in your wallet.

Let’s start with foundation—the cornerstone of any flawless makeup look. Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation is a standout choice, offering lightweight yet buildable coverage that blurs imperfections and controls shine, all for under $10. With its diverse range of shades, finding your perfect match is a breeze, ensuring a seamless blend for a natural, matte finish.

Next up, let’s talk about concealer. Say goodbye to dark circles and blemishes with the LA Girl Pro Conceal HD Concealer. Priced at just a few dollars, this cult favorite delivers impressive coverage without creasing or caking, thanks to its creamy formula and easy-to-use wand applicator. Plus, with its extensive shade range, you’re sure to find the ideal match for your skin tone.

No makeup look is complete without a touch of color on the cheeks, and for that, look no further than the Milani Baked Blush. Available in an array of stunning shades, this velvety-smooth blush adds a radiant flush to the complexion, imparting a healthy, luminous glow that lasts all day. And priced under $10, it’s a steal that punches well above its weight in terms of quality.

When it comes to eye makeup, the Wet n Wild Color Icon Eyeshadow Palette is a game-changer. Featuring a mix of mattes and shimmers in versatile, wearable hues, this budget-friendly palette allows you to create endless eye looks, from subtle daytime glam to sultry evening allure. With its buttery-smooth texture and long-lasting formula, it’s a must-have for any makeup enthusiast, priced at around $5.

No makeup look is complete without a swipe of lipstick, and the NYX Butter Gloss is a perennial favorite for good reason. Available in an extensive range of shades, from soft nudes to bold berries, this lightweight gloss delivers sheer, glossy color with a buttery-smooth texture that hydrates and nourishes lips. And priced at under $5, you can afford to stock up on multiple shades to suit every mood and occasion.

Last but not least, let’s talk about setting spray—the secret weapon for locking in your makeup look and ensuring it stays put all day long. The e.l.f. Cosmetics Mist & Set Setting Spray is a budget-friendly option that performs like a high-end counterpart, keeping your makeup fresh and flawless for hours on end. Priced at just a few dollars, it’s a small investment with big payoff in terms of longevity and wearability.

In conclusion, achieving a flawless makeup look doesn’t have to break the bank, thanks to these budget-friendly drugstore finds under $20. From foundation to lipstick, these affordable options deliver impressive quality and performance without compromising on price. So go ahead, indulge your inner beauty enthusiast and treat yourself to these must-have makeup treasures—you won’t be disappointed!
