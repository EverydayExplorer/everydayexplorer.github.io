Engineering Podcasts: Exploring the Latest Industry Trends


In the fast-paced world of engineering, staying updated on the latest industry trends and innovations is crucial for professional growth and success. One of the most convenient and informative ways to stay in the loop is by tuning in to engineering podcasts. These audio shows offer valuable insights, expert interviews, and in-depth discussions on a wide range of engineering topics, making them a go-to resource for engineers looking to expand their knowledge and expertise. In this blog post, we’ll explore the exciting world of engineering podcasts, highlighting some of the top shows that are shaping the industry and providing listeners with valuable information on the latest trends.

1. The Engineering Career Coach Podcast
Hosted by Anthony Fasano, PE, and Jeff Perry, this podcast is a must-listen for engineers at all stages of their careers. The show covers a variety of topics, including career development, leadership, project management, and personal branding. With expert interviews and actionable advice, The Engineering Career Coach Podcast equips listeners with the tools they need to excel in their professional lives and stay ahead of industry trends.

2. The Civil Engineering Podcast
For civil engineers seeking to stay informed about the latest developments in their field, The Civil Engineering Podcast, hosted by Anthony Fasano, PE, and Christian Knutson, PE, offers valuable insights and practical tips. The show covers a wide range of civil engineering topics, including infrastructure, construction, and project management, providing listeners with a comprehensive understanding of the industry’s current trends and challenges.

3. The Engineering Influence Podcast
Hosted by D.C. based math and science enthusiast, Chris Mitchell, The Engineering Influence Podcast explores the intersection of engineering, technology, and policy. With a focus on the societal impact of engineering and the evolving role of engineers in shaping public policy, the show provides a unique perspective on the industry’s latest trends and the opportunities and challenges they present.

4. The Structural Engineering Channel
Hosted by Anthony Fasano, PE, and Mathew Picardal, PEng, The Structural Engineering Channel is dedicated to providing structural engineers with the knowledge and resources they need to thrive in their careers. From seismic design to building information modeling, the show delves into the latest trends and innovations shaping the field of structural engineering, offering practical insights and expert perspectives.

5. The Manufacturing Report
For engineers involved in manufacturing and production, The Manufacturing Report, hosted by Chris Mitchell, offers a deep dive into the latest trends and developments in the manufacturing industry. The show covers topics such as advanced manufacturing technologies, supply chain management, and industrial automation, providing listeners with valuable insights to stay ahead in this dynamic sector.

In conclusion, engineering podcasts are a valuable resource for professionals looking to stay informed about the latest industry trends and developments. With their engaging content, expert interviews, and actionable insights, these shows offer a convenient and accessible way to expand one’s knowledge and expertise in the field of engineering. Whether you’re a civil engineer, structural engineer, or involved in manufacturing, there’s a podcast out there to keep you up to date and inspired. So, grab your headphones and tune in to the latest episodes – your engineering journey awaits!
