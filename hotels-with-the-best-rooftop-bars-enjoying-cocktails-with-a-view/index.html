Hotels with the Best Rooftop Bars: Enjoying Cocktails with a View


Picture this: you’re on top of the world, sipping a refreshing cocktail, and taking in breathtaking views of the city skyline. There’s something magical about rooftop bars at hotels that elevates the experience of enjoying a drink. From panoramic vistas to vibrant atmospheres, these rooftop bars offer a unique blend of luxury, relaxation, and a touch of glamour. So, if you’re a cocktail enthusiast and love to savor your drinks with a view, here’s a list of hotels with the best rooftop bars that will leave you in awe.

1. The Standard High Line, New York City, USA:
Perched above the iconic High Line park, The Standard Hotel boasts one of the most sought-after rooftop bars in New York City. With its trendy atmosphere and stunning views of the cityscape, it’s the perfect spot to unwind after a busy day exploring the Big Apple. Sip on a classic Manhattan cocktail while watching the sunset over the Hudson River – a truly unforgettable experience.

2. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:
Redefining the concept of rooftop bars, the Marina Bay Sands in Singapore offers a truly surreal experience. Located on the 57th floor, the rooftop bar offers breathtaking views of the city’s skyline, including the iconic Gardens by the Bay. Sip on a Singapore Sling or a refreshing tropical cocktail while taking in the mesmerizing sights – it’s an experience like no other.

3. The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong:
Situated on the 118th floor of the International Commerce Centre, the rooftop bar at The Ritz-Carlton in Hong Kong is the highest rooftop bar in the world. With its panoramic views of Victoria Harbour and the cityscape, this bar offers a truly luxurious experience. Treat yourself to a glass of champagne or a handcrafted cocktail while enjoying the enchanting vistas – it’s a touch of opulence that will leave you speechless.

4. The Ozone, Bangkok:
Located on the 63rd floor of the Lebua at State Tower, The Ozone offers breathtaking views of Bangkok’s skyline. With its modern design and vibrant atmosphere, it’s the perfect spot to indulge in creative cocktails and tantalizing bites. Sip on a signature Ozone Martini or a Thai-inspired cocktail while marveling at the city below – an experience that will leave you buzzing with excitement.

5. The Rock Bar, Bali, Indonesia:
Perched on the cliffs of Ayana Resort and Spa, The Rock Bar in Bali offers an unforgettable setting for enjoying cocktails with a view. As the waves crash against the rocks, you can sip on a tropical concoction and watch the sun dip below the horizon. With its dramatic location and awe-inspiring vistas, The Rock Bar is a must-visit for cocktail enthusiasts and sunset lovers alike.

6. The Roof at ME Milan Il Duca, Milan, Italy:
Located on the top floor of the ME Milan Il Duca hotel, The Roof offers panoramic views of Milan’s skyline and iconic landmarks. This stylish rooftop bar combines contemporary design with a relaxed atmosphere, making it the perfect place to unwind after a day of exploring the fashion capital of Italy. Sip on a Negroni or a refreshing Aperol Spritz while taking in the stunning views – a true taste of la dolce vita.

7. The Press Lounge, New York City, USA:
Situated atop the Ink48 hotel, The Press Lounge offers sweeping views of the Manhattan skyline, including the Hudson River and the iconic Empire State Building. With its chic and sophisticated ambiance, this rooftop bar is a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Savor a craft cocktail or a fine wine while enjoying the glittering lights of the city – a quintessential New York experience.

So, whether you’re a globetrotter looking for the best rooftop bars or a local in search of a new favorite spot, these hotels offer unforgettable experiences. From New York City to Bali, each rooftop bar brings its own unique charm and mesmerizing views. So, raise your glass and toast to the world – there’s no better way to enjoy cocktails than with a view that takes your breath away. Cheers!
