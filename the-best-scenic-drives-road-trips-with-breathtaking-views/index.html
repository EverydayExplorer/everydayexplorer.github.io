The Best Scenic Drives: Road Trips with Breathtaking Views


Are you tired of the everyday hustle and bustle? Do you crave a sense of freedom and adventure? If so, then a scenic road trip with breathtaking views might just be what you need. There’s something magical about hitting the open road, with the wind in your hair and the promise of new horizons on the horizon. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, a photography lover, or simply seeking a break from the monotony of daily life, a road trip with stunning views is a surefire way to rejuvenate your senses.

But with so many options to choose from, where should you start? Fear not, because we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll take you on a journey to explore some of the best scenic drives around the world. So grab your map, pack your bags, and let’s dive into the adventure of a lifetime!

1. The Pacific Coast Highway, USA:
Embark on a breathtaking journey along the Pacific Coast Highway, a stretch of road that hugs the edge of the Pacific Ocean in California. This iconic drive offers stunning vistas of rugged cliffs, golden beaches, and crashing waves. From the dramatic landscapes of Big Sur to the vibrant cityscape of San Francisco, the Pacific Coast Highway is a road trip that will leave you in awe at every turn.

2. The Great Ocean Road, Australia:
For nature enthusiasts and beach lovers, the Great Ocean Road in Victoria, Australia, is a must-visit. It is famous for its towering limestone cliffs, pristine beaches, and iconic rock formations like the Twelve Apostles. As you drive along this scenic route, you’ll be treated to panoramic views of the Southern Ocean and lush rainforests. Don’t forget to keep an eye out for local wildlife, including koalas and kangaroos!

3. The Ring Road, Iceland:
If you’re seeking a road trip like no other, then the Ring Road in Iceland should be at the top of your list. This 828-mile long circular route takes you around the entire island, offering breathtaking views of glaciers, volcanoes, waterfalls, and hot springs. From the otherworldly landscapes of the Golden Circle to the stunning black sand beaches of Vik, the Ring Road is a photographer’s paradise.

4. The Amalfi Coast, Italy:
For a taste of la dolce vita, hop in a car and drive along the picturesque Amalfi Coast in Italy. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is renowned for its cliffside roads, charming villages, and turquoise waters. As you wind your way along the coast, you’ll pass by lemon groves, colorful cliffside houses, and idyllic beaches. Make sure to stop in Positano and Ravello for some Instagram-worthy shots!

5. The Garden Route, South Africa:
For nature lovers, the Garden Route in South Africa offers a diverse array of landscapes and experiences. Stretching from Mossel Bay to Storms River, this scenic drive takes you through lush forests, towering mountains, and pristine beaches. Along the way, you can visit nature reserves, spot dolphins and whales, and even go on a safari. Don’t forget to sample some local wines and indulge in delicious seafood along the way!

6. The North Coast 500, Scotland:
If you’re looking for a road trip in Europe that rivals some of the best in the world, then the North Coast 500 in Scotland should be on your radar. This 500-mile long route takes you through the Scottish Highlands, offering breathtaking views of rugged mountains, pristine lochs, and historic castles. From the famous Loch Ness to the remote beaches of the North Coast, this drive is a true Scottish adventure.

7. The Garden Route, New Zealand:
Last but not least, the Garden Route in New Zealand is a road trip that will take your breath away. As you drive through the South Island, you’ll be treated to jaw-dropping views of snow-capped mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and lush rainforests. Make a pit stop at Milford Sound, a fjord known for its dramatic beauty, and hike the famous Hooker Valley Track for an experience you’ll never forget.

In conclusion, embarking on a scenic road trip with breathtaking views is an incredible way to explore the world and reconnect with nature. Whether you choose to drive along the Pacific Coast Highway in the USA, explore the Great Ocean Road in Australia, or venture into the wild landscapes of Iceland’s Ring Road, each of these drives promises unforgettable experiences and stunning vistas. So why wait? Start planning your next road trip adventure today and get ready to be blown away by the beauty of our planet.
