The Top 10 Craft Liquors You Must Try in 2021


Craft liquors have been growing in popularity in recent years, offering a unique and artisanal alternative to mass-produced spirits. With the abundance of craft liquor options available, it can be overwhelming to choose which ones to try. That’s why we’ve curated a list of the top 10 craft liquors that you must try in 2021. From whisky to rum to gin, this list covers a range of spirits that are sure to impress even the most discerning connoisseurs.

1. Dead Rabbit Irish Whiskey - Known for its smooth and rich flavor profile, Dead Rabbit Irish Whiskey is a must-try for whisky enthusiasts. Crafted with a blend of malt and grain whiskies, this spirit offers complexity and depth that will surely delight your taste buds.

2. Greenhook Ginsmiths American Dry Gin - If you’re a gin lover, Greenhook Ginsmiths American Dry Gin is a game-changer. Distilled in small batches using a copper pot still, this gin showcases a unique blend of botanicals that result in a clean and crisp taste. With notes of juniper, elderflower, and cinnamon, it’s the perfect base for classic cocktails like the martini or gin and tonic.

3. St. George Spirits Breaking & Entering Bourbon - Crafted by one of California’s most renowned distilleries, Breaking & Entering Bourbon is a true gem. This small-batch bourbon offers an exquisite balance of sweetness and spice, with flavors of caramel, vanilla, and a hint of clove. Whether you prefer it neat or in a cocktail, this bourbon is sure to delight.

4. Don Q Reserva 7 Rum - For rum enthusiasts, Don Q Reserva 7 Rum is a must-have in your collection. Made from a blend of rums aged for a minimum of seven years, this Puerto Rican rum boasts a velvety smooth texture and complex flavors of caramel, vanilla, and tropical fruits. Sip it on its own or use it to elevate your favorite rum-based cocktails.

5. Coppercraft Distillery Applejack - If you’re looking for a unique craft liquor to try, Coppercraft Distillery Applejack is a great option. Made from a blend of apple brandy and neutral spirits, this spirit offers a delightful balance of sweetness and tartness. With notes of ripe apples, caramel, and spice, it’s perfect for sipping on a cozy evening or incorporating into your favorite autumn cocktails.

6. Castle & Key Restoration Rye Whiskey - Castle & Key Restoration Rye Whiskey is a prime example of the craft distilling revolution. Made using locally sourced grains and traditional distillation techniques, this rye whiskey showcases a rich and spicy flavor profile. With hints of black pepper, cinnamon, and caramel, it’s a true delight for whiskey enthusiasts.

7. Far North Spirits Solveig Gin - Hailing from the northernmost distillery in the contiguous United States, Solveig Gin by Far North Spirits is a unique craft gin worth trying. This small-batch gin is made with a unique blend of traditional botanicals, including juniper, coriander, and citrus peel, as well as a touch of local wild botanicals. The result is a gin with a distinct character and a smooth, refined taste.

8. Freeland Spirits Geneva Gin - Geneva gin, a precursor to the modern gin we know today, is gaining popularity among craft spirit enthusiasts. Freeland Spirits Geneva Gin is an excellent representation of this traditional style. Made with a blend of malted barley, rye, and botanicals, this gin offers a warm and earthy flavor profile, with notes of juniper, nutmeg, and citrus.

9. Balcones Texas Single Malt Whisky - For whisky lovers looking to explore something different, Balcones Texas Single Malt Whisky is a must-try. Crafted by one of the pioneers of the American craft distilling movement, this single malt whisky is made from locally sourced malted barley and aged in a variety of cask types, resulting in a rich and complex flavor profile. With notes of dark chocolate, dried fruits, and smoky oak, it’s a whisky that demands attention.

10. Corsair Barrel-Aged Gin - Combining the best of both worlds, Corsair Barrel-Aged Gin offers a unique twist on traditional gin. This craft liquor is made by aging gin in barrels, imparting it with a rich and woody flavor profile. With notes of vanilla, spice, and a hint of citrus, it’s a gin that stands out from the crowd and is perfect for sipping on its own or using as the base for a classic cocktail.

In conclusion, the craft liquor movement has brought forth a plethora of exciting options for spirit enthusiasts. Whether you’re a whisky aficionado, gin lover, or rum connoisseur, there is a craft liquor out there waiting to be discovered. From unique flavor profiles to small-batch production, these top 10 craft liquors are the perfect way to elevate your drinking experience in 2021. So go ahead, explore the world of craft liquors and savor the craftsmanship and passion that goes into every bottle. Cheers!
