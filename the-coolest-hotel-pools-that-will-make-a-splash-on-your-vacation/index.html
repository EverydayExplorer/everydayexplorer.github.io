The Coolest Hotel Pools that Will Make a Splash on Your Vacation


When it comes to planning a vacation, finding the perfect hotel with a stunning pool can take your experience to the next level. Whether you’re looking to relax under the sun or make a splash with your loved ones, the coolest hotel pools can turn an ordinary vacation into an extraordinary one. In this blog post, we’ll dive into some of the most jaw-dropping hotel pools around the world that are sure to make a splash on your next getaway.

1. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:
Towering over the city’s skyline, Marina Bay Sands boasts the world’s largest rooftop infinity pool. With breathtaking views of the cityscape, this pool offers a truly unforgettable experience. Imagine taking a dip while enjoying Singapore’s iconic skyline, a sight that will leave you in awe.

2. The Joule, Dallas, Texas:
Located in the heart of downtown Dallas, The Joule features a one-of-a-kind rooftop pool. Suspended over the edge of the building, this stunning pool offers swimmers the illusion of swimming off into the horizon. With its modern design and panoramic views, it’s no wonder why The Joule is a favorite among travelers.

3. The Cambrian Hotel, Adelboden, Switzerland:
Nestled in the picturesque Swiss Alps, The Cambrian Hotel boasts an infinity pool that blends seamlessly with the surrounding mountains. Imagine soaking in warm water while surrounded by snow-capped peaks, creating a truly magical and tranquil experience.

4. The Standard, High Line, New York City:
Situated in the trendy Meatpacking District, The Standard’s rooftop pool offers unparalleled views of the Manhattan skyline. As you take a refreshing swim, you’ll be treated to breathtaking vistas of the city that never sleeps. This pool is a must-visit for those seeking a unique urban oasis.

5. The Hanging Gardens of Bali, Bali, Indonesia:
Tucked away in the lush jungles of Bali, The Hanging Gardens is famous for its iconic split-level infinity pool. With its cascading design, it appears as if the pool is suspended in the treetops, providing an enchanting experience. Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Bali as you swim in this breathtaking pool.

6. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, India:
Set against the backdrop of Lake Pichola, The Oberoi Udaivilas features a stunning pool that seems to merge seamlessly with the lake’s waters. Surrounded by intricate architecture and lush gardens, this pool offers a serene and luxurious experience, making it perfect for a romantic getaway.

7. The Library, Koh Samui, Thailand:
Known for its vibrant red color, The Library’s pool is a visual masterpiece. The striking contrast of the red tiles against the turquoise water creates a captivating sight. Relax in one of the pool’s comfortable lounge chairs and enjoy a refreshing cocktail while taking in the breathtaking views of the Gulf of Thailand.

8. The Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti, Tanzania:
Located in the heart of the Serengeti National Park, this hotel’s infinity pool overlooks the vast African plains. Imagine taking a dip while watching elephants, zebras, and other wildlife roam freely in their natural habitat. It’s a pool experience like no other, offering a unique way to connect with nature.

These are just a few of the coolest hotel pools that will make a splash on your vacation. No matter where you choose to stay, a remarkable pool can elevate your travel experience and create unforgettable memories. So, pack your swimsuit, grab your sunscreen, and get ready to dive into a world of luxury and relaxation.
