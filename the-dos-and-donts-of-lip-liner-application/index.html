The Do's and Don'ts of Lip Liner Application


Lip liner is a versatile makeup product that can enhance the shape and definition of your lips. When applied correctly, it can create a polished and professional look. However, many people struggle with lip liner application, making it a challenging step in their makeup routine. If you’re one of those individuals, fear not! In this blog post, we will discuss the do’s and don’ts of lip liner application to help you achieve flawless results.

Do: Choose the Right Shade
Selecting the correct shade of lip liner is crucial for a seamless application. Ideally, you want a shade that matches your natural lip color or the lipstick shade you plan to wear. This will ensure that your lip liner blends effortlessly with your lipstick, creating a cohesive look.

Don’t: Apply Too Much Pressure
When applying lip liner, it’s important to use a light hand. Applying too much pressure can lead to harsh lines and an unnatural appearance. Instead, gently trace the outline of your lips, following their natural shape. This will result in a more subtle and flattering look.

Do: Start From the Center
To achieve a balanced and symmetrical look, start applying your lip liner from the center of your upper lip. This will allow you to focus on defining your cupid’s bow, which is the curve at the center of your upper lip. Once you’ve outlined the cupid’s bow, move on to the outer corners and finish by connecting the lines along your lower lip.

Don’t: Overline Excessively
While it’s tempting to overline your lips for a fuller appearance, it’s important to do so in moderation. Overlining excessively can look unnatural and draw attention to the fact that you’re wearing lip liner. Instead, focus on enhancing your natural lip shape and only slightly extend the line if desired.

Do: Blend and Fill In
After outlining your lips, don’t forget to fill them in with your lip liner. This will create a base for your lipstick, making it last longer and appear more vibrant. Use gentle strokes to fill in your lips, paying attention to any areas that need extra definition.

Don’t: Forget to Sharpen Regularly
To ensure precise and accurate application, it’s essential to keep your lip liner sharp. A blunt tip can make it difficult to achieve clean lines and precise definition. Invest in a good quality lip liner sharpener and sharpen your lip liner before each use.

Do: Experiment with Lip Liner Styles
Lip liner doesn’t have to be limited to just outlining your lips. Get creative and experiment with different styles! You can try a slightly darker shade of lip liner in the corners of your lips to create a subtle ombré effect. Or, use a nude lip liner to contour and add dimension to your lips. The possibilities are endless!

In conclusion, mastering lip liner application can take some practice, but with the right techniques and a little bit of patience, you can achieve flawless results. Remember to choose the right shade, apply with a light hand, start from the center, blend and fill in, regularly sharpen your lip liner, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles. With these do’s and don’ts in mind, you’ll be well on your way to achieving a perfectly defined pout!
