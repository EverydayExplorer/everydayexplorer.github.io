Wearable Summer Makeup Trends to Try


Hello there, beauty enthusiasts! With the summer season upon us, it’s time to revamp our makeup routines and embrace the fresh and vibrant trends that are dominating the runway and the streets. From bold pops of color to dewy and glowing skin, this summer’s makeup trends are all about embracing your individuality and having fun with your look. In this blog post, we’ll be diving deep into the world of wearable summer makeup trends that you absolutely need to try. Get ready to slay the summer heat with confidence!

First up, let’s talk about the power of a vibrant lip color. This summer, bold and punchy shades are stealing the show. Think bright corals, fiery reds, and vivid pinks. These colors instantly add a pop of energy to any look and can be paired with a minimal eye makeup for a fresh and effortless vibe. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different textures too - opt for a matte finish for a bold statement or go for a glossy sheen for a more playful and fun look. Whatever you choose, a vibrant lip color is an absolute must-have this summer.

Next, let’s move on to the eyes, where shimmer and shine are taking center stage. Metallic and glitter eyeshadows are all the rage this season, giving your eyes a mesmerizing and ethereal look. Whether you prefer a subtle wash of shimmer or a full-on glitter extravaganza, there’s no denying that this trend will make your eyes sparkle like never before. Pair your shimmering lids with a strong winged eyeliner for a touch of drama, or keep it simple and let the eyes do all the talking. Either way, you’re guaranteed to turn heads with this dazzling trend.

Now, let’s not forget about the importance of a glowing and radiant complexion during the summer months. The ’no-makeup’ makeup look is always in style, but this summer, it’s all about embracing your natural beauty and letting your skin shine. Ditch the heavy foundation and opt for a lightweight and breathable tinted moisturizer or BB cream instead. These products will even out your skin tone while allowing your skin to breathe and glow. For an extra boost of radiance, highlight your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose with a luminous liquid highlighter. The result? A fresh-faced and sun-kissed look that’s perfect for the summer season.

Lastly, let’s talk about the importance of setting your makeup in place to withstand the summer heat. As temperatures rise, it’s crucial to invest in a good setting spray to keep your hard work intact. Look for a lightweight and long-lasting formula that will lock in your makeup and provide a refreshing burst of hydration. With the right setting spray, you can enjoy your summer activities without worrying about your makeup melting away.

So there you have it, a roundup of the hottest wearable summer makeup trends to try this season. From vibrant lip colors to shimmering eyes and a radiant complexion, these trends are all about embracing your individuality and having fun with your look. Remember, makeup is a form of self-expression, so don’t be afraid to get creative and step out of your comfort zone. Allow your inner beauty to shine through and let your makeup be an extension of your personality. Summer is all about making memories, and with these makeup trends, you’re sure to make a lasting impression. Get ready to slay the summer like the beauty queen you are!
