Tips for Booking Last Minute Hotels and Getting the Best Deals


Are you a spontaneous traveler who loves the thrill of booking last minute? Whether you have a sudden urge to explore a new city, or you’ve had a change of plans and need a place to stay at the eleventh hour, booking last minute hotels can be a great way to save money and embark on an exciting adventure. In this blog post, we will share with you some tips to help you score the best deals when booking last minute hotels.

### 1. Be flexible with your travel dates

One of the biggest advantages of booking last minute is that you have the flexibility to choose your travel dates. Being flexible with your travel dates can increase your chances of finding better deals. Try avoiding peak travel times as hotel rates tend to be higher during those periods. Instead, look for weekdays or less popular travel seasons when hotel occupancy is lower, which often leads to discounted rates.

### 2. Utilize hotel aggregators and booking apps

One of the easiest and most effective ways to find the best deals on last minute hotels is by using hotel aggregators and booking apps. These platforms give you the ability to compare prices across multiple hotels and provide you with real-time availability. Some popular hotel aggregators and booking apps include Booking.com, Expedia, Hotels.com, and Kayak. These platforms often offer exclusive deals and discounts for last minute bookings.

### 3. Sign up for hotel newsletters and loyalty programs

Another great way to gain access to exclusive deals is by signing up for hotel newsletters and loyalty programs. Hotels frequently send out special promotions and discounts to their subscribers, including last minute deals. Additionally, loyalty programs can offer perks such as free room upgrades or early check-in, making your last minute hotel booking experience even more enjoyable. So, take a few minutes to sign up for hotel newsletters and join loyalty programs to maximize your chances of finding great last minute hotel deals.

### 4. Consider alternative accommodations

When booking last minute, consider exploring alternative accommodations beyond traditional hotels. Platforms like Airbnb, Vrbo, and HomeAway offer a wide range of options such as private apartments, vacation rentals, and even unique properties like houseboats or treehouses. These alternatives often provide more flexibility in terms of pricing and availability. Additionally, you may find hidden gems in lesser-known neighborhoods that offer a more authentic experience of your destination.

### 5. Contact hotels directly

While online booking platforms are convenient, contacting hotels directly can sometimes yield better deals, especially for last minute bookings. Hotel staff might be more inclined to negotiate a lower price over the phone, especially if they have vacant rooms that they want to fill. Don’t hesitate to give them a call and ask if they have any special rates or discounts available for last minute bookings. You might be surprised by the deals they can offer.

### 6. Set price alerts

If you have a particular hotel or destination in mind, but are waiting for the perfect deal, consider setting price alerts. Some booking platforms allow you to set up alerts for specific hotels or destinations, notifying you when prices drop. This can be a great strategy for scoring last minute hotel deals, as you’ll receive notifications when prices become more affordable. Just be prepared to act quickly when you receive an alert, as these deals often go fast.

### 7. Be open to different locations

Flexibility is key when booking last minute, and this includes being open to different locations. If you can’t find a suitable hotel deal in your desired area, consider expanding your search to nearby neighborhoods or towns. You might discover a hidden gem just a short distance away that offers a better rate. Additionally, being open to different locations can add an element of adventure to your travels, allowing you to explore new areas you may not have considered before.

### 8. Check for flash sales and daily deals

Keep an eye out for flash sales and daily deals offered by hotels or booking platforms. These deals often have limited availability and short booking windows, but they can offer substantial discounts on last minute stays. Follow hotels and booking platforms on social media, subscribe to their newsletters, or join travel deal websites to stay updated on any flash sales or daily deals happening. This is a great way to snag a fantastic last minute hotel deal.

### 9. Book directly on hotel websites

Sometimes, the best deals can be found by booking directly on hotel websites. Hotels often offer exclusive rates or deals for their website visitors that may not be available on other platforms. Additionally, booking directly on hotel websites gives you the assurance of dealing with the hotel directly, allowing for better customer service and potentially easier cancellations or modifications. So, don’t forget to check hotel websites for any last minute offers that you can take advantage of.

### 10. Be prepared to make a decision quickly

When booking last minute, it’s important to be prepared to make a decision quickly. Last minute deals can come and go in a matter of minutes, so once you find a good deal, act on it promptly. Have your payment information ready and be prepared to finalize your booking. Remember, the early bird gets the worm, so don’t let that perfect last minute hotel deal slip through your fingers.

In conclusion, booking last minute hotels can be an exciting and budget-friendly way to satisfy your urge to travel. By being flexible with your travel dates, utilizing hotel aggregators and booking apps, signing up for hotel newsletters and loyalty programs, considering alternative accommodations, contacting hotels directly, setting price alerts, being open to different locations, checking for flash sales and daily deals, booking directly on hotel websites, and being prepared to make a decision quickly, you can increase your chances of finding the best deals on last minute hotels. So, go ahead and embrace your spontaneous side, pack your bags, and get ready for an unforgettable last minute travel adventure!

Remember that when booking last minute hotels, it’s crucial to do your own research and compare prices across different platforms to ensure you’re getting the best deal. Happy booking!

*Note: This blog post provides tips and suggestions for booking last minute hotels and finding the best deals. We encourage you to always follow the guidelines and recommendations of reputable health organizations, prioritize your safety, and stay informed about travel restrictions and guidelines in your destination before making any travel plans.
