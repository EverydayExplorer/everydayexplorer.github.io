10 Must-Have Hunting Apps for Every Outdoorsman


Before we dive into the world of hunting apps, it’s important to note that technology has revolutionized the way we approach outdoor activities. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or a beginner exploring the world of hunting, having the right tools at your fingertips can greatly enhance your experience and increase your chances of success. With that in mind, we’ve compiled a list of 10 must-have hunting apps for every outdoorsman.

1. HuntStand: This comprehensive hunting app offers a wealth of features to help you plan your hunts effectively. From property boundaries to topographic maps and weather forecasts, HuntStand has it all. You can also log your hunting activities and keep track of your harvests, making it an indispensable tool for passionate hunters.

2. ScoutLook Hunting: If you’re keen on scouting and need accurate weather information, ScoutLook Hunting is the app for you. It provides hyper-local weather conditions and forecasts based on your GPS location. Additionally, you can log your hunts, mark locations, and even analyze wind patterns for specific stands or hunting spots.

3. onX Hunt: This app is a game-changer for hunters looking for detailed land ownership boundaries and public access information. With onX Hunt, you can easily differentiate between private and public land, ensuring you stay within legal boundaries. The app also provides topographical maps, trail information, and hunting unit boundaries, making it an indispensable tool for any hunter.

4. Powderhook: Having trouble finding places to hunt? Powderhook connects hunters with local resources and hunting opportunities. From private landowners offering hunting rights to nearby hunting events, this app helps you discover new hunting spots and connects you with other hunters in your area. It’s a great tool for expanding your hunting network.

5. HuntWise: With real-time weather updates, solunar data, and interactive maps, HuntWise helps hunters plan their trips and increase their chances of success. The app also provides detailed hunting forecasts, game tracking capabilities, and a community platform that allows hunters to share tips, stories, and hunting information.

6. DeerCast: Developed by renowned hunter Mark Drury, DeerCast is a premium app specifically tailored for deer hunters. It provides deer hunting forecast predictions based on weather, moon phase, and lunar position. With DeerCast, you can plan your hunts based on the best times for deer movement, increasing your chances of spotting and harvesting a trophy buck.

7. Mossy Oak: Mossy Oak is a well-known name in the hunting community, and their hunting app is a great companion for any outdoorsman. From hunting weather conditions to rut predictions and even a blood trailing feature, Mossy Oak covers all the bases. The app also offers a live chat feature, allowing you to connect with other hunters and share valuable insights.

8. Hunt Predictor: This unique app analyzes a variety of data points to predict the best times for hunting different game species. By taking into account variables such as wind speed, temperature, and moon phase, Hunt Predictor offers insightful information to help you plan your hunts more effectively.

9. iHunt Journal: If you’re a data-driven hunter, iHunt Journal is the perfect app for you. With advanced logging capabilities, you can record your hunts, track your harvests, and analyze trends over time. The app also offers detailed weather data and sunrise/sunset times, ensuring you’re always prepared for your next hunt.

10. Hunt’n Buddy: Last but not least, Hunt’n Buddy is an all-in-one app that caters to the needs of hunters. It provides features such as weather forecasts, solunar tables, hunting logbooks, blood trailing, and even a flashlight function. With Hunt’n Buddy, you’ll have all the essential tools in one place, making your hunting experience seamless and enjoyable.

In conclusion, hunting apps have become an integral part of the modern-day outdoorsman’s toolkit. From navigating property boundaries to predicting game movement and tracking your hunting activities, these apps offer a wide range of features to enhance your hunting experience. So, if you’re an avid hunter looking to up your game, be sure to check out these 10 must-have hunting apps and take your outdoor adventures to the next level!
