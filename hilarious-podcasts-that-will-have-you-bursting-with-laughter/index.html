Hilarious Podcasts That Will Have You Bursting with Laughter


Are you in need of a good laugh? Look no further than these hilarious podcasts that are guaranteed to have you bursting with laughter! In this blog post, we will explore some of the funniest podcasts out there, sure to brighten up your day and lift your spirits. So, grab a cup of coffee, sit back, and get ready to dive into the world of comedic podcasting!

1. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’:
Known for its raw and unfiltered conversations, ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ is not only thought-provoking but also packed with hilarious moments. Joe Rogan, a stand-up comedian and popular UFC commentator, invites a wide range of guests, including comedians like Bill Burr, Tom Segura, and Theo Von. The witty banter and humorous anecdotes shared by these guests will keep you laughing from start to finish.

2. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend’:
Conan O’Brien, the iconic TV host, brings his quick wit and hilarious personality to the podcasting world. In each episode, Conan engages in candid and often hilarious conversations with celebrities and comedians, such as Paul Rudd, Will Ferrell, and John Mulaney. The unpredictable nature of these conversations makes for a truly entertaining experience that will leave you in stitches.

3. ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’:
Hosted by the incredibly talented Scott Aukerman, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ is a podcast that seamlessly blends improv, comedy sketches, and interviews. Each episode features a mix of celebrity guests and regular characters who engage in hilarious and often absurd conversations. With its unique format and offbeat humor, this podcast is a must-listen for comedy lovers.

4. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’:
If you’re looking for something truly outlandish and hilarious, ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this podcast explores the hilariously bad erotic novel written by Jamie’s own father. Each episode, the hosts read a chapter from the book and provide hilarious commentary, often struggling to contain their laughter. Be prepared for some truly cringe-worthy moments that are bound to leave you gasping for air.

5. ‘How Did This Get Made?’:
Ever watched a terrible movie and wondered how it even got made? Well, wonder no more! Hosted by Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, ‘How Did This Get Made?’ is a podcast dedicated to dissecting and mocking some of the most absurd movies ever made. The hosts, along with featured guests, analyze the nonsensical plotlines and baffling choices made by filmmakers, resulting in side-splitting laughter that will make even the worst movies enjoyable.

6. ‘The Dollop’:
‘The Dollop’ is a history podcast unlike any other. Hosted by Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, this podcast delves into bizarre and often hilarious stories from American history. From the forgotten tales of eccentric individuals to absurd historical events, the hosts bring these stories to life with their comedic commentary. If you have a love for history and enjoy a good laugh, this podcast is a perfect choice.

7. ‘My Favorite Murder’:
For true crime enthusiasts with a sense of humor, ‘My Favorite Murder’ is the podcast to listen to. Hosted by comedians Karen Kilgariff and Georgia Hardstark, this podcast combines true-crime storytelling with dark humor. Each episode, the hosts discuss infamous murder cases in a way that is equal parts informative and funny. This unique approach to true crime will keep you entertained while still respecting the seriousness of the subject matter.

8. ‘The Misfits Podcast’:
If you’re a fan of gaming and internet culture, ‘The Misfits Podcast’ is a must-listen. Hosted by a group of YouTubers and Twitch streamers, this podcast offers a combination of hilarious stories, controversial discussions, and the occasional prank. The hosts, including Fitz, SwaggerSouls, and Zuckles, are known for their irreverent humor and outrageous antics, making this podcast a laugh-out-loud experience from start to finish.

9. ‘The Hilarious World of Depression’:
While the title may seem contradictory, ‘The Hilarious World of Depression’ is a podcast that sheds light on mental health issues through humor. Hosted by comedian John Moe, this podcast features candid conversations with comedians, artists, and musicians who have experienced depression. The guests share their personal stories, using humor as a coping mechanism and highlighting the power of laughter in the face of adversity.

10. ‘No Such Thing As A Fish’:
From the minds behind the hit quiz show ‘QI,’ ‘No Such Thing As A Fish’ is a podcast that delves into fascinating and humorous facts. Each episode, the hosts share their favorite bizarre and obscure facts they’ve come across that week, turning them into hilarious anecdotes and jokes. With its well-researched content and witty banter, this podcast is a perfect combination of education and entertainment.

So there you have it, a curated list of some of the funniest podcasts that are sure to bring joy and laughter to your day. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up comedy, true crime, or bizarre facts, there’s a podcast on this list to suit your comedic tastes. So sit back, relax, and prepare to burst with laughter as you delve into the wonderful world of comedic podcasting! Happy listening!
