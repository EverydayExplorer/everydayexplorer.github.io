The Last Word: A Complex and Herbal Gin Cocktail


If you’re a fan of gin cocktails, there’s a high chance you’ve heard of The Last Word. This classic drink has been making waves in the cocktail world for years, and for good reason. It’s a complex and herbal concoction that is sure to delight your taste buds and impress your guests. In this blog post, we’ll dive into the history of The Last Word, its ingredients, and how to make this delightful drink at home.

The Last Word is believed to have originated during the Prohibition era in the United States. It was created at the Detroit Athletic Club, a private social club that operated during the 1920s. This cocktail was a favorite among the club’s members and quickly gained popularity beyond its walls.

What sets The Last Word apart from other gin cocktails is its unique combination of ingredients. It is made with equal parts gin, green Chartreuse, maraschino liqueur, and freshly squeezed lime juice. Each ingredient brings its own distinct flavor profile, resulting in a harmonious and well-balanced drink.

Let’s break down the ingredients:

1. Gin: The base of The Last Word, gin provides the drink with its signature botanical notes. Opt for a quality gin that complements the other flavors in the cocktail.

2. Green Chartreuse: This herbal liqueur adds a complex and aromatic element to The Last Word. Made from a secret blend of 130 different herbs, Green Chartreuse brings a unique and vibrant flavor to the cocktail.

3. Maraschino Liqueur: Made from the distillation of Marasca cherries, maraschino liqueur adds a subtle sweetness and a hint of nuttiness to The Last Word. It balances out the herbal notes and adds depth to the overall flavor profile.

4. Lime Juice: Freshly squeezed lime juice provides the necessary acidity to brighten up the cocktail. It adds a refreshing and citrusy kick that complements the other ingredients perfectly.

Now that we’ve covered the ingredients, let’s move on to the preparation:

1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.

2. Add equal parts gin, green Chartreuse, maraschino liqueur, and freshly squeezed lime juice to the shaker.

3. Shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds to ensure all the ingredients are well mixed and chilled.

4. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.

5. Garnish with a lime twist or a maraschino cherry, if desired.

The Last Word is a versatile cocktail that can be enjoyed year-round. Its complex flavors lend themselves well to various occasions, whether it’s a cozy night in or a festive gathering with friends. Experiment with different gin brands and adjust the ratios of the ingredients to suit your personal taste.

In conclusion, The Last Word is a cocktail that deserves a spot in every gin lover’s repertoire. Its complex and herbal flavors make it a standout among other gin cocktails. Whether you’re a seasoned mixologist or a novice bartender, The Last Word is sure to impress with its unique and delightful taste. So, gather your ingredients, shake up a glass, and savor the magic of The Last Word. Cheers!
