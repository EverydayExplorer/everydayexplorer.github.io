10 Exciting Game Releases of 2021 to Look Forward To


2021 is shaping up to be an incredible year for gaming enthusiasts. With the launch of next-gen consoles and a slew of highly anticipated releases, gamers have exciting times ahead. Whether you’re a fan of action-packed shooters, immersive RPGs, or gripping narratives, this year’s lineup has something for everyone. So, get ready to dive into a world of excitement and adventure with these ten highly anticipated game releases of 2021.

1. ‘Resident Evil Village’:
For horror enthusiasts, Capcom’s ‘Resident Evil Village’ is an absolute must-play. As the eighth installment in the iconic franchise, this game promises an immersive and terrifying experience that will keep players on the edge of their seats.

2. ‘Elden Ring’:
From the masterminds behind the ‘Dark Souls’ series and George R. R. Martin, ‘Elden Ring’ combines Martin’s storytelling prowess with FromSoftware’s renowned gameplay mechanics. This action role-playing game is expected to offer an expansive open world and a dark fantasy narrative that will captivate players.

3. ‘Hogwarts Legacy’:
Potterheads worldwide are eagerly awaiting the release of ‘Hogwarts Legacy.’ Set in the Wizarding World, this action RPG allows players to create their own characters and experience the magic of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as they navigate through a captivating storyline.

4. ‘Horizon Forbidden West’:
Sequel to the widely-loved ‘Horizon Zero Dawn,’ ‘Horizon Forbidden West’ takes players on another thrilling adventure as Aloy, a skilled hunter, in a post-apocalyptic world. Expect stunning visuals, a captivating storyline, and innovative gameplay mechanics that will keep you hooked.

5. ‘Far Cry 6’:
Ubisoft’s highly popular ‘Far Cry’ franchise returns with ‘Far Cry 6.’ Set in the fictional country of Yara, players will find themselves in the middle of a revolution against a ruthless dictator. With a vast open world and intense action, this first-person shooter is set to be a fan-favorite.

6. ‘God of War: Ragnarok’:
Fans of the award-winning ‘God of War’ series were ecstatic when Sony announced the release of ‘God of War: Ragnarok.’ This highly anticipated sequel continues the journey of Kratos and his son Atreus, diving deeper into Norse mythology and delivering epic battles and a rich storyline.

7. ‘Final Fantasy XVI’:
The beloved ‘Final Fantasy’ series returns with ‘Final Fantasy XVI.’ This upcoming installment promises a fresh take on the franchise, with dark and mature themes, breathtaking graphics, and a dynamic combat system. Prepare for an epic RPG adventure like no other.

8. ‘Halo Infinite’:
The iconic ‘Halo’ franchise returns with ‘Halo Infinite.’ Powered by the new Slipspace Engine, this first-person shooter aims to deliver an expansive universe and a gripping storyline as players once again step into the armor of Master Chief, battling against the forces of the Banished.

9. ‘Deathloop’:
From the talented developers at Arkane Studios comes ‘Deathloop.’ Set in a time loop on the enigmatic Blackreef island, players assume the role of Colt as he tries to break free from the loop by assassinating targets amidst a stylish ’60s-inspired aesthetic.

10. ‘Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart’:
PlayStation fans will be delighted with the release of ‘Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart.’ Developed by Insomniac Games, this action-packed platformer showcases the power of the PlayStation 5, with stunning visuals and seamless gameplay across different dimensions.

These ten game releases provide a mere glimpse into the excitement the gaming industry has in store for us in 2021. With a diverse range of genres and experiences, gamers worldwide can look forward to immersing themselves in these thrilling adventures. From heart-pounding horror to epic RPGs, the choices are endless. So, buckle up, grab your controllers, and get ready for an unforgettable gaming journey!
