Hotels with Iconic Rooftop Bars: Sipping in Style


There’s something undeniably magical about sipping a refreshing cocktail while enjoying breathtaking views from a rooftop bar. Whether it’s gazing at a city skyline or admiring a stunning sunset over the ocean, rooftop bars offer a truly luxurious and unforgettable experience. In this blog post, we’ll be exploring the world of hotels with iconic rooftop bars, where you can indulge in the best of both worlds - sipping in style and soaking up the scenery.

The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong  
Standing tall on the 118th floor of the International Commerce Centre, The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong boasts the highest rooftop bar in the world. Ozone offers an unparalleled, panoramic view of the city's stunning skyline. This sky-high bar is the perfect place to enjoy an expertly crafted cocktail while taking in the breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour and the city's iconic landmarks.

Marina Bay Sands, Singapore  
Perched 57 stories above the ground, the Marina Bay Sands SkyPark in Singapore is home to the legendary Sands SkyPark Infinity Pool, as well as several rooftop bars. From Lavo to Ce La Vi, these rooftop establishments offer not only delightful concoctions but also mesmerizing views of the cityscape. Whether you're gazing at the stunning architecture or watching the enchanting light show, you're guaranteed an unforgettable experience.

The Peninsula, Paris  
Located just a stone's throw away from the iconic Arc de Triomphe, The Peninsula Paris offers guests the opportunity to sip cocktails while enjoying impeccable views of the Eiffel Tower. L'Oiseau Blanc, the hotel's rooftop bar and restaurant, not only serves delightful French cuisine but also provides a stunning vantage point to admire the City of Lights.

The Standard, Downtown LA  
Overlooking the vibrant city of Los Angeles, The Standard Rooftop Bar provides guests with a trendy and chic atmosphere. With its iconic blue Astroturf deck, cozy waterbed pods, and stunning views of the city skyline, this rooftop bar is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike. Enjoy a refreshing cocktail while soaking up the California sun or take in the breathtaking views of the city lights at night.

The Ned, London  
Housed in a former bank in the heart of London, The Ned offers a rooftop bar experience like no other. Located on the roof of the building, the Ned's rooftop bar boasts a stunning heated pool, plush loungers, and an array of delectable cocktails. With views of St. Paul's Cathedral, the London skyline, and the iconic Gherkin building, this rooftop bar provides an unforgettable setting for sipping in style.

The Rock Bar, Bali  
Nestled amidst the dramatic cliffs of Ayana Resort and Spa in Bali, The Rock Bar is a true gem. This open-air bar offers panoramic views of the Indian Ocean, making it the perfect spot to enjoy a cocktail as the sun sets. With its unique location and stunning backdrop, The Rock Bar has gained international acclaim and continues to be a must-visit destination for those seeking a one-of-a-kind rooftop bar experience.

The Nomad Hotel, New York City  
Located in the historic Flatiron District of Manhattan, The Nomad Hotel houses a stunning rooftop bar aptly named The Rooftop. With its lush greenery, cozy seating, and breathtaking views of the New York City skyline, this hidden oasis provides a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city below. Enjoy a craft cocktail or a glass of bubbly while taking in the iconic sights of the Empire State Building and the surrounding cityscape.

The Fullerton Bay Hotel, Singapore  
Situated on the waterfront of Marina Bay, The Fullerton Bay Hotel offers guests the chance to unwind at Lantern, its rooftop bar and pool. Surrounded by stunning panoramic views of the city skyline and Marina Bay Sands, Lantern provides a stylish and sophisticated setting for sipping cocktails. Whether you're lounging by the infinity pool or enjoying a drink at the bar, this rooftop destination is a must-visit for any cocktail enthusiast.

The Berkeley, London  
The Berkeley, located in the upscale neighborhood of Knightsbridge, is home to the stunning Berkeley Rooftop. This rooftop bar offers guests the chance to enjoy a cocktail while overlooking Hyde Park and the surrounding cityscape. With its elegant and luxurious ambiance, the Berkeley Rooftop provides an unforgettable setting for a night of sipping in style.

The Four Seasons, Istanbul  
Located on the rooftop of the Four Seasons Hotel Istanbul at the Bosphorus, the Aqua Lounge offers breathtaking views of the iconic Istanbul skyline. With its cascading pools, comfortable seating, and vibrant atmosphere, this rooftop bar offers a unique and memorable experience. Sip on a refreshing cocktail while taking in the panoramic views of the Bosphorus Strait and the historic city below.

In conclusion, hotels with iconic rooftop bars provide the perfect opportunity to sip in style while enjoying stunning views of some of the most breathtaking cityscapes and natural landscapes in the world. From Hong Kong to London, these destinations offer an unparalleled experience that combines the luxury of a hotel with the beauty of a rooftop bar. So, whether you’re seeking a romantic evening, a vibrant night out, or simply a relaxing escape, make sure to add these hotels to your bucket list and indulge in a memorable experience of sipping in style.
