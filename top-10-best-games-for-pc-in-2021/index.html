Top 10 Best Games for PC in 2021


Are you ready to dive into the exciting world of PC gaming? Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or just getting started, the world of PC gaming offers an extensive array of immersive and thrilling experiences. With a vast library of games available, it can be challenging to decide which ones are worth your time and money. To help you navigate this exciting landscape, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 best games for PC in 2021. From action-packed adventures to thought-provoking narratives, there’s something for every type of gamer on this list.

1. Cyberpunk 2077
Step into the futuristic open world of Night City in Cyberpunk 2077. Developed by CD Projekt Red, this action RPG offers a gripping storyline, stunning visuals, and deep character customization. With its engaging narrative and immersive gameplay, Cyberpunk 2077 is a must-play for fans of the genre.

2. Red Dead Redemption 2
Immerse yourself in the breathtaking open world of the Wild West with Red Dead Redemption 2. Developed by Rockstar Games, this epic tale of outlaws and gunslingers offers a richly detailed world, compelling characters, and thrilling gameplay. Whether you’re exploring the vast landscapes or engaging in intense shootouts, Red Dead Redemption 2 delivers an unforgettable gaming experience.

3. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
Embark on a captivating adventure in the fantasy realm of The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. Developed by CD Projekt Red, this critically acclaimed RPG features a richly detailed world, complex characters, and morally ambiguous choices. With its engaging storytelling and immersive gameplay, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a must-play for fans of fantasy RPGs.

4. Doom Eternal
Get ready for non-stop action and intense combat in Doom Eternal. Developed by id Software, this adrenaline-fueled first-person shooter delivers fast-paced gameplay, stunning visuals, and a metal soundtrack that will keep you on the edge of your seat. If you’re a fan of high-octane, demon-slaying action, Doom Eternal is the game for you.

5. Microsoft Flight Simulator
Experience the thrill of flying with Microsoft Flight Simulator. Developed by Asobo Studio, this stunningly realistic flight simulation game offers breathtaking visuals, accurate real-world landscapes, and a vast array of aircraft to pilot. Whether you’re a seasoned aviator or a casual gamer, Microsoft Flight Simulator offers an unparalleled flying experience.

6. Hades
Descend into the underworld and battle your way to freedom in Hades. Developed by Supergiant Games, this action-packed rogue-like game features fast-paced combat, engaging storytelling, and stunning visuals. With its addictive gameplay and compelling narrative, Hades is a standout title in the indie gaming scene.

7. Half-Life: Alyx
Immerse yourself in the world of virtual reality with Half-Life: Alyx. Developed by Valve, this groundbreaking VR experience offers stunning visuals, immersive gameplay, and a compelling narrative set in the iconic Half-Life universe. Whether you’re a fan of the series or new to VR gaming, Half-Life: Alyx sets a new standard for immersive storytelling in gaming.

8. Assassin’s Creed Valhalla
Embark on a Viking saga in Assassin’s Creed Valhalla. Developed by Ubisoft, this epic action RPG offers a vast open world to explore, intense combat, and a captivating story set in medieval England. With its stunning visuals and engaging gameplay, Assassin’s Creed Valhalla is a worthy addition to the acclaimed series.

9. Death Stranding
Embark on a journey across a post-apocalyptic landscape in Death Stranding. Developed by Kojima Productions, this thought-provoking game offers a unique blend of exploration, survival, and social interaction. With its hauntingly beautiful world and innovative gameplay mechanics, Death Stranding delivers a truly unique gaming experience.

10. Ghost of Tsushima
Step into the shoes of a samurai in Ghost of Tsushima. Developed by Sucker Punch Productions, this action-adventure game offers a visually stunning open world, intense sword combat, and a compelling story set in feudal Japan. With its breathtaking visuals and engaging gameplay, Ghost of Tsushima is a standout title for fans of open-world adventures.

These top 10 games for PC in 2021 offer a diverse range of experiences, from epic adventures to intense action and thought-provoking narratives. Whether you’re a fan of RPGs, shooters, or open-world exploration, there’s something for everyone in this list. So, grab your gaming rig, buckle up, and get ready to embark on unforgettable gaming journeys with these top-rated PC games.
