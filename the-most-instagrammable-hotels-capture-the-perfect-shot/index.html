The Most Instagrammable Hotels: Capture the Perfect Shot


In today’s digital age, social media has become an integral part of our lives. Platforms like Instagram have revolutionized the way we share our experiences with others. From mouthwatering food to breathtaking landscapes, we’re constantly on the lookout for aesthetically appealing content that will garner likes and followers. One trend that has taken Instagram by storm is capturing the perfect shot at some of the most Instagrammable hotels around the world. These hotels offer stunning backdrops, unique designs, and picture-perfect settings that are sure to elevate your Instagram feed.

1. The Icehotel, Sweden:
Nestled in the quaint village of Jukkasjärvi, Sweden, The Icehotel is a true winter wonderland. Every year, this hotel is completely reconstructed using ice and snow, offering a magical experience for its guests. The intricate ice sculptures, frozen furnishings, and ethereal lighting create a dreamy atmosphere that is simply begging to be captured and shared with a global audience.

2. The Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
If you’ve ever dreamt of breakfasting with giraffes, then The Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya is the place for you. This boutique hotel allows guests to interact with these majestic creatures and even feed them from their bedroom windows. Imagine the envy-inducing shots you’ll get as you strike a pose with a giraffe photobombing in the background.

3. The Conrad Maldives Rangali Island, Maldives:
Renowned for its underwater restaurant, the Conrad Maldives Rangali Island offers a surreal experience like no other. Transparent walls surround the 14-seater Ithaa Undersea Restaurant, treating diners to panoramic views of the stunning marine life. Underwater shots here will leave your followers in awe, as you capture the vibrant colors of the coral reefs and tropical fish.

4. The Cavo Tagoo, Greece:
Located in Mykonos, Greece, The Cavo Tagoo is a luxurious hotel perched on a cliffside overlooking the Aegean Sea. The minimalist all-white design and infinity pool create a striking contrast against the deep blue sea and sky. Whether you’re lounging by the pool or capturing the mesmerizing sunset, this Instagrammable hotel will transport your followers to paradise.

5. The Gritti Palace, Italy:
Steeped in history and elegance, The Gritti Palace in Venice, Italy exudes luxury from every corner. The opulent interiors, lavish furnishings, and breathtaking views of the Grand Canal make it a photographer’s dream. Wander through its grand halls, snap a shot in one of its ornate suites, or capture the magic of a gondola passing by. This hotel is sure to elevate your Instagram feed to new heights.

6. The Amangiri, USA:
Nestled in the heart of the Utah desert, The Amangiri offers a unique blend of luxury and rugged natural beauty. The hotel’s sleek design seamlessly integrates with the surrounding canyons and rock formations, creating a visually stunning backdrop for your photos. From the iconic pool to the untouched landscapes, every angle here is a photographer’s delight.

7. The Sun Rocks Boutique Hotel, Greece:
Perched atop the cliffs of Santorini, Greece, The Sun Rocks Boutique Hotel offers breathtaking views of the caldera and the Aegean Sea. The whitewashed stone buildings, infinity pool, and private balconies provide the perfect setting for capturing those envy-inducing shots. Whether you’re snapping a picture of the iconic blue-domed churches or enjoying a romantic sunset dinner, this hotel is an Instagrammer’s paradise.

Remember, when visiting these Instagrammable hotels, be respectful of the property and other guests. Follow any guidelines or restrictions in place and always ask for permission before taking photos of individuals. Capture the beauty and uniqueness of each location, but don’t forget to put down your phone and truly immerse yourself in the experience. After all, the most memorable moments happen beyond the lens.

So, pack your bags, charge your camera, and get ready to explore these Instagrammable hotels. Your feed will thank you for it!
