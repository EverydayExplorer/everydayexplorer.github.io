The Best Hotels for Honeymooners: Romantic Retreats for Newlyweds


Are you planning your dream honeymoon and searching for the perfect romantic retreat to celebrate your newlywed status? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best hotels that cater to honeymooners. From exotic beach destinations to luxurious city escapes, these hotels offer a range of amenities and services that are sure to create an unforgettable experience for you and your partner.

1. The St. Regis Bora Bora Resort - French Polynesia
Immerse yourself in the beauty of the South Pacific at The St. Regis Bora Bora Resort. Set on a private island, this stunning resort boasts overwater villas with direct access to the turquoise lagoons. Relax on your own private terrace, indulge in a couples’ spa treatment, or explore the vibrant coral reefs together. With personalized butler service and breathtaking views, this is the ultimate romantic paradise.

2. The Oberoi Udaivilas - Udaipur, India
If you’re looking for a fairytale-like honeymoon experience, consider The Oberoi Udaivilas in Udaipur, India. This palatial hotel is situated on the banks of Lake Pichola and offers opulent accommodations fit for a king and queen. Explore the historic city of Udaipur, dine on the rooftop overlooking the lake, or take a romantic boat ride at sunset. The Oberoi Udaivilas is the epitome of romantic luxury.

3. Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea - Hawaii, USA
Hawaii has always been a popular destination for honeymooners, and the Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea is a top choice for romance. With its stunning ocean views, world-class dining, and impeccable service, this resort offers an unforgettable honeymoon experience. Lounge by the infinity pool, indulge in a couple’s massage, or embark on a romantic sunset sail. The natural beauty of Maui combined with the luxurious amenities of this resort will make your honeymoon truly special.

4. Belmond Hotel Caruso - Ravello, Italy
If you’ve always dreamed of a honeymoon in Italy, look no further than the Belmond Hotel Caruso in Ravello. Perched on a cliff above the Amalfi Coast, this stunning hotel offers breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea. Enjoy a candlelit dinner on the terrace, take a romantic stroll through the charming town of Ravello, or relax in the infinity pool overlooking the coastline. The Belmond Hotel Caruso is the perfect destination for a romantic Italian escape.

5. The Brando - Tetiaroa, French Polynesia
For a truly exclusive and secluded honeymoon, consider The Brando on Tetiaroa Island. This private island retreat offers luxurious villas surrounded by pristine white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. Discover the island’s rich biodiversity, dine at Michelin-starred restaurants, or indulge in a couples’ spa treatment. The Brando is the ultimate destination for couples seeking privacy and tranquility.

6. Jade Mountain - St. Lucia
Nestled amidst the lush landscape of St. Lucia, Jade Mountain is a stunning resort that offers panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea. Each of its spacious and open-air sanctuaries comes with a private infinity pool, creating the perfect environment for a romantic getaway. Enjoy a couples’ massage in the open-air spa, savor gourmet meals prepared by renowned chefs, or take a dip in the warm waters of the Caribbean. Jade Mountain is a haven for honeymooners seeking a blend of luxury and natural beauty.

7. Aman Venice - Venice, Italy
Venice is known as one of the most romantic cities in the world, and Aman Venice offers the perfect backdrop for your honeymoon. Housed in a 16th-century palazzo, this luxurious hotel combines historical charm with modern comforts. Indulge in a gondola ride through the picturesque canals, enjoy a private candlelit dinner, or simply relax in your elegantly appointed suite. Aman Venice is the ultimate choice for a honeymoon in the city of love.

8. Amanyara - Turks and Caicos
Located on the pristine shores of Providenciales in the Turks and Caicos, Amanyara is a secluded paradise that caters to honeymooners. With its stunning white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and luxurious villas, this resort offers the perfect setting for a romantic escape. Indulge in a couples’ massage at the award-winning spa, explore the vibrant coral reefs through snorkeling, or simply unwind by your private infinity pool. Amanyara is the ideal destination for couples seeking relaxation and tranquility.

9. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto - Kyoto, Japan
For a unique and culturally immersive honeymoon experience, consider The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto. Nestled along the banks of the Kamogawa River and surrounded by tranquil gardens, this hotel offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of Japan’s ancient capital. Explore the city’s historic temples and gardens, take a romantic boat ride along the river, or indulge in traditional Japanese cuisine. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto combines luxury with the rich heritage of Japan, making it a perfect choice for honeymooners.

10. Banyan Tree Seychelles - Seychelles
Last but not least, the Banyan Tree Seychelles offers a secluded and idyllic escape for honeymooners. Located on the pristine Intendance Bay in the Seychelles, this resort showcases the natural beauty of the islands. Relax in your private pool villa, indulge in a couples’ spa treatment, or explore the coral reefs just steps from your doorstep. The Banyan Tree Seychelles provides the perfect blend of luxury, privacy, and natural beauty.

In conclusion, choosing the right hotel for your honeymoon is essential to create lasting memories. Whether you prefer a tropical beach destination, a luxurious city escape, or an intimate and secluded retreat, these hotels offer the perfect settings for romance and relaxation. Consider these top honeymoon hotels and embark on the journey of a lifetime with your loved one.
