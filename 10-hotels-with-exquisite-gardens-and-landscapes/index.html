10 Hotels with Exquisite Gardens and Landscapes


Imagine a world where lush greenery, vibrant flowers, and serene landscapes surround you, creating an oasis of tranquility. Now, picture yourself staying at a hotel that not only offers luxurious accommodations but also boasts exquisite gardens and landscapes that will leave you in awe. In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to discover ten hotels around the world that have mastered the art of creating stunning outdoor spaces. From meticulously manicured gardens to breathtaking natural backdrops, these hotels are a nature lover’s paradise. So, pack your bags, and let’s embark on a botanical adventure!

1. The Chateau de Versailles, France:
Nestled just outside Paris, the Chateau de Versailles is not only a historical masterpiece but also home to some of the most beautiful gardens in the world. Designed by André Le Nôtre, these gardens feature perfectly trimmed hedges, grand fountains, and countless statues that transport you back to the French Renaissance.

2. Keukenhof Gardens, Netherlands:
Known as the ‘Garden of Europe,’ Keukenhof Gardens is a floral wonderland that showcases over seven million flowers. Located in Lisse, just a short drive from Amsterdam, this vibrant park is famous for its tulips, daffodils, and hyacinths, creating a mesmerizing tapestry of colors.

3. The Butchart Gardens, Canada:
Located on Vancouver Island, The Butchart Gardens is a horticultural marvel that has attracted visitors from around the globe since 1904. With over fifty acres of meticulously planned gardens, including a sunken garden, a Japanese garden, and a rose garden, this Canadian gem is a true testament to the beauty of nature.

4. The Royal Botanic Garden, Sydney, Australia:
Situated in the heart of Sydney, the Royal Botanic Garden offers visitors a stunning combination of manicured lawns, exotic plants, and breathtaking harbor views. Take a leisurely stroll through the garden’s various themed sections, including the succulent garden, the rainforest walk, and the herb garden.

5. The Lodge at Kauri Cliffs, New Zealand:
Perched atop the stunning cliffs of Matauri Bay, The Lodge at Kauri Cliffs provides guests with not only luxurious accommodations but also access to a 6,000-acre property with breathtaking landscapes. Explore the native New Zealand bush, stroll through manicured gardens, and marvel at the panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean.

6. The Taj Lake Palace, India:
Floating gracefully on Lake Pichola in Udaipur, the Taj Lake Palace is an architectural marvel surrounded by picturesque gardens. Adorned with fountains, exotic plants, and fragrant flowers, the palace gardens offer a tranquil retreat from the bustling city, allowing guests to unwind and reconnect with nature.

7. The Biltmore Estate, United States:
Nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains of North Carolina, the Biltmore Estate is a grand mansion surrounded by stunning gardens. Designed by renowned landscape architect Frederick Law Olmsted, these gardens feature a mix of formal and informal elements, including Italian gardens, a rose garden, and a conservatory with tropical plants.

8. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto, Japan:
Immerse yourself in the beauty of traditional Japanese gardens at The Ritz-Carlton in Kyoto. With meticulously manicured landscapes, serene water features, and Zen-inspired designs, these gardens provide a peaceful sanctuary in the heart of the city.

9. The Four Seasons Resort, Costa Rica:
Nestled amidst the pristine rainforests of the Papagayo Peninsula, the Four Seasons Resort in Costa Rica offers guests a chance to experience the beauty of nature up close. The resort’s gardens blend seamlessly with the surrounding jungle, creating a harmonious environment where guests can relax and rejuvenate.

10. The Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden, South Africa:
Located at the foot of Table Mountain in Cape Town, Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden is a haven for plant enthusiasts. With its diverse collection of indigenous flora, including proteas, fynbos, and cycads, this garden showcases the unique beauty of South Africa’s plant kingdom.

These ten hotels with exquisite gardens and landscapes offer a truly immersive experience, allowing guests to connect with nature while enjoying luxurious accommodations. Whether you’re a nature lover, a photography enthusiast, or simply seeking a peaceful retreat, these hotels are sure to leave a lasting impression. So, why not add a touch of natural beauty to your next getaway and let these stunning gardens and landscapes take your breath away?
