The Top Fishing Documentaries to Watch for Inspiration


If you’re an avid angler or someone who simply appreciates the beauty of the great outdoors, then you’re in luck! There’s a treasure trove of captivating fishing documentaries out there that not only showcase the thrill of the catch but also offer valuable insights into the art and science of fishing. Whether you’re looking for inspiration for your next fishing adventure or simply want to immerse yourself in the world of angling from the comfort of your own home, these top fishing documentaries are sure to reel you in.

  1. ‘Rivers of a Lost Coast’: Dive into the rich history of fly fishing in California’s iconic rivers through this mesmerizing documentary. From the golden days of steelhead fishing to the challenges facing modern anglers, ‘Rivers of a Lost Coast’ offers a captivating glimpse into the soul of fly fishing and the profound connection between anglers and the waters they cherish.

  2. ‘The End of the Line’: Explore the complex issues surrounding commercial fishing and its impact on marine ecosystems in this eye-opening documentary. Through stunning underwater footage and interviews with experts, ‘The End of the Line’ sheds light on the urgent need for sustainable fishing practices to preserve our oceans for future generations.

  3. ‘Mighty Rivers’: Join biologist and angler Jeremy Wade as he embarks on epic journeys to uncover the mysteries of the world’s most iconic rivers. From the Amazon to the Congo, ‘Mighty Rivers’ takes viewers on a thrilling adventure filled with awe-inspiring landscapes, fascinating wildlife, and heart-pounding encounters with some of the largest and most elusive fish species on the planet.

  4. ‘Artifishal’: Delve into the controversial world of fish hatcheries and the unintended consequences of salmon farming in this thought-provoking documentary. Through interviews with scientists, activists, and fishermen, ‘Artifishal’ explores the complex relationship between humans and wild salmon and the urgent need to protect and restore their natural habitats.

  5. ‘Finding Joe Brooks’: Follow in the footsteps of legendary fly fisherman Joe Brooks as his daughter embarks on a journey to uncover the untold story of her father’s pioneering adventures. ‘Finding Joe Brooks’ celebrates the life and legacy of one of the most influential figures in fly fishing history and offers a poignant reminder of the transformative power of the sport.

  6. ‘A River Runs Through It’: Based on Norman Maclean’s classic novella, this timeless film follows the lives of two brothers growing up in rural Montana and their deep connection to fly fishing. With stunning cinematography and a moving story of family, love, and the beauty of the natural world, ‘A River Runs Through It’ continues to inspire generations of anglers around the world.

  7. ‘Chasing Silver’: Join renowned angler and filmmaker Jamie Howard as he sets out on an epic quest to catch the elusive tarpon on fly. ‘Chasing Silver’ offers a thrilling glimpse into the adrenaline-fueled world of saltwater fly fishing and the relentless pursuit of one of the ocean’s most prized game fish.

Whether you’re a seasoned angler or a newcomer to the sport, these top fishing documentaries are guaranteed to inspire, educate, and entertain. So grab your popcorn, sit back, and prepare to be transported to some of the most beautiful and exciting fishing destinations on Earth. Who knows? You might just discover your next fishing obsession along the way.
