Creative Ways to Use Buttons in Your DIY Crafts


Buttons are not just functional pieces for securing clothes; they can also be used as creative embellishments in your DIY crafts. Whether you have a stash of old buttons or you’re simply looking for new ideas to spruce up your craft projects, buttons can add a touch of charm and uniqueness to your creations. In this blog post, we will explore some innovative and creative ways to use buttons in your DIY crafts.

  1. Button art: Create stunning button artworks by arranging buttons of various shapes, sizes, and colors on a canvas or a piece of fabric. You can create intricate designs, such as flowers, animals, or abstract patterns. Use a strong adhesive to securely attach the buttons to the surface. This button art can serve as eye-catching wall decor or can be transformed into greeting cards or bookmarks.

  2. Button jewelry: Make your own fashionable accessories by incorporating buttons into your jewelry designs. Thread buttons onto a string or a wire to create unique necklaces, bracelets, or earrings. You can mix and match buttons of different sizes and colors for a striking effect. For a more sophisticated look, combine buttons with beads or charms. Button jewelry is an excellent way to showcase your creativity and style.

  3. Button embellishments: Buttons can serve as decorative embellishments on various craft projects. Sew buttons onto pillows, blankets, or clothing to add a personalized touch. You can create patterns or designs with buttons or use them as focal points. Additionally, buttons can be used to spruce up picture frames, lampshades, or even shoes. Let your imagination run wild and add buttons to any craft project that needs a little extra flair.

  4. Button magnets: Transform plain fridge magnets into charming decorative pieces by gluing small buttons onto them. Choose buttons of similar sizes and colors to create a cohesive look. Button magnets not only add a pop of color to your refrigerator but can also hold notes, memos, or grocery lists.

  5. Button bookmarks: Create cute and functional bookmarks by attaching buttons to ribbons or thin strips of fabric. You can sew or glue the buttons onto the ribbon, making sure they are firmly attached. Button bookmarks are a great way to add a touch of whimsy to your reading material.

  6. Button ornaments: Add a festive touch to your holiday decorations by incorporating buttons into your ornaments. Glue buttons onto foam balls, wooden cutouts, or even empty toilet paper rolls for a unique and personalized ornament. You can also string button ornaments together to create a garland or wreath.

  7. Button hair accessories: Give your hair a touch of flair by using buttons to create unique hair accessories. Glue buttons onto hairpins, headbands, or hair ties for a playful and whimsical look. You can choose buttons that match your outfit or go for a contrasting color to make a statement.

  8. Button embellished cards: Personalize your greeting cards by adding buttons as decorative accents. Use buttons to create flower petals, snowflakes, or balloons on the front of your cards. This creative touch will make each card unique and special.

In conclusion, buttons are versatile and can be used in a variety of creative ways in your DIY crafts. From button art and jewelry to embellishments and ornaments, buttons add charm and uniqueness to your creations. So, go ahead and raid your button stash or start collecting buttons to unleash your creativity and transform your craft projects into works of art.
