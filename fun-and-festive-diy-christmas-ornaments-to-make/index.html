Fun and Festive DIY Christmas Ornaments to Make


‘Tis the season to get crafty! Christmas is just around the corner, and what better way to embrace the festive spirit than by creating your own DIY Christmas ornaments? Not only do these ornaments add a personal touch to your holiday decor, but they also make for great family activities and thoughtful gifts. In this blog post, we’ll explore some fun and festive DIY Christmas ornaments that you can make with just a few simple materials. So let’s dive in and get creative!

1. Pom-Pom Baubles:
Add a pop of color to your Christmas tree with these adorable pom-pom baubles. All you need are some colorful yarn, a few pom-pom makers, and some string. Simply wrap the yarn around the pom-pom maker, tie it off, and trim the edges to create a fluffy pom-pom. Attach a string to the pom-pom, and voila! You have a cute and vibrant ornament to hang on your tree.

2. Cinnamon Stick Stars:
Embrace the cozy scent of the holiday season with these cinnamon stick stars. Gather some cinnamon sticks and bundle them together in the shape of a star. Secure them with twine or ribbon, and add a small bow for an extra touch. Hang these fragrant ornaments on your tree or use them as festive gift toppers.

3. Button Snowmen:
Put those spare buttons to good use with these adorable button snowmen ornaments. Grab some white buttons in different sizes, along with some orange and black felt for the carrot nose and eyes. Use a hot glue gun to stack the buttons in a snowman shape, and attach the felt pieces for the facial features. Add a small loop of string at the top, and you’ll have a whimsical snowman ornament ready to hang.

4. Salt Dough Ornaments:
Create lasting memories with salt dough ornaments. Mix together 1 cup of flour, 1/2 cup of salt, and 1/2 cup of water to form a dough. Roll out the dough and use cookie cutters to create shapes like stars, Christmas trees, or gingerbread men. Make a small hole at the top of each shape for hanging. Bake the ornaments at 200°F for about 2 hours or until they harden. Once cooled, paint them with acrylic paints and seal with a clear varnish for a glossy finish.

5. Wine Cork Reindeer:
For all the wine enthusiasts out there, this one’s for you! Collect some wine corks and transform them into adorable reindeer ornaments. All you need are some googly eyes, small red pom-poms for the nose, and brown pipe cleaners for the antlers. Glue the eyes and nose onto the cork, and twist the pipe cleaners to create antlers. Attach a loop of string, and your reindeer ornament is ready to hang.

These are just a few ideas to get you started on your DIY Christmas ornament journey. Feel free to let your creativity shine and experiment with different materials and designs. Whether you’re crafting with kids, enjoying some alone time, or hosting a holiday party, making your own ornaments is a wonderful way to celebrate the joy and magic of Christmas. So gather your supplies, put on some festive tunes, and let the ornament-making festivities begin!

Remember to share your creations on social media using the hashtag #DIYChristmasOrnaments. Happy crafting and happy holidays!
