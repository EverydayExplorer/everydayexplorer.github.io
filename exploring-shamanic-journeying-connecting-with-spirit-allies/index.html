Exploring Shamanic Journeying: Connecting with Spirit Allies


Have you ever felt a deep longing to connect with something beyond the physical realm? Do you find yourself drawn to ancient wisdom and mystical traditions? If so, you may be interested in exploring the practice of shamanic journeying as a means of connecting with spirit allies.

Shamanic journeying is an ancient practice rooted in indigenous cultures around the world. It involves entering an altered state of consciousness in order to access the realm of spirits and ancestors. Through this journey, one can receive guidance, healing, and insight from these non-physical beings, known as spirit allies.

The process of shamanic journeying is typically facilitated with the use of rhythmic drumming or rattling, which helps to induce a trance-like state. In this altered state, the journeyer is able to travel to and interact with different realms and beings. It is important to note that shamanic journeying is not the same as astral projection or lucid dreaming, as it involves intentionally seeking out and connecting with specific spirit allies.

Spirit allies can take many forms, ranging from power animals, ancestral spirits, and spiritual guides. They can offer support, guidance, and protection on your spiritual journey. Each individual’s spirit allies are unique to them and may change over time. Building a relationship with these allies is a process of trust, respect, and reciprocity.

One of the key aspects of shamanic journeying is setting intention. Before embarking on a journey, it is important to clearly state your intention and ask for permission to enter the spirit realms. This helps to establish a clear and focused purpose for the journey, as well as ensure that you are entering the realm of spirit allies with respect and humility.

Once you have set your intention, it’s time to begin the journey. Find a quiet and comfortable space where you won’t be disturbed, and prepare yourself for the experience. Close your eyes and begin to focus on your breath, allowing yourself to relax and let go of any tensions or distractions.

As the drumming or rattling starts, allow yourself to be carried away by the rhythm. Imagine yourself descending into a tunnel or crossing a threshold into another realm. Trust your intuition and follow your inner guidance as you explore this realm. You may encounter different landscapes, beings, or symbols along the way. Pay attention to any insights or messages that come to you during the journey.

When it feels right, approach a spirit ally and introduce yourself. Ask for their guidance, support, or healing. Be open to receiving their wisdom in whatever form it comes. Remember to always approach spirit allies with respect and gratitude, acknowledging their presence and the gifts they have to offer.

After spending time with your spirit allies, it is important to bring your journey to a close. Take a moment to express gratitude for the experience and to thank your spirit allies for their guidance. Slowly bring your awareness back to your physical body and gently open your eyes.

It is recommended to journal about your journey afterwards, recording any insights, experiences, or messages you received. This will help you integrate and remember the wisdom gained during the journey.

Shamanic journeying can be a profound and transformative practice. It allows us to tap into the vast realms of spirit and expand our understanding of the interconnectedness of all things. By connecting with our spirit allies, we can access guidance, healing, and support on our spiritual path.

If you feel called to explore shamanic journeying, it is important to seek guidance from experienced practitioners or teachers who can provide support and mentorship along the way. They can help you navigate the various realms and ensure a safe and grounded practice.

Remember, shamanic journeying is a personal and deeply spiritual experience. It is a practice that invites us to develop a relationship with the unseen realms and the beings that reside there. Approach it with an open heart, a humble attitude, and a willingness to listen and learn from the wisdom of spirit allies.

As we explore shamanic journeying and connect with our spirit allies, we open ourselves to a world of wisdom, healing, and transformation. Embrace this ancient practice and embark on a journey of self-discovery and connection with the unseen realms. The path awaits – are you ready to explore?
