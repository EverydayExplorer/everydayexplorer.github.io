Scenic Train Journeys: Exploring the World's Most Breathtaking Routes


All aboard! Scenic train journeys are a fantastic way to explore the world’s most breathtaking landscapes. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or someone looking for a new adventure, hopping on a train and taking in the stunning views is an experience like no other. In this blog post, we’ll dive into some of the most awe-inspiring train routes around the globe that will leave you in awe of the beauty of our planet.

  1. The Rocky Mountaineer, Canada: One of the most famous scenic train journeys in the world, the Rocky Mountaineer takes you through the Canadian Rockies, showcasing towering mountains, towering trees, and pristine lakes. The train takes you through Banff and Jasper National Parks, offering views of turquoise lakes, snow-capped peaks, and wildlife like bears and eagles. This journey is a photographer’s dream, with endless opportunities to capture the beauty of the Canadian wilderness.

  2. The Glacier Express, Switzerland: Switzerland is known for its stunning alpine scenery, and the Glacier Express is the perfect way to experience it. This iconic train journey takes you from Zermatt to St. Moritz, passing through charming mountain villages, lush valleys, and dramatic gorges. The train traverses over 291 bridges and through 91 tunnels, providing passengers with a front-row seat to some of the most beautiful landscapes in Europe.

  3. The Ghan, Australia: For a true outback adventure, hop on board The Ghan in Australia. This legendary train journey takes you from Darwin in the north to Adelaide in the south, crossing the heart of Australia’s red desert landscapes. Along the way, you’ll witness the changing scenery of the Outback, from rugged mountains to vast plains, and maybe even spot some kangaroos or camels along the tracks.

  4. The Trans-Siberian Railway, Russia: Spanning over 9,000 kilometers, the Trans-Siberian Railway is the longest train journey in the world and one of the most epic. The route takes you from Moscow to Vladivostok, crossing through eight time zones and showcasing the vastness of Russia’s landscape. From the rolling hills of the Ural Mountains to the pristine waters of Lake Baikal, this journey is a true bucket-list experience for any train enthusiast.

  5. The TranzAlpine, New Zealand: New Zealand is renowned for its natural beauty, and the TranzAlpine train journey is the perfect way to soak it all in. This scenic route takes you from Christchurch on the east coast to Greymouth on the west coast, passing through the Southern Alps and showcasing stunning views of snow-capped peaks, lush forests, and crystal-clear rivers. Keep your camera ready, as you never know when you’ll come across a picture-perfect moment.

  6. The Inca Rail, Peru: For a journey back in time, embark on the Inca Rail in Peru. This train journey takes you from Cusco to Machu Picchu, offering breathtaking views of the Andes Mountains and the Urubamba River. As you wind your way through the mountains, you’ll pass by ancient Inca ruins and colorful Andean villages, providing a glimpse into the rich history and culture of Peru.

  7. The Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, India: Travel back to the colonial era with a ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway in India. This UNESCO World Heritage Site takes you from New Jalpaiguri to Darjeeling, climbing over steep gradients and through lush tea plantations. The toy train, as it’s affectionately called, offers unmatched views of the Himalayas and the charming hill stations of West Bengal, making it a must-do for train enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

In conclusion, scenic train journeys offer a unique way to explore the world’s most breathtaking landscapes. From the rugged mountains of Canada to the rolling hills of New Zealand, these train routes will take you on a journey like no other. So, pack your bags, grab your camera, and get ready for an unforgettable ride through some of the most beautiful places on Earth. Happy travels!
