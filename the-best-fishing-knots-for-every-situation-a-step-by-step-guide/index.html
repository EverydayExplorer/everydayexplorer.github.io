The Best Fishing Knots for Every Situation: A Step-by-Step Guide


When it comes to fishing, having the right knot can make all the difference between a successful catch and a frustrating experience. With so many different knots to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know which one to use in a particular situation. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll cover the best fishing knots for every situation, providing a step-by-step guide to help you master them.

THE FIGURE EIGHT KNOT

The figure eight knot is a versatile knot that can be used for a variety of applications, including connecting a fishing line to a hook, swivel, or lure. It’s also a good knot to use when joining two lines together.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Create a loop in the line by holding the end of the line in your non-dominant hand and wrapping the line around your dominant hand.
  2. Pass the end of the line through the loop you just created.
  3. Take the end of the line and pass it around the standing part of the line, keeping it close to the loop.
  4. Pass the end of the line through the loop again, from the same side as before.
  5. Moisten the knot and pull it tight to secure it.
