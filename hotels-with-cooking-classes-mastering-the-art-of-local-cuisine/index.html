Hotels with Cooking Classes: Mastering the Art of Local Cuisine


Looking to take your culinary skills to the next level while on vacation? Hotels with cooking classes offer guests the opportunity to learn how to master the art of local cuisine and bring new flavors and techniques back home. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best hotels around the world that offer cooking classes, providing guests with a unique and delicious experience during their stay.

1. Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan
Nestled in the lush jungles of Ubud, the Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan offers guests the chance to learn the secrets of Balinese cuisine through its cooking school. Led by expert chefs, the classes take place in an open-air pavilion overlooking the Ayung River. Guests can learn to prepare traditional dishes such as nasi goreng, satay, and gado-gado, using locally sourced ingredients. After the class, guests can enjoy their creations while taking in the stunning views of the surrounding rice paddies and jungle.

2. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto
Located in the heart of Japan’s ancient capital, The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto offers guests the opportunity to learn about the art of Japanese cuisine through its cooking classes. Led by the hotel’s master chef, the classes provide hands-on instruction in the preparation of sushi, tempura, and other traditional dishes. Guests can also enjoy a tea ceremony demonstration and learn about the cultural significance of Japanese cuisine. After the class, guests can savor their creations in the hotel’s tranquil Japanese garden.

3. Grand Velas Los Cabos
Set on the pristine shores of the Sea of Cortez in Mexico, the Grand Velas Los Cabos offers guests the chance to learn about the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine through its cooking classes. The classes take place in the hotel’s state-of-the-art kitchen, where guests can learn to make classic dishes such as ceviche, mole, and chiles en nogada. The hotel also offers tequila and mezcal tastings for guests to experience the spirit of Mexico. After the class, guests can enjoy their dishes while taking in the stunning ocean views.

4. Belmond La Residencia, Mallorca
Nestled in the picturesque village of Deià on the island of Mallorca, Belmond La Residencia offers guests the chance to learn about the flavors of Mediterranean cuisine through its cooking classes. Led by the hotel’s Michelin-starred chef, the classes take place in the hotel’s scenic kitchen garden, where guests can learn to prepare regional specialties such as paella, gazpacho, and ensaimadas. After the class, guests can enjoy their creations while taking in the breathtaking views of the Tramuntana mountains and the Mediterranean Sea.

5. The Siam, Bangkok
Located in the heart of Bangkok’s historic district, The Siam offers guests the chance to learn about the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine through its cooking classes. Led by the hotel’s expert chefs, the classes take place in a traditional Thai house situated on the banks of the Chao Phraya River. Guests can learn to prepare classic dishes such as tom yum soup, pad thai, and green curry, using fresh herbs and spices from the hotel’s organic garden. After the class, guests can enjoy their dishes while watching the sunset over the river.

In conclusion, hotels with cooking classes offer guests the chance to learn about the flavors and techniques of local cuisine, providing a unique and delicious experience during their stay. Whether you’re learning the art of sushi in Kyoto, exploring the flavors of Mexico in Los Cabos, or savoring the tastes of Thailand in Bangkok, these hotels provide the perfect base for a culinary adventure.
