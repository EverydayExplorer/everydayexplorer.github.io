How to Organize Your Craft Supplies: DIY Storage Solutions


Crafting is a beloved hobby for many, but it often comes with a common problem: cluttered and disorganized craft supplies. If you find yourself constantly searching for that one elusive thread or losing track of your favorite paintbrush, it’s time to declutter and create a functional storage system for your craft supplies. In this blog post, we will explore some creative and affordable DIY storage solutions that will help you organize your craft supplies once and for all.

1. Assess your space and needs
Before diving into the world of DIY storage, take a step back and assess your craft space and needs. Consider the size of your collection, the types of supplies you have, and how often you use them. This will help you determine the best storage solutions for your specific situation.

2. Sort and categorize
To start organizing, gather all your craft supplies in one place and sort them into categories. This will make it easier to see what you have and what storage solutions you need. Some common categories include paper and cardstock, paints and brushes, sewing notions, and knitting or crochet supplies.

3. Repurpose everyday items
Don’t rush to the store just yet! Look around your home for items that can be repurposed as craft storage. Mason jars, shoeboxes, and ice cube trays can be transformed into convenient storage containers for small items like buttons, beads, and pins. Get creative and think outside the box!

4. Use wall space
Maximize your space by utilizing the walls. Install pegboards or corkboards to hang tools, scissors, and rulers. This not only keeps them easily accessible but also adds a decorative touch to your craft room. You can also consider using hanging organizers with clear pockets to store fabric, yarn, or even scrapbooking supplies.

5. Invest in storage containers
Investing in storage containers is a great way to keep your craft supplies organized and protected. Opt for clear plastic containers so you can easily see what’s inside. Stackable containers are also a smart choice, as they save space and allow for easy access. Label each container to avoid confusion and make finding supplies a breeze.

6. Think vertically
If you’re short on floor space, think vertically. Install shelves or cubbies to store larger items like fabric bolts, scrapbooking albums, or storage bins. You can also hang a curtain rod and use S-hooks to hang rolls of ribbon or spools of thread.

7. Create a portable solution
If you like to craft on the go or frequently move your supplies around, consider creating a portable solution. A rolling cart with multiple drawers or compartments is perfect for storing and transporting your craft supplies. Plus, it can be easily tucked away when not in use.

8. Maintain and declutter regularly
Once you’ve organized your craft supplies, make it a habit to maintain and declutter regularly. Take a few minutes at the end of each crafting session to put things back in their designated places. This will help prevent clutter from accumulating and make future organizing tasks less overwhelming.

By following these DIY storage solutions, you can transform your cluttered craft space into an organized oasis. Remember, the key is to find storage solutions that work for you and your crafting needs. Get creative, repurpose everyday items, and have fun organizing your craft supplies! Happy crafting!
