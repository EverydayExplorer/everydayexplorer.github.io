Funny on the Go: Best Comedy Podcasts for a Good Laugh


In today’s hectic and fast-paced world, we all need a good laugh to help us unwind and relieve our stress. Luckily, there are countless comedy podcasts available that can provide us with endless laughter, no matter where we are. Whether you’re commuting to work, exercising at the gym, or just looking for some entertainment during your free time, comedy podcasts are the perfect solution. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best comedy podcasts for those who are always on the go and in need of a good laugh.

1. The Joe Rogan Experience

With over 200 million downloads per month, The Joe Rogan Experience is one of the most popular comedy podcasts out there. Hosted by Joe Rogan, a comedian and UFC commentator, this podcast features a wide range of guests, including comedians, actors, musicians, and even scientists. The show covers various topics, from current events to personal stories, making it both entertaining and informative.

2. Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend

Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend is the perfect podcast for fans of the hilarious late-night talk show host. In each episode, Conan sits down with celebrities and engages in witty conversations that are bound to make you laugh out loud. With his unique humor and quick wit, Conan brings the best out of his guests, making this podcast a definite must-listen.

3. Comedy Bang! Bang!

For those who appreciate improvised comedy, Comedy Bang! Bang! is the podcast for you. Hosted by Scott Aukerman, this show features a mix of celebrity interviews and improv games with comedians. With its unpredictable and hilarious nature, Comedy Bang! Bang! is a surefire way to keep you entertained and laughing throughout your day.

4. The Dollop

If you’re a history buff with a sense of humor, The Dollop is the podcast you didn’t know you needed. Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, this show explores bizarre and often unknown stories from American history. With a comedic twist, Dave and Gareth engage in humorous banter as they recount these historical events, making them enjoyable and educational at the same time.

5. My Dad Wrote A Porno

My Dad Wrote A Porno is a unique comedy podcast that has gained a massive following. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this hilarious show involves Jamie reading a chapter from his dad’s amateur erotic novel. With witty commentary and hilarious reactions from the hosts, My Dad Wrote A Porno offers a one-of-a-kind listening experience that will have you laughing uncontrollably.

6. The Comedy Store Podcast

For those interested in the inner workings of one of the most renowned comedy clubs, The Comedy Store Podcast is a fantastic choice. Hosted by comedian and former doorman of The Comedy Store, Eleanor Kerrigan, and national headliner, Rick Ingraham, this podcast features interviews with some of the biggest names in comedy. With behind-the-scenes stories and insider knowledge, The Comedy Store Podcast is a must-listen for comedy enthusiasts.

7. No Such Thing As A Fish

No Such Thing As A Fish is an award-winning comedy podcast hosted by the researchers behind the popular British TV show, QI. In each episode, the hosts share bizarre and interesting facts they have come across during the week. With their witty banter and intelligent humor, this podcast is not only entertaining but also educational, providing you with a wealth of fascinating trivia to share with friends.

So, whether you’re stuck in traffic, working out at the gym, or just looking for some entertainment on the go, these are some of the best comedy podcasts to keep you laughing. With their unique and hilarious content, these podcasts are sure to bring some much-needed joy and laughter into your daily routine. So, grab your headphones, hit play, and get ready to laugh your way through the day!

Disclaimer: This list is subjective and there are countless other comedy podcasts that may suit your sense of humor. These are just a few recommendations to get you started on your comedy podcast journey. Enjoy!
