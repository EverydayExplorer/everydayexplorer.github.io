Gear Review: Best Game Calls for Successful Hunting


Welcome, fellow hunters and outdoor enthusiasts! Today, we are delving into the exciting world of gear reviews for game calls. Whether you are an experienced hunter or just starting out, having the right game call in your arsenal can make all the difference in your hunting success. Join us as we explore some of the best game calls available on the market today.

  1. Primos Hunting 372 ‘The Third Degree’ Xtra Loud Elk Call:

Let’s kick things off with a bang! The Primos Hunting 372 ‘The Third Degree’ Xtra Loud Elk Call is a game changer when it comes to attracting elusive elk. Designed with three different reed frames, this call produces a wide range of sounds that perfectly mimic the various vocalizations of elk. Its extra loud feature ensures your calls reach far and wide, increasing your chances of a successful hunt. With its durable construction and user-friendly design, this call is a must-have for any serious elk hunter.

  1. FOXPRO Inferno Electronic Game Call:

If you’re looking for a versatile and technologically advanced game call, the FOXPRO Inferno Electronic Game Call should be on your radar. This compact call offers a wealth of features, including an incredible 75 preloaded sounds to attract a variety of game species. Its remote control allows you to easily switch between sounds and control the volume, giving you the ultimate control over your hunting strategy. With its rugged build and long battery life, the FOXPRO Inferno is an excellent choice for both beginners and experienced hunters.

  1. Flextone Thunder Creeper Turkey Call:

Gobble, gobble! Turkey hunters, listen up! The Flextone Thunder Creeper Turkey Call is a game call designed specifically to bring in those elusive gobblers. Its unique design allows you to manipulate the sound by squeezing or releasing the flexible body, producing incredibly realistic turkey calls. The Thunder Creeper also features a removable cap that exposes the striker, giving you the ability to produce soft purrs and clucks. With its portability and ease of use, this call is an essential tool for any turkey hunting expedition.

  1. Primos Hunting PS222 ‘The Can’ Deer Call:

Let’s not forget about our dear deer hunters! The Primos Hunting PS222 ‘The Can’ Deer Call is a tried-and-true classic that has been a favorite among hunters for years. This simple yet effective call produces authentic-sounding doe bleats, perfect for luring in bucks during the rut. Its compact and lightweight design allows for easy carrying, while its durable construction ensures it can withstand the rigors of the field. For those looking for a reliable and affordable deer call, ‘The Can’ is a top-notch option.

  1. MOJO Outdoors Super Critter Predator Decoy:

Last but certainly not least, the MOJO Outdoors Super Critter Predator Decoy is a game-changing accessory for predator hunters. This motion decoy replicates the movement of small prey animals, attracting predators and luring them within range. With its realistic fur and lifelike motion, it adds an extra level of realism to your hunting setup. The Super Critter comes with a remote control that allows you to control the movement and sound, giving you an edge in outsmarting even the most cunning predators.

In conclusion, having the right game calls can greatly enhance your hunting experience and increase your chances of success. Whether you’re after elk, turkey, deer, or predators, these top-notch game calls are sure to impress. Remember to practice using your calls before heading out into the field, as mastering the various sounds and techniques is key. So gear up, get out there, and happy hunting!
