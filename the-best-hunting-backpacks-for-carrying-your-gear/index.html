The Best Hunting Backpacks for Carrying Your Gear


When it comes to hunting, having the right gear can make all the difference. And one essential piece of equipment every hunter needs is a reliable and sturdy hunting backpack. A hunting backpack not only allows you to carry all your gear conveniently, but it also keeps everything organized and easily accessible. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, finding the best hunting backpack for your needs is crucial.

In this blog post, we will explore some of the top hunting backpacks available in the market today. We will consider various factors such as durability, capacity, comfort, and features to help you make an informed decision.

1. Tenzing TZ 2220 Hunting Backpack
The Tenzing TZ 2220 is a popular choice among hunters due to its versatility and durability. With a total capacity of 2,245 cubic inches, it offers ample space for your hunting essentials. It features a unique foldout bow and gun carrying boot, allowing you to transport your weapons securely. The backpack also comes with multiple pockets and compartments to keep your gear organized and within reach.

2. Badlands 2200 Hunting Backpack
The Badlands 2200 is another excellent option for hunters looking for a durable and feature-packed backpack. It boasts a capacity of 2,195 cubic inches and comes with a built-in meat shelf, making it ideal for those who plan on field dressing their game. The backpack’s KXO-32 fabric is both waterproof and tough, ensuring it can withstand the harshest hunting conditions.

3. ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Hunting Backpack
If you’re in need of a backpack that can handle heavy loads, the ALPS OutdoorZ Commander is worth considering. With a whopping 5,250 cubic inches of storage space, this backpack is designed for hunters who require extra capacity. It also features a detachable frame and a meat hauling system, allowing you to carry your game back to camp effortlessly.

4. Eberlestock X1A2 Hunting Backpack
The Eberlestock X1A2 is a compact and lightweight hunting backpack that doesn’t compromise on functionality. With a capacity of 1,800 cubic inches, it’s perfect for hunters who prefer a minimalist approach. The backpack’s unique design allows for quick and easy access to your gear, reducing the time spent rummaging through pockets.

5. Mystery Ranch Metcalf Hunting Backpack
For hunters who prioritize comfort and load-carrying capability, the Mystery Ranch Metcalf is an excellent choice. It features a unique three-zip design that allows for easy access to the main compartment. The backpack’s patented Futura Yoke system ensures a comfortable fit, even when carrying heavy loads. With a capacity of 4,000 cubic inches, it offers plenty of room for all your hunting gear.

In conclusion, finding the best hunting backpack is essential for any avid hunter. Consider factors such as durability, capacity, comfort, and features when making your decision. The Tenzing TZ 2220, Badlands 2200, ALPS OutdoorZ Commander, Eberlestock X1A2, and Mystery Ranch Metcalf are all excellent options to consider. Remember to choose a backpack that suits your specific hunting needs and preferences. Happy hunting!
