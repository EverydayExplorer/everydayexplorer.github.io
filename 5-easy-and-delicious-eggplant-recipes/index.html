5 Easy and Delicious Eggplant Recipes


Eggplants, also known as aubergines, are a versatile vegetable that can be used in a variety of dishes to add flavor, texture, and nutrition. Whether you’re a die-hard eggplant fan or someone looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into your diet, these 5 easy and delicious eggplant recipes are sure to satisfy your taste buds and leave you coming back for more.

  1. Eggplant Parmesan

  2. Ingredients:

    • 2 medium eggplants, sliced into 1/4 inch rounds
    • 2 cups breadcrumbs
    • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • 2 cups marinara sauce
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

  3. Instructions:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking sheet.
    • Dip eggplant slices in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan cheese.
    • Place coated eggplant slices on the baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
    • Spread marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, layer baked eggplant slices on top, then cover with more marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Bake for an additional 20 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Serve hot and enjoy!

  4. Grilled Eggplant Sandwiches

  5. Ingredients:

    • 1 large eggplant, sliced lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick slices
    • 4 ciabatta rolls
    • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
    • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
    • 1 tomato, sliced
    • 4 slices mozzarella cheese

  6. Instructions:

    • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
    • Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    • Grill eggplant slices for 3-4 minutes on each side until tender and lightly charred.
    • Assemble sandwiches by spreading pesto on the bottom half of each ciabatta roll, then layering with grilled eggplant slices, spinach leaves, tomato slices, and mozzarella cheese.
    • Top with the other half of the ciabatta roll and grill for an additional 2-3 minutes until the cheese is melted and the bread is toasted. Serve warm and enjoy!

  7. Eggplant Stir-Fry

  8. Ingredients:

    • 2 medium eggplants, cubed
    • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
    • 1 onion, sliced
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • Cooked rice, for serving

  9. Instructions:

    • Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add minced garlic and sliced onion, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
    • Add cubed eggplant and sliced bell peppers to the skillet, and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until vegetables are tender.
    • In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Pour over the vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
    • Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Serve hot over cooked rice and enjoy!

  10. Eggplant and Chickpea Curry

  11. Ingredients: * 2 medium eggplants, diced * 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed * 1 onion, diced * 2 cloves garlic, minced * 1 tablespoon curry powder * 1 teaspoon ground cumin * 1 teaspoon ground coriander * 1/2 teaspoon turmeric * 1 can coconut milk * Salt and pepper, to taste * Fresh cilantro, for garnish

  12. Instructions:

*   Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened.
*   Add diced eggplant to the pot, along with curry powder, cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Cook for 5-6 minutes until eggplant begins to soften.
*   Stir in chickpeas and coconut milk, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10-12 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly and the flavors meld together.
*   Serve hot over cooked rice, garnished with fresh cilantro. Enjoy this flavorful and comforting curry!

  1. Eggplant Caponata

  2. Ingredients: * 2 medium eggplants, diced * 1 onion, diced * 2 cloves garlic, minced * 1 can diced tomatoes * 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar * 2 tablespoons capers * 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil * Salt and pepper, to taste * Crusty bread, for serving

  3. Instructions: * Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened. * Add diced eggplant to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes until softened and slightly browned. * Stir in diced tomatoes, balsamic vinegar, capers, and chopped fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. * Simmer for 10-12 minutes until the flavors meld together and the caponata thickens slightly. * Serve warm or at room temperature, with crusty bread for dipping. Enjoy this classic Italian dish bursting with flavor!

Eggplants are not only delicious but also packed with nutrients like fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. Incorporating these 5 easy and delicious eggplant recipes into your weekly meal rotation is a great way to enjoy this versatile vegetable and reap its health benefits. Whether you’re grilling, baking, or stir-frying, there’s a recipe here to suit every taste preference and culinary skill level. So why wait? Head to your local market, grab some fresh eggplants, and get cooking! Your taste buds will thank you.
