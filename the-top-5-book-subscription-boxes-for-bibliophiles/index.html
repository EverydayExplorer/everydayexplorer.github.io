The Top 5 Book Subscription Boxes for Bibliophiles


Are you a book lover who enjoys the thrill of receiving a new book in the mail every month? If so, you might want to consider subscribing to a book subscription box. These curated boxes are designed to delight bibliophiles with carefully selected books and bookish goodies. With so many options available, it can be challenging to choose the best one for you. To help you make an informed decision, we’ve put together a list of the top 5 book subscription boxes for bibliophiles.

1. Book of the Month

Book of the Month is a classic in the world of book subscription boxes. Each month, subscribers can choose from a selection of five newly released hardcover books curated by a team of literary experts. This allows you to tailor your subscription to your specific reading tastes. The chosen book is then delivered straight to your door, making it perfect for those who love the excitement of a monthly literary surprise.

2. Once Upon a Book Club

Once Upon a Book Club offers a unique and interactive reading experience. With this subscription, you’ll receive a newly released book along with 3-5 wrapped gifts. These gifts are labeled with page numbers, so you can unwrap them at specific points in the story. It’s a delightful way to bring the book to life and make the reading experience even more immersive.

3. OwlCrate

OwlCrate is designed for lovers of young adult literature. Each month, subscribers receive a newly released YA book, along with 3-5 bookish items related to the book’s theme. From exclusive covers to author letters, OwlCrate aims to create an enchanting reading experience for its subscribers.

4. The Bookish Box

The Bookish Box is perfect for those who love literary-themed goodies. In addition to a newly released book, subscribers receive 3-5 bookish items such as candles, bookmarks, and art prints. Each box is carefully curated to celebrate the joy of reading and the beauty of bookish treasures.

5. Scribbler

Scribbler is a unique subscription box designed specifically for writers. In addition to a newly released book, subscribers receive writing-related items such as writing prompts, advice from bestselling authors, and access to an exclusive online writing community. It’s the perfect choice for book lovers who also have a passion for writing.

Choosing the right book subscription box is a personal decision that depends on your reading preferences and what you value most in a subscription service. Whether you’re looking for a classic book club experience, an interactive reading adventure, or a box filled with bookish treats, there’s a subscription box out there to cater to your literary desires.

In conclusion, book subscription boxes offer a delightful way to discover new books and connect with fellow book lovers. With the variety of options available, there’s a subscription box for every type of bibliophile. So why not treat yourself or a fellow bookworm to the joy of a monthly literary surprise? Happy reading!
