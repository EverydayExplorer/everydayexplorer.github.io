Tips for Getting Upgrades and VIP Treatment at Hotels


There’s something undeniably exciting about entering a luxurious hotel and being treated like a VIP. From room upgrades to personalized service and exclusive amenities, there’s no doubt that these perks can enhance your overall hotel experience.

But how exactly do you go about getting upgrades and VIP treatment at hotels? The truth is, it’s not just about luck or chance; there are a few insider tips and tricks that can significantly increase your chances of enjoying that extra touch of luxury. In this blog post, we’ll uncover some of the most effective strategies for receiving upgrades and VIP treatment during your hotel stays. So, read on and prepare to elevate your hotel experience to new heights!

1. Join the Loyalty Program: One of the easiest and most effective ways to increase your chances of receiving upgrades and VIP treatment is to join the hotel’s loyalty program. Signing up for membership not only demonstrates your loyalty to the brand but also gives you access to exclusive perks and rewards. As a member, you’ll often receive priority treatment, including complimentary room upgrades, late check-outs, and access to executive lounges.

2. Be Polite and Engage with the Staff: The power of a friendly attitude and genuine engagement with hotel staff should never be underestimated. Remember to treat everyone you encounter, from the front desk clerks to the concierge, with respect and kindness. Engaging in casual conversation, showing appreciation for their assistance, and a simple smile can go a long way in creating a positive rapport. This positive rapport may increase the likelihood of receiving upgrades or special treatment.

3. Choose the Right Time to Book: The timing of your booking can also impact your chances of getting upgrades and VIP treatment. Try to avoid booking during peak travel seasons when hotels tend to be at their busiest. Instead, opt for lower-demand periods when hotels have more flexibility to accommodate special requests. Weekends may also offer better chances of upgrades, as business travelers often go home during this time, leaving more rooms available. By booking strategically, you may find yourself with a higher chance of receiving an upgrade.

4. Utilize Social Media: In this digital age, leveraging social media is a powerful tool when it comes to getting upgrades and VIP treatment at hotels. Follow your preferred hotels on platforms like Twitter, Instagram, or Facebook, and actively engage with their content. Occasionally, hotels will offer exclusive promotions or perks to their social media followers. Additionally, if you’re celebrating a special occasion, make sure to mention it in a message or comment. Hotels often appreciate the opportunity to create memorable experiences for their guests and might surprise you with an upgrade or thoughtful gesture.

5. Mention Special Occasions and Celebrations: Speaking of special occasions, don’t hesitate to let the hotel staff know if you’re celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or any other significant event during your stay. Hotels are often more inclined to offer upgrades or additional perks to guests celebrating such milestones. When making a reservation or checking in, politely mention the occasion, and who knows, you might find yourself getting upgraded to a stunning suite or receiving a complimentary bottle of champagne.

6. Arrive Early, but Not Too Early: Arriving early at the hotel not only allows you to make the most of your stay, but it can also increase your chances of getting an upgrade. By arriving before the afternoon rush, you’ll have a better chance of securing a room upgrade if available. However, be cautious not to arrive too early, as housekeeping might still be busy preparing the rooms. Check in at a reasonable time to maximize your chances without inconveniencing the hotel staff or disrupting their operation.

7. Use a Travel Advisor: Consider enlisting the services of a trusted travel advisor to help secure upgrades and VIP treatments. Their industry connections and expertise can significantly enhance your chances of receiving special perks during your hotel stay. Travel advisors often have access to exclusive partnerships and can negotiate on your behalf, ensuring you receive the best treatment possible. Additionally, they can provide valuable advice on selecting hotels that are known for their exceptional service and generous upgrade policies.

8. Book Higher Category Rooms: It may seem counterintuitive, but booking a higher category room can potentially increase your chances of receiving an upgrade. When hotels have available inventory in higher-tier rooms, they often prioritize upgrading guests who have already booked higher-priced accommodations. So, if you’re seeking an upgrade, consider booking a more expensive room category, as it might sway the hotel’s decision in your favor.

9. Leave Positive Online Reviews: After your stay, take a few minutes to leave a positive online review about your experience at the hotel. Hotels highly value guest feedback, and positive reviews can significantly impact their reputation. By sharing your positive experience, you may catch the attention of hotel management, who might extend future VIP treatment or upgrades as a thank-you gesture.

10. Be a Repeat Guest: Finally, loyalty and repeat business often result in better treatment and perks at hotels. By consistently choosing to stay at the same hotel or hotel brand, you’re more likely to build a strong relationship with staff and management. This loyalty can lead to upgraded room assignments, personalized amenities, and other exclusive treatment during your visits. Showcasing your loyalty and returning to a hotel you love can pay off in terms of receiving VIP treatment.

In conclusion, snagging upgrades and VIP treatment at hotels isn’t just a matter of chance; it requires a strategic approach and a little bit of effort. By utilizing these ten tips, including joining loyalty programs, engaging with hotel staff, booking strategically, and leveraging social media, you can increase your chances of enjoying that extra touch of luxury during your hotel stays. So, go ahead and put these tips into practice on your next trip, and prepare to be treated like a true VIP!

Remember, it’s important to approach each interaction with hotel staff with kindness and gratitude, as they work hard to ensure outstanding experiences for all guests. With the right attitude and a little bit of luck, you might find yourself enjoying upgrades and VIP treatment more often than not. Happy travels!
