Embracing Change: The Wisdom of Transits and Progressions


Change is an inevitable part of life. It can be unsettling and uncomfortable, but it is also a powerful catalyst for growth and transformation. Embracing change requires a certain level of courage and openness, as it often pushes us out of our comfort zones and challenges our existing beliefs and routines. One way to navigate change and gain insight into its purpose and timing is through the study of transits and progressions in astrology.

Transits refer to the current positions of the planets in relation to our birth chart. They indicate the energies and influences that are impacting our lives at any given moment. Progressions, on the other hand, are a method of symbolic timekeeping in astrology, where each day after birth represents a year of life. Progressions offer a deeper understanding of the long-term cycles and developmental stages we go through.

By studying transits and progressions, we can gain valuable insights into the changes and challenges we are currently experiencing. Astrology provides a language to interpret these cosmic energies and understand how they are shaping our lives. It helps us make sense of the shifts and upheavals we go through, allowing us to navigate them with greater clarity and purpose.

One of the key teachings of transits and progressions is that change is not random or arbitrary. Every planetary movement carries a unique energy and intention, and by aligning ourselves with these cosmic currents, we can harness their transformative power. Rather than resisting change or clinging to the familiar, astrology encourages us to flow with the natural rhythm of the universe and embrace the opportunities for growth and evolution that it presents.

For example, let’s say you are going through a challenging transit of Saturn to your natal Sun. Saturn is often associated with discipline, responsibility, and hard work. During this transit, you may find yourself facing obstacles or limitations that require you to reassess your goals and priorities. It may feel like a period of contraction and restriction, but it is also an invitation to develop inner strength, resilience, and a more mature approach to life.

Similarly, progressions can shed light on the larger cycles and themes that are unfolding in our lives. For instance, a progressed Moon entering a new sign or house may mark the beginning of a significant emotional shift or a change in our personal needs and desires. It can be a time of emotional renewal and exploration, as we step into a new phase of our lives.

Embracing change requires a willingness to let go of old patterns, beliefs, and attachments that no longer serve us. It invites us to trust in the inherent wisdom of the universe and surrender to the unfolding of our own unique journey. By studying transits and progressions, we can gain a deeper understanding of the cosmic forces at play and align ourselves with the natural flow of life.

In conclusion, astrology offers a valuable tool for navigating change and embracing the wisdom of transits and progressions. By attuning ourselves to the cosmic energies and cycles, we can gain insight into the purpose and timing of the changes we experience. It empowers us to embrace change with courage and curiosity, knowing that it is a powerful catalyst for growth and transformation. So, let us welcome change with open arms and embark on a journey of self-discovery and evolution.
