The Best Hotels for Destination Weddings: Saying 'I Do' in Paradise


Destination weddings have become increasingly popular over the years, and it’s no surprise why. Combining the joyous celebration of marriage with a breathtaking setting can create a truly unforgettable experience. If you’re planning a destination wedding and searching for the perfect venue, look no further. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best hotels for destination weddings, where you can say ‘I do’ in paradise.

1. Bora Bora Pearl Beach Resort & Spa, French Polynesia:
Imagine exchanging vows on a pristine white-sand beach, surrounded by crystal-clear turquoise waters. The Bora Bora Pearl Beach Resort & Spa offers an idyllic setting for a romantic and intimate wedding. The resort’s overwater bungalows provide luxurious accommodations and stunning views, making it a dream destination for couples seeking an unforgettable wedding experience.

2. Amalfi Coast, Italy:
The Amalfi Coast is renowned for its picturesque beauty and romantic atmosphere, making it an ideal location for a destination wedding. With its stunning cliffside views, charming coastal towns, and delicious cuisine, it’s no wonder the Amalfi Coast is a sought-after wedding destination. From the iconic Villa Cimbrone in Ravello to the historic Hotel Santa Caterina in Amalfi, there are plenty of luxurious hotels to choose from to make your special day even more magical.

3. Villa del Balbianello, Lake Como, Italy:
Nestled on the shores of Lake Como, Villa del Balbianello is a stunning 18th-century villa that offers a fairytale-like setting for a destination wedding. Surrounded by lush gardens and boasting panoramic views of the lake, this venue exudes elegance and romance. The villa’s terraced gardens provide a breathtaking backdrop for an outdoor ceremony, while its historic interiors offer a touch of old-world charm.

4. Amanjena, Marrakech, Morocco:
For couples seeking a destination wedding with a touch of exoticism, Amanjena in Marrakech is the perfect choice. This luxurious resort captures the essence of Moroccan architecture and offers a tranquil oasis for your special day. Whether you choose to exchange vows in the resort’s lush gardens or in the intimate setting of a private pavilion, Amanjena promises an unforgettable wedding experience.

5. Four Seasons Resort, Maui, Hawaii:
Hawaii has long been a popular destination for weddings, and the Four Seasons Resort in Maui is one of the best places to tie the knot. With its stunning ocean views, lush tropical gardens, and world-class service, this resort offers the perfect blend of luxury and natural beauty. From intimate beach ceremonies to grand ballroom receptions, the Four Seasons Resort ensures that every wedding is tailored to the couple’s unique vision.

Planning a destination wedding allows you to combine your love for travel and your celebration of love in a truly remarkable way. Whether you choose a tropical paradise, a historic villa, or a cultural haven, the best hotels for destination weddings will create the perfect backdrop for your special day. So, pack your bags, gather your loved ones, and prepare to say ‘I do’ in paradise. This is the beginning of your happily ever after.
