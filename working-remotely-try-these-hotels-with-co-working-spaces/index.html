Working Remotely? Try These Hotels with Co-Working Spaces


As the world continues to embrace remote work, more and more professionals are trading in their traditional office spaces for the flexibility and freedom of working remotely. Whether you’re a digital nomad, a freelance creative, or a remote employee for a global company, finding the perfect workspace is essential for productivity and success. Fortunately, a growing number of hotels are catering to the needs of remote workers by offering dedicated co-working spaces that combine the comforts of a hotel with the amenities of a modern office. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the top hotels with co-working spaces where you can work remotely in style and comfort.

The Rise of Remote Work

With advancements in technology and a shifting attitude towards work-life balance, remote work has become increasingly popular in recent years. Whether it’s avoiding long commutes, escaping office politics, or simply enjoying the flexibility to work from anywhere in the world, there are countless benefits to embracing the remote work lifestyle. However, finding the right environment to stay focused and productive can be a challenge, especially for those who don’t have a dedicated home office. That’s where hotels with co-working spaces come in, providing remote workers with all the tools and amenities they need to thrive while on the road.

Amenities for Productivity

Hotels with co-working spaces offer a range of amenities designed to enhance productivity and comfort for remote workers. From high-speed Wi-Fi and ergonomic workstations to complimentary refreshments and access to printing and copying services, these spaces are equipped with everything you need to stay connected and focused throughout the day. Many hotels also offer private meeting rooms and event spaces for collaborative work sessions or client meetings, allowing you to conduct business seamlessly while on the road. Whether you’re catching up on emails, attending virtual meetings, or brainstorming ideas with colleagues, these co-working spaces provide a professional and conducive environment for getting work done.

Inspiring Environments

One of the benefits of working remotely is the ability to choose your own workspace, whether it’s a cozy cafe, a bustling coworking space, or a tranquil hotel lobby. Hotels with co-working spaces offer a unique blend of comfort and inspiration, providing guests with stylish and well-designed environments that stimulate creativity and productivity. From sleek and modern interiors to lush greenery and natural light, these spaces are thoughtfully curated to enhance your work experience and inspire your best ideas. Whether you prefer working in a communal setting with other like-minded professionals or retreating to a quiet corner for focused solo work, these hotels offer a variety of environments to suit your needs and preferences.

Work-Life Balance

One of the challenges of remote work is maintaining a healthy work-life balance, especially when your office is just steps away from your bedroom. Hotels with co-working spaces offer the perfect solution by providing a dedicated workspace separate from your accommodations, allowing you to maintain boundaries between work and leisure. Whether you’re traveling for business or pleasure, having access to a professional workspace within the hotel allows you to stay focused during work hours and fully enjoy your downtime when the workday is done. Plus, many hotels offer amenities such as fitness centers, pool areas, and on-site dining options, allowing you to recharge and rejuvenate after a productive day of work.

Top Hotels with Co-Working Spaces

  1. Ace Hotel, New York City: Known for its trendy vibe and creative atmosphere, Ace Hotel offers a spacious lobby area with plenty of seating, high-speed Wi-Fi, and a buzzing energy that’s perfect for remote work.

  2. The Hoxton, London: With multiple locations around the world, The Hoxton offers stylish co-working spaces with comfortable seating, complimentary refreshments, and a vibrant atmosphere that’s sure to inspire your creativity.

  3. Selina, Various Locations: Selina is a global chain of hotels and co-working spaces designed for digital nomads and remote workers. With locations in cities and beach destinations around the world, Selina offers flexible workspaces, comfortable accommodations, and a vibrant community of like-minded travelers.

  4. Freehand, Miami: Located in the heart of Miami Beach, Freehand offers a laid-back co-working space with plenty of natural light, comfortable seating, and a relaxed vibe that’s perfect for remote work.

  5. Moxy, Various Locations: Moxy Hotels are known for their trendy design and social atmosphere, making them popular among young professionals and digital nomads. With co-working spaces equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi and plenty of seating, Moxy Hotels offer a fun and energetic environment for getting work done.

In conclusion, hotels with co-working spaces offer remote workers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and productivity. Whether you’re traveling for business or pleasure, these hotels provide all the amenities you need to stay connected, focused, and inspired while on the road. So why settle for working from a cramped hotel room or noisy cafe when you can enjoy the benefits of a dedicated co-working space within the comfort of a stylish and well-appointed hotel? Upgrade your remote work experience and discover the freedom of working remotely from anywhere in the world with these top hotels with co-working spaces.
