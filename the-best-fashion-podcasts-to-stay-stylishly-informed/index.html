The Best Fashion Podcasts to Stay Stylishly Informed


Are you a fashion enthusiast looking for new ways to stay stylishly informed? With the rise of podcasts, fashion lovers can now tune in to insightful conversations, expert tips, and inspiring stories from the industry’s leading voices. Whether you’re a fashion student, a trendsetter, or simply someone who wants to elevate their fashion game, podcasts can be a valuable resource to keep you up to date with the latest trends, news, and insights. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best fashion podcasts that will help you stay ahead of the curve.

1. ‘The Business of Fashion Podcast’:
Hosted by Imran Amed, the founder and editor-in-chief of The Business of Fashion, this podcast delves into the inner workings of the fashion industry. From interviews with industry titans to discussions on sustainability and technology, this podcast provides valuable insights into the business side of fashion.

2. ‘American Fashion Podcast’:
If you’re interested in the American fashion scene, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Charles Beckwith and Cathy Schepis, American Fashion Podcast features interviews with designers, entrepreneurs, and industry insiders, giving listeners a behind-the-scenes look at the American fashion landscape.

3. ‘Fashion No Filter’:
Fashion No Filter, hosted by Monica Ainley and Camille Charriere, offers a fresh perspective on the fashion industry. With a focus on sustainability, diversity, and inclusivity, this podcast explores the social and cultural impact of fashion, making it a must-listen for those interested in the intersection of fashion and society.

4. ‘The Glossy Podcast’:
Hosted by Glossy’s editor-in-chief, Jill Manoff, The Glossy Podcast brings you interviews with fashion and beauty industry leaders. From designers to CEOs, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including brand strategy, innovation, and the future of fashion.

5. ‘Dressed: The History of Fashion’:
If you’re a fashion history buff, ‘Dressed: The History of Fashion’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this podcast explores the fascinating stories behind fashion’s most iconic garments, from the ancient world to the present day.

6. ‘Fashion Revolution Podcast’:
Fashion Revolution is a global movement advocating for a more sustainable and ethical fashion industry. Their podcast brings together experts, activists, and industry insiders to discuss topics such as fair trade, labor rights, and the environmental impact of fashion.

7. ‘The Wardrobe Crisis’:
Hosted by Clare Press, sustainability editor-at-large for Vogue Australia, The Wardrobe Crisis dives deep into the issues surrounding fast fashion and offers practical tips on how to build a more conscious and sustainable wardrobe.

8. ‘The Style Success Podcast’:
If you’re looking for fashion advice and styling tips, The Style Success Podcast is a great resource. Hosted by Mallory Sills, a certified personal stylist, this podcast covers everything from body positivity to building a versatile wardrobe.

9. ‘The Cut on Tuesdays’:
From New York Magazine’s The Cut, this podcast covers a range of topics, including fashion, culture, and current events. With a unique and engaging format, The Cut on Tuesdays is sure to keep you entertained and informed.

10. ‘Fashion Is Your Business’:
Hosted by a team of industry experts, Fashion Is Your Business takes a deep dive into the world of fashion technology and innovation. From wearable tech to e-commerce, this podcast explores how technology is shaping the future of fashion.

By tuning in to these fashion podcasts, you’ll gain valuable insights, discover new perspectives, and stay up to date with the latest trends and news in the fashion industry. So grab your headphones and get ready to stay stylishly informed!

Please note: The inclusion of these podcasts does not constitute an endorsement by the author or this blog. We recommend exploring these podcasts to find the ones that resonate with your personal interests and values.
