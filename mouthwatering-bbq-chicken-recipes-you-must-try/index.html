Mouthwatering BBQ Chicken Recipes You Must Try


Barbecue chicken is a timeless classic that never fails to please. The smoky, tender meat combined with deliciously tangy and flavorsome sauces make for a mouthwatering dining experience. Whether you’re hosting a backyard get-together, enjoying a family meal, or just craving something delicious, these BBQ chicken recipes are sure to impress. In this blog post, we will be sharing some of the most delicious and easy-to-make BBQ chicken recipes that you must try. Get ready to fire up your grill and prepare to be amazed!

1. Classic BBQ Chicken:

Let’s start with a classic BBQ chicken recipe that never goes out of style. To make this mouthwatering dish, you’ll need:

- 4 chicken breasts
- ½ cup of BBQ sauce
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
3. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil.
4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side.
5. Brush BBQ sauce onto the chicken during the last few minutes of cooking.
6. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

2. Sticky Asian BBQ Chicken:

Looking to add an Asian twist to your BBQ chicken? Try this sticky and flavorful recipe. Here’s what you’ll need:

- 8 chicken drumsticks
- ¼ cup of hoisin sauce
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons of honey
- 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
2. In a bowl, whisk together the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, grated ginger, and minced garlic.
3. Place the chicken drumsticks in a resealable bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and let the chicken marinate in the fridge for at least 1 hour.
4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and grill for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.
5. Brush the chicken with the remaining marinade during the last few minutes of cooking.
6. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the chicken before serving.

3. Spicy BBQ Chicken Skewers:

If you’re a fan of spicy foods, these BBQ chicken skewers are perfect for you. Here’s what you’ll need:

- 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
- ½ cup of BBQ sauce
- 2 tablespoons of hot sauce (adjust according to your taste)
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 1 teaspoon of paprika
- 1 teaspoon of chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Wooden skewers

Instructions:

1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
2. In a bowl, mix together the BBQ sauce, hot sauce, olive oil, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Thread the chicken cubes onto the wooden skewers.
4. Brush the chicken skewers with the BBQ sauce mixture, making sure to coat all sides.
5. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally and brushing with more sauce.
6. Remove the skewers from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Conclusion:

These mouthwatering BBQ chicken recipes are sure to leave your taste buds wanting more. From the classic BBQ chicken to the sticky Asian-inspired dish and the spicy skewers, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, fire up your grill, gather your friends and family, and indulge in these delicious recipes. Trust us, you won’t be disappointed!
