The Funniest Podcasts You Need to Subscribe to Right Now


Are you tired of the same old jokes and repetitive humor? Do you find yourself scrolling endlessly through podcast after podcast, searching for something that will make you laugh out loud? Look no further! We’ve compiled a list of the funniest podcasts that are guaranteed to keep you entertained and laughing.

1. The Comedy Bang! Bang! Podcast

Hosted by Scott Aukerman, The Comedy Bang! Bang! Podcast is a hilarious blend of improv and stand-up comedy. Each episode features a different celebrity guest, who joins in on the fun and engages in off-the-wall conversations and absurd games. With its quick-witted humor and unpredictable nature, this podcast is a must-listen for comedy enthusiasts.

2. The Joe Rogan Experience

Joe Rogan, the comedian and martial artist, invites a wide range of guests, from fellow comedians to scientists and musicians, for candid and often hilarious conversations. With its long-form format, The Joe Rogan Experience allows for in-depth discussions and funny moments that you won’t find anywhere else.

3. My Dad Wrote A Porno

If you’re in the mood for something truly unique and laugh-out-loud funny, look no further than My Dad Wrote A Porno. Jamie Morton, along with his friends Alice Levine and James Cooper, take turns reading a chapter from Jamie’s dad’s erotic novel and provide hilarious commentary along the way. With its cringe-worthy dialogue and unintentional humor, this podcast is an absolute gem.

4. How Did This Get Made?

Ever wondered how some of the worst movies ever made actually got made? How Did This Get Made? is the podcast that answers that question. Hosted by Paul Scheer, Jason Mantzoukas, and June Diane Raphael, this podcast features the hosts dissecting and ridiculing the plot holes, bad acting, and bizarre choices of some of the most notorious films in history. Prepare to have your sides splitting from laughter!

5. Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend

Conan O’Brien, the beloved late-night talk show host, invites a multitude of guests onto his podcast to find out if he can make a real friend. With his quick wit and self-deprecating humor, Conan engages in hilarious conversations that are equally entertaining and genuine. This podcast is a refreshing and hilarious look at one of the most well-known personalities in comedy.

6. The Dollop

Hosted by Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, The Dollop takes a unique approach to storytelling by focusing on strange and often outrageous moments in history. Dave provides the research and knowledge, while Gareth brings the humor and improvisation. Together, they create a podcast that is educational and side-splittingly funny.

7. How To Fail With Elizabeth Day

While not a traditional comedy podcast, How To Fail deserves a spot on this list for its candid and funny conversations about failure. Host Elizabeth Day interviews different guests who open up about their biggest failures and what they’ve learned from them. The conversations are honest, inspiring, and often accompanied by bursts of laughter. This podcast perfectly blends humor with introspection.

8. No Such Thing As A Fish

If you love trivia and interesting facts, No Such Thing As A Fish is the podcast for you. Hosted by the researchers behind the hit British TV show QI, this podcast presents bizarre and hilarious facts from around the world. The hosts engage in witty banter and provide fascinating insights, making this podcast both educational and side-splittingly funny.

9. The Office Ladies

For fans of the hit TV show The Office, The Office Ladies podcast is a must-listen. Angela Kinsey and Jenna Fischer, who played Angela and Pam on the show, rewatch each episode and provide behind-the-scenes anecdotes and hilarious stories. This podcast is a treasure trove of inside jokes and nostalgia for die-hard fans.

10. The Ricky Gervais Show

Last but certainly not least, The Ricky Gervais Show is a classic podcast that has stood the test of time. Featuring Ricky Gervais, Stephen Merchant, and Karl Pilkington, this podcast is a hilarious mix of absurd conversations, witty banter, and Karl’s unique take on the world. Their chemistry and humor make this podcast a timeless gem.

So there you have it, the funniest podcasts that you need to subscribe to right now. Whether you’re a fan of improv, stand-up comedy, storytelling, or quirky conversations, there’s something for everyone on this list. These podcasts are sure to brighten your day and have you laughing uncontrollably. So go ahead, subscribe and get ready for some serious laughter!
