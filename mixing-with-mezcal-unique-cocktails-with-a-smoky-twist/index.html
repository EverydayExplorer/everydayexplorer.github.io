Mixing with Mezcal: Unique Cocktails with a Smoky Twist


Mezcal, with its distinct smoky flavor, has been gaining popularity among cocktail enthusiasts in recent years. This Mexican spirit, made from the heart of the agave plant, offers a unique twist to traditional mixed drinks. If you’re looking to elevate your cocktail game and experiment with bold flavors, mixing with mezcal is the way to go. In this blog post, we’ll explore some creative and delicious mezcal cocktails that are sure to impress your guests.

1. Smoky Margarita:
A mezcal twist on the classic margarita, this cocktail combines the smoky notes of mezcal with the tangy sweetness of lime and the subtle bitterness of orange liqueur. Shake together 2 ounces of mezcal, 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and 1 ounce of orange liqueur with ice. Strain into a salt-rimmed glass and garnish with a lime wedge. The result is a smoky, refreshing drink that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

2. Mezcal Old Fashioned:
For those who enjoy the timeless elegance of an Old Fashioned, adding mezcal brings a new level of complexity. Start by muddling a sugar cube and a few dashes of aromatic bitters in a glass. Add 2 ounces of mezcal and stir with ice until well chilled. Strain the mixture into a rocks glass over fresh ice and garnish with an orange twist. The smokiness of the mezcal adds depth and richness to this classic cocktail.

3. Mezcal Negroni:
The Negroni is a beloved cocktail, known for its bitter and herbal flavors. By swapping out the traditional gin for mezcal, you can create a smoky twist on this iconic drink. Combine equal parts mezcal, sweet vermouth, and Campari in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until well chilled, then strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice. Finish with an orange twist for a touch of citrus aroma. The result is a complex and intriguing cocktail that will leave a lasting impression.

4. Oaxaca Mule:
For a mezcal-infused take on the classic Moscow Mule, try the Oaxaca Mule. In a copper mug filled with ice, combine 2 ounces of mezcal, 1 ounce of fresh lime juice, and 4 ounces of ginger beer. Stir gently to combine, and garnish with a lime wheel. The smoky mezcal adds a unique depth to the refreshing ginger and lime flavors, creating a cocktail that is both vibrant and intriguing.

5. Spicy Mezcal Paloma:
The Paloma is a popular tequila-based cocktail, but swapping tequila for mezcal gives it a spicy twist. In a glass filled with ice, combine 2 ounces of mezcal, the juice of half a grapefruit, the juice of half a lime, and a splash of soda water. Stir gently to combine, and garnish with a slice of grapefruit. The smokiness of the mezcal pairs beautifully with the tart grapefruit and the subtle heat of chili flakes, creating a refreshing and fiery cocktail.

Mixing with mezcal opens up a world of possibilities for cocktail enthusiasts. Its smoky flavor profile adds a unique twist to classic recipes, allowing you to explore new and exciting taste sensations. Whether you prefer a margarita with a smoky kick, a mezcal-infused twist on an Old Fashioned, or a spicy Paloma, mezcal cocktails are sure to impress. So go ahead, grab a bottle of mezcal, and get creative with your cocktail creations. Cheers to mixing with mezcal!
