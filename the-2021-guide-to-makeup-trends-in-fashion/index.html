The 2021 Guide to Makeup Trends in Fashion


With each passing year, the world of fashion undergoes significant changes and transformations. This holds true not only for clothing and accessories but also for makeup trends. Makeup plays a crucial role in enhancing one’s natural beauty and complementing their overall look. Therefore, keeping up with the latest makeup trends is essential for anyone who wants to stay in line with the fashion industry.

In 2021, we have witnessed a plethora of exciting and groundbreaking makeup trends that have taken the fashion world by storm. From bold and colorful eye looks to effortless natural beauty, there is something for everyone. In this comprehensive guide, we will delve into the hottest makeup looks of the year and provide you with tips on how to recreate them.

1. Dewy Skin
The ’no-makeup’ makeup look has been popular for years, and in 2021, it has reached new heights. The focus is on achieving a fresh and dewy complexion that appears effortlessly flawless. To achieve this look, opt for lightweight, sheer foundation or a tinted moisturizer. Add a touch of highlighter to the high points of your face for a luminous glow.

2. Graphic Eyeliner
Bold and graphic eyeliner designs have become a major trend in 2021. Whether it’s a sleek winged cat-eye or avant-garde geometric shapes, the key is to make a statement with your eyeliner. Experiment with different colors and shapes to add a playful element to your makeup look.

3. Pop of Color
Colorful eyeshadow and vibrant eyeliner have also made a comeback this year. From pastel shades to neon hues, adding a pop of color to your eyes can instantly elevate your look and make a bold statement. Don’t be afraid to play with different color combinations and embrace your creativity.

4. Glossy Lips
Move over matte lips; glossy lips are back in trend. This year, the focus is on achieving plump and luscious lips with a high-shine finish. Choose a lip gloss or opt for a glossy lipstick to achieve this look. Experiment with different shades to find the perfect glossy lip color that suits your skin tone.

5. Feathered Brows
Gone are the days of perfectly sculpted and filled-in brows. In 2021, feathered brows have taken center stage. The aim is to achieve a natural and feathery look by brushing the brow hairs upwards and filling in any sparse areas with a light hand. This creates a more soft and effortless look.

6. Monochromatic Makeup
For a harmonious and cohesive look, monochromatic makeup has become a go-to trend in 2021. The concept is simple – use the same shade for your eyes, cheeks, and lips. This creates a sense of balance and uniformity in your makeup look. Experiment with different shades and textures to find your perfect monochromatic match.

7. Blush Draping
Blush draping, a technique from the 70s, has made a comeback this year. It involves incorporating blush as a contouring element by sweeping it from the cheeks up toward the temples. This creates a sculpted and lifted effect while adding a flush of color to the face. Choose a blush shade that complements your skin tone and blends seamlessly into your overall look.

8. Negative Space Eyeshadow
Negative space eyeshadow is a trend that allows you to get creative with your eye makeup. It involves leaving a portion of the eyelid bare while applying eyeshadow around it. This results in a unique and artistic look that is sure to turn heads. Experiment with different color combinations and shapes to create a personalized negative space eyeshadow design.

9. Glitter Everything
If there is one thing that can instantly elevate any makeup look, it’s glitter. Glittery eyeshadow, sparkly highlighter, or shimmer-packed lip gloss, the options are endless. Embrace your inner sparkle and experiment with different glittery products to add a touch of glamour to your makeup look.

10. Soft Smoky Eyes
The smoky eye has always been a classic makeup look, and in 2021, it has taken a softer turn. Instead of the intense and dramatic smoky eye, opt for a softer and more diffused version. Use neutral tones or soft earthy shades to create a blended and sultry look.

Now that you’re armed with the knowledge of the hottest makeup trends of 2021, it’s time to experiment and have fun. Remember, makeup is a form of self-expression and should be a reflection of your unique style and personality. Whether you prefer a natural and minimal look or enjoy going all out with bold and creative makeup, there is a trend for everyone. So go ahead, play with colors, embrace glitter, and make a statement with your makeup choices.
