The Top 10 Football Players to Watch in the Premier League


When it comes to the Premier League, there is no shortage of talented football players showcasing their skills on the pitch. The level of competition in the league is incredibly high, and fans are always on the lookout for the next big star. In this blog post, we will be taking a look at the top 10 football players to watch in the Premier League. These players have not only proven their abilities in previous seasons, but they continue to impress with their performances week in and week out.

1. Kevin De Bruyne (Manchester City):
Starting our list is Kevin De Bruyne, the Belgian maestro who has been a key figure for Manchester City. De Bruyne’s vision, passing ability, and long-range shooting make him a nightmare for any opposing defense. He has consistently been one of the best midfielders in the Premier League and is a player to keep an eye on.

2. Mohamed Salah (Liverpool):
Ever since his arrival at Liverpool, Mohamed Salah has been on fire. The Egyptian forward’s pace, dribbling skills, and clinical finishing have made him one of the deadliest attackers in the league. Salah’s consistency in front of goal has been crucial to Liverpool’s success, and he is undoubtedly a player to watch this season.

3. Harry Kane (Tottenham Hotspur):
Harry Kane is a player who needs no introduction. The English striker has been a consistent goal scorer for Tottenham Hotspur and has established himself as one of the best forwards in the league. Kane’s ability to score from almost anywhere on the pitch makes him a constant threat to opposing defenses.

4. Bruno Fernandes (Manchester United):
Since joining Manchester United in January 2020, Bruno Fernandes has had a massive impact on the team. The Portuguese midfielder’s creativity, passing range, and goal-scoring ability have revitalized United’s attack. Fernandes is a player with immense talent and a player to watch in the Premier League.

5. Jack Grealish (Aston Villa):
Jack Grealish has been a shining star for Aston Villa. The English midfielder’s dribbling skills, vision, and ability to create chances for his teammates make him a joy to watch. Grealish’s performances have caught the attention of top clubs, and he is definitely a player to keep an eye on.

6. Sadio Mane (Liverpool):
Sadio Mane has been a key player for Liverpool in recent seasons. The Senegalese forward’s incredible pace, skill, and goal-scoring ability have made him an indispensable asset to the team. Mane’s ability to create opportunities out of nothing is what makes him a player worth watching in the Premier League.

7. Raheem Sterling (Manchester City):
Raheem Sterling has been a consistent performer for Manchester City. The English forward’s speed, dribbling skills, and goal-scoring ability have made him a nightmare for opposing defenses. Sterling’s ability to make an impact in big games makes him a player to watch in the Premier League.

8. Son Heung-min (Tottenham Hotspur):
Son Heung-min has been a standout player for Tottenham Hotspur. The South Korean forward’s pace, dribbling skills, and clinical finishing have made him a fan favorite. Son’s ability to score goals from both inside and outside the box makes him a player worth watching in the Premier League.

9. Trent Alexander-Arnold (Liverpool):
Trent Alexander-Arnold has been a revelation for Liverpool. The young English right-back’s crossing ability, vision, and defensive skills have made him one of the best in the league. Alexander-Arnold’s ability to contribute both defensively and offensively makes him a player to watch this season.

10. Phil Foden (Manchester City):
Phil Foden is a rising star for Manchester City. The English midfielder’s technical skill, vision, and ability to score goals have caught the attention of many. Foden’s performances for both club and country have marked him as one to watch in the Premier League.

In conclusion, the Premier League is filled with talented football players who continue to entertain and amaze fans with their skills. These top 10 players mentioned in this blog post have proven their abilities on the pitch and are players worth watching this season. Whether it’s their goal-scoring prowess, dribbling skills, or creative passing, these players are sure to provide fans with thrilling moments throughout the Premier League season.
