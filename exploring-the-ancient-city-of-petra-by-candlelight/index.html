Exploring the Ancient City of Petra by Candlelight


When it comes to marvels of the ancient world, few destinations captivate the imagination quite like the city of Petra. Nestled in the heart of Jordan, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is a breathtaking testament to the rich history and impressive architectural prowess of the Nabataeans. While Petra’s elaborate rock-cut architecture is awe-inspiring during the day, it takes on an entirely new level of enchantment when explored by candlelight.

Every year, thousands of travelers from around the globe flock to Petra to witness the magical experience of the Petra by Candlelight event. On select nights, the ancient city opens its doors after sunset to transport visitors back in time through a mesmerizing path illuminated solely by the soft glow of thousands of candles. This unique journey allows visitors to immerse themselves in the ancient world and truly appreciate the architectural wonders of Petra.

As the sun sets behind the sandstone cliffs of Wadi Musa, the entrance to Petra comes alive with flickering lights, casting an ethereal glow on the rose-colored rocks. Walking through the narrow and winding Siq, a canyon-like pathway, visitors feel a sense of anticipation as they inch closer to the ancient city. The narrow passage, at times barely wide enough to accommodate two people, is cloaked in darkness, with only the subtle illumination of flickering candlelight guiding the way.

Emerging from the Siq, visitors are greeted by the iconic Treasury, known as Al-Khazneh. This grand facade carved into the rock face is magnificently illuminated, creating an otherworldly sight that leaves visitors in awe. Countless candles adorn the area, casting a warm glow and providing an intimate atmosphere that allows visitors to appreciate the intricate details of the Treasury in a new light.

Beyond the Treasury, the candlelit path winds its way through the ancient city, passing by remarkable structures such as the Street of Facades, the Theater, and the Royal Tombs. Each of these architectural marvels takes on a new dimension as the candlelight illuminates their intricate carvings and highlights their unique features.

One of the highlights of the Petra by Candlelight experience is the opportunity to witness a traditional Bedouin performance. Bedouin musicians and dancers gather in the candlelit darkness, captivating the audience with their enchanting music and graceful movements. This cultural display adds an extra layer of authenticity and allows visitors to immerse themselves in the rich traditions of the region.

While Petra is undoubtedly magnificent during the day, exploring it by candlelight brings a whole new level of enchantment. The absence of daylight allows visitors to see the city in a different perspective, showcasing its intricate details and architectural splendor in a more intimate setting. The flickering light of the candles creates a sense of mystery and adds to the allure of this ancient wonder.

Planning a visit to Petra by Candlelight requires careful consideration. The event takes place only on specific evenings, usually on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays. It is advisable to check the official Petra website or consult with local tour operators for the most up-to-date information on schedules and ticket availability.

If you’re lucky enough to witness Petra by Candlelight, be sure to dress appropriately for the occasion. Comfortable walking shoes, layered clothing, and a flashlight are essential for navigating the dark paths. It’s also wise to bring a camera or smartphone to capture the captivating moments, although the experience itself is best enjoyed when fully present and immersed in the magical ambiance.

Exploring Petra by candlelight offers a unique opportunity to connect with the ancient city and appreciate its beauty in a truly extraordinary way. Whether you are a history enthusiast, an adventure seeker, or simply someone looking for an unforgettable experience, Petra by Candlelight is an absolute must-see. The combination of ancient architectural wonders, soft candlelight, and cultural performances creates an unforgettable atmosphere that will leave you in awe of this magnificent ancient city. Discover the magic of Petra like never before and create memories that will last a lifetime.
