Bookstores around the World: Must-Visit Destinations for Bookworms


There’s something magical about walking into a bookstore. The faint smell of old and new books, the sight of endless aisles filled with literary treasures, and the feeling of curiosity buzzing in the air make every trip to a bookstore a unique experience for book lovers. If you’re a true bookworm, then you’re going to love discovering bookstores around the world that are considered must-visit destinations for bibliophiles. In this blog post, we are going to explore some of the most captivating and enchanting bookstores that will leave you in awe and inspire your next reading adventure.

1. Shakespeare and Company - Paris, France

Located in the heart of Paris, Shakespeare and Company is not just a bookstore; it’s a literary institution. Established in 1951, this iconic bookstore has become a sanctuary for writers, artists, and book lovers from all over the world. With its cozy nooks and crannies, inviting reading corners, and a rich selection of books in different languages, stepping into Shakespeare and Company feels like stepping into a time capsule. It’s also known for its tradition of offering aspiring writers a place to sleep in exchange for working in the bookstore for a few hours each day.

2. Powell’s City of Books - Portland, Oregon, USA

In the land of coffee and hipsters, Powell’s City of Books stands tall as the world’s largest independent bookstore. Spanning an entire city block, this massive bookstore is a true paradise for book enthusiasts. With over one million books, organized by color-coded rooms, Powell’s has a little bit of everything for every kind of reader. From bestsellers to obscure titles, you’ll find it all in this literary wonderland. Don’t forget to grab a cup of coffee and lose yourself in the endless rows of bookshelves!

3. El Ateneo Grand Splendid - Buenos Aires, Argentina

Once an opulent theater, El Ateneo Grand Splendid is now one of the most beautiful bookstores in the world. Located in Buenos Aires, this bookstore will transport you back in time with its stunning architecture and grandeur. The stage is now a cafe where you can sip a coffee while surrounded by thousands of books. Walking through its ornate balconies, under the golden chandeliers, and up to the old theater boxes will make you feel like a literary character in a classic novel.

4. Daunt Books - London, England

In the heart of London’s Marylebone neighborhood, you’ll find Daunt Books, a quaint and charming bookstore known for its Edwardian style and long oak galleries. Daunt Books specializes in travel literature, making it a perfect destination for adventurers and armchair travelers. The bookstore is organized by country, guiding readers on a literary journey around the globe. Pick up a book about your dream destination and let your imagination run wild as you explore this book lover’s paradise.

5. Atlantis Books - Santorini, Greece

Imagine a bookstore overlooking the sparkling blue waters of the Aegean Sea, with the white-washed buildings of Santorini surrounding you. That’s exactly what Atlantis Books offers. This unique bookstore was founded by a group of friends who shared a love for literature and a dream of creating a place where books and beauty meet. With its stunning location and carefully curated collection of books, Atlantis Books is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a tranquil and inspiring reading experience.

6. Selexyz Dominicanen - Maastricht, Netherlands

Housed in a 13th-century Gothic church, Selexyz Dominicanen is a bookstore that seamlessly blends history, spirituality, and literature. The high ceilings and ornate decorations of the church make for a truly awe-inspiring setting to discover new books. While browsing the selection, you can appreciate the remnants of the church, including the beautiful stained glass windows and the grand organ. Whether you’re religious or not, this bookstore is a testament to the power of books to connect people and transcend time.

Conclusion: Embark on a Literary Journey

Bookstores hold a special place in the hearts of bookworms. They are not just places to buy books; they are destinations that spark our imagination, kindle our curiosity, and connect us with like-minded individuals. Whether you find yourself in Paris, Portland, Buenos Aires, London, Santorini, or Maastricht, take the opportunity to explore these enchanting bookstores. Each one offers a unique experience that will surely leave an indelible mark on your bibliophile soul. So, pack your bags, grab your reading list, and embark on a literary journey around the world. Happy reading!
