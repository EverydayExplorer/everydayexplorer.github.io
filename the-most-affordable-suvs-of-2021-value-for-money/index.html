The Most Affordable SUVs of 2021: Value for Money


The auto industry is constantly evolving, and buyers have a wide range of options when it comes to purchasing a new car. With an increasing number of SUVs hitting the market, finding the most affordable SUV with the best value for money can be a daunting task. Luckily, we have done the research for you and compiled a list of the most affordable SUVs of 2021. Whether you are a budget-conscious buyer or simply looking for a great deal, these SUVs offer excellent features at an affordable price point.

1. Hyundai Venue

Starting at just under 0,000, the Hyundai Venue offers impressive value for money. This compact SUV comes loaded with features such as an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and a rearview camera. With its stylish design and fuel efficiency of up to 32 MPG, the Hyundai Venue is perfect for urban dwellers looking for an affordable and practical SUV.

2. Kia Seltos

The Kia Seltos is another top pick for budget-conscious buyers. Starting at around 1,000, this compact SUV offers a spacious interior, user-friendly technology, and a comfortable ride. It comes equipped with a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system, smartphone connectivity, and an optional all-wheel drive. With its stylish and modern design, the Kia Seltos gives you the feel of a more expensive SUV at an affordable price.

3. Nissan Rogue Sport

The Nissan Rogue Sport is a versatile and affordable compact SUV that starts at around 4,000. It offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and plenty of cargo space. The Rogue Sport comes with advanced safety features such as forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and blind-spot monitoring. With its fuel efficiency of up to 31 MPG, the Rogue Sport is an excellent choice for budget-conscious buyers who value safety and practicality.

4. Honda CR-V

The Honda CR-V has long been a popular choice among SUV buyers, and for good reason. Starting at around 5,000, this compact SUV offers a comfortable and spacious interior, excellent fuel efficiency, and a reputation for reliability. It comes with a range of advanced safety features such as adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking. With its strong resale value and low cost of ownership, the Honda CR-V is a great value for money SUV.

5. Toyota RAV4

The Toyota RAV4 is known for its reliability, and the 2021 model continues this tradition. Starting at around 6,000, the RAV4 offers a spacious and well-built interior, excellent fuel efficiency, and a host of standard features. It comes with a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, as well as advanced safety features such as automatic emergency braking, lane-keeping assist, and adaptive cruise control. With its strong reputation for reliability and resale value, the Toyota RAV4 is a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers.

6. Subaru Crosstrek

The Subaru Crosstrek is a compact SUV that offers a rugged design, standard all-wheel drive, and excellent resale value. Starting at just under 3,000, the Crosstrek comes equipped with advanced safety features such as pre-collision braking, adaptive cruise control, and lane-keeping assist. With its competent off-road capabilities and fuel efficiency of up to 33 MPG, the Subaru Crosstrek is a great value for money SUV for those who love outdoor adventures.

7. Mazda CX-5

The Mazda CX-5 is a stylish and fun-to-drive compact SUV that starts at around 6,000. It offers a premium interior, advanced safety features, and a smooth and refined ride. The CX-5 comes with a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and a range of driver assistance features such as blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert. With its upscale design and impressive handling, the Mazda CX-5 offers excellent value for money.

In conclusion, the SUV market has plenty of affordable options that offer great value for money. From compact SUVs to more spacious models, these choices provide comfort, safety, and modern features at a budget-friendly price. Whether you prefer a stylish design, fuel efficiency, or advanced safety features, there is an SUV on this list that will suit your needs. So, if you’re in the market for a new SUV in 2021, keep these affordable options in mind and make the most out of your hard-earned money.
