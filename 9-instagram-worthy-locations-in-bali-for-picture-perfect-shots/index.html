9 Instagram-Worthy Locations in Bali for Picture-Perfect Shots


Bali, the picturesque island in Indonesia, has become a top destination for Instagrammers and travel enthusiasts alike. With its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and undeniable charm, Bali offers endless opportunities for capturing picture-perfect shots. Whether you’re an avid photographer or simply seeking Instagram-worthy locations for your feed, Bali will not disappoint. In this blog post, we will explore nine of the most eye-catching spots in Bali that are sure to make your followers go ‘wow!’

1. Pura Lempuyang Temple:
Located in East Bali, Pura Lempuyang Temple is one of the most iconic and photogenic places on the island. The temple is known for its famous ‘Gates of Heaven,’ which frame Mount Agung, Bali’s highest volcano. The combination of the temple’s intricate architecture, the majestic backdrop of the volcano, and the mystical atmosphere will undoubtedly result in awe-inspiring photos.

2. Tegalalang Rice Terraces:
Situated in Ubud, the Tegalalang Rice Terraces are a sight to behold. These beautifully sculpted rice paddies offer a breathtaking view of Bali’s lush green landscapes. Whether you capture the terraces from the viewpoint or venture into the fields for a more immersive experience, this location provides ample opportunities for stunning nature shots.

3. Sekumpul Waterfall:
For those seeking an adventure, Sekumpul Waterfall is a must-visit. Located in the northern part of Bali, this seven-tiered waterfall cascades through the tropical jungle, creating an enchanting backdrop. The trek to the waterfall might be challenging, but the panoramic views and the chance to capture the raw beauty of nature are worth every step.

4. Handara Gate:
The Handara Gate, located near Bedugul, has gained massive popularity on Instagram, and for a good reason. This towering gate is surrounded by lush greenery and is adorned with intricate Balinese designs. Whether you pose underneath the gate or capture it from a distance, this spot will make your photos stand out and transport your followers into a mystical realm.

5. Tirta Gangga Water Palace:
Tirta Gangga, meaning ‘Water of the Ganges,’ is a serene and elegant water palace located in East Bali. The palace showcases stunning water features, lush gardens, and exquisite Balinese architecture. Strolling through the palace’s grounds, you can capture countless picture-perfect moments amidst the lotus-filled ponds, fountains, and stone statues.

6. Bali Swing:
The Bali Swing has recently become a hot spot for adrenaline junkies and Instagrammers alike. Located in Ubud, this thrilling attraction offers a chance to soar above the treetops while capturing a bird’s-eye view of the jungle. Whether you’re swinging solo or posing for the perfect couple shot, the Bali Swing is sure to add an element of excitement to your Instagram feed.

7. Tanah Lot Temple:
Perched on a rocky outcrop, Tanah Lot Temple is one of Bali’s most iconic and picturesque sea temples. The temple’s unique location, with crashing waves and shimmering sunsets as a backdrop, makes it a paradise for photographers. Timing is crucial here, as the best photo opportunities arise during low tide or at sunset when the temple is beautifully silhouetted against the vibrant colors of the sky.

8. Nusa Penida:
Nusa Penida, a neighboring island to Bali, boasts some of the most jaw-dropping landscapes you’ll ever encounter. From the famous Kelingking Beach to the enchanting Broken Beach and the stunning Angel’s Billabong, Nusa Penida is a treasure trove of Instagram-worthy locations. Whether you’re standing on the cliff’s edge, capturing the turquoise waters below, or exploring the natural rock formations, Nusa Penida guarantees awe-inspiring shots.

9. Tegenungan Waterfall:
Conveniently located near Ubud, Tegenungan Waterfall is a favorite among photography enthusiasts. This breathtaking waterfall plunges into a pool surrounded by lush green vegetation, creating a picturesque scene straight out of a fairy tale. The misty cascades combined with the refreshing pool make for a mesmerizing experience and provide ample opportunities for stunning photographs.

In conclusion, Bali offers a plethora of Instagram-worthy locations that will take your photography to the next level. From ancient temples and enchanting waterfalls to stunning rice terraces and breathtaking cliffs, this island has it all. So pack your camera, explore these nine picturesque spots, and capture the beauty of Bali to share with your Instagram followers. Get ready to create an envy-inducing feed that will transport your followers to the exotic and breathtaking island of Bali!
