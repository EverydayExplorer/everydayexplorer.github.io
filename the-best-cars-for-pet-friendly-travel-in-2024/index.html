The Best Cars for Pet-Friendly Travel in 2024


When it comes to traveling with pets, having the right car can make all the difference. In 2024, pet-friendly travel is more popular than ever, and car manufacturers have been paying attention. From spacious interiors to pet-friendly accessories, there are plenty of options for pet owners looking to hit the road with their furry companions. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best cars for pet-friendly travel in 2024, taking into account factors such as comfort, safety, and convenience for both pets and their owners.

1. Subaru Outback: The Subaru Outback has long been a favorite among pet owners for its spacious interior, rugged design, and standard all-wheel drive. With ample cargo space and a low load floor, it’s easy for pets to climb in and out of the vehicle. Additionally, Subaru offers a range of pet-friendly accessories, such as seat protectors and pet harnesses, making it an ideal choice for pet owners on the go.

2. Honda CR-V: The Honda CR-V is known for its reliability and family-friendly features, making it a great option for pet owners as well. With a roomy interior and a low liftover height, it’s easy for pets to hop in and out of the vehicle. The CR-V also offers a range of pet-friendly accessories, including a rear seat cover and a cargo tray, to keep your car clean and your pets comfortable during travel.

3. Volvo V60 Cross Country: For pet owners who prioritize safety and luxury, the Volvo V60 Cross Country is an excellent choice. With a spacious and well-appointed interior, as well as Volvo’s reputation for safety, this car provides a comfortable and secure environment for pets. Additionally, Volvo offers a range of pet-friendly accessories, such as a dog gate and a protective steel grille, to ensure that pets are safe and secure while on the road.

4. Tesla Model Y: As electric vehicles become more popular, pet owners may be wondering if there are options for them as well. The Tesla Model Y offers a spacious interior, ample cargo space, and a range of pet-friendly features, such as a rear cargo cover and a pet mode that ensures a comfortable climate for pets when left in the car. With zero emissions and advanced safety features, the Model Y is a great choice for eco-conscious pet owners.

5. Toyota RAV4: The Toyota RAV4 is a practical and versatile option for pet-friendly travel, with a spacious interior and a range of pet-friendly accessories, including a pet ramp and a cargo liner. Toyota’s reputation for reliability and affordability makes the RAV4 a popular choice for pet owners looking for a budget-friendly and pet-friendly vehicle.

In conclusion, there are plenty of options for pet-friendly travel in 2024, with car manufacturers offering a range of features and accessories to accommodate pets and their owners. Whether you prioritize space, safety, luxury, or eco-friendliness, there’s a car out there to suit your needs for pet-friendly travel. With the right vehicle, traveling with pets can be a comfortable and enjoyable experience for everyone involved.
