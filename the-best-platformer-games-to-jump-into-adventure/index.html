The Best Platformer Games to Jump into Adventure


Platformer games have long been a staple in the world of gaming, captivating players of all ages with their engaging gameplay and thrilling adventures. From classic titles to modern masterpieces, platformers have a way of bringing out the kid in all of us as we navigate treacherous terrains, overcome challenging obstacles, and ultimately save the day.

With so many platformer games available, it can be overwhelming to decide which ones to jump into. That’s why we’ve compiled a list of the best platformer games that are sure to keep you hooked from start to finish. Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or new to the genre, these titles are bound to provide you with hours of fun and excitement.

1. Super Mario Odyssey: No list of platformer games would be complete without mentioning the iconic Super Mario franchise. Super Mario Odyssey takes players on a grand adventure as they join Mario on his quest to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser. With its stunning visuals, innovative gameplay mechanics, and vast open-world environments, this game is a must-play for any platformer fan.

2. Hollow Knight: If you’re looking for a platformer with a darker and more atmospheric tone, Hollow Knight is the game for you. Set in the eerie underground kingdom of Hallownest, players navigate a hauntingly beautiful world filled with unique creatures, challenging boss battles, and secrets to uncover. With its precise controls and compelling storyline, Hollow Knight is a standout in the genre.

3. Celeste: Prepare yourself for a heartfelt and emotionally charged platforming experience with Celeste. This indie gem tells the story of Madeline, a young girl embarking on a journey to climb the mythical Celeste Mountain. As players navigate through pixel-perfect levels and encounter a variety of obstacles, they’ll also unravel Madeline’s personal struggles. With its tight controls and beautiful soundtrack, Celeste is a masterclass in both gameplay and storytelling.

4. Ori and the Blind Forest: Immerse yourself in a stunningly beautiful world with Ori and the Blind Forest. This enchanting platformer follows the adventures of Ori, a guardian spirit, as they navigate through a gorgeously hand-painted forest. With its captivating narrative, breathtaking visuals, and challenging gameplay, Ori and the Blind Forest is a must-play for any platformer enthusiast.

5. Rayman Legends: If you’re looking for a platformer game that’s just pure fun, then look no further than Rayman Legends. With its whimsical art style, fast-paced gameplay, and a wide variety of levels to explore, this game is perfect for gamers of all ages. Whether you’re playing alone or with friends in co-op mode, Rayman Legends is sure to bring a smile to your face.

6. Shovel Knight: Paying homage to the classic platformers of the past, Shovel Knight delivers a retro-inspired gaming experience with a modern twist. Players take on the role of the titular hero as they wield a trusty shovel to defeat enemies, explore challenging levels, and uncover hidden treasures. With its tight controls, charming art style, and nostalgic gameplay, Shovel Knight is a must-play for fans of old-school platformers.

7. Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze: Join Donkey Kong and his friends on a tropical adventure in Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze. This side-scrolling platformer offers challenging levels, vibrant visuals, and a toe-tapping soundtrack. With its delightful gameplay and nostalgic nods to the Donkey Kong franchise, Tropical Freeze is a must-play for fans of the series and platformer enthusiasts alike.

These are just a few of the many incredible platformer games available today. Whether you’re a fan of the classics or looking for something more modern and innovative, there’s a platformer out there for everyone. So grab your controller, buckle up, and get ready to jump into a world of adventure and excitement!
