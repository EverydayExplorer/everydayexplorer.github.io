Hotel Pools with a View: Dive into Paradise


Imagine this. You’re lounging beside a pool, sipping your favorite cocktail, and soaking up the warm sun. As you look around, you’re surrounded by breathtaking views of pristine beaches, lush mountains, or stunning city skylines. This is the epitome of paradise - a hotel pool with a view.

For many travellers, a hotel pool can make or break their vacation experience. And when that pool comes with a view, it takes their stay to a whole new level. So, if you’re planning your next vacation, here are some incredible hotel pools with a view that will make you feel like you’re diving into paradise.

1. Marina Bay Sands Hotel, Singapore: Perched on top of the world-famous Marina Bay Sands hotel is an iconic infinity pool that offers unparalleled views of the Singapore skyline. From here, you can admire the stunning cityscape, including the futuristic Gardens by the Bay.

2. Tierra Patagonia Hotel & Spa, Chile: Located in the heart of the Torres del Paine National Park, this hotel features a pool that overlooks the vast Patagonian landscape. As you swim, you’ll be mesmerized by the snow-capped mountains, turquoise lakes, and grazing guanacos.

3. Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea, Hawaii: Nestled on the picturesque Wailea Beach, this luxury resort boasts a stunning oceanfront infinity pool. From here, you can bask in the warm Hawaiian sun while enjoying panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and neighboring islands.

4. Belmond Hotel Caruso, Italy: Situated on a cliff above the Amalfi Coast, the infinity pool at Belmond Hotel Caruso offers a dreamy view of the coastline. Surrounded by lush gardens, this pool allows you to unwind while gazing at the azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

5. The Cambrian Adelboden, Switzerland: Tucked away in the Swiss Alps, this hotel offers a magical heated outdoor pool that overlooks snow-covered mountains and sprawling valleys. Soaking in the warm water while surrounded by breathtaking alpine scenery is truly an unforgettable experience.

6. Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia: This award-winning resort is home to one of the most stunning infinity pools in the world. Perched on the edge of a lush jungle, the pool offers sweeping views of the Ayung River and surrounding tropical greenery.

7. Jade Mountain Resort, St. Lucia: Nestled amidst the majestic Pitons, this eco-luxury resort features a series of private infinity pools that provide uninterrupted views of the Caribbean Sea and the lush rainforest. Each pool is designed to blend seamlessly with the natural surroundings, creating a truly immersive experience.

8. The Mulia, Bali: Located on the southern coast of Bali, The Mulia boasts a picturesque infinity pool overlooking the Indian Ocean. With its cascading design and stunning sea views, this pool offers the perfect setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.

9. The Library, Koh Samui, Thailand: This unique boutique resort features a stunning red-tiled infinity pool that is the epitome of tranquility. As you swim, you can admire the breathtaking views of the turquoise Gulf of Thailand and the neighboring islands.

10. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, India: Situated on the banks of Lake Pichola, this luxurious hotel offers a serene pool that appears to blend seamlessly with the lake. From here, you can marvel at the sparkling waters, the majestic City Palace, and the Aravalli Mountains in the distance.

In conclusion, if you’re looking to take your vacation to the next level, consider booking a hotel with a pool that offers breathtaking views. Whether you’re swimming in an infinity pool overlooking a city skyline or lounging beside a pool with panoramic ocean views, these experiences will leave you feeling like you’re diving into paradise. So, pack your swimsuit, grab your sunglasses, and get ready to indulge in the ultimate luxury - a hotel pool with a view.
