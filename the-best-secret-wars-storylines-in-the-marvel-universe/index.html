The Best Secret Wars Storylines in the Marvel Universe


The Marvel Universe is known for its epic crossovers and massive events, and one of the most iconic of these events is undoubtedly Secret Wars. First published in 1984, Secret Wars brought together Marvel’s greatest heroes and villains in a battle for the fate of the universe. Over the years, numerous Secret Wars storylines have captivated readers with their high-stakes action, complex character arcs, and mind-bending twists. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best Secret Wars storylines in the Marvel Universe.

1. Secret Wars (1984-1985)

The original Secret Wars storyline kicks off our list with its groundbreaking concept and massive impact on the Marvel Universe. Written by Jim Shooter and illustrated by Mike Zeck, Secret Wars brings together heroes like Spider-Man, Captain America, Thor, and the X-Men, along with villains like Doctor Doom and Magneto, on an alien planet called Battleworld. Here, they are forced to fight each other under the manipulations of a cosmic being known as The Beyonder.

This storyline not only showcased the grandeur of a superhero crossover but also marked the first appearance of the symbiotic alien entity known as Venom. The ramifications of Secret Wars are still felt in the Marvel Universe today, making it a must-read for any fan.

2. Secret Wars II (1985-1986)

A direct sequel to the original Secret Wars storyline, Secret Wars II takes the Marvel Universe to new heights of complexity and philosophical exploration. Written by Jim Shooter and illustrated by Al Milgrom, this storyline sees the Beyonder arrive on Earth in human form and becomes obsessed with understanding humanity.

While Secret Wars II received mixed reviews upon its release, it remains an important part of the Secret Wars saga, as it delves into the nature of power and the consequences of unchecked ambition. Plus, it features appearances from various Marvel heroes and villains, including the Avengers, X-Men, and Doctor Doom.

3. Secret Wars (2015)

In 2015, Marvel launched a new Secret Wars event, reshaping the entire Marvel Universe and introducing a new Battleworld. Written by Jonathan Hickman with artwork by Esad Ribic, this storyline brings together elements from various Marvel multiverses, resulting in a massive, sprawling crossover that redefines the playing field for the Marvel heroes and villains.

What sets this Secret Wars storyline apart is its scale and ambition. The stakes are higher than ever before, with different versions of Marvel characters colliding and confronting their alternate selves. It also serves as a culmination of various storylines from Jonathan Hickman’s Avengers and New Avengers runs, rewarding long-time readers with an epic conclusion.

4. Secret Wars 2099 (2015)

Spin-offs and tie-ins are a familiar aspect of major events in the Marvel Universe, and one standout example is Secret Wars 2099. Written by Peter David and illustrated by Will Sliney, this storyline takes place in the year 2099 and features futuristic versions of Marvel characters, including Spider-Man, Captain America, and the X-Men.

Secret Wars 2099 offers a fresh take on familiar characters and explores their struggle to survive in a dystopian future. It serves as an excellent entry point for readers who are interested in exploring different iterations of their favorite Marvel heroes.

5. Secret Wars: Journal (2015)

Another intriguing spin-off from the 2015 Secret Wars event is Secret Wars: Journal. This anthology series features short stories written and illustrated by various creative teams, providing a deeper and more personal look into the Battleworld.

Secret Wars: Journal showcases a range of genres, including horror, comedy, and drama, allowing readers to experience different facets of the Secret Wars event. It’s an excellent companion to the main Secret Wars storyline and adds depth and diversity to the overall narrative.

Conclusion

The Marvel Universe is no stranger to epic crossovers, and the Secret Wars storylines have consistently delivered high-stakes action, compelling character arcs, and universe-altering consequences. Whether you’re a long-time Marvel fan or a newcomer to the Marvel Universe, these Secret Wars storylines are sure to captivate and enthrall you. So, grab your favorite comic and prepare to be whisked away to a battle that will shape the destiny of the Marvel Universe!
