10 Essential Gardening Podcasts to Listen to


When it comes to gardening, there’s always something new to learn. Whether you’re a seasoned green thumb or just starting out, podcasts can be a fantastic resource for expanding your gardening knowledge. In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 essential gardening podcasts that are sure to inspire and educate.

1. ‘The Joe Gardener Show’: Hosted by Joe Lamp’l, this podcast covers a wide range of gardening topics, from growing your own food to sustainable practices. Lamp’l brings in experts and shares practical tips that any gardener can benefit from.

2. ‘Gardener’s Question Time’: A long-running BBC radio show turned podcast, ‘Gardener’s Question Time’ features a panel of gardening experts who answer listener questions. It’s a great way to learn from experienced gardeners and get answers to your own queries.

3. ‘The RHS Gardening Podcast’: Produced by the Royal Horticultural Society, this podcast delves into various gardening subjects, including plant care, design ideas, and interviews with renowned horticulturists. It’s a must-listen for anyone looking to enhance their gardening skills.

4. ‘The Garden Log’: Host Ben Dark shares his experiences as a professional gardener and offers insights into the day-to-day challenges and joys of tending to different types of gardens. This podcast provides a unique perspective and plenty of inspiration.

5. ‘The On The Ledge Podcast’: Focusing on indoor gardening, host Jane Perrone shares her expertise on growing plants in limited spaces. From houseplants to terrariums, this podcast is a treasure trove of tips for those with a green thumb indoors.

6. ‘The Urban Farm Podcast’: If you’re interested in urban gardening, this podcast is for you. With a focus on sustainable and self-sufficient practices, host Greg Peterson interviews experts who share their knowledge on topics like permaculture, beekeeping, and urban homesteading.

7. ‘The Organic Gardener Podcast’: Hosted by Jackie Marie Beyer, this podcast explores organic gardening methods and sustainable living. Beyer interviews organic gardeners, farmers, and experts who share their experiences and advice for growing healthy, chemical-free plants.

8. ‘The Native Plant Podcast’: If you’re passionate about native plants and their role in the ecosystem, this podcast is a must-listen. The hosts, John Magee and Mike Berkeley, discuss native plant gardening, conservation, and the benefits of incorporating native plants into your landscape.

9. ‘The Epic Gardening Podcast’: Covering a wide range of gardening topics, host Kevin Espiritu shares practical tips and techniques for growing everything from vegetables to ornamental plants. This podcast is perfect for those looking to maximize their garden’s potential.

10. ‘The Timber Press Podcast’: Hosted by the team behind Timber Press, a renowned publisher of gardening books, this podcast features interviews with gardening authors, industry professionals, and experts. It’s a great way to stay up to date with the latest trends and discoveries in the gardening world.

These 10 essential gardening podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge and inspiration for both beginners and experienced gardeners alike. With their diverse range of topics and expert hosts, you’ll be sure to find something that piques your interest and helps you take your gardening skills to the next level. So grab your headphones, get out in the garden, and start listening to these fantastic gardening podcasts. Happy listening and happy gardening!
