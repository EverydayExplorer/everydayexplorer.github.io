The Ultimate Guide to Highlighter: Where and How to Apply


Are you looking to add a touch of glow to your makeup routine? Look no further than highlighter! This magical product can instantly enhance your features and give you that coveted lit-from-within radiance. But with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right highlighter and apply it correctly. That’s why we’ve put together the ultimate guide to highlighter, where we’ll show you where and how to apply it for a flawless and luminous finish.

Before we dive into the application techniques, let’s start with the basics. Highlighter is a makeup product that comes in different forms such as powder, liquid, cream, or stick. It contains light-reflecting particles that create a luminous effect when applied to the high points of your face. These high points include the cheekbones, brow bone, bridge of the nose, cupid’s bow, and the inner corners of your eyes.

Now, let’s move on to the fun part – applying highlighter! Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve a natural and glowing look:

1. Prep your skin: Before applying any makeup, it’s essential to prep your skin. Start by cleansing, toning, and moisturizing your face. This will create a smooth canvas for your highlighter and help it blend seamlessly.

2. Choose the right shade: Highlighters come in various shades, from champagne to rose gold and even holographic hues. The key is to select a shade that complements your skin tone. Fair skin tones generally look best with champagne or pink undertones, while deeper skin tones can rock golden or bronze shades.

3. Find the right formula: Highlighters come in different formulas, each with its own pros and cons. Powder highlighters are great for oily or combination skin as they tend to be long-lasting. Liquid or cream highlighters work well for dry or mature skin as they provide a dewy finish. Experiment with different formulas to find the one that suits your skin type and desired look.

4. Apply with precision: To achieve a natural and sculpted look, use a small, fluffy brush or a damp beauty sponge to apply your highlighter. Start by gently tapping the product onto the high points of your face and blend it outwards. Remember, a little goes a long way, so start with a small amount and build up if needed.

5. Experiment with different areas: While the cheekbones are the most common area to apply highlighter, don’t be afraid to experiment with other areas too. Applying highlighter on the brow bone, bridge of the nose, cupid’s bow, and inner corners of the eyes can create a youthful and radiant effect.

6. Set it in place: To ensure your highlighter stays put all day, lightly dust a translucent powder over the areas where you applied the product. This will help to set it and prevent any unwanted shine.

Remember, makeup is all about experimentation and finding what works best for you. So, don’t be afraid to play around with different techniques and products to achieve your desired glow. With the help of this ultimate guide to highlighter, you’ll be shining bright like a diamond in no time!

Now that you have all the tips and tricks, it’s time to go out and embrace your inner glow. Whether you’re going for a subtle radiance or a blinding highlight, the key is to have fun and feel confident in your own skin. So grab your favorite highlighter and get ready to shine!
