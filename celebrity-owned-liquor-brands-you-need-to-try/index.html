Celebrity-Owned Liquor Brands You Need to Try


There’s no denying that celebrities are constantly branching out into various industries, and one area where they’ve made a significant impact is the liquor industry. From musicians to actors to athletes, many famous faces have launched their own liquor brands, offering consumers a taste of their personal style and flavor preferences. If you’re a fan of both spirits and celebrities, here are some celebrity-owned liquor brands that you definitely need to try.

Ryan Reynolds – Aviation American Gin

Known for his quick wit and charm, Ryan Reynolds added ’entrepreneur’ to his resume when he acquired an ownership stake in Aviation American Gin in 2018. Reynolds was drawn to the smooth and versatile characteristics of the gin, which can be enjoyed neat or mixed in a cocktail. With his signature humor and marketing savvy, Reynolds has helped elevate Aviation Gin to new heights, making it a favorite among gin enthusiasts.

George Clooney – Casamigos Tequila

When it comes to celebrity-owned liquor brands, Casamigos Tequila is undoubtedly one of the most well-known. Founded by George Clooney, along with Rande Gerber and Mike Meldman, Casamigos quickly gained a loyal following for its ultra-smooth and flavorful tequila. Clooney and his partners were inspired to create a tequila that they could enjoy themselves, and their attention to quality and detail has paid off, with Casamigos becoming a staple at bars and restaurants around the world.

Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson – Teremana Tequila

Known for his larger-than-life persona and dedication to fitness, Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson decided to venture into the world of spirits with the launch of Teremana Tequila. Made in the highlands of Jalisco, Mexico, Teremana is a premium tequila that embodies Johnson’s passion for authenticity and tradition. With a commitment to sustainable practices and a focus on quality, Teremana has quickly become a favorite among tequila aficionados.

Jay-Z – Armand de Brignac Champagne

As one of the most successful and influential music artists of all time, Jay-Z has always had a keen eye for business opportunities. In 2014, he acquired Armand de Brignac Champagne, also known as ‘Ace of Spades,’ and has since been instrumental in elevating the brand to new heights. With its distinctive gold bottles and luxurious reputation, Armand de Brignac has become a favorite choice for celebrations and special occasions.

Post Malone – Maison No. 9 Rosé

Known for his eclectic style and chart-topping hits, Post Malone decided to try his hand at the wine business with the launch of Maison No. 9 Rosé. Produced in Provence, France, Maison No. 9 is a refreshing and crisp rosé that reflects Post Malone’s laid-back and artistic aesthetic. With its eye-catching branding and delicious taste, Maison No. 9 has quickly gained popularity among wine lovers.

These are just a few of the celebrity-owned liquor brands that have made a splash in the industry. Whether you’re a fan of gin, tequila, champagne, or rosé, there’s a celebrity-owned brand out there for you to try. So, next time you’re looking to toast to a special occasion or simply enjoy a relaxing drink, consider reaching for a bottle from one of these famous faces. Cheers to the perfect blend of celebrity and spirits!
