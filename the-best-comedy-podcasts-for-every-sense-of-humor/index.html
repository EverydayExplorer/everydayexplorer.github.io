The Best Comedy Podcasts for Every Sense of Humor


Are you tired of endlessly scrolling through your podcast app, searching for the perfect comedy podcast to tickle your funny bone? With the vast array of options available, it can be overwhelming to find the one that suits your unique sense of humor. Fear not, because I’ve compiled a comprehensive list of the best comedy podcasts out there, catering to every conceivable taste. Whether you’re into witty banter, hilarious storytelling, or absurd humor, there’s something on this list for you.

  1. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’: Hosted by comedian Joe Rogan, this podcast features candid conversations with a wide range of guests, from comedians to scientists to athletes. With its mix of humor, intellectual discussion, and occasional absurdity, ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ is perfect for those who enjoy a blend of comedy and thought-provoking content.

  2. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’: If you’re in the mood for something truly unique, look no further than ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno.’ Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this podcast takes listeners on a hilarious journey as Morton reads aloud chapters from the erotic novels written by his father. The result is a side-splitting mix of cringe-worthy prose and witty commentary from the hosts.

  3. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend’: In this podcast, late-night host Conan O’Brien sits down with celebrities and fellow comedians to have candid and often hilarious conversations about life, friendship, and everything in between. With O’Brien’s trademark wit and charm, ‘Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend’ is a must-listen for fans of smart, irreverent humor.

  4. ‘The Dollop’: Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, ‘The Dollop’ is a comedic history podcast that explores bizarre and often overlooked stories from the past. With Anthony providing the research and Reynolds offering his unique comedic spin, each episode is a wild ride through some of history’s strangest moments.

  5. ‘How Did This Get Made?’: Ever find yourself watching a terrible movie and wondering how it ever got made? That’s the premise behind ‘How Did This Get Made?’ Hosted by Paul Scheer, Jason Mantzoukas, and June Diane Raphael, this podcast features the hosts dissecting and mocking some of the worst movies ever made, with hilarious results.

  6. ‘2 Dope Queens’: Hosted by comedians Phoebe Robinson and Jessica Williams, ‘2 Dope Queens’ features a mix of stand-up comedy, storytelling, and candid conversations about life as women of color. With its sharp wit and unapologetic humor, this podcast offers a fresh and unique perspective on modern life.

  7. ‘The Last Podcast on the Left’: For fans of the strange and macabre, ‘The Last Podcast on the Left’ is a must-listen. Hosted by Ben Kissel, Marcus Parks, and Henry Zebrowski, this podcast explores topics ranging from true crime to conspiracy theories to the paranormal, all with a healthy dose of dark humor.

  8. ‘Office Ladies’: If you’re a fan of the hit TV show ‘The Office,’ then ‘Office Ladies’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by Jenna Fischer and Angela Kinsey, who played Pam and Angela on the show, respectively, this podcast offers behind-the-scenes insights, trivia, and hilarious anecdotes from the making of the beloved sitcom.

  9. ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’: Hosted by comedian Scott Aukerman, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ is a mix of improvisational comedy, celebrity interviews, and absurd humor. With its rotating cast of characters and unpredictable format, this podcast is a favorite among comedy fans looking for something truly out of the ordinary.

  10. ‘The Daily Show With Trevor Noah: Ears Edition’: For fans of political satire, ‘The Daily Show With Trevor Noah: Ears Edition’ offers a comedic take on the day’s news and current events. With Noah’s sharp wit and insightful commentary, this podcast is a must-listen for anyone looking to stay informed while also having a good laugh.

Whether you’re a fan of stand-up comedy, improv, or just good old-fashioned storytelling, there’s a comedy podcast out there for you. So why wait? Start exploring these hilarious and entertaining podcasts today, and prepare to laugh until your sides hurt.
