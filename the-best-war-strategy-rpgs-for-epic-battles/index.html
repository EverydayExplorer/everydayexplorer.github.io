The Best War Strategy RPGs for Epic Battles


In the realm of gaming, there’s a genre that combines the thrill of tactical warfare with the depth of role-playing. We’re talking about War Strategy RPGs, where players can immerse themselves in epic battles and make strategic decisions that can turn the tide of war. If you’re a fan of this genre or just looking to explore it, we’ve compiled a list of the best War Strategy RPGs that are sure to satisfy your thirst for strategic conquests.

1. ‘Fire Emblem: Three Houses’: This critically acclaimed Nintendo Switch title takes players on a journey through the land of Fódlan, where they must lead their chosen house and its students into battle. With its engaging narrative, deep character development, and challenging tactical battles, ‘Fire Emblem: Three Houses’ is a must-play for any War Strategy RPG enthusiast.

2. ‘Valkyria Chronicles’: Set in a fictional European continent inspired by World War II, ‘Valkyria Chronicles’ offers a unique blend of turn-based strategy and real-time action. Players take control of a group of soldiers known as Squad 7 as they fight against an invading empire. With its stunning visuals, engaging storyline, and innovative gameplay mechanics, ‘Valkyria Chronicles’ is a standout title in the genre.

3. ‘Final Fantasy Tactics’: This classic PlayStation game combines the beloved Final Fantasy franchise with strategic gameplay. Players take on the role of Ramza Beoulve, a noble warrior caught in a web of political intrigue and war. ‘Final Fantasy Tactics’ offers a deep and complex storyline, a wide range of job classes to customize your characters, and challenging battles that require careful planning and execution.

4. ‘XCOM 2’: In this sci-fi War Strategy RPG, players lead the resistance against an alien occupation on Earth. As the commander of XCOM, you must manage resources, research new technologies, and make tough decisions that can mean life or death for your soldiers. With its intense turn-based combat, deep customization options, and a sense of urgency in every decision, ‘XCOM 2’ is a thrilling experience for strategy enthusiasts.

5. ‘Disgaea 5: Alliance of Vengeance’: If you’re looking for a War Strategy RPG with a touch of humor, look no further than ‘Disgaea 5.’ Set in the Netherworld, players control a group of demons as they seek revenge against the powerful Overlords. With its over-the-top characters, deep gameplay mechanics, and endless hours of content, ‘Disgaea 5’ offers a unique and enjoyable experience.

6. ‘Advance Wars’: Originally released for the Game Boy Advance, ‘Advance Wars’ is a turn-based strategy game that focuses on military tactics. Players take control of various commanding officers and lead their armies to victory. With its challenging missions, addictive gameplay, and a wide variety of units and strategies to explore, ‘Advance Wars’ remains a beloved title in the War Strategy RPG genre.

7. ‘Banner Saga’: Set in a Viking-inspired world on the brink of apocalypse, ‘Banner Saga’ combines turn-based combat with a compelling narrative. Players must make tough decisions that affect the story and the survival of their caravan. With its beautiful hand-drawn art style, memorable characters, and a sense of consequence in every choice, ‘Banner Saga’ offers a unique and emotionally engaging experience.

These are just a few of the best War Strategy RPGs out there, each offering their own unique blend of tactical gameplay, immersive storytelling, and epic battles. Whether you’re a seasoned strategist or new to the genre, these games are sure to provide hours of thrilling gameplay and strategic challenges. So grab your weapons, rally your troops, and prepare for the ultimate test of wits in these immersive and epic War Strategy RPGs.
