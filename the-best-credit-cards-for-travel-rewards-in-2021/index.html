The Best Credit Cards for Travel Rewards in 2021


When it comes to travel rewards, having the right credit card can make a world of difference. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best credit card for travel rewards in 2021. But worry not, as we have done the research for you and compiled a list of the top credit cards that will help you earn and maximize your travel rewards.

1. Chase Sapphire Preferred

The Chase Sapphire Preferred is consistently ranked as one of the best credit cards for travel rewards. It offers a generous sign-up bonus and allows you to earn points on every purchase. One of the standout features is the ability to transfer points to popular travel partners, such as airlines and hotels, at a 1:1 ratio. Additionally, the card does not charge foreign transaction fees, making it perfect for international travel.

2. American Express® Gold Card

The American Express Gold Card is another excellent option for travel rewards in 2021. The card offers generous rewards on dining and groceries, two categories that most frequent travelers spend heavily on. Additionally, the card provides credits for airline fees and dining, which can offset the annual fee. With its wide range of transfer partners and access to exclusive travel benefits, the American Express Gold Card is worth considering.

3. Capital One Venture Rewards Credit Card

If simplicity is what you seek, the Capital One Venture Rewards Credit Card may be the perfect choice. With this card, you earn unlimited 2 miles for every dollar spent on all purchases. The miles earned can be redeemed for travel expenses, including flights, hotels, and car rentals, with no blackout dates. The card also offers a great sign-up bonus and comes with a Global Entry or TSA PreCheck credit, making it a great option for frequent travelers.

4. Citi Premier Card

The Citi Premier Card is designed with travel in mind. It offers a generous sign-up bonus and allows you to earn triple points on travel and gas purchases, making it ideal for those who frequently hit the road or take to the skies. The card also provides access to Citi’s ThankYou Points program, which includes various travel transfer partners, further enhancing its value for travel rewards.

5. Southwest Rapid Rewards Priority Credit Card

If you’re a frequent traveler with Southwest Airlines, the Southwest Rapid Rewards Priority Credit Card is a must-have. This card offers various perks and benefits designed specifically for Southwest Airlines’ loyalists. It provides an annual statement credit, bonus points on your account anniversary, and priority boarding. With the ability to earn points towards the coveted Southwest Companion Pass, this card is a great way to maximize your travel rewards if you frequently fly with Southwest.

6. Bank of America® Travel Rewards credit card

The Bank of America Travel Rewards credit card is an excellent option for those who want flexibility in their travel rewards. With no annual fee and the ability to earn unlimited points on every purchase, this card is a great choice for everyday spending. The points earned can be redeemed towards flights, hotels, or even as statement credits. If you have a significant relationship with Bank of America, you may be eligible for additional rewards and benefits, making it an even more attractive option.

7. The Platinum Card® from American Express

For those seeking luxury travel experiences, The Platinum Card from American Express is a top-tier option. While it carries a steep annual fee, the card offers an array of premium travel benefits, including airport lounge access, hotel elite status, and a generous annual airline fee credit. With extensive travel insurance coverage and access to Amex’s Fine Hotels & Resorts program, this card is perfect for the frequent traveler who values comfort and exclusivity.

In conclusion, the best credit card for travel rewards in 2021 depends on your spending habits and travel preferences. Whether you prioritize earning points on specific categories, want flexibility in redeeming your rewards, or desire luxury travel benefits, there is a credit card out there for you. Remember to consider the sign-up bonus, earning potential, transfer partners, and any additional perks or benefits that suit your needs. With the right credit card, you can make your travel dreams a reality while earning valuable rewards along the way.
