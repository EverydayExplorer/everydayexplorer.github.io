The Role of Dreams in Fantasy: Portals, Prophetic Dreams, and Dreamwalking


Fantasy literature has always captivated readers with its enchanting worlds and magical elements. One recurring theme that adds depth and intrigue to these stories is the role of dreams. Dreams provide a gateway to the realm of the subconscious, where anything is possible. In this blog post, we will explore how dreams are used in fantasy narratives, specifically focusing on portals, prophetic dreams, and dreamwalking.

Portals: Bridging the Gap Between Worlds

Dreams often serve as portals that connect different dimensions or realms within a fantasy narrative. These portals can transport characters to otherworldly places, where they encounter mythical creatures, magical landscapes, and extraordinary adventures.

In J.R.R. Tolkien’s “The Lord of the Rings,” the character Frodo Baggins experiences vivid dreams that reveal the existence of the One Ring. These dreams guide Frodo on a perilous journey to destroy the ring and save Middle-earth. The dreams act as portals, providing glimpses into the future and guiding the protagonist towards his destiny.

Similarly, in C.S. Lewis’s “The Chronicles of Narnia,” the Pevensie children stumble upon a wardrobe that acts as a portal to the magical land of Narnia. Their dreams foreshadow their adventures and draw them into a world of talking animals, mythical creatures, and epic battles between good and evil.

Dream portals not only add an element of mystery and anticipation to the narrative but also allow authors to explore themes of destiny, self-discovery, and the interconnectedness of different realms.

Prophetic Dreams: Insights into the Future

Prophetic dreams are a common trope in fantasy literature, offering characters glimpses of events yet to come. These dreams can be both a blessing and a curse, as they provide valuable insights but also burden the dreamer with knowledge of the future.

In George R.R. Martin’s “A Song of Ice and Fire” series, the character Bran Stark possesses the ability to dream prophetic dreams, known as “greendreams.” These dreams allow him to see events unfolding in distant lands and offer critical information that shapes the course of the story.

Prophetic dreams not only heighten the suspense and tension within a fantasy narrative but also provide opportunities for foreshadowing and character development. They add an element of unpredictability and intrigue, keeping readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the meaning behind these visions.

Dreamwalking: Exploring the Subconscious

Dreamwalking is a concept that allows characters to enter and explore the dreams of others. This unique ability enables them to delve into the subconscious mind, uncover secrets, and influence the dreamer’s thoughts and actions.

In Robin Hobb’s “Realm of the Elderlings” series, the protagonist, FitzChivalry Farseer, possesses the skill of the Wit, which grants him the ability to bond with animals and enter their dreams. Through dreamwalking, he gains insights into the animal’s experiences and emotions, forming a deeper connection with the natural world.

Dreamwalking not only serves as a tool for character development and world-building but also explores the power of empathy and the interconnectedness of all living beings. It allows authors to delve into the complexities of the human psyche and blur the lines between reality and imagination.

Conclusion

Dreams play a significant role in the realm of fantasy literature, offering a rich tapestry of portals, prophetic dreams, and dreamwalking. These narrative devices not only transport readers to magical realms but also provide insights into the future, unravel hidden truths, and explore the depths of the human mind.

As readers, we are captivated by the allure of dreams, as they invite us to suspend our disbelief and embark on extraordinary journeys alongside our favorite characters. So, the next time you pick up a fantasy novel, pay attention to the role of dreams and let them transport you to a world where anything is possible.

Remember, dreams are not mere figments of our imagination; they are the portals to our subconscious, the keys to unlocking our deepest desires, and the catalysts for incredible adventures.
