The Best Tech Podcasts to Follow in 2024


In the rapidly evolving world of technology, staying updated is more important than ever. Podcasts have emerged as a popular medium for tech enthusiasts to dive into discussions, analyses, and updates on the latest trends, innovations, and breakthroughs in the tech industry. Whether you’re a seasoned professional, a curious learner, or someone simply interested in the tech world, there’s a podcast out there just for you. This guide will introduce you to the best tech podcasts to follow in 2024, ensuring you remain informed and entertained.

1. TechStuff

TechStuff is a staple for those who want to understand the mechanics behind tech trends. Hosted by Jonathan Strickland, this podcast delves into the history of technology, the latest gadgets, and the impact of tech on society. Each episode combines storytelling with factual information, making complex topics accessible. Whether it’s exploring the latest in artificial intelligence or discussing the evolution of gaming consoles, TechStuff provides listeners with a comprehensive understanding of the tech landscape.

Why Listen?

  • Engaging Storytelling: Jonathan’s ability to weave narratives around technology keeps listeners hooked.
  • Broad Topics: Ranging from historical tech events to contemporary innovations, there’s something for everyone.
  • Expert Insights: The podcast often features guest experts who provide valuable perspectives on various tech sectors.

2. Reply All

A unique blend of investigative journalism and tech culture, Reply All is perfect for those who love stories about the internet and its quirks. Hosted by PJ Vogt and Alex Goldman, this podcast explores the bizarre and often humorous side of technology. With episodes that cover everything from internet mysteries to the impact of social media on our lives, Reply All captivates audiences with its relatable content and engaging storytelling.

Why Listen?

  • Intriguing Stories: Each episode feels like a mini-documentary, providing an engaging narrative on tech-related topics.
  • Cultural Relevance: The podcast delves into contemporary issues surrounding technology, making it highly relevant.
  • Community Engagement: The hosts often involve listeners in their stories, creating a sense of community and shared experience.

3. The Vergecast

For those who want up-to-date news and analysis on the latest gadgets and tech developments, The Vergecast is a must-listen. Hosted by Nilay Patel, Paul Miller, and Verge editor-in-chief, this podcast brings together three tech experts who discuss the week’s biggest tech news. Their chemistry and banter, combined with deep insights, make this podcast both informative and entertaining.

Why Listen?

  • Timely Updates: The Vergecast provides weekly updates, ensuring listeners are always in the loop.
  • Expert Opinions: With a team of seasoned tech journalists, listeners gain insights from industry veterans.
  • Casual Format: The conversational style makes complex tech news digestible and relatable.

4. StartUp Podcast

StartUp Podcast offers a behind-the-scenes look at what it’s like to start a business in the tech industry. Hosted by Alex Blumberg and Matthew Lieber, this podcast follows the journey of various startups, showcasing their challenges, successes, and lessons learned. It’s a fantastic resource for aspiring entrepreneurs and those interested in the intersection of technology and business.

Why Listen?

  • Real Stories: The podcast presents raw and honest stories about the struggles of starting a tech company.
  • Valuable Lessons: Entrepreneurs share their experiences, providing listeners with actionable insights.
  • Inspiring Journeys: Each episode highlights the resilience and creativity that drives tech entrepreneurs.

5. The Daily

While not exclusively a tech podcast, The Daily from The New York Times often covers significant tech-related stories that shape our world. Hosted by Michael Barbaro, this podcast provides a deep dive into one major news story each day, offering context and analysis that helps listeners understand the implications of technological advancements.

Why Listen?

  • In-depth Analysis: The Daily offers a thorough exploration of important tech news, connecting it to broader societal issues.
  • High-Quality Journalism: As a production of The New York Times, listeners can expect rigorous journalism and fact-based reporting.
  • Diverse Perspectives: The podcast features a variety of voices, enriching the discussions around tech issues.

6. TED Radio Hour

TED Radio Hour is a perfect blend of technology, innovation, and personal stories. Each episode centers around a specific theme and features TED Talks related to that theme. The host, Manoush Zomorodi, curates talks that explore the intersection of technology and human experience, making it a thought-provoking listen for anyone interested in the future of tech.

Why Listen?

  • Inspirational Content: The podcast offers enlightening ideas that can spark creativity and innovation.
  • Varied Topics: Each episode covers a unique theme, providing a wide range of insights.
  • Engaging Format: The combination of TED Talks and interviews keeps the content dynamic and engaging.

7. The CultCast

If you’re an Apple enthusiast, The CultCast is the podcast for you. Hosted by Erfon Elijah, this show covers all things Apple, including product reviews, news, and rumors. It’s a lively and fast-paced podcast that caters to both die-hard Apple fans and casual listeners interested in the tech giant’s latest developments.

Why Listen?

  • Focused Content: The podcast specifically targets Apple products and news, making it ideal for fans of the brand.
  • Energetic Hosts: The hosts’ enthusiasm for Apple makes the content lively and engaging.
  • Insightful Analysis: Listeners gain valuable insights into the latest Apple products and industry trends.

8. The Hacker News Podcast

For those interested in cybersecurity and the darker side of technology, The Hacker News Podcast is an essential listen. This podcast features discussions on the latest cybersecurity news, trends, and tactics. It’s perfect for IT professionals, developers, and anyone who wants to stay informed about online safety.

Why Listen?

  • Relevant Topics: The podcast covers timely topics in cybersecurity, making it a great resource for professionals.
  • Expert Guests: The show often features industry experts who share their knowledge and insights.
  • Educational Content: Listeners can learn about best practices for online safety and security.

9. The AI Alignment Podcast

As artificial intelligence continues to evolve, understanding its implications becomes crucial. The AI Alignment Podcast focuses on the challenges and considerations surrounding AI development. Hosted by Lucas Perry, this podcast features discussions with leading researchers and thinkers in the field of AI, exploring topics such as ethics, safety, and the future of technology.

Why Listen?

  • Expert Insights: The podcast features discussions with renowned experts in AI, providing listeners with cutting-edge knowledge.
  • Informed Discussions: The focus on alignment and ethics makes it a thought-provoking listen for those interested in AI’s future.
  • Emerging Trends: Stay informed about the latest developments in AI research and technology.

10. The Changelog

The Changelog is a must-listen for developers and tech enthusiasts alike. This podcast covers open-source software, programming languages, and development tools. Hosted by Adam Stacoviak and Jerod Santo, it features interviews with developers and tech leaders, making it a great resource for anyone looking to enhance their coding skills or stay updated on the latest in software development.

Why Listen?

  • Diverse Topics: The podcast covers a wide range of topics related to software development and open-source projects.
  • Engaging Interviews: The hosts interview industry leaders, providing insights into their experiences and projects.
  • Community Focus: The Changelog emphasizes the importance of community in tech, making it a valuable resource for developers.

Conclusion

In 2024, the landscape of technology continues to evolve at a rapid pace, making it essential for enthusiasts and professionals to stay informed. The podcasts mentioned above offer diverse perspectives, insights, and stories that cater to various interests within the tech industry. Whether you’re looking for updates on the latest gadgets, in-depth analyses of tech trends, or inspiring entrepreneurial journeys, these podcasts will keep you engaged and informed. So plug in, tune in, and let the world of tech unfold in your ears!
