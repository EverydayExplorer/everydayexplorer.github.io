Styling Tips for Wearing Bold Prints and Patterns


Bold prints and patterns can be intimidating to wear, but when done right, they can add a vibrant and eye-catching touch to your outfit. Whether it’s a colorful floral dress or a geometric patterned blouse, incorporating bold prints and patterns into your wardrobe can take your style to the next level. In this blog post, we will discuss some styling tips for wearing bold prints and patterns, so you can confidently rock this fashion trend.

1. Start with one statement piece

When it comes to bold prints and patterns, less is often more. Instead of overwhelming your entire outfit with multiple prints, choose one statement piece to be the focal point of your look. This could be a patterned skirt, a printed blazer, or even a pair of bold patterned pants. By keeping the rest of your outfit simple and neutral, you allow the statement piece to shine and become the center of attention.

2. Mix and match with solids

If you’re hesitant about wearing bold prints, a great way to ease into this trend is by mixing and matching with solid-colored items. For example, pair a printed blouse with a solid-colored skirt or trousers. This creates a balanced and cohesive look, while still allowing the bold print to stand out. Alternatively, you can also opt for a printed accessory, like a scarf or a handbag, to add a pop of color and interest to an otherwise solid-colored outfit.

3. Coordinate colors

When wearing bold prints and patterns, it’s important to pay attention to the colors in the print. Look for colors that complement your complexion and coordinate with the rest of your outfit. By choosing colors that work well together, you can create a harmonious and put-together look. If you’re unsure about which colors to choose, start with neutrals like black, white, or beige, as they can easily be paired with any print or pattern.

4. Play with scale and proportion

The scale and proportion of the print can greatly impact the overall look of your outfit. Larger prints tend to make a bolder statement, while smaller prints can be more delicate and subtle. Consider your body shape and figure when choosing the scale of the print. If you’re petite, opt for smaller prints that won’t overwhelm your frame. On the other hand, if you’re tall or have a larger build, larger prints can help create a more balanced and proportionate silhouette.

5. Add texture and layers

To add depth and visual interest to your outfit, consider incorporating different textures and layers. This can be done by mixing fabrics with different finishes or adding a textured outer layer, like a chunky knit cardigan or a leather jacket. Adding texture not only adds dimension to your look but also helps to break up the bold print and create a more visually appealing ensemble.

6. Confidence is key

Above all, the most important tip for wearing bold prints and patterns is to have confidence in your outfit choices. Fashion is all about self-expression and embracing your personal style. If you feel good in what you’re wearing, it will show through your confidence and poise. So, don’t be afraid to take risks and experiment with different prints and patterns. Own your bold style and rock it with confidence!

In conclusion, wearing bold prints and patterns can be a fun and exciting way to elevate your style. By following these styling tips, you can confidently incorporate bold prints into your wardrobe and create fashionable and eye-catching outfits. Remember to start with one statement piece, mix and match with solids, coordinate colors, play with scale and proportion, add texture and layers, and most importantly, have confidence in your style choices. So go ahead, embrace the bold, and let your outfit make a statement!
