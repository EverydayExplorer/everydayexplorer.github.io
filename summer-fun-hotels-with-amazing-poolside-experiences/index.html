Summer Fun: Hotels with Amazing Poolside Experiences


With the arrival of summer, many travelers are eagerly planning their next getaway to soak up the sun and enjoy some poolside relaxation. From rooftop infinity pools with breathtaking city views to secluded oases surrounded by lush tropical gardens, hotels around the world are offering guests unforgettable experiences that combine luxury with fun in the sun. If you’re dreaming of a summer escape filled with refreshing dips and poolside cocktails, look no further than these hotels with amazing poolside experiences.

1. Marina Bay Sands - Singapore
Perched atop the iconic Marina Bay Sands hotel, the infinity pool here is not just a swimming spot but a true architectural marvel. With panoramic views of the Singapore skyline stretching out before you, taking a dip in this pool feels like floating above the city. Whether you’re lounging on a sunbed with a cocktail in hand or capturing envy-inducing photos for your Instagram feed, the pool experience at Marina Bay Sands is sure to be a highlight of your summer getaway.

2. The Beverly Hills Hotel - Los Angeles, USA
Nicknamed the ‘Pink Palace,’ The Beverly Hills Hotel is a Hollywood institution known for its timeless glamour and legendary pool scene. Surrounded by palm trees and lush gardens, the hotel’s heated outdoor pool is the perfect place to see and be seen. From celebrity sightings to poolside cabana service, every moment spent here feels like a scene from a classic Hollywood movie. Soak up the sun in style at this iconic hotel and experience the epitome of Beverly Hills luxury.

3. Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc - French Riviera, France
Nestled along the stunning coastline of the French Riviera, Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc exudes old-world charm and sophistication. Its iconic saltwater infinity pool, carved into the rocky cliffs overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, offers guests a truly unforgettable swimming experience. Surrounded by manicured gardens and majestic pine trees, this poolside oasis provides the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Whether you’re lounging on a sun-drenched terrace or taking a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear waters, the pool at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc is the epitome of Riviera chic.

4. The Standard High Line - New York City, USA
Located in the heart of Manhattan’s trendy Meatpacking District, The Standard High Line boasts one of the city’s coolest pool scenes. Perched above the bustling streets below, the hotel’s rooftop pool offers panoramic views of the Hudson River and the iconic High Line park. Whether you’re relaxing in a poolside cabana or mingling with fellow guests at one of the hotel’s legendary pool parties, the vibe here is always lively and electric. Soak up the summer sun in style at this chic urban oasis in the heart of New York City.

5. The Mulia - Bali, Indonesia
Situated along the pristine shores of Nusa Dua Beach in Bali, The Mulia is a luxury resort renowned for its stunning infinity pools and beachfront tranquility. With three interconnected pools spanning over 5,000 square meters, guests have plenty of space to swim, splash, and soak up the sun. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens and swaying palm trees, the pool area at The Mulia feels like a secluded paradise where you can unwind and recharge in style. Whether you’re sipping cocktails at the poolside bar or indulging in a relaxing massage at the nearby spa, every moment spent here is pure bliss.

From Singapore to Los Angeles, the French Riviera to Bali, these hotels offer guests unforgettable poolside experiences that are sure to make this summer one to remember. Whether you’re seeking stunning views, celebrity sightings, or simply a place to relax and unwind, these destinations have something for everyone. So pack your swimsuit, grab your sunscreen, and get ready to make a splash at these amazing hotels around the world.
