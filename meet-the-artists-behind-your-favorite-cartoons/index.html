Meet the Artists Behind Your Favorite Cartoons


Have you ever wondered about the creative individuals responsible for bringing your favorite cartoons to life? Behind each animated series lies a team of talented artists who pour their hearts and souls into making these characters and worlds come alive on screen. From character design to animation, these artists play a crucial role in capturing our imaginations and making us laugh.

In this article, we’ll introduce you to some of the incredible artists behind popular cartoons that have captured the hearts of fans around the world. Let’s dive in and get to know the creative minds behind the magic!

1. Character Designers

Character designers are responsible for creating the visual appearance of the characters in a cartoon. They work closely with the show’s creators to bring their ideas to life. Character designers use their artistic skills to develop unique looks and personalities for each character, ensuring that they stand out and resonate with the audience.

One notable character designer is Alex Hirsch, the creator of the beloved series ‘Gravity Falls.’ Hirsch’s distinctive art style, combined with his ability to create diverse and relatable characters, brought the show to life and drew viewers in with its unforgettable visuals.

2. Background Artists

Background artists are the unsung heroes behind the stunning worlds that exist in cartoons. They create detailed and immersive environments that provide a backdrop for the characters and their adventures. Whether it’s a bustling cityscape or a magical fantasy realm, background artists bring these settings to life with their expertise in color, perspective, and composition.

One exceptional background artist is Mary Blair, who worked on various Disney films and particularly left her mark on classics like ‘Cinderella’ and ‘Alice in Wonderland.’ Her vibrant and imaginative backgrounds set the stage for these timeless stories and continue to inspire artists even today.

3. Storyboard Artists

Storyboard artists are responsible for visually mapping out the sequences and actions within a cartoon. They work closely with the show’s writers and directors to translate scripts into compelling visuals. Storyboards serve as a blueprint for the animation process and help bring the story to life, frame by frame.

Rebecca Sugar, the creator of the critically acclaimed series ‘Steven Universe,’ started her career as a storyboard artist for the popular show ‘Adventure Time.’ Her unique storytelling style and ability to create emotionally resonant scenes played a significant role in the success and popularity of both shows.

4. Animators

Animators are at the heart of bringing characters to life through movement. They use their skills and techniques to give characters personality and expression. Animators meticulously craft each frame, ensuring smooth and engaging motion that captivates and entertains the audience.

Glen Keane, one of the most renowned animators in the industry, has worked on several iconic Disney films, such as ‘The Little Mermaid’ and ‘Beauty and the Beast.’ Keane’s ability to breathe life into characters through fluid and expressive animation has left a lasting impression on generations of animation lovers.

5. Voice Actors

While not visual artists by definition, voice actors are crucial to the success of cartoons. They provide the voices that bring the animated characters to life, adding depth and personality through their performances. Voice actors master the art of conveying emotions through their vocal abilities, creating recognizable voices that become synonymous with beloved characters.

Tara Strong, known for her roles as Bubbles in ‘The Powerpuff Girls’ and Twilight Sparkle in ‘My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic,’ has become one of the most prolific and versatile voice actors in the industry. Her ability to embody a wide range of characters with distinct voices has made her a fan favorite.

Behind every cartoon, there is a team of talented artists who work tirelessly to bring these stories and characters to life. Their dedication, creativity, and attention to detail ensure that these animated worlds continue to captivate and inspire both children and adults alike.

Next time you tune in to watch your favorite cartoon, take a moment to appreciate the artists behind the scenes. They are the true magicians who make the impossible possible and transport us to imaginative worlds filled with laughter, adventure, and endless possibilities.
