Top 10 Must-Watch TV Shows of the Decade


In the past decade, the television industry has witnessed a remarkable shift in the quality and variety of programming available to viewers. With the rise of streaming platforms and the increased competition among networks, we have seen an influx of incredible TV shows that have captivated audiences and garnered critical acclaim. From gripping dramas to hilarious comedies, the past ten years have given us an abundance of must-watch TV shows. In this blog post, we will explore the top 10 TV shows of the decade that have left a lasting impact on viewers of all ages.

1. Game of Thrones (2011-2019)

Undoubtedly, the biggest cultural phenomenon of the decade, Game of Thrones took the world by storm and became one of the most-watched TV shows of all time. Based on George R.R. Martin’s fantasy novels, this epic series combines intricate storytelling, complex characters, and stunning visuals to create a truly immersive experience. It’s a must-watch for those who enjoy political intrigue, epic battles, and unexpected plot twists.

2. Breaking Bad (2008-2013)

Breaking Bad is often regarded as one of the best TV dramas ever made, and for good reason. This gripping series follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer. With a brilliant script, nuanced performances, and a dark and intense atmosphere, Breaking Bad is a masterclass in storytelling and character development.

3. Stranger Things (2016-present)

Stranger Things is a nostalgic trip back to the 80s, filled with supernatural encounters, government conspiracies, and a group of lovable misfit kids. With its compelling storyline, endearing characters, and homage to classic films of the era, this Netflix series has captured the hearts of audiences worldwide. If you are a fan of sci-fi, horror, or coming-of-age stories, Stranger Things is a must-watch.

4. Black Mirror (2011-present)

Black Mirror is a thought-provoking anthology series that explores the dark side of technology and its impact on society. Each episode of this British show presents a standalone story, offering a chilling reflection of our increasingly interconnected world. With its intelligent writing, mind-bending concepts, and social commentary, Black Mirror stands out as a groundbreaking series that pushes the boundaries of storytelling.

5. The Crown (2016-present)

The Crown offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of Queen Elizabeth II and the inner workings of the British monarchy. With its lavish production design, impeccable acting, and compelling storytelling, this Netflix series has received widespread acclaim. The Crown is a must-watch for history buffs, fans of British dramas, and anyone interested in the complexities of power and monarchy.

6. The Handmaid’s Tale (2017-present)

Based on Margaret Atwood’s dystopian novel, The Handmaid’s Tale depicts a chilling totalitarian society where women are subjugated and their rights are stripped away. This Hulu series is a harrowing exploration of oppression, resilience, and the fight for freedom. With its powerful performances, stunning visuals, and timely themes, The Handmaid’s Tale is a must-watch for those who appreciate thought-provoking dramas.

7. Fleabag (2016-2019)

Fleabag is a British comedy-drama created and written by Phoebe Waller-Bridge. This critically acclaimed series follows the trials and tribulations of an unnamed woman navigating her messy and often chaotic life. With its sharp writing, dark humor, and outstanding performances, Fleabag has become a cult classic and a must-watch for fans of character-driven comedies.

8. Chernobyl (2019)

Chernobyl is a hard-hitting and emotionally charged miniseries that dramatizes the 1986 nuclear disaster in Soviet Ukraine. With its meticulous attention to detail, haunting atmosphere, and exceptional performances, this HBO series has garnered critical acclaim and numerous awards. Chernobyl is a must-watch for history enthusiasts and those interested in human resilience in the face of catastrophe.

9. Succession (2018-present)

Succession is an HBO drama series that delves into the complex dynamics of a wealthy and dysfunctional family running a global media empire. With its sharp writing, stellar ensemble cast, and biting satire, this series has gained a devoted following. Succession is a must-watch for fans of character-driven dramas and those intrigued by the machinations of wealth and power.

10. The Good Place (2016-2020)

The Good Place is a refreshing and inventive comedy that explores philosophical questions about the afterlife, morality, and personal growth. With its witty writing, lovable characters, and unexpected twists, this NBC sitcom has gained widespread popularity and critical acclaim. The Good Place is a must-watch for those seeking a combination of humor, heart, and existential musings.

Conclusion

The past decade has undoubtedly been a golden age of television, with a plethora of must-watch TV shows that have entertained and captivated audiences worldwide. From the epic and fantasy realms of Game of Thrones to the dark and morally complex world of Breaking Bad, these shows have set new standards in storytelling, character development, and production quality. Whether you enjoy drama, comedy, sci-fi, or fantasy, there is something on this list for everyone. So grab your popcorn, find a cozy spot on the couch, and embark on a binge-watching journey through the top 10 must-watch TV shows of the past decade.
