Laugh Till Your Stomach Hurts: The Best Comedy Specials on Netflix


Are you in the mood for some laughter? Look no further! Netflix has become a hub for comedy specials, offering a wide array of talented comedians who will have you rolling on the floor in stitches. Whether you enjoy stand-up comedy, improv, or sketch comedy, Netflix has something for everyone. Get ready to laugh till your stomach hurts as we dive into the best comedy specials on Netflix.

1. Dave Chappelle: Sticks & Stones

Known for his bold and controversial style, Dave Chappelle returns to the spotlight with ‘Sticks & Stones.’ In this special, Chappelle fearlessly tackles sensitive topics such as cancel culture, political correctness, and the Michael Jackson controversy. His sharp wit and unique storytelling abilities will keep you entertained from start to finish.

2. John Mulaney: Kid Gorgeous at Radio City

John Mulaney’s ‘Kid Gorgeous at Radio City’ is a masterclass in stand-up comedy. The special, filmed at New York City’s famous Radio City Music Hall, showcases Mulaney’s knack for observational humor and his hilarious storytelling abilities. With impeccable timing and charming delivery, Mulaney effortlessly takes you on a journey filled with laughter and relatable anecdotes.

3. Hannah Gadsby: Nanette

Hannah Gadsby’s ‘Nanette’ revolutionized the world of comedy. Blurring the lines between stand-up comedy and theater, Gadsby takes the audience on an emotional rollercoaster. This special explores topics like gender, sexuality, and trauma, breaking the conventional comedy formula along the way. ‘Nanette’ is a thought-provoking and powerful performance that challenges the boundaries of what comedy can be.

4. Ali Wong: Baby Cobra

Ali Wong’s ‘Baby Cobra’ is a hilarious and unapologetically raunchy comedy special that tackles taboo topics with finesse. Wong fearlessly dives into motherhood, relationships, and the complexities of being a woman in the modern world. Her sharp wit, self-deprecating humor, and relatable anecdotes make this special an absolute delight.

5. Aziz Ansari: Buried Alive

Aziz Ansari’s ‘Buried Alive’ showcases his signature charm and relatability. Ansari’s storytelling abilities shine through as he discusses topics such as love, relationships, and the struggles of adulthood. With his unique perspective, Ansari crafts a hilarious narrative that will resonate with audiences of all ages.

6. Gina Yashere: Laughing to America

British comedian Gina Yashere’s ‘Laughing to America’ is a hidden gem in the world of comedy specials. Yashere, known for her witty observations and infectious energy, offers a fresh and unique perspective on American culture, race, and identity. Her animated storytelling style will have you in stitches, making this special a must-watch.

7. Bo Burnham: Make Happy

Bo Burnham’s ‘Make Happy’ is a one-of-a-kind comedy special that transcends traditional stand-up comedy. Combining music, poetry, and multimedia elements, Burnham’s performance is a thought-provoking exploration of fame, depression, and the pursuit of happiness. The seamless blend of comedy and introspection in ‘Make Happy’ creates a truly unforgettable experience.

Conclusion

Netflix continues to deliver top-tier comedy specials that cater to a diverse range of tastes and preferences. From the bold and controversial humor of Dave Chappelle to the thought-provoking and boundary-pushing performances of Hannah Gadsby, these comedy specials will have you laughing till your stomach hurts. So grab some popcorn, settle into your comfiest chair, and get ready for a night filled with laughter and entertainment. Netflix truly offers the best comedy specials that are sure to bring joy to your life!
