Cartoon Crossovers We'd Love to See Happen


Cartoon crossovers have always been a topic of fascination among fans. The idea of our favorite characters from different shows coming together in one epic adventure is simply thrilling. While we have seen some amazing crossovers in the past, there are still plenty of untapped possibilities that would make for incredible collaborations. In this blog post, we will explore some cartoon crossovers that we’d love to see happen.

1. SpongeBob SquarePants meets The Powerpuff Girls
Imagine the underwater world of SpongeBob colliding with the vibrant and action-packed city of Townsville, where the Powerpuff Girls fight crime. This crossover would bring together two iconic cartoons and create a hilarious and action-packed adventure. We can already picture SpongeBob and Patrick teaming up with Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup to defeat a common enemy, all while delivering plenty of laughs along the way.

2. Adventure Time meets Steven Universe
Adventure Time and Steven Universe are both beloved cartoons known for their whimsical storytelling and endearing characters. A crossover between these two shows would be a match made in animated heaven. Finn and Jake embarking on an interdimensional adventure with Steven and the Crystal Gems would not only be visually stunning but also provide a deep and emotional narrative. This crossover could explore themes of friendship, love, and the power of self-discovery.

3. Gravity Falls meets Rick and Morty
Gravity Falls and Rick and Morty are two shows that have captivated audiences with their clever writing and mind-bending mysteries. A crossover between these two shows would be a mind-blowing experience. Imagine Dipper and Mabel Pines stumbling upon one of Rick’s interdimensional portals, leading them to go on an adventure with Rick and Morty through various dimensions. This crossover would combine the humor and charm of Gravity Falls with the twisted and sci-fi elements of Rick and Morty, resulting in a truly unforgettable crossover event.

4. The Simpsons meets Family Guy
The Simpsons and Family Guy are two iconic adult animated sitcoms known for their irreverent humor and satirical take on family life. A crossover between these two shows would be a comedic clash of epic proportions. Picture the Griffin family from Quahog moving to Springfield and interacting with the iconic Simpson family. This crossover would provide endless opportunities for hilarious misunderstandings, cultural references, and meta-commentary on the state of animated television.

5. Teen Titans Go! meets The Amazing World of Gumball
Teen Titans Go! and The Amazing World of Gumball are both shows that have garnered a dedicated fanbase with their zany humor and unique animation styles. A crossover between these two shows would be a visual treat for fans. Imagine the Teen Titans accidentally stumbling into the animated world of Elmore, where Gumball and his friends reside. This crossover would blend the superhero antics of the Teen Titans with the surreal and imaginative world of The Amazing World of Gumball, resulting in a crossover that is both action-packed and hilariously absurd.

Cartoon crossovers offer a unique opportunity for fans to see their favorite characters from different shows come together in unexpected ways. While these crossovers may be purely hypothetical, the possibilities are endless. Whether it’s seeing SpongeBob team up with the Powerpuff Girls or witnessing the hilarious clash between The Simpsons and Family Guy, cartoon crossovers have the power to ignite our imaginations and create unforgettable moments. So, let’s keep dreaming and hoping that someday these crossovers might become a reality.
