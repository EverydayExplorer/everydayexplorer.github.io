DIY Tassel Projects: Fringe and Fun!


Tassels are one of those timeless embellishments that instantly add a touch of charm and flair to any project. Whether you’re sprucing up an old handbag, adding some personality to your keychain, or giving your home decor a bohemian makeover, tassels are the perfect way to add a pop of color and texture. Plus, they’re incredibly easy and fun to make! In this guide, we’ll explore a variety of DIY tassel projects that are sure to inspire your next crafting adventure.

Materials You’ll Need:

Before we dive into the projects, let’s gather our materials. Luckily, making tassels requires only a few basic supplies:

  • Yarn or embroidery floss in your desired colors
  • Scissors
  • Cardboard or a tassel maker (optional)
  • Beads, charms, or other embellishments (optional)

Once you have these supplies on hand, you’re ready to start making tassels!

Basic Tassel Making:

Let’s start with the basics. Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to making your own tassels:

  1. Wrap the Yarn: Take your yarn or embroidery floss and start wrapping it around a piece of cardboard or a tassel maker. The more you wrap, the fuller your tassel will be. For a standard-sized tassel, aim for around 20-30 wraps.

  2. Tie Off the Top: Carefully slide the wrapped yarn off the cardboard, keeping the loops intact. Then, cut a separate piece of yarn and tie it tightly around the top of the loops. Leave a tail of yarn that you can use to attach the tassel to your project.

  3. Cut the Bottom Loops: Use your scissors to cut through the loops at the bottom of the tassel. Be sure to hold the top of the tassel securely so that the loops don’t come undone.

  4. Trim and Shape: Once you’ve cut the loops, give your tassel a little shake to fluff it up. Then, use your scissors to trim the ends of the yarn so that they’re all the same length. You can trim them shorter for a neater look or leave them longer for a more bohemian vibe.

And that’s it! You’ve just made your own tassel. Now, let’s explore some creative ways to use them in various projects.

1. Tassel Keychains:

Keychains are one of the easiest and most practical ways to use tassels. Simply attach a tassel to a keyring using the tail of yarn you left when tying off the top of the tassel. You can make them in all different colors and sizes to match your keys or your mood.

2. Boho Wall Hangings:

Tassels are a staple of bohemian decor, so why not use them to create your own wall hanging? String several tassels onto a length of yarn or twine, alternating colors and sizes for visual interest. Then, hang your tassel garland on a wooden dowel or branch for a rustic, boho vibe.

3. Tassel Earrings:

Make a statement with DIY tassel earrings that show off your unique style. To make them, simply attach a tassel to an earring hook using a jump ring. You can use small tassels for a subtle look or go big and bold with oversized tassels that demand attention.

4. Tassel Garland:

Add a festive touch to any celebration with a tassel garland. String several tassels onto a length of string or ribbon, spacing them evenly apart. Then, hang your garland above a dessert table, photo booth, or entryway for a playful accent that’s sure to impress your guests.

5. Tassel-Trimmed Home Decor:

Give your home decor a bohemian makeover with tassel-trimmed accents. Add tassels to throw pillows, curtains, or lampshades for a custom look that’s sure to impress. You can even use them to embellish blankets, table runners, or plant hangers for a stylish upgrade.

In conclusion, DIY tassel projects are a fun and easy way to add a pop of color and personality to just about anything. Whether you’re making keychains, earrings, wall hangings, or home decor accents, tassels are sure to add a playful touch that’s uniquely you. So grab your yarn and scissors, and let your creativity run wild with these fringe-tastic ideas!
