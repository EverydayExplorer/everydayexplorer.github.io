Hotels with Outdoor Ice Skating Rinks: Gliding on Frozen Delights


As winter descends upon us, hotels around the world are transforming their outdoor spaces into magical wonderlands, complete with shimmering ice skating rinks. There’s something truly enchanting about gliding across the ice beneath the open sky, surrounded by the twinkling lights and festive decorations of the holiday season. In this blog post, we’ll take a closer look at some of the top hotels that offer outdoor ice skating rinks, allowing guests to indulge in the timeless joy of this winter pastime.

  1. The St. Regis Aspen Resort, Colorado Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, The St. Regis Aspen Resort offers an unforgettable winter experience with its seasonal ice skating rink. Surrounded by majestic snow-capped peaks, guests can lace up their skates and glide across the ice against the backdrop of Aspen’s stunning scenery. After a day on the rink, warm up by the fire pits with a cup of hot cocoa or enjoy après-skate cocktails at the resort’s elegant bar.

  2. The Plaza Hotel, New York City In the heart of Manhattan, The Plaza Hotel transforms its iconic Plaza Fountain into a picturesque ice skating rink during the winter months. Guests can twirl and glide on the ice while admiring the dazzling lights of Fifth Avenue and Central Park. The rink is adorned with festive decorations, creating a magical atmosphere that captures the holiday spirit of the city. After skating, guests can treat themselves to a gourmet meal at one of the hotel’s acclaimed restaurants or indulge in a relaxing spa treatment.

  3. Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, Alberta Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Lake Louise and the Canadian Rockies, the Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise offers a truly idyllic setting for outdoor ice skating. Guests can skate on the frozen lake, surrounded by towering mountains and pristine wilderness. The rink is meticulously maintained, providing a smooth surface for leisurely gliding or spirited hockey games. After skating, guests can warm up with a cup of mulled wine by the outdoor fire pits or unwind with a luxurious spa treatment overlooking the stunning landscape.

  4. Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah Escape the winter chill and head to the sunny shores of Dubai, where the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah offers a unique twist on outdoor ice skating. The hotel’s synthetic ice rink allows guests to enjoy the thrill of skating year-round, without worrying about melting ice or chilly temperatures. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens and panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf, the rink offers a one-of-a-kind experience for guests of all ages. After skating, guests can relax in a private cabana by the pool or dine al fresco at one of the hotel’s gourmet restaurants.

  5. The Ritz-Carlton, Lake Tahoe Perched high in the Sierra Nevada mountains, The Ritz-Carlton, Lake Tahoe offers an enchanting outdoor ice skating experience in the heart of the picturesque Northstar California Resort. Guests can skate beneath the towering pines, surrounded by the natural beauty of the surrounding wilderness. The rink is complemented by festive holiday decorations, live music, and seasonal activities for the whole family to enjoy. After skating, guests can cozy up by the fire pits with a s’mores kit or unwind with a soak in the hotel’s heated outdoor pool.

In conclusion, hotels with outdoor ice skating rinks offer guests a magical opportunity to embrace the joys of winter and create cherished memories with loved ones. Whether surrounded by snow-capped mountains or city skyscrapers, these enchanting rinks provide the perfect setting for an unforgettable winter getaway. So lace up your skates and glide into the season with these frozen delights awaiting at hotels around the world.
