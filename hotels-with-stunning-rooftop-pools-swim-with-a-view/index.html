Hotels with Stunning Rooftop Pools: Swim with a View


Imagine lounging by a shimmering pool, sipping on a refreshing cocktail, and taking in breathtaking views of the cityscape or the surrounding natural beauty. Now, imagine doing all of this on a rooftop, where you can swim with a view. If you’re a fan of luxurious accommodations and stunning vistas, then hotels with rooftop pools are the perfect choice for your next getaway. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the most extraordinary hotels around the world that offer this unique and unforgettable experience.

1. Marina Bay Sands - Singapore:
At the top of our list is the iconic Marina Bay Sands in Singapore. This architectural marvel boasts the world’s largest rooftop infinity pool, located 57 stories above the city. With its mesmerizing skyline views, this pool is a true oasis in the sky. Whether you’re swimming or simply lounging by the edge, you’ll feel like you’re floating above the clouds.

2. The Joule - Dallas, Texas:
Located in the heart of downtown Dallas, The Joule is home to a spectacular rooftop pool that hangs over the edge of the building. This gravity-defying pool offers swimmers a sense of weightlessness as they take in panoramic views of the city. With a stylish design and a vibrant atmosphere, The Joule is a must-visit destination for pool enthusiasts.

3. The Standard - Downtown LA, California:
Perched atop a historic building in downtown Los Angeles, The Standard’s rooftop pool offers a trendy and chic setting for a refreshing swim. With stunning views of the city’s skyline and the nearby Hollywood Hills, this pool is the perfect place to relax and unwind after a day of exploring the bustling streets of LA.

4. The Ned - London, United Kingdom:
Situated in the heart of London, The Ned is a stunning hotel that combines classic elegance with modern luxury. Its rooftop pool provides guests with a serene and peaceful retreat, offering panoramic views of the city’s iconic skyline. Whether you’re taking a dip in the pool or enjoying a cocktail at the rooftop bar, The Ned guarantees an unforgettable experience.

5. Hotel Unique - Sao Paulo, Brazil:
With its distinctive architecture and avant-garde design, Hotel Unique in Sao Paulo stands out as one of the most unique hotels in the world. Its rooftop pool, shaped like a red half-moon, offers guests a one-of-a-kind swimming experience. Surrounded by lush greenery and overlooking the city, this pool is a true hidden gem.

6. The Oberoi Udaivilas - Udaipur, India:
Nestled on the banks of Lake Pichola, The Oberoi Udaivilas in Udaipur is a luxurious oasis that transports guests to a world of opulence and tranquility. Its rooftop pool provides breathtaking views of the lake, the Aravalli Mountains, and the nearby City Palace. With its regal ambiance and serene setting, this pool offers a truly magical experience.

7. The Pool at the Joule - Dallas, Texas:
The second Dallas entry on our list, The Pool at the Joule offers a unique swimming experience that is unlike any other. Located in a former 1920s bank vault, this underground pool features a stunning glass wall that allows swimmers to peer into the bustling downtown streets. It’s a truly remarkable sight that combines history with modernity.

These are just a few examples of hotels around the world that offer stunning rooftop pools. Whether you’re a fan of city skylines, natural landscapes, or architectural wonders, there’s a rooftop pool out there that will take your breath away. So, why not add a touch of luxury to your next vacation and swim with a view? Indulge in the ultimate relaxation and elevate your travel experience by choosing a hotel with a stunning rooftop pool.
