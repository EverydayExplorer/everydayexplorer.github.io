Giggles Guaranteed: Top Comedy Specials of the Year


Looking for a good laugh? Look no further than this year’s top comedy specials. From stand-up comics to sketch comedians, this year has seen an abundance of fresh, hilarious material that is sure to make you giggle guaranteed.

First up is Bo Burnham’s ‘Inside.’ Burnham’s special, released on Netflix, takes a unique approach to comedy by exploring the strange and isolating experience of lockdown. The result is a hilarious yet poignant commentary on mental health, social media, and the human experience. ‘Inside’ has already generated a lot of buzz, and it’s not hard to see why.

Next on the list is ‘Yearly Departed,’ a comedy special that celebrates the end of 2020 in style. Featuring an all-star lineup of female comedians, including Tiffany Haddish and Sarah Silverman, this special is a hilarious send-off to a year that many would like to forget. Whether you’re looking to laugh off the stress of the past year or just want to enjoy some great jokes, ‘Yearly Departed’ is definitely worth a watch.

Another hilarious special from this year is ‘Jack Whitehall: I’m Only Joking.’ Whitehall, a popular British comedian, brings his signature wit and irreverence to the Netflix stage in this special. With a mix of observational humor and self-deprecating jokes, ‘I’m Only Joking’ is a fun and lighthearted comedy that is perfect for a night in with friends.

Finally, we have ‘Maria Bamford: Weakness is the Brand.’ Bamford’s comedy is unique and often bizarre, but always hilarious. In this special, she explores everything from mental health to the #MeToo movement with her trademark wit and offbeat humor. If you’re looking for something truly unique, ‘Weakness is the Brand’ is definitely worth a watch.

These are just a few of the top comedy specials from this year. Whether you’re in the mood for stand-up, sketch, or something in between, there’s something for everyone in this year’s lineup. So sit back, relax, and get ready to laugh your way through 2021.
