Fun Family Traditions to Start This Holiday Season


‘Tis the season to be jolly, and what better way to spread joy and create lasting memories than by starting some fun family traditions this holiday season? Traditions are a wonderful way to bring loved ones together, build stronger bonds, and create a sense of belonging. Whether you’re starting new traditions or reviving old ones, these ideas are sure to add extra sparkle to your holiday celebrations. So, gather around the virtual fireplace as we dive into some delightful and festive family traditions!

1. The Great Holiday Bake-Off:
Get your aprons ready and let the delicious aroma of freshly baked cookies fill the air! Dedicate a day to a friendly family baking competition. Each member can choose their favorite holiday recipe, and then everyone can vote on the tastiest creation. The winner gets bragging rights and a special prize, of course!

2. Random Acts of Kindness:
The holiday season is the perfect time to spread some cheer throughout your community. Encourage your family to perform random acts of kindness, such as leaving handwritten notes for neighbors, donating to local charities, or volunteering at a shelter. This tradition will not only brighten someone else’s day but also instill gratitude and compassion in your loved ones.

3. Storytime by the Fire:
There’s something magical about snuggling up together by the warm glow of a crackling fireplace. Make it a tradition to gather around the fire and read holiday-themed stories aloud. Choose classic tales like ‘A Christmas Carol’ or ‘The Night Before Christmas,’ or even create your own family story to add a personal touch.

4. Festive Movie Marathon:
Create a cozy movie night at home with a lineup of beloved holiday classics. Decorate your living room with twinkling lights, fluffy blankets, and plenty of pillows. Then, settle down with some hot cocoa and popcorn as you watch timeless films like ‘Home Alone,’ ‘Elf,’ or ‘It’s a Wonderful Life.’ Don’t forget to create a family rating system for each movie and see which one becomes an all-time favorite!

5. DIY Decorations:
Add a personal touch to your holiday decorations by making them together as a family. Set aside an afternoon to create handmade ornaments, wreaths, or garlands. You can use materials like paper, pinecones, or even recycled items. These homemade decorations will not only brighten up your home but also serve as nostalgic reminders of the love and creativity that went into making them.

6. Game Night Extravaganza:
Turn off the screens and bring out the board games! Have a designated night each week during the holiday season where the family gathers for some friendly competition. From classic games like Monopoly and Scrabble to holiday-themed trivia games, there’s something for everyone. This tradition is guaranteed to create laughter, enhance problem-solving skills, and foster healthy competition amongst family members.

7. Outdoor Adventures:
Embrace the beauty of the winter season by planning outdoor activities for the whole family. Whether it’s building snowmen, having a friendly snowball fight, or taking a scenic hike, being in nature can be a fantastic bonding experience. Don’t forget to bring along some thermoses filled with hot cocoa or warm apple cider to keep everyone toasty!

8. Gratitude Jar:
Encourage gratitude and reflection by starting a gratitude jar. Have each family member write down something they’re thankful for every day and place it in the jar. On New Year’s Eve, gather around and read all the notes. This tradition will remind everyone of the little moments of joy and blessings throughout the holiday season.

9. Virtual Caroling:
Bring the joy of holiday caroling into the digital age! Set up a virtual karaoke session where each family member takes turns singing their favorite holiday songs. You can even invite extended family members or friends to join in via video call. This tradition will create unforgettable memories and spread holiday cheer, even from afar.

10. Family Photo Session:
Capture the magic of the season by having an annual family photo session. Choose a festive location, dress up in coordinating outfits, and let a professional photographer or even a tripod capture the joy and love your family shares. These photos will become cherished memories that you can display year after year.

These are just a few fun and creative family traditions to start this holiday season. The most important aspect is spending quality time together, creating beautiful memories, and cherishing the love and connection that make this time of year so special. So, gather your loved ones, embrace the spirit of the season, and have a jolly good time creating traditions that will be cherished for years to come. Happy holidays!
