The Top 10 Most Underrated Cartoons You Should Be Watching


When it comes to animated entertainment, there are a plethora of options available, but some hidden gems often go unnoticed. Whether you’re a die-hard animation fan or just looking for something new to watch, these underrated cartoons deserve your attention. From heartfelt storytelling to stunning visuals, these shows offer a unique and refreshing take on the animated genre. Without further ado, here are the top 10 most underrated cartoons you should be watching.

1. ‘Infinity Train’: This mind-bending series follows a young girl named Tulip as she navigates through a mysterious train with an ever-changing number of cars. Each car presents a new and unique challenge, making for an enthralling and unpredictable viewing experience.

2. ‘Hilda’: Based on the graphic novel series by Luke Pearson, ‘Hilda’ follows the adventures of a fearless and curious young girl as she encounters magical creatures and explores a world filled with wonder and danger. With its beautiful art style and captivating storytelling, ‘Hilda’ is a must-watch for fans of fantasy and adventure.

3. ‘The Dragon Prince’: From the creators of ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender,’ ‘The Dragon Prince’ is a captivating fantasy series that follows two human princes and an elven assassin as they embark on a perilous journey to prevent a war between their kingdoms. With its rich world-building and diverse cast of characters, this show is a hidden gem for fantasy enthusiasts.

4. ‘Craig of the Creek’: This heartwarming and wholesome series follows the imaginative adventures of Craig and his friends as they explore the untamed wilderness of their neighborhood creek. With its relatable characters and genuine portrayal of childhood friendships, ‘Craig of the Creek’ is a delightful watch for viewers of all ages.

5. ‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’: Set in a post-apocalyptic world where mutated animals roam the earth, ‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’ follows the journey of a young girl named Kipo as she navigates through this vibrant and dangerous landscape. With its stunning visuals and diverse cast of characters, this show is a breath of fresh air in the animated genre.

6. ‘The Hollow’: This gripping mystery series follows three teens who wake up in a strange world with no memory of how they got there. As they navigate through bizarre and dangerous challenges, they unravel the secrets of the enigmatic world they find themselves in. With its intriguing plot and suspenseful storytelling, ‘The Hollow’ is a hidden gem for fans of mystery and adventure.

7. ‘Harvey Street Kids’: This lighthearted and comedic series follows the misadventures of a group of quirky kids as they navigate the ups and downs of life on Harvey Street. With its vibrant animation and lovable characters, ‘Harvey Street Kids’ is a delightful and upbeat watch for viewers of all ages.

8. ‘We Bare Bears’: This charming and endearing series follows three bear brothers—Grizzly, Panda, and Ice Bear—as they navigate the modern world and embark on humorous escapades. With its relatable humor and heartwarming moments, ‘We Bare Bears’ is a hidden gem that is sure to bring a smile to your face.

9. ‘Star vs. The Forces of Evil’: This adventurous and whimsical series follows a teenage princess named Star Butterfly as she battles supernatural forces and navigates the challenges of growing up. With its vibrant animation and engaging storytelling, ‘Star vs. The Forces of Evil’ is a delightful watch for fans of fantasy and comedy.

10. ‘Mao Mao: Heroes of Pure Heart’: This action-packed and comedic series follows the adventures of Sheriff Mao Mao, a brave and determined cat, as he protects the citizens of Pure Heart Valley from all manner of threats. With its dynamic animation and hilarious antics, ‘Mao Mao: Heroes of Pure Heart’ is a hidden gem for fans of action and comedy.

In conclusion, these underrated cartoons offer a diverse range of storytelling, characters, and animation styles that deserve recognition and praise. Whether you’re a fan of fantasy, adventure, comedy, or mystery, there is something on this list for everyone. So, the next time you’re looking for something new to watch, consider giving one of these hidden gems a chance—you won’t be disappointed.
