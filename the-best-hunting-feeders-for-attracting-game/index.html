The Best Hunting Feeders for Attracting Game


Hunting is a time-honored tradition that has been passed down through generations. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, one thing is certain: attracting game to your hunting grounds is essential for a successful hunt. That’s where hunting feeders come into play. These invaluable tools can help you draw game to a specific area, increasing your chances of a successful hunting outing. In this blog post, we’ll explore the best hunting feeders on the market and how they can enhance your hunting experience.

When it comes to choosing the best hunting feeder, there are several factors to consider. The first consideration is the type of game you are targeting. Different feeders are designed to attract specific types of game, so it’s important to choose one that aligns with your hunting goals. For example, if you’re targeting deer, a gravity feeder may be the best option, as it allows for a continuous, reliable food source. On the other hand, if you’re hunting for smaller game such as turkeys or hogs, a spin-cast feeder may be more suitable.

In addition to the type of game you’re targeting, the capacity of the feeder is another important consideration. The size of your hunting grounds and the frequency of your visits will determine the ideal capacity for your feeder. For hunters with larger properties, a high-capacity feeder that can hold a significant amount of feed may be necessary to maintain a consistent food source. Conversely, hunters with smaller properties may opt for a smaller feeder with a lower capacity.

Durability is also a crucial factor when selecting a hunting feeder. The feeder will be exposed to the elements and wildlife, so it’s important to choose a feeder that can withstand harsh weather conditions and potential damage from wildlife. Look for feeders constructed from sturdy materials such as metal or heavy-duty plastic to ensure longevity and reliable performance.

When it comes to attracting game, the type of feed used in the feeder plays a significant role. Different types of game are attracted to different types of feed, so it’s important to choose a feed that aligns with your targeted game. For example, deer are attracted to corn, while turkeys may be drawn to a mix of grains. Some feeders even offer the flexibility to dispense a variety of feeds, allowing you to cater to multiple types of game.

In addition to these considerations, technological advancements in hunting feeders have led to the development of innovative features that can enhance their performance. Some feeders are equipped with timers and programmable settings, allowing you to schedule feed dispersal at specific times of day. This can help establish a feeding pattern and attract game to your hunting grounds during prime hunting hours. Additionally, some feeders are designed with built-in varmint guards to deter unwanted wildlife from accessing the feed.

Now that we’ve explored the key considerations when choosing a hunting feeder, let’s take a look at some of the best options available on the market. The Moultrie Deer Feeder Pro is a popular choice among hunters, known for its durability, large capacity, and programmable timer. For hunters seeking a versatile option, the Wildgame Innovations 6V Digital Power Control Unit offers a variety of feed settings and a durable construction. Those looking for a high-capacity feeder may find the Banks Outdoors Feed Bank 600 to be a suitable choice, with its ability to hold up to 600 pounds of feed.

In conclusion, hunting feeders are an essential tool for attracting game to your hunting grounds. By considering factors such as the type of game targeted, feeder capacity, durability, and feed type, hunters can select the best hunting feeder to enhance their hunting experience. With technological advancements and a wide range of options available, there’s a hunting feeder to suit every hunter’s needs. Whether you’re pursuing deer, turkeys, or other game, a well-chosen hunting feeder can increase your chances of a successful and rewarding hunting experience.
