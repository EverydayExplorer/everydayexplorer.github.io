10 Sustainable Living Blogs to Follow for Inspiration


Looking for inspiration to live a more sustainable lifestyle? Look no further! We’ve curated a list of ten amazing sustainable living blogs that are sure to inspire you to make eco-friendly choices in your daily life. From zero waste tips to ethical fashion advice, these blogs cover a wide range of topics to help you live more sustainably. So grab a cup of fair-trade coffee and get ready to be inspired!

1. Sustainable Daisy

Sustainable Daisy is your go-to resource for all things sustainable living. From DIY zero waste hacks to ethical shopping guides, founder Emily shares practical tips and advice for reducing your environmental footprint. With a focus on simplicity and mindfulness, Sustainable Daisy proves that living sustainably can be both easy and rewarding.

2. Eco Warrior Princess

Eco Warrior Princess is not just a blog – it’s a movement. Founded by Jennifer Nini, this empowering platform offers a wealth of resources for eco-conscious individuals who want to make a positive impact on the planet. From sustainable fashion reviews to in-depth articles on environmental justice, Eco Warrior Princess is a must-read for anyone passionate about sustainability.

3. Going Zero Waste

Ready to say goodbye to single-use plastics and embrace a zero waste lifestyle? Going Zero Waste is here to help. Author Kathryn Kellogg shares her journey towards zero waste living, along with practical tips and tricks for reducing waste in every area of your life. With easy-to-follow guides and inspiring stories, Going Zero Waste proves that living sustainably is both achievable and enjoyable.

4. TreeHugger

For over a decade, TreeHugger has been a leading voice in the sustainability movement. With a team of expert writers and contributors, this comprehensive blog covers a wide range of topics, from green technology and renewable energy to sustainable travel and eco-friendly home design. Whether you’re looking for the latest environmental news or practical tips for greener living, TreeHugger has you covered.

5. The Minimalist Vegan

Living sustainably goes hand in hand with minimalist living, and The Minimalist Vegan is the perfect resource for those looking to simplify their lives and reduce their environmental impact. With a focus on plant-based eating and conscious consumerism, authors Michael and Maša offer practical advice for living a more intentional life. From delicious vegan recipes to minimalist living tips, The Minimalist Vegan is a treasure trove of inspiration.

6. My Plastic-Free Life

Plastic pollution is one of the biggest environmental challenges we face today, but author Beth Terry is on a mission to change that. My Plastic-Free Life chronicles Beth’s journey towards living plastic-free, along with helpful resources and tips for reducing plastic waste in your own life. With insightful articles and personal stories, My Plastic-Free Life is a valuable resource for anyone looking to kick their plastic habit.

7. The Good Trade

The Good Trade is more than just a blog – it’s a community dedicated to ethical and sustainable living. With a focus on conscious consumerism and social justice, this empowering platform offers a wealth of resources for those looking to make a positive impact on the world. From ethical fashion guides to sustainable travel tips, The Good Trade inspires readers to live with purpose and intention.

8. Ethical Unicorn

Ethical Unicorn is on a mission to make ethical living accessible to all. Founder Francesca Willow shares her passion for ethical fashion, sustainable living, and social justice through thought-provoking articles and practical guides. With a focus on inclusivity and intersectionality, Ethical Unicorn encourages readers to think critically about the impact of their choices and take action for a more sustainable future.

9. Trash is for Tossers

Lauren Singer is living proof that zero waste living is not only possible but also incredibly empowering. Trash is for Tossers documents Lauren’s journey towards producing zero waste, along with helpful tips and resources for living a more sustainable lifestyle. From DIY beauty recipes to eco-friendly cleaning hacks, Trash is for Tossers proves that living sustainably can be chic, stylish, and fun.

10. Sustainable Jungle

Sustainable Jungle is your ultimate guide to living a more sustainable and eco-conscious life. With a focus on environmental sustainability, social justice, and mindful living, this comprehensive blog offers a wealth of resources for those looking to make a positive impact on the planet. From sustainable fashion reviews to eco-friendly travel guides, Sustainable Jungle empowers readers to live with purpose and passion.

In conclusion, living sustainably is not just about making individual choices – it’s about joining a global movement towards a more just and equitable world. These ten sustainable living blogs offer valuable resources, inspiration, and community for anyone looking to make a positive impact on the planet. So why wait? Start exploring these blogs today and embark on your own journey towards a more sustainable future.
