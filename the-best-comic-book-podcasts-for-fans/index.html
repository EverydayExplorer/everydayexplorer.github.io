The Best Comic Book Podcasts for Fans


Are you a comic book enthusiast looking for the perfect podcast to feed your passion for all things superheroes, villains, and epic storylines? Look no further! We’ve scoured the digital airwaves to bring you a curated list of the best comic book podcasts that cater to every kind of fan. Whether you’re into Marvel, DC, indie comics, or graphic novels, there’s a podcast out there waiting for you to tune in and geek out.

1. Fatman Beyond

Hosted by Kevin Smith and Marc Bernardin, Fatman Beyond is a must-listen for fans of both comic books and pop culture. With a blend of humor, insightful discussions, and insider knowledge, Kevin and Marc delve into the latest happenings in the world of comic books, movies, and TV shows. Their deep dives into comic book lore and their engaging banter make Fatman Beyond a top choice for comic book aficionados.

2. Jay & Miles X-Plain the X-Men

For those who can’t get enough of the mutants from the Marvel Universe, Jay & Miles X-Plain the X-Men offers a deep exploration of all things X-Men. Hosted by Jay Edidin and Miles Stokes, this podcast dissects the complex world of the X-Men, from classic storylines to the latest comic releases. With a mix of analysis, humor, and a genuine love for the source material, this podcast provides a comprehensive look at the ever-evolving X-Men universe.

3. Word Balloon

Hosted by John Siuntres, Word Balloon is a treasure trove of interviews with some of the biggest names in the comic book industry. From writers and artists to editors and publishers, John’s podcast offers listeners an insider’s view of the comic book world. The in-depth interviews provide valuable insights into the creative process behind crafting compelling comic book narratives, making Word Balloon a must-listen for anyone interested in the behind-the-scenes of the industry.

4. iFanboy Pick of the Week

iFanboy Pick of the Week delivers exactly what its name promises – a weekly pick of the best comic book releases. Hosted by Conor Kilpatrick, Josh Flanagan, and Ron Richards, this podcast offers reviews, recommendations, and discussions about the latest comic book releases. Whether you’re a seasoned reader or just starting to dip your toes into the comic book world, iFanboy Pick of the Week is a great resource for staying updated on the newest and most exciting titles.

5. The Comic Conspiracy

Hosted by a group of comic book store employees, The Comic Conspiracy provides a casual and friendly atmosphere for discussing all things related to comics. With a focus on both mainstream and indie titles, this podcast offers a diverse range of perspectives and recommendations. The hosts’ genuine love for comic books shines through in their discussions, making The Comic Conspiracy a welcoming space for fans of all kinds.

6. Super Skull

Super Skull is the perfect podcast for fans who want to stay informed about the latest happenings in the comic book industry. Hosted by Nick, Curtis, and Marcus, this podcast covers a wide range of comic-related topics, including industry news, reviews, and in-depth discussions about various titles. The hosts’ dynamic chemistry and in-depth knowledge make Super Skull an engaging listen for fans who want to keep their finger on the pulse of the comic book world.

7. The Adventure Zone

While not exclusively focused on comic books, The Adventure Zone offers a unique blend of storytelling and role-playing games that will appeal to comic book fans who appreciate epic narratives. Hosted by the McElroy family, this podcast takes listeners on imaginative and hilarious adventures through different tabletop role-playing games, creating immersive and entertaining stories that will resonate with comic book enthusiasts.

Whether you’re a die-hard superhero fan or a casual reader looking to expand your comic book knowledge, these podcasts offer a wealth of entertainment, insights, and recommendations to keep you engaged and informed. So, grab your headphones, tune in, and let these podcasts take you on a thrilling journey through the captivating world of comic books!
