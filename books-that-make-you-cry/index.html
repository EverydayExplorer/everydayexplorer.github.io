Books That Make You Cry


As readers, we often seek books that evoke a wide range of emotions within us. While joy, excitement, and laughter are commonly associated with reading, there is a unique power in a book that can make us shed tears. These books have the ability to touch our hearts, resonate with our deepest emotions, and leave a lasting impact on our souls. In this blog post, we will explore a curated list of books that have the power to make you cry.

1. ‘The Kite Runner’ by Khaled Hosseini:
Set against the backdrop of war-torn Afghanistan, this powerful novel follows the lives of two childhood friends, Amir and Hassan. The story delves into themes of guilt, redemption, and the complexities of human relationships. Through its poignant narrative, ‘The Kite Runner’ explores the depths of human suffering and the pursuit of forgiveness.

2. ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ by John Green:
A heart-wrenching tale of young love, this novel takes us on a journey with Hazel and Gus, two teenagers battling cancer. John Green masterfully portrays the raw emotions of his characters, capturing the beauty and fragility of life. ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ reminds us of the power of love and the strength we find in facing our mortality.

3. ‘Me Before You’ by Jojo Moyes:
This bittersweet love story follows the unconventional relationship between Louisa Clark, a young woman with a vibrant spirit, and Will Traynor, a quadriplegic man with a desire to end his life. Jojo Moyes explores themes of sacrifice, personal growth, and the pursuit of happiness. ‘Me Before You’ is guaranteed to leave you with a tear-stained heart.

4. ‘A Little Life’ by Hanya Yanagihara:
A deeply moving and emotionally challenging novel, ‘A Little Life’ chronicles the lives of four friends over several decades. Hanya Yanagihara explores the depths of trauma, friendship, and the enduring power of love. This book is a testament to the resilience of the human spirit, but be prepared for an emotional rollercoaster.

5. ‘The Book Thief’ by Markus Zusak:
Narrated by Death, this hauntingly beautiful novel is set in Nazi Germany and follows the life of Liesel Meminger, a young girl who finds solace in books during a time of darkness. Markus Zusak’s lyrical prose and unique storytelling make ‘The Book Thief’ an unforgettable read that will leave you both devastated and inspired.

6. ‘The Lovely Bones’ by Alice Sebold:
Told from the perspective of a young girl who has been murdered, ‘The Lovely Bones’ explores the aftermath of tragedy and the healing process of those left behind. Alice Sebold’s poignant writing style and exploration of grief make this book a tear-jerker that will stay with you long after you turn the final page.

These are just a few examples of the many books that have the power to make you cry. Each one explores different themes, but they all share a common thread of tapping into our deepest emotions. Whether you’re in the mood for a heart-wrenching love story, a tale of resilience in the face of adversity, or a journey through grief, these books will undoubtedly leave their mark on your soul.

So, grab a box of tissues and immerse yourself in these emotionally charged stories. Prepare to have your heartstrings tugged and your tears flow as you embark on a journey through the pages of these books that make you cry.
