The Best SUVs for Families: Top Picks and Reviews


When it comes to finding the perfect SUV for your family, there are countless options to choose from. With so many different makes and models on the market, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. That’s why we’ve compiled a list of the best SUVs for families, complete with top picks and reviews. Whether you’re looking for ample seating, advanced safety features, or impressive cargo space, we’ve got you covered.

1. Honda CR-V: The Honda CR-V has long been a favorite among families, and for good reason. With its spacious interior and comfortable seating for up to five people, it offers plenty of room for everyone. The CR-V also boasts impressive fuel efficiency, making it a practical choice for those long family road trips. Plus, it comes equipped with advanced safety features like collision mitigation braking and adaptive cruise control, giving you peace of mind on the road.

2. Toyota Highlander: If you’re in need of a larger SUV to accommodate your growing family, the Toyota Highlander is a great option. With seating for up to eight people, it offers plenty of space for everyone to spread out. The Highlander also comes with a range of family-friendly features, such as a rear-seat entertainment system and a hands-free power liftgate. And with its strong safety ratings and reputation for reliability, you can trust that the Highlander will keep your family safe and secure.

3. Ford Explorer: The Ford Explorer is a classic choice for families who need a versatile and capable SUV. With its three rows of seating, it can comfortably accommodate up to seven passengers. The Explorer also offers a range of powerful engine options, allowing you to choose the level of performance that suits your needs. And with its available advanced safety features like blind-spot monitoring and lane-keeping assist, you can feel confident behind the wheel.

4. Subaru Ascent: For families who prioritize safety and all-wheel drive capability, the Subaru Ascent is a top pick. With its spacious interior and seating for up to eight people, it has room for the whole family and then some. The Ascent also comes with standard EyeSight Driver Assist Technology, which includes features like pre-collision braking and lane departure warning. And with its impressive towing capacity, the Ascent is ready for all of your family’s adventures.

5. Volvo XC90: If luxury is a priority for your family, look no further than the Volvo XC90. With its elegant design and high-quality materials, it offers a refined and comfortable driving experience. The XC90 also comes with a range of advanced safety features, such as blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert. And with its spacious interior and seating for up to seven people, it’s perfect for families who want to travel in style.

In conclusion, finding the best SUV for your family doesn’t have to be a daunting task. With our top picks and reviews, you can confidently choose a vehicle that meets all of your family’s needs. Whether you prioritize space, safety, or luxury, there’s an SUV on this list that’s perfect for you. So go ahead and start your family’s next adventure in one of these top-rated SUVs.
