The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Prints and Patterns


As a fashion enthusiast, I couldn’t be more excited to delve into the fascinating world of prints and patterns. Whether you’re a fashion-forward trendsetter or simply looking to express your unique style, prints and patterns can elevate your outfits to new heights. However, like any fashion choice, there are a few dos and don’ts to keep in mind when it comes to incorporating prints and patterns into your wardrobe. In this blog post, we’ll explore some valuable tips and tricks to help you navigate the wonderful world of prints and patterns with confidence.

DO experiment with mixing prints and patterns:
Gone are the days when mixing prints was considered a fashion faux pas. This season, embrace your creativity and experiment with combining different prints and patterns. The key to nailing this trend is to choose complementary colors and scales. For instance, pair a bold floral top with a subtle striped skirt for a chic and eclectic look. Remember, confidence is key when it comes to pulling off this trend, so don’t be afraid to step out of your comfort zone.

DO start with small doses:
If you’re new to prints and patterns, it’s perfectly fine to start small. Begin by incorporating printed accessories like scarves, handbags, or shoes into your outfits. These smaller pieces can add a pop of interest to a monochromatic or neutral ensemble without overwhelming your overall look. As you become more comfortable, you can gradually introduce larger prints into your wardrobe.

DO consider your body shape:
Not all prints and patterns are created equal, and it’s essential to consider your body shape when choosing the right prints for you. If you have a curvier figure, opt for prints with vertical lines to create the illusion of a longer and leaner silhouette. On the other hand, if you have a slimmer frame, larger prints can add volume and give the appearance of curves. Remember, the goal is to enhance your natural shape, so choose prints that flatter your body type.

DON’T be afraid of bold prints:
While subtle prints can be versatile and timeless, don’t shy away from bold and vibrant patterns. These eye-catching prints can instantly elevate your outfit and make a statement. However, balance is key. When wearing a bold print, keep the rest of your ensemble simple and let the print take center stage. Pair a vibrant floral dress with minimal accessories and neutral shoes to allow the print to shine.

DON’T forget about proportion:
One common mistake when wearing prints and patterns is disregarding proportion. If you’re wearing a bold and busy print on top, balance it out with a more subdued pattern or a solid color on the bottom. Similarly, if you opt for a printed skirt or pants, pair it with a simpler top to maintain a harmonious look. Remember, the goal is to create a balanced and cohesive outfit that doesn’t overwhelm the eye.

DON’T forget about the occasion:
While prints and patterns can be fun and playful, it’s crucial to consider the occasion when selecting your outfit. For formal events or professional settings, opt for more subdued and sophisticated prints like pinstripes, houndstooth, or subtle geometric patterns. Save the bold and vibrant prints for casual outings or social gatherings where you can let your personality shine.

In conclusion, wearing prints and patterns is all about self-expression and embracing your unique style. By following these dos and don’ts, you can confidently navigate the world of prints and patterns and create visually stunning outfits that reflect your personality. So go ahead, mix and match, experiment, and most importantly, have fun with your fashion choices.
