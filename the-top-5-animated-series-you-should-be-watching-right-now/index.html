The Top 5 Animated Series You Should Be Watching Right Now


Animation has come a long way from being just for kids. With the rise of streaming services and a growing appreciation for the art form, there’s an abundance of incredible animated series that cater to audiences of all ages. Whether you’re a fan of action, comedy, drama, or fantasy, there’s something for everyone. To help you navigate the vast world of animated series, here are the top 5 shows that you should be watching right now.

1. “Avatar: The Last Airbender”

“Avatar: The Last Airbender” is a timeless classic that continues to captivate audiences with its rich storytelling and compelling characters. Set in a world where people can manipulate the elements, the series follows Aang, the last Airbender and the Avatar, as he embarks on a journey to save the world from the tyrannical Fire Nation. With its blend of humor, action, and profound themes, “Avatar: The Last Airbender” is a must-watch for anyone seeking an epic adventure.

2. “Bojack Horseman”

“Bojack Horseman” is a darkly comedic animated series that delves into the life of its titular character, a washed-up actor who also happens to be a horse. This show expertly tackles themes of depression, addiction, and the pursuit of happiness, all while maintaining a sharp wit and clever humor. With its unique blend of animation and mature storytelling, “Bojack Horseman” stands out as a poignant and thought-provoking series that shouldn’t be missed.

3. “Rick and Morty”

“Rick and Morty” has taken the animated world by storm with its irreverent humor, mind-bending sci-fi adventures, and complex characters. Following the escapades of the eccentric scientist Rick Sanchez and his grandson Morty, the show seamlessly combines zany humor with existential themes, creating a viewing experience that is as intellectually stimulating as it is entertaining. If you’re a fan of off-the-wall humor and multiverse shenanigans, “Rick and Morty” is a must-add to your watchlist.

4. “Gravity Falls”

“Gravity Falls” is a charming animated series that follows the adventures of twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend their summer with their great-uncle in the enigmatic town of Gravity Falls. Filled with mystery, supernatural occurrences, and heartwarming moments, the show strikes a perfect balance between lighthearted fun and compelling storytelling. With its well-crafted plot and endearing characters, “Gravity Falls” is a delightful journey that will leave you wanting more.

5. “The Dragon Prince”

“The Dragon Prince” is a fantasy epic that transports viewers to the magical world of Xadia, where humans and magical creatures are embroiled in a conflict that threatens to consume the land. With its stunning animation, intricate world-building, and diverse cast of characters, “The Dragon Prince” offers a captivating narrative that appeals to fans of high fantasy and epic adventures. If you’re craving a rich fantasy world filled with magic and intrigue, “The Dragon Prince” is the perfect series to dive into.

In conclusion, the world of animated series is brimming with diverse and captivating shows that cater to a wide range of tastes and preferences. Whether you’re in the mood for epic adventures, profound storytelling, or offbeat humor, the top 5 animated series mentioned above are sure to offer an enriching and entertaining viewing experience. So, grab your popcorn, settle in, and let these animated gems transport you to worlds beyond your imagination. Happy watching!
