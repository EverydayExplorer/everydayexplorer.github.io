Embracing Diversity: Games That Highlight Different Cultures and Perspectives


In today’s increasingly interconnected world, embracing diversity has become more important than ever. Cultural diversity brings richness, uniqueness, and a wider range of perspectives to our lives. It allows us to learn and appreciate different customs, traditions, and ways of thinking. While there are various mediums through which we can explore and celebrate diversity, one particularly powerful platform is video games.

Video games have evolved from simple entertainment to immersive experiences that can tell complex stories, foster empathy, and provide a platform for cultural representation. They have the potential to bridge gaps, challenge stereotypes, and promote understanding among people from diverse backgrounds. In this blog, we will explore some games that effectively highlight different cultures and perspectives, allowing players to immerse themselves in diverse worlds and narratives.

1. ‘Never Alone’ (Kisima Ingitchuna):
Developed in collaboration with the Alaska Native community, ‘Never Alone’ is a beautiful puzzle-platformer that explores the indigenous culture of the Iñupiat people. The game showcases their stories, folklore, and values. Through stunning visuals and engaging gameplay, players not only enjoy a captivating gaming experience, but also gain insight into an often overlooked and misunderstood culture.

2. ‘Celeste’:
Although primarily a challenging platformer, ‘Celeste’ delves into themes of mental health and self-discovery. The game follows the journey of Madeline, a young woman battling with anxiety and depression, as she climbs a treacherous mountain. By exploring Madeline’s inner struggles, players gain a deeper understanding of mental health issues, promoting empathy and reducing the stigma associated with them.

3. ‘Journey’:
‘Journey’ is a visually stunning adventure game that emphasizes connection and cooperation among players. Set in a vast desert, the game encourages exploration and discovery while fostering a sense of companionship. As players encounter one another in the game world, they communicate through nonverbal actions, breaking down linguistic and cultural barriers. This unique multiplayer experience promotes a sense of unity and appreciation for diverse interactions.

4. ‘Papo & Yo’:
‘Papo & Yo’ is an emotional puzzle-platformer inspired by the designer’s own experiences growing up in Brazil. The game metaphorically explores the complex relationship between a young boy, Quico, and his alcoholic and abusive father figure, referred to as Monster. By using magical realism, the game addresses themes of addiction, domestic violence, and the power of forgiveness. It offers players a novel perspective on these issues, challenging societal norms and encouraging empathy.

5. ‘Assassin’s Creed’ series:
Known for its historical accuracy and attention to detail, the ‘Assassin’s Creed’ series has transported players to various time periods and locations around the world. Each installment presents an opportunity to explore different cultures, such as Renaissance Italy, ancient Egypt, and feudal Japan. By immersing players in richly recreated historical settings, the games offer a glimpse into diverse societies, customs, and perspectives throughout history.

These are just a few examples of games that embrace diversity and provide unique experiences to players. By incorporating elements of different cultures and shedding light on important social issues, these games foster understanding, empathy, and cultural appreciation. They remind us of the value of diversity and the power of interactive storytelling in promoting inclusivity.

As we continue to push the boundaries of video game narratives, it is essential that developers and players alike recognize the importance of embracing diversity. By supporting and celebrating games that highlight different cultures and perspectives, we contribute to a more inclusive and understanding society. Let us continue to explore, learn, and grow through the medium of video games, and embrace the richness that diversity brings to our gaming experiences.
