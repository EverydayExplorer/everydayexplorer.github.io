10 Genius Kitchen Hacks to Simplify Your Cooking


We’ve all been there - standing in the kitchen, trying to whip up a meal, but feeling like there just isn’t enough time or energy to get it all done. Thankfully, there are some amazing kitchen hacks that can help simplify your cooking process and save you both time and effort. In this article, we’ll explore 10 genius kitchen hacks that will revolutionize the way you cook.

## 1. Freezing Fresh Herbs

Fresh herbs add incredible flavor to our dishes, but they often go bad before we can use them all. A fantastic hack is to freeze any leftover herbs in ice cube trays filled with olive oil. This way, you’ll always have a supply of herb-infused oil ready to use in your cooking.

## 2. Easy Garlic Peeling

Peeling garlic can be a tedious task, but it doesn’t have to be. Simply place the clove on a cutting board and apply light pressure with the flat side of a knife. The skin will loosen, making it easy to remove.

## 3. Quick and Efficient Tomato Slicing

Slicing cherry tomatoes individually can take up a lot of time. But by placing them between two plates and using a sharp knife to slice through the space in between, you can cut multiple tomatoes in one go.

## 4. Speedy Citrus Juicing

Getting the most juice out of your citrus fruits can be a challenge. To make it easier, roll the fruit back and forth on the countertop while applying gentle pressure. Then, slice it in half and use a fork to easily extract all the juice.

## 5. Instant Egg Ripeness Test

Ever wondered if your eggs are still fresh? Fill a bowl with water and gently place the eggs inside. If the eggs sink to the bottom, they are fresh. If they float to the top, they are no longer good to eat.

## 6. Efficient Avocado Preparation

Avocados are delicious, but sometimes it’s hard to know when they’re ripe. To speed up the process, place unripe avocados in a paper bag with a banana or apple. The ethylene gas emitted by these fruits will help ripen the avocado faster.

## 7. Perfect Pancakes Every Time

Achieving evenly cooked pancakes can be tricky, but there’s a hack to ensure perfection. Use an ice cream scoop to measure out the batter for each pancake. This guarantees consistent size and cooking time.

## 8. Preventing Over-boiling

Have you ever had a pot of water boil over while cooking pasta or rice? To prevent this, place a wooden spoon across the top of the pot. It will break the surface tension and prevent the water from boiling over.

## 9. Mess-free Breading

Coating chicken or vegetables in breadcrumbs can be a messy process. To make it easier, place the breadcrumbs in a resealable plastic bag along with the food item. Shake it gently until everything is evenly coated.

## 10. Instant Ripening of Bananas

If you’re hankering for a ripe banana but only have green ones, there’s a hack for that too. You can speed up the ripening process by placing the bananas in a brown paper bag. The ethylene gas released by the bananas will make them ripen faster.

By implementing these 10 genius kitchen hacks, you’ll be able to simplify your cooking, save time, and minimize the stress associated with meal preparation. Give them a try and watch as your culinary skills become more efficient and enjoyable.
