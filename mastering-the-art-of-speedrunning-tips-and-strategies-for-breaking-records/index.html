Mastering the Art of Speedrunning: Tips and Strategies for Breaking Records


Speedrunning has become a popular and competitive form of gaming over the past few decades. Whether it’s breaking a record time in a classic platformer or completing a game as quickly as possible, speedrunning requires a unique set of skills and strategies. In this blog post, we will delve into the world of speedrunning and provide tips and strategies for aspiring speedrunners looking to break records.

The first thing to understand about speedrunning is that it’s not just about playing a game quickly. It’s about finding the most efficient routes, utilizing glitches and exploits, and mastering precise movements. To truly excel in speedrunning, you need to have a deep understanding of the game you’re playing.

One of the first steps in mastering the art of speedrunning is to choose a game that you’re passionate about. It’s important to choose a game that you enjoy playing over and over again, as speedrunning often involves countless attempts and a significant time investment. When you’re passionate about a game, you’ll be more motivated to put in the hours of practice needed to break records.

Once you’ve chosen a game, it’s time to start studying it. Watch speedruns of the game on platforms like Twitch or YouTube and take notes on the strategies used by top runners. Look for shortcuts, sequence breaks, and strategies that can help you save time. The more you understand the game and its mechanics, the better equipped you’ll be to find ways to exploit them and shave off precious seconds.

Another important aspect of speedrunning is optimization. This involves finding the most efficient routes through a game, using the optimal weapons or power-ups, and minimizing time spent on nonessential tasks. Pay attention to the movement and actions of top speedrunners in your chosen game and try to replicate their techniques. Sometimes, a seemingly small movement or action can make a significant difference in your overall time.

In addition to optimizing routes and movements, speedrunners often rely on glitches and exploits to gain an advantage. Glitches are unintended errors in a game’s code that can be used to skip sections or gain an unfair advantage. Exploiting these glitches requires an in-depth understanding of the game’s mechanics and often involves precise timing and execution. However, it’s important to note that not all speedruns rely on glitches, as some categories have specific rules against their use. Be sure to familiarize yourself with the rules of your chosen speedrun category before attempting to use glitches.

Practice, practice, practice. This is perhaps the most important tip for aspiring speedrunners. Speedrunning requires a high level of skill and muscle memory, which can only be gained through repetitive practice. Set aside dedicated time each day to practice your chosen speedrun, and don’t be discouraged by failure. Speedrunning is all about pushing your limits and constantly striving for improvement. It’s not uncommon for speedrunners to spend months or even years practicing a single run before achieving a record-breaking time.

Finally, join the speedrunning community. Sharing your accomplishments and getting feedback from other speedrunners can be incredibly valuable. Online forums, Discord servers, and social media groups are great places to connect with like-minded individuals who can offer advice and support. Participating in speedrunning events and competitions can also help you improve your skills by providing opportunities to learn from experienced runners and compete against others.

In conclusion, mastering the art of speedrunning requires dedication, skill, and a deep understanding of the game you’re playing. Choosing a game you’re passionate about, studying top runners, optimizing routes and movements, utilizing glitches and exploits (if allowed), practicing consistently, and engaging with the speedrunning community are all key components to breaking records in speedrunning. So, grab your controller, choose your game, and start mastering the art of speedrunning today!
