10 Hotels with Beautiful Gardens and Landscapes


Nestled amidst the hustle and bustle of urban life, there are sanctuaries of tranquility and beauty waiting to be discovered. Hotels with stunning gardens and landscapes offer a retreat from the ordinary, inviting guests to immerse themselves in nature’s splendor. From lush tropical havens to meticulously manicured botanical wonders, these 10 hotels stand out for their breathtaking outdoor spaces.

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto, Japan
Transport yourself to a world of serene elegance at The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto. The hotel’s traditional Japanese garden, complete with a tranquil pond and meticulously pruned trees, provides a peaceful escape in the heart of the city.

2. Belmond Hotel Caruso, Amalfi Coast, Italy
Perched on a cliff overlooking the Amalfi Coast, Belmond Hotel Caruso boasts terraced gardens adorned with vibrant flowers and fragrant citrus trees. Guests can wander among the blooms while taking in panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea.

3. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, India
Immerse yourself in the opulence of The Oberoi Udaivilas, where the sprawling gardens reflect the grandeur of the surrounding palatial architecture. Reflecting pools, fountains, and lush greenery create a sense of serenity and romance.

4. Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise, Alberta, Canada
Set against the backdrop of the majestic Canadian Rockies, Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise is renowned for its stunning alpine gardens. Guests can explore the grounds, framed by snow-capped peaks and the glittering turquoise waters of Lake Louise.

5. The Brando, Tetiaroa, French Polynesia
Surrounded by the crystal-clear waters of the South Pacific, The Brando’s private island setting is complemented by lush, tropical gardens. Native flora flourishes in this paradise, providing a vibrant contrast to the azure seas and pristine beaches.

6. The Datai Langkawi, Malaysia
Enveloped by ancient rainforest, The Datai Langkawi offers a verdant oasis for nature lovers. The hotel’s gardens are a harmonious blend of exotic plants, cascading streams, and the enchanting sounds of wildlife.

7. Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort, Thailand
Nestled in the heart of the Golden Triangle, this resort boasts gardens that harmonize with the natural beauty of the surrounding landscape. Guests can stroll through the lush foliage and admire panoramic views of three countries intersecting at the Mekong River.

8. Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora, French Polynesia
Picture-perfect views and tropical splendor await at Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora. The resort’s gardens showcase a riot of color, with fragrant blooms set against the backdrop of Mount Otemanu and the sparkling lagoon.

9. The Chedi Muscat, Oman
Reflecting the beauty of Omani architecture and design, The Chedi Muscat’s gardens are a study in refined elegance. Tranquil ponds, manicured lawns, and ornamental trees create a sense of serenity in this coastal oasis.

10. The Ritz-Carlton, Half Moon Bay, California, USA
Overlooking the rugged California coastline, The Ritz-Carlton, Half Moon Bay invites guests to explore its coastal gardens. Winding paths lead through native flora, offering glimpses of the Pacific Ocean and a chance to reconnect with nature.

In conclusion, these 10 hotels with beautiful gardens and landscapes offer a sensory journey through some of the most captivating natural settings around the world. Whether it’s the tranquility of a Japanese garden or the vibrancy of a tropical paradise, these hotels provide an opportunity to escape into the beauty of nature while enjoying luxurious accommodations and exceptional hospitality.
