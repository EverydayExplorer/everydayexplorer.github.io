How to Incorporate Animal Print into Your Outfits


Animal print has been a prominent fashion trend for many years, and it continues to dominate the runways and the streets. Whether you’re a fan of the classic leopard print or the bold zebra stripes, incorporating animal print into your outfits can add a touch of sophistication and style. In this article, we will explore different ways to embrace this trend and make it a statement piece in your wardrobe.

Start with Accessories

If you’re new to animal print or you’re not sure how to incorporate it into your outfits, accessories are a great way to start. Animal print scarves, belts, and handbags can instantly elevate even the simplest of outfits. They add a pop of print and allow you to experiment with different color combinations. For a subtle look, try pairing a leopard print belt with a black dress or jeans and a crisp white shirt.

Mix and Match

One of the key rules when it comes to incorporating animal print into your outfits is to treat it like a neutral. This means you can mix it with other patterns and colors for a chic and fashion-forward look. For example, try pairing a leopard print blouse with a striped skirt or a zebra print jacket with a floral dress. The key is to balance the prints and colors, so they complement each other rather than clash.

Go All Out

If you’re feeling bold and adventurous, don’t be afraid to go all out with animal print. A statement animal print coat or a pair of leopard print pants can make a powerful fashion statement. Just remember to keep the rest of your outfit simple to let the animal print take center stage. Pair a bold animal print dress with neutral accessories and shoes to create a striking and stylish look.

Play with Textures

Animal print doesn’t have to be limited to clothing. You can also incorporate it into your outfits through textured fabrics. Look for animal print accessories or clothing made from materials like faux fur, snakeskin, or crocodile leather. Adding these textures to your outfits creates visual interest and adds a luxurious touch. Consider a faux snakeskin bag or a leopard print faux fur jacket to make a bold style statement.

Use Animal Print as a Pop of Color

Animal print often comes in neutral hues like browns, blacks, and tans. To add a pop of color to your outfits, look for animal print pieces that incorporate vibrant shades. For instance, you can find leopard print shoes with red or blue accents, or zebra print tops with colorful stripes. Pair these bold animal print pieces with monochromatic outfits to let the colors shine.

Don’t Forget about Animal Print Footwear

Animal print shoes are a fantastic way to incorporate this trend into your outfits. Whether you opt for leopard print pumps, zebra print sneakers, or snakeskin boots, animal print footwear can instantly elevate any outfit. Treat them as a statement piece and build your outfit around them. For example, pair leopard print pumps with a little black dress or wear snakeskin boots with jeans and a cozy sweater for a chic and edgy look.

Conclusion

Incorporating animal print into your outfits can instantly elevate your style and make a bold fashion statement. Start with accessories and gradually experiment with mixing prints and textures. Be bold and go all out with animal print garments, but remember to keep the rest of your outfit simple. Be sure to also use animal print as a pop of color and don’t forget about animal print footwear. With these tips in mind, you can confidently embrace this timeless trend and make it your own.
