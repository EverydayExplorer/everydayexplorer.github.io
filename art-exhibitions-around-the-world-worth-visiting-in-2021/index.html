Art Exhibitions Around the World Worth Visiting in 2021


As we enter the new year, art enthusiasts and culture lovers are eagerly anticipating the return of art exhibitions around the world. After a challenging year where many exhibitions were canceled or postponed, 2021 promises to be a vibrant and exciting year for the art world. In this blog, we will explore some of the most anticipated art exhibitions happening across the globe in 2021.

1. ‘The Future Is Now’ - Museum of Modern Art (MoMA), New York City, USA
Kicking off our list is the renowned Museum of Modern Art’s exhibition, ‘The Future Is Now.’ This exhibition showcases groundbreaking contemporary artworks that explore the intersection of art and technology. From immersive installations to interactive digital art pieces, MoMA promises to transport visitors into a world where art and technology merge seamlessly.

2. ‘Renaissance Reimagined’ - Louvre Museum, Paris, France
The Louvre Museum, home to some of the world’s most iconic masterpieces, presents ‘Renaissance Reimagined.’ This exhibition celebrates the timeless beauty of Renaissance art while reimagining it through a modern lens. Visitors will have the opportunity to witness the juxtaposition of classic and contemporary art forms, showcasing the enduring influence of the Renaissance period.

3. ‘The Power of Nature’ - National Gallery, London, UK
Nature has always been an inspiration for artists, and the National Gallery in London is bringing this connection to life with their exhibition, ‘The Power of Nature.’ Through a curated collection of landscape paintings, visitors will be able to immerse themselves in the raw beauty and untamed power of nature as depicted by some of the greatest artists in history.

4. ‘Beyond Boundaries’ - Art Gallery of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia
The Art Gallery of New South Wales presents ‘Beyond Boundaries,’ an exhibition that challenges traditional notions of art by exploring the boundaries between different artistic mediums. From paintings and sculptures to video installations and performance art, this exhibition pushes the boundaries of what art can be, blurring the lines between different art forms.

5. ‘Surreal Visions’ - Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofia, Madrid, Spain
If you’re a fan of surrealism, the Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofia in Madrid is the place to be in 2021. The exhibition ‘Surreal Visions’ brings together the works of renowned surrealist artists such as Salvador Dali, Rene Magritte, and Joan Miro. Visitors will have the opportunity to delve into the enigmatic and dreamlike world of surrealism, where reality and imagination intertwine.

These are just a few of the many art exhibitions happening around the world in 2021. Whether you’re a seasoned art connoisseur or simply looking to immerse yourself in the beauty of artistic expression, these exhibitions offer a unique and enriching experience. Don’t miss the opportunity to witness the creativity and innovation of artists from around the globe as they showcase their work in these exceptional exhibitions. Plan your art-filled journey now and let the magic of art transport you to new heights in 2021.
