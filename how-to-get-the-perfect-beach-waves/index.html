How to Get the Perfect Beach Waves


Are you dreaming of having that effortlessly stunning hairstyle that beach waves offer? Beach waves have become incredibly popular due to their natural and relaxed look. Whether you have short or long hair, you can achieve those perfect beach waves in just a few simple steps. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to get the perfect beach waves and rock that beachy look all year round.

Step 1: Prepare your Hair

Before you start creating those beautiful beach waves, it’s important to prepare your hair properly. Start by washing your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will add texture and fullness to your hair, providing a great base for the waves.

After washing, towel-dry your hair until it is slightly damp. Avoid vigorous rubbing, as it can cause frizz and damage to your hair. Instead, gently pat or squeeze out excess water.

Step 2: Apply a Sea Salt Spray

To achieve that characteristic beachy texture, you’ll need to use a high-quality sea salt spray. Sea salt sprays add volume and create a tousled look, giving you those perfect beach waves.

Spray the sea salt spray evenly throughout your damp hair, focusing on the midsections and ends. Avoid spraying it directly on your roots as it can make your hair look greasy. Once applied, scrunch your hair with your fingers to enhance the effect of the waves.

Step 3: Divide and Conquer

Now that your hair is prepped and sprayed with the sea salt product, it’s time to divide your hair into sections. This step will make it easier for you to work on your hair and ensure you don’t miss any parts.

Take a comb or your fingers and create two sections. Part your hair in the middle and separate it into two equal parts. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip, leaving the bottom section open for styling.

Step 4: Curl and Twist

In this step, you’ll be curling and twisting your hair to create those beautiful beach waves. With a curling iron or a wand, take small sections of your hair and wrap them around the curling tool, leaving the ends out to have a more natural-looking finish.

Hold the hair wrapped around the curling tool for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this process with all the sections of your hair, alternating the direction of the curls to create a more natural and effortless look.

Step 5: Finger Comb and Set

Once you have curled all your hair, it’s time to let it cool down and set. Allow your hair to cool completely before touching it, as this will help the waves hold their shape.

Once your hair has cooled, gently run your fingers through the waves to loosen them and create that effortless beachy look. You can also use a wide-toothed comb or a paddle brush to further comb through the waves, but make sure to avoid brushing too aggressively to prevent the waves from frizzing out.

Step 6: Finish and Set

The last step in achieving the perfect beach waves is to finish and set your hair. Applying a lightweight hairspray will help hold the waves in place and keep them from falling out throughout the day.

Spray a small amount of hairspray over your hair and then use your fingers to scrunch and tousle the waves lightly. This step will enhance the texture and make your waves look more natural.

Maintenance Tips

To maintain your beach waves and ensure they last longer, you can follow these simple tips:

  1. Avoid washing your hair every day. Overwashing can strip away natural oils and make your waves fall flat. Instead, opt for dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.
  2. Before going to bed, loosely braid your hair or create a loose top knot to preserve the waves overnight.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair oil or leave-in conditioner to the ends of your hair to hydrate and prevent frizz.

In conclusion, getting the perfect beach waves is easier than you think. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve that laid-back, effortlessly beautiful look. Remember to prepare your hair properly, use a sea salt spray, curl and twist your hair, finger comb and set, and finish it off with a lightweight hairspray. By following these steps and the maintenance tips, you’ll be able to rock those beach waves all year long. So go ahead, embrace that beachy vibe and enjoy your gorgeous waves!
