Hotels with Secret Speakeasies: Uncovering Hidden Drinking Dens


Imagine stepping into a luxurious hotel lobby, adorned with elegant decor and a sophisticated ambiance. You check into your room, but little do you know that just beyond the walls lies a hidden secret - a secret speakeasy, waiting to be discovered. Welcome to the world of hotels with secret speakeasies, where hidden drinking dens offer an escape from the ordinary and transport you back to the era of prohibition.

These clandestine establishments, reminiscent of the 1920s speakeasies, have become a popular trend in the hospitality industry. Tucked away behind unmarked doors, disguised as bookshelves or hidden within basements, these secret speakeasies offer an exclusive and intimate experience for those in the know. Let’s take a journey and uncover some of the most intriguing hidden drinking dens within hotels around the world.

1. The Backroom at The Beekman Hotel, New York City:
Located in the heart of Lower Manhattan, The Beekman Hotel is a historic landmark renowned for its stunning architecture. However, it is the hidden gem known as The Backroom that truly captures the essence of a bygone era. Step through a discreet entrance and find yourself transported back to the 1920s. With its dimly lit atmosphere, vintage furnishings, and live jazz music, The Backroom offers a truly immersive experience.

2. The Blind Pig at Hotel Royal, Singapore:
Nestled within the vibrant district of Chinatown, Hotel Royal is home to The Blind Pig, a secret speakeasy that pays homage to the city’s rich history. Enter through a hidden door concealed within a tailor shop and be transported to a world of glamour and intrigue. Indulge in finely crafted cocktails inspired by the Prohibition era, while enjoying the live performances and the speakeasy’s mysterious ambiance.

3. The Gibson at The Sterling Hotel, London:
Located in the bustling neighborhood of Fitzrovia, The Sterling Hotel hides a secret in its basement - The Gibson. This hidden gem is known for its exquisite cocktails and unique concoctions. The speakeasy’s art deco-inspired interior, complete with plush velvet seating and dim lighting, creates an intimate atmosphere for guests to enjoy their drinks. The Gibson’s menu features a wide array of spirits, including their signature gin-based cocktails.

4. The Pharmacy at The Nomad Hotel, Los Angeles:
Tucked away within The Nomad Hotel in downtown Los Angeles is The Pharmacy, a hidden speakeasy that pays tribute to the building’s former life as a bank. The entrance, hidden behind a vault-like door, sets the tone for a secretive and exclusive experience. Inside, guests can enjoy expertly crafted cocktails, along with a menu inspired by the Prohibition era. The Pharmacy also offers a range of live music performances to enhance the ambiance.

5. The Secret Bar at The Lanesborough, London:
Situated in the heart of London’s prestigious Knightsbridge neighborhood, The Lanesborough is known for its luxurious accommodations. However, hidden within its opulent halls is a secret bar, aptly named The Secret Bar. Accessed through a concealed entrance, this speakeasy offers a refined and intimate setting for guests to enjoy their favorite libations. With its extensive collection of spirits and expert mixologists, The Secret Bar is a hidden gem for cocktail enthusiasts.

Hotels with secret speakeasies offer a unique and enchanting experience for those seeking something beyond the ordinary. These hidden drinking dens provide a sense of exclusivity and a chance to step back in time to an era of mystery and allure. Whether you’re a history aficionado, a cocktail enthusiast, or simply someone looking for a memorable night out, uncovering these hidden gems within hotels will undoubtedly leave you with unforgettable memories. So, next time you check into a hotel, take a moment to explore beyond the lobby, because you never know what hidden treasures may be waiting to be discovered.
