Hotels with Vintage Train Rides: Traveling Back in Time


Embark on a journey back in time with a stay at one of the many hotels that offer vintage train rides as part of their guest experiences. From the majestic landscapes of the countryside to the bustling city streets, these historic trains transport passengers to a bygone era, allowing them to experience the romance and nostalgia of rail travel. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the hotels around the world that offer vintage train rides and the unforgettable experiences they provide.

  1. Napa Valley Wine Train, California Step aboard the Napa Valley Wine Train for a journey through California’s famed wine country. Departing from downtown Napa, this beautifully restored vintage train takes passengers on a scenic ride through the picturesque vineyards and rolling hills of the Napa Valley. Along the way, guests can indulge in gourmet cuisine and world-class wines, making for an unforgettable culinary experience amidst stunning scenery.

  2. Royal Scotsman, Scotland Experience the charm of the Scottish Highlands aboard the Royal Scotsman, a luxury train that offers guests a truly unforgettable journey through some of Scotland’s most breathtaking landscapes. Departing from Edinburgh, this elegant train takes passengers on a scenic tour of castles, lochs, and glens, with stops at historic sites and charming villages along the way. With luxurious accommodations and fine dining on board, the Royal Scotsman offers a truly indulgent way to explore the beauty of Scotland.

  3. Venice Simplon-Orient-Express, Europe Step back in time to the golden age of rail travel with a journey aboard the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express. Departing from Venice, this iconic train takes passengers on a luxurious journey through Europe’s most scenic landscapes, including the Swiss Alps, the Austrian countryside, and the French Riviera. With its elegant Art Deco interiors and impeccable service, the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express offers a truly glamorous way to experience the romance of rail travel.

  4. The Ghan, Australia Experience the vastness of the Australian Outback aboard The Ghan, one of the world’s great train journeys. Departing from Adelaide, this legendary train takes passengers on a three-day journey through the heart of Australia, passing through rugged desert landscapes, ancient Indigenous sites, and historic Outback towns. With its comfortable accommodations and gourmet dining, The Ghan offers a unique and unforgettable way to explore the beauty and diversity of Australia’s interior.

  5. Blue Train, South Africa Embark on a journey through the stunning landscapes of South Africa aboard the Blue Train, one of the world’s most luxurious trains. Departing from Pretoria, this elegant train takes passengers on a scenic journey through the rolling hills of the Cape Winelands, the stark beauty of the Karoo desert, and the breathtaking scenery of the Garden Route. With its opulent accommodations and gourmet cuisine, the Blue Train offers a truly indulgent way to experience the beauty and diversity of South Africa.

In conclusion, hotels that offer vintage train rides provide guests with a unique opportunity to experience the romance and nostalgia of rail travel while exploring some of the world’s most breathtaking landscapes. Whether you’re journeying through the vineyards of Napa Valley or the rugged Outback of Australia, these historic trains offer an unforgettable way to travel back in time and create memories that will last a lifetime.
