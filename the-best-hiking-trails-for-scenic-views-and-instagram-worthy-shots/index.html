The Best Hiking Trails for Scenic Views and Instagram-Worthy Shots


Hiking is a wonderful way to reconnect with nature, challenge yourself physically, and capture some amazing scenery for your Instagram feed. Whether you’re an avid hiker or just starting out, there’s no denying the thrill of reaching a mountaintop or a picturesque overlook.

If you’re looking for the best hiking trails that offer both scenic views and Instagram-worthy shots, you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the most breathtaking hiking trails around the world that will not only leave you in awe but also provide the perfect backdrop for your next Instagram post.

1. The Inca Trail, Peru:
The Inca Trail is a legendary hiking route that takes you through the Peruvian Andes, leading to the iconic Machu Picchu. This ancient trail boasts stunning views of snow-capped mountains, cloud forests, and Incan ruins. Every step you take on this trail will leave you mesmerized and eager to capture the experience for your Instagram followers.

2. Torres del Paine, Chile:
Located in the Patagonia region of Chile, Torres del Paine National Park is a hiker’s paradise. The park is home to several breathtaking trails, including the famous W Trek and the challenging Circuit Trek. With its dramatic landscapes of towering granite peaks, glaciers, and stunning turquoise lakes, this destination is a dream come true for outdoor enthusiasts and Instagrammers alike.

3. Zion National Park, USA:
Zion National Park in Utah offers some of the most dramatic and awe-inspiring landscapes in the United States. The park features a variety of trails, ranging from easy strolls to demanding hikes. Angels Landing and The Narrows are two iconic trails that offer breathtaking views of towering cliffs, narrow canyons, and the Virgin River. Don’t forget to bring your camera along to capture the stunning red rock formations and natural beauty of this unique landscape.

4. Cinque Terre, Italy:
Cinque Terre, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is a collection of five charming fishing villages nestled along the rugged coastline of the Italian Riviera. The hiking trails that connect these picturesque villages are not only a delight for the eyes but also a photographer’s dream come true. From vineyard-covered hillsides to colorful cliffside houses, every corner of Cinque Terre offers a photo opportunity you won’t want to miss.

5. Haiku Stairs, Hawaii:
Also known as the Stairway to Heaven, the Haiku Stairs in Oahu, Hawaii, is a thrilling hike that offers breathtaking aerial views of the island. This stairway, consisting of nearly 4,000 steps, takes you through lush rainforest and leads to a panoramic vista of the island’s coastline. While this trail is technically closed to the public, it continues to attract adventurous hikers seeking the perfect Instagram shot.

6. Trolltunga, Norway:
Trolltunga, or the Troll’s Tongue, is a famous cliff rock formation in Norway that juts out horizontally over Lake Ringedalsvatnet. The hike to Trolltunga is challenging but definitely worth the effort. Along the way, you’ll encounter stunning alpine scenery, deep fjords, and snow-capped mountains. Standing on Trolltunga’s edge and snapping a photo will make for an unforgettable Instagram post that will make all your friends jealous.

7. Mount Fuji, Japan:
Mount Fuji is not only the highest mountain in Japan but also an iconic symbol of the country. Thousands of hikers visit Mount Fuji each year to conquer its peak and enjoy its breathtaking views. The climb to the summit is challenging, but the reward of witnessing the sunrise from the top is unparalleled. Capture the mystical beauty of this sacred mountain with your camera and share it with your Instagram followers.

8. Tongariro Alpine Crossing, New Zealand:
Considered one of the best day hikes in the world, the Tongariro Alpine Crossing takes you through a volcanic landscape with emerald lakes, steam vents, and stunning panoramic views. This trail offers a diverse range of scenery, including towering mountains, alpine meadows, and rugged terrain. The vivid colors and unique geology of this trail will provide countless Instagram-worthy shots.

9. The Great Wall of China, China:
The Great Wall of China is not only a magnificent architectural wonder but also an incredible hiking destination. Several sections of the wall are open for hiking, with each offering its own unique scenery. Whether you choose to hike the popular Mutianyu section or venture off the beaten path, walking along the Great Wall will provide you with unparalleled views of the surrounding landscapes and a chance to capture some truly remarkable Instagram photos.

10. Blue Mountains, Australia:
Located just a short distance from Sydney, the Blue Mountains is a paradise for nature lovers and hikers. The rugged cliffs, dense eucalyptus forests, and stunning waterfalls make this World Heritage-listed region a perfect spot for hiking and photography. The famous Three Sisters rock formation and the Katoomba Falls are must-visit attractions that will add a touch of magic to your Instagram feed.

In conclusion, these ten hiking trails offer not only scenic views that will take your breath away but also the perfect backdrop for your next Instagram-worthy shot. So lace up your hiking boots, grab your camera, and explore these incredible destinations that will provide unforgettable memories and stunning photos for your Instagram feed. Happy hiking and snapping!
