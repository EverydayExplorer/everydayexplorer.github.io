The Perfect Road Trip: Hotels Near Iconic Driving Routes


Are you ready to embark on an unforgettable road trip adventure? Exploring iconic driving routes is not only a great way to experience the beauty of the world, but it also allows you to create lasting memories. And what better way to enhance your road trip experience than by staying at hotels that are conveniently located near these breathtaking routes? In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to discover some of the best hotels near iconic driving routes around the globe. So, fasten your seatbelts and let’s hit the road!

Pacific Coast Highway, California

The Pacific Coast Highway, also known as California State Route 1, stretches along the stunning coastline of California. This iconic driving route offers awe-inspiring views of the Pacific Ocean, rugged cliffs, and charming coastal towns. As you make your way along this scenic highway, be sure to check out the beautiful Bixby Creek Bridge and the picturesque towns of Carmel-by-the-Sea and Santa Barbara.

When it comes to finding the perfect hotel along the Pacific Coast Highway, the Post Ranch Inn in Big Sur is a top choice. Nestled on a cliff overlooking the ocean, this luxurious eco-resort offers breathtaking views, world-class amenities, and an unforgettable dining experience. If you prefer a more budget-friendly option, the Carmel Mission Inn in Carmel-by-the-Sea is a great choice, offering comfortable accommodations and easy access to the beach.

Great Ocean Road, Australia

If you find yourself in Australia, don’t miss the opportunity to explore the Great Ocean Road. This scenic route spans along the southeastern coast of Australia, offering stunning views of the rugged coastline, pristine beaches, and iconic landmarks such as the Twelve Apostles rock formations. As you drive along this picturesque road, make sure to stop by the charming seaside towns of Lorne and Apollo Bay.

For a truly unique and luxurious experience, consider staying at the Alkina Lodge in Wattle Hill. This eco-friendly accommodation offers modern and spacious villas nestled in the heart of the Otway Ranges, surrounded by native wildlife and breathtaking landscapes. If you’re looking for a more budget-friendly option, the Lorne Hotel in Lorne provides comfortable rooms and a central location, making it a perfect base for exploring the Great Ocean Road.

Ring Road, Iceland

Iceland’s Ring Road, also known as Route 1, is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts. This 1,332-kilometer road circles the entire island, offering an incredible display of Iceland’s natural wonders, including glaciers, waterfalls, volcanoes, and geothermal hot springs. Along the way, make sure to visit the famous Golden Circle, which includes attractions like the Thingvellir National Park, the Geysir Geothermal Area, and the Gullfoss Waterfall.

For a truly magical experience, consider staying at the Ion Adventure Hotel in Selfoss. This unique boutique hotel is located in the heart of Iceland’s Golden Circle and offers stunning views of the surrounding volcanic landscape. With its modern design, luxurious amenities, and proximity to some of Iceland’s most famous attractions, the Ion Adventure Hotel is the perfect base for exploring the Ring Road. If you’re looking for a more budget-friendly option, the Fosshotel Núpar in Kirkjubæjarklaustur offers comfortable accommodations and easy access to the stunning Skaftafell National Park.

Conclusion

Embarking on a road trip along iconic driving routes is an adventure like no other. By choosing hotels that are strategically located near these routes, you can enhance your experience and make the most of your journey. Whether you’re driving along the Pacific Coast Highway in California, exploring the Great Ocean Road in Australia, or circling Iceland’s Ring Road, there are plenty of hotels that offer comfort, convenience, and breathtaking views. So, pack your bags, hit the road, and get ready for an unforgettable road trip experience!
