The Best Antique Stores in Melbourne: A Guide


If you’re a lover of all things vintage and antique, Melbourne is the place to be. This vibrant city is home to a treasure trove of antique stores, offering a wide range of unique and rare finds. Whether you’re a seasoned collector or simply enjoy browsing for one-of-a-kind pieces, this guide will take you on a journey through the best antique stores in Melbourne.

1. Vintage Emporium

Located in the heart of Melbourne’s bustling CBD, Vintage Emporium is a haven for antique enthusiasts. This multi-level store is filled to the brim with a diverse collection of vintage furniture, clothing, jewelry, and more. As you explore the aisles, you’ll be transported back in time, discovering hidden gems at every turn. With knowledgeable staff on hand to assist you, Vintage Emporium is a must-visit for any antique lover.

2. Lost and Found Market

Tucked away in the trendy suburb of Fitzroy, Lost and Found Market is a true gem. This expansive warehouse-like space is home to over 100 stalls, each offering a unique selection of antiques and collectibles. From retro clothing and vinyl records to antique furniture and artwork, you’ll find it all here. The best part? The prices are reasonable, making it a favorite among both collectors and budget-conscious shoppers.

3. Chapel Street Bazaar

For a truly eclectic shopping experience, head to Chapel Street Bazaar in Prahran. This iconic store has been a Melbourne institution for over 40 years and is renowned for its vast array of vintage and antique items. From antique homewares and curiosities to retro clothing and accessories, you’ll be spoilt for choice. The winding aisles and quirky displays add to the charm of this beloved store, making it a must-visit for antique enthusiasts.

4. The Junk Company

If you’re on the hunt for unique industrial and rustic pieces, look no further than The Junk Company in Collingwood. This warehouse-style store is a treasure trove of salvaged architectural items, vintage signage, and industrial furniture. Whether you’re looking to add a touch of vintage charm to your home or searching for a statement piece, The Junk Company has something for everyone. Don’t forget to explore the outdoor area, where you’ll find garden antiques and reclaimed materials.

5. The Antique Perk

Nestled in the trendy suburb of Fitzroy North, The Antique Perk is a hidden gem worth discovering. This boutique antique store specializes in fine European and Australian antiques, offering a carefully curated selection of furniture, artwork, and decorative pieces. The friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to share the stories behind each item, adding an extra layer of intrigue to your shopping experience.

6. The Vintage Shed

For a unique and charming shopping experience, venture out to The Vintage Shed in Tyabb. This converted shed is home to over 60 stalls, each showcasing a wide range of vintage and antique treasures. From retro clothing and accessories to antique furniture and collectibles, you’ll find it all here. The welcoming and friendly atmosphere makes browsing a pleasure, and you’re sure to leave with a special find.

Conclusion

Melbourne’s antique stores offer a glimpse into the past, with each store showcasing a unique collection of treasures waiting to be discovered. Whether you’re seeking a specific item or simply enjoy the thrill of the hunt, these stores will satisfy your antique cravings. So, grab your wallet and embark on a journey through the best antique stores in Melbourne. Who knows what hidden gems you’ll uncover along the way?
