The Best Places to Go Mountain Biking in the U.S.


Are you ready to embark on an exciting and adrenaline-pumping adventure? Look no further than the United States for some of the best mountain biking experiences in the world! From rugged terrains to breathtaking views, this country has it all. So grab your bike, put on your helmet, and let’s explore the best places to go mountain biking in the U.S.!

1. Moab, Utah: Nestled in the heart of red rock country, Moab is a mountain biking paradise. With its extensive trail system, including the iconic Slickrock Trail, you’ll find yourself navigating through challenging terrain that will push your skills to the limit. The stunning landscapes, such as the famous Canyonlands National Park, make Moab a must-visit destination for any mountain biking enthusiast.

2. Crested Butte, Colorado: Known as the “Mountain Biking Capital of the World,” Crested Butte offers a plethora of trails for riders of all skill levels. From the picturesque Lupine Trail to the adrenaline-pumping 401 Trail, you’ll be treated to breathtaking wildflower meadows, aspen groves, and panoramic mountain views that will leave you awestruck.

3. Bentonville, Arkansas: Don’t underestimate this hidden gem! Bentonville has quickly become a mountain biking mecca, with over 200 miles of world-class trails. With options ranging from flowy singletracks to technical downhill descents, there’s something for everyone. The town’s commitment to mountain biking is evident in its dedicated trail systems and bike-friendly culture.

4. Park City, Utah: As the host of the annual Sundance Film Festival, Park City is known for more than just its cinematic charm. It boasts an exceptional mountain biking scene, with the renowned Park City Mountain Resort serving as a hub for riders. The area offers a vast network of trails catering to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced riders seeking an adrenaline rush.

5. Bellingham, Washington: Nestled in the lush Pacific Northwest, Bellingham offers a diverse range of trails that showcase the region’s natural beauty. From the technical challenges of Galbraith Mountain to the scenic Chuckanut Mountain trails, you’ll find yourself immersed in dense forests, breathtaking waterfalls, and sweeping views of the Puget Sound.

6. Sedona, Arizona: Prepare to be enchanted by the red rock landscapes of Sedona. With its unique geological formations and challenging trails, this desert town is a favorite among mountain bikers seeking a thrilling experience. The famous Hiline Trail and the picturesque Cathedral Rock Trail are just a couple of highlights worth exploring.

7. Kingdom Trails, Vermont: If you’re seeking an East Coast adventure, look no further than Kingdom Trails in Vermont. With over 100 miles of interconnected trails, you’ll be treated to a mix of technical challenges and flowy descents. The stunning views of the Green Mountains and the charming New England atmosphere make this destination a true delight.

8. Downieville, California: For those seeking an adrenaline rush, Downieville is the place to be. This small town in the Sierra Nevada Mountains is home to some of the most exhilarating downhill trails in the country. The iconic Downieville Downhill race attracts riders from all over the world, and the breathtaking scenery along the Yuba River adds to the allure.

9. Oakridge, Oregon: Tucked away in the Cascade Mountains, Oakridge is a hidden gem for mountain biking enthusiasts. With its extensive trail system, including the famous Alpine Trail, riders can experience the thrill of fast descents and technical challenges amidst towering forests and sparkling alpine lakes.

10. Pisgah National Forest, North Carolina: Known for its rugged beauty and challenging terrain, Pisgah National Forest offers a mountain biking experience like no other. With its numerous trails, including the legendary Black Mountain Trail, riders can expect steep climbs, technical descents, and captivating views of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

No matter where you roam in the United States, you’re bound to find a mountain biking destination that will leave you with unforgettable memories. So grab your bike, pack your sense of adventure, and get ready to conquer some of the best trails the U.S. has to offer. Happy riding!
