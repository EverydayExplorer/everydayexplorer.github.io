Mouth-Watering BBQ Side Dishes to Complement Your Meat


Summer is here, and that means that it’s time to fire up the grill! There’s nothing quite like the mouth-watering taste of BBQ meats hot off the grill, but it’s the side dishes that really complete the meal. Whether you’re planning a big BBQ party or just grilling up some burgers for dinner, here are some irresistible BBQ side dishes that are sure to impress your guests and leave them begging for more.

1. Coleslaw

No BBQ is complete without a delicious and refreshing coleslaw. This classic side dish is a perfect accompaniment to grilled meats, and it’s easy to make. Simply shred some cabbage and carrots, toss them with a creamy dressing, and you’re good to go. You can also add some other vegetables like bell peppers or cucumbers to give it some extra crunch.

2. Baked Beans

Baked beans are another classic BBQ side dish that everyone loves. These sweet and savory beans are slow-cooked with bacon, onions, and brown sugar, giving them a rich and smoky flavor that complements grilled meats perfectly. You can either make your own from scratch or use canned baked beans and doctor them up with your own flavorings.

3. Corn on the Cob

Corn on the cob is a summer staple, and it’s even better when grilled. Simply brush some butter on the corn, sprinkle it with some salt and pepper, and grill it until it’s tender and golden brown. You can also experiment with different toppings like chili powder, garlic, or parmesan cheese to give it some extra flavor.

4. Potato Salad

Potato salad is another classic BBQ side dish that’s always a hit. Creamy, tangy, and filling, this side dish is perfect for balancing out the rich flavors of grilled meats. You can make your own potato salad with boiled potatoes, celery, onions, and a mayonnaise-based dressing, or try a German-style potato salad with vinegar and bacon.

5. Grilled Vegetables

Grilled vegetables are a healthy and delicious side dish that’s perfect for vegetarians or anyone looking to add some extra vitamins to their meal. Simply marinate some vegetables like zucchini, peppers, eggplant, or mushrooms in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, and herbs, and grill them until they’re tender and caramelized.

6. Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese may not be the healthiest side dish, but it’s definitely one of the most popular. Creamy, cheesy, and comforting, this classic dish is a crowd-pleaser that everyone will love. You can make your own mac and cheese from scratch, or use boxed mac and cheese and add your own twists like bacon, jalapeños, or breadcrumbs.

7. Watermelon Salad

Watermelon salad is a refreshing and light side dish that’s perfect for hot summer days. Simply cube some watermelon, add some crumbled feta cheese, and drizzle it with a balsamic glaze for a sweet and tangy flavor that complements grilled meats perfectly.

In conclusion, when it comes to BBQ side dishes, there’s no shortage of delicious options to choose from. Whether you’re looking for something sweet, savory, or healthy, these mouth-watering side dishes are sure to complement your meat perfectly and leave your guests feeling satisfied and impressed. So fire up the grill and get ready to enjoy a summer full of delicious BBQ meals and unforgettable memories.
