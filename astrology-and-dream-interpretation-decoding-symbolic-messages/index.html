Astrology: Mapping the Celestial Tapestry

Astrology, the study of the movements and positions of celestial bodies, has long been revered as a powerful tool for understanding the human experience. By examining the placement of the sun, moon, planets, and stars at the time of one’s birth, astrologers believe they can gain insight into a person’s personality, relationships, and destiny. Each zodiac sign and planetary alignment is believed to carry specific energies and traits, shaping the intricate tapestry of human existence.
