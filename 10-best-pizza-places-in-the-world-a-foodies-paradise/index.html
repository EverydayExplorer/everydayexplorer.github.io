10 Best Pizza Places in the World: A Foodie's Paradise


Pizza, a beloved dish that originated in Naples, Italy, has become a staple in kitchens and restaurants across the globe. With its incredible versatility and irresistible flavors, it’s no wonder that pizza has become a favorite for foodies everywhere. In this article, we will explore the 10 best pizza places in the world, each offering their own unique twist on this classic dish.

1. Pizzeria Bkerkeck, Beirut, Lebanon
Located in the heart of Beirut, Pizzeria Bkerkeck is a family-owned establishment that has been serving up delicious pizzas for over 50 years. Known for their thin, crispy crusts and generous toppings, their Margherita pizza is a must-try.

2. Di Fara Pizza, Brooklyn, New York, USA
Founded in 1965 by Dom DeMarco, Di Fara Pizza has become a legendary pizzeria in the United States. With a focus on quality ingredients and a meticulous preparation process, their signature pizza is a perfect blend of cheese, sauce, and toppings.

3. Pizzeria Gino Sorbillo, Naples, Italy
Established in 1984, Pizzeria Gino Sorbillo is a must-visit destination for pizza enthusiasts. With a strong emphasis on using local, organic ingredients, their wood-fired pizzas showcase the true essence of Neapolitan pizza.

4. Motorino, New York City, USA
Founded by Mathias and Anthony Mangieri in 2003, Motorino has become a staple in the New York pizza scene. Known for their unique toppings and creative combinations, their Brussels Sprouts pizza is a crowd favorite.

5. Franco’s Pizzeria, Sydney, Australia
Located in the heart of Sydney, Franco’s Pizzeria is a family-owned pizzeria that has been serving up authentic Italian pizzas since 1981. Their Napoletana pizza, with its San Marzano tomatoes and Fior di Latte mozzarella, is a testament to their dedication to quality ingredients.

6. Pizzarium, Rome, Italy
Opened in 2003 by Gabriele Bonci, Pizzarium has quickly become a favorite among Rome’s foodies. Known for their inventive toppings and unique pizza shapes, they offer a modern twist on the classic Italian pizza.

7. Pizzaiolo, Oakland, California, USA
Founded by Charlie Hallowell, Pizzaiolo is a beloved pizzeria that focuses on using local, seasonal ingredients. Their Margherita pizza is a simple yet elegant dish, showcasing the quality of their ingredients.

8. Pizza Rock, Las Vegas, Nevada, USA
Located in the heart of the Las Vegas strip, Pizza Rock is a unique pizzeria that offers a diverse menu of pizzas with creative toppings. Their Truffle Pizza, with its rich truffle cream and fresh mozzarella, is a must-try for truffle lovers.

9. Piz, Copenhagen, Denmark
Piz, founded by chef Nicola Fanetti, offers a modern take on traditional Italian pizza. With a focus on using organic, locally sourced ingredients, their Capricciosa pizza is a perfect example of their commitment to quality.

10. Pizza Pilgrims, London, UK
Founded by twin brothers James and Thom Elliot, Pizza Pilgrims brings authentic Neapolitan pizza to the streets of London. Their Margherita pizza is a testament to their dedication to using the finest ingredients and traditional techniques.

In conclusion, pizza lovers have a wealth of options when it comes to finding the best pizza places in the world. From Neapolitan classics to inventive modern creations, these 10 establishments offer a truly unforgettable pizza experience. So, grab your forks and knives, and embark on a global pizza adventure!
