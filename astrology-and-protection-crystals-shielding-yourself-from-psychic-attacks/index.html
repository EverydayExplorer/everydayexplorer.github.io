Astrology and Protection Crystals: Shielding Yourself from Psychic Attacks


In a world where energy is ever-changing and vibrations constantly fluctuate, it’s important to arm ourselves with the tools necessary to protect our spiritual and emotional well-being. Psychic attacks can come in various forms and have the potential to disrupt our harmony, but luckily, astrology and protection crystals offer powerful defenses against these negative influences.

Astrology, often referred to as the study of celestial bodies and their influence on human affairs, has been practiced for centuries. Its roots can be traced back to ancient civilizations such as the Greeks, Egyptians, and Babylonians. Today, astrology remains a captivating field that helps individuals gain insight into their personalities, relationships, and life paths.

When it comes to shielding ourselves from psychic attacks, astrology provides valuable guidance by analyzing the positions and interactions of celestial bodies at the time of our birth. By understanding our astrological birth chart, we can identify potential areas of vulnerability and take appropriate actions to protect ourselves.

One of the most effective ways to enhance our spiritual shielding is through the use of protection crystals. Crystals have long been revered for their metaphysical properties and healing abilities. Each crystal possesses unique energy vibrations that can aid in strengthening our psychic defenses.

Black Tourmaline is a powerful crystal often used for psychic protection. With its grounding energy, it forms a protective shield around the wearer, creating a boundary that repels negative influences. This crystal is particularly helpful in deflecting psychic attacks and absorbing negative energies.

Another crystal that is renowned for its protective properties is Amethyst. Its high vibrational energy creates a bubble of spiritual protection, shielding the user from negative influences and promoting a tranquil state of mind. Amethyst is also known for its ability to enhance intuition, making it an ideal crystal for those seeking psychic protection.

Selenite is a crystal with a unique ability to cleanse and purify energy. It acts as a shield against psychic attacks, clearing the auric field and removing any unwanted energies or attachments. Selenite is particularly effective in protecting against negative thought forms and psychic intrusions.

Labradorite is a captivating crystal that not only offers protection but also strengthens intuition and spiritual gifts. Its iridescent flashes of color serve as a shield, deflecting unwanted energies and psychic attacks. Labradorite is an excellent choice for those seeking to enhance their psychic abilities while maintaining strong spiritual boundaries.

Pyrite, also known as ‘Fool’s Gold,’ is a crystal that not only possesses protective qualities but also brings abundance and prosperity into one’s life. Its energy acts as a shield, preventing psychic attacks and negative energies from penetrating one’s aura. Pyrite is especially useful for those seeking to protect their energy while manifesting their desires.

When utilizing protection crystals, it’s important to cleanse and charge them regularly to maximize their effectiveness. This can be done by placing them under running water, burying them in the earth, or placing them in sunlight or moonlight.

In addition to astrology and protection crystals, there are various practices that can help strengthen your psychic defenses. Meditation, visualization, and affirmations are powerful tools for clearing and fortifying your energetic field. Regularly practicing these techniques can enhance your resilience against psychic attacks and create a sense of inner peace.

It’s important to remember that protecting yourself from psychic attacks is not about living in fear, but rather about taking proactive steps to safeguard your spiritual well-being. By harnessing the power of astrology and protection crystals, you can shield yourself from negative energies and maintain a sense of harmony in your life.

In conclusion, astrology and protection crystals offer invaluable resources in safeguarding ourselves from psychic attacks. By understanding our astrological birth chart and utilizing crystals such as Black Tourmaline, Amethyst, Selenite, Labradorite, and Pyrite, we can fortify our psychic defenses and maintain a positive energetic balance. Combined with practices like meditation, visualization, and affirmations, we can create a powerful shield against negative influences and foster a sense of spiritual well-being. Embrace these tools and empower yourself on your spiritual journey.
