The Best Hotels for Nature Lovers


Are you ready to immerse yourself in the beauty of nature while enjoying the comforts of luxury accommodation? If you’re a nature lover seeking the perfect retreat, look no further. We’ve curated a list of the best hotels around the world that offer an unparalleled experience for those who appreciate the great outdoors. From stunning views to eco-friendly amenities, these hotels are a nature lover’s dream come true.

1. Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort, Thailand
Nestled in the lush jungles of Northern Thailand, the Anantara Golden Triangle Elephant Camp & Resort offers a unique blend of luxury and wildlife conservation. Wake up to the sight of elephants roaming freely in their natural habitat, and embark on unforgettable experiences such as elephant trekking and guided nature walks. The resort’s eco-friendly initiatives and commitment to sustainability make it a top choice for environmentally conscious travelers.

2. Post Ranch Inn, California, USA
Perched high on the cliffs of Big Sur, California, the Post Ranch Inn provides a secluded haven for nature enthusiasts. Surrounded by breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean and the Santa Lucia Mountains, this award-winning hotel offers an unparalleled connection to nature. Guests can stargaze from the comfort of their own private deck, indulge in spa treatments inspired by the elements, and partake in guided nature walks to discover the area’s diverse flora and fauna.

3. Fogo Island Inn, Newfoundland, Canada
For a truly off-the-grid experience, Fogo Island Inn is a must-visit destination for nature lovers. Situated on a remote island off the coast of Newfoundland, this architectural gem blends seamlessly with the rugged coastal landscape. Guests can explore the island’s dramatic coastline, encounter local wildlife, and engage with the vibrant community of artists and artisans. The hotel’s commitment to preserving the island’s natural beauty and supporting the local community makes it a standout choice for those seeking an authentic nature retreat.

4. Singita Sasakwa Lodge, Tanzania
Tucked away in the vast expanse of the Serengeti, Singita Sasakwa Lodge offers an unparalleled safari experience with a touch of luxury. Set within a 340,000-acre private reserve, the lodge provides guests with front-row seats to the annual migration of wildebeest and zebra. With expansive views of the African savannah, guests can unwind in opulent suites, embark on game drives to encounter the Big Five, and revel in the untamed beauty of the natural surroundings.

5. Explora Patagonia, Chile
Surrounded by the untamed wilderness of Torres del Paine National Park, Explora Patagonia invites guests to immerse themselves in the awe-inspiring landscapes of southern Chile. This all-inclusive hotel offers a variety of guided excursions for guests to explore the diverse ecosystems, from glacial lakes to ancient forests. With a deep respect for the environment and a commitment to sustainable tourism, Explora Patagonia provides an enriching experience for nature lovers.

Whether you’re drawn to the rugged coastline of California or the untamed wilderness of Africa, these hotels offer an unparalleled opportunity to reconnect with nature without sacrificing the comforts of luxury accommodation. Embrace the serenity of the natural world and create unforgettable memories at these exceptional destinations tailored for nature lovers.
