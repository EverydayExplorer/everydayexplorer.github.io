The Best Fashion Podcasts for Style Inspiration


Are you tired of scrolling through endless fashion blogs and magazines in search of style inspiration? Look no further, because we’ve got you covered with a curated list of the best fashion podcasts that will keep you up-to-date with the latest trends, industry insights, and style tips. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or a style-conscious individual looking for a fresh perspective, these podcasts are sure to inspire and elevate your fashion game. So, grab your headphones and get ready to dive into the world of fashion through your ears!

1. ‘The Business of Fashion Podcast’: Hosted by Imran Amed, founder, and CEO of Business of Fashion, this podcast delves into the business side of the fashion industry. From interviews with industry leaders to in-depth discussions about the latest fashion news, this podcast offers a comprehensive understanding of the ever-evolving world of fashion.

2. ‘Fashion No Filter’: In this podcast, fashion journalists Camille Charriere and Monica Ainley take listeners on a journey behind the scenes of the fashion industry. With a focus on sustainability, inclusivity, and diversity, they explore the stories and voices shaping the future of fashion.

3. ‘The Glossy Podcast’: Hosted by Glossy’s editor-in-chief, Jill Manoff, this podcast features interviews with top executives, designers, and influencers in the fashion and beauty industries. Dive into conversations about brand strategies, industry trends, and the future of fashion with this insightful podcast.

4. ‘UnStyled’: Refinery29’s global editor-in-chief, Christene Barberich, hosts this podcast that explores personal style and the stories behind it. From fashion designers to celebrities, each episode features honest conversations about fashion, identity, and self-expression.

5. ‘Fashion Revolution Podcast’: If you’re passionate about ethical fashion and sustainability, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Orsola de Castro, co-founder of Fashion Revolution, this podcast delves into the social and environmental issues within the fashion industry, while also highlighting innovative solutions and inspiring stories.

6. ‘Dressed: The History of Fashion’: Explore the fascinating history of fashion with hosts Cassidy Zachary and April Calahan. From ancient garments to iconic fashion moments, this podcast offers a deep dive into the evolution of fashion and its cultural significance.

7. ‘The Wardrobe Crisis’: Hosted by Clare Press, sustainability editor-at-large for Vogue Australia, this podcast explores the intersection of fashion, sustainability, and ethics. Through interviews with industry experts and thought-provoking discussions, it encourages listeners to rethink their relationship with fashion and make conscious choices.

8. ‘American Fashion Podcast’: Join hosts Charles Beckwith and Cathy Schepis as they tackle various topics related to the American fashion industry. From interviews with designers and fashion entrepreneurs to discussions about the future of American fashion, this podcast provides valuable insights into the industry.

9. ‘The Fashion Hags’: This podcast, hosted by Abby Shumka, Evan Ducharme, and Katie Garnham, offers a fun and candid look at the world of fashion. With a mix of humor, personal anecdotes, and industry insights, this podcast is perfect for those looking for a lighthearted take on fashion.

10. ‘The Style Success Podcast’: Are you struggling with your personal style? Hosted by Mallory Sills, a certified image consultant, this podcast provides practical tips and advice to help you define and refine your personal style. From wardrobe essentials to confidence-building techniques, this podcast will empower you to embrace your unique style.

So, whether you’re a fashion enthusiast, a style-conscious individual, or simply looking for some inspiration, these fashion podcasts are a must-listen. From industry insights to personal style journeys, they offer a wealth of knowledge and inspiration that will keep you ahead of the fashion game. So, plug in your headphones and get ready to elevate your style with these top fashion podcasts.
