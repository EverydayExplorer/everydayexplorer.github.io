How to Wear Metallics Without Going Overboard


If you want to add a touch of glamour to your wardrobe, metallics are the way to go. However, wearing metallics can be tricky. It’s easy to go overboard and end up looking like a disco ball. But fear not! With a few simple tips and tricks, you can rock metallics without looking like you’re trying too hard. Here’s how:

1. Choose One Statement Piece

The key to wearing metallics without going overboard is to choose one statement piece and build your outfit around it. Whether it’s a metallic skirt, a pair of shiny shoes, or a sparkly top, let that piece be the star of the show. Keep the rest of your outfit simple and let the metallic piece shine.

2. Mix Metallics with Neutrals

To create a balanced and sophisticated look, pair your metallics with neutral colors. Metallics can be quite bold, so pairing them with neutrals like black, white, or beige can tone down the look and make it more wearable. For example, a silver metallic skirt paired with a white blouse and black heels creates a chic and polished outfit.

3. Opt for Subtle Metallic Accents

If you’re not ready to go all out with a full-on metallic piece, opt for subtle metallic accents instead. A metallic belt, a pair of metallic earrings, or a metallic clutch can add just the right amount of shine to your outfit without overwhelming it. These small touches can elevate a simple outfit and make it more interesting.

4. Mix Metallics with Textures

Another way to wear metallics without going overboard is to mix them with different textures. Pair a metallic top with a textured skirt or pants to create a visually appealing and balanced look. For example, a metallic blouse paired with a tweed skirt creates a beautiful contrast between the shiny and the textured.

5. Layer with Metallic Outerwear

If you’re not ready to fully commit to a metallic outfit, you can still incorporate metallics into your look by layering with metallic outerwear. A metallic jacket or a shimmery coat can instantly elevate any outfit and add a touch of glamour. Layer it over a simple dress or a basic jeans-and-t-shirt combination for a stylish and eye-catching look.

6. Keep the Rest of Your Look Simple

When wearing metallics, it’s important to keep the rest of your look simple. Let the metallic piece be the focal point and avoid adding too many competing elements. Stick to clean lines, minimal accessories, and neutral makeup to create a cohesive and balanced look.

7. Confidence is Key

Last but not least, the most important tip for wearing metallics without going overboard is to wear them with confidence. Own your look and rock those metallics with pride. Confidence is the best accessory you can wear, and it will instantly elevate any outfit.

So, next time you want to add a little sparkle to your wardrobe, remember these tips on how to wear metallics without going overboard. Choose one statement piece, mix metallics with neutrals, opt for subtle accents, experiment with textures, layer with metallic outerwear, keep the rest of your look simple, and most importantly, wear your metallics with confidence. With these tips in mind, you’ll be able to rock metallics like a fashion pro!
