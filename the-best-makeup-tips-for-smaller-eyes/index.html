The Best Makeup Tips for Smaller Eyes


If you have smaller eyes, don’t worry! With the right makeup techniques, you can enhance your eyes and make them appear bigger and brighter. In this blog post, we will discuss the best makeup tips for smaller eyes, so that you can achieve a stunning and alluring look.

1. Prime your eyelids: Before applying any eyeshadow, it’s important to start with a good primer. This will help create a smooth base and ensure your eyeshadow lasts longer. Look for a primer that is specifically formulated to brighten the eye area.

2. Use light and shimmery eyeshadows: Lighter shades of eyeshadow can make your eyes appear bigger and more open. Opt for neutral shades with a hint of shimmer, as they reflect light and create the illusion of larger eyes. Avoid using dark and matte shades, as they can make your eyes look smaller.

3. Focus on the inner corners: Applying a light, shimmery eyeshadow to the inner corners of your eyes can instantly brighten and open them up. This trick helps to create the illusion of wider-set eyes. Choose a highlight shade that complements your skin tone and gently blend it into the inner corner.

4. Extend your eyeshadow beyond the outer corner: To make your eyes appear larger, extend your eyeshadow slightly beyond the outer corner of your eyes. This creates the illusion of a wider eye shape. Use a darker shade to create a subtle winged effect.

5. Define your crease: Creating definition in your crease can make your eyes appear more prominent. Use a medium-toned eyeshadow shade and apply it to your crease using a small blending brush. Blend the color in a soft, circular motion to create a natural-looking gradation.

6. Tightline your eyes: Tightlining your eyes involves applying eyeliner to your upper waterline. This technique gives the appearance of thicker lashes and defines your eyes without closing them off. Use a waterproof eyeliner in a shade that complements your eye color.

7. Avoid heavy eyeliner on the lower lash line: Applying heavy eyeliner on the lower lash line can make your eyes look smaller and more closed off. Instead, opt for a softer look by using a thin line of eyeliner or a light wash of eyeshadow along the lower lash line.

8. Curl your lashes: Curled lashes can instantly make your eyes appear bigger and more awake. Use an eyelash curler before applying mascara to achieve a wide-eyed look. For an added boost, consider using a lengthening or volumizing mascara to further enhance your lashes.

9. Highlight your brow bone: Applying a highlighting shade to your brow bone can accentuate your eyes and give them a lifted appearance. Use a light, shimmery shade and apply it just below your eyebrows. Blend well to create a seamless finish.

10. Don’t forget the brows: Well-groomed eyebrows can frame your eyes beautifully and draw attention to them. Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in any gaps and define the shape of your brows. Remember to keep them natural-looking and not too thick, as overly thick brows can overpower smaller eyes.

In conclusion, having smaller eyes doesn’t mean you can’t achieve a stunning eye makeup look. By following these makeup tips, you can enhance your eyes and create the illusion of bigger and brighter eyes. Experiment with different techniques and shades to find what works best for you. Whether it’s using light eyeshadows, extending your eyeshadow beyond the outer corner, or tightlining your eyes, there are plenty of tricks that can enhance your eye shape. Remember, the key is to have fun and embrace your unique beauty!
