Fashionable and Sustainable: The Best Upcycled Fashion Brands


In the ever-evolving world of fashion, sustainability has become a hot topic. With growing concerns about the environmental impact of the fashion industry, many brands have started embracing the concept of upcycling. Upcycling is the process of transforming discarded materials into new, high-quality fashion pieces. This not only reduces waste but also gives new life to materials that would have otherwise ended up in landfills. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best upcycled fashion brands that are not only fashionable but also sustainable.

1. Patagonia: Known for its commitment to environmental responsibility, Patagonia has been a pioneer in the upcycled fashion movement. Their ‘Worn Wear’ program encourages customers to repair and upcycle their old Patagonia garments instead of buying new ones. They even offer a range of upcycled products made from materials like recycled polyester and reclaimed cotton.

2. Reformation: Reformation is a brand that combines style with sustainability. They prioritize using deadstock fabric, which refers to leftover fabric from other fashion brands that would have otherwise been discarded. By utilizing these materials, Reformation creates unique and limited-edition pieces while reducing waste.

3. Eileen Fisher: Eileen Fisher is committed to sustainable fashion through their ‘Take Back’ program. They encourage customers to return their old Eileen Fisher garments, which are then upcycled into new designs. This closed-loop system ensures that their clothing’s lifecycle is extended, making them a leader in the upcycling movement.

4. Stella McCartney: Stella McCartney has been a vocal advocate for sustainable fashion for years. Her brand utilizes upcycled materials, such as recycled nylon and polyester, to create stylish and eco-friendly pieces. McCartney’s commitment to ethical and sustainable practices has earned her brand a reputation as a leader in the upcycled fashion industry.

5. Tonlé: Tonlé is a zero-waste fashion brand that uses every scrap of fabric in their designs. They even go a step further by collecting textile waste from larger garment factories and transforming it into unique, handcrafted clothing. Tonlé’s approach to upcycling promotes a circular economy and reduces the fashion industry’s impact on the environment.

6. Amour Vert: Amour Vert’s mission is to create sustainable, timeless fashion. They use upcycled materials and prioritize natural and eco-friendly fabrics in their designs. By focusing on quality and longevity, Amour Vert aims to reduce the environmental impact of the fashion industry.

7. Rothy’s: Rothy’s is known for its stylish and comfortable shoes made from recycled plastic bottles. By upcycling these materials, Rothy’s diverts waste from landfills and creates fashionable footwear. Their commitment to sustainability has made them a popular choice for environmentally conscious consumers.

These are just a few examples of the best upcycled fashion brands in the industry. By supporting these brands, you can make a positive impact on the environment while still looking stylish and fashionable. Remember, sustainable fashion is not only a trend but a responsible choice that benefits both the planet and future generations. So, why not embrace upcycled fashion and make a statement with your wardrobe?
