Top 10 Video Games to play this Year


The world of video games is constantly evolving, with new titles being released on a regular basis. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which ones are worth investing your time and money in. However, fear not! We have compiled a list of the top 10 video games to play this year to help you narrow down your choices.

1. ‘The Last of Us Part II’

‘The Last of Us Part II’ is a highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed original game. Developed by Naughty Dog, this action-adventure game takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where players must navigate through dangerous environments and combat infected creatures. With its immersive storytelling and stunning visuals, ‘The Last of Us Part II’ is a must-play for fans of the genre.

2. ‘Cyberpunk 2077’

‘Cyberpunk 2077’ has been generating a lot of buzz in the gaming community, and for good reason. Developed by CD Projekt Red, this open-world game is set in the futuristic Night City, where players take on the role of a mercenary named V. With its deep character customization, branching narratives, and breathtaking visuals, ‘Cyberpunk 2077’ promises to be an unforgettable gaming experience.

3. ‘Ghost of Tsushima’

‘Ghost of Tsushima’ is an action-adventure game developed by Sucker Punch Productions. Set in feudal Japan, players assume the role of a samurai named Jin Sakai as he fights to protect his homeland from Mongol invaders. With its stunning visuals, immersive world, and engaging combat mechanics, ‘Ghost of Tsushima’ offers a unique and captivating gaming experience.

4. ‘Doom Eternal’

If you’re a fan of fast-paced, adrenaline-pumping shooters, ‘Doom Eternal’ is the game for you. Developed by id Software, this first-person shooter game puts players in the role of the Doom Slayer as he battles against hordes of demons from Hell. With its intense combat, visceral gore, and pulse-pounding soundtrack, ‘Doom Eternal’ delivers an exhilarating gaming experience.

5. ‘Assassin’s Creed Valhalla’

The ‘Assassin’s Creed’ series has long been known for its immersive historical settings and engaging gameplay, and ‘Assassin’s Creed Valhalla’ is no exception. Set during the Viking Age, players take on the role of Eivor, a Viking raider, as they lead their clan to conquer the lands of England. With its vast open world, epic battles, and intriguing story, ‘Assassin’s Creed Valhalla’ is a game that shouldn’t be missed.

6. ‘Animal Crossing: New Horizons’

‘Animal Crossing: New Horizons’ offers a relaxing and charming gaming experience that is perfect for unwinding after a long day. Developed by Nintendo, this life simulation game allows players to create their own tropical island paradise, complete with cute animal villagers. With its endless customization options, peaceful gameplay, and joyful atmosphere, ‘Animal Crossing: New Horizons’ is a delightful game to play.

7. ‘Final Fantasy VII Remake’

Fans of the original ‘Final Fantasy VII’ will be thrilled with the remake, which brings the beloved characters and story to life with stunning graphics and modern gameplay mechanics. Developed by Square Enix, this role-playing game follows the story of Cloud Strife and his friends as they fight against the evil Shinra corporation. With its epic battles, deep storytelling, and rich world-building, ‘Final Fantasy VII Remake’ is a game that will captivate both new and old fans alike.

8. ‘Hades’

‘Hades’ is a roguelike dungeon crawler developed by Supergiant Games. Set in the Greek underworld, players assume the role of Zagreus, the son of Hades, as he attempts to escape his father’s realm. With its dynamic combat, compelling story, and beautifully hand-drawn artwork, ‘Hades’ offers a fresh and addictive gaming experience.

9. ‘Marvel’s Spider-Man: Miles Morales’

‘Marvel’s Spider-Man: Miles Morales’ is a standalone expansion to the critically acclaimed ‘Marvel’s Spider-Man.’ Developed by Insomniac Games, this action-adventure game puts players in the shoes of Miles Morales as he takes on the mantle of Spider-Man and defends his home of Harlem. With its exhilarating web-slinging mechanics, engaging storyline, and stunning visuals, ‘Marvel’s Spider-Man: Miles Morales’ is a must-play for superhero fans.

10. ‘Watch Dogs: Legion’

‘Watch Dogs: Legion’ is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft. Set in a near-future London, players must recruit and build a resistance to take down an oppressive surveillance state. With its unique play-as-anyone mechanic, expansive open world, and gripping story, ‘Watch Dogs: Legion’ offers a thrilling and immersive gaming experience.

Conclusion

With these top 10 video games to play this year, you’re sure to find something that suits your gaming preferences. Whether you’re into fast-paced shooters, immersive open worlds, or relaxing life simulations, there’s something for everyone on this list. So grab your controller, put on your gaming headset, and get ready for an unforgettable gaming experience!
