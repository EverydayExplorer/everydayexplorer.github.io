Tips for Creating an Instagram-Worthy Hotel Stay


Instagram has become the go-to platform for sharing travel moments and experiences with others. When guests have a fantastic stay at a hotel, they are likely to showcase it on their Instagram profile. Therefore, it’s essential for hotels to provide an Instagram-worthy experience that will entice guests to capture and share their beautiful moments. In this blog post, we will explore some tips for creating an Instagram-worthy hotel stay.

1. Stunning Interior Design: First impressions matter the most. Invest in creating a visually appealing and Instagram-friendly interior design for your hotel. Choose a color scheme that reflects your brand personality and creates a cohesive look throughout the property. Consider incorporating unique and eye-catching elements such as statement walls, art pieces, or stylish furniture that will make a great backdrop for Instagram-worthy photos.

2. Breathtaking Views: A picturesque view can make all the difference. If your hotel is in a location that offers stunning natural vistas or cityscapes, make sure to showcase them in your rooms or common areas. Guests are more likely to capture and share their experience when they are greeted with a breathtaking view right from their windows or balconies. Prioritize room placement and give guests the opportunity to enjoy the best views your property has to offer.

3. Unique Experiences: Provide guests with memorable and unique experiences that they will want to capture and share. Organize events, workshops, or experiences that are aesthetically pleasing and align with current trends. For example, you could host a flower arranging class, a cocktail-making workshop, or a themed dinner night. The more visually appealing and Instagrammable the experience is, the more likely guests are to share it on their social media platforms.

4. Instagrammable Food and Drinks: Nowadays, food photography is a major trend on Instagram. Enhance your food and drink offerings by making them visually appealing. Present your dishes with artistic plating techniques and use vibrant colors. Incorporate unique ingredients or garnishes that create an element of surprise. Additionally, pay attention to the presentation of your cocktails and mocktails. Investing in stylish glassware and garnishes will make them highly Instagrammable.

5. Photogenic Spaces and Props: Set up Instagrammable spaces throughout your hotel to encourage guests to take photos. These could include a beautifully styled seating area, a wall with a catchy slogan or mural, or a swing or hammock with a stunning backdrop. To make these spaces even more appealing, provide guests with photo props such as hats, sunglasses, or quirky accessories that they can use to add an extra touch of fun to their pictures.

6. Engage with Influencers: Collaborating with social media influencers who have a strong following on Instagram can greatly enhance your hotel’s visibility and reach. Engage with popular travel or lifestyle influencers who align with your hotel’s target audience. Invite them for a complimentary stay in exchange for sharing their experience on their Instagram profile. Their posts will not only showcase your hotel but also introduce it to a wider audience, potentially leading to increased bookings.

7. Encourage User-Generated Content: Actively encourage your guests to share their experiences on Instagram and feature them on your hotel’s social media platforms. Create a unique hashtag for your hotel, and encourage guests to use it when posting about their stay. Offer incentives such as a chance to win a free stay or a special discount for guests who share their experiences using your hashtag. This will not only increase guest engagement but also generate authentic content for your hotel.

8. Optimize Your Instagram Profile: Make sure your own Instagram profile is optimized to attract potential guests. Use high-quality images of your hotel, its facilities, and the surrounding area. Craft compelling captions that share the story behind your property and its unique features. Encourage guests to tag your hotel in their posts and engage with them by responding to comments and direct messages promptly.

In conclusion, creating an Instagram-worthy hotel stay requires strategic planning and attention to detail. By investing in a visually appealing interior design, breathtaking views, unique experiences, photogenic spaces, and engaging with influencers, your hotel can become a highly sought-after destination for Instagrammers. Remember to actively encourage guests to share their experiences and leverage user-generated content to promote your hotel. With these tips, you’ll be well on your way to creating an unforgettable and Instagrammable hotel stay experience.
