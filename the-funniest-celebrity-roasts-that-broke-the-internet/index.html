The Funniest Celebrity Roasts That Broke the Internet


Celebrity roasts have become a staple of the entertainment industry, offering a platform for comedians to poke fun at their famous peers in a lighthearted and often hilarious way. While many roasts have been entertaining, a select few have truly stood out for their side-splitting humor and ability to break the internet.

Justin Bieber’s Roast (2015)

Justin Bieber’s roast in 2015 was a star-studded affair, featuring a lineup of comedians that included Kevin Hart, Snoop Dogg, and Martha Stewart. Hart, known for his acerbic wit, took aim at Bieber’s youthful appearance, saying, ‘Justin, you look like a 12-year-old who just got his first acne medication.’ Snoop Dogg, in his signature laid-back style, joked about Bieber’s love of weed, saying, ‘Justin, I’m proud of you for coming out about your love of marijuana. It shows that you’re a role model for kids who want to get high and achieve their dreams.’

Charlie Sheen’s Roast (2011)

Charlie Sheen’s roast in 2011 was a raucous affair, with Sheen himself taking center stage and delivering a self-deprecating monologue that left the audience in stitches. Sheen joked about his infamous ’tiger blood’ comments, saying, ‘I’m not bipolar, I’m bi-winning.’ He also poked fun at his tumultuous relationship with Denise Richards, saying, ‘Denise Richards is here tonight. She’s the one who put the ‘pro’ in ‘protection order.’’

Bruce Willis’ Roast (2018)

Bruce Willis’ roast in 2018 was a testament to the actor’s enduring popularity and his ability to laugh at himself. Among the comedians who roasted Willis were Edward Norton, Martha Stewart, and Kevin Pollak. Norton, known for his sharp wit, joked about Willis’ action movie persona, saying, ‘Bruce, you’re like the Chuck Norris of acting. You can kill a man with a look, but you can also make him laugh with a fart.’

James Franco’s Roast (2013)

James Franco’s roast in 2013 was a surreal and unforgettable experience. Franco, known for his eclectic personality, invited a diverse group of comedians, including Sarah Silverman, Andy Samberg, and Jonah Hill. Silverman, in her typically blunt style, joked about Franco’s appearance, saying, ‘James, you look like a Ken doll that got lost in a meth lab.’ Samberg, meanwhile, poked fun at Franco’s academic pursuits, saying, ‘James, I know you’re a serious actor and a scholar, but you also look like the guy who sells weed outside of a Phish concert.’

Betty White’s Roast (2010)

Betty White’s roast in 2010 was a heartfelt tribute to the beloved actress and comedian. The roast featured a lineup of comedians that included Tina Fey, Amy Poehler, and Chelsea Handler. Fey, in her signature deadpan style, joked about White’s age, saying, ‘Betty, you’re so old, you remember when dinosaurs were around. And you weren’t even the oldest one.’ Poehler, meanwhile, praised White’s enduring popularity, saying, ‘Betty, you’re like a fine wine. You just get better with age.’

Conclusion

Celebrity roasts have become a cultural phenomenon, offering a unique opportunity for comedians to showcase their wit and for celebrities to show their sense of humor. While many roasts have been entertaining, the ones mentioned above have truly stood out for their side-splitting humor and ability to break the internet. Whether you’re a fan of Justin Bieber, Charlie Sheen, Bruce Willis, James Franco, or Betty White, these roasts are sure to provide hours of laughter and entertainment.
