The Best Games for Traveling Virtually


In today’s fast-paced world, technology has brought us closer to various experiences that were once limited by physical boundaries. One such experience is traveling virtually, which allows us to explore new destinations and cultures from the comfort of our own homes. And what better way to enhance our virtual travel adventures than by playing games that immerse us in these virtual worlds? In this blog post, we will explore some of the best games for traveling virtually, providing you with hours of entertainment and a taste of different places around the globe.

1. ‘The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim’

Let’s start with a game known for its vast open world and beautiful landscapes – ‘The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim.’ This award-winning role-playing game takes players on an epic journey through the breathtaking province of Skyrim. From towering mountains to dense forests and ancient ruins, Skyrim offers a virtual travel experience like no other. With its realistic graphics and immersive gameplay, this game will make you feel like you’re exploring a rich and diverse fantasy world.

2. ‘Assassin’s Creed’ Series

If historical accuracy and stunning recreations of iconic cities are what you seek, look no further than the ‘Assassin’s Creed’ series. With games set in various time periods and locations such as Renaissance Italy, Ancient Egypt, and Victorian London, this franchise allows players to step back in time and become a part of pivotal moments in history. Explore the winding streets of Florence, climb the pyramids of Giza, or traverse the rooftops of industrial revolution-era London – the possibilities are endless!

3. ‘No Man’s Sky’

For those with an adventurous spirit, ‘No Man’s Sky’ is the ultimate game for virtual exploration. This space exploration and survival game boasts a procedurally generated universe with over 18 quintillion planets to discover. From barren wastelands to lush alien jungles, each planet offers unique flora, fauna, and landscapes. Fly through space, land on uncharted planets, and unearth the secrets of the universe in this vast and awe-inspiring game.

4. ‘Microsoft Flight Simulator’

For virtual travel enthusiasts who have a soft spot for aviation, ‘Microsoft Flight Simulator’ is a must-play game. Using real-world data and cutting-edge technology, this simulation game provides an incredibly realistic flying experience. Soar through the skies and witness breathtaking views of famous cities, landmarks, and natural wonders. Whether you’re piloting a small propeller plane or a massive jumbo jet, ‘Microsoft Flight Simulator’ will quench your thirst for virtual travel.

5. ‘Far Cry’ Series

If you enjoy immersive open-world games with captivating storylines, the ‘Far Cry’ series should be on your list. With settings ranging from tropical islands to African savannahs, these games offer stunning environments to explore. Immerse yourself in lush jungles, encounter exotic wildlife, and unravel gripping narratives that will keep you hooked from start to finish. Each installment in the series offers a unique location and atmosphere, ensuring hours of thrilling virtual travel.

6. ‘World of Warcraft’

For fans of the fantasy genre, ‘World of Warcraft’ (WOW) is a legendary game that needs no introduction. With its vast and visually stunning world of Azeroth, WOW has captured the hearts of millions of players worldwide. Embark on epic quests, join guilds, and interact with players from all corners of the globe. From dense forests to towering castles, the world of WOW is a treasure trove of virtual exploration.

In conclusion, virtual travel has become an exciting alternative for those seeking new experiences. Through these immersive games, we can venture into fantastical realms, relive historical moments, and explore uncharted territories – all from the comfort of our own homes. So grab your controller, put on your virtual reality headset, and let these games transport you to incredible virtual worlds. Happy travels!
