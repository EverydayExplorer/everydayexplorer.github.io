How to Wear Jewelry at the Beach: Styling Tips for a Chic and Practical Look


Heading to the beach is an exciting and fun experience, but figuring out what jewelry to wear can be a bit challenging. You want to strike the perfect balance between looking effortlessly chic and ensuring that your accessories are practical for a day in the sand and surf. In this blog post, we’ll explore some styling tips for wearing jewelry at the beach to help you achieve a stylish and practical look.

1. Opt for Minimal Pieces

When it comes to beach jewelry, less is more. Opt for minimalistic pieces that won’t weigh you down or get tangled in your bathing suit or beach cover-up. Simple necklaces with delicate pendants, thin bracelets, and dainty rings are perfect choices. Consider thin hoop earrings or small studs to add a touch of elegance to your beach ensemble.

2. Choose Waterproof Materials

Since you’ll be spending time in and around water, it’s essential to choose jewelry made of waterproof materials. Look for pieces made of stainless steel, sterling silver, or gold-filled materials, as they are less likely to tarnish or get damaged by water and salt. Avoid wearing jewelry that is susceptible to rust or may change color when exposed to moisture, such as costume jewelry or anything made of base metals.

3. Go for Natural Elements

Embrace the beach vibes by opting for jewelry with natural elements. Shell necklaces, wooden bangles, or beaded bracelets can add a bohemian touch to your beach look. These pieces not only complement beach attire but also give off a relaxed and earthy vibe that’s perfect for the beach environment.

4. Layer Delicate Pieces

Layering jewelry is a great way to create a stylish and personalized look. For a beach-appropriate style, layer thin necklaces with different lengths and add a few delicate bracelets to your wrist. This creates a visually interesting effect without being overbearing. Avoid chunky or heavy pieces, as they can feel uncomfortable and may weigh you down.

5. Consider Adjustable and Versatile Pieces

When selecting beach jewelry, consider choosing pieces that can be adjusted or worn in different ways. For instance, choose a necklace that can be wrapped around your wrist to create a stacked bracelet look. This versatility allows you to adapt your jewelry to suit different beach activities and outfit changes throughout the day.

6. Mindful Anklets

Anklets are a popular choice for beach jewelry, but it’s important to wear them mindfully. Opt for anklets made of waterproof materials and avoid bulky or excessively decorative styles that may get damaged during beach activities. Keep in mind that anklets can get tangled in seaweed or caught in sandy areas, so be cautious when wearing them.

7. Remove Jewelry When Needed

While it’s fun to accessorize at the beach, it’s also important to consider the practicality of your jewelry choices. If you plan on swimming, playing beach volleyball or engaging in any water-based activities, it’s best to remove your jewelry to avoid damage, loss, or injury. Store your jewelry safely in a waterproof bag or pouch to ensure their protection.

8. Apply Sunscreen with Care

When applying sunscreen, take extra care not to smudge or damage your jewelry. Apply sunscreen to your skin first and allow it to dry before putting on your jewelry. This helps prevent sunscreen residue from getting on your pieces, which can tarnish or corrode certain materials over time.

9. Clean and Care for Your Jewelry

After a day at the beach, make sure to clean and care for your jewelry properly. Remove any saltwater, sand, or sunscreen residue by rinsing your jewelry with clean water and drying it gently with a soft cloth. If necessary, use a jewelry cleaning solution specifically designed for the materials your pieces are made of.

10. Confidence is the Key

Last but not least, remember that confidence is the key to rocking any piece of jewelry. Regardless of your choices, wear your jewelry with pride and embrace your personal style. The beach is a place to have fun and enjoy yourself, so let your accessories reflect that carefree spirit.

By following these styling tips, you can achieve a chic and practical look when wearing jewelry at the beach. Remember to keep it minimal, opt for waterproof materials, and choose natural and versatile pieces. With the right selection and a little care, you can elevate your beach look and feel effortlessly stylish. So, go ahead and shine bright on the sand and under the sun!
