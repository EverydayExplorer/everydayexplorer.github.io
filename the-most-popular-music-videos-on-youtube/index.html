The most popular music videos on YouTube


Welcome to our blog post! Today, we’re going to dive into the fascinating world of music videos on YouTube. From catchy tunes to breathtaking visuals, music videos have become an integral part of our online entertainment experience. In this article, we’ll explore the most popular music videos on YouTube and why they have captured the hearts of millions around the globe. So, grab your popcorn and let’s get started!

1. “Despacito” by Luis Fonsi ft. Daddy Yankee
With a staggering 7.1 billion views (and counting), “Despacito” holds the title for the most-viewed music video on YouTube. This reggaeton-infused hit not only became an international sensation but also broke several records along the way. Its catchy melody and vibrant visuals transport viewers to the streets of Puerto Rico, making it an irresistible choice for music lovers worldwide.

2. “Baby Shark Dance” by Pinkfong
Love it or hate it, there’s no denying the global phenomenon that is “Baby Shark Dance.” This incredibly catchy children’s song has taken YouTube by storm, with over 6.6 billion views. Its simple lyrics, repetitive melody, and adorable shark characters have captivated both children and adults, making it one of the most popular music videos on the platform.

3. “Shape of You” by Ed Sheeran
Ed Sheeran’s “Shape of You” became an instant hit and has garnered over 5.1 billion views. The video showcases a heartwarming storyline of a boxer and dancer falling in love. The combination of Sheeran’s soulful voice, addictive beat, and relatable narrative struck a chord with audiences worldwide, making it a chart-topping hit.

4. “Gangnam Style” by Psy
Who could forget the viral sensation that was “Gangnam Style”? Psy’s eccentric music video became a global phenomenon, amassing over 4.1 billion views. Its catchy tune, unique dance moves, and humorous visuals created a perfect storm for internet stardom. Even years after its release, “Gangnam Style” remains one of the most iconic music videos in YouTube history.

5. “See You Again” by Wiz Khalifa ft. Charlie Puth
“See You Again” gained massive popularity after being featured in the movie “Furious 7” as a tribute to the late Paul Walker. This emotional ballad has garnered over 4 billion views, touching the hearts of viewers with its heartfelt lyrics and powerful melody. The video beautifully captures the essence of friendship and loss, making it an emotionally resonant experience.

6. “Uptown Funk” by Mark Ronson ft. Bruno Mars
With over 3.9 billion views, “Uptown Funk” brings a catchy blend of funk, pop, and R&B to the YouTube scene. This retro-inspired music video showcases Bruno Mars’ incredible dance moves, combined with Mark Ronson’s infectious beats. It’s impossible to resist grooving along to this upbeat and energetic track.

7. “Sorry” by Justin Bieber
Justin Bieber’s “Sorry” has amassed over 3.3 billion views, solidifying his comeback after a brief hiatus. The music video features a captivating blend of vibrant colors, catchy choreography, and a catchy pop melody. Bieber’s reinvention as a pop superstar propelled “Sorry” to immense popularity, helping it become one of the most-watched music videos on YouTube.

8. “Roar” by Katy Perry
Katy Perry’s empowering anthem, “Roar,” has garnered over 3.1 billion views. The music video portrays Perry as a survivor, embracing her strength and liberation. With its catchy chorus, visually stunning scenes, and inspirational message, “Roar” became a global hit and resonated with millions of viewers worldwide.

9. “Sugar” by Maroon 5
Maroon 5’s “Sugar” showcases the band’s unique style and has accumulated over 3 billion views. This heartwarming video features the band crashing real weddings, surprising couples with impromptu performances. The combination of romantic gestures, sweet lyrics, and Maroon 5’s signature sound turned “Sugar” into an instant YouTube favorite.

10. “Counting Stars” by OneRepublic
“Counting Stars” by OneRepublic is a catchy alternative rock anthem that has gained over 2.9 billion views. The video features the band performing in various locations, showcasing their dynamic energy and passion for music. Its relatable lyrics and uplifting melody have helped it become one of the most beloved music videos on YouTube.

In conclusion, the world of music videos on YouTube is a vibrant and ever-evolving landscape, capturing the attention and imagination of millions worldwide. From record-breaking hits to heartwarming stories, these top music videos have left an indelible mark on popular culture.
