The Most Romantic Hotels with Private Jacuzzis: Soak in Luxury


Are you in search of the perfect romantic getaway? Look no further than these luxurious hotels with private Jacuzzis. Imagine you and your loved one relaxing in a warm, bubbling Jacuzzi, surrounded by stunning views and indulgent amenities. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or simply want to escape from the daily grind, these hotels offer the ultimate in romance and luxury.

  1. Amangiri - Utah, USA Nestled amidst the breathtaking canyons of southern Utah, Amangiri is a romantic oasis in the heart of nature. Each suite comes with its own private terrace and plunge pool, perfect for enjoying the picturesque views. But for the ultimate indulgence, book a room with a private Jacuzzi. Imagine soaking in warm, soothing waters while gazing up at the expansive starry sky. This secluded retreat is the epitome of luxury and romance.

  2. Jade Mountain Resort - St. Lucia If you’ve ever dreamed of staying in a romantic sanctuary with your own private infinity pool and Jacuzzi, Jade Mountain Resort is the place for you. Located amidst the lush mountains of St. Lucia, this resort offers stunning panoramic views of the Caribbean Sea. Each suite, or ‘sanctuary,’ is uniquely designed with an open fourth wall, providing uninterrupted views. Enjoy a romantic soak in your private Jacuzzi while being surrounded by tropical paradise.

  3. Anantara Kihavah Maldives Villas - Maldives The Maldives is renowned for its stunning beauty and luxury resorts, and the Anantara Kihavah Maldives Villas is no exception. This island paradise boasts luxurious overwater villas, each with its own private pool and Jacuzzi. Step onto your private terrace and soak in the warm waters while enjoying the incredible views of the Indian Ocean. With its intimate setting and breathtaking surroundings, this resort is the perfect destination for a romantic getaway.

  4. Amanjena - Morocco Escape to the magical world of Marrakech and experience the ultimate in luxury and romance at Amanjena. This stunning resort combines traditional Moroccan architecture with modern-day luxury. Each pavilion or villa comes with a private plunge pool, but for an extra-special treat, book a suite with a private Jacuzzi. Unwind in the warm waters while surrounded by lush gardens and tranquil fountains. Amanjena is a true oasis of romance and relaxation.

  5. Hapuku Lodge & Tree Houses - New Zealand For a truly unique and unforgettable romantic experience, head to Hapuku Lodge & Tree Houses in New Zealand. Nestled among the treetops, these luxurious tree houses offer the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. Each tree house comes with its own Jacuzzi, where you can soak in the warm waters while enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and the Pacific Ocean. Fall asleep to the sounds of nature and wake up to breathtaking views in this one-of-a-kind destination.

Planning a romantic getaway is all about finding the perfect location and accommodations. These hotels with private Jacuzzis offer the ultimate in luxury and romance. Whether you prefer secluded canyons, tropical islands, or treetop retreats, there is a romantic hotel with a private Jacuzzi that is perfect for you. Soak in the luxury, indulge in the romance, and create memories that will last a lifetime.
