10 Must-Watch Sports Documentaries


Are you a sports enthusiast looking for some top-notch sports documentaries to add to your watchlist? Well, you’re in luck because I’ve compiled a list of 10 must-watch sports documentaries that will leave you inspired, entertained, and in awe of the incredible stories within the world of sports. Whether you’re a fan of basketball, soccer, boxing, or any other sport, there’s something for everyone on this list. Let’s dive in and explore these captivating documentaries that offer a unique perspective on the world of sports.

1. ‘The Last Dance’ - This documentary series chronicles the career of basketball legend Michael Jordan and the Chicago Bulls’ quest for a sixth NBA championship. Packed with behind-the-scenes footage and interviews, it’s a must-watch for any basketball fan.

2. ‘Senna’ - A gripping documentary that delves into the life and tragic death of Brazilian Formula One driver Ayrton Senna. This film provides an intimate look at Senna’s passion for racing and the impact he had on the sport.

3. ‘The Two Escobars’ - Soccer fans will be captivated by this documentary, which explores the intertwining stories of Colombian soccer player Andrés Escobar and drug lord Pablo Escobar, shedding light on the complex relationship between sports and crime.

4. ‘Hoop Dreams’ - Widely regarded as one of the greatest sports documentaries of all time, ‘Hoop Dreams’ follows the journeys of two promising basketball players from inner-city Chicago as they navigate the challenges of pursuing their hoop dreams.

5. ‘When We Were Kings’ - This documentary transports viewers to the historic ‘Rumble in the Jungle’ boxing match between Muhammad Ali and George Foreman in Zaire. It captures the cultural significance of the event and Ali’s enduring legacy.

6. ‘Free Solo’ - While not a traditional sports documentary, ‘Free Solo’ offers a heart-stopping look at rock climber Alex Honnold’s quest to conquer the infamous El Capitan without ropes. The film showcases the sheer determination and fearlessness required in extreme sports.

7. ‘The Battered Bastards of Baseball’ - Baseball fans will love this underdog story about the Portland Mavericks, a ragtag minor league baseball team that captured the hearts of fans in the 1970s. It’s a feel-good documentary that celebrates the love of the game.

8. ‘The Undefeated’ - Explore the remarkable story of Sarah Pavan and Melissa Humana-Paredes, two Canadian beach volleyball players aiming for gold at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. This documentary captures their journey and the unbreakable bond forged through sport.

9. ‘The Barkley Marathons: The Race That Eats Its Young’ - Delve into the world of ultra-running with this fascinating documentary about the grueling Barkley Marathons, a 100-mile race through the unforgiving terrain of Tennessee. It’s a test of physical and mental endurance like no other.

10. ‘First Team: Juventus’ - Go behind the scenes with one of the most storied soccer clubs in the world, Juventus FC. This documentary series provides unprecedented access to the players, coaches, and inner workings of the club, offering a unique perspective for football fans.

These 10 sports documentaries offer a diverse range of captivating stories, from triumphs and tragedies to the sheer dedication and passion found within the world of sports. Whether you’re a die-hard fan or simply appreciate a compelling story, these documentaries are sure to leave a lasting impression. So, grab your popcorn, settle in, and prepare to be transported into the captivating world of sports through the lens of these extraordinary documentaries.
