The Latest Shoe Trends: Whats\ In\ and\ Whats Out


The world of fashion is constantly evolving, and one of the most important accessories to keep up with is shoes. As we step into a new year, it’s time to take a look at the latest shoe trends and bid farewell to the ones that have left their mark in the past. Whether you’re a sneakerhead, a lover of heels, or a devotee of flats, we’ve got you covered with the hottest shoe trends to keep you stylish and on-trend in 2021.

In with the New: What’s Hot in Shoes

1. Chunky Sneakers: The sneaker trend shows no signs of slowing down in 2021. Chunky sneakers, also known as ‘dad sneakers,’ continue to dominate the street style scene and bring a touch of nostalgia to any outfit. Pair them with jeans, dresses, or even suits for an effortlessly cool look.

2. Square-Toe Shoes: Say goodbye to pointy-toe shoes and hello to their square-toe counterparts. Square toes are making a strong comeback this year, adding a modern twist to both heels and flats. These shoes are not only trendy but also more comfortable, as they provide more space for your toes to wiggle.

3. Platform Sandals: Elevate your summer style with platform sandals. These shoes feature a thick sole that adds height without sacrificing comfort. From casual wedges to dressier blocked heels, platform sandals are the ultimate footwear choice for both day and night.

4. Bright Colors: Ditch the neutrals and embrace vibrant hues in your shoe collection. From bold reds to electric blues, this year is all about making a statement with your footwear. Opt for a single pop of color or go all out with a monochrome outfit paired with equally bold shoes.

5. Minimalist Sneakers: On the other end of the spectrum, minimalist sneakers are also having a moment. These simple and understated shoes, often in white or pastel shades, provide a clean and crisp look that complements any outfit. Whether you’re running errands or going out for brunch, minimalist sneakers are a go-to option.

Out with the Old: Trends to Leave Behind

1. Thin Stiletto Heels: Bid adieu to uncomfortable stilettos that leave you wobbling. This year, the focus is on chunkier heels that provide stability and comfort. Ditch the thin stiletto heels and opt for block heels or kitten heels, which offer both style and ease of wear.

2. Ultra-Pointy Toes: While pointy-toe shoes have had their moment for quite some time, it’s time to let them go and embrace the square-toe trend. The narrow, ultra-pointy toe is being replaced by a more relaxed and geometric shape that adds a contemporary and edgy element to your footwear.

3. Overly Ornamental Shoes: Forget about excessive embellishments and overly ornamental shoes. This year, less is more. Clean lines and minimalistic designs are taking center stage. Embrace simplicity and opt for shoes with sleek silhouettes and subtle details for a more refined and modern look.

4. Uncomfortable Flats: Flats don’t have to equal discomfort. Leave behind flats that offer little support and choose styles that provide both style and comfort. Look for flats with cushioned insoles, arch support, and soft materials to keep your feet happy while still looking chic.

5. Traditional Sneaker Colors: While classic white sneakers will never go out of style, this year, it’s all about exploring different colors and patterns. Step outside of your comfort zone and try sneakers in bold shades or with interesting prints to add a playful and adventurous touch to your look.

In conclusion, 2021 brings with it a fresh wave of shoe trends that cater to both style and comfort. As we bid farewell to some old favorites, we welcome new and exciting designs that reflect the ever-changing fashion landscape. Whether you’re a sneaker enthusiast or a lover of heels, there’s something for everyone this year. So, go ahead, revamp your shoe collection, and step into the latest trends with confidence and style.
