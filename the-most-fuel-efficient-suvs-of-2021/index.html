The Most Fuel-Efficient SUVs of 2021


Gas prices are on the rise once again, making fuel efficiency a top priority for many individuals looking to purchase a new vehicle. For those in need of the space and versatility that SUVs offer, it’s good to know that fuel-efficient options are now readily available on the market. In this blog post, we will explore the most fuel-efficient SUVs of 2021, providing you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

1. Toyota RAV4 Hybrid
Starting off our list is the Toyota RAV4 Hybrid, which offers impressive fuel efficiency without compromising on performance. Equipped with a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine and an electric motor, this SUV delivers a combined fuel economy of around 40 miles per gallon (MPG). Its innovative hybrid technology allows for seamless transitions between gas and electric power, making it a standout option for those looking to minimize their carbon footprint.

2. Ford Escape Hybrid
The Ford Escape Hybrid is another excellent choice for fuel-conscious SUV buyers. With its 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine and electric motor, this SUV delivers an impressive combined fuel economy of around 41 MPG. The Escape Hybrid also offers a spacious interior, advanced safety features, and an array of technology options, making it a well-rounded choice for families and individuals alike.

3. Honda CR-V Hybrid
Next up is the Honda CR-V Hybrid, known for its reliability and fuel efficiency. With its 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine and electric motor, this SUV achieves a combined fuel economy of around 40 MPG. The CR-V Hybrid offers a comfortable ride, ample cargo space, and a host of driver-assistance features, making it an appealing option for those seeking a practical and economical SUV.

4. Subaru Crosstrek Hybrid
Subaru has long been known for its all-wheel drive prowess, and the Crosstrek Hybrid is no exception. With a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, this SUV achieves an impressive combined fuel economy of around 90 MPGe (Miles Per Gallon of gasoline-equivalent) when running in electric-only mode. It also offers an estimated 480 miles of total driving range, making it a great choice for those who frequently embark on long road trips.

5. Lexus UX 250h
Luxury meets fuel efficiency in the Lexus UX 250h hybrid SUV. With its 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine and electric motor, the UX 250h achieves a combined fuel economy of around 43 MPG. Not only does it offer exceptional fuel efficiency, but it also boasts a stylish and comfortable interior, advanced safety features, and a smooth ride. If you’re after a luxurious and eco-friendly SUV, the UX 250h is worth considering.

6. Hyundai Tucson Hybrid
The Hyundai Tucson Hybrid is a standout in terms of fuel efficiency and value. Equipped with a 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine and an electric motor, this SUV achieves a combined fuel economy of around 38 MPG. It also offers a spacious interior, user-friendly infotainment system, and a long list of standard features. With its attractive price point and impressive fuel efficiency, the Tucson Hybrid is a strong contender in the SUV market.

7. Kia Niro Hybrid
Last but not least is the Kia Niro Hybrid, a compact SUV that combines practicality with impressive fuel efficiency. With its 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine and electric motor, the Niro achieves a combined fuel economy of around 50 MPG. It also offers a comfortable and well-designed interior, user-friendly technology, and a smooth ride. If you’re in the market for a fuel-efficient SUV with ample cargo space and a reasonable price tag, the Kia Niro Hybrid is certainly worth considering.

In conclusion, fuel-efficient SUVs are no longer a rare find. With advancements in hybrid technology, automakers are now providing consumers with a wide range of options to choose from. Whether you prioritize space, luxury, or value, there is a fuel-efficient SUV to suit your needs. The Toyota RAV4 Hybrid, Ford Escape Hybrid, Honda CR-V Hybrid, Subaru Crosstrek Hybrid, Lexus UX 250h, Hyundai Tucson Hybrid, and Kia Niro Hybrid are among the top contenders for the most fuel-efficient SUVs of 2021. Make sure to consider your priorities and take these options for a test drive to find the perfect fuel-efficient SUV for you.
