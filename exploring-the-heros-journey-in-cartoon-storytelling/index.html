Exploring the Hero's Journey in Cartoon Storytelling


Cartoons aren’t just for kids anymore. In fact, they’ve become a powerful medium for storytelling that can captivate audiences of all ages. One of the reasons cartoons have become so popular is their ability to tap into timeless storytelling techniques, such as the Hero’s Journey.

The Hero’s Journey is a narrative framework that outlines the typical journey of a hero as they embark on an adventure, face challenges, and ultimately undergo personal growth. This framework, popularized by scholar Joseph Campbell, has been used in countless stories throughout history, including many beloved cartoons.

So, let’s take a closer look at how the Hero’s Journey is utilized in cartoon storytelling, and how it enhances the viewer’s experience.

The Call to Adventure

Every hero’s journey begins with a call to adventure, and cartoons are no exception. Whether it’s a young boy setting out to become a Pokémon Master or a group of turtles learning the art of ninjutsu, cartoons often start with the protagonist being called to embark on a journey or face a challenge.

In shows like ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender,’ we see Aang being tasked with mastering all four elements and defeating the Fire Lord to restore balance to the world. This call to adventure sets the stage for the rest of the story and hooks the audience from the very beginning.

Crossing the Threshold

Once the hero has answered the call to adventure, they must cross the threshold into the unknown. This is where they leave behind their familiar world and step into the realm of adventure and danger. In cartoons, this often involves the hero leaving home or venturing into unfamiliar territory.

Take, for example, ‘Adventure Time,’ where Finn and Jake traverse the Land of Ooo, encountering all manner of strange creatures and challenges. Each new adventure they embark on pushes them further out of their comfort zone and tests their abilities.

Challenges and Temptations

No hero’s journey would be complete without facing a series of challenges and temptations along the way. In cartoons, these challenges can take many forms, from battling evil villains to overcoming personal doubts and insecurities.

In ‘Steven Universe,’ the titular character faces numerous challenges as he learns to control his powers and protect the Earth from various threats. Along the way, he must also navigate the complexities of friendship and love, facing temptations that test his resolve and character.

The Abyss

At the lowest point of the hero’s journey lies the abyss, where they must confront their greatest fears and overcome seemingly insurmountable odds. This is often the moment of truth for the hero, where they must dig deep and find the strength to continue on their journey.

In ‘Gravity Falls,’ Dipper and Mabel face their own abyss when they confront the true identity of the mysterious town founder and must confront their own fears and insecurities in order to save the town and each other.

Transformation and Return

Finally, the hero emerges from the abyss transformed, having undergone a profound change or gained new insights that will forever alter their path. In cartoons, this transformation is often accompanied by a return to the hero’s ordinary world, where they must use their newfound wisdom to bring about positive change.

In ‘The Legend of Korra,’ Korra undergoes a transformative journey as she learns to embrace her role as the Avatar and bring balance to the world. By the end of her journey, she has not only mastered the elements but also become a symbol of hope and inspiration for others.

Conclusion

Cartoons have long been a source of entertainment and inspiration for audiences of all ages, and their use of the Hero’s Journey is just one of the many storytelling techniques that make them so compelling. By following the hero’s journey, cartoon creators are able to take viewers on epic adventures filled with excitement, danger, and personal growth. So the next time you sit down to watch your favorite cartoon, take a moment to appreciate the hero’s journey unfolding before your eyes.
