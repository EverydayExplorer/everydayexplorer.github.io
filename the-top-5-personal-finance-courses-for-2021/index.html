The Top 5 Personal Finance Courses for 2021


In the fast-paced world of today, financial literacy has become a crucial skill for everyone, regardless of age or profession. As the saying goes, ‘if you fail to plan, you plan to fail.’ To help you navigate the complex world of personal finance and make the most of your hard-earned money, we have compiled a list of the top 5 personal finance courses for 2021. These courses will empower you with the knowledge and tools necessary to achieve financial freedom and live a life of abundance.

1. Personal Finance for Smart People by BiggerPockets

Personal Finance for Smart People is a comprehensive course designed to help you master your finances and achieve financial independence. Created by the renowned real estate investing platform BiggerPockets, this course covers a wide range of topics, including budgeting, saving, investing, and debt management. With over 50 lessons and 8 hours of video content, this course is perfect for beginners and seasoned investors alike.

Key takeaways:

* Learn the fundamentals of personal finance
* Understand the importance of budgeting and saving
* Discover various investment strategies
* Master debt management techniques

2. Financial Freedom by Grant Sabatier

Financial Freedom is a best-selling book turned online course by Grant Sabatier, who managed to save $1 million by the age of 30. The course is designed to help you achieve financial independence and early retirement by following Sabatier’s proven strategies. With over 40 lessons and 10 hours of video content, this course covers topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and side hustles.

Key takeaways:

* Learn how to save and invest your way to financial freedom
* Discover the power of side hustles and passive income
* Master the art of budgeting and saving
* Gain insights into real-life success stories and strategies

3. Financial Education by Ramit Sethi

Financial Education is a course by best-selling author and entrepreneur Ramit Sethi, who has helped thousands of people improve their financial lives. This course offers a unique blend of psychology, behavioral economics, and practical financial advice to help you make better decisions with your money. With over 20 lessons and 6 hours of video content, this course covers topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and mindset shifts.

Key takeaways:

* Understand the psychology behind financial decision-making
* Learn how to create a personalized financial plan
* Discover the secrets to successful budgeting and saving
* Master the art of investing and wealth building

4. The Total Money Makeover by Dave Ramsey

The Total Money Makeover is a best-selling book turned online course by personal finance expert Dave Ramsey. This course is designed to help you achieve financial freedom by following Ramsey’s 7-step plan, which includes topics such as budgeting, saving, debt management, and investing. With over 30 lessons and 10 hours of video content, this course is perfect for those looking for a structured approach to financial success.

Key takeaways:

* Learn the 7-step plan to financial freedom
* Discover how to create a budget and stick to it
* Understand the importance of debt management
* Master the art of investing and wealth building

5. The Simple Path to Wealth by JL Collins

The Simple Path to Wealth is a best-selling book turned online course by financial expert JL Collins. This course is designed to help you achieve financial independence through a simple, low-cost, and long-term investing strategy. With over 20 lessons and 8 hours of video content, this course covers topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and retirement planning.

Key takeaways:

* Learn the secrets to long-term investing success
* Discover the power of low-cost index funds
* Understand the importance of budgeting and saving
* Master the art of retirement planning and wealth building

Conclusion

Achieving financial freedom is a journey that requires dedication, discipline, and the right knowledge. By enrolling in one of these top 5 personal finance courses for 2021, you will be well on your way to unlocking your financial potential and living a life of abundance. Remember, the best time to start was yesterday, and the second-best time is right now.
