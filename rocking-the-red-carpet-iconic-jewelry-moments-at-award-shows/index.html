Rocking the Red Carpet: Iconic Jewelry Moments at Award Shows


The red carpet at award shows is not just a runway for celebrities to show off their designer gowns and stylish suits, but also a stage for showcasing some of the most exquisite and unforgettable jewelry pieces ever created. From stunning diamond necklaces to extravagant statement earrings, the red carpet has witnessed numerous iconic jewelry moments that have left a lasting impression on fashion enthusiasts and jewelry lovers around the world.

1. Elizabeth Taylor’s Emerald and Diamond Necklace:
At the 1961 Academy Awards, Elizabeth Taylor made heads turn by wearing a show-stopping emerald and diamond necklace. This iconic piece, known as the ‘Taylor-Burton Necklace,’ featured a 69.42-carat pear-shaped diamond pendant surrounded by smaller pear-shaped diamonds and emeralds. It remains one of the most notable jewelry moments in red carpet history.

2. Angelina Jolie’s Emerald Earrings:
Angelina Jolie is known for her timeless beauty and impeccable style, and her appearance at the 2009 Oscars was no exception. She accessorized her elegant black gown with a pair of mesmerizing emerald drop earrings, which perfectly complemented her emerald ring. The combination of the emerald jewelry against her contrasting dress created a striking and memorable look.

3. Nicole Kidman’s Diamond Choker:
Nicole Kidman graced the red carpet at the 1997 Oscars wearing a stunning diamond choker necklace by L’Wren Scott. The eye-catching choker featured 7,600 individual diamonds set in intricate detail. Kidman’s choice to pair the choker with a sleek, black dress allowed the diamonds to become the focal point of her elegant ensemble.

4. Marion Cotillard’s Chopard Necklace:
At the 2008 Academy Awards, Marion Cotillard made a statement by wearing a breathtaking Chopard necklace. The necklace featured a combination of pink, white, and yellow diamonds, totaling a remarkable 70 carats. This iconic jewelry piece perfectly accentuated Cotillard’s ethereal Dior gown and further solidified her status as a red carpet fashion icon.

5. Rihanna’s Statement Choker:
Known for her bold fashion choices, Rihanna made a memorable entrance at the 2017 Met Gala with a custom-made floral-inspired choker by Japanese designer Tasaki. The choker featured 30 oversized pearls, positioned to resemble petals of a flower, and was paired with a dramatic Comme des Garçons ensemble. This iconic jewelry moment showcased Rihanna’s unique style and artistic vision.

6. Jennifer Lawrence’s Back Necklace:
During the 2013 Academy Awards, Jennifer Lawrence made headlines not only for winning the Best Actress award but also for her jaw-dropping back necklace. The necklace, a delicate strand of diamonds worn backwards across her bare back, added a touch of elegance and sophistication to her Dior gown. This unconventional jewelry choice sparked a new trend in red carpet fashion.

7. Cate Blanchett’s Armani Privé Earrings:
Cate Blanchett is known for her impeccable style, and she certainly didn’t disappoint at the 2014 Golden Globe Awards. She accessorized her black lacy gown with a pair of statement earrings designed by Armani Privé. The earrings featured a stunning combination of white diamonds, yellow diamonds, and opals, making them the perfect finishing touch to her glamorous look.

In conclusion, the red carpet has seen countless iconic jewelry moments at award shows over the years. From Elizabeth Taylor’s emerald and diamond necklace to Cate Blanchett’s Armani Privé earrings, these jewelry pieces have not only added an extra touch of glamour to the celebrities’ outfits but have also become unforgettable moments in fashion history. These iconic jewelry moments continue to inspire and captivate jewelry enthusiasts and fashion lovers alike, cementing their place in the annals of red carpet fashion.
