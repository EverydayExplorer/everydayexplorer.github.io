The Great Outdoors: Hotels Near National Parks


When it comes to experiencing the beauty of nature, nothing beats a visit to one of our nation’s stunning national parks. With their breathtaking landscapes and abundant wildlife, these protected areas offer a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. And what better way to enhance your outdoor adventure than by staying at a hotel near a national park? In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotels located in close proximity to these natural wonders.

1. Yosemite National Park: Nestled in the heart of California’s Sierra Nevada Mountains, Yosemite National Park is known for its towering granite cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and ancient giant sequoias. For visitors looking for a cozy retreat after a day of exploration, The Ahwahnee Hotel is a perfect choice. With its rustic charm and stunning views of Yosemite Valley, this historic hotel offers a unique blend of luxury and natural beauty.

2. Grand Canyon National Park: One of the most iconic natural landmarks in the world, the Grand Canyon is a must-visit destination for any outdoor enthusiast. Located on the South Rim, El Tovar Hotel provides guests with a front-row seat to the breathtaking vistas of the canyon. Built in 1905, this historic hotel offers a charming atmosphere and easy access to hiking trails and scenic overlooks.

3. Rocky Mountain National Park: Situated in the heart of the Colorado Rockies, Rocky Mountain National Park is a paradise for hikers, climbers, and wildlife enthusiasts. For those seeking a luxurious mountain getaway, The Stanley Hotel in Estes Park is a top choice. This historic hotel, famous for its role in inspiring Stephen King’s ‘The Shining,’ offers breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks and a wide range of outdoor activities.

4. Acadia National Park: Located on Mount Desert Island in Maine, Acadia National Park is a true gem of the East Coast. For visitors looking for a charming coastal experience, The Bar Harbor Inn is the perfect choice. Situated just minutes away from the park’s entrance, this waterfront hotel offers stunning views of Frenchman Bay and easy access to the park’s hiking trails and carriage roads.

5. Zion National Park: With its towering sandstone cliffs and narrow slot canyons, Zion National Park in Utah is a paradise for adventurers. The Cliffrose Lodge & Gardens, located just a short walk from the park’s entrance, offers a tranquil oasis for visitors. Surrounded by lush gardens and the Virgin River, this boutique hotel provides a peaceful retreat after a day of exploring the park’s stunning landscapes.

Whether you’re a seasoned outdoor enthusiast or a first-time visitor, staying at a hotel near a national park is a fantastic way to immerse yourself in the beauty of the great outdoors. From luxurious mountain retreats to charming coastal inns, these hotels offer the perfect balance of comfort and proximity to nature. So, plan your next adventure and make the most of your national park experience by choosing one of these incredible accommodations.
