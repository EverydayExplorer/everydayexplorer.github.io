The Best Cars for Families with Pets: Spaciousness and Convenience


When it comes to choosing a car that accommodates the needs of your entire family, including your furry friends, spaciousness and convenience are key factors to consider. As a pet owner, you want a vehicle that not only offers ample room for your pets to move around comfortably but also provides convenient features that make traveling with them a breeze. In this blog post, we will explore the best cars for families with pets, focusing on their spaciousness and convenience.

1. Subaru Outback: The Subaru Outback is a popular choice among pet owners due to its generous cargo space and pet-friendly features. With its 32.5 cubic feet of cargo space, you can easily fit a large dog crate or multiple pet carriers. The Outback also offers an available rear-seat reminder system, which alerts you if your pet is still in the backseat after you’ve parked and turned off the engine, ensuring their safety.

2. Honda CR-V: The Honda CR-V is known for its spacious interior and versatile cargo area, making it an ideal choice for families with pets. With 39.2 cubic feet of cargo space behind the rear seats, there’s plenty of room for your pets to relax during your journeys. The CR-V also offers a low cargo floor, making it easy for your pets to climb in and out of the vehicle. Additionally, Honda offers pet-friendly accessories such as seat covers and cargo area liners to protect your car from pet hair and dirt.

3. Toyota RAV4: The Toyota RAV4 is another excellent option for families with pets. With its 37.6 cubic feet of cargo space, you can comfortably transport your pets and their belongings. The RAV4 also features a hands-free power liftgate, allowing you to easily access the cargo area even when your hands are full. Furthermore, Toyota offers optional pet travel accessories, including a rear bumper protector and a pet carrier that fits perfectly in the cargo area.

4. Ford Escape: The Ford Escape offers a spacious and versatile interior, making it a great choice for families with pets. With 37.5 cubic feet of cargo space, you can easily fit your pets’ crates or carriers. The Escape also features an available foot-activated liftgate, enabling you to open the cargo area with a simple kick under the rear bumper. Additionally, Ford offers pet-friendly accessories like seat covers and pet ramps to enhance your pets’ comfort and safety.

5. Volvo XC60: If you’re looking for a luxury option, the Volvo XC60 is worth considering. With its 22.4 cubic feet of cargo space, it may have slightly less room than other vehicles on this list, but it makes up for it with its pet-friendly features. The XC60 offers an available integrated pet harness system, designed to keep your pets secure during travel. It also features a removable and washable cargo area floor mat, making it easy to clean up any messes your pets may leave behind.

When choosing the best car for your family with pets, keep in mind the importance of spaciousness and convenience. Consider the cargo space, accessibility features, and any pet-friendly accessories offered by the vehicle. By selecting a car that meets these criteria, you can ensure that your furry friends are comfortable and safe during your family adventures. Happy travels!
