10 Amazing Photography Blogs You Should Follow


Are you passionate about photography? Are you looking for a source of inspiration, tips, and tricks, or just want to indulge in the beauty of captivating visuals? Look no further! In this blog post, we will unveil a list of 10 amazing photography blogs that you should definitely follow. Whether you’re a professional photographer or an amateur enthusiast, these blogs are bound to tickle your creative senses and provide you with a wealth of knowledge. So, buckle up and prepare to embark on a visual journey!

1. ‘The Digital Story’ - A blog created by Derrick Story, an experienced photographer and writer, who shares his insights on a range of photography topics. From gear reviews to post-processing techniques, Derrick’s blog is a treasure trove of information.

2. ‘Stuck in Customs’ - Trey Ratcliff takes you on awe-inspiring adventures through his captivating images. His blog is a blend of stunning landscape and travel photography, accompanied by personal anecdotes that make you feel like you’re right there with him.

3. ‘DIY Photography’ - If you’re a photography enthusiast on a budget or simply enjoy experimenting with your gear, this blog is a goldmine. DIY Photography offers creative DIY hacks, gear reviews, and helpful tutorials to help you achieve professional results without breaking the bank.

4. ‘Ming Thein’s Blog’ - Ming Thein is a renowned commercial and fine art photographer. His blog delves into the intricate details of photographic composition, aesthetics, and the art of storytelling through images. Prepare to be inspired!

5. ‘The Phoblographer’ - Dedicated to all things photography, The Phoblographer covers a wide range of genres and techniques. From interviews with professional photographers to gear reviews and thought-provoking articles, this blog has something for everyone.

6. ‘Digital Photography School’ - As the name suggests, this blog is like attending a photography school from the comfort of your own home. DPS offers a plethora of articles, tutorials, and resources for photographers of all levels. It’s a one-stop-shop for honing your skills and expanding your knowledge.

7. ‘The Sartorialist’ - Created by Scott Schuman, this blog beautifully captures the essence of street fashion and style through the lens of a camera. Schuman’s keen eye for detail and his ability to capture fleeting moments make this blog a must-follow for both fashion and photography lovers.

8. ‘Photography Life’ - If you’re seeking a comprehensive and well-rounded photography blog, look no further. Photography Life covers a wide range of topics, from gear reviews to post-processing techniques, and even has a dedicated section for beginners. It’s a fantastic resource for photographers at any stage of their journey.

9. ‘Petapixel’ - With a vast community of contributors, Petapixel is known for its diverse range of articles, tutorials, and industry news. This blog keeps you up to date with the latest trends and developments, making it an indispensable resource for photographers of all levels.

10. ‘Chase Jarvis’ - Chase Jarvis is a world-renowned photographer and entrepreneur. His blog offers a unique blend of photography, business, and creative inspiration. Through his interviews with industry leaders and thought-provoking articles, Chase Jarvis challenges photographers to push their boundaries and think outside the box.

These ten photography blogs are just a starting point on your quest for inspiration and knowledge. Each one brings something unique to the table, providing you with a wealth of resources to fuel your creativity and enhance your skills. So, grab your camera, explore these blogs, and let your imagination run wild. Happy shooting!
