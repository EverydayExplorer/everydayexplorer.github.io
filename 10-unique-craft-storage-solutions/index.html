10 Unique Craft Storage Solutions


Crafting is a beloved hobby for many, but it often comes with a challenge: finding the perfect storage solutions for all those supplies. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned crafter, having a well-organized space can make all the difference in fueling your creativity. In this blog post, we will explore ten unique craft storage solutions that will help you keep your supplies neat, accessible, and ready for your next project.

1. Pegboard Wall Organizer: A pegboard is a versatile solution that allows you to hang various craft tools such as scissors, rulers, and paintbrushes. Customize it by adding hooks, baskets, and shelves to store smaller items like thread spools, beads, and buttons. This wall-mounted system not only keeps your supplies organized but also adds a decorative touch to your craft room.

2. Mason Jar Storage: Mason jars are not just for canning! They can be repurposed as stylish and practical storage containers for craft supplies. Fill them with buttons, ribbons, or even small paintbrushes. Arrange them on a shelf or attach them to a wooden board for easy access and a visually appealing display.

3. Hanging Shoe Organizer: Don’t let your shoe organizer go to waste! Utilize the pockets to store yarn, fabric scraps, and other small craft items. Hang it on the back of a door or on a wall for quick and convenient access to your supplies.

4. Magnetic Strip for Metal Tools: Keep your metal craft tools, such as scissors, tweezers, and paper punches, within arm’s reach by installing a magnetic strip on your wall or the inside of a cabinet. This simple solution ensures that your tools are easily accessible and eliminates the need for rummaging through drawers.

5. Rolling Cart with Drawers: A rolling cart with drawers is a fantastic solution for crafters who like to work on different projects in different areas. Fill each drawer with specific craft supplies, such as paints, markers, or adhesive tapes. The mobility of the cart allows you to effortlessly move your supplies wherever you need them.

6. Wall-Mounted Wrapping Paper Organizer: If you love gift wrapping, keep your wrapping paper neatly stored and easily accessible with a wall-mounted organizer. This solution keeps your rolls of paper from getting crushed or damaged and makes it simple to find the perfect wrap for any occasion.

7. Spice Rack Storage: Don’t underestimate the power of a spice rack! Use it to store small containers of glitter, beads, or buttons. This allows you to see all your supplies at a glance and keeps them organized and within reach.

8. Hanging Fabric Organizer: Repurpose a hanging shoe organizer or create your own fabric organizer by sewing pockets onto a piece of fabric. Hang it on a wall or the back of a door, and use it to store fabric scraps, embroidery floss, or even knitting needles. This solution not only keeps your supplies organized but also adds a splash of color to your craft room.

9. Tiered Tray for Small Items: A tiered tray is a stylish and functional storage solution for small craft supplies like beads, sequins, and charms. Place it on your work table or shelf, and easily access your supplies without them getting mixed up.

10. Repurposed Furniture: Get creative with repurposing furniture to store your craft supplies. An old dresser can be transformed into a craft cabinet with ample storage space for papers, paints, and fabric. A vintage toolbox can hold your knitting needles and crochet hooks. Look for unique furniture pieces that can be given a new life in your craft room.

With these ten unique craft storage solutions, you can transform your craft room into an organized and inspiring space. From pegboards to mason jars and rolling carts to repurposed furniture, there are endless possibilities to suit your needs and style. Get started on decluttering and organizing your supplies, and watch your creativity flourish!
