Hidden Gems: Underrated Comic Book Series You Should Know


Underrated comic book series often get overlooked in the vast world of comics. While popular titles like Batman, Spider-Man, and Superman dominate the shelves, there are some hidden gems that deserve recognition. These unsung heroes of the comic book world offer unique storytelling, captivating characters, and stunning artwork that shouldn’t go unnoticed.

One such hidden gem is ‘Saga’ by Brian K. Vaughan and Fiona Staples. Described as a space opera/fantasy epic, Saga explores the lives of star-crossed lovers from opposite sides of an intergalactic war. The series is known for its diverse cast of characters, complex world-building, and mature storytelling. With its thought-provoking themes and stunning visuals, Saga has gained a devoted fanbase.

Another underrated comic series is ‘Locke & Key’ by Joe Hill and Gabriel Rodriguez. This dark fantasy series follows the Locke family as they discover mysterious keys that unlock a variety of supernatural powers in their ancestral home. Alongside the thrilling narrative, Locke & Key explores themes of grief, family dynamics, and the power of imagination. With its unique blend of horror, fantasy, and drama, this series offers a refreshing take on the genre.

‘Y: The Last Man’ by Brian K. Vaughan and Pia Guerra is a post-apocalyptic science fiction comic that tells the story of Yorick Brown, the last man on Earth after a mysterious plague wipes out all other male mammals. This critically acclaimed series delves into various issues such as gender dynamics, social hierarchies, and the resilience of humanity. With its gripping storyline and compelling characters, Y: The Last Man stands out as a must-read for comic book enthusiasts.

For fans of superhero comics, ‘Invincible’ by Robert Kirkman and Cory Walker is a hidden gem worth exploring. This series follows the journey of Mark Grayson, a teenager who discovers he has inherited superpowers from his superhero father. While the premise might seem familiar, Invincible subverts expectations with its engaging narrative and unpredictable twists. With its poignant exploration of heroism, responsibility, and the consequences of choice, Invincible stands out as a masterpiece in the genre.

Moving away from superheroes, ‘Black Science’ by Rick Remender and Matteo Scalera is a mind-bending science fiction series that explores the consequences of interdimensional travel. The story follows a group of scientists as they jump between alternate dimensions, each with its own dangers and consequences. Black Science combines stunning artwork with intricate storytelling to create a thrilling and thought-provoking reading experience.

These are just a few examples of underrated comic book series that deserve more recognition. While they may not enjoy the same level of popularity as mainstream titles, they offer fresh perspectives, compelling storytelling, and memorable characters. If you’re a comic book enthusiast looking to broaden your reading list, be sure to check out these hidden gems. You won’t be disappointed.
