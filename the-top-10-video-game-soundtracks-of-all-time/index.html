The Top 10 Video Game Soundtracks of All Time


When it comes to video games, there are many aspects that contribute to the overall gaming experience. From stunning graphics to immersive gameplay, video games have come a long way in providing entertainment. However, one aspect that often gets overlooked but is equally important is the soundtrack. A well-crafted soundtrack can elevate the gaming experience to a whole new level, immersing you in the game’s world and creating lasting memories. In this blog post, we will be counting down the top 10 video game soundtracks of all time.

1. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
The soundtrack of The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time is nothing short of iconic. Composed by Koji Kondo, it perfectly captures the epic adventures of Link. From the serene melodies of Kokiri Forest to the haunting notes of the Temple of Time, this soundtrack has become synonymous with gaming excellence.

2. Final Fantasy VII
Nobuo Uematsu is a name that is synonymous with brilliance when it comes to video game soundtracks. The soundtrack of Final Fantasy VII is a testament to his talent. With its sweeping orchestral score and emotionally charged themes, it sets the stage for one of gaming’s most memorable stories.

3. Super Mario Bros.
No list of video game soundtracks would be complete without mentioning the iconic music of Super Mario Bros. Composed by Koji Kondo, the catchy tunes of this game have become instantly recognizable. From the upbeat theme of the overworld to the tense music in the underwater levels, this soundtrack is a true classic.

4. The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim
Jeremy Soule’s composition for The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is a masterpiece in its own right. The haunting melodies and epic orchestration perfectly capture the vastness of the game’s world. From the opening theme ‘Dragonborn’ to the ambient tracks that play while exploring the wilderness, this soundtrack sets the mood for countless hours of adventure.

5. Halo: Combat Evolved
The Halo series is known for its epic science fiction storytelling, and the soundtrack of the first installment, Halo: Combat Evolved, is no exception. Composed by Martin O’Donnell and Michael Salvatori, it features a mix of choir-based melodies and intense orchestration that perfectly complement the action-packed gameplay.

6. Chrono Trigger
The soundtrack of Chrono Trigger, composed by Yasunori Mitsuda, is often regarded as one of the best video game soundtracks of all time. It seamlessly blends different musical styles, from classical to jazz, creating a unique and memorable experience. The tracks ‘Corridors of Time’ and ‘Schala’s Theme’ are particularly poignant and are sure to tug at your heartstrings.

7. The Last of Us
Gustavo Santaolalla’s composition for The Last of Us perfectly captures the post-apocalyptic world of the game. With its melancholic guitar melodies and haunting vocals, the soundtrack creates a sense of despair and hope that is essential to the game’s narrative. The main theme, ‘The Last of Us,’ is a standout track that beautifully sets the tone for the entire game.

8. Mass Effect 2
Jack Wall and Sam Hulick’s soundtrack for Mass Effect 2 is a perfect blend of sci-fi ambiance and action-packed moments. The stirring orchestral score and electronic elements create a sense of awe and excitement as you traverse the galaxy. The tracks ‘Suicide Mission’ and ‘Leaving Earth’ are particularly powerful and leave a lasting impression.

9. Metal Gear Solid
The Metal Gear Solid series is known for its cinematic storytelling, and the soundtrack plays a crucial role in enhancing the game’s atmosphere. Composed by Harry Gregson-Williams, the soundtrack features a mixture of ambient tracks and intense action themes. The main theme, ‘Metal Gear Solid Main Theme,’ is instantly recognizable and sets the stage for the game’s espionage-filled world.

10. Journey
Austin Wintory’s composition for the indie game Journey is a true work of art. The emotionally charged and beautifully orchestrated soundtrack perfectly captures the game’s themes of companionship and self-discovery. The tracks ‘Nascence’ and ‘Apotheosis’ are particularly stunning and create a sense of wonder that stays with you long after the game is over.

In conclusion, video game soundtracks have the power to transport us to another world and evoke a wide range of emotions. The above-mentioned soundtracks are just a few examples of the incredible musical compositions that have graced the gaming industry. Whether it’s the nostalgic tunes of Super Mario Bros. or the emotionally charged melodies of The Last of Us, these soundtracks have become an integral part of the gaming experience. So, the next time you embark on a gaming adventure, don’t forget to appreciate the incredible work that goes into creating these memorable soundtracks.
