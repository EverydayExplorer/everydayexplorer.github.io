Bestselling Books of the Decade: A Retrospective


The past decade has been a remarkable one for literature, with a myriad of bestselling books capturing the hearts and minds of readers across the globe. From gripping thrillers to heartwarming romances, this decade has seen the release of some truly unforgettable stories that have left a lasting impact on readers. As we look back on the bestselling books of the past ten years, it’s clear that these stories have not only entertained us but have also shaped our cultural landscape in profound ways.

One of the standout bestsellers of the decade is ‘The Girl on the Train’ by Paula Hawkins. This psychological thriller took the literary world by storm when it was first published in 2015, quickly capturing the top spot on bestseller lists around the world. The novel follows the story of a woman who becomes entangled in a missing person investigation, with twists and turns that keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page. With its compelling storyline and flawed yet relatable characters, ‘The Girl on the Train’ remains a standout thriller that continues to be beloved by readers.

Another bestselling book that has left a lasting impact on readers is ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ by John Green. This heartfelt young adult novel tells the story of two teenagers who fall in love while dealing with the challenges of living with cancer. Despite its emotionally heavy subject matter, ‘The Fault in Our Stars’ resonated with readers of all ages, thanks to its poignant exploration of love, loss, and the beauty of life. The novel’s powerful themes and memorable characters have cemented its status as a modern classic that continues to be cherished by readers around the world.

In the realm of fantasy fiction, no book has made quite as big of an impact as ‘A Game of Thrones’ by George R.R. Martin. The first book in the acclaimed ‘A Song of Ice and Fire’ series, ‘A Game of Thrones’ introduced readers to the epic world of Westeros, complete with warring families, political intrigue, and high-stakes battles for power. The book’s complex characters and intricate plotlines have earned it a devoted fan base and spawned a hit television series that further propelled its popularity. As one of the defining fantasy novels of the decade, ‘A Game of Thrones’ has left an indelible mark on the genre and continues to be celebrated by fans of epic storytelling.

Of course, these are just a few of the bestselling books that have defined the past decade in literature. From gripping thrillers to heartwarming romances, the past ten years have seen a diverse array of stories that have captivated readers and sparked important conversations. As we look back on the bestselling books of the decade, it’s evident that these stories have not only entertained us but have also enriched our lives in countless ways. Whether you’re a fan of mystery, romance, or fantasy, there’s no shortage of incredible books to choose from that have made their mark on the literary world. As we move into a new decade, we can only hope that the next ten years will bring us even more unforgettable stories that will shape and inspire us for years to come.
