Fun DIY pet toys you can make at home


Pets are not just an animal that we own, they are our friends, our companions and sometimes even our family members. We all love our pets and want them to be happy and content, and what better way to do that than by making them some fun DIY toys at home?

DIY toys are a great way to keep your pet’s mind and body active, while also saving some money by not buying expensive store-bought toys. Plus, it’s a fun activity that you and your pet can enjoy together.

Here are some simple and fun DIY pet toys that you can make at home:

1. Sock Toys

Do you have some old socks lying around the house? Don’t throw them away just yet! Turn them into fun toys for your pets. Cut off the toe part of the sock, stuff it with some cotton or old clothes and tie the end with a knot. You can even add some catnip or treats to make it more interesting for your pet.

2. Paper bag toy

Cats love playing with paper bags. All you need to do is take a paper bag, sprinkle a bit of catnip inside, and crumple it up. Your cat will have hours of fun playing with it. Just make sure to supervise them while they play with it, to avoid any accidents.

3. Tennis ball treat toy

This one is perfect for dogs who love to play fetch. Take a tennis ball and make a small cut on it. Then, fill it up with some treats and voila, you have a treat-dispensing ball toy that your dog will love.

4. Tug of war toy

Tug of war is a great game to play with your dog, and you can easily make a tug toy at home. All you need is an old t-shirt or a rope. Cut it into strips and braid it together. Tie knots at the end, and you have a fun toy that you and your furry friend can enjoy together.

5. Cardboard scratcher

Cats love to scratch surfaces, and a homemade cardboard scratcher is a great way to keep them away from your furniture. Cut a piece of cardboard into a rectangular shape and wrap it in some jute or twine. Your cat will love scratching it and will leave your furniture alone.

In conclusion, making DIY toys at home is a great way to keep your pets happy and entertained. Not only is it a fun activity, but it also saves some money. These simple DIY ideas can be made with everyday items that you might have lying around the house. Give it a try, and you and your pets are sure to have a great time together.
