Funny YouTube Channels: Must-Follow Creators for Endless Laughs


Are you tired of scrolling endlessly through your YouTube feed, desperately seeking a good laugh? Look no further! In this blog post, we’re diving headfirst into the wild world of funny YouTube channels, where laughter knows no bounds and entertainment reigns supreme. Get ready to chuckle, giggle, and maybe even snort with these must-follow creators who specialize in serving up endless doses of hilarity.

1. JennaMarbles: The OG Queen of Comedy

When it comes to funny YouTube channels, JennaMarbles is an absolute legend. With her quirky humor and relatable content, Jenna has been dominating the platform for over a decade. From attempting bizarre beauty hacks to transforming into her beloved dogs, Kermit and Marbles, Jenna’s videos never fail to leave viewers in stitches. Trust us, once you start watching JennaMarbles, you’ll be hooked for life.

2. The Try Guys: Hilarity Ensues

If you’re in the mood for a good laugh, The Try Guys have got you covered. Known for their outrageous challenges and hilarious experiments, Keith, Ned, Zach, and Eugene are guaranteed to keep you entertained for hours on end. Whether they’re testing the limits of their taste buds with weird food combinations or attempting to master bizarre skills, The Try Guys never fail to deliver gut-busting laughs.

3. Smosh: Comedy Gold Since 2005

Smosh has been making audiences laugh since the early days of YouTube, and they show no signs of slowing down. With a combination of sketch comedy, parody videos, and absurd challenges, Ian and Anthony (and the rest of the Smosh crew) continue to keep viewers in stitches with their off-the-wall humor. Whether they’re poking fun at pop culture or diving into the absurdity of everyday life, Smosh is always a guaranteed laugh riot.

4. Good Mythical Morning: Start Your Day with a Smile

If you’re in need of a pick-me-up, look no further than Good Mythical Morning. Hosted by the dynamic duo of Rhett and Link, this daily talk show is chock-full of bizarre experiments, taste tests, and hilarious challenges. With their infectious chemistry and boundless creativity, Rhett and Link have cultivated a loyal fanbase who tune in religiously for their daily dose of laughter.

5. TheOdd1sOut: Where Animation Meets Comedy

James Rallison, better known as TheOdd1sOut, has taken the YouTube world by storm with his witty animations and relatable storytelling. With a knack for turning mundane experiences into laugh-out-loud anecdotes, James has amassed a huge following of fans who eagerly await each new upload. Whether he’s recounting awkward encounters or poking fun at everyday struggles, TheOdd1sOut never fails to bring a smile to viewers’ faces.

6. Cody Ko: Sarcasm at Its Finest

If sarcasm is your love language, then Cody Ko is the YouTuber for you. With his razor-sharp wit and deadpan delivery, Cody hilariously skewers everything from internet trends to celebrity culture. Whether he’s roasting cringeworthy TikToks or riffing on ridiculous Instagram influencers, Cody’s videos are guaranteed to leave you in stitches.

7. CollegeHumor: Comedy Central on YouTube

Last but certainly not least, we have CollegeHumor, the ultimate destination for comedy on YouTube. With a talented team of writers, actors, and comedians, CollegeHumor produces a wide range of hilarious content, from sketch comedy to original series. Whether you’re a fan of absurd humor or clever satire, CollegeHumor has something for everyone.

In conclusion, if you’re in need of a good laugh, look no further than these hilarious YouTube channels. Whether you’re a fan of sketch comedy, vlogs, or animated storytelling, there’s something for everyone in the wild world of YouTube comedy. So sit back, relax, and prepare to laugh until your sides hurt!
