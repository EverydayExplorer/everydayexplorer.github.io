10 Quick and Easy Dinner Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Weeknights can be hectic, and finding time to prepare a wholesome meal can often feel like a daunting task. Between work, family commitments, and daily errands, cooking a nutritious dinner might seem impossible. However, with a bit of planning and some creative recipes, you can enjoy delicious meals without spending hours in the kitchen. Here are ten quick and easy dinner recipes perfect for those busy weeknights.

1. One-Pot Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine pasta, chicken broth, milk, and minced garlic.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until pasta is cooked and liquid is mostly absorbed, about 10-12 minutes.
  3. Stir in Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

2. Sheet Pan Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced
  • 3 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 packet fajita seasoning mix
  • Tortillas for serving
  • Toppings: sour cream, guacamole, salsa

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. On a sheet pan, toss chicken, bell peppers, and onion with olive oil and fajita seasoning.
  3. Spread everything out in an even layer.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve with warm tortillas and your favorite toppings.

3. Quick Shrimp Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce until everything is evenly coated.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.

4. Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, and garlic.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Add broccoli and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  5. Pour sauce over beef and broccoli, stirring to combine.
  6. Cook until sauce has thickened, about 2 minutes.
  7. Serve over cooked rice.

5. Caprese Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut a pocket into each chicken breast.
  3. Stuff each pocket with mozzarella, tomato slices, and basil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Sear chicken breasts until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Transfer skillet to the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  8. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

6. Vegetarian Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn or flour tortillas for serving
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, salsa, sour cream

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine black beans, corn, avocado, red onion, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until well combined.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Spoon the filling into the tortillas.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, salsa, and sour cream.

7. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, sautéing until garlic is golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti and reserved pasta water to the skillet.
  5. Toss until pasta is well coated.
  6. Stir in parsley and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Season with salt to taste and serve.

8. Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Place salmon fillets on the baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Top each fillet with lemon slices and sprinkle with dill.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve with a side of steamed vegetables or a salad.

9. Chicken Caesar Wraps

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 4 large tortillas
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded chicken, Caesar dressing, romaine lettuce, and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Lay tortillas flat and spoon the chicken mixture down the center of each one.
  4. Fold the sides over the filling and roll up tightly.
  5. Slice in half and serve.

10. Quick Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Toppings: sour cream, salsa, guacamole

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  2. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add taco seasoning mix and 1/4 cup of water to the skillet.
  4. Stir until well combined and cook until most of the liquid has evaporated.
  5. Warm taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  6. Spoon the beef mixture into the shells.
  7. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and cheddar cheese.
  8. Add your favorite toppings before serving.

These ten recipes are not only quick and easy but also packed with flavor and nutrition. They are perfect for busy weeknights when time is limited but you still want to enjoy a delicious homemade meal. Happy cooking!
