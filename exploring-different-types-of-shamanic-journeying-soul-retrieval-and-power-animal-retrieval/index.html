Exploring Different Types of Shamanic Journeying: Soul Retrieval and Power Animal Retrieval


Shamanism is an ancient spiritual practice that has been around for thousands of years. It is a way of connecting with the spiritual realm in order to gain wisdom, guidance, and healing. Shamanic journeying is one of the fundamental practices in shamanism, and it involves entering an altered state of consciousness to communicate with spirits and receive messages.

In this blog post, we will explore two types of shamanic journeying: soul retrieval and power animal retrieval. These practices are both powerful tools for personal growth and transformation.

Soul Retrieval

Soul retrieval is a shamanic practice that focuses on reclaiming lost parts of the soul. According to shamanic belief, when we experience trauma or profound loss, a part of our soul may fragment and dissociate as a way to protect us. This can result in feelings of emptiness, disconnection, and a lack of vitality.

During a soul retrieval journey, the shaman enters a trance state and travels to the spiritual realm to search for the lost soul fragments. The shaman communicates with helping spirits and guides who provide information about where the soul fragments may be and how they can be retrieved.

Once the soul fragments are located, the shaman brings them back to the individual and reintegrates them into their being. This process can bring about deep healing, wholeness, and a renewed sense of purpose and vitality.

Soul retrieval is particularly beneficial for individuals who have experienced significant trauma, such as abuse, accidents, or the death of a loved one. It can help to heal emotional wounds, release stuck energy, and restore a sense of inner balance and harmony.

Power Animal Retrieval

Power animal retrieval is another form of shamanic journeying that focuses on connecting with a personal power animal. In shamanic belief, a power animal is a spiritual guide in animal form that provides strength, protection, and guidance to the individual.

During a power animal retrieval journey, the shaman enters an altered state of consciousness and travels to the spirit realm to seek out their client’s power animal. The shaman may use drumming, chanting, or other techniques to facilitate the journey.

Once the power animal is found, the shaman establishes a connection with it and brings it back to the individual. The power animal becomes a companion and ally, offering its wisdom and support in times of need.

Power animal retrieval can be a transformative experience, as it allows individuals to tap into their innate power and connect with their true nature. The power animal serves as a source of guidance and protection, helping to navigate life’s challenges and develop a deeper understanding of oneself.

Benefits of Shamanic Journeying

Both soul retrieval and power animal retrieval offer numerous benefits for personal growth and spiritual development. Here are some of the key advantages of practicing shamanic journeying:

  1. Healing: Shamanic journeying can facilitate deep emotional and energetic healing. It allows individuals to release past trauma, restore balance, and experience profound inner transformation.

  2. Self-Discovery: Journeying opens the door to self-discovery and self-awareness. Through direct experiences in the spirit realm, individuals can gain insights into their true nature, purpose, and potential.

  3. Guidance and Support: Shamanic journeying provides a direct line of communication with spiritual guides and helpers. These allies offer guidance, wisdom, and support in navigating life’s challenges and making important decisions.

  4. Connection to the Natural World: Shamanic journeying reconnects individuals with the natural world and the spiritual realms. It fosters a sense of unity and interconnection, promoting reverence and respect for all living beings.

  5. Empowerment: By connecting with their power animal and reclaiming lost soul fragments, individuals experience a profound sense of empowerment and self-confidence. They realize their innate strength and gain the tools to live a more authentic and fulfilling life.

How to Begin Your Shamanic Journeying Practice

If you are interested in exploring shamanic journeying, it is recommended to work with an experienced shamanic practitioner who can guide you safely through the process. They can provide you with the necessary tools, techniques, and support to embark on your personal journey.

To prepare for a shamanic journey, it is important to create a sacred space where you feel safe and comfortable. This may involve lighting candles, burning incense, or placing meaningful objects around you. You can also use rhythmic drumming, chanting, or guided meditation to induce a trance state.

Once you are in an altered state of consciousness, set an intention for your journey and invite your spirit guides or power animals to join you. Trust your intuition and let the journey unfold naturally. Remember to journal and reflect on your experiences afterward to deepen your understanding and integration of the journey.

In conclusion, shamanic journeying is a powerful spiritual practice that offers profound healing, guidance, and transformation. Whether through soul retrieval or power animal retrieval, these practices provide a direct connection to the spiritual realms and facilitate personal growth and self-discovery. If you are called to explore the world of shamanism, consider embarking on your own journey with the guidance of an experienced practitioner.
