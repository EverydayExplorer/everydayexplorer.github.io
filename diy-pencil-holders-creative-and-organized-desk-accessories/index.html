DIY Pencil Holders: Creative and Organized Desk Accessories


A cluttered desk can be a major distraction when you’re trying to focus on your work. Finding the right pencil holder can help you keep your desk organized and make it easier to find what you need at a moment’s notice. But why settle for a generic store-bought pencil holder when you can create your own unique and creative designs with just a few simple materials? In this blog post, we will explore the world of DIY pencil holders and share some creative ideas to inspire you.

Benefits of DIY Pencil Holders

Before we dive into the various DIY pencil holder ideas, let’s discuss why you should consider making your own instead of buying one from a store.

Firstly, DIY pencil holders allow you to showcase your creativity and personal style. You can choose the colors, materials, and designs that best reflect your personality. Plus, you can create multiple pencil holders and switch them out whenever you feel like it, giving your desk a fresh look.

Secondly, DIY pencil holders can save you money. Most store-bought desk accessories can be quite pricey, especially if you’re looking for something unique. By making your own pencil holder, you can save a significant amount of money while still achieving a stylish and organized workspace.

Lastly, creating your own pencil holders can be a fun and rewarding DIY project. It’s a great way to unleash your creativity, relax, and unwind while also being productive.

DIY Pencil Holder Ideas

Now that we’ve highlighted the benefits of making your own pencil holders, let’s explore some creative and easy-to-create designs:

1. Mason Jar Pencil Holder

Mason jars are versatile and trendy, making them a perfect material for a pencil holder. You can paint the jar in any color you desire or use decorative tape to add patterns. Additionally, you can attach hooks to the lid to hang smaller items like paperclips or rubber bands.

2. Recycled Can Pencil Holder

Recycling used cans is an eco-friendly way to create a simple and functional pencil holder. Start by cleaning and removing any labels from the can. Then, take decorative paper, fabric, or even washi tape to wrap around the can to give it a personalized look.

3. Lego Brick Pencil Holder

If you’re a fan of building blocks, why not incorporate them into your pencil holder? Grab a Lego baseplate and attach Lego bricks to it to create a customized pencil holder. You can shape it in any way you like, making it a fun and playful addition to your desk.

4. Cork Board Pencil Holder

Cork boards are not just for pinning up notes and memos; they can also double as a pencil holder. Start by cutting a square or rectangular cork board piece and attach small wooden dowels or thumbtacks to hold your pencils vertically. This design is not only practical but also adds a touch of rustic charm to your desk.

5. PVC Pipe Pencil Holder

PVC pipes are readily available and can be easily transformed into a cool and modern pencil holder. Cut the PVC pipe into desired lengths, and then paint or decorate them with patterns or motifs that match your preferences. Stack them together in a creative arrangement, and voila! You have a unique and functional pencil holder.

6. Wooden Block Pencil Holder

For a more natural and timeless look, consider using wooden blocks to create your pencil holder. You can drill holes of different sizes into the block to hold various writing utensils. Sand the block to a smooth finish and apply a coat of varnish or paint to protect it and add a touch of shine.

Conclusion

A well-organized desk is key to productivity, and a DIY pencil holder can be the perfect solution. By creating your own pencil holders, you can showcase your creativity, save money, and have fun in the process. Whether you opt for a Mason jar, recycled can, Lego bricks, cork board, PVC pipe, or wooden block, the possibilities are endless. So, roll up your sleeves, gather your materials, and get ready to create a one-of-a-kind pencil holder that will bring creativity and organization to your desk.
