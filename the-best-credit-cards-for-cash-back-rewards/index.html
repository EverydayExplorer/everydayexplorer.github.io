The Best Credit Cards for Cash Back Rewards


Credit cards have become an integral part of our daily lives, and we use them for almost every transaction these days. The reason for their popularity is that they provide users with several perks and benefits like cashback rewards, points, miles, discounts, and more.

When it comes to the best credit cards for cashback rewards, there are quite a few options available in the market, and it can be challenging to choose the right one for you. In this blog, we will discuss some of the most popular credit cards for cashback rewards and help you make an informed decision.

1. Chase Freedom Unlimited:

The Chase Freedom Unlimited credit card is a popular choice among consumers because of its flat-rate cashback rewards program. With this card, you can earn 1.5% cashback on all purchases, with no limit on the amount you can earn. Additionally, if you spend 00 in the first three months of opening your account, you’ll receive a 00 cashback bonus.

One of the best features of the Chase Freedom Unlimited is that it doesn’t charge an annual fee, which is a significant benefit for those looking to save money. Moreover, the card has a 0% introductory APR for the first 15 months on purchases and balance transfers, giving consumers plenty of time to pay off their debts without incurring interest.

2. Citi Double Cash:

The Citi Double Cash credit card is another popular option for those seeking cashback rewards. This card rewards users for their purchases in two ways – you earn 1% cashback when you purchase something, and then another 1% when you pay off the balance.

Unlike other cards, which may set a limit on the amount of cashback you can earn, the Citi Double Cash has no limits, and you can earn an unlimited amount of cashback rewards. This card also doesn’t charge an annual fee, which is another significant benefit.

3. Blue Cash Preferred from American Express:

The Blue Cash Preferred credit card from American Express is an excellent choice for those looking to earn cashback rewards on their everyday purchases. This card offers a range of cashback rewards on different categories, such as:

• 6% cashback at US supermarkets (up to ,000 per year in purchases, then 1% cashback)
• 6% cashback on select US streaming subscriptions
• 3% cashback at US gas stations and on transit expenses
• 1% cashback on all other purchases

4. Capital One Savor Cash Rewards:

The Capital One Savor Cash Rewards credit card is an excellent choice for those who love dining and entertainment, as it offers generous cashback rewards on these categories. This card offers:

• 4% cashback on dining and entertainment purchases
• 2% cashback at grocery stores
• 1% cashback on all other purchases

If you spend 000 in the first three months of opening your account, you’ll receive a 00 cashback bonus. This card has an annual fee of 5, but the value of the cashback rewards makes up for it.

5. Discover it Cash Back:

The Discover it Cash Back credit card is a great choice for those looking for a cashback rewards card with no annual fee. This card offers:

• 5% cashback on rotating categories like gas stations, grocery stores, restaurants, and more (up to ,500 per quarter)
• 1% cashback on all other purchases

Also, if you’re a new cardholder and make purchases in the first three months, Discover will match all cashback rewards earned during that period. This means that whatever cashback rewards you earn during your first three months will be doubled.

In conclusion, cashback rewards credit cards are an excellent way to earn some money back on your everyday purchases. Each of the cards mentioned above has its unique cashback rewards program and other benefits, so it’s essential to research and choose the one that suits your spending habits and needs. Remember to use your credit card responsibly and pay off the balance in full each month to avoid accruing interest and additional debt.
