The Top 5 Foundations for Oily Skin


When it comes to finding the perfect foundation for oily skin, it can often feel like an uphill battle. With the constant struggle to keep shine at bay and prevent makeup from sliding off, it’s important to choose a product that not only provides coverage but also controls oil production throughout the day.

To help you in your search for the best foundation for oily skin, we have rounded up the top 5 foundations that are specifically formulated to combat excess oil and keep your complexion looking fresh and matte.

1. Estée Lauder Double Wear Stay-in-Place Foundation:
This cult-favorite foundation is known for its long-lasting formula that stays put even in the most humid conditions. The oil-free formula offers medium to full coverage with a matte finish, making it ideal for oily skin types. It’s also resistant to sweat, humidity, and transfer, ensuring that your makeup stays intact all day long.

2. Fenty Beauty Pro Filt’r Soft Matte Longwear Foundation:
Created by Rihanna, this foundation has gained a massive following for its ability to provide a soft matte finish without clogging pores or feeling heavy on the skin. The oil-free formula is buildable, allowing you to achieve your desired coverage, while the long-wearing properties keep excess oil at bay for hours on end.

3. Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation:
A drugstore favorite, the Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation is a budget-friendly option that doesn’t compromise on quality. It has a lightweight, oil-free formula that offers buildable coverage and effectively blurs the appearance of pores. The matte finish helps control oil throughout the day, leaving your skin looking fresh and shine-free.

4. NARS Velvet Matte Skin Tint:
For those who prefer a more lightweight option, the NARS Velvet Matte Skin Tint is a great choice. This oil-free foundation has a velvety, semi-matte finish that provides a natural-looking coverage while controlling oil and shine. It’s also enriched with vitamins C and E to nourish the skin and improve its overall texture and tone.

5. Smashbox Studio Skin 15 Hour Wear Hydrating Foundation:
While it may seem counterintuitive to use a hydrating foundation on oily skin, the Smashbox Studio Skin 15 Hour Wear Hydrating Foundation is formulated with a unique oil-free, gel-based formula that is designed to control oil while providing a natural, satin finish. It’s long-lasting, sweat-proof, and humidity-resistant, ensuring that your makeup holds up, even on the hottest of days.

In conclusion, finding the perfect foundation for oily skin can be a daunting task, but it’s not impossible. By opting for oil-free formulas with a matte or semi-matte finish, you can effectively control oil production and keep your makeup looking fresh and shine-free throughout the day. The Estée Lauder Double Wear Stay-in-Place Foundation, Fenty Beauty Pro Filt’r Soft Matte Longwear Foundation, Maybelline Fit Me Matte + Poreless Foundation, NARS Velvet Matte Skin Tint, and Smashbox Studio Skin 15 Hour Wear Hydrating Foundation are all excellent options that cater specifically to those with oily skin.
