The Top 5 Affordable Sports Cars for 2021


When it comes to sports cars, many people assume that they are expensive and out of reach for the average car enthusiast. However, this is not always the case. In fact, there are several affordable sports cars available in the market today that offer impressive performance and thrilling driving experiences without breaking the bank. If you’re in the market for an affordable sports car in 2021, look no further! In this blog post, we will be exploring the top five affordable sports cars for 2021.

1. Mazda MX-5 Miata:
The Mazda MX-5 Miata has long been hailed as one of the best affordable sports cars on the market, and for good reason. It combines a lightweight design with excellent handling, making it a joy to drive on winding roads or even on the track. The MX-5 Miata is powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 181 horsepower, which may not sound like a lot, but given its lightweight construction, it offers plenty of thrills. With a starting price under 0,000, the MX-5 Miata is an excellent choice for those seeking an affordable sports car.

2. Ford Mustang:
The Ford Mustang is an iconic American muscle car that has been around for decades, and it continues to deliver impressive performance at an affordable price. The base model comes equipped with a 2.3-liter EcoBoost four-cylinder engine that produces 310 horsepower, while the higher trims offer more powerful V6 and V8 engine options. The Mustang offers a comfortable interior, modern technology features, and a starting price just under 0,000, making it an excellent choice for those looking for a balance between performance and affordability.

3. Subaru BRZ:
The Subaru BRZ is a compact sports car that offers a unique rear-wheel-drive experience at an affordable price. It is powered by a 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine that delivers 228 horsepower, providing plenty of power for spirited driving. The BRZ offers excellent handling and a well-tuned suspension, making it a joy to drive on twisty roads. With a starting price just over 8,000, the BRZ offers great value for money in the sports car segment.

4. Hyundai Veloster N:
The Hyundai Veloster N is a hot hatchback that offers a thrilling driving experience at an affordable price. It is powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 275 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful options in its class. The Veloster N features sporty exterior styling, a well-appointed interior, and a long list of standard features. With a starting price just under 0,000, the Veloster N is an excellent choice for those who want a practical and fun-to-drive sports car.

5. Toyota 86:
The Toyota 86 is a compact sports car that focuses on delivering a pure driving experience at an affordable price. It is powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 205 horsepower, providing plenty of power for spirited driving. The 86 features a lightweight and balanced chassis, precise steering, and excellent handling, making it a joy to drive on twisty roads. With a starting price just over 7,000, the 86 offers great value for money in the sports car segment.

In conclusion, if you’re in the market for an affordable sports car in 2021, you don’t have to compromise on performance or style. The Mazda MX-5 Miata, Ford Mustang, Subaru BRZ, Hyundai Veloster N, and Toyota 86 are all excellent options that offer thrilling driving experiences at a price that won’t break the bank. So go ahead, break free from the misconceptions about affordability and enjoy the exhilarating world of sports cars without emptying your wallet.
