The Dos and Don'ts of Mascara Application: Expert Advice


Do you ever find yourself struggling to achieve perfect mascara application? Fret not, because we’ve got you covered! In this comprehensive blog post, we will delve into the dos and don’ts of mascara application, providing you with expert advice to help you achieve those luscious lashes you’ve always dreamed of. So grab your favorite mascara wand and let’s get started!

DO: Start with Clean Lashes

Before you begin applying mascara, it’s essential to start with clean lashes. Make sure to remove any residual makeup or oils from your lashes to ensure optimal application. Use a gentle eye makeup remover or micellar water to cleanse your lashes thoroughly.

DON’T: Pump the Mascara Wand

Here’s a common mistake many people make – pumping the mascara wand in and out of the tube. This action introduces air into the tube, causing the mascara to dry out faster. Instead, gently swirl the wand inside the tube to pick up the desired amount of product. This will help maintain the longevity of your mascara.

DO: Curl your Lashes

For an extra oomph, curl your lashes before applying mascara. Using an eyelash curler, position it at the base of your lashes and gently squeeze for a few seconds. This step will help lift and open up your eyes, making your mascara application appear more impactful.

DON’T: Apply Too Many Coats

While it’s tempting to pile on the coats of mascara, less is more in this case. Applying too many coats can lead to clumpy and spidery lashes. Instead, start with one coat, allowing it to dry before deciding if you need additional layers. This will help you achieve a more natural and separated lash look.

DO: Wiggle the Wand

To avoid clumps and ensure even distribution of mascara, wiggle the wand from the root to the tip of your lashes. This technique helps to coat each lash evenly, giving you a fuller and more voluminous look. Take your time and be patient – the results will be worth it!

DON’T: Forget the Lower Lashes

While the focus is often on the upper lashes, don’t neglect your lower lashes. Use a light hand and apply a minimal amount of mascara to avoid a heavy or smudged look. This step will help balance your overall eye makeup and create a cohesive look.

DO: Remove Excess Mascara

If you accidentally apply too much mascara or notice any clumps, don’t panic! Take a clean spoolie brush or a disposable mascara wand and gently comb through your lashes to remove any excess product. This simple step will help separate and define your lashes, giving them a more polished appearance.

DON’T: Sleep with Mascara On

Last but certainly not least, never sleep with mascara on! Leaving mascara on overnight can lead to lash breakage, clumping, and even eye infections. Always remember to remove your mascara before you hit the hay using a gentle eye makeup remover or cleansing oil.

In conclusion, mastering the art of mascara application takes a bit of practice and knowledge of the dos and don’ts. By following these expert tips, you’ll be well on your way to achieving stunning, fluttery lashes that will make heads turn. So go ahead, embrace your inner lash artist and let your eyes do the talking!
