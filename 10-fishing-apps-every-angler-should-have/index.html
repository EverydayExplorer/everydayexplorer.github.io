10 Fishing Apps Every Angler Should Have


Fishing has always been a beloved pastime for many, and with the advancement of technology, anglers now have access to a wide array of fishing apps that can enhance their fishing experience. These apps are designed to provide helpful information, improve navigation, track catches, and connect with fellow anglers. In this blog post, we will explore 10 fishing apps that every angler should have in their arsenal.

1. Fishbrain - Fishbrain is a popular app among anglers, offering a comprehensive fishing logbook, weather updates, and a social network where you can connect with other fishing enthusiasts. It also provides real-time fishing spots and catches, allowing you to stay up-to-date with the latest trends.

2. Navionics - Navionics is a must-have app for any angler who wants to improve their navigation skills. It offers detailed nautical charts, GPS tracking, and route planning, ensuring that you never get lost on the water.

3. Fishing Points - Fishing Points is a versatile app that combines various features, including fishing forecasts, solunar charts, and GPS navigation. It also allows you to save your favorite fishing spots and record your catches, making it a great tool for tracking your fishing progress.

4. iSolunar Fishing & Hunting Times - This app utilizes solunar theory to provide accurate fishing and hunting times. It takes into account factors like the moon phase, sunrise, and sunset times to determine the best times for fishing. It’s a great tool for maximizing your chances of catching fish.

5. Fishidy - Fishidy is an excellent app for anglers who enjoy exploring new fishing spots. It provides detailed maps, fishing reports, and local tips from experienced anglers. The app also allows you to connect with other anglers and share your catches.

6. Pro Angler - Pro Angler is a comprehensive fishing app that offers a wealth of information. It includes tide charts, fishing regulations, fish identification, and even a fish weight calculator. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned angler, this app is a valuable resource.

7. Fishing Knots - As any angler knows, tying the right knot is crucial for successful fishing. The Fishing Knots app provides step-by-step instructions for tying various knots, ensuring that you always have the right knot for the job.

8. My Fishing Forecast - My Fishing Forecast is a handy app that provides accurate weather forecasts specifically tailored for fishing. It takes into account factors like wind speed, barometric pressure, and moon phase to help you plan your fishing trips more effectively.

9. FishTrack - FishTrack is a fantastic app for offshore anglers. It offers real-time satellite imagery, sea surface temperature charts, and current data, allowing you to locate fish more efficiently. It’s a valuable tool for those who enjoy offshore fishing.

10. Fishing Spots - Fishing Spots is an app that provides detailed information about fishing spots around the world. It includes maps, angler reviews, and catch data, helping you find the best fishing spots in your area or wherever you plan to travel.

In conclusion, these 10 fishing apps are essential for any angler who wants to take their fishing game to the next level. Whether you’re looking for fishing spots, weather forecasts, navigation assistance, or social networking opportunities, these apps have got you covered. So, download them today and make your fishing adventures even more enjoyable and successful. Happy fishing!
