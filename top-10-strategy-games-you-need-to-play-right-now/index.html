Top 10 Strategy Games You Need to Play Right Now


Are you tired of playing the same old games over and over again? Do you want to challenge your strategic thinking and put your skills to the test? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the top 10 strategy games that you absolutely need to play right now. These games will not only entertain you but also sharpen your mind and keep you engaged for hours on end. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of strategy gaming!

1. ‘Civilization VI’
Civilization VI is a turn-based strategy game that allows you to build and expand your own civilization from scratch. With a wide range of options and strategies to choose from, this game will keep you hooked for hours. Explore new territories, develop technologies, and engage in diplomacy to become the ultimate ruler of the world.

2. ‘XCOM 2’
If you’re a fan of alien invasions and tactical combat, then XCOM 2 is the game for you. As the commander of a secret resistance group, you’ll lead your squad in intense battles against extraterrestrial forces. Plan your moves carefully, utilize cover, and make use of different classes to outsmart your enemies and save humanity.

3. ‘Total War: Warhammer II’
Total War: Warhammer II combines the best of both worlds - the grand strategy of the Total War series and the epic fantasy of the Warhammer universe. Choose from a variety of factions, each with their own unique playstyle, and conquer the world through diplomacy, warfare, and cunning tactics.

4. ‘Stellaris’
In Stellaris, you’ll embark on a journey through the vastness of space as the leader of a space-faring civilization. Explore uncharted galaxies, encounter alien species, and shape the destiny of your empire through diplomacy, colonization, and warfare. With its deep gameplay mechanics and endless possibilities, Stellaris will keep you engaged for hours on end.

5. ‘Company of Heroes 2’
Set during World War II, Company of Heroes 2 is a real-time strategy game that focuses on tactical combat and resource management. Lead your troops through historically accurate battles, utilize terrain to your advantage, and make strategic decisions that can turn the tide of war. This game will test your ability to think on your feet and adapt to changing situations.

6. ‘Crusader Kings III’
Crusader Kings III is a grand strategy game that puts you in the shoes of a medieval ruler. Manage your dynasty, navigate political intrigue, and expand your realm through diplomacy, warfare, and strategic marriages. With its complex character interactions and dynamic world, this game offers a unique and immersive strategy experience.

7. ‘Hearts of Iron IV’
If you’re a history buff and enjoy World War II-themed games, Hearts of Iron IV is a must-play. As the leader of a country, you’ll guide your nation through the challenges of war, manage resources, and make crucial strategic decisions. From planning military campaigns to negotiating alliances, every move you make can alter the course of history.

8. ‘Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition’
Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition is a classic real-time strategy game that has stood the test of time. Build and manage your civilization, gather resources, and lead your armies to victory in epic battles. With its rich historical setting and addictive gameplay, this game is a must-have for strategy enthusiasts.

9. ‘Europa Universalis IV’
Europa Universalis IV is a grand strategy game that allows you to rewrite history. Take control of a nation and guide it through centuries of political, economic, and military challenges. From colonizing new territories to forming alliances, your decisions will shape the course of events and determine the fate of your empire.

10. ‘Sid Meier’s Pirates!’
Sid Meier’s Pirates! is a strategy game with a twist - it lets you live the life of a pirate in the golden age of piracy. Sail the high seas, engage in naval combat, and plunder treasure as you build your reputation and amass wealth. With its open-world gameplay and swashbuckling adventures, this game offers a unique and immersive strategy experience.

So there you have it, the top 10 strategy games that you need to play right now. Whether you’re a fan of turn-based or real-time strategy, historical or fantasy settings, there’s something for everyone on this list. So gather your wits, sharpen your skills, and embark on a strategic journey like no other. Happy gaming!
