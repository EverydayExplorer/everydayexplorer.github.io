Summer Shoe Trends: Sandals, Slides, and More


The sun is shining, the birds are singing, and the flowers are blooming - it’s officially summer time! As we swap our winter boots for something more suitable for the warmer weather, let’s look at the summer shoe trends of 2021.

Sandals

Nothing screams summer quite like a pair of sandals. And this year, there are a variety of styles to choose from. One of the most popular trends is strappy sandals, with thin straps and minimalist designs. They allow your feet to breathe and look great with almost any summer outfit. Espadrille sandals are also trending, with their rope soles and simple yet chic designs.

Slides

Slides have been trending for the past few years, and they’re not going anywhere in 2021. They’re easy to slip on and off, making them perfect for a quick trip to the beach or pool. The most popular style this year is furry slides. They add a touch of luxury and glamour to an otherwise casual shoe.

Chunky Soles

We’re seeing chunky soles everywhere this year, and not just in shoes - they’re also trending in sneakers and boots. But in sandals, chunky soles give a modern twist to a classic shoe. They’re perfect for adding a little height without sacrificing comfort.

Platform Sandals

If you’re looking for even more height, platform sandals are the way to go. They’re trendy and chic, but also comfortable enough to wear all day. This year, we’re seeing a lot of platform sandals with a square toe. They give a nod to the ’90s while still being current.

Wedge Sandals

Wedge sandals are a summer classic, and for good reason. They’re comfortable and give a little extra height without sacrificing stability. This year, we’re seeing a lot of wedge sandals with colorful patterns and prints. They’re the perfect way to add a pop of color to an otherwise neutral outfit.

Wrap Around Sandals

Wrap around sandals are another trend we’re seeing a lot of this year. They have straps that wrap around your ankle, giving a feminine touch to any outfit. The most popular style is the gladiator sandal - it’s been around for a while, but it’s still going strong.

In conclusion, whether you prefer strappy sandals, furry slides, chunky soles, platform sandals, wedge sandals, or wrap around sandals - there’s a shoe style for everyone this summer season. So, kick off your boots and slip into something more comfortable and trendy!
