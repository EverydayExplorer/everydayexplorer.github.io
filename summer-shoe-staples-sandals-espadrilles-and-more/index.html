Summer Shoe Staples: Sandals, Espadrilles, and More


As the sun shines brighter and temperatures rise, it’s time to revamp your footwear collection for the summer season. The right pair of shoes can elevate your outfit, provide comfort during hot days, and keep you stylish whether you’re heading to the beach, brunch, or a casual evening out. In this post, we’ll explore the essential summer shoe staples, from classic sandals to trendy espadrilles and beyond, ensuring you’re well-equipped to step out in style this summer.

The Versatility of Sandals

Types of Sandals

Sandals are a quintessential summer staple, offering both comfort and style. They come in a variety of styles, making them suitable for different occasions. Here are some popular types to consider adding to your collection:

  • Flip-Flops: The ultimate casual footwear, flip-flops are perfect for beach days or lounging by the pool. They’re lightweight and easy to slip on, making them a go-to choice for many.

  • Slides: Slides have gained popularity in recent years, thanks to their convenience and chic aesthetic. They can be worn at the beach, poolside, or even as part of a casual outfit for a day out.

  • Strappy Sandals: For those looking to add a touch of elegance to their summer outfits, strappy sandals are a fantastic option. They can be paired with dresses, shorts, or skirts for a more polished look.

  • Gladiator Sandals: These bold sandals come with multiple straps that wrap around the ankle and up the leg. They add an edgy flair to any outfit and are perfect for making a statement.

Styling Sandals

When it comes to styling sandals, the options are endless. For a laid-back look, pair flip-flops with your favorite swimsuit or shorts. Strappy sandals can elevate a simple sundress, while slides work beautifully with casual denim. The key to styling sandals is to balance comfort with chicness.

Espadrilles: The Summer Classic

Espadrilles have been a summer favorite for decades, known for their lightweight design and stylish appeal. Traditionally made with canvas or cotton fabric and esparto rope soles, they come in various styles, from flats to wedges.

The Allure of Espadrilles

What makes espadrilles so appealing? Their versatility! They can easily transition from a day at the beach to a dinner out. Wedge espadrilles provide height without sacrificing comfort, making them perfect for outdoor summer events. Pair them with a flowing maxi dress or cropped trousers for a bohemian vibe.

Caring for Your Espadrilles

To keep your espadrilles looking fresh throughout the season, it’s essential to care for them properly. Avoid wet conditions, as moisture can damage the fabric and degrade the rope sole. Clean them gently with a damp cloth, and consider applying a fabric protector spray to safeguard against stains.

Sneakers: The Unexpected Summer Staple

While sandals and espadrilles dominate summer footwear, don’t underestimate the power of a good pair of sneakers. They’re not just for the gym; summer sneakers can add a sporty edge to any outfit.

Choosing the Right Sneakers

Opt for lightweight, breathable materials to ensure comfort during hot days. White sneakers are particularly popular, as they can be paired with virtually anything, from dresses to shorts. Look for styles with fun colors or patterns for a more playful summer aesthetic.

Styling Your Sneakers

Sneakers can be styled in numerous ways. For a casual outing, pair them with denim shorts and a graphic tee. For a slightly dressier look, wear them with a sundress or a skirt. The key is to keep the look balanced and effortless.

The Rise of Platform Shoes

Platform shoes are making a comeback this summer, offering a unique blend of comfort and height. These shoes come in various styles, including sandals, espadrilles, and even sneakers, making them a versatile addition to your summer wardrobe.

Why Choose Platform Shoes?

Platform shoes provide the height of heels without the discomfort that often comes with them. They distribute weight more evenly, making them easier to walk in for extended periods. This makes them an excellent choice for long summer days filled with activities.

Styling Platforms

Platforms can be dressed up or down. For a casual look, pair platform sandals with a romper or denim skirt. For evening outings, opt for platform wedges with a chic dress. The added height can enhance your silhouette, making you feel confident and stylish.

Sustainable Summer Footwear Options

As we become more conscious of our environmental impact, sustainable footwear options are gaining traction. Many brands are now offering eco-friendly sandals and espadrilles made from recycled materials or sustainable practices.

Why Go Sustainable?

Choosing sustainable footwear is not only good for the planet but often results in high-quality, durable products. Look for brands that prioritize ethical manufacturing and use environmentally-friendly materials. Investing in sustainable summer shoes can help reduce waste and promote a healthier planet.

Brands to Consider

Several brands are leading the charge in sustainable summer footwear. Look for options from companies that emphasize eco-friendly practices and transparency in their production methods. Brands like Rothy’s and Allbirds offer stylish choices that are as kind to the earth as they are to your feet.

Conclusion

This summer, step into the season with confidence by curating a stylish and functional footwear collection. From the timeless appeal of sandals and espadrilles to the unexpected versatility of sneakers and platforms, there’s a perfect pair for every occasion. Remember to consider comfort, style, and sustainability when making your choices. With the right summer shoe staples, you’ll be ready to embrace all the adventures the sunny season has to offer. Enjoy the warmth, the sunshine, and of course, the fabulous footwear that makes summer so enjoyable!
