The Most Instagrammable Hotels to Inspire Your Wanderlust


Are you an avid traveler with a passion for stunning aesthetics? Do you love documenting your journeys on Instagram and filling your feed with envy-inducing snapshots? If so, you’re in luck because we have compiled a list of the most Instagrammable hotels that are sure to inspire your wanderlust and leave your followers in awe.

1. The Plaza Hotel, New York City, USA
When it comes to iconic luxury and timeless elegance, nothing beats The Plaza Hotel in the heart of Manhattan. From its majestic facade to its opulent interiors, every corner of this legendary hotel screams Instagram-worthy. Capture the golden glow of the chandeliers in the grand lobby or take a selfie in front of the ornate staircase - you’ll feel like royalty in no time.

2. Santorini Grace Hotel, Santorini, Greece
Known for its breathtaking sunsets and picturesque landscapes, Santorini is a dream destination for Instagrammers. And nestled in the cliffs of this idyllic Greek island is the Santorini Grace Hotel. From its white-washed walls and infinity pool overlooking the Aegean Sea to its traditional Cycladic architecture, every photo you capture here will transport your followers to paradise.

3. Amangiri, Canyon Point, USA
For those seeking ultimate seclusion and stunning desert views, the Amangiri in Canyon Point, Utah, is a must-visit. Surrounded by the rugged beauty of the American Southwest, this luxury resort offers minimalist design, infinity pools, and breathtaking landscapes that are Instagram gold. The juxtaposition of the sleek architecture against the natural beauty of the red rocks makes for stunning photo opportunities.

4. Icehotel, Jukkasjärvi, Sweden
If you’re after a truly unique and otherworldly experience, look no further than the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden. As the world’s first hotel made entirely of ice and snow, this place is a frozen wonderland. From intricately carved ice sculptures to ice beds covered in reindeer skins, there’s no shortage of captivating backdrops for your Instagram shots. Just don’t forget to bundle up!

5. Giraffe Manor, Nairobi, Kenya
Are you a wildlife enthusiast with a love for giraffes? Then Giraffe Manor in Nairobi should be at the top of your travel list. This boutique hotel offers guests the opportunity to dine with resident giraffes, who often visit during breakfast and tea time. Imagine capturing a selfie with one of these majestic creatures peering into your frame - it’s an Instagram moment that will surely make your followers ‘aww’ in delight.

6. Riad Yasmine, Marrakech, Morocco
For those who appreciate the magic of Moroccan aesthetics, Riad Yasmine in Marrakech is an Instagrammer’s dream come true. With its lush courtyard, vibrant tiles, and intricately decorated rooms, this boutique hotel offers endless opportunities for visually stunning photos. Whether you’re lounging by the pool or sipping tea on the rooftop terrace, every corner of Riad Yasmine is a masterpiece waiting to be captured.

7. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore
No list of Instagrammable hotels would be complete without mentioning the iconic Marina Bay Sands in Singapore. With its three towering sky-high hotel towers and impressive rooftop infinity pool, this architectural masterpiece is a sight to behold. Capture the breathtaking cityscape views, lounge by the pool with the Singapore skyline in the background, or snap a photo in front of the famous ship-like structure - there’s no shortage of Instagram-worthy moments here.

So there you have it - a compilation of the most Instagrammable hotels that are sure to inspire your wanderlust. From luxurious landmarks to hidden gems, each of these hotels offers a unique experience and endless photo opportunities. Whether you’re a seasoned Instagrammer or just looking to up your travel photography game, these destinations are guaranteed to make your followers green with envy. Start planning your next adventure and get ready to create some unforgettable Instagram memories!
