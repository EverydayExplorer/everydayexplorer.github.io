The Best Hotels for Nature Lovers: Surrounded by Natural Beauty


Nature lovers are always on the lookout for destinations that offer breathtaking natural beauty. There’s nothing quite like staying in a hotel that is surrounded by greenery, mountains, or stunning landscapes. If you’re someone who loves to immerse themselves in nature, then this blog post is for you. We have compiled a list of the best hotels for nature lovers, where you can wake up to the sounds of birds chirping, breathe in the fresh air, and take in the awe-inspiring sights. So, pack your bags, and get ready for an unforgettable nature-filled getaway!

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA Nestled in the heart of Utah’s iconic desert landscape, Amangiri is a luxury resort that offers a truly unique experience for nature enthusiasts. Surrounded by dramatic cliffs, endless canyons, and untouched wilderness, this hotel allows guests to disconnect from the outside world and reconnect with nature. Wake up to stunning sunrise views, embark on guided hikes through the serene desert, or simply relax by the pool as you soak in the breathtaking surroundings.

2. Anse Chastanet, St. Lucia Located in the Caribbean, Anse Chastanet is a paradise for nature lovers. Situated on a hill overlooking the pristine waters of the Caribbean Sea, this hotel is surrounded by lush tropical gardens and a stunning coral reef just a few steps away from your room. Spend your days snorkeling among vibrant fish and marine life, hiking through the nearby rainforests, or simply lounging on the white sandy beaches. With its eco-friendly practices and commitment to sustainability, Anse Chastanet is a perfect choice for nature lovers who also care about the environment.

3. Hotel Budir, Iceland For those who crave a different kind of natural beauty, Hotel Budir in Iceland is the ideal destination. Located on the Snaefellsnes Peninsula, this hotel offers mesmerizing views of the surrounding lava fields, dramatic mountains, and the majestic Snæfellsjökull glacier. Whether you’re chasing the Northern Lights in the winter or exploring the vibrant flora and fauna during the summer, Hotel Budir provides a tranquil retreat for nature enthusiasts. Don’t forget to indulge in the local cuisine, which features fresh seafood sourced from the nearby waters.

4. The Oberoi Vanyavilas, Ranthambhore, India If you’re a wildlife lover, a stay at The Oberoi Vanyavilas is a dream come true. Located adjacent to the famous Ranthambhore Tiger Reserve in India, this luxury hotel offers guests the opportunity to witness the majesty of tigers in their natural habitat. The hotel itself is surrounded by lush gardens teeming with birdlife, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts. Wake up to the sounds of the jungle, take guided safaris to spot the elusive tigers, and come back to the comfort of the hotel’s luxurious amenities.

5. Nimmo Bay Wilderness Resort, British Columbia, Canada For a truly remote and untouched natural experience, head to Nimmo Bay Wilderness Resort in British Columbia, Canada. Accessible only by helicopter or seaplane, this eco-friendly resort is nestled in the heart of the Great Bear Rainforest. With snow-capped mountains, pristine fjords, and an abundance of wildlife, it’s a nature lover’s paradise. Explore the surrounding forests on guided hikes, go kayaking with seals and dolphins, or enjoy a scenic helicopter tour to witness the breathtaking beauty from above. At the end of the day, unwind in your private cabin and listen to the sounds of nature.

Conclusion For nature lovers, finding the perfect hotel surrounded by natural beauty is an essential part of planning a memorable vacation. Whether you prefer stunning desert landscapes, tropical beaches, icy glaciers, lush jungles, or remote wilderness, there is a hotel out there that caters to your desires. The list above highlights just a few of the best hotels for nature lovers around the world. So, go ahead and choose your destination, pack your bags, and get ready to embark on a nature-filled adventure that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime!
