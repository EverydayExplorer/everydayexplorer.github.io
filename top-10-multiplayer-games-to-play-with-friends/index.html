Top 10 Multiplayer Games to Play with Friends


Are you looking for some thrilling and engaging multiplayer games to enjoy with your friends? Look no further! We’ve curated a list of the top 10 multiplayer games that are guaranteed to provide hours of entertainment, laughter, and friendly competition. Whether you’re into action-packed shooters, immersive role-playing games, or light-hearted party games, there’s something for everyone on this list. So grab your friends, power up your consoles or PCs, and get ready to dive into the ultimate multiplayer gaming experience.

1. Overwatch

Overwatch is a fast-paced team-based shooter that offers a diverse cast of characters, each with unique abilities and playstyles. Team up with your friends to form a balanced team and compete against other players in objective-based game modes. With its colorful and vibrant art style, Overwatch is not only visually appealing but also incredibly addictive.

2. Rocket League

Combining soccer with high-powered rocket cars, Rocket League delivers an adrenaline-fueled multiplayer experience like no other. Team up with your friends to score epic goals, perform aerial stunts, and outmaneuver your opponents in this physics-based sports game. The easy-to-learn, difficult-to-master gameplay makes it perfect for players of all skill levels.

3. Fortnite

Fortnite has taken the gaming world by storm with its battle royale mode, where 100 players compete to be the last one standing. Team up with your friends in duos or squads, and put your building and shooting skills to the test as you fight to survive in a constantly shrinking play area. With frequent updates and events, Fortnite always has something new to offer.

4. Among Us

In Among Us, players work together on a spaceship, but one or more of them are impostors trying to sabotage the mission. It’s up to the crewmates to identify the impostors before it’s too late. With its deceitful gameplay and intense social deduction, Among Us is perfect for a night of mystery and betrayal with friends.

5. Minecraft

Minecraft’s multiplayer mode allows you and your friends to explore, build, and survive together in a blocky, procedurally-generated world. Whether you’re constructing elaborate structures, embarking on epic adventures, or simply hanging out in your virtual world, Minecraft offers endless possibilities for cooperative play.

6. Call of Duty: Warzone

As a free-to-play battle royale game, Call of Duty: Warzone lets you and your friends drop into a massive map and fight to be the last squad standing. With its realistic gunplay and intense firefights, Warzone provides an exhilarating multiplayer experience for fans of first-person shooters.

7. Jackbox Party Pack

For a change of pace, the Jackbox Party Pack offers a collection of hilarious and unconventional party games that can be played using smartphones as controllers. From trivia quizzes to drawing challenges, Jackbox games are perfect for lighthearted gatherings and social events with friends.

8. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a crossover fighting game featuring iconic characters from various gaming franchises. Gather your friends for epic battles on dynamic stages, and unleash powerful attacks to send your opponents flying. With its easy-to-pick-up gameplay and depth of strategy, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a multiplayer gem.

9. Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale game set in the Titanfall universe. Form a squad with your friends and select from a roster of unique legends, each with their own abilities, as you compete against other teams in a fast-paced, action-packed battle royale experience.

10. FIFA 20

For fans of soccer, FIFA 20 offers an immersive multiplayer experience with its competitive online modes. Whether you’re playing head-to-head matches or teaming up in co-op seasons, FIFA 20 provides a realistic and thrilling soccer experience that’s best enjoyed with friends.

There you have it – our top 10 multiplayer games to play with friends. From intense shooters to lighthearted party games, these titles are sure to bring endless fun and excitement to your gaming sessions with friends. So gather your crew, fire up your gaming platform of choice, and get ready for some unforgettable multiplayer moments. Happy gaming!
