Stay Active: Hotels with the Best Fitness Facilities


When it comes to travel, maintaining an active lifestyle can be a challenge. But fear not, fitness enthusiasts! There are hotels around the world that not only provide luxurious accommodations but also boast top-notch fitness facilities. So, whether you’re a gym junkie, a yoga lover, or simply someone who wants to stay active while on vacation, these hotels have got you covered. Join me as we explore some of the best hotels with fitness facilities that will make your stay both comfortable and invigorating.

1. The Westin Workout, The Westin New York Grand Central - New York City, USA
Located in the heart of Manhattan, The Westin New York Grand Central offers a fitness facility that will impress even the most dedicated fitness enthusiasts. The Westin Workout provides state-of-the-art equipment, including cardio machines, free weights, and strength training equipment. Additionally, they offer fitness classes such as yoga and Pilates, ensuring there’s something for everyone. After an intense workout session, you can relax in their sauna or indulge in a rejuvenating massage at their spa.

2. The Ritz-Carlton Spa & Fitness Center - Kapalua, Maui, Hawaii, USA
If you’re looking for a tropical getaway with a focus on fitness, The Ritz-Carlton Kapalua is the perfect destination. Their expansive fitness center offers panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, creating a serene and inspiring environment. You’ll find a variety of equipment, from treadmills and ellipticals to weight machines and a dedicated yoga studio. The hotel also offers fitness classes and personalized training sessions for those seeking extra guidance. After your workout, take a dip in their stunning outdoor pool or unwind with a massage at their luxurious spa.

3. The Peninsula Spa - Bangkok, Thailand
Combining luxury with fitness, The Peninsula Spa in Bangkok offers a holistic approach to wellness. Their fitness center features state-of-the-art equipment, including Technogym machines and a dedicated Pilates studio. The hotel also offers a range of fitness classes, from high-intensity interval training to calming yoga sessions. For a unique experience, try their Muay Thai boxing classes, led by experienced trainers. After your workout, indulge in their spa treatments, including traditional Thai massages and rejuvenating facials.

4. The Conrad Spa & Fitness Center - Tokyo, Japan
The Conrad Tokyo is not only known for its luxurious accommodations but also for its exceptional fitness facilities. Their fitness center provides a wide range of equipment, including cardio machines, weight training equipment, and a dedicated stretching area. The hotel also offers fitness classes, such as Zumba and aqua aerobics, for those looking for a fun and energetic workout. After your exercise routine, relax in their sauna or enjoy a dip in their indoor pool, offering stunning views of Tokyo’s skyline.

5. The St. Regis Athletic Club - Aspen, Colorado, USA
Nestled in the scenic mountains of Aspen, The St. Regis Athletic Club is a fitness enthusiast’s dream. With a focus on outdoor activities, the club offers a variety of fitness options, including hiking, biking, and skiing. Inside their fitness center, you’ll find a range of equipment, from cardio machines to free weights. They also offer yoga and Pilates classes for those seeking a mindful workout. After your exercise session, unwind in their outdoor hot tub or indulge in a soothing massage at their spa.

These hotels go above and beyond to cater to fitness enthusiasts, ensuring that you can maintain your active lifestyle while enjoying a luxurious vacation. So, the next time you plan a trip, consider staying at one of these hotels and make fitness a part of your travel experience. After all, there’s no better feeling than staying active and rejuvenated while exploring the world.
