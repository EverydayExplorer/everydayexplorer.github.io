The Best Hunting GPS Devices for Navigation


When it comes to hunting, having the right equipment can make all the difference. One essential tool that every hunter should consider investing in is a hunting GPS device. These devices are designed to help hunters navigate through unfamiliar terrain, mark important locations, and track their movements. With so many options available on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best hunting GPS device. In this blog post, we will explore some of the top hunting GPS devices that can enhance your hunting experience.

1. Garmin GPSMAP 64st:
The Garmin GPSMAP 64st is a top-of-the-line hunting GPS device that offers a wide range of features. With its high-sensitivity GPS and GLONASS receiver, it provides accurate positioning even in dense forests or deep canyons. The device comes preloaded with TOPO U.S. 100K maps, which include detailed maps of terrain, waterways, and various points of interest. It also features a 2.6-inch color display that is easy to read in any lighting condition. Additionally, the GPSMAP 64st allows you to share your waypoints, tracks, and routes wirelessly with other compatible Garmin devices.

2. Magellan eXplorist 710:
The Magellan eXplorist 710 is another excellent hunting GPS device that offers advanced navigation capabilities. It features a 3-inch color touch screen that allows for easy and intuitive operation. The device comes preloaded with detailed topographic maps, allowing you to navigate through various terrains effortlessly. The eXplorist 710 also includes a built-in 3.2-megapixel camera, which allows you to capture high-quality photos of your hunting adventures. Additionally, it is equipped with a barometric altimeter and a three-axis electronic compass, providing accurate elevation and direction information.

3. Bushnell BackTrack D-TOUR:
If you’re looking for a compact and lightweight hunting GPS device, the Bushnell BackTrack D-TOUR is a great option. While it may not have all the advanced features of other devices, it excels in its simplicity and ease of use. The BackTrack D-TOUR allows you to mark up to five locations and easily navigate back to them using its intuitive interface. It also includes a built-in digital compass and displays essential information such as latitude, longitude, altitude, and temperature. Additionally, the device can store up to 48 hours of trip data, allowing you to review your hunting routes later.

4. Garmin Oregon 650t:
The Garmin Oregon 650t is a versatile hunting GPS device that offers a wide range of features. It features a 3-inch sunlight-readable touch screen display, making it easy to view maps and navigate through various terrains. The device comes preloaded with TOPO U.S. 100K maps and includes an 8-megapixel autofocus camera for capturing high-quality photos. The Oregon 650t also incorporates a barometric altimeter and a three-axis compass, providing accurate elevation and direction information. Additionally, it is compatible with Garmin’s BaseCamp software, allowing you to plan and organize your hunting trips in advance.

In conclusion, investing in a hunting GPS device can greatly enhance your hunting experience by providing accurate navigation, marking important locations, and tracking your movements. The Garmin GPSMAP 64st, Magellan eXplorist 710, Bushnell BackTrack D-TOUR, and Garmin Oregon 650t are all top-notch devices that offer unique features to suit different hunting needs. Whether you prioritize advanced navigation capabilities, simplicity, or versatility, there is a hunting GPS device out there that will help you stay on track and make the most of your hunting adventures. So, gear up with one of these devices and take your hunting game to the next level!
