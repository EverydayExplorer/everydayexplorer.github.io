Hotels with Scenic Golf Courses: Tee Off in Style


Are you tired of the same old hotel experience? Looking for a vacation that combines luxury accommodation with breathtaking views and a chance to pursue your passion for golf? Look no further! In this blog post, we will take you on a journey through some of the world’s most stunning hotels with scenic golf courses. Get ready to tee off in style and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature while enjoying a challenging game of golf.

1. Pebble Beach Resorts, California, USA:
Nestled on the rugged coastline of California’s Monterey Peninsula, Pebble Beach Resorts offers a golf experience like no other. With five championship courses, including the iconic Pebble Beach Golf Links, golf enthusiasts can enjoy stunning ocean views as they navigate the challenging fairways. The resort’s luxurious accommodations and world-class amenities make it the perfect destination for golfers and non-golfers alike.

2. St. Andrews Links, Scotland:
No list of scenic golf courses would be complete without mentioning St. Andrews Links in Scotland. Known as the Home of Golf, this historic destination boasts seven courses, with the Old Course being the most famous. Imagine playing on the same hallowed grounds where legends such as Jack Nicklaus and Tiger Woods have competed. The surrounding Scottish countryside provides a picturesque backdrop, making it a dream destination for any golfer.

3. Cape Kidnappers, New Zealand:
Perched on cliffs high above the Pacific Ocean, Cape Kidnappers in New Zealand offers a golfing experience that is both challenging and visually stunning. Designed by legendary golf architect Tom Doak, the course features rolling fairways, dramatic cliffs, and panoramic ocean views. After a round of golf, retreat to the luxurious lodge and indulge in world-class cuisine while taking in the breathtaking surroundings.

4. Casa de Campo, Dominican Republic:
Escape to the Caribbean paradise of Casa de Campo in the Dominican Republic. This luxurious resort boasts three championship golf courses, including the world-renowned Teeth of the Dog course. With its stunning coastline, lush green fairways, and tropical landscapes, golfers can enjoy a unique and challenging experience. After a day on the links, relax on the pristine beaches or explore the vibrant local culture.

5. Fancourt, South Africa:
Situated in the heart of South Africa’s Garden Route, Fancourt offers a golfing experience surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. The estate features three world-class courses, including the highly acclaimed Links course designed by Gary Player. With its rolling hills, indigenous flora, and views of the Outeniqua Mountains, Fancourt is a golfer’s paradise. After a round, unwind at the spa or indulge in gourmet dining at one of the resort’s renowned restaurants.

These are just a few examples of hotels with scenic golf courses that offer a unique blend of luxury, natural beauty, and world-class golfing. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or just starting out, these destinations will leave you in awe of their stunning landscapes and unforgettable golfing experiences. So pack your clubs, book your stay, and get ready to tee off in style at one of these incredible golf resorts.
