Creating the Perfect Winged Eyeliner Look


One of the most iconic makeup looks of all time is the winged eyeliner. It has the power to instantly transform any eye shape and add a touch of glamour to any makeup look. But let’s be honest, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner can be quite tricky. One wrong move and you could end up with a lopsided or smudged line. However, with the right techniques and a bit of practice, anyone can master the art of winged eyeliner. In this blog post, we will discuss step-by-step how to create the perfect winged eyeliner look.

Before we dive into the process, it’s important to understand that winged eyeliner is not a ‘one size fits all’ kind of technique. The shape of your eye and the look you want to achieve will determine the style of winged liner that suits you best. Whether you prefer a subtle flick or a dramatic wing, these steps will help you achieve a flawless look.

Step 1: Prep Your Eyelids

Start by prepping your eyelids. Remove any residue or oil with a gentle eye makeup remover or micellar water. Then, apply an eyeshadow primer to ensure that your eyeliner stays in place and lasts throughout the day. This step is essential for preventing smudging or fading.

Step 2: Choose Your Eyeliner

There are various types of eyeliners available in the market, including pencil, gel, and liquid. Each has its own advantages, so choose the one that you feel most comfortable working with. Liquid eyeliners tend to give a more precise and intense look, while pencils are great for beginners as they offer more control.

Step 3: Start with a Sketch

Instead of going straight for the wing, start by drawing a thin line as close to your lash line as possible. This will serve as the base for your wing. Use small, light strokes to achieve a smooth and even line. If you make any mistakes, don’t worry! A cotton swab dipped in makeup remover can easily fix any mishaps.

Step 4: Create the Wing

To create the wing, imagine an invisible line extending from the outer corner of your eye towards the end of your eyebrow. Use a small brush or a piece of tape as a guide to draw the wing. The length and angle of the wing can be adjusted based on personal preference and eye shape. Remember to keep the wing subtle for a more natural look or extend it for a more dramatic effect.

Step 5: Connect the Dots

Once you have drawn the wing, it’s time to connect it to the base line. Start from the outer corner and gradually taper the line towards the inner corner of your eye. The thickness of the line can be adjusted as per your preference. Take your time and keep the strokes as steady as possible.

Step 6: Clean Up and Touch Up

After creating the wing and connecting the lines, it’s time to clean up any smudges or unevenness. Use a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover or a concealer brush with some concealer to clean up any mistakes and sharpen the lines. This step will give your winged eyeliner a more polished and professional look.

Step 7: Finishing Touches

To complete the winged eyeliner look, curl your lashes and apply mascara to add volume and definition. You can also apply eyeshadow on your lids to enhance the overall look. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t be discouraged if you don’t ace it on your first try. With time and patience, you will master the art of winged eyeliner.

In conclusion, creating the perfect winged eyeliner look requires practice, patience, and the right techniques. By following these step-by-step instructions, you’ll be well on your way to achieving flawless and beautiful winged eyeliner. Remember to experiment with different styles and angles to find the look that suits you best. So go ahead, grab your eyeliner, and get ready to rock that winged eyeliner look like a pro!
