The Top 10 Underrated Cartoons You Need to Watch


When it comes to cartoons, we often think of the popular ones that have gained widespread recognition. However, there are many hidden gems out there that often go unnoticed. These underrated cartoons have their own unique charm and deserve to be on your watchlist. In this blog post, we will explore the top 10 underrated cartoons that you need to watch.

1. ‘Gravity Falls’ - This animated series follows the adventures of twins Dipper and Mabel as they uncover mysteries in the small town of Gravity Falls. With its intriguing storyline, witty humor, and well-developed characters, this show offers a perfect blend of mystery and comedy.

2. ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender’ - Despite being critically acclaimed, ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender’ doesn’t always get the recognition it deserves. Set in a fascinating world of elemental bending, this show is a perfect blend of action, adventure, and mythology. With its complex characters and thought-provoking themes, it is a must-watch for both children and adults.

3. ‘The Legend of Korra’ - A sequel to ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender,’ ‘The Legend of Korra’ continues the story in a new era. This show explores themes of identity, spirituality, and political turmoil in a mature and engaging manner. With stunning animation and incredible character development, it is a worthy addition to the ‘Avatar’ universe.

4. ‘Over the Garden Wall’ - This miniseries is a beautiful blend of dream-like storytelling and dark fairytale elements. Following two lost brothers as they journey through a mysterious forest, ‘Over the Garden Wall’ is a visually stunning and emotionally captivating adventure that will leave you wanting more.

5. ‘Steven Universe’ - With its inclusive themes of love, friendship, and identity, ‘Steven Universe’ has garnered a dedicated fan base over the years. This animated series tells the story of a young boy named Steven and his magical adventures with the Crystal Gems. It tackles important social issues through its diverse and well-developed characters.

6. ‘The Adventure Time’ - Filled with bizarre and imaginative concepts, ‘Adventure Time’ is a surreal and whimsical cartoon that has gained a cult following over the years. It chronicles the adventures of Finn, Jake, and their friends in the Land of Ooo. This show is known for its quirky sense of humor and deep emotional moments.

7. ‘Star vs. The Forces of Evil’ - Combining elements of fantasy, comedy, and coming-of-age themes, ‘Star vs. The Forces of Evil’ is a delightful animated series. It follows the adventures of a young princess named Star Butterfly as she navigates life on Earth while battling supernatural forces. With its vibrant animation style and endearing characters, it is a hidden gem worth discovering.

8. ‘Young Justice’ - While not as widely known as other superhero cartoons, ‘Young Justice’ stands out for its mature storytelling and complex character arcs. This series follows a group of teenage superheroes as they navigate their personal lives and fight against supervillains. With its intricate plotlines and character development, it appeals to both comic book fans and casual viewers.

9. ‘The Spectacular Spider-Man’ - Often overshadowed by other Spider-Man adaptations, ‘The Spectacular Spider-Man’ is a beautifully animated series that captures the essence of the iconic superhero. With its engaging storylines and well-executed action sequences, it is a must-watch for any Spider-Man fan.

10. ‘The Penguins of Madagascar’ - Based on the popular movie franchise, ‘The Penguins of Madagascar’ is an underrated gem within the animated series landscape. It follows the misadventures of four penguins as they carry out covert missions while trying to maintain their cover at the New York City Zoo. With its fast-paced humor and lovable characters, it provides an enjoyable viewing experience for all ages.

In conclusion, these underrated cartoons offer a refreshing change from the mainstream options. From gripping storylines and well-developed characters to stunning animation and unforgettable moments, they have all the elements that make a cartoon worth watching. So, if you’re looking for something new to binge-watch, give these hidden gems a try. You won’t be disappointed!
