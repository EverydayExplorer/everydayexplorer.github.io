Wholesome and Delicious: Homemade Granola Recipes


Granola is a versatile and nutritious snack that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Packed with wholesome ingredients like oats, nuts, and seeds, homemade granola is a great way to fuel your body with energy and satisfy your taste buds. In this blog post, we will explore some delicious and easy-to-make homemade granola recipes that are sure to become a staple in your kitchen.

Classic Crunchy Granola

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans)
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup dried fruits (raisins, cranberries, apricots)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, mixed nuts, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the honey and coconut oil over low heat until melted. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the honey mixture over the dry ingredients and mix well, ensuring all the oats and nuts are coated.
  5. Spread the mixture evenly onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown, stirring halfway through.
  6. Allow the granola to cool completely on the baking sheet. Once cooled, stir in the dried fruits.
  7. Store in an airtight container for up to two weeks.

Chocolate Peanut Butter Granola Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup puffed rice cereal
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Line a square baking dish with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, puffed rice cereal, chopped peanuts, and salt.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the peanut butter and honey over low heat until melted and well combined.
  4. Pour the peanut butter mixture over the dry ingredients and mix well, ensuring everything is evenly coated.
  5. Fold in the dark chocolate chips.
  6. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and press it down firmly using the back of a spoon.
  7. Place the dish in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to allow the bars to set.
  8. Once set, remove from the refrigerator and cut into desired-sized bars.
  9. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Tropical Paradise Granola

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup dried pineapple, chopped
  • 1/2 cup dried mango, chopped
  • 1/2 cup macadamia nuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, shredded coconut, dried pineapple, dried mango, and macadamia nuts.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the coconut oil and honey over low heat until melted. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Pour the coconut oil mixture over the dry ingredients and mix well, ensuring everything is coated.
  5. Spread the mixture evenly onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown, stirring halfway through.
  6. Allow the granola to cool completely on the baking sheet.
  7. Once cooled, transfer to an airtight container and store for up to two weeks.

Conclusion

Homemade granola is a simple and delicious way to add some wholesome goodness to your day. With these recipes, you can customize your granola to suit your taste preferences and dietary needs. Whether you prefer a classic crunchy granola, a chocolate peanut butter treat, or a tropical paradise blend, homemade granola is sure to become a favorite in your household. So go ahead, grab your ingredients, and get creative in the kitchen!
