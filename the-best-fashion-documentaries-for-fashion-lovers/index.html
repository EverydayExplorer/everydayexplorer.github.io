The Best Fashion Documentaries for Fashion Lovers


Fashion documentaries have the power to transport us into the captivating world of haute couture and reveal the inner workings of the fashion industry. They offer a glimpse into the creative processes, the trials and tribulations, and the larger than life personalities that make fashion what it is today. Whether you are a fashion enthusiast, a budding designer, or simply someone who appreciates the artistry behind fashion, these documentaries are sure to captivate your attention. In this blog post, we will explore the best fashion documentaries that are a must-watch for all fashion lovers.

1. ‘The September Issue’ (2009)
Shot over a period of nine months, ‘The September Issue’ takes us behind the scenes of legendary Vogue editor-in-chief Anna Wintour’s preparations for the 2007 September issue – the most important issue of the year. This documentary offers a fascinating glimpse into the high-pressure world of fashion, showcasing the creative process, interpersonal dynamics, and the role of fashion magazines in shaping trends.

2. ‘Dior and I’ (2014)
Directed by Frédéric Tcheng, ‘Dior and I’ offers an intimate look at the making of Raf Simons’ debut haute couture collection for the iconic house of Christian Dior. This documentary showcases the creative process behind the scenes, capturing the emotional journey and the meticulous craftsmanship that goes into creating a couture collection. It also explores the rich history of the brand and the pressure to live up to the legacy of the house.

3. ‘The Eye has to Travel’ (2011)
Directed by Lisa Immordino Vreeland, ‘The Eye has to Travel’ is a documentary that pays tribute to the legendary fashion editor Diana Vreeland. With a career spanning over five decades, Vreeland was known for her bold approach to fashion and her ability to spot trends before they even emerged. This film provides an in-depth look at her life and career, showcasing her influence on the fashion industry and her unique perspective on style.

4. ‘McQueen’ (2018)
Directed by Ian Bonhôte and Peter Ettedgui, ‘McQueen’ tells the powerful and haunting story of the late British fashion designer Alexander McQueen. This documentary delves into his extraordinary talent, his unconventional vision, and the dark side of fame. It offers a deep exploration of McQueen’s creative process and the emotional journey he went through to create his provocative and boundary-pushing collections.

5. ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ (2010)
Directed by Richard Press, ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ pays homage to the late Bill Cunningham, the beloved street-style photographer for The New York Times. This documentary offers a glimpse into the life and work of an icon in the fashion industry, showcasing his dedication, passion for fashion, and his ability to capture the essence of street style in New York City.

6. ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ (2008)
Directed by Matt Tyrnauer, ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ follows the legendary fashion designer Valentino Garavani during the final two years of his career. This documentary captures the opulence and glamour of Valentino’s world, as well as the meticulous attention to detail that goes into creating his iconic designs. It offers a rare behind-the-scenes look at the fashion industry and the unique relationship between Valentino and his business partner, Giancarlo Giammetti.

7. ‘The Gospel According to André’ (2017)
Directed by Kate Novack, ‘The Gospel According to André’ celebrates the life and career of fashion journalist and former Vogue editor André Leon Talley. This film explores Talley’s journey from growing up in the segregated South to becoming one of fashion’s most influential figures. It delves into his personal life, his contributions to the fashion industry, and his unwavering commitment to diversity and inclusion.

These fashion documentaries offer a fascinating and immersive insight into the world of fashion. They showcase the dedication, creativity, and hard work that goes into creating iconic designs and shaping the fashion industry. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast, a budding designer, or simply someone who appreciates the beauty of fashion, these documentaries are a must-watch. So, grab some popcorn, settle into your favorite armchair, and prepare to be inspired by the incredible talent and passion showcased in these films.
