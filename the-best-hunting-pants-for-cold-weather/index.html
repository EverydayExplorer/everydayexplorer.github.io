The Best Hunting Pants for Cold Weather


When it comes to hunting in cold weather, having the right gear is essential. And one crucial piece of equipment that often gets overlooked is a good pair of hunting pants. The right hunting pants can make all the difference in keeping you warm, comfortable, and focused on the hunt. In this blog post, we’ll explore the best hunting pants for cold weather, highlighting their features, benefits, and why they should be a part of your hunting gear arsenal.

1. Sitka Gear Men’s Kelvin Lite Pant:
The Sitka Gear Men’s Kelvin Lite Pant is a top-of-the-line option for hunters who need lightweight, yet warm pants for cold weather. Made with a durable polyester shell and insulated with PrimaLoft® Silver Hi-Loft insulation, these pants provide exceptional warmth without adding bulk. The Kelvin Lite Pant also features a water-repellent finish, ensuring you stay dry even in wet conditions. With an articulated fit and functional pockets, these pants offer both comfort and practicality.

2. First Lite Men’s Uncompahgre Puffy Pants:
If extreme cold is in the forecast, the First Lite Men’s Uncompahgre Puffy Pants are a game-changer. These pants are insulated with 37.5™ Active Particle Technology, which traps heat and wicks away moisture, keeping you warm and dry throughout your hunt. The Uncompahgre Puffy Pants are also designed with a DWR finish to repel water and a reinforced seat and knees for added durability. With a full-length zippered leg, these pants can be easily worn over other layers, making them versatile for varying weather conditions.

3. Kryptek Men’s Kratos II Insulated Pants:
The Kryptek Men’s Kratos II Insulated Pants are another excellent option for hunters braving the cold. These pants are designed with a lightweight and durable polyester shell and filled with PrimaLoft® Gold insulation, providing optimal warmth without sacrificing mobility. The Kratos II Insulated Pants also feature a DWR finish and a reinforced seat and knees for added durability. With a fully gusseted crotch and articulated knees, these pants allow for unrestricted movement, making them perfect for active hunts.

4. Under Armour Men’s ColdGear Infrared Scent Control Pants:
For hunters looking for a versatile option that combines warmth, comfort, and scent control, the Under Armour Men’s ColdGear Infrared Scent Control Pants are a great choice. These pants feature ColdGear Infrared technology, which uses a soft, thermo-conductive inner coating to absorb and retain body heat, keeping you warm in even the coldest conditions. The Scent Control technology helps to prevent odors from giving away your presence to game animals. Made with a blend of polyester and elastane, these pants offer a comfortable fit and excellent mobility.

In conclusion, investing in the right hunting pants for cold weather is crucial for a successful and comfortable hunting experience. The Sitka Gear Men’s Kelvin Lite Pant, First Lite Men’s Uncompahgre Puffy Pants, Kryptek Men’s Kratos II Insulated Pants, and Under Armour Men’s ColdGear Infrared Scent Control Pants are all excellent options that provide warmth, durability, and functionality. Choose the pants that best suit your needs and enjoy your hunting adventures, even in the coldest conditions. Stay warm, stay focused, and happy hunting!
