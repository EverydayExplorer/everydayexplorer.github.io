Hidden Gems: Hotels Off the Beaten Path


Have you ever wanted to escape the hustle and bustle of popular tourist destinations and discover a hidden gem? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we will be exploring some of the best-kept secrets in the world of hotels – those tucked away off the beaten path. These hidden gems offer unique experiences, breathtaking surroundings, and a chance to truly immerse yourself in the local culture. So, pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable adventure!

1. Casa de la Montaña, Costa Rica
Nestled in the heart of the Costa Rican rainforest, Casa de la Montaña is a tranquil oasis that will transport you to another world. This eco-friendly hotel offers luxurious accommodations, complete with private balconies that offer stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Wake up to the sound of howler monkeys and enjoy a refreshing dip in the hotel’s natural spring-fed pool. With its commitment to sustainability and its close proximity to national parks and wildlife reserves, Casa de la Montaña is the perfect choice for nature lovers.

2. Riad El Fenn, Morocco
Located in the vibrant city of Marrakech, Riad El Fenn is a hidden gem that combines traditional Moroccan architecture with modern luxury. This boutique hotel boasts beautifully designed rooms and suites, each with its own unique style and charm. Take a stroll through the hotel’s lush gardens, relax by the rooftop pool, or indulge in a traditional hammam spa treatment. With its central location and impeccable service, Riad El Fenn is the ideal base for exploring the enchanting streets of Marrakech.

3. Treehotel, Sweden
If you’ve ever dreamed of spending a night among the treetops, then Treehotel in Sweden is the place for you. This extraordinary hotel offers a collection of treehouses, each designed by a different architect, blending seamlessly with the surrounding forest. From the futuristic Mirrorcube to the whimsical Bird’s Nest, each treehouse provides a unique and unforgettable experience. Enjoy panoramic views of the Swedish Lapland, go hiking, or embark on a dog sledding adventure. Treehotel is a true hidden gem that will leave you feeling closer to nature.

4. Azulik, Mexico
Tucked away on the pristine shores of Tulum, Mexico, Azulik is an eco-friendly resort that offers a one-of-a-kind experience. This adults-only retreat features stunning treehouse-style villas, built entirely from local materials and designed to blend harmoniously with the natural surroundings. Unplug and unwind in the hotel’s private plunge pools, indulge in a spa treatment overlooking the turquoise waters, or simply relax on the white sandy beach. With its emphasis on sustainability and its breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea, Azulik is a hidden paradise for those seeking tranquility and seclusion.

5. Fogo Island Inn, Canada
Perched on the rugged shores of Fogo Island, off the coast of Newfoundland, Fogo Island Inn is a contemporary masterpiece that celebrates the island’s rich cultural heritage. This award-winning hotel features luxurious suites with floor-to-ceiling windows that offer breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean. Immerse yourself in the local community, explore the island’s stunning landscapes, and indulge in the hotel’s farm-to-table cuisine. Fogo Island Inn is a hidden gem that combines modern design with a deep respect for nature and the surrounding community.

These hidden gems offer a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers seeking something off the beaten path. Whether you’re looking for a secluded eco-lodge in the rainforest, a luxurious riad in the heart of a bustling city, or a treehouse escape in the Swedish wilderness, these hotels will not disappoint. So, next time you’re planning a trip, consider stepping away from the well-trodden tourist paths and discover a hidden gem that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.
