Funny Books That Will Make You Laugh Out Loud


When it comes to finding a good book to read, it’s hard to beat the laughter-inducing power of a funny book. Whether you’re in the mood for some light-hearted humor or just want to escape into a world of laughter, funny books can provide the perfect escape. In this blog post, we’ll be exploring some hilarious books that are guaranteed to make you laugh out loud.

1. ‘Bridget Jones’s Diary’ by Helen Fielding: This iconic book follows the humorous misadventures of Bridget Jones, a single woman in her 30s who is desperately searching for love. With its relatable characters and witty writing style, this book is sure to have you laughing from start to finish.

2. ‘The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy’ by Douglas Adams: This sci-fi comedy novel is a must-read for fans of both genres. It follows the story of Arthur Dent, a hapless human who is swept off Earth just before its destruction to make way for a galactic freeway. The book is filled with clever wordplay and absurd situations that will have you chuckling throughout.

3. ‘Bossypants’ by Tina Fey: If you enjoy comedic memoirs, then ‘Bossypants’ is a must-read. Written by the talented Tina Fey, this book offers a hilarious look into her life and career in comedy. Fey’s wit and self-deprecating humor shine through as she shares anecdotes and insights that are both funny and relatable.

4. ‘Me Talk Pretty One Day’ by David Sedaris: David Sedaris is known for his sharp wit and darkly humorous essays, and ‘Me Talk Pretty One Day’ is no exception. In this collection of comedic essays, Sedaris takes readers on a hilarious journey, recounting his experiences as an American living in France. From language barriers to cultural differences, Sedaris finds humor in the everyday struggles of life.

5. ‘The Princess Bride’ by William Goldman: While it’s perhaps more well-known as a beloved film, ‘The Princess Bride’ also happens to be a fantastically funny book. With its quirky characters, witty dialogue, and satirical take on classic fairy tales, this book is a true comedic gem.

6. ‘Catch-22’ by Joseph Heller: This satirical novel takes a darkly comedic look at the absurdity of war. Set during World War II, the book follows the story of American bombardier Yossarian as he tries to navigate the illogical rules and bureaucracy of the military. ‘Catch-22’ is filled with clever wordplay, humor, and an ironic twist that will leave you laughing and contemplating the nature of war at the same time.

7. ‘Good Omens’ by Terry Pratchett and Neil Gaiman: This collaborative work between two renowned authors is a hilarious take on the end of the world. With its blend of fantasy, satire, and humor, ‘Good Omens’ tells the story of an angel and a demon who team up to prevent the apocalypse. The book is filled with witty banter, eccentric characters, and laugh-out-loud moments.

8. ‘Where’d You Go, Bernadette’ by Maria Semple: This comedic mystery novel tells the story of Bernadette Fox, a once-successful architect who goes missing just before a family trip to Antarctica. The book is told through a series of emails, documents, and narratives, which adds to the humor and intrigue. With its engaging storyline and quirky characters, ‘Where’d You Go, Bernadette’ is sure to keep you laughing and guessing until the very end.

9. ‘Big Little Lies’ by Liane Moriarty: While primarily known as a suspenseful drama, ‘Big Little Lies’ also has its fair share of humorous moments. Set in an affluent beachside town, the book explores the lives of three women and the secrets that bind them. Moriarty’s clever writing and sharp wit make for an entertaining read that balances humor with more serious themes.

10. ‘The Rosie Project’ by Graeme Simsion: This delightful romantic comedy follows the story of Don Tillman, a socially awkward genetics professor who embarks on a quest to find the perfect wife. Don’s offbeat personality and humorous observations make for a charming and laugh-out-loud funny read.

Whether you’re in the mood for a light-hearted comedy or a satirical take on life, these funny books are sure to make you laugh out loud. With their clever writing, relatable characters, and hilarious situations, these books offer the perfect escape from the everyday grind. So grab a cup of tea, settle into a cozy chair, and get ready to be entertained by these fantastic books that are guaranteed to leave you in stitches.
