Vegetarian BBQ: Exciting Recipes for Plant-Based Grilling


Summer is just around the corner, and that means it’s time to break out the grill! But what if you’re a vegetarian? No worries! There are plenty of exciting and delicious plant-based options for grilling. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best vegetarian BBQ recipes out there to help you create a plant-based feast to remember.

1. Grilled Portobello Mushroom Burgers

Portobello mushrooms are the perfect meaty substitute for burgers. All you need to do is brush them with a little olive oil, garlic, and your favorite seasoning (we like smoked paprika and black pepper), and then grill for a few minutes on each side. Serve them on a bun with all your favorite toppings.

2. Grilled Veggie Kebabs

Kebabs are a classic BBQ dish, and they can easily be made vegetarian. Simply skewer your favorite veggies (such as bell peppers, onions, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes) and brush them with a little olive oil and your favorite seasoning. Grill for a few minutes on each side until they’re golden brown and tender.

3. Grilled Corn on the Cob

Corn on the cob is a BBQ staple, and it’s naturally vegetarian. Simply soak your corn in water for 15-30 minutes, then brush with a little olive oil and seasoning (we like chili powder and lime juice). Grill for a few minutes on each side until it’s charred and tender.

4. Grilled Tofu Skewers

Tofu is a great source of protein for vegetarians, and it’s perfect for grilling. Simply cube your tofu, skewer it with your favorite veggies (we like bell peppers and onions), and brush with a little olive oil and your favorite seasoning. Grill for a few minutes on each side until it’s golden brown and crispy.

5. Grilled Pineapple

Grilled fruit is a delicious way to end your BBQ meal, and pineapple is one of our favorites. Simply slice your pineapple into rounds and grill for a few minutes on each side until it’s caramelized and tender. Serve with a scoop of ice cream or whipped cream for a sweet ending to your meal.

There are plenty of vegetarian options to choose from when it comes to BBQ. Whether you’re grilling up portobello mushroom burgers, veggie kebabs, or tofu skewers, there’s something for everyone. So fire up your grill and get ready to enjoy some delicious plant-based BBQ this summer!
