The Ultimate Guide to Achieving the Perfect Cat Eye


If you’re a makeup enthusiast or someone who loves experimenting with different eye makeup looks, then you’ve definitely come across the iconic cat eye. This timeless and classic eye makeup technique adds a touch of elegance and allure to any look, making it a go-to choice for many. But achieving the perfect cat eye can be quite the challenge, especially if you’re a beginner. Don’t worry, though! In this ultimate guide, we’ll walk you through step-by-step on how to master the art of the cat eye, ensuring flawless results every time.

Step 1: Prep and Prime
Before diving into the actual cat eye application, it’s crucial to prep your eyelids properly. Start by cleansing your face and moisturizing the eye area. Then, apply a primer specifically designed for the eyelids. This will ensure that your eyeshadow and eyeliner stay in place throughout the day.

Step 2: Choose the Right Tools
To achieve the perfect cat eye, you’ll need the right tools. A fine-tip liquid eyeliner or a gel eyeliner with a thin brush will give you the precision and control you need. Additionally, keep some cotton buds and a makeup remover close by, just in case you make any mistakes along the way.

Step 3: Map Out Your Cat Eye Shape
Before applying the eyeliner, it’s helpful to map out the shape of your cat eye. Take a pencil eyeliner or an angled brush and hold it against the outer corner of your eye, aligning it with the edge of your nose. Make a small dot where the tip of the wing should end. This dot will act as your guide as you apply the eyeliner.

Step 4: Start with the Wing
Now, it’s time to create the wing. Start from the dot you made in the previous step and draw a diagonal line towards the outer corner of your eye. The length and angle of the wing are up to you, depending on the desired intensity of the cat eye. Once you’ve drawn the initial line, connect it to your upper lash line, creating a triangle shape. Fill in the triangle with the eyeliner, ensuring a smooth and even application.

Step 5: Line the Upper Lash Line
With the wing in place, it’s time to line your upper lash line. Starting from the inner corner of your eye, draw a thin line along your lashes, gradually thickening it as you move towards the outer corner. Ensure that the line connects seamlessly with the wing you created earlier.

Step 6: Fill in the Gaps
After lining your upper lash line, check for any gaps or uneven areas. Use the eyeliner to fill in these gaps, ensuring a consistent and bold cat eye look. Take your time and make small adjustments as needed.

Step 7: Perfect the Flick
To add a touch of finesse to your cat eye, perfect the flick at the inner corner of your eye. Extend the eyeliner slightly towards your tear duct, creating a delicate and elongated flick. This will give your cat eye a polished and professional finish.

Step 8: Clean Up and Set
Lastly, clean up any smudges or mistakes using a cotton bud dipped in makeup remover. Once you’re satisfied with the result, set your cat eye with a setting spray or a translucent powder to ensure longevity.

Now that you’ve mastered the art of the cat eye, it’s time to rock this timeless look with confidence. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t get discouraged if it takes a few tries to achieve your desired cat eye shape. With patience and perseverance, you’ll soon be able to create flawless cat eyes effortlessly. So go ahead, embrace your inner feline and let your eyes do the talking!
