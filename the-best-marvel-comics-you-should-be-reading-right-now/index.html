The Best Marvel Comics You Should be Reading Right Now


Marvel comics have always been a hot topic among the die-hard fans of graphic novels and comics. The stories they tell have captured the imagination of people all over the world with their incredible superhero line-ups and stunning artwork. Since the early ’60s when Stan Lee and Jack Kirby first created the Marvel Universe, we have been blessed with a plethora of unforgettable characters, story arcs, and groundbreaking moments. There’s no doubt in the fact that Marvel has been leading the way in producing some of the best graphic novels and comics of all time. Today, we will take a look at some of the best Marvel Comics that you should be reading right now.

1. Immortal Hulk:
Immortal Hulk is arguably the best ongoing comic Marvel has been putting out in recent years. This series reintroduces us to Bruce Banner, who, at night, transforms into the monstrous creature known as the Hulk. The writing in this series is top-notch, and the story is both tense and creepy, with artwork that will leave you in awe. This isn’t your average superhero story, but it’s a must-read for anyone who loves horror comics.

2. Thor:
The current Thor series is one of the best comics Marvel is publishing right now. This series features the God of Thunder fighting his way through both cosmic and mythological threats. The writing in this series is phenomenal, with Jason Aaron crafting some of the best Thor stories ever told. This series is a must-read for fans of Norse mythology, as well as fans of epic superhero tales.

3. Daredevil:
Daredevil has always been one of Marvel’s most beloved characters, and the current run is no exception. This series sees Matt Murdock struggling with personal demons while still fighting crime as the vigilante Daredevil. This series is a perfect blend of superhero action and character drama. If you are interested in street-level heroes, this series is an absolute must-read.

4. Spider-Man:
Spider-Man is one of the most popular characters in all of comics, and the current series is a perfect example of why. This series continues the story of Peter Parker as he attempts to balance his personal life with the call of being a superhero. The writing is strong, and the artwork is some of the best in the business. This series is a great jumping-on point for new readers, as well as a must-read for Spidey fans of all ages.

5. Black Panther:
Black Panther has become one of Marvel’s most popular characters thanks to the success of the movie franchise, and the comics are no different. The current Black Panther series sees T’Challa trying to rule his kingdom, Wakanda, while also dealing with threats from within and from outside his borders. The writing is top-notch, and the artwork is breathtaking. If you love political intrigue mixed with high-stakes action, this series is perfect for you.

6. X-Men:
The X-Men have always been one of Marvel’s most popular franchises, and the current series does not disappoint. This series sees the X-Men trying to find a new place in the world after the events of ‘House of X/Powers of X.’ The writing is stellar, and the artwork is some of the best in the business. If you love mutants and superhero team-ups, this series is an absolute must-read.

Overall, Marvel Comics continues to be a powerhouse when it comes to producing some of the best graphic novels and comics on the market today. Whether you’re a longtime fan of the Marvel Universe or a newcomer looking to dive into the world of comic books, the above list of comics is a great starting point to get you hooked on the stories, characters, and artwork that make Marvel so unique and beloved. So, what are you waiting for? Start reading now and immerse yourself in the exciting world of Marvel Comics.
