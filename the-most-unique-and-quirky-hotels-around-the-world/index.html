The Most Unique and Quirky Hotels Around the World


When it comes to planning a vacation, finding unique and quirky accommodation can be a memorable and exciting part of the experience. Imagine staying in a hotel that is as extraordinary as the destination itself, where the design, ambiance, and amenities are unlike anything you’ve ever seen before. From underwater hotels to treehouses and converted prisons, the world is filled with an array of eccentric accommodations that cater to the unconventional traveler. In this blog post, we explore some of the most unique and quirky hotels around the world that are sure to leave a lasting impression.

1. The Manta Resort, Pemba Island, Zanzibar: If you’ve ever dreamt of sleeping underwater while surrounded by marine life, The Manta Resort is the perfect choice. Situated off the coast of Pemba Island, this floating hotel offers an exceptional experience with an underwater bedroom. Immerse yourself in the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean and witness a stunning array of tropical fish and coral reefs from the comfort of your bed.

2. Dog Bark Park Inn, Idaho, USA: To experience true dog lover’s bliss, head to Cottonwood, Idaho, and check into the Dog Bark Park Inn. Shaped like a giant beagle, this bed and breakfast is a tribute to man’s best friend. Guests are welcomed by massive dog sculptures and can sleep inside the cozy belly of the giant beagle. The inn is filled with canine-inspired decor and offers a unique opportunity to embrace your love for dogs in a truly quirky way.

3. Giraffe Manor, Nairobi, Kenya: For wildlife enthusiasts, Giraffe Manor provides an unrivaled experience. This luxurious boutique hotel is set in 12 acres of private land in Nairobi and is home to a herd of resident giraffes. These gentle giants casually roam the grounds and often poke their heads through the windows, allowing guests to feed them right from their breakfast table. Encounters with these beautiful creatures make for unforgettable moments and excellent photo opportunities.

4. Hotel Kakslauttanen, Lapland, Finland: If you dream of witnessing the Northern Lights from the comfort of your own room, Hotel Kakslauttanen is the place to be. Tucked away in the Finnish Lapland, this hotel offers a unique accommodation experience with its glass igloos. Imagine lying in bed, gazing up at the starry night sky, and being mesmerized by the dancing colors of the aurora borealis. It’s a truly magical and awe-inspiring sight.

5. Propeller Island City Lodge, Berlin, Germany: For those seeking a hotel stay that defies convention, Propeller Island City Lodge in Berlin is the answer. This avant-garde hotel is the brainchild of artist Lars Stroschen, who designed each room to have a distinct theme and style. From rooms with slanted floors and hanging beds to upside-down furniture and mirrored ceilings, every corner of this hotel is an artistic masterpiece. Staying here is a surreal experience that blurs the lines between imagination and reality.

6. Free Spirit Spheres, Vancouver Island, Canada: Nestled amongst the tall trees of Vancouver Island’s rainforest, the Free Spirit Spheres offer a truly unique and immersive experience. These suspended spherical treehouses are crafted from wood and give guests the feeling of floating among the treetops. Sleeping in these cozy and compact treehouses is like staying in a fairytale and offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by nature.

7. Malmaison Oxford Castle, Oxford, England: If you’re feeling adventurous and don’t mind spending the night in a converted prison, the Malmaison Oxford Castle is perfect for you. This luxury boutique hotel is built within a former Victorian prison and offers a fascinating mix of historic architecture, modern amenities, and a hint of the building’s previous purpose. The cells have been transformed into stylish guest rooms, and the hotel’s charm lies in its ability to seamlessly blend contemporary comforts with a rich heritage.

8. Icehotel, Jukkasjärvi, Sweden: Situated in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel is the epitome of quirkiness and creativity. This hotel is entirely constructed from ice and snow and is rebuilt every year, creating a completely different experience for each visitor. The Icehotel features rooms with ice sculptures, a bar made entirely of ice, and even a chapel for those seeking a unique wedding venue. Staying in this frozen wonderland is an otherworldly adventure that is sure to leave you in awe.

Whether you’re seeking an underwater escapade, a quirky animal encounter, an artistic haven, or an icy adventure, these unique and quirky hotels offer an unparalleled experience that will ignite your imagination and create memories to last a lifetime. From the depths of the ocean to the tops of trees, these extraordinary accommodations prove that sometimes it’s the journey to your destination that provides the most unforgettable moments. Next time you plan your vacation, dare to be different and consider one of these extraordinary hotels for a one-of-a-kind experience.
