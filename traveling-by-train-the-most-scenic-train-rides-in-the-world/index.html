Traveling by Train: The Most Scenic Train Rides in the World


When it comes to traveling, there is no denying that trains offer a unique and fascinating experience. Unlike other means of transportation, trains offer panoramic and stunning views that are impossible to capture from any other vehicle. Train journeys often take travelers through breathtaking landscapes, from snow-capped mountains to roaring waterfalls, verdant valleys, and rugged coastlines.

So, let’s take a look at some of the most scenic train rides in the world that shouldn’t be missed:

1. The Glacier Express, Switzerland

Switzerland’s Glacier Express is a scenic train ride that takes travelers on a 7.5-hour journey from Zermatt to St. Moritz. The route covers over 170 miles and passes through picturesque mountain landscapes featuring 91 tunnels and 291 bridges. The train ride passes through quaint Swiss villages, glaciers, and stunning alpine scenery, allowing you to see the best of Switzerland.

2. The Rocky Mountaineer, Canada

The Rocky Mountaineer, Canada is a two-day train journey from Vancouver to Calgary. This train ride takes travelers through the Canadian Rockies, passing by steep cliffs, sparkling clear lakes, and dense forests. The journey includes several breathtaking highlights, such as the Spiral Tunnels and the stunning Banff National Park.

3. The Western Highland Line, Scotland

The Western Highland Line in Scotland is one of the best train rides in the UK, offering stunning views of rugged hillside scenery and beautiful lochs. The journey begins in Glasgow and takes travelers to the remote fishing village of Mallaig. The train passes over the Glenfinnan Viaduct, which has become famous for appearing in the Harry Potter movies.

4. The Palace on Wheels, India

The Palace on Wheels is a luxury train ride that takes travelers through Rajasthan, India. This adventurous train ride allows you to experience the grandeur and opulence of Indian royalty. The train takes travelers on a journey through some of India’s most historic and culturally rich cities, such as Jaipur, Jaisalmer, and Udaipur.

5. The Trans-Siberian Railway, Russia

The Trans-Siberian Railway is the longest train ride in the world, taking travelers from Moscow to Vladivostok. The train ride covers more than 9,000 km and passes through several time zones. Traveling from Europe to Asia, this train journey offers breath-taking sceneries of the vast Siberian wilderness, large rivers, grasslands, and mountain ranges.

6. The Flam Railway, Norway

The Flam Railway is a 20-km train journey that takes travelers through the stunning Norwegian fjords. The train ride takes travelers through steep mountains covered in lush vegetation. The route includes spectacular views of cascading waterfalls, snow-capped peaks, verdant valleys and pristine lakes. This train journey is considered one of the most scenic train rides in the world.

In Conclusion

These are just a few of the most scenic train rides in the world. Train journeys offer travelers a unique way to experience the world’s natural and cultural wonders, while enjoying some of the most beautiful landscapes on the planet. While train travel may not be the fastest mode of transportation, it is undoubtedly one of the most scenic and memorable ways to experience a destination. So, next time you plan your trip, consider adding a scenic train ride to your itinerary, and you won’t regret it.
