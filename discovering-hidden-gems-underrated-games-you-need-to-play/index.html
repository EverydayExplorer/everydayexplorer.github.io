Discovering Hidden Gems: Underrated Games You Need to Play


In the vast world of video games, there are always those special gems that tend to fly under the radar. These are the underrated games that deserve much more attention than they usually get. While the gaming industry is often dominated by big-budget titles and popular franchises, there are countless hidden gems waiting to be discovered. So, today we’re going to dive into the world of underrated games that you absolutely need to play.

1. ‘Celeste’

If you’re a fan of challenging platformers, ‘Celeste’ is a game you shouldn’t miss. Developed by Matt Makes Games, this indie masterpiece tells the story of Madeline, a young woman on a journey to climb the treacherous Celeste Mountain. With its tight controls and mesmerizing pixel art, ‘Celeste’ offers a compelling narrative about mental health and self-discovery.

2. ‘Hollow Knight’

Taking place in the forgotten kingdom of Hallownest, ‘Hollow Knight’ is a beautiful and atmospheric game that combines exploration, combat, and metroidvania mechanics. Team Cherry’s debut title has received critical acclaim for its hauntingly beautiful art style, challenging gameplay, and deep lore. As you delve into the depths of Hallownest, you’ll uncover secrets and face off against formidable foes.

3. ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’

Developed by Moon Studios, ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ is a breathtakingly beautiful platformer that will tug at your heartstrings. Playing as Ori, a white guardian spirit, you embark on a quest to restore the forest’s balance and uncover your destiny. With its stunning visuals, emotional narrative, and captivating soundtrack, ‘Ori and the Blind Forest’ is an unforgettable experience.

4. ‘Gris’

If you’re looking for a game that pushes the boundaries of what a video game can be, look no further than ‘Gris.’ Developed by Nomada Studio, this indie title is a work of art in every sense of the word. With its hand-drawn animations, ethereal soundtrack, and emotional storytelling, ‘Gris’ takes players on a beautiful journey of self-discovery and healing.

5. ‘Undertale’

‘Undertale’ is a game that defies expectations and subverts traditional RPG tropes. Developed by Toby Fox, this quirky indie title offers a unique blend of humor, heartwarming moments, and thought-provoking choices. As you navigate the underground world of monsters, your actions have consequences, and every choice matters. With its memorable characters and multiple branching paths, ‘Undertale’ is an experience like no other.

6. ‘Stardew Valley’

For fans of relaxing and addictive farming simulations, ‘Stardew Valley’ is a hidden gem that shouldn’t be missed. Developed solely by Eric ‘ConcernedApe’ Barone, this indie game offers a charming and immersive escape from reality. As you move to the countryside and inherit your grandfather’s old farm, you’ll embark on a journey of building relationships, exploring the town, and growing your farm. With its lovable cast of characters, endless activities, and peaceful atmosphere, ‘Stardew Valley’ will keep you hooked for hours.

7. ‘Katana ZERO’

If you’re a fan of stylish action games with a gripping narrative, ‘Katana ZERO’ is a game you’ll want to check out. Developed by Askiisoft, this indie game puts you in the shoes of a samurai assassin who must navigate through a mixture of intense combat sequences and conversation-based choices. With its tight gameplay mechanics, engaging story, and an incredibly catchy synth-driven soundtrack, ‘Katana ZERO’ is a must-play for action enthusiasts.

These are just a handful of the many underrated games that deserve more recognition. While the gaming industry often highlights the big-budget titles, it’s essential to explore the hidden gems that are waiting to be discovered. So, go out there and give these underrated games a chance – you won’t be disappointed. Happy gaming!
