10 Underrated Cartoons That Deserve More Recognition


Cartoons are a universal language that brings people together. They have the power to make us laugh, cry and even help us learn valuable life lessons. While some shows have become household names with massive followings, others have been overlooked and underrated throughout the years. In this blog post, we’ll be shining a light on 10 underrated cartoons that deserve more recognition.

1. ‘Harvey Beaks’
‘Harvey Beaks’ is a show about a young bird named Harvey who wants to experience everything the world has to offer. Despite being the smallest bird in the forest, Harvey’s curiosity and kindness help him make friends and navigate the ups and downs of childhood. With compelling characters and a charming art style, ‘Harvey Beaks’ is a show that deserves more recognition.

2. ‘Mighty Magiswords’
‘Mighty Magiswords’ is an animated TV series that follows two warriors, Prohyas and Vambre, who use their collection of magical swords to complete quests and fight villains. With a unique visual style and action-packed storytelling, ‘Mighty Magiswords’ deserves more recognition as it’s an engaging show that can keep viewers of all ages entertained.

3. ‘Wander Over Yonder’
‘Wander Over Yonder’ is a sci-fi comedy show that follows a friendly alien named Wander and his doofy sidekick Sylvia as they explore the universe and spread kindness wherever they go. With a colorful art style, witty humor, and lovable characters, ‘Wander Over Yonder’ is a hidden gem that deserves to be seen by more people.

4. ‘Stretch Armstrong and the Flex Fighters’
‘Stretch Armstrong and the Flex Fighters’ is an action-comedy cartoon that follows three teenagers who become superheroes after a scientific experiment goes wrong. With epic action sequences and a diverse cast of characters, ‘Stretch Armstrong and the Flex Fighters’ is a show that deserves more credit for its engaging storytelling.

5. ‘The Dragon Prince’
‘The Dragon Prince’ is an animated fantasy series that follows two princes and an elf who set out on a quest to stop a war between humans and magical creatures. With stunning animation and immersive world-building, ‘The Dragon Prince’ deserves more recognition as a fantastic show that offers a unique take on the fantasy genre.

6. ‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’
‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’ is a post-apocalyptic cartoon that follows a young girl named Kipo as she ventures to the surface world to find her father. With a diverse cast of characters and a vibrant art style, ‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’ is a show that deserves more recognition for its expert storytelling.

7. ‘Elena of Avalor’
‘Elena of Avalor’ is a show about a Latina princess who must learn to be a leader and protect her kingdom from an evil sorceress. With a strong message of empowerment and cultural representation, ‘Elena of Avalor’ deserves more recognition for its positive representation of Latinx culture.

8. ‘Trollhunters’
‘Trollhunters’ is an action-packed show that follows a teenager named Jim who becomes a defender of the magical world after discovering an amulet that allows him to enter a secret realm. With cinematic visuals and engaging storytelling, ‘Trollhunters’ deserves more recognition for its thrilling adventure.

9. ‘OK K.O! Let’s Be Heroes’
‘OK K.O! Let’s Be Heroes’ is a show about a young boy named K.O. who dreams of becoming the greatest hero of all time. With a nostalgic 80s art style and fast-paced storytelling, ‘OK K.O! Let’s Be Heroes’ is a show that deserves more recognition for its entertaining action and lovable characters.

10. ‘Big City Greens’
‘Big City Greens’ is a show about a country boy named Cricket who moves to the big city with his family. With witty humor and endearing characters, ‘Big City Greens’ deserves more recognition for its relatable storytelling about finding one’s place in the world.

Overall, these 10 underrated cartoons have proven to be fantastic shows that are deserving of more recognition. From their engaging characters to their immersive storytelling, these shows have something to offer viewers of all ages and backgrounds. So next time you’re looking for a new cartoon to watch, be sure to check out one of these underrated gems.
