The Best Hotels for Stargazing: Where to Book for Clear Skies


When was the last time you looked up at the night sky and were truly captivated by the brilliance of the stars? For many, stargazing is not just a pastime; it’s an enchanting experience that connects us to the universe. If you’re a celestial enthusiast or simply someone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life, booking a stay at a hotel renowned for its stargazing opportunities can transform your nights into magical adventures. In this guide, we’ll explore some of the best hotels for stargazing around the world, where clear skies and stunning celestial views await you.

1. Under Canvas, Moab, Utah

Nestled near Arches National Park and Canyonlands National Park, Under Canvas offers an unparalleled glamping experience. Imagine waking up to the breathtaking views of red rock formations, and come nightfall, the skies transform into a mesmerizing canvas of stars. The lack of artificial light and the high elevation of this region make it one of the best spots for stargazing in the U.S.

Key Features:

  • Luxury Tents: Enjoy the comfort of a king-size bed, private bathrooms, and wood-burning stoves in your tent.
  • Stargazing Events: The hotel often hosts stargazing events with local astronomers who guide guests through constellations and celestial phenomena.
  • Outdoor Activities: Engage in hiking, rock climbing, and other outdoor adventures during the day before settling down for a night under the stars.

2. The Little Nell, Aspen, Colorado

Known for its world-class skiing and vibrant arts scene, Aspen also offers stunning stargazing opportunities. The Little Nell provides luxury accommodations right at the heart of this beautiful mountain town. After a day on the slopes, you can relax in their outdoor hot tubs while gazing at the clear mountain skies filled with twinkling stars.

Key Features:

  • Ski-in/Ski-out Access: Perfect for winter sports enthusiasts who want to experience both skiing and stargazing.
  • On-site Dining: Indulge in gourmet meals that can be enjoyed under the stars on the terrace.
  • Proximity to Nature: With the Maroon Bells and other natural wonders nearby, you’ll have plenty of opportunities for both daytime exploration and nighttime sky-watching.

3. The Observatory Hotel, Sydney, Australia

For those who prefer an urban setting without sacrificing stargazing, The Observatory Hotel in Sydney is a fantastic choice. This luxury hotel is located near the Sydney Observatory, which has been a center for astronomical research since 1858. Guests can enjoy the city’s skyline while also having easy access to stargazing events.

Key Features:

  • Historic Significance: The hotel is steeped in history and provides an elegant experience that combines luxury with a touch of the past.
  • Astronomy Programs: Participate in organized stargazing sessions at the nearby observatory, where you can view planets and stars through powerful telescopes.
  • Views of the Harbour: The hotel offers breathtaking views of Sydney Harbour, making it a perfect backdrop for sunset and stargazing.

4. Château Mont Royal, Chantilly, France

Set in the picturesque Chantilly Forest, Château Mont Royal offers a fairy-tale escape just outside of Paris. This luxury hotel not only boasts exquisite architecture and lush gardens but is also far enough from the city lights to provide a stunning view of the night sky.

Key Features:

  • Elegant Accommodations: Experience the charm of a historic château with modern comforts.
  • Nature Trails: Explore the surrounding forest during the day and set up a cozy blanket for stargazing at night.
  • Fine Dining: Enjoy culinary delights crafted from local ingredients, with the option to dine alfresco under the stars.

5. Alma de Sedona Inn, Sedona, Arizona

Sedona is known for its stunning red rock formations and vibrant arts community. Alma de Sedona Inn is a cozy bed and breakfast that offers an intimate setting for stargazing enthusiasts. The clear desert skies make it an ideal location for witnessing celestial events.

Key Features:

  • Personalized Experience: With only a few rooms available, guests receive personalized service that enhances their stay.
  • Stargazing Kits: The inn provides guests with telescopes and guides to help them explore the night sky.
  • Cultural Activities: Engage in local art and culture during the day before immersing yourself in the serene desert evenings.

6. L’Auberge de Sedona, Sedona, Arizona

Also located in Sedona, L’Auberge de Sedona takes luxury to the next level. This creekside resort allows guests to unwind in a serene setting while offering some of the best stargazing in the region.

Key Features:

  • Creekside Cottages: Stay in beautifully designed cottages with private decks, perfect for stargazing.
  • Outdoor Fire Pits: Enjoy a cozy evening by the fire, sipping wine while gazing up at the stars.
  • Spa Services: Treat yourself to a spa day, then relax under the expansive Arizona sky at night.

7. Hotel des Grands Boulevards, Paris, France

In the heart of Paris, Hotel des Grands Boulevards offers a unique twist on stargazing. While the city itself is known for its bright lights, the hotel features a rooftop terrace where guests can enjoy a different perspective of the night sky, surrounded by the Parisian skyline.

Key Features:

  • Rooftop Terrace: The terrace provides a beautiful viewpoint for stargazing amidst the iconic Paris skyline.
  • Culinary Delights: Enjoy exquisite meals at the hotel’s restaurant, followed by a drink while gazing at the stars.
  • Cultural Hub: Located near many of Paris’ famous attractions, guests can experience the city by day and the stars by night.

8. The Wharekauhau Country Estate, New Zealand

For those seeking an off-the-beaten-path stargazing destination, The Wharekauhau Country Estate in New Zealand is a hidden gem. Located on the North Island, this luxurious property offers breathtaking views of the night sky, particularly during the Southern Hemisphere’s winter months.

Key Features:

  • Secluded Location: The estate is surrounded by lush landscapes, providing an ideal setting for stargazing without light pollution.
  • Private Dining: Enjoy gourmet meals prepared with local ingredients, and opt for a private dining experience under the stars.
  • Adventure Activities: Engage in outdoor activities such as horseback riding and hiking during the day, then relax with the stars at night.

9. Tenaya Lodge at Yosemite, California

Yosemite National Park is renowned for its stunning landscapes, and Tenaya Lodge offers the perfect base for exploring this natural wonder. The lodge is located just outside the park, providing easy access to some of the best stargazing spots in the area.

Key Features:

  • Proximity to Nature: The lodge is a short drive from Yosemite Valley, where guests can witness some of the best night skies in the country.
  • Guided Stargazing Tours: The lodge offers guided tours that educate guests about the stars and constellations visible in the Sierra Nevada.
  • Spa Facilities: After a day of exploration, guests can unwind with spa treatments and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

10. The Inn at Weathersfield, Vermont

The Inn at Weathersfield is a charming bed and breakfast located in the serene countryside of Vermont. This quaint inn offers a cozy atmosphere perfect for couples or those seeking solitude while providing exceptional stargazing opportunities.

Key Features:

  • Charming Accommodations: Enjoy beautifully decorated rooms with a homey feel.
  • Starry Nights: The inn is situated away from city lights, providing a clear view of the night sky.
  • Farm-to-Table Dining: Experience locally sourced meals that showcase the best of Vermont’s culinary scene.

Conclusion

Stargazing is an experience that transcends the ordinary, connecting us to the vastness of the universe. Whether you choose a luxurious hotel in a bustling city or a secluded retreat in nature, each of these destinations offers unique opportunities to marvel at the night sky. So pack your bags, grab a telescope, and prepare for unforgettable nights filled with wonder and awe. Book your stay at one of these remarkable hotels, and immerse yourself in the beauty of the cosmos.
