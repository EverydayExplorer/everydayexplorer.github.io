The Best Hotels for Nature Lovers: Retreats in Scenic Settings


When it comes to planning a vacation, nature lovers often seek out hotels that offer a retreat in scenic settings. These hotels provide a unique opportunity to immerse oneself in the beauty of the natural world, while also enjoying comfortable accommodations and exceptional amenities. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotels for nature lovers, where you can experience the tranquility of nature without sacrificing luxury.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA

Nestled in the heart of Canyon Point, Utah, Amangiri offers a one-of-a-kind experience for nature enthusiasts. Surrounded by striking red rock formations and dramatic canyons, this hotel seamlessly blends into its natural surroundings, providing guests with stunning views from every angle. The hotel also offers a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, horseback riding, and hot air ballooning, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the beauty of the desert landscape.

2. Fogo Island Inn, Newfoundland, Canada

Located on a remote island off the coast of Newfoundland, Fogo Island Inn is a haven for those seeking seclusion and natural beauty. This contemporary hotel embraces the local culture and sustainable practices, offering guests a truly unique experience. The hotel’s stunning architecture blends seamlessly with the rugged landscape, and each room offers panoramic views of the North Atlantic Ocean. Guests can explore the island’s stunning fjords, hike through the ancient forests, or simply relax on the pristine beaches.

3. Singita Sabi Sand, South Africa

For a truly unforgettable safari experience, Singita Sabi Sand is the ideal choice. Situated in the Sabi Sand Game Reserve, this luxurious hotel offers guests the opportunity to observe Africa’s iconic wildlife in their natural habitat. With expert guides leading game drives and bush walks, visitors can witness the beauty of the African bush with its diverse flora and fauna. The hotel’s eco-friendly practices and commitment to conservation further enhance the experience, making it a perfect choice for nature lovers.

4. Nayara Springs, Costa Rica

Nestled in the heart of the Costa Rican rainforest, Nayara Springs is an idyllic retreat for nature lovers. This adults-only hotel offers spacious villas with private plunge pools, allowing guests to soak in the serenity of the surrounding jungle. The hotel also offers a variety of activities, including guided hikes through the rainforest, visits to nearby hot springs, and wildlife sightings. With its breathtaking natural beauty and commitment to sustainability, Nayara Springs is a true paradise for nature enthusiasts.

5. Six Senses Yao Noi, Thailand

Located on a secluded island in Thailand’s Phang Nga Bay, Six Senses Yao Noi offers guests a tranquil escape amidst stunning natural beauty. The hotel’s private villas offer panoramic views of the limestone karsts, emerald waters, and lush greenery. Guests can explore the surrounding islands by kayak, embark on a snorkeling adventure, or simply relax on the pristine beaches. With its commitment to sustainable practices and its stunning location, Six Senses Yao Noi is a perfect choice for nature lovers seeking a luxurious getaway.

In conclusion, for nature lovers seeking hotels that offer retreats in scenic settings, these options are sure to provide an unforgettable experience. From the striking red rock formations of Amangiri to the rugged beauty of Fogo Island, these hotels offer a perfect blend of natural beauty and luxurious amenities. Whether you’re looking to embark on a safari adventure in South Africa or relax in a private villa surrounded by Costa Rica’s rainforest, these hotels cater to the unique needs and desires of nature enthusiasts. So, go ahead and plan your next vacation at one of these incredible hotels, and let nature take center stage as you enjoy a truly unforgettable experience.
