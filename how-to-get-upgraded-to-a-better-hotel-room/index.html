How to Get Upgraded to a Better Hotel Room


Have you ever checked into a hotel and wished for a better room? We’ve all been there. Whether it’s a special occasion or simply a desire for a more luxurious experience, getting upgraded to a better hotel room can make your stay even more enjoyable. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to increase your chances of being upgraded to a better hotel room. So, let’s dive in!

1. Join the Loyalty Program: One of the easiest ways to increase your chances of getting a room upgrade is by joining the hotel’s loyalty program. Most hotels offer exclusive perks to their loyal members, including room upgrades. By staying with the same hotel chain regularly and accumulating loyalty points, you’ll be more likely to receive a complimentary upgrade.

2. Book Directly with the Hotel: When making a reservation, always try to book directly with the hotel instead of using third-party websites. Hotels value direct bookings and often prioritize their direct customers when it comes to upgrades. Additionally, booking directly allows you to communicate your preferences and special requests directly to the hotel staff, increasing your chances of a better room.

3. Be Polite and Friendly: Remember, a little kindness goes a long way. When checking in, be polite and friendly to the hotel staff. Engage in pleasant conversation and show appreciation for their service. By building a positive rapport, you may find yourself on the receiving end of a generous upgrade.

4. Arrive Early or Late: Timing can play a crucial role in getting an upgrade. Arriving early or late to the hotel can increase your chances of securing a better room. If you arrive early, the hotel may have more availability and be more inclined to upgrade you. On the other hand, arriving late gives the hotel a chance to assess their room inventory and potentially offer upgrades to guests who haven’t checked in yet.

5. Celebrate Special Occasions: If you’re celebrating a special occasion like a birthday, anniversary, or honeymoon, be sure to mention it during the booking process or at check-in. Hotels often go the extra mile to make these occasions memorable, and a room upgrade might just be part of the special treatment.

6. Be a Repeat Guest: If you’ve stayed at the same hotel before and had a positive experience, consider returning for your next trip. Hotels appreciate repeat business and are more likely to reward loyal guests with room upgrades as a token of appreciation.

7. Be Flexible: When booking your reservation, be open to different room types. If the hotel is overbooked in the category you’ve chosen, they may offer you an upgrade to a higher category room. Being flexible with your preferences can increase your chances of a better room.

Remember, getting upgraded to a better hotel room is never guaranteed, but by following these tips, you can significantly increase your chances. Join loyalty programs, book directly with the hotel, be polite and friendly, arrive early or late, celebrate special occasions, be a repeat guest, and be flexible with your room preferences. With a little luck and a positive attitude, you just might find yourself enjoying a luxurious upgrade on your next hotel stay. Happy travels!
