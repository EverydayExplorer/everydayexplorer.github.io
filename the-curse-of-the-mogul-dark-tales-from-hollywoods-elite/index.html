The Curse of the Mogul: Dark Tales from Hollywood's Elite


In this gripping anthology, we delve into the lives of the powerful and influential figures who shaped the course of cinema history. Behind their glamorous façades lay a world of secrets, scandals, and the haunting consequences of their insatiable thirst for success.

In the second story, we meet a brilliant director, revered for his artistic vision, who made a Faustian pact to secure his place at the top. The third tale plunges us into the depths of the modern-day Hollywood, where a rising starlet, driven by ambition, made a sinister deal with a mysterious figure. As her star ascended, she found herself trapped in a web of manipulation and deceit, forced to sacrifice her own happiness for the sake of fame.

Each story in ‘The Curse of the Mogul: Dark Tales from Hollywood’s Elite’ explores the twisted desires, moral compromises, and haunting consequences that lurk beneath the glitz and glamour of Tinseltown. From the legendary moguls of the past to the power players of today, this anthology serves as a cautionary reminder that success often comes at a price.

As we peel back the layers of this curse, we are left wondering: Are the moguls of Hollywood truly destined for a life of darkness and despair, or can they break free from the clutches of this malevolent force? ‘The Curse of the Mogul: Dark Tales from Hollywood’s Elite’ is a chilling reminder that the pursuit of power and fame can lead to a fate far worse than failure.
