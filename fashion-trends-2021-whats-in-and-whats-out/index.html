Fashion Trends 2021: Whats In and Whats Out


Welcome to our ultimate fashion guide for 2021! As the year unfolds, we can’t help but get excited about all the new and exciting fashion trends hitting the runways and the streets. From bold colors to classic silhouettes, this year promises to be a year of inspiration and self-expression through style. In this blog post, we’ll delve into what’s in and what’s out in the world of fashion for 2021. So, grab a cup of coffee, sit back, and let’s dive into the latest trends!

What’s In:

1. Pastel Power: Soft, dreamy pastel shades are making a comeback this year. From soft pinks to delicate blues, these colors are perfect for creating a romantic and ethereal look. Incorporate these hues into your wardrobe through flowy dresses, tailored blazers, or even accessories like handbags and shoes.

2. Statement Sleeves: Say goodbye to basic sleeves because 2021 is all about making a statement. Bold and voluminous sleeves are taking center stage, adding drama and flair to any outfit. Whether it’s puffed, ruffled, or balloon-shaped sleeves, these attention-grabbing details are sure to turn heads.

3. Sustainable Fashion: With a growing consciousness about the environment, sustainability is a trend that’s here to stay. Embrace eco-friendly materials, recycled fabrics, and ethically made clothing. From upcycled denim to organic cotton, choosing sustainable fashion not only looks good but also feels good.

4. Relaxed Tailoring: Bid farewell to overly structured and fitted pieces because relaxed tailoring is having a moment. Think oversized blazers, wide-leg trousers, and slouchy shirts. This trend embraces comfort and ease without compromising style.

5. Chunky Boots: Step up your shoe game with the trendiest footwear of the year – chunky boots. They add an edgy touch to any outfit and are the perfect juxtaposition to flowy dresses or tailored separates. Whether you opt for combat boots or platform styles, these statement shoes will elevate your look.

What’s Out:

1. Skinny Jeans: While they had their heyday, skinny jeans are slowly fading into the background. With a shift towards comfort and relaxed fits, wider-legged denim styles are taking their place. Embrace mom jeans, wide-leg trousers, or even flared pants for a more current look.

2. Micro Handbags: Goodbye to tiny bags that barely fit your essentials. This year, it’s all about practicality. Opt for roomy totes, oversized clutches, or even bucket bags that can carry all your daily necessities. After all, fashion should be both stylish and functional.

3. Neon Overload: While pops of color are still in, neon is taking a backseat this year. Instead, focus on softer and more muted tones for a sophisticated and chic look. Pastels and earthy hues are the way to go when it comes to color choices.

4. Ultra-Fitted Silhouettes: Tight and figure-hugging clothing is giving way to looser and more comfortable silhouettes. Embrace flowy dresses, oversized tops, and relaxed tailoring for a more laid-back yet stylish vibe. Comfort is key!

5. Fast Fashion: In a world that’s becoming increasingly aware of the impact of fast fashion on the environment, the trend of disposable clothing is slowly fading away. Embrace slow fashion, invest in quality pieces that will stand the test of time, and support brands with sustainable practices.

As we embark on a new year, fashion trends are continuously evolving. Whether you decide to embrace the latest trends or create your own unique style, remember that fashion should always be a reflection of who you are. So go ahead, experiment, and have fun with your personal style. Here’s to a year filled with fashion-forward choices and endless possibilities!
