Achieving the Perfect Winged Eyeliner


The quest for the perfect winged eyeliner can often feel like an uphill battle. One wrong move and your carefully crafted wings can turn into a disaster. But fear not, because today, we are going to demystify the art of achieving the perfect winged eyeliner.

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before we dive into the technique, it’s essential to have the right tools at your disposal. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A good quality eyeliner pen or liquid eyeliner
  • An angled eyeliner brush (optional)
  • A steady hand and a dash of confidence

Step 2: Prep Your Canvas

Like any work of art, a good base is essential. Start by ensuring your eyelids are clean and free of any oil or residue. Use a gentle cleanser or makeup remover to get rid of any excess oils or makeup.

Step 3: Find Your Angle

Now, it’s time to determine the angle of your wings. Take a moment to visualize the shape you desire. A general rule of thumb is to follow the natural curve of your lower lash line and extend it outward. The angle can be subtle or more dramatic, depending on your personal preference.

Step 4: Start Small

Remember, Rome wasn’t built in a day, and neither are perfect wings. It’s best to start with small, gradual strokes rather than attempting one bold line. Begin by drawing a thin line along your upper lash line, starting from the inner corner and working your way outward.

Step 5: Connect the Dots

To create the wing, imagine an invisible line extending from the outer corner of your eye towards your temple. This line will serve as a guide for your wing’s length. Using small strokes, connect the outer end of your initial line to the imaginary line you just drew.

Step 6: Fill in the Blanks

Once you have your basic wing shape, it’s time to fill it in. Using the same eyeliner, carefully fill in the outline you created, making sure to stay as close to the lash line as possible. If you prefer a more intense look, you can go over the line once or twice to build up the color.

Step 7: Clean up the Edges

To achieve a crisp, precise wing, it’s essential to clean up any smudges or mistakes. Dip a cotton swab or a small angled brush in some makeup remover and gently trace along the edges of your wing to sharpen the shape.

Step 8: Practice Makes Perfect

Remember, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner takes practice. Don’t be discouraged if your first attempts aren’t flawless. Keep practicing, experimenting with different techniques and products, until you find what works best for you.

Final Thoughts

The perfect winged eyeliner is within your reach. With the right tools, technique, and a little bit of patience, you can master this art form. So, next time you want to add a touch of glamour to your makeup look, don’t shy away from those wings. Embrace the challenge and let your eyes take flight!

Now go forth, armed with knowledge, and conquer the world, one wing at a time!
