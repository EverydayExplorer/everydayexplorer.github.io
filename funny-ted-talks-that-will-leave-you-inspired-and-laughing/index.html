Funny Ted Talks That Will Leave You Inspired and Laughing


Are you in need of a good laugh and a dose of inspiration? Look no further than these hilarious TED Talks that are guaranteed to leave you in stitches while also leaving you feeling motivated and inspired. TED Talks are known for their ability to educate, entertain, and inspire, and these funny talks are no exception. So, without further ado, let’s dive into some of the funniest TED Talks that will have you laughing and feeling inspired at the same time.

1. Celeste Barber: ‘Funny Life Lessons from a Comedian’

In this side-splitting and relatable talk, comedian Celeste Barber takes a hilarious approach to sharing life lessons through her own experiences. With her unique comedic style, she tackles the unrealistic expectations set by social media and embraces the imperfections and realities of everyday life. By challenging society’s obsession with perfection, Barber reminds us to find humor in the ordinary and to be true to ourselves.

2. Ze Frank: ‘Nerdcore Comedy’

If you’re a fan of geek culture, you won’t want to miss Ze Frank’s TED Talk on nerdcore comedy. Frank, a comedian and web producer, delves into the world of nerd humor and reveals the power of embracing your own quirks and passions. With witty and clever jokes, Frank entertains while also reminding us that our differences should be celebrated rather than hidden.

3. James Veitch: ‘This is What Happens When You Reply to Spam Email’

Have you ever wondered what would happen if you replied to those annoying spam emails? James Veitch did, and his hilarious TED Talk shares his experiences of engaging with spammers. With his quick wit and sharp humor, Veitch takes us on a journey through his email exchanges, turning the tables on the spammers and leaving the audience in stitches. This talk is not only laugh-out-loud funny but also serves as a reminder to never take life too seriously.

4. Maysoon Zayid: ‘I Got 99 Problems… Palsy Is Just One’

Maysoon Zayid’s TED Talk is both funny and incredibly inspiring. Zayid, a comedian and advocate for disability rights, shares her experience of living with cerebral palsy while incorporating humor to break down misconceptions and stereotypes. With her witty one-liners and infectious laughter, Zayid challenges societal norms and encourages us to embrace diversity and inclusivity.

5. Shawn Achor: ‘The Happy Secret to Better Work’

While not a stand-up comedy routine, Shawn Achor’s TED Talk will still have you laughing while learning about the science of happiness. Achor, a positive psychology researcher, uses humor as he explores the link between happiness and success. With his witty anecdotes and clever insights, Achor teaches us how to cultivate happiness in our lives and create a more positive work environment.

In conclusion, these funny TED Talks prove that laughter and inspiration go hand in hand. Whether it’s through the comedic storytelling of Celeste Barber, the nerdcore humor of Ze Frank, or the thought-provoking comedy of Maysoon Zayid, these speakers remind us of the power of laughter and its ability to inspire and motivate us. So the next time you’re in need of a good laugh, consider tuning into one of these TED Talks for a mix of comedy and inspiration. You won’t be disappointed!

Remember, laughter is the best medicine, and these TED Talks have got plenty of it. Get ready to be inspired and entertained as you embark on an amusing journey through the world of TED Talks. So kick back, relax, and get ready to laugh and be inspired by these fantastic speakers.
