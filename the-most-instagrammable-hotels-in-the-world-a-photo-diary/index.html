The Most Instagrammable Hotels in the World: A Photo Diary


The world we live in has become increasingly visual, with social media platforms like Instagram playing a huge role in our everyday lives. From picturesque landscapes to mouth-watering food shots, Instagram has revolutionized the way we share and consume content. And when it comes to travel, there’s no denying that Instagram plays a significant role in not only inspiring us to explore new destinations but also in helping us choose where to stay.

In this blog post, we take you on a virtual journey through some of the most Instagrammable hotels in the world. These hotels are not just beautiful in person, but they have also mastered the art of creating spaces that are picture-perfect for your Instagram feed. So get ready to feast your eyes on breathtaking architecture, stunning interior design, and awe-inspiring views!

First up on our list is the Amangiri Resort, located amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Utah, USA. This luxury resort blends seamlessly with its surroundings, boasting sleek design elements that perfectly complement the rugged beauty of the desert. Imagine capturing the perfect shot of yourself lounging by the iconic pool or against the backdrop of the dramatic sandstone cliffs. The Amangiri Resort is truly a photographer’s paradise.

If tropical vibes are more your style, then the Hanging Gardens of Bali should be on your radar. Nestled in the heart of the lush Ubud rainforest, this hotel offers a mesmerizing experience. Each room features a private infinity pool overlooking the dense jungle canopy, making for an epic photo opportunity. You can’t help but feel like you’re living in a fairytale as you capture the vibrant greenery and cascading waterfalls surrounding you.

For those seeking a fusion of modern aesthetics and historical charm, look no further than the Conservatorium Hotel in Amsterdam. Housed in a beautifully restored 19th-century bank building, this hotel effortlessly combines contemporary design with classical architecture. Picture yourself posing in front of the grand entrance or capturing the natural light flooding through the floor-to-ceiling windows in your room. The Conservatorium Hotel is a haven for design enthusiasts and Instagrammers alike.

If you’re craving an urban retreat, then Tokyo’s Aman Tokyo is the place to be. This luxury hotel offers panoramic views of the city skyline, blending traditional Japanese elements with modern minimalism. Whether it’s a shot of the serene Zen garden or a glimpse of the bustling streets below, every corner of Aman Tokyo screams elegance and sophistication.

Last but not least, we have the magical ice hotel, Icehotel 365, located in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden. As the name suggests, this hotel is entirely made of ice and snow, providing a truly unique and ethereal experience. From the intricately carved ice sculptures to the surreal ice suites, every inch of this hotel is a work of art waiting to be captured on camera. Imagine the likes pouring in as you showcase the frosty wonderland that is Icehotel 365.

These are just a few examples of the most Instagrammable hotels in the world. Each one offers a distinctive experience and a plethora of photo opportunities that will make your followers jealous. So, whether you’re a travel enthusiast looking for your next dream destination or an avid Instagrammer in search of the perfect backdrop for your feed, these hotels are guaranteed to make your stay unforgettable and your photos truly mesmerizing. Happy snapping!
