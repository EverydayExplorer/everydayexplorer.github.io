Hilarious Texts from Mom That Prove Parents Shouldn't Text


Parents navigating the world of texting can sometimes lead to hilariously awkward moments that prove why they might be better off sticking to phone calls or face-to-face conversations. From autocorrect mishaps to emoji confusion, here are some of the funniest texts from moms that will make you appreciate the simplicity of a good old-fashioned phone call.

Autocorrect Fails

Autocorrect is both a blessing and a curse, often turning innocent texts into unintentionally hilarious messages. One mom, attempting to invite her children over for dinner, inadvertently texted, ‘Come over for some hot dog organs!’ instead of ‘hot dogs and corn.’ Another mom, trying to express her excitement, texted, ‘I’m so grape!’ instead of ‘great.’ These autocorrect fails not only provide a good laugh but also serve as a reminder to double-check your messages before hitting send – especially when it comes to food-related invitations!

Emoji Overload

Emojis can add a fun and expressive element to text messages, but they can also lead to confusion – especially for parents who may not be as emoji-savvy as their children. One mom, attempting to express her love for her child, sent a string of emojis that included a heart, a smiley face, a cat, and a thumbs-up – leaving her child scratching their head and wondering what exactly she meant. Another mom, trying to convey her excitement about a family gathering, sent a series of random emojis that left everyone in the family group chat puzzled. Lesson learned: sometimes, words are clearer than emojis!

Mom’s Tech Woes

Navigating technology can be a challenge for anyone, and parents are no exception. One mom, trying to send a photo of her new haircut, accidentally sent a picture of her dog instead – complete with a caption that read, ‘Check out my new ‘do!’ Another mom, attempting to share a funny video, accidentally uploaded a video of herself making funny faces instead. These tech mishaps not only highlight the learning curve of new technology but also serve as a reminder that laughter is the best remedy for technological hiccups.

Unintentional Comedy

Sometimes, the funniest texts from moms are the ones that are completely unintentional. One mom, trying to express her concern for her child’s wellbeing, texted, ‘Are you eating enough vegetables? I heard they’re good for preventing scurvy!’ Another mom, attempting to be supportive, texted, ‘Remember, you can do anything you set your mind to – except maybe skydiving, that seems a bit risky.’ These unintentionally comedic texts show the love and care that moms have for their children, even if their delivery is a bit unconventional.

The Importance of Laughter

While texting mishaps can be embarrassing or confusing in the moment, they also provide moments of levity and laughter in our daily lives. Instead of getting frustrated or annoyed, embracing the humor in these situations can strengthen family bonds and create lasting memories. So the next time your mom sends a text that leaves you scratching your head or laughing out loud, remember to appreciate the unique quirks and humor that make each text message a little piece of family history.
