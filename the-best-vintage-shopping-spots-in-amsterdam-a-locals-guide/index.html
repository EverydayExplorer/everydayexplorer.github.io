The Best Vintage Shopping Spots in Amsterdam: A Local's Guide


Are you a vintage fashion enthusiast? Do you love the thrill of finding unique and one-of-a-kind pieces that transport you to another era? Look no further than Amsterdam, a city known for its vibrant and diverse vintage shopping scene. In this local’s guide, we’ll take you on a journey through the best vintage shopping spots in Amsterdam, where you can uncover hidden treasures and add a touch of vintage charm to your wardrobe.

1. Episode

Located in the heart of Amsterdam’s famous Nine Streets district, Episode is a vintage lover’s paradise. This spacious store offers a vast selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories from the 60s, 70s, and 80s. Whether you’re searching for a retro dress, a colorful jacket, or a funky pair of platform shoes, Episode has it all. Explore their racks and discover unique pieces that will make you stand out from the crowd.

2. Marbles Vintage & Design

Tucked away in the trendy De Pijp neighborhood, Marbles Vintage & Design is a hidden gem that shouldn’t be missed. This charming boutique specializes in carefully curated vintage and designer clothing, with a focus on high-quality pieces from the 20th century. From elegant dresses to tailored suits, Marbles Vintage & Design offers a wide range of options for those with a discerning taste for vintage fashion.

3. Zipper

If you’re a fan of retro fashion with a rock ’n’ roll edge, then Zipper is the place for you. With two locations in Amsterdam, this popular vintage store offers an eclectic mix of clothing, accessories, and even vinyl records. Step inside and immerse yourself in a world of punk, grunge, and alternative fashion. Whether you’re on the hunt for a band t-shirt, a leather jacket, or a pair of vintage Levi’s jeans, Zipper has you covered.

4. Laura Dols

For those seeking a touch of vintage glamour, Laura Dols is a must-visit destination. Located near the famous Dam Square, this elegant store specializes in vintage clothing and accessories from the 1920s to the 1980s. Step into Laura Dols and be transported to a bygone era filled with sequins, feathers, and timeless elegance. Whether you’re attending a special event or simply want to add a touch of vintage sophistication to your everyday style, Laura Dols has the perfect piece for you.

5. I Love Vintage

True to its name, I Love Vintage is a boutique that celebrates all things vintage. Located in Amsterdam’s bustling Jordaan neighborhood, this store offers a carefully curated collection of vintage-inspired clothing and accessories. From retro-style dresses to cute cardigans, I Love Vintage captures the essence of past eras while catering to modern tastes. Whether you’re a fan of the 50s, 60s, or 70s, you’ll find something to love at this charming boutique.

6. Bij Ons Vintage

Tucked away in the vibrant Oud-West neighborhood, Bij Ons Vintage is a cozy boutique that showcases a wide range of vintage finds. From clothing and accessories to home decor items, this store is a treasure trove for vintage lovers. Browse through racks of unique garments and discover hidden gems that will add character to your wardrobe. With its friendly atmosphere and carefully curated selection, Bij Ons Vintage is a must-visit for those in search of vintage treasures.

Conclusion

Amsterdam is a city that embraces its rich history and vibrant culture, and its vintage shopping scene is no exception. From retro fashion to timeless elegance, the best vintage shopping spots in Amsterdam offer a diverse range of options for every vintage enthusiast. So, grab your tote bag and embark on a shopping adventure through the streets of Amsterdam, where you’ll discover unique pieces that tell stories of the past and add a touch of nostalgia to your personal style.

Please note: Due to the ever-changing nature of retail, it’s always a good idea to check the opening hours and availability of stores before visiting.

That concludes our local’s guide to the best vintage shopping spots in Amsterdam. Happy shopping and may you find the perfect vintage treasures to express your unique style!
