The Best Sci-Fi and Fantasy Book Clubs to Join


If you’re a lover of speculative fiction, immersing yourself in the worlds of science fiction and fantasy is not just about reading books; it’s about sharing experiences, thoughts, and interpretations with fellow enthusiasts. Book clubs provide a fantastic platform to dive deeper into the themes, characters, and narratives that shape these genres. Here’s an exploration of some of the best sci-fi and fantasy book clubs you might consider joining, whether online or in-person.

1. The Science Fiction and Fantasy Book Club (Goodreads)

Goodreads hosts a myriad of book clubs, but the Science Fiction and Fantasy Book Club stands out. This online community boasts thousands of members who passionately discuss their favorite titles. The club frequently updates its reading list, ensuring members have access to both classic and contemporary works. With discussion threads that encourage in-depth analysis and a focus on diverse voices, this book club is perfect for anyone wanting to expand their reading horizons.

Why Join?

  • Diverse Reading Selections: Engages with a wide range of sci-fi and fantasy authors.
  • Active Discussions: Members regularly post threads for each book, prompting insightful conversations.
  • Reading Challenges: Participate in themed challenges that push you to explore new sub-genres.

2. Sword & Laser

Sword & Laser is a unique book club that caters to both sci-fi and fantasy fans. Founded by Veronica Belmont and Tom Merritt, this club combines a podcast with a vibrant online community. Members read one book a month, alternating between sci-fi and fantasy selections. The accompanying podcast features author interviews and discussions about the chosen books, making it an immersive experience.

Why Join?

  • Access to Authors: Enjoy exclusive interviews and discussions with popular authors.
  • Community Engagement: Participate in forums and social media discussions.
  • Multimedia Experience: Engage with the content through both reading and listening.

3. The Fantasy Book Club (Facebook Group)

The Fantasy Book Club on Facebook is a welcoming community for fantasy lovers. With thousands of members from around the world, this group focuses solely on fantasy literature. Members share recommendations, reviews, and participate in monthly read-alongs. The club’s emphasis on community makes it a great place to connect with fellow fantasy aficionados.

Why Join?

  • Strong Community Feel: Engage in friendly discussions and make new friends.
  • Monthly Read-Alongs: Participate in structured reading plans that keep you on track.
  • Book Recommendations: Get personalized suggestions from passionate readers.

4. The Sci-Fi & Fantasy Book Club (Meetup)

For those who prefer face-to-face interactions, the Sci-Fi & Fantasy Book Club on Meetup is an excellent option. This club organizes regular meetups in various cities, allowing members to discuss their favorite books over coffee or at local bookstores. The emphasis on social interaction fosters a deeper connection with fellow members.

Why Join?

  • In-Person Connections: Build friendships with local book lovers.
  • Engaging Discussions: Enjoy lively debates and discussions about your favorite titles.
  • Variety of Locations: Find clubs in your area that meet regularly.

5. The Speculative Fiction Book Club (Discord)

If you’re comfortable with technology and looking for a dynamic online community, consider joining the Speculative Fiction Book Club on Discord. This club allows members to engage in real-time discussions through text and voice channels. With a focus on both sci-fi and fantasy, this club often hosts events like author Q&As and themed discussions.

Why Join?

  • Real-Time Conversations: Engage in immediate discussions with fellow readers.
  • Themed Events: Participate in special events like trivia nights and book giveaways.
  • Diverse Channels: Explore various topics related to speculative fiction.

6. Book Riot’s SFF Book Club

Book Riot, a popular website dedicated to books and reading, offers an SFF (Science Fiction and Fantasy) Book Club that is perfect for genre enthusiasts. This online club focuses on diverse and inclusive titles within the sci-fi and fantasy realms. Each month, members vote on the next read, allowing for a collaborative and democratic approach to selecting books.

Why Join?

  • Inclusive Selections: Focus on diverse voices and lesser-known authors.
  • Community Engagement: Engage in discussions across various social media platforms.
  • Monthly Themes: Explore different themes related to sci-fi and fantasy.

7. The Urban Fantasy Book Club (Facebook Group)

For those who love the blend of urban settings with fantasy elements, the Urban Fantasy Book Club on Facebook is a must-join. This community focuses on urban fantasy novels where magic meets the modern world. Members discuss popular series like “The Dresden Files” and “Mercy Thompson” while also discovering hidden gems in the genre.

Why Join?

  • Specialized Focus: Dive deep into the urban fantasy sub-genre.
  • Engaging Discussions: Share your thoughts on plot twists and character development.
  • Community Recommendations: Discover new titles based on fellow readers’ suggestions.

8. The Sci-Fi Book Club (Slack)

The Sci-Fi Book Club on Slack offers a modern twist to the traditional book club format. This platform allows for organized discussions, real-time messaging, and the ability to connect with members across the globe. The club usually reads a blend of classic and contemporary sci-fi, providing a comprehensive perspective on the genre.

Why Join?

  • Organized Discussions: Easily navigate conversations through dedicated channels.
  • Global Community: Connect with sci-fi fans from around the world.
  • Flexible Participation: Engage in discussions at your convenience.

9. The Fantasy and Sci-Fi Book Club (Instagram)

For visually-oriented readers, the Fantasy and Sci-Fi Book Club on Instagram is an excellent way to engage with books through photos and posts. Members share their current reads, participate in challenges, and contribute to discussions through comments and stories. The vibrant community often hosts readathons and book giveaways.

Why Join?

  • Visual Engagement: Enjoy beautiful book aesthetics and creative content.
  • Interactive Challenges: Participate in fun reading challenges and prompts.
  • Book Giveaways: Have the chance to win free books and merchandise.

10. Local Library Book Clubs

Don’t overlook your local library when searching for a sci-fi and fantasy book club. Many libraries host monthly meetings where patrons can discuss various genres, including sci-fi and fantasy. This is a great way to meet fellow readers in your community while enjoying the benefits of free resources.

Why Join?

  • Free Membership: Enjoy access to books and discussions at no cost.
  • Community Connections: Build relationships with local readers and authors.
  • Diverse Selections: Explore a range of titles curated by library staff.

Final Thoughts

Joining a sci-fi and fantasy book club is an enriching experience that can enhance your reading life and introduce you to new perspectives and ideas. Whether you prefer in-person meetings or online discussions, there’s a community out there that aligns with your interests. Embrace the opportunity to connect with fellow readers, share your thoughts, and celebrate the imaginative worlds offered by your favorite genres. Happy reading!
