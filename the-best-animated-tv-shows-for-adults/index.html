The Best Animated TV Shows for Adults


Animated TV shows are not just for kids anymore. In recent years, the world of animation has evolved to cater to a more mature audience, offering a wide range of compelling and entertaining content. From witty comedies to thought-provoking dramas, these shows have captivated adults with their unique storytelling and stunning visuals. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best animated TV shows for adults, delving into their themes, characters, and why they have become must-watch series.

1. ‘BoJack Horseman’:
‘BoJack Horseman’ is a dark comedy-drama that follows the life of its titular character, a washed-up sitcom star who happens to be a horse. The show tackles serious issues such as mental health, addiction, and the pursuit of happiness. With its complex characters and profound exploration of human emotions, ‘BoJack Horseman’ has gained critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base.

2. ‘Rick and Morty’:
‘Rick and Morty’ is a sci-fi animated series that combines humor, adventure, and existentialism. The show follows the misadventures of a genius scientist, Rick, and his naive grandson, Morty, as they travel through different dimensions and encounter bizarre creatures. With its clever writing and mind-bending concepts, ‘Rick and Morty’ has become a cultural phenomenon, attracting viewers with its unique blend of comedy and philosophical undertones.

3. ‘Archer’:
If you’re a fan of spy thrillers and witty banter, ‘Archer’ is the show for you. This animated series revolves around a dysfunctional group of spies who work for the International Secret Intelligence Service. With its fast-paced action, sharp humor, and memorable characters, ‘Archer’ has become a beloved adult animated show, known for its clever wordplay and pop culture references.

4. ‘The Boondocks’:
‘The Boondocks’ is a satirical animated series that delves into themes of race, politics, and social commentary. The show follows the lives of the Freeman family, who move from Chicago to the predominantly white suburb of Woodcrest. Through its unique blend of humor and social critique, ‘The Boondocks’ tackles important issues in a thought-provoking and entertaining manner.

5. ‘Big Mouth’:
‘Big Mouth’ is a coming-of-age animated series that explores the awkwardness and challenges of puberty. The show follows a group of middle school students as they navigate the ups and downs of adolescence, with their own hormone monsters guiding them along the way. With its honest and relatable portrayal of puberty, ‘Big Mouth’ has struck a chord with audiences, blending humor with heartfelt moments.

6. ‘BoJack Horseman’:
Another show that deserves a mention is ‘BoJack Horseman’. This dark comedy-drama follows the life of its titular character, a washed-up sitcom star who happens to be a horse. The show tackles serious issues such as mental health, addiction, and the pursuit of happiness. With its complex characters and profound exploration of human emotions, ‘BoJack Horseman’ has gained critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base.

These are just a few examples of the best animated TV shows for adults that have captivated audiences in recent years. Whether you’re looking for thought-provoking dramas or hilarious comedies, the world of adult animation has something for everyone. So grab some popcorn, sit back, and immerse yourself in the captivating stories and stunning visuals that these shows have to offer. Happy watching!
