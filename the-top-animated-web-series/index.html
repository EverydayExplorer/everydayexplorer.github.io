The Top Animated Web Series


Welcome to our blog, where we delve into the fascinating world of animated web series! In this post, we will be exploring some of the top animated web series that have captured the hearts of viewers around the globe. So grab your popcorn and get ready to embark on a thrilling journey through the realm of animation!

1. “RWBY” - Created by Monty Oum, this action-packed web series follows the adventures of four girls as they battle supernatural forces. With its unique blend of fantasy and vibrant animation, “RWBY” has become a sensation among animation enthusiasts.

2. “Bravest Warriors” - From the brilliant minds behind “Adventure Time,” “Bravest Warriors” takes viewers on a wild ride through space and time. This hilarious and quirky series promises non-stop laughter and unexpected twists.

3. “Bee and PuppyCat” - This charming series revolves around the unconventional friendship between a young woman, Bee, and a mysterious creature called PuppyCat. With its delightful animation style and lovable characters, “Bee and PuppyCat” has gained a dedicated fanbase.

4. “Hazbin Hotel” - Prepare to enter the outrageous world of demons and redemption in this dark comedy web series. With its unique art style and witty dialogue, “Hazbin Hotel” has quickly gained popularity and showcases the incredible creativity of its creator, Vivienne Medrano.

5. “Hilda” - Based on the beloved graphic novel by Luke Pearson, “Hilda” follows the adventures of a fearless young girl in a world filled with magical creatures. The stunning animation and heartwarming storytelling make this series a must-watch for fans of all ages.

6. “Critical Role: The Legend of Vox Machina” - This epic fantasy web series is based on the popular Dungeons & Dragons campaign by the same name. With its captivating storyline and incredible voice acting from the cast of the original podcast, “Critical Role” transports viewers to a world of adventure and intrigue.

7. “Carmen Sandiego” - Inspired by the iconic video game character, “Carmen Sandiego” brings the enigmatic thief to life in an exciting animated series. With its clever puzzles and globe-trotting adventures, this show is a hit among both fans of the original game and newcomers alike.

8. “The Dragon Prince” - From the creators of “Avatar: The Last Airbender,” “The Dragon Prince” takes viewers on an epic journey filled with magic, dragons, and complex characters. The stunning animation and captivating storyline make this series a must-watch for fantasy enthusiasts.

9. “Hilda” - This enchanting web series follows the adventures of a young girl named Hilda and her encounters with magical creatures. With its beautiful animation and heartfelt storytelling, “Hilda” has won the hearts of viewers worldwide.

10. “Infinity Train” - Prepare to embark on a mind-bending journey through a mysterious train in this thought-provoking animated series. With its clever storytelling and intriguing concept, “Infinity Train” captivates viewers with each episode.

These are just a few examples of the incredible animated web series that have taken the internet by storm. Whether you’re a fan of action, comedy, fantasy, or adventure, there’s something for everyone in the world of animated web series.

So, grab your favorite snack, get cozy, and dive into the fascinating worlds brought to life through animation. These top animated web series will surely leave you entertained, inspired, and eagerly awaiting the next episode!
