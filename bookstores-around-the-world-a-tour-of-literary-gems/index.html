Bookstores around the World: A Tour of Literary Gems


If you’re a book lover, there’s nothing quite like stepping into a bookstore and immersing yourself in shelves upon shelves of books. From the moment you walk through the doors, you’re instantly transported to a world of imagination, knowledge, and adventure. Bookstores are not just spaces filled with books, but they are gateways to new ideas, stories, and perspectives. In this blog post, we’ll take you on a tour of literary gems around the world, showcasing some of the most extraordinary bookstores that every bookworm should visit.

1. Shakespeare and Company - Paris, France:

Located in the heart of Paris, Shakespeare and Company is a legendary bookstore that has become an icon for book lovers around the world. Originally opened in 1919, the bookstore has served as a haven for writers and artists throughout the years. Its cozy interior, unique collection, and welcoming atmosphere make it a must-visit for anyone traveling to the City of Lights.

2. El Ateneo Grand Splendid - Buenos Aires, Argentina:

Housed in a beautifully restored theater, El Ateneo Grand Splendid in Buenos Aires is a marvel to behold. With its stunning architecture, ornate decorations, and red velvet curtains, it’s easy to lose yourself in the enchanting ambiance. This bookstore offers a vast selection of books in both Spanish and English, making it a haven for bookworms of all backgrounds.

3. Livraria Lello - Porto, Portugal:

Frequently described as one of the most beautiful bookstores in the world, Livraria Lello is a true architectural gem. Its neo-gothic façade and exquisite stained glass skylight make it a sight to behold. J.K. Rowling, the famous author of the Harry Potter series, was inspired by this bookstore and its stunning staircase when she lived in Porto. Today, Livraria Lello continues to attract visitors from near and far who are in search of literary magic.

4. Powell’s City of Books - Portland, Oregon, USA:

Spanning an entire city block, Powell’s City of Books is a paradise for book lovers. With its vast collection of new and used books, Powell’s offers something for everyone. The bookstore is organized by color-coded rooms, making it easy to navigate and explore. Bookworms can easily spend hours getting lost in the aisles, discovering hidden literary treasures along the way.

5. Atlantis Books - Santorini, Greece:

Nestled on the idyllic Greek island of Santorini, Atlantis Books is a charming bookstore known for its breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. Founded by a group of friends in 2004, this bookstore has become a beloved destination for locals and tourists alike. With its cozy atmosphere and carefully curated selection of books, Atlantis Books is a haven for literary enthusiasts seeking a serene reading experience.

6. The Last Bookstore - Los Angeles, California, USA:

The Last Bookstore in Los Angeles is not just a bookstore; it’s a haven for creativity and imagination. Housed in an old bank building, the store features a labyrinth of rooms filled with books, art installations, and even a secret passageway. The Last Bookstore also hosts events and performances, making it a vibrant hub for the local literary community.

7. Daunt Books - London, United Kingdom:

With its beautiful Edwardian interior and expertly curated selection of books, Daunt Books in London is a true literary treasure. The store specializes in travel literature and is known for its extensive collection of books on all corners of the globe. Daunt Books also has a unique organizational layout, with sections organized by country, enticing readers to explore new destinations through the power of words.

Whether you’re an avid reader, a literary connoisseur, or simply someone who appreciates the magic of books, visiting these extraordinary bookstores will awaken your love for literature and transport you to new worlds. From the historical Shakespeare and Company in Paris to the enchanting Livraria Lello in Porto, each of these bookstores offers a unique experience that will leave you captivated and inspired. So, embark on this literary tour and immerse yourself in the wonder and beauty of bookstores around the world. Your next literary adventure awaits!
