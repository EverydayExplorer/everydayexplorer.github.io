5 Effective Drills to Increase Soccer Ball Control


If you want to take your soccer skills to the next level, one area you should focus on is ball control. Having excellent ball control allows you to maneuver the ball with ease, dribble past opponents, and maintain possession. In this blog post, we will explore five effective drills that can help you improve your soccer ball control.

1. Cone Dribbling

Cone dribbling is a fundamental drill that enhances your close ball control, agility, and coordination. Set up a series of cones in a straight line, approximately one yard apart. Start dribbling the ball through the cones using short, controlled touches. As you progress, increase your speed while maintaining control. Challenge yourself to complete the drill as quickly as possible without knocking over any cones.

2. Wall Passing

Wall passing is an excellent drill to improve your first touch and accuracy when receiving and passing the ball. Find a sturdy wall and stand about five yards away from it. Pass the ball against the wall using the inside or outside of your foot and receive it with the same foot. Focus on controlling the ball with a soft touch and passing it back accurately. Vary the pace and angle of your passes to challenge yourself further.

3. Figure 8 Dribbling

The figure 8 dribbling drill helps improve your close ball control, agility, and ability to change direction quickly. Set up two cones in a figure 8 shape, with each cone approximately three yards apart. Start dribbling the ball through the figure 8 pattern using both feet. Focus on keeping the ball close to your feet, using small touches to navigate the cones smoothly. As you become more comfortable, increase your speed while maintaining control.

4. Ladder Lateral Dribbling

Ladder lateral dribbling is a drill that enhances your ball control while improving your footwork and coordination. Set up an agility ladder on the ground and stand facing it. Start dribbling the ball laterally through the ladder, using quick and controlled touches. Focus on keeping your eyes up and maintaining a low center of gravity. Challenge yourself to complete the drill as fast as possible without losing control of the ball.

5. 1v1 Cone Challenge

The 1v1 cone challenge is a competitive drill that improves your dribbling, ball control, and decision-making skills. Place two cones about ten yards apart and stand next to one cone. Start dribbling the ball towards the other cone while an opponent tries to steal the ball. Your objective is to reach the other cone without losing control of the ball. Switch roles with your opponent after each run and keep track of how many successful runs you have.

By incorporating these five effective drills into your training routine, you can significantly enhance your soccer ball control. Remember to practice regularly, focus on technique, and challenge yourself to improve. With dedication and perseverance, you can become a master of ball control and elevate your overall soccer skills. Keep practicing, and success will follow!

Now it’s time to hit the field and put these drills into action. Lace up your boots, grab your soccer ball, and get ready to take your ball control to new heights!
