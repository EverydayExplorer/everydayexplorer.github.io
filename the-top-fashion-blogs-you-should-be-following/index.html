The Top Fashion Blogs You Should Be Following


In today’s digital age, fashion enthusiasts have a plethora of resources at their fingertips to stay up-to-date with the latest trends, styles, and fashion advice. One of the most popular mediums for fashion inspiration and guidance is fashion blogs. These platforms offer a unique blend of personal style, industry insights, and expert tips, making them a must-follow for anyone with a passion for fashion.

In this blog post, we will explore the top fashion blogs that you should be following to elevate your style game. From street style to high-end couture, these blogs cover a wide range of fashion genres, ensuring that there’s something for everyone.

1. The Blonde Salad
Started by the fashion icon Chiara Ferragni, The Blonde Salad is a go-to blog for fashion enthusiasts. With a blend of personal style posts, fashion week coverage, and beauty tips, this blog offers a comprehensive fashion experience.

2. Man Repeller
If you’re looking for a blog that challenges traditional fashion norms and embraces individuality, Man Repeller is the one for you. Founded by Leandra Medine, this blog celebrates fashion for women by women, offering a fresh and unconventional perspective on style.

3. The Sartorialist
If you’re a fan of street style, then The Sartorialist is a blog that should be on your radar. Run by Scott Schuman, this blog captures the essence of street style from all corners of the world, providing endless inspiration for your everyday looks.

4. Song of Style
Song of Style, helmed by Aimee Song, is a blog that effortlessly blends fashion, travel, and lifestyle. With stunning visuals and relatable content, this blog is a go-to for those seeking fashion inspiration for both everyday wear and special occasions.

5. Atlantic-Pacific
Blair Eadie, the creator of Atlantic-Pacific, has gained a massive following by showcasing her impeccable taste in fashion. Her blog features effortlessly chic outfits and a keen eye for detail, making it a must-follow for fashion enthusiasts.

6. The Fashion Guitar
Charlotte Groeneveld, the face behind The Fashion Guitar, offers a mix of high-end fashion, wearable looks, and beauty tips. Her blog is known for its sophisticated style and attention to detail, making it a top choice for fashion lovers.

7. Cupcakes and Cashmere
Emily Schuman’s Cupcakes and Cashmere is a lifestyle blog that covers a wide range of topics, including fashion, beauty, home decor, and more. With relatable and accessible content, this blog is perfect for those looking to incorporate style into their everyday lives.

8. The Coveteur
The Coveteur takes a unique approach to fashion by offering an inside look into the wardrobes and lives of the industry’s biggest influencers. With stunning visuals and in-depth interviews, this blog provides an exclusive peek into the world of fashion.

9. Style Bubble
Helmed by Susie Lau, Style Bubble is a blog that celebrates creativity and individuality in fashion. With a focus on emerging designers and unconventional styles, this blog is a treasure trove for those seeking unique and avant-garde fashion inspiration.

10. The Fashion Spot
Last but not least, The Fashion Spot is a comprehensive fashion blog that covers everything from runway trends to celebrity style. With a team of dedicated fashion enthusiasts, this blog offers a diverse range of fashion content, making it a must-follow.

By following these top fashion blogs, you can stay on top of the latest trends, gain style inspiration, and immerse yourself in the world of fashion. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or simply looking to revamp your wardrobe, these blogs will provide you with the guidance you need to elevate your style game. So, start following these fashion influencers today and get ready to take your fashion journey to new heights!
