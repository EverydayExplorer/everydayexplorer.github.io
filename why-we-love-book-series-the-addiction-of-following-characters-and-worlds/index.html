Why We Love Book Series - The Addiction of Following Characters and Worlds


Humans have an innate desire to immerse themselves in enthralling stories and become invested in the lives of the characters they love. This is especially true when it comes to book series, which have captured the hearts and minds of readers for centuries. From the Harry Potter series to The Hunger Games, there is no shortage of beloved book series that have enraptured readers around the world. But what is it about book series that makes us so addicted to following the characters and worlds they create? In this blog post, we’ll explore just a few of the reasons why we love book series.

Firstly, book series allow us to develop strong emotional connections to the characters. When we read a standalone novel, we might grow attached to the protagonist over the course of the story, but once the book is finished, our time with that character is over. With book series, however, we get to spend much more time with the characters we love, watching them grow and change over multiple books. This extended time with the characters allows us to form deeper emotional connections with them, making us more invested in their lives and the outcome of their stories.

Secondly, book series often create intricate and fascinating worlds that we become engrossed in. Whether it’s the magical world of Harry Potter or the dystopian future of The Hunger Games, these worlds are often so captivating that we can’t help but become addicted to following them. We want to learn more about the history and culture of these worlds, and we become invested in the conflicts and struggles that take place within them.

Another reason why we love book series is that they offer a sense of familiarity and comfort. When we return to a beloved book series, we know what to expect from the characters and the world they inhabit. This familiarity can be incredibly comforting, especially during times of stress or uncertainty in our own lives. The predictability of a book series can provide a welcome escape from the chaos of the world around us.

Finally, book series often leave us wanting more. When we finish one book in a series, we’re left with questions and cliffhangers that keep us eagerly anticipating the next installment. This sense of anticipation can be incredibly addictive, and it’s what keeps us coming back to book series time and time again.

In conclusion, there are many reasons why we love book series, from the emotional connections we form with the characters to the fascinating worlds they create. Whether we’re seeking comfort, entertainment, or an escape from reality, book series offer something for everyone. So why not dive into a new series today and see what all the fuss is about?
