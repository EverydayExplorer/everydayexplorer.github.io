Scenic Drives: 10 Road Trips with Breathtaking Views


Are you tired of the same old city life? Do you long for a change of scenery, a chance to escape the hustle and bustle and immerse yourself in nature’s beauty? If so, then scenic drives might just be the perfect solution for you. Whether you’re a seasoned traveler or an adventure-seeking newbie, hitting the open road and embarking on a road trip to witness breathtaking views can be an unforgettable experience. In this blog post, we will take you through ten of the most scenic drives around the world, each offering its own unique landscape and captivating beauty.

1. The Pacific Coast Highway, California, USA:
Starting our list is the iconic Pacific Coast Highway, stretching along the stunning California coastline. This winding highway offers mesmerizing views of dramatic cliffs, golden beaches, and the sparkling blue waters of the Pacific Ocean. Drive through charming seaside towns, visit magnificent state parks, and witness glorious sunsets that will leave you in awe.

2. The Great Ocean Road, Victoria, Australia:
Next on our list is the Great Ocean Road, which showcases the natural wonders of Australia’s southeastern coast. This drive will take you through lush rainforests, panoramic viewpoints, and iconic landmarks such as the Twelve Apostles. Don’t forget to keep an eye out for the stunning wildlife, including koalas and kangaroos, that call this area home.

3. Cabot Trail, Nova Scotia, Canada:
Prepare to be captivated by the rugged beauty of the Cabot Trail in Nova Scotia, Canada. This 298-kilometer loop takes you along the coast, offering breathtaking vistas of the Atlantic Ocean, soaring cliffs, and breathtaking waterfalls. Autumn brings an explosion of vibrant colors, turning this scenic drive into a photographer’s paradise.

4. Ring Road, Iceland:
Iceland’s Ring Road is a legendary road trip that will lead you to some of the most awe-inspiring landscapes on Earth. Drive through otherworldly lava fields, past cascading waterfalls, towering glaciers, and geothermal hot springs. This unique route allows you to witness the diverse beauty of Iceland, from black sand beaches to picturesque fjords.

5. The Amalfi Coast, Italy:
Embark on a journey along the Amalfi Coast and discover the charms of the Italian Riviera. This scenic drive winds its way along the Mediterranean coastline, offering views of cliffside villages, colorful buildings clinging to the mountainside, and crystal-clear waters. Indulge in authentic Italian cuisine, explore charming towns like Positano and Ravello, and enjoy the warm Mediterranean breeze as you drive.

6. Trollstigen, Norway:
Get ready for an adrenaline rush as you navigate the hairpin bends and steep inclines of Trollstigen in Norway. This thrilling mountain road showcases the awe-inspiring beauty of Norwegian fjords, towering mountains, and cascading waterfalls. Take your time to absorb the breathtaking views, as well as the sheer scale and grandeur of the Norwegian landscape.

7. Garden Route, South Africa:
The Garden Route in South Africa is a stunning stretch of coastline that combines breathtaking landscapes with vibrant wildlife. Drive through lush forests, explore beautiful lagoons, and encounter diverse wildlife, including elephants and penguins. With its picturesque beaches, nature reserves, and charming seaside towns, the Garden Route is a true paradise for nature lovers.

8. Overseas Highway, Florida Keys, USA:
Connecting the mainland of Florida with the tropical paradise of the Florida Keys, the Overseas Highway is a scenic drive that is truly unforgettable. This iconic drive takes you over pristine turquoise waters, offering spectacular views of the Atlantic Ocean and the Gulf of Mexico. Don’t miss the opportunity to snorkel or dive in the vibrant coral reefs along the way.

9. The North Coast 500, Scotland:
Discover the wild beauty of the Scottish Highlands on the North Coast 500. This epic road trip takes you through rugged mountains, expansive lochs, and dramatic coastlines. Immerse yourself in ancient castles, charming villages, and the legendary Loch Ness. The ever-changing landscape will leave you spellbound at every turn.

10. The Great Ocean Road, New Zealand:
Last but not least, we have the Great Ocean Road in New Zealand. This scenic drive will take you through the crown jewel of New Zealand’s South Island, Fiordland National Park. Marvel at the awe-inspiring Milford Sound and the majestic peaks of the Southern Alps. This drive is a true visual feast, making it a must-do for any nature enthusiast.

In conclusion, scenic drives offer a unique opportunity to escape the urban chaos and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature. From the rugged coastline of California’s Pacific Coast Highway to the enchanting fjords of Norway’s Trollstigen, these road trips are sure to leave you in awe and wondering why you didn’t hit the road sooner. So, pack your bags, fuel up your car, and be prepared for an unforgettable journey filled with breathtaking views and lasting memories.
