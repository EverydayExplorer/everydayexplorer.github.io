The Best Hunting Apps and Online Tools for Tracking and Planning


Hunting has been a popular activity for centuries, but with advancements in technology, the way we approach this ancient tradition has changed. Gone are the days of relying solely on instinct and intuition while out in the wilderness. Today, hunters have a wealth of information at their fingertips thanks to hunting apps and online tools.

Whether you’re an experienced hunter or just starting out, these digital resources can greatly enhance your hunting experience by providing you with real-time data, tracking capabilities, and invaluable planning tools. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hunting apps and online tools available, designed to help you track and plan your hunting trips more effectively.

1. OnX Hunt

One of the most popular hunting apps on the market, OnX Hunt offers a comprehensive solution for hunters of all levels. This app provides detailed maps with property boundaries, public land data, and other essential information like topography and satellite imagery. With OnX Hunt, you can easily track your route, mark waypoints, and share your location with others.

Moreover, OnX Hunt enables you to view hunting unit boundaries and various types of land ownership, allowing you to plan your hunting trips accordingly. This app is available for iOS and Android devices and offers both free and paid versions with additional features such as offline maps and private landowner information.

2. HuntStand

HuntStand is another powerful hunting app that offers a wide range of features designed to enhance your hunting experience. With this app, you can access satellite imagery, topographic maps, and weather information specific to your hunting location. HuntStand also provides property boundaries, providing you with valuable insights on where you can legally hunt.

One of the standout features of HuntStand is its deer movement prediction tool, which uses various factors such as weather patterns, moon phases, and barometric pressure to predict when deer are likely to be active. This can be a game-changer for hunters trying to maximize their chances of a successful hunt. HuntStand is available for both iOS and Android devices.

3. ScoutLook

ScoutLook is an excellent hunting app that focuses on providing hunters with accurate weather forecasts and hunting-specific information. The app offers detailed weather reports, wind direction, and other crucial data that can help you plan your hunting trips effectively. Additionally, ScoutLook integrates with Google Earth, allowing you to scout your hunting areas virtually.

ScoutLook also includes a feature called ScentCone, which uses weather data and wind direction to predict how your scent will travel. This can be highly valuable information when trying to stay undetected by game animals. ScoutLook is available for iOS and Android devices, and a web version is also available.

4. HuntWise

HuntWise is a comprehensive hunting app that offers a wide range of features for hunters. With this app, you can access hunting maps, property boundaries, and various hunting-specific data such as moon phases and solunar activity. HuntWise also includes a powerful tracking feature that allows you to record your hunting routes and mark waypoints.

Additionally, HuntWise has a unique feature called HuntCast, which provides real-time hunting forecast based on factors like weather, barometric pressure, and more. This can help you determine the best time to be in the field. HuntWise is available for iOS and Android devices, and a web version is also available.

5. BaseMap

BaseMap is an all-in-one outdoor app that caters to hunters, anglers, and outdoor enthusiasts. This app offers detailed maps, property boundaries, and useful features such as waypoint marking and tracking. BaseMap also includes a powerful 3D mapping tool that allows you to visualize any landscape and plan your hunting trips more effectively.

Additionally, BaseMap provides various hunting layers that display information like hunting unit boundaries, landowner names, and public land data. This can greatly assist you in finding the best hunting spots and avoiding unauthorized areas. BaseMap is available for both iOS and Android devices.

Online Tools for Tracking and Planning

While hunting apps are great for on-the-go tracking and planning, online tools can provide additional resources when you’re at home or in the planning stages of your hunting trip. Here are two notable online tools to consider:

1. Google Earth

Google Earth is a powerful tool that allows you to explore any location on the planet in detail. As a hunter, you can use Google Earth to scout potential hunting areas, identify terrain features, and plan your routes. The satellite imagery and 3D mapping capabilities make it an invaluable resource for understanding the lay of the land before stepping foot in the wilderness.

2. Powderhook

Powderhook is an online tool specifically designed to help hunters and anglers find access to quality hunting and fishing areas. This platform allows users to search for public land, private land leases, hunting clubs, and other hunting opportunities near their desired location. Powderhook provides a wealth of information and resources to help you plan your next hunting adventure effectively.

In conclusion, hunting apps and online tools have revolutionized the way we approach hunting, providing us with real-time data, tracking capabilities, and invaluable planning tools. By utilizing these resources, hunters can enhance their overall hunting experience while increasing their chances of a successful hunt. Whether you’re a novice or an experienced hunter, consider utilizing these apps and tools to take your hunting adventures to the next level.
