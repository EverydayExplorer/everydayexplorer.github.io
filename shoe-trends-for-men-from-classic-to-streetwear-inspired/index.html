Shoe Trends for Men: From Classic to Streetwear-Inspired


Shoes are an essential part of any man’s wardrobe, and like all fashion items, they come in a wide variety of styles and designs. From classic dress shoes to trendy sneakers, there are shoe trends for men to suit every taste and occasion. Whether you prefer a timeless look or a more modern and streetwear-inspired style, here are some of the top shoe trends for men in 2021.

Classic Dress Shoes:

Classic dress shoes are a timeless staple in any man’s wardrobe. These shoes are perfect for formal events or office wear and add a touch of sophistication to any outfit. Some popular styles of classic dress shoes for men include Oxfords, Brogues, Derby shoes, and Monk straps.

Oxfords are a classic choice for formal occasions and feature a closed lacing system and sleek, polished leather. Brogues are another popular choice and are characterized by their decorative perforations and wingtip design. Derby shoes are similar to Oxfords but have an open lacing system, making them a more versatile option for both formal and casual wear. Monk strap shoes feature a unique buckle closure instead of laces and add a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

Sneakers:

Sneakers have become a wardrobe staple for men of all ages and are no longer just reserved for athletic activities. These versatile shoes are perfect for everyday wear and come in a wide range of styles, colors, and materials. Whether you prefer a classic white leather sneaker or a bold and colorful design, there is a sneaker to suit every style.

Some popular sneaker trends for men in 2021 include chunky dad sneakers, sleek minimalist trainers, retro-inspired styles, and high-top sneakers. Chunky dad sneakers feature a thick sole and oversized design, adding a trendy and sporty touch to any outfit. Minimalist trainers are sleek and understated, making them a versatile option for both casual and dressier occasions. Retro-inspired sneakers are also making a comeback, with brands reviving classic designs from the ’80s and ’90s. High-top sneakers are another popular trend for men and add a stylish and edgy vibe to any outfit.

Loafers:

Loafers are a stylish and versatile shoe option for men that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. These slip-on shoes are perfect for casual wear or can be paired with a suit for a more formal look. Some popular styles of loafers for men include penny loafers, horsebit loafers, and tassel loafers.

Penny loafers are a classic choice and feature a small decorative slot across the front, which was traditionally used to hold a penny. Horsebit loafers feature a metal decorative strap across the front of the shoe and add a touch of luxury to any outfit. Tassel loafers are another popular style and feature decorative tassels on the front of the shoe, adding a playful and stylish touch.

Boots:

Boots are a versatile shoe option for men that can be worn in any season and with a variety of outfits. From classic Chelsea boots to rugged work boots, there is a boot style to suit every man’s taste and lifestyle. Some popular boot trends for men in 2021 include Chelsea boots, combat boots, and desert boots.

Chelsea boots are a classic and timeless option for men and feature a sleek, ankle-high design with elastic side panels for easy on and off. Combat boots are a more rugged and durable option, with a chunky sole and lace-up design perfect for outdoor activities or casual wear. Desert boots are another popular choice and feature a lightweight design with a suede or leather upper, making them a versatile option for both casual and dressier occasions.

Streetwear-Inspired Sneakers:

Streetwear-inspired sneakers are a popular trend for men who want to add a trendy and urban touch to their outfits. These sneakers often feature bold colors, unique designs, and innovative materials, making them a standout addition to any wardrobe. Some popular streetwear-inspired sneaker trends for men in 2021 include high-top basketball sneakers, chunky trainers, and retro-inspired styles.

High-top basketball sneakers are a trendy and sporty option for men and feature a high ankle design and supportive cushioning, making them perfect for everyday wear. Chunky trainers are another popular streetwear-inspired option and feature a thick sole, oversized design, and bold colorways for a fashion-forward look. Retro-inspired sneakers are also making a comeback in men’s fashion, with brands reviving classic designs from past decades in new and exciting ways.

In conclusion, there are a wide variety of shoe trends for men to choose from in 2021, from classic dress shoes to streetwear-inspired sneakers. Whether you prefer a timeless and sophisticated look or a more modern and trendy style, there is a shoe to suit every man’s taste and occasion. By incorporating these popular shoe trends into your wardrobe, you can elevate your style and make a fashionable statement wherever you go.
