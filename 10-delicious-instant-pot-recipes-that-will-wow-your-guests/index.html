10 Delicious Instant Pot Recipes That Will Wow Your Guests


In recent years, the Instant Pot has become a popular kitchen appliance among home cooks and food enthusiasts. This versatile gadget combines the functions of a pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, and more, making it a must-have for those looking to save time in the kitchen. Not only does the Instant Pot allow for quick and easy meal preparation, but it also produces amazing flavors that will impress your guests. In this article, we will explore ten delicious Instant Pot recipes that are sure to wow your guests and leave them wanting more.

1. Pulled Pork One of the most popular Instant Pot recipes is pulled pork. This dish requires minimal ingredients and is incredibly easy to make. Simply season the pork shoulder with a blend of spices, add some liquid (such as broth or apple cider vinegar), and set the Instant Pot to high pressure for about 60-90 minutes. The result is tender, succulent pulled pork that can be used in sandwiches, tacos, or simply enjoyed on its own.

2. Chicken Tikka Masala If you’re a fan of Indian cuisine, you’ll love this Instant Pot recipe for chicken tikka masala. In just under 30 minutes, you can have a flavorful and aromatic curry dish that is perfect for impressing your guests. Marinate the chicken in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then cook it in the Instant Pot with tomatoes, onions, and a blend of fragrant spices. Serve it with steamed rice or naan bread for a complete meal.

3. French Onion Soup Nothing warms the soul quite like a bowl of French onion soup. Traditionally, this hearty soup takes several hours to make on the stovetop, but with the Instant Pot, you can achieve the same rich flavors in a fraction of the time. Sautee the onions in the Instant Pot until caramelized, then add beef broth, herbs, and Worcestershire sauce. Cook on high pressure for about 15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Top each bowl with a toasted baguette slice and melted cheese for an impressive appetizer or main course.

4. Beef Stew Beef stew is a classic comfort food that is perfect for any occasion. With the Instant Pot, you can achieve tender, melt-in-your-mouth beef and perfectly cooked vegetables in a fraction of the time. Simply brown the beef, add in your favorite vegetables (carrots, potatoes, celery, and peas work well), and cook on high pressure for about 30 minutes. The result is a hearty and flavorful stew that will impress your guests and leave them wanting more.

5. Creamy Tomato Basil Soup If you’re looking for a simple yet elegant Instant Pot recipe, look no further than creamy tomato basil soup. This comforting soup combines the tanginess of tomatoes with the freshness of basil and the creaminess of heavy cream. Sautee onions, garlic, and canned tomatoes in the Instant Pot, then add vegetable broth and basil. Cook on high pressure for about 10 minutes, then use an immersion blender to create a smooth and creamy texture. Serve with crusty bread for a delicious and satisfying meal.

6. Shrimp Scampi Impress your guests with a mouthwatering seafood dish like shrimp scampi. This Instant Pot recipe allows the shrimp to cook quickly and evenly, resulting in tender and succulent shrimp that is bursting with flavor. Sautee garlic, red pepper flakes, and butter in the Instant Pot, then add the shrimp, lemon juice, and white wine. Cook on high pressure for just a few minutes, and voila! Serve over pasta or with crusty bread for a delicious and impressive meal.

7. Vegetarian Chili For those who prefer meatless meals, a vegetarian chili made in the Instant Pot is the way to go. Packed with hearty vegetables, beans, and flavorful spices, this chili is sure to impress even the most die-hard meat lovers. Simply sautee onions, garlic, and bell peppers in the Instant Pot, then add beans, tomatoes, and spices. Cook on high pressure for about 15 minutes, and you’ll have a delicious and satisfying vegetarian chili that will leave your guests asking for the recipe.

8. Mushroom Risotto Risotto is a classic Italian dish that can be time-consuming to make on the stovetop. However, with the Instant Pot, you can achieve perfectly cooked and creamy mushroom risotto in a fraction of the time. Sautee mushrooms, onions, and garlic in the Instant Pot, then add Arborio rice, vegetable broth, and seasonings. Cook on high pressure for about 5 minutes, then stir in parmesan cheese and butter for a rich and indulgent dish that will impress your guests.

9. Lemon Garlic Butter Chicken If you’re looking for a quick and flavorful chicken recipe, try lemon garlic butter chicken in the Instant Pot. The combination of tangy lemon, fragrant garlic, and rich butter creates a mouthwatering sauce that takes ordinary chicken breasts to the next level. Simply brown the chicken in the Instant Pot, then add chicken broth, lemon juice, garlic, and butter. Cook on high pressure for about 8 minutes, and you’ll have tender and juicy chicken that will wow your guests.

10. Chocolate Lava Cake No meal is complete without a sweet treat to end the evening. Impress your guests with a decadent and gooey chocolate lava cake made in the Instant Pot. Mix together a simple batter made of flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and butter, and pour it into individual ramekins. Cook on high pressure for about 8-10 minutes, and you’ll have a rich and indulgent dessert that will have your guests begging for seconds.

Conclusion The Instant Pot is a game-changer in the kitchen, allowing you to create flavorful and impressive dishes in a fraction of the time. From pulled pork and chicken tikka masala to French onion soup and mushroom risotto, these ten delicious Instant Pot recipes are sure to wow your guests and leave them wanting more. So dust off your Instant Pot and get cooking – your taste buds and your guests will thank you!
