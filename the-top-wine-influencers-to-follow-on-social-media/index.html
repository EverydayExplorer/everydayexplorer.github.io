The Top Wine Influencers to Follow on Social Media


When it comes to wine, there are plenty of experts and enthusiasts who are making a big impact on social media. Whether you’re a seasoned wine connoisseur or just starting to explore the world of wine, following these top influencers is a great way to stay informed, inspired, and entertained. Here are some of the top wine influencers to follow on social media:

1. Gary Vaynerchuk (@garyvee)
Gary Vaynerchuk is a well-known entrepreneur, author, and wine expert who rose to fame with his popular YouTube channel, Wine Library TV. His no-nonsense approach to wine tasting and reviews has made him a go-to source for wine enthusiasts looking for honest and straightforward advice. You can find him on Instagram and Twitter sharing his latest wine discoveries and insights.

2. Jancis Robinson (@jancisrobinson)
Jancis Robinson is one of the most respected wine critics and writers in the industry. Her website, jancisrobinson.com, is a treasure trove of wine information, from tasting notes and vintage charts to articles and videos. Follow her on Twitter and Instagram for her latest wine recommendations and musings on all things wine-related.

3. Jon Bonné (@jbonne)
Jon Bonné is a renowned wine writer and critic who has contributed to publications such as The New York Times and Punch. He is known for his deep knowledge of the wine industry and his insightful commentary on trends and developments in the wine world. Follow him on Twitter for his take on the latest wine news and trends.

4. Alder Yarrow (@vinography)
Alder Yarrow is the man behind Vinography, one of the most popular wine blogs on the internet. With a focus on wine tasting notes, reviews, and interviews with winemakers, Vinography is a must-read for wine lovers. Follow Alder on Instagram and Twitter for his latest wine discoveries and tasting experiences.

5. Marissa A. Ross (@marissaaross)
Marissa A. Ross is a wine writer, author, and comedian known for her fresh and fun approach to wine. Her book, ‘Wine. All the Time.: The Casual Guide to Confident Drinking,’ is a bestseller and a favorite among wine enthusiasts. Follow her on Instagram for her humorous take on all things wine-related.

6. Eric Asimov (@ericasimov)
Eric Asimov is the wine critic for The New York Times and a respected authority on wine. His articles and reviews are highly regarded for their depth and insight, making him a must-follow for anyone interested in wine. Follow him on Twitter for his latest wine recommendations and commentary on the wine industry.

7. Levi Dalton (@leviopenswine)
Levi Dalton is a sommelier and wine industry veteran known for his podcast, ‘I’ll Drink to That!’ With interviews featuring some of the biggest names in the wine world, Levi’s podcast is a must-listen for anyone looking to learn more about wine. Follow him on Instagram for behind-the-scenes glimpses of the wine world and updates on his latest projects.

8. Wine Folly (@winefolly)
Wine Folly is a popular website and social media presence that aims to make wine education fun and accessible for everyone. With colorful infographics, informative articles, and a range of wine-related products, Wine Folly is a great resource for wine enthusiasts of all levels. Follow them on Instagram and Twitter for their latest tips, tricks, and wine recommendations.

9. Delectable (@delectable)
Delectable is a popular wine app that allows users to track and share their wine experiences. With a community of wine lovers from around the world, Delectable is a great place to discover new wines, read reviews, and connect with fellow wine enthusiasts. Follow them on Instagram and Twitter for daily wine inspiration and recommendations.

10. Wine Enthusiast (@wineenthusiast)
Wine Enthusiast is a leading wine publication that covers everything from wine reviews and buying guides to travel tips and food pairings. With a team of expert writers and contributors, Wine Enthusiast is a trusted source for all things wine-related. Follow them on Instagram and Twitter for their latest articles, reviews, and recommendations.

These are just a few of the top wine influencers to follow on social media. Whether you’re looking for expert wine recommendations, entertaining wine content, or a community of like-minded wine enthusiasts, following these influencers is a great way to stay connected to the exciting and ever-evolving world of wine. Cheers!
