Comedy and Music: Songs That Will Make You Burst with Laughter


It’s a universal truth that laughter is the best medicine. And what better way to induce a fit of giggles than through the magical combination of comedy and music? In this blog post, we’ll explore a curated selection of songs that are guaranteed to make you burst with laughter. From witty lyrics to hilarious melodies, these tunes are the perfect remedy for a bad day or a case of the blues.

1. ‘Weird Al’ Yankovic - ‘Eat It’:
Starting off our list with a bang, ‘Weird Al’ Yankovic is a master of musical comedy. With his parody of Michael Jackson’s ‘Beat It,’ Yankovic serves up a hilarious take on overindulgence in the form of food. The clever lyrics and catchy chorus will have you laughing and singing along in no time.

2. Flight of the Conchords - ‘Business Time’:
This New Zealand comedy duo knows how to tickle our funny bones with their unique blend of humor and music. ‘Business Time’ takes a humorous look at the mundane aspects of relationships, specifically those intimate moments when things might not go as planned. The deadpan delivery and clever wordplay make this song an instant classic.

3. The Lonely Island - ‘I’m on a Boat’:
Known for their viral comedy sketches and catchy tunes, The Lonely Island delivers a hilarious anthem with ‘I’m on a Boat.’ Featuring T-Pain, this song celebrates the absurdity of bragging about ordinary accomplishments in the most outrageous way possible. Prepare to laugh out loud as you imagine yourself sailing the high seas on a vessel of questionable seaworthiness.

4. Monty Python - ‘Always Look on the Bright Side of Life’:
From the iconic film ‘Life of Brian,’ this song is a comedic masterpiece by the legendary Monty Python troupe. With its tongue-in-cheek lyrics and cheerful melody, ‘Always Look on the Bright Side of Life’ reminds us to find humor in even the most challenging situations. It’s a song that will have you whistling and smiling through life’s ups and downs.

5. Tenacious D - ‘Tribute’:
Comedy rock duo Tenacious D, comprised of Jack Black and Kyle Gass, delivers a hilarious and epic tribute to the greatest song in the world. With their signature blend of rock and comedy, ‘Tribute’ takes you on a wild journey filled with supernatural encounters and musical showdowns. It’s a song that will leave you in stitches while rocking out to the infectious riffs.

6. The Lonely Island - ‘Lazy Sunday’:
Returning to The Lonely Island, ‘Lazy Sunday’ is a comedic rap anthem that perfectly captures the joy of a lazy weekend day. With references to pop culture and mundane activities, this song will have you nodding your head and chuckling along as you relate to the hilarity of ordinary life.

7. Ray Stevens - ‘The Streak’:
Closing out our list is a classic comedy song from the ’70s. Ray Stevens’ ‘The Streak’ tells the hilarious tale of streaking, a popular fad at the time. With its catchy chorus and witty lyrics, this song will transport you back to a time when streaking was all the rage and laughter filled the air.

So there you have it, a collection of songs that will undoubtedly make you burst with laughter. From parody to rap, these comedic tunes offer a delightful blend of humor and music that is sure to brighten your day. So put on your headphones, crank up the volume, and get ready to laugh your heart out with these side-splitting songs. Just be careful not to burst into laughter at inappropriate moments!
