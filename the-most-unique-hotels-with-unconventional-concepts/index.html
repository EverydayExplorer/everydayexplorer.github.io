The Most Unique Hotels with Unconventional Concepts


The hospitality industry is always evolving, and with it comes a demand for unique and unconventional hotels that offer guests an experience like no other. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most extraordinary hotels around the world, each with its own unconventional concept that sets it apart from the rest.

1. The Icehotel, Sweden: Located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel is rebuilt every year using ice and snow from the nearby Torne River. Guests can sleep in rooms made entirely of ice or opt for a warmer option in the heated accommodations. The hotel also features an ice bar, ice sculptures, and even a chapel for those looking to tie the knot in a truly unique setting.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya: Ever dreamed of having breakfast with giraffes? At Giraffe Manor, that dream becomes a reality. This boutique hotel in Nairobi allows guests to get up close and personal with the resident Rothschild giraffes. These gentle giants freely roam the grounds and often pop their heads through the windows during mealtime, creating an unforgettable experience.

3. The Treehotel, Sweden: Nestled in the forest of Harads, the Treehotel offers guests a chance to stay in one-of-a-kind treehouses designed by some of Scandinavia’s leading architects. Each treehouse has its own distinct design, from a mirrored cube to a UFO-inspired pod. Guests can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding nature and immerse themselves in the tranquility of the forest.

4. The Dog Bark Park Inn, USA: Located in Idaho, this hotel is shaped like a giant beagle. Inside, guests will find cozy accommodations and dog-themed decor. The hotel is run by a couple who are skilled chainsaw artists, and their unique creations can be found throughout the property. Dog lovers will surely find this quirky hotel a delight.

5. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland: For a truly magical experience, look no further than Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort. This remote resort offers guests the chance to stay in glass igloos, allowing them to admire the Northern Lights from the comfort of their warm beds. In addition to the igloos, the resort also offers traditional log cabins and snow igloos for a more adventurous stay.

6. The Manta Resort, Tanzania: This underwater hotel takes luxury to new depths. Located on Pemba Island, The Manta Resort features an underwater room where guests can sleep surrounded by the vibrant marine life of the Indian Ocean. The room’s large windows offer breathtaking views, making it a truly unforgettable experience.

7. The Free Spirit Spheres, Canada: Tucked away in the lush forests of Vancouver Island, these spherical treehouses offer a unique and immersive stay. Suspended from trees, the spheres provide a cozy and eco-friendly accommodation option. Guests can enjoy the tranquility of the forest and reconnect with nature in this unconventional hotel.

These are just a few examples of the incredible hotels with unconventional concepts that exist around the world. Whether you’re looking for a stay in an ice palace, a treehouse, or an underwater paradise, these unique hotels offer experiences that go far beyond the ordinary. So, if you’re seeking an unforgettable adventure, consider stepping outside the traditional hotel box and exploring these remarkable destinations.
