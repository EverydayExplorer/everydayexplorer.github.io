Crafting with Seashells: Beachy Decor and Keepsakes


Seashells are not only beautiful treasures found along the shore, but they also hold a world of creative possibilities. Whether you’re a beach enthusiast or simply looking to bring a touch of the coast into your home, crafting with seashells can be a delightful and rewarding experience. In this blog post, we will explore various ways to use seashells to create beachy decor and keepsakes that will infuse your living space with a coastal charm.

1. Seashell Wind Chimes:
One of the most enchanting ways to incorporate seashells into your home decor is by creating a seashell wind chime. Start by collecting an assortment of seashells in different sizes and shapes. Then, using a sturdy string or fishing line, thread the shells together, leaving enough space between each shell to allow for movement. Hang your seashell wind chime near a window or on your porch, and let the gentle sea breeze create a soothing melody.

2. Seashell Picture Frames:
Turn ordinary picture frames into beach-inspired masterpieces by adorning them with seashells. Begin by selecting a plain wooden or plastic frame. Apply a layer of craft glue onto the frame’s surface, and carefully arrange the seashells in a pattern or design of your choice. Allow the glue to dry completely before inserting your favorite beach vacation photo or a beautiful seashore landscape.

3. Seashell Candles:
Create a warm and inviting ambiance by making seashell candles. Start by melting wax in a double boiler and adding your favorite fragrance oil for a coastal scent. Place a wick in the center of a seashell, and carefully pour the melted wax into the shell. Allow the wax to cool and solidify before trimming the wick. These unique seashell candles will add a touch of coastal elegance to any room.

4. Seashell Jewelry:
Transform seashells into stunning pieces of jewelry that capture the essence of the beach. Choose shells with natural holes or drill small holes in them. Thread a thin wire or string through the holes, and add beads or charms for extra flair. Create necklaces, bracelets, earrings, or even anklets that will remind you of the ocean’s beauty wherever you go.

5. Seashell Terrariums:
Bring a piece of the beach indoors by creating seashell terrariums. Begin by selecting a glass container with a lid, such as a mason jar or a fishbowl. Fill the bottom with sand or small pebbles, and arrange an assortment of seashells, along with some dried seaweed or beach grass. Add a few air plants or succulents for a touch of greenery. Place the lid on the container, and display your seashell terrarium on a shelf or as a centerpiece.

Crafting with seashells allows you to tap into your creativity while bringing the beauty of the beach into your home. Whether you choose to make wind chimes, picture frames, candles, jewelry, or terrariums, each project will serve as a reminder of the tranquility and serenity found by the seashore. So go ahead, gather your seashells, and let your imagination take you on a coastal crafting adventure.
