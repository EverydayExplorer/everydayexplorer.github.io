Targeting Trophy Pike: Tactics and Locations


When it comes to targeting trophy pike, there are a few tactics and locations that avid anglers swear by. Pike fishing can be an exhilarating experience, especially when you land a massive trophy fish. In this blog post, we will be delving into some proven tactics and top locations for targeting trophy pike.

Tactics for Targeting Trophy Pike

  1. Choosing the Right Lure: Pike are known for their aggressive nature and will strike at almost anything that resembles a potential meal. However, there are a few tried and tested lure types that work particularly well for targeting trophy pike. Large spoons, topwater plugs, and jerkbaits are all effective choices. Using lures that mimic the natural prey of pike, such as smaller fish or injured baitfish, can greatly increase your chances of catching a trophy pike.

  2. Retrieving Techniques: The retrieve technique can greatly influence the success of your pike fishing expedition. For trophy-sized pike, a slow and steady retrieve is often more effective. Pike are known for their ambush strategies, so a slower retrieve can entice them to strike. Additionally, incorporating pauses and jerks into your retrieval can mimic injured prey and trigger a pike’s predatory instincts.

  3. Fishing Time: Pike are notorious for being most active during low-light conditions, such as early mornings or late evenings. This is when they are most likely to be feeding and searching for prey. Adjust your fishing schedule accordingly to maximize your chances of landing a trophy-sized pike.

  4. Using Wire Leaders: Pike have razor-sharp teeth that can easily cut through traditional fishing lines. To prevent losing your catch, it is essential to use wire leaders when targeting pike. Wire leaders not only provide the necessary strength to handle aggressive strikes but also act as a protective barrier between the pike’s teeth and your fishing line.

  5. Location, Location, Location: The choice of fishing location can greatly impact your chances of catching trophy pike. Focus on areas with ample cover, such as weed beds, fallen logs, and rocky shorelines. Pike use these structures as hiding spots to ambush their prey. Shallow bays and narrow channels are also prime locations to target trophy pike, as they offer ideal conditions for their hunting strategies.

Top Locations for Targeting Trophy Pike

  1. Northern Canada: The expansive lakes and rivers of Northern Canada are known for harboring monster pike. Destinations such as Great Slave Lake, Lake Athabasca, and Lac La Martre offer prime fishing grounds for trophy-sized pike. With their vast water bodies, plentiful baitfish populations, and limited fishing pressure, these remote locations provide the perfect habitat for trophy pike.

  2. Alaska: Alaska is a renowned destination for fishing enthusiasts, and pike fishing is no exception. The state offers numerous lakes and rivers that are home to massive pike. The iconic Kenai River, Chena River, and Shageluk Slough are just a few examples of top pike fishing locations in Alaska. The abundance of salmon and other food sources in these waters makes them a haven for trophy pike.

  3. Scandinavia: Countries like Sweden, Finland, and Norway are also known for their world-class pike fishing. The lakes and rivers of Scandinavia are rich in underwater structures and offer ample opportunities to target trophy pike. Destinations like the Archipelago Sea, Lake Inari, and the Dalälven River are well-regarded for their pike fishing potential.

  4. Northern United States: Several states in the northern U.S. are home to exceptional pike fisheries. Minnesota, Wisconsin, and Michigan are known for their abundance of lakes and rivers that provide excellent habitat for trophy pike. In these regions, anglers can target pike in both large and small bodies of water, each with its own unique charm and challenges.

  5. European Waters: Pike fishing in Europe has gained significant popularity, especially in countries like Ireland, England, and the Netherlands. Europe offers diverse fishing opportunities, ranging from big lakes to intricate canal systems. The picturesque landscapes and rich pike populations make these destinations ideal for targeting trophy-sized pike.

In conclusion, targeting trophy pike requires a combination of effective tactics and fishing in the right locations. Armed with the right lures, proper retrieval techniques, and knowledge of the best fishing spots, you can significantly increase your chances of catching a trophy pike. Whether you venture to remote Canadian lakes or explore the waters of Scandinavia, the thrill of landing a massive pike is a reward in itself. So grab your gear, study the tactics mentioned above, and embark on your next adventure in pursuit of trophy pike!
