10 Underrated Music Artists You Need to Listen to


Are you tired of listening to the same mainstream music over and over again? Do you crave something fresh, unique, and truly underrated? Then this blog post is for you! We have compiled a list of 10 incredibly talented music artists who deserve way more recognition than they currently receive. From various genres and backgrounds, these artists will blow your mind with their exceptional skills and captivating music.

1. Natalie Prass

If you’re a fan of alternative pop, then Natalie Prass is the artist you need to listen to. Hailing from Richmond, Virginia, her soulful vocals and enchanting melodies will transport you to another world. With introspective lyrics and dreamy orchestration, Natalie Prass’s music is a breath of fresh air in the pop music scene.

2. Moses Sumney

Moses Sumney is an artist who defies categorization. His music sits comfortably between experimental soul, folk, and R&B, creating a mesmerizing blend that is entirely his own. With powerful lyrics and a voice that can move mountains, Sumney’s music is both thought-provoking and emotionally charged.

3. Hurray for the Riff Raff

Hurray for the Riff Raff, fronted by the talented Alynda Segarra, is a folk band that effortlessly blends Americana, blues, and indie rock. Their music is raw, evocative, and deeply rooted in storytelling. With socially conscious lyrics and soul-stirring melodies, Hurray for the Riff Raff is a hidden gem in the music industry.

4. Tank and The Bangas

Tank and The Bangas are an eclectic group hailing from New Orleans. their music is an exhilarating mix of funk, soul, hip-hop, and spoken word poetry. Led by the charismatic Tarriona ‘Tank’ Ball, their live performances are an experience like no other, filled with contagious energy and infectious grooves.

5. Big Thief

Big Thief’s music is intimate, raw, and deeply personal. Fronted by the soulful voice of Adrianne Lenker, this indie folk-rock band has captured the hearts of music aficionados around the world. With hauntingly beautiful melodies and introspective lyrics, Big Thief’s music will tug at your heartstrings and leave you longing for more.

6. Noname

Noname, also known as Fatimah Warner, is a talented rapper and poet from Chicago. Her music is a refreshing departure from the mainstream rap scene, with intricate wordplay and socially conscious lyrics. Noname’s unique style and thought-provoking verses make her one of the most exciting voices in hip-hop today.

7. Amber Mark

Amber Mark is a rising star in the R&B and pop music scene. With her warm vocals and infectious beats, she effortlessly blends genres and creates a sound that is uniquely her own. Amber Mark’s music is a testament to her versatility as an artist, and her catchy tunes will have you singing along in no time.

8. serpentwithfeet

serpentwithfeet, the stage name for Josiah Wise, is an experimental artist who pushes the boundaries of R&B and gospel music. His powerful vocals, combined with intricate production and haunting melodies, create a truly immersive and captivating listening experience. serpentwithfeet’s music is bold, innovative, and demands your full attention.

9. Raveena

Raveena is a rising star in the neo-soul and R&B genre. Her sultry vocals, combined with dreamy production, create an ethereal and enchanting atmosphere. Raveena’s music is intimate and introspective, exploring themes of love, self-acceptance, and healing. Her soothing melodies and heartfelt lyrics will transport you to a place of tranquility.

10. Cigarettes After Sex

Cigarettes After Sex is an indie pop and dream pop band known for their atmospheric soundscapes and hazy melodies. Led by the haunting vocals of Greg Gonzalez, their music is deeply emotional and evokes a sense of longing and nostalgia. Cigarettes After Sex’s music is the perfect soundtrack for late-night introspection and contemplation.

In conclusion, these 10 underrated music artists are a testament to the vast amount of talent present in the music industry. Each artist brings something unique to the table, whether it be soul-stirring lyrics, captivating melodies, or boundary-pushing experimentation. So, take a break from the mainstream and immerse yourself in the undiscovered brilliance of these artists. You won’t be disappointed!
