Cooking with Kids: Fun and Easy Recipes for Family Time


Cooking with kids can be a fun and rewarding experience for both parents and children. Not only does it allow for quality family time, but it also teaches kids valuable skills such as following directions, measuring ingredients, and problem-solving.

However, it can be difficult to find recipes that are both easy enough for kids to participate in and enjoyable for the whole family to eat. That’s why we’ve gathered a list of fun and easy recipes that are sure to become family favorites.

1. Pizza Bagels

Pizza is a classic favorite for kids and adults alike, and making mini pizzas on bagels is a simple and easy way to get everyone involved in the cooking process. All you need are bagels, tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings of your choice. Let your kids spread the sauce, sprinkle the cheese, and add their favorite vegetables or meats. Pop them in the oven for a few minutes and you have your own personalized pizzas!

2. Tacos

Tacos are another family favorite that can be easily customized to individual preferences. Set up a taco bar with cooked ground beef, shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, lettuce, salsa, and toppings like sour cream and guacamole. Let your kids assemble their own tacos and have fun with it.

3. Pancakes

Breakfast for dinner is always a hit with kids, and cooking pancakes together is a fun and easy way to get everyone involved. Use a simple pancake mix and have your kids measure out the ingredients, mix the batter, and pour it on the griddle. Top with fresh fruit, chocolate chips, or whipped cream for an extra treat.

4. Smoothies

Smoothies are a healthy and delicious way to sneak in some fruits and veggies. Let your kids pick out their favorite ingredients and blend them together for a fun and easy snack. Frozen fruit, yogurt, milk, and spinach make for a tasty and nutritious smoothie.

5. S’mores Dip

Who says you can’t have dessert for dinner? S’mores dip is a fun and easy way to enjoy the classic campfire treat without leaving your kitchen. All you need is chocolate chips, mini marshmallows, and graham crackers for dipping. Melt the chocolate and marshmallows together in the oven or microwave and enjoy!

Cooking with kids doesn’t have to be complicated or intimidating. These fun and easy recipes are sure to become family favorites and create lasting memories. So why not take some time to cook together as a family and enjoy the delicious results?
