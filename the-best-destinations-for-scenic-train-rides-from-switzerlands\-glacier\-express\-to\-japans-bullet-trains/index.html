The Best Destinations for Scenic Train Rides: From Switzerlands\ Glacier\ Express\ to\ Japans Bullet Trains


When it comes to experiencing breathtaking landscapes, few modes of transportation can compare to train rides. Whether it’s the mesmerizing views of snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, or serene coastal stretches, train journeys offer a unique perspective that allows travelers to soak in the beauty of their surroundings. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best destinations for scenic train rides, from Switzerland’s Glacier Express to Japan’s famous Bullet Trains.

1. Switzerland: The Glacier Express

Switzerland’s Glacier Express is often referred to as the ‘world’s slowest express train,’ and for good reason. This scenic train route takes passengers on a mesmerizing 8-hour journey from Zermatt to St. Moritz, covering over 180 miles in the process. The train winds its way through the picturesque Swiss Alps, offering panoramic views of majestic peaks, quaint villages, and pristine alpine meadows. This classic train ride is perfect for those who want to experience Switzerland’s natural beauty in all its glory.

2. Norway: The Flam Railway

Considered one of the most beautiful train rides in the world, the Flam Railway in Norway takes passengers on a journey through breathtaking fjords, cascading waterfalls, and snow-capped mountains. The train ride starts in Flam and ends in Myrdal, offering travelers a 20-kilometer journey that takes approximately an hour. Along the way, you’ll have the opportunity to witness some of Norway’s most stunning scenery, including the Aurlandsfjord and the UNESCO-listed Nærøyfjord.

3. Canada: The Rocky Mountaineer

Venture to the heart of the Canadian Rockies with the Rocky Mountaineer, a luxurious train journey that showcases the beauty of Western Canada. This iconic train ride offers various routes, including the First Passage to the West, which takes passengers from Vancouver to Banff, and the Journey through the Clouds, which travels from Vancouver to Jasper. Onboard the train, you’ll be treated to immense wildlife, towering mountains, and stunning rivers. The Rocky Mountaineer is truly a bucket list experience for nature enthusiasts.

4. Japan: The Bullet Trains

Known for their speed and efficiency, Japan’s Bullet Trains, or Shinkansen, offer a unique way to explore the country’s diverse landscapes. These high-speed trains connect major cities such as Tokyo, Osaka, and Kyoto, and offer passengers a comfortable and efficient way to travel between destinations. While the scenery might not be as dramatic as other train rides on this list, the Bullet Trains provide glimpses of Japan’s beautiful countryside, including stunning views of Mount Fuji on clear days.

5. Scotland: The Jacobite Steam Train

For fans of the Harry Potter series, the Jacobite Steam Train in Scotland holds a special allure. This iconic train journey takes passengers on a scenic ride from Fort William to Mallaig, passing through the beautiful Scottish Highlands. Often described as one of the greatest train journeys in the world, the Jacobite Steam Train offers breathtaking views of Loch Shiel, Glenfinnan Viaduct (famously featured in the Harry Potter films), and the rugged landscape of the Scottish countryside.

6. New Zealand: The TranzAlpine

New Zealand’s TranzAlpine is a train journey that showcases the country’s stunning natural beauty. The route starts in Christchurch and ends in Greymouth, taking passengers through the heart of the Southern Alps. Along the way, you’ll witness snow-capped peaks, river valleys, and lush beech forests. The TranzAlpine is known for its panoramic windows and open-air viewing carriage, allowing travelers to fully immerse themselves in the breathtaking scenery.

Conclusion

Embarking on a scenic train ride is a wonderful way to explore the natural beauty of a country. From Switzerland’s Glacier Express to Japan’s Bullet Trains, each destination offers a unique and unforgettable experience for travelers. Whether you’re seeking breathtaking alpine views, mesmerizing fjords, or picturesque countryside, these scenic train rides are sure to leave you in awe. So, hop on board and get ready for a journey that will take you through some of the world’s most stunning landscapes.
