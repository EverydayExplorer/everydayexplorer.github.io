10 Makeup Trends to Try This Season: Runway-Inspired Beauty Looks


Hello there! Are you ready to dive into the fascinating world of makeup trends? Look no further, because we’re here to guide you through the top 10 runway-inspired beauty looks of the season. Get ready to add a touch of creativity and glamour to your makeup routine!

1. Graphic Eyeliner: Take your eyeliner game to new heights this season by experimenting with bold graphic shapes. From dramatic wings to geometric shapes, this trend allows you to express your artistic side and make a statement with your eyes.

2. Neon Pop: Embrace the vibrant energy of neon hues and incorporate them into your makeup palette. Whether it’s a neon pink lip or a pop of fluorescent eyeshadow, this trend adds an instant burst of color and excitement to your overall look.

3. Glitter Everywhere: Sparkle and shine like never before with the glitter trend that has taken the runways by storm. Apply glitter to your eyelids, cheeks, or even your lips to create a dazzling and magical effect.

4. Glossy Lips: Bid farewell to matte lips and welcome back the glossy pout. This season, opt for shiny and luscious lips that give off a youthful and fresh vibe. Experiment with clear gloss or try on bold, glossy colors to make a statement.

5. Blurred Lips: Achieve that effortless, just-bitten look with the blurred lips trend. This technique involves softly blending your lip color towards the edges, creating a soft and slightly smudged effect. It’s perfect for those who want a more subtle and natural lip look.

6. Monochromatic Makeup: Create a harmonious and cohesive look by matching your eyeshadow, blush, and lip color. The monochromatic makeup trend allows you to experiment with various shades within the same color family, resulting in a polished and sophisticated appearance.

7. Floating Eyeliner: Break free from traditional eyeliner styles and try the floating eyeliner trend. Instead of lining your upper lash line, apply a thin line of eyeliner slightly above the crease or on the lower lash line for a modern and edgy look.

8. Bold Brows: Frame your face with bold, well-defined eyebrows. This season, embrace the power of brows by filling them in with precision and allowing them to make a statement. Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to achieve a strong, yet natural-looking brow.

9. Unexpected Color Pairings: Dare to be different by combining unexpected color combinations in your makeup looks. Whether it’s a bold orange eyeshadow paired with a blue eyeliner or a lavender blush with a coral lip, this trend encourages you to step out of your comfort zone and have fun with color.

10. Dewy Skin: Achieve that coveted healthy glow with the dewy skin trend. Opt for lightweight foundations or tinted moisturizers that give your skin a radiant and luminous finish. Set it with a subtle highlighter on your cheekbones and the bridge of your nose for an ethereal glow.

Now that you’re armed with the top 10 makeup trends of the season, it’s time to get creative and experiment with these runway-inspired beauty looks. Remember, makeup is all about self-expression and having fun, so don’t be afraid to step outside your comfort zone and embrace your inner artist. Let your imagination run wild and enjoy the journey of discovering new and exciting makeup trends to enhance your natural beauty.
