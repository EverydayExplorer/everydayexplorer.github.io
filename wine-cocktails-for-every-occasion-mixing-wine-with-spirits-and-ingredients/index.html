Wine Cocktails for Every Occasion: Mixing Wine with Spirits and Ingredients


When we think of wine, the mind often drifts to a cozy night in, a fine dining experience, or perhaps a toast during a momentous occasion. Rarely do we consider the versatility of wine in the realm of cocktails. Wine, when combined with various spirits and ingredients, can create a delightful array of beverages perfect for any event or gathering.

In this comprehensive guide, we will embark on a journey to discover wine cocktails for every occasion, ensuring that you always have the perfect blend for that special moment.

1. Sparkling Wine Spritzers

Occasion: Brunch or summer garden parties.

Ingredients: - Sparkling wine (like Prosecco or Cava) - A splash of soda water - A slice of citrus (like orange or lemon) - A sprig of fresh herbs (like mint or basil)

Instructions: Fill half a glass with sparkling wine, add a splash of soda, garnish with citrus and herbs. This spritzer is refreshing and light - perfect for a midday get-together.

2. Sangria

Occasion: BBQs or casual gatherings.

Ingredients: - Red wine (like Tempranillo, Garnacha) - Chopped fruits (oranges, lemons, apples, berries) - 1-2 tbsp sugar - A splash of brandy or rum - Soda water or lemon-lime soda

Instructions: In a large pitcher, combine wine, fruits, sugar, and brandy. Let it sit for a few hours or overnight. Before serving, add soda for a fizzy touch.
