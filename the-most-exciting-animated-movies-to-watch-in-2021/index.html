The Most Exciting Animated Movies to Watch in 2021


Welcome, movie enthusiasts! Today, we are here to dive into the exciting world of animated movies and explore the most anticipated releases of 2021. From heartwarming tales to action-packed adventures, this year promises to be a treat for animation lovers of all ages.

1. “Raya and the Last Dragon”: Kicking off our list is Disney’s much-anticipated film, “Raya and the Last Dragon.” This epic fantasy adventure takes us on a journey to the mystical world of Kumandra, where Raya, a young warrior, embarks on a quest to find the last dragon and save her realm from an ancient evil. With stunning visuals, a captivating story, and a diverse cast, this film is set to be an instant classic.

2. “Luca”: From the talented creators at Pixar Animation Studios comes “Luca,” a heartwarming story set on the Italian Riviera. This coming-of-age tale follows a young sea monster named Luca, who experiences an unforgettable summer filled with friendship and self-discovery. With its charming animation style and heartfelt narrative, “Luca” promises to be an enchanting delight for all ages.

3. “The Mitchells vs. The Machines”: Get ready for a hilarious and action-packed ride with “The Mitchells vs. The Machines.” Produced by Phil Lord and Christopher Miller, the masterminds behind “Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse,” this film follows the misadventures of the quirky Mitchell family as they find themselves in the middle of a robot uprising. With its irreverent humor and visually stunning animation, this movie is sure to keep you entertained from start to finish.

4. “Spirit Untamed”: For those who love spirited adventures, DreamWorks Animation’s “Spirit Untamed” is a must-watch. This film is a continuation of the beloved “Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron” and follows the journey of a young girl named Lucky, who forms a special bond with a wild mustang named Spirit. With its breathtaking animation and empowering story, “Spirit Untamed” celebrates the unbreakable bond between humans and animals.

5. “Vivo”: From the creators of “Moana” and “Hamilton” comes “Vivo,” a musical adventure that will have you tapping your feet and singing along. This Sony Pictures Animation film follows a musically gifted kinkajou named Vivo as he embarks on a journey to deliver a song to his long-lost love. With original songs by Lin-Manuel Miranda and vibrant visuals, “Vivo” promises to be a joyful celebration of music, friendship, and following your dreams.

6. “The Addams Family 2”: If you’re a fan of the kooky and spooky, “The Addams Family 2” is here to satisfy your cravings. In this animated sequel, our favorite eccentric family embarks on a hilarious road trip across America, encountering all sorts of quirky characters along the way. With its dark humor, unique animation style, and lovable characters, this film is a treat for both existing fans and newcomers alike.

7. “Lightyear”: Prepare for a journey to infinity and beyond with “Lightyear,” an exciting prequel to the iconic “Toy Story” franchise. This film takes us back to the origins of Buzz Lightyear, the beloved space ranger, and explores the thrilling adventures that shaped him into the hero we know and love. With state-of-the-art animation and an all-star cast, “Lightyear” is set to redefine the boundaries of animated storytelling.

These are just a few of the many animated movies set to grace the screens in 2021. From epic fantasies to heartwarming tales, this year’s lineup promises to captivate audiences with its stunning visuals, compelling stories, and unforgettable characters. So mark your calendars, gather your loved ones, and get ready to embark on these exciting animated adventures. Happy movie-watching!
