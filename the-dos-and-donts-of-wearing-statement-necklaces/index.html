The Dos and Don'ts of Wearing Statement Necklaces


Statement necklaces are a fantastic way to add a touch of glamour and personality to any outfit. These bold and eye-catching accessories can instantly elevate your look and make a statement. However, it’s important to know the dos and don’ts of wearing statement necklaces to ensure that you rock them with confidence and style. In this blog post, we will explore some valuable tips that will help you master the art of wearing statement necklaces.

Dos:

1. Choose the Right Neckline: When wearing a statement necklace, it’s essential to consider the neckline of your outfit. Opt for necklines that create a blank canvas for your necklace to shine. Scoop necks, strapless, and off-the-shoulder styles are perfect choices. These necklines provide enough space for the necklace to take center stage without overwhelming your overall look.

2. Keep it Simple: Statement necklaces are meant to be the focal point of your outfit, so it’s important not to overpower them with other accessories. Keep your earrings, bracelets, and rings minimal to let your necklace steal the show. A simple pair of stud earrings or no earrings at all will keep the focus on the statement necklace.

3. Understand Proportions: Consider the size and scale of the necklace in relation to your body frame. If you have a petite frame, go for smaller, delicate statement necklaces. For those with a larger frame, bolder and chunkier statement necklaces will complement your body shape better. It’s all about finding the right balance between the necklace and your body proportions.

4. Play with Colors and Patterns: Statement necklaces come in a variety of colors and patterns, so don’t be afraid to experiment. Use them as an opportunity to add a pop of color or introduce a new pattern to your outfit. Just make sure that the colors and patterns complement your outfit and don’t clash with it.

Don’ts:

1. Avoid Busy Necklines: Avoid wearing statement necklaces with tops or dresses that have busy patterns or embellishments around the neckline. This can create a cluttered look and distract from the beauty of the necklace. Opt for simpler necklines that allow the necklace to stand out.

2. Say No to Statement Earrings: While it’s tempting to pair statement necklaces with equally bold earrings, it’s best to avoid this combination. The two can compete for attention and create an overwhelming look. Instead, let the necklace be the star and keep your earrings understated.

3. Don’t Wear with High Necklines: High necklines, such as turtlenecks or crew necks, can clash with statement necklaces. The two can create a visually cluttered look, and the necklace may get hidden or tangled in the fabric. Stick to lower necklines that allow the necklace to sit comfortably on your collarbone or chest.

4. Avoid Overmatching: While it’s important to coordinate your accessories with your outfit, avoid overmatching your statement necklace with the rest of your jewelry. Instead, choose complementary pieces that add a touch of elegance and balance to your overall look.

In conclusion, statement necklaces are a fabulous way to showcase your style and personality. By following these dos and don’ts, you’ll be able to confidently wear statement necklaces and turn heads wherever you go. Remember, it’s all about finding the right balance and letting your necklace take center stage. So go ahead, embrace the boldness, and make a statement with your necklaces!
