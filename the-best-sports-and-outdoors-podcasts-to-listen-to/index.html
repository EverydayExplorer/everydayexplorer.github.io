The Best Sports and Outdoors Podcasts to Listen to


Are you tired of the same old music playlist during your workouts or outdoor adventures? Looking for some inspiration or entertainment to keep you motivated while you break a sweat or explore the great outdoors? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be diving into the world of sports and outdoors podcasts. These podcasts are not only entertaining but also informative, providing you with a wealth of knowledge and inspiration in the realm of sports and outdoor activities. So, grab your headphones and let’s explore some of the best sports and outdoors podcasts to listen to.

1. ‘The Adventure Zone’ - If you’re a fan of tabletop gaming and hilarious storytelling, this podcast is for you. Join the McElroy brothers and their dad as they embark on epic adventures in the world of Dungeons & Dragons. From thrilling battles to heartwarming character development, this podcast is a must-listen for any fan of fantasy and adventure.

2. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ - Hosted by comedian Joe Rogan, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including sports, fitness, and outdoor adventures. With guests ranging from professional athletes to outdoor enthusiasts, you’ll get a unique perspective on the world of sports and outdoor activities.

3. ‘The Rich Roll Podcast’ - If you’re interested in endurance sports, plant-based nutrition, and personal growth, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Rich Roll, an ultra-endurance athlete and wellness advocate, this podcast features inspiring interviews with world-class athletes, thought leaders, and experts in the field of health and wellness.

4. ‘The Dirtbag Diaries’ - This podcast is all about celebrating the adventurous spirit and the stories of people who push their limits in the great outdoors. From rock climbing to mountaineering, this podcast will leave you inspired to get outside and explore.

5. ‘The Bill Simmons Podcast’ - Hosted by sports analyst Bill Simmons, this podcast covers a wide range of sports, including basketball, football, and baseball. With in-depth analysis, interviews, and entertaining discussions, this podcast is a must-listen for any sports fan.

6. ‘The MeatEater Podcast’ - If you’re interested in hunting, fishing, and wild game cooking, this podcast is for you. Join host Steven Rinella as he explores the world of hunting and fishing, sharing stories, tips, and recipes along the way.

7. ‘The Outside Podcast’ - Produced by Outside Magazine, this podcast covers a wide range of outdoor topics, including adventure travel, environmental issues, and outdoor gear. With captivating storytelling and expert interviews, this podcast is perfect for anyone looking to connect with nature and learn more about the world around them.

So, there you have it - a list of some of the best sports and outdoors podcasts to listen to. Whether you’re a sports enthusiast, an outdoor adventurer, or simply looking for some inspiration, these podcasts are sure to keep you entertained and informed. So, grab your phone, subscribe to these podcasts, and get ready for a whole new world of sports and outdoor adventures. Happy listening!
