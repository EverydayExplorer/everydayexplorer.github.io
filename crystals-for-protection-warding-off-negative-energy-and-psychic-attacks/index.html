Crystals for Protection: Warding Off Negative Energy and Psychic Attacks


In the modern world, many people are becoming more interested in alternative methods of healing and protection. One of the most popular methods is the use of crystals. Crystals are believed to possess powerful energy that can help in various aspects of life, including protection from negative energy and psychic attacks. In this blog post, we will explore the different types of crystals that are commonly used for protection purposes and how they can be incorporated into your daily life.

1. Black Tourmaline
Black Tourmaline is one of the most powerful crystals for protection. It is known for its ability to transmute negative energy into positive energy, making it an excellent choice for shielding against psychic attacks. Black Tourmaline is also believed to create a protective barrier around the wearer, preventing any negative energy from entering their aura. This crystal can be worn as jewelry, carried in your pocket, or placed in your home or office to create a protective space.

2. Amethyst
Amethyst is a popular crystal known for its spiritual properties and protective energy. It is often used for psychic development and is believed to enhance intuition and psychic abilities. Amethyst can also act as a shield against negative energies and can help in clearing negative thoughts and emotions. Placing Amethyst in your bedroom or meditation space can create a peaceful environment and protect you from psychic attacks.

3. Selenite
Selenite is a crystal with a high vibration that is often used for cleansing and protection. It is believed to be a powerful tool for removing negative energy and promoting a sense of calm and clarity. Selenite can be placed in your home or workplace to create a protective field and to ward off negative energies. Additionally, Selenite can be used to cleanse other crystals, making it an essential part of any crystal collection.

4. Labradorite
Labradorite is a stone often associated with magic and protection. It is known for its iridescent colors, which are believed to help shield against negative energy and psychic attacks. Labradorite is said to create a barrier that deflects unwanted energies and prevents them from entering your aura. This crystal is also believed to enhance intuition and psychic abilities, making it a powerful ally for spiritual protection.

5. Clear Quartz
Clear Quartz is known as the master healer and amplifier of energy. It is believed to enhance the properties of other crystals and can be used to cleanse and charge them. Clear Quartz can also be programmed with your intentions and affirmations, making it an ideal crystal for protection. By infusing Clear Quartz with your intention to ward off negative energy and psychic attacks, it can act as a powerful shield and amplifier of positive energy.

6. Hematite
Hematite is a stone known for its grounding and protective properties. It is believed to absorb and transmute negative energy, making it an excellent choice for protection from psychic attacks. Hematite can be worn as jewelry or carried in your pocket to create a sense of security and grounding. This crystal is also often used for protection during spiritual practices, such as meditation and energy work.

7. Obsidian
Obsidian is a powerful protective stone that is often used for spiritual and energy protection. It is believed to create a shield that deflects negative energy, psychic attacks, and even psychic vampires. Obsidian can help in grounding and cleansing the energy field, promoting a sense of protection and empowerment. This crystal can be placed in your home or carried with you to create a protective barrier.

Incorporating crystals into your daily life for protection doesn’t have to be complicated. You can choose one or more of these crystals based on your personal intuition or the specific type of protection you are seeking. You can wear them as jewelry, carry them in your pocket or purse, or place them strategically in your home or workplace.

It’s important to remember that while crystals can be powerful tools for protection, they should not replace professional medical or mental health advice. If you are experiencing significant negative energy or psychic attacks, it’s important to seek support from a trained professional.

In conclusion, crystals can be an excellent tool for protection against negative energy and psychic attacks. Whether you choose Black Tourmaline, Amethyst, Selenite, Labradorite, Clear Quartz, Hematite, Obsidian, or a combination of these crystals, they can help create a sense of protection and empowerment in your life. Embrace the energy of these beautiful minerals and let them help you ward off negative energies and enhance your spiritual well-being.
