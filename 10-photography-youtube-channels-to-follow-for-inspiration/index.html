10 Photography YouTube Channels to Follow for Inspiration


In today’s digital age, YouTube has become a treasure trove of inspiration for photographers of all skill levels. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn new techniques or a seasoned professional seeking fresh ideas, there are countless YouTube channels out there that can offer valuable insights and ignite your creative spark. In this blog post, we will be highlighting 10 photography YouTube channels that you should definitely follow for inspiration. So grab your camera and let’s dive in!

1. Peter McKinnon - With his charismatic personality and stunning cinematography, Peter McKinnon has become one of the most popular photography YouTubers. From tutorials on editing techniques to vlogs showcasing his adventures, Peter’s channel is a must-follow for any aspiring photographer.

2. Mango Street - Rachel and Daniel of Mango Street offer a unique blend of education and creativity. Their channel features tutorials, gear reviews, and photo challenges that will help you improve your skills while encouraging you to think outside the box.

3. Jessica Kobeissi - Known for her fashion and portrait photography, Jessica Kobeissi’s channel is a goldmine for anyone interested in capturing the beauty of people. Her videos range from photo shoots with models to behind-the-scenes looks at her editing process.

4. Thomas Heaton - If landscape photography is your passion, Thomas Heaton’s channel is a must-visit. His breathtaking shots of nature, combined with his thoughtful commentary, will not only inspire you but also teach you valuable techniques for capturing stunning landscapes.

5. The Art of Photography - Hosted by Ted Forbes, this channel delves deep into the artistic side of photography. Ted explores various photography genres, interviews industry professionals, and discusses the philosophy behind creating meaningful images.

6. Irene Rudnyk - For those interested in portrait and fashion photography, Irene Rudnyk’s channel offers a wealth of knowledge. She shares her expertise on lighting, posing, and editing, helping aspiring photographers create captivating images.

7. Sean Tucker - Sean Tucker’s channel is a treasure trove of insightful and thought-provoking content. He explores the intersection of photography and philosophy, offering a fresh perspective on the art form and inspiring viewers to think deeper about their craft.

8. Tony Northrup - Tony Northrup’s channel covers a wide range of photography topics, from gear reviews to tutorials on composition and post-processing. With his engaging and informative videos, Tony simplifies complex concepts, making them accessible to photographers of all levels.

9. Jared Polin (FroKnowsPhoto) - Jared Polin’s energetic personality and in-depth reviews make his channel a go-to resource for gear enthusiasts. He also provides tips and tricks to help photographers improve their skills and make the most of their equipment.

10. Lindsay Adler - Lindsay Adler is a renowned fashion photographer whose channel offers a wealth of knowledge on shooting and posing models, as well as creative lighting techniques. Her tutorials and behind-the-scenes videos will undoubtedly inspire your next fashion shoot.

These 10 photography YouTube channels are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to the wealth of inspiration available on the platform. By following these talented content creators, you’ll be well on your way to honing your craft, exploring new techniques, and capturing breathtaking images. So grab your camera, subscribe to these channels, and let the inspiration flow! Happy shooting!
