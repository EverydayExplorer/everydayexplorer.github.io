Hidden Gems: Unique and Unconventional Hotels You Need to Visit


Are you tired of the same old hotel experiences? Looking for something out of the ordinary, something that will leave a lasting impression on your travel memories? Look no further! In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to discover some of the world’s most unique and unconventional hotels that you absolutely need to visit.

1. The Treehotel, Sweden:
Nestled amidst the lush forests of Harads, Sweden, The Treehotel offers a truly enchanting experience. Each room is suspended high up in the trees, blending seamlessly with nature. From the Mirrorcube, where you can literally disappear into the surroundings, to the UFO, resembling a spaceship, this hotel is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

2. Giraffe Manor, Kenya:
Ever dreamt of having breakfast with a giraffe? At Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya, this dream becomes a reality. This boutique hotel allows guests to interact with endangered Rothschild giraffes, who often poke their heads through the windows during meal times. It’s a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to get up close and personal with these majestic creatures.

3. Icehotel, Sweden:
Experience the magic of a winter wonderland at the world-famous Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden. Built entirely out of ice and snow, this hotel is a breathtaking work of art. Each year, artists from around the globe carve unique designs into the ice, creating a truly mesmerizing environment. From sleeping in ice beds to enjoying a drink at the ice bar, this hotel promises an unforgettable icy adventure.

4. Free Spirit Spheres, Canada:
For those seeking a truly immersive nature experience, Free Spirit Spheres in Vancouver Island, Canada, is a must-visit. These suspended spherical treehouses allow guests to sleep among the treetops, swaying gently with the wind. Surrounded by the serene beauty of the forest, this unconventional hotel offers a sense of tranquility and connection with nature.

5. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Finland:
Escape to a winter wonderland at Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finnish Lapland. Here, you can stay in glass igloos, providing a front-row seat to the mesmerizing Northern Lights. Imagine lying in bed, gazing up at the colorful dance of the aurora borealis overhead. It’s a truly magical experience that will leave you in awe of nature’s beauty.

6. Dog Bark Park Inn, USA:
For dog lovers, the Dog Bark Park Inn in Cottonwood, Idaho, is a dream come true. This unique hotel is shaped like a giant beagle, complete with dog-themed decor. Stay in the belly of the beagle and enjoy cozy accommodations with a quirky touch. It’s an offbeat experience that will surely bring a smile to your face.

These hidden gems of the hotel world offer more than just a place to rest your head – they offer a whole new level of adventure and immersion. So, step out of your comfort zone and explore these unconventional hotels that are bound to create unforgettable memories. Whether you choose to sleep among the trees, in an ice palace, or even in the belly of a beagle, these unique accommodations will make your travel experience truly extraordinary.
