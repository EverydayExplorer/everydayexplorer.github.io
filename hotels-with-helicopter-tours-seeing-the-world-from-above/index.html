Hotels with Helicopter Tours: Seeing the World from Above


Imagine soaring through the sky, high above the world, witnessing breathtaking landscapes and iconic landmarks from a whole new perspective. If you’re seeking an unforgettable adventure, look no further than hotels that offer helicopter tours. These luxurious accommodations not only provide top-notch comfort and amenities but also give you the opportunity to see the world from above. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most remarkable hotels that offer helicopter tours, allowing you to embark on a journey of a lifetime.

1. Atlantis The Palm, Dubai:
Situated on the iconic Palm Jumeirah Island, Atlantis The Palm is renowned for its opulence and grandeur. What sets this hotel apart is its exclusive helicopter tours, offering guests a chance to witness the dazzling skyline of Dubai, the Palm Jumeirah archipelago, and the vast Arabian Desert from above. As you soar through the sky, you’ll be captivated by the architectural marvels and awe-inspiring views.

2. Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea, Hawaii:
Immerse yourself in the tropical paradise of Maui and experience the beauty of the Hawaiian islands like never before. The Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea offers helicopter tours that take you above the pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and majestic volcanoes. From the breathtaking Haleakala Crater to the stunning coastline of the Road to Hana, this tour will leave you in awe of the natural wonders of Hawaii.

3. The Ritz-Carlton, New York Central Park:
Escape the hustle and bustle of Manhattan and indulge in a helicopter tour that showcases the iconic skyline of New York City. The Ritz-Carlton, located adjacent to Central Park, offers guests the opportunity to take to the skies and witness the towering skyscrapers, the Statue of Liberty, and the sprawling Central Park from a unique perspective. It’s a truly unforgettable experience that combines luxury with an unparalleled view of the city.

4. Singita Lebombo Lodge, South Africa:
Nestled within the breathtaking landscapes of Kruger National Park, Singita Lebombo Lodge offers an extraordinary safari experience. As part of their exclusive helicopter tours, guests can embark on an aerial adventure, where they’ll witness the vast African savannah, majestic wildlife, and the meandering rivers from above. It’s a safari experience like no other, offering a different dimension to the traditional game drives.

5. Shangri-La Hotel, Paris:
Immerse yourself in the romance and elegance of the City of Love, Paris, with a helicopter tour offered by the luxurious Shangri-La Hotel. Take off from the city and soar above the iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. As you glide through the sky, you’ll be enchanted by the beauty of the city’s architecture and the winding Seine River.

In conclusion, hotels that offer helicopter tours provide an unmatched experience for travelers seeking adventure and a unique perspective. Whether you’re soaring above the skyscrapers of Dubai, the tropical paradise of Hawaii, the concrete jungle of New York City, the African savannah, or the romantic streets of Paris, these hotels will take your journey to new heights. So, pack your bags, book your stay, and get ready to see the world from above.
