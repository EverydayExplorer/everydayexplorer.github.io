The Best Hunting Gloves for Cold Weather


1. Sitka Gear Coldfront Gloves: Engineered to withstand the harshest conditions, Sitka Gear Coldfront Gloves are a popular choice among serious hunters. With a GORE-TEX membrane, these gloves offer exceptional waterproof and windproof protection, keeping your hands warm and dry even in the most extreme weather. The PrimaLoft insulation provides ample warmth without sacrificing dexterity, allowing you to handle your firearm or equipment with ease.

2. Under Armour ColdGear Infrared Scent Control Gloves: If scent control and warmth are your top priorities, look no further than the Under Armour ColdGear Infrared Scent Control Gloves. These gloves feature an innovative infrared lining that retains and reflects your body heat, ensuring maximum warmth during long hours in the field. The UA Scent Control technology helps minimize odor, giving you the advantage of stealth while tracking your game.

3. Harkila Pro Hunter X Gloves: Designed with versatility in mind, the Harkila Pro Hunter X Gloves are suitable for a wide range of hunting scenarios. Crafted from durable and water-resistant buffalo leather, these gloves offer excellent grip and protection against abrasions. The PrimaLoft Gold insulation ensures optimal warmth, while the adjustable cuffs provide a snug fit, keeping the cold air out.

4. Outdoor Research Alti Gloves: For hunters venturing into extreme cold conditions, the Outdoor Research Alti Gloves are an excellent choice. These expedition-grade gloves feature durable leather palms, reinforced with Kevlar stitching for enhanced grip and durability. The Gore-Tex insert provides exceptional waterproofing, while the removable liner allows for customizable warmth based on the weather conditions.

5. First Lite Aerowool Liner Gloves: Sometimes, a lightweight and breathable liner glove is all you need to keep your hands warm and agile. The First Lite Aerowool Liner Gloves are made from a blend of merino wool and nylon, providing excellent moisture-wicking properties and natural odor resistance. These gloves can be worn alone in mild cold or as a base layer under heavier gloves for added warmth.

When selecting the best hunting gloves for cold weather, it’s crucial to consider factors such as insulation, waterproofing, dexterity, and scent control. Each of the gloves mentioned above offers a unique set of features tailored to suit different hunting styles and preferences. Remember to choose gloves that fit properly and allow for unrestricted movement to ensure a successful and comfortable hunting experience.

In conclusion, investing in high-quality hunting gloves for cold weather is a must for any dedicated hunter. Whether you opt for the Sitka Gear Coldfront Gloves, Under Armour ColdGear Infrared Scent Control Gloves, Harkila Pro Hunter X Gloves, Outdoor Research Alti Gloves, or First Lite Aerowool Liner Gloves, you can trust that your hands will stay warm and protected as you navigate the frosty wilderness. So gear up, stay warm, and embark on your hunting adventures with confidence!
