The Most Instagram-Worthy Hotels: Perfect for Travel Bloggers


Are you an aspiring travel blogger looking for the most Instagram-worthy hotels to feature on your blog? Look no further! In this article, we will be showcasing some of the most aesthetically pleasing and Instagram-worthy hotels from around the world. These accommodations will not only provide you with a one-of-a-kind experience but also double as the perfect backdrop for your Instagram feed. Get ready to elevate your travel content with these stunning hotels. Let’s dive in!

  1. The Icehotel, Jukkasjärvi, Sweden Located in the charming village of Jukkasjärvi, the Icehotel is a mesmerizing creation made entirely of ice and snow. This unique hotel offers intricate ice sculptures, frozen art, and beautifully designed ice suites. Imagine the incredible shots you could capture in these ice-carved rooms! From the frozen interiors to the ethereal Northern Lights dancing above, this hotel is an Instagram dream come true.

  2. Giraffe Manor, Nairobi, Kenya If you’ve ever dreamt of sharing your breakfast with a giraffe, Giraffe Manor in Nairobi is the place for you. This boutique hotel allows you to dine with a herd of giraffes wandering freely on the property. With stunning colonial-style architecture and the majestic presence of giraffes, this hotel provides endless Instagram opportunities. Get ready to capture those unforgettable Safari shots!

  3. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort, Saariselkä, Finland Nestled in the Arctic wilderness, the Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort is renowned for its iconic glass igloos. Imagine sleeping under the stars while being comfortably tucked in a warm glass dome. Witness the magic of the Northern Lights from the comfort of your own bed. These dreamy igloos make for stunning photographs, allowing you to showcase the beauty of the Arctic landscape effortlessly.

  4. Rayavadee Resort, Krabi, Thailand Rayavadee Resort, located amidst the lush vegetation of Krabi, Thailand, offers a tropical paradise that is perfect for Instagram enthusiasts. Surrounded by towering cliffs and crystal-clear waters, this luxury resort boasts stunning beaches, cozy villas, and breathtaking sunsets. Capture the vibrant marine life while snorkeling in the Andaman Sea or simply relax by the pool and soak up the sun. Every corner of this resort is Instagram-ready!

  5. Mondrian Los Angeles, California For those seeking a more glamorous and vibrant Instagram look, the Mondrian Los Angeles is the place to be. Known for its modern design, stylish rooftop pool, and panoramic views of the Hollywood Hills, this hotel offers endless photo opportunities. Take a selfie in one of the trendy poolside cabanas or capture the stunning cityscape from the Skybar. This Instagrammable hotel guarantees likes and engagement on your feed.

  6. El Fenn, Marrakech, Morocco El Fenn in Marrakech is a bohemian oasis that combines traditional Moroccan architecture with a contemporary twist. This boutique hotel features colorful courtyards, intricate tile work, and stunning rooftop terraces decorated with vibrant textiles. Each corner of this riad-style hotel offers a unique photo opportunity. From the stunning views of the Atlas Mountains to the Instagram-worthy sunsets over the city, El Fenn will transport your followers to a whole new world.

  7. Mykonos Blu, Mykonos, Greece With its whitewashed walls, picturesque windmills, and crystal-clear turquoise waters, Mykonos is a haven for Instagrammers. Mykonos Blu, a luxury beach resort, offers a seamless blend of traditional Mediterranean architecture with modern design. You can capture the iconic blue-domed churches, wander through the charming narrow streets of Mykonos Town, or relax on the pristine beaches. This Greek paradise is sure to give your Instagram feed a touch of summer magic.

  8. Laucala Island Resort, Fiji If you’re searching for ultimate luxury and exclusivity, look no further than Laucala Island Resort. Located in Fiji, this private island resort promises a once-in-a-lifetime experience. From private villas with infinity pools to pristine white sandy beaches, this resort offers sublime beauty at every corner. Take stunning underwater shots while exploring the vibrant coral reefs or capture the tranquility of the palm-fringed beaches. Laucala Island Resort is the epitome of luxury and Instagram-worthy content.

  9. Aman Tokyo, Tokyo, Japan Immerse yourself in the rich culture and futuristic cityscape of Tokyo at Aman Tokyo. This luxury hotel combines traditional Japanese design with contemporary elegance. Located on the top floors of the Otemachi Tower, Aman Tokyo offers panoramic views of the city skyline, including the iconic Tokyo Skytree. Capture the blend of modernity and tradition with photos of the minimalist interiors, tranquil gardens, and traditional tea ceremonies. Aman Tokyo will provide you with endless Instagram inspiration.

  10. Amangiri, Utah, USA Tucked away in the heart of the American Southwest, Amangiri is a luxury desert resort that combines natural beauty with minimalist design. This secluded sanctuary offers breathtaking views of the Utah desert, including the iconic horseshoe-shaped Bend of the Colorado River. From infinity pools blending seamlessly with the landscape to luxurious desert-view suites, this hotel is a photographer’s paradise.

These Instagram-worthy hotels are not only destinations but also experiences that will elevate your travel content and inspire your followers. From icy wonderlands to tropical paradises, there’s a hotel for every type of travel blogger. So pack your bags, grab your camera, and get ready to create stunning content that will captivate your audience. Happy exploring and happy Instagramming!
