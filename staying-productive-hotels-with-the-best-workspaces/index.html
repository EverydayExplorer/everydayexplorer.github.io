Staying Productive: Hotels with the Best Workspaces


Are you tired of working in the same old office space day in and day out? Do you find it hard to stay focused and motivated when you’re surrounded by distractions? If so, then it may be time for a change of scenery. Consider checking into a hotel with the best workspaces, where you can combine productivity with the comfort and luxury of a hotel stay. In this blog post, we will explore the benefits of working in a hotel and highlight some of the best hotels with work-friendly spaces. So grab your laptop and let’s dive in!

Working in a hotel can provide a number of advantages for those looking to stay productive. First and foremost, hotels are designed to be welcoming and comfortable spaces. With their stylish decor, plush seating, and modern amenities, they provide an atmosphere that can help you feel inspired and motivated to get your work done. In addition, hotels often offer a wide range of services that can cater to your specific needs as a business traveler. Whether it’s high-speed internet, business centers, or meeting rooms, you can rest assured that hotels are equipped with the tools and resources necessary for you to stay connected and stay on task.

Now that we’ve established the benefits of working in a hotel, let’s take a look at some of the best hotels that offer exceptional workspaces.

1. The Hoxton, London
Located in the heart of Shoreditch, The Hoxton is not only known for its stylish accommodations but also its vibrant workspaces. With its open-plan lobby area that doubles as a co-working space, you’ll have access to a variety of seating options, including comfortable sofas, communal tables, and private nooks. The hotel also offers complimentary Wi-Fi and printing services, making it an ideal choice for freelancers, entrepreneurs, and digital nomads.

2. Ace Hotel, New York
For those seeking a trendy and creative environment to work in, the Ace Hotel in New York is an excellent choice. With its industrial-chic design and abundance of communal workspaces, this hotel has become a favorite among creative professionals. The lobby features long communal tables, comfortable seating areas, and even a Stumptown coffee bar, so you can fuel your productivity while enjoying a delicious cup of joe. The hotel also offers private meeting rooms and event spaces, perfect for hosting client meetings or workshops.

3. CitizenM, Amsterdam
Situated in Amsterdam’s bustling Zuidas district, CitizenM is not your typical hotel. With its innovative design and tech-savvy features, it’s a haven for tech enthusiasts and digital nomads. The hotel’s Living Room is a multi-functional space that combines work and relaxation. It offers a variety of seating options, from desk pods to lounge areas, so you can choose the setting that suits your needs. With its fast Wi-Fi, Mac workstations, and 24/7 service, CitizenM ensures that you have everything you need to stay productive around the clock.

4. The Westin Hotels & Resorts
If you’re a business traveler who values wellness and work-life balance, The Westin Hotels & Resorts should be on your radar. Known for their emphasis on health and wellbeing, these hotels offer a range of amenities that can help you stay focused and energized. From ergonomic workstations in your room to fitness centers, spas, and healthy dining options, The Westin Hotels & Resorts are committed to providing an environment that supports productivity and self-care. Some locations even offer WorkWell meeting spaces, which are specifically designed to promote collaboration and engagement.

5. W Hotel, Barcelona
For those who prefer to work with a view, the W Hotel in Barcelona offers stunning panoramic vistas of the city and the sea. The hotel’s W Lounge features a sleek and modern design, with comfortable seating areas and a co-working space overlooking the Mediterranean. With its high-speed Wi-Fi, business center, and meeting rooms, you’ll have all the tools you need to stay connected and productive. And when you need a break, you can unwind at the hotel’s rooftop pool or indulge in a spa treatment.

In conclusion, working in a hotel can be a refreshing and productive experience. Whether you’re a digital nomad, a business traveler, or simply looking for a change of scenery, hotels with the best workspaces offer the perfect balance between productivity and comfort. From stylish co-working spaces to tech-savvy amenities, these hotels provide the resources and environment necessary to stay focused and get your work done. So the next time you’re in need of a workspace, consider checking into one of these top hotels and watch your productivity soar!
