Hotels with Unforgettable Helicopter Tours


Are you tired of the same old vacation experience? Do you dream of soaring above breathtaking landscapes and taking in stunning aerial views? Look no further, because we have the perfect solution for you. Imagine staying at a luxurious hotel and embarking on an unforgettable helicopter tour. This unique experience combines the thrill of helicopter travel with the comfort and opulence of a high-end hotel. In this blog, we will explore some of the most incredible hotels around the world that offer unforgettable helicopter tours.

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch, Colorado, USA:
Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch offers an unparalleled experience. Guests can take a helicopter tour over the majestic mountain ranges, witnessing the beauty of Colorado’s wilderness from above. Marvel at the snow-capped peaks, deep canyons, and crystal-clear lakes as you soar through the sky. After your thrilling adventure, return to the hotel and indulge in luxurious spa treatments and exquisite dining.

2. The Brando, Tetiaroa, French Polynesia:
Escape to a tropical paradise at The Brando, located on the private island of Tetiaroa. This eco-friendly resort offers guests the opportunity to explore the stunning atolls and turquoise waters from above. Hop on a helicopter and witness the mesmerizing beauty of the South Pacific, with its pristine beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and lush greenery. Back at the hotel, unwind in your private villa, surrounded by nature and tranquility.

3. Amangiri, Utah, USA:
Located in the heart of the American Southwest, Amangiri offers a truly unique experience. Take a helicopter tour over the dramatic landscapes of Utah, including the iconic Grand Canyon. Soar above the red rock formations, deep canyons, and vast desert landscapes, and witness nature’s masterpiece from a bird’s-eye view. After your adventure, relax in the hotel’s infinity pool, which blends seamlessly with the surrounding desert.

4. Singita Sasakwa Lodge, Serengeti, Tanzania:
Combine luxury with wildlife on a helicopter tour from Singita Sasakwa Lodge. Explore the vast Serengeti National Park from above, spotting herds of wildebeest, elephants, and other iconic African wildlife. Witness the Great Migration, where millions of animals make their way across the plains in search of greener pastures. After your safari adventure, retreat to the lodge and enjoy world-class service and breathtaking views of the African savannah.

5. The St. Regis Bora Bora Resort, French Polynesia:
Experience the ultimate luxury at The St. Regis Bora Bora Resort, while also enjoying a thrilling helicopter tour. Fly over Bora Bora’s iconic turquoise lagoon, admire the stunning coral reefs, and get a glimpse of Mount Otemanu, a dormant volcano that dominates the island’s skyline. After your helicopter adventure, relax in your overwater villa and indulge in the resort’s exquisite dining options.

These are just a few examples of hotels that offer unforgettable helicopter tours. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or a combination of both, these hotels will provide you with an experience like no other. So, why settle for an ordinary vacation when you can elevate your trip to new heights? Book your stay at one of these extraordinary hotels and prepare for an unforgettable adventure that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.
