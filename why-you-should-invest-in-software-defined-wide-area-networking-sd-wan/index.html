Why You Should Invest in Software-Defined Wide Area Networking (SD-WAN)


Software-defined wide area networking (SD-WAN) is a comprehensive solution that changes the way enterprises connect their different sites and branch offices. SD-WAN technologies are rapidly gaining popularity and are becoming the preferred choice for large and small businesses alike. This emerging technology utilizes software to configure and manage WAN networks, offering a range of benefits over traditional WAN architecture. In this blog post, we will explore why you should invest in SD-WAN.

Increased Network Reliability and Performance

Are you tired of slow and unreliable network connections that hinder your business operations? SD-WAN provides a solution to this problem by delivering high network performance and reliability. With SD-WAN, the use of multiple internet connections in a single network ensures that if one connection fails, another one picks up the slack, leading to close to 100% network uptime. Additionally, with the ability to prioritize application traffic, bandwidth to critical applications is guaranteed, which significantly improves performance.

Improved Network Security

Cybersecurity is an increasing concern for modern businesses, and SD-WAN incorporates contemporary security features into its architecture. Unlike traditional WAN networks, SD-WAN includes built-in encryption protocols and advanced security techniques that secure data transmissions and protect against cyber threats. Furthermore, SD-WAN enables centralized security configurations, making it easy to manage policies effectively across all sites.

Scalability and Cost Savings

It is costly to implement and manage traditional WAN infrastructure, particularly when it comes to maintenance, upgrades, and site-to-site connectivity. SD-WAN provides a comprehensive solution that saves on costs while providing maximum scalability. With SD-WAN, users can utilize a variety of links, including broadband or fiber to achieve optimal performance and savings on operational and hardware expenses.

Simplified Network Management and Administration

Managing complex network architectures can be resource-intensive and time-consuming for businesses. With SD-WAN, automation of network management is possible, which simplifies the network-administration process and reduces the need for IT staff. SD-wan offers a central and secure management console to visualize and manage all network configurations. In addition to simplifying the administration process, SD-WAN also provides real-time analytics and visibility across the entire infrastructure.

Flexibility and Ease of Deployment

Modern businesses rely heavily on cloud-based applications for their daily operations. Traditional WAN architecture can be rigid and hinder access to these applications. SD-WAN enables users to connect with various cloud providers and deploy a WAN architecture that is highly flexible and scalable. The ease of deployment of SD-WAN allows a smooth transition from legacy systems to the new architecture and provides a faster ROI by enabling a faster time to market for remote sites with consistent security and performance.

In Conclusion

Advancements in technology are reshaping the way businesses operate. As businesses increasingly rely on remote working, SD-WAN offers a reliable, efficient, secure, and cost-effective solution that enhances performance and enhances security across your entire network, distributed infrastructure. It is time to start exploring this emerging technology and invest in SD-WAN for all your network infrastructure needs.
