The Dos and Don'ts of Lipstick Application and Maintenance


When it comes to enhancing our natural beauty, lipstick has always been a go-to choice for many people. It adds a touch of color and can instantly transform a simple look into something more glamorous. However, applying and maintaining lipstick isn’t always as easy as it seems. To help you achieve the perfect pout, here are some dos and don’ts of lipstick application and maintenance.

DO: Exfoliate Your Lips
Before applying lipstick, it’s essential to exfoliate your lips to remove any dead skin cells. This will create a smooth canvas for the lipstick and prevent it from settling into cracks and crevices. You can use a gentle lip scrub or make your own DIY exfoliator using sugar and honey.

DON’T: Skip the Lip Balm
Moisturized lips are crucial for a flawless lipstick application. Before applying any color, make sure to apply a hydrating lip balm. This will keep your lips soft and prevent the lipstick from drying them out.

DO: Use a Lip Liner
Using a lip liner can make a world of difference in achieving a well-defined lip shape and preventing the lipstick from bleeding or feathering. Choose a lip liner that matches your lipstick shade or opt for a clear one for versatility. Start by outlining your lips and then fill them in for a longer-lasting lip color.

DON’T: Forget to Blot
After applying lipstick, use a tissue to gently blot your lips. This will remove any excess product and help set the color. Blotting also prevents lipstick from transferring onto your teeth or smudging throughout the day.

DO: Apply Lipstick with a Brush
While applying lipstick directly from the tube is convenient, using a lip brush can give you more control and precision. A brush allows you to evenly distribute the color and reach the corners of your lips more easily. It also helps in achieving a more professional and polished finish.

DON’T: Overlook Lipstick Maintenance
To make your lipstick last longer, there are a few maintenance tips you should keep in mind. Firstly, avoid touching or pressing your lips together too often, as this can cause the color to fade or smudge. Secondly, if you notice any feathering or bleeding, use a concealer or a colorless lip liner to clean up the edges.

DO: Consider Lipstick Formulas
Different lipstick formulas have different finishes and staying power. If you want a long-lasting and smudge-proof option, go for matte or liquid lipsticks. If you prefer a hydrating and glossy look, opt for creamy or satin formulas. Experiment with different options to find the one that suits your preferences and needs.

DON’T: Neglect Lipstick Removal
At the end of the day, it’s crucial to remove your lipstick properly to avoid staining or drying out your lips. Use a gentle makeup remover or micellar water to dissolve the color. Afterward, apply a lip balm to nourish your lips overnight.

In conclusion, mastering the art of lipstick application and maintenance requires a combination of technique and proper care. By following these dos and don’ts, you can achieve a flawless and long-lasting lip look that complements your overall makeup. So, go ahead and experiment with different shades and formulas to find your perfect pout!
