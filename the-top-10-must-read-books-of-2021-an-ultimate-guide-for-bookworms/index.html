The Top 10 Must-Read Books of 2021 - An Ultimate Guide for Bookworms


The world of literature is a treasure trove for avid readers, offering a vast array of genres, authors, and stories to explore. As 2021 draws to a close, it’s the perfect time to reflect upon and celebrate some of the standout books that have graced our bookshelves this year. Whether you’re a dedicated bookworm or simply looking for your next captivating read, this ultimate guide presents the top 10 must-read books of 2021.

1. ‘Project Hail Mary’ by Andy Weir
Andy Weir, the mastermind behind ‘The Martian,’ returns with another thrilling science fiction novel. ‘Project Hail Mary’ takes readers on an interstellar journey alongside a protagonist fighting to save humanity. Weir’s knack for blending science and suspense effortlessly transports readers to a world of extraterrestrial mysteries.

2. ‘Klara and the Sun’ by Kazuo Ishiguro
Nobel laureate Kazuo Ishiguro delivers a poignant exploration of artificial intelligence and human emotions in ‘Klara and the Sun.’ This thought-provoking novel follows Klara, an Artificial Friend, as she tries to understand the complexities of love, sacrifice, and the meaning of being human.

3. ‘The Push’ by Ashley Audrain
A gripping psychological thriller, ‘The Push’ delves into the darker side of motherhood and the impact of intergenerational trauma. Ashley Audrain’s debut novel pushes the boundaries of conventional motherhood narratives, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

4. ‘The Four Winds’ by Kristin Hannah
Historical fiction enthusiasts will find themselves captivated by Kristin Hannah’s ‘The Four Winds.’ Set during the Great Depression, this novel chronicles the resilience and strength of one woman as she confronts the harsh realities of poverty, drought, and societal upheaval.

5. ‘Great Circle’ by Maggie Shipstead
Spanning continents and eras, ‘Great Circle’ tells the enthralling story of Marian Graves, a pioneering aviator determined to circumnavigate the globe. With its richly developed characters and vivid imagery, Maggie Shipstead’s epic novel takes readers on a thrilling adventure.

6. ‘The Sanatorium’ by Sarah Pearse
Mystery and suspense lovers will find themselves engrossed in ‘The Sanatorium.’ Set in an isolated Swiss hotel-turned-sanatorium, this atmospheric thriller weaves together elements of history, family secrets, and a series of chilling murders. Sarah Pearse’s masterful storytelling keeps readers guessing until the very end.

7. ‘The Code Breaker’ by Walter Isaacson
In ‘The Code Breaker,’ Walter Isaacson delves into the groundbreaking research of Jennifer Doudna, the co-discoverer of CRISPR-Cas9. This engrossing biography explores the ethical dilemmas and scientific advancements that have revolutionized the field of gene editing.

8. ‘Empire of Pain’ by Patrick Radden Keefe
In this meticulously researched expose, Patrick Radden Keefe sheds light on the Sackler family’s involvement in the opioid crisis. ‘Empire of Pain’ offers a deep dive into the pharmaceutical industry, unraveling the web of greed, deception, and the devastating consequences of addiction.

9. ‘No One Is Talking About This’ by Patricia Lockwood
Blurring the lines between fiction and memoir, ‘No One Is Talking About This’ explores the intersection of the virtual and real worlds. Patricia Lockwood’s irreverent humor and poignant insights into the human condition make this novel a standout of 2021.

10. ‘Concrete Rose’ by Angie Thomas
Angie Thomas, acclaimed author of ‘The Hate U Give,’ returns to Garden Heights with ‘Concrete Rose.’ This powerful prequel explores the life of Maverick Carter, a young father navigating the complexities of gang life, fatherhood, and personal growth.

With this ultimate guide to the top 10 must-read books of 2021, you’ll find yourself spoiled for choice when it comes to engaging, thought-provoking, and captivating reads. Whether you’re drawn to science fiction, historical fiction, mysteries, or biographies, there’s something for every bookworm within these pages. So, grab a cozy blanket, curl up with one of these incredible books, and let your imagination soar in the boundless realms of literature. Happy reading!
