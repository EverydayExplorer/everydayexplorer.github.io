Top 10 Comedy Podcasts for Non-Stop Laughter


Comedy podcasts have become the go-to source for non-stop laughter and entertainment. Whether you’re stuck in traffic, working out, or just need a good laugh, these hilarious shows are guaranteed to brighten your day. With so many options available, we’ve narrowed down the top 10 comedy podcasts that will leave you in stitches.

1. The Joe Rogan Experience

Hosted by stand-up comedian Joe Rogan, this podcast features hilarious conversations with some of the biggest names in comedy. From comedians like Bill Burr and Tom Segura to actors like Eddie Murphy and Dave Chappelle, you never know who will be dropping by for a laugh. With over 1,500 episodes to choose from, there’s never a shortage of funny moments on The Joe Rogan Experience.

2. Comedy Bang! Bang!

Scott Aukerman’s Comedy Bang! Bang! is a surreal and hilarious podcast that combines improv, sketches, and interviews. Aukerman, along with a rotating cast of comedians, creates a unique and unpredictable show that will have you laughing out loud. The podcast has featured guests like Paul F. Tompkins, Weird Al Yankovic, and Ben Schwartz, making it a must-listen for comedy lovers.

3. The Dollop

If you’re a fan of history and comedy, then The Dollop is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, this show delves into bizarre and lesser-known historical events. With Anthony providing the historical context and Reynolds providing hilarious commentary, The Dollop is both educational and entertaining.

4. My Dad Wrote A Porno

My Dad Wrote A Porno is a one-of-a-kind podcast that brings hilarity to the world of erotic literature. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, the show features Morton reading chapters from his dad’s poorly written adult novel. The hosts’ reactions and commentary add an extra layer of comedy to this unique and side-splitting podcast.

5. How Did This Get Made?

Ever watched a terrible movie and wondered, ‘How did this get made?’ Well, comedian Paul Scheer, actress June Diane Raphael, and actor Jason Mantzoukas have got you covered. On How Did This Get Made?, the hosts discuss and dissect some of the worst movies ever made. With their witty banter and hilarious insights, this podcast is a hilarious treat for movie enthusiasts.

6. The Misfits Podcast

The Misfits Podcast is a group of YouTubers and friends who come together to share funny stories, discuss pop culture, and provide a glimpse into their lives. With a mix of hilarious anecdotes and unfiltered conversations, this podcast offers a refreshing and entertaining take on comedy.

7. No Such Thing As A Fish

If you’re a fan of trivia and comedy, then No Such Thing As A Fish should be on your playlist. Hosted by the researchers of the British comedy panel show QI, this podcast offers bizarre and mind-blowing facts wrapped in humor. You’ll be laughing and learning at the same time!

8. The Bill Simmons Podcast

While primarily a sports podcast, The Bill Simmons Podcast often features hilarious and insightful interviews with comedians like Bill Hader, Jim Carrey, and Seth Rogen. Simmons’ conversational style and the guests’ comedic talents make for an entertaining and laughter-filled experience.

9. Beautiful Stories from Anonymous People

Comedian Chris Gethard hosts this podcast where he takes anonymous phone calls from strangers. The guests share their stories, and Gethard provides comedic commentary and empathy. With each episode bringing us closer to the human experience, this podcast offers laughter, heartwarming moments, and a glimpse into the lives of people from all walks of life.

10. Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend

Conan O’Brien is known for his quick wit and comedic genius, and his podcast is no exception. Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend features conversations with celebrities like Will Ferrell, John Mulaney, and Tina Fey. O’Brien’s self-deprecating humor, combined with the hilarious banter between him and his guests, creates a podcast that guarantees non-stop laughter.

In conclusion, these top 10 comedy podcasts provide the perfect blend of laughter, wit, and entertainment. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up comedy, improv, history, or just looking for a good laugh, these podcasts have got you covered. So, grab your headphones and get ready to laugh till your cheeks hurt!
