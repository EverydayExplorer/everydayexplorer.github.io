How to Cook Perfectly Crispy Bacon in the Oven


Bacon lovers rejoice! If you’re tired of the mess and hassle of frying bacon on the stovetop, we have the perfect solution for you - cooking it in the oven. Not only does this method result in perfectly crispy bacon, but it also saves you time and eliminates the greasy splatters that are inevitable with stovetop cooking. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps to cook your bacon to perfection in the oven.

First of all, let’s talk about the type of bacon you should be using. Thin-cut bacon works best for oven cooking as it cooks more evenly and quickly. Thick-cut bacon tends to take longer to cook and may not crisp up as well. Look for bacon with a good balance of fat and meat, as this will ensure the perfect texture and flavor.

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper. This will make cleaning up a breeze once your bacon is cooked. Place a wire rack on top of the lined baking sheet. This helps elevate the bacon and allows the excess fat to drip away, resulting in crispy bacon.

Lay the bacon strips in a single layer on the wire rack. Make sure not to overlap them, as this will prevent proper air circulation and result in uneven cooking. If needed, you can cook multiple batches.

Once your oven has reached the desired temperature, place the baking sheet on the middle rack. Let the bacon cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of crispiness. Keep an eye on it during the cooking process to prevent overcooking or burning.

It’s important to note that cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of your bacon and your personal preference for crispiness. Thinner bacon slices will cook faster, while thicker slices will take slightly longer. Adjust your cooking time accordingly.

When your bacon is ready, carefully remove the baking sheet from the oven. Use a pair of tongs to transfer the crispy bacon to a plate lined with paper towels. This helps absorb any excess grease, making your bacon less greasy and more enjoyable to eat.

Allow the bacon to cool for a few minutes before serving. Your perfectly crispy oven-cooked bacon is now ready to be devoured!

Apart from being a convenient and mess-free method, cooking bacon in the oven also offers other advantages. For instance, it allows you to cook a large batch of bacon at once, which is perfect for feeding a crowd or meal prepping. Additionally, the wire rack allows for better air circulation, resulting in a more evenly cooked and crispier bacon.

Now that you know the secrets to achieving perfectly crispy bacon in the oven, there’s no going back to the stovetop method. Give it a try and enjoy the deliciousness that awaits you. Happy cooking!
