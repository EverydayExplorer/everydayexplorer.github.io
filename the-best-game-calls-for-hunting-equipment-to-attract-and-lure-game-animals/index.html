The Best Game Calls for Hunting: Equipment to Attract and Lure Game Animals


When it comes to hunting, having the right equipment is crucial to your success. One of the most effective tools in a hunter’s arsenal is a game call. Game calls are devices that mimic the sounds of different animals to attract and lure game animals closer to the hunter. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced hunter, having the best game calls can greatly improve your chances of a successful hunt. In this article, we will explore some of the top game calls on the market and discuss their features and benefits.

Duck Calls

Duck calls are designed to imitate the sounds of ducks, including quacks, feeding calls, and mating calls. These calls are used by hunters to attract ducks to their hunting location. One of the best duck calls on the market is the DUCK COMMANDER Triple Threat Duck Call. This call is made from high-quality material and produces a wide range of realistic duck sounds. It is also very easy to use, making it a great option for beginners.

Deer Calls

Deer calls are used by hunters to imitate the sounds made by deer, such as grunts, bleats, and rattling sounds. These calls can be used to attract deer and provoke a response, making them an essential tool for deer hunting. One popular deer call is the Primos The Original CAN Deer Call. This call is compact and easy to use, producing accurate doe estrus bleats. It is also designed to be hands-free, allowing hunters to keep their hands on their weapon while using the call.

Turkey Calls

Turkey calls are used to imitate the sounds of turkeys, such as gobbles, clucks, and purrs. These calls are essential for turkey hunting, as they help hunters to locate and attract turkeys. One of the top turkey calls on the market is the Primos Hunting Hook-Up Magnetic Box Call. This call is known for its realistic sounds and ease of use. The magnetic design ensures that the paddle is always perfectly aligned, producing consistent and accurate turkey calls.

Elk Calls

Elk calls are used by hunters to imitate the sounds made by elk, such as bugles, cow calls, and chuckles. These calls are effective for locating elk and attracting them closer to the hunter. One highly recommended elk call is the Rocky Mountain Hunting Calls Bully Bull Extreme Elk Call. This call features an adjustable tube, allowing hunters to produce a wide range of elk sounds. It is also made from durable materials, ensuring that it can withstand rugged hunting conditions.

Predator Calls

Predator calls are used to imitate the sounds of various predators, such as coyotes, foxes, and bobcats, in order to attract other predators for hunting purposes. One of the top predator calls available is the FOXPRO Shockwave Digital Game Call. This electronic call comes pre-loaded with a wide range of realistic predator sounds and offers customizable settings. It also features a high-quality speaker system, ensuring that the calls can be heard from a distance.

Conclusion

Having the best game calls can greatly enhance your hunting experience and increase your chances of success. Whether you are targeting ducks, deer, turkeys, elk, or predators, there is a game call out there to meet your needs. The key is to choose a call that is high-quality, easy to use, and produces realistic sounds. By investing in the right game calls and learning how to use them effectively, you can take your hunting skills to the next level. So, before you head out on your next hunting trip, make sure you have the best game calls in your backpack. Happy hunting!
