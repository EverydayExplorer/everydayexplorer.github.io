The Best Outdoor Fitness Apps to Track Your Progress and Stay Motivated


In today’s fast-paced world, maintaining an active and healthy lifestyle is more important than ever. With our increasingly busy schedules, it can be challenging to find the time to hit the gym or head to a fitness class. Fortunately, technology has made it easier than ever to stay on top of your fitness goals, even when you’re on the go. Outdoor fitness apps have become essential tools for those looking to track their progress and stay motivated while enjoying the great outdoors. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best outdoor fitness apps available today.

1. Strava

Strava is a popular outdoor fitness app that caters to both cyclists and runners. It’s known for its robust tracking capabilities and social networking features. With Strava, you can track your workouts, set goals, and compete with friends and other users for added motivation. The app also offers detailed statistics and analysis of your performance, including distance, speed, elevation, and more. Strava’s Beacon feature allows you to share your real-time location with trusted contacts for added safety during outdoor workouts.

2. Nike Run Club

If you’re a runner, the Nike Run Club app is a must-have. It offers a range of features to help you improve your running performance, including GPS tracking, audio-guided runs, and personalized coaching plans. The app also provides social features, allowing you to connect with friends, join challenges, and compete on leaderboards. Nike Run Club is an excellent choice for those looking to take their running to the next level while staying motivated and connected with a supportive community.

3. MapMyFitness

MapMyFitness, powered by Under Armour, is a versatile app that covers a wide range of outdoor activities, including running, cycling, hiking, and more. It offers GPS tracking, route planning, and a comprehensive workout log to help you monitor your progress over time. One standout feature is the ability to discover new routes and explore local trails, making it perfect for those who love to mix up their outdoor workouts. MapMyFitness also integrates with MyFitnessPal for a holistic approach to health and fitness tracking.

4. Runkeeper

Runkeeper is another top-rated app for runners and outdoor enthusiasts. It offers GPS tracking, customizable training plans, and audio cues to keep you informed during your workouts. The app’s user-friendly interface makes it easy to track your runs and view detailed performance metrics. Runkeeper also allows you to set goals and join challenges to keep your motivation levels high. With its integration with Spotify, you can even create the perfect workout playlist to keep you pumped up during your outdoor sessions.

5. AllTrails

For those who enjoy hiking and trail running, AllTrails is a fantastic app to explore. It provides a vast database of trail maps, including user-generated reviews and photos, to help you find the perfect outdoor adventure. AllTrails offers GPS tracking, so you can stay on the right path and never worry about getting lost. You can also record your hikes and share them with the AllTrails community. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or just starting, this app will elevate your outdoor experience.

6. Runtastic

Runtastic, now known as adidas Running, is an excellent app for both runners and outdoor enthusiasts. It offers GPS tracking, voice coaching, and real-time performance feedback to help you reach your fitness goals. The app also includes features like hydration tracking and nutrition planning to ensure you’re taking care of your overall well-being. With its integration with the adidas Training app, you can create a comprehensive fitness routine that combines running with strength and flexibility workouts.

7. Endomondo

Endomondo is a versatile fitness app that caters to a wide range of outdoor activities, from running and cycling to kayaking and skiing. It offers GPS tracking, route planning, and a training log to help you monitor your progress. One of its standout features is the ability to analyze your heart rate data when using a compatible heart rate monitor. Endomondo also allows you to join challenges, connect with friends, and share your achievements on social media for added motivation.

8. MyFitnessPal

While MyFitnessPal is primarily known for its nutrition tracking capabilities, it also offers excellent tools for tracking outdoor workouts. You can sync the app with various fitness trackers and apps to consolidate your data in one place. MyFitnessPal’s extensive food database and calorie tracking features make it easy to monitor your diet and exercise simultaneously, helping you achieve a balanced approach to health and fitness.

9. Zombies, Run!

If you’re looking to add an element of fun and excitement to your outdoor runs, Zombies, Run! is the app for you. This unique fitness app combines storytelling with running, immersing you in a post-apocalyptic world where you’re the hero. As you run, you’ll collect supplies and complete missions while being chased by zombies. It’s a creative way to stay motivated and entertained during your outdoor workouts.

10. Fitbod

Fitbod is a comprehensive fitness app that focuses on strength training and building muscle. While it’s not specifically designed for outdoor activities, it’s a valuable tool for anyone looking to incorporate strength workouts into their fitness routine. Fitbod generates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. You can easily adapt these workouts for outdoor settings, such as a local park or your backyard, making it a versatile choice for those who prefer outdoor strength training.

In conclusion, these outdoor fitness apps offer a wide range of features to help you track your progress, stay motivated, and enjoy your workouts in the great outdoors. Whether you’re a dedicated runner, cyclist, hiker, or someone who enjoys a mix of outdoor activities, there’s an app on this list to suit your needs. By harnessing the power of technology, you can take your outdoor fitness journey to the next level and achieve your health and wellness goals. So, pick the app that resonates with you the most and start tracking your progress today!
