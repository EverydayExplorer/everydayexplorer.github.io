Comedy in Podcasts: The Funniest Shows to Keep You Laughing


In the world of entertainment, podcasts have become an increasingly popular medium. With their ability to connect with audiences on a personal level and deliver content that is both informative and entertaining, it’s no wonder that podcasts have found a special place in our hearts. And when it comes to comedy, podcasts have proven to be a goldmine of laughter.

Whether you’re looking for a chuckle during your daily commute or a belly laugh to brighten up your day, there are countless comedy podcasts out there that are sure to keep you entertained. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the funniest shows in the podcasting world that are guaranteed to tickle your funny bone.

1. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’: Hosted by the charismatic Joe Rogan, this podcast features a wide range of guests, from comedians to scientists and everything in between. With its laid-back atmosphere and unfiltered conversations, ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ will have you laughing out loud while also providing thought-provoking insights.

2. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend’: Late-night talk show host Conan O’Brien brings his trademark humor to the podcasting world with this hilarious show. Joined by celebrity guests, Conan engages in candid and often outrageous conversations, leaving no stone unturned in his quest for friendship.

3. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’: If you’re looking for something truly unique and utterly hilarious, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’ takes a comedic deep dive into Jamie’s father’s self-published erotic novel. The hosts’ witty commentary and infectious laughter make for a side-splitting experience.

4. ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’: Hosted by Scott Aukerman, this podcast is a treasure trove of comedy. Featuring a mix of celebrity interviews, improv games, and sketch comedy, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ is a rollercoaster ride of laughter that will keep you hooked from start to finish.

5. ‘The Dollop’: Comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds take listeners on a hilarious journey through bizarre and forgotten moments in history. With Dave’s storytelling and Gareth’s comedic commentary, ‘The Dollop’ is an educational and hilarious experience that will leave you wanting more.

6. ‘How Did This Get Made?’: If you’re a fan of movies, this podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Paul Scheer, Jason Mantzoukas, and June Diane Raphael, ‘How Did This Get Made?’ delves into the world of bad movies, dissecting their absurdity with hilarious banter and expert analysis.

7. ‘The Hilarious World of Depression’: While the title may sound somber, this podcast is actually a comedy show that explores mental health with a lighthearted approach. Host John Moe interviews comedians who have experienced depression, creating a unique blend of humor and vulnerability that is both enlightening and entertaining.

These are just a few examples of the many comedy podcasts available to tickle your funny bone. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up, improv, or just good old-fashioned humor, there’s a podcast out there that’s perfect for you. So, the next time you’re in need of a good laugh, give one of these shows a try and let the laughter begin!
