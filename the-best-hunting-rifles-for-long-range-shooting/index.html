The Best Hunting Rifles for Long-Range Shooting


When it comes to long-range shooting in the realm of hunting, having the right rifle can make all the difference. The ability to accurately hit targets at extended distances requires a rifle that is designed specifically for this purpose. In this article, we will explore some of the best hunting rifles for long-range shooting.

1. Remington 700 Long Range

One of the most popular choices among hunters for long-range shooting is the Remington 700 Long Range rifle. Known for its exceptional accuracy and durability, this bolt-action rifle is a favorite among many experienced hunters. Its heavy barrel and chambered in powerful calibers like .300 Win Mag or .338 Lapua Magnum, make it ideal for shooting over long distances.

2. Browning X-Bolt Long Range

Another exceptional choice for long-range shooting is the Browning X-Bolt Long Range rifle. This rifle comes with a heavy barrel and features Browning’s renowned accuracy and reliability. It is available in various calibers and offers excellent performance at extended distances. The adjustable trigger and free-floating barrel contribute to its incredible accuracy.

3. Tikka T3x TAC A1

The Tikka T3x TAC A1 is a precision rifle that is designed for long-range shooting. It features a modular chassis system that allows for easy customization and adjustment to fit the shooter’s preferences. The cold-hammer-forged barrel ensures exceptional accuracy, and the rifle comes with a two-stage trigger that can be adjusted to suit individual shooting styles.

4. Savage Arms 110 Long Range Hunter

The Savage Arms 110 Long Range Hunter is another excellent option for hunters who are looking for long-range shooting capabilities. This rifle features a carbon steel barrel, an AccuFit stock system, and the Savage AccuTrigger, which is known for its crisp and adjustable pull. With its exceptional accuracy and affordability, this rifle is a popular choice among hunters.

5. Christensen Arms Mesa Long Range

If you are in search of a lightweight and accurate long-range hunting rifle, look no further than the Christensen Arms Mesa Long Range. This rifle features a carbon fiber barrel and a precision-machined receiver, resulting in a lightweight yet extremely durable firearm. With its sub-MOA accuracy guarantee, the Mesa Long Range is sure to impress even the most demanding hunters.

In conclusion, long-range shooting in hunting requires a rifle that is specifically designed to excel at extended distances. The Remington 700 Long Range, Browning X-Bolt Long Range, Tikka T3x TAC A1, Savage Arms 110 Long Range Hunter, and Christensen Arms Mesa Long Range are all excellent choices for hunters who are looking to take accurate shots at long distances. When choosing a hunting rifle for long-range shooting, it’s essential to consider factors such as accuracy, durability, and adjustability to ensure you have the best shooting experience possible. Happy hunting!
