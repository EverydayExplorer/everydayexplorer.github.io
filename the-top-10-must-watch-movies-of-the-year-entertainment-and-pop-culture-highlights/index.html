The Top 10 Must-Watch Movies of the Year: Entertainment and Pop Culture Highlights


In a world of endless possibilities, there is one form of entertainment that continues to captivate audiences across the globe - movies. Every year, we are treated to a plethora of incredible films that take us on thrilling adventures, make us laugh uncontrollably, and bring tears to our eyes. And as we approach the end of another year, it’s time to reflect on the movies that have made their mark on 2019. In this blog, we will be counting down the top 10 must-watch movies of the year, showcasing the very best that the world of cinema has to offer.

1. Avengers: Endgame

At the top of our must-watch movies list is the superhero epic, Avengers: Endgame. This movie served as the culmination of over a decade of storytelling within the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Audiences around the world eagerly awaited the conclusion of the Infinity Saga, and the film did not disappoint. With its stunning visual effects, emotional storytelling, and jaw-dropping action sequences, Avengers: Endgame is a must-watch for any fan of superhero movies.

2. Joker

Joker, directed by Todd Phillips, took a deep dive into the origin story of one of Batman’s most iconic villains. The film received critical acclaim for its gritty and intense portrayal of Gotham City and its troubled protagonist, played by Joaquin Phoenix. Joker is a thought-provoking character study that delves into the complexities of mental illness and societal decay.

3. Parasite

Parasite, directed by Bong Joon-ho, made history by becoming the first Korean film to win the prestigious Palme d’Or at the Cannes Film Festival. This dark comedy-thriller explores the class divide in South Korea through the lens of two families - one wealthy and the other poor. With its sharp social commentary, brilliant performances, and unexpected twists, Parasite is a cinematic masterpiece that deserves a spot on any must-watch list.

4. Once Upon a Time in Hollywood

Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, directed by Quentin Tarantino, takes us back to the golden age of Hollywood in the late 1960s. The film follows the story of a fading television actor and his stunt double as they navigate a changing industry and the looming presence of the Manson Family. With its stellar cast, nostalgic atmosphere, and Tarantino’s signature style, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood is a must-watch for cinephiles and fans of the director.

5. Marriage Story

Marriage Story, directed by Noah Baumbach, is an emotional and raw portrayal of a couple going through a divorce. Adam Driver and Scarlett Johansson deliver powerhouse performances as the lead characters, capturing the complexities of love, loss, and the dissolution of a marriage. This heart-wrenching drama offers an intimate and thought-provoking look at the struggles of modern relationships.

6. Jojo Rabbit

Jojo Rabbit, directed by Taika Waititi, is a unique blend of comedy and drama set during World War II. The film follows the story of a young boy in Nazi Germany who finds solace in his imaginary friend, Adolf Hitler. With its bold and unconventional storytelling, Jojo Rabbit tackles sensitive subjects with humor and heart, delivering a powerful message about love and acceptance.

7. The Irishman

Directed by Martin Scorsese, The Irishman reunites powerhouse actors Robert De Niro, Al Pacino, and Joe Pesci in a gripping crime drama. The film spans several decades, exploring the life of Frank Sheeran, a hitman involved with the Bufalino crime family. With its masterful storytelling, stellar performances, and Scorsese’s signature directing style, The Irishman is a cinematic experience not to be missed.

8. 1917

1917, directed by Sam Mendes, takes us on a harrowing journey through the trenches of World War I. Shot in one continuous take to give the impression of a real-time story, the film follows two British soldiers tasked with delivering a message that could potentially save thousands of lives. With its technical brilliance, immersive storytelling, and emotional impact, 1917 is a cinematic achievement that deserves recognition.

9. Little Women

Directed by Greta Gerwig, Little Women is a fresh and modern take on the beloved classic novel by Louisa May Alcott. The film tells the story of the March sisters as they navigate the challenges of growing up in 19th century America. With its compelling performances, stunning visuals, and Gerwig’s unique perspective, Little Women is a heartwarming and empowering film for audiences of all ages.

10. Knives Out

Rounding out our must-watch movie list is Knives Out, a clever and entertaining whodunit mystery directed by Rian Johnson. The film boasts an impressive ensemble cast, including Daniel Craig, Chris Evans, and Jamie Lee Curtis, who bring the dysfunctional family at the center of the story to life. With its sharp writing, brilliant performances, and unexpected twists, Knives Out is a delightful homage to classic murder mysteries.

In conclusion, 2019 has been a remarkable year for movies. From superhero blockbusters to poignant dramas and everything in between, there is truly something for everyone. The top 10 must-watch movies of the year have brought joy, laughter, tears, and powerful messages to audiences around the world. So grab some popcorn, find a comfortable seat, and get ready to be transported to new worlds and emotionally charged stories. These movies are the perfect way to escape reality and immerse yourself in the magic of cinema.
