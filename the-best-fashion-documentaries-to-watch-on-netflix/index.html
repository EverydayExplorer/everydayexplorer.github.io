The Best Fashion Documentaries to Watch on Netflix


Fashion documentaries have long been a source of inspiration and insight for both fashion enthusiasts and industry professionals. With the rise of streaming platforms like Netflix, viewers now have access to a plethora of fashion documentaries that showcase the world of haute couture, high fashion, and street style.

In this post, we will explore some of the best fashion documentaries available on Netflix that are not only entertaining but also educational and insightful. Whether you’re a fashionista looking for your next binge-worthy watch or someone interested in learning more about the history and culture of fashion, these documentaries are sure to captivate and inspire you.

1. ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ (2008)
Directed by Matt Tyrnauer, this documentary follows the life and career of legendary fashion designer Valentino Garavani. From his lavish lifestyle to his iconic designs, viewers get an intimate look at the man behind the brand. With appearances from celebrities like Gwyneth Paltrow and Anna Wintour, this film offers a glimpse into the world of high fashion and the creative genius behind one of the most iconic fashion houses in the world.

2. ‘The First Monday in May’ (2016)
This documentary takes viewers behind the scenes of the annual Met Gala, one of the most prestigious events in the fashion calendar. Directed by Andrew Rossi, the film explores the meticulous planning and preparation that goes into creating the iconic fashion exhibit at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City. From choosing the theme to selecting the guest list, viewers get a rare glimpse into the inner workings of the fashion industry and the cultural impact of this star-studded event.

3. ‘Dior and I’ (2014)
Directed by Frédéric Tcheng, this documentary offers a behind-the-scenes look at the creation of Raf Simons’ first haute couture collection for the House of Dior. From the initial sketches to the final runway show, viewers witness the creative process and the dedication of the talented team behind one of the most acclaimed fashion houses in the world. With stunning visuals and intimate interviews with the designers and seamstresses, this film is a must-watch for anyone interested in the artistry and craftsmanship of haute couture.

4. ‘Jeremy Scott: The People’s Designer’ (2015)
Directed by Vlad Yudin, this documentary follows the life and career of fashion designer Jeremy Scott, known for his bold and unconventional designs. From his humble beginnings in Missouri to becoming the creative director of Moschino, viewers get a glimpse into the creative process and inspiration behind Scott’s eclectic and playful collections. With appearances from celebrities like Miley Cyrus and Katy Perry, this film celebrates the power of creativity and individuality in the world of fashion.

5. ‘The True Cost’ (2015)
While not a traditional fashion documentary, ‘The True Cost’ explores the impact of fast fashion on the environment and the lives of garment workers around the world. Directed by Andrew Morgan, the film sheds light on the dark side of the fashion industry and challenges viewers to rethink their consumption habits. By highlighting the social and environmental consequences of cheap and disposable fashion, this documentary serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of ethical and sustainable fashion practices.

In conclusion, these fashion documentaries offer a diverse and engaging look into the world of fashion, from the glamour of high fashion to the challenges facing the industry today. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast, a budding designer, or simply curious about the creative process behind your favorite brands, these films are sure to entertain, educate, and inspire. So grab your popcorn, cozy up on the couch, and prepare to be transported into the captivating world of fashion through the lens of these must-watch documentaries on Netflix.
