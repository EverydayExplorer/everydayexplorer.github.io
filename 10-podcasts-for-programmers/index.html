10 Podcasts for Programmers


Are you a programmer looking for a way to stay updated with the latest industry trends, gain new insights, and connect with like-minded professionals? If so, you’re in luck! In this blog post, we’ll be showcasing 10 amazing podcasts that are tailored specifically for programmers. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced developer, these podcasts will provide you with valuable information, tips, and inspiration to help enhance your programming skills.

1. Software Engineering Daily
The Software Engineering Daily podcast is a great resource for programmers who want to stay up-to-date on the rapidly evolving world of software engineering. Hosted by Jeff Meyerson, this podcast features in-depth interviews with industry experts and explores various topics such as machine learning, cloud computing, and cybersecurity.

2. Syntax
If you’re interested in web development, Syntax is the podcast for you. Hosted by Wes Bos and Scott Tolinski, this podcast covers everything from frontend frameworks to backend technologies. Their episodes are packed with tips, tricks, and real-world examples that will help you become a better web developer.

3. The Changelog
The Changelog podcast is dedicated to open source software, providing programmers with insights into the world of open source development. Hosted by Adam Stacoviak and Jerod Santo, this podcast features interviews with developers who are creating and maintaining influential open source projects.

4. The Bike Shed
The Bike Shed is a podcast that focuses on a wide array of programming topics, making it a great resource for programmers of all levels. Hosted by Steph Viccari and Chris Toomey, this podcast explores topics such as code quality, testing, productivity, and career growth.

5. CodeNewbie
CodeNewbie is a podcast for people who are new to programming. Hosted by Saron Yitbarek, this podcast features interviews with individuals who have successfully made the transition into the world of programming. It’s a great source of inspiration and motivation for those starting their programming journey.

6. The Freelancer’s Show
The Freelancer’s Show is a podcast that caters to freelance programmers and developers. Hosted by seasoned freelancers, this podcast provides valuable insights and tips on how to navigate the freelance world, land clients, and manage projects effectively.

7. The Laravel Podcast
For developers working with Laravel, The Laravel Podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by Matt Stauffer and Taylor Otwell, the creator of Laravel, this podcast focuses on all things related to the popular PHP framework. It covers best practices, new features, and community news.

8. Programming Throwdown
Programming Throwdown is a podcast that explores a wide range of programming languages, tools, and concepts. Hosted by Patrick Wheeler and Jason Gauci, this podcast dives deep into the technical details and provides listeners with a comprehensive understanding of programming topics.

9. Developer Tea
If you’re a busy programmer looking for short, bite-sized episodes, Developer Tea has got you covered. Hosted by Jonathan Cutrell, this podcast features quick discussions on a variety of programming-related topics, such as productivity, career development, and code optimization.

10. Hanselminutes
Hanselminutes, hosted by Scott Hanselman, is a podcast that covers a diverse range of technology topics, including programming, artificial intelligence, and software development methodologies. Scott interviews industry experts and thought leaders, providing valuable insights and perspectives.

In conclusion, these 10 podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge, insights, and inspiration for programmers of all experience levels. Whether you’re looking to stay updated with the latest industry trends, master a specific programming language, or improve your career prospects, these podcasts will undoubtedly help you on your programming journey. Tune in to these podcasts during your commute, workout, or downtime, and take your programming skills to the next level!
