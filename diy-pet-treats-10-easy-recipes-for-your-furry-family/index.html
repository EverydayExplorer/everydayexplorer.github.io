DIY Pet Treats: 10 Easy Recipes for Your Furry Family


As pet owners, we love to pamper our furry friends with delicious treats that not only satisfy their taste buds but also promote their health and well-being. Store-bought treats can be expensive, and sometimes their ingredients are questionable. That’s why creating homemade pet treats is a fantastic way to ensure that your pets receive the best quality snacks while also saving money. In this blog post, we will explore 10 easy and delicious DIY pet treat recipes that your furry family will adore.

1. Peanut Butter Pupcakes

Ingredients:
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 1/4 cup natural peanut butter
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup low-fat milk

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, applesauce, peanut butter, egg, and milk until well combined.
3. Fill each muffin liner about two-thirds full with the batter.
4. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
5. Allow the pupcakes to cool completely before serving.

2. Sweet Potato and Carrot Dog Treats

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked and mashed sweet potatoes
- 1 cup grated carrots
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/2 cup oat flour

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the sweet potatoes, carrots, whole wheat flour, and oat flour until a dough forms.
3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to 1/4-inch thickness and use cookie cutters to create shapes.
4. Place the treats on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until firm.
5. Allow the treats to cool completely before serving.

3. Fish and Oatmeal Cat Treats

Ingredients:
- 1 can of tuna in water (drained)
- 1 cup cooked oatmeal
- 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the tuna, oatmeal, and parsley until well combined.
3. Use a spoon or your hands to shape the mixture into small balls or patties.
4. Place the treats on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until firm.
5. Allow the treats to cool completely before serving.

4. Pumpkin and Cinnamon Cat Treats

Ingredients:
- 1 cup canned pumpkin puree
- 1/2 cup oat flour
- 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the pumpkin puree, oat flour, whole wheat flour, and cinnamon until a dough forms.
3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to 1/4-inch thickness and use cookie cutters to create shapes.
4. Place the treats on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until firm.
5. Allow the treats to cool completely before serving.
