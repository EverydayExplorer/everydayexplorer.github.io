The Best Fashion Apps for Outfit Planning and Inspiration


In today’s fast-paced world, where fashion trends change at lightning speed, staying stylish can feel overwhelming. Thankfully, the rise of technology has brought a wave of fashion apps that help you plan outfits, gather inspiration, and streamline your wardrobe choices. Whether you’re looking for ways to maximize your closet space, experiment with new styles, or simply find the perfect outfit for an event, there’s an app for that. Here, we explore some of the best fashion apps that make outfit planning a breeze and provide endless inspiration.

1. Cladwell

Cladwell is a unique app designed to help you create a capsule wardrobe. By encouraging users to focus on essential pieces, Cladwell simplifies your daily outfit choices. After you upload your clothing items, the app will generate daily outfit suggestions based on your personal style and the weather.

Key Features:

  • Personalized Outfit Suggestions: Cladwell learns your style preferences and curates outfit combinations tailored to your taste.
  • Weather Integration: It provides outfit recommendations based on current weather conditions.
  • Closet Management: You can track what you own and receive insights on how to maximize your wardrobe.

This app is perfect for those looking to declutter their closet while maintaining a stylish appearance.

2. Stylebook

Stylebook is a comprehensive wardrobe management app that allows you to digitize your closet. By taking photos of your clothing, you can create a virtual wardrobe that enables you to mix and match pieces effortlessly.

Key Features:

  • Outfit Planning: Create outfits for different occasions and save them for later reference.
  • Lookbook: Compile your favorite outfits into a lookbook to inspire future choices.
  • Packing Lists: Perfect for travelers, Stylebook allows you to create packing lists based on your planned outfits.

Stylebook is an excellent tool for fashion enthusiasts who love to plan ahead and experiment with styles before stepping out.

3. Polyvore (now part of Ssense)

While Polyvore may no longer exist in its original form, its legacy lives on through various platforms. The essence of Polyvore was to allow users to create mood boards and outfit collages, inspiring countless fashionistas around the world.

Key Features:

  • Collage Maker: Utilize a vast library of clothing items to create unique outfit combinations.
  • Community Sharing: Engage with a community of fashion lovers by sharing your creations and drawing inspiration from others.
  • Shopping Links: Easily find and purchase items directly from your collages.

For those seeking a creative outlet, apps that offer similar functionalities can be found on platforms like Ssense and other fashion boards.

4. Chicisimo

Chicisimo serves as both an outfit planner and a community-driven inspiration platform. Users can upload their outfits and tag items, allowing others to browse and find ideas based on specific pieces.

Key Features:

  • Outfit Inspiration: Browse thousands of user-uploaded outfits for fresh ideas tailored to your preferences.
  • Closet Organization: Keep track of what you own and discover new ways to style your items.
  • Event Planning: Find outfit suggestions for specific events, whether it’s a wedding, a casual outing, or a business meeting.

Chicisimo fosters a sense of community, making it easier for users to connect and share their love for fashion.

5. Pinterest

Pinterest is perhaps the most versatile app on this list, serving as a visual search engine for fashion inspiration. By following boards and pins related to your style, you can curate a collection of outfit ideas that resonate with you.

Key Features:

  • Endless Inspiration: Pin outfits, accessories, and trends that catch your eye for future reference.
  • Custom Boards: Create boards for specific occasions, seasons, or personal styles.
  • Search Functionality: Use keywords to find exactly what you’re looking for, whether it’s boho chic outfits or office attire.

Pinterest is invaluable for anyone looking to explore diverse styles and discover new trends.

6. ShopLook

ShopLook is a direct evolution of the Polyvore spirit, allowing users to create outfit sets and mood boards with ease. This app enables users to browse a wide variety of clothing items and accessories from different retailers.

Key Features:

  • Outfit Creation: Build outfits using an extensive library of clothing items from various brands.
  • Community Engagement: Share your creations and receive feedback from other users.
  • Shopping Integration: Direct links to retailers make shopping for your favorite looks simple and convenient.

ShopLook is perfect for those who love to curate looks from multiple sources and share their fashion sense with others.

7. ClosetSpace

ClosetSpace is designed to help you overcome the dilemma of what to wear each day. It combines elements of closet organization with outfit planning to create a personalized experience.

Key Features:

  • Daily Outfit Suggestions: Based on your wardrobe, weather, and personal style, ClosetSpace recommends outfits for you.
  • Inventory Management: Track your clothing items and see what you wear most frequently.
  • Style Goals: Set personal goals for your wardrobe and receive tips on how to achieve them.

This app is ideal for those who want to simplify their mornings and eliminate decision fatigue.

8. GlamOutfit

GlamOutfit is a stylish outfit planner that allows users to upload photos of their clothing and accessories. It simplifies outfit planning while offering a clean and user-friendly interface.

Key Features:

  • Outfit Planning: Organize outfits for various occasions and save them for quick access.
  • Virtual Closet: Upload items to create a virtual representation of your wardrobe.
  • Weather-Based Suggestions: Get outfit recommendations based on the weather forecast.

For anyone looking to streamline their outfit selection process, GlamOutfit is a fantastic choice.

9. Fashmates

Fashmates is another platform inspired by the original Polyvore, focusing on fashion collaboration and creativity. Users can create, share, and browse outfits while connecting with a community of fashion lovers.

Key Features:

  • Outfit Creation Tools: Build outfits using clothing pieces from a diverse array of retailers.
  • Social Features: Follow other users, comment on their outfits, and engage with the community.
  • Shopping Options: Easily shop for items featured in your favorite outfits.

Fashmates encourages creativity and exploration, making it a go-to app for fashion enthusiasts.

10. FashionTap

FashionTap is a social media platform specifically for fashion lovers. Users can post their outfits and get feedback and inspiration from a community of like-minded individuals.

Key Features:

  • Inspiration Feed: Discover new trends and styles through user-generated content.
  • Outfit Posting: Share your outfits and get feedback from followers.
  • Fashion Challenges: Participate in challenges to showcase your creativity and engage with the community.

FashionTap is perfect for those who enjoy sharing their style and connecting with others in the fashion world.

Conclusion

With the myriad of fashion apps available today, planning outfits and seeking inspiration has never been easier. From comprehensive wardrobe management tools like Stylebook to socially-driven platforms like FashionTap, there’s something for everyone. These apps not only simplify the process of getting dressed but also foster a sense of community and creativity among fashion enthusiasts.

Embrace the digital age of fashion and transform your wardrobe experience with these innovative tools. Whether you’re looking to declutter your closet or simply find new ways to express your style, these fashion apps will empower you to dress confidently and creatively. Dive into the world of outfit planning and inspiration today!
