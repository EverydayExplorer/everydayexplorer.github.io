Best Bourbon Brands to Try This Year


With the surge in popularity of bourbon over recent years, navigating the vast array of options can feel like exploring a whiskey wonderland. From small-batch craft distilleries to well-established titans of the industry, the bourbon landscape offers something for every palate. Whether you’re a seasoned connoisseur or just beginning your bourbon journey, there’s never been a better time to explore the diverse world of this quintessential American spirit. So, pour yourself a glass, sit back, and let’s delve into some of the best bourbon brands to tantalize your taste buds this year.

  1. Buffalo Trace: A staple in any bourbon enthusiast’s collection, Buffalo Trace offers a range of expressions that consistently deliver on quality and flavor. From their flagship Buffalo Trace Bourbon to the coveted Pappy Van Winkle lineup, this distillery’s commitment to excellence shines through in every bottle.

  2. Woodford Reserve: Nestled in the heart of Kentucky’s bourbon country, Woodford Reserve is renowned for its meticulous craftsmanship and attention to detail. Their flagship bourbon boasts a rich, full-bodied flavor profile with notes of caramel, vanilla, and toasted oak, making it a go-to choice for whiskey lovers worldwide.

  3. Four Roses: With a history dating back to the 19th century, Four Roses has earned its place as one of the most respected names in the bourbon world. Their lineup includes a variety of expressions, each meticulously crafted to showcase the distinctive character of their unique mash bills and yeast strains.

  4. Blanton’s: Widely regarded as one of the finest single barrel bourbons available, Blanton’s is synonymous with luxury and sophistication. Each bottle is hand-selected to ensure exceptional quality, resulting in a bourbon that’s as elegant as it is delicious.

  5. Maker’s Mark: Known for its iconic red wax seal, Maker’s Mark has been producing premium bourbon since the 1950s. Their signature wheated mash bill gives their whiskey a smooth, approachable character that’s perfect for sipping neat or mixing into your favorite cocktails.

  6. Wild Turkey: For those who prefer their bourbon with a bit more bite, Wild Turkey delivers in spades. With bold flavors and a higher proof, their lineup caters to the adventurous palate, offering an array of expressions that are sure to leave a lasting impression.

  7. Angel’s Envy: Finished in port wine barrels, Angel’s Envy is a bourbon unlike any other. The additional aging imparts a unique complexity to the whiskey, resulting in a smooth, velvety mouthfeel and layers of rich, nuanced flavor.

  8. Old Forester: As America’s oldest continuously operating distillery, Old Forester has a storied history of producing exceptional bourbon. Their flagship expression, Old Forester 86 Proof, is a true classic, with a balanced flavor profile that’s both timeless and versatile.

  9. Eagle Rare: True to its name, Eagle Rare is a rare find indeed. This award-winning bourbon is aged for a minimum of ten years, resulting in a whiskey of unparalleled depth and complexity. With its smooth, silky texture and layers of caramel, oak, and spice, Eagle Rare is a must-try for any bourbon aficionado.

  10. Booker’s: Named in honor of legendary master distiller Booker Noe, Booker’s bourbon is a tribute to tradition and craftsmanship. Bottled at cask strength, each batch offers a unique tasting experience, with robust flavors and a powerful finish that’s sure to leave a lasting impression.

In conclusion, the world of bourbon is rich and diverse, with a myriad of brands and expressions to explore. Whether you’re drawn to the bold flavors of high-proof bourbons or prefer the smooth elegance of a well-aged single barrel, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So, raise a glass to the best bourbon brands of the year and savor the flavors of this timeless American spirit. Cheers!
