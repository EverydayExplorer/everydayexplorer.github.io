Funny YouTube Channels That Will Make Your Day


Do you find yourself in need of a good laugh? Look no further than the world of YouTube, where you can find an abundance of funny channels that are guaranteed to brighten your day. Whether you’re in the mood for hilarious skits, clever parodies, or just some good old-fashioned humor, these YouTube channels have got you covered. Get ready to dive into a world of laughter and entertainment as we explore some of the funniest YouTube channels that will make your day.

1. ‘Epic Rap Battles of History’ - If you love history and rap, then this channel is the perfect combination for you. Witness some of the most iconic figures from different eras engage in epic rap battles. From Einstein vs. Stephen Hawking to Darth Vader vs. Hitler, these creative and witty rap battles will leave you in stitches.

2. ‘Good Mythical Morning’ - Hosted by the hilarious duo Rhett and Link, this channel offers a daily dose of laughter and entertainment. From taste tests to bizarre challenges, Rhett and Link’s dynamic chemistry and quirky humor will keep you hooked. Prepare to laugh out loud as they explore the weird and wonderful world around us.

3. ‘The Slow Mo Guys’ - Ever wondered what everyday objects look like in slow motion? Look no further than ‘The Slow Mo Guys.’ This channel, hosted by Gavin Free and Daniel Gruchy, captures ordinary events and objects in astonishingly slow motion. Their curious experiments and humorous commentary make for a unique and entertaining viewing experience.

4. ‘Screen Junkies’ - Are you a movie enthusiast? If so, ‘Screen Junkies’ is the channel for you. From their popular series ‘Honest Trailers,’ where they hilariously dissect and poke fun at movies, to their insightful movie reviews and analysis, this channel offers a fresh and humorous take on the world of cinema.

5. ‘FailArmy’ - Sometimes, laughter can be found in the misfortunes of others. ‘FailArmy’ is a compilation channel that features the funniest fail videos from around the world. From epic stumbles to hilarious accidents, this channel is guaranteed to make you laugh until your sides hurt.

6. ‘Jenna Marbles’ - Known for her unique brand of comedy and relatable content, Jenna Marbles has become one of the most popular YouTube personalities. With her humorous vlogs, hilarious skits, and entertaining challenges, Jenna’s channel is a must-watch for anyone in need of a good laugh.

7. ‘Miranda Sings’ - If you enjoy comedy that pushes boundaries and blurs the line between reality and fiction, then ‘Miranda Sings’ is the channel for you. Created by comedian Colleen Ballinger, this character-driven channel features Miranda Sings, a delusional and talentless singer with a huge personality. Prepare for outrageous performances and plenty of laughter.

8. ‘Conan O’Brien’ - Although not a traditional YouTube channel, Conan O’Brien’s late-night show has a strong presence on the platform. With his comedic sketches, celebrity interviews, and hilarious travel adventures, Conan brings his signature wit and charm to the small screen.

These are just a few of the many funny YouTube channels that are guaranteed to make your day. Whether you’re looking for a quick laugh or a longer binge-watching session, these channels offer a wide variety of comedic content to suit every taste. So sit back, relax, and get ready to laugh your way through the day with these entertaining YouTube channels.
