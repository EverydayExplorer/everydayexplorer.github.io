The Best Hotels for Adventure Seekers: Outdoor Activities and Beyond


When it comes to planning a vacation for adventure seekers, one of the most important considerations is finding the perfect hotel that not only provides comfortable accommodation but also offers a wide range of outdoor activities. For thrill-seekers and nature lovers, the hotel experience goes beyond just a place to rest - it becomes an integral part of their adventure. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotels for adventure seekers, where outdoor activities are not only available but are also emphasized and celebrated.

1. The Lodge at Ashford Castle - County Mayo, Ireland

Situated on a vast estate in the picturesque County Mayo, The Lodge at Ashford Castle is a haven for adventure enthusiasts. With its stunning location on the shores of Lough Corrib, guests have access to a multitude of outdoor activities such as fishing, kayaking, and cycling. The hotel even offers its own falconry school, where visitors can learn the art of flying and handling birds of prey. For those seeking a more relaxed adventure, the estate also boasts beautiful gardens and walking trails, allowing guests to immerse themselves in nature.

2. Amangiri - Canyon Point, Utah, USA

Nestled in the heart of the American Southwest, Amangiri offers a truly unique adventure experience. Surrounded by mesmerizing desert landscapes and close to iconic national parks such as Bryce Canyon and Zion, this luxury hotel is a gateway to outdoor exploration. Guests can embark on guided hikes, hot air balloon rides, or even exhilarating helicopter tours to witness the stunning geological formations of the region. After a day of adventure, relax in the hotel’s spa, which is built around a natural rock formation, providing a serene and rejuvenating experience.

3. Fogo Island Inn - Newfoundland, Canada

For adventure seekers who crave a taste of the wild and desire a truly remote experience, Fogo Island Inn is the answer. Situated on a rugged, windswept island off the coast of Newfoundland, this award-winning hotel immerses guests in the natural beauty of the area. Explore the island’s dramatic coastline with activities such as sea kayaking, iceberg watching, and hiking along the scenic trails. The hotel also offers opportunities for cultural immersion, with visits to traditional fishing communities and workshops with local artisans.

4. Explora Patagonia - Torres del Paine National Park, Chile

Located in the breathtaking Torres del Paine National Park, Explora Patagonia is the perfect base camp for outdoor adventures in one of the world’s most spectacular landscapes. From hiking through stunning mountain vistas to horseback riding across vast grasslands, there is an endless array of activities to suit every adventurer’s taste. The hotel’s expert guides are on hand to help guests navigate the park’s trails and share their knowledge of the local flora and fauna. After a day of exploration, unwind in the hotel’s hot tubs, which offer panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

5. The Brando - Tetiaroa, French Polynesia

For adventure seekers who prefer a tropical paradise, The Brando is an idyllic retreat on the private island of Tetiaroa in French Polynesia. Surrounded by turquoise waters and pristine white-sand beaches, this eco-friendly resort offers a variety of outdoor activities, both on land and in the ocean. Guests can explore the coral reefs through snorkeling and diving excursions, paddleboard through the calm lagoon, or take guided nature walks to discover the island’s wildlife. The resort also offers cultural activities, such as traditional Polynesian dancing and art workshops.

6. Singita Sasakwa Lodge - Serengeti National Park, Tanzania

For wildlife enthusiasts, Singita Sasakwa Lodge in the heart of Tanzania’s Serengeti National Park is a dream come true. This luxury safari lodge offers an immersive experience in the African wilderness, with close proximity to the Great Migration and abundant wildlife viewing opportunities. Guests can partake in game drives, guided bush walks, and even hot air balloon safaris to witness the stunning landscapes and diverse wildlife of the Serengeti. After a day of adventure, relax in the lodge’s elegant rooms, which offer sweeping views of the plains.

These are just a few examples of hotels that cater to adventure seekers and offer a wealth of outdoor activities. Whether you’re seeking adrenaline-pumping adventures or serene nature escapes, these hotels provide the perfect combination of comfort, service, and unforgettable experiences. So, why settle for a mundane hotel when you can elevate your adventure by choosing a destination that goes above and beyond to provide an immersive and thrilling experience? Book your next adventure at one of these exceptional hotels and embark on a journey of a lifetime.
