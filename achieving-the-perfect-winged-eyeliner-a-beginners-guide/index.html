Achieving the Perfect Winged Eyeliner: A Beginner's Guide


Achieving the perfect winged eyeliner can be a daunting task, especially for beginners. The sleek and dramatic look of winged eyeliner has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it’s no wonder why. It adds a touch of glamour and sophistication to any makeup look, instantly elevating your overall appearance. However, mastering this art form requires precision, practice, and a few tips and tricks. In this beginner’s guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect winged eyeliner look.

1. Gather your tools: Before you start, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You’ll need an eyeliner pencil or gel liner, an angled eyeliner brush, and a steady hand. If you’re new to winged eyeliner, it may be helpful to start with a pencil liner, as it’s easier to control.

2. Prep your eyes: Begin by prepping your eyes with an eyeshadow primer or a concealer. This will create a smooth canvas for your eyeliner and help it last longer. Set the primer with a translucent powder to prevent smudging.

3. Start with a guide: Creating a guide will make it easier to achieve symmetry. Take a small piece of tape or a business card and align it with the outer corner of your eye, angling it towards the end of your brow. This will create a straight line for your wing.

4. Outline the wing: Using your eyeliner pencil or gel liner, start by outlining the wing. Begin from the outer corner of your eye and follow the angle set by the guide. Keep the line thin and gradually thicken it as you move towards the center of your eye.

5. Connect the line: Once you’ve outlined the wing, draw a line along your upper lash line towards the inner corner of your eye. This line should gradually thin out as you reach the inner corner.

6. Fill in the wing: With your angled eyeliner brush, fill in the wing with small, precise strokes. This will ensure a smooth and even application. If you prefer a more intense look, you can go over the wing with another layer of eyeliner.

7. Clean up any mistakes: Use a cotton swab dipped in makeup remover to clean up any mistakes or smudges. Take your time and be patient, as this step can make a big difference in achieving a polished look.

8. Enhance your lashes: To complete your winged eyeliner look, apply mascara to your lashes. This will help define and frame your eyes, complementing the winged eyeliner.

9. Practice makes perfect: Remember, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner takes practice. Don’t be discouraged if your first attempts don’t turn out exactly as you envisioned. Keep practicing, experimenting with different techniques and products until you find what works best for you.

10. Confidence is key: The most important tip of all is to wear your winged eyeliner with confidence. Embrace the uniqueness of your own style and don’t be afraid to rock the look. Remember, makeup is all about self-expression and having fun.

In conclusion, achieving the perfect winged eyeliner can be a challenge, but with practice and the right techniques, you’ll be able to master this art form. Follow these steps, gather your tools, and embrace your inner artist. With time and patience, you’ll be rocking flawless winged eyeliner looks in no time. So go ahead, experiment, and let your eyes do the talking!
