The Best Sports Documentaries on Netflix: Compelling Stories for Sports Fans


Sports documentaries have become increasingly popular in recent years, offering viewers a unique glimpse into the lives and stories of athletes from various disciplines. With the rise of streaming platforms like Netflix, sports fans now have access to a wide range of captivating and compelling sports documentaries at their fingertips. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best sports documentaries available on Netflix, guaranteed to entertain and inspire sports enthusiasts.

1. ‘Last Chance U’

‘Last Chance U’ takes viewers inside the world of college football, following the trials and tribulations of talented but troubled athletes as they navigate their way through the highly competitive world of junior college football. This series provides an intimate look at the relentless pursuit of success, highlighting the challenges faced by both players and coaches. With its gripping storytelling and raw emotions, ‘Last Chance U’ is a must-watch for any football fan.

2. ‘Icarus’

‘Icarus’ is a thought-provoking documentary that delves into the dark underbelly of sports doping. Filmmaker Bryan Fogel sets out to uncover the truth behind the scandalous world of performance-enhancing drugs by subjecting himself to a rigorous doping regimen while competing in a cycling race. As the story unfolds, the documentary takes an unexpected turn, revealing a web of corruption that reaches far beyond the world of sports. ‘Icarus’ is a compelling exploration of the lengths athletes will go to in order to gain a competitive edge.

3. ‘The Last Dance’

‘The Last Dance’ chronicles the final season of the iconic Chicago Bulls basketball team, led by Michael Jordan. This documentary provides an in-depth look at Jordan’s legendary career, as well as the tumultuous dynamics within the team. With never-before-seen footage and exclusive interviews, ‘The Last Dance’ offers a fascinating insight into the mindset and determination of one of the greatest basketball players of all time.

4. ‘Sunderland ‘Til I Die’

For soccer fans, ‘Sunderland ‘Til I Die’ is a captivating documentary series that follows the ups and downs of the Sunderland Association Football Club. The series provides an intimate portrayal of the club’s struggles on and off the field, capturing the unwavering passion and dedication of its fans. ‘Sunderland ‘Til I Die’ is a compelling reminder of the emotional rollercoaster that comes with being a die-hard sports supporter.

5. ‘Cheer’

‘Cheer’ takes viewers inside the world of competitive cheerleading, showcasing the incredible physical and mental demands of the sport. This documentary series follows the Navarro College cheerleading team as they prepare for the National Cheerleading Championship. With its heartwarming and inspiring storytelling, ‘Cheer’ highlights the dedication and perseverance required to excel in this often-underappreciated sport.

These are just a few examples of the best sports documentaries available on Netflix. Whether you’re a fan of football, basketball, cycling, soccer, or cheerleading, these documentaries offer a captivating blend of drama, inspiration, and human resilience. So grab your popcorn, settle into your favorite spot on the couch, and immerse yourself in these compelling stories that will leave you cheering for more.
