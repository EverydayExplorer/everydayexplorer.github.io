The Ultimate Guide to Fashion Blogs: Must-Follow Fashionistas


Fashion blogging has become a massive industry in recent years, and with good reason. Fashion influencers have a unique ability to inspire and engage audiences, offering a glimpse into their stylish lives and sharing their thoughts on all things fashion-related. If you’re looking for some serious style inspiration, look no further than the following fashionistas, who have mastered the art of the fashion blog.

1. Chiara Ferragni - The Blonde Salad
With over 18.9 million Instagram followers and counting, Chiara Ferragni has become a household name in the fashion industry. Her blog, The Blonde Salad, covers everything from latest fashion trends to lifestyle and travel. Chiara’s impeccable sense of style, coupled with her charismatic personality, has earned her collaborations with major fashion brands and a spot on Forbes’ 2017 list of the world’s highest-paid fashion influencers.

2. Aimee Song - Song of Style
Aimee Song’s blog, Song of Style, has been a go-to destination for fashion enthusiasts since its inception in 2008. Aimee’s relatable and accessible approach to fashion has garnered her a loyal following of 5.4 million Instagram followers. Not only does she share her own personal style, but Aimee also provides insights into the latest runway shows and fashion events. Her expertise has landed her collaborations with renowned brands like Dior and Gucci.

3. Nicole Warne - Gary Pepper Girl
Nicole Warne, the creative mind behind Gary Pepper Girl, is known for her distinct vintage-inspired style. Her blog showcases her stunning fashion editorials and travel adventures, all infused with her sophisticated aesthetic. Nicole’s impeccable taste has garnered her collaborations with luxury brands like Louis Vuitton and Bulgari, solidifying her status as a must-follow fashionista.

4. Danielle Bernstein - We Wore What
Danielle Bernstein launched We Wore What in 2010, and it quickly gained attention for its unique mix of high fashion and street style. With her vast knowledge of trends and ability to curate unique looks, Danielle has amassed a dedicated following of 2.5 million Instagram followers. In addition to her blog, Danielle has successfully launched her own clothing line and accessories collection.

5. Blair Eadie - Atlantic-Pacific
Blair Eadie’s blog, Atlantic-Pacific, is a visual feast for any fashion enthusiast. Known for her vibrant and bold style, Blair effortlessly mixes patterns and textures to create eye-catching outfits. Her impeccable street style has earned her collaborations with major brands like Nordstrom and Halogen, making her a force to be reckoned with in the fashion blogging world.

6. Wendy Nguyen - Wendy’s Lookbook
Wendy Nguyen’s blog, Wendy’s Lookbook, is a true representation of her personal journey through fashion. With a focus on affordable and accessible style, Wendy inspires her followers to experiment and have fun with their clothing choices. Her YouTube channel, which features fashion and beauty tips, has amassed over one million subscribers. Wendy’s dedication to empowering her audience to embrace their unique style makes her an essential fashionista to follow.

7. Bryan Grey Yambao - Bryanboy
Bryan Grey Yambao, better known as Bryanboy, is a pioneer in the fashion blogging world. With his quirky and flamboyant sense of style, Bryan has captivated audiences worldwide. From attending fashion shows to collaborating with luxury brands, Bryan has cemented his position as one of the most influential fashion bloggers of our time.

These fashion bloggers have mastered the art of inspiring and engaging their audiences with their unique takes on fashion. Whether you’re looking for high-end luxury inspiration or affordable street style ideas, these must-follow fashionistas have got you covered. So, grab your coffee, fire up your laptop, and immerse yourself in the fabulous world of fashion blogging!
