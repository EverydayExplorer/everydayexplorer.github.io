10 Hair Color Trends to Try in 2021


Are you looking to refresh your look in 2021? One way to do so is by changing up your hair color. Whether you’re looking for a subtle change or a bold statement, there are plenty of hair color trends to try in 2021. In this blog post, we’ll explore 10 of the hottest hair color trends that you should consider this year. So, let’s dive in!

1. Balayage: This technique is still going strong in 2021. Balayage creates a natural, sun-kissed effect that adds dimension to your hair. It involves hand-painting highlights onto specific sections of your hair, resulting in a seamless blend of colors.

2. Babylights: If you’re looking for a more delicate and subtle change, babylights are perfect for you. These fine, delicate highlights mimic the natural highlights children get from the sun. They add a touch of brightness and dimension to your hair without overpowering your base color.

3. Money Piece: This trend is all about making a statement. A money piece involves adding a chunky highlight or a bold streak of color at the front of your hair. It adds a pop of color and draws attention to your face, creating a trendy and edgy look.

4. Rooted Blonde: A rooted blonde look is achieved by keeping the roots slightly darker than the rest of your hair. This creates a low-maintenance color that grows out seamlessly. It’s a great option for those who want to go blonde but don’t want the constant upkeep.

5. Platinum Blonde: If you’re feeling daring, going platinum blonde might be the trend for you. This icy and ultra-light shade of blonde has been popular among celebrities and influencers. It demands attention and creates a high-fashion look that is both striking and glamorous.

6. Chocolate Brown: For those who prefer a darker shade, chocolate brown is a timeless color choice. It adds warmth and depth to your hair, and it suits almost all skin tones. This versatile shade can be easily customized to create highlights or lowlights to add dimension.

7. Ashy Gray: Gray hair has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it’s not going away anytime soon. The ashy gray trend involves achieving a cool-toned gray color that looks effortlessly chic. It’s a bold and modern choice that can be customized with hints of lavender, blue, or silver for added uniqueness.

8. Copper Red: If you want to make a statement, copper red is the way to go. This fiery shade of red is vibrant and attention-grabbing. It suits a wide range of skin tones and adds warmth to your overall look. Whether you opt for a full head of copper red or just a few highlights, this shade is sure to turn heads.

9. Rose Gold: Rose gold has been a popular hair color trend for several years, and it shows no signs of slowing down. This romantic and trendy shade blends pink and gold tones to create a beautiful and feminine look. It’s a great choice for those who want to add a touch of whimsy to their hair.

10. Ombre: Ombre has been a popular hair color trend for several years, and it’s still going strong in 2021. This technique involves blending two or more colors seamlessly, usually with a darker shade at the roots blending into a lighter shade at the ends. Ombre gives your hair a gradient effect that adds depth and dimension.

These are just 10 of the many hair color trends to try in 2021. Whether you’re looking for a subtle change or a bold statement, there’s a trend that suits your style and personality. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist to find the best color and technique for your hair type and desired look. So, go ahead and experiment with these trends, and embrace a fresh new look this year!
