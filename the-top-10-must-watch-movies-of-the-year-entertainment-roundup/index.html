The Top 10 Must-Watch Movies of the Year: Entertainment Roundup


As the year comes to a close, it’s time to take a look back at the best movies that 2021 had to offer. From thrilling action flicks to heartwarming dramas, this year’s film lineup did not disappoint. In this entertainment roundup, we bring you the top 10 must-watch movies of the year.

1. ‘The Trial of the Chicago 7’

‘The Trial of the Chicago 7’ is a historical legal drama, directed by Aaron Sorkin, that follows the infamous trial of seven individuals charged with conspiracy and inciting a riot during the 1968 Democratic National Convention in Chicago. With a star-studded cast and powerful performances, this movie is a thought-provoking journey into an important chapter of American history.

2. ‘Nomadland’

‘Nomadland’ is a poignant and beautiful drama that tells the story of a woman, played by Frances McDormand, who embarks on a journey across the American West, living as a modern-day nomad. Directed by Chloé Zhao, this film captures the spirit of resilience and the search for meaning in a world that often leaves people behind.

3. ‘Promising Young Woman’

‘Promising Young Woman’ is a gripping and darkly comedic thriller that follows the story of Cassie, played by Carey Mulligan, a woman seeking revenge against those who have wronged her. This thought-provoking film, directed by Emerald Fennell, tackles themes of consent, misogyny, and the consequences of actions.

4. ‘Minari’

‘Minari’ is a heartwarming drama that explores the immigrant experience through the eyes of a Korean-American family who moves to rural Arkansas in the 1980s. Directed by Lee Isaac Chung, this film captures the struggles and triumphs of pursuing the American Dream, while also celebrating the importance of family and resilience.

5. ‘Sound of Metal’

‘Sound of Metal’ is a powerful drama that delves into the life of a heavy-metal drummer, played by Riz Ahmed, who begins to lose his hearing and must come to terms with his new reality. This thought-provoking film, directed by Darius Marder, explores themes of identity, acceptance, and the power of human connection.

6. ‘The Father’

‘The Father’ is a deeply emotional and intimate drama that takes an unflinching look at the experience of living with dementia. Directed by Florian Zeller, this film stars Anthony Hopkins and Olivia Colman, who deliver incredible performances that will leave you moved and introspective.

7. ‘Soul’

Pixar’s ‘Soul’ is a visually stunning and deeply philosophical animated film that explores the concepts of life, purpose, and what it means to have a soul. Directed by Pete Docter and Kemp Powers, this movie takes viewers on a journey that will make them reflect on life’s big questions while being entertained by colorful characters and a captivating story.

8. ‘Ma Rainey’s Black Bottom’

‘Ma Rainey’s Black Bottom’ is a powerful drama that brings to life the story of legendary blues singer Ma Rainey, played by Viola Davis, and the challenges she faced as a Black woman in the music industry. Directed by George C. Wolfe, this film highlights the strength and resilience of Ma Rainey while shedding light on important racial and cultural issues.

9. ‘Mank’

‘Mank’ is a visually stunning and meticulously crafted drama directed by David Fincher. The film explores the life of renowned screenwriter Herman J. Mankiewicz, played by Gary Oldman, as he races to finish the screenplay for ‘Citizen Kane.’ With stunning cinematography and a captivating story, this movie offers a glimpse into the Golden Age of Hollywood and the challenges faced by those within the industry.

10. ‘Tenet’

‘Tenet’ is a mind-bending and action-packed thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. This movie follows a secret agent, played by John David Washington, as he embarks on a mission to prevent a global catastrophe. With its intricate plot and breathtaking visual effects, ‘Tenet’ is a must-watch for fans of intelligent and thought-provoking cinema.

These are just a few of the remarkable movies that made waves in 2021. From thought-provoking dramas to thrilling action flicks, this year’s film lineup has something for everyone. So grab the popcorn, sit back, and enjoy the cinematic journey that these movies have to offer.
