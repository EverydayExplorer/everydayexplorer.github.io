Fashionable Ways to Wear Animal Print


Animal prints are a timeless trend that has made a major comeback in recent years. Whether it’s the classic leopard print, zebra stripes, or snake skin, animal prints can instantly elevate your outfit and make a bold fashion statement. From clothing to accessories, there are numerous stylish ways to incorporate animal print into your wardrobe. In this blog post, we will explore some fashionable ways to wear animal print that will help you stand out from the crowd.

1. Make a Statement with a Printed Coat

One of the easiest ways to incorporate animal print into your outfit is by opting for a printed coat. Whether it’s a leopard print trench coat or a zebra-striped faux fur coat, an animal print outerwear piece can instantly transform a simple outfit into a stylish one. Pair it with basic pieces like a monochrome dress or jeans and a plain top to let the coat take center stage. This is a perfect option for those who want to embrace animal print without going overboard.

2. Mix and Match with Neutral Colors

Animal prints are often vibrant and attention-grabbing, so it’s important to balance them with neutral colors to create a sophisticated look. Opt for solid black, white, beige, or grey pieces to complement the boldness of the animal print. For example, pair a leopard print blouse with black high-waisted trousers or a snakeskin skirt with a white blouse. This combination allows the animal print to be the focal point of the outfit while maintaining a polished and fashion-forward look.

3. Accessorize with Animal Print

If you are not ready to commit to a full-on animal print outfit, start by incorporating animal print accessories into your ensemble. A leopard print handbag, a snakeskin belt, or zebra print heels can add a touch of excitement to your outfit without overwhelming it. These accessories can be paired with monochrome or solid-colored outfits to make a statement while keeping the overall look balanced.

4. Experiment with Mix-and-Match Patterns

For the fashionably bold, don’t be afraid to experiment with mixing different patterns with animal prints. Animal prints can surprisingly work well with other patterns, such as stripes, polka dots, or floral prints. The key is to make sure the colors in the patterns complement each other. For example, pair a leopard print skirt with a striped top in similar hues or a zebra print blouse with a floral skirt. Mixing patterns is a stylish way to express your creativity and add visual interest to your outfit.

5. Try Animal Print Shoes

Shoes are another great way to incorporate animal print into your wardrobe. Animal print shoes can instantly elevate a simple outfit and add a touch of elegance and edginess. Whether it’s leopard print pumps, snake print ankle boots, or zebra print flats, animal print shoes can be styled with various outfits, from dresses and skirts to jeans and trousers. They are a versatile addition to any wardrobe and can effortlessly make a fashion statement.

6. Layer with Animal Print Scarves or Shawls

Scarves and shawls are not only practical accessories but also an excellent way to add a pop of animal print to your outfit. Whether it’s a leopard print silk scarf or a zebra print shawl, draping it around your neck or shoulders can instantly elevate your look. Animal print scarves or shawls can be paired with almost anything in your wardrobe, from casual outfits to formal ensembles. They are an easy and cost-effective way to incorporate animal print into your style.

7. Mix Different Animal Prints

For the fashion risk-takers, consider mixing different animal prints for a truly bold and unique look. Camouflage leopard print with zebra stripes or pair snake skin with cheetah spots for an unexpected fashion statement. When mixing different animal prints, keep the colors and proportions in mind to ensure a harmonious and cohesive look. This trend is not for the faint of heart, but when done right, it can create a head-turning outfit that showcases your confidence and fashion-forwardness.

In conclusion, animal prints can add a touch of glamour and boldness to your wardrobe. Whether you choose to incorporate them through clothing, accessories, or both, there are numerous fashionable ways to wear animal print and make a statement. From mixing and matching with neutral colors to experimenting with patterns and layering, the possibilities are endless. Embrace your wild side and have fun styling animal prints in your outfits. Remember, confidence is the key to pulling off this trend successfully!
