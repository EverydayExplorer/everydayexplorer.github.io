Crafting with Buttons: Unique Projects to Try


Crafting with buttons is a fantastic way to unleash your creativity and add a unique touch to any project. Buttons come in all shapes, sizes, and colors, making them an incredibly versatile crafting material. From clothing accessories to home decor items, the possibilities are endless when it comes to incorporating buttons into your DIY projects. In this blog post, we will explore some unique crafts that you can try using buttons and provide step-by-step instructions for each project.

Button bookmarks are a simple yet effective way to add a touch of personality to your reading routine. To create your own button bookmark, you will need a few basic supplies. Start by choosing a decorative button that matches your style and desired aesthetic. Then, cut a piece of ribbon or felt measuring about 12 inches in length. Fold the ribbon or felt in half, and sandwich the folded end between the buttonholes of your chosen button. Use a needle and thread to sew the ribbon or felt in place. Once secure, you can trim the ribbon or felt to your desired length. Voila! You now have a beautiful button bookmark that is perfect for marking your place in your favorite books.

Another fun project involving buttons is button magnets. These cute and functional magnets can be used to hold up notes, pictures, or any other lightweight item on your fridge or magnet board. To make button magnets, you will need small flat-backed buttons, strong magnets, and adhesive. Simply add a small amount of adhesive to the flat part of the button and attach the magnet. Let the adhesive dry completely, and your button magnets are ready to use. You can create an array of button magnets using different sizes and colors to add a playful touch to your magnet collection.

If you’re looking to add a pop of color to your clothing, button earrings are a fantastic option. These earrings are not only stylish but also incredibly easy to make. To create button earrings, you will need button shanks (the loop on the back of the button), earring posts, and earring backs. Start by using wire cutters to remove the button shanks from your chosen buttons. Then, apply a dab of super glue to the back of the button and attach the earring post. Let the glue dry completely, and attach the earring backs for extra security. Now, you have a pair of unique and eye-catching button earrings that are sure to turn heads.

Buttons can also be used to create beautiful wall art. To make button art, you will need a blank canvas, various buttons in different colors and sizes, and a hot glue gun. Start by arranging your buttons on the canvas in a pattern or design of your choice. Once you’re satisfied with the placement, use the hot glue gun to secure the buttons to the canvas. Be careful not to burn yourself while using the hot glue gun. Once the buttons are securely attached, let the glue dry completely. Hang your button art on the wall and admire your creative masterpiece.

In addition to these projects, there are countless other ways to incorporate buttons into your crafting endeavors. You can use buttons to embellish clothing, create button bouquets, or even make button-embellished throw pillows. The key is to let your imagination run wild and have fun with your creations.

In conclusion, crafting with buttons opens up a world of possibilities for creative expression. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or just starting out, incorporating buttons into your DIY projects can add a unique and personal touch. From button bookmarks to button magnets and button earrings, there are endless ways to utilize buttons in your crafts. So why wait? Start experimenting with buttons today and let your creativity soar!
