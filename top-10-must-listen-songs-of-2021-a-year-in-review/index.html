Top 10 Must-Listen Songs of 2021: A Year in Review


2021 has been an eventful year for the music industry, with groundbreaking releases across various genres. Whether you’re a die-hard music fan or someone who simply enjoys listening to catchy tunes, this year had something for everyone. As we approach the end of the year, it’s the perfect time to look back and review the top 10 must-listen songs of 2021. Whether you missed out on some hits or simply want to relive the best moments, this list will have you covered.

1. ‘drivers license’ by Olivia Rodrigo
Arguably one of the most talked-about songs of the year, ‘drivers license’ became an instant sensation. This breakout hit from Olivia Rodrigo catapulted her to stardom and topped charts around the world. With heartfelt lyrics and a powerful vocal delivery, this song struck a chord with listeners of all ages.

2. ‘Leave The Door Open’ by Silk Sonic (Bruno Mars, Anderson .Paak)
Get ready to groove because Silk Sonic’s ‘Leave The Door Open’ is a modern take on classic R&B. Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak join forces to create a soulful masterpiece that showcases their incredible talent. The smooth melodies and nostalgic vibe make this track a must-listen for any music lover.

3. ‘Good 4 U’ by Olivia Rodrigo
Continuing her streak of chart-topping hits, Olivia Rodrigo graces our list once again with ‘Good 4 U’. This energetic pop-rock anthem showcases Rodrigo’s versatility as an artist. With its catchy hooks and relatable lyrics, it’s no wonder this song became an instant favorite among fans.

4. ‘Peaches’ by Justin Bieber ft. Daniel Caesar, Giveon
‘Peaches’ brought together an all-star lineup of talent, including Justin Bieber, Daniel Caesar, and Giveon. This laid-back summer jam is perfect for sunny days and road trips. With its catchy chorus and smooth vocals, it’s no surprise this song became an instant hit.

5. ‘Montero (Call Me By Your Name)’ by Lil Nas X
Lil Nas X made a splash with his daring and provocative single, ‘Montero (Call Me By Your Name)’. This song defied expectations and pushed boundaries, solidifying Nas X’s status as an innovative artist. With its infectious beat and memorable lyrics, this track is a must-listen for anyone looking for something unique.

6. ‘Save Your Tears’ by The Weeknd & Ariana Grande (Remix)
When two powerhouse artists collaborate, magic happens. The Weeknd and Ariana Grande joined forces for a remix of ‘Save Your Tears’, and it didn’t disappoint. The combination of The Weeknd’s haunting vocals and Grande’s signature harmonies created a mesmerizing rendition of an already brilliant song.

7. ‘Levitating’ by Dua Lipa ft. DaBaby
Dua Lipa had another standout year with her infectious hit, ‘Levitating’. This disco-inspired track became an instant favorite for many. The addition of DaBaby’s rap verse added a fresh twist to an already catchy song, making it a must-have on any 2021 playlist.

8. ‘Kiss Me More’ by Doja Cat ft. SZA
Doja Cat and SZA teamed up to deliver the ultimate summer anthem with ‘Kiss Me More’. This catchy pop track oozes with infectious melodies and the perfect blend of Doja Cat’s rap verses and SZA’s smooth vocals. It’s a feel-good song that instantly transports you to a sunny paradise.

9. ‘Goodbye’ by Billie Eilish
Billie Eilish captivated audiences once again with her hauntingly beautiful track, ‘Goodbye’. Known for her introspective and vulnerable lyrics, Eilish delivers a mesmerizing performance in this song. With its unique blend of haunting melodies and emotional storytelling, ‘Goodbye’ is a standout track from 2021.

10. ‘Deja Vu’ by Olivia Rodrigo
Closing out our list is yet another hit from Olivia Rodrigo. ‘Deja Vu’ perfectly showcases Rodrigo’s ability to captivate listeners with her raw and emotional songwriting. The catchy hooks and relatable lyrics make this track a must-listen for any music enthusiast.

These top 10 must-listen songs of 2021 represent the diverse range of music that dominated the year. From pop to R&B, and everything in between, this list has something for everyone. Whether you’re a fan of catchy hooks, emotional ballads, or innovative soundscapes, these songs are a testament to the incredible talent and creativity of artists in 2021.

As we look forward to the new year, it’s important to appreciate the impact music has on our lives. These songs have the power to evoke emotions, transport us to different times and places, and create memories that will last a lifetime. So, sit back, relax, and enjoy the top 10 must-listen songs of 2021. Happy listening!
