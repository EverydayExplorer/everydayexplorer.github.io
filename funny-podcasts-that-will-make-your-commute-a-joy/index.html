Funny Podcasts That Will Make Your Commute a Joy


Are you tired of the monotonous and mundane commute to work every day? Looking for something to brighten up those long hours stuck in traffic or on public transportation? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the funniest podcasts out there that will turn your daily commute into a joyride. Prepare to laugh out loud and forget about the never-ending traffic!

1. ‘The Comedy Bang! Bang! Podcast’:
Hosted by the hilarious Scott Aukerman, this podcast features interviews with some of the biggest names in comedy. With a mix of improv, character sketches, and absurd conversations, ‘Comedy Bang! Bang!’ will have you in stitches from start to finish. Get ready for a wild ride full of unexpected twists and turns!

2. ‘My Dad Wrote A Porno’:
If you’re up for some adult humor, this podcast is a must-listen. Jamie Morton reads chapters from his dad’s erotic novel, ‘Belinda Blinked,’ while being joined by his friends James Cooper and Alice Levine. The trio’s hilarious commentary and reactions to the outrageous storylines will leave you doubled over with laughter. Just make sure to wear headphones if you’re listening in public!

3. ‘Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend’:
Late-night talk show host Conan O’Brien brings his quick wit and comedic genius to the podcasting world. In each episode, Conan engages in candid and hilarious conversations with celebrities, comedians, and other notable figures. With his trademark self-deprecating humor and quick comebacks, he will have you laughing so hard that you’ll forget about the long commute ahead.

4. ‘How Did This Get Made?’:
Join comedians Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas as they dissect and mock the most baffling and absurd movies ever made. From cult classics to box office bombs, they leave no stone unturned in their quest to uncover the secrets behind these cinematic disasters. Their witty banter and comedic chemistry will have you snorting with laughter, even if you haven’t seen the movies they’re discussing.

5. ‘The Dollop’:
If you’re a fan of history and comedy, ‘The Dollop’ is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, each episode explores a bizarre and hilarious story from American history. From forgotten presidents to strange events, they bring these tales to life with their unique blend of humor and research. Get ready to learn something new and laugh uncontrollably at the same time.

6. ‘No Such Thing As A Fish’:
This podcast is a treasure trove of weird and wonderful facts. Hosted by the writers of the hit BBC show ‘QI,’ Dan Schreiber, James Harkin, Andrew Hunter Murray, and Anna Ptaszynski, each episode features a plethora of bizarre and fascinating trivia. Their witty banter and infectious enthusiasm will keep you entertained and enlightened throughout your commute.

7. ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’:
Known for his candid conversations and diverse range of guests, Joe Rogan’s podcast is a must-listen for comedy lovers. From comedians to scientists, the podcast covers a wide range of topics, all delivered with Joe’s signature humor and curiosity. Whether you’re interested in current events, pop culture, or the mysteries of the universe, ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’ has something for everyone.

So, next time you find yourself dreading your daily commute, plug in your headphones and tune into one of these hilarious podcasts. They will turn your journey into a laughter-filled adventure and make you forget about the traffic woes. Get ready to laugh your way to work and brighten up your day with these funny podcasts!
