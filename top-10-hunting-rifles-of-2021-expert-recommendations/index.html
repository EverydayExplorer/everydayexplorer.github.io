Top 10 Hunting Rifles of 2021: Expert Recommendations


Are you an avid hunter in search of the perfect rifle for your next hunting expedition? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the top 10 hunting rifles of 2021, based on expert recommendations. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, these rifles are sure to meet your needs and help you achieve success in the field.

1. Remington Model 700
The Remington Model 700 is a timeless classic that has been a favorite among hunters for decades. Known for its accuracy, reliability, and durability, this bolt-action rifle is a popular choice for both big game hunting and long-range shooting. It is available in various calibers, allowing hunters to customize their rifle to suit their specific needs.

2. Browning X-Bolt
The Browning X-Bolt is another top choice among hunters. With its sleek design and innovative features, this rifle offers excellent accuracy and precision. It features a smooth and crisp trigger pull, making it easy to maintain accuracy even during rapid shots. The X-Bolt also has a detachable magazine, allowing for quick and easy reloading in the field.

3. Savage 110 Storm
Designed for harsh and challenging weather conditions, the Savage 110 Storm is a reliable and rugged rifle. Its stainless steel barrel and action are resistant to corrosion, ensuring that it can handle anything Mother Nature throws at it. The AccuFit system allows hunters to adjust the length of pull and comb height, ensuring a comfortable fit and consistent accuracy.

4. Tikka T3x
The Tikka T3x is a lightweight and accurate rifle that is perfect for hunters on the move. It features a smooth bolt action and a crisp trigger, allowing for quick and reliable shots. The T3x also has a modular stock, making it easy to customize and adapt to different hunting situations.

5. Weatherby Vanguard Series 2
Known for its exceptional accuracy, the Weatherby Vanguard Series 2 is a reliable rifle that won’t break the bank. It features a 3-position safety, a match-quality two-stage trigger, and a sub-MOA guarantee, making it a top choice for both seasoned hunters and beginners.

6. Winchester Model 70
The Winchester Model 70 is a classic rifle that has stood the test of time. Renowned for its accuracy and smooth action, the Model 70 is an excellent choice for both experienced hunters and collectors. Available in various calibers and configurations, this rifle offers versatility and performance in the field.

7. Ruger American Rifle
The Ruger American Rifle is a budget-friendly option that doesn’t compromise on quality. It offers a lightweight design, a durable synthetic stock, and a reliable rotary magazine. The Ruger Marksman adjustable trigger allows for a crisp and clean break, ensuring accuracy with every shot.

8. Kimber 84M Mountain Ascent
If you’re a hunter who values lightweight and maneuverability, the Kimber 84M Mountain Ascent is the rifle for you. This ultra-lightweight bolt-action rifle weighs as little as 4 pounds, making it easy to carry on long treks in rugged terrain. Despite its light weight, it still delivers excellent accuracy and reliability.

9. Bergara B-14 Ridge
The Bergara B-14 Ridge is a precision bolt-action rifle that offers exceptional performance in the field. Its free-floating barrel, adjustable trigger, and smooth action ensure accuracy and consistency. The B-14 Ridge also features a hinged floorplate magazine, allowing for easy manipulation of cartridges.

10. Christensen Arms Ridgeline
The Christensen Arms Ridgeline is a high-performance rifle that combines accuracy with lightweight design. It features a carbon fiber-wrapped barrel, reducing weight without compromising accuracy. The Ridgeline also has a match-grade trigger, ensuring a crisp and clean break.

In conclusion, when it comes to choosing the top hunting rifle for 2021, there are plenty of excellent options available. Whether you prioritize accuracy, reliability, or maneuverability, these expert recommendations provide a range of choices to suit your needs. Happy hunting!
