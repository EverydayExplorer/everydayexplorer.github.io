The Most Successful Animated Movies in the Box Office


Welcome to our blog post where we delve into the world of animated movies, exploring the most successful ones that have graced the box office. From heartwarming tales to laugh-out-loud comedies, these animated films have captured the hearts of audiences worldwide. So, grab some popcorn and get ready to dive into the enchanting world of animation!

1. The Lion King (1994)
Let’s kick off our list with a true classic. “The Lion King” roared onto the big screen and became an instant hit. This Disney masterpiece tells the story of a young lion named Simba who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and ultimately claims his rightful place as king. With captivating animation, memorable songs, and a powerful message, “The Lion King” has earned over $968 million worldwide, making it one of the highest-grossing animated films to date.

2. Frozen (2013)
Next up, we have “Frozen,” the icy sensation that took the world by storm. This heartwarming tale of two royal sisters, Elsa and Anna, captured the imagination of audiences of all ages. With its catchy songs, stunning visuals, and empowering message of love and self-acceptance, “Frozen” became a cultural phenomenon. It earned over $1.2 billion at the box office, solidifying its place as one of the most successful animated movies ever.

3. Toy Story 4 (2019)
The “Toy Story” franchise has become a beloved part of cinematic history, and the fourth installment did not disappoint. Woody, Buzz, and the gang returned for another heartfelt adventure that tugged at our heartstrings. With its clever storytelling, emotional depth, and stunning animation, “Toy Story 4” charmed audiences worldwide and grossed over $1 billion at the box office.

4. Minions (2015)
Who can resist those adorable yellow creatures known as the Minions? These lovable sidekicks from the “Despicable Me” franchise got their own spin-off film, aptly titled “Minions.” Audiences were captivated by their mischievous antics and endearing personalities. With its unique humor and family-friendly appeal, “Minions” raked in a whopping $1.16 billion globally, proving that these little yellow creatures have universal charm.

5. Incredibles 2 (2018)
Fans had waited fourteen long years for a sequel to “The Incredibles,” and when it finally arrived, it did not disappoint. “Incredibles 2” brought back the beloved superhero family and delivered an action-packed and hilarious adventure. With its stellar animation, relatable characters, and thrilling storyline, the film soared at the box office, earning over $1.24 billion worldwide.

6. Finding Dory (2016)
Dory, everyone’s favorite forgetful fish, took center stage in this heartwarming sequel to “Finding Nemo.” “Finding Dory” followed her journey to reunite with her family, charming audiences with its humor and emotional depth. The film became a massive hit, grossing over $1.02 billion worldwide, proving that the oceanic adventures of these beloved characters resonate with audiences of all ages.

7. Shrek 2 (2004)
“Shrek 2” waltzed its way into the hearts of audiences with its unique blend of humor, satire, and heartwarming storytelling. This animated gem continued the adventures of Shrek and his eccentric friends, introducing us to memorable new characters along the way. Grossing over $919 million worldwide, “Shrek 2” proved that fairy tales could be reimagined and resonate with a wide audience.

8. Zootopia (2016)
In the animal-centric metropolis of Zootopia, a rookie bunny cop named Judy Hopps teams up with a sly fox named Nick Wilde to solve a mysterious case. “Zootopia” seamlessly combined a compelling detective story with themes of diversity, inclusion, and breaking stereotypes. This clever and visually stunning film earned over $1.02 billion, showcasing the power of animation to deliver important messages.

These are just a few examples of the most successful animated movies in the box office. From timeless classics to modern hits, these films have left an indelible mark on the world of animation. So, whether you’re a fan of heartwarming tales or action-packed adventures, there’s an animated movie out there for everyone to enjoy. Lights, camera, animation!
