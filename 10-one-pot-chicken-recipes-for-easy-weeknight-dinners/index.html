10 One-Pot Chicken Recipes for Easy Weeknight Dinners


One-pot meals are a savior when it comes to weeknight dinners. They’re convenient, easy to make, and require minimal clean-up. And if you’re a chicken lover, you’re in luck! Today, we’re going to share 10 delicious one-pot chicken recipes that will not only simplify your cooking process but also leave your taste buds satisfied. From creamy pastas to flavorful stews, these recipes are sure to become new favorites in your household.

1. Lemon Chicken and Rice Skillet: This citrusy and savory dish is perfect for a quick and hearty meal. Simply sear chicken thighs in a skillet, add rice, lemon juice, and spices, and let it all simmer to perfection. The result is a flavorful and comforting one-pot wonder.

2. Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry: For a healthier option, try this colorful stir-fry. Sauté chicken breast with a medley of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots. Season with soy sauce and garlic for an Asian-inspired flavor. Serve it alongside some steamed rice for a complete meal.

3. Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta: If you’re craving something indulgent, this creamy pasta dish is for you. Sauté chicken with garlic, onion, and sun-dried tomatoes, then add heavy cream, spinach, and parmesan cheese to create a luscious sauce. Toss in your favorite pasta and dinner is ready in no time!

4. BBQ Chicken and Sweet Potato Foil Packets: Take your cooking outdoors with these flavorful foil packets. Combine BBQ-marinated chicken, sweet potatoes, and a medley of vegetables, then wrap them up tightly in foil and bake or grill until the chicken is tender and juicy. This recipe is perfect for summer gatherings or camping trips.

5. Chicken and Lentil Curry: For an exotic yet comforting meal, look no further than this chicken and lentil curry. Simmer chicken, lentils, and aromatic spices in a spiced tomato sauce for a dish that will warm you from the inside out. Serve with naan bread or steamed rice for a truly satisfying dinner.

6. Chicken and Spinach Tortellini Soup: This cozy soup is perfect for colder nights. Combine chicken, tortellini, spinach, and flavorful broth for a hearty and nutritious one-pot meal. Top it off with some grated parmesan cheese and serve with crusty bread for dipping.

7. Chicken and Mushroom Risotto: Risotto may seem intimidating, but with this one-pot recipe, it is a breeze. Sauté chicken and mushrooms, then add Arborio rice, chicken broth, and parmesan cheese. Stir consistently until the rice is creamy and cooked to perfection – no constant stirring required!

8. Lemon Garlic Chicken and Potatoes: This zesty and satisfying recipe is a crowd-pleaser. Coat chicken thighs and baby potatoes in a mixture of lemon zest, garlic, and herbs, then roast them together in a single pan until golden and crispy. This dish is simple, yet packed with incredible flavors.

9. Teriyaki Chicken and Rice: Bring the flavors of Japan to your dinner table with this easy teriyaki chicken and rice dish. Sauté chicken with vegetables and a homemade teriyaki sauce, then add cooked rice and let it all simmer together. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions for a beautiful and flavorful finish.

10. Chicken and Cheese Enchilada Casserole: This Mexican-inspired casserole is a family favorite. Layer tortillas, shredded chicken, enchilada sauce, and cheese in a baking dish, then bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Top it off with sour cream, guacamole, and fresh cilantro for a delicious and satisfying meal.

In conclusion, these 10 one-pot chicken recipes are a game-changer when it comes to easy weeknight dinners. From skillet meals to casseroles, there’s something for everyone’s taste buds. Not only are these recipes convenient and time-saving, but they also deliver on flavor and satisfaction. So, the next time you’re short on time but still want a delicious and wholesome meal, give one of these recipes a try. You won’t be disappointed!
