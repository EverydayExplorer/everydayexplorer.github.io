DIY Outdoor Games: Fun Activities for Summer Entertaining


Now that summer is here, it’s time to take advantage of the beautiful weather and enjoy some outdoor fun. One great way to entertain friends and family during these warm months is by organizing a DIY outdoor games day. Not only are these activities a blast, but they also promote physical activity and provide endless entertainment for all ages.

When it comes to planning an unforgettable outdoor games event, creativity is key. There are countless game options that you can easily set up in your backyard or local park without breaking the bank. From classic favorites like sack races and tug-of-war to more unique options such as giant Jenga or homemade water balloon piñatas, the possibilities are endless.

One popular game that never fails to bring out everyone’s competitive spirit is cornhole. This simple yet addictive bean bag toss game involves throwing bags into small holes on a raised platform. You can easily build your own cornhole boards using plywood and 2x4s following online tutorials or purchase them at affordable prices from various stores. Cornhole tournaments are sure to keep your guests entertained for hours!

If you’re looking for something more active, why not try setting up an obstacle course? This can be done with simple household items such as hula hoops, cones, ropes, and even old tires if you have them lying around. Designing a challenging course will not only test participants’ physical abilities but also their problem-solving skills as they navigate through different obstacles.

For those who prefer less physically demanding games but still enjoy friendly competition, consider organizing a scavenger hunt or treasure hunt. Create clues leading participants from one location to another while incorporating interesting facts about your neighborhood or personal anecdotes related to each clue’s destination. This type of game encourages teamwork and exploration while keeping everyone engaged throughout the day.

Water-based games are always a hit during hot summer days! Set up a DIY slip ’n slide using inexpensive plastic sheeting or create an exciting water balloon war zone. Water balloon tosses, catch games, and even water balloon piñatas are guaranteed to keep everyone cool and entertained. Just make sure to have plenty of towels on hand for drying off afterward!

Another classic outdoor game that never goes out of style is horseshoes. This simple yet addictive activity involves throwing horseshoes towards a stake in the ground, aiming for a ringer or as close as possible. You can easily create your own stakes using PVC pipes and some creativity. Horseshoes is perfect for both casual play and organized tournaments.
