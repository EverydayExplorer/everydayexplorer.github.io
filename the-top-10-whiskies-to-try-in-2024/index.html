The Top 10 Whiskies to Try in 2024


As we step into the exciting year of 2024, whisky enthusiasts around the world are eager to explore new flavors and indulge in the timeless pleasure of a fine dram. With the ever-expanding world of whisky, it can be overwhelming to choose which ones to try. Fear not, for we have curated a list of the top 10 whiskies that you must add to your tasting journey this year. From the traditional to the innovative, these whiskies are sure to delight your palate and leave a lasting impression.

1. Macallan Sherry Oak 25 Year Old: Known for its rich and luscious character, this single malt Scotch whisky boasts a symphony of flavors, with notes of dried fruits, spices, and dark chocolate. A true masterpiece that exemplifies the art of aging whisky in sherry casks.

2. Yamazaki Mizunara Cask 2019 Edition: Hailing from Japan, this limited edition whisky showcases the unique influence of Mizunara oak casks. With its delicate balance of floral and spicy notes, it offers a one-of-a-kind tasting experience that is sure to captivate whisky lovers.

3. Lagavulin 16 Year Old: For those who appreciate the peaty and smoky side of whisky, Lagavulin 16 Year Old is a must-try. This Islay single malt is renowned for its intense flavors of smoke, sea salt, and a hint of sweetness, making it a true classic.

4. Glenfiddich Experimental Series - Fire & Cane: Breaking boundaries with its innovative approach, Glenfiddich’s Fire & Cane is a delightful blend of peated and rum cask-aged whiskies. The result is a harmonious fusion of smoky, sweet, and fruity flavors that will pleasantly surprise your taste buds.

5. Hibiki Japanese Harmony: A true reflection of Japanese craftsmanship, Hibiki Japanese Harmony is a blended whisky that embodies harmony and balance. With its delicate aromas of rose, lychee, and a touch of spice, this whisky showcases the art of blending at its finest.

6. Redbreast 21 Year Old: If you’re a fan of Irish whiskey, Redbreast 21 Year Old is a must-have in your collection. This single pot still whiskey is a masterclass in elegance and complexity, with flavors of dried fruits, spices, and a long, satisfying finish.

7. Balvenie DoubleWood 17 Year Old: Aged in traditional American oak barrels and finished in European sherry casks, Balvenie DoubleWood 17 Year Old offers a symphony of flavors. Expect notes of honey, vanilla, and dried fruits, with a hint of spice that adds depth to this exceptional whisky.

8. Booker’s Bourbon: For bourbon lovers, Booker’s Bourbon is a powerhouse that demands attention. This small-batch bourbon is unfiltered and bottled at cask strength, delivering robust flavors of toasted oak, caramel, and a touch of vanilla. A true Kentucky gem.

9. Talisker 18 Year Old: From the rugged shores of the Isle of Skye comes Talisker 18 Year Old, a whisky that embodies the spirit of its maritime surroundings. With its briny, smoky character and hints of pepper and dried fruits, it’s a whisky that will transport you to the wild Scottish coastline.

10. GlenDronach 18 Year Old Allardice: Rounding off our list is GlenDronach 18 Year Old Allardice, a rich and indulgent Highland single malt. Matured in Spanish Oloroso sherry casks, this whisky offers a symphony of flavors, including dark chocolate, dried fruits, and warm spices.

As we embark on a new year filled with exciting possibilities, make sure to explore these top 10 whiskies and savor the flavors that each one has to offer. Whether you prefer the peaty, smoky whiskies of Islay or the delicate and complex blends from Japan, there is something for every whisky lover on this list. So raise a glass and toast to the joy of discovery, as you embark on a whisky tasting journey like no other in 2024. Cheers!
