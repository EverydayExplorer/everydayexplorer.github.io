DIY Outdoor Games: Fun Activities for Summer Gatherings


Summer is the perfect time to enjoy the great outdoors with family and friends. And what better way to bring everyone together than with some fun and interactive outdoor games? In this blog, we’re going to explore DIY outdoor games that are not only entertaining but also easy to make and play. So, let’s get started!

1. Cornhole
Cornhole is a classic outdoor game that is loved by people of all ages. To build your own cornhole boards, you’ll need some plywood, screws, and bean bags. The objective of the game is to toss the bean bags into the hole on the opposite board. Cornhole can be played in teams or one-on-one, making it a versatile game for any gathering.

2. Giant Jenga
Put a twist on the traditional Jenga game by going big! Create your own giant Jenga set using some wooden blocks. Stack the blocks in a tower and take turns removing one block at a time without toppling the tower. Giant Jenga adds an element of suspense and excitement, keeping players engaged for hours.

3. Lawn Twister
Twister is a classic indoor game, but why not take it outside? Create your own lawn twister board using some spray paint and a grassy area. Simply spray the colored circles onto the grass, and you have yourself a life-size twister game. This game is guaranteed to bring out lots of laughter and friendly competition.

4. Water Balloon Piñatas
Cool down on a hot summer day with water balloon piñatas. Hang up some water-filled balloons from a tree branch or clothesline and take turns blindfolded, trying to burst them open with a stick. It’s a fun and refreshing game that is perfect for outdoor parties and gatherings.

5. Washer Toss
Washer toss is a simple yet highly addictive game. All you need is a few washers and a couple of wooden boxes with holes in them. The objective of the game is to toss the washers into the holes, scoring points for each successful throw. Washer toss is a great game for enhancing hand-eye coordination and friendly competition.

6. Giant Yard Pong
Put a twist on the popular party game of beer pong by turning it into a family-friendly outdoor game. Use large buckets or trash cans and oversized balls to create your own giant yard pong set. The rules are similar to regular beer pong, but without the alcohol. It’s a guaranteed hit at any summer gathering.

7. Noodle Hockey
Transform pool noodles into hockey sticks and have some fun playing noodle hockey. Use a beach ball or a soft foam ball as the puck and set up goals with some cones or objects. Noodle hockey is a great game to get everyone active and moving, and it’s perfect for both kids and adults.

These DIY outdoor games are not only a great way to have fun but also a fantastic opportunity to showcase your creativity. Plus, they can be easily customized to suit different age groups and themes. So, gather your loved ones, head outside, and enjoy hours of fun and laughter with these exciting outdoor games.

In conclusion, hosting a summer gathering is all about creating memorable experiences for everyone involved. By incorporating these DIY outdoor games into your event, you can guarantee a day filled with laughter, friendly competition, and lots of fun. From cornhole to giant Jenga, lawn twister to water balloon piñatas, there’s something for everyone. So, get ready to soak up the sunshine and make lasting memories with your loved ones this summer!
