10 Inspiring Art Blogs to Fuel Your Creativity


In today’s digital age, art lovers and creatives alike have found a new outlet for inspiration and connection through art blogs. These online platforms have become a hub for artists, enthusiasts, and learners to share their work, explore new ideas, and fuel their creativity. If you’re looking to dive into the world of art blogs and discover some inspiring content, look no further. In this article, we’ve compiled a list of 10 outstanding art blogs that are bound to ignite your imagination and take your creativity to new heights.

1. Colossal

Colossal is a well-established art blog that covers a wide range of artistic mediums, from painting and sculpture to photography and installations. With its visually captivating content and in-depth artist interviews, Colossal is a go-to source for anyone seeking fresh ideas and visual inspiration.

2. My Modern Met

My Modern Met celebrates the intersection of art, design, and culture. Their art blog features a variety of content, including fascinating artist spotlights, art history lessons, and articles on the latest trends in the art world. Whether you’re an art aficionado or just starting your creative journey, My Modern Met has something for everyone.

3. Hi-Fructose

Are you a fan of contemporary art? Hi-Fructose is the place to be. This art blog focuses on showcasing the works of groundbreaking contemporary artists and features in-depth interviews, studio visits, and exhibition reviews. Hi-Fructose is a treasure trove of inspiration for those who appreciate art that pushes the boundaries.

4. Artsy

Artsy is not just an art blog; it’s a comprehensive platform that connects art lovers to a vast collection of artwork from around the world. Browse through their extensive database, discover new artists, and stay updated with the latest happenings in the art industry. Artsy is a great resource for artists and art enthusiasts to explore, learn, and get inspired.

5. Beautiful Bizarre Magazine

If you’re into dark art, fantasy, and surrealism, Beautiful Bizarre Magazine is a must-read. This art blog showcases stunning artworks that explore the unconventional, the whimsical, and the macabre. So, if you’re looking for something outside the box, Beautiful Bizarre Magazine will not disappoint.

6. Juxtapoz

For over twenty years, Juxtapoz has remained at the forefront of contemporary art. With its focus on urban art, illustration, and underground culture, Juxtapoz offers a refreshing perspective that challenges traditional notions of art. Dive into their blog to discover exciting artists and gain a deeper understanding of the evolving art scene.

7. The Jealous Curator

The Jealous Curator is a blog that celebrates the work of talented contemporary artists. What sets this art blog apart is that it also delves into the stories behind the art, exploring the artists’ creative journeys and the emotions that drive their work. The Jealous Curator is a great place to find both inspiration and insight into the artistic process.

8. Booooooom

Booooooom is an art blog that showcases emerging artists from all over the world. With its emphasis on community and collaboration, Booooooom offers a platform for artists to share their work, participate in art challenges, and connect with like-minded individuals. It’s a wonderful space to discover new talent and become part of a supportive artistic community.

9. Design Milk

Design Milk highlights the intersection of art, design, and architecture. Aside from featuring stunning visual content, this art blog also explores the relationship between art and functionality. Whether you’re an artist seeking inspiration or a design enthusiast looking for innovative ideas, Design Milk is the place to be.

10. Creative Bloq

Last but not least, Creative Bloq covers a wide range of creative disciplines, including art, design, and digital media. This blog offers inspiration, tutorials, and tips to help artists and designers refine their skills and explore new techniques. If you’re looking for practical advice and creative resources, Creative Bloq has something for you.

In conclusion, art blogs have become an invaluable resource for artists, enthusiasts, and learners. The 10 art blogs mentioned in this article are just a taste of the myriad of inspiring platforms available to explore and fuel your creativity. Whether you’re seeking visual stimulation, artist interviews, or practical advice, these blogs are sure to ignite your imagination and provide you with ample inspiration on your artistic journey. Happy exploring!
