10 Up-and-Coming Indie Artists You Need to Know


Are you tired of listening to the same mainstream music on repeat? Do you crave something fresh and unique? Then you’re in luck! In this blog post, we will be exploring 10 up-and-coming indie artists who are making waves in the music industry. These artists may not be topping the charts just yet, but they definitely have the talent and potential to do so in the near future.

  1. Alex G: Known for his intimate and emotional lyrics, Alex G is an indie sensation that you don’t want to miss. His blend of indie folk and lo-fi rock creates a soothing and melancholic sound that will melt your heart.

  2. Clairo: Clairo is a young indie pop singer-songwriter who gained popularity through her DIY-style music videos on YouTube. With her catchy melodies and relatable lyrics, she has already captured the attention of millions of fans worldwide.

  3. Wallows: This indie rock band composed of three childhood friends has been making waves with their energetic and infectious tunes. Their nostalgic sound will transport you back to the good old days of alternative rock.

  4. Phoebe Bridgers: Phoebe Bridgers is a rising star in the indie folk scene. Her hauntingly beautiful voice, combined with her introspective lyrics, make her songs deeply captivating and emotionally charged.

  5. Rex Orange County: With his soulful vocals and genre-bending style, Rex Orange County has become a force to be reckoned with in the indie music scene. His music effortlessly blends elements of pop, hip-hop, and alternative rock.

  6. Mitski: Mitski is an indie rock powerhouse known for her raw and honest songwriting. Her powerful vocals and thought-provoking lyrics tackle themes of love, loss, and identity, resonating deeply with her listeners.

  7. Snail Mail: Snail Mail, led by singer-songwriter Lindsey Jordan, is a band that combines indie rock with dreamy pop melodies. Their music is a perfect blend of nostalgia and contemporary soundscapes.

  8. Phantogram: Phantogram is an electronic rock duo that creates a captivating blend of pop, rock, and trip-hop. Their unique sound, combined with their mesmerizing live performances, has earned them a dedicated fanbase.

  9. Weyes Blood: Weyes Blood, also known as Natalie Mering, is an indie folk artist who creates lush and ethereal soundscapes. Her hauntingly beautiful voice and poetic lyrics will transport you to another world.

  10. The Japanese House: The Japanese House, a solo project of Amber Bain, creates dreamy and atmospheric indie pop music. With her soft vocals and synth-driven sound, her music is the perfect soundtrack for late-night drives.

These artists may not have the mainstream recognition just yet, but they are undoubtedly making a name for themselves in the indie music scene. With their unique sounds and raw talent, it’s only a matter of time before they become household names. So, if you’re looking to discover some fresh and exciting music, be sure to check out these 10 up-and-coming indie artists. Trust us, you won’t be disappointed!

--

And there you have it! A comprehensive list of 10 up-and-coming indie artists that you need to know. Whether you’re a fan of indie folk, indie rock, or indie pop, there’s something for everyone on this list. So, go ahead and give these artists a listen. Who knows, you might just discover your new favorite artist!
