The Best Hunting Resources: Books, Websites, and More


Hunting is not just a pastime; it is an art, a tradition, and for many, a way of life. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or just starting out, having access to the best resources can make a significant difference in your experience and success in the field. From comprehensive books to informative websites and useful mobile applications, the resources available today are vast and varied. This guide will delve into some of the best hunting resources that every hunter should consider.

Essential Hunting Books

Books have long been a cornerstone of hunting knowledge, providing both foundational techniques and advanced strategies. Here are some must-read titles to enhance your hunting skills:

1. “The Ultimate Guide to Deer Hunting” by John E. Phillips

This book is a treasure trove for anyone interested in deer hunting. It covers everything from selecting the right gear to understanding deer behavior. Phillips shares personal anecdotes and expert tips that can help you increase your success in the field.

2. “The Complete Guide to Hunting, Butchering, and Cooking Wild Game” by Steven Rinella

Rinella’s work is not only a manual on hunting but also a commentary on the ethics and responsibilities that come with it. The book offers practical advice on hunting various game and includes step-by-step instructions on how to butcher and cook your harvest.

3. “Game Change: The Complete Guide to Transforming Your Hunting Experience” by Barry Wensel

Wensel shares his decades of experience in the field, offering insights into improving your hunting techniques. This book focuses on the mental aspects of hunting, helping you develop a winning mindset.

4. “Hunting and Cooking Wild Game” by John McCormack

This book is perfect for those who want to delve into both the hunting and culinary worlds. McCormack provides recipes that celebrate the flavors of wild game, making it a great resource for hunters who also love to cook.

5. “The Modern Day Hunter” by Mark C. McKinney

This contemporary guide addresses the challenges of hunting in today’s world, including ethical considerations, environmental impacts, and modern techniques. It’s an essential read for the conscientious hunter.

Online Resources and Websites

The internet has revolutionized how hunters share and access information. Here are some of the best websites that provide valuable insights, tips, and community support:

1. HuntingNet.com

HuntingNet is a comprehensive online platform that covers a wide range of topics related to hunting. It features forums where hunters can share their experiences, ask questions, and exchange advice. From gear reviews to hunting tips, HuntingNet is a one-stop shop for enthusiasts.

2. Outdoor Life

Outdoor Life is a leading magazine that has transitioned into a robust online resource. It offers articles, gear reviews, and hunting strategies for various game species. Their writers are seasoned hunters, providing practical advice that you can apply in the field.

3. Field & Stream

Field & Stream has a long-standing reputation as a premier hunting and fishing magazine. Its website features a wealth of articles, videos, and expert advice on hunting techniques, gear selection, and wildlife conservation.

4. The National Wild Turkey Federation

For those interested in turkey hunting, the National Wild Turkey Federation (NWTF) is an invaluable resource. The website offers information on turkey hunting techniques, conservation efforts, and local chapters where hunters can connect.

5. The Quality Deer Management Association (QDMA)

QDMA focuses on deer management and conservation. Their website provides educational resources, research articles, and best practices for managing deer populations and habitats, making it a crucial resource for deer hunters.

Mobile Applications for Hunters

In this digital age, mobile applications can be incredibly helpful for hunters. Here are some of the top applications that can enhance your hunting experience:

1. OnX Hunt

OnX Hunt is a GPS mapping application that provides detailed maps for hunting areas. It includes information about land ownership, boundaries, and terrain. This app is particularly useful for planning your hunting trips and ensuring you stay on the right side of property lines.

2. HuntStand

HuntStand offers a suite of tools for hunters, including mapping, weather forecasts, and property ownership information. The app allows you to track your hunts and share your experiences with a community of hunters.

3. My Campfire

This app serves as a social platform for hunters. You can share your hunting stories, photos, and tips with others. My Campfire also includes features for tracking your hunts and connecting with local hunters.

4. Weather Underground

Weather is a crucial factor in hunting success. Weather Underground provides hyper-local weather forecasts, allowing hunters to plan their trips according to the best conditions for their target game.

5. iHunt

iHunt is a sound library app that provides calls for various game species. It’s an excellent tool for hunters looking to attract game with realistic calls. The app includes tips and tricks for using the calls effectively in the field.

Hunting Forums and Communities

Joining a hunting community can be one of the most enriching aspects of the sport. Engaging with fellow hunters allows you to share experiences, learn from one another, and foster friendships. Here are some popular hunting forums and communities:

1. ArcheryTalk

ArcheryTalk is a forum dedicated to archery and bow hunting enthusiasts. It covers a wide range of topics, including gear discussions, hunting stories, and technique tips. The community is supportive and offers valuable insights for both beginners and experienced hunters.

2. The Hunting Beast

The Hunting Beast is a forum designed for serious hunters, particularly those who hunt big game. It features discussions about advanced hunting strategies, gear recommendations, and personal success stories.

3. Duck Hunting Chat

For waterfowl hunters, Duck Hunting Chat is a fantastic resource. The forum covers everything from gear to hunting locations, and members share tips and experiences that can help you become a better duck hunter.

4. Predator Masters

Predator Masters is a community focused on hunting predators like coyotes and bobcats. Users share their techniques, gear recommendations, and hunting stories, making it a valuable resource for those interested in predator hunting.

5. HuntingNet Forums

As mentioned earlier, HuntingNet has an active forum section where hunters can discuss a variety of topics. From hunting tactics to gear reviews, this community is a great place to connect with fellow hunters.

YouTube Channels for Visual Learning

Visual learning can be incredibly effective, and YouTube hosts a plethora of hunting channels that offer tutorials, gear reviews, and hunting vlogs. Here are some top channels to check out:

1. MeatEater

Hosted by Steven Rinella, MeatEater combines hunting and cooking in an engaging format. The channel features hunting adventures, cooking tips, and discussions about wildlife conservation.

2. The Hunting Public

The Hunting Public focuses on public land hunting and provides valuable insights into various hunting strategies. Their content includes tips for scouting, gear recommendations, and real-time hunting experiences.

3. Wired to Hunt

Wired to Hunt offers a wealth of information on whitetail deer hunting. The channel features expert interviews, hunting tips, and gear reviews tailored to deer hunters.

4. Hushin

Hushin is a channel dedicated to big game hunting, showcasing various hunts and providing tips on techniques and gear. The hosts share their experiences and insights, making it a relatable and informative channel.

5. Outdoor Channel

The Outdoor Channel offers a variety of hunting shows, featuring different species and hunting styles. It’s a great resource for learning about various hunting techniques and getting inspired for your next adventure.

Hunting Gear Resources

Selecting the right gear can significantly impact your hunting experience. Here are some resources to help you make informed decisions on hunting equipment:

1. Cabela’s and Bass Pro Shops

These two outdoor retailers offer a wide range of hunting gear, from firearms to clothing and accessories. Their websites feature customer reviews, which can help you choose the best products for your needs.

2. REI

REI is known for its high-quality outdoor gear, including hunting equipment. Their website includes expert advice on selecting the right gear, as well as user reviews that can guide your purchasing decisions.

3. Field & Stream Gear Reviews

Field & Stream’s website includes detailed gear reviews for hunters. They test various products and provide unbiased reviews, helping you make informed choices when investing in hunting equipment.

4. Hunting Insider

Hunting Insider is a great resource for finding the latest gear and equipment. The website features product reviews, comparisons, and tips for selecting the right gear for your hunting style.

5. Backcountry

For those who hunt in more remote areas, Backcountry offers gear specifically designed for rugged outdoor adventures. Their reviews and expert recommendations can help you find the best products for your needs.

Conclusion

With the wealth of resources available today, hunters have unprecedented access to knowledge, community, and tools that can enhance their hunting experience. Whether you prefer reading books, browsing the internet, engaging with fellow hunters, or watching informative videos, there is something for everyone. By utilizing these resources, you can improve your skills, make informed decisions, and fully embrace the art of hunting. Happy hunting!
