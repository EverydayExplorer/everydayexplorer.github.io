Top 10 SNL Sketches That Will Make You Cry with Laughter


Saturday Night Live has been a staple in the world of comedy since 1975. Over the years, the show has produced countless unforgettable moments and sketches that have left viewers in stitches. From iconic characters to hilarious parodies, it’s no wonder that SNL has become synonymous with laughter. In this blog post, we will take a trip down memory lane and explore the top 10 SNL sketches that are guaranteed to make you cry with laughter.

  1. More Cowbell Who can forget the classic sketch featuring Will Ferrell as Gene Frenkle, a legendary cowbell player? This sketch, with Christopher Walken as the record producer, is an absolute gem. It’s impossible to watch this sketch without cracking up and singing along to the infectious cowbell beat.

  2. Matt Foley: Motivational Speaker Chris Farley brought his unparalleled energy to the sketch as Matt Foley, a motivational speaker who ’lives in a van down by the river.’ Farley’s physical comedy and over-the-top performance never fail to bring tears of laughter to our eyes.

  3. Celebrity Jeopardy One of the most popular recurring sketches on SNL, Celebrity Jeopardy never disappoints. With Alex Trebek (played by Will Ferrell) trying to wrangle unruly and clueless celebrities like Sean Connery (played by Darrell Hammond), the comedic timing and hilarious banter in this sketch make it a must-watch.

  4. Schweddy Balls In this raunchy sketch, Alec Baldwin plays Pete Schweddy, a guest on a fictional radio show. Baldwin’s deadpan delivery of the lines about his famous holiday treat, Schweddy Balls, is nothing short of genius. The innuendos and innuendo-laden reactions from the hosts make this sketch a laugh riot.

  5. The Californians This recurring sketch takes a hilarious jab at the peculiar speech patterns and habits of Californians. The exaggerated accents and over-the-top drama in this soap opera-style sketch will leave you in stitches. Kristen Wiig and Fred Armisen shine with their impeccable comedic timing.

  6. Lazy Sunday Andy Samberg and Chris Parnell’s rap sketch about their mundane adventures in New York City became an instant viral hit. The clever lyrics, catchy beat, and perfectly timed delivery make this sketch a laugh-out-loud experience.

  7. The Ladies Man Tim Meadows brought the smooth-talking ‘Ladies Man’ to life in this recurring sketch. His suave demeanor and hilarious advice on matters of love and attraction never fail to generate uproarious laughter.

  8. Wayne’s World Mike Myers and Dana Carvey’s Wayne’s World sketches were a cultural phenomenon. From the catchphrases like ‘party on’ to the iconic headbanging in the car, this sketch had it all. The chemistry between Myers and Carvey was palpable and the hilarity ensued.

  9. Debbie Downer Rachel Dratch’s portrayal of a perpetual pessimist in the Debbie Downer sketch is guaranteed to make you laugh and relate to all those moments when you’ve been a ‘Debbie Downer’ too. The cast’s inability to keep a straight face only adds to the hilarity of the sketch.

  10. Gumby Eddie Murphy’s take on the lovable but temperamental clay animation character, Gumby, is comedic gold. His exaggerated gestures and outbursts of anger make this sketch a timeless classic that will have you crying with laughter.

In conclusion, Saturday Night Live has given us countless memorable sketches over the years. Whether it’s iconic characters, hilarious parodies, or improvised comedy, the show has consistently delivered laughter to audiences around the world. The top 10 SNL sketches mentioned in this blog post are just a glimpse of the hilarious moments the show has provided us. So, grab some popcorn, sit back, and enjoy the comedy gold that is SNL!
