Content Marketing Done Right: Creating Valuable and Shareable Content


Content marketing has become an integral part of the digital marketing landscape. It involves creating and distributing valuable content that can attract, engage, and retain a clearly defined audience. Most companies today have realized the importance of creating valuable content, but not all succeed in making it shareable. If your content is not getting shared, then it is not being seen, and it is not having the impact you intended. In this blog, we will discuss how to create content that is both valuable and shareable, and how to leverage it to increase your brand’s visibility.

Create Content with Value

Creating content that is valuable requires you to understand your target audience. You need to know what their pain points are, what interests them, what questions they have, and what challenges they face. Once you have a clear idea of your target audience, you can create content that addresses their needs and provides solutions to their problems.

Your content should be educational, informative, and fact-based. Use research, statistics, and case studies to back up your claims and provide proof of your expertise. Readers will appreciate the value you provide and will be more likely to consider you a trusted source.

Make Your Content Shareable

Creating valuable content is not enough. You need to make sure that your content is shareable. Shareable content has a higher chance of reaching a wider audience and, therefore, can generate more leads and traffic to your website. Here are some tips to make your content shareable:

1. Use an Eye-Catching Title and Image

Your title and image are the first things that people will see when they come across your content. Make sure they are eye-catching and attention-grabbing. Use words that spark curiosity or evoke emotion in your title, and choose an image that is relevant and visually appealing.

2. Format Your Content for Easy Scanning

Most people skim through content instead of reading it from start to finish. Make your content easy to skim by using headings, bullet points, and short paragraphs. This will make it more digestible and easier to share.

3. Offer Value in Every Piece of Content

People share content that they find valuable or helpful. Make sure every piece of content you create offers something of value to the reader. This could be a new perspective, a solution to a problem, or actionable tips that they can apply in their lives.

4. Provide Social Sharing Buttons

Make it easy for readers to share your content by including social sharing buttons on your website or within your content. This will allow them to share your content on their social media networks with just a click of a button.

5. Engage with your Audience

Engaging with your audience on social media is essential in creating shareable content. Respond to comments and messages from your followers, ask them questions, and encourage them to share their opinions and experiences. This will build trust and community around your brand.

Leverage Your Content

Once you have created valuable, shareable content, you need to leverage it to increase your brand’s visibility. Here are a few ways to do this:

1. Share Your Content on Social Media

Share your content on your social media networks to increase its visibility. Use hashtags, tag relevant people and companies, and encourage your followers to share your content.

2. Offer Guest Posts

Offer to write guest posts for other websites or invite industry influencers to write guest posts for your blog. This will increase your visibility and reach a wider audience.

3. Repurpose Your Content

Turn your blog posts into infographics, videos, or podcasts to reach a broader audience. Repurposing your content also allows you to create content that is shareable across different platforms.

4. Monitor Analytics

Monitor your content’s performance using analytics tools. This will help you identify what type of content is resonating with your audience, and what needs to be improved.

Finally, creating valuable and shareable content takes time and effort. But if done right, it can help you build a loyal customer base, increase your brand’s visibility, and drive more traffic to your website. Follow the tips above and create content that offers value and engages your audience.
