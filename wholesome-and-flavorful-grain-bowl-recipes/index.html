1. Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl:
Ingredients:
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 cup cucumber, diced
- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
- 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Add salt and pepper to taste. Toss well to combine. Garnish with fresh parsley. Enjoy!

2. Teriyaki Salmon Brown Rice Bowl:
Ingredients:
- 1 cup cooked brown rice
- 1/2 pound salmon fillet
- 3 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
- 1 cup steamed broccoli florets
- 1/2 cup shredded carrots
- 1/4 cup sliced scallions
- 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions:
Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush the salmon with teriyaki sauce and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until cooked through. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before flaking it into smaller pieces.

In a bowl, combine the cooked brown rice, steamed broccoli florets, shredded carrots, sliced scallions, and flaked salmon. Drizzle with additional teriyaki sauce if desired. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the top. Mix well and enjoy!

3. Mexican Quinoa Bowl:
Ingredients:
- 1 cup cooked quinoa
- 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 avocado, diced
- 1/2 cup corn kernels
- 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1/2 teaspoon cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, black beans, diced avocado, corn kernels, red onion, and chopped cilantro. Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture. Sprinkle with cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine. Serve chilled and enjoy!
