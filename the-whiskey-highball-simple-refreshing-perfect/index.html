The Whiskey Highball: Simple, Refreshing, Perfect


When it comes to classic cocktails, the Whiskey Highball is a drink that stands the test of time. Simple, refreshing, and undeniably perfect for any occasion, this cocktail has been a favorite of whiskey enthusiasts for generations. Whether you’re sipping it at a swanky cocktail party or enjoying it in the comfort of your own home, the Whiskey Highball is a drink that never goes out of style. In this blog post, we’ll explore the history, ingredients, and variations of this beloved libation.

A Brief History

The origins of the Whiskey Highball can be traced back to the late 19th century in the United States. It was during this time that the Highball became a popular way to enjoy whiskey. The simplicity of the cocktail made it accessible to a wide range of people, and its refreshing nature made it an ideal choice for warm weather.

The basic Whiskey Highball consists of just two ingredients: whiskey and carbonated water. It’s traditionally served in a tall glass filled with ice and garnished with a lemon or lime wedge. This straightforward recipe allowed bartenders to quickly and easily serve customers, making it a staple in bars across the country.

Ingredients

  1. Whiskey: The choice of whiskey is crucial to the flavor of your Highball. While some prefer a smoother and milder bourbon, others opt for the boldness of a rye whiskey. It’s all a matter of personal preference, so feel free to experiment until you find your favorite.

  2. Carbonated Water: This is where the ‘high’ in Highball comes from. The effervescence of carbonated water adds a delightful fizz to the drink, making it incredibly refreshing.

  3. Ice: A Whiskey Highball is typically served over ice, which not only chills the drink but also dilutes it slightly, mellowing out the whiskey’s intensity.

  4. Garnish: While optional, a lemon or lime wedge can be added to the glass for a subtle citrusy aroma that complements the whiskey beautifully.

Making the Perfect Whiskey Highball

Now that we have the ingredients, let’s talk about how to make the perfect Whiskey Highball:

  1. Fill a Highball glass with ice: The first step is to fill a tall glass with ice cubes. The colder, the better!

  2. Add the whiskey: Pour 2 oz (60 ml) of your chosen whiskey over the ice.

  3. Top with carbonated water: Gently pour 4-6 oz (120-180 ml) of carbonated water into the glass. You can adjust the amount to your taste, adding more for a lighter drink or less for a stronger one.
