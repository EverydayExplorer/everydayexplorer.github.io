The Dos\ and\ Donts of Eyeliner Application


Are you tired of trying to master the skill of applying eyeliner? Don’t worry, you are not alone. Achieving the perfect eyeliner look can be challenging, especially if you are new to makeup or have shaky hands. However, with the right techniques and a little practice, you can elevate your eye game and create stunning looks. In this blog post, we will discuss the do’s and don’ts of eyeliner application.

The Do’s:

  1. Choose the right type of eyeliner: There are various types of eyeliners available, such as pencil, gel, liquid, and felt-tip. Select the one that you are most comfortable and confident using. Beginners often find it easier to start with a pencil or felt-tip liner.

  2. Prime your eyelids: Before applying eyeliner, it’s crucial to start with a smooth canvas. Use an eyeshadow primer or a concealer to create a base for your eyeliner. This will help it last longer and prevent smudging.

  3. Use short strokes: Instead of trying to draw one continuous line, use short strokes to create your desired shape. This technique provides more control and helps in achieving a precise application.

  4. Start thin and build up: Start with a thin line close to your lashes and gradually build up the thickness if desired. It’s easier to add more eyeliner than to remove excess product.

  5. Experiment with different styles: Don’t limit yourself to just one eyeliner style. Experiment with cat-eye, winged, or smudged looks to find what suits you best. Different styles can complement your eye shape and enhance your overall makeup look.

  6. Use a clean brush: If you’re using gel or liquid eyeliner, ensure that your brush is clean before each application. This helps in achieving a more precise and controlled line.

  7. Set your eyeliner: If you want your eyeliner to last longer, consider setting it with an eyeshadow of the same color. Apply the eyeshadow using a small brush over the eyeliner to prevent smudging and increase longevity.

The Don’ts:

  1. Don’t pull your eyelids: Avoid pulling or tugging on your eyelids while applying eyeliner. This can cause premature aging and wrinkles. Instead, look straight into the mirror and gently lift your chin to create a relaxed canvas.

  2. Don’t rush: Take your time while applying eyeliner. Rushing can lead to mistakes and a messy application. Set aside a few extra minutes in your makeup routine for eyeliner application to ensure better results.

  3. Avoid skipping the primer: Using an eyeshadow primer is essential to create a smooth base for your eyeliner. It helps the product adhere better and prevents creasing or smudging throughout the day.

  4. Don’t apply thick eyeliner all around: Applying thick eyeliner all around your eyes can make them appear smaller. Instead, consider using a thinner line on the inner corner and gradually thickening it toward the outer corner for a more flattering look.

  5. Avoid using the wrong color: While experimenting with different eyeliner colors can be fun, it’s essential to choose shades that complement your eye color and skin tone. Avoid using colors that clash or overpower your natural features.

  6. Don’t forget to remove eyeliner: Leaving eyeliner on overnight can lead to eye irritation and clogged pores. Ensure that you remove your eyeliner thoroughly before going to bed using a gentle makeup remover.

  7. Avoid applying eyeliner too far out: Extending your eyeliner too far outward can give a droopy or sad appearance to your eyes. Aim to keep your eyeliner within the natural shape of your eyes for a more flattering effect.

By following these do’s and don’ts, you can enhance your eyeliner application technique and create beautiful eye makeup looks. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don’t get discouraged if you don’t achieve your desired results right away. Keep experimenting, have fun with different styles, and soon you’ll become an eyeliner pro!
