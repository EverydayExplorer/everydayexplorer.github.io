The Top 5 Hunting Magazines to Stay Informed and Entertained


If you’re an avid hunter, staying informed and entertained is crucial to improve your skills and keep up with the latest trends in the hunting world. One great way to achieve this is by reading hunting magazines. These magazines not only offer articles and features on various hunting techniques, gear, and destinations but also provide a wealth of knowledge from experienced hunters and experts in the field. In this blog, we will discuss the top five hunting magazines that will keep you updated and entertained throughout your hunting journey.

1. Field & Stream Field & Stream is widely regarded as one of the oldest and most respected hunting and fishing magazines in the United States. Since its establishment in 1871, it has been a go-to resource for hunters and outdoor enthusiasts alike. This publication provides articles on a wide range of hunting topics, including gear reviews, hunting techniques, conservation, and hunting stories shared by dedicated hunters. Field & Stream also features insightful interviews with renowned hunters and industry experts, making it a must-read for passionate hunters.

2. Outdoor Life Outdoor Life is another leading magazine in the hunting and outdoor space. Founded in 1898, this publication has a rich history of providing valuable information and entertainment to hunters. Outdoor Life covers a broad spectrum of hunting-related topics, including hunting strategies, survival skills, gear reviews, and outdoor adventures. The magazine also includes in-depth features on hunting rifles, bows, optics, and other essential equipment. With its reader-submitted hunting stories and breathtaking photography, Outdoor Life offers an immersive experience for all hunting enthusiasts.

3. North American Whitetail For those passionate about whitetail deer hunting, North American Whitetail is the ultimate resource. This specialized hunting magazine focuses on everything related to hunting this majestic species. From tactics and strategies to land management and gear recommendations, North American Whitetail provides valuable insights for avid whitetail hunters. The magazine is renowned for its expert-written articles, extensive coverage of whitetail hunting techniques, and amazing hunting stories shared by passionate hunters from across North America.

4. Bowhunter If you prefer hunting with a bow, Bowhunter magazine is a must-read for you. As the name suggests, this publication specializes in bowhunting and covers all aspects of this thrilling pursuit. Bowhunter offers articles on selecting the right bow, effective shooting techniques, bowhunting gear reviews, and detailed coverage of various archery hunts. The magazine also features stories of successful bowhunting adventures from experienced hunters, making it an excellent resource for bowhunters of all skill levels.

5. Petersen’s Hunting Petersen’s Hunting is a well-rounded magazine that caters to hunters of all types. Whether you are interested in big game hunting, waterfowl hunting, upland bird hunting, or hunting exotic species, Petersen’s Hunting covers it all. The magazine’s articles include hunting tips, gear reviews, destination suggestions, and engaging stories from hunters who have undertaken remarkable hunting expeditions across the globe. Petersen’s Hunting is known for its informative content and visually impressive photography, ensuring you’re captivated with each issue.

In conclusion, staying informed and entertained as a hunter is vital for honing your skills and making the most of your outdoor adventures. The top five hunting magazines mentioned above provide a wealth of knowledge, techniques, and engaging stories that will keep you engaged and inspired. So, pick up an issue of these magazines and prepare to immerse yourself in the world of hunting!
