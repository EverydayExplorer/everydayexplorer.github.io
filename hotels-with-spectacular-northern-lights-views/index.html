Hotels with Spectacular Northern Lights Views


When it comes to witnessing the mesmerizing beauty of the Northern Lights, there’s nothing quite like experiencing it from the comfort of a cozy hotel. Imagine stepping out onto your balcony or gazing through your room’s window to behold the ethereal dance of vibrant colors illuminating the night sky. In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to discover some of the most enchanting hotels around the world that offer spectacular views of the Northern Lights.

1. Aurora Sky Hotel - Fairbanks, Alaska

Nestled amidst the vast wilderness of Alaska, the Aurora Sky Hotel provides an ideal vantage point for observing the Northern Lights in all their glory. Located far away from city lights, this hotel offers a tranquil and uninterrupted experience. With its large windows and spacious balconies, guests can immerse themselves in the breathtaking spectacle without ever leaving the comfort of their rooms. The hotel also provides cozy lounges and outdoor fire pits, perfect for snuggling up under the starry sky while waiting for the lights to appear.

2. Icehotel - Jukkasjärvi, Sweden

For a truly unique and magical experience, the Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden offers an unforgettable encounter with the Northern Lights. As the world’s first and largest hotel made entirely of ice and snow, guests can sleep in intricately carved ice rooms that feature glass ceilings. This allows visitors to marvel at the celestial display of the Aurora Borealis from the warmth of their cozy ice beds. The hotel also offers guided tours, snowmobile safaris, and even ice sculpting classes to make your stay even more adventurous.

3. Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort - Saariselkä, Finland

Situated in the heart of Lapland, Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort is renowned for its glass igloos, offering an extraordinary way to witness the Northern Lights. These unique accommodations provide a magical and uninterrupted view of the sky, allowing you to enjoy the dancing lights while staying warm and comfortable. The resort also offers a range of winter activities, including reindeer safaris and husky sledding, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a complete Arctic experience.

4. Hotel Rangá - Hella, Iceland

Iceland is a prime location for viewing the Northern Lights, and Hotel Rangá offers a luxurious and cozy retreat for guests seeking this natural wonder. Located in a rural area, away from light pollution, the hotel’s outdoor hot tubs provide a perfect spot to relax while gazing at the vibrant lights. The hotel also offers wake-up calls when the Northern Lights are visible, ensuring that guests never miss a chance to witness this awe-inspiring phenomenon.

5. Abisko Mountain Lodge - Abisko, Sweden

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Swedish Lapland, Abisko Mountain Lodge is renowned for its ideal location for Northern Lights viewing. Situated within the Arctic Circle, the lodge benefits from a unique microclimate that results in clear skies and a high frequency of Northern Lights displays. Guests can enjoy cozy log cabins or comfortable rooms with large windows, allowing them to witness the captivating spectacle from the warmth and comfort of their accommodations.

Conclusion

If you’re seeking a truly awe-inspiring experience, staying at a hotel with a spectacular Northern Lights view is an absolute must. From the rugged wilderness of Alaska to the icy landscapes of Sweden, these hotels offer the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and natural beauty. So, pack your bags, bundle up, and prepare to be mesmerized as the Northern Lights cast their enchanting glow upon the world.
