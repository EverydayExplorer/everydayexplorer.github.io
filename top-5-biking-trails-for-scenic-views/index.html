Top 5 Biking Trails for Scenic Views


Biking is not just a mode of transportation; it’s a way to connect with nature and experience the beauty of the world around us. If you’re a fan of scenic views and love exploring on two wheels, then you’re in for a treat. In this blog post, we’ll take a journey through the top five biking trails that offer breathtaking views of nature’s wonders. So grab your helmet, hop on your bike, and let’s go!

1. Pacific Coast Highway, California, USA
Stretching along the stunning California coastline, the Pacific Coast Highway offers one of the most scenic biking experiences in the world. As you pedal along this iconic route, you’ll be treated to breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean, rugged cliffs, and picturesque beaches. Don’t forget to stop and take in the beauty of Big Sur, where the road winds through towering redwoods and coastal bluffs.

2. Trollstigen, Norway
Prepare to be amazed as you tackle the challenging yet rewarding Trollstigen biking trail in Norway. This mountain road, known as the ‘Troll’s Path,’ winds through the majestic Scandinavian landscape, offering panoramic views of steep mountains, cascading waterfalls, and deep valleys. It’s a true feast for the eyes and a test of your cycling skills.

3. The Great Ocean Road, Victoria, Australia
Down under, the Great Ocean Road is a must-visit for biking enthusiasts seeking scenic beauty. This iconic Australian route stretches along the southeastern coast, showcasing stunning ocean views, dramatic cliffs, and the famous Twelve Apostles rock formations. As you pedal, keep an eye out for native wildlife, including koalas, kangaroos, and colorful parrots.

4. Ring of Kerry, Ireland
Ireland’s Ring of Kerry is a paradise for bikers who appreciate rolling green hills, rugged coastlines, and quaint villages. This 179-kilometer loop takes you through some of Ireland’s most stunning landscapes, including Killarney National Park, the breathtaking Gap of Dunloe, and the picturesque coastal towns of Kenmare and Portmagee. Don’t forget to stop for a pint of Guinness at a local pub along the way!

5. Munda Biddi Trail, Western Australia
For off-road biking enthusiasts, the Munda Biddi Trail in Western Australia is an adventure like no other. This 1,000-kilometer trail winds through the picturesque forests, rivers, and valleys of the state’s southwest region. Along the way, you’ll be treated to stunning views of wildflowers, ancient trees, and native wildlife, making it a truly immersive experience in nature.

Remember, when embarking on these scenic biking trails, always prioritize safety. Make sure your bike is in good condition, wear appropriate protective gear, and follow any local regulations or guidelines. Take your time, enjoy the journey, and savor the breathtaking views that these trails have to offer.

So, whether you’re a seasoned cyclist or just starting out, these top five biking trails for scenic views are sure to leave you in awe. Get ready to pedal your way through some of the most beautiful landscapes on Earth. Happy biking!
