10 Hotels with Stunning Ocean Views


As you plan your next vacation, are you dreaming of waking up to the gentle sound of waves crashing against the shore? If so, you’re in luck! There are countless hotels around the world that offer stunning ocean views, promising a truly unforgettable experience. Whether you’re seeking a serene escape or a luxurious seaside retreat, these 10 hotels are sure to take your breath away.

  1. Amanemu, Japan Nestled in the serene surroundings of Ise Shima National Park, Amanemu offers breathtaking views of Ago Bay. With its minimalist design and traditional Japanese hot spring baths, this resort provides the perfect blend of luxury and tranquility.

  2. Jade Mountain, St. Lucia Perched on a hillside overlooking the Caribbean Sea, Jade Mountain is renowned for its open-air suites that provide uninterrupted views of the sparkling waters below. Each suite features a private infinity pool, ensuring a truly immersive experience with the ocean.

  3. InterContinental Bora Bora Resort & Thalasso Spa, French Polynesia Situated on a private motu in Bora Bora, this resort boasts overwater villas with glass floors, allowing guests to marvel at the vibrant marine life beneath them. The azure lagoon and Mount Otemanu provide a picturesque backdrop for an idyllic escape.

  4. Post Ranch Inn, California Perched on the cliffs of Big Sur, Post Ranch Inn offers unparalleled views of the Pacific Ocean. The architecture seamlessly integrates with the natural surroundings, providing a sense of serenity and seclusion.

  5. Amankila, Bali Set against the backdrop of Mount Agung, Amankila offers sweeping views of the Lombok Strait. The resort’s three-tiered infinity pool and elevated suites create an atmosphere of understated elegance and tranquility.

  6. One&Only Reethi Rah, Maldives Boasting expansive villas and private pools, this Maldivian resort is a haven for those seeking a luxurious oceanfront escape. The crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean and pristine white-sand beaches set the stage for an unforgettable retreat.

  7. Fogo Island Inn, Canada Located on a remote island off the coast of Newfoundland, Fogo Island Inn offers panoramic views of the North Atlantic. The modern design of the inn harmonizes with the rugged coastal landscape, providing a one-of-a-kind experience for nature enthusiasts.

  8. Soneva Jani, Maldives Set within a private lagoon in the Noonu Atoll, Soneva Jani showcases overwater villas with retractable roofs, allowing guests to stargaze from the comfort of their own beds. The unobstructed views of the ocean create a sense of boundless freedom and tranquility.

  9. Laucala Island, Fiji Accessible only by private plane, Laucala Island offers unparalleled seclusion and natural beauty. The resort’s hilltop villas and panoramic views of the South Pacific Ocean provide an exclusive retreat for discerning travelers.

  10. Alila Villas Uluwatu, Bali Perched on a clifftop overlooking the Indian Ocean, Alila Villas Uluwatu offers a modern and sustainable approach to luxury. The infinity pool and private cabanas provide the perfect vantage point for admiring the mesmerizing ocean vistas.

From the crystal-clear waters of the Maldives to the rugged coastlines of Canada, these 10 hotels promise awe-inspiring ocean views that will leave a lasting impression. Whether you’re seeking a romantic getaway, an adventure-filled escape, or simply a moment of tranquility, these destinations offer the perfect backdrop for your next vacation. So, why wait? Start planning your oceanfront retreat today and immerse yourself in the beauty of these stunning seaside paradises.
