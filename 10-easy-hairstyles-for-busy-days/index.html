10 Easy Hairstyles for Busy Days


Are you constantly rushing in the morning and struggling to find time to do your hair? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we will share 10 easy hairstyles that are perfect for busy days. These hairstyles are quick, simple, and require minimal effort, allowing you to look effortlessly stylish in no time.

1. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic go-to hairstyle for busy days. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around, and secure it with bobby pins. Don’t worry about making it perfect – the messier, the better! This hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

2. The Half-Up Top Knot

If you want a hairstyle that is trendy and easy to achieve, the half-up top knot is the way to go. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it into a high ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. This hairstyle is perfect for adding a touch of style to your everyday look.

3. The Low Ponytail

The low ponytail is a sleek and chic hairstyle that is perfect for busy days. Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. For an extra touch of style, wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to conceal it. This hairstyle is versatile and can be dressed up or down.

4. The Side Braid

The side braid is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that is perfect for busy days. Start by gathering your hair to one side and dividing it into three sections. Cross the sections over each other, adding more hair as you go, until you reach the end. Secure the braid with an elastic band and pull out a few strands to create a relaxed, boho look.

5. The Twisted Ponytail

The twisted ponytail is a unique and easy hairstyle that is perfect for adding a twist to your everyday look. Start by gathering your hair into a ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Then, divide the ponytail into two sections and twist them around each other until you reach the end. Secure the twisted ponytail with another elastic band for a polished finish.

6. The Sleek Low Bun

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that is both elegant and easy to achieve, the sleek low bun is the way to go. Start by gathering your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. Then, twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins. This hairstyle is perfect for formal occasions or when you want to add a touch of sophistication to your look.

7. The Double Dutch Braids

If you have longer hair and want a hairstyle that keeps your hair out of your face, the double Dutch braids are a great option. Start by parting your hair down the middle and dividing it into two sections. Then, create Dutch braids on each side by crossing the sections under each other instead of over. Secure the braids with elastic bands and you’re good to go!

8. The Messy Fishtail Braid

The messy fishtail braid is a boho-inspired hairstyle that is perfect for adding a touch of whimsy to your look. Start by gathering your hair into a low ponytail and divide it into two sections. Take a small section from the outside of one section and cross it over to the inside of the other section. Repeat this process, alternating sides, until you reach the end. Secure the fishtail braid with an elastic band and pull out a few strands for a relaxed, messy look.

9. The Twist and Pin

The twist and pin hairstyle is a quick and easy way to add a touch of elegance to your everyday look. Start by twisting a small section of hair away from your face and secure it with a bobby pin. Repeat this process on the other side, twisting the hair towards your face this time. Continue twisting and pinning sections of hair until you achieve your desired look.

10. The Headband Tuck

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that is both stylish and effortless, the headband tuck is the way to go. Start by placing a headband over your head, positioning it a few inches back from your hairline. Then, take small sections of hair and tuck them into the headband, working your way around your head until all the hair is tucked. This hairstyle is perfect for when you’re short on time but still want to look put together.

So there you have it – 10 easy hairstyles for busy days. Whether you’re running late for work or simply want a quick and stylish look, these hairstyles are sure to save the day. Give them a try and let us know which one is your favorite!
