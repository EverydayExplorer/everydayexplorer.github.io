The Best Game Development Podcasts to Follow


In the rapidly evolving world of game development, staying updated with the latest trends, techniques, and insights can be a challenge. Podcasts have emerged as a popular medium for developers, designers, and enthusiasts alike to share knowledge, stories, and experiences in an engaging format. Whether you are a seasoned developer or just starting your journey in the gaming industry, there is a wealth of information available through these audio resources. Below is a curated list of some of the best game development podcasts that you should follow to enhance your skills, broaden your understanding, and connect with the community.

1. Game Maker’s Notebook

Overview: Hosted by the Academy of Interactive Arts & Sciences, Game Maker’s Notebook features interviews with influential figures in the gaming industry. The podcast delves into the creative processes behind game development, offering listeners a glimpse into the minds of successful game makers.

Why Listen? This podcast not only highlights the experiences of game developers but also explores the challenges they face in the industry. Every episode provides valuable insights into the artistic and technical aspects of game creation, making it a must-listen for anyone serious about game development.
