Easy and Delicious Instant Pot Soup Recipes for Cozy Nights


As the temperatures start to drop and the nights get colder, there’s nothing quite like curling up with a warm and comforting bowl of soup. And what better way to make delicious soups quickly and easily than with an Instant Pot? With its pressure-cooking capabilities, the Instant Pot allows you to whip up flavorful soups in a fraction of the time it would take on the stovetop. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most easy and delicious Instant Pot soup recipes that will surely make your cozy nights even more enjoyable.

Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

There’s nothing quite like a classic tomato soup to warm you up on a chilly night. This creamy tomato basil soup is rich, flavorful, and incredibly easy to make in your Instant Pot. Simply sauté onions and garlic, add canned tomatoes, vegetable broth, and spices, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. Once the soup is cooked, use an immersion blender to make it smooth and creamy. Garnish with fresh basil and serve with a side of grilled cheese for the ultimate cozy night meal.

Hearty Chicken Noodle Soup

Chicken noodle soup is a timeless classic, and making it in the Instant Pot couldn’t be easier. In this recipe, chicken thighs are cooked with onions, carrots, celery, and spices to create a flavorful broth. After the chicken is cooked, shred it with a fork and add dry noodles to the pot. Let the noodles cook for a few minutes, and you’ll have a delicious and hearty soup ready to be enjoyed. This comforting soup is perfect for those nights when you need a warm hug in a bowl.

Savory Butternut Squash Soup

Butternut squash is a popular ingredient during the fall season, and this Instant Pot recipe takes full advantage of its natural sweetness and creaminess. Simply sauté onions and garlic, add cubed butternut squash, spices, and vegetable broth to the Instant Pot, and let it cook under pressure until the squash is tender. Finish the soup by blending it until smooth and garnish with a drizzle of cream and toasted pumpkin seeds. This butternut squash soup is not only deliciously cozy but also packed with vitamins and nutrients.

Flavorful Lentil Soup

Lentil soup is a healthy and hearty option that is packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. With the help of an Instant Pot, you can have a delicious lentil soup ready in no time. Sauté onions, garlic, and spices, then add lentils, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes to the pot. Pressure cook until the lentils are tender, and you’ll have a flavorful and comforting soup that will keep you full and satisfied on those chilly nights.

Spicy Taco Soup

If you’re craving a soup with a bit of a kick, this spicy taco soup is the perfect choice. Ground beef, onions, garlic, and spices are sautéed in the Instant Pot before adding diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and vegetable broth. Pressure cook everything until the flavors meld together, and you’ll have a hearty and spicy soup that will warm you from the inside out. Top it off with some shredded cheese, sour cream, and tortilla chips for an extra delicious touch.

Conclusion

Cozy nights call for cozy meals, and what better way to warm up than with a comforting bowl of soup? Thanks to the Instant Pot, you can enjoy these easy and delicious soup recipes without spending hours in the kitchen. From classic tomato soup to spicy taco soup, there’s something for everyone’s taste. So grab your Instant Pot, stock up on ingredients, and get ready to enjoy some cozy nights with these fantastic soup recipes. Your taste buds will thank you, and your cozy nights will never be the same!
