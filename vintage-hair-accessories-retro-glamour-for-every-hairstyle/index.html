Vintage Hair Accessories: Retro Glamour for Every Hairstyle


Vintage hair accessories are a fabulous way to add retro glamour to any hairstyle. Whether you’re going for a classic updo or a chic bob, incorporating vintage hair accessories can instantly elevate your look and transport you back to the glamorous days of old Hollywood.

In this blog post, we will explore the beauty of vintage hair accessories and how they can add character and charm to any hairstyle. From delicate hairpins to bold statement pieces, there is a vintage hair accessory to suit every taste and occasion.

The Allure of Vintage Hair Accessories

There is something undeniably captivating about vintage hair accessories. They have a timeless quality that can completely transform a hairstyle. Whether it’s a flapper-inspired headband, a sparkly barrette from the 1950s, or an intricately designed comb from the Victorian era, vintage hair accessories carry a sense of history and elegance.

These accessories not only serve a practical purpose of keeping your hair in place, but they also serve as beautiful pieces of art that complement your overall look. They can be the perfect finishing touch to any outfit, adding a touch of glamour and sophistication.

Styles and Designs

Vintage hair accessories come in a wide range of styles and designs, making it easy to find the perfect match for your personal style and hairstyle. Here are a few popular vintage hair accessories that are making a comeback:

1. Hairpins and Bobby Pins

Hairpins and bobby pins were commonly used in the early to mid-20th century to secure hairstyles in place. Today, vintage hairpins and bobby pins are highly sought after for their intricate designs and nostalgic charm. They can be adorned with crystals, pearls, or floral accents, adding a touch of glamour to any hairstyle.

2. Headbands and Hairbands

Headbands and hairbands became popular in the 1920s during the flapper era. They were worn low on the forehead, adding a glamorous and chic touch to any hairstyle. Vintage headbands often feature intricate beadwork or feathers, making them the perfect accessory for a retro-inspired look.

3. Barrettes and Hair Clips

Barrettes and hair clips were a staple in the 1950s and 1960s. They come in a wide array of styles, from simple and understated to bold and eye-catching. Vintage barrettes and hair clips often feature colorful and unique designs that can add a pop of retro glamour to any hairstyle.

4. Combs and Hair Comb Sets

Vintage combs and hair comb sets were commonly worn in the Victorian and Edwardian eras. They are typically made of delicate materials such as ivory, tortoiseshell, or silver, and feature intricate designs that are true works of art. Vintage combs and hair comb sets are perfect for adding an elegant and refined touch to any hairstyle.

How to Style Vintage Hair Accessories

Now that you have an idea of the different types of vintage hair accessories available, let’s explore how to incorporate them into your hairstyle for a truly glamorous look.

1. Updos and Chignons

Vintage hair accessories can beautifully adorn an updo or chignon hairstyle. For a classic and sophisticated look, try adding a vintage hairpin or comb to secure your updo in place. You can also use a headband or hairband to add a touch of retro charm to your hairstyle.

2. Half-Up Hairstyles

For a more relaxed yet elegant look, consider incorporating vintage hair accessories into a half-up hairstyle. Use a stylish barrette or hair clip to pin back a section of your hair, adding a touch of vintage glamour to your overall look.

3. Loose Waves or Curls

If you prefer wearing your hair down, vintage hair accessories can still be incorporated to enhance your style. Opt for a delicate hairpin or bobby pin with crystal or pearl accents to add a subtle vintage touch to your loose waves or curls.

4. Ponytails and Braids

Even simple everyday hairstyles like ponytails and braids can benefit from the addition of vintage hair accessories. Try securing your ponytail or braid with a vintage barrette or hair clip to instantly elevate the look and add a touch of retro glamour.

Where to Find Vintage Hair Accessories

Now that you’re inspired to add some vintage glamour to your hairstyle, you may be wondering where to find these fabulous accessories. Fortunately, there are several options available:

1. Antique Stores and Vintage Boutiques

Antique stores and vintage boutiques are treasure troves for finding unique and authentic vintage hair accessories. Take the time to explore these stores and discover one-of-a-kind pieces that will truly make a statement.

2. Online Marketplaces

Online marketplaces such as Etsy and eBay have a wide selection of vintage hair accessories available. You can easily browse through different styles and designs from the comfort of your own home, and have them delivered right to your doorstep.

3. Family Heirlooms and Thrift Stores

Don’t forget to check your own family heirlooms for vintage hair accessories. You may be surprised to find beautiful pieces tucked away in your grandmother’s jewelry box. Thrift stores and second-hand shops can also be great places to find vintage treasures.

In Conclusion

Vintage hair accessories are the perfect way to add retro glamour and elegance to any hairstyle. With their timeless designs and unique charm, they can instantly transform your look and transport you back to a bygone era of style and sophistication.

Whether you opt for delicate hairpins, bold barrettes, or intricate combs, vintage hair accessories offer countless possibilities for creative hairstyling. By incorporating these pieces into your hairstyles, you can embrace the beauty of the past while still staying on-trend.

So go ahead, unleash your inner vintage goddess, and let your hair accessories do the talking. With vintage hair accessories, every day can be a glamorous affair!
