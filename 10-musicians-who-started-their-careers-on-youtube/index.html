10 Musicians Who Started Their Careers on YouTube


In today’s digital age, YouTube has become more than just a platform for sharing videos. It has catapulted numerous talented individuals into the limelight and changed the way we discover new music. Many aspiring musicians have utilized this powerful tool to kickstart their careers and gain a dedicated fan base worldwide. In this blog post, we will explore 10 remarkable musicians who started their journey on YouTube and eventually found success in the music industry.

1. Justin Bieber: We can’t talk about musicians on YouTube without mentioning Justin Bieber. Discovered by talent manager Scooter Braun, Bieber’s angelic voice and boy-next-door charm captured the hearts of millions. From his early covers of popular songs to becoming an international pop sensation, Bieber’s rise to stardom is a testament to the power of YouTube.

2. Alessia Cara: Hailing from Canada, Alessia Cara began posting acoustic covers on her YouTube channel at the tender age of 13. Her soulful voice and introspective lyrics quickly gained attention, leading to her breakthrough hit ‘Here.’ Today, she is known for her empowering anthems and vulnerable songwriting that resonates with audiences around the world.

3. Troye Sivan: Starting his career as a YouTuber, Troye Sivan uploaded original music and covers on his channel, captivating viewers with his heartfelt performances. His raw talent and ability to connect with his fans led him to sign with a major record label. Sivan’s introspective pop sound and LGBTQ+ advocacy have made him a prominent figure in the music industry.

4. Charlie Puth: Initially gaining recognition through his viral YouTube videos, Charlie Puth caught the attention of artist Wiz Khalifa, leading to their collaboration on the chart-topping track ‘See You Again.’ Puth’s smooth vocals and impressive musicality have since propelled him to mainstream success, with hits like ‘Attention’ and ‘One Call Away.’

5. Tori Kelly: Tori Kelly’s journey on YouTube began when she participated in a talent competition show. Although she didn’t win, her incredible vocal range and soulful performances attracted a loyal following online. Today, Kelly is known for her powerful vocals and has even received Grammy nominations for her music.

6. Shawn Mendes: With his captivating voice and boy-next-door charm, Shawn Mendes gained popularity by posting covers on Vine before transitioning to YouTube. His acoustic renditions of popular songs caught the attention of both fans and record labels, eventually leading to his breakout hit ‘Stitches.’ Mendes has since become one of the biggest names in pop music.

7. Grace VanderWaal: At just 12 years old, Grace VanderWaal wowed judges and audiences on America’s Got Talent with her original songwriting abilities and distinctive ukulele playing. Her unique style and genuine talent earned her the Golden Buzzer and a massive following on YouTube. VanderWaal continues to create heartfelt music that resonates with listeners of all ages.

8. Lindsey Stirling: Combining her love for violin and dance, Lindsey Stirling showcased her mesmerizing talents on YouTube. Her innovative fusion of classical music and electronic beats garnered millions of views, leading to collaborations with renowned artists and a thriving career as a modern-day violinist.

9. Postmodern Jukebox: What happens when you mix together nostalgic tunes with a modern twist? You get Postmodern Jukebox, a collective of talented musicians who gained fame by reimagining popular songs in vintage styles. Their infectious covers attracted a massive following on YouTube and led to sold-out world tours.

10. Pentatonix: Renowned for their breathtaking a cappella arrangements, Pentatonix rose to prominence after winning the reality show The Sing-Off. Their sublime harmonies and creative interpretations of popular songs captivated viewers on YouTube, propelling them to become one of the most successful a cappella groups in the world.

These are just a few examples of musicians who kickstarted their careers on YouTube. Their journeys serve as a testament to the power of this platform in shaping the music industry and showcasing raw talent to a global audience. As we enter a new era of music discovery, it’s exciting to think about the next wave of artists who will find their voice through YouTube and leave an indelible mark on the world of music.
