The Top Car Auctions Around the World: Exotic and Classic Cars


Welcome to the exciting world of car auctions! Whether you’re a car enthusiast, a collector, or simply someone with an appreciation for fine automobiles, attending a car auction can be a thrilling experience. In this blog post, we will take you on a global journey to explore some of the top car auctions around the world, where exotic and classic cars take center stage. From the glamorous streets of Monaco to the bustling cities of the United States, let’s dive into the world of automotive auctions.

1. Barrett-Jackson, United States:
Starting off our list is Barrett-Jackson, a name synonymous with classic car auctions. With its headquarters in Scottsdale, Arizona, Barrett-Jackson has become a go-to destination for car enthusiasts. This auction house is renowned for its wide range of classic and exotic cars, attracting collectors and buyers from all over the world. From iconic muscle cars to rare vintage vehicles, Barrett-Jackson never fails to deliver an unforgettable experience.

2. RM Sotheby’s, Worldwide:
As one of the leading auction houses globally, RM Sotheby’s hosts auctions in key cities around the world, including Monterey, California, and London, UK. With a focus on rare and high-performance automobiles, RM Sotheby’s boasts an impressive catalog of classic and contemporary vehicles. The auctions provide a platform for buyers and sellers to connect and showcase their prized possessions.

3. Bonhams, United Kingdom:
Based in the United Kingdom, Bonhams has a long-standing reputation for conducting successful car auctions. Their international presence extends to the United States, Europe, and Asia, ensuring that car enthusiasts worldwide have access to some of the most sought-after vehicles. Bonhams is known for its captivating range of classic cars, including those with historical significance and great provenance.

4. Gooding & Company, United States:
With its auctions held in California during the prestigious Monterey Car Week, Gooding & Company has established itself as a premier collector car auction house. This event draws the attention of automotive enthusiasts and collectors from around the globe. Gooding & Company’s auctions feature an array of exceptional and rare automobiles, often breaking records with their sales.

5. Artcurial, France:
Situated in the heart of Paris, Artcurial offers a unique blend of art and automotive auctions. This renowned auction house showcases both classic cars and modern supercars, attracting a diverse range of buyers. Artcurial’s events create a vibrant atmosphere, combining the love for automobiles with the allure of the city of lights.

6. Mecum Auctions, United States:
For those seeking a wide variety of classic and muscle cars, Mecum Auctions is a must-visit destination. With auctions held in different cities across the United States, Mecum offers an extensive selection of vehicles to suit various tastes and budgets. Their events are known for their high-energy atmosphere and the opportunity to witness rare automotive gems find new owners.

7. Coys, United Kingdom:
Rounding out our list is Coys, a British auction house that has been captivating car enthusiasts since the 1980s. Coys auctions present a diverse range of vehicles, including classic cars, modern supercars, and even racing memorabilia. Their events attract a loyal following of buyers and sellers alike, making it a popular platform for collectors to expand their collections.

These are just a few examples of the top car auctions around the world, each offering a unique experience and an opportunity to acquire some of the most cherished automobiles in existence. Whether you’re a seasoned collector or simply an admirer of automotive excellence, attending one of these auctions will undoubtedly ignite your passion for cars. So, keep an eye on the upcoming auctions and prepare to be amazed by the stunning vehicles that grace the auction blocks. Happy bidding!
