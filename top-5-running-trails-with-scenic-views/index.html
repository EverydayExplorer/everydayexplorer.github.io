Top 5 Running Trails with Scenic Views


Are you a running enthusiast seeking picturesque landscapes to elevate your cardio routine? Look no further! Whether you’re a seasoned marathoner or just starting out on your fitness journey, there’s something invigorating about hitting the trails amidst breathtaking scenery. Imagine the rhythm of your footsteps synchronizing with the natural beauty around you, each stride a testament to the wonders of the great outdoors. If you’re ready to lace up your running shoes and explore some of the most scenic trails around, here are the top five destinations that promise to take your breath away – in more ways than one!

1. Grand Canyon Rim Trail, Arizona

When it comes to awe-inspiring vistas, few places can rival the Grand Canyon. The Rim Trail offers runners an unparalleled opportunity to experience the grandeur of this natural wonder up close. Stretching for 13 miles along the South Rim, this trail boasts sweeping panoramas of the canyon’s rugged cliffs and winding Colorado River below. Whether you opt for a leisurely jog or a more intense sprint, every step along the Rim Trail is a reminder of the Earth’s majestic beauty.

2. Big Sur Trail, California

For those craving a coastal escape, the Big Sur Trail delivers in spades. Located along California’s rugged central coast, this iconic route offers runners a chance to marvel at the dramatic meeting of land and sea. As you weave your way through redwood forests and rocky cliffs, the sound of crashing waves provides a soothing soundtrack to your run. With breathtaking vistas around every bend, the Big Sur Trail is a must-visit for any runner seeking inspiration from nature’s bounty.

3. Banff Legacy Trail, Alberta, Canada

Nestled amidst the stunning Canadian Rockies, the Banff Legacy Trail is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. This scenic route spans 22 miles from the town of Banff to the neighboring town of Canmore, offering runners a front-row seat to some of the most spectacular mountain views in the world. With snow-capped peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and lush alpine meadows as far as the eye can see, every mile of the Banff Legacy Trail is a testament to the raw beauty of the Canadian wilderness.

4. Cinque Terre Coastal Trail, Italy

Experience la dolce vita as you run along the breathtaking Cinque Terre Coastal Trail in Italy. This UNESCO World Heritage site boasts a network of ancient footpaths that wind their way along the rugged Ligurian coastline, connecting the five colorful villages of Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore. With sweeping views of the turquoise Mediterranean Sea and terraced vineyards clinging to steep cliffs, this trail offers a sensory feast for runners seeking both physical and visual stimulation.

5. Great Ocean Walk, Victoria, Australia

Prepare to be mesmerized by the rugged beauty of Australia’s Great Ocean Walk. Stretching for 65 miles along the southeastern coast of Victoria, this epic trail takes runners on a journey through ancient rainforests, towering sea cliffs, and pristine beaches. Highlights include the iconic Twelve Apostles rock formations and the lush Otway Ranges, where native wildlife abounds. Whether you tackle the entire route in one go or opt for shorter segments, the Great Ocean Walk promises an unforgettable running experience amidst some of Australia’s most breathtaking scenery.

In conclusion, these top five running trails with scenic views offer a perfect blend of physical challenge and natural beauty. Whether you’re drawn to the majestic canyons of Arizona, the rugged coastline of California, the pristine wilderness of Canada, the charming villages of Italy, or the dramatic landscapes of Australia, there’s a trail here to suit every runner’s tastes. So lace up your shoes, hit the trails, and prepare to be inspired by the wonders of the great outdoors!
