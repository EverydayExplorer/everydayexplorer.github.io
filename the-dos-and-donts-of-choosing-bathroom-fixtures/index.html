The Dos and Don'ts of Choosing Bathroom Fixtures


When it comes to designing your dream bathroom, one of the most important decisions you’ll make is choosing the right fixtures. From faucets to showerheads, these elements not only play a functional role but also contribute to the overall aesthetic appeal of your space. To help you navigate the vast world of bathroom fixtures, we’ve compiled a list of dos and don’ts to keep in mind. Let’s dive in!

The Dos

  1. Do Consider Your Bathroom Style: Before you start selecting fixtures, take a moment to think about the overall style and theme of your bathroom. Are you going for a modern, minimalist look, or do you prefer a more traditional, vintage vibe? Matching your fixtures to your bathroom’s style will create a cohesive and visually pleasing space.

  2. Do Prioritize Quality: When it comes to fixtures, quality matters. Invest in high-quality materials that are durable and long-lasting. Opting for fixtures made from solid brass or stainless steel ensures they will withstand the test of time and daily use.

  3. Do Pay Attention to Water Efficiency: In today’s eco-conscious world, water efficiency is paramount. Look for fixtures that are labeled as WaterSense certified, as they meet the Environmental Protection Agency’s (EPA) criteria for water efficiency. This not only helps the environment but also saves you money on your water bill.

  4. Do Consider Functionality: Think about how you will be using your bathroom and choose fixtures that align with your needs. For example, if you love taking long showers, consider installing a rain showerhead for a luxurious experience. If accessibility is a concern, opt for fixtures that are ADA-compliant.

  5. Do Think About Maintenance: Cleaning and maintaining bathroom fixtures can be a chore, so choose fixtures that are easy to clean and resistant to water stains. Finishes like chrome and brushed nickel are not only stylish but also require minimal upkeep.

The Don’ts

  1. Don’t Sacrifice Comfort for Style: While aesthetics are important, don’t forget about comfort. Ensure your fixtures are ergonomically designed and comfortable to use. For faucets, look for ones with easy-to-use handles that provide a smooth water flow.

  2. Don’t Overlook Proper Installation: Proper installation is crucial for the longevity and functionality of your fixtures. Unless you have experience, it’s best to hire a professional plumber to ensure everything is installed correctly. Improper installation can lead to leaks and other issues down the line.

  3. Don’t Forget About Lighting: Lighting plays a significant role in enhancing the ambiance of your bathroom. When choosing fixtures, consider how they will interact with your lighting choices. For instance, a statement chandelier might clash with a sleek, modern faucet.

  4. Don’t Neglect Storage: Bathroom fixtures aren’t just limited to faucets and showerheads. Don’t forget about storage solutions like towel bars, shelves, and medicine cabinets. These fixtures not only add functionality but also help keep your bathroom organized and clutter-free.

  5. Don’t Rush Your Decision: Choosing bathroom fixtures is an investment, so take your time. Research different brands, read reviews, and visit showrooms to get a feel for the fixtures in person. A well-informed decision will result in a bathroom that you’ll love for years to come.

Remember, the key to choosing bathroom fixtures is finding the perfect balance between style, functionality, and quality. By following these dos and don’ts, you’ll be well on your way to creating a bathroom that is both beautiful and practical. Happy fixture hunting!

Note: This blog post is purely informational and does not constitute professional advice. Always consult with a professional before making any decisions regarding your bathroom fixtures.
