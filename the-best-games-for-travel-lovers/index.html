The Best Games for Travel Lovers


Traveling is a wonderful experience that allows us to explore new places, immerse ourselves in different cultures, and create lasting memories. For many travel lovers, the thrill of discovering unknown destinations goes hand in hand with the joy of indulging in their favorite hobby – gaming. Whether you’re on a long-haul flight, waiting at the airport, or simply looking to pass the time during your travels, having a selection of engaging games can make your journey even more enjoyable. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best games for travel lovers.

1. Monument Valley:
Inspired by surreal architecture and optical illusions, Monument Valley is a visually stunning puzzle game that will captivate your imagination. As you guide the silent princess, Ida, through a series of mind-bending mazes, you’ll encounter mesmerizing landscapes and encounter challenging puzzles. Its intuitive touch controls and immersive sound design make it an ideal game for travel.

2. Ticket to Ride:
If you’re a fan of strategy board games, then Ticket to Ride is a must-play. This award-winning game takes you on a cross-country adventure as you compete against other players to build railway routes across different continents. With its simple rules, engaging gameplay, and offline capabilities, Ticket to Ride is perfect for both solo play and multiplayer sessions during long journeys.

3. Alto’s Odyssey:
Escape to the serene and captivating world of Alto’s Odyssey. This endless runner game combines breathtaking visuals, soothing music, and smooth gameplay to create a truly immersive experience. Embark on a sandboarding journey through stunning desert landscapes, perform tricks, and overcome challenges as you chase the horizon. Alto’s Odyssey is a beautiful game that will keep you entertained for hours.

4. Carcassonne:
Another classic board game that has made a successful transition to the digital realm is Carcassonne. This tile-placement game challenges you to build cities, roads, and farms while strategically claiming areas for yourself and blocking your opponents. With its easy-to-learn mechanics and multiplayer support, Carcassonne is a great game to play with friends or against AI opponents during your travels.

5. Mini Metro:
For those who enjoy problem-solving and city planning, Mini Metro offers a unique and addictive experience. As the architect of a growing subway network, your goal is to efficiently connect stations and manage passenger flow. With its minimalist visuals and relaxing soundtrack, Mini Metro is a great game to unwind with while on the go.

6. The Room:
Step into the mysterious world of The Room, a puzzle game that will challenge your perception and problem-solving skills. Explore intricately crafted boxes, unlock secrets, and uncover hidden objects as you solve puzzles within puzzles. With its atmospheric visuals and engrossing storyline, The Room is perfect for those seeking a captivating gaming experience during their travels.

7. Reigns:
If you’re looking for a game that combines strategy and storytelling, look no further than Reigns. In this unique card-based game, you play the role of a monarch and make decisions by swiping left or right, just like in a dating app. Your choices will determine the fate of your kingdom and its various factions. With its clever writing and addictive gameplay, Reigns is a game that will keep you entertained for hours on end.

In conclusion, having a selection of engaging games can greatly enhance your travel experience. Whether you prefer puzzle games, board games, or immersive adventures, there are plenty of options available to cater to every traveler’s preferences. With these recommendations, you can make your journeys more enjoyable and memorable. So, pack your bags, get ready for your next adventure, and don’t forget to bring your favorite games along!
