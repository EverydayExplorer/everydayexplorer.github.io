Crafting with Washers: Industrial-Inspired Decor


When it comes to home decor, there’s a growing trend towards industrial-inspired designs. People are embracing the raw and rustic look of exposed materials like metal, wood, and concrete. One such material that has gained popularity in the crafting world is washers. Yes, those small metal discs traditionally used in construction and mechanics can be transformed into stunning pieces of art for your home. In this blog post, we will explore the world of crafting with washers and discover how you can create unique and industrial-inspired decor for your space.

1. Washer Wall Art:

Let’s start with a simple yet impactful project - washer wall art. Begin by selecting a variety of different-sized washers. Arrange them in a pattern of your choice on a wooden board or canvas. You can choose to leave the washers in their original metallic finish or paint them in bold colors for a pop of vibrancy. Once you’re satisfied with the arrangement, glue the washers onto the board and let it dry. Hang your washer wall art in your living room or bedroom to add an industrial touch to your space.

2. Washer Coasters:

Protect your furniture in style with DIY washer coasters. Start by gathering a set of large washers and small cork circles or felt pads. Using strong adhesive, attach the cork or felt to the bottom of the washers, ensuring that they cover the entire surface. These washer coasters not only serve a functional purpose but also add a unique industrial flair to your coffee table or dining area.

3. Washer Wind Chimes:

Bring the soothing sound of wind chimes into your outdoor space with a washer wind chime. Begin by collecting a variety of washers in different shapes and sizes. Attach them to a sturdy piece of string or fishing line, leaving enough space between each washer. For an added touch, incorporate other industrial elements such as nuts, bolts, or chains. Hang your washer wind chime in your garden or patio and let the breeze create a melodic symphony.

4. Washer Vase:

Create a striking centerpiece for your dining table or mantelpiece with a washer vase. Start by taking a cylindrical glass or plastic container and gluing washers around its exterior, ensuring that they are tightly packed together. You can choose to leave the washers in their original metallic finish or spray paint them for a more cohesive look. Fill the vase with your favorite flowers or greenery, and let the industrial charm take center stage.

5. Washer Jewelry:

Add a touch of industrial chic to your personal style with washer jewelry. Start by selecting washers of different sizes and shapes. Using jump rings or small chains, link the washers together to create unique necklaces, bracelets, or earrings. You can leave the washers bare or experiment with different finishes like painting, rusting, or distressing. Wear your washer jewelry with pride and let it be a conversation starter wherever you go.

Crafting with washers opens up a world of possibilities for creating industrial-inspired decor. From wall art to jewelry, there are endless ways to incorporate this humble hardware into your home. So, unleash your creativity and embrace the beauty of industrial design with these washer craft projects. Get ready to turn ordinary washers into extraordinary pieces of art that will transform your space into a stylish, industrial haven.

Remember to share your creations on social media using #WasherCrafting and inspire others to explore this unique crafting trend. Happy crafting!
