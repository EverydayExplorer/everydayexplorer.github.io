Funny Videos Gone Viral: Internet Sensations That Had Us Rolling on the Floor


The internet has become a treasure trove of hilarious content, with funny videos going viral and leaving us in fits of laughter. These internet sensations have a way of capturing our attention and spreading like wildfire across social media platforms. In this blog post, we will take a look at some of the most memorable funny videos that had us rolling on the floor with laughter.

1. The Sneezing Baby Panda
Who can forget the adorable sneezing baby panda? This video took the internet by storm and had everyone chuckling at the little panda’s unexpected sneeze. Its innocent and cute demeanor made it an instant hit, and it quickly racked up millions of views. This video serves as a reminder that even the simplest things can bring us joy and laughter.

2. The Dramatic Chipmunk
One of the earliest viral videos to hit the internet, the dramatic chipmunk became an instant sensation. In this short clip, a chipmunk turns its head dramatically towards the camera, creating a dramatic effect. The video’s simplicity and unexpectedness made it incredibly funny, and it became a favorite for meme creators and internet users alike.

3. Charlie Bit My Finger
This video captured a hilarious and relatable moment between two brothers. In the clip, a young boy named Harry puts his finger in his baby brother Charlie’s mouth, only to be met with a bite. The genuine reactions and adorable British accents made this video a viral sensation, and it has since amassed millions of views and countless parodies.

4. The Evolution of Dance
In this video, comedian Judson Laipply takes us on a journey through the evolution of dance, showcasing various dance moves from different eras. Laipply’s energetic performance and comedic timing had audiences laughing throughout the video. It quickly went viral and became one of the most-watched videos on the internet, proving that laughter is a universal language.

5. The Numa Numa Dance
Gary Brolsma’s iconic lip-syncing performance to the Romanian pop song ‘Dragostea Din Tei’ became an internet sensation. Brolsma’s contagious energy and enthusiastic dance moves had viewers around the world laughing and dancing along with him. The video’s popularity led to numerous parodies and remixes, solidifying its place in internet history.

6. The Double Rainbow Guy
Paul Vasquez’s awe-inspired reaction to witnessing a double rainbow became an internet phenomenon. His genuine amazement and emotional response struck a chord with viewers, who found his enthusiasm both endearing and hilarious. The video quickly went viral, with people sharing their own interpretations and remixes of Vasquez’s iconic moment.

7. The ‘Charlie, Charlie’ Challenge
While not traditionally funny, the ‘Charlie, Charlie’ Challenge became a viral sensation due to its absurdity and the reactions it elicited. This challenge involved summoning a Mexican demon named Charlie using pencils arranged in a cross formation. The video sparked a wave of parodies and memes, with people mocking the seriousness and superstition surrounding the challenge.

These funny videos gone viral remind us of the power of laughter and how the internet can bring people together through shared humor. Whether it’s a sneezing panda or a dramatic chipmunk, these videos have become a part of internet culture and continue to make us laugh years after their initial release. So the next time you need a good laugh, revisit these internet sensations and let the chuckles roll in!
