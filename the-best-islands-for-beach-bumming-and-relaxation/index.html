The Best Islands for Beach Bumming and Relaxation


Summer is the perfect time to indulge in some sun, sand, and sea. Whether you’re looking to catch up on your reading, work on your tan, or simply unwind and let your troubles melt away, there’s nothing quite like a beach vacation. And what better place to do it than on an island? From the Caribbean to the Mediterranean and beyond, here are some of the best islands for beach bumming and relaxation.

1. Bora Bora, French Polynesia - With its crystal-clear lagoon, powdery white sand beaches, and overwater bungalows, Bora Bora is the quintessential tropical paradise. Whether you’re snorkeling, sunbathing, or strolling along the shore, this island has something for everyone. And with only a handful of resorts, you’re sure to feel like you have the place to yourself.

2. Maui, Hawaii - With its rugged coastline, lush rainforests, and world-renowned surf breaks, Maui is a beach lover’s dream. Whether you’re lounging on the golden sands of Kaanapali Beach, hiking through the bamboo forests of the Road to Hana, or catching waves at Hookipa Beach Park, there’s plenty to see and do on this island.

3. Santorini, Greece - With its whitewashed villages, volcanic cliffs, and electric blue sea, Santorini is one of the most picturesque islands in the world. And when it comes to beaches, this island doesn’t disappoint. From the black sands of Perissa Beach to the red sands of Red Beach, there’s a beach for every taste on Santorini.

4. Palawan, Philippines - From the limestone cliffs and turquoise waters of El Nido to the hidden coves and lagoons of Coron, Palawan is a tropical wonderland. Whether you’re island hopping, snorkeling, or simply soaking up the sun on a secluded beach, this island will take your breath away.

5. St. Barts, Caribbean - With its French flair, celebrity sightings, and chic beach clubs, St. Barts is the epitome of island luxury. Whether you’re sipping champagne on the sands of Nikki Beach or lounging on the unspoiled shores of Saline Beach, this island oozes glamour and sophistication.

6. Koh Phangan, Thailand - With its laid-back atmosphere, hippie vibe, and legendary full moon parties, Koh Phangan is the ultimate island for beach bums and party animals alike. Whether you’re sunbathing on the beach, exploring the island’s waterfalls, or dancing the night away at a beach party, Koh Phangan has something for everyone.

7. Maldives - With its turquoise waters, vibrant coral reefs, and overwater bungalows, the Maldives is the ultimate luxury island escape. Whether you’re snorkeling with whale sharks, indulging in a spa treatment, or simply soaking up the sun on a private beach, this island will take your breath away.

In conclusion, these are just a few of the many islands around the world that provide the perfect environment for beach bumming and relaxation. Whether you’re looking for overwater bungalows, secluded coves, or epic surf breaks, there’s an island out there that’s perfect for you. So go ahead and book that dream vacation, pack your sunscreen, and get ready to soak up the sun on some of the world’s best beaches.
