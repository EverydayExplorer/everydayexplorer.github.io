The Best Instant Pot Soup Recipes


When the cold weather sets in, there’s nothing quite like a comforting bowl of soup to warm you up from the inside out. And if you’re a fan of quick and easy cooking, then the Instant Pot is your best friend. This magical multi-cooker can whip up delicious soups in a fraction of the time it takes on the stovetop, making it the ultimate kitchen companion for busy home cooks. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of the best Instant Pot soup recipes that will tantalize your taste buds and keep you cozy all winter long.

  1. Classic Chicken Noodle Soup

  2. This timeless favorite gets a modern twist with the Instant Pot. Tender chunks of chicken, hearty vegetables, and slurp-worthy noodles come together in a rich and flavorful broth that’s ready in a fraction of the time.

  3. Luscious Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

  4. Say goodbye to canned tomato soup and embrace the homemade goodness of this Instant Pot version. Velvety smooth and bursting with the fresh flavors of ripe tomatoes and aromatic basil, this soup is an instant hit with the whole family.

  5. Soul-Warming Beef Stew

  6. Let the Instant Pot work its magic on this classic comfort food. Succulent beef, tender vegetables, and a robust, savory broth come together to create a stew that tastes like it’s been simmering all day, but is ready in a fraction of the time.

  7. Spicy Tortilla Soup

  8. Turn up the heat with this zesty and vibrant Mexican-inspired soup. Packed with tender shredded chicken, spicy chilies, and crunchy tortilla strips, this Instant Pot creation is a fiesta for your taste buds.

  9. Cozy Butternut Squash Soup

  10. Embrace the sweetness of butternut squash in this velvety smooth soup that’s perfect for chilly evenings. The Instant Pot transforms the humble squash into a luxurious and creamy delight that’s as satisfying as it is nutritious.

  11. Hearty Lentil Soup

  12. Packed with protein and fiber, this Instant Pot lentil soup is a wholesome and satisfying meal in a bowl. Earthy lentils, aromatic spices, and a hint of tangy tomato come together to create a soup that’s as good for you as it is delicious.

  13. Flavorful Chicken Tortilla Soup

  14. Add a Tex-Mex flair to your Instant Pot repertoire with this vibrant and satisfying soup. Tender chicken, smoky spices, and a generous squeeze of lime make this soup a fiesta for your taste buds.

  15. Savory Mushroom Barley Soup

  16. Elevate the humble mushroom to star status in this comforting and nourishing soup. Hearty barley, earthy mushrooms, and aromatic herbs create a soup that’s as satisfying as it is wholesome.

  17. Velvety Potato Leek Soup

  18. Indulge in the creamy richness of this classic soup made effortlessly in the Instant Pot. Tender potatoes, delicate leeks, and a hint of cream come together to create a soup that’s pure comfort in a bowl.

  19. Soothing Chicken and Wild Rice Soup

*   Embrace the rustic flavors of wild rice in this hearty and wholesome Instant Pot creation. Tender chicken, nutty wild rice, and a medley of vegetables come together in a nourishing soup that's perfect for chilly evenings.

There you have it - a tantalizing collection of the best Instant Pot soup recipes that are sure to warm your soul and delight your taste buds. Whether you’re craving a classic favorite or yearning to explore new flavors, the Instant Pot is your ticket to quick, easy, and utterly delicious soups that will keep you cozy all season long. So, dust off your Instant Pot, stock up on your favorite ingredients, and get ready to indulge in a world of soul-warming soups that are just a button press away.
