Books That Will Make You Believe in the Power of Music: Musical Reads


In a world filled with words and melodies, there is a magical connection between literature and music. Both have the power to transport us to different realms, evoke deep emotions, and inspire us in ways we never thought possible. If you’re a bookworm and a music lover, then this blog post is for you. We have curated a list of books that will make you believe in the power of music. These musical reads will take you on a journey through the harmonies of life, exploring the transformative and healing qualities of music. So, grab a cup of tea, put on your favorite tunes, and let’s dive into the world of musical reads.

1. ‘The Music Shop’ by Rachel Joyce: Set in a quaint record store, this enchanting story follows the life of Frank, a music aficionado who believes that the right song can change a person’s life. When a mysterious woman walks into his shop, Frank’s world is turned upside down. Through the power of music, he discovers the healing and transformative nature of love and friendship.

2. ‘The Song of Achilles’ by Madeline Miller: This captivating retelling of the Trojan War takes us on a journey through the eyes of Patroclus, a young prince who becomes the companion and lover of Achilles, the greatest warrior of his time. Music plays a significant role in this epic tale, as it captures the essence of their forbidden love and the beauty of their tragic fate.

3. ‘High Fidelity’ by Nick Hornby: This modern classic explores the life of Rob Fleming, a record store owner and music obsessive. Through his top five lists and witty observations, we delve into the intricate relationships between music, love, and self-discovery. Hornby’s writing captures the essence of the music scene and its impact on our lives.

4. ‘The Sound of a Wild Snail Eating’ by Elisabeth Tova Bailey: This unique memoir tells the story of a woman who becomes bedridden due to a mysterious illness. In her solitude, she discovers solace and companionship in the presence of a tiny snail. Through her observations, she unravels the hidden symphony of nature’s music, finding hope and resilience in the smallest of creatures.

5. ‘The History of Love’ by Nicole Krauss: This beautifully written novel weaves together the lives of two characters, Leo Gursky and Alma Singer, through the power of a book and the melodies of a lost love. Music acts as a guiding force, connecting the characters across time and space, reminding them of the universal language of the heart.

6. ‘Bel Canto’ by Ann Patchett: Set in an unnamed South American country, this gripping novel explores the power of music to transcend boundaries and bring together people from different walks of life. When a group of terrorists takes hostages during a concert, the captives and captors find solace and connection through the beauty of music.

7. ‘Just Kids’ by Patti Smith: This memoir by renowned artist Patti Smith takes us on a journey through her relationship with photographer Robert Mapplethorpe. Music acts as a guiding force throughout their lives, shaping their art and binding them together in a profound and enduring friendship.

These musical reads will transport you to a world where words and melodies intertwine, where the power of music can heal, transform, and inspire. Whether you’re a lover of fiction, memoirs, or historical retellings, these books will make you believe in the magic of music and its ability to touch our souls. So, pick up a book, put on your favorite song, and let the symphony of words and melodies sweep you away.
