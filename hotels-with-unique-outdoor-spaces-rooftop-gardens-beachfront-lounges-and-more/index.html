Hotels with Unique Outdoor Spaces: Rooftop Gardens, Beachfront Lounges, and More


One of the most important aspects travelers consider when choosing a hotel is the availability of outdoor spaces. Whether it’s a relaxing rooftop garden, a beachfront lounge, or a lush courtyard, these unique and inviting areas can significantly enhance a guest’s experience. In this blog post, we will explore some of the most stunning hotels around the world that boast exceptional outdoor spaces.

Rooftop gardens have gained immense popularity in recent years. Not only do they provide guests with a breathtaking view of the city skyline, but they also serve as tranquil retreats amidst the hustle and bustle of urban life. One such hotel is The Nomad Hotel in New York City. Situated in the heart of Manhattan, this hotel features a lush rooftop garden complete with cozy seating areas, greenery, and even a designated space for rooftop yoga classes. Guests can unwind and enjoy a cocktail while taking in the stunning views of the surrounding skyscrapers.

Moving away from the city, let’s explore hotels with beachfront lounges. Nothing beats the feeling of sinking your toes into the warm sand while sipping a refreshing drink as the waves crash nearby. The Surin Phuket in Thailand is a prime example of a luxury hotel that offers guests a chance to experience this idyllic setting. Nestled on the shores of Pansea Beach, this hotel boasts an expansive beachfront lounge area where guests can relax on sun loungers, enjoy a beachside massage, or take a dip in the crystal-clear waters.

For those seeking a more intimate and secluded outdoor space, hotels with lush courtyards or garden terraces provide a serene oasis away from the crowds. The Belmond Villa San Michele in Florence, Italy, is a stunning example of a hotel that combines old-world charm with a picturesque outdoor space. Its terraced gardens, adorned with colorful flowers, offer guests an opportunity to unwind in a peaceful setting while enjoying panoramic views of the city below.

In addition to providing guests with a serene environment, hotels with unique outdoor spaces also offer a variety of activities and amenities. For instance, some hotels organize outdoor movie nights under the stars, allowing guests to enjoy the latest blockbusters or timeless classics in a truly one-of-a-kind setting. Others may offer outdoor dining experiences, where guests can savor delicious cuisine while being surrounded by nature. These activities help create memorable experiences that guests will cherish long after their stay.

From rooftop gardens to beachfront lounges and lush courtyards, hotels with unique outdoor spaces have an undeniable appeal. They provide guests with a chance to reconnect with nature, unwind in a serene setting, and create lasting memories. Whether you’re a traveler looking for a relaxing retreat or a hotelier seeking to offer a distinctive experience, investing in outdoor spaces can be a game-changer. So, the next time you’re planning a trip or looking for a hotel, don’t forget to consider the outdoor spaces on offer - they might just be the highlight of your stay!
