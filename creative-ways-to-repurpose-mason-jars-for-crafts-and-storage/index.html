Creative Ways to Repurpose Mason Jars for Crafts and Storage


Mason jars are a versatile and affordable option for crafting and storage. Whether you have a collection of mason jars lying around or you’re looking for a new and creative way to repurpose them, this article is for you. Mason jars not only add a rustic charm to any space, but they also provide endless possibilities for DIY projects and storage solutions. In this blog post, we will explore some creative and innovative ways to repurpose mason jars for crafts and storage.

1. Mason Jar Organization: One of the simplest and most practical uses for mason jars is organizing small items. From craft supplies to kitchen utensils, mason jars can help keep everything in its place. You can attach mason jar lids to the underside of a shelf or cabinet, creating an instant storage solution. Simply screw the jar onto the lid, and you have a convenient and space-saving way to store items.

2. Mason Jar Vases and Centerpieces: Another popular use for mason jars is as vases and centerpieces. With their clear glass bodies, mason jars create a beautiful and rustic look when filled with flowers or greenery. Add some twine or ribbon around the rim for an extra touch. You can also paint the jars in your favorite colors to match your decor. Place them on dining tables, mantels, or windowsills to add a charming touch to any space.

3. Mason Jar Herb Garden: If you love fresh herbs but lack the garden space, mason jars can be your solution. With some potting soil and herb seeds, you can create a mini herb garden indoors. Simply fill each jar with soil, plant your favorite herbs, and place them on a sunny windowsill. Not only does this allow you to have fresh herbs at your fingertips, but it also adds a touch of greenery to your kitchen decor.

4. Mason Jar Candle Holders: Create a cozy and romantic atmosphere with mason jar candle holders. Fill the jars with sand or colored stones and place a candle in the center. Light the candle and let the soft glow illuminate the room. You can also experiment with different sizes and shapes of mason jars to create a visually appealing display. Remember to exercise caution when using candles and never leave them unattended.

5. Mason Jar Soap Dispenser: Transform a mason jar into a stylish and functional soap dispenser. Remove the jar lid and drill a hole in the center to fit a pump dispenser. Insert the pump and secure it with a waterproof adhesive. Fill the jar with your favorite liquid soap or hand sanitizer and replace the lid. This DIY project adds a touch of vintage charm to your bathroom or kitchen while keeping your hands clean and hygienic.

6. Mason Jar Lanterns: Enhance your outdoor space with mason jar lanterns. Insert a small string of fairy lights or LED candles into the jars, and string them up on your porch or hang them from tree branches. The soft and warm glow emitted from the jars will create a magical ambiance for evening gatherings or summer parties. Plus, the jars’ glass material will protect the lights from wind and rain, making them perfect for outdoor use.

7. Mason Jar Sewing Kit: If you are a crafty person or enjoy sewing, repurposing a mason jar into a sewing kit is a great idea. Fill the jar with sewing essentials such as needles, thread, buttons, and small scissors. You can even attach pincushions or a magnetic strip to the jar lid to keep your needles organized. This sewing kit is not only functional but also adds a touch of vintage style to your sewing space.

8. Mason Jar Terrarium: Bring a piece of nature indoors with a mason jar terrarium. Layer the bottom with small rocks or pebbles for drainage, followed by a layer of activated charcoal to keep the soil fresh. Add potting soil and your choice of small plants or succulents. Finish it off with decorative moss or small figurines to create a miniature garden inside the jar. Terrariums make a unique and eye-catching addition to any room.

9. Mason Jar Gift Containers: Get creative with your gift packaging by using mason jars as containers. Fill the jars with homemade cookies, candies, or bath salts, and decorate them with ribbons, labels, or wrapping paper. Not only will your gift look stylish and thoughtful, but the recipient can also reuse the jar for their own storage needs.

10. Mason Jar Salad Containers: If you’re someone who enjoys packing lunch or eating salads on the go, mason jars can be your meal prep ally. Layer your favorite salad ingredients in a jar, starting with the dressing at the bottom and finishing with the greens on top. When it’s time to eat, simply shake the jar, and your salad will be perfectly dressed. The airtight seal of the jar keeps the ingredients fresh and prevents any leaks in your bag.

In conclusion, mason jars offer endless opportunities for creativity and practicality. Whether you’re looking to organize small items, create beautiful decorations, or repurpose them for storage, mason jars are a versatile choice. This article has explored just a few of the many creative ways to repurpose mason jars for crafts and storage. So, grab your collection of mason jars and let your imagination run wild. Get ready to transform these humble jars into stunning creations that will add a touch of charm to your home.
