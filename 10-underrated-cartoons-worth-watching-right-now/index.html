10 Underrated Cartoons Worth Watching Right Now


When it comes to cartoons, most people tend to think of the classic favorites like SpongeBob SquarePants, The Simpsons, or Adventure Time. However, there are plenty of underrated gems out there that deserve just as much attention. If you’re in the mood for something new and different, here are 10 underrated cartoons that are worth watching right now.

  1. ‘Over the Garden Wall’: This beautifully animated miniseries follows two half-brothers as they journey through a mysterious forest. With its enchanting storyline and hauntingly beautiful music, this show is a must-watch for anyone who enjoys a touch of whimsy and a hint of darkness.

  2. ‘Gravity Falls’: While it gained a dedicated fan base, ‘Gravity Falls’ still remains underappreciated by the masses. This clever and quirky show follows twins Dipper and Mabel Pines as they spend the summer with their great-uncle Stan in a strange and supernatural town. Packed with humor, mystery, and heart, this is a cartoon that appeals to both kids and adults.

  3. ‘The Dragon Prince’: From the creators of ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender,’ this fantasy adventure series is a hidden gem on Netflix. With its diverse characters, intricate world-building, and compelling storyline, ‘The Dragon Prince’ deserves to be on everyone’s watchlist.

  4. ‘Hilda’: Based on the graphic novel series, ‘Hilda’ follows the adventures of a fearless young girl in a magical world full of mystical creatures. With its charming animation and captivating stories, this show is perfect for anyone who loves a good fantasy tale.

  5. ‘Star vs. The Forces of Evil’: This vibrant and action-packed show follows the adventures of Star Butterfly, a magical princess from another dimension, as she battles villains and navigates high school. With its blend of humor, heart, and epic battles, ‘Star vs. The Forces of Evil’ is a delightful watch for fans of all ages.

  6. ‘The Legend of Korra’: While ‘Avatar: The Last Airbender’ receives a lot of love, its sequel series ‘The Legend of Korra’ often flies under the radar. Set in the same beloved universe, this show follows the new Avatar, Korra, as she faces political unrest and spiritual challenges. With its mature themes and complex characters, ‘The Legend of Korra’ is a must-see for fans of thoughtful storytelling.

  7. ‘We Bare Bears’: This charming and heartwarming series follows three bear brothers, Grizzly, Panda, and Ice Bear, as they navigate the modern world and search for acceptance. With its endearing characters and positive messages about friendship and diversity, ‘We Bare Bears’ is a delightful watch for all ages.

  8. ‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’: Set in a post-apocalyptic world, this visually stunning show follows Kipo, a girl who embarks on a journey to find her father while encountering bizarre creatures and making new friends. With its diverse cast, catchy music, and engaging plot, ‘Kipo and the Age of Wonderbeasts’ is a hidden gem on Netflix.

  9. ‘Craig of the Creek’: This heartwarming and nostalgic show follows Craig and his friends as they explore the untamed wilderness of the creek in their neighborhood. With its relatable adventures and themes of friendship and imagination, ‘Craig of the Creek’ is a delightful watch for kids and adults alike.

  10. ‘The Owl House’: This enchanting and imaginative series follows a teenage girl named Luz who discovers a magical realm and befriends a rebellious witch and a tiny warrior. With its captivating world-building and diverse characters, ‘The Owl House’ is a must-watch for fans of fantasy and adventure.

In conclusion, these 10 underrated cartoons offer a diverse range of genres, themes, and art styles that are sure to captivate audiences of all ages. Whether you’re in the mood for fantasy, adventure, comedy, or heartwarming stories, these shows have something for everyone. So, if you’re looking for something new to binge-watch, give these underrated cartoons a chance – you won’t be disappointed!
