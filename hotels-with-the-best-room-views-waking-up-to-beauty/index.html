Hotels with the Best Room Views: Waking up to Beauty


Imagine waking up to a breathtaking view that takes your breath away. The sun gently peeks through the curtains, casting a warm glow on your face as you open your eyes. You’re not in a dream; you’re in a hotel room with the most incredible view imaginable. In this blog post, we will explore hotels around the world that offer the best room views, allowing you to wake up to beauty every day.

1. Amangiri, Utah, USA:
Nestled in the heart of the American Southwest, Amangiri offers a unique experience with its stunning desert backdrop. From your room, you’ll be treated to panoramic views of the otherworldly landscape, where dramatic cliffs and mesas meet the endless sky. As the sun rises, the colors of the desert come alive, painting the horizon in a mesmerizing display of nature’s art.

2. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:
Located in the vibrant city of Singapore, Marina Bay Sands offers rooms with a view that will leave you speechless. Perched high above the city, you’ll have an uninterrupted view of the iconic skyline, including the breathtaking Marina Bay and its stunning light show. As night falls, the city transforms into a glittering wonderland, creating a magical atmosphere that will enchant you.

3. Hotel del Coronado, California, USA:
Situated on the picturesque island of Coronado, just off the coast of San Diego, Hotel del Coronado offers rooms with views of the sparkling Pacific Ocean. Wake up to the sound of crashing waves and step out onto your balcony to witness the sun-kissed beach and vibrant surfers catching waves. The sight of dolphins playing in the distance adds an extra touch of magic to this already idyllic setting.

4. Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, Dubai, UAE:
Known for its opulence and grandeur, the Burj Al Arab Jumeirah in Dubai boasts rooms with unparalleled views of the Arabian Gulf. As you wake up in this luxurious hotel, you’ll be greeted by crystal-clear turquoise waters and pristine white sandy beaches. The sight of sail-shaped yachts gliding across the horizon adds to the sense of exclusivity and luxury that this hotel offers.

5. Jade Mountain, St. Lucia:
Perched high on a hillside in the Caribbean, Jade Mountain offers rooms with breathtaking views of the iconic Pitons, two volcanic spires that rise majestically from the sea. Each room is an architectural masterpiece, with an open fourth wall that allows you to fully immerse yourself in the beauty of the surrounding nature. The vibrant colors of the tropical gardens and the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean Sea create a paradise that is truly unforgettable.

6. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, India:
Experience the romance and charm of Udaipur, the ‘City of Lakes,’ at The Oberoi Udaivilas. This hotel offers rooms with views of Lake Pichola, where the reflection of the majestic City Palace shimmers on the water’s surface. Enjoy the sight of traditional boats gliding across the lake as the sun sets, painting the sky in hues of orange and pink. The timeless beauty of this setting will transport you to a world of enchantment.

In conclusion, waking up to beauty is a priceless experience that these hotels can offer. Whether it’s the awe-inspiring landscapes, city skylines, or pristine beaches, these hotels will make your mornings extraordinary. So, if you’re seeking a truly memorable stay, consider booking a room in one of these hotels and let the beauty unfold before your eyes.
