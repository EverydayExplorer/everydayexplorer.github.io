Homemade Bread Recipes for Bread Lovers


Welcome to our blog dedicated to all the bread lovers out there! In this article, we will share some delicious homemade bread recipes that will surely satisfy your cravings. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or just starting out, these recipes are perfect for anyone looking to enjoy the warm and comforting taste of freshly baked bread.

1. Classic French Baguette
Ingredients:
- 500g bread flour
- 10g salt
- 7g instant yeast
- 350ml water

Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and yeast.
2. Gradually add water while mixing until a sticky dough forms.
3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
5. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
6. Shape the dough into a baguette shape and place it on a baking sheet.
7. Make diagonal slashes on the surface of the dough using a sharp knife.
8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
9. Let it cool on a wire rack before serving.

2. Sourdough Bread
Ingredients:
- 400g bread flour
- 100g whole wheat flour
- 10g salt
- 200g active sourdough starter
- 300ml water

Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, whole wheat flour, and salt.
2. Add the sourdough starter and water, and mix until a shaggy dough forms.
3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 4-6 hours, or until doubled in size.
5. Preheat the oven to 230°C (450°F) with a Dutch oven inside.
6. Carefully transfer the dough into the preheated Dutch oven, cover with the lid, and bake for 30 minutes.
7. Remove the lid and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown.
8. Let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.

3. Cinnamon Raisin Bread
Ingredients:
- 500g bread flour
- 10g salt
- 7g instant yeast
- 50g sugar
- 300ml warm milk
- 50g unsalted butter, melted
- 150g raisins
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, salt, yeast, and sugar.
2. Add the warm milk and melted butter, and mix until a sticky dough forms.
3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
5. Punch down the dough and knead in the raisins and ground cinnamon.
6. Shape the dough into a loaf and place it in a greased loaf pan.
7. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
8. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
9. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
10. Let it cool on a wire rack before slicing.
