Classic Cocktails: Recipes for Happy Hour


Are you tired of the same old drinks at happy hour? It’s time to elevate your cocktail game with some classic recipes that never go out of style. Whether you’re hosting a party or just unwinding after a long day, these timeless concoctions are sure to impress. So, grab your shaker and let’s dive into the world of classic cocktails!

1. The Old Fashioned
Let’s start with a true classic – the Old Fashioned. This cocktail dates back to the 19th century and has stood the test of time for good reason. To make this sophisticated drink, muddle a sugar cube with a few dashes of Angostura bitters in a glass. Add a splash of water, a large ice cube, and 2 ounces of bourbon. Stir gently and garnish with an orange twist. The result? A smooth, aromatic drink that exudes elegance.

2. The Martini
Shaken or stirred? That’s the eternal question when it comes to the Martini. This iconic cocktail has been a symbol of sophistication for decades. For a classic Martini, combine 2 1/2 ounces of gin and 1/2 ounce of dry vermouth in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or olives, and voilà – you have a timeless, refined cocktail in hand.

3. The Margarita
Transport yourself to a sunny beach with a refreshing Margarita in hand. This beloved cocktail is a perfect blend of sweet, sour, and salty flavors. To make a classic Margarita, combine 2 ounces of tequila, 1 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and 3/4 ounce of agave nectar in a shaker with ice. Shake well and strain into a salt-rimmed glass. Garnish with a lime wheel, and you’re ready to savor the taste of summer anytime.

4. The Mojito
Embrace the vibrant flavors of the Caribbean with a zesty Mojito. This Cuban cocktail is a delightful mix of mint, lime, and rum, making it a favorite for those who crave a refreshing drink. To concoct a traditional Mojito, muddle fresh mint leaves and 1 ounce of simple syrup in a glass. Add 1 1/2 ounces of white rum, 3/4 ounce of freshly squeezed lime juice, and ice. Top it off with club soda, stir gently, and garnish with a mint sprig. With each sip, you’ll feel the tropical breeze sweep you away.

5. The Manhattan
For those who appreciate the allure of whiskey, the Manhattan is a must-try. This bold and complex cocktail is a blend of rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters, creating a drink that exudes sophistication. To make a classic Manhattan, combine 2 ounces of rye whiskey, 1 ounce of sweet vermouth, and a dash of Angostura bitters in a mixing glass with ice. Stir well and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a cherry, and enjoy the rich, timeless flavors.

These classic cocktails are more than just drinks – they’re a celebration of tradition and craftsmanship. By mastering these timeless recipes, you’ll be able to impress your guests and elevate any gathering. So, the next time you’re craving a sophisticated libation, look no further than these iconic concoctions. Cheers to the art of mixology and the joy of happy hour!
