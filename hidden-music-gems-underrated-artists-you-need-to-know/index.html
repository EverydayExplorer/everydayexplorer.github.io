Hidden Music Gems: Underrated Artists You Need to Know


In today’s music industry, it’s easy to get caught up in the mainstream hits that dominate the airwaves and streaming platforms. While these popular artists may be talented and have achieved great success, there is a whole world of talented musicians and bands that often go unnoticed. These hidden music gems, these underrated artists, deserve your attention and recognition.

Discovering underrated artists and delving into their music can be an incredibly rewarding experience. Not only does it allow you to expand your musical horizons and explore different genres, but it also provides an opportunity to support independent artists who are truly passionate about their craft.

So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of hidden music gems and uncover some of the most underrated artists that you need to know.

1. LEON - LEON is a Swedish singer-songwriter whose soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics are bound to captivate you. Her music blends elements of pop, folk, and soul, creating a unique and mesmerizing sound. With songs like ‘Tired of Talking’ and ‘Liar,’ LEON has proven to be a rising star in the music industry.

2. Raveena - Raveena is an R&B artist known for her soothing vocals and introspective lyrics. Her music is a blend of soul, jazz, and pop, creating a dreamy and ethereal atmosphere. Songs like ‘Honey’ and ‘If Only’ showcase Raveena’s talent for creating beautiful melodies that will transport you to another world.

3. Khruangbin - Khruangbin is a three-piece band from Texas that specializes in instrumental music. Their unique sound draws inspiration from various genres, including psychedelic rock, funk, and Thai funk. With their groovy basslines, funky guitar riffs, and tight drums, Khruangbin offers a refreshing and captivating listening experience.

4. Dermot Kennedy - Hailing from Ireland, Dermot Kennedy is a singer-songwriter who has gained a dedicated following with his heartfelt and emotionally charged music. His powerful voice combined with his introspective lyrics creates a raw and authentic sound. Tracks like ‘Outnumbered’ and ‘Power Over Me’ demonstrate his ability to convey deep emotions through his music.

5. Lianne La Havas - Lianne La Havas is a British singer-songwriter known for her soulful voice and intricate guitar playing. Her music is a fusion of soul, folk, and R&B, creating a truly unique and captivating sound. With songs like ‘Unstoppable’ and ‘Bittersweet,’ Lianne La Havas has proven herself to be a talented artist deserving of recognition.

6. Vulfpeck - Vulfpeck is a funk band hailing from Michigan, known for their catchy hooks, tight rhythms, and infectious energy. Their music is a throwback to the golden era of funk, with influences from artists like Stevie Wonder and James Brown. Tracks like ‘Dean Town’ and ‘Back Pocket’ will have you grooving and dancing in no time.

7. Yuna - Yuna is a Malaysian singer-songwriter with a dreamy and enchanting sound. Her music is a blend of alternative pop and R&B, infused with elements of folk and electronic music. With her angelic vocals and introspective lyrics, Yuna’s music is both soothing and captivating.

8. Daniel Caesar - Daniel Caesar is a Canadian R&B artist who has garnered critical acclaim for his soulful voice and introspective lyrics. His music often explores themes of love, faith, and personal growth, creating a deeply emotional listening experience. Tracks like ‘Get You’ and ‘Best Part’ showcase Caesar’s incredible vocal range and songwriting abilities.

9. Big Thief - Big Thief is an indie folk-rock band known for their raw, heartfelt lyrics and unique storytelling. Their music is intimate and vulnerable, with poignant melodies and intricate guitar work. Songs like ‘Paul’ and ‘Not’ showcase Big Thief’s ability to create deeply emotional and introspective music.

10. The Japanese House - The Japanese House, the moniker of British artist Amber Bain, creates dreamy and atmospheric music characterized by ethereal vocals and lush production. Her music blends elements of indie pop, electronica, and dream pop, creating a mesmerizing listening experience. Tracks like ‘Still’ and ‘Lilo’ will transport you to a world of sonic beauty.

These artists represent just a fraction of the hidden music gems waiting to be discovered. By exploring the music of underrated artists, not only are you supporting independent musicians, but you are also opening yourself up to a world of creativity and unique perspectives.

So, take a break from the mainstream hits and dive into the world of hidden music gems. You never know, you might just discover your new favorite artist.
