The 5 Best Hunting Knives You Need in Your Collection


Hunting is a skill that has been practiced for centuries. Whether you are a professional hunter or an outdoor enthusiast, having the right tools is essential. One of the most important tools a hunter can have is a reliable hunting knife.

A hunting knife is more than just a tool for cutting and skinning game. It is an extension of the hunter, a trustworthy companion in the wild. Therefore, it is crucial to invest in a high-quality hunting knife that is durable, versatile, and dependable. In this blog post, we will explore the five best hunting knives that should be in every hunter’s collection.

1. Benchmade Bushcrafter

When it comes to hunting knives, Benchmade is a brand that stands out for its exceptional craftsmanship. The Benchmade Bushcrafter is a top-notch choice for any hunting enthusiast. It features a full-tang, drop-point blade made from premium S30V stainless steel, ensuring superior edge retention and corrosion resistance. The handle is made from durable G10 with a diamond-like carbon coating, providing excellent grip and stability. This knife excels in versatility, making it suitable for various hunting tasks such as skinning, field dressing, and even campsite chores.

2. Buck 119 Special

The Buck 119 Special is a classic hunting knife that has stood the test of time. This iconic knife is known for its durability and reliability, making it a favorite among hunters for over 75 years. It features a 6-inch clip-point blade made from high-grade 420HC stainless steel, providing excellent sharpness and edge retention. The handle is made from phenolic, with an aluminum pommel and a guard for added safety. The Buck 119 Special is a trusted companion for any hunting expedition, offering precise cutting and ease of use.

3. Gerber StrongArm

If you are looking for a tactical-style hunting knife, the Gerber StrongArm is an excellent choice. This knife is designed with military-grade proficiency and is built to withstand extreme conditions. It features a 4.8-inch drop-point blade made from high-carbon stainless steel, ensuring excellent strength and edge retention. The rubberized diamond-texture handle provides a secure grip, even in wet or slippery conditions. The Gerber StrongArm also includes a striking pommel at the base of the handle, which can be used as a hammer or for self-defense. This versatile knife is perfect for hunting as well as other outdoor activities such as camping and survival situations.

4. ESEE 6P-B

When it comes to ruggedness and resilience, the ESEE 6P-B is hard to beat. This knife is designed to withstand heavy-duty use and harsh environments. It features a 6.5-inch drop-point blade made from high-carbon 1095 steel, known for its exceptional toughness and edge retention. The full-tang construction adds to its durability, allowing for heavy chopping and prying tasks. The handle is made from Micarta, offering excellent grip even in wet conditions. The ESEE 6P-B is a reliable and robust hunting knife that will endure the toughest of hunting adventures.

5. Havalon Piranta Bolt

For hunters who value precision and ease of use, the Havalon Piranta Bolt is a game-changer. This unique hunting knife features a replaceable blade system, allowing for quick and easy blade swaps on the go. It comes with 12 additional stainless steel #60A blades, each with a surgical-grade sharpness. The handle is made from durable ABS plastic, with an ergonomic design for comfortable and secure handling. With its lightweight and compact size, the Havalon Piranta Bolt is perfect for hunters who need a reliable and sharp knife for field dressing and skinning game.

In conclusion, a hunting knife is an essential tool for any hunter. The five hunting knives mentioned above - the Benchmade Bushcrafter, Buck 119 Special, Gerber StrongArm, ESEE 6P-B, and Havalon Piranta Bolt - are all excellent choices that offer durability, versatility, and reliability. Whether you are a seasoned hunter or just starting your hunting journey, investing in a high-quality hunting knife is a decision you won’t regret. So gear up and make sure you have one of these top hunting knives in your collection before your next hunting expedition. Happy hunting!
