Chase the Sun: Hotels with Spectacular Sunset Views


Imagine a luxurious getaway, where the golden rays of the sun paint the sky in breathtaking hues, casting a mesmerizing glow over the horizon. Picture yourself sipping a refreshing cocktail, lounging on a plush, comfortable terrace, and witnessing nature’s most dazzling spectacle. If this idyllic scenario piques your interest, then you’re in for a treat! Welcome to a captivating journey through some of the world’s most extraordinary hotels that boast spectacular sunset views, where you can chase the sun and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Our first stop takes us to the picturesque island of Santorini, Greece. Perched high above the Aegean Sea, the enchanting Canaves Oia Hotel offers an unrivaled vantage point to witness the sun’s descent into the endless blue waters. Immerse yourself in the island’s charm, as you unwind in your private infinity pool, savoring the harmonious blend of vibrant colors and tranquility.

Next, we venture to the captivating city of Dubai, where the Burj Al Arab Jumeirah stands tall as an architectural marvel. Prepare to be awestruck as you indulge in the hotel’s Terrace Bar, situated on the 27th floor. From this elevated oasis, you can relish the panoramic views of the sun setting over the sparkling city skyline, creating a truly magical ambiance.

No journey would be complete without a visit to the beautiful Hawaiian island of Maui. Nestled along the pristine Wailea Beach, the Andaz Maui at Wailea Resort offers a front-row seat to nature’s grand performance. Settle into a comfortable lounger on the hotel’s expansive oceanfront lawn and marvel at the kaleidoscope of colors painting the sky as the sun gracefully bids the day adieu.

In the heart of Africa, within the Serengeti National Park, lies a hidden gem known as the Singita Sasakwa Lodge. Here, guests can indulge in the ultimate African safari experience while also enjoying jaw-dropping sunset vistas. Imagine sipping a glass of champagne as you witness the sun casting its golden glow over the vast savannah, creating a scene straight out of a nature documentary.

If you’re searching for a tropical paradise with unbeatable sunset views, look no further than the Maldives. Situated on its own private island, the luxurious Gili Lankanfushi offers the perfect setting to witness the sun’s descent into the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean. Unwind on your own private villa’s sundeck, as you watch the sky transform into a masterpiece of fiery reds, vivid oranges, and gentle purples.

As evening approaches, our journey takes us to the bustling metropolis of New York City. The Refinery Hotel’s Rooftop Lounge is an urban oasis that offers an extraordinary perspective of the iconic Manhattan skyline. Sip on a handcrafted cocktail while soaking in the breathtaking views of the city as the sun sets behind the towering skyscrapers, casting a warm glow over the bustling streets below.

Our final destination transports us to the serene coastal town of Positano, Italy. The iconic Le Sirenuse Hotel perches on a cliffside, overlooking the azure waters of the Amalfi Coast. Be captivated by the striking views as the sun sinks below the horizon, casting a golden light on the pastel-colored buildings that cascade down the hillside, creating a scene straight out of a romance novel.

Whether you seek a beachfront retreat, an urban escape, or an exotic adventure, these hotels promise unforgettable sunset views that will leave you awe-inspired. So, pack your bags and embark on a journey to chase the sun, for in the embrace of nature’s most captivating spectacle, you’ll find tranquility, beauty, and a sense of wonder that will stay with you long after the sun has bid its final farewell.
