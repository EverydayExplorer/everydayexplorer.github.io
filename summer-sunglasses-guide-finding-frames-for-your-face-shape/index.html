Summer Sunglasses Guide: Finding Frames for Your Face Shape


Summer is synonymous with sunshine, outdoor activities, and, of course, stylish sunglasses. Not only do sunglasses protect our eyes from harmful UV rays, but they also add a touch of flair to any summer ensemble. However, finding the perfect pair of sunglasses can be a daunting task, especially with so many shapes, styles, and colors to choose from. To help you narrow down your options and find frames that flatter your face shape, we’ve put together this summer sunglasses guide.

Determining Your Face Shape

Before diving into sunglasses styles, it’s essential to determine your face shape. There are generally six face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To identify your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Pay attention to the widest points of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to determine which shape best describes your facial proportions.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape characterized by soft, curved lines and equal width and length, opt for sunglasses with angular frames to add definition and balance. Square or rectangular frames with sharp edges help elongate the face and create the illusion of more angular features. Avoid round or oversized frames, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Oval Face Shape

Lucky you! Oval face shapes are versatile and can pull off almost any sunglasses style. With balanced proportions and gently rounded features, oval faces are well-suited to a wide range of frame shapes, from classic aviators to trendy cat-eye styles. Experiment with different styles and have fun accessorizing with bold colors and patterns.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape characterized by strong, angular features and a defined jawline, look for sunglasses with softer, rounded edges to complement your facial structure. Oval or round frames help soften the angles of your face and add a touch of femininity. Steer clear of square or geometric frames, as they can emphasize the angularity of your features.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. To balance out your facial proportions, opt for sunglasses with bottom-heavy frames that draw attention downward and away from the forehead. Cat-eye or butterfly frames are particularly flattering for heart-shaped faces, as they accentuate the cheekbones and add width to the lower half of the face.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond-shaped faces are characterized by narrow foreheads and jawlines with wider cheekbones. To complement your unique facial structure, choose sunglasses with distinctive, angular frames that highlight your cheekbones and draw attention to your eyes. Aviator or geometric frames with exaggerated shapes are perfect for adding drama and flair to your summer look.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape characterized by long, narrow proportions, opt for sunglasses that add width and volume to your face. Oversized frames with thick rims or embellishments help create the illusion of a wider face and balance out your facial proportions. Round or square frames with deep lenses also work well for oblong faces, adding contrast to your elongated features.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect pair of sunglasses is all about understanding your face shape and choosing frames that complement your unique features. Whether you have a round, oval, square, heart, diamond, or oblong face shape, there’s a perfect pair of sunglasses out there for you. Experiment with different styles, shapes, and colors to find the frames that make you look and feel your best this summer. And remember, sunglasses aren’t just a fashion statement—they’re a vital accessory for protecting your eyes from the sun’s harmful rays. So before you head out into the summer sunshine, be sure to find the perfect pair of shades to keep your eyes safe and stylish all season long.
