Hunting Backpacks: Features and Recommendations


Are you an avid hunter on the lookout for the perfect backpack to carry all your hunting gear? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be discussing the various features to consider when purchasing a hunting backpack, as well as provide you with some top recommendations in the market. So, read on to find out which hunting backpack suits your needs!

Key Features to Consider

1. Size and Capacity

One of the essential factors to consider when choosing a hunting backpack is the size and capacity. It’s important to select a backpack that is spacious enough to accommodate all your hunting essentials, including your rifle or bow, clothing, first aid kit, snacks, water bladder, and other accessories. Look for backpacks with multiple compartments and external attachments to help you organize your gear effectively. Additionally, consider the type of hunting you partake in and choose a backpack accordingly. For instance, if you plan on backpack hunting, opt for a larger pack with a higher capacity.

2. Comfort and Fit

Comfort and fit play a significant role when it comes to choosing a hunting backpack. You’re likely to be carrying your pack for extended periods, so it’s essential to find one that distributes weight evenly and has padded shoulder straps and a supportive waist belt. Look for adjustable straps and suspension systems that can be tailored to your body shape for enhanced comfort. Some backpacks even offer frame options, such as internal frames or external frames, depending on your preferences.

3. Durability and Weather Resistance

Hunting often takes you through challenging terrains and unpredictable weather conditions. Therefore, it’s crucial to choose a backpack that is durable and weather-resistant. Look for packs constructed with high-quality materials such as nylon or polyester that can withstand wear and tear. Additionally, consider backpacks with water-resistant or waterproof coatings to keep your gear dry during rain or snow. Reinforced stitching and sturdy zippers are also indicators of a durable backpack.

4. Accessibility and Organization

Quick and easy access to your gear is essential while in the field. Look for backpacks with multiple pockets, compartments, and attachment points for efficient organization and easy retrieval of your equipment. Some backpacks also offer specialized storage compartments for items like a spotting scope, rangefinder, or hydration bladder. Additionally, consider backpacks with a dedicated weapon-carrying system if you need to transport your rifle or bow safely.

5. Camouflage and Concealment

Hunting often requires an element of stealth and camouflage. Look for backpacks that come in various camouflage patterns to help you blend into your surroundings. Additionally, consider backpacks with multiple attachment points for adding additional camouflage elements such as foliage or branches. This will help you maintain your concealment and increase your chances of a successful hunt.

Top Hunting Backpack Recommendations

Now that we’ve discussed the essential features to consider let’s dive into some top hunting backpack recommendations that have been highly rated by hunters worldwide:

  1. Badlands 2200 Hunting Pack: This pack is known for its durability, comfort, and excellent organizational features. It offers a spacious main compartment, multiple pockets, and a rifle or bow-carrying system. The Badlands 2200 also comes with a lifetime warranty, giving you peace of mind.

  2. Eberlestock X2 Hunting Pack: The Eberlestock X2 is a compact, lightweight pack perfect for day hunts. It has a unique rifle or bow-carrying system and offers excellent maneuverability in tight spaces. The pack also features a hydration bladder sleeve and multiple small pockets for easy accessibility.

  3. ALPS OutdoorZ Commander Frame Pack: For those who prefer external frame backpacks, the ALPS OutdoorZ Commander is an excellent choice. It offers a high load-carrying capacity, multiple compartments, and a detachable day pack. The exterior lashing straps are handy for attaching extra gear or meat hauls.

  4. Tenzing 2220 Hunting Day Pack: The Tenzing 2220 is great for short day hunts. It provides ample storage space, multiple pockets, and a bow-carrying boot. The pack is comfortable, lightweight, and durable, making it a popular choice among hunters.

  5. Mystery Ranch Metcalf Hunting Pack: If you’re looking for a backpack with a higher load-carrying capacity, the Mystery Ranch Metcalf is worth considering. This pack features an innovative design, excellent suspension system, and is exceptionally comfortable even with heavy loads. The pack also offers easy access to the main compartment.

Remember that choosing the right hunting backpack ultimately depends on your personal preferences, hunting style, and individual needs. Consider the features discussed in this blog post, read reviews, and try out different packs before making your final decision. Happy hunting!
