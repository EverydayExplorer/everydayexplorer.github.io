10 Easy and Creative Crafts Using Mason Jars


Crafting has always been a beloved activity for many people around the world. It allows us to express our creativity and create unique and personalized items for our homes. One particular item that has become incredibly popular in the crafting world is the mason jar. Mason jars were originally used for canning and preserving food, but they have since evolved into a versatile and trendy crafting material. In this blog post, we will explore 10 easy and creative crafts using mason jars that you can try yourself.

1. Mason Jar Lanterns: One of the simplest and most beautiful crafts you can make using mason jars is lanterns. Simply place a tea light candle or LED candle inside the jar and use decorative elements such as ribbons, lace, or twine to enhance the overall look. These lanterns are perfect for outdoor gatherings and can add a cozy and romantic atmosphere to any space.

2. Mason Jar Herb Garden: If you love cooking and want to have fresh herbs at your fingertips, a mason jar herb garden is a perfect DIY project. Fill a mason jar with soil, plant some herb seeds, and watch them grow. You can paint or decorate the jar for an added touch of personalization. Place your mason jar herb garden on a windowsill or countertop and enjoy cooking with aromatic and flavorful herbs right from your kitchen.

3. Mason Jar Soap Dispenser: Give your bathroom or kitchen sink a rustic and stylish touch with a mason jar soap dispenser. Simply remove the pump from a store-bought soap dispenser and replace it with the lid and pump from a mason jar. Fill the jar with your favorite liquid soap and screw the lid back on. Not only is this a practical and eco-friendly option, but it also adds a charming farmhouse look to your space.

4. Mason Jar Vases: Looking for a unique way to display your flowers? Mason jar vases are the answer. Paint your mason jars with your favorite colors or wrap them with decorative fabric or twine for a more rustic look. These vases can be used for fresh or dried flowers and make for beautiful centerpieces or home decor accents.

5. Mason Jar Terrarium: Terrariums are miniature gardens enclosed in glass, and mason jars are perfect for creating these magical landscapes. Layer soil, rocks, sand, and small plants inside the jar to create a self-sustaining ecosystem. These garden-in-a-jar creations are not only easy to make but also make for stunning displays that bring nature indoors.

6. Mason Jar Snow Globes: Create your very own winter wonderland with mason jar snow globes. Fill the jar with water, add a dash of glycerin or baby oil, and then sprinkle in glitter and small figurines. Securely attach the lid and tip the jar upside down to create a beautiful snowfall effect. These homemade snow globes make great decorations or thoughtful gifts during the holiday season.

7. Mason Jar Chandelier: For those looking to add a touch of rustic elegance to their home, a mason jar chandelier is the perfect DIY project. Collect a few mason jars, attach them to a wooden board or metal frame, and hang the chandelier from your ceiling. Place candles or battery-operated LED lights inside the jars for a warm and inviting glow.

8. Mason Jar Organizer: Keep your office or craft supplies organized with a mason jar organizer. Attach mason jars to a wooden board using hose clamps or metal brackets, and fill them with items such as pens, pencils, scissors, or paintbrushes. This practical storage solution not only helps declutter your space but also adds a touch of charm to your work area.

9. Mason Jar Bird Feeder: Turn your mason jar into a bird feeder and attract your feathered friends to your backyard. Simply attach a bird feeder base to the lid of the mason jar, fill it with birdseed, and hang it from a tree or hook. Not only will this provide nourishment for birds, but it also gives you the opportunity to observe and appreciate the beauty of nature from your own home.

10. Mason Jar Candle Holders: Lastly, you can create stunning candle holders using mason jars. Paint the inside of the jars with translucent glass paint or add colorful tissue paper to the outside to create a stained glass effect when a candle is lit inside. These candle holders add a warm and cozy ambiance to any room and can be customized to match your home decor.

Crafting with mason jars offers endless possibilities for creativity and personalization. These 10 easy and creative crafts using mason jars are just the beginning of what you can create. Whether you’re a seasoned crafter or a beginner looking for a fun and fulfilling project, these crafts will inspire you to transform simple mason jars into beautiful and functional works of art. So gather your supplies, unleash your creativity, and let the magic of mason jars bring joy and inspiration into your crafting journey.
