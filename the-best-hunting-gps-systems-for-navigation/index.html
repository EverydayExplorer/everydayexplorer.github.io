The Best Hunting GPS Systems for Navigation


When it comes to hunting, having a reliable navigation system is crucial. Hunting GPS systems have become increasingly popular among modern-day hunters, providing them with accurate and detailed maps, as well as a range of other features to enhance their hunting experience. In this blog post, we will take a closer look at some of the best hunting GPS systems available on the market today. Whether you are a seasoned hunter or a beginner, these GPS systems will prove to be invaluable tools for your next hunting expedition.

The Importance of Hunting GPS Systems

Gone are the days of relying solely on compasses and topographic maps. Hunting GPS systems have revolutionized the way hunters navigate through the wilderness. These devices combine the power of GPS technology with advanced mapping capabilities, ensuring that hunters can accurately track their location and explore new hunting grounds with ease.

One of the key benefits of using a hunting GPS system is the ability to mark waypoints. By marking various waypoints throughout the hunting area, hunters can easily navigate back to specific locations, such as tree stands or game trails. This not only saves time but also increases the chances of success during hunting trips.

Additionally, hunting GPS systems provide hunters with detailed topographic and satellite maps, allowing them to identify key terrain features, water sources, and potential game habitats. This information is invaluable when planning hunting strategies and determining the best routes to take.

Top Hunting GPS Systems

  1. Garmin GPSMAP 66i

The Garmin GPSMAP 66i is an all-in-one GPS system that combines satellite communication and mapping capabilities. It features a large color display with easy-to-use controls, making it a favorite among hunters. The device offers preloaded topographic maps, as well as the ability to download additional maps, including BirdsEye Satellite Imagery.

The GPSMAP 66i also includes inReach technology, which allows users to send and receive messages, track their location, and initiate an SOS signal in case of emergencies. This feature is especially useful for hunters venturing into remote areas with no cellular coverage.

  1. OnX Hunt

OnX Hunt is a popular hunting GPS system that provides hunters with access to detailed property boundaries, landowner information, and public land data. The system can be used on a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and GPS units, making it incredibly versatile.

With the OnX Hunt app, hunters can view private and public land boundaries, track their movements, and mark waypoints. The app also allows users to download maps for offline use, ensuring that hunters can navigate even in areas with no cellular coverage.

  1. Magellan eXplorist 350H

Designed specifically for hunters, the Magellan eXplorist 350H offers advanced GPS navigation features in a rugged and durable package. The device comes preloaded with detailed topographic maps, as well as hunting-specific data, such as game management units and hunting regulations.

The eXplorist 350H also includes features like geocaching and paperless geocaching, allowing hunters to easily find hidden treasures in the wilderness. The device is waterproof and impact-resistant, making it ideal for hunters who often encounter rough terrain and adverse weather conditions.

Conclusion

Navigating through the wilderness is an essential aspect of hunting, and having a reliable GPS system can greatly enhance your hunting experience. The Garmin GPSMAP 66i, OnX Hunt, and Magellan eXplorist 350H are among the best hunting GPS systems available on the market today. These devices offer advanced mapping capabilities, rugged durability, and additional features that make them indispensable tools for hunters of all skill levels.

When choosing a hunting GPS system, consider your specific needs and preferences. Look for devices that offer accurate mapping, waypoint marking capabilities, and features that are tailored to hunting activities. With a high-quality hunting GPS system in hand, you can navigate confidently, explore new hunting grounds, and increase your chances of success in the field.
