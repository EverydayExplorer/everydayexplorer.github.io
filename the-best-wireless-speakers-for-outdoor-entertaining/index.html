The Best Wireless Speakers for Outdoor Entertaining


When it comes to outdoor entertaining, having the right speakers can make all the difference. Whether you’re hosting a backyard barbecue, a pool party, or simply enjoying a relaxing evening on your patio, wireless speakers can provide the perfect soundtrack to accompany your festivities. But with so many options available, how do you choose the best wireless speakers for outdoor entertaining? In this blog post, we’ll explore some top-rated options that not only offer exceptional sound quality but also durability and convenience.

  1. Bose SoundLink Revolve+: Known for their superior audio performance, Bose speakers are a popular choice among audiophiles. The SoundLink Revolve+ is no exception. This wireless speaker delivers powerful 360-degree sound that fills any outdoor space. With a durable aluminum body and an IPX4 water-resistant rating, it can withstand splashes and light rain. The rechargeable battery provides up to 16 hours of playtime, allowing you to keep the party going all night long.

  2. JBL Charge 4: JBL is another reputable brand known for producing high-quality speakers. The Charge 4 is a versatile wireless speaker that boasts powerful bass and crystal-clear sound. With an IPX7 waterproof rating, it can handle more than just a few splashes – it can be fully submerged in water without any damage. The built-in rechargeable battery offers up to 20 hours of playtime and can even charge your devices through the USB port.

  3. UE Megaboom 3: Ultimate Ears has made a name for themselves in the portable speaker market, and the Megaboom 3 is a fantastic choice for outdoor entertaining. This cylindrical speaker not only offers 360-degree sound but also features a rugged design that can withstand drops and bumps. With an IP67 rating, it is both water and dustproof. The Megaboom 3 has a 20-hour battery life and can be paired with other UE speakers for a more immersive listening experience.

  4. Sonos Move: If you’re looking for a wireless speaker that can seamlessly transition between indoor and outdoor use, the Sonos Move is worth considering. This portable speaker delivers rich sound with deep bass and clear highs. With an IP56 rating, it can handle unpredictable weather conditions and is resistant to dust and moisture. The Move includes both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity options, allowing you to stream music from your favorite services or directly from your device.

  5. Anker Soundcore Flare: For those on a tighter budget, the Anker Soundcore Flare offers excellent value for your money. This cylindrical speaker features 360-degree sound and customizable LED lights that add ambiance to your outdoor space. With an IP67 rating, it is resistant to water and dust, making it ideal for poolside parties. The built-in rechargeable battery provides up to 12 hours of playtime, and you can even pair multiple Flare speakers for a more immersive sound experience.

When searching for the best wireless speakers for outdoor entertaining, it’s important to consider factors such as sound quality, durability, and battery life. The speakers mentioned in this blog post are all excellent options that can enhance your outdoor gatherings. Whether you opt for the premium sound of Bose and JBL, the ruggedness of UE and Sonos, or the affordability of Anker, there’s a wireless speaker that suits your needs and budget. So go ahead and take your outdoor entertaining to the next level with the perfect wireless speakers!
