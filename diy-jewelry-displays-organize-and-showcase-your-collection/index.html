DIY Jewelry Displays: Organize and Showcase Your Collection


Are you tired of your jewelry being tangled up or hidden away in drawers? It’s time to showcase your collection with some DIY jewelry displays that will not only organize your pieces but also add a touch of creativity to your space. In this blog post, we’ll explore a variety of DIY jewelry display ideas that are easy to make and will beautifully showcase your favorite pieces.

  1. Frame Jewelry Display One of the easiest and most stylish ways to showcase your jewelry is by creating a frame jewelry display. Simply take an old picture frame, remove the glass, and replace it with a piece of wire mesh or a decorative fabric. You can then hang your earrings, necklaces, and bracelets on the mesh or use pins to secure them in place. This not only keeps your jewelry organized but also creates a beautiful wall display.

  2. Branch Jewelry Stand If you’re looking for a more natural and rustic display, consider creating a branch jewelry stand. Find a sturdy branch with multiple offshoots and secure it in a decorative base such as a small pot or a piece of driftwood. You can then hang your necklaces and bracelets from the offshoots, creating a unique and organic display for your jewelry.

  3. Vintage Saucer Jewelry Holder For a touch of vintage charm, repurpose old saucers and teacups into a jewelry holder. Simply glue a saucer onto a teacup to create a pedestal, and then use the saucer to hold rings and earrings, while the teacup can hold bracelets and necklaces. This not only keeps your jewelry organized but also adds a whimsical touch to your dresser or vanity.

  4. Shadow Box Display If you have a collection of statement necklaces or bold earrings, a shadow box display is the perfect way to showcase them. Find a shallow shadow box or create your own using a wooden frame and a piece of glass or clear plastic. Line the back of the box with decorative paper or fabric and then use pins or hooks to display your chunky necklaces and earrings. This not only keeps your jewelry organized but also turns them into a work of art.

  5. Pegboard Jewelry Organizer For those with a larger jewelry collection, a pegboard jewelry organizer can be a game-changer. Install a pegboard on your wall and add hooks, pegs, and small shelves to hold your jewelry. You can customize the layout to accommodate different types of jewelry, from earrings and necklaces to rings and bracelets. This not only keeps your jewelry organized but also allows for easy customization and reorganization as your collection grows.

  6. Magnetic Makeup Board If you’re short on space, consider creating a magnetic makeup board that doubles as a jewelry display. Simply attach a sheet of metal to a decorative frame, and then use small magnets to hang your earrings and necklaces. This not only keeps your jewelry organized but also adds a stylish and space-saving touch to your room.

In conclusion, showcasing your jewelry collection doesn’t have to be a daunting task. With these DIY jewelry display ideas, you can organize and showcase your pieces in a way that is both practical and visually appealing. Whether you prefer a rustic branch display or a stylish frame display, there’s a DIY jewelry display idea for everyone. So, roll up your sleeves, get creative, and start showcasing your jewelry collection in style!
