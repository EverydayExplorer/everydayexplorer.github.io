Delicious and Easy Instant Pot Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Do you want to enjoy delicious and nutritious meals without all the hassle? Well, look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing some mouthwatering instant pot recipes that are not only easy to make but also perfect for those busy weeknights.

1. Instant Pot Chicken and Rice:
Ingredients:
- 2 cups of rice
- 1 pound of chicken breasts
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 cup of chicken broth
- 1 teaspoon of paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
- Add the rice, chicken breasts, onion, garlic, chicken broth, paprika, salt, and pepper to the instant pot.
- Close the lid and set the instant pot to manual mode for 8 minutes.
- Once the time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
- Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.

2. Instant Pot Beef Stew:
Ingredients:
- 1.5 pounds of beef stew meat, cubed
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 potatoes, diced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 cups of beef broth
- 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon of thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
- Add the beef stew meat, carrots, potatoes, onion, garlic, beef broth, tomato paste, thyme, salt, and pepper to the instant pot.
- Close the lid and set the instant pot to manual mode for 30 minutes.
- Once the time is up, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
- Give it a good stir and serve hot.

3. Instant Pot Spaghetti Bolognese:
Ingredients:
- 1 pound of ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 can of diced tomatoes
- 1 can of tomato sauce
- 1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 8 ounces of spaghetti noodles, broken in half

Instructions:
- Set the instant pot to sauté mode and cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is browned.
- Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the instant pot.
- Break the spaghetti noodles in half and layer them on top of the sauce.
- Close the lid and set the instant pot to manual mode for 8 minutes.
- Once the time is up, do a quick release of the pressure and give it a good stir before serving.

4. Instant Pot Lemon Garlic Chicken:
Ingredients:
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
- Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
- Set the instant pot to sauté mode and heat the olive oil.
- Sear the chicken breasts on both sides until golden brown.
- Remove the chicken from the instant pot and set aside.
- Add the lemon juice, lemon zest, and minced garlic to the instant pot.
- Place the chicken breasts back into the instant pot.
- Close the lid and set the instant pot to manual mode for 10 minutes.
- Once the time is up, do a quick release of the pressure and serve hot.

5. Instant Pot Vegetable Curry:
Ingredients:
- 2 cups of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 can of coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons of curry powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
- Set the instant pot to sauté mode and cook the onion and garlic until they are translucent.
- Add the mixed vegetables, coconut milk, curry powder, salt, and pepper to the instant pot.
- Close the lid and set the instant pot to manual mode for 5 minutes.
- Once the time is up, do a quick release of the pressure and serve hot with rice or naan bread.

These delicious and easy instant pot recipes are sure to become your go-to meals for those busy weeknights. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, you can enjoy a homemade meal that will leave you feeling satisfied and ready to take on the world. So, dust off your instant pot and get cooking!
