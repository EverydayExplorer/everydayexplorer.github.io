The Best Hotels for Outdoor Dining: Alfresco Delights


As the warm breeze gently caresses your face and the sun sets in the distance, there’s something undeniably magical about dining outdoors. Whether you’re on a romantic date or enjoying a meal with friends, alfresco dining adds an extra touch of charm and ambiance to the experience. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best hotels around the world that offer exceptional outdoor dining experiences, where you can indulge in delicious cuisine while surrounded by breathtaking views.

1. Amangiri - Canyon Point, Utah, USA:
Nestled in the heart of the dramatic Utah desert, Amangiri is a luxury hotel that offers a unique outdoor dining experience. Imagine dining on a terrace overlooking the rugged landscape, as the colors of the sunset paint the sky. The hotel’s restaurant showcases a menu inspired by the local flavors, with dishes crafted from seasonal ingredients sourced from nearby farms.

2. Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc - Antibes, France:
Situated on the French Riviera, Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc boasts an idyllic location overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. Dine on the hotel’s stunning terrace, surrounded by lush gardens and panoramic views of the coastline. The menu features exquisite French cuisine, with an emphasis on fresh seafood and locally sourced ingredients.

3. Giraffe Manor - Nairobi, Kenya:
For a truly unique dining experience, Giraffe Manor in Nairobi offers the opportunity to dine alongside graceful giraffes. This boutique hotel allows guests to enjoy breakfast on a terrace while these majestic creatures roam freely. It’s a once-in-a-lifetime experience that combines incredible wildlife encounters with delicious meals.

4. Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan - Ubud, Bali, Indonesia:
Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Ubud’s lush rainforest at the Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan. The resort’s riverside dining pavilion offers a serene setting, where you can savor authentic Balinese cuisine while listening to the sounds of the nearby river. The menu highlights traditional dishes made from locally sourced ingredients, ensuring a truly authentic dining experience.

5. The Grotto Bay Beach Resort - Hamilton Parish, Bermuda:
Overlooking the turquoise waters of Bermuda, The Grotto Bay Beach Resort offers a picturesque outdoor dining experience. Dine on the hotel’s cliffside terrace, surrounded by stunning ocean views and soothing breezes. The menu features a combination of international and local cuisine, with an emphasis on fresh seafood and tropical flavors.

6. Six Senses Yao Noi - Phang Nga, Thailand:
Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Thailand at Six Senses Yao Noi. This luxury resort offers several unique outdoor dining options, including private beachfront dinners and dining pods nestled among the treetops. Indulge in a culinary journey through Thai flavors, with dishes crafted from organic ingredients sourced from the resort’s own gardens.

7. Hotel La Mamounia - Marrakech, Morocco:
Transport yourself to the vibrant city of Marrakech at Hotel La Mamounia. The hotel’s outdoor terrace offers a stunning setting, with views of the historic gardens and the Atlas Mountains in the distance. The menu showcases a fusion of Moroccan and international cuisine, with dishes that celebrate the flavors and spices of the region.

These are just a few examples of the best hotels around the world that offer exceptional outdoor dining experiences. Whether you’re seeking breathtaking views, unique wildlife encounters, or a tranquil setting surrounded by nature, these hotels will leave you with unforgettable memories and a taste of culinary excellence. So, the next time you plan a trip, consider adding one of these alfresco dining destinations to your itinerary for a truly unforgettable experience.
