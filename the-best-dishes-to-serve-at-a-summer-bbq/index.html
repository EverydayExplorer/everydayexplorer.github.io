The Best Dishes to Serve at a Summer BBQ


Summer is the perfect time to gather friends and family for a backyard barbecue. The smell of sizzling meat on the grill, the laughter of loved ones, and the warm sun shining down - it’s a recipe for a memorable day. And of course, no summer BBQ is complete without delicious food. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best dishes to serve at a summer BBQ, guaranteed to satisfy everyone’s taste buds.

1. Grilled Burgers: Let’s start with a classic. Juicy, flame-grilled burgers are a must-have at any BBQ. Choose high-quality ground beef, season it with your favorite spices, and form patties. Grill them to perfection and serve with a variety of toppings like cheese, lettuce, onions, and pickles. Don’t forget the buns!

2. BBQ Ribs: Tender, fall-off-the-bone ribs are a BBQ favorite. Marinate your ribs overnight in a flavorful BBQ sauce, then slow-cook them on the grill until they’re tender and caramelized. The combination of smoky flavors and tangy sauce will leave your guests wanting more.

3. Grilled Chicken Skewers: Lighter fare doesn’t mean sacrificing flavor. Skewer marinated chicken chunks along with colorful vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and cherry tomatoes. Grill them until the chicken is cooked through and the veggies are slightly charred. The result? A healthy and delicious option that’s perfect for summer.

4. Corn on the Cob: Sweet and crunchy, corn on the cob is a BBQ side dish that everyone loves. Peel back the husks, but leave them attached, then remove the silk. Brush the corn with melted butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Grill the corn until it’s slightly charred and serve with extra butter on the side.

5. Watermelon Salad: Summer is all about refreshing flavors, and a watermelon salad hits the spot. Combine juicy watermelon chunks with feta cheese, fresh mint leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. This sweet and savory salad will be a hit at your BBQ.

6. Grilled Pineapple: Take your taste buds on a tropical journey with grilled pineapple. Cut a fresh pineapple into slices, brush them with a mixture of honey and lime juice, and grill until caramelized. Serve as a side or as a topping for burgers or grilled chicken.

7. Potato Salad: No BBQ is complete without a classic potato salad. Boil potatoes until tender, then mix them with mayo, mustard, chopped onions, celery, and hard-boiled eggs. Season with salt, pepper, and a dash of paprika for a flavorful side dish that pairs well with any BBQ main course.

8. S’mores: End the BBQ on a sweet note with a quintessential summer treat - s’mores. Set up a s’mores station with graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate bars. Let your guests roast their marshmallows over the grill and assemble their own gooey, chocolatey creations.

There you have it - a delicious lineup of dishes to serve at your summer BBQ. From juicy burgers to flavorful sides and sweet treats, these options are sure to satisfy even the pickiest eaters. So fire up the grill, gather your loved ones, and enjoy a memorable summer day filled with great food and even better company. Happy BBQing!
