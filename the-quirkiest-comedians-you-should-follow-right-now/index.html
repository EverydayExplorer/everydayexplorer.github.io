The Quirkiest Comedians You Should Follow Right Now


Are you tired of watching the same old comedy shows and looking for something fresh and exciting? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the quirkiest comedians that you should be following right now. These talented individuals will leave you in stitches with their unique style and unconventional humor. Get ready to laugh until your sides hurt!

1. Eddie Izzard
Known for his surreal and absurdist style, Eddie Izzard is a British comedian who can make even the most mundane topics hilarious. His quick wit and impeccable timing have made him a favorite among comedy enthusiasts. Whether he’s talking about history, religion, or everyday observations, Izzard’s performances are always a treat.

2. Maria Bamford
Maria Bamford is a master of voices and impressions. Her comedy often delves into the dark and personal aspects of her life, tackling subjects such as mental health and social norms. With her unique delivery and willingness to address taboo topics, Bamford’s stand-up is both thought-provoking and hysterical.

3. Tim Minchin
Tim Minchin is not your typical comedian. He is also an accomplished musician, combining comedy and music in his acts. With his wild hair and witty lyrics, Minchin brings a fresh and eccentric perspective to the stage. His satirical songs touch on everything from politics to religion, making him a favorite among fans of intellectual humor.

4. Kristen Schaal
Kristen Schaal’s quirky and off-beat humor has made her a standout in the comedy world. Whether she’s playing a ditsy character or delivering deadpan punchlines, Schaal always brings a unique energy to her performances. Her ability to find humor in the absurdities of everyday life sets her apart from traditional comedians.

5. Reggie Watts
Reggie Watts is a comedian, musician, and improvisational genius. Known for his unique blend of beatboxing, comedy, and surreal improvisation, Watts creates moments of pure hilarity on stage. His ability to seamlessly transition between topics and create absurd scenarios is simply mesmerizing.

6. Demetri Martin
Demetri Martin’s deadpan delivery and clever one-liners have made him a favorite among comedy fans. His intellectual humor often focuses on wordplay and absurd observations, allowing his audience to see the world in a new and hilarious way. Martin’s unique style has earned him critical acclaim and a dedicated following.

7. Tig Notaro
Tig Notaro is a master of storytelling and observational comedy. Her deadpan delivery and dry humor make her performances unforgettable. Notaro fearlessly discusses personal topics, including her battle with cancer and other life struggles, bringing a raw and vulnerable element to her comedy.

8. Eric Andre
Eric Andre’s unique brand of chaotic comedy has made him a cult favorite. His unapologetic and boundary-pushing humor pushes the limits of what is considered acceptable in comedy. With his bizarre sketches, unpredictable pranks, and fearless approach to performance, Andre is sure to keep his audience on their toes.

9. Bo Burnham
Bo Burnham is a multi-talented comedian, musician, and actor. He is known for his witty and introspective comedy that often touches on social issues. Burnham’s performances are a mix of comedic songs, spoken word, and observational humor that take the audience on a roller coaster of emotions.

10. Sarah Silverman
Last but not least, Sarah Silverman is a comedian who never shies away from controversial topics. Her unfiltered and fearless approach to comedy has made her a polarizing figure, but also a beloved one. Silverman’s ability to blend political commentary with personal anecdotes creates a unique and eye-opening comedic experience.

In conclusion, if you’re looking for some comedic brilliance that goes beyond traditional stand-up routines, these quirky comedians are a must-follow. Their unique styles, unconventional humor, and fearless approach to comedy will surely keep you entertained. So, sit back, relax, and get ready to laugh until your sides hurt with these amazing comedians.
