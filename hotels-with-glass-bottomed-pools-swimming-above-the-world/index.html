Hotels with Glass-Bottomed Pools: Swimming Above the World


Imagine diving into a pool and feeling like you’re floating above the world, with nothing but a transparent floor separating you from the breathtaking views below. Hotels with glass-bottomed pools offer guests a unique and exhilarating experience, allowing them to swim and relax while enjoying panoramic vistas that span from the city skyline to the natural wonders of the world. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the top hotels with glass-bottomed pools, where you can take a dip and feel like you’re swimming above the world.

  1. The Joule, Dallas Located in the heart of downtown Dallas, The Joule is a chic and contemporary hotel that boasts a stunning rooftop pool with a glass bottom that extends out over the street below. As you swim or lounge by the pool, you’ll have a bird’s-eye view of the bustling cityscape beneath you. The pool is especially mesmerizing at night when the city lights illuminate the glass, creating a magical atmosphere.

  2. Hotel Indigo, Hong Kong Island Perched high above the vibrant city of Hong Kong, Hotel Indigo offers guests a rooftop swimming experience like no other. The hotel’s glass-bottomed infinity pool provides swimmers with an unobstructed view of Victoria Harbour and the iconic Hong Kong skyline. Whether you’re doing laps or simply soaking in the sun, the pool offers a serene oasis amidst the urban jungle.

  3. The Cambrian, Switzerland Nestled in the Swiss Alps, The Cambrian provides guests with a picturesque setting for their glass-bottomed pool experience. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush valleys, the hotel’s indoor pool offers a tranquil retreat with panoramic views of the dramatic Swiss landscape. Immerse yourself in warm water and let your gaze wander across the breathtaking scenery below.

  4. Amangiri, Utah Situated in the heart of the Utah desert, Amangiri is a luxury resort that seamlessly blends with its natural surroundings. The hotel’s stunning desert-view pool features a glass bottom that allows swimmers to float above the rugged landscape. Admire the otherworldly rock formations and vast desert expanse as you cool off in the refreshing water.

  5. Grace Santorini, Greece Perched on the cliffs of Santorini, Grace Santorini offers guests a truly magical swimming experience. The hotel’s infinity pool boasts a transparent edge, giving swimmers the sensation of swimming on the edge of the world. As you glide through the crystal-clear water, you’ll be treated to uninterrupted views of the Aegean Sea and the iconic Santorini Caldera.

In conclusion, hotels with glass-bottomed pools offer guests a unique and thrilling way to enjoy stunning views while swimming or relaxing. From cityscapes to natural wonders, these pools provide an unforgettable experience that combines the tranquility of water with the awe-inspiring beauty of the world below. So, whether you’re seeking a city escape or a serene mountain retreat, consider checking into a hotel with a glass-bottomed pool. It’s an opportunity to immerse yourself in luxury and swim above the world.
