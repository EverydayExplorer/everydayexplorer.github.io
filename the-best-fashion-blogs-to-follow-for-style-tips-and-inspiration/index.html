The Best Fashion Blogs to Follow for Style Tips and Inspiration


Fashion is a constantly evolving industry, with new trends and styles emerging every season. For fashion enthusiasts, staying up-to-date with the latest fashion news and finding inspiration for their own personal style can be a fun and rewarding pursuit. In today’s digital age, fashion blogs have become an invaluable resource for individuals seeking style tips and inspiration. With a plethora of options available, it can be challenging to find the best fashion blogs to follow.

To make your search easier, we have curated a list of the top fashion blogs that are sure to offer you a wealth of style inspiration and expert advice. Whether you’re looking for tips on incorporating the latest fashion trends into your wardrobe, searching for outfit ideas for a specific occasion, or simply want to indulge in some fashion eye candy, these blogs have got you covered.

1. The Blonde Salad

Founded by Italian fashion influencer Chiara Ferragni, The Blonde Salad is one of the most well-established and influential fashion blogs in the industry. With its mix of personal style posts, fashion news, and lifestyle content, this blog has something for everyone. Chiara’s impeccable taste and ability to effortlessly blend high-end designer pieces with high-street finds make her blog a must-read for fashion enthusiasts.

2. Man Repeller

Man Repeller is not your average fashion blog. Founded by Leandra Medine, this blog takes a unique approach to fashion by celebrating personal style that may not necessarily conform to traditional beauty norms or attract the opposite sex. With a focus on self-expression and individuality, Man Repeller encourages its readers to embrace fashion as a form of self-empowerment. From thought-provoking essays to creative outfit ideas, this blog is a refreshing departure from the mainstream.

3. The Glamourai

Led by Kelly Framel, The Glamourai is a fabulous combination of fashion, beauty, and lifestyle content. Known for her impeccable taste and exquisite style, Kelly presents her readers with stunning visuals and thoughtful narratives. Whether she’s documenting her travels or sharing her latest fashion finds, The Glamourai is a true visual feast that will leave you feeling inspired and ready to experiment with your own personal style.

4. Song of Style

With a following of over 5 million, Aimee Song’s blog, Song of Style, has become one of the most influential fashion blogs on the internet. Aimee’s ability to effortlessly mix high-end designer pieces with affordable fashion finds has made her a favorite among fashion enthusiasts. From street style inspiration to travel diaries, Song of Style offers a diverse range of content that will keep you coming back for more.

5. The Budget Fashionista

For those on a tight budget, The Budget Fashionista is a must-follow blog. Founded by Kathryn Finney, this blog aims to prove that style doesn’t have to come with a hefty price tag. Featuring tips on how to shop smart and find the best deals, The Budget Fashionista offers practical advice for those looking to elevate their style without breaking the bank. With a focus on affordability and accessibility, this blog is a valuable resource for fashion enthusiasts of all budgets.

6. Paris in Four Months

Paris in Four Months, created by Carin Olsson, offers a glimpse into the glamorous world of Parisian fashion. As a Swedish expat, Carin brings a fresh perspective to the fashion scene in the City of Lights. Through her stunning photography, Carin captures the essence of Parisian style and shares her insider tips on the best places to shop, dine, and explore in the French capital. Whether you’re planning a trip to Paris or simply want to infuse your style with a touch of French elegance, Paris in Four Months is a blog worth following.

7. The Sartorialist

Scott Schuman, the man behind The Sartorialist blog, is widely regarded as one of the pioneers of street style photography. With his keen eye for style and ability to capture the essence of a moment, Scott has revolutionized the way we perceive and consume fashion. The Sartorialist not only offers a diverse range of style inspiration but also provides a deeper insight into the cultural and social aspects of fashion around the world.

8. Cupcakes and Cashmere

Cupcakes and Cashmere, founded by Emily Schuman, is a blog that seamlessly combines fashion, beauty, home decor, and lifestyle content. With her relatable writing style and knack for creating visually stunning content, Emily has amassed a large following of loyal readers. Whether she’s sharing outfit ideas, home decor tips, or her favorite recipes, Cupcakes and Cashmere offers a little something for everyone.

9. The Fashion Guitar

Charlotte Groeneveld, the creative mind behind The Fashion Guitar, is a fashion blogger known for her effortlessly chic style and impeccable taste. With her minimalist approach to fashion, Charlotte offers her readers a fresh perspective on how to create elegant and timeless looks. Whether she’s sharing her latest fashion finds or offering style advice, The Fashion Guitar is a treasure trove of inspiration for those seeking a sophisticated and refined aesthetic.

10. Garance Doré

Garance Doré is a renowned fashion illustrator and photographer who has transitioned into the world of blogging. Through her eponymous blog, Garance shares her unique perspective on fashion, beauty, and lifestyle. With her signature wit and charm, she offers an insider’s view into the glamorous world of fashion. From interviews with industry insiders to her personal musings on style, Garance Doré offers a refreshing and authentic take on the fashion world.

In conclusion, these are just some of the best fashion blogs to follow for style tips and inspiration. Whether you’re looking for personal style inspiration, the latest fashion news, or practical advice on how to elevate your style, these blogs offer a wealth of valuable content. So, get ready to bookmark these blogs, because they are sure to become your go-to resources for all things fashion!
