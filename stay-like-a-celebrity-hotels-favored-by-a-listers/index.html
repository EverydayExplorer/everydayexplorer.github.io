Stay Like a Celebrity: Hotels Favored by A-Listers


Whether they’re attending red carpet events, filming on location, or simply taking a much-needed vacation, celebrities often find themselves staying in luxurious and glamorous hotels around the world. These hotels are renowned for providing top-notch service, breathtaking accommodations, and an overall experience fit for a star. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the most famous hotels favored by A-listers.

1. The Beverly Hills Hotel, Los Angeles

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills, this iconic pink palace has been a hotspot for celebrities since it opened its doors in 1912. Known for its luxurious bungalows and beautiful grounds, The Beverly Hills Hotel offers privacy and exclusivity to its guests. It has hosted countless stars, including Marilyn Monroe, Frank Sinatra, and Elizabeth Taylor. With its legendary Polo Lounge and elegant spa, The Beverly Hills Hotel remains a favorite among Hollywood’s elite.

2. Chateau Marmont, Los Angeles

Situated on Sunset Boulevard, Chateau Marmont is a legendary hotel that embodies old Hollywood charm. Built in the 1920s, this European-style castle has long been a favorite among artists, musicians, and actors. Known for its discretion and privacy, Chateau Marmont has hosted celebrities like James Dean, Jim Morrison, and Johnny Depp. Its iconic poolside bungalows and famous restaurant attract A-listers who crave the glamour of the past.

3. Hotel Costes, Paris

When it comes to celebrity hotspots, Hotel Costes in Paris is at the top of the list. Famous for its opulent interiors, exquisite design, and vibrant nightlife, this boutique hotel has become a magnet for stars visiting the City of Love. The hotel’s bar is a favorite haunt of fashion icons and musicians, attracting the likes of Madonna, Kanye West, and Rihanna. If you’re looking to stay like a celebrity in Paris, Hotel Costes is the place to be.

4. The Mark, New York City

If you’re in search of luxury and sophistication, The Mark in New York City is a must-visit. Located in Manhattan’s Upper East Side, this five-star hotel offers elegance and style at every turn. With spacious suites designed by legendary French designer Jacques Grange, The Mark has welcomed countless celebrities, including Beyoncé, Oprah Winfrey, and Justin Bieber. Its central location and world-class amenities make it a favorite among A-listers visiting the Big Apple.

5. The Mandarin Oriental, Tokyo

Offering stunning views of Tokyo’s skyline, The Mandarin Oriental is a haven for celebrities seeking relaxation and impeccable service. Situated in the heart of the city’s bustling financial district, this luxury hotel boasts spacious rooms, Michelin-starred restaurants, and a serene spa. With its discreet luxury and unparalleled attention to detail, The Mandarin Oriental has attracted the likes of Brad Pitt, Angelina Jolie, and Tom Cruise.

6. The Savoy, London

Dating back to 1889, The Savoy in London is a timeless classic that has hosted numerous celebrities throughout its history. Located on the banks of the River Thames, this iconic hotel exudes elegance and charm. With its iconic American Bar and glamorous Art Deco interiors, The Savoy continues to attract A-listers from all over the world. Stars such as Audrey Hepburn, Marilyn Monroe, and Frank Sinatra have all called The Savoy their home away from home.

7. The Bowery Hotel, New York City

For a more modern and trendy experience, The Bowery Hotel in New York City is a favorite among the young Hollywood crowd. Located in the trendy Lower East Side neighborhood, this boutique hotel offers a unique blend of old-world charm and contemporary design. With its celebrity-filled bar, spacious rooms, and fabulous rooftop terrace, The Bowery Hotel has become a go-to spot for stars like Channing Tatum, Emma Stone, and Ryan Gosling.

In conclusion, staying like a celebrity is not out of reach if you choose the right hotel. These renowned establishments have built their reputation on providing a level of luxury and service that is fit for the stars. From iconic Hollywood destinations like The Beverly Hills Hotel and Chateau Marmont to glamorous European getaways like Hotel Costes, these hotels offer an unforgettable experience that will make you feel like an A-lister. So, why not indulge in the celebrity lifestyle and book your stay at one of these esteemed hotels? You might just find yourself surrounded by the same stars you admire on the silver screen.
