The Best Fashion Podcasts to Tune in for Style Tips


In the fast-paced world of fashion, staying ahead of the curve is essential. Whether you’re a seasoned style aficionado or just dipping your toes into the world of fashion, podcasts are a fantastic resource for staying up-to-date with the latest trends, gaining insight from industry experts, and discovering new perspectives on style. With countless fashion podcasts available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. That’s why we’ve curated a list of the best fashion podcasts to tune in to for style tips, covering everything from runway trends to sustainable fashion and beyond.

  1. The Business of Fashion Podcast: Hosted by Imran Amed, founder, and CEO of Business of Fashion, this podcast delves deep into the business side of the fashion industry. From interviews with top designers to discussions on the future of retail, this podcast offers valuable insights for anyone interested in the intersection of fashion and commerce.

  2. Dressed: The History of Fashion: If you’re a fashion history buff, then ‘Dressed’ is the podcast for you. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, each episode explores the cultural and social significance of clothing throughout history. From ancient garments to modern fashion icons, ‘Dressed’ provides a fascinating look into the evolution of style.

  3. Fashion No Filter: Hosted by fashion journalists Camille Charrière and Monica Ainley, ‘Fashion No Filter’ takes listeners behind the scenes of the fashion industry. With candid interviews with industry insiders and discussions on everything from influencer culture to sustainability, this podcast offers an insider’s perspective on the world of fashion.

  4. The Cutting Room Floor: Hosted by designer Recho Omondi, ‘The Cutting Room Floor’ offers a fresh take on fashion commentary. With a focus on diversity, inclusivity, and creativity, each episode explores a different aspect of the fashion industry, from runway shows to street style. If you’re looking for a podcast that challenges the status quo, ‘The Cutting Room Floor’ is a must-listen.

  5. Wardrobe Crisis: Hosted by sustainable fashion advocate Clare Press, ‘Wardrobe Crisis’ explores the environmental and ethical issues facing the fashion industry today. From the impact of fast fashion to the rise of slow fashion brands, this podcast offers thought-provoking discussions on how we can create a more sustainable and ethical fashion industry.

  6. The Glossy Podcast: If you’re interested in the intersection of fashion and technology, then ‘The Glossy Podcast’ is the perfect listen. Hosted by Glossy editor-in-chief Jill Manoff, each episode features interviews with fashion and beauty industry leaders who are innovating and disrupting the status quo. From e-commerce to influencer marketing, ‘The Glossy Podcast’ offers insights into the future of fashion.

  7. American Fashion Podcast: For a comprehensive look at the American fashion industry, look no further than the ‘American Fashion Podcast.’ Hosted by fashion industry veterans Charles Beckwith, Seth Friedermann, and Lisa-Maria Radano, this podcast covers everything from design and production to retail and marketing. Whether you’re a fashion entrepreneur or just curious about how the industry works, ‘American Fashion Podcast’ has something for everyone.

  8. The Style Success Podcast: Hosted by personal stylist Mallory Sills, ‘The Style Success Podcast’ is perfect for anyone looking to elevate their personal style. With practical tips on everything from wardrobe organization to shopping on a budget, this podcast is a valuable resource for anyone looking to look and feel their best.

  9. Fashion Revolution Podcast: If you’re passionate about ethical fashion, then the ‘Fashion Revolution Podcast’ is a must-listen. Hosted by the team behind the Fashion Revolution movement, each episode explores the social and environmental impact of the fashion industry and offers insights into how consumers can make more informed choices.

  10. The Fashion Geeks: Hosted by fashion enthusiasts Kristina Zias and Megan Collins, ‘The Fashion Geeks’ is a fun and lighthearted take on all things style. From celebrity fashion fails to red carpet hits, this podcast covers the latest trends and gossip in the world of fashion.

Whether you’re a fashion novice or a seasoned style expert, these podcasts offer valuable insights, inspiration, and entertainment for anyone with a passion for fashion. So grab your headphones and start tuning in to elevate your style game!
