Hotels with Unique Swimming Pools: Dive into Adventure


When it comes to planning a vacation, the hotel pool can be a deciding factor for many travelers. While a standard rectangular pool is nice, there are some hotels that take swimming to a whole new level with their unique and breathtaking pool designs. From infinity pools perched on cliff edges to pools with underwater music systems, these hotels offer an adventure in aquatic luxury that is simply unparalleled. If you’re looking to make a splash on your next vacation, here are some of the world’s most unique hotel swimming pools that will have you diving into adventure.

1. The Cambrian Hotel, Switzerland
Tucked away in the picturesque Swiss Alps, The Cambrian Hotel boasts a stunning infinity pool that overlooks the snow-capped mountains. Guests can take a relaxing swim while enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding natural beauty. Whether you’re soaking in the pool during the warm summer months or taking a refreshing dip after a day of skiing in the winter, this pool offers an experience that is both serene and exhilarating.

2. The Library, Koh Samui, Thailand
The Library hotel in Koh Samui, Thailand, is home to one of the most unique pools in the world. The pool’s vibrant red color, created by mosaic tiles, creates a striking contrast against the turquoise waters of the Gulf of Thailand. It’s a photographer’s dream and a truly unforgettable swimming experience.

3. The Joule, Dallas, Texas
Located in the heart of downtown Dallas, The Joule is home to an awe-inspiring rooftop pool that extends eight feet beyond the building’s edge. Swimmers can take a daring plunge and look down through the pool’s transparent side, giving the illusion of swimming off the edge of the building. It’s a thrilling experience that combines luxury with a touch of adrenaline.

4. The Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia
Nestled in the lush jungles of Bali, The Hanging Gardens is famous for its split-level infinity pool that overlooks the surrounding rainforest. The pool appears to float above the treetops, offering a sense of tranquility and connection with nature that is truly unmatched.

5. The San Alfonso del Mar, Chile
This resort in Algarrobo, Chile, boasts the world’s largest swimming pool, spanning over 20 acres and holding 66 million gallons of crystal-clear seawater. Guests can enjoy kayaking, paddle boarding, and even small boat rides in this colossal pool, making it an aquatic playground like no other.

6. The Nemo 33, Brussels, Belgium
For those who prefer an underwater adventure, The Nemo 33 in Brussels is the place to be. This hotel features the world’s deepest indoor swimming pool, reaching a depth of 108 feet. It’s a paradise for scuba diving enthusiasts and offers a truly unique underwater experience without having to leave the comfort of the hotel.

7. The Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti, Tanzania
Imagine taking a dip in a pool while watching elephants and other wildlife roam freely in the distance. At the Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti, guests can do just that. The infinity pool overlooks the vast plains of the Serengeti, providing an unparalleled opportunity to connect with nature while enjoying a luxurious swim.

These are just a few examples of the incredible hotel swimming pools around the world that offer a truly unique and adventurous experience. Whether you’re looking for a serene mountain view, a daring rooftop plunge, or an underwater escapade, these pools are sure to make a splash on your next vacation. So, pack your swimsuit and get ready to dive into adventure at one of these extraordinary hotel swimming pools.
