The Influence of Australian Games: From Oddworld to Hollow Knight


Australia may be known for its stunning landscapes, unique wildlife, and laid-back lifestyle, but it also has a vibrant and thriving gaming industry. Over the years, Australian game developers have made a significant impact on the global gaming scene, creating unique and innovative titles that have captured the hearts of players worldwide. In this blog post, we will explore the influence of Australian games, from classics like Oddworld to modern gems like Hollow Knight.

One of the most iconic Australian games of all time is Oddworld: Abe’s Oddysee. Released in 1997 by Melbourne-based game development studio Oddworld Inhabitants, this platformer quickly became a cult classic. The game’s unique art style, dark humor, and challenging gameplay set it apart from other titles of its time. Oddworld: Abe’s Oddysee introduced players to a dystopian world where they controlled the protagonist, Abe, as he embarked on a journey to save his fellow Mudokons from becoming snacks for the evil Glukkons. The game’s success led to several sequels and spin-offs, cementing its place in gaming history.

Fast forward to 2017, and we have another Australian game that took the gaming world by storm: Hollow Knight. Developed by Team Cherry, a small indie studio from Adelaide, Hollow Knight is a mesmerizing metroidvania that combines beautiful hand-drawn visuals, atmospheric exploration, and challenging combat. Set in the decaying underground kingdom of Hallownest, players take on the role of the silent knight as they navigate through intricate labyrinthine areas, encountering bizarre creatures and uncovering the mysteries of the kingdom. Hollow Knight’s success can be attributed to its intricate level design, deep lore, and tight gameplay mechanics, making it a must-play for any fan of the genre.

The influence of Australian games extends beyond their individual successes. These games have showcased the creativity and talent of Australian developers to the world, putting them on the map in the global gaming industry. The success of titles like Oddworld: Abe’s Oddysee and Hollow Knight has opened doors for other Australian game developers, inspiring them to create unique and captivating experiences.

Furthermore, Australian games have also contributed to the evolution of the gaming industry as a whole. They have pushed boundaries, introducing new gameplay mechanics, storytelling techniques, and artistic styles. By taking risks and daring to be different, Australian game developers have shown that innovation can come from unexpected places.

In conclusion, the influence of Australian games, from classics like Oddworld: Abe’s Oddysee to modern gems like Hollow Knight, cannot be understated. These games have not only entertained players around the world but have also showcased the creativity and talent of Australian developers. By pushing boundaries and introducing unique gameplay mechanics, Australian games have made a lasting impact on the gaming industry, inspiring future generations of game developers. So the next time you pick up a controller or sit down at your gaming PC, take a moment to appreciate the influence of Australian games and the experiences they have brought to the gaming world.
