10 Sustainable Living Instagram Accounts to Follow for Inspiration


As concerns about the environment grow, so does the demand for information and inspiration on how to live sustainably. Instagram has become a platform where influencers, activists, and experts share valuable tips, resources, and success stories on sustainable living. If you’re looking to make eco-friendly changes in your life and need some motivation, here are 10 sustainable living Instagram accounts you should follow:

1. @zerowastechef

This account is run by Anne Marie Bonneau, who has dedicated her life to the zero waste movement. On her Instagram, you’ll find tips on how to reduce waste in every aspect of your life, from cooking to cleaning and everything in between. Anne Marie also shares recipes that minimize food waste and emphasize local, seasonal ingredients. Her posts are a blend of practical advice and personal stories that make sustainable living accessible and relatable.

2. @going.zero.waste

Kathryn Kellogg is the face behind this popular account. She provides practical zero waste tips and advocates for sustainable fashion, personal care, and lifestyle choices. Kathryn’s feed is a mix of helpful how-to guides, sustainable product recommendations, and honest reflections on her own journey towards a more sustainable lifestyle. Her approachable and down-to-earth attitude makes her content easy to connect with and apply in your own life.

3. @dani_bakker

Dani Bakker focuses on sustainable living and minimalism, often sharing her experiences as a mom who wants to create a better world for her children. Her account is full of beautiful photos of her eco-friendly lifestyle and sustainable home, as well as tips on natural parenting, ethical fashion, and zero waste living. Dani’s content is inspiring for anyone looking to adopt a more intentional and conscious lifestyle.

4. @juliancarley

Julian Carley is a passionate advocate for sustainability, with a particular focus on sustainable fashion and ethical living. His Instagram feed features stylish outfits made from eco-friendly materials, as well as tips for shopping secondhand and caring for your clothes to extend their lifespan. Julian also shares insights on ethical brands and sustainable fashion trends, making it easy for followers to stay informed and make conscious choices.

5. @sustainablykindliving

This account, run by Laura, offers tips and tricks for living a more sustainable and mindful life. Laura’s posts cover a wide range of topics, including zero waste, plant-based living, eco-friendly beauty, and slow fashion. Her content is well-researched and practical, making it easy for followers to take actionable steps towards a more sustainable lifestyle. Laura’s passion for the planet shines through in every post.

6. @earthyandie

Andie takes a holistic approach to sustainability, focusing on eco-conscious living, veganism, and self-care. Her Instagram account is a treasure trove of delicious plant-based recipes, DIY beauty products, and eco-friendly home hacks. Andie’s positive energy and genuine enthusiasm for sustainable living make her account a must-follow for anyone looking to embrace a greener lifestyle.

7. @simply.living.well

Simply Living Well, run by Julia Watkins, is all about living well while treading lightly on the planet. Julia shares tips on making your own natural cleaning products, gardening, and reducing waste in your everyday life. Her Instagram feed is a beautiful blend of rustic aesthetics and actionable advice, providing a sense of calm and simplicity that aligns perfectly with the sustainable living ethos.

8. @ecogreenmom

Jenica is a mother and environmentalist who shares her journey towards zero waste living with her family. Her account is full of tips on sustainable parenting, eco-friendly meal planning, and reducing plastic use. Jenica’s honesty about the challenges of sustainable living and her commitment to making small, consistent changes make her content relatable and inspiring.

9. @mindfully.sustainable

Ashley focuses on living sustainably and mindfully, with an emphasis on self-care and intentionality. Her Instagram account is full of soothing visuals and thoughtful insights into the intersection of sustainability and mental well-being. Ashley’s posts encourage followers to slow down, appreciate the beauty of the natural world, and make conscious choices that benefit both themselves and the planet.

10. @livekindly

Livekindly offers a wide range of content related to sustainable living, from vegan recipes to environmental news and ethical lifestyle tips. The account features collaborations with activists, experts, and influencers, providing diverse perspectives on sustainability and plant-based living. Livekindly’s content is informative, engaging, and empowering, making it a great resource for anyone looking to make positive changes in their life.

These 10 Instagram accounts are excellent sources of inspiration for anyone interested in sustainable living. By following these influencers, you’ll gain access to a wealth of knowledge and practical tips that can help you make eco-friendly choices in your daily life. From zero waste practices to sustainable fashion and plant-based diets, these accounts offer a diverse range of content that can guide you on your journey towards a more sustainable and mindful lifestyle.
