Best Apps for Managing Your Finances in 2021


When it comes to managing our finances, staying organized and in control is essential. With the rapid advancements in technology, there are now numerous apps available that can help us effectively manage our money. These apps provide a convenient and user-friendly platform to keep track of our income, expenses, budgets, and investments. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best apps for managing your finances in 2021.

1. Mint: Mint is a widely popular personal finance app that allows users to connect their bank accounts, credit cards, and other financial accounts in one place. It provides an overview of your financial health, tracks your spending, and helps you create budgets. Mint also sends alerts for bill payments and monitors unusual account activities, ensuring the security of your finances.

2. YNAB (You Need a Budget): YNAB follows a unique budgeting approach that focuses on assigning every dollar a job. It helps you create a budget, set financial goals, and tracks your progress in real-time. YNAB syncs across all your devices and enables you to make informed financial decisions. With its intuitive interface and powerful features, this app is a fantastic tool for effective financial management.

3. Personal Capital: Personal Capital is designed to assist with investment tracking and retirement planning. It allows you to link your investment accounts and provides a holistic view of your financial portfolio. The app offers tools for analyzing investments, monitoring fees, and optimizing asset allocation. Personal Capital also includes a retirement planner that helps you estimate whether you’re on track to meet your retirement goals.

4. Acorns: Acorns is an excellent app for those looking to invest with small amounts of money. It rounds up your daily purchases to the nearest dollar and invests the spare change into a diversified portfolio. Acorns also offers additional features such as automated recurring investments and personalized advice based on your financial goals.

5. PocketGuard: PocketGuard is a budgeting and expense tracking app that simplifies your financial life. It connects to your bank accounts, credit cards, and bills to provide an overview of your cash flow. PocketGuard’s intuitive interface categorizes your expenses and tracks your income, enabling you to set realistic budgets and save money. The app also offers personalized tips to help you make smarter financial decisions.

6. Expensify: Expensify is an app designed to simplify expense tracking, especially for business owners, freelancers, and employees who need to manage their expenses. It allows you to scan receipts, generate expense reports, and track mileage. Expensify integrates with accounting software, making it easy to manage and reconcile expenses for tax purposes.

7. Splitwise: Splitwise is a fantastic app for managing shared expenses among friends, roommates, or colleagues. It helps you split bills and IOUs, keep track of who owes whom, and send payment reminders. Splitwise ensures that everyone stays on top of their financial responsibilities and eliminates the hassle of manually calculating shared expenses.

8. Wally: Wally is a budgeting app that offers simplicity and effectiveness. It allows you to track your expenses, set savings goals, and manage your income. Wally’s insights and visualizations provide a clear picture of your spending habits, helping you make informed financial decisions and achieve your financial goals.

9. Honeydue: Honeydue is an app designed specifically for couples to manage their finances together. It enables you to track individual and shared expenses, set budgets, and receive notifications for bill payments. Honeydue also facilitates communication between partners regarding financial matters, promoting transparency and collaboration in managing shared finances.

10. Venmo: Venmo is a popular peer-to-peer payment app that allows you to send and receive money effortlessly. It eliminates the need for cash and checks, making it convenient for splitting bills or paying back friends. Venmo is secure and user-friendly, with built-in social features that allow you to see your friends’ transactions, adding a social element to the app.

In conclusion, managing your finances has never been easier with the abundance of apps available today. Whether it’s tracking expenses, creating budgets, investing, or collaborating with others, there is an app to cater to every financial need. These ten apps mentioned above are among the best for managing your finances in 2021. Choose the one that aligns with your financial goals and start taking control of your money today.
