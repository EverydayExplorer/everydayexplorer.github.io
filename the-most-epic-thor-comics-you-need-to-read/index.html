The Most Epic Thor Comics You Need to Read


When it comes to comic book superheroes, Thor has carved out his place as one of the most formidable and beloved characters in the Marvel Universe. With his mighty hammer Mjolnir and godlike powers, Thor has captivated readers for decades with his epic adventures and larger-than-life persona. Whether you’re a die-hard fan or just starting to explore the world of Thor, here are some of the most epic Thor comics you need to read.

1. ‘Thor: God of Thunder’ by Jason Aaron
If you’re looking to dive headfirst into Thor’s mythos, ‘Thor: God of Thunder’ is an excellent starting point. Written by Jason Aaron, this run explores the thunder god’s past, present, and future as he battles gods, monsters, and cosmic threats. With stunning artwork and a gripping narrative, ‘Thor: God of Thunder’ truly showcases the epic nature of Thor’s adventures.

2. ‘The Mighty Thor’ by Walter Simonson
Walter Simonson’s run on ‘The Mighty Thor’ is widely regarded as one of the best in the character’s history. Spanning from the 1980s to the 1990s, Simonson’s epic storytelling and dynamic artwork bring Thor’s world to life in a way that resonates with readers even today. This run features some of the most iconic Thor storylines, including the epic battle with the monstrous Midgard Serpent.

3. ‘Thor’ by J. Michael Straczynski
J. Michael Straczynski’s run on ‘Thor’ is a modern classic that redefined the character and introduced new and exciting aspects to his mythology. In this series, Thor returns to Earth and takes on the mantle of a mortal, embracing his humanity as he battles old and new enemies. With compelling character development and stunning visuals, Straczynski’s ‘Thor’ is a must-read for any fan.

4. ‘Thor: Ragnarok’ by Walt Simonson
Walt Simonson’s second entry on this list, ‘Thor: Ragnarok,’ is a story arc that delves into the apocalyptic event that is predicted to occur in Norse mythology. As the gods face their ultimate destruction, Thor embarks on a desperate mission to prevent Ragnarok and save Asgard from annihilation. With a gripping narrative and breathtaking artwork, ‘Thor: Ragnarok’ is a thrilling and epic read.

5. ‘Thor: The God Butcher’ by Jason Aaron
Another stellar entry from Jason Aaron’s tenure, ‘Thor: The God Butcher’ explores the dark side of Thor’s mythology. In this story, Thor confronts Gorr, a merciless god killer who has slain countless deities across the cosmos. As Thor faces this formidable foe, he questions the nature of his own godhood and the purpose of his existence. ‘Thor: The God Butcher’ is a gritty and thought-provoking tale that showcases the complexities of Thor as a character.

6. ‘Thor: The Death of Odin’ by Dan Jurgens and John Romita Jr.
In ‘Thor: The Death of Odin,’ the legendary father of the gods meets his demise, leading to a power struggle among Asgard’s inhabitants. As Thor and his fellow gods navigate the political turmoil, they must confront the consequences of Odin’s death and face off against powerful adversaries seeking to exploit the situation. This storyline is a must-read for any fan seeking a deeper understanding of Thor’s world.

7. ‘Thor: God of Thunder - The Last Days of Midgard’ by Jason Aaron
Jason Aaron’s third entry on this list, ‘Thor: God of Thunder - The Last Days of Midgard,’ is a powerful storyline that explores Thor’s duty as the protector of the nine realms. As the industrialized world threatens the realms with pollution and destruction, Thor must confront the dark underbelly of humanity and decide how best to protect the realms he loves. With striking artwork and a thought-provoking narrative, this story sheds light on Thor’s role as a guardian of the cosmos.

In conclusion, these are just a few of the many epic Thor comics that have enthralled readers over the years. From classic runs to modern storytelling, each of these comics showcases the grandeur, power, and depth of Thor as a character. Whether you’re a longtime fan or new to this thunderous superhero, diving into these Thor comics will transport you to a world of epic adventure and divine power. Get ready to wield Mjolnir and embark on an unforgettable journey through the realms!
