Gear Review: Top Hunting GPS Devices for Navigation


Welcome to our comprehensive gear review of the top hunting GPS devices for navigation! If you’re an avid hunter or someone who simply enjoys exploring the great outdoors, you’ll find this guide incredibly helpful in choosing the perfect GPS device to enhance your hunting experience. With the advancements in technology, GPS devices have become an indispensable tool for navigation, ensuring that you stay on track and never lose your way. So, without further ado, let’s dive into the world of hunting GPS devices!

Garmin GPSMAP 64st:  
The Garmin GPSMAP 64st is a highly popular choice among hunters due to its rugged design and reliable performance. The device features a 2.6-inch color display and a high-sensitivity GPS receiver, providing accurate positioning even in dense forests or deep valleys. With its preloaded topographic maps and a built-in 3-axis compass, you can confidently navigate through challenging terrains without any worries.

Magellan eXplorist 350H:  
Designed specifically for hunting enthusiasts, the Magellan eXplorist 350H offers a range of features tailored to improve your hunting experience. It comes with preloaded hunting-specific waypoints and features such as Moon Phase Calendar, HuntMetrics, and a digital compass, providing you with valuable information to make informed decisions while in the field.

Garmin eTrex 30x:  
The Garmin eTrex 30x is a compact and lightweight hunting GPS device that doesn't compromise on performance. It boasts a 2.2-inch sunlight-readable color display and a 3-axis electronic compass. With its highly sensitive GPS receiver and compatibility with both GPS and GLONASS satellite systems, you can expect fast and accurate positioning, even in challenging environments.

Bushnell BackTrack D-Tour:  
If you're looking for a simple yet effective GPS device, the Bushnell BackTrack D-Tour might be the perfect choice. This handheld device allows you to save up to five locations, making it easy to mark your hunting spots or track your path back to camp. It also features a digital compass, temperature and altitude readings, and a built-in accelerometer for accurate distance measurements.

Garmin Montana 680t:  
For those seeking a more advanced GPS device with a large touchscreen display, the Garmin Montana 680t is a fantastic option. It comes with a 4-inch dual-orientation, glove-friendly touchscreen, preloaded topographic maps, and a built-in 8-megapixel camera. Additionally, it offers GPS and GLONASS support, so you can expect exceptional accuracy during your hunting adventures.

Now that you’re familiar with some of the top hunting GPS devices available, it’s time to consider your specific needs and preferences. Think about the terrain you’ll be hunting in, the features that are most important to you, and your budget. By doing so, you can make an informed decision and choose the perfect GPS device to accompany you on your hunting journeys.

Remember, having a reliable hunting GPS device is not only about navigation but also about safety. It helps ensure that you can find your way back home, even if you venture deep into unfamiliar territory. So, equip yourself with one of these top hunting GPS devices, and get ready for thrilling and successful hunting expeditions!
