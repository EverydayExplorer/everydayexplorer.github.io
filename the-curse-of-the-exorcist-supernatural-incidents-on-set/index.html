The Curse of the Exorcist: Supernatural Incidents on Set


Lights, camera, action! The world of cinema is often regarded as glamorous and enchanting. However, behind the scenes, there are tales of paranormal occurrences that can send shivers down your spine. One such eerie legend is known as ‘The Curse of the Exorcist,’ a series of supernatural incidents that plagued the set of the iconic horror film ‘The Exorcist.’ In this blog post, we delve into the mysterious events that unfolded during the making of this cult classic.

‘The Exorcist,’ released in 1973, is widely considered one of the scariest movies of all time. Directed by William Friedkin and based on the novel by William Peter Blatty, the film follows the story of a young girl possessed by a demonic entity and the desperate attempts to save her soul. Little did the cast and crew know that the horror they were capturing on camera would soon manifest in their own lives.

The first strange occurrence took place during the pre-production phase, as the team scouted for locations. The crew stumbled upon an abandoned church in Georgetown, where they decided to film some key scenes. However, as soon as the crew began setting up their equipment, they reported a chilling sensation and unexplained noises echoing through the halls. Some even claimed to have seen shadowy figures lurking in the corners of their vision.

Once filming began, the supernatural incidents escalated. Actors and crew members started experiencing a series of bizarre accidents and unexplained injuries. Actress Ellen Burstyn, who played the mother of the possessed girl, suffered a permanent spinal injury during a particularly intense scene. It was as if the malevolent force from the screenplay had somehow transcended the boundaries of fiction and infiltrated their reality.

But it wasn’t just physical harm that plagued the set. The cast and crew also fell victim to inexplicable illnesses and psychological distress. The film’s special effects technician, John Morris, mysteriously contracted pneumonia, despite being in perfect health before production began. Additionally, several members of the crew reported experiencing vivid nightmares and disturbing visions, leading to sleepless nights and a sense of impending doom.

As the incidents continued, rumors of a ‘curse’ started circulating among the film industry. Some believed that the subject matter of the film had stirred up dark energies, while others speculated that the abandoned church had been a site of occult rituals in the past. Regardless of the cause, the curse seemed to have a relentless grip on those involved with the film.

Despite the supernatural turmoil, ‘The Exorcist’ went on to become a critically acclaimed hit, earning ten Academy Award nominations and cementing its place in horror cinema history. However, the eerie incidents on set left a lasting impact on the cast and crew. Many believed that the curse had followed them long after filming had ended, with some experiencing strange phenomena and a sense of unease for years to come.

To this day, the legend of ‘The Curse of the Exorcist’ continues to fascinate and intrigue horror enthusiasts. While skeptics dismiss it as mere coincidence, others remain convinced that there was something truly supernatural at play during the making of this iconic film. Whether you believe in curses or not, the eerie incidents surrounding ‘The Exorcist’ serve as a chilling reminder that sometimes, the line between fiction and reality can become blurred, leaving a lasting imprint on those who dare to delve into the realm of the supernatural.
