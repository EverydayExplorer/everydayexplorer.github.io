Top 10 Comedy Specials to Watch on Netflix


Netflix has become the go-to platform for entertainment, offering a wide range of genres to suit everyone’s taste. One genre that has been gaining immense popularity on Netflix is comedy specials. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose which ones to watch. That’s why we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 comedy specials on Netflix that are guaranteed to tickle your funny bone and leave you in stitches.

  1. ‘Dave Chappelle: Sticks & Stones’ - Dave Chappelle is a comedic genius, and his latest special is no exception. In ‘Sticks & Stones,’ he fearlessly tackles controversial topics with his trademark wit and humor. Prepare to be both entertained and enlightened.

  2. ‘Hannah Gadsby: Nanette’ - Hannah Gadsby’s ‘Nanette’ is a thought-provoking and boundary-breaking comedy special that challenges the conventions of stand-up comedy. With her unique storytelling approach, Gadsby delivers a powerful message about identity, trauma, and the importance of speaking one’s truth.

  3. ‘Bo Burnham: Inside’ - ‘Inside’ is a one-of-a-kind comedy special by Bo Burnham, filmed entirely during the COVID-19 pandemic. With a mix of songs, skits, and poignant commentary, Burnham captures the essence of isolation and reflects on the state of the world through his signature dark humor.

  4. ‘Ali Wong: Baby Cobra’ - Ali Wong’s ‘Baby Cobra’ is a hilarious and unapologetic exploration of motherhood, marriage, and the struggles faced by women in a male-dominated world. Wong’s comedic timing and sharp observations make this special a must-watch.

  5. ‘John Mulaney: Kid Gorgeous at Radio City’ - John Mulaney’s ‘Kid Gorgeous at Radio City’ showcases his undeniable talent for storytelling and his ability to turn everyday experiences into laugh-out-loud moments. With his trademark charm and wit, Mulaney delivers a performance that will leave you wanting more.

  6. ‘Amy Schumer: The Leather Special’ - Amy Schumer is known for her fearless and boundary-pushing comedy, and ‘The Leather Special’ is no exception. With her trademark candidness and raunchy humor, Schumer tackles topics like relationships, body image, and the struggles faced by women in the entertainment industry.

  7. ‘Trevor Noah: Son of Patricia’ - Trevor Noah’s ‘Son of Patricia’ combines his personal experiences growing up in South Africa with his unique perspective on global politics and culture. Noah’s intelligent and thought-provoking humor makes this special a standout.

  8. ‘Kevin Hart: Irresponsible’ - Kevin Hart’s ‘Irresponsible’ showcases his energetic and animated style of comedy. From hilarious anecdotes about fatherhood to his own personal growth, Hart’s special is filled with laugh-out-loud moments that will leave you entertained from start to finish.

  9. ‘Aziz Ansari: Buried Alive’ - Aziz Ansari’s ‘Buried Alive’ is a masterclass in observational comedy. Ansari’s witty commentary on relationships, dating, and the trials and tribulations of adulthood will have you laughing and nodding in agreement.

  10. ‘Chris Rock: Tamborine’ - Chris Rock’s ‘Tamborine’ is a triumphant return to the stage for this comedy legend. With his insightful observations on relationships, race, and society, Rock delivers a performance that is both hilarious and thought-provoking.

So, there you have it – the top 10 comedy specials to watch on Netflix. Whether you’re in the mood for witty social commentary, relatable personal anecdotes, or just a good old-fashioned laugh, these specials have got you covered. Grab some popcorn, get comfortable on your couch, and prepare to laugh until your sides hurt. Happy binge-watching!
