The Top Gaming Influencers to Follow for Gaming News and Updates


When it comes to staying updated with the latest gaming news and updates, following the right influencers can make all the difference. These individuals have carved a niche for themselves in the gaming industry, providing insightful content, reviews, and exclusive information to their followers. In this article, we will take a look at some of the top gaming influencers that you should follow to stay on top of your game.

1. PewDiePie:
Felix Kjellberg, famously known as PewDiePie, is one of the most influential figures in the gaming world. With over 110 million subscribers on his YouTube channel, he has built a massive following that eagerly awaits his gaming content. PewDiePie is known for his hilarious commentary, entertaining playthroughs, and quirky personality, making him a must-follow for any gaming enthusiast.

2. Ninja:
Tyler ‘Ninja’ Blevins is a household name in the gaming industry. He gained widespread popularity through his Fortnite gameplay streams, which attracted millions of viewers on platforms like Twitch. Ninja’s success and his transition from a professional gamer to a mainstream celebrity have made him an influential figure and a go-to source for gaming news and updates.

3. Jacksepticeye:
Sean McLoughlin, popularly known as Jacksepticeye, is an Irish YouTuber who has captured the attention of millions of gamers around the world. With his enthusiastic and energetic personality, Jacksepticeye offers a unique perspective on various games, often sharing his candid thoughts and reactions. His engaging style and frequent updates make him a must-follow for fans of all types of games.

4. Shroud:
Michael Grzesiek, better known as Shroud, rose to prominence as a professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. After retiring from competitive gaming, he focused on streaming and creating content on platforms like Twitch and YouTube. Shroud is known for his exceptional skills and in-depth analysis of gameplay, making him a valuable source of information and updates for gamers.

5. TheRadBrad:
Bradley Colburn, also known as TheRadBrad, has been providing comprehensive gaming walkthroughs and playthroughs on YouTube for over a decade. With his calm and detailed approach, TheRadBrad has garnered a loyal following of gamers who rely on him for reviews, tips, and updates on the latest game releases. His dedication to completing games and his ability to create engaging content have made him a trusted influencer in the gaming community.

6. Alia ‘SypherPK’ Hassan:
Alia Hassan, known as SypherPK, is a Fortnite specialist on Twitch. With his in-depth analysis of gameplay, tips, and strategy guides, SypherPK has gained a massive following that looks to him for expert advice on improving their Fortnite skills. His combination of skill and educational content has made him an influential figure in the competitive gaming scene.

7. CohhCarnage:
Ben Cassell, popularly known as CohhCarnage, has been streaming on Twitch for years and has built a strong community of passionate gamers. CohhCarnage offers a variety of content, ranging from game playthroughs to live commentary. His consistent streaming schedule and engaging personality make him an excellent source for gaming news and updates.

8. Summit1g:
Jaryd Lazar, better known as Summit1g, is a variety streamer who has a loyal fanbase on Twitch. While not exclusively focused on gaming news, Summit1g provides entertaining content and frequent updates, fostering a sense of community among his followers. His interactions with chat and genuine passion for gaming make him a popular choice for those looking for fun and informative streams.

9. DrLupo:
Benjamin ‘DrLupo’ Lupo is another influential Fortnite streamer who has gained a dedicated following on Twitch. DrLupo is known for his high-level gameplay, engaging personality, and his commitment to charity streams. His charitable efforts and exceptional gameplay have made him a respected figure in the gaming community.

10. Imane ‘Pokimane’ Anys:
Imane Anys, popularly known as Pokimane, is one of the most successful female streamers on Twitch. While Pokimane does not exclusively focus on gaming, her entertaining personality and unique content make her a must-follow for gamers looking for diverse streams. From gameplay to IRL streams, Pokimane offers a wide range of content that keeps her audience engaged.

In conclusion, following gaming influencers who provide reliable news and updates is essential for any gaming enthusiast. These top influencers have established themselves as reliable sources of information, offering entertaining and educational content for their followers. Whether you are looking for game reviews, tips and tricks, or the latest updates, these gaming influencers are sure to keep you well-informed and entertained. Make sure to check them out and stay ahead in the gaming world.
