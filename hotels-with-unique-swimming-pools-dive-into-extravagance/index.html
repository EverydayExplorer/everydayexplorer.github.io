Hotels with Unique Swimming Pools: Dive into Extravagance


When it comes to planning a vacation, one of the key elements that can take your trip from ordinary to extraordinary is finding a hotel with a unique swimming pool. These magnificent pools not only offer a refreshing escape from the summer heat but also provide a visual feast for the eyes. In this blog post, we will take a deep dive into the world of hotels with unique swimming pools, exploring the most extravagant and awe-inspiring designs that are sure to leave you breathless.

1. The Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia:
Nestled amidst the lush jungles of Bali, The Hanging Gardens is a true oasis of luxury. Their stunning bi-level infinity pool offers guests a swim amidst the treetops, with the surrounding greenery creating a tranquil and serene atmosphere. The pool’s unique design gives the illusion of water cascading down into the valley below, making it a picture-perfect spot for a relaxing swim.

2. The Library, Koh Samui, Thailand:
If you’re a fan of vibrant colors, you won’t want to miss out on The Library’s iconic red pool. This modern and artistic swimming pool features a striking deep red hue, contrasting beautifully with the azure blue of the ocean in the background. Whether you’re lounging by the poolside or taking a dip in its refreshing waters, this pool offers a truly unique and Instagram-worthy experience.

3. The Grace Santorini, Greece:
Perched on the cliffs of Santorini, the infinity pool at The Grace is a sight to behold. With its breathtaking panoramic views of the Aegean Sea and the iconic Santorini sunset, this pool provides an unforgettable experience for guests. Immerse yourself in the pool’s crystal-clear waters as you soak up the stunning vistas, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

4. The Cambrian Hotel, Switzerland:
Located in the picturesque Swiss Alps, The Cambrian Hotel boasts an infinity pool that overlooks the snow-capped mountains and lush valleys below. As you take a dip in this heated outdoor pool, you’ll feel like you’re swimming amongst nature, surrounded by the stunning alpine scenery. The experience is particularly magical during the winter months when you can enjoy the warmth of the water while being surrounded by a winter wonderland.

5. The Marina Bay Sands, Singapore:
No list of unique swimming pools would be complete without mentioning the iconic rooftop pool at The Marina Bay Sands. Situated 57 stories above ground, this infinity pool offers guests an unparalleled view of the Singapore skyline. Whether you’re swimming laps or simply basking in the sun on one of the poolside loungers, the experience of being at the top of the world is truly awe-inspiring.

These hotels with unique swimming pools are just a taste of the extravagant experiences that await you. Whether you’re seeking relaxation, adventure, or simply a one-of-a-kind experience, these pools will leave you feeling pampered and rejuvenated. So, dive into the world of extravagance and make your next vacation truly unforgettable by choosing a hotel with a unique swimming pool.
