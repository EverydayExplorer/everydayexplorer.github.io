10 Easy Instant Pot Recipes for Quick Weeknight Dinners


In today’s fast-paced world, finding time to cook a delicious and nutritious meal after a long day at work can often feel like a daunting task. However, thanks to the wonders of modern technology, there’s a solution that can help you whip up delectable dishes in a fraction of the time it would take using traditional cooking methods. Enter the Instant Pot – a versatile kitchen appliance that has taken the culinary world by storm.

If you’re not already familiar with the Instant Pot, it’s essentially a multi-functional electric pressure cooker that can also be used as a slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, sauté pan, and even a yogurt maker. Its ability to cook food quickly under high pressure makes it perfect for busy weeknights when you need to get dinner on the table fast.

To help you make the most of your Instant Pot, we’ve put together a list of 10 easy and delicious recipes that are perfect for quick weeknight dinners. These recipes are not only simple to make, but they’re also packed full of flavor, ensuring that you and your family will enjoy every bite.

  1. Chicken and Rice: This classic comfort food gets a modern twist with the Instant Pot. Simply toss in some chicken thighs, rice, chicken broth, and your favorite seasonings, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. In just a matter of minutes, you’ll have a hearty and satisfying meal that’s sure to please even the pickiest eaters.

  2. Beef Stew: There’s nothing quite like a warm bowl of beef stew on a chilly evening, and thanks to the Instant Pot, you can enjoy this comforting dish in record time. Just throw together some cubed beef, potatoes, carrots, onions, and broth, then let the Instant Pot do its thing. In no time at all, you’ll have a rich and flavorful stew that tastes like it’s been simmering for hours.

  3. Pulled Pork: Craving barbecue but don’t have hours to spare? No problem! With the Instant Pot, you can have tender and juicy pulled pork on the table in under an hour. Simply season a pork shoulder with your favorite spices, add some broth or barbecue sauce, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. Serve the pulled pork on buns with coleslaw for a delicious and satisfying meal.

  4. Vegetable Soup: This hearty vegetable soup is a great way to sneak in some extra veggies and is perfect for those nights when you’re craving something light yet satisfying. Just toss in your favorite vegetables, some broth, and a handful of pasta or rice, and let the Instant Pot do the rest. In no time at all, you’ll have a nutritious and delicious soup that’s perfect for dinner or lunch the next day.

  5. Chili: Craving something hearty and comforting? Look no further than this Instant Pot chili recipe. Simply brown some ground beef or turkey, add in some beans, tomatoes, spices, and broth, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. In just a matter of minutes, you’ll have a rich and flavorful chili that’s perfect for topping with your favorite toppings like cheese, sour cream, and green onions.

  6. Lemon Garlic Chicken: This bright and flavorful dish is perfect for those nights when you’re craving something light yet satisfying. Simply season some chicken breasts with lemon, garlic, and herbs, add some chicken broth, and let the Instant Pot do its thing. In just a matter of minutes, you’ll have tender and juicy chicken that’s perfect for serving with rice, quinoa, or roasted vegetables.

  7. Macaroni and Cheese: Craving comfort food but don’t want to spend hours in the kitchen? No problem! With this Instant Pot macaroni and cheese recipe, you can have creamy and cheesy pasta on the table in under 20 minutes. Simply toss together some pasta, milk, cheese, and spices, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. In no time at all, you’ll have a decadent and indulgent dish that’s perfect for satisfying those comfort food cravings.

  8. Teriyaki Chicken: Skip the takeout and make your own delicious teriyaki chicken at home with this easy Instant Pot recipe. Simply toss together some chicken thighs, soy sauce, honey, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. In just a matter of minutes, you’ll have tender and flavorful chicken that’s perfect for serving with rice and steamed vegetables.

  9. Spaghetti and Meatballs: Craving Italian but don’t want to spend hours slaving over a hot stove? No problem! With this Instant Pot spaghetti and meatballs recipe, you can have a delicious and comforting meal on the table in under 30 minutes. Simply toss together some pasta, marinara sauce, meatballs, and broth, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. In no time at all, you’ll have a hearty and satisfying dish that’s perfect for serving with garlic bread and a salad.

  10. Vegetable Curry: This hearty and flavorful vegetable curry is perfect for those nights when you’re craving something warm and comforting. Simply toss together some vegetables, coconut milk, curry paste, and spices, and let the Instant Pot work its magic. In just a matter of minutes, you’ll have a rich and flavorful curry that’s perfect for serving with rice or naan bread.

In conclusion, the Instant Pot is a game-changer when it comes to cooking quick and delicious meals on busy weeknights. With these 10 easy recipes, you’ll be able to whip up satisfying dinners in no time at all, leaving you with more time to relax and enjoy your evening. So dust off your Instant Pot and get cooking – your taste buds will thank you!
