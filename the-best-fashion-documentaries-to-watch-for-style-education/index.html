The Best Fashion Documentaries to Watch for Style Education


Fashion documentaries offer a captivating glimpse into the world of style, allowing viewers to gain a deeper understanding of fashion icons, trends, and the creative process behind the clothes we wear. Whether you’re a fashion enthusiast or simply curious about the industry, watching fashion documentaries can be an engaging and educational experience.

In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best fashion documentaries that not only entertain but also provide valuable insights for those looking to learn more about fashion and style.

1. ‘The September Issue’ (2009)

‘The September Issue’ is a must-watch documentary that takes you behind the scenes of Vogue magazine’s most important issue of the year. Directed by R.J. Cutler, this film offers a rare glimpse into the intense and often glamorous world of fashion publishing. It follows the legendary editor-in-chief, Anna Wintour, and her team as they navigate the process of creating the iconic September issue. This documentary is a testament to the power and influence of the fashion industry.

2. ‘Dior and I’ (2014)

Directed by Frédéric Tcheng, ‘Dior and I’ offers an intimate look at the creation of Raf Simons’ first haute couture collection for the House of Dior. The film showcases the intricate craftsmanship, meticulous attention to detail, and the immense pressure faced by the design team in bringing Simons’ vision to life. It is a beautifully shot documentary that captures the essence of fashion as an art form.

3. ‘The True Cost’ (2015)

If you’re interested in understanding the impact of the fashion industry on the environment and the lives of garment workers, ‘The True Cost’ is a must-watch. Directed by Andrew Morgan, this eye-opening documentary explores the dark side of fast fashion, tracing the journey of our clothes from production to disposal. It raises important questions about ethical fashion and encourages viewers to think more consciously about their purchasing decisions.

4. ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ (2008)

This documentary by Matt Tyrnauer provides an intimate portrayal of the legendary fashion designer Valentino Garavani. Known for his glamorous creations and extravagant lifestyle, Valentino takes center stage in this film as he prepares for his final haute couture show before retirement. ‘Valentino: The Last Emperor’ is a fascinating exploration of the dedication, passion, and artistry that goes into creating high fashion.

5. ‘Iris’ (2014)

Directed by Albert Maysles, ‘Iris’ offers a glimpse into the life and style of Iris Apfel, an iconic fashion icon known for her eclectic and fearless approach to fashion. This documentary celebrates individuality and the joy of self-expression through clothing, showcasing Apfel’s unique personal style and her impact on the fashion industry. It is an inspiring film that celebrates aging gracefully and embracing one’s own personal style.

6. ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ (2010)

For anyone interested in fashion photography and street style, ‘Bill Cunningham New York’ is a must-watch. Directed by Richard Press, this documentary follows the legendary New York Times fashion photographer Bill Cunningham as he bikes around the city, capturing the most stylish looks on the streets. The film offers a glimpse into Cunningham’s life, work ethics, and his singular dedication to capturing the essence of fashion in its natural environment.

7. ‘McQueen’ (2018)

‘McQueen’ is a powerful documentary that tells the story of the late fashion designer Alexander McQueen. Directed by Ian Bonhôte and Peter Ettedgui, this film delves into McQueen’s extraordinary life, his creative genius, and the dark challenges he faced. With a mix of archival footage, interviews, and behind-the-scenes moments, ‘McQueen’ offers an intimate and raw look at one of the most influential figures in contemporary fashion.

Watching these fashion documentaries not only provides entertainment but also broadens your understanding of the fashion industry. Whether you’re interested in the creative process, the business side of fashion, or the social impact of the industry, these films offer valuable insights and inspiration.

So grab your popcorn, get cozy, and embark on a stylish and educational journey through the world of fashion by watching these incredible documentaries. Happy viewing!
