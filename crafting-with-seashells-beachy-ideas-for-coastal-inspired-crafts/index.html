Crafting with Seashells: Beachy Ideas for Coastal-Inspired Crafts


If you’re someone who loves spending time by the beach, you’ve probably come across seashells scattered along the shore. These beautiful treasures can be found in various shapes, sizes, and colors, making them perfect for crafting. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned crafter, there are plenty of coastal-inspired craft ideas that can be created using seashells. In this blog post, we’ll explore some beachy ideas for crafting with seashells, and how you can incorporate them into your coastal-inspired decor.

1. Seashell Wind Chimes
One of the most popular ways to use seashells in crafting is by creating wind chimes. Seashell wind chimes not only add a coastal touch to your outdoor space but also produce a soothing sound when the wind blows. You can create a simple wind chime by attaching seashells to a piece of driftwood or a metal ring using twine or fishing line. Experiment with different shell sizes and colors to create a visually appealing display.

2. Seashell Jewelry
Another creative way to incorporate seashells into your crafting is by making jewelry. Seashell earrings, necklaces, and bracelets can add a unique touch to your summer outfits. Create a statement piece by selecting larger seashells and pairing them with colorful beads or crystals. You can also make pendants by drilling holes into the shells and attaching them to chains or cords. Don’t forget to coat the shells with a protective sealant to ensure their longevity.

3. Seashell Picture Frames
Give your favorite beach memories a coastal-inspired touch by creating seashell picture frames. Start with a plain wooden frame and use a hot glue gun to attach seashells around the edges. You can also add additional embellishments like starfish, coral, or small pieces of sea glass for added interest. These frames make great gifts and will remind you of the beach every time you look at them.

4. Seashell Candles
If you’re looking to create a beachy ambiance in your home, seashell candles are the perfect choice. To make seashell candles, melt wax flakes in a heat-resistant container and add your favorite essential oils for a refreshing scent. Pour the melted wax into clean seashells and insert a wick through the center. Let the wax cool and solidify before lighting the candle. These unique candles will add a touch of coastal charm to any room.

5. Seashell Wall Art
Transform a plain wall into a coastal paradise by creating seashell wall art. Arrange seashells in a pattern of your choice on a canvas or a wooden board, securing them with hot glue. You can create simple designs like seashell monograms, seashell flowers, or even seashell mandalas. Hang your artwork on the wall or display it on a shelf to bring the beach indoors.

Incorporating seashells into your crafting projects allows you to bring a piece of the beach into your home. Whether you choose to create wind chimes, jewelry, picture frames, candles, or wall art, seashell crafts can add a beachy, coastal vibe to any space. So the next time you visit the beach, don’t forget to collect some seashells and get ready to unleash your creativity!
