Chasing Sunsets: Hotels with Breathtaking Views for the Perfect Evening


When it comes to planning a romantic evening or simply unwinding after a long day, there are few experiences more magical than watching the sunset from a stunning vantage point. Whether you prefer beachfront bliss, a mountaintop retreat, or a cityscape panorama, the world is full of hotels that offer breathtaking views for the perfect evening. From luxurious resorts to boutique hideaways, here are some of the most spectacular places to chase sunsets around the globe.

  1. Amangiri, Utah, USA Tucked away in the heart of the American Southwest, Amangiri is a luxurious desert oasis that offers unparalleled views of the rugged Utah landscape. Guests can watch the sun dip below the horizon from the hotel’s infinity pool or from the comfort of their own private terrace. With its sleek, contemporary design and dramatic desert backdrop, Amangiri is the ultimate destination for sunset seekers.

  2. Katikies Hotel, Santorini, Greece Santorini is famous for its breathtaking sunsets, and there’s no better place to experience them than from the luxurious confines of Katikies Hotel. Perched on the cliffs of Oia, this stunning hotel offers panoramic views of the Aegean Sea and the island’s iconic whitewashed buildings. Guests can savor the sunset from the hotel’s infinity pool or from one of its stylish suites, each of which features its own private veranda.

  3. Pacuare Lodge, Costa Rica For a truly immersive sunset experience, head to Pacuare Lodge in the heart of the Costa Rican rainforest. This eco-friendly retreat is nestled amongst the treetops and offers sweeping views of the lush jungle and the Pacuare River below. Guests can watch the sun set over the canopy from their luxurious bungalow or from the lodge’s open-air restaurant, which serves up delicious farm-to-table cuisine.

  4. Six Senses Yao Noi, Thailand Located on the unspoiled island of Yao Noi in Thailand’s Phang Nga Bay, Six Senses Yao Noi is a tropical paradise with views to match. The hotel’s private villas are perched on the hillside overlooking the bay, offering guests uninterrupted views of the sun setting over the limestone karsts. With its sustainable ethos and stunning natural surroundings, Six Senses Yao Noi is the perfect place to unwind and watch the world fade into twilight.

  5. Azulik, Tulum, Mexico For a sunset experience unlike any other, head to Azulik in the bohemian town of Tulum, Mexico. This eco-friendly resort is known for its avant-garde design and its stunning views of the Caribbean Sea. Guests can watch the sun set over the ocean from their treehouse-style villa or from one of the hotel’s cliffside nests, which offer panoramic views of the coastline. With its focus on sustainability and its breathtaking surroundings, Azulik is the ideal destination for sunset lovers.

  6. Fogo Island Inn, Newfoundland, Canada Perched on the rocky shores of Fogo Island in Newfoundland, Canada, Fogo Island Inn is a striking modern retreat with views that stretch out to the Atlantic Ocean. Guests can watch the sun set over the water from the hotel’s floor-to-ceiling windows or from the comfort of their own cozy room. With its unique architecture and its remote location, Fogo Island Inn offers a sunset experience that is truly one-of-a-kind.

  7. One&Only Reethi Rah, Maldives The Maldives are known for their picture-perfect sunsets, and there’s no better place to enjoy them than from the luxurious confines of One&Only Reethi Rah. This exclusive resort is set on its own private island and offers stunning views of the Indian Ocean from every vantage point. Guests can watch the sun set over the water from their overwater villa or from one of the hotel’s beachfront restaurants, which serve up gourmet cuisine against a backdrop of shimmering seas.

In conclusion, chasing sunsets from the comfort of a luxury hotel is a truly unforgettable experience. Whether you prefer a desert retreat, a tropical paradise, or a cliffside hideaway, there are hotels around the world that offer breathtaking views for the perfect evening. So pack your bags, head to one of these stunning destinations, and prepare to be dazzled as the sun dips below the horizon in a blaze of colors and light.
