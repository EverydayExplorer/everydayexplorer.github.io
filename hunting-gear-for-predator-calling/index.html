Hunting Gear for Predator Calling


Predator calling is a hunting technique that involves mimicking the sounds of prey to attract predators such as coyotes, foxes, and bobcats. This type of hunting requires specialized hunting gear to be successful. Here is a list of equipment that will help you become an expert predator caller.

1. Predator Calls

Predator calls are the most essential hunting gear for predator calling. The best predator call should be able to replicate the sound of different types of prey animals. For example, rabbit distress calls, bird distress calls, and rodent distress calls are most commonly used. The predator calls that are most effective are electronic calls, which produce realistic and loud sounds to attract predators from far away.

2. Decoy

A decoy is a device that resembles the prey and is used to attract predators. Decoys are usually available in the form of stuffed animals or plastic replicas of different types of prey. These devices will help increase your chances of attracting predators. Be sure to place the decoy in a visible location but also in a safe distance from you.

3. Hunting Clothing

Your hunting clothes should be comfortable, durable, and provide protection against the elements. Camouflage is preferred for predator calling hunts as it blends with the surrounding. Invest in a quality hunting jacket, pants, and boots so you can stay comfortable and safe in any weather conditions. It is best to choose clothing made with silent materials that do not produce rustling sounds, which could alert the predators.

4. Hunting Backpacks

A hunting backpack will help you carry all the necessary hunting gear that you need for predator calling. It should be big enough to hold all the items needed but also lightweight for ease of movement. Consider a backpack with multiple compartments, which will help you keep everything organized.

5. Knives and Sharpener

When hunting predators, a good knife is essential. Make sure to carry a sharp and durable hunting knife, which will make it easier for you to skin and field-dress your catch. Also, carry a sharpener to keep your knife in optimal condition.

6. Headlamp or Flashlight

Predator calling sometimes requires hunting in low-light conditions. A headlamp or flashlight will help you navigate through dark terrain. It is essential to choose a light that is bright enough to illuminate objects but also not too bright for the predators to notice.

7. Range Finder

A range finder is necessary for predator calling hunts since predators are fast and stealthy creatures. A range finder will help you determine the distance between you and the predator, allowing you to make accurate shots.

Conclusion

Predator calling can be a thrilling experience. However, to have successful hunts, you need the right hunting gear. Make sure to invest in quality predator calls, decoys, knives, backpacks, headlamps or flashlights, and range finders. Also, choose clothing that provides comfort and protection, which will make your hunting trips more enjoyable. Remember, the gear you choose will play a significant role in determining the success of your predator calling hunt.
